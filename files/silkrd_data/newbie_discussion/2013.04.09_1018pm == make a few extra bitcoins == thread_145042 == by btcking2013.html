Even with a clearnet warning. I would not download anything linked in this forum. A watering hole attack is a very obvious risk. I'm assuming the download is for a pdf, or some other document capable of hosting executable code. The attacker would know that anybody downloading it has an SR account, and likely has BTC in a wallet on their computer, or they regularly access accounts with BTC online. It could also be LE going on a fishing trip. Create a document that when opened pings, or sends some small amount of data to their server. Now they have an IP and can ask your ISP to let them monitor you. Or just put extra scrutiny on your mail. There is absolutely reason to mix illegal activity with anything else. When you are on a site that is used for illegal trade YOU DON'T FUCKING DO ANYTHING ELSE!

If you break that simple rule, and that results in misfortune...too bad. It's not very hard to take common sense precautions. Scammers need people to scam, and police need people to lock up. While both do happen to good people who do take the necessary precautions, at least when it's because of a stupid mistake, it gets rid of stupid people who make everyone else less safe.
