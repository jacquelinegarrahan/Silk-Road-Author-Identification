Title: My experience after 6 months on SR, the very good and the very very bad [READ!]
Post by: Hardcore999 on September 13, 2013, 11:37 pm
Hey,

I from France and I discovered SR by myself in February 13. Now I've already spent 6000$+, mainly on hash and coke + some MDMA and LSD. Here's my review:

the +:
- as a daily cannabis user, it's a great life enhancing: I was used to smoke B+ hash, A class very rare here. In SR I could find the best and most exotic hash produced on earth: premium Morocco, red Lebanese (and soon yellow Leb and Turkish cream thank's to Cloneuk), Charas, Filipino... Even if prices can be 2x what I find here, it's much better;
What I didn't expected is that best products are sold in UK, it's more expensive but there are genuine artist vendors there, hash can be much better that what you can find in coffee shops in Dam;
- as an irregular coke user, it's also a great life enhancing: I couldn't do coke in my city because of the poor quality. On SR I could find very good quality products although it's not easy and that I could find better products through real life trips;
- for MDMA I'm happy I can find clean exctazy (well, I believe they are clean), I find very much nicer to take the product this way;
- I received all the orders I made (30+);

the -:
[READ]
- 3 months ago vendor named Rails came with low cost coke wich appeared to be very nice fishscale; there was a guy named Marlon Brando doing pub for him and other good reviews; I bought 7g for me and friends, transaction was smooth and when I received the product it looked very good indeed and effects were there and strong.
But we all immediatly felt strange symptoms: pressure felt in brain, problem with swallowing saliva; I even went to a doctor but he wasn't worried about my case so I told myself: "ok, it's just very good product I'm not used too, it's all fine". I sniffed like 2.5g of it, sometimes way too much and I had some really bad moments.
So it's now more than 3 months from there, I had other coke since there but I'm now clean for 3 weeks and I still can feel those symptoms almost everyday, they have never stopped nor decreased. I feel them more intensely when I smoke hash.
I've no money to buy me IRM and hear a doctor say: "ok there's nothing to do". I've found similar symptoms by a coke user testimony on internet saying it's permanent cerebral sequels. And it's what I immediately thought when I realized they were there even when I wouldn't sniff. I'm capable to live with it but it sucks. My friends took less of this coke and I don't want to talk about that because I'm ashamed.
So I think Rails coke was a trash and dangerous product (you know coke is manufactured with very dangerous products, it's easy for the producer to miss his product), and that's why it was cheap. The guy Marlon Brando disappeared from the forum from what I can see.

other -:
- I took way too many drugs since I discovered SR, sometimes fun but...
- I took LSD and I don't like it (but it's off topic);
- my hash budget has explode;
- I also had some mid grade hash sold as premium.

conclusion:
- if you just smoke cannabis, SR is paradise and you can't go wrong (but concentrates and oils can be dangerous);
- for other product, SR can be a very good place but it also can be much worse than the street. Products like coke are dangerous, in street the guy you know wouldn't fuck with your health because you know him, he knows you, he will have correct product more or less cut with more or less nocive products, but he won't sell poison. In comparison SR is a wild place but I don't incriminate the site, I'm responsible for my sort.
- my experience reinforce my support for legalization: dangerous products that people use must be quality controlled, it's a responsibility for governments.

tips:
- do not buy cheap products;
- buy from UK;
- when you buy a potentially dangerous product, buy it from a very solid vendor;
- don't rely very much on reviews;

best vendors from my experience :
hash:
- Technohippy: a few orders, all top quality (+ extazy / mdma);
- Atlas2012: a few orders, Rif and Manali are top quality products;
- Cloneuk: 1 order not received yet but he now offers the mythicals yellow Leb and Turkish cream and it's huge;
- Ganjaman: 1 order, made me try good hash from Filippines, how nice!

coke:
- Bungee: 1 order, was pure but not the best coke I had;
- DrCol: 1 order, not pure but very nice;
- CharlieandMolly: 1 order,maybe cut with amphet or leva but very good for one night;
