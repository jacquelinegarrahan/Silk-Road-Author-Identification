Title: Re: FE?? Please help
Post by: android465764E on March 01, 2013, 02:24 am
If you're a new customer, vendors often ask for FE.

Some vendors never ask for FE regardless of your status.

Some vendors ask for FE only on certain items.

It all depends on the vendor.

There's no set rules for FE, but if you DO FE and something goes wrong, you're pretty much on your own.

If you don't FE and the item never arrives, you have options via SR resolve.

In my opinion, you should research the vendor. Check their feedback and check their review threads thoroughly.

FE is always a risk. You effectively pay up-front.
When I first joined, I FE'd once and it was fine.

Some people may get burned, but in all honesty it's your choice.

If it's not a huge amount of money, just do it. Chances are you'll be fine.
