Title: Re: Getting started on SR
Post by: mcguire39 on June 18, 2013, 11:56 pm
Welcome!

1. Right, just look at the PGP stickied thread at the top. It's pretty basic. Just make your own keys and practice it first. You can use something like Kleopatra, or there are some websites that will do it (via Javascript so the data never leaves your computer).

2. Fastest (but not cheapest) way is to get MoneyPak or Reloadit converted to BTC. There's vendors in the 'services' section on SR. MoneyHoney is good and fast.

3. Escrow is automatic; it happens when you buy on SR. Then you 'finalize' after you're happy and that releases the funds to the vendor (or after about 17 days that will happen automatically, but don't wait that long. Just finalize when you receive the item).

4. Yes, don't contact a vendor asking how to encrypt. See #1 above. Read the seller's product description and their vendor page carefully, some have certain ways they want the delivery address, stuff like that. A lot of vendors have every detail spelled out, so there is generally not much reason to msg them to ask questions beforehand. Some will require new buyers to FE, that's finalize early (see #3) above, to guarantee they get their money (sometimes new buyers claim they never received the item even when they did and then want a refund).
