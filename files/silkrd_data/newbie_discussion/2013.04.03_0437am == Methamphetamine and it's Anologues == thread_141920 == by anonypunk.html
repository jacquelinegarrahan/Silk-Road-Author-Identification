Title: Methamphetamine and it's Anologues
Post by: anonypunk on April 03, 2013, 04:37 am
I just wanted to throw this out there after I made a purchase on SR recently. There are companies that sell methamphetamine analogues that can have a crystalline powder look and can be bought as shards as well. The initial feeling is similar to Methamphetamine and they all vary depending on many different factors like ROA, chemical structure, and the user but they dont have a rush that I'm aware of.. I've only had a couple and both had no euphoric high to them but were straight stimulants like caffeine and required much larger doses than you'd normally live through on meth. I'm no meth expert in any way and my favorite has always been cocaine but research the effects of ethylphenidate, methiopropamine (MPA), and propylhexadrine and you'll see that it would be easy to fool someone initially with shards of these chemicals.
