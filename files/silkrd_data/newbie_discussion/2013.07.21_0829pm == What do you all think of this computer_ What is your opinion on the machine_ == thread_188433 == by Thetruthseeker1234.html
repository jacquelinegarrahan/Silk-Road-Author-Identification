Title: Re: What do you all think of this computer? What is your opinion on the machine?
Post by: Annex on July 22, 2013, 09:36 am
I probably wouldn't buy a macbook for gaming at all.
Its also probably not that bright an idea to be doing your personal activities and running a vendor account on the same machine but that may just be me.

As for tails, I'm sitting here on tails running through the very same machine you listed. There are some things you can't do like set up a persistent volume but that is due to be fixed in the next release. It also pretty much forces you to run from a live disc rather that a USB drive. this sounds like it suck but if you use a non-rewriteable DVD it's more secure as nothing can maliciously write to your live disk (like say a trojan or keylogger for example)

I would probably follow the advice above though, build an SR specific machine and use a secure disti of linux like liberte or tails if you want too. Tails is nice because its very user-friendly but, as you can see, it has some detractors

Hope this helps,

Annex
