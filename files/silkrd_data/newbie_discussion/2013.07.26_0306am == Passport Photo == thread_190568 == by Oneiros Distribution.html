I would suggest the following information is supplied with each photo:

Gender: Male or Female, N/A if unknown.
Age: Approximate Age, N/A if unknown.
Ethnicity: A guess on what area of the world the person originates from, N/A if unknown.
Original: The original photo is supplied along with a country of choice by using the "Postage" option so an edited photo for that country is included.

In addition I would recommend the following:

Variation: The photo should preferably have some kind of minor editing each time a new customer gets it, although obviously magic can't be made and the person won't look like another person they shouldn't be able to check that two photos are technically absolutely the same.
Preference: The buyer gets to pick through for example postage what preference they have in terms of Gender, Ethnicity will kind of be automatic if one gets to pick country of choice - if nothing is picked they get an entirely random photo instead.
