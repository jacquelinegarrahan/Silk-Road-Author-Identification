Title: Re: Hi from France
Post by: Ron Swanson on April 21, 2013, 03:49 pm
bonjour! FE = finalise early. this means you pay the seller before they send your order. it is not recommended. try to find a vendor who does not require this. you become trusted by completing more orders, releasing your payment as soon as the order arrives and ideally having 0% refund rate. these are the things that are shown in your buyer stats when you make an order.
the reason many vendors ask for FE is because often new buyers forget to come back and release the payment or try to scam vendors by saying the order never arrived when it did.
