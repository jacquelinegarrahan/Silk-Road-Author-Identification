Title: Re: What is "Good Stealth"
Post by: TA on September 16, 2013, 05:21 am
Moisture barrier bag. Different mbb's have different MVTR ratings. MVTR is moisture vapor transmission rate. For example the best of the best, 3M 3700 series MBB's Have a MVTR of .0003. Meaning if a square meter of the material was in a 100% relative humidity environment .0003 grams of water would pass through the material in 24 hours. Double bag at this rate and you are at nearly zero.


Dont feel bad, if you dont ask you dont know!
