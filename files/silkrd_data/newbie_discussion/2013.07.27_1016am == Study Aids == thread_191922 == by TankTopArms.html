Title: Re: Study Aids
Post by: awkwardalchemist on July 27, 2013, 10:30 am
I recently purchased some amphetamine sulphate powder for just this purpose.  It's super cheap and is essentially identical to Adderall in terms of effects.  Amphetamine sulphate is usually a racemic mixture of dextroamphetamine and levoamphetamine.  Adderall is roughly 75% dexto- and 25% levoamphetamine.

I purchased about 10g (which is a lot, typical doses range from 10mg-80mg) for just over $100.  Really pure stuff, as well.
