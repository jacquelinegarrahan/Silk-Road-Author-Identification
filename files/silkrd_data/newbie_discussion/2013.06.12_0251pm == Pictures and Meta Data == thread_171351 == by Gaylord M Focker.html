Hello, as you will see I have an awesome profile picture. I will now walk you threw this.

 Step one, get a program to delete the META DATA. I use BatchPurifier. Google it, it's clearnet so be careful. Install it.

Based on the program you chose you may now be given several options on what data you wish to remove, remove it all!

When you are done editing, save the newly edited photo and right click on it, go to properties, then select details. If no data shows up, you did good. Then just upload the photo to SR ;)
