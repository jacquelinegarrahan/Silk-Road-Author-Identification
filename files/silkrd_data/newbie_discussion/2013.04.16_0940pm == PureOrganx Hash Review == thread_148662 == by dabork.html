Title: PureOrganx Hash Review
Post by: dabork on April 16, 2013, 09:40 pm
So I finally got around to ordering some good hash, instead of just the cheap Moroccan that's on here.  I wanted to go domestic for obvious reasons and came across PureOrganx.  He had about 60 transactions when I ordered from him but had good feedback and a few good reviews so I went ahead and ordered a gram of the 45 Micron Girl Scout Cookie hash.

He shot me a message a couple days later to let me know I had forgotten the city and state in my address (doh!), and that he had been unable to message me before then because of all the downtime.  He got me shipped out on Friday and messaged me on Monday to let me know it should be showing up soon.  Unfortunately, due to some issue with the address, it ended up being returned to him after making it all the way to my post office.  So by all technicalities it arrived on Monday after ordering on Tuesday night and it being shipped on Friday.  He checked the tracking Wednesday when it had not yet arrived and saw that there was a problem.  A few messages later, and we had it all sorted out and I was set for a reship.  he shipped it back out that Friday and it was in my hands on Monday. 

Now, about the actual product.  First off, the package looked pretty good.  Printed labels covered in tape to make them waterproof.  Absolutely no hint of a smell until all 3 vacuum bags were broken.  He included half a gram of the Humboldt Haze for free.  Whether it was because of the mixup or because of the 4/20 sale, I'm not sure, but free hash isn't really something I'm prone to questioning. 

I loaded up a couple little pebbles of the GSC (it came in several small chunks, whereas the HH was in one stiffer lump) into a bowl.  I knew this wasn't exactly the best way to be smoking it, but I wanted a taste of it quickly without looking for any of the random shit I use when I have more patience.  The first hit was delicious.  Smooth and nutty without any overpowering tastes.  I let it go across my tongue as I hit it as slow as I could and felt the slight numbing I know and love.  I watched the hash as I continued to hit it, and as expected it soon began to bubble.  It didn't sizzle or pop, but it didn't melt down either, so I'd call this half-melt.  After about 4 strong hits, the bowl was done, and I was decidedly high.  I wouldn't get a chance to try the HH for about 2 more hours because of how distracted I was.  It was a very cerebral high with no couch lock to speak of and it creeped up for about an hour after I smoked.  It left enough of a body tingle to lull me into a dreamy state but didn't come off as "dopey".  Overall, it was easily some of the best hash I have had the pleasure of trying, and is the next best thing to some grade A full-melt, which makes it great for people on a bit of a budget. 

The Humboldt Haze is some pretty standard burner hash.  Tastes decent, leaves me pretty stoned, and makes a great bowl topper.  Of course it's nothing compared to the GSC, but it's not supposed to be, and it was free, so I'm more than satisfied. 

Overall, PureOrganx was a pleasure to deal with from beginning to end.  He did everything in his power to make things go smoothly even after a couple bumps in the road and was easily one of the best vendors I've bought from.  He's pretty new but if he keeps up the work ethic he's got right now, he'll fit in here just fine.
I'll definitely be buying from him again, and I'd encourage anyone in the US looking for a good domestic hash man to give him a shot too.
