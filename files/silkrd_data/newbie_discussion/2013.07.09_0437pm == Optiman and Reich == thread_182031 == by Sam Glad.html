Yesterday I received two packages. 

One was a 100 x 0.01 g scale ordered from Optiman.  The scale looks cheap, but that's what I expected from a $20 scale.  I tried it out last night.  It seems to work just fine.

The other package was 500mg of Ketamine from Reich.  He is on a vacation right now, so the review won't help anybody. This is my first time with ketamine, so it's not all that helpful anyway.  The product weighed out to just over 500 mg.   I made myself a pile of .07 - .08 g (70 - 80 mg) and cut it into five lines.  I was cautious, so I took about 30 min to do four of them, reached a point where I felt comfortable and then left the last one for later.

A truly unique experience ketamine is.  I felt like I was intoxicated on alcohol, but didn't have the dumbness/dizziness.  I felt sedated as on morphine, but without the difficulty performing actions.  For me to have reached this sedation on morphine I would have been unable to type, but on ketamine typing was manageable.  I can't wait to give it another try.  With friends.
