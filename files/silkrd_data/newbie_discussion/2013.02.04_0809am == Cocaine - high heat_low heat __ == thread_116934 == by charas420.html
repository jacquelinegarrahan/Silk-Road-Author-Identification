Post by: p3nd8s on February 04, 2013, 09:16 am
I am an expert in Cocaine but hi, med, and  low heat cocaine? WTF is that, and who came up with it?
It must be street  slang for something, definitely not found in scientific literature.

Btw, cocaine freebase (crack) decomposes at about 90 degrees celsius, so if you overheat it, the molecule will decompose and it will lose its narcotic  effect (no euphoria, no nothing). On the other hand, cocaine HCl decomposes at about 185 degrees celsius.

So those of you cooking up crack be careful with the temperatures, use a thermometer to make sure you don't go above 90 degrees, keep it low, not higher than 80 degrees Celsius to be safe. If you go above, you lose the product.
