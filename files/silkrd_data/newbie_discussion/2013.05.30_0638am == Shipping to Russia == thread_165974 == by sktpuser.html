I would like to tell all the sellers about shipping to Russian Federation - be ready to face the fact that all of your sending can come within 1.5-2.5 mnths.

I've ordered twice unsigned letter from Canada - both mails arrived after 2 months and 10 or 15 days.
I've ordered package from Ireland (which is much closer to RU than overseas) - the situation was the same.
I've ordered from CANNA INC (German vendor) and the package spent in the way about 1 month and 20-25 days

I'm honestly warning all my respective sellers that it can be huge timespan before package will arrive and beforehand excusing for our postal system. but i got all of my mails. sooner or later they all are coming straight to my drop address (except vendor scammer i.e. LucyDrop experience for example)
