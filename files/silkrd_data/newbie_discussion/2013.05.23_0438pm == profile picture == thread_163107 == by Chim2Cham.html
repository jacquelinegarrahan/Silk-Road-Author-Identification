Once you login to the forums you can click "Profile" at the top, then choose "Forum Profile" from the drop down.  It should take you right to the "Modify Profile" page.  On that page you can select Choose an Avatar from the Gallery or Upload an Avatar. 

If you already knew that and you wanted to know specifically how to upload an avatar photo.  I normally just save an image to my desktop or somewhere, then I would just have to browse for it in the Upload Avatar option and it uploads it automatically, let me know if it works for you.
