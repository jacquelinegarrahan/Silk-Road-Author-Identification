Soak them all in some high proof alcohol, denatured works well. Stir them every 20-30 mins for about 30 seconds. Let them settle until the top layer (alcohol/amphetamine) is clear orange red, in your case blue.
Pour this through 4-5 filter papers in a Buchner funnel, which is fed into a Flask, Erlenmeyer is my preference.
Heat liquid in flask to a slight boil, pull off heat add about a third of the volume of blue amphetamine liquid with acetone.
