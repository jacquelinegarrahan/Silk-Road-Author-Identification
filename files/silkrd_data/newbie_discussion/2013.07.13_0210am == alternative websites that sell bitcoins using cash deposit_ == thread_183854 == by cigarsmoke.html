Title: Re: alternative websites that sell bitcoins using cash deposit?
Post by: PowerToCharm on July 13, 2013, 03:29 am
Coin vendors on Localbitcoin choose how they will receive funds, and some accept cash deposit into a national bank, typically either Bank of America, Citibank, or Wells Fargo. Escrow protects you from the vendor running with your cash, but expect to pay as much as ten dollars per coin above the Mt. Gox average. It's safe, fast, convenient, just expensive. But bitcoin is on the rise right now, so if you bought some coins tomorrow and held them for a few days, you'd probably end up losing no money at all.
