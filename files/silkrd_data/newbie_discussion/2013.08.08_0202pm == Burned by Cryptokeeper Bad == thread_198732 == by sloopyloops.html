I sent my info and moneypak number and gave him the 24 hours he said it would take- at the longest. Most buyers claim it was only a few hours.

Nothing. Started messaging. Not being a dick, just wondering what's going on...are u there? I figure fuck this guy I'll just deposit my moneypak into my paypal account and find another seller. Got there and Paypal informed me that my moneypak was worth zero. Motherfucker already had my money.

Not that it really matters, but that money was for medication. I am a single Mom with a slipped disc and a torn rotator cuff. I live in a state that is so anti opiates that you have to be damn near dead before you get a script bigger than 20 vics. So the pills I buy on the road help me so much. With the pain managed I can actually do things with my kids. It's summertime, I wanna go hiking and swimming with them. I wanna live like a semi-healthy individual. He only stole 215.00 from me but I don't make a lot of money, and that's a lot for me. So I won't be able to buy my meds for two weeks, and it sucks. Actually, it makes me cry.

Not that I think my story makes me better than anyone else. If you are on the road to get high you should be shown respect and not ripped off. I know crypto has done this before, and I suspect he is targeting newbies. You know, if I auto-finalize that will be on my stats forever, but it's the only way to leave the horrible feedback that he deserves. Or I cancel it myself, report him and never buy btc on the road again.

I haven't decided what to do. Any advice would be greatly appreciated. Thanks for stopping to read.
