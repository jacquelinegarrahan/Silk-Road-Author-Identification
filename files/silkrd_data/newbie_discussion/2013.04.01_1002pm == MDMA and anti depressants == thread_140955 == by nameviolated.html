Quote from: nameviolated on April 02, 2013, 01:56 am
Thanks for the info, I take Pristiq and Abilify, I will have to do some checking around to see if they have SSRI in them.

SSRI refers to how the drug works, it's not something that's "in" the drug.

Desvenlafaxine(Pristiq) is an SNRI(Like an SSRI but it works for noradrenaline as well), for all intensive purposes, that means you should not take MDMA if you have taken Pristiq recently(A month or so).

Aripiprazole(Abilify) is a dopamine agonist, meaning it replicates dopamine(Probably inaccurate, so take this with a dose of skepticism), it would probably be a bad idea to take MDMA with this, because these drugs are generally used for people who do not have normal levels of dopamine.

If you are prescribed these drugs it would probably be safer to stick to cannabis for recreational use.
