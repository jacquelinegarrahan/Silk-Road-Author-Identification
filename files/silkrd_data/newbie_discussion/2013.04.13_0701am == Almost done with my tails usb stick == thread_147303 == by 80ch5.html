pertinent info for anyone trying to do this with a MBP:
intel-based Macbook pro's will NOT be able to boot from live usb stick.
I went as far as to boot from a dvd with rEFIt burned on it (with the tails stick plugged in). rEFIt recognized and displayed the usb stick as linux (I jumped up and gave the air a fist of success), I tried to boot from it and strangely my computer booted up my windows7 partition....(really killed my party)
HOWEVER, you can boot from tails dvd, and make a usbstick that way.
Macbook airs, on the other hand, and maybe chromebooks(although I don't know anything about built in security problems) may be able to boot from a usb stick.
