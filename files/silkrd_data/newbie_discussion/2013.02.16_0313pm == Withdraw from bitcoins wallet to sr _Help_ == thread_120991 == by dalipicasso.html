Title: Re: Withdraw from bitcoins wallet to sr *Help*
Post by: maple on February 16, 2013, 05:46 pm
I am assuming you are trying to move money INTO your SR account.

Open up your bitcoin wallet, go to send coins. On your SR account page you will see a BTC address, this is the address you need to send the coins to for them to show up in your account. Copy and paste the address, hit send, and there you go.
