Well, you'll soon get used to the volatility of bitcoins! Sometimes it's in your favour, sometimes you get nailed. Best thing is to get them on the same day as you plan to spend them and you shouldn't get too screwed.

I suggest spendbitcoins.com - you can go to several bank branches and pass cash over the counter and they will deposit them.

Also, you could hang out on the mining websites/irc forums - you might find people wanting to offload some. BUT - I find it ends up being quite expensive that way, because people want to hold on to them in case they increase in value (more)

Good luck!
