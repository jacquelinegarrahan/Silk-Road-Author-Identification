Title: Re: Weed afficianados; do you....
Post by: t-bone on May 28, 2013, 06:01 pm
Its quite easy, so once you have your liquid green butter run it through the food processor with the sweated shallots and freshly chopped tarragon.

It will firm up like whipped butter. then use a flexible spatula and scrape it out onto some rolled out seran wrap/cling film in an even length across and enclose.

Roll it up to a cylinder shape, tie off the ends and refrigerate. then every time you have a nice grilled steak that's been properly rested, cut off a little disc and enjoy!

If you need to add a splash of vinegar to prevent the butter from splitting in food processor.
