Title: Re: Connecting to SR via TOR through 'Anonymous' VPN??
Post by: donkeykong1234 on August 15, 2013, 01:29 pm
VPN first and then run TOR through your VPN. The VPN gives you end to end encryption and it's pretty commonly used for businesses so your traffic blends in and then the VPN provider just looks like they are connecting to TOR instead of you.

If you find a good anonymous one in another country, it makes the possibility of someone tracing you all the way nearly statistically impossible based on traffic analysis alone. Most people get in trouble because of their mouth though.
