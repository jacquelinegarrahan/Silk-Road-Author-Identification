I was with you right up until I got here:
Quote
I messaged the vendor who initially side stepped my gentle inquiry about quality - then received a second message from the vendor saying that they had had another customer complaint of a similar nature and that they would re check with their supplier.

OK. First, I doubt that he's testing every batch. But even if he were, it's possible that he stepped on it and just got a little to carried away. No coke is 100% pure. Anyone telling you that is full of shit. Same is try for any manufactured street drug. There's a cut at every level.

With that said, look at it from his perspective. It's possible he didn't know how bad it was stepped on (he may not be into coke). Also, there are so many asshole buyers (I'm a buyer; I've never been a vendor so this isn't a vendor rant) who try to scam vendors claiming their bags don't make weight or aren't good enough quality wise. I'd be skeptical if only one person complained; he admitted to you that he had another complaint (he probably waited for that before getting back to you) and said he'd look into it.

No here's how you handle it. You make it clear that you're unhappy, ask very nicely if there's anything he can do for you because you'd like to maintain a business relationship with him. If he refuses, you never buy from him again and you come here and write a bad review. Most of the vendors here are shrewd business people (some are Grade A morons, but they are the exception to the rule).

It's sounds like what you got had some amount of cocaine in it (I had an 8 ball a day habit when I was in high school, so I know quite a bit about coke). It was just shit quality. Use it as a learning experience and move on. You can't use illegal drugs without getting burnt from time to time.

Sorry it happened to you, but accept it, move on, and learn from it.  :)

Oh, and one more thing:
Quote
so anyway i guess i realised that if a vendor thinks your a chump or low stats etc they can pretty much sell you any old weak shit if they have a great reputation
Report him if you believe he scammed you. You can get away with that once or twice, but if everyone writes a negative review, gives low feedback, and reports them, then they are history. That's the power that buyers have collectively.

Finally, for any drug like coke, you shouldn't be finalizing before you sample it. It's easier to get a vendor to work to make things right BEFORE YOU FINALIZE. I never finalize for Adderall until I can feel the effects (I'm not the reagent type -- I'm a great chemist, but too fucking lazy for that LOL).

Good luck to you in the future! Bon soir mon frere!

Yeah this really is the root of alot of problems with any source of illegal drugs... testing is great but every batch is gonna be different. There's huge incentives on the vendor side to provide a great quality product only when you know it'll be tested, or to have different qualities on hand to use depending on the type of buyer, to cut the fuck out of your product, and so on. Unscrupulous vendors do even better here where they can be anonymous and the worst thing they have to fear from a disgruntled buyer is a strongly worded review...

Some folks have started "Avenger" threads trying to deal with this problem for other drugs like MDMA, LSD, etc. It's tough work, made all the more difficult since there is no easy way to determine quality.
