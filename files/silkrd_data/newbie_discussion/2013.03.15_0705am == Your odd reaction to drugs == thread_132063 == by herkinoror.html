So I know there have got to be some other people out there like myself that get queer reactions to drugs.  Let us share and discuss till 50 posts! :)

I personally am what the Shulgins termed a "hard head" much to my chagrin and befuddlement.  I need sometimes signifigantly more of whatever substance we are consuming then the people I am with to get to a similar level of effects.  For instance;  with hatter26s BTH cold shoot most of my cohorts got their rocks off at between 10 - 15mls of the stuff whereas the bottom of my "I'm defenitley pleasantly high curve" started at 20mls and extended all the way up to thirty for a good hard nod.  This is with no tolerance mind you.  And I love 2C-E.  It's one of my favourite substances.  I've got a pre-illegalization Gram sitting around for going to shows and for combining with other substances.  But I pack my caps fat. Typically between 25 - 30mgs.  When I give these to other people they lose their shit.  Now with psychedelics in general, specifically phenethylamines, I get the same sort of reaction.  I feel the threshold at the same level as everyone else; I had a VERY fun trip on just 7 mgs of 2C-I and again on 15 mgs however I vary rarely experience strong visuals.  Shrooms though.  They seem to get me though.  I assume all of this however has to do/come from my child hood and my (probably) odd neurochemistry and phisiology.  I'm a type one Diabetic, have been since the age of 2 and I'm apperently an ADHD kid.  Thusly on top of the insulin I was prescribed all the powerful ADHD stimulants since ealry childhood.  I think I may have been given Desoxyn one time.  But I digress.

I also don't seem to "get" the same feeling most people get from some of the most popular drugs.  I often wonder if this is just my personal taste or if it hearkens back to my body/brain.  I don't much like the effects of Booze,  I used to like weed a little but now I just get really paranoid and loopy from a small amount and I seem to be sensitive to both of these in stark opposition to many other substances.  The couple times I've gotten my hands on pure MDMA I have not liked it much at all even though I do love other  drugs like it (Mephedrone, Methylone, Butylone, Meth, and Amphetamine are all great).  When I finally got a full dose all I wanted to do was Zen out.  I didn't want to think, breathe, experience, or be.  I wanted to not.  And I certainly did not want to get up and dance, or talk to , or hug my friends.  I have however had experiences like those which are from what I understand typical of MDMA on Mephedrone, and felt the awe and raptre of exploring a fresh new world while playing portal 2 on Methylone. 

So what gives?  Do any of ya'll out there have similar stories to share?  POSTPOSTPOST!!! ;D
