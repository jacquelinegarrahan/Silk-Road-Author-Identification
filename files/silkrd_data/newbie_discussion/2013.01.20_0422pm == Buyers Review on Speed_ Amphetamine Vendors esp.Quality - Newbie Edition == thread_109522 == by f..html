Title: Re: Buyers Review on Speed/ Amphetamine Vendors esp.Quality - Newbie Edition
Post by: kissthemoon on January 27, 2013, 05:45 pm
Bought a gram from CapeCrusader, received the product in 12 days, delivered to the EU.

Products looks fine, white/slighly yellow powder, almost completely dry. Dont have a scale so can't comment on the weight.

As for the effects of the drug, I don't have any previous experiences with speed so can't compare the quality, but for what it's worth;

I didn't have a scale, so started with very small line and waited to see where it got me. After sniffing a couple of very small lines I got a nice buzz going. The stuff isn't bad on the nose imo, a slight burn for a couple seconds, kinda enjoyed it :p The drip wasn't bas either.
Experienced an energy rush and some euphoria. Felt very clearheaded, noticed an increase in focus, normally I'm easily disctracted but now I could focus on tasks with ease. The energy was mainly in my head, the product was very easy on my body. Did have tense yaws and wide eyes, also a bit dilated pupils. All in all the feeling of this speed was very enjoyable.
In about 1-1,5 hours after the last dose, the energy rush seemed to die down. Was still very awake tho, just no energy rush or euphoria anymore. Redosed a couple of times. The only negative thing I noticed when using this speed was that I was having very cold hands and overall felt pretty cold. Turning up the heat didn't help much.
Checked my resting pulse between doses a couple times, was at 80-100 most of the time, and at 120 after a relative big line. I checked this just out of curiousity, I never felt my heart beating hard or anything. Stopped redosing after 7 hours from the start.

Started this experiment at 11:00 and stopped around 18:00. Felt the urge to redose but didn't know yet how I would react to speed sleep wise, and didn't want to risk a sleepless night, so didn't redose. Wasn't hungry at all, but felt an emptyness in my stumach so forced myself to eat a meal. At 03:00 I was still awake, feeling kinda tired but awake at the same time. Suspecting I would lie awake if I would go to bed in this state, I popped a 5mg valium and 0,5mg xanax and a glass of wine, fell asleep in minutes without any troubles. Slept for 7 hours, feeling fine the day after, no hangover whatsoever, maybe a tiny bit groggy but that might just as well came from the benzo's.
