Title: Re: Dealing with paranoia and stress
Post by: hornblower on January 27, 2013, 09:29 pm
I think you should reexamine how you define profit.

Sounds like you're financially profiting from your business BUT suffering emotionally and mentally.

This is similar to those who are unhappy with their day jobs with the following important distinction:

You're suffering from FEAR not unhappiness.  FEAR that you will get caught.  FEAR that, if caught, you'll lose your freedom.  FEAR that, if you lose your freedom, your life as you enjoy it will be over.

The problem, however, is that if you're suffering from as much anxiety and paranoia as you say, then you're not exactly enjoying your life with your financial profits.  And if that's the case, then you're not really profiting from your business, at all.  In my opinion, you're actually operating at a loss!

Final note:  The mind can do terrible things, and it can actually betray you.  Your unconscious mind might trip you up so that you do get caught (as a defensive act) just to relieve the overwhelming pressure of fear of getting caught!

I wish you the best of luck!
