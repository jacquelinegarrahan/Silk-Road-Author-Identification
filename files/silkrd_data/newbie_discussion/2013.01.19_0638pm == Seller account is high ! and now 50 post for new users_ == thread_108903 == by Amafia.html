   I became a vendor a few months ago so apologise if I'm wrong and things have changed but I don't think there's an initial seller fee, just a $500 bond that is returned to you if you meet SR vendor reqs. SR charges fees for transactions only. Side note, because you get the amount of coin you deposited, with btc fluctuations, it's possible to make a profit on your bond. I received, in dollar terms, almost double what I gave. I'm not sure if you can also lose on it, but probably.

   The 50 post thing, in theory, is to encourage SR newbies to educate themselves and become familiar with the fundamentals of the Road, which is particularly necessary for individuals interested in becoming vendors. Good luck with your vendor endeavours :), AB.
