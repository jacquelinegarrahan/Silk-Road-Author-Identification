Title: Re: How do vendors feel about shipping to Japan??
Post by: drinkNlove on September 16, 2013, 10:22 am
I'm sure there are PLENTY of vendors out there that don't mind sending things to Japan.

Just check under "Item Info" on a particular item (it's usually located on the top right of the listing). It will say where it is shipped from (some don't disclose that info) and it'll say where they'll ship to. If it says "worldwide," then, considering Japan is in the world, they'll send it there no problem!

Some may even say that it ships strictly to Japan, but I've never seen one. Most of the time when it says it'll only ship to one particular country, it's the country from which it is being sent.


So, yeah. There's plenty of vendors that'll send to Japan. Just go have a look!
