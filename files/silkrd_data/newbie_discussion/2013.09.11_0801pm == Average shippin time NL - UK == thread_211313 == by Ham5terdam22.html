Title: Re: Average shippin time NL - UK
Post by: Rotaluc on September 11, 2013, 09:46 pm
I recently got a package from the UK (not from SR), took 6 days to arrive in NL. It's the other way around, but still, same distance. ;)

I wouldn't worry, not yet. I've seen packages take longer. Just don't finalize early. If customs would seize anything, you would get a Love Letter from them. As long as you hear nothing, you're good and the package is still on it's way or just lost.

If you get a Love Letter, ignore it. Customs will destroy the package after a while and nothing else will happen. If they actively call you (they probably won't), tell them you have no idea what the hell this is all about. Nobody can prove (unless they find something on your computer if they have a search warrant - which they don't get for a couple of pills) you've ordered anything.

If you've ordered 50 pounds of pot, well, godspeed.
