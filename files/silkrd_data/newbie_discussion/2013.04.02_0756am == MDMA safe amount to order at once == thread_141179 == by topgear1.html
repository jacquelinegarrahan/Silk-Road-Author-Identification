Title: Re: MDMA safe amount to order at once
Post by: Hanzelpie on April 04, 2013, 05:53 am
I've ordered stateside and intl and basically it comes down to whether your vendor is careful or not.

MDMA is quite stinky if you really take time to sniff it, and imagine trained animals if you can smell something. It must be packed so that no residue is left outside the sealed bag and measures taken to obfuscate contents from x-rays.

As a rule, I never order from vendors who don't give GPG keys -- or worse no GPG key and they suggest you use some website that logs your IP to paste your address... thats even worse than entering your addy plaintext into the SR form imo. This has served me well in avoiding negligent vendors thus far.

When giving feedback, make sure to VAGUELY note the discreetness and security of how it was packed. Example "very discreet, vacuumed, no telltales A+" bad example... "Shipped in blue envelope from Uiop, Estonia with a yellow foil bag" lol. Be verbose in feedback without compromising the vendor's security (too much details aids in customs profiling...)

Remember you can edit your feedback to review purity, weight etc later. And if asking a vendor something with sensitive details, encrypt your msg for your sake and theirs.

Verbose feedback goes a long way to keeping SR healthy. For new vendors, dont be too harsh about these things, approach them with links to the tutorials here about GPG, packing methods, etc but veteran vendors who refuse to use GPG are imo also going to be prone to shipping in ways that result in seizure love letters.

No offense intended to vendors OR buyers who send their dox plaintext -- thats your prerogative I am not judging you :)
