If you hang around the forums long enough you'll figure it out.  I've been around SR for 6 mo and I'd say that the largest reason is that there are so many dumb questions that could be answered by a forum search and reading a few threads.

The experience that buyers and vendors have gained (especially those who form the core of these forums) is hard earned.  I have literally spent hours before scouring posts for info on tor security, BTC, vendor reviews, anything you can think of. 

Members with this knowledge are generally very helpful to newbies who can show some initiative, but nobody wants to spoonfeed and already exploding SR population.  Not because they're being exclusive, but its not doing you any favors to learn the simplest step-by-step tutorials.  If you really want to use SR safely and reliable there is a very large learning curve, but its 100% worth the time!
