Title: Re: Anyone have some advice for a new seller?
Post by: jekyll on June 05, 2013, 03:43 am
Most new vendors have to give away, or practically give away, some number of samples, to generate feedback.  It's hard to get the first few customers because with no feedback, people have to risk the unknown.  I like to order small amounts from new vendors, because their prices are usually good, and sometimes you find a great new source, but addies aren't my thing. It won't take too many though, just enough to show that your packaging won't get anyone arrested and that your products are legit. Good luck.
