This could be good or bad, depends on your mindset.  Does camping alone [no one else in sight] in a national forest for a week sound like an awesome vacation?  If so, you are probably going to be ok alone on a low dose of acid.  If it sounds terrifying, well, don't do acid alone  ;).

Only smoked weed for a month, huh?  Have you ever gotten so out-of-your-head you started thinking really, really, _really_ strange things about alternate realities?  Talked to inanimate objects?  Thought you overdosed and worried you were going to die?
If you got through those ok, then you'll probably be ok on a low dose of acid.  If you _haven't_ ever been to a place in your head where you doubted your own reality yet, probably don't try acid alone.

Title: Re: Questions about LSD
Post by: hayzeus on August 01, 2013, 06:21 pm
If you are as drug naive as you say, I would be careful.  I would recommend if you have to do it by yourself then just take half a hit for your first time.  Go out in to the woods, get with nature, take half a hit of LSD and go hug some trees.  If after 2 hours of so you feel good, take the other half of the hit.

If everything goes well, next time you do it (wait at least 2-3 weeks to get your tolerance back down to zero) take 1 full hit.  Practice taking that size dose a couple times.  Next time try taking 2.  Spend some time in that head space for a while and learn what you can.  When you are REALLY ready - Dig in a take a full trip.  Drop 3 and get ready for a beautiful time.

Mind state and setting is everything.  Read up as much as you can on erowid.org about how to make sure you understand what all this means.  Read a bunch of trip reports and be prepared.  AND HAVE FUN!  LSD is the most beautiful molecule on this planet.   ;D

Post by: doodoo79 on August 02, 2013, 04:03 am
My two cents... If you're new to psychedelics then I would defo recommend trying LSD before 2CB. LSD can be used as standard to judge other psychedelics against. It has a little of everything. OEV's, CEV's, head trip, and body load. Not extremely intense in one specific category like most RC's are. I've been into psychedelics for about 20 years now and played with a good portion of the RC's out there. Now I'm not trying to tell you what to do or sound like a know it all, because I certainly don't know it all, but if I was in your shoes I would start with getting LSD, MDMA, and mushrooms under my belt before moving on to the exotics. Some RC's can be overwhelming even to experienced psychonauts. Knock out the big 3 and see what you're more into; visuals, tactile stimulation, soul searching, and so on. You may want to rethink tripping alone, especially on your first trip. Could go really well, could be a total disaster. Good luck , be safe, and have fun.
