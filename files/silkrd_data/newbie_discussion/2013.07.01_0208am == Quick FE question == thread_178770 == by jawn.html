First of all NEVER FE...ever. If a vendor makes you FE because of the rapidly fluctuting costs of the bitcoin(or at least use that as an excuse) inform him of escrow heding which is a silk road service which takes 4% of the order cost and if the bitcoin goes up in value they get less BTC, if it goes up they get more.

Another reason would be a huge order, the fact that you are asking me this question tells me that you aren't making a huge order and if you are...dont'.

Anyways to get back to the topic at hand, no.
When you finalize your order has been processed, it's gone. Over.

You aren't even allowed to finalize until after the vendor marks it "shipped" anyways.

But never ever ever FE, the escrow system is all that is protecting you from a greedy or untrustworthy vendor saying "fuck off".
