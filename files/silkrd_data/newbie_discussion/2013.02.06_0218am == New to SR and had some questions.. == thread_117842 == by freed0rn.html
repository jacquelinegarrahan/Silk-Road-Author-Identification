I have about 6 orders so far.  Some vendors respond to messages, some dont.  Some expect u to order without asking any questions at all, if u ask too many questions they get annoyed and dont want to sell to you.

Title: Re: New to SR and had some questions..
Post by: chaosmix on February 06, 2013, 04:05 am
As you may have heard there are changes at the Post office, these cchanges are making it hard for vendors to purchase tracking for their items to ensure they dont get ripped off. they actually have to go inside and purchase the tracking. vendors with lots of orders who still want tracking for their items have to make drops at other post offices because it would look funny if some guy in a hat and shades was dropping off 10=15 packages a day when tracking can be purchased online.the problem w going inside is that if LE orders from them then it would be possible for them to go and check the cctv at the post office where the item was sent from and possibly start investigating the vendor.
