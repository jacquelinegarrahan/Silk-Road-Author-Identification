Title: Re: Australian Weed Dealers Why don't you grow?
Post by: Dingo Ate My Drugs on April 02, 2013, 12:49 pm
I could never see the point in importing weed into Australia & I have never known anyone or heard of anyone who has imported weed. Waste of time - The risk of it getting seized is too high for the marginally lower cost overseas. Obviously importing MDMA / Amphetamine / Cocaine / Heroin would be more worth while because the cost is much, much lower overseas.

If you want to grow weed AND sell weed but not in massive quantities, you need a grow house unless you want to be the stupid person who grows weed in their own home and sells it as well. If you get caught selling 1 ounce weed, the cops are most likely going to go back and search your home. If you were growing at home they found find that as well. So to sell and grow, you really need a separate location to grow at. It's not essential, but as a weed vendor, I would never take that risk.

At the moment I don't have another location which it would be safe to grow at, so that's why I buy my stock. The grower gets the easiest money, but has the most risk. The higher the risk, the bigger the return. In the future I would like to be able to have a good setup and grow my own weed - But not until I have everything planned out and organised.

Also, whilst I don't know much about growing weed yet, I do know that an average outdoor plant yields 6-9 ounces. I know the weed I sell comes from a plant that yields about 8-9 ounces per tree. But this is variant when weather is abnormal.
