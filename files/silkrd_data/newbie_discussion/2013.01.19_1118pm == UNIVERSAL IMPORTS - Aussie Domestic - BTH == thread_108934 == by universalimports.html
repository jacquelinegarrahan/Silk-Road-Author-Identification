This vendor is really good for delivery speed and service!  He's also willing to work problems through very easily, so full marks for UI!

I think buyer reports are a particularly good idea here, because there is a LOT of contradictory information on the web about what BTH is and how it should be administered - particularly for injection!   Probably this is because the BTH itself probably varies a shitload in composition & properties between different batches, etc.  I'm on an IV kick now, so I did some reading around in preparation.

This stuff doesn't smell vinegary, and tastes bitter.  So, it seems like the acetic acid from the manufacture has been effectively quenched.  This is apparently not the case with some mexican BTH, as seen in the west US (according to online forums).  This suggested to me that some citric acid might help dissolution (since the H would be in base form in BTH).  For BTH with unquenched acetic in it, there may be enough acid in the BTH to promote dissolution (consistent with apparent lack of acid use, according to online forums).

I've tried two shots of this stuff.  It gets me high, but is clearly not as powerful per unit weight as the best #4 on offer. 

Here are some notes on my two shooting experiences so far:

GET A WHEEL FILTER IF YOU ARE GOING TO SHOOT THIS!!!  Seriously, anybody who wants to shoot this stuff needs to use a wheel filter.  There is a SHITLOAD of apparently insoluble material, of varying size.  I recommend 0.2 micron, because its clear from reading around that some nasty bacteria can live just fine in and on BTH.  They can be purchased from needle exchanges for ~1 AUD.

I've tried this twice so far, both times shooting, and I have to say it's bloody hell to work with.  Both times I ended up with more water than I wanted (trying to dissolve it), and I've had to divide up shots because of this (ended up with >1cc!  I don't like shooting any more volume than 50 units in a shot, normally). 

The first time I didn't know what I was doing, and probably ended up wasting half the shot.  This is just because I wasn't patient enough waiting for the shit to dissolve!  The second time I heated the mix 3-4 times in cycles, letting it cool each time before reheating.  I even put in enough citric acid to make the sourness swamp the bitterness of the gear, but I'm not sure this helped.  Before shooting, I added some bicarb of soda to bring the pH back up a bit.  I don't want acid-scarred veins!  I got the citric (McKenzie's) at Foodworks, in the baking section.

The stuff is effective, but nowhere near like pure #4 in equal amounts.  I divided my sample into 8 pieces by rolling into a bar and cutting equal pieces.  UI says that the samples are mostly 1-2 points, so the best estimate is ~19mg/piece.  This would be a 2-4X standard dose for pure H, according to various sources.  I'm high but not nodding right now, some 20 min after injection of one such piece.

I will continue to post as I try to continue to work with this stuff to get it into a good shot.  Hopefully, this will help my peers.  From what I can glean, BTH is not the most common form of H in Oz -- although I've heard of homebake being a sometime product of WA, and from what I understand BTH and homebake are essentially the same thing.
