Post by: Cynosure on March 15, 2013, 06:54 pm
In my area, there are all types of marijuana available.

A lot of the poorer people are smoking, dealing, and trade very low quality stuff.  It's around $90-100/oz and is a dark nasty green with a disgusting sewage-like smell.  It's hard as a rock, has lots of seeds, and is flat (almost looks like it was once crystally and was washed to make has and then re-dried and sold).  Shit's gross, but it's everywhere.

The majority of stuff is mid-grade.  It can range from the price of schwag to the price of medical depending upon which area you get it from.  Most college students are smoking this stuff now adays.  It's super dry.  This is what most of my friends end up with.  I haven't messed with it much.

Since the legalization in the states out west, we have been getting in some good medical stuff (indoor and outdoor).  It has been what I've been paying out of my ass to get for about a year now.  It seems to come in from very few sources and I refuse to pay $20-25/g and was buying by the ounce at $340.  No one seems to have steady supply, so bouncing around to dealers is required (and they bullshit and delay a lot).  My first order here on the road was this quality bud at over $100 less per ounce.

The extreme, high-quality, organicm etc. medical grade is unheard of here.  Occasionally you can find a bowl pack of it from someone who paid out of the ass for a gram from a friend visiting from Colorado.
