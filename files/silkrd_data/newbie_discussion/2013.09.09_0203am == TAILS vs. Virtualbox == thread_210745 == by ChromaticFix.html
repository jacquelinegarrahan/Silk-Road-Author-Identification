Title: Re: TAILS vs. Virtualbox
Post by: neplusultra on September 09, 2013, 03:03 am
If you're using Ubuntu or any other linux distro, burning the tails ISO to a usb is just like any other live CD. Use the dd command:

dd if=~/Desktop/tails.iso of=/dev/sdx bs=1048576

adjust the command to reflect the location of your tails.iso and the correct hdd designation.
