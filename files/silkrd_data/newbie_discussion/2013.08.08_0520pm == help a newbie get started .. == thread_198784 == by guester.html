Post by: rainbowwaffle on August 08, 2013, 05:30 pm
not sure about the email thing. but yeah that PGP you have is fine. I delete the vendor keys after I use them. It's just as easy to load their keys again as you need them as it is to bring it up with kleopatra or whatever you use.

I'm sure it's possible and going through a VPN would add another layer of security, but you have to make sure the VPN client takes your anonymity VERY seriously. Most people will not go through the extra step to find a reputable VPN client, being as they are exceedingly difficult to find and feel that TOR works well enough for most purposes.

It is perfectly legal to create a bitcoin wallet on any device with any operating system. I just use firefox and the clearnet when getting bitcoin. Nothing illegal with that.
