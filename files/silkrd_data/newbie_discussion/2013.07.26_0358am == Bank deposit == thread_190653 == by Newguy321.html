Post by: dontek on July 26, 2013, 01:47 pm
Just like on SR, check the vendor reviews to decide who you will go with.

It's easy once you do that to make a bank transfer.

I use BOA. Just go in and get a deposit slip. FYI, if it's BOA, you will need a pink "Out of State" deposit slip if you're buying from someone out of state.

Fill out the info that the seller gives you, which will probably just be a name, city and state, acct #. You do not need to give any of your info to the bank period. If by some crazy reason they did ask, just say that it's a relative or something you are sending money to. More than likely they won't ask and even if they do, it's none of their damn business anyways. I have a stack of deposit slips I picked up at BOA so all I have to do is go through the drivethru with the deposit slip filled out and cash, and BOOM. Done. Once you're done, message back the vendor with the info that they requested from the deposit receiept. Once they get that, they will release the bitcoins and they will show in your localbitcoin wallet and you can transfer from there to wherever you would like.

First time I used localbitcoin I was amazed at the quickness and the communication from the seller.
