Title: Re: How to use Psilocybe cyanescens in powder form?
Post by: sonarjapan on August 10, 2013, 05:25 pm
there are lots of ways to consume dried mushrooms. my favorites are probably putting the powder in melted chocolate (good for storing, too) and just mixing the powder with OJ (warning: can be a little strange looking). you can also just eat it straight, but i can see how a bunch of dry mushroom powder would be unpleasant. to avoid the taste, you can put it in gelcaps and swallow it that way (parachuting would also work if you can swallow that much volume at once). as long as it gets into your stomach, it will be effective. basically the thing you have to keep in mind is to not get it too hot. the actives start to degrade around 250F from what i understand (there is some debate about this). most would advise you not to cook with it, but there are probably some people who have done it and would say it works just fine.

even with that said, a lot of people make mushroom tea by pouring boiling water over the powder, letting it sit for a while, and straining out the matter (you can eat it afterwords, and i find it to be relatively tasteless after having been steeped). throw in some of your favorite tea bags and some honey, and you've got a delicious pot of mushroom tea! this might be a good way for you and your friends since you can just divide a pot of liquid into 6 equal cups for a 1g per dose. i have tried 1g from cyanspore, and I agree that it's a good place to start for a beginner. i'd say it's a little bit weaker than he claims though, but i've only bought it once. i'd compare it to about 1.5g of cubensis, but i guess if you haven't taken mushrooms before that wouldn't mean anything to you.

hope that's helpful. have fun! don't eat too much.

Title: Re: How to use Psilocybe cyanescens in powder form?
Post by: cyanspore on August 17, 2013, 03:16 pm
well, you could have asked me

first of all, a freezer isn't the best place to store because that can introduce moisture, although it's unlikely if it's kept in the sealed bag and it's already dried. Just keep it in a dark dry place is good enough

if you don't have a scale, 1 gram = 1 teaspoon level

you should just start with 1 gram or less if it's your first time. keep the other 5 grams in the same plastic bag, but NOT in the refrigerator/freezer, just in a dark dry place like a drawer. Keeping them dry is the most important. Even if the vacuum seal is broken, they will still last at least a month with little potency loss

as for the powder, you can mix it with anything. Orange juice seems to be popular, but milkshakes, any liquid or even food. Mix it with hot tea or coffee or cocoa and it will hit you a little faster
