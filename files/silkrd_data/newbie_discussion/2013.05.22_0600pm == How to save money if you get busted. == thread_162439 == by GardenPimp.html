Title: How to save money if you get busted.
Post by: GardenPimp on May 22, 2013, 06:00 pm
Hello SRoaders,
Its been a while since I've posted, so here if a little insight...
Step one, KEEP YOUR MOUTH SHUT. No matter what angle LE comes at you, just refrain from saying anything until you can speak with an attorney.
Step two; get a court appointed attorney. Do not go out and get the "big dog" drug attorney to represent you, yet.
Depending on your circumstance/evidence and charges brought upon you, let the court appointed attorney do all the leg work for you. The DA will sometimes give them a plea deal and now you have a starting point to go to the "big dog" and say can you do better? This will save you thousands of dollars in b.s. charges that the "big dogs" will milk out of you.

"Lawyers are the root of all evil"

My 2 cents,
GP
