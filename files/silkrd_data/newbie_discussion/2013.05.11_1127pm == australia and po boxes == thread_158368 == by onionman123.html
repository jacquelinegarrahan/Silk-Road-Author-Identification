I did hear some things about sending to Oz. Roughly 2 years ago a group of people were sending to Oz. The substance was ground to a powder and sealed. Some of the addresses were po boxes, others were houses. The story goes that the Boxes were with fake names through a dirty employee. As for beating customs, I'm not sure what your operation is like but this was what I call an acceptable loss zerg. By that I mean that each 1kg of substance was broken up into many smaller packages. Enough arrived that it was worth the losses as the cost "here" vs the cost "there" was enough to make up for what didn't arrive.  This was a fairly large operation though but it functioned with a good deal of profit for quite some time.
