Title: Re: Heroin vs. Dilaudid
Post by: captainjojo on September 15, 2013, 07:56 pm
It depends on how many 10-12mg shots of hydro you do in a day.  If you are doing 10 shots a day then your tolerance is fairly high.

I've done both.  To be honest I never felt much from 8-12mg of hydro.

If you are going to IV some H, I would start with around 50mg.  Dissolve it into 2ml of sterile water, enough for two syringes.  Do the first one slowly, stop about halfway for 10 seconds to see how it's hitting you, then finish off.

If after 15 minutes you don't feel too much from the first syringe, do the second.  My guess is you will need a bit more, as hydro is something like 2 times as strong as H.

Just go slowly and you should be OK.  Don't pile shot after shot just because you don't get a rush the first time.  Wait 3-4 hours and then try hitting a full 50mg if you don't get a rush off the first 50 divided in two.
