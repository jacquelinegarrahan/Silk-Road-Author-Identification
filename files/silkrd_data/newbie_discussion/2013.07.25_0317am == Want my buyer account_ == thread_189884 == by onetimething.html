@Libertas:

does that mean i could sell my buyer accounts via Gumtree or other venders who sell in commision?

That would be permitted although you would not be allowed to advertise them here. The forums cannot be used for sales, and cannot be used for advertising items unless those items are offered for sale in a listing on the main site.

Your buyer account is yours to do with as you please; however, offering an account for sale on a clearnet website (such as Gumtree) when it has a verifiable history of illegal transactions - and presumably accepting payment for it via a relatively traceable method - wouldn't be the smartest idea.

Other vendors could, in theory, list your account for you. However, they would be leaving themselves open to a lot of risk if either party is dissatisfied with the trade as it is the vendor's feedback that would take the hit.
