Starry Eyed, I'd be very curious to learn how the microdosing experiment goes.  I thought of doing this with psilocybin a while back but never followed through.   Please update us on the project :).

I took ~20mcg after breakfast. I wasn't sure this would even produce a noticeable change in perception, but it did. Not too long after ingesting (~40 minutes), I began to experience a sort of shift in my thoughts. I felt much more relaxed than usual, but also very much in control of things. My vision became clearer, and I didn't have to strain my eyes to see anything (I have experienced this phenomenon on other psychedelics, especially psilocybe mushrooms). I went for a walk, and physical sensations were pronounced. I was much more aware of the movements of my body. Colors seemed to fill the air between me and the objects I looked at. I smoked a little bit of cannabis sativa, and this was pure pleasure. This strain (Amnesia Skunk) can set my mind spinning in loops sometimes, but today, it made everything even more right.

I took another 20mcg at about the 3-hour mark, then went to the grocery store. I normally feel kind of bogged down by this sort of activity, but it was a fun experience today. I bought way more stuff than I would have--not because I was feeling frivolous, but because my mind was open to new possibilities, whereas I'm usually trying to get out of the store. I enjoyed the colors and people and the very sensation of taking it all in. I engaged people on a more relaxed plane. A lot of times, I think conversation with me can be like pulling teeth for both parties. Today, it flowed naturally, and I enjoyed being a part of it.

Everything was easier, calmer, and more fun to do today. Life felt less burdensome, and more promising. 20mcg acted as a nice combination anti-depressant, sedative, and stimulant. The additional 20mcg boosted these feelings to a level of greater vibrancy, though for the sake of experimentation, I wish I'd stuck with the initial dose. The positive effects have lasted me all day. I haven't decided whether or not to dose tomorrow, and if so, how much.
