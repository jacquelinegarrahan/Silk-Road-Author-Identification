Chance of getting caught? Depends on a few things:

1. How well its packaged (basically whether it stinks or not)
2. Where its coming from (i.e. are you importing it? US to UK).
3. The Shipping method (State ran vs. DHL/UPS/FedEx)

Now, if its packaged really well, that certainly reduces the risk.  But not all that much if its being imported. Customs agencies in many countries do in fact use drug dogs with packages being imported.  They just walk up and down the row of packages and wait for a signal from the dog.  Of course, the chances of having a dog would be higher if the package is coming from areas known for drug trafficking, e.g. Pakistan, Afghanistan, Iran (heroin), India (lots of pharma's, also heroin, opium, ketamine, etc..), anywhere in South America (cocaine/heroin) and of course the golden triangle (Thailand, Myanmar, Laos - heroin). If its coming from the US, there's likely less of a chance that the dogs will be present, but its still certainly a good chance.  This is where the shipping method comes in.

Shipping method - State ran postal services (USPS, Royal Mail, Canada Post) have far fewer resources than say, a FedEx or UPS.  So the chances of the package being thoroughly checked is much less than with a FedEx, DHL or UPS.  They certainly still check them, but its generally random, not as extensive, and is therefore much easier to get things through.  Not the case with FedEx for instance.  They're really good at checking parcels.

Anyway, if you're not importing it, I'd say there's very little risk (I won't import anything anymore).  If you are, just make sure you don't sign for anything at all.  And if you get the package to your house, sit on it for at least 24 hours before opening it.  Remember, plausible deniability  - "no officer, I had no idea there was a key of bud in that package, who could have sent that to me?" If its not open, and you don't sign for it, you can deny you had anything to do with it.  If they do set you up, they'll come after you immediately, so 24 hours should be plenty of time to sit on it. 

Hope that helps
