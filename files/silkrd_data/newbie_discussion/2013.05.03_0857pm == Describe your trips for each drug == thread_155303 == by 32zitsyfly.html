Fuck. I had an epic post and lost it. Here's a shitty version with a lot less drugs and description:

shrooms (7+ grams): i lost my fucking mind. the world stopped making sense and I have never laughed so long and so hard ever before in my life. My friend would just say something like "reality exists!" and it would be the most fucked-up, absurd idea in the world and it was hilarious. I don't know if either of us were even making sense or we just couldn't make sense of what we were hearing.

nitrous: you go to nitrous land, kind of like candy land.
