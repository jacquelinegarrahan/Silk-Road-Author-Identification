Quote
Ultimately my question is this - is it normal for established sellers to wait a certain period for new buyers before processing/shipping?

It varies by vendor, but the average is 48 hours or less (not counting weekends -- many take weekends off).

In your case, however, you're a new buyer. What's the vendor's policy on buyers with no history? They may either not want to sell to you or want you to FE first. Have you tried messaging them? As a rule of thumb, brand new buyers should message the vendor first to make sure they'll accept your order.

If it's not marked in transit by Sunday, you can cancel the order (if you bought Wednesday).

Title: Re: Standard Processing Time?
Post by: IForgotMyFuckingPassword on September 27, 2013, 10:48 pm
If they're a high volume vendor, they may be swamped with purchases and bombarded with emails. so be patient. One message to the vendor is sufficient.

Quote
For my first purchase (Wednesday) the seller did not specifically state they would not sell to first time buyers - seems legit

Couple things:

Just because they didn't explicitly state that they won't sell to newbies, doesn't mean they will. You may end up canceling your order on that one.

Also, none of what you, I, or anyone else has said in this thread indicates that the vendor is not legit. If I were a vendor, I'd probably be skeptical about brand new buyers. Keep in mind that vendors are the primary target of LE. They have to protect themselves.
