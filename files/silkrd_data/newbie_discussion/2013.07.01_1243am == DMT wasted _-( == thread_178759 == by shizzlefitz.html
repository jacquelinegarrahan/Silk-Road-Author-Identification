Post by: gr8ape on August 14, 2013, 04:51 am
Put it on weed or vape it in a crack pipe.

Putting it on weed will waste more of it, but it works. It's the easiest way (if you already smoke weed), and it's how I usually smoke it when I have it.

Vaporizing it is the "best" way, as in, you inhale most of what you put in the pipe. You need glass. Crack pipe/meth pipe/hash pipe/light bulb (for real, clean it out first).Put the powder in/on the glass, apply heat evenly to the glass. When it gets hot enough, you'll see the vapor rising (subliming) and that is what you inhale. It cools fast, so get it while it's hot. The burning on your lips is a good thing.
