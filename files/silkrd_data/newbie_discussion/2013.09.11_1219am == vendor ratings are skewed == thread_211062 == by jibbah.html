But, if your vendor delivered a less than 5* performance, why would you want to buy from them again?

As far as I'm aware, there's nothing that's offered by only one vendor.

If you need to leave <5 feedback, as long as your reasons are genuine, and you've spoken to the vendor first, then that's the feedback you should leave. Then find a new vendor (you will).

Especially good reason to find a new vendor if they say that unless you leave 5 feedback then they won't do business with you again. In my opinion, if a vendor says that to me, they're getting very poor feedback, and no return custom as a direct consequence of that demand.
