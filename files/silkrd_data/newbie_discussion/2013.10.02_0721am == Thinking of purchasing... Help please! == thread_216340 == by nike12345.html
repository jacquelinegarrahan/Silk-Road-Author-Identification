P.O. Box is a very valid option with or without your id, or someone else setting up account for you. 
    DO NOT send to abandoned house with tall grass and junk mail stuffed in mail box, it will not end well
    I would say the majority of SR travelers who are buying for personal use, feel safe using home address/name (lookup plausible deniability)

Quote
Where I should buy from (the cocaine that I am interested in is from the Netherlands and would mean posting it a long distance) (should I only buy local?)

I would recommend domestic. (I'm guessing US?) Although international may have cheaper product/higher quality you have to take into account the    extra risk of the package having to go through customs.  The vendors stealth comes into play much more when going through customs.  Make sure and read vendor profiles to see their policies on int'l shipping and they usually address what will happen if package goes MIA.  Also, you should read vendor profile even for domestic orders to know their policies.  This also goes to say that I am seriously considering looking into Canadian bud vendors.

Quote
I'd want to buy 3.5g of cocaine - is this too much if I want to be discreet as possible?
I can't speak to this size,drug or vendor specifically but 3.5g of powder is a bit harder to hide than some RC,LSD,Benzo Blotter.  Also, I'd bet a dollar that packages from the Netherlands receives extra scrutiny (nothing to back this up, just gut feeling)

As someone who was recently "noobish", after a few transactions you get the hang of it.  Just remember to not do anything stupid and you are responsible for your actions.  If you let a vendor convince you to FE early(release funds to seller before you receive package) you will have no hope of getting refund/reship unless vendor goes out of the way(RARE).  Also, learn PGP many vendors require it, ALL of the good ones I've dealt with require it.

Ratings are not always the be all, end all, you'll have lots of fun weighing out risk/reward between vendors.  Newer vendors who may not have that many transactions alot of times will go out of their way to make you super happy.  They want that 5/5 rating but due to being new may slack on labeling and stealth in general.  Where as a vendor who's been around for a year will, usually, be flooded with messages and generally won't respond to questions and will be more expensive.

When traveling the road, always remember to take care of your shoes.

Like the doctor mentioned, eliminate as many potentials for screwing up as possible. Stick with domestic and you can't get caught in customs. Shipping to a visibly empty house has an increased potential for getting caught. Small amounts means the feds can't be bothered with you much.

If you do use a PO box monitor it closely. They run drug and bomb dogs through the post offices every now and then. The longer your care package sits in one the more likely you are to have bad luck.

On the 1 in a zillion chance the cops find drugs in a package sent to you just remember: you know nothing about it.

Post by: goldfinger on October 02, 2013, 03:49 pm
If you're dealing with a vendor that's active and 6+ months with nearly perfect feedback receiving at your home should be no issue.

A PO Box is ideal though as you can rest easy that no matter what where you sleep is not compromised. And if you get a big enough
one, depending on the location you can enter at night with little to no traffic to retrieve your pack!

Ordering from the Netherlands is a bad idea in general, imo. There may be some vendors that are more than capable there but the
country is a horrible place to receive packages from as it is assumed by most people to be something illegal.

If you want your packs quick just order from your home country as dealing with customs increases the risk of seizure. Yes the prices
are higher but its the fastest/safest option always.
