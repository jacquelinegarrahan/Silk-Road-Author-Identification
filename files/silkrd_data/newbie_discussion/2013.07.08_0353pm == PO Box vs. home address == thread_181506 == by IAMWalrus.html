I'd say go with the P.O. Box, they're so convenient for basically everything. Truthfully I'm a n00b so I'm not sure whether I'd recommend setting one up with your real dox or some novelties. Novelty would be ideal but at the same time if you happen to get an astute eagle eyed employee or something doesn't scan right you could be on the hook for trying to use a falsified government ID at a government facility so it's hard to tell what you could get stuck with there. With your real name you could at least theoretically put an alias on a "test" shipment of low value and see if it gets through. I imagine LSD would be one of the safer substances to order.

You should start getting junk mail automatically with the box, or at least I did. If you want to step it up some send some dummy letters to yourself occasionally and order a subscription or something to it.

Also as has been said always go at night. This makes it easier to identify potential surveillance and makes it easier to go in and get out quickly and before arousing any suspicion. If you open your box and see a package that looks suspicious or like it's been opened, DON'T TAKE IT. Remove it from the box and look it over while trying to give your best puzzled look as if you genuinely don't know wtf this is even for, then put it back in the box and walk away. If there's anybody watching they'll assume that you weren't aware anything was coming, and you can go back in a couple days to see if it's still there.
