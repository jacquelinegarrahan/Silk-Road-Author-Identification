Post by: justaperson on September 20, 2013, 05:53 am
THC has a very high variability in this regard. Generally it's a couple weeks to even a month and a half. A good friend of mine had not smoked a single joint in two months, but she went to jail because on the day she had court she failed her drug test by a slim margin. Obviously, it didn't help her case. You can speed up the process by jogging, eating a low fat diet and, well, not smoking any more cannabis. THC is fat soluble, which means that if you have more body fat it'll take more time for you to lower your THC level to the point where you would pass a test. Drug testing kits can easily be purchased at your local dollar tree or similar stores to give yourself an idea of whether or not you'd be able to pass. It's worth noting that tests utilizing your hair would come up positive if you've smoked at any point in time you've been growing your hair, but it's very rarely done. I'm assuming that you're more concerned about a piss test, but it's definitely worth giving a heads up about.
