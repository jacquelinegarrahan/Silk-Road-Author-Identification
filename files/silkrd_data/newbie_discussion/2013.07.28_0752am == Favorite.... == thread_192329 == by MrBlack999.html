what is a ripple bar IggyBurdowski?

Sold in England, simply delicious. Here is a review I found online.
Mars Galaxy Ripple is a chocolate candy bar with a folded flaky chocolate interior which crumbles and melts in the mouth. The Ripple name is a derivative of the Galaxy and Dove brands. Galaxy bars are typically wrapped in brown and cream wrappers, and the Mars Galaxy Ripple is no exception. This could be inspired by the thought of enjoying the creamy, chocolate candy inside and is in direct contrast to the hallmark bright purple of Cadbury's. Galaxy chocolate tastes different to other chocolate, and is not to everyone's taste. The Ripple bar is typically sweet, syrupy and almost toffee-like in taste, but with a ripple to bite through in the middle. The rippled chocolate is firm to bite, but then melts very quickly and smoothly. The Mars Galaxy Ripple is a little like the bright yellow Cadbury's Flake bar in concept, but does not crumble everywhere in such an irritating way.
