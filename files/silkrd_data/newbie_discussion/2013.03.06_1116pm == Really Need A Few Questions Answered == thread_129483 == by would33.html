It means free early. The vendor wants you to release the funds from escrow before he/she ships. If you do so and anything goes wrong SR support will not help you.

Your choices are:

1) Free the funds and ( fingers crossed ) hope nothing goes wrong.

2) ask the vendor to cancel the order and find a vendor that will not ask you to FE.
