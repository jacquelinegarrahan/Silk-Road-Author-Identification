Title: Re: Home address delivery? Safe enough or stupid as hell?
Post by: Thekla1 on August 07, 2013, 09:41 pm
Hi.

For starters, if you can, for personal small orders, use vendors within your own borders. If there is another name still attached to your address that regularly still has mail delivered there, use that as the name. My landlord's wife has lots of tax/mortgage letters arrive here (even though I have been here for years, naughty landlord!) and has no idea of the goodies that have been arriving in her name.

There is no definitive answer to this question, it will be down to your judgement and local knowledge of LE in your country. You are ALWAYS taking a risk having your goods delivered, remember what this wonderful site is - an anonymous market for lots of things authorities have declared illegal. So critical to your thinking is to minimize any activity that arouses suspicion - there are billions of pieces of mail moving round the world at any one time and if the vendors do good stealth (check on this on forums and feedback before ordering) then it is suspicious deliveries and/or behaviour that will red flag a package. A little package arriving in your mail with a previous tenant's name on it, or a concerned citizen calling LE because they saw someone regularly checking the mail box of an empty house they had no right to be at? What do you think will raise attention?

I get mine to the door. Over 20 transactions and no issues. But you never know, the next one could fuck me. It is ALWAYS a risk.

Take care.
