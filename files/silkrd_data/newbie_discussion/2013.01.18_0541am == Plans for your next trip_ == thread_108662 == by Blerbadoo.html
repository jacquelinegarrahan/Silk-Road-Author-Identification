Tesselated & JerseyCow have the shit!  Clean and reasonably priced.  Potency has been an issue for me with Tesselated, but I'd rather get a clean but weak tab and have a museum experience, than to feel sick as a dog and and freak out worry about wether I got slipped an RC.  There was some discussion on the LSD Avengers thread regarding the differences between Canadian and EU acid.  The general consensus is that the Canadian is mellow, minimal body load, but mild visuals,  EU stuff on the other hand was rated as causing more physical discomfort, but also giving the user strong visuals. I really ain't buying it.  If both substances are LSD, I would say that the users are getting a higher dose from the EU, plain and simple.   

I ate 2 1/2 of Tesselated's WoW tabs and then smoked DMT 3 hours later.  I can't say that I will ever do that again.  DMT is just too dark for me, it accentuated the negative for the 15-30 minutes that it was active. DMT is sort of malevolently alien to me. On a positive note, I was awestruck by the intensity of the visuals.  While walking by a table covered with bronze figurines and antique watches and an antique lamp, I could see each and every object in high detail simultaneously.  I never imagined that so much visual information could be processed at once. I will probably sample JerseyCow's Gandalf blotter this weekend......Sans the DMT.
