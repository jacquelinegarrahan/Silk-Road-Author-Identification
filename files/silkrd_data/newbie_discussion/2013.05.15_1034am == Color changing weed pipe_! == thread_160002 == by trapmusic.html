Title: Re: Color changing weed pipe?!
Post by: zetterberg99 on May 15, 2013, 10:44 am
There's plenty of online pipe shops if theres not one in your area.  If theres a glass shop near you with even a mediocre selection they should have some color changing glass.  first website that comes to mind is grasscity.com, but I've never been a customer.

Its not a change from lighting the bowl, but rather the buildup of resin on the inside of the glass.  gold or silver "fumed" coatings on the inside change either red or blue (i forget which is which) as the light coming through the glass is refracted by the buildup of resin.  cool thing is when you clean it it will go back to clear and start all over!

Glass is awesome.  ;D
