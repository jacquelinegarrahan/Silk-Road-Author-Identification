Title: Re: Difference between Tan MDMA and White MDMA??
Post by: northsouth on February 08, 2013, 09:34 am
As mentioned before, the color is due to impurity (which means it's probably not 84% MDMA, but it's ok, they all lie). The brown/tan color most likely comes from MDP-2-P, a precursor to MDMA, which was left over from a synthesis that's was not 100% professional. The THEORETICAL maximum MDMA content of MDMA-HCl is 87%, but 84% is a more reliable number. Pure MDMA-HCl should be white, but this doesn't always mean that white MDMA = pure, since the impurities might be white as well.
