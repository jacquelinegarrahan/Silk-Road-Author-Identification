Title: Re: Finalizing Early
Post by: Codesr on March 28, 2013, 02:13 am
Quote from: kingghb on March 28, 2013, 01:52 am
People say not to finalize early, but if you are a new buyer and have no feedback I believe nearly all vendors require it especially as large of orders that I'm making. What are your thoughts?

If you are really uncomfortable with FE, message and talk to the vendor. You might find a vendor that will work with you through escrow. You should read the buyers guide. It advises against FE. It does seem to be common for established vendors to require new buyers to FE though. It's a contradiction I know. Check the age of the vendors account, the number of fans, their feedback, their satisfaction and rating, and their forum review thread. If a vendor has a good track record, good feedback, good reviews here in the forum, and no scam threads, you should be fine. Same risk handing over money first on the street, dealer: "I'll be right back", lol.
