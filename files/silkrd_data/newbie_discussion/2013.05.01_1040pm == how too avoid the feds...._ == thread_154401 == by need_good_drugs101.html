Title: Re: how too avoid the feds....?

Do your research on vendors and their stealth. Use PGP for your address and other sensitive information. Don't order huge quantities, especially right from the get-go. Use a real name/address for delivery. Know what a controlled delivery would look like (be wary of signing for things and keep an eye out for tampered with packages). Of course, odds of this are pretty low if you're ordering discreet quantities. You should do your own research though.
