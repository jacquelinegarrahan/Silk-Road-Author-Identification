Title: Re: Processing an order
Post by: dothedamnthing on April 27, 2013, 12:07 pm
The vendor has to mark the order in transit, so there's no set time for when that will happen. Some vendors only ship on certain days of the week. They will usually make note of this on their vendor page. It's been processing for less than two days, so that's not all that uncommon. You will be able to cancel the order if it is still processing after four days.
Title: Re: Processing an order
Post by: gwendlesphere on April 27, 2013, 12:12 pm
Some of my orders have been "Processing" for up to 5 days... But they get round to it eventually. Generally it's due to their shipping procedures and business parameters. Often they never ship on Fridays, and a lot of vendors take frequent time away from SR. Generally they'll update.

If you are that impatient, you can message them.. But I'd wait 3 or 4 days, and if you can't be fucked waiting, just cancel after the 4th day. It's uncommon that they'll just leave an order "processing" forever. They get to it eventually. Relax guy :)
