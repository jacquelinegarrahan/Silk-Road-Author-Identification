Title: Re: Please Help! Bitcoin Problem:(
Post by: rustbucket41 on March 13, 2013, 07:42 pm
As per my understanding, you can't "withdraw" bitcoins like you can a bank account. You can only send bitcoins from an address under your control to another address you specify. So that means you need to go to your non-SR wallet to send the bitcoins to your SR address (which you should update each time you send bitcoins). It takes a while (~8 hours), but that's just due to the need to get confirmations and I think SR also does some wallet tumbling to cover your tracks.
