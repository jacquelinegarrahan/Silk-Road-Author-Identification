1st of all, regarding the signature issue, I've HAD A VENDOR WHO WAS NEW NOT REALIZE THAT HE HAD TO WAIVE THE SIG OPTION, AND , I've had just gung ho postal workers ask for a sig just because they want to cover their own ass. Thats doing something unusual and might raise a flag, if a mailman hands me a box, any box, and asks for a sig then I sign for it even if I don't know what it is. Who doesn't like presents and surprises. It does NOT compromise your plausible deniability at all.  I've signed a few times, once I had to march into the post office and sign there. You should however get the tracking info and confirm delivery yourself.

Do you live in an area where it might not be unthinkable that someone just randomly stole your mail, not even aware what was in it??
