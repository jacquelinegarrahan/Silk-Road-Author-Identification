Hey Dream Captain,

I remember getting my head around the whole thing... I'll try and summarise, but Libertas is right - read the wiki.

You can buy bitcoins off legit websites where you google. That 'code' you saw when you clicked 'make a deposit', think of it like your 'wallet'. You can put bitcoins into that wallet.

Once you find a vendor on google/by searching the forum they will explain step by step what it required with their service. Some require documents to verify who you are, others you can deposit cash into a bank account and they will send them to your 'wallet'.

It's really up to you which way you want to go. To add another step in the mix, you can use a 'bitcoin anonymiser' Some don't, some do. They charge a small fee to make your bitcoins more anonymous. Some see it as necessary - others are 'swapping dirty coins, for dirty coins'.

Any other questions, feel free to ask!
