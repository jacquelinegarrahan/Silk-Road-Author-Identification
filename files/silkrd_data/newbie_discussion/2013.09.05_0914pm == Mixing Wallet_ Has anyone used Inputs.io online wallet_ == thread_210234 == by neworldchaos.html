Title: Mixing Wallet: Has anyone used Inputs.io online wallet?
Post by: neworldchaos on September 05, 2013, 09:14 pm
There has been a lot of press lately about Bitcoin's lack of true anonymity. It seems transactions can definitely be tracked as going into (though not coming out of) SR. Naturally that's a concern.

Recently on coindesk I read about an online wallet at a site called Inputs.io. Online wallets are risky in general, but this one seems different. They claim security is their #1 concern, and the business looks legit. https://inputs.io/faq#c4

They advertise "automatic free mixing for your privacy" and if you open a wallet on Tor, then you can access it from Tor from any IP (whereas non-Tor accounts must be accessed from the same IP).

Anyone have any experience with it? Seems like it might be a good fit for certain, ahem, activities.
