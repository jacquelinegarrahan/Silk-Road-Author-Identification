Post by: top44 on April 19, 2013, 12:44 am
welcome to SR, take your time reading wikipedia guide on SR, as well as searching the forums.

In few words things are like these.
1.You buy bitcoins using an exchange site. (pm to suggest a couple)
2. You transfer the BTC to BTC address that you already have once you have made an account at silkroad.
3. You wait, see the amount of BTC on your silk road account, and you are ready to order.

Be careful with bitcoin it is not a stable currency. Usually it goes up, as more people get into BTC exchanging. So if you want to buy something that costs 100 dollars, deposit 150, just to be sure, even in a 50% crash. (If not, the rest of the money you will use them with your next order, cause once you make your first order, you will go for another , again and again :D)   you can buy the product you wanted at first, because the prices at SR are fixed at USD, and the BTC amount that you need usually changes, but has the same Value in USD.

I suggest you go to your account page at SR, choose to see prices in dollars.
