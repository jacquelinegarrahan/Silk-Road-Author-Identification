Title: Re: Isn't it extremely suspicious ordering from California?...
Post by: postrex on July 30, 2013, 06:28 am
Shipping to where - somewhere else in the USA?  If so, don't sweat it.  Find a buyer with good smell stealth (MBB are a must), and your package will another parcel in the mail.

Really... Think of how many packages and letters ship out from California on a day-to-day basis.  Even if the proportion of weed being shipped is slightly higher then other states, it is still a miniscule portion of the total postal volume.

When considering instincts like this it helps to analyze yourself with the question, "who/what am I afraid of?"  In my mind, if you are a domestic buyer that takes reasonable precautions (basically - deal with trustworthy vendors.  For unknowns, start small and see how their packaging is), there is very little to be afraid of.  Everyone has their own fears, their own dangers based on their situation.  Identify yours, take precautions, always have a worst-case plan (I don't know anything! -stick with that- "my lawyer advised me not to answer questions without them present" is good if you are ever asked more then 2 questions by LE).

Basically... I highly doubt it matters what state is market on the return address of your weed shipments...

-postrex
