Title: Re: Bitcoin confusion
Post by: Blueness on September 25, 2013, 09:44 am
I'm not sure exactly what information you're missing. When you buy your bitcoins they will have to be sent to a wallet (which can just be on a website, or can be on your computer) which has an address where they can stay. Your SR account has its own wallet with its own address, which can be found at silkroadvb5piz3r.onion/account or by clicking "account" at the top of the screen. If your BTC is in another wallet after you buy them, you'll have to hit withdraw on whatever site they're on and put in that bolded number on SR. When you're sending them, you don't have to worry about the address of the wallet they're already in, but only the number you're sending them to.
