Title: Re: New Vendor FREE Samples
Post by: dothedamnthing on April 01, 2013, 06:31 am
1. Not sure how you've been a buyer for some time without using PGP or even knowing what it is. Learning that should be the first thing you do. Not too many buyers are going to want to send you their address unencrypted.

2. Once you hit 50 posts you'll be able to post in the Product Offers section about your samples. Although, it sounds like you have quite a bit of research to do before you're ready for that.

3. Mentioning that you vacuum seal or other similar vague details isn't a problem. Lots of vendors do that. You shouldn't be going into any more detail than that, though.

Good luck to you.
