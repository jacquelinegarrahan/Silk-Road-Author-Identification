Title: **VENDOR REVIEW- HappySmoke420**
Post by: tiger23 on June 14, 2013, 09:16 pm
Very happy so far with my purchase from HappySmoke420.  Good prices and quality herb.  Ordered (2) different 1/2ozs (blue dream and green crack) Tuesday, marked In-Transit Tuesday and received the first package today (friday) in Central US.  Still waiting on the other half of my order.  I wish they would have shipped together however I assume there is a method to this madness and it will be arriving soon.  I will update once other is received.  Stealth was good with 2x vaccum sealing and plain packaging. 

Order: 1/2oz Blue Dream
Look: Exactly like pictured in the ad. Covered in crystals. Nice cure. At least on weight if not over (no scale)
Smell: Not overly smelly or "blue dreamy" but a nice hazey smell with fruity notes.
Taste: Hints of lilac.  Very smooth.
High: Very nice and long lasting.  A very good day time smoke!

Will update on the Green Crack once it arrives.  Otherwise A+.
