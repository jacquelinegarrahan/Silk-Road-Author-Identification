Europe+USA call different chems speed. In Europe it's amphetamine sulphate, in USA it's methamphetamine sulphate.

Amphetamine sulphate is really a powder. The paste is amphetamine mixed with a liquid, not sure what the liquid is, might be a leftover from the lab or added by dealers to pad it.

Whatever it is, it evaporates quickly, spread it on a plate and in an hour or two it's powder  then sniff, swallow or IV.

Forget about the paste, these vendors sell amphetamine dry and uncut: Boymaster, CapeCrusader, iisthisisi.
