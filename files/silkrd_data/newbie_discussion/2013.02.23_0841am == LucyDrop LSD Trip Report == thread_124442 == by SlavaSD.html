Hi, i want to share my tripreport here. I've ordered 10 strip from LD. I got the letter after 18 days to middle east. I have no torelance to LSD, it will be my first time  but i did shrooms 30+ times in the past. I've read some complains about the quality of his last batches, so i started with 3 strip with empty stomach (last meal 6 hours ago). I started to feel the effect after 20 minutes and got to the peak probably after 1-2 hours. I was indoors at onset, i had some body temperature discomfort, feeling cold especially hands. But when i reach the peak, everything was great OEV and CEV. I had my friend babysitting me, so i told him that we should go out for a walk. We went outside and i had a lot of energy, amplified colors, trails and voices everywhere. We probably walked like 10 km in the center of the city,  drank a coffee with milk and rasberry syrup at starbucks and had a lot of fun.
Overall it was a 5/5 trip, thanks LucyDrop. After 7 hours, i decided to take 2mg clonazepam, because it was enough experience and i needed to sleep. It was a good decision, i've slept well, woke up with a smily face and writing this trip report now.
I need a breakfast now :) peace!
