Title: Re: Usps, UPS and FedEx
Post by: Grey Matter on August 27, 2013, 03:42 pm
I think the general consensus is that the USPS handles quite a bit more volume, so the possibility of added scrutiny involved in delivering the packages is significantly lower.

Basically they've got a shit ton of mail and not enough time / man power to look all that closely at every / your package (assuming there aren't any issues with the package.)

Issues being things like watched addresses, the name on the package not being a resident of the address it's being shipped to, bad / fake addresses, etc.
