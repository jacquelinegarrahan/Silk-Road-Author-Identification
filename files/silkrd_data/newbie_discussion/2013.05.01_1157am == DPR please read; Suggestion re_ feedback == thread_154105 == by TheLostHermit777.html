Post by: TheLostHermit777 on May 01, 2013, 11:57 am
I've noticed about 70% of a vendors feedback is comprised of people saying "Finalized early, will update when arrived". I think you should put up a notice on the feedback page stating that feedback is final and cannot be updated. A person could quite easily build up a decent positive reputation simply asking for people to FE and leave that 70% of newbies to give them 5/5 before receiving any package.

Also perhaps give some suggestions as to what to write, I noticed quite a few feedbacks simply saying "thanks" or "great", silly things like that. There's no point leaving feedback if you're not really going to leave feedback. Things like "How stealthy was the package?", "How long did it take to arrive?", "Is it as described?" etc. Would make SR a lot more suited for people who want to get not just a cheap, anonymous deal but good quality stuff that's confirmed for it's truth by another, as feedback should be.

As always, thank you for your work and good to see SR is back on-line; I know you want to keep SR as cheap as possible for it's users, but perhaps you should look into investing in decent DDoS protection if you're able to do so anonymously. I'd be happy to chip in and I'm sure many others would be if it means ~24/7 uptime.
