Title: Re: Make a first deposit
Post by: therabbithole on July 03, 2013, 02:06 am
To make a deposit on SR (simple way):
1. Create a wallet in https://blockchain.info/wallet
2. Deposit money in your bitcoin wallet. You could buy bitcoins online or go to a bitcoin money changer.
3. Now go to your SR account, there's a bitcoin address assigned to receive bitcoin from you. Copy the address.
4. Go to your bitcoin wallet at https://blockchain.info/wallet. Paste the address and send the deposit the the address.
5. Check your SR account, in just a few minutes, your deposit will appear.
