Title: Re: In Transit 7 Days and No Response from ChemicalMan?
Post by: zipp on February 15, 2013, 09:17 am
If there's only 1-2 days left to go until auto-finalize, make sure you hit 'resolve' or else he'll be sent the bitcoins and you'll have an auto-finalize on your account.
You should also check his profile to see if the seller uses tormail.  Even when/if you do receive the item, remember that you can leave feedback for the seller after the transaction.  Be sure to leave feedback so others know to avoid certain sellers.
If you end up receiving a refund, yeah I would just create another SR account or else your refund rate will be at 100% and sellers will want you to FE.
