When they updated the feedback system, they gave users the option of concealing their user names or creating alias. Those are probably the latter and are why you can't locate them.

The old feedback system didn't list names at all.

Trust your gut instinct; if you think a vendor is suspect, then save them for a last resort. Otherwise, look for patterns in their writing or anything that looks/sounds too good to be true.
