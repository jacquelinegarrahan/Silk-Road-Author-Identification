Patience is Key, For me, I'm located in Malaysia, so south east asia. I ordered on the 18th and 19th from 2 vendors in Netherlands, one from Italy. I received 2 of my deliveries, 1 from Netherlands and Italy, and Am still waiting for the other one in Netherlands.

Anyways, I've never ordered from AUS before though, and I've just ordered from UK so that might take another 10 days...

Not much to say, pick your vendors wisely and always scope their profile!

Haha yeah, but good luck if you want to start becoming a vendor, I suggest checking out the packaging section and invest in some proper bags and what not, unless you can get them yourself! And no harm starting off small and gaining more experience till you are confident to start going in bulk. As for knowing if a vendor is legit or not is difficult, but just looking through their history of feedbacks may help, if it looks too obvious that its a self rating and whatt not. Not to mention his rating out of 100 by his name, that will be a good indicator.

Sometimes being scammed is inevitable, this is because some great vendors are known to selectively scam, and use FE methods on newbies as well. I saw that last year or so some Canadian vendor who was super well known for his heroin and meth who was only dealing in Canada, decided to make a sale for 420 day and make his shippings worldwide. He was known for his accountability and trust, but in the end he just scammed everyone and closed his account, then was never heard of!

I have no experience in dealing or being a vendor so I don't have much to say, hopefully some experienced vendor can jump in this thread and give you some advice.. Just do as much reading as you can on the forums, I'm pretty sure to be a vendor here you have to be somewhat devoted to ensure safety between you and your client.
