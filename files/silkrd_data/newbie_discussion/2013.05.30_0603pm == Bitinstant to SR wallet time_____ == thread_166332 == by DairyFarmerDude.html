Title: Re: Bitinstant to SR wallet time?????
Post by: SmokesHisBroccoli on June 03, 2013, 01:27 am
It's been awhile since I've made a deposit via bitinstant, but I'll be making one in the near future and that's how I came upon this thread. 

Anyways, when I was making deposit via bitinstant a few months back I always went to the same Walmart and used the same information each time.  Always bogus information, but a real address of a business in town that I have no affiliation with.  They ask me every single time if I've ever used moneygram before and I always said yes.  Then they punch in your phone and info and look you up.  If you aren't consistent in putting down the exact same information that might get them asking questions.  But not questions about what the money is for.  Just questions about what your phone number or address is because you wrote it down incorrectly. 

One time the girl asked me what zipzap was (this is the company you're sending the money to on the moneygram slip).  I just told her it was so I could buy some virtual video game stuff.  Too many people are paranoid that they are nosy.  They're not.  It's a human being making small talk.  Nothing more. It's walmart they don't care.  They want to punch out and go home. 

A few times I had them send it to my e-mail.  But a few times I had it go direct to my SR address.  Again I think people on here are too paranoid about which to use.  Nobody is going to go to walmart and check video cameras because you bought a couple hundred dollars in bitcoins.  They have no idea where the money is going.  Walmart doesn't and neither does bitinstant.  That's the beauty of bitcoins. 

Now in my opinion the only time to get paranoid in the whole process is when the stuff gets delivered.  For whatever reason the USPS scares the shit out of me.  All it takes is one postal inspector to do his job and them to come after you with the cooperation of law enforcement and you're screwed.  So from the time I place my order until the time it arrives I'm a pretty nervous guy.
