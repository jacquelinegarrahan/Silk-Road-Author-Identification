Post by: leavesbrown on April 02, 2013, 03:39 am
I would advise printing off your own labels with a cheap printer and A4 sheets of blank stickers that you can buy from any office supplies shop. MS office as well as the much preferred free alternative OpenOffice have templates premade so you can just add in your addresses into text fields and just print off.

For added stealth stick in a made up company logo and name to print along the edges of your stickers.

Just go to your local stationary shop and ask for printable adhesive labels. Just be careful not to leave fingerprints on the back of them when sticking them to parcels, use latex gloves when packaging is general good advice.
