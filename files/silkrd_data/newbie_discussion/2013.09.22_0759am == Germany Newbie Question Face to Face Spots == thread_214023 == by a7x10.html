"Street dealers" may be safe if you're lucky, but you can never guarantee it.
I wouldn't recommend approaching anyone on the street at all.  If you really don't want to use Silk Road, your 'safest' bet is probably to try to get some contacts in nightclubs (somewhere like Salon Zur Wilden Renate in Berlin might be a good example of the kind of place).
Here in Hannover, I know of a total of TWO areas where you could theoretically approach people on the street.  Neither of them are something I'd be particularly keen on doing.  I also know of one club where the ecstasy and weed are in plentiful use; but I also wouldn't feel so comfortable about just walking up to a random stranger (especially since I'm a bit older and they'd probably think I'm a cop).
But personally, I'd say Silk Road is still way safer than any of these options.  If you're totally unhappy with the idea of having it delivered to your home, maybe talk to your friends and see if anyone would accept being the delivery location for you (in exchange for sharing a little of your coke of course)
