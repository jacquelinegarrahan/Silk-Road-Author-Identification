Title: Re: How to smoke Hash Oil
Post by: NewStem on July 02, 2013, 11:58 pm
With finesse, you can also vaporize it in a glass pipe. Dab the oil in, hold the lighter close until it bubbles, and stop lighting pretty shortly after. You should see vapor rising off the oil, hopefully in thick and long-lasting wisps. Inhale either through the pipe or directly above the wisps. This isn't an efficient way to hit dabs, maybe as efficient as combining it with weed or tobacco, but if you have enough to be a little wasteful and need to get high with what you got, then it works.

Title: Re: How to smoke Hash Oil
Post by: farmer1 on July 06, 2013, 08:34 am
The best is a nail/fishbowl setup for your bong. Get a butane torch to heat your nail, slide the globe over the nail, and drop your dab. This is going to be the most bang for your buck with oil.

If you have some oil but no equipment then you are going to want to hot-knife it. 2 butter knives required. Heat one knife on the stove, dab goes on the other one. Press together over a bong stem and draw in the smoke. More wasteful then dabs, but at least you have the tools in your standard kitchen.

Most wasteful is to just drip some onto a bud, ash, whatever, or smear it on a rolling paper. It will certainly work, but not as well.
