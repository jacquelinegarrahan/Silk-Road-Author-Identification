Title: Re: The difference between using and dealing!
Post by: leavesbrown on April 02, 2013, 01:25 pm
Completely depends on where in the world you are and on many circumstances surrounding the raid.
If the cops find 2g of bud, 500 empty baggies, some well worn scales and a tick list full of names and amounts of money owed, then intent to deal is a very probably charge. At the same time, possession of an ounce or too combined with a head of 3 foot long dreadlocks and playing bass in a reggae band could very easily, and rightfully be argued as personal possession. Totally depends on circumstances.

You need to be clever about things like this if you want to deal, that you need to ask this question to a web forum indicates to me that you probably shouldn't be dealing
