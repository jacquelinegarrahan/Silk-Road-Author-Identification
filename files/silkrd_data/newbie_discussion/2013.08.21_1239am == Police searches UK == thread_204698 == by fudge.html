Title: Re: Police searches UK
Post by: Annex on August 21, 2013, 02:15 pm
If you wrap your stuff up nice and good, you won't need to worry about any sort of searching.

Cling-film and alcohol wipes will get almost anything through, provided you wrap as close to entering the festival as possible. Also think about putting it in your hood or under your hat, further from the dog's nose.

The crotch is a notoriously bad place to put things because;
1. It's nice and warm (Mmmm...)
2. Constantly moving (imagine bag full of flour that you constantly squeeze, some is going to puff out eventually
3. It's literally perfect 'dog-head' height, They're already pretty good at what they doo, why make it easier?

If you do get stopped, remember that taking drugs isn't illegal per se so if they ask;
Have you consume xyz - Yes
Do you have any on you - no

Another good idea would be to look up the thresholds for drug offences for the drugs you'll be carrying. Keeping it to the personal use levels is highly recommended. Burden of proof on you to prove you weren't planning on selling would be a real bitch.

Just out of curiosity, what are you taking in?
Enjoy the festival!
