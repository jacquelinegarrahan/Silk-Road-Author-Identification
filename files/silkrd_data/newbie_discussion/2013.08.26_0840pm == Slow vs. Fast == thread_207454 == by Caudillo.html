Title: Re: Slow vs. Fast
Post by: SmokesHisBroccoli on August 27, 2013, 12:34 am
I've read up a lot on these forums regarding shipping methods.  Of course most of it is speculation and you have to sift through the information and make a decision.  But from what I've read and my experiences the best shipping methods are in this order:

1. USPS Priority
2. First Class or Regular Snail Mail
3. USPS Express

Express is too risky and a lot of vendors don't offer it.  Priority is the most popular method vendors offer.  I honestly like first class/regular mail the most but most vendors don't offer it.  The reason I like it the most is the largest amount of mail falls under this category.  And if you're ordering something small like pills they can easily fit in a small envelope with a stamp on them and I'm pretty confident they don't check small envelopes like they do big heavy boxes.
