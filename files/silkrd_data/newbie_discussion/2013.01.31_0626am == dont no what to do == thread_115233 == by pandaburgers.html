Title: Re: dont no what to do
Post by: mindmatter on January 31, 2013, 07:05 am
Yep. Unless you can work something out with the vendor, you'll just have to take it as a loss.
It sucks, but that's the reason for escrow. SR support won't interfere with a transaction after the buyer finalizes.
You'll never see a vendor 'finalize early' and send you drugs before you enter escrow; or let you off a couple of coins because you're only just short for an order. The vendors have to make sure they don't go out of pocket, but so do the buyers. There's no profit in consuming drugs; and its more fun to personally stick money in strangers pockets than paying for invisible drugs to not arrive.
Escrow's the only protection for buyers, I'd use it all the time now. Don't be bullied into finalizing; you can always cancel before you finalize early.
