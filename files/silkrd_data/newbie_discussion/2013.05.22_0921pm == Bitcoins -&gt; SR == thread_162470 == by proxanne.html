Title: Re: Bitcoins -> SR
Post by: DoctorFate on May 23, 2013, 01:39 am
It can take a few hours sometimes.  Check blockchain.info and enter your btc address it will tell you how many confirmations you have, it can anywhere from 6 to 12 confirmation before it shows up in our account.

You may want to change your SR btc address every so often, many people like to use bitinstant to send to another wallet, then transfer to SR.  People send directly to SR too but you can never be too careful.
