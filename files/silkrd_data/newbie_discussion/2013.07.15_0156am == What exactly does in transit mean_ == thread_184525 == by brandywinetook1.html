Title: Re: What exactly does "in transit" mean?
Post by: frivolous on July 15, 2013, 02:02 am
When your order says "processing" it means your vendor has received it and should be getting it ready to be sent out. When the order says "in transit" it means that the order has been shipped and is on your way to you (if the vendor is being honest, which hopefully they are.) Don't forget to finalize once you get your order  :)

processing means the order has been sent to the seller.  It doesn't mean the seller has seen it or done anything with it.  Only when it's marked "in transit" can you be sure that the vendor has seen the order and done something with it.
