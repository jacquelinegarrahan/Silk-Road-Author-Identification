Title: Re: Have i fucked up?
Post by: Charlesnoir on June 03, 2013, 04:37 am
What I gleaned from reading the buyer's guide and forums before I placed my first order:

1.  Use your real name and address.  There is no advantage to using a fake name and/or address,  and as the above poster said, many disadvantages.  For further detail on this, read the Security forum...lots of very important info and will put your mind at ease after reading postal employees' threads.

2.  NEVER FE.  DO NOT BUY FROM VENDORS WHO REQUIRE THIS. 

3.  Most vendors are great with stealth, but if you receive a delivery of product that is not very carefully sealed (NOT plastic seal-a-meal or only baggies with no outer air tight wrap), say something in your review or pm the vendor.  Vendors put themselves and you at risk and will lose business because of bad stealth...they need to know, and want to know if they are falling short in this area. 


It is so awesome to have your product of choice delivered straight to you at your own address.  If you follow the guidelines for buyers and advice in the forums, you will avoid ending up in a situation like the OP's.

Here's what can happen when using names of people that used to live there.

1) Their transfer of mail service could have been delayed for any one of many reasons with the simplest being they did not turn it in til later. Others could be the service took longer than expected to process correctly.

2) The owners or agent for the property could have just made the transfer or even put a hold on their service, with their approval or not. Owners and   agents have more pull over their property than people realize, because they usually know the postal carries by name and through gifts. (agents is    referring to leasing not alphabet mafia)

3) The mail carrier could know the previous tenants well enough to know they no longer live there.

Now these are just three simple explanations as to why the packages could not have shown up and with more time invested could come up with a grocery list more. Especially given the fact that you FE'd these 5 items. WHY?

As well why haven't you contacted the V's to find out if they could give you any additional info? They may have had delays or might actually have Tracking.
