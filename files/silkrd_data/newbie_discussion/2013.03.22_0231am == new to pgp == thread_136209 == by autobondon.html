Title: Re: new to pgp
Post by: lokiju on March 22, 2013, 03:02 am
You can get a bunch of help on the thread, there's people who want the exact same thing. You need a little background info first, find out what a public key is, a private (or secret key is) the idea of non symmetrical encryption etc. It's the concepts that are most important. You don't want to try to import a PGP message instead of a key, it won't work! And if you try to encrypt a message with your key to somebody it won't work either.
Wiki is also a good place to start (as well as for BitCoin, BTC)
