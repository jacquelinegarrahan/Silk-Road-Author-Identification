Title: Re: Should I use my real name or fake name when giving delivery info to vendor?
Post by: BillWithers on July 25, 2013, 05:51 pm
A fake name arouses more suspicion than a real name. Most vendors state right in their presentation no fake names, or no refunds for fake names, fake addresses etc.

General suggestion is to mail at least six letters to the fake name to make sure it actually gets delivered reliably.
Title: Re: Should I use my real name or fake name when giving delivery info to vendor?
Post by: DanDanTheIceCreamMan on July 25, 2013, 05:54 pm
That is one of vendors' biggest pet peevs is using your address but a fake name. You shouldn't do that, that is one of the main reasons for packages not being delivered or packages being flagged. Whether it is in your name or not as long as you did not sign for it you have the exact same amount of plausible deniability. But putting a fake name just makes it more suspicious looking. Always use real name. If you're that worried about it then just get it sent to a friends house or find a drop location.
