Title: Re: Sending shipping info to vendor
Post by: mankeyhank on May 16, 2013, 02:16 am
This is something which is covered in the wiki, so if you haven't read through the wiki, you should.

In answer to your question, there is indeed a point in the order process in which you input your shipping information; while it is not required, what you should do for this is encrypt it with the seller's PGP key the same as you would a message. This helps ensure security and anonymity to anybody other than the seller. Best wishes on your first order!
