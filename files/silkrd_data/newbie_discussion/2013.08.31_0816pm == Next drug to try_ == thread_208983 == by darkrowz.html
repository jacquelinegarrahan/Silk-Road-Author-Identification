It's really all up to your preference, or what you're looking to get out of altered states.  I personally recommend psilocybin, hashish, MDMA or MDA, and MXE.  Bear in mind that all those are compounds that should be taken with more caution than cannabis warrants.  I'd also like to throw in that I personally recommend staying away from opiates, benzodiazepines, and stimluants.  I can honestly say that I've never seen anybody being better off as a result of those types of drugs.
