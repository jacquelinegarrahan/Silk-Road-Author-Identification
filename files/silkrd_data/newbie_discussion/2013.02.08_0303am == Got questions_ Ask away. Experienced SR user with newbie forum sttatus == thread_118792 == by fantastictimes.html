GPG should be used whenever possible. This includes messages to and from sellers as well as when you submit the address to deliver the product to. Your public key you can give to anyone and everyone if you want (I put it with the message I send to sellers). The only thing people can do with your public key is encrypt messages that only you can decrypt.

If you are waiting for bitcoins to arrive at an address from another it normally takes an hour but it has been known to take variable amounts of time so do not worry if it takes as long as a day or so.
