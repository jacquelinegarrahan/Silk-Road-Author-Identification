Title: Re: Why I do not want free drugs mailed to me
Post by: snark on July 31, 2013, 10:38 pm
Here to play devils advocate a bit. I have done quite a few samples for review for new vendors, and maybe I've just been lucky, but those orders have almost always had some of the best quality packaging and awesome drugs. I think many new vendors have something to prove when they are doing samples, and actually put a lot of effort in. I only order domestic, so I don't have to worry about unknown packs coming to me through customs. The worst packaging and quality has actually been from well known vendors with high ratings, possibly it's that they know they've got business so they get a bit lazy, or get overwhelmed with orders and can't keep up. Always encrypt your address and don't go too trigger happy with ordering tons of samples and it's reasonably safe.
