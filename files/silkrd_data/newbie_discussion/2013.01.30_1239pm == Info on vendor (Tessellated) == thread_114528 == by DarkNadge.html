200ug is a lot for a single blotter. I appreciate that the Avengers bring awareness to the general lack of quality LSD available, however other than the occasional test kit result which is rarely posted it would be nice to see the results of more thorough chemical analysis like gc/ms. The JoR incident is funny and interesting because the tabs were listed as being laid with a higher dose and I am not sure who ran the test, but when the results came back 56ug after everybody raved about them I just chuckled. I am not sure where they pull their numbers from, but it seems very subjective to me. However, 200ug is not unmanageable but once you move past 300ug it's a different drug. For example I was given ~400ug recently at a party and I started to perceive time moving forwards and backwards like somebody was flipping the pages of a picture book. It was more annoying at the time, although very interesting, because it was a birthday party and I was just a little too out of it to really socialize. So I think the 'danger' in misrepresented tab strength is if you're lucky that they're not lying hold on, but more than likely they are lying and it'll skew your perspective on what LSD really is and when you do run into some really good hits have fun. LSD is nice though because once you pass these milestones you won't have a problem at that level again unless set and setting start to bother you. I am lucky that I've always had access to real good and most importantly reasonably priced (I paid $10 for 4). The cheaper the better unless it's a random kid on the lot. There is good LSD available locally, but you need to find the right people. So go to concerts but I would avoid acts like pretty lights, bassnectar, or any big act with a young scene because a wook can smell a custy from a mile away. With the Avengers though roll the dice at least you won't get a research chemical, but don't think if you take 5 you're really eating a ten strip because when I've had that much I couldn't move or really even think. You should get visuals with just 100ug if that's how you want to measure intensity.
