Hi everyone !

I just wanted to create this topic to let french cannabis buyers give feedbacks about their recent orders.

Here is what shall be discussed here :

1) Any vendor that sent cannabis (Weed/Hash/Other) to you in France: Please remember -> Do NOT give specific details about the way your order was packaged !
2) Any French cannabis seller.
3) Shipment issues/questions directly linked to France.

As I don't think we should speak French here, please stick to English as much as possible !


Here is a very basic template to give a feedback about a vendor:

Code: [Select]
- Vendor: VENDOR_NAME (W/5)
- Packaging: X/5
- Product Quality: Y/5
- Order Fullfillment time : Z Days
- FE Required : Yes/No

With :
W > Out of five points, how would you rate that vendor ? Was he responsive ? How fast was the shipment ?
X > Overall quality and stealth of the received package (NO SPECIFIC DETAILS PLEASE)
Y > Subjective quality of received product
Z > Number of days from ordering to receiving the package (Remember that a vendor is most of the time not responsible of postal delays...)

This topic will remain in the newbie section until i get better stats over here :)

Anyway, I hope this topic will help some french SR users !
