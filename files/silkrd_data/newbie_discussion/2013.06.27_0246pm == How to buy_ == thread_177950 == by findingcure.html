Title: Re: How to buy?
Post by: highroller420 on June 27, 2013, 03:23 pm
Read the links, but in short-

1- Join silk road
2- Buy bit coins
3- Have bit coins sent to SR account
4- Put fun items in SR shopping basket
5- Use vendors PGP to generate a PGP with your address for bits to be sent to
6- Make order
7- Await fun items

By reading the guides given already i got it all figured out and some orders made within about an hour, and I'm pretty thick, me
