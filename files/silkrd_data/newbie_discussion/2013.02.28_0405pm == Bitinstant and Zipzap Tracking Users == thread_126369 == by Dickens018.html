What can be done?  I would love to hear ideas,  but only think I can come up with is to treat Bitcoins as investment (100% increase in months),  and use wire transfers in amounts of  $500 to $5000,  depending on your needs and financial condition.  A series of twenty $200 purchases over a year could raise flags,  but a single $4000 purchase looks like a simple investment transaction.  If possible, avoid spending all the money you put in, so it looks like investment.   This should give plausible cover to 99% of users. 

If ever questioned by LE,  tell them to discuss it will your lawyer.  Any decent lawyer can argue this was completely legal and  except for tax purposes,  you have no obligation to describe your private investments.   As for any Bitcoins used for private purchases,   your lawyer could
suggest that from time to time you like to gamble,  mostly with negative results,  just like the most gamblers.
