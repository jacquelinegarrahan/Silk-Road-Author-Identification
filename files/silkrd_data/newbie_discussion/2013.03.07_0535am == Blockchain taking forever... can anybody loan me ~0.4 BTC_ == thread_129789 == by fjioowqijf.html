Entirely speculating, but it seems the tumbler scans through all addresses ever generated (which would be consistent with being able to send to previous addresses in your account). Whenever it hits your address, your account will show as credited if the transaction has been confirmed. I've never seen my own credited before 3 transactions and never took longer than 8 (other than when I got caught in the deposit bug a couple weeks ago). Then that deposit is marked to tumble and will be withdrawn at some point, but your account is credited immediately. So no I don't think BTC activity affects it and yes if you're at 3 confirmations I would think they would arrive in the next few blocks... hopefully there still aren't deposit issues...

Again though all of that is just speculation based off only my own experiences.
