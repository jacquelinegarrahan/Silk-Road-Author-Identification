I've been researching 25i for a year or so pretty in depth, and watching the growing mass of hospitalization reports regarding it, as well as experimenting with it myself.

The good things I'll note about it are the strong visuals and overall pretty rewarding trip at the right dose, which I would think to be between 500-2000ug.

The negative though is that this chem has a weird tendency to cause extremely wild freak outs and hospitalizations, and even deaths in multiple cases. That said I have used this chem personally quite a few times without incident, although people that I know have had trouble.

My advice is to take reasonable doses, and work your way up to be sure that you don't react badly to it. I think the bad reactions may be largely a result of individual chemistry, as the trend seems to be with most psychedelics, so by taking care to not dose big your first time you should be able to avoid any problems.

I'd say start off with a half of one of those, and work your way up. 1.5 might be a really good level, and if you feel comfortable with that then 2 of those tabs could be a more intense but still reasonably safe trip. I would be really wary about increasing the dose any further than that though.

The last comment I would give is that you should never ever ever sell or give anyone 25i with the idea that it's acid. This is where the problems arise, because LSD is safe to take a large dose, and 25i is very likely NOT. The tendency is for people to buy this stuff dirt cheap and distribute it falsely for insane profit, and then for the duped customers to take excessive doses, not knowing that it could be potentially dangerous.

I don't mean to accuse you at all, just putting that idea out there for anyone who might benefit from it.

Post by: valpal on July 02, 2013, 10:52 am
Quote from: williamheisenberg on July 02, 2013, 07:26 am


Thanks for the advice mate, :D would it be a bad idea to use any other drug with LSD, for example cannabis?

Cannabis can really kick start some visuals, but it can also kind of "dull" the mind opening effects (in my experience anyway), i would definitely recommend smoking a few hours into your trip, but not before/at the start for your first time. It can also have a calming effect for some people if you start having a bad time (Its very unlikely you'll have a bad time though, it will be one the greatest times of your life)

Also, some more personal advice, go for a quite large dose for your first time. Some people recommend 50ug-100ug for the first time, but i feel that is too small to get the full effect, and may leave you feeling disappointed, as it wont live up to expectation. I would recommend at least 200ug, maybe even 300ug if you feel confident. But, this is just my personal experience, if you're a rather anxious person when it comes to stuff like this (eg, if you have suffered from panic attacks from cannabis or something) go for the lower dose to see the genesis of the body/mind effects, then go for the higher dose
