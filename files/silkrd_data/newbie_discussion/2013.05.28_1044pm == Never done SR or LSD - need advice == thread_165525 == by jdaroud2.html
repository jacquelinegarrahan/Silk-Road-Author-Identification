Acid is an amazing drug, but it is also a powerful one.  If your gf hasn't even smoked pot, acid might be a little too powerful for her.  One thing you could do is have a small 'social' dose, about 40 or 50 mcgs to familiarize you and her with it.  You won't really get an amazing experience, but it should lessen your gf's concern about it once she knows roughly what it does.  Then wait a little while, a week or so, and take a full dose of perhaps 150 mcgs.  I really recommend that you do it together, as others have said.  It's a wonderful experience to share, and if she is just a sitter and has no idea what your experiencing you'll miss the intimacy of sharing together.  Also, and this is very important, take it somewhere where you have quiet and not a lot of vibes from other people.  Somewhere in nature is the absolutely best place to take it, rather than in a house or out with other people running about.  I particularly like taking it in the desert.  It is so quiet and free of vibes from other people.  Nature is definitely the best place to take it.

Title: Re: Never done SR or LSD - need advice
Post by: fatbrian on June 02, 2013, 05:44 am
Dosage, Set, and Setting

Dosage:  There are arguments for taking only a  1/2 hit your first time.  I myself did 1 whole hit my first time.  YMMV

Set:  Your mindset.  Are you generally looking forward to a good time, for a 8 hour adventure through wonderland?  No big things worrying or bothering you?  Good.

Setting:  Where you are.  Is it safe to run around and giggle and have a good time without other people weirding out on you?  You'll want to be indoors for part of it, then at some point you'll need to be outdoors for the space.  Its hard to explain but once you're tripping, you'll know what I mean.

Its best if you and your GF do it together.  If she's a very straight laced person, then maybe you both do half hits as sort of a training run.

Post by: lanman on June 03, 2013, 11:01 pm
If you have a girlfriend or a close friend, I would go ahead and do it with them. This way you can share  the experience with each other. If you have a sober sitter, I would recommend that he stays removed from most of the trip, unless you ask for his/her help, since a sober sitter is not going to be able to add much to the trip, except for helping calm you down if you get too stressed.

Regarding setting, I would do it either at home on a quiet day with some nice music or outdoors, and not somewhere where you will have to have social interaction.

Just remember that regardless of what happens everything will be over in 6-8 hours, so just relax, take what comes, and don't panic. LSD is wonderful!
