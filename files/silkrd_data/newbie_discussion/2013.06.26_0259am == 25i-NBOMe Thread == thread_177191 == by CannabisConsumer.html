While we all get our psot count up let's have a thread about 25i-NBOMe.

Good experiences, bad experiences, side effects, tolerance, cross tolerance, interactions, let's talk about it all!

I'll start us off. The first time I which I took 25i I was expecting a bit more. 1mg didn't even show me visuals although I was feeling extreme euphoria and smiling quite controllably.

The second time was a different story, I began by taking 1.5mg sublingually. Then the trip took off.

I began by watching The Lorax. Then after that I began to peak, seeing my first visuals and seeing fractal patterns. I felt a strongly enhanced sense of morality and I admit I regressed quite a bit but it was overall a positive experience and the only thing I wish is that I could trust the drug's future side effects. Everything I take it I become a hypochondriac, blaming the 25i for all medical problems regardless of whether they actually stemmed from it or not. An example of this would be a nasty twitch I get in my shoulder after taking the 25i and still get once and a while.

Anyways cannabis and 25i go together brilliantly. It really brings out the visuals and helps with discomfort caused by vasoconstriction.

Always start as low as you can on 25i as the dosage required varies GREATLY between each person. I take .5-1 tab of 1200ug every week or two weeks and even with tolerance I still get where I want to be. For example me and most of my close friends for the first time took 1 tab each and boy did that blow our minds. The first time I tried over a tab the trip was quite uncomfortable(racing rushing thoughts, thinking I was dieing etc...fun times in the end) so I don't do over 1200ug now. On the other side of the spectrum another friend who tried half a tab for his first time was throwing up within 40 mins and said he literally felt his brain frying and now will never touch it again...so for your own sake I recommend starting on a low dosage.
