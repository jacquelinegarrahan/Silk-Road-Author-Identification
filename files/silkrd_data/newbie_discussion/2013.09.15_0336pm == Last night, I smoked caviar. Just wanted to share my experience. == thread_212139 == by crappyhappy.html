Title: Last night, I smoked caviar. Just wanted to share my experience.
Post by: crappyhappy on September 15, 2013, 03:36 pm
I recently purchased 1 gram of caviar gold from vendor QualityKings (great transaction by the way, highly recommend). For those who don't know, caviar is weed that's been soaked in BHO and rolled in kief. I first vaped a small amount on a bed of regular weed And after (what I thought was) a good session, I was quite stoned. It was a really pleasant, euphoric kind of high. I then went to smoke the ABV out of my pipe. On inspection, the weed STILL HAD TONS OF GREEN. After about a half-hour smoke session, The weed hadn't even been fully vaped!! So my friend and I smoked the rest out of his pipe (if I did it again, I would have just finished it in the vape on a higher temperature) and got FUCKED. It was awesome. And I still have a ton left!! Later in the night, we did some dabs of QualityKing's OG business oil that he gave me as a free sample. I'm defiantly going to have to but some more of that. It was an awesome night.
