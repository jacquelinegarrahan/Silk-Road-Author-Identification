What SR really needs though is:
Better buyer protection; full monetary recompense upon unsuccessful deliveries; perhaps an exclusive SR currency not subjected to the volatility of the bitcoin
Less collusion
Elimination of oligopolies
More free market qualities
A reduction in the remarkably overpriced products
More consistent and higher quality products
More competition
More recourse options to mitigate deceptive vendor tactics resulting in unsatisfactory transactions
A realization that without the people the vendors, DPR, and SR would not exist in this profit making capacity SR affords them. Yet everything obtainable at higher risk and higher cost on SR will always remain available IRL, absent many of the injustices buyers are subject to on SR
