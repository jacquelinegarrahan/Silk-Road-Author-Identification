Title: Re: Busted with h-ron
Post by: 3eyes on April 09, 2013, 12:55 am
If you get a letter ignore it and consider your address flagged.

Some people say they still get packages after they get a letter and some don't.  If your source is in the same country as you it is much easier to get around a flagging.

Pay attention to who your post man is and what time he comes. If you notice anything different or are asked to sign for a package refuse to. Without your signature there is no proof you knew about the item sent.

Some vendors seems to offer a signature for an extra charge on big orders so both ends agree it got there but for small amounts it is not needed.
