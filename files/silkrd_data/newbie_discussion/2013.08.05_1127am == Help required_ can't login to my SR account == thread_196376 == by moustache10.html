Title: Re: Help required: can't login to my SR account
Post by: Turkey for Breakfast on August 05, 2013, 11:37 am
A few things you should try. Close TOR and reopen it. See if you can log in.

If that doesn't work restart you computer and try again.

If that doesn't work try to create a new account and check if that works..

If even that doesn't get you at least logged in, just wait a while and try again in a few hours..

If the new account does work, contact customer support and ask what is wrong with your original account.
