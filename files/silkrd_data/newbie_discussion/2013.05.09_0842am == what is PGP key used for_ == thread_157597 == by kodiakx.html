Title: what is PGP key used for?

I see PGP key all the time in posts and vendor descriptions, what is it used for?
Title: Re: what is PGP key used for?
Post by: onufri on May 09, 2013, 09:17 am
It's a method of encrypting messages. Someone posts a 'public key' that allows you to encrypt a message to them that (theoretically) only they can open. If the message is intercepted somewhere along the way, it shouldn't be readable. You can post your own public key so that other people can send messages to you that only you can read (assuming your computer or your passphrase for privately decrypting messages to you hasn't been compromised by law enforcement).

It's an added layer of security above and beyond Tor.
