Title: Re: Bitcoin Help
Post by: TheEmeraldTriangle on February 01, 2013, 07:46 pm
FYI I'm a vendor here and have been for 6 months but, unfortunately got trapped by the 50 post rule. (Not much time to spend on here.)

Anyway... The other thing you should be aware of is that it can take quite some time for your bitcoins to show up. Or not. :) It depends on how busy the SR servers are.

Another thing to keep in mind is, always put a few more coins in your SR wallet than you think you'll need. Shipping fees vary, and prices can too when bitcoin is volatile. I don't know how many PMs I get every week asking for some sort of special arrangement because the buyer did put quite enough coins in their wallet to cover their planned purchase. Vendors, especially the good ones, are VERY busy people and it takes quite a bit of time to put up a new and special listing just for you. Nobody's fault. Tor is slow. So don't go there unless you just gotta. :P
