Title: Re: Whats means FE?
Post by: JohnJohnerson333 on September 13, 2013, 04:57 pm
FE means "Finalize Early".  I means you have to finalize the order, so the vendor gets paid, before you receive the order. 

I'm not a fan of FE for ANY reason.... But, if you are a new buyer with no stats, a lot of vendors will ask you to FE so they don't have to risk waiting the 17 days for it to auto finalize.  Once you have a few recorded transactions, with a solid history, most vendors will not ask you to FE. 

If you are dealing with a trusted vendor, and you should be, then FE isn't going to be that big of a deal.
