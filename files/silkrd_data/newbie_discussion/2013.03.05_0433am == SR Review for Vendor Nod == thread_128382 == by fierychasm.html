Title: SR Review for Vendor Nod
Post by: fierychasm on March 05, 2013, 04:33 am
C&H Review for Vendor: Nod

H 5/5- Absolutely as good as BT gets; dissolves completely in water without heat, pungent vinegar aroma, no mysterious 'floaters" in the spoon, great solid warm rush upon IV, and the buzz has legs.

C 5/5- All solid pieces consisting of beautiful pearl scales. Once the packaging was opened, I could smell that this was the real deal. Snorting a bump gives one an instant high with that long-lost numb-face that connoisseurs will recall from the days of C's heyday. I have been using C since 1986, and during the 90s my upstream connection bought by the key so I know the diff between "off the brick" and "re-rock", as well as the pollutants and additives that corrupted the C over the past few decades . This is excellent gear.

Packaging 5/5- Solid and stealthy packaging that uses double packaging and wrapping throughout such that even an abused package won't cause a breach. Nothing on the exterior that would cause any suspicion. I have placed two orders on Thursday evening, and received both Saturday morning (using express).

Customer Service 5/5- Responsive honest polite. Definitely worth doing biz with again!
