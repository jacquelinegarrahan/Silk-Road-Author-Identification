Post by: ganjaman18 on September 29, 2013, 11:06 pm
On the subject of Vaporizers, I would choose a Herbalaire over a Volcano... I never really saw the appeal of them aside from the design.

The Herbalaire allows you to fill up bags with your medicine or direct draw straight from the unit or Filter through a Bong. It is also about half the price of a Volcano and saves you having to grind into a powder before use. If you are in need of something portable, all the resources on the net seem to suggest that an Arizer Solo is one of the best units around. I am planning on buying one of those this week for the car and at friends houses.

The Magic Flight Launch Box, from what I've heard, is a very good discreet and portable vaporizer, however it is very easy to combust your materials instead of heating them up to the correct Vaporization temperature (which, while we are on the subject is about 190-200 Celsius) as they do not have a built in temperature controller, it is down to you inserting and removing the battery at the right times.

If you are not a recreational user, I doubt you will care much about the "stoned" feeling, but just in case, and for anyone reading this who is interested, Vaporizers produce a very different type of high, it is much more cerebral, more of a head high and you will want to use a higher grade plant in them to produce big clouds of intoxicating, healing, delicious vapor.

I couldn't recommend any good domestic vendors for you as I'm not from the US but I will advise you to check the vendors feedback, never FE on a large order if you can't afford to lose it and most of all enjoy! If you have any questions about herbs or vapes, feel free to ask. I wish you the best of luck with your treatment and with your travels down the Silk Road.
