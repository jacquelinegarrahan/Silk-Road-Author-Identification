Title: Re: Meth users: what's so good about it?
Post by: yourm0m on May 13, 2013, 02:40 pm
Alright, I think I am the only actual meth advocate posting here yet, so let me break down why meth is my DoC currently with some background:

--Have been a heavy cocaine (powder, nasal) user
--Have been a heavy heroin (powder, IV) user at another point
--Love MDMA to this day

Meth combines the positive aspects of all of these drugs without the negative.  It gives me the verbal eloquence that cocaine does without the tweak/fiending, the pleasant clarity of heroin without the physically addictive properties, and the extroversion of MDMA without feeling inebriated.

For reference, I use meth mostly orally as my RoA; I have never smoked or IV'ed it.  I usually do a bump nasally when I first get it to get going, but I really prefer taking it orally.  It lasts longer, doesn't have the sharp 'edge', and I personally get a better euphoria/sense of well-being with it.

Another positive of meth for me is that I can ONLY get it on the road where I live; it simply isn't available elsewhere, despite almost every other drug being around.  Well, I'm sure  I could synthesize some bathtub meth, but I'm not interested in that.

Disclaimer: This is only my personal experience; I know that for many others that meth is more addictive than any of the other drugs I have used, but for me it seems to be a happy and affordable mid-ground.  Also, even buying it on SR, I feel it is VERY economical for the amount I use vs. what I pay, significantly more so than anything other than maybe imported MDMA, which you can't use for more than two doses in a row without feeling tapped out due to tolerance and the like.
