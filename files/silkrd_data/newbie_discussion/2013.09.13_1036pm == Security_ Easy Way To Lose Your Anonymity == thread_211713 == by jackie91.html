NEVER communicate with anyone regarding SR matters using ANY email address. You will instantly negate the anonymity SR provides and you and everything about you can become an open book.
In my opinion these messages are LE phishing attempts and if you fall for it, you put yourself and others at risk.
I hope this post is totally unnecessary and anyone who reads it thinks "man this person must think we are complete morons". And I hope that's what you think because that means you are being safe. I don't think anyone on SR is a moron, but there are people, especially newbies, who just may not have given any thought to this particular method of LE attempting to get you to reveal yourself. My mantra is "Protect myself, protect myself". When I protect myself I am also helping to protect everyone else. There is no such thing as being too safe.
