However, we are in the process of securing a large order of them because we are as paranoid as you probably are. If that's what it will take for our customers to feel safe, then that's what we'll provide! Of course it doesn't matter how thick the bag is, or if it has several aluminum layers to obfuscate its contents within an x-ray machine. If cannabis stays in ANY bag long enough, it's odors WILL eventually steep out. The thicker the bag, the longer it takes for the smell to steep. SO, PACKAGING MATERIALS + THICKNESS (MIL)  = RATE AT WHICH CANNABIS ODORS WILL EVENTUALLY STEEP. --=Do not forget this fact!=--

Once a bag begins to steep its contents odors, human olfactory glands may not be able to pickup on it but K9's without a doubt, surely would. So despite what method is used, the trick is to reduce the time between packaging and shipment so as to "beat the clock" of the rate at which cannabis odors will steep. This is why we triple seal and only ship priority! The less time it spends in transit the safer you are!

Sometimes I would open a package and get SUPER concerned about whether or not I was being monitored or watched since many sellers do a VERY poor job at properly packaging for the safety of their customers. Trust me when I say that as sellers we are going ABOVE and beyond what other vendors have used to ship me their wares. We're all really paranoid about being on SR to begin with and even go to great extents to ensure no fingerprints are left on the outside or inside of the items we plan to ship. Our packaging materials and shipping labels NEVER come into contact with any our herb or other wares. As a secondary precaution, we also wipe down the outside of our packages with rubbing alcohol JUST in case there are ANY trace amounts of illicit substances on it. We even wear gloves when dropping of our packages into the priority blue bins! Yes, we're THAT careful.

We are confident that we've established a  solid process for our shipping practices.
