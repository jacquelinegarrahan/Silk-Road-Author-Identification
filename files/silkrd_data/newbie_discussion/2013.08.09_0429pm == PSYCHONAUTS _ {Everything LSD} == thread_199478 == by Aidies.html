Hopefully Jannis will make a comeback sometime soon...

MAHAKALA  He was my first - my reintroduction into LSD...and it was glorious. I had one of his candies...bought on a late night whim with just enough for one... after that I continued to explore the psychedelic route.... his dali lamas- candies- 2cb and mescaline are all superior quality. Like Jannis he does not have any listings currently...surely hell return sometime soon.

TESS: The white on white - the look of construction board- these were also excellent. The transition in was a bit harsher than the previous two but it was well worth it. Like a Mhakala trip- Tess's also made everything look like an intricate pattern...a layer of interconnected pieces that covered the earth. i felt overwhelmingly positive and hopeful...very good experience.

HollandOnline: I tried his double-sided hoffmans when he was still open to U.S buyers...I tried with a group of people and it was divided...almost- exactly- half felt nothing...I included...the rest felt what was described as something comparable to Jannis...light but wonderful.
