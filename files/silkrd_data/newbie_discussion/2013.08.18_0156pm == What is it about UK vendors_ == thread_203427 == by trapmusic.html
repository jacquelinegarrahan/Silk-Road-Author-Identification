Title: Re: What is it about UK vendors?
Post by: Hornswaggled on August 18, 2013, 02:03 pm
I believe it's because most drugs are manufactured outside the UK, so there are more middle-men in the loop by the time it gets to them, so more people taking their cut along the way.

Plus the fact the they have already taken the risk of getting the stuff through customs.

If you ship from, say, the Netherlands, then the drug may well be shipping directly from the lab/farm/cook-shop so there are fewer mark-ups.

That's my theory, anyway, and I think that's why the UK dealers do good business with UK customers - there is no risk of customs stopping the package.
