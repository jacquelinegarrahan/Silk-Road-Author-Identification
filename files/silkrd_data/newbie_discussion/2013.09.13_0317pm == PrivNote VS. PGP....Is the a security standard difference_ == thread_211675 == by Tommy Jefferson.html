Title: Re: PrivNote VS. PGP....Is the a security standard difference?
Post by: CaliTrees on September 13, 2013, 03:44 pm
There is a huge difference.

PGP is the technology for encryption.

PrivNote is a website that attempts to make that PGP encryption easier for the user.

The difference is that when you use PGP you are responsible for all the data because you keep the PRIVATE KEY.

When you use PrivNote, they keep the PRIVATE KEY, so they can decrypt your data.  You must trust PrivNote, a third party.  You must trust that they do not abuse their access to the private key and you must trust that they are in fact deleting your messages after they are read.  There is no way to know if they are doing this.  There is no way to know that PrivNote isn't a honeypot setup by the NSA for the sole purpose to read messages that are thought to be encrypted.

The difference is where the private key is stored, PGP it is stored on your computer and you are in control.  PGP is a much much better solution if you actually care about your data.
