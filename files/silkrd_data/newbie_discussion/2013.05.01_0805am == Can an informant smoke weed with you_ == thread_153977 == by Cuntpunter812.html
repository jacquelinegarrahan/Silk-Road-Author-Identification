I do believe and am pretty certain that cops themselves CAN smoke weed or any related activities when this is to further an investigation. Undercover police often have to infiltrate very deep into gangs and organizations. When dealing with drug gangs, you often need to start at the bottom which often involves some kind of illegal activities, like buying or smoking weed. Since they're allowed to buy drugs, why wouldn't they be allowed to smoke it ? It's not a bigger offense in any way.

Moreover if a cop smokes weed (when undercover) and by law it still constituted an offense, then it's very unlikely that he would get a punishment in court.
