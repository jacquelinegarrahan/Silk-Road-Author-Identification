No you can enter what you like. Do bear in mind that you don't need to create a public/private keyset if your just trying to send messages to people who have already provided you with their public key - you can just use that.

If you want to provide a public key to pass around to other people for them to send you encrypted messages, then you'd need to generate a keyset.

Hope that makes sense!
