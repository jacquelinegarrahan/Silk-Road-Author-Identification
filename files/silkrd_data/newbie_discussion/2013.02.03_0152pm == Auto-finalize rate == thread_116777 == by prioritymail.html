Title: Re: Auto-finalize rate
Post by: 1337ninjer on February 03, 2013, 04:23 pm
Well if you look at SR as being like a video game, this is like dying on level 1. The games not over, but you might as well start over because it won't really set you back too far and you may need that extra life later.

If you see SR as more like a puppy, then making a new account is akin to having it put to sleep because it pooped in your bedroom once.

When I have instances where I'll be away when my package arrives, I have just gone ahead and finalized. It wasn't even because I was worried about AF, but because I did not think it was right to keep the seller waiting for payment because I chose to be unavailable when I was expecting a package to arrive. If the package did not arrive or there was some problem with the product and after contacting the seller made it obvious I had been burned and I was SOL because I had already finalized, I'd give 1/5 feedback and explain what happened in the review. This is exactly why if you ever do FE, you should not submit 5/5 feedback stating that you finalized early, but wait until you have received and tried the product and then post the review. That way it shows up at the top of the seller's feedback and not three pages back where your "FE Will update" will be if anyone ever bothered to update. I see a 3 month old 'FE will update" and realize why these people were asked to FE in the first place.

In a nutshell, it's better to FE than to AF and if you do FE it does not stand for "Feedback Early".

As for whether or not to start a new account, I think Marlo said it best, "Eeither do it or don't, but I got some place to be"
