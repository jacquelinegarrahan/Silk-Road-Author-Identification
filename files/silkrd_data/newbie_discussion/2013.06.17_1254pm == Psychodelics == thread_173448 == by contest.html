Post by: driver81 on June 17, 2013, 02:03 pm
Hey there man, I recently began my psychedelic experimentation too. I believe ketamine to be an absolutely fantastic starting point because of the short come up and come down. Also it is generally much safer and purer than a lot of psychedelics around at the moment. I wouldn't class it as a true psychedelic like LSD or the 2Cx family, but it introduces you to ego loss and CEVs (closed eye visuals) in a much more controlled way.

There's been a huge amount of NBOMe family chemicals as well as DOx family chemicals being laid onto tabs that look like LSD. The only way to know with moderate certainty what you have is using a chemical test (such as an eztest Marquis test kit). You do need to be careful because you could develop a taste for dropping large amounts of LSD, then repeat the same dose with NBOMe and seriously freak yourself out. I am a fan of NBOMe but you have to watch out.

If all else fails, stick to the classical psychedelics - LSD, psychedelic mushrooms, DMT, mescaline and (arguably) the 2Cx family.

Hope that helps!
