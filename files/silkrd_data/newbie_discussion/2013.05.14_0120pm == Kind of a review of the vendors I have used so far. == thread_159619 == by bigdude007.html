So I placed the order, used PGP for the first time properly and I didn't get a message back saying WTF! or any others saying that it was incorrect or my address was missing.  I have put that into the success side of things.
This was all last Wednesday.  As of this moment I am still without the order.  Had hoped it had got to me for the weekend, but that can't be helped from this end.
I checked MR A's vendor page to see when I should start chucking him messages about orders that hadn't arrived, but lack-of-drug anxiety hit and I messaged him.
