If your looking to smoke meth, youll need a meth bowl, or bulb. Or you can make your own from a light bulb. Best bet if your going to choose smoking as your ROA, is to spend the 10-20 bucks on a legit meth bowl. There are some for sale on SR.

Snorting is a very common ROA, and my personal choice. Ingestion with capsules, or simply weighing out and swallowing a shard as is, will work just as well. In fact it will be more time efficient as far as how long until it takes effect.

Then theres of coarse always IV. I know nothing about this ROA. Now keep in mind, that IVing and smoking meth are two totally different highs you get, as opposed to ingestion or snorting. I have never personally smoked or banged (IV) meth, so i cant explain how, but all the people i speak to that do smoke or inject it make it very clear that those two ROA's create an entirely different animal than snorting or ingesting will.

Its important to have self control and will power when beginning to use meth, along with knowing moderation and responsibility. If you do not posses these traits, then meth is NOT the drug for you.
