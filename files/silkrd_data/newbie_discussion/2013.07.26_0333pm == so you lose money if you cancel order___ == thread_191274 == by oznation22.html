Well that actually depends on whether or not the purchase was hedged. Do you remember seeing it say "hedged" or "unhedged" by the order when you placed it? If it is hedged you get back the exact dollar amount you paid. Title: Re: so you lose money if you cancel order???
Post by: odd on July 27, 2013, 01:26 pm
It's all 100% automatic every time you place an order.  Anytime you place an order a percentage of the cost is sent to SR for fees.  It's not that the SR admins are picking and choosing who gets hit with a fee it happens automatically with every order placed.  So whether the vendor cancels or you cancel the order the fees are taken out.  This is why it is so important to message your vendor before placing an order to make sure you are both on the same page and your order doesn't get canceled.
