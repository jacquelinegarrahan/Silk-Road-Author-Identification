Title: Re: Need Advice on Funding SR Account
Post by: BlackIris on June 08, 2013, 06:36 pm
You have to first create a wallet address on the SR in case you have not already done so. You find how to do so by clicking on the account link up above (where it is also listed the amount of BTC you have - 0.0 now, naturally). You will find a button in the page that opens that will tell you that since you have not yet deposited any funds you have to create an address first. Simply click on that button and let it generate one.

After you have the wallet address simply transfer the BTC from Pecunix to that wallet address (beware of not mistaking! copy & paste to be sure). Done.

P.S: It will take a little while before the funds are deposited, so don't panic. You can search on blockchain.info about your transaction by looking for the SR wallet address. Usually it takes about 6 confirmations before the funds are transferred.
