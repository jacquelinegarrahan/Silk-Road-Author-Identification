Title: Something I find weird about people perception of coke
Post by: beezerbuz on September 20, 2013, 03:59 pm
Something I don't get.  Reading many reviews I see people say, "hey this was great blow, I was able to eat and sleep after an hour of doing my gram".  Or "what great stuff, didnt numb my mouth too much, and no jonsing when I was done". 

I was fortunate enough to live in Colombia as a teenager in the mid to late 80's, and let me tell you, those are not the signs of good coke.  First off I will never forget the smell, it smelled kind of like a hospital, maybe slight ether or something, but very clean.  And numbmenss after just a line, your throat, mouth, nose, face, you could probably chop it off and not feel it.   Eating, forget about it, no change of eating a thing, sleep, forget it, after several hours of jonsing to the max you would eventually fall asleep.  The jonsing was probably the worst part of it, but it would fade after a few hours.  Another thing I thought of, teeth grinding, man, it could drive you crazy.  Lastly you could blow through an 8 ball and your nose would be none the worse for wear, no burning, stuffiness, just clean.  As for the buzz, just pure euphoria, not speedy at all, no jitters, just a clean superman like feeling.  I have long given up the search for that kind of blow as I guess it just dos not exist anymore (unless you get right down to the source I suppose), but still grateful that there is someplace like this around to get a small taste of the glory days.

So to wrap up, I am thinking that most of those people that say those kind of things are the younger generation, never haven been exposed to the real thing.  And BTW if you do happen to know where that old school stuff does exist please let me know  ;) 

Just some thoughts.
