Personally, if I were unable to memorize the URL, I would set up an e-mail account on tormail.org and keep all my information on there. You can PGP it if you're going to put sensitive information up there, you just have to store your private key in some safe place and password protect it (I would use a USB stick for this purpose). Optionally you can just get a USB stick, put your stuff on there and encrypt the disk itself.
