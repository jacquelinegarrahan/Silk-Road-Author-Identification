Post by: Vinospear on May 10, 2013, 08:03 am
If you click on "Account" in SR, it will give you a bitcoin address to send your bitcoins to.

Copy this address, then go to your regular browser, go to coinbase.com and log in, then go to Transactions and click on Send Bitcoins - then paste the address.

To add an extra level of anonymity, send first to an anonymous account you set up at blockchain.info, then on to SR. However, SR has its own mixers, so the SR account you send to will not be the same one the vendors take their money out of. People with more experience than me will be able to advise you if this is safe for all concerned.
