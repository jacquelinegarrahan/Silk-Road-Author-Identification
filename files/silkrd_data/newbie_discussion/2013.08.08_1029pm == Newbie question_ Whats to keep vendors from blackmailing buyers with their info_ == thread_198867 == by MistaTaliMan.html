Title: Re: Newbie question: Whats to keep vendors from blackmailing buyers with their info?
Post by: toweringjack on August 08, 2013, 10:43 pm
Bad for business.  Drug dealers come here to make money. The more good quality feedback they get, the more people will want to buy from them. If they are "skull fucking" their customers, they probably won't get too many once the word leaks out. (And it would, even if there was attempted extortion.) Plus, dealers have a lot more to lose than the people they sell to. They aren't about to risk everything to snitch on someone they don't even know. But if you are still worried, check their feedback, if they have made hundreds of transactions and everyone seems happy, you have no reason to be afraid. :)

Title: Re: Newbie question: Whats to keep vendors from blackmailing buyers with their info?
Post by: toweringjack on August 08, 2013, 11:02 pm
Sellers are supposed to destroy their copy of your address as soon as they ship your package. But even if they don't, they still couldn't extort you because that's all they have. Anyone with access to a phone book can find a public address, it proves nothing. And if they had proof that you bought drugs from them they aren't about to turn it over to the police because they would be facing even heavier time. Trust me it's just paranoia, I buy here all the time. You're safe. :)
