How did you get them sent to your email address ? , bitcoin is kept in a wallet on your pc as far as i am aware.
Are you sure that was not an email receipt sent to you and the coin is waiting to be transfered to your wallet from bitinstant ?

If you want to transfer funds to SR, you first need to login to your account and then  go to account at top of page.
On your account page you will see an address where you can send your bitcoin. That is your address at SR and no one else has access to that.  You send the bitcoin to that addy from your bitcoin client on your pc.
It can take a while, anything from 20 mins to several hours depending.
