Title: Re: Help me find a cannabis strain - Anxiety issues
Post by: mcguire39 on July 02, 2013, 05:29 pm
I believe leafly dot com has a 'finder' that will let you specify the characteristics you're looking for, then recommend strains based on that. Based on the things you listed I would say you will need a sativa/indicia hybrid. Basically too much indicia and it might leave you couch locked; not enough indicia and it won't have much 'body high'.

Also methods of ingestion might be worth looking at. Some people get a different or 'clearer' high from edibles than from smoking.

The list of things you said you specifically don't want, to me, seems to be best avoided by not 'overdoing it'.

What might work for you is to find a vendor who carries a number of strains and then order a 'sampler pack'. Then you get a couple grams each of a bunch of different strains so you can see which works best for you, and then order just a larger amount of your favorite next time.
