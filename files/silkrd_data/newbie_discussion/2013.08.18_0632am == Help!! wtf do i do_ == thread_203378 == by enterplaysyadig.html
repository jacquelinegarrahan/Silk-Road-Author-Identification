Title: Re: Help!! wtf do i do?
Post by: Donna Freck on August 18, 2013, 07:08 am
If you're anywhere near a decently sized city, look on craigslist under the sublets section. They only want cash, and you can find good apartments or rooms this way, occasionally for a pretty good discount, for weeks or even months at a time. And I've not come across someone who wanted to sublet their place for anything other than cash. Alternatively, assuming you're near a city, you could get a prepaid debit card and check out airbnb.

If you're not near a city, small-ish towns frequently have motels with very agreeable rates.

Five grand is a lot of money for someone in your situation. You'll be fine. In fact, use it as an opportunity to step outside your comfort zone a little bit. Buy a one-way plane ticket to a city you've always wanted to visit (can you do that in cash? I don't know if you can anymore, but maybe someone with a credit card will help you out if you pay them the price of the ticket), find a cheap sublet or hostel and enjoy yourself. If you're in Europe, get on a train. There are hostels everywhere. Go places. Do things. See the world. You'll learn quickly that things aren't nearly as bad as you think they are.

Good luck.
