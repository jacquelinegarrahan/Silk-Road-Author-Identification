Title: Newb Reviews: Drugsbunnies
Post by: LilSebastian on April 03, 2013, 08:55 am
You might be a newb but you can still vendor review...in the the Newbie Discussion board.

Anyhoo thought I write a review for the LSD seller Drugsbunnies. When doing research I couldn't find anything recent about their tabs on the forums, so  here's an attempt to amend that.

Bought a bunch of Drugsbunnies 100-110ug  white fluff LSD tabs. They arrived reasonably quickly and when the seller said they would. Stealth was...well I'm able to type this review so clearly the stealth worked.
However, this acid is very mild, I had two tabs and was just feeling it. I mean the trip was enjoyable but not particularly psychedelic or intense. Just some mild sensory intensity type stuff, and a bit of visuals (walls melting/patterns).
These were priced at around $27 which is kind standard for this sellers country but overpriced for strength. Very pleased (and annoyed :P) to see that the prices have decreased considerably since then.

All in all, good communication, mild product but good to see they're adjusting prices accordingly. It would be nice to see a bit more Drugsbunnies when you search the forums.
