Title: Re: Do You Only Buy From Sellers with 100% Feedback?
Post by: mcguire39 on June 23, 2013, 05:48 pm
I don't select a vendor based on feedback percentage alone. Here's my top 10 list of things to look for in a vendor:

1. Domestic
2. Generally prefer vendors who sell cannabis only. It's ok if they sell other non-drug related things, but generally prefer the only drug they sell is cannabis.
3. Seller account age; older or least a month or so generally preferred
4. Prefer to see some feedback saying excellent stealth, or vendor explicitly says MBB's or mylar used
5. Prefer there to be some recent feedback, within the last couple days.
6. Vendor has been online within the last couple days (can't tell that though if they're in stealth mode).
7. Forum search reveals some good posts from people with + karma and a good number of posts. I think this is harder to 'fake' then vendor feedback (more important for newer vendors imho).
8. Requires or at least encourages use of PGP for sensitive info.
9. Has to be the type of cannabis I'm looking for obviously, in the quantity I am seeking.
10. Vendor profile page is fairly detailed and has halfway decent grammar. Doesn't have to be perfect but needs to show effort and good understanding of product and a concern for customers.

So with those requirements there's maybe a dozen vendors on my short list, so far all tried have been excellent!

In general dealing with vendors:
When purchasing if the vendor has a tormail or other contact info in case SR is down, it's good to make a note of that in case you need it.
Read the product description and vendor page carefully; some want address in all caps, some want zip + 4, etc., I try to follow the format they ask for.
