to Question #1, The truth is most packages get through safely as seen by vendor's feedbacks, however there is always a risk of doing something illegal.  It just comes down to whether or not the benefit outweighs the risk for you.  It is not really safe to drive a car.  One of the leading causes of death is car accidents, yet people still drive cars every day.  That is one perspective anyways.

to Question #2  Escrow is the system where two interested parties are making a transaction, but they agree to have a 3rd party hold onto the funds temporarily.  Silk Road acts as the escrow service for us.  When u purchase a product, the money is held by SR and is not released to the seller until you finalize your order.  If there is a problem with your order or it does not arrive, that is when the escrow service is really needed.  This means there could be a dispute between buyer and seller.  The SR admins will look at the situation and decide who rightfully deserves the money or resolve the issue another way such as reshipping.  This is what escrow does, and it helps keep scammers at bay, and promotes more honest transactions.  Just note that if you Finalize Early, you are abandoning the escrow system and putting your trust completely on the seller to ship your product. 

PGP is something entirely different than escrow, this is just for encrypting messages back and forth.  It is really useful for sending your address to vendors safely.  If you want to learn PGP, which I suggest you do, it is easy, check out Astor's tutorial:

Astor's GPG4USB Tutorial: http://32yehzkk7jflf6r2.onion/gpg4usb/

GPG4USB homepage: http://gpg4usb.cpunk.de/index.html


To Question #3 it depends on what country your in.  There are different services in different countries I believe and in some countries bitcoins are easier to acquire.  I would not worry about being suspicious about buying bitcoins, as people buy them for all sorts of reasons.  People buy them either as an investment, or to transfer money, or use them to buy goods online.  Even some physical businesses accept bitcoins for payment.  The bitcoin is just a new currency.
