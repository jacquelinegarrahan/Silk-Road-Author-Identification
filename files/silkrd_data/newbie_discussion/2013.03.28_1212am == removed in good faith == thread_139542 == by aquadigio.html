Title: Re: Whats to keep a shady vendor from blacmailing us anonymously on forum?
Post by: Codesr on March 28, 2013, 12:30 am
Scams are very possible. Avoid any vendor that requires FE (finalizing early) if you do not trust them or their reputation and you will be fine. New vendors pay a $500 bond when they open the vendor account. New vendors wouldn't want to loose the bond, but I'm sure someone could scam more than $500. Well established vendors wouldn't typically scam because the business is good here and it takes time to build a reputation and relationships. However, I read a thread about an established vendor that eventually scammed using FE and ran off with a lot of money. I'm sure that there are threads about new vendor scams. Hope that helps.
