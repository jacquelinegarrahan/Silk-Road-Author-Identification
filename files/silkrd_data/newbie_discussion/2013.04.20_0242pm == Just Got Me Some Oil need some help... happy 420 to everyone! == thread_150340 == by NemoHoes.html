You should try hot knives with the oil. It's my favorite. No waste and it will last a lot longer.

Another tip,  put a later of ashes in the bowl first. Then, drop a drop of oil (melted from a lighter off a pin ) onto the ashes.

Then, light and smoke.  THe ashes hold the oil from going down the screen.  It burns up in the ashes.  It's like smoking crack.

Another fun one is oil off of foil. Not the healthiest and a little ghetto .    Drop oil onto foil. Hold lighter under it. Use a straw or tube to suck up the smoke.  Use a straw with knives too so you don't burn your lips in an accident.

1lb propane tank - any hardware store or walmart $5
pencil flame tip for soldering $10-15
kitchen knife - heat center of knife til red hot bend it until pointing nearly straight down
big safety pin - stretch out and curve the sharp pointy tip slightly
empty bic pen (melt the air hole closed)

knife will easily hang off tip of torch which will keep it red hot. tank lasts 45-50 hours.

1g oil is approx 150-200 hoots

This will be best 20 bucks u spend, lasts years too
