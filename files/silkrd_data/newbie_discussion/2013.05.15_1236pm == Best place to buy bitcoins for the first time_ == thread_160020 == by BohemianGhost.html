Title: Re: Best place to buy bitcoins for the first time?
Post by: cheaplime on May 15, 2013, 01:51 pm
Localbitcoins - UK bank transfer or meet in person.

With UKBT you should have your BTC in minutes providing the seller is online and decent. I have had no problems with this service. There is a mark-up of about £5 at least.

Alternatively you can send £ to an exchange such as Bitstamp. Their bank is based in Slovenia I think so you have to do a SEPA transfer which also incurs costs (conversion/bank charges).

My method has been to buy more than I need at localbitcoins and send the surplus to Bitstamp where I wait for BTC value to rise. Once it has risen I sell the BTC to at least cover my costs. The fiat stays in Bitstamp ready to be exchanged when I want to make another purchase.
