A lot of the information on the way THC effects the brain suggests it has a both negative and positive effect on dreaming. Hence, it is down to the individual.
THC in itself is technically classed as a hallucinogen, and from experience if i sleep on other hallucinogens i either have an amazingly vivid lucid dream, or i dont remember shit. I think the same can be said about THC because i have the same sort of thing from smoking. Sometimes dreams, sometimes not.
Other cannibinoids in the cannbis plant, mainly sativa as mentioned before, cause very nice and vivid dreams down to the cerebral high. However indica tends to be knock out sleep state where the brain to be seems like its deactivated lol.
Eating sativa cheeba chews usually bring about a pretty intense dream when i finally decide its time to sleep, and its a really nice high.

Best way for you would be to experiment, keep a dream journal, and find the ideal strain and/or method that gets you the desired results as its the only conclusive method.
