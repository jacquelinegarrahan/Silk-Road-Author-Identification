talk to the seller and see if they would send some test letters to see if they arrive. get them to put something like flour in a vacuum pack just to see if they arrive. if he could send about 5 and none turn up then you know its the mail. if it is your postman then he will soon work out you're on to him and might stop or just get fed up risking stealing your mail just for flour.

or try sending envelopes to yourself containing a royal mail headed letter stating that it is one of a series of test deliveries and that the receiver should call the post office to let them know it has arrived and if there are any signs of tampering. something like that should shit the life out of your postman if he is stealing your mail as he will know how seriously they take mail theft. would work better if you could arrange one to be sent from holland looking like it could be a silk road letter.
