False. And this can (and has been) demonstrated experimentally a few different time in a few different ways. The simplest is to set up a speed-dating thingy. The experimenter secretly records the race and gender of each participant. Then the "who would you be willing to date" list speed-daters turn in is kept and analyzed. Using this, one can easily determine the preference for each race each person has and the exact amount they prefer that race. It turns out men don't prefer asian women at all. In fact, men don't seem to prefer any race at all. Even in men who state beforehand that they prefer certain races over others.

In fact, it is the women who hold all the cards. Even women who claim not to have racial preferences, in fact do when it comes to experimental conditions (set up to strongly relate to the real world - in this experiment real dates were had). Turns out women overwhelmingly prefer men of their own race. Except for Asian women.

So the question isn't "why do white men like Asian women" it's "why do black women hate Asian men?"
