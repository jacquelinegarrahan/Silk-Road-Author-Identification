Title: Re: How do I look at a potential buyers history?
Post by: MaidMandy on June 11, 2013, 02:13 pm
The only way you can see a buyers stats are if they place an order with you. You could set up a free listing and ask them to order it; then you could see their stats.

Or else you could ask them to place the custom order and if you don't like the stats, you could cancel the order. If you do this, make sure the order isn't hedged or the customer will lose 4% of their money (hedging fee) if you cancel the order on account of not liking their stats.

Hope this helps :)
