Title: Re: has sr ever been breached
Post by: Busy Bee on January 26, 2013, 03:31 am
Yes, there was an SQL injection attack not too long ago - the "SR Quickbuy Scam." In addition, the site has fallen to DDOS-like attacks for days at a time on a couple of occasions now. There's no way of knowing if these attacks advanced any further though. Most worrying of all perhaps is the attacks/breaches we will never hear about - and it would be foolish to say that such an event is impossible.

All of this should be of little worry to you, however, if you are encrypting your information! Excluding the possibility of a man-in-the-middle attack, PGP encryption means SR won't be able to read or store your personal info. Moral of the story: join the PGP club if you haven't already done so!
