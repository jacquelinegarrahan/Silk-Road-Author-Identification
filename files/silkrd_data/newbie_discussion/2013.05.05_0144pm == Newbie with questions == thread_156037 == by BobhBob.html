Title: Re: Newbie with questions
Post by: cerealbox on May 05, 2013, 01:52 pm
Bitcoin market's always been up and down, though it seems to get particularly volatile the higher it's value becomes. As for messing up, depends on the vendor, I guess. It's not likely that someone's going to go "oh, he finalized early, I don't have to send it now!" The scammers tend to be the scammers. Generally the good vendors don't just sell to "good buyers." Some of them may require early finalization or not ship large amounts or whatever, but it's just a precaution that they'll get their money, not that they're trying to form a secret club.

You should be fine, though with pot I'd recommend you check out the rumor mill before ordering. Packaging is notoriously difficult with marijuana.
