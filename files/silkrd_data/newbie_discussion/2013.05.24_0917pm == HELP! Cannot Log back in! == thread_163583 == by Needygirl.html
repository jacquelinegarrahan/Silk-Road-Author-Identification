Post by: DoctorFate on May 24, 2013, 10:01 pm
I believe the SR address has been the same since its deployment in 2011.

If you went to any site other than http://silkroadvb5piz3r.onion/ and it looked like the SR login site asking for your pin, then you were at a phishing site. 

Which is basically a site modeled to look like SR designed to steal your username, password, and pin.  The phisher would then get your account, drain it of btc and do whatever else with it.

I don't think SR support will help because warnings about this are everywhere.  They will probably just direct you to the wiki.  Just make sure you know now that SR would never ask for your pin outside of placing an order or transferring coin.
