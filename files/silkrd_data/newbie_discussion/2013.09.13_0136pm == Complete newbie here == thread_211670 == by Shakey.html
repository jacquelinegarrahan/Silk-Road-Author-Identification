Welcome to the road!

Ok so when you get you BTC you transfer them into you SR account so they can be used to purchase of the site. To get the BTC address of your SR account you just log on and select my account. If its your first time there should be a link saying something along the lines of 'click here to make a deposit' Once you have your BTC from localbitcoins you just send them to that SR address. You can also look into using a coin tumbler service such as bitcoinfog which will increase anonymity by mixing your coins with other peoples before giving you back the same amount (minus a fee).

for information on LSD vendors you want to check the avengers LSD thread which will pretty much tell you everything you need to know about the vendors on here.

Post by: dirac on September 13, 2013, 03:12 pm
Welcome! Enjoy your stay, be safe.

The bitcoin buying market is always a pain to navigate. He's not taking new customers right now, but in the future JumboMonkeyBiscuit runs a great cash-in-mail service. Don't bother using a tumbler- it's not illegal to have bitcoin in a wallet managed by Silk Road, and the Road will tumble your btc. You can change your wallet address on the Road whenever you'd like, and this will anonymize them somewhat as well.

You should try to avoid finalizing early, but for the first few transactions you may not be able to help it. Just make sure you pick reputable vendors with lots of transactions- their reputation is a lot more important to them than screwing you out of a few bitcoin. Start small and build up- maybe even order a legal thing or two.

When you do go for shrooms and acid, MrPsych is currently on vacation but has a huge variety of shrooms and ships from the UK. I haven't used his shrooms, but his 2C-T-2 was quite good. Jannis sells the best acid I've (or anyone I know) has ever had. Beautiful dolphin blotter, and can ship within the EU.

Happy tripping :)
