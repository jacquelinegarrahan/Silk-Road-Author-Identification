Title: Review of drzheng's Methylone (M1)

About 2 months ago I ordered 2 grams of methylone from drzheng and promised him I'd write a review.

It took so long because I have never tried M1 before and I usually like a good stretch of free time before I try out a new drug.

I have to say this is definetly not something that experienced festie kids or ravers are likely to eat up, but for the price i think it is a pretty good buy.
I ingested 50 mg with a friend around 9. I could feel the come up effects within a minute but they seemed to develope slower than say molly. After an hour I felt as though they were fully developed and I was in a very serene state with only slight stimulation and more in a mood to sit and chill. Had I been at a club I dont think Id of been in the mood to dance.

My buddy and me decide to weigh out 50mg bumpers  and each do another. This bump definetly upped the bliss and also made me feel a little vasoconstriction, nothing to bad but worth mentioning. The two of us putt on some lighter trance and just chilled on X youd normally be talk eachotheres ears off with "I love you mans" and constant hugs. This blissful state was more of a content "I just love being alive" feeling. another hour later we redosed another 50 mg to extend the feeling.

I could definetly tell when the comedown started but the whole process was lond so I didnt feel the need to redose again since this experiment to place at my friends apartment. It might be more of a party drug mixed with alcohol or something but after doing X so much I dont think this would be a up to par. That being said I think M1 would be fun with some friends around a camp fire and for the price ( 2g/$40) its as cheap as drinking.
