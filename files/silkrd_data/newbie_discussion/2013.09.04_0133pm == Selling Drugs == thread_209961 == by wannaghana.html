Title: Re: Selling Drugs
Post by: conflicted on September 04, 2013, 07:51 pm
That could be your problem, if you can you need to be cash up front with everyone and only let people pay you in a similar fashion, take for instance:
You have a very good trusting business relationship with your local dealer and let him have the goods on credit, this is all good for a while and then suddenly he gets robbed or caught by LE. He owes you for a Keys worth of goods. Can be a tricky situation.

For me in my country/area, the SR prices on most goods wouldn't allow enough margin to be worth selling locally, although quality in many cases is better, in fact for many products you pay a premium here, however that premium is often worth it to me for the quality.

Good luck if you decide to go for it!
