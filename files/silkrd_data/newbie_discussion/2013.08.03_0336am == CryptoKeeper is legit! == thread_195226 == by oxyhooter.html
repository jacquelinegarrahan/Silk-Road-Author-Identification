Long story short, a little over 24 hours later, the BTC were in my account and the fee he charges isn't outrageous. His feedback could have been padded and he easily could have taken my money and ran but thankfully that is not the case. Cryptokeeper is running a legit operation and is NOT a scammer.

So if I am correct you would probably get faster service using a Vanilla or other reload card. I also see that he pulled his Greendot listings (maybe temporary but I'm not sure) but left up his other listings so I think I am correct about Greendot being the bottleneck. Bottom line is that CryptoKeeper really came through for me and I wanted to say that any mention of him possibly being a scammer are purely speculation.
