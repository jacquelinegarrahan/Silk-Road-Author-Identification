M39 is what has been going around the NE US as "beasters" for decades now.  It's pretty good weed has some crystals usually nicer looking buds than mids.  M39 is basically the step between mids and Kush and usually goes for about $40 1/8  $75 1/4   $250 an ounce on the street around where i live.
