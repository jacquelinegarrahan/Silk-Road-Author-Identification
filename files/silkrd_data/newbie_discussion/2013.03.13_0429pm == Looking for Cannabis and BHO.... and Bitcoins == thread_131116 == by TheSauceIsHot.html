Title: Re: Looking for Cannabis and BHO.... and Bitcoins
Post by: jmoney113 on March 13, 2013, 04:46 pm
Bitinstant, Bitcopia, and some of the sellers here on SR are all reliable methods to obtain your coins.

To answer your question, newbie buyer resistance is generally done in a case by case basis. Some won't deal with new buyers, some will send you bulk products. All depends on your attitude with a vendor really. Start small, work your way up if you intend on purchasing in bulk. Always use the escrow system in the future, it's only there to protect you. Aside from that, build a good reputation and buyer stats. finalize promptly, leave quality reviews, and understand how SR works and everything along with it.

Welcome to the road.
