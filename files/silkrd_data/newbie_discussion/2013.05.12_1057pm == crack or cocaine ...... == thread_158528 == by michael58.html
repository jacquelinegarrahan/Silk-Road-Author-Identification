mix all until Cocaine HCL dissolves in solution with a pencap
heat over bic lighter until heady (beer style) bubbles produce and almost overflow the tablespoon
remove heat
let sit for 30 seconds or intil bubbles START to subside
apply lighter once again
you want to move lighter around to produce an oily puddle which floats on top of the water.
once therre is an oil puddle on top of the water let sit for 1 to 3 minutes
the oil will either congeal into a small disc or sink to the bottom in small balls
use a pencap to harvest the oil or disc from the spoon
place onto a 3x3 tin foil sheet
let dry for 5 mins
cut a .5 cm or .25 in piece from the foil and place on a 2 in by 6 in piece of foil
