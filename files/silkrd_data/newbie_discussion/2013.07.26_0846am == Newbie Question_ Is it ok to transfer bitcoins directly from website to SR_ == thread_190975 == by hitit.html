Title: Re: Newbie Question: Is it ok to transfer bitcoins directly from website to SR?
Post by: triangle_list on July 26, 2013, 09:02 am
generally, yes it is fine.  LE can't realistically track bitcoin transactions and certainly won't bother if you're only buying personal use-type amounts. I would at least change your SR receiving address regularly though. If you're transferring large amounts of cash for not-personal use and using a bank account with your name on it then it is riskier. But in that case you should be more worried about the taxman or the old-fashioned controlled delivery bust by postal inspectors than any sort of NSA-type numerical analysis of bitcoin transactions :) Even if they could or would do that, transferring it through an intermediary wouldn't be much of an obstacle
