Title: Re: Do packages get probed?
Post by: 3eyes on April 09, 2013, 01:01 am
Depends on what country you are in.

For example
In the USA they can not open anything without a warrant which can be obtained by a suspicious smell or something like a hand written address.  There is a long list of suspicious criteria including fake return address,  mail from certain country's, uneven weight distribution etc.
 
The postal service will then use a chemical swab on the outside of the box to test for drug residue.  If they find anything the package will be opened.

In England customs can and will open whatever they feel like without a warrant

But in your case OP, it sounds like the tear is from shipping.  I have worked in shipping and the boxes can take a beating unless it specifically says "fragile".
