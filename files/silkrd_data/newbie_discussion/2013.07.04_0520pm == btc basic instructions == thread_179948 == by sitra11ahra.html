Post by: jlranier on July 09, 2013, 04:39 pm
I'm no expert and have no personal experience but my understanding of it is this:

1. Download btc and generate an address to send coins to. Also download blockchain onto your phone and pair it with a wallet that you start on blockchain.info. I recommend using the blockchain address to send btc to when you buy them since you can keep your phone with you and check for them and use it to receive payments from people.

2. You either find a vendor on here or use a service like localbitcoins for the best service in my understanding. Some vendors on here take Moneypaks at ~10% fee and put bitcoins in your account. Others sell via Western Union. Many have a minimum order though. I'm not sure what people on localbitcoins charge if anything for bank deposits or cash. From everything I've read though it's a very reliable service, easy and discreet. Have the vendor or btc seller send the coins to your blockchain wallet address listed on your phone.

3. Now with the btc in your blockchain, I recommend sending them to the address you started on your computer. I don't think it's required though. Once you identify an item you want to buy on here or if you want to put coins in your SR account while they have good value, go to your account on SR main page and use the address on the deposit tab to send coins to.

4. Wait 12-24 hours and enjoy your coins!
