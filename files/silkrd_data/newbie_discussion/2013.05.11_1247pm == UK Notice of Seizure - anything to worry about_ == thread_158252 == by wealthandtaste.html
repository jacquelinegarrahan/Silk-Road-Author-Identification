Title: Re: UK Notice of Seizure - anything to worry about?
Post by: RevDrGod on May 11, 2013, 01:38 pm
Personally I have never been caught smuggling through UK customs, but US Customs has grabbed me coming into the country with hash. Customs considers a border crossing, a Constitutionally exempt area. In other words you have no rights and National Security  is put ahead of any rights you may have while within border.
 Drug Transshipment areas-  in US Customs and US Postal service terms are areas that frequent drug traffic. Such as traffic from Holland, or Columbia, etc.
 Drug transshipment weight requirements for mandatory inspection are any package who's combined total weight is 1/2 lb or more (over 252 grams). Any package weighing more than 1/2 lb is subject to warrantless search and seizure.
 If you pay attention to the arrests that are being made, you will notice that the weight of the product being intercepted is always over 100 grams, and in the US, that almost always results in an arrest
