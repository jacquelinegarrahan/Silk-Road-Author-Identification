Title: Re: Help with MDMA crystals
Post by: eetandern on January 23, 2013, 11:23 am
keep it in an airtight container, it can absorb moisture from the air - ziplock bags work well, just don't use one thats too big. 

as far distribution goes - that depends on what level you want to distribute at. 

if you want to sell to the end user, putting it in capsules is a good option.  you need a scale that can weigh to 100mg within 5-10% and a small funnel (the metal tips to pastry bags work well) and a lot of patience.  capsules will take longest, but they will fetch the highest price.  also they will allow customers to keep track of their dose and/or redistribute to other customers.  capsules are good for small ammounts, sold to friends.

if you are not selling to the end user, you want to leave the crystals as intact as possible - only breaking them when you need for weighing purposes.  you can even make your crystals bigger, if you know what you're doing - people love moonrocks.

you sound like you have no idea what you are doing, don't get in over your head - mdma can get you in a lot of trouble  ;)
