Title: Re: A few questions re: anonymity
Post by: lagunka on March 27, 2013, 07:51 am
1) No it is not necessary to use a public computer to purchase your bitcoins. I mean if you're using a service such as bitintasnt you're using you're real name and going into a store anyways so that extra caution becomes quite redundant.

2) You should put them in a wallet like blockchain first just don't deposit directly into your SR account. You can send your coins to another wallet like instawallet but laundering your bitcoins is kind of unnecessary. You should be more worried about your package getting seized or getting caught with drugs IRL.

3) Shipments through the regular mail won't have to be signed for. You should be informed before ordering if you do have to sign and should only be for priority shipping i.e. next day shipping. And you really should never sign for a package, it's only going to incriminate you further.
