Title: Re: Why is adderall so expensive?
Post by: nodialtone on August 11, 2013, 10:46 pm
I have a feeling that many vendors are simply too far down in the supply chain to sell for low prices. Think of it this way - the vendor has to earn a fair profit while also paying for his source's profit, a profit for his source's source and, possibly, a profit for his source's source's source.

In other words, some of the vendors here are pretty much at the level of people who buy drugs on the street. Maybe they bought 20mg for $3 but sell for $5 to allow themselves to turn a profit. Plus, you're paying for the risk the vendor is taking by sending drugs through the mail.

As a buyer, you have to consider whether the higher price is worth the (arguably) lesser risk of buying on the street.
