Title: Re: High prices, and the front product
Post by: InterestedParty on August 17, 2013, 02:32 am
I think it's great that you're asking these questions. You don't know until you know, right? I don't have answers to all of your questions, but I think I can take a swing at one or two:

I think the "front" thing would just not work well here for many reasons. I've only ever had stuff fronted from dealers who I had longstanding relationships with, and they knew they could trust me.

The prices are what they are because there are many fees attached to using the Road and to buying then selling bitcoins (the "money" that is used here). Many sellers *do* offer bulk discounts seems only to established customers. I think you might be in a position where you have to spend some money on the site before you aren't treated with suspicion (law enforcement IS on here).

I have never seen a free sample from any vendor; it wouldn't make much sense since the vendor has to pay a bunch of fees to send you a "free" sample.

I hope that helps. Good luck to you!
