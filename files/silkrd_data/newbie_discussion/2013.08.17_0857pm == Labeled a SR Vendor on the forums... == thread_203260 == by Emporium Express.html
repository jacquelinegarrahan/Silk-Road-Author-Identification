How do you get labeled as an official SR Vendor on the forums. I believe I was just sent a message by the SR staff through my vendor's account and could not read it.. considering I may not be listed as a Vendor on here. How do I go about this?
Title: Re: Labeled a "SR Vendor" on the forums...
Post by: CHROOT on August 17, 2013, 09:52 pm
In the vendors guide it says you have to put a link to your vendor page in your forums signature, be using the same name in here and on SR, and send a message to vendor support on SR requesting access to the vendor roundtable. Those who ask nice are usually set up in no time.
Title: Re: Labeled a "SR Vendor" on the forums...
Post by: rockwaterwind on August 17, 2013, 10:01 pm
You can also request this by going to Profile > Account Settings > Modify Profile > Group Membership

You can then request membership to the SR Vendor group.
