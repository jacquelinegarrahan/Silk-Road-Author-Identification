Hi backpackjackson,

Your deposit can take up to 24 hours to arrive at your Silk Road account, however they normally arrive within a few hours. If after 24 hours they have failed to arrive, simply message SR Support using the 'support' link in the bottom right hand corner on the main site - include your deposit address and the time & date of the transfer and they will credit them manually for you.
