Post by: Mr Bubbles on July 05, 2013, 02:31 am
As you said, a lot of things you're asking are not consensual. The effects largely varies from person to person. I'll try to answer within my experience.

1) The first time I tried LSD I took 1 tab. After 50 minutes with no effect, I took another one. 5 minutes later I was tripping balls. With 100ug I have a very light trip, with 200ug I have a decent trip. To have a brainfuck trip I need around 500ug. It's up to you to decide what you're looking for on the first time. I don't think nobody can estimate how long your trip gonna last. A 100ug dosage doesn't last more than 6 hours for me.

2) If you're thinking about spliting, take 100ug, wait 2 hours, and take another 100ug. That will give you enough time to see how deep you're going. I put it under the tongue for 15 minutes, chew the blotter and swallow.

3) After 4-5 hours I'm so mentally tired that I don't have too much will to redose. Tell me how it works for you.

4) If you have to handle parents after 10 hours, don't redose after 4 hours! After 10 hours after a single dose you should be able to handle casual social interactions, but if you redose there's a risk you gonna be tripping balls after that time.
