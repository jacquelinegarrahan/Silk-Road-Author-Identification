Post by: IUAEfndfzdff on June 25, 2013, 04:22 pm
The best method that works for little amounts for substances like benzos, amphetamine, buprenorphine and maybe other opioids is to make a clear solution or (if not possible) a suspension.

Just filling the solution/suspension into a suitable (liquid) medicines bottle will do the job. Suitable medicines may be Cough-Drops, wound-care solutions etc.

Of course it depends on the substance you want to use, some may smell too much if someone opens the bottle for a smell-check. Besides you may have to know about the solubility of your substance...

This method worked out fine for some people i know, but in the most cases it was about pharmaceutical drugs. At least it should also work for amphetamine, meth, heroine etc. The only problem can be, that it won't be that easy to recover the substances to their original state (solution to powder, if it was amphetamine powder before). But if it doesn't matter for you to consume your substances in liquid form, why not trying it this safe way?

If you dropped in 1g of Amphetamine in a 50ml 'carrier', you can easily calculate your doses and prepare them by using a syringe or a measuring cup.

But: This method may not prevent getting busted when drug dogs are around...
