Title: Re: LSD at night. does it make any difference?
Post by: hayzeus on August 20, 2013, 09:55 pm
Out of my 300-400 LSD trips in my life, I'd say 85% of them were at night actually.  I really enjoy it at night. I love laying on the ground in a field and staring at the stars and the moon.  It is the most beautiful thing in the world. 

That, or dancing all night at a warehouse party.  I don't know that I'd go to one these days, but back in the mid 90's and early 2000's, those parties were absolutely love filled psychedelic trip fests.  The vibe at parties these days is pretty gross. 

Tripping during the day is really fun too though.  I've tripped twice in the last month.  Both times I took my doses at about 2:00pm and tripped til about 10pm-Midnight.  I was out in the woods / nature on both occasions and spent a lot of time enjoying touching trees and big rocks, really connecting with nature on a personal level.  I highly recommend this experience as well.  The first time I tripped this month I was out on a small river full of little waterfalls and there were NO other people the ENTIRE time we tripped.  When I tripped this last weekend, I went to the beach and the amount of people kept GROWING and GROWING.  I started to get uncomfortable being around so many non-tripping people and really hot in the mid day sun @ 80+ degrees.  If you are tripping during the day, make sure you eat a GOOD meal for breakfast, pack a bag with fresh fruits (citrus fruits are the best, peaches, strawberries, etc), some salty nuts / energy nut bars, WATER, and enough supplies to keep you going for the whole day.  Pick a place where you don't have to drive once you get there and be prepared to stay there for 12+ hours =)

Enjoy yourself!  Happy tripping and BE SAFE!

Oh yeah, when your tripping during the day,  8) BRING SUNGLASSES!  8)
