I believe Tails comes with its own internal encryption suite, so it should be possible, yes.

The only issue I have with the hidden volume is that if they are REALLY determined to be through, and please correct me if I am wrong, I believe they could actually read the raw data off the disk, and this would let them know that there is a hidden volume.

I would just find a damn good hiding place for the flash drive. Or, you could try using an SD card and a USB adapater for it, as an SD card would be even easier to hide than a flash drive.

I do not think that is needed if you are just going to be a buyer, having PGP keys is perfectly legal. Although they might be able to take them and say that they are proof of contact, but they would have to prove they were actually used and that they actually are the keys that belong to the vendors they think they belong to. Not sure if that would work in court.

As for links, if it is an item/vendor on SR, you can just bookmark/become a fan. If it is non SR, I use OnionBookmark http://x7yxqg5v4j6yzhti.onion/ as it also comes with a nice tag feature that is useful to sort your bookmarks.
