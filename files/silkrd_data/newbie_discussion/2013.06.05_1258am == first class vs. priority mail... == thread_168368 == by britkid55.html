Title: Re: first class vs. priority mail...
Post by: semi_feral_human on June 05, 2013, 01:28 am
I'm basing this on years of online shopping, sending/receiving birthday/christmas cards, paying bills, and not silk road purchases... but i don't see why it would change it much.

Priority mail - Almost always takes about 48 hours to arrive. (mailed monday morning, receieved wednsday with the mail.) two exceptions. i once lived in a rural area faaaaaar from the sorting center in which it would take about 72 hours arrive. the other exception was dealing with a retailer several hours away who had the same sorting center, in this case it was next day. in extremely rare cases they can send your package to the wrong destination sorting center, and it can take 4 days to infinity to arrive.

First class - Usually delivered within 24 - 72 hours. Rule of thumb: within the state, next day; couple of thee states away, 2 days; across the entire country, 3 days.

Obviously on silk road you don't know where it's coming from like with other things.

I deal with two vendors that ship to me from the opposite coast. On uses Priority, the other First Class.



If I order something on a Monday, and gets sent out with Tuesday's mail:

Generally,  Priority is 2 days (Thursday's mail) and First Class is 3 (Friday's mail). I've never done Express mail. I imagine that would be next day. One time, 1st class took 4 days, but that was around the holidays.

It would be nice to know, at least, the zone the vendor is shipping from, especially if there is an Express or Priority option. Shipping in the  same zone is usually next day with 1st class. I generally don't like to ask with the first order from a new vendor. I don't want to  make anyone uncomfortable.
