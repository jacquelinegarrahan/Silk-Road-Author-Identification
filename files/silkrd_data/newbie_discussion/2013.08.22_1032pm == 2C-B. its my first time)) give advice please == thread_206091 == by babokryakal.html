Post by: Rainbowbleh on August 22, 2013, 11:08 pm
Me n my friends just recently got into the bees.  I will say.. and this is very important.. DOSAGES VARY GREATLY from person to person.  Even the first time.  Me and 2 friends tried some a few months back.  We decided to try 15mg as a "low dose" to get our bearings.  It was like the 3 bears lol.. one friend it overwhelmed and he felt like it was too much (next time he took 10 and it was better for him).  He was tripping hard.  Other friend said it was perfect.
