FE - Finalise Early

Basically means that you complete the transaction before the seller sends the item.  If you do this, the BTC will go to the vendor before he sends the stuff!! See the problems there?

BitCoin is fine, you wont have any dramas - use an online wallet such as Instawallet, Strongcoin and Blockchain.

You can download and hold a Bitcoin wallet yourself, both ways have their pro's and con's.
