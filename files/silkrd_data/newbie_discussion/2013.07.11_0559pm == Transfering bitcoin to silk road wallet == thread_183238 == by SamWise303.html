Title: Re: Transfering bitcoin to silk road wallet
Post by: mcguire39 on July 11, 2013, 06:06 pm
How long ago did you transfer it and did you assign a fee (if localbitcoin allows assigning a fee for the transfer)? It can take several hours to show up in your SR account. You can track it on blockchain.info or blockexplorer to see how many confirmations it has. It takes at least 6 confirmations to get into your SR account.
