Title: Re: anyone ever make mescaline?
Post by: wobble32 on February 28, 2013, 01:37 am
It actually requires quite a bit of work (grinding,slicing, juicing), takes a fair amount of time due to defatting and emulsions forming, you will need to work with dangerous and toxic chemicals (xylene, sodium hydroxide, strong mineral acids) and it will never be cheap because san pedro is not cheap, and one 12" cutting typically contains less than a full dose of mescaline.

Also almost all extraction procedures leave you with a mixture of cactus alkaloids not pure mescaline. Ingesting the end product results in a much "cleaner" and less visual trip than eating the actual cactus...


Also there are probably better species to work with than Pachanoi..
