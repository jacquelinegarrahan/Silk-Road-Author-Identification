OK guys, since SR went down we're all looking for a new home. The two obvious options are Sheep Marketplace and BMR.

Most of you have been attracted to sheep because of the clean UI and fast servers. With all kind of 3 letter agencies targeting hidden marketplaces like this UI should be at the back of the priority list for users.

The communication from the owners is terrible! Look at all the issues people are having on the forum and they're receiving no replies.

The admins are stupid enough to have a clearnet version of the site http://sheepmarketplace.com/

The domain has an email address linked to it, and the server is only hidden behind cloudflare, a simple Warrant forces cloudflare to reveal the servers real location, hopefully that server isn't used to host the actual marketplace, but regardless there will be traces of the admins identity left behind on that server, or in the payment for that server. (and if law enforcement gain access to the server they could inject could into the browsers of users to reveal users real ip addresses)

TLDR;
The admins of this site just don't seem to have the skillset required to take on such a large operation. BMR looks like a much better option at this moment in time.
