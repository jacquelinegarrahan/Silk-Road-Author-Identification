Quote from: Niki10 on July 05, 2013, 05:16 pm
But what about MDMA? is there a bigger chance that it can be cleaner than XTC pill and thus less unpleasant feeling afterwards?

I think you mean powder vs. pills? In theory anything can be in powder and anything can be in a pill. If I buy a large batch I always get it lab tested with the anonymous service provided by erowid/ecstasydata.org -- it's fantastic, but pricy. ($140 for a powder sample.)

In practice, I've found powder to be more reliably pure MDMA than pills. Some of the batches I have received have been cut with MSM, which is mostly harmless and just reduces the potency. Scroll through the reports on ecstasydata.org and see if you can find a match for the pills you bought -- it could be that someone else sent one in for testing.

CLEARNET WARNING:

A pill with MDMA and caffeine at a 12:1 ratio: http://www.ecstasydata.org/view.php?id=2765
A powder sample that 100% Methylone, but was sold as MDMA: http://www.ecstasydata.org/view.php?id=2757

It's just a couple of examples, but as you scroll through the list, you'll be alarmed at how many things sold as "Ecstasy" have very little (or NO) MDMA at all. Sad state of affairs that prohibition brings us to.

The last pill you took sounds like it could have been something else, but I would be surprised if *some* of the pills you had were legit and one was not. Don't underestimate the power that overdoing it with MDMA can have on your body and its ability to make you feel like shit. Seriously... It's intense... I would suggest waiting 1-3 months between rolling, if at all possible, and maybe longer. It's such an amazing time. Don't blow out your brain and lose the magic...

Post by: grizzbrah on July 05, 2013, 08:23 pm
You're serotonin levels are depleted. Believe it or not, serotonin is also affected when you take other drugs such as LSD, weed, alcohol, etc.

MDMA works differently by releasing your serotonin in vast quantities. Serotonin is a neurotransmitter used in regulating mood, sleep, memory, appetite, along with other brain functions.

Imagine your normal serotonin levels are like a dam with a lot of water. When you take MDMA, the dam is released, and your brain is flooded with serotonin beyond what it is normally used to in its regulated pathways. This causes the extreme happiness that you feel, along with that intense feeling of intimacy.

Do realize that when your "dam" is released, it takes awhile to refill the reservoir. Sleep, a healthy diet, and just getting rest will slowly refill your serotonin levels.

I would wait at least a week, better for 2 weeks, before rolling face again.

You should always stick with MDMA in powder form imo, because pills can be cut with adulterants.
