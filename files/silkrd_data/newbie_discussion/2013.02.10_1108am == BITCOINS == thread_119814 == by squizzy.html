Title: Re: BITCOINS
Post by: PrincessHIGH on February 10, 2013, 11:30 am
They shouldn't be as the majority of listings are pegged to the US dollar, to see the current price go here (clearwebalert) https://mtgox.com/index.html?Currency=USD simly divide the listing price by the weighted average to work out how much your listing costs. Alternatively you can do this through your SilkRoad account by clicking on account ฿ -.-- (however many bitcoins you have) > Settings > click display prices in Dollars > Update profile. If you do this all your listings will be in US dollars, you can always change it back to Bitcoins if you need to. Hope this helps you :)
