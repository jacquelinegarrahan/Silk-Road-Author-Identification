Post by: PlanB on July 19, 2013, 10:22 am
Many people within the LSD community understand that different batches of LSD can have different loads on the body and mind. When reviewing people may say "Awesome visuals and really small body load" what there basically saying is the Lucy they had didn't have much of a physical effect on there body like you did when you dropped. People ten not to like large loads on their body for obvious reasons, it stops them doing things they want when tripping. You may find when you next drop with different stuff the body load maybe different, but keep trying different stuff until you find a batch you really like.
In terms of your other questions well "WoW" stands for White on White, this is usually when high quality white crystal LSD is laid onto white blotter. It's mostly a sign of some good stuff among the acid community when people sell "WoW"but of course looks can be deceiving so be cautious.
White Fluff is a name given to a certain potency of Acid, below is the potency and corresponding name among the community given to that range.

Needlepoint -very pure(95%) white powdery crystal,was available in small amount`s. The best of the best

White Fluff -Very pure(95%) white light flakes of crystal. Still around and the most sought after. very pure

Silver -Good and clean(85-90%)-light greyish crystal. Was an unbelievable amount of this around in the late eighty`s and early nineties. Very good stuff.

Amber -Decent(70%?) This crystal varied from a light amber color to an almost dark brown color.Was always available.One batch called quadriceps amber was the color of light honey and was very good.Lot`s a people worked with this crystal but I always would use silver instead since it was better and the same price.

Lavender -(60-70%?) light purple to almost black colored crystal. Like amber it varied batch to batch.

Champagne -(50-60%) Lowest quality crystal you could get.

Hope this has been some help to you, happy tripping and welcome to the Road.
