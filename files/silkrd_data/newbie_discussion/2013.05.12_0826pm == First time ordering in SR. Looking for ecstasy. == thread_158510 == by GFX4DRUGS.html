I buy by the gram and wash using dry acetone.

Ingredients:

A litre of acetone
Small bag of epsom salts
Coffee filters
Stirring rod
Two beakers / glasses
Hair dryer

Instructions:

Cook epsom salts in the oven at 200 degrees c for an hour.
Let the salts cool down for a few minutes.
Add to a litre of acetone and shake well.
Wait 20 mins or so for the cloudy salts to settle.
You now have acetone with no water content; a polar solvent.

Crush MDMA rock (or cocaine etc) and add to first beaker.
Pour a small amount of dry acetone on top.
Swill it around and mix it with your stirring rod until it's dissolved the bad stuff.
Put coffee filter into second beaker and slowly pour the solution through.
Keep topping up and filtering until you've got everything out of the second beaker.
You've now got a coffee filter full of pure MDMA!

Flatten the filter and give it a blast with the hair dryer.
Once completely dry open it up and scrape out the contents.

At this point you can weigh it to see how much you've lost, but you can be sure that the remaining MDMA will be very fucking strong and free of junk and any left-over safrole from the manufacturing process (a weak carcinogen in rats and what MDMA smells of).
