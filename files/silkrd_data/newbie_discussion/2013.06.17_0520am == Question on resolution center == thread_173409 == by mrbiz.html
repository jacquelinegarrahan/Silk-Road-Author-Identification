If it has not arrived, I would put it into resolution and ask for 100%, do not accept 5%, if you do and the product does not arrive, then you can do nothing about it, and to be quite honest, it sounds a bit shady, why did he have to reship, he should know if he sent it the first time? Also, if he really did reship it, why did it not arrive the second time? Are you prepared to name the vendor, what is his rating, if it is 90 or below, then I would certainly presume he was trying to scam you! As I said, propose 100%, you can always reduce this if the other party does not accept it. If he has got a bad reputation, then the chances are that SR staff know this and will give you the 100% refund. I have had the misfortune to have a couple of transactions go sour and each time SR staff have given me the refund because it was obvious that I was not at fault.
Hope all goes well, in future, research thoroughly on the forums before you go with a new vendor- take their feedback on their page with "a pinch of salt".
