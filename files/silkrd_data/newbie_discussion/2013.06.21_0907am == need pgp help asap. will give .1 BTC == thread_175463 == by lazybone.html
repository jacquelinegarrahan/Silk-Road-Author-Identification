Download gpg4usb here : gpg4usb.cpunk.de/download.html, then open it and on the wizard, an option to create your own key will be on the second or third page I think. There you enter your user name, a fake or real tormail email address, set the key size to at least 2048bit and make a strong password, including lower case, upper case, numbers and symbols that's 13 characters long or more if you want it to be really secure.
