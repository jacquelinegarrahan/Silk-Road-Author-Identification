Post by: dontek on July 01, 2013, 11:55 am
Pricing and quality really depends on what you're used to.

Where I am, we aren't near any legalized state, and there really isn't any "variety". You get what you get. Either really good shit or mid-grade seedy shit. The good shit could be indica, could be sativa. Who knows. It's just whatever happens to be "in stock" with the local dealer. And here, good/medical goes for an average of $110-$125/quarter.

On SR, you get your preference of what kind of weed you want. I especially like the vendors that allow you to "Mix-And-Match" strains.

From what I have seen on the vendors that I am using, is that 1/8 or 1/4 really isn't that great of a deal. Almost about the same as what you're going to pay "street prices" once you factor in the shipping. However, moving up to 1/2oz-1oz at a time is a significant "discount". Like I said, an OZ would run about $440+ on the streets here and I can get an oz of medical shit for about $320 here.

As far as favorite bud, ALL OF THEM! lol. OGKush, Bubba Kush, Purple Skunk, Sour Diesel. Alaskan Thunder Fuck, etc. ALL OF THEM! I like to have an array of both sativa and indica depending on what I am using it for. If I want to get high and work on a project or something, sativa. Want to smoke a bowl and get knocked on your ass and stuck to your couch? Indica.
