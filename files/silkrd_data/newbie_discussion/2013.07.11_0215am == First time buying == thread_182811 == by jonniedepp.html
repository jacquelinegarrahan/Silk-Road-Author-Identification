Title: Re: First time buying
Post by: monday.123 on July 11, 2013, 02:36 am
When you're in your cart completing your order (choosing mail option and number of chosen product), there's the little box underneath that. That's where you include your address (encrypted using the vendor's PGP public key - found on their vending page). Then you fill in your pin just under that, double check your order, make sure you have nothing else heading your way (and that your house and pc are clean), and click confirm.

Happy shopping  ;)
