So with that in mind, I wish everyone the best when touring the Road. Be safe, and ask questions. You will always get negative responses, so be patient and keep an open mind. Even positive responses are sometimes loaded with more holes than Swiss cheese. Be careful, and do what makes the most sense.

Traffic stop? NEVER SAY ANYTHING THAT CAN BE PROVEN FALSE. When you lie to Johnny Law, then you immediately enter the realm of suspicion. Pay attention to the conversation, and don't elaborate or go beyond the question(s). Be helpful to the officer, but limit your own exposure. Don't ask for--or mention the need for--a lawyer unless you are being arrested. It reveals that you are anxious about something that has not been explicitly stated.

DUI? Sometimes you have to take a few lumps. If you ever do get charged, then make sure that you don't carry gear... Friday night... going to a party... Carrying? Carry sober. Party... go home empty handed... DUI? Then it will ONLY be a DUI instead of possession charges, etc....
