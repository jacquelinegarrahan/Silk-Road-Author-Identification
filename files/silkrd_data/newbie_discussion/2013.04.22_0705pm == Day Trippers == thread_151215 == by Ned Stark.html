lol yeah i am noticing that as well. is 2cb, 2ci, or 2ci comparable to acid?

In my experience, 2Cs are a shorter, more intense trip. Too intense to be enjoyable for me. LSD is smoother, it lasts a long time but on your comedown you will have lots of nice little "peaks" where things start to get trippy again for a moment, long enough to enjoy what's going on. LSD won't have you pumped up and excited the same way 2Cs will, you will be giddy, but not nearly the same way as 2Cs will.

But that's just my opinion, some people actually prefer 2Cs

Sources: My own limited experience with psychedelics.
