Title: Re: Is it worth it?
Post by: SudoMan on March 16, 2013, 02:58 pm
I agree with the rest of the posts on here. Def use PGP. It is there so the buyer can encrypt their address. I am sure you wouldn't want to give your address in plain text to a complete stranger. Who knows if they get raided and your address is on  the computer. Then it is bad news for everyone.

Use PGP and for the more noobish customers I would offer Privnote. Privnote is however 3rd party, so I wouldn't put all of my trust in something like that. Again, PGP is mainly there for the BUYERS protection. Do good business and do everything you can to protect your customers, that is if you plan on keeping them as customers...

Title: Re: Is it worth it?
Post by: jmoney113 on March 16, 2013, 11:34 pm
PGP is so damn easy to learn. Why skip a step to keep your identity that much more anonymous and secure? I'm sure there will be people that would be willing to deal with you without PGP, but they are few and far between.

Yes, weed is legal around you, but I'm assuming some of the places you will be shipping to, weed isn't exactly so legal. Safety and anonymity should be your top priorities when doing business on SR. SR isn't just shipping between "friends", it's a business in which both the buyer and seller take risks, and your buyers identities shouldn't be compromised because you want to take the easy route.
