Basically, you cant just create another identity.  At least from what I've read.  The best you can do is ghost someone... which is nearly impossible to do as well.

Ghosting is what abdomen is talking about... you pretend to be someone that's dead.  Like Principle Skinner on the Simpsons.  The problem with this is that you must match up someone that is around your age, and more importantly, the government cant know this guy is dead.  The social security administration keeps a Master Death File and a Social Security Death Index which is open published.

So if you were a killer and knew someone around your age that had no family... you could secretly kill him, obviously destroy all traces of his body, and assume his identity preferably across the country someplace where nobody knew you or him.

But once a SSN is entered into the SSDI, that number is useless.  Not sure how banks etc. check the numbers, but that doesn't matter because they obviously do.

Or perhaps you could pay some foreign person for all their info etc., then try to live as them here somehow.  Not sure how this would work out though.

Obviously the government doesn't want people to be able to do this... so over the years they have closed virtually every loophole.  Even if you stole someone's identity, that would only be good for maybe a year (taxes)... then you'd have to become someone else.

Maybe there is an easier way, but I haven't come across it... real life isn't the movies.
