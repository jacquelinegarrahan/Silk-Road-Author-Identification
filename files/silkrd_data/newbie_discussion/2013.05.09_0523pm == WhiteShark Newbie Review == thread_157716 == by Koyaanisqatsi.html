Late December 2012, I notice a new weed vendor. Based in Canada. WhiteShark. She seems very honest, I really like the message on her vendor page, the prices are ok, she promises good quality. Oh, and she maybe cooks, since she also sells weed cakes. I must be one of her first customers.

First order :
I make my first order with her. I trust her enough to FE.
I receive my package, from overseas, within a week (I dont remeber exactly).
Now, my name and first name are both quite short, but both contain errors. Oh...
But it does not make any problem. I go to the post office, retrieve my package, and go back home.
There, I discover a ok-good package, and very beautiful nugs.
Now, understand me. I don't give a f... about weed appearance. I want to smoke, not contemplate it.
But I did contemplate WhiteShark's weed. It was beautiful, smelled really nice and I loved to smoke it. I was a very, very happy customer. (Did i mention she threw a little extra ?)

Second order :
Ok, so, second order was shipped exremly fast. Again, I think I did not wait for more than a week to get my weed.
There was no problem on the spelling of my name and first name.  I had to go to the post office to get my package (which I give 0 fucks about), and there... there... packaging guys... the packaging that brings a SMILE on your face ! Really.
Once home, I see beautiful nugs, and nice smell.
i love to smoke it, but, as i wrote in my purchase review, the weed is very dry. Dry enough to make my lips very dry after 2 joints.
But, you know what ? I had been warned. Because this woman tells the truth. I knew it was dry weed. and it is still very very good.

Summary :
This woman seems to work hard. She communicates well. She ships fast. I trust her. I FE twice and will do it again.

To be quite honest whiteShark asked me if I could write a review. What I wrote is 100% what I think.
