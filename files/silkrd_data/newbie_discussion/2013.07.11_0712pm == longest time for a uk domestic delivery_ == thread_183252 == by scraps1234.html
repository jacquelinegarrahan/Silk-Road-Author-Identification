First class uk domestic does normally take 1-2 days, it can take longer however. I'd allow at least a week or two before starting to get concerned.

Weekends cause delays as there are less collections, no deliveries on Sundays and the number of Royal Mail staff on generally is I believe much lower than on weekdays.

The vast majority of first class mail arrives the next working day - just as it did when you sold on ebay.  The only people that say otherwise are lazy ebay/amazon/Silk Road vendors.

I post thousands of letters/parcels each year and 95% arrive the next working day.  Unless Royal Mail think I'm some sort of God, the same applies to every other sender.  If first class mail doesn't turn up within two days the most likely explanation is that the sender has lied to you about when they sent it.
