It varies, vendor by vendor.
Some vendors NEVER change the order from "processing" to "in transit" until the Post Office has possession.
Some put the transaction "in transit" when it's still on their desk at home and the vendor is nodding off in his bed.

And, every possibility you can think of between these 2 examples can happen.  Your best hope is that your vendor went out to do his PO drops, and he just has not got home yet to update the transaction on his puter.
Good luck
