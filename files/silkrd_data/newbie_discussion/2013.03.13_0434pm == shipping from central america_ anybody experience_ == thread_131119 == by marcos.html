Marcos,

It is certainly possible -- It is seriously hit-or-miss though. Central American postal services are extremely unreliable, and you really have no guarantee of whether your package will take 3 months, will show up with nothing inside it, or will even show up at all. The fact of the matter is you're taking a gamble, but if the price is right and you feel it's worth the risk, then there's really only one way to find out, eh?
