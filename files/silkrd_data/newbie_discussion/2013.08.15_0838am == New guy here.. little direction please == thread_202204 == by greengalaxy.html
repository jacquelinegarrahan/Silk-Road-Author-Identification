Title: Re: New guy here.. little direction please
Post by: shizzlefitz on August 15, 2013, 09:36 am
I don't see your wifi as an issue unless you're planning on moving kilos of hard drugs. If I were you I'd worry a whole lot more on how you plan on

1) putting your merchandise in containers that don't leak out at all. Even micrograms that escape a plastic bag through pores in the membrane put your package at risk of interception
2) Shipping parcels in such a way that you leave no personal marks that could be traced back to you or your home or wherever you're preparing your stuff from
3) Choosing dropoff locations and times that are randomized enough that you never create a pattern LE can get hip to

I would think another weak link is postage. How do you pay for shipping without arousing suspicion? If you are a business you can put your drug business into the same stream and hide the trail. Then again it also sounds like doing it this way raises the risk for your business considerably.

We really need a newbie vendor guide but the successful sellers aren't likely to want to give up their secrets because they don't want the competition and they don't want to raise their risk profile either so no matter which way you cut it you're putting yourself at risk.

My guess is that reselling RCs would be the lowest risk drug business to get your feet wet since they are so small and scent free that they can be sent in regular prepaid envelopes.
