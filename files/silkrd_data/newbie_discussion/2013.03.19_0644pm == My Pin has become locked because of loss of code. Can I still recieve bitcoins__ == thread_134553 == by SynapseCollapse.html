Title: Re: My Pin has become locked because of loss of code. Can I still recieve bitcoins??
Post by: Experienced Dreamer on March 19, 2013, 07:17 pm
After you make the maximum number of attempts allowed, your pin will become locked for 72 hrs I believe.  After the 72 hours you may create a new pin, creating this new pin also creates a new account address.  Any funds in the previous account will be lost, but I may be wrong.  You might be able to talk to the SR people about your problem.  Until then, I would advise you to not get any bitcoins sent to your old account address.
