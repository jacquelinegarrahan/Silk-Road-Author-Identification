Title: Re: Do BTC prices really affect SR product prices? My account is set to USD./Bitcopi
Post by: ladyjane on March 27, 2013, 05:11 pm
Most of the time it will not affect SR product prices, because majority of vendors peg the price to the USD and not the bitcoin. So if a product costs you just pay whatever bitcoin is equal to the price of $50.
BUT the bitcoin value affects how much money you have in your account. So while the product remains at $50, if bitcoin crashes to $2 / coin, you will have less money in your account to spend and cant buy the $5o thing.
It also means you give more bitcoins and since they are rising in value, the less bitcoins  you give away the better.

So i hope that helps im high so maybe not the easiest to understand explanation but its right ;)
