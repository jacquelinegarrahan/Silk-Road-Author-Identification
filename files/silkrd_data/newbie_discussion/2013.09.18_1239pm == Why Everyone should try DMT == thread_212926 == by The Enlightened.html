I was really anxious when I did both acid and dmt but once I started to notice the effects I just had no choice than to be both ecstatic and amazed. Admittedly I didn't break through on dmt but it was enlightening nonetheless.

On both occasions I was doing it with friends though which helped a lot with the anxiety and is something I would thoroughly recommend if you can, especially for a first time.
