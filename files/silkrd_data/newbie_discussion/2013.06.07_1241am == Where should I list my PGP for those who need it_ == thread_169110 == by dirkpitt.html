I'll just chime in since it seems like you are still confused. Generally we don't encrypt most of our conversations. We (should) ALWAYS encrypt any personal info. The other conversations can't harm us unless they connect it to you personally. Also vendors don't want to deal with encrypting/unencrypting non-sensitive information because it is time consuming.

So if want to ask a question about the product it doesn't need to be encrypted. It is already public knowledge that "VendorJohn" sells this product.
You are ready to order and send the vendor your address. This NEEDS to be encrypted. This insures no one but the vendor can read your message.
