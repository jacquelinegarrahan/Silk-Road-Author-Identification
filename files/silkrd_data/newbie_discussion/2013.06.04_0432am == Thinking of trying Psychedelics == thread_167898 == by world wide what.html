Title: Re: Thinking of trying Psychedelics
Post by: alchemetric on June 04, 2013, 05:56 am
Definitely LSD. It was the first psychedelic I took and it set me on a path in my life that has been full of wonder and amazingness as I explore the infinite possibilities that phsycedelics offer :D And I've managed to stay sane, get a degree, a 'proper' job and all that normal shit. I truly believe my psychedelic adventures have made me a much more intelligent and open-minded person. So go for it!

Since trying LSD when I was 16 I've moved on to other stuff. I love shrooms, mostly because I pick them myself and I just love that feeling of tripping on something that I picked out of the ground myself. If I were you I'd steer clear of the nbome, DMT, 2c families etc for now. At the end of the day they are all just variations on what you get from LSD and they are all very new and haven't been tested thoroughly. There's a reason LSD has been so popular for so long...it works and there's plenty of info out there about it. Stick with the original and the best. 

But the most important thing to learn about any psychedelic experience is set and setting and getting yourself in the right headspace. So if you haven't done the work and researched how to get the most of out a psychedelic experience, you should really do that before worrying about what drug to take.

Happy to offer more advice if you want :)
