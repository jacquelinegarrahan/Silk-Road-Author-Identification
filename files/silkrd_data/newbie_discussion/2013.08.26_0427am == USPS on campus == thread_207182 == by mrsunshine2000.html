Title: Re: USPS on campus
Post by: DarkLord2012 on August 26, 2013, 04:35 am
They must abide by the same rules.  That is "Official" United States Postage, and it is bound by the same rules and regulations as any where else.  Ex ~ Someone Else's mail comes to your house...you open it...even though it was on your property, you committed a felony by opening it.  Whom ever it is addressed to is the only one who rights to that letter, package, and etc.  Colleges and etc will not risk the chance of getting dragged into a major suit or a privacy rights infringement battle in court.   The same abides with the P.O. Boxes, unless you are in a prison they must handle any official postage by the Postal regulations.  Hope the info helped.
