Title: Re: How to check when shipment was marked in transit?
Post by: MrAnonymous on March 05, 2013, 07:35 pm
If it says processing then it's yet to be marked in transit.

When it is marked in transit, you have 17 days to finalize before it auto finalizes.

So, if it says "A/F in 15 days" then it's been in transit for 2 days.

Explained it the best I could, hope it helps.
