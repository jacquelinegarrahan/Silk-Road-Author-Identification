Title: Re: i have a question for experienced psychedelic users
Post by: noncents on May 08, 2013, 01:01 am
You are talking about what they call a "Level 5" trip, described as a "Total loss of visual connection with reality. The senses cease to function in the normal way. Total loss of ego. Merging with space, other objects, or the universe. The loss of reality becomes so severe that it defies explanation. The earlier levels are relatively easy to explain in terms of measurable changes in perception and thought patterns. This level is different in that the actual universe within which things are normally perceived, ceases to exist! Satorienlightenment (and other such labels)."

Assuming you are eating  psilocybe cubensis that haven't been sitting around for months, I would say six or seven grams should do the trick depending your personal tolerance.  (If you are eating old shrooms it may take a considerable amount more.)   If that's not intense enough, up the dose by a gram the next time.  Just remember that once you are an hour and a half or so into your trip, eating more won't do much good.  you will just be wasting shrooms.
