Title: Re: Question about FE as a newb
Post by: abdomen on July 21, 2013, 12:08 am
An important thing to understand is that they're actually not getting their money until you finalize. Everyone who wants you to FE isn't necessarily scamming you.

It's just that if you DO FE, you will have no recourse if they DO end up scamming you.

Basically, don't finalize early on any order where you wouldn't be okay with just losing the money and never getting any product.
