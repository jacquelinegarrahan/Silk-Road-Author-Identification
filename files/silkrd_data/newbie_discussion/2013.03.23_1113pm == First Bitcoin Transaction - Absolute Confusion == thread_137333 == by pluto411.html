Title: Re: First Bitcoin Transaction - Absolute Confusion
Post by: Gatz on March 23, 2013, 11:33 pm
Your initial transaction has enough confirmations (6), before your Silkroad account shows the bitcoin balance however you will need to wait for the transaction between your deposit address and your silkroad wallet to reach (6). If you go on the blockchain and search your deposit address you should see an outgoing transaction to your silkroad wallet address. You can track progress there.
