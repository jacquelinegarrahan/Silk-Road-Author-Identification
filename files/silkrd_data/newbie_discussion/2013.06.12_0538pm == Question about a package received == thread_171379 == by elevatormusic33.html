Title: Re: Question about a package received
Post by: smokingUK on June 12, 2013, 05:48 pm
The best thing you can do is if you are unsure whether your package is correct is to take detailed photos of the package PRE opening showing the sealed labelled Vac bags and products within.  Then photo the process of opening the product and detailed pics of said product.

It will help you out when corresponding with the vendor.  Already opened pics are useless as obviously you could have tampered with it after opening.

Hope that helps and good luck!
