Title: Re: STICKY Brown MDMA ?? Not ROCKY ??
Post by: captainv on February 13, 2013, 11:14 pm
a lot of people get concerned with the color of MDMA and from what I understand the color is most often associated with how many times it is washed after the synthesis process. The more times the product is washed the more white or clear it can become. As far as sticky goes sounds like something went wrong in the synthesis process so it did not crystallize properly. If it tests dark blue I'm sure there's no real reason for alarm I feel. It may even be because there is too much MDMA in the product and not enough binding elements so you end up with something that did not crystallize correctly. However if it feels really processed and gummy kind of like the white powder you find on doughnuts it is most likely cut heavily and you just may not have the best time taking it.
