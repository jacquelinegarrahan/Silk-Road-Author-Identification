Title: Keep 50 Post Rule For Noobs: Quit Your Whining-Why This Rule Is Important
Post by: Charlesnoir on June 12, 2013, 08:20 pm
Some thoughts on the 50 Post rule for Newbies and the Forums:

In No Particular Order of Importance

-Having to make 50 posts gets Noobs to become familiar the forums
-Reading the forums is important for information exchange and an education of all things SR
-Keeps the repetitive Noob questions in one place allowing the other forums to be free of static
-Encourages interaction for Noobs and other members in a forum where they can ask any Noob question without trepidation of seeming Noobish

For those who have been around awhile but have not posted to 50...Why Not?  Your input as a member would be appreciated.  You most likely have a lot to offer by sharing your experiences.

For those who "Spam to 50":  Your Loss.
You are cheating yourself out of a learning experience.
You are also, generally speaking, impatient and looking for the easy way out and it shows. It will be even more glaring when you spam to 50 and start some irritating, ignorant rant in the other forums.  This leads to the question I would want to know about you as a buyer or seller: 
What other shortcuts do you take in life?
