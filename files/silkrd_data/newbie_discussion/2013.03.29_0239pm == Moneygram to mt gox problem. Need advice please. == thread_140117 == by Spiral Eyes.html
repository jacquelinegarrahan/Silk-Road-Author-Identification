Post by: knexx on April 07, 2013, 08:12 pm
If you purchased from BitInstant to a mtgox account, they won't deposit the funds directly into your account. What will happen is that you'll get an email from BitInstant confirming that the transaction has completed and it will look like a receipt.

Then, like 7-8 lines into the receipt information there will be a line that reads:

APIResponse: MTGOX-USD-xxxxx-xxxxx-xxxxx-xxxxx

That's a coupon code. You have to copy and paste that code into the correct box in your account information and the funds should appear.

I hope you get your funds squared away. BitInstant is a hassle and does not effectively communicate how to use Mt. Gox coupon codes :(. BTC is not a perfect currency, there are many headaches associated with it.

Edit: did not see that you already tried to use the code before posting this. There's a BitInstant newbie help thread on their forums that a moderator will help you out, sort of like a livesupport system. I'd try there if their email response is slow.
