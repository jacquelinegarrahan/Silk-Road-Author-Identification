Title: Re: BTCs to my Wallet Question
Post by: 88fxstc on June 11, 2013, 06:20 pm
I'm guessing your BTC's are in another wallet somewhere, so, goto the Road, click on account and there is an address for your SR wallet. Copy that addy , goto your other wallet and somewhere, somehow there is a link to send money, click it and paste your SR wallet addy in the appropriate place . It's pretty easy, your BTC's usually won't show up immediately in your SR account, it can take up to a few hours usually.
