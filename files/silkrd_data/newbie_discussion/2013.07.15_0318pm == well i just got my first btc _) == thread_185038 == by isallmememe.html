Post by: mcguire39 on July 15, 2013, 03:25 pm
Congratulations on getting your BTC. Wallets like Electrum let you specify the transfer fee you want to assign. Fortunately even a small fee such as .0005 helps things move along a little faster. You can certainly assign no fee when you transfer, but it tends to make the confirmations take a lot longer since transactions with a fee seem to all get processed first.
