Title: Re: A few questions from a noob
Post by: abby on April 03, 2013, 11:25 pm
I would suggest you contact your vendor of choice and ask what kind of packaging they use.  they won't tell you what the stealth is I suspect but they should be able to tell you how many times they bag it and what types of bags they use.  You could even ask them about their failure rate for delivery through europe.

I'd also suggest you go for a look through the forums, particularly the security one.  you'll get an idea of the ideas bandied around and why packaging is done a certain way.  It will make you much more comfortable when you go to ask the vendor (if they haven't already posted in one of the threads there about upgrading their packaging).  You could even go check out their review thread if they have one as there may be some information there.

I doubt many vendors these days are sloppy enough to just to chuck it in a bag but it's better to be safe than sorry.
