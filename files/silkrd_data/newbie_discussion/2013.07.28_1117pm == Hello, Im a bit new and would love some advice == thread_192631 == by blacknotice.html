Post by: mcchickenrulz on July 28, 2013, 11:31 pm
BTC! first step is getting the currency. You cant do anything without money hehe :P
There are many ways of doing this, research is your friend. I use coinbase personally.

After you have some currency, MORE RESEARCH! scan all the listings, find out what you want to buy. Read up on all the vendors you can selling what you want. Compare prices. Dont forget Domestic vs International. You may want to do some small domestic orders first to get the hang of it all.

NEVER FE! I can not stress that enough. The escrow system is there to protect from scammers. Do not let it go to waste.

TL;DR: RESEARCH! Learn everything you possibly can about shipping, your vendor, your drug, bitcoins, everything. The forums are your friend.
