I came across the silkroad after looking into bitcoin. I kid you not I had a grin from ear to ear for at least 3 days.

After considering the risks I decided to take a punt. I moved to an area where, whilst there is plenty on offer, the quality is appalling. Subsequently I gave up one of my favorite part time activities. It's now been quite a few years since I had any quality cocaine.

Aside from the actual recreation itself I just love the fact of a libertarian consumer service. Even if I had given up for good I would feel it is my duty to buy something, anything, through this service just to stick it to the man.

I've now got two successful transactions under my belt and I could not be happier. The way the recent attacks have been batted off - The open and forthright nature of the vendors (well, the two that I've dealt with) - and the fact this service exists at all - is just a joy to behold.

I just wanted to post this and say thanks! Seriously, this is one of the best things that has happened in my lifetime.
