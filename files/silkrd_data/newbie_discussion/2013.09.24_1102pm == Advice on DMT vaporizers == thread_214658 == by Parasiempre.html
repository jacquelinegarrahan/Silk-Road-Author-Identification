Honestly you cant prevent from burning it.... The mission is to inhale smoke!!! But the trick is to not get the smoke to hot...

Process:

Start by slowly heating the meth pipe (this is dependent on glass thickness).......Once the glass starts getting warm (approaching dmt vaping point) the DMT will turn "oily" and literally start running from the flame/heat... at this point start rolling the bowl (simply rolling/twisting the pipe with your fingers, while inhaling, so the flame/heat is spread evenly across the glass globe) so to avoid "hot spots"... Inhale until you cant......exhale....and repeat (if possible)....


Heating:

The heating part is key with this type of pipe or any DMT pipe, if not done right you will waste product, not achieve desired effects, and could burn your lungs/mouth... This is why vaporizers/vape genie's are recommended..(easy/simple/efficient)... Also "the machine" is much more full proof and efficient plus easy to make and cheap/free....

The meat:

That being said, dump dosage in pipe,  Try and keep the flame ~2-1 inches below the glass/dmt (slowly heating)....At the first signs of vapors/smoke start inhaling slowly (hard enough to keep smoke rolling in the pipe but slow enough so you can get a full hit) you don't have to pull hard, just long....

Keep in mind to heat enough (by controlling the lighters distance from the glass) to get continuous smoke..... but once you start seeing smoke you have reached DMT's vaping point and should try and "keep" that temperature....
