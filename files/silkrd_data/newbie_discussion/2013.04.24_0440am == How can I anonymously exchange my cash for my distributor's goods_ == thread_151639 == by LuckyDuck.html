You are definitely on the right track to getting a good system. One thing to consider, is generating a pattern. The Public is very keen on strange behavior. When people follow the traditional system of corner dealing, there is a recognizable pattern that is 1) atypical behavior 2) predictable.

If you are located in one place, selling drugs, then you are a red flag. In the conventional narcotic enterprise, this is the drill. Stand in one place, have a few guards, a look out, and it's on. It takes care of marketing: word of mouth + open-air sales. Security is already intertwined with this approach, but it still leaves a world of hurt: rival gangs and LEO harassment.

Even if you are two people making harmless Cannabis exchanges, if it looks suspicious to a nosy neighbor, then it is a dangerous risk to business. These are your finances at risk--retirement, charity, food, shelter, rent, etc. Your livelihood is at stake by Capture Troopers. Live a normal life, and your life will be normal. However, live a normal life on the Road, and you're doing something right.  8)
