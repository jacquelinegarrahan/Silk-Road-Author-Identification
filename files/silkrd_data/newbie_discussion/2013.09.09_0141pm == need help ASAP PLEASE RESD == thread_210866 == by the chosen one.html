Children 6 mo of age and older

Usual daily dose PO 1 to 2.5 mg 3 or 4 times daily initially; increase gradually as needed and tolerated.

Sounds like he took amount that they would space out throughout the day. Taking it all at once is on the high end of recommended doses.

Here is more specific information.

Quote
Usual Pediatric Dose for Light Sedation

Conscious sedation for procedures:
Oral:
1 to 12 years: 0.2 to 0.3 mg/kg orally 45 to 60 minutes before procedure, up to a maximum of 10 mg

If you take the weight of the kid in kilograms and multiply it by .3mg then you will see the highest mg that would be used for a doctor to sedate a kid for a procedure.

The max of 10mg would work out to a 33kg child, if you think 5mg was consumed then a 16kg child would be given that dose.

If your kid is under 16kg then medical attention is probably a good idea. Even though overdoses of benzos are rarely fatal they are still dangerous.
