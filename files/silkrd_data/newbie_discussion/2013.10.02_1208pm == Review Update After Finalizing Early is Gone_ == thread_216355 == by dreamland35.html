Title: Re: Review Update After Finalizing Early is Gone?
Post by: Rocknessie on October 02, 2013, 12:44 pm
The ability to change reviews has gone. You can delete reviews but not modify them. This is to prevent holding Vendors "review hostage".

In future DO NOT leave a review when you FE. Just wait for the product, go into Account History and you will be able to place your review.
