Yes. You can still place an order. The vendor likely put the listing in "stealth" mode which means you can only access the listing if you have the link. The reason vendors might put a listing in stealth is if they have a limited supply or are getting too many orders at once. However, the stealth mode listing allows previous customers,  or in your case, those have saved the URL, to order. The vendor may decline your order because a possible reason he went it to stealth mode was to save product for long-time customers.
