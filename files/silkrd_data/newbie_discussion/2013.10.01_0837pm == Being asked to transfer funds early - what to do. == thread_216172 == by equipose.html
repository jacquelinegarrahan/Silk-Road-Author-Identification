Welcome equipose,

If you go to the vendor's page, what does he/she state is the FE (finalize early) policy generally, and policy toward newbies?

Ultimately this is a judgement call.  The standard advice is "never FE."  You can't go wrong with this advice.  The other side of the advice is that you may be willing to FE if you are totally OK with losing the money and never receiving anything, either because the order is tiny or you are as rich as Bill Gates.

From the vendor's perspective understand where he/she is coming from.  Some first timers are actually scammers, and some just make annoying mistakes that can compromise a vendor, like using fake addresses that are undeliverable.  I'm sure you won't do these things, but this vendor likely has seen it before.

Cheers,
lola
