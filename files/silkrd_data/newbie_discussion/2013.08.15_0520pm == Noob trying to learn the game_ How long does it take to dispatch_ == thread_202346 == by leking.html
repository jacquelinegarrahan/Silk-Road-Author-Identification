Post by: DudeAbider on August 18, 2013, 03:40 pm
New member here so take my advice with a grain of salt... but it's not all that uncommon for legal online purchases to take awhile to process.  And those sites aren't even exposing themselves to any level of risk.  Delays happen, sometimes it's just the way people handle their business.  I totally get where you're coming from that vendors with super fast shipping and amazing customer service should get the highest ratings, and certainly agree when it comes to sites likes amazon, ebay and etsy.

But on SR, there's a ton of risk for both buyers and vendors and I think it's fair to expect some delays after making a purchase.  Either one of them could get charges filed against them unless the vendor stays on the top of their game.  To me, the ratings system is a way for buyers to get a better idea which vendors are the most trusted rather than those that give the most amazing customer service, since trust is everything here.  With that said, it's awesome to see vendors making daily updates, having the time to answer the plethora of PMs they get, making good on mishaps with their customers.. things that make for great customer service.  I just think that trust is the bottom line rather than customer service, and in that respect any vendor that gives updates and/or ships within a week deserves a 5.
