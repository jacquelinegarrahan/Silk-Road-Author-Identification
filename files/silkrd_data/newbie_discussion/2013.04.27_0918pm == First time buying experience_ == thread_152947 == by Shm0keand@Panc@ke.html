Title: Re: First time buying experience?
Post by: parkdanger on April 28, 2013, 02:22 am
Quote from: heavysmoker on April 27, 2013, 11:26 pm
Ive never been ripped off on SR, and i think a big part of that is because i have never FEed either.   

FE is not something that exists for vendors to burn customers.

If the vendor is reputable, handling a great volume of transactions, they aren't going to be ripping people off just like that. Even on the street but especially on SR, success is an indicator of good business practice: burning customers isn't going to get you more, especially when they write a nasty review.

Quote from: avatar2013 on April 28, 2013, 01:02 am
As far as I understand, the escrow system is meant to keep the buyers safe - so why on earth should a buyer finalize early?

You should always read a vendor's page before placing an order with them. Some require early finalization with certain types of transactions. Even if not, a buyer may not need to finalize early, but it is a sign of good-faith and may expedite and improve your service by helping the vendor out in this way. Read the profiles.
