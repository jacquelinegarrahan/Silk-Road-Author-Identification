Title: Re: How long is normal "order processing"?
Post by: Libertas on May 08, 2013, 06:10 pm
Vendors have 4 days from the time you place your order to mark it 'in transit'. This was temporarily extended to 7 days to allow for the downtime caused by the DDoS attack, but is back to 4 days now. If they do not ship the order within that 4 day time-frame you are free to cancel it.

Please keep in mind though that many vendors are incredibly busy catching up on things. However, as Silk Road is a free market, you are free to cancel any orders that take longer than 4 days to process and order elsewhere.

Libertas
