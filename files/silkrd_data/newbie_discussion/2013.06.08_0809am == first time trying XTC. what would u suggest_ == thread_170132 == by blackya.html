Post by: mrmdma on June 08, 2013, 11:40 am
100mg is plenty for the first time. Don't redose. Drink adequate water if you are moving or in a hot environment. Don't over-drink though, many people think they need to drink like a gallon of water when taking MDMA. - Absolutely bullshit. I drink around 0,5 litres per hour when I am at a rave or festival. - Never had a single incident.

Eat a proper meal around 3-4 hours before you take the MDMA. If you have just had a meal before dropping MDMA, you might not feel the full effects. I once wasted 200mg completely by dropping just after I had eaten a kilo of pasta...

MDMA can make some people uneasy, it will skyrocket your cortisol (stress hormone) levels and might induce some paranoia and uneasiness in some people (bugging out as some people say). This usually happens when the MDMA is just starting to work - If this happens to you, just tell yourself that it will pass and everything will be fine (and it 99% of the time will be fine :)).
