Title: Re: How long does a order take to fill?
Post by: ConcernedCitizen on April 20, 2013, 04:35 pm
Learn how to use PGP and practice it in the stickied thread of this forum (make a new key for this! dont use the same key on SR and the forums!). That way, you won't waste a vendor's time when they fail to decrypt your message and have to tell you that you PGP'ed wrong.

After that, always encrypt your address and other sensitive information. Avoid vendors who do not use PGP. Always use your real name and address unless you have a sophisticated scheme to use another address. Triple check your address before encrypting and check it against the standard mail address format which can be found through a google search.

Title: Re: How long does a order take to fill?
Post by: safras on April 20, 2013, 07:25 pm
Some vendors often mark it as "in transit" the same day or next day (depending on time you ordered). But there are others I've ordered from who take as much as 3 days to dispatch. Try to know when you'll need your drugs at least 2 weeks in advance, this way you have time to sort your Bitcoins out, transfer them, make the order, wait for the vendor to send it and wait for the postal service to deliver it. It usually takes less than that. I've had orders in the space of two days, but just in case, you should always plan ahead. SR does go down, and popular vendors do get swamped sometimes with a backlog of orders.
