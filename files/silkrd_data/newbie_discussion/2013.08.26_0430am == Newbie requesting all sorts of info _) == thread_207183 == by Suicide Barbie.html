Post by: Divo on August 26, 2013, 05:31 am
Hi, welcome to SR!

I've actually always been under the impression using a PO box is a bad idea.  I've always just ordered to my address, using my name, and never had any issues.  Though I just purchase small amounts for personal use.  As long as you are selecting a respected, domestic vendor who is on point with their shipping and stealth, you shouldn't have any issues.  By BTC converter, I'm assuming you mean what the $ value is currently?  If you look in the bottom left hand corner of SR when you are logged on it should say 1btc = $x or something simliar, but to make it even easier, in your account settings you can select which currency you want prices to be displayed in so the site will just do the conversion for you.  As for purchasing bitcoins, localbitcoins is a good option, especially if you want to pay cash and keep things as anonymous as possible.  There are a handful of sites online you can purchase from, Coinbase, campbx, etc, but they all require some kind of linkage to your real identity in some way.  If you go that route, it's a good idea to move the BTC around a few times to addresses that aren't linked with your identity before finally moving them on to SR.  There is a wealth of information regarding all of this in the forums here, spend as much time as you need to read, read, read until you have a firm understanding of how things are working and always feel free to ask questions.  Hope I've helped a little!
