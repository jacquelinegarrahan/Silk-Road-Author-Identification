Title: Re: Oh boy! Could I use some help please.
Post by: monday.123 on February 23, 2013, 04:53 am
1. Karma is the +0/-0 by your name and avatar. If someone likes your posts, they give you +1. If they don't like one, they'll give you -1. It's kind of like in Reddit. It's supposed to give you some idea of people's reputations around here, but it can be easily abused and is sometimes disregarded.
2. SR offers great tools for keeping safe (anonymity, hedging, etc.). You can trade outside of the SR, but it's safer to pay for a vendor account if that strikes your fancy.
3. Read the forums. Decent vendors will usually not require you to FE (unless it's your first couple of purchases). I'd suggest reading the vendor you're interested in's review thread. I'd also suggest trying to find a vendor who doesn't require FE from new customers. Find a well-reviewed vendor who does this and you're set.
4. No idea I'm afraid. (Is this the PCP you were looking for: http://silkroadvb5piz3r.onion/silkroad/category/62 ?)
5. Make sure you know how to encrypt your address (with PGP). You'll need to send it encrypted when you make your purchase. Make sure to read the forums about your intended vendor to make sure you don't get scammed. Never sign for a package (or choose a postage option that requires signing). And be curteous with vendors, some users seem to think this is Walmart's drug section. Best of luck and happy tripping friend, this is a fucking amazing place we've stumbled upon.
