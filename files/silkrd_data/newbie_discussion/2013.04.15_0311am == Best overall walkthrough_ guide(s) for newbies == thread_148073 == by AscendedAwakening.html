There won't be a form to fill out from Bitfloor. They will just give you the instructions you need for your deposit. All you really need is the account number and the deposit amount that they give you. Make certain you deposit the EXACT amount, since this is how they identify you. The form you will fill out is a BoA out-of-state deposit slip (assuming you don't live in NY). The email is just used to notify you when your deposits or withdraws are confirmed; you can use a Tormail address for this.

Once you have money in your Bitfloor account and trade for BTC, you will use the "Withdraw" option to send them to your SR address (under the account tab on the main page). You can send them through an extra wallet or two for added anonymity, but SR already tumbles your BTC as it is.

PGP is not hard to learn; check out this tutorial and you should have it down in no time. ---> http://32yehzkk7jflf6r2.onion/gpg4usb/
