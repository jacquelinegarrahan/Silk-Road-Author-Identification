Title: Re: Thinking about becoming a Mushroom Vendor here on SR
Post by: acider on March 28, 2013, 12:20 pm
Hi and welcome.
EU shroom market is unstable, sometimes it's filled with nice offers and sometimes it's dry. So your business depends on the current market conditions. But generally it's not very competitive.
The fact that you have some relatively rare strains will help, although 11euro/g is a bit pricey imo. But if you give options for lower prices on higher quantities I believe you ll get many sales.
Usual prices on 5-10g listings for simple cubensis are at 5-6 euros/g but currently most of the vendors are sold out.

I suppose you also have plenty in stock to make this plan viable, in that case if you find hard to get first sales consider giving out cheap samples to get some feedback. Not necessary but a good method if you find your business stuck at the beginning.

Good luck!
