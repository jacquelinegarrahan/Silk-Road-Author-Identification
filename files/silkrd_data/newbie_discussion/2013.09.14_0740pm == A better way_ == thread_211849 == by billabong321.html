Title: Re: A better way?
Post by: Al Capone on September 14, 2013, 08:17 pm
Sounds like you've taken almost every precaution. Next, you'll want to use PGP to encrypt your address when sending it to the vendor. This ensures that the vendor is the only one to read your address.

Past that, just make sure you are using a reputable vendor. If they have poor/no feedback, I'd stay away. Everything should work out just fine. Good luck, and welcome to Silk Road!
