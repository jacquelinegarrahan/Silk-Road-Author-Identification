Title: IMPORTANT ADVICE TO NEWBIES BUYING BULK
Post by: smuggles on April 25, 2013, 08:48 pm
I've been on the silk road for a little over a year. I don't usually post in these forums, because I like to stay low key. I'm not interested in the petty drama or people crying over their 1 gram purchase. However, I know a lot of you are like me and looking for bulk ketamine or other products. Yes, getting a gram or two is a cake walk. In fact in my experience getting even 50g on here that actually arrives and is good quality is a walk in the park. However, those of you looking to pick up 250g-1kg you will be fighting every step of the way. There are a lot of bad vendors on here, which took me by surprise last year. So far I've dumped over 40k onto this market with good and bad results. This is what I've found out the hard way:

1. DON'T TRUST FEEDBACK: If you look a lot of the vendors feedback, it is mostly if not all people who finalized early. Vendors hold everyone hostage on here to give 5/5 or end up on a blacklist as a scammer and tell the rest of the vendors not to deal with you, regardless of quality, no ships, etc. 5/5 is excellent service. However, if you look at the actual comments people will complain about the service and sometimes not even recieve products and still give 5/5 hoping to get their money back. I've been there myself, but now I refuse to be the one. If you need a refund that is not excellent service. You actually get a bad mark for getting a refund on here and it makes vendors suspicious of you. That is poor service anywhere else in the world including the drug market. If you leave a dishonest score for the vendors benefit you are cheating yourself, contributing to the large amount of scammers on here, and disempowering the buyers. If someone gives you bad product, delays shipping, or does not send the product leave a realistic score for them. They won't blacklist you. It's an empty threat.

2. NEVER FINALIZE EARLY: Vendors will tell you to 'FE', because of your stats. You don't have a history and they want to 'protect themselves' from being 'scammed' or you got a refund from a vendor who gave you bad service and they are saying that they are aftraid you are a scammer. As a new user you will most likely fall for this, trusting the feedback. Don't. I lost $14,000 the first time I came on here to GOOGLEDEYED on buying a kg of Ketamine. He told me that it was shipped UK, which is not a designated drug country and guaranteed 100% reship. In reality it was sent from India (Flagged country) and it got caught by customs of course. He never sent the reship and cut off communication. Every transaction after I have not FE'd and when they went sideways I was thankful, because SR staff could oversee the process to make sure I'm protected as a buyer. The system is there for a reason.

3. DON'T TRUST MIDDLEMEN: Learn to identify who's sending direct and who's taking your money to send it to another country. PILLPOPPERZ and GOOGLEDEYED are examples of middlemen. There are others on here I thankfully figured out were middlemen before I did business with them, but since I have not purchased from them, so I will not put their names out there. Middlemen will have you FE and then they will take the money and order it from someone else in India or Pakistan. Just like your shady friend of a friend on the street who asks for your money to go around the corner and pick up product for you and does not return or gives you bad product.

They send your personal information to people you have not communicated with, so they can fulfill the order. If it doesn't make it they will cut off contact or give you a hard time on refunding you if you did not FE. Middlemen just like on the street are not real wholesalers or professionals. They don't even have enough money to purchase the product they are selling. You can't communicate with the actual supplier so you are playing the telephone game the entire time. If the quality is bad, the package does not arrive, or it is light, they will pass the responsibility to vague outside influences. Example of PILLPOPPERZ is he sent weak ketamine and only sent part of the order. It came again from a designated drug country: India. The part he did send was 10grams light and weak. The other 250g package I never recieved a tracking number for an entire month. He kept saying that the police was after his supplier and he was running around like a fugitive with my package trying to send it for an entire month, till I finally told him I wanted a refund. Again, because I didn't FE I got my money back.

4. RESEARCH YOUR NEW SUPPLIER: Don't just go through the menu, see an unbelievable price, and jump on it. Bad move. This is a lot of money and a serious business relationship you are initiating. All of us are in a rush to get pipelines up that are down, find someone with better quality than your current supplier, or get a fairer price. However, some scammers are on here posting unbelievable prices and asking you to FE. They will send out smaller orders to get their feedback up and turn around and walk off with the large orders. They won't care about the account being closed, because they will just make a new one. Research the vendor on the forums first. Remember just because someone said something bad about them, doesn't make them a scammer. This is drug dealing. There are always conflicts and issues and people can blow them out of proportion. There's names I put in here to as a warning, don't believe me. Check for yourself. See if the vendor responds to the person openly on the forum and judge for yourself who's right. Look for corroborating evidence. If there are scores of people saying the same thing, it is most likely true.

5. INTERVIEW YOUR SUPPLIER: There is a twisted logic on Silk Road that you are at the mercy of the vendor. This is not a reality unless you make it so. Remember if you give undeserved feedback or FE you are putting yourself at risk of losing money, and contributing to the growing amount of vendors who scam or selectively scam people. Bulk buyers are at the highest risk for this. Vendors might fulfill orders for small customers with ease all day long. However, when you show up with several thousand dollars it turns into a whole new game. You wouldn't trust someone with several thousand dollars just because some drug users that buy 20 bucks worth of smack said he was a stand up guy in real life, so don't do it here. Interview the vendor. Ask them when you will recieve the tracking number once the orders made. Ask them what country it's being sent from and if they are personally handling the package. Tell them your country and ask them their success rate in sending there. Fact check all of it. Information is important. If they ask you to FE tell them you are uncomfortable with that arrangement and ask them why they would want to circumvent the process. SR staff reviews refunds and there should be a tracking number for your shipment. There is no room on your end to scam them, so if they tell you they are protecting themselves it is bullshit. If they want to get into the bulk game, they need a proven track record of bulk orders for you to trust them or they need to keep the money in escrow till it arrives.

I am currently doing business with another bulk vendor on here for Ketamine and MDMA. I am not going to promote or give negative reviews yet, because it is too early. However, as soon as we have done a few more orders with him I will let you guys know if he is a trusted vendor. I would appreciate other reviews here of BULK vendors not small orders (we have plenty of those already). Good luck out there and don't get caught.

-Smuggles
