yes you do receive a letter informing you that he has been seized it is the law  but cops usually take a long time before giving notice because they want to seize more,your address will obviously be flagged so I d advise you to change your drop address
also you should receive the package (without drugs)with a stickers on it mentinonning the content  has been seized
the package itself isnt illegal therefore if they dont send it back to you it is theft
depending on the quantity the process can take up to a month
small amount you generally get informed within 5 days
