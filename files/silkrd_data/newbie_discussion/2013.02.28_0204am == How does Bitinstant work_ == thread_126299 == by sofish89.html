Typically, if you have a Mt. Gox wallet, you can link BitInstant to transfer funds there, from which you'll then reroute them to your SR account. The deposit slip they use is usually through a third party bill payment called ZipZap. Usually you just need to present it to the cashier when making your transaction. You'll find more specific instructions on your payment slip.
