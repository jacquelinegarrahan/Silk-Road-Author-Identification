Title: Re: I'm confused about an order
Post by: The Bubble Dreamer on May 01, 2013, 11:20 pm
That's rounding. Internally the prices are calculated with much more precision than the 2 digits displayed.

So, in your case it could be 0.073, which is rounded to 0.07. But the total of 0.073 x 3 = 0.219, which is rounded to 0.22

If bitcoin rate goes up, prices will probably be displayed with more precision or in mBTC.
