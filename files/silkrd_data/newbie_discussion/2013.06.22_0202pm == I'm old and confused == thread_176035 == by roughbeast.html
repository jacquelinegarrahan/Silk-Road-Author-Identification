Post by: Authbyma on June 22, 2013, 02:07 pm
AFAIK, it's up to SilkRoad itself to handle these claims. I'm sure they use their best judgement to determine how to handle each case. If you have a no-show product & they've had 10 other people also contact them about the same vendor in the past 2 weeks, there's probably a good chance you'll get most or all of your coins back.

But if you're the only person to report the vendor in months of perfect service, they're more likely to assume that it just got lost in the mail, rather than the vendor simply not sending it.
