A game to help get our post no's up.

The game works like this - the first post starts at Jan 1.

The second poster can change EITHER the month OR the date, to any legitimate month or date. ie they could say Jan 30 (date change), or they could say Mar 1 (month change), but they couldn't say Mar 30 because that changes both the month and date.

Whoever gets to December 31 wins.
