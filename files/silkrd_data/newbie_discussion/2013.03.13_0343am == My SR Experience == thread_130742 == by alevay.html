You are welcome to all who thanked me, this is the kind of stuff I would like to see more of as it aids new buyers. Most vendors want to see a minimum of transactions, a good way to build you number is to buy digital goods and lottery. They usually do not require minimum transactions, that is what I did anyway. Look at the sellers numbers, most of the digital goods and lottery are up near 100, so you are pretty safe there. Look out for viruses when purchasing digital goods, use a san box, look it up if you do not know what one is!!

I have another to add to add to my experiences:

Natural Weed 3/5 Product "Dutch Speed"- The guy is nice and I want to believe that he is honest. My product never came however and I got nothing but an apology in return. I do not 0/5 the guy because I want to believe, but the product never came either way.

I have one other order from EU on the way and if that does not come, I am through. With their rigid return policy it would be too easy to sucker US buyers into a scam to be honest. If you agree to some policy that only gives you 10% or nothing back, you are honor bound to give the 5/5 feedback if they abide by it. It is too easy to think that a guy can sit back and wait a month for a 90-100% return on the money and have the SR policy that you agreed to backing him up. And if you complain? Well you are a new buyer and they are an established SR seller...

Not saying that is always the case, just that the opportunity is there and could be taken by people who are prone to easy money.

Like I said, one more and I am through. This (if it does not come through) will make EU 0/3 in delivering. I never order much, so it is better to learn from mistakes than to have the mark of a scammer or refund on your stats. There are very honest sellers out there, I have named a few on this thread.
