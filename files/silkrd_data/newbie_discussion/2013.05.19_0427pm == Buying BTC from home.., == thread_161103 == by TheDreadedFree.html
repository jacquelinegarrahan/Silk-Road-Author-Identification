Any business that accepts a wholly reversible method of payment for something that is entirely irreversible would be in business for all of about 3 minutes, TheDreadedFree. :P

So that's a no, I'm afraid. It was tried before but the company that offered it were targeted by people who were using stolen credit card details and after they'd sent out the Bitcoin, the banks got on to them and reversed the credit card transactions leaving the Bitcoin seller with no money and no Bitcoin!

If I recall correctly there IS a site that will allow you to buy very minute amounts (I'm talking 0.01 BTC amounts) using a credit card, but they require "CIA Top Secret" style identity verification so it really wouldn't be worth it!
