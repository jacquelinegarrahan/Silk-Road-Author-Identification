Hi All
AFter trawling through pages of advice for new users, one of the most important things that I came across was advice for new users who have to FE.
Whilst I agree it's best to avoid FE, if you do YOU DON'T HAVE TO LEAVE FEEDBACK straight away. You can click "FE" then navigate away from the feedback page and leave the feedback when your package comes and you are happy..
Leaving feedback that says "FE" is really not a helpful way for other buyers to gauge the reliability of a vendor, or the quality of his product..
Cheers All.
