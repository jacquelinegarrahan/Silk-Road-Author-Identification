Title: Re: Recrystallizing Ketamine
Post by: blimthepixie on May 14, 2013, 10:16 am
Yes this would work. I have tried it when buying ketamine solution before.

I used a large plate and a microwave on low power, checking every few seconds or so.

If you use a pan, make sure it is non-stick and I think it will be a better method as you could see what is happening, rather than staring through the mesh of a microwave...

Use smallest amount of water as possible, just to reduce the time it takes for it to evaporate off..
