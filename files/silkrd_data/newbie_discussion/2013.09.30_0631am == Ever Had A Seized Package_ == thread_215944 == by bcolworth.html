Title: Re: Ever Had A Seized Package?
Post by: bluealbum on September 30, 2013, 07:00 am
I've never had an intercepted package, but just want to share my perspective.

Since the shipping itself is handled by the vendor, the only ways I can see you mitigating risk are in where the package is shipped and how you transmit your shipping information to the vendor.

   You can mitigate the risk of communicating your address with pgp (which most vendors require anyway). In receiving larger packages of cannabis, it's also a good idea to have it delivered to an address you don't have anything illegal at, just in case. Some of the especially careful (or paranoid) leave the package unopened for a day or two, just to be safe. If you want really useful information, I'd recommend the review threads for the specific vendors you're interested in. The reviews in those threads contain lots of useful bits of info, not limited to the quality of stealth shipping employed. Just my two cents.
