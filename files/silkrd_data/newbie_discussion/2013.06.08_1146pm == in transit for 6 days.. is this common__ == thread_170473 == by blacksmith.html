sorry for misunderstanding the question - i thought maybe you expected it to change from "in transit" to something else automatically.

anyway, i don't think you need to worry yet for a few reasons:

1.  just because the vendor marked it 'in transit' 6 days ago doesn't mean that he actually put your order in the mail that same day.  some vendors mark it in transit after they've gotten it packaged up and ready to go, but then don't actually send it that same day.

2.  unless they're shipping via Express Mail, then it's not unreasonable for it to take a week or so to reach you.

what you can do, if you don't receive it in the next few days, is click on "resolve" when that option becomes available to you -- that will just extend the finalization date and give it more time to arrive.
