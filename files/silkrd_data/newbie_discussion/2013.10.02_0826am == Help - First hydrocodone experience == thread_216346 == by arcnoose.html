Title: Help - First hydrocodone experience
Post by: arcnoose on October 02, 2013, 08:26 am
Hello all,

I'd like to start by saying, I'm not that new to opiates, I have been taking oxycodone 20mg three times a day for a number of months, as well as 200-400mg codeine a few times a week when I'm out of oxycodone.

Yesterday I got a hold of two 10/325 hydrocodone. I figured since hydro is reportedly weaker compared to oxy, I'd pop both and just relax and watch some TV. Here's what happened:

5:30 - Chewed a few times and popped two 10x325 (20mg)
5:45 - Start to sense that subtle warm feeling I'm so used to
5:55 - Starting to sweat a bit, stood up and walked around, body was definitely starting to feel a bit numbed out.
6:00 - Mouth started to get a bit dry, and it almost seemed as though there was some sort of numbing agent in the pills, as my mouth was not only dry, but very dumb and my speech starting getting a bit slurred. Turned on the TV to watch some shows.
6:10 - Vision is getting blurry, body feels extremely warm but not the same way I'm used to, this time it's much more sedating and numbing as compared to oxy which is much more uplifting, no real euphoria yet.
6:30 - I can't see shit at all, fully have double vision, I could see well enough if I closed one eye but just couldn't focus on anything whatsoever. I got up to use the washroom, definitely lost some coordination in my body because I couldn't really feel anything.
6:45 - Vision just kept getting worse, but now I am starting to close my eyes because I'm getting a headache, but when I do I start to feel extreme relaxation, warm and cozy. But when I open my eyes it almost seemed as though I were drunk.

I think I fell asleep after that or something, as next thing I knew it was 7:30, vision was a bit better but still definitely blurry. Massive massive headache, I took some aspirin and took a nap, woke up a few house later, still a headache, took a nap and woke up again, this time feeling fine.

All in all, hydrocodone wasn't at all what I was expecting. If someone were to have given that to me and told me it was some sort of tranquilizer, I would have believed them. Normally codeine/oxy gives me a great euphoric feeling, as well as *improved* cognitive function, and although I can't feel much of my body, they certainly don't have tranquilizer-like effects on me. The hydro seemed much more of a downer drug, whereas oxy to me is definitely an upper.

Anyone had any experience with the two and have similar findings? I'm not sure if I want to try hydrocodone again, I really didn't like how the high was almost like being drunk (especially the vision impairment part) BUT I would definitely say the 20mg hydro did in fact have a better pain management effect on my body (I literally couldn't feel a damn thing).

Do you think I took too much my first time? I know different opiates have different effects on people but I really didn't think 20mg would be a lot. This stuff hit me very hard, very different from oxy.

Thanks for any help, cheers
