Hey people I thought I would share some knowledge...

If you need to replace a door in your house you will need to tell the salesperson about it. First measure the width of the door panel itself. 36" 24" ect. then open the door and stand with your back to the hinge side of the frame. From here does the door swing left or right? If it swings left and the panel measured 36" then you would tell the salesperson you need a 36" left hung door.
