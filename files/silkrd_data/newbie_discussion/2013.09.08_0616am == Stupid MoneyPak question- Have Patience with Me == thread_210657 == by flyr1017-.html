Title: Re: Stupid MoneyPak question- Have Patience with Me
Post by: cloudedhouse on September 09, 2013, 06:00 am
1. purchase money pack. bring like $20 extra to cover any fees/exchange rates/etc. you can buy them at cvs, walgreens, walmart, places like that.

2. localbitcoins.com find someone that accepts moneypak and contact them via their preferred method. i text message my vendor.

3. take a clear photo of the receipt + card number and send it to them once you have worked out a deal

4. transfer the coins from your localbitcoins wallet to your personal wallet, maybe bounce them around a few other wallets, and then send them to your silkroad account.
