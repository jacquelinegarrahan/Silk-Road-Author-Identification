Title: Re: how to finalize
Post by: pintofwater on February 13, 2013, 06:47 am
As for signing for a package, ask the seller if you will need to sign for it.

If the seller says you don't need to sign for it and then the mail-person says you have to sign for it, then do not sign for it. That is an admission of guilt and is most likely a trap and you will be arrested. It's better to lose an order than going to jail.
