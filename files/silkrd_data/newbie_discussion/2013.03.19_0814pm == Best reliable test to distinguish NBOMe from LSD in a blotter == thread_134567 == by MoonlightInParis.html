Title: Re: Best reliable test to distinguish NBOMe from LSD in a blotter

Just tried the blacklight trick with 4 different types of blotter, three of which i believe to be LSD and one of which is almost certainly an NBOMe (sold to me locally as L). The NBOMR was by far the brightest under black light but im pretty sure this is just because it was laid on extremely white/clean paper. I don't think this test is reliable for much.

I just picked up some Elrich tests which should only react (turn purple) with indolol rings central to tryptamines, however I have read about some people getting pink colors and even purples with NBOMes. I have not yet tried it on my NBOMe blotter.

Marquis may be able to distinguish LSD (black) from 2c-x/NBOMes (no reaction or yellow)

I got the info above mostly from browsing clearnet forums and no sources are reliable enough to cite....
