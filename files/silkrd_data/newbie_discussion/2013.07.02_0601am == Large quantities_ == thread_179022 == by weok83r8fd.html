Title: Re: Large quantities?
Post by: milliday on July 02, 2013, 07:32 am
I believe there are quite a few people who use the road to move bulk amounts. I think the most important thing is to do the proper research about any vendor you want to order from, and develop a rapport with the ones that you can determine to be legit. Buy small amounts or samples to test, and to help make judgement about the overall quality of the vendor before putting a lot of money at stake. There's also a certain amount of concern you might want to give to your receiving address if you want to place bulk orders on a regular basis.

It seems that a large amount of the community on SR is there for personal use and such, so to answer your question, the road caters to both.

Also as it seems it can't be said enough, never finalize early.
