Title: Re: Someone willing to go step by step on how to use SR?
Post by: proxanne on June 14, 2013, 02:02 am
I think everyone does things a little different.

I'll start.

PGP needs to be used to encrypt sensitive information (name, address) when placing orders.  I wont explain how to use PGP here because its been covered numerously on the forums.
To obtain bitcoins, I would use bitinstant. You can send a moneygram in exchange for bitcoins.  I'm pretty sure you can use fake info on bitinstant.  They have some form of ID check but I think its there to make sure you aren't a terrorist or some shit.  Pay a moneygram at walmart if you have one in your area.  They do not ID.  From there, the bitcoins will deposit where ever it is you selected them to from the bitinstant website.  You can have them sent to an email (use tormail for this,) or directly to an address.  You can create a bitcoin address at a site called bitcoin fog.  You deposit coins there and, for a small fee, they randomize your coins with their entire bitcoin flow (making your BTC untraceable).  From there you can deposit them into your SR btc address where you can spend them as you wish. 

You can run TOR from a flash drive.  Same with a PGP program.  (search pgp4usb)  There is potential security benefits in doing this.
