Sometimes, yes.  Those with the simplistic answer, "karma will get them", "stay still, take no action" etc., imo don't really understand karma.   Do they really think that they're accumulating "good karma" by just watching more wrongs be inflicted?  e.g. Just stands there while a mass murderer rapes and kills babies, when they have the ability to stop such wrongs.  I don't see any universal force/god etc. taking the side of the coward, and rewarding him/her for not stopping a wrong when they could have. 

"According to the Vedas, if one sows goodness, one will reap goodness; if one sows evil, one will reap evil"    Karma literally means "action"...  so a lack of action does not always equate to something good.  Lack of action can be wrong and evil.  I mean if your values dictate that murder is "good", then in that case, just sitting there watching the murdering babies might be wrong as well... because you didn't step in and kill too.  I'm just trying to convey the point that inaction doesn't always equate to "good"... or "evil". 

If a pedophile rapist murderer is going door to door in you neighborhood... you weren't able to stop him before your family was victim of this guy, but now you're able to stop him?... I'd say stop him.    For the betterment of the world, the society in general, you're doing an altruistic service.   Like what is justice supposed to be?... that's what it's supposed to be, right?  For the betterment of society, people that don't live by the accepted role of conduct, are accordingly punished. 

Kinda OT: Now Lady Justice/Justitia is blind... she didn't have the blindfold until the 15th century or something ...  people seem to think that blindfold means "objectivity".  Well, then why didn't she originally have the blindfold on to begin with?  Why is she mostly depicted these days having the scales of truth and fairness tilted?  If she's objective, wouldn't the scales be equal?... like the gods/previous personifications before her.   How is she to see if that things are weighted properly if she's blindfolded?
