Clearnet = non-Onion based addresses. You access .Onion addresses through TOR. You may not want to access a clearnet site through TOR as your TOR given IP address may be recorded by the site. A link can possibly be established between your Tor activities and your clearnet activities if you access both from TOR.

Best practice is to access clearnet using a standard browser. Or using a standard or Tor browser, using the search engine "start page" and using their proxy to access the site in question.
