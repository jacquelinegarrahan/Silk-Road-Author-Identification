Title: my noob experience getting scag on sr
Post by: fatfrak on March 25, 2013, 11:17 pm
i decided to try silk road.  been off and on with junk for about 8 years.  on mmt for the last 5 or so.  on a very low dose, so i still like to treat myself now and again, but am not in the game enough to have good hookups, so s.r. seemed like a godsend.  spent a little time researching it and figuring out how everything works.  looked around the forums to see what i could find out about the different vendors.  i've gotten 3 orders so far.  got smallish orders from fast forward and indubitably while i was waiting to get my long awaited order from smiles a while.  both of the domestic orders of #4 that i got were pretty weak.  very weak if we're being honest.  i've regularily gotten better dope off of the street where i live and i live in the middle of nowhere at all.  the order that i got from saw was pretty great.  took about a week to get instead of the 3-4 days for domestic orders, but well worth the wait. 

what i learned from all of this is, do your homework and go with what folks are saying.  if everybody says there are only one or two decent vendors, then go with one of the good ones even if it costs a little more or takes a little longer.  ends up being well worth it.
