Title: Re: Undeclared Country for vendor ?
Post by: tree on June 20, 2013, 09:14 pm
It's usually because the seller ships from a not so popular area and feels that disclosing could deanonymize him. They usually tell you if they're in the EU but don't want you to know exactly what country they ship from and things like that. If it's just completely undisclosed then it's most likely coming from a third world country somewhere in Asia, south America or Africa. I've had an order come through from a location like that and it turned out okay, except it took a real long time to come.
