Title: Re: bitinstant account
Post by: mcguire39 on June 18, 2013, 07:14 pm
Right there are other options depending on how anonymous you want to be. I'm just saying the fastest way I've seen is using say reloadit and then having the vendor credit you the BTC right on SR. No waiting for the six blockchain confirmations. Just make sure you pick a good vendor with positive recent reviews. And _read_ their instructions before buying anything; some have certain minimums, or certain ways you must purchase the card, etc. Generally you do _not_ register/activate the card yourself before giving them the # that would be bad. Yes the fees can be sort of high 10-20% but considering how anonymous and fast it is it is a great way to get started on SR. Blue Sky Traders is nice but I think they have a $400 minimum and you need to have a Bank of America branch close enough to you to do the deposit.
