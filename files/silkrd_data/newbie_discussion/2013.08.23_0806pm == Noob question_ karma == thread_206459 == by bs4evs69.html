Title: Re: Noob question: karma
Post by: incubusdriver on August 23, 2013, 08:17 pm
I haven't found it in any FAQ yet, but if you're used to using forums it should make sense.

In case you're not used to using forums, here's the basics:

1. Members with higher rank are allowed to dish out 'karma' points

2. In most forums, the 'karma' points a user can dish out within a set period of time will be limited to avoid hate-bombing or the opposite

3. When dishing out a 'karma' point to a user, the 'disher' can choose from + or -

4. The more + you get, the more people liked something you did and said "yeah, this is a good guy"

5. The more - you get, the more people hated something you did and said "woah hold on dicksplash"

6. + = good - = bad.

Capisce?
