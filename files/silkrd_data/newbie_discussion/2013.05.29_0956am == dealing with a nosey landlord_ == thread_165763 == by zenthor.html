Post by: BlackIris on May 29, 2013, 10:37 am
Don't you have a key to your room so that you can close it?

This is the simplest thing to do, however remember this: if you close your room up nosey people will just become even more interested in what's there. The best course of action is to simply let them do what they want to do and ignore them for some time. After a while if they don't find nothing of interest they will simply stop to do it. If you give a motivation for these sort of people to suspect about something they will literally turn your life into hell.

Simply play strategically. For example willingly go away from home with the door wide open leaving nothing to find. You know that they will go in and search around, and you do this appositely for them to do so. When you are home, in the same way, keep the door open so they will naturally watch inside. Naturally to do this until they are bored to look at you, you will have to be sure you are not doing anything of illegal or that you don't want them to know. If you want to do so then you will have to lock the door, but this will naturally make their curiosity go sky-high and will worsen the thing.

In any case rest assured that nosey people will never change. Or you go elsewhere or you will have to face this trouble every time you do something that re-builds their suspects. For many people doing these things is all they have in life. To really let them not bother you anymore you will have to do something to make them have fear of yourself, but this will naturally mean that you will have no more an home in a short time if you have not something to, sort of, "blackmail" them so that they cannot make you go away without repercussion (this can also be tied to the "fear method" you use, but beware of it not being an illegal one or in the case that it's not directly tied to yourself).

I lived once in a room with two very strict Jehovah Witnesses room-mates that gave me the same problem. It was very simple to handle them: I just let them find occult paraphernalia in my room with a "secret diary" (naturally created ad-hoc) of how many people I turned life hell ritually with the help of Satan. They had so much fear of me doing the same with them that they left me alone for the remaining of the time I was there. They had even too much fear of even sending me away for fear of a repercussion, nor they had anything legal to work against me (actually they tried going to the police with an "anonymous" snip that I was a "Satanist child murderer" but naturally nothing was farther from the truth and the thing just fired back to them).

So your choises are really only three: or you lock your room and you bear them for the remainder of your time in the house (or the similar strategic play of making them search until they are bored, but in this case you will have to always do so for all the duration of your residence there), or you leave (probably the easiest choice) to find better room-mates, or you find something with which they will be so much afraid of you that they will leave you alone without sending you away (this is the most difficult of the three but if you can do it, it is both very fun and a way to don't come to compromises with yourself; but, as I said, beware that the fear is not build up by illegal methods or if it is it's not tied directly to yourself, you must study something intelligent).
