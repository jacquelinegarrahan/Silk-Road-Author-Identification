Title: Re: FE
Post by: gn0ssos on September 29, 2013, 06:15 pm
It means Finalize Early. You should always, always, always avoid this. Finalizing your order is where you release the funds to the vendor from escrow when you receive your order in the mail and everything is as it should be. Finalizing early means you're sending them the money before you've got the product, and you never know for sure that the product is really on its way. If you don't finalize early, you will be able to resolve your issue with customer support if you don't get your order or something's amiss. If you do FE, it's all your fault and you're just fucked if the vendor doesn't send it or gets something wrong.
