Post by: j1m1th1ng on February 11, 2013, 08:32 pm
As a vendor here are some of the steps I take.  Never use my return address, but use a real one.  Every few days I pick a small biz from my area with a generic name.  Change them often.  Don't interact with postal employees if at all possible. If I am at the front counter every day someone is more apt to notice the ever changing addresses.  I live a block from our regional hub but I am in and out like a ninja if I must enter at all.  I buy all my postage from the auto teller, don't use a cc or dc in your name.  Make your package look good print right onto usps stickers, print directly on envelope when possible. Used windowed envelopes, blend in with bills.  Do your best to leave no prints and make sure the package is clean and smudge free.  Have all needed items on hand, don't have to purchase postage or fill out forms for anything your dropping off...that should have had been done at home.  As you grow and network find trusted people in other states.  Train them in your packaging method.  Have them ship now and then for you so you have your things coming from more then one location.  Drive across the state boarder if its close when mailing to your shippers.  Finally clean cut, look normal.  Most importantly in all things hide in plain site.  People give me rave reviews on my stealth.  Feel free to ask me anything else as I've been vending a bit still have that (100) but am just now getting around to the forums and need post count.
