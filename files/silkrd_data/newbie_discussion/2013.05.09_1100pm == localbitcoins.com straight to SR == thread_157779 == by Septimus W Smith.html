Title: Re: localbitcoins.com straight to SR
Post by: wl2ong1 on May 09, 2013, 11:13 pm
This is the only way I get BTC. I either get them from localbitcoins or CL. I only meet in person and pay cash for them. If the person you purchase your BTC from insists on following localbitcoins procedure, they will send the BTC do your localbitcoin wallet, if they already have the BTC in their wallet, it will be an instant transfer to your wallet and then you send to SR wallet. If they are willing, then you can give them your SR wallet and they can send straight to that. Just wait for the blockchain.info/address/"youraddress" show 1 confirmation. More than likely by the time you get home, the BTC will be in your SR wallet.

I find it to be anonymous... I've met with multiple people in different cities/states and they've all gone well. They range from 5-10%.. I shoot for 5-8%, which is better than you get anywhere else. Paying 835 for 800 is a good fee in my opinion.
