AfterHour:

I have tried his coke. No probs with ordering and FE requirement, received within 1 week, very good stealth.

I am not very experienced user, I've only tried diff street coke few times.

Very nice white color, scent not very intensive like ones I tried before.

UPDATE: after trying different suppliers, I must admit that this coke clogged my nose for the whole next day, which wasn't very pleasant. Friend said it had too much speedy effect.

Budworx UK

Got 3.5 g from Budworx UK, was a bit afraid with FE requirement, but took a risk.

Delivered within 1 week in EU.

The quality is very good, very smooth for nose, no clogging, which was happening with other vendors.

First evening got 0.7g divided between two ppl and had great 6 hrs, no big problems with falling asleep after that.

As above, no noticeable comedown you just want to do more ;)

Will be ordering again! So far, the best I have got from SR.

Update: my friend liked other coke more than this one, for me - still the best one.

Lloydsbrothers

Veeeery smooth and nice C! Got only half gram within less than 1 week! 2 ppl had amazing evening! Very nice white color, shiny, very intensive scent and really smooth for nose! Pretty nice numbing effect. No urge to redose, took some time to fall asleep, but anyway it was pleasant, had many many nice dreams after that! Friend of mine said that woke up with grind on a face and next day was just great! No noticeable calm down, definitely worth trying!
However, vendor could improve stealth a bit.

Will be definitely ordering again! Friend's favorite, mine - 2nd place

c63amg
I liked it very much, though it burns nose just a bit. I ordered only sample (got 0.2g) - received also very quickly - the best stealth here! Friend of mine also tried but after 1 line gave up because of burning (but tbh it's not the worst burning you can have). Also nice white color, but a bit different consistency than Bud and Lloyd's.
Had this sample for myself and I would put it just below Budworx (though still in favor to Budworx's).

Will be ordering again if I will lack of BTC ;)
