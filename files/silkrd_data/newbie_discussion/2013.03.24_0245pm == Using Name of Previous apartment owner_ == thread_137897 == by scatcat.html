Do you get ALL his mail? Some countries have a system, where the post automatically diverts your mail to your new address, even if the letter was labeled to your old address. This does not necessarily work perfectly, and sometimes letters etc. still goes to the wrong (old) address. If your country has this kind of system, your drug shipment might end up to that other person, to his new address.
