Title: Re: What service do you use to fund your SR account from $$$
Post by: YolkyPalky on June 27, 2013, 09:42 pm
I'm new to SR, and just this week used Blue Sky to fund Bitcoins for the first time.  I was caught in the middle of one of their hiccups that they talk about on their vendor page.  It turned out not to be a big deal, I got my coins about 24 hours later than I normally would have.  Still, it freaked me out a little bit since so many people said they got their coins from BST in a hour or so.  Nevertheless, they were very upfront with communication, and let me know right away when they had a problem with a replenishment partner and therefore my coins would be delayed.  I will be interested to know how their model is changing, and what "higher fees" will be charged.  I think I was charged between 3-4%, acceptable right now in the BTC market I suppose.

So going forward, I would use Blue Sky again depending on the changes to their model.  Or I might try LocalBitcoins.com, I found a guy locally who'll sell BTC for MtGox Last Price plus 2% on top, pretty good deal I think.  Downside, you have to arrange to meet the guy somewhere in a coffee shop (public wifi), and your anonymity is kind of lost in some respects.  Blue Sky was simple, BofA didn't ask any questions, just made the deposit and that was it.  Of course, more trust is required for Blue Sky since you are fronting money first to receive BTC later, but they told me that too many people actually know who they are and where they are for it ever to turn into some kind of "cash grab".

In Summary, I heartily recommend Blue Sky, although of course we'll have to see what their "new" model is on Monday.

Post by: f1nest on June 28, 2013, 07:31 pm
Now, disclaimer as I'm not 100% sure this is completely anonymous - but here is how I've gotten my BTC to the road for well over a year.
https://www.virwox.com/[1] - It is a Second Life (the game) currency exchange. They offer MANY (paypal, wire transfer, etc...) ways to deposit cash into what are called Linden Dollars. This is the currency used in second life. After converting to Linden Dollars you can INSTANTLY transfer to BTC. After that all it takes is withdrawing BTC to your Silk Road Wallet. Entire process takes about an hour - and then your coins are in your wallet.
Let me know if it isn't the most secure form of making BTC deposits... but it has worked excellently for me for well over a year.
