Title: Re: How much is too much?
Post by: Young Morpheus on June 19, 2013, 07:37 pm
(Someone please correct me if I'm wrong) Local PD wouldn't run a CD because it's a federal crime through a federal service. I very seriously doubt the federal government would waste their time with a half zip of weed.

If you are worried make sure you ask questions about stealth, and read the vendors feedback concerning stealth.

Never risk sending to anywhere that you do not 100% trust whoever OWNs the property. The idea is to do things to look out of the ordinary. A 1/2 isn't that big at all, people get QPs through the mail.

Think backwards, think from the source and think from the perspective of the post office. Would one big shipment be more or less strange than a machine gun rapid amount of little ones from potentially different sources?
