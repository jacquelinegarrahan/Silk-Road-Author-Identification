Welcome to the Road,  you need to send your BTC to your Silk Road Bitcoin address which is found in your SR account, so you need to paste in that address to your Block Chain account (where it says send to or pay Bitcoins to)  locate where it says that in your Block Chain account, then paste your Silk Road BTC address into that and hit the send tab.   ;)
To reiterate

1)Find your Block Chain acount, where it says  send bitcoins to or pay to
2) locate/find your Silk Road btc address
3) paste your SR address into your Block chain account to send your btc to SR
4) hit the send tab.
Title: Re: Transfering BitCoins to SRd to make purchases
Post by: hryder on March 26, 2013, 12:04 am
The 'code' you see when you click on 'click here to make a deposit' in your sr account settings is your sr bitcoin address. Send the coins from your wallet to this address, after a few hours the amount you sent will be credited to your sr account and you're ready to make purchases.

Never sent coins to anyone from external wallets! If a vendor demands this he's most likely a scammer because this way you're not protected by sr's escrow system.
