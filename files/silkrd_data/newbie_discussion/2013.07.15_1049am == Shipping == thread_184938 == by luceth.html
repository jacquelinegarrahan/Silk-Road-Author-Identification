Post by: Red Rover on July 15, 2013, 09:21 pm
A few POUNDS?  Whoa.  I don't know much about the vendor angle, but I can't see any reason to send pounds of drugs in the mail.  Anyone buying that much is obviously going into business for themselves, not purchasing for personal use.

Sending that much in the mail is really, really risky, and the penalties, if you're caught, will be severe.  Personally, I would never put myself at such risk, just to help someone else set up shop for themselves.

If they need a few pounds of weed or coke or whatever to start their business, let them look for local suppliers in their area.

Again, I don't know much about the vendor angle, but I would assume "bulk shipping" means a few ounces or so.  Not a few pounds.
