OK to sell you must buy a vendors account last i saw was 4.5BTC go to your account then on the right hand side there is the option to become a vendor read all rules

FE means finalize early as in you finalize the payment as soon as the vendor marks your package in transit as a new vendor you will not be allowed to ask for FE or they can close your account read vendor info here http://dkn255hz262ypmii.onion/wiki/index.php?title=Seller%27s_Guide
