Title: Re: Mushrooms or Acid?
Post by: lanman on May 21, 2013, 10:28 pm
They are different, each one has its effects.

I personally prefer mushrooms, as I find that they have more character and spirituality. There is a feeling that the mushroom entity is communicating its wisdom.

The LSD trip is more objective. Your mind accelerates and you can make mental connections and reason much faster.

But I do find that mushrooms tend to work better in quiet environments. I personally find it very difficult to enjoy a mushroom trip in a social setting, such as a club or concert. I prefer to take them in nature or at home by myself or with some close friends who are also taking mushrooms. Mushrooms also tend to make you want to be still and quiet, even lay down. On the other hand, I enjoy taking LSD in social setting, concert, or club. LSD makes me more energetic, while mushrooms tend to make me more introspective.

Regarding aftereffects, I find that both drugs are very clean, and produce almost no hangover. For mushrooms I prefer having them in tea to minimize any disturbances to the stomach.
