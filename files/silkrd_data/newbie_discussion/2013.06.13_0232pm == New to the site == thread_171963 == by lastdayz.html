Lots of good info above.  I think someone already mentioned localbitcoins ... so I just wanted to second that nomination and share my experience

localbitcoins.com is awesome and gives you lots of different options for buying bitcoins and once the deal is done you get the BTC immediately.

If you're not familiar with BTC beware that it can take hours to transfer - some exchanges let you "jump the queue" if you agree to give a tiny percentage in processing fees (like 0.01 BTC, etc.). 

Also, even some of the prominent exchanges like blockchain.info seem to have fairly frequent technical issues so it might be an idea to allow heaps of time for bitcoins to reach their destination.
