Post by: neuromancer on May 06, 2013, 06:41 am
MDMA is not without side effects and as someone else said, everyone is different and you might just be more sensitive to it. Pressure in the head and a down mood is pretty normal IME. MDMA fucks with blood pressure and I've personally gotten super light-headed when I stand up from a horizontal or seated position -- occasionally to the point where I've fainted a couple of times. Drink lots of water to offset the dehydration, which doesn't help the funny feeling in your head. And even though you're not really going to be hungry while rolling, avoid skipping meals like I did yesterday because the low blood-sugar next morning just makes everything worse. These side effects should be relatively short-lived...most go away within 24-48 hours for me. Usually the worst and longest-lasting side effects for me is sore jaw for a few days from all of the clenching (and sometimes I gnaw on my cheeks, so they get sore and irritated too).

5-HTP (you can get it from GNC) is said to help the following week, although I still feel pretty unmotivated and irritable for a few days even with it.

You'll be fine. Just lay off the molly for a few weeks. :)

Post by: nightlylightly on May 07, 2013, 10:29 pm
A lot of stuff has already been said but here are a couple of other things I used to do when the comedown was chronic.

1) try not to panic. This comes with a bit of experience but panic makes things worse. You are going to feel rotten for a few days then things will return to normal. What you have/are experiencing is not that uncommon.

2) replenish your body. after a long session your body will be depleted on nearly all levels. Salt, Sugar, vitamins etc. Don't overdo these either. You can get a lot of what you need from fruit, fat and protein. Make it a pleasure, have a nice, warm, chocolate milk before bed and some good fried food.

3) if you start spiraling out, take a few deep breaths and remind yourself it's just a comedown. Try to take your mind off it with something else (I've been bed ridden due to shit mdma with all manner of pain. Lounging in bed with back to back tv/movies and a constant stream of tea kind of helps. Also napping)

4) if the serious symptoms last more that a few days and/or are not bearable consider visiting the hospital. Unless you are in serious medical distress and just appear anxious they may take pity on you and give you a short course of benzos. I'm really not a doctor and this is just my experience but I have rarely dropped anything without knowing I've got access to diazepam/alprazolam. Short of going blind, deaf or immobile a little bit of mothers helper is just the ticket.

5) it's been said, but I will say it again with experience. Do not drop again too soon. leave it a month at least, probably more. I seriously hurt myself with overuse (a looooong time ago) and am now on SSRI's for life. It took years to regain some normalcy. Of course everyone is different but - Be warned and above all, be safe. Aint no high worth permanently frying your noodle for. I wish I had better access to information back then....
