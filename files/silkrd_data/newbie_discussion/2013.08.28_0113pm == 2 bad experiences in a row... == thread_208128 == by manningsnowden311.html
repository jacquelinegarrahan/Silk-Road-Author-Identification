Post by: Divo on August 28, 2013, 01:35 pm
You just never know, I placed an order with a newer vendor and waited 14 days, it still hadn't shown up.  I wrote the vendor and they couldn't locate the package via tracking, so just cancelled the order.  Well, wouldn't you know it, the package showed up the very next day, so it took 15 days from intransit to reach my location, and this was domestic.  The dates marked by USPS on the package confirmed when it had been shipped, and it indeed was in transit this long.  Sometimes the mail system is just a mystery and it will still show up.  I've no experience with resolution yet, but if it were me I certainly wouldn't let it auto-finalize.  I'd click resolution a day or two before, explain the situation and suggest a few days extension just to make sure it doesn't show up.

As an aside, and to plug one of my favorite vendors so far, check out TheDreamatorium for a future order.  Product is very good and it's always made it to my mailbox within 4-5 days.  Enjoy the road!
