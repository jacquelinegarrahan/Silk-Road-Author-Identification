Title: Re: A shipping scenario...which is worse?
Post by: zerik on January 19, 2013, 01:35 am
I wouldn't FE early. You are just asking for it. Especially since you have another option.

The escrow program is the only protection you have.

Let's assume he is a great seller and has no intention of ripping you off. There are any number of things that can happen ( besides theft  )that could prevent the seller from shipping after he has your coin. Life happens to people and you would be stuck.

On the other hand let's assume you are an honest buyer you find a buyer that won't require you to FE so you Ho with him/her.  You use the escrow system and life happens to you.

The seller will still get his coin and a positive rating auto.

Just make sure you promptly free the funds and leave meaningful feedback.

I understand the sellers irritation. If they ship well, fast and product is as described they are entitled to quick payment and positive feedback.

It only takes a minute.

Hope it all goes well for you.
