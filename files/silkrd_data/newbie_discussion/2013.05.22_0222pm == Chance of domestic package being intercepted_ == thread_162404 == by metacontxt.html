Well yeah if your package happens to be open someone looks in and it is obvious what it is then you may have trouble this is highly unlikely.  I've read reviews where a package actually was open, product fell out, PO worker put sealed it back up and sent it off.  I think it was BHO or something so maybe it wasn't a big deal.
