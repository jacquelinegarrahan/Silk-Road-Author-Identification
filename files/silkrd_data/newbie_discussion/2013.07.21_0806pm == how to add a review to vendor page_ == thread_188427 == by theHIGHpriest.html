Title: Re: how to add a review to vendor page?
Post by: abdomen on July 21, 2013, 08:50 pm
You get to leave a review when you finalize it. Don't FE until you get the bitcoins in your account.

That said, it usually takes a few hours for Bitcoins to arrive in a SR account because they have to be confirmed. Then they have to go through the tumbler, and so on, and so on.
