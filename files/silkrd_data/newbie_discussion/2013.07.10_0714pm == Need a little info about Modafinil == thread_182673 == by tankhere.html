I just fly on that stuff, speed-rappin, joking along.  Much better than Ritalin, not quite as good as Dexedrine.  The euphoria for me lasts 4 hours or so, but it takes 16 hours or more before I can sleep.  Mixes fine with alcohol, although you will be drunker than you think you are, so don't try to drive if you've been drinking, no matter how alert you feel ldo. 

Recreational dose for me is 200mg, usually with a booster four hours later.  It has effects at 50-100mg but I'm still a little crazy even at those low doses.  I'll take it to stay alert for tasks that require such a thing but it will definitely affect the way you are perceived by others.  You will come across at least hypomanic, and possibly a little aggressive at times.  Take this into account and make sure your heart is filled with love before you drop. 

Do NOT mix this with psychedelics, it will turn a beautiful mushroom trip into a sweaty, nauseous pain in the ass. 

I love this stuff.  It's in my top ten, easily.  Right up there with nicotine and caffeine.
