A lot of folks like localbitcoin. The problem with buying from vendors on here is it isn't covered by the resolution center, so when/if the bit coin vendor decides to run off with everybody's moneypaks or whatever, not much can be done about it.

If being absolutely anonymous isn't one of your chief concerns when buying bitcoins, coinbase has been reliable for me in the past. There is a 4 business day wait for your transaction to finalize to begin with but once you hit level 2 it's about an hour.

Like another poster said, plenty of research can be done to give you enough choices to choose a method right for you.
