First off, 25i-nbome and 2ci-nbome are the same thing.
25i is an analogue of 2c-i, which basically means 2c-i is like the parent and 25i the child, they are very similar with some differences, and act very similar.  25-i is active at VERY small doses (in the micrograms), with 1 milligram being considered a relatively high dose.  2c-i however, is active in the milligrams, with the average dose being about 25mg.  I actually just ordered some 25-i, it should be here tomorrow.  i haven't tried it yet, but I have tried 2c-i.  It was mildly similar to LSD, with much more of a body high and less of a mindfuck.  It definitely is a psychedelic, it's just a fully synthetic one instead of a semi-synthetic one like LSD.
