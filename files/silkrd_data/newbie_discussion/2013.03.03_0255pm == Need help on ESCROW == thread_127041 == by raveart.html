if i get you right then you're saying you have registered with some independent escrow service somewhere on clearnet?
if that's the case then you won't be needing that - "escrow" is just a term designating a third party, in our case silkroad, that is going to supervise your transaction, e.g. buying drugs for bitcoins
(meaning your bitcoins are under lock and key with SR until you receive your package -> after you receive, you are supposed to "finalize" your order, thereby releasing the funds from escrow into the vendors account - but you'll do all of that via silkroad)

the only service recommended apart from silkroad is one for the purpose of buying bitcoins, e.g. bitcoin-24 as mentioned by you, which you will then have to transfer into your silkroad wallet
