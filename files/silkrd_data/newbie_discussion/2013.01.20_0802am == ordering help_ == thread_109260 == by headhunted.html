Title: Re: ordering help?
Post by: scout on January 20, 2013, 08:08 am
If you're trying to encrypt a message, then you would just encrypt the text, copy it, and paste it in the message box if you're sending a message, or in the address field for your shipping address.  It's considerate to include your key in the encrypted message, or at least message it to the vendor in case they want to use it.
Title: Re: ordering help?
Post by: 1byday43 on January 20, 2013, 08:12 am
Yes, it is that simple.  In addition I follow a suggestion made by a vendor some time in the past.  I put my plain text public key  in the bottom of my plain text file containing my purchase and address info and then encrpyt that file.  Then paste the encrypted result in the address window.  Also don't forget to include the vendor's key in you encrypted text so that can decrypt and read you message.
