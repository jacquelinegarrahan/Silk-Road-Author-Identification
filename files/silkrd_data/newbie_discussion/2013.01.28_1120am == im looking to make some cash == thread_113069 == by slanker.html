Since I'm asking id also contribute a little with going a little indepth with barcoding

Things you will need
A brain
A computer
Printer
Ruler


First off. Id go to a store that is not a target store. This store is used for you to gather your information on the product.
A peace of cardboard that you can tuck between your fingers with measuring marks is recommended. This will make sure you cut your UPC in the right dimensions to cover the old ones. You only need the numbers off the UPC. the UPC generator will generate the UPC for you based off the numbers you enter.

When targeting a product.
Keep this in mind some high end products at Walmart have two UPCs on them. One is the standard one that is apart of the box but the other is one created by the store. The store 1 is not pure white. It is yellow and white. This sticker contains the product name that gets rang up at the register. So the name on that sticker is what the cashier will see when it is rang up.
You want to keep products with the same brand. You don't want to put a legos UPC on a computer's UPC. The cashier would see that you are buying legos when you in fact have a full blow computer.

UPC generators
http://www.barcoding.com/upc/
http://sourceforge.net/projects/zint/
Also google is a powerful source. They are not hidden just look around a little. Any upc on a box you can get generated yourself via software.

Paper
You can get shipping labels from warlmart and print on them.

Cashiers
Target younger cashiers. The older ones tend to take their job too far for being paid min wage. Just look around for a derp. Also keep in mind these people are fucking cashiers. They are derpy. Just find the derpiest one. I have seen people just fucking take paper and tape it onto spry paint cans and buy a 10 dollar can for .99



Your look
Don't go dressed like some sorta punk. If you are over 18 I would recommend some decent clothes. This is all for you to get past the cashier and if they call you out on it so you can be better off saying I don't know.


Know your state laws.

Keep in mind things you get via this method could be looked into. You should keep in mind stores hire private investigators to look into this because the police really don't give a fuck after you leave the store. These guys try to track you down. So avoid being seen on cam when placing the new barcodes. If the store does not have video proof of you putting the barcode on than police wont arrest you even if pulled into the back room since they don't have proof that you did it.

Selling on cragslist and ebay can be risky depending on how long you wait, and many factors.
