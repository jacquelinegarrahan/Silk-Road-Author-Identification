Title: Warbeast: A killer new vendor with a killer product
Post by: turtle134298 on August 23, 2013, 11:58 pm
This is my more thorough review of Warbeast after receiving from him today:

It is my pleasure to have "met" Warbeast and to finalize for him.  Let me just say I am astounded and blown away by what he offers.  His product is nothing like what else is offered on SR.  Where do I even start?  Even though I have never had grade A pure meth, this is the closest I've come and I believe it has little to no cut in it.  It is potent as hell.  I felt it on the first pipe hit.  The second hit I got high, real high.  The third, I just started getting wasted.  Course had to have a couple more cause it is just such a joy to smoke. Pleasant "no taste" and appears to leave no residue (not sure 2+ hours later I've had about 15 hits and the bowl is still going good . . .).  Also, about the potency . . . I've had other SR meth where I took 30+ hits back to back over the course of a half hour to hour and felt little.  With this, I can't take more than 5 good puffs without getting overwhelmed and "crazy".  Also, started sweatin on just my second puff.  As for snortin', did a 1" x 1/8" line and it burned quite a bit.  Unfortunately I couldn't judge the effect of the line cause I was just too high from smoking.  And let me just say, this shit is clean.  I didn't know meth could be like this.  No sleepiness, no confusion, no headache, no lack of attention/focus, no lack of sex drive (this shit will make ya real horny!).  Oh, and how does the man weigh his bags?  Fat!!!  I ordered 2gs and got 2.5 on arrival.  0.5 was a little shake and lots of little crystals (real crystals, irregular, splinters, and slivers) and a full 2.0 was small shards (up to 0.6 the largest was).  Shipping, man Warbeast is quick.  Within 45min. of my order at 11pm, he had it in transit.  Late tuesday night in transit, had it Friday afternoon for the weekend!  How's that for perfect?!!!  The package was plain without a lot of stealth but it appeared quite passable to me.  Also, no smell whatsoever, WB does take time and effort to ensure this and I do appreciate it.  Overall, and I hate to say this because of possible consequences, shit this quality at this price is a steal.  If Warbeast can keep the price down like this and deliver this quality of product over and over again, and in larger quantities as well, the market shall be his.  I encourage you all to sample what this fine vendor offers.  For me, I have found what I consider the Holy Grail for myself.  Thank you so much Beast, you've made my day and weekend and found a new loyal customer.  Take care Beast, until next time.  Take care everyone else and may you find what you are looking for.

Turtle
