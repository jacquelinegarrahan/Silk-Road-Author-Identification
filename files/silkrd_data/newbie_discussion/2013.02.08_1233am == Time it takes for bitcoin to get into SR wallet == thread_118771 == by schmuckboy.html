It can take an hour or two. You must have at least 6 confirmations on your transaction by the bitcoin network before it will appear in your account.

You can check this by putting the address into blockchain.info and then looking at the associated transaction. After you have 6 confirmations it should show up in about 15 minutes or so.

Title: Re: Time it takes for bitcoin to get into SR wallet
Post by: wonko on February 08, 2013, 10:36 am
A simple guide on how to check your transfer status.

* In Tor Browser go to blockchain.info
* Put the address you have sent the transfer to in the search box
* You should then have a page called 'Bitcoin Address'
* On that page will be a list of transactions, normally you should only see one (assuming you create a new SR bitcoin address for each transaction, which you should do...). With a green arrow indicating funds were sent TO that address.
* Click the transaction id, the really long string of characters. You will then see one of these things:
** Estimated confirmation time - This means no-one has yet confirmed the transaction on the network, the estimate is how long it expects for the transaction to receive the first confirmation.
** Confirmations - The number of times the transaction has been confirmed on the network. Your SR wallet will update soon after you have received 6 confirmations
