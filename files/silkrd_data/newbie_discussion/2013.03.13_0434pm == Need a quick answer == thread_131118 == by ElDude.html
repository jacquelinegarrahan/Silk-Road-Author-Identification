Title: Re: Need a quick answer
Post by: rustbucket41 on March 13, 2013, 07:34 pm
The vendors that carry the products I want (at the prices I want) with the reputation I want all require FE for new buyers. I'm making small purchases from each of them to build a report offering FE each time. I figure that it isn't worth their time/effort to try and scam such a small fish when they have 10s of transactions each day for values far higher than my one transaction. I'd honestly rather FE for a reputable vendor on a small (~1 BTC) order than trust a new vendor with that same BTC.

One thing to note, if you play to FE, you should inform the vendor while ordering so that the vendor knows to expect an FE and won't cancel your order due to insufficient buyer stats.
