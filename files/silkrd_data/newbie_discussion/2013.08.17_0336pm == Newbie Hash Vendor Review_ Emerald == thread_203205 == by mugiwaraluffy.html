Title: Newbie Hash Vendor Review: Emerald
Post by: mugiwaraluffy on August 17, 2013, 03:36 pm
I ordered 50g of his Ali Baba hash a few days just before Tormail went down.

I had messaged him a few times before buying from him to see how well he responded etc. He ws very responsive and answered all of my questions promptly.

I was supposed to receive my package in 3-6 days according to Emerald's profile. It took two (2) weeks :-\

Not sure why but USPS took forever in delivering this order and at the time Emerald took a small hiatus according to his profile. I tried messaging him after waiting 8 days, but he did not respond after 3 days. To his credit, he did post on his profile that he would be slow to respond to messages during his hiatus. I tried him again 4 days after i sent the first message and this time he responded the next day with my tracking information. At least then my worries eased as I could easily see he had indeed shipped my package and the problem was not on his end.

I received it 14 days after Emerald sent it thanks to USPS.

The packaging was indeed stealthy. no smell leaking from it. The product is good and the price is great. No complaints whatsoever about Emerald. I recommend him for good decent hash for a great price.

All I can say is use tracking when ordering and if you don't receive it during his estimated shipping time, just send him a message and he'll give you the tracking info. I'd advise giving him your pgp key so he can encrypt it to you if you feel so inclined. I totally forgot when I asked for the tracking info. Otherwise, it was a smooth though lengthy transaction.
