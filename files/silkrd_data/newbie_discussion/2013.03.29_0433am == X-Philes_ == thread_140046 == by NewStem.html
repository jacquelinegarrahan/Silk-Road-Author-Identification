Favourite episodes (if even possible to choose):

War of the Coprophages (S3E12)
-A New England town is ravaged by death, and cockroaches are at every scene. This episode is made by its hilarious self-awareness: a look at the supernatural in its most plausible form, borne of mass hysteria. Also, Scully gets a little sassy and I'm into that.

Blood (S2E3)
-A Pennsylvania town is ravaged by death, and broken electronics are at every scene. A psychological thriller of an episode, no necessity of the paranormal. Especially relevant to the American gun climate of today. Makes suggestions at the latent violence of a culture that demotes self assertion to taboo.

Humbug (S2E20)
-A Florida town is ravaged by death, and evidence of an amphibious "mermaid" is at every scene. The episode that pays clearest homage to its roots in the Twilight Zone, Mulder and Scully enter and exit a freak show town to observe a tragedy with zero impact as causal agents. Probably the weirdest episode yet, maaaybe at par only with Jose Chung's From Outer Space (S3E20).

And so many more, oh lord!

Anyways, yeah. Netflix. Everyone get on it.
