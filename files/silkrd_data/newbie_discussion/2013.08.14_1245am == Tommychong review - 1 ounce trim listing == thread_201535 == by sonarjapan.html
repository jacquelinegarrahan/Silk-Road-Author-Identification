Title: Tommychong review - 1 ounce trim listing
Post by: sonarjapan on August 14, 2013, 12:45 am
Overall, I am very satisfied with my order! This was my first purchase of marijuana on SR, so I was a little nervous to order something so voluminous and detectable (have previously dealt mostly with LSD paper and small quantities of psych powders).

Shipping: Placed order on Tuesday around 5pm EST, made it from California to New York by Saturday. Pretty damn fast! Package was small and very discreet. Addresses printed on labels. I'm pretty sure he spelled my name wrong, which is kind of a shame. I need to check my original message to see if I did that by accident, but I don't think I did (I've had years of practice spelling my own name, have it pretty down pat ;) ). Could have caused a problem, but I still received my package without issue.

Customer service: Excellent! Fast and friendly communication. Very generous, too. I told him I'd take some pics of his product, so he threw in a 1.6g nice bit of his Medical Purple Urkle for me. That was a special treat. Bud had nice purple tinges and that nice pine-y purple smell. Smoked very nicely and truly felt medical! I was cured!

The 1 ounce of shake is nice, too. I was a little disappointed since I had heard some others who received say that it was "more like commercial bud than trim". I'm not sure what they meant by "commercial bud", but it was pretty leafy in my opinion. There was definitely a solid about of bud fragments interspersed--I'd guess maybe a third of it was straight bud, and I'd compare the fragments that were there to commercial bud. Not an overwhelming amount of stems. I was honestly a little disappointed with that because I've been collecting stems to make infused vodka, but it's good to have more to smoke!

For my purposes, the shake works pretty well. I chopped it all up to distribute the bud bits amongst the leaves and make a homogenized smoking mix. It smells and feels very '70s grass. It tastes a little off because of the leaves, but it gets me pretty high! The high itself is pretty interesting. I really think I feel the mixed effects of different cannibinoid ratios in the various strains that went into this stuff...or maybe I'm just high :)

Would order again! Good, cheap smoke for the non-connoisseur on a budget. Check out some pics below **CLEARNET WARNING**:
