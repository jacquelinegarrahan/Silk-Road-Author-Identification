Post by: spunjtom on July 08, 2013, 05:55 pm
Quote from: jlranier on July 08, 2013, 05:49 pm
Maybe I'm completely fucking everything up but whenever I want to try to remove data from pictures I just pull the pic up on my computer and screenshot it, crop it and then save that.

That technique works fine.  Another way to do it is open the image in a program that allows you to export is as a .PNG.  Unlike JPG, PNG has no capability to store exif data.  Just be very sure when you upload your pic that you choose the .PNG version instead of the .JPG.  To be safe I just delete the .JPG images from the folder where they are stored, leaving only the .PNG's.
