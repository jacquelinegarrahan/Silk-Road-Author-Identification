The problem is, you're trying to use the service as a guest. It didn't allow me to do that yesterday. You have to sign up and then have your identity verified. If you're using a fake name, they will generate questions based on the real person with that name. I was locked out for a week from one account by trying to guess the information of a fake person and somehow managed to guess the three questions correctly for another fake name...then it worked flawlessly. My advice is to try bitinstant again with a registered account. It will/should work.
