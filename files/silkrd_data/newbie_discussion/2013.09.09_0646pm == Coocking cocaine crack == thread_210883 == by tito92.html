Title: Re: Coocking cocaine crack
Post by: Davey Jones on September 13, 2013, 11:03 pm
Depending on how much coke your working with.  Good quality coke rocks up fairly quick and turns hard.  The cut coke doesn't rock up fast and doesn't turn hard.  Baking soda and water and coke.  You'll need a heat source, the coke placed in a glass tube, then add baking soda(I'd say an eight ball of coke just put a half gram baking soda)then cover with water and heat till it bubbles while moving the glass tube around.  It should rock up fairly quick, the mixture should be close to clear and as you move the tube around you'll see the hardened product.  Then dump it out and dry off rock or let dry, then sample.  Good coke will be an ear ringer.
