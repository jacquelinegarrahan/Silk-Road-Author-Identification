Title: Re: Need advice! Vendor agreed to reship but autofinalize is approaching?
Post by: wellwellnow on September 08, 2013, 12:14 am
Autofinalizing is bad because it makes it seem like you don't come back on to report that you got your shipment.
Finalizing early puts you at risk of not having the SR escrow service at your back if the drugs fail to show up multiple times.

What you can do is open up a ticket with the resolution center, and suggest a 7-10 day extension for the vendor to ship!
This solution is desireable for everyone, unless the vendor has asked you explicitly to FE (I still wouldn't do it)
