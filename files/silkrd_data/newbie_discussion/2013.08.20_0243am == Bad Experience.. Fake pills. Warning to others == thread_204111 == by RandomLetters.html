are they easy to use too? and how many uses can you get? I'd like to know some more information on these they sound useful.

Yes, it's incredibly easy:) The kit comes with the bottle of acidic solution, a small vial and a pretty useless information sheet.

Basically you take a tiny little scraping off of your pill of choice, put it in the vial, add one drop of the solution and in about 20 seconds it will change to a color. It tests a multitude of drugs and the colors range through the whole spectrum so there should be no confusion. For instance, if you have oxy and you test it, the test should turn a pale violet for the 'Marquis Test'.

Just keep in mind there are a couple different kinds of tests. I recommend the Marquis like I was saying above and since the information you get sent isn't great, look up the color chart on google and there will be a list of all the drugs available to test and what colors they should be when tested.

Hopefully that made sense...
