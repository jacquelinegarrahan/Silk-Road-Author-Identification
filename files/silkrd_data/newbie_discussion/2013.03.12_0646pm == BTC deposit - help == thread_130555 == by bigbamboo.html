hi. i just deposited my first BTCs yesterday. it took about 1.5 hrs after i sent them to be verified in my wallet, but i think this also depends on how much you pay for your transaction fee and the size of transaction (i did 0.001 fee and about 2.5 BTC transfer).

after that, i didn't check my account until 4.5 hrs later, which is when i saw it in my SR account; so probably less time than this.

you can check where your BTCs are after you send them at blockchain.info by typing in the address you sent it to. don't worry if you see them being sent from that address to other ones. this is what SR does; it tumbles your coins for anonymity.

hope this helps 8)
