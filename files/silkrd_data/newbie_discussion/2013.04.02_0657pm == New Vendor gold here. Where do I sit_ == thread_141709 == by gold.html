A few things to consider:

Coins and bars will always be higher than spot price since there is purifying, coining, and distribution costs involved. On the flip side, if you resell them you will always get paid above spot price.

If you compare my prices to coin dealers, you should see I'm on par. Plus they usually require shipping insurance and restricted delivery which drive your shipping costs to like $50 or more.

It may seem high for the smaller coins, but that's the commission taking a bite at a higher rate for smaller purchases. Plus, smaller coins are always higher per spot price.
