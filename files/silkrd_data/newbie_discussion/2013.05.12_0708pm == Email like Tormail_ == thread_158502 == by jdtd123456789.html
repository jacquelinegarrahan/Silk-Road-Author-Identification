Title: Re: Email like Tormail?
Post by: Libertas on May 12, 2013, 07:38 pm
Tormail is not safe by default, nor is Countermail nor any other mail service that you may wish to use. Do not get lulled into a false sense of security when communicating via these methods. ALWAYS use PGP to encrypt your messages, and only access your email provider through Tor if using them for business that is in any way illegal.

Libertas
Title: Re: Email like Tormail?
Post by: z3n on May 12, 2013, 08:09 pm
Something important to keep in mind imo is never trust your email provider, either with your IP address or with any information that might lead to identify you...

Always encrypt your messages, write/check it at various times, ask others to send you encrypted messages...

tormail might pretty much be a honeypot, as well as any other 'security conscious' email provider. And even if it wasn't, sometimes an operator will have no choice but to give the information s/he has about you... or the server can be compromised...

Encrypt everything, and always use tor.
