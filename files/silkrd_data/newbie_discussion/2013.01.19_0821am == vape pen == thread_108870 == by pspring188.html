I have a MFLB, it was the first vape I tried after being a daily smoker for awhile (preference were bongs), it does the job and also is very good stealth wise, very portable. But after about a month it began to satisfy me less and less and I found every so often when I'd smoke a bong I'd feel like I was missing out and kinda wasting my weed. But for health reasons I really wanted to like vaping so I took the plunge and bought a desktop vape: The Da Buddha, hoping that it would give me those strong hits when I wanted them, happy to say it definitely does and I hardly ever smoke anymore.

I still have the MFLB, I keep it for when I want a portable solution, don't get me wrong it does the job if you are an occasional herb user but for a regular user I found it's only good for so long and not to be used as a primary device. I read all the reviews before I purchased it and a lot of people seem to say that it gets the job done as well as a desktop vape (or a higher end portable vape? not sure haven't tried any others) but it really doesnt, you can get clouds out of it but definitely not as big clouds as a good desktop vape.
