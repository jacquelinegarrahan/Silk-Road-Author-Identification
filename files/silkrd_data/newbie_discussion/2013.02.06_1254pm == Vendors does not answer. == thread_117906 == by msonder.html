Title: Re: Vendors does not answer.
Post by: AfternoonDelight on February 06, 2013, 01:57 pm
Vendors are probably busy with orders.
The interface is pretty slow, and you want to make absolutely sure you have the customers info correct and the package is sealed well before you even think about visiting a mailbox. Then you have to compare orders to messages, just in case a customer has a change to the order.

PGP is great, but some vendors don't like having to use it for every communication... I don't mind myself, but I'm just a small fry, so it's not a big deal to decrypt a couple messages.
PGP your address, and re-ask the question from the folks who've already read your note.  As long as you don't put any personal info in the message, there should be no problem.
If they give you a hard time for the second message, then they probably aren't worth buying from.
