Title: Re: Noob Mylar Questions
Post by: PowerToCharm on August 11, 2013, 02:51 pm
My understanding is that the MBB's can be sealed with any heat source, such as a hair-straightener or iron. Of course that wouldn't give you any vacuum action, but vacuum sealing doesn't really increase stealth, other than to make the product more compact. We bought an impulse sealer for about forty bucks from a seller on eBay. They have all kinds of applications in light industry and household use, so buying one is not all that suspicious.
