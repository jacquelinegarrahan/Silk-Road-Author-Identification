Post by: Generation Y on February 27, 2013, 07:16 am
First off, dont sell drugs on the street. Its stupid, and its how people go to jail. Start off drug dealing by getting to know some people who are into the scene, and then get one of them to start selling for you. So long as they aren't a junkie you can start out by giving it to them on tick, which allows you to earn good money for very little work. Then build up, get another dealer or two, start selling it in bulk. NEVER sell drugs on the street yourself.
