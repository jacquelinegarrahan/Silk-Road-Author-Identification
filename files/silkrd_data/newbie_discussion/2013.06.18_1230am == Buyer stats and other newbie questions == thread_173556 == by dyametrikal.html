Title: Re: Buyer stats and other newbie questions
Post by: ritzlover on June 18, 2013, 12:46 am
Click on your account icon at the top of the page. On your account page there will be a list of links on the right hand side. That is where your purchasing stats are.

The vendors can see your auto-finalize rate, how many transactions you have made, I believe how many bitcoins you have spent on SR, and your refund rate.

I put my Bitcoins in a separate wallet before I transfer them to my SR wallet. Read the security forums and follow the experienced members' advice.
