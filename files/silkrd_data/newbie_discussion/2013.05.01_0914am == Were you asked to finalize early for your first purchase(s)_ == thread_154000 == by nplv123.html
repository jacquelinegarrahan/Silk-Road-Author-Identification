Title: Re: Were you asked to finalize early for your first purchase(s)?
Post by: TheIndigoChild on May 01, 2013, 10:26 am
Exactly what Hryder said.

 But to give you more of an image of what to expect in the wild here's a guide on what you'll see.

ALWAYS FE (normally a scammer especially if theyre a new account

FE to Certain zones

FE for people with under a certain amount of money spent

FE to people who have a certain amount of transactions OR money spent


My tip to you is to stick to vendors who dont require you to FE, and also to make small purchases first.
You can buy E-Books for 20-90 cents each to build up your transaction history but your money spent will still be low. This will assist you in not having to FE almost ever after your first order, unless you're trying to spend a few 1000$ your second transaction some sellers may ask you to gain more experience as people with low stats can easily be scammers and scammers do start to get expensive over time for vendors.
