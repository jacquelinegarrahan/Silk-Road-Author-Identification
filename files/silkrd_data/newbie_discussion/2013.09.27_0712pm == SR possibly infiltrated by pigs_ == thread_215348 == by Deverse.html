Title: Re: SR possibly infiltrated by pigs?
Post by: IForgotMyFuckingPassword on September 27, 2013, 07:26 pm
If you're asking if there are LE here posing as buyers (and sometimes vendors), then the answer is they've been here forever. There have been two Gawker articles (at least) about this site and several in Forbes as well (including an interview with DPR). With that much mainstream press coverage, of course there's LE here. To say they've "infiltrated" the site is a bit of exaggeration though.

There's no verification process, so anyone with an internet connection and torbrowser can create an account.

That said, if you're buying personal quantities, LE is not interested in wasting resources on busting you. Remember this the FBI/DEA's jurisdiction, not your local/state police. They don't have the resources to apprehend and prosecute small-time buyers.

Now if you're buying wholesale quantities to resell, then you might need to worry. Their primary target is the vendors though.
