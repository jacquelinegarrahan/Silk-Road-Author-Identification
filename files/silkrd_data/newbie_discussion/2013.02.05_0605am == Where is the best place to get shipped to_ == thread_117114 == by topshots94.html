Title: Re: Where is the best place to get shipped to?
Post by: android465764E on February 05, 2013, 05:03 pm
Most people just ship it to their own homes, but with a false name.
For added security, after the package arrives you can write "return to sender" on the from and leave it in the hall for a few days. So if you do get a visit, however unlikely, you have a plan in place.

Tbh though, small amounts sent domestically are fine to your own address.
If you're order from international sellers, or you're ordering large amounts, it might be an idea to set up a 'drop' - friend's house, other property, PO Box etc..  just depends how far you wanna take it.
