Title: A few tips to help be as safe as possible (also, a warning about media)
Post by: kdizzle12 on August 21, 2013, 09:49 pm
Recently, news/media haven't been able to keep their mouth shut about SR or it's related marketplaces, which doesn't surprise me too much, but lets get to the real issue here.

Many people decide that they should brag about how they just bought a couple mollies or a few hits of acid from the road, so they blindly post a picture and tag accordingly, just search the silkroad tag on tumblr and you'll see what I mean.

These shitty writers are starting to learn how to use the internet, and are including these tagged photos in their posts, many of which show methods of stealth some vendors use, blotter art, etc.

This is very, very bad. SR is a beautiful thing, but it is mostly populated by people who can think for themselves, people who aren't dumb enough to believe that every package and letter ever sent through anything is throughly searched. In my opinion, this is a huge reason for its' success. It's a community of mostly intelligent people who know how to protect their own and their vendor's privacy.

When you show methods of shipping, you're completely exposing what the vendor worked hard to keep from getting exposed. When you show blotter art, it makes it very easy to pinpoint the vendor it came from (I recognized a couple on a NYT post if I recall correctly).

Seriously, you're buying drugs, not food, keep your shit to yourself and off of your instagrams.

On another note, a primary focus of the articles are how teens, you read that right, kids, are buying from the road.

Now, there's really no way to know if your customer is an adult or not, so vendors can't really help fight this.

If you're a kid, you likely live with your family in the same house. Ordering from SR puts everyone and everything inside said house at risk. Also remember that your parents or legal guardians are the ones who take the biggest fall if anything from LE comes into play. Please, don't be that guy. Just wait until you're an adult.

Anyways, here's some very easy tips to keep yourself safe.

1: Keep your mouth shut regarding anything to do with mail ordering drugs

This is a must. If anyone asks where you got that o of dank ass buds, say your boy hooked you up, or, you know, don't answer.

2: PGP

This is also a must. Encrypt messages to vendors, your address, and anything else sensitive. You wouldn't see so many people telling you to use it if it wasn't that important. It's very easy to do, I promise.

3: Never, ever order anything with a fake name

It used to work, but LE is getting smarter. Only order using your real name. Using a name that has never received mail at the address prior, or that is currently receiving mail at a different address is almost always a guaranteed intercept, love letter, or for the really unlucky, a controlled delivery.

4: Never disclose your vendor's methods of stealth to anyone!

This one is also important. It's called stealth for a reason, keep it that way.

5: Never finalize early (FE)

Every so often, SR gets a round of scammers. They're actually very easy to detect. 99% of the time, they require FE, and usually they have lots of repeated feedback/FE's in their feedback. The escrow system is there for your protection, USE IT.

6: Don't make it easy for someone to figure out who you are.

A compromised identity is one of those things you just won't want. It puts you at risk. If anyone finds out your identity, trash it and make a new one. Simple as that. Don't continue using a compromised handle.

7: Pictures

Seriously, this isn't food you're buying here, don't show it off to your instagram / tumblr / etc followers, as well as the public eye. Pictures usually contain a geotag, with exact coordinates to where the picture was taken. That's not good.

8: The Silk Road subreddit

I still to this day question why this exists. It is on clearnet, and it is *reddit*. It's safe to say you should avoid it.

9: Multiple packages

It's a very bad idea to have multiple packages coming in the same period of time. Space things out.

10: Using anything but tor to access SR

Do not use services like onion.to to access SR, it defeats the whole purpose of tor and exposes everything to your ISP, which means LE can see it too.

11: Tails

Tails is a beautiful thing, and all you need to run it is a flash drive. It's an entire operating system based on privacy. It routes all connections via Tor, erases everything once you're done, and even has a Windows XP mode if you don't want people to think you're using it. Using Tails is an easy way to help keep you as hidden as possible.


I've likely missed a few things here, but following these steps should only help to keep you safer.

Happy tripping / rolling / blazing / etc! :)
