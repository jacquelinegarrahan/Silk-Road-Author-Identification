hello bbcooltwo and congrats on jumping in and making your first purchase
thanks for your input and i agree with you, people scamming vendors and vendors scamming buyers is horse shit. it ruins it for everyone else who just want to buy and sell professionally. following SR guidelines helps ensure safety for buyers and sellers as much as possible. if you are a new buyer and really want to purchase off a specific vendor who requires FE, ask if you can stay in escrow but pay for tracked shipping. the buyer will have proof that the package was delivered incase of a dispute and doesn't have to worry about loosing him money and you will have security knowing the package was shipped. the one exception is if your ordering international, sometimes tracking requires a signature and i personally would never sign for anything but it works great for domestic orders with vendors who are weary about your stats.
