Title: Re: UK ROAD USERS HELP
Post by: Meerkovo on May 12, 2013, 06:21 pm
Hello fellas, I will speak here based on experience and by way of educated guess.

Customs - are and are not police in other way. They only give a damn of whats comes in the country and what leaves the country. However when they intercept prohibited items must I say, they in co-op with Royal Mail would inspect and remove the illegal goods and send you the form. However if its something worth their while, they in co-op with the police would probably do an investigation, or better yet observation as they call it. They will know how to deal with the situation as they see fit.

I would say currently in the UK we benefit slightly from the lack of co-op between government agencies.

Royal Mail - as someone said deals with more letters and parcels then you can probably imagine, I mean we still do or did up to a certain point have some of the biggest sorting offices in EU. Domestic mail, unless really suspicious looking, almost never fails and reaches destination.

Now for the educated guess, my honest opinion is your address is probably burned from overseas mail however for domestic your still good.

As for another delivery address, my best advice is to find a friend or some sort where you would have access to a house where multiple occupants live. Example, 4 bed house, where people individually rent rooms only. In these instance, the tenants change more quickly then you can count and its a right mess in most cases, but for you this mess is probably the best thing that can happen, as the mail can be in any name and is probably left by the main door and anyone who's not too nosy on touching other peoples mail, I would say you package would be pretty safe.

Hope this helps.

Meerkovo
