Post by: Stridulate on May 15, 2013, 08:50 am
I kinda do this the other way around.....

I keep a diary (in a code) of what I got high on at the last party/festival/nite etc. and try and do something different at the next one!

I tend to agree tho that you play it by ear...drugs dont submit well to plotting (i usually lose the plot!) but look at your consumption and keep it varied.

Being all geeky about it you seem to have chosen drugs which affect different neurotransmitters/receptors on diferent days, a good plan but hard to do as so many drugs are fairly non selective, especially at high doses. ketamine for example mainly affects the NMDA receptors but also produces effects dopamine and serotonin neurons, especially at higher doses.

I would always leave some time between MDMA rolls (did 3 days of a festival on MDMA pretty constant, 1.5g total, this year and was fine....except I spent the tuesday crying!!) its also pretty inadvisable to do too many combinations as things can get unpredictable...especially salvia.

probably the one drug to avoid mixing the others with is alcohol as it puts a huge strain on your body...i usually drink water etc when im high and drink alcohol on its own.

enjoy ;)
