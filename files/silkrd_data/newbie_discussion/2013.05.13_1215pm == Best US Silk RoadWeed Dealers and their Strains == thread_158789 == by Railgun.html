I have used a few vendors, and I can offer what I know based on those transactions.  As far as price and quality, MotherOf2 was selling cherry pie that blew my mind.  It was perfect looking, tasting, and beat the hell out of most anything else I've ever tried... though I will say I prefer other strains during the day.  I have also worked with Caliconnect for Purple Trainwreck, which was lower in price but not as tasty.  It did look and smell great though, and I would never say I felt ripped off by any means.  Trader Joe's also had God's Gift, which totally lived up to its name.  Of these 3 vendors, I would work with all 3 again.  I will also update you on PureOganx when the time comes.

For those vendors I do not trust, I will say 420Connections.  I ordered from them and received a bunch of crap.  I had already finalized, so I was stuck with garbage.  After confronting the vendor, he/she said they would offer a discount on the next order.  How in the hell am I supposed to know if the next order will be any better?  I'm not throwing my money away... Trust is earned, not given.  It just seems like this vendor is much more interested in volume and money than he is in working with his customers.
