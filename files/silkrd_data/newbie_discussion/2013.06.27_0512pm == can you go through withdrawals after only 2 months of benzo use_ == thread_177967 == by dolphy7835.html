Title: Re: can you go through withdrawals after only 2 months of benzo use?
Post by: Tessellated on June 27, 2013, 05:16 pm
You can certainly get withdrawl from that much over that time.

Do not go cold turkey on benzos, it can cause seizures. There is some good advice on erowid about weaning off. Valium is best for weaning off xanax because it has a cross tolerance, a long half-life and is more easily divided into small doses.

A good rule of thumb is always go at least 3 days in a row without it each week if you want to avoid dependance. If you manage your intake then you should not need more than 2mg, needing more is a sign of tolerance.
