We welcome NEW and/or LOW PURCHASING STAT BUYERS to order from us with the protection of ESCROW. The way the Silk Road experience is meant to be.

Our ESCROW policy is simple:

◉ For New or Low Purchasing Stat BUYERs, (Buyers with purchasing stats of <=5 total purchases and/or <$300 spent on SR)

◾ If your order total is $500 or less (before shipping/handling costs):
your order will be held in escrow and you will NOT be asked to FE (finalize early, finalize before shipping).

This applies per order. So if you place 2 orders of $500 or less (same day), they will both be held in escrow and will NOT require FE.

◾ If your order total is GREATER than $500 (before shipping/handling costs):
you *MAY* be asked to finalize before shipping. It all depends on your stats.

So, if you have a refund rate is relatively high and/or auto-finalize rate greater than 0%,
the more likely we *MAY* ask that your order be finalized before shipping if it's greater than $500.

◾NOTE: If you're a repeat buyer of ours with low purchasing stats, you will have NO limits and ALL your orders with us, no matter how much they are for, will be held in ESCROW.

◾ If you're not sure if you might have to finalize early, just ask. We're not so rigid about our policy. It's more of a guideline than a rule.

Or we can check your stats by ordering our FREE Buyer Purchasing Stat Check listing; http://silkroadvb5piz3r.onion/silkroad/item/baa3366e26
As soon as we check your stats, we'll cancel the order and your stats with remain unaffected by the cancellation. And we'll let you know if you can order and at what limit. You may find that we will allow you to order >$500 and remain in escrow.

Remember, a vendor cannot see your stats until you place an order.

◾ If we discover that you happen to be on SR Vendor's Private Bad Buyer Blacklist, we reserve the right to exercise one of the following actions:
- Limit your transaction to $200 or less in order to be protected by escrow.
- Ask that you finalize early (depending on why your buyer name is on the list)
- Refuse your order and cancel it (if more than one vendor submitted your buyer name to the list or a particularly bad experience was detailed by a vendor).

But we're pretty flexible and reasonable. So if you have a pretty good explanation, we'll go with that and give you the benefit of the doubt.


◉ For established SR Buyers (Buyers with purchasing stats >5 total purchases and/or >$500 spent on SR)

◾ All orders handled in Escrow, period. ;)
