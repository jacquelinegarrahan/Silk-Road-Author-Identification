Title: Re: International v Domestic
Post by: gharaniq on March 02, 2013, 01:04 am
Bear in mind if you are receiving drugs through the mail whether domestically or not then there is always a risk. Yes domestic is the less risky way to do it, but if you want to get a better quality product/ better price/ something unavailable domestically then ordering internationally becomes a more attractive option. If you weigh the risks against the benefits and you think it's worth it then go ahead.

Drugs going through customs makes two outcomes more likely. First is that you will lose your drugs and your money, second is that you will  lose your drugs and your money and also end up being arrested and/or having your place searched.

The first outcome (depending on the amount, what kind of drug, and the laws in your country) is a hell of a lot more likely than the second. This is because unless you live in some kind of insane dictatorship, the fact that someone sent you drugs in the mail does not make you guilty of anything at all. It takes a lot of precious man hours for police to prove that you ordered and paid for drugs in the mail, hence the existence of the customs love letter.

Some basic things to safeguard yourself are to have no drugs or evidence of illegal activity in your house or computer/storage devices. Don't tell anyone about your dealings on the Silk Road or on any online drug market place, because if you do then you are fucked because it's extremely likely they will rat you out if questioned. If you have these basics covered then the only thing you have to worry about is yourself. By this I mean are you prepared to be interrogated and intimidated without folding? Do you know your rights, etc? Knowing for sure you personally can deal with police in the correct way is the single biggest safeguard to protecting yourself, and it is not something that comes naturally to the average person.
