Title: Re: Panic-attacks from sativa-dominant weed.
Post by: theswampmonster on February 04, 2013, 10:20 pm
Hey first of all I'm sorry to hear you're going through these panic attacks while trying to enjoy some bud.  I've got a lot of first hand experience (and especially more recently) with smoking and panic attacks.  I smoked a lot of weed for years, but never had panic attacks or any adverse effects while smoking. 

Recently my circumstances changed from where I was smoking with good friends or by myself in my apartment in California to having to move back in with my rents in PA after getting laid off.  Now when I smoke I am having to do it at a friends apartment and they smoke cigs (I don't smoke them) and I found that this def. increases my anxiety (just the 2nd hand smoke in their small apartment).  I also began (and since stopped) using Xanax and Valium to get to sleep.  That stuff will cause panic attacks when you are getting off of it. 

So I guess my point is try to analyze your situation and figure out why you are getting the panic attacks.  It probably doesn't have as much to do with the weed as it does with your circumstances, surroundings when you smoke, and other drugs/substances you are exposed to.  Get yourself in a good place where you are comfortable and happy and you should be better off. 

Good luck!
