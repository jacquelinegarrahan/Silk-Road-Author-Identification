I would say look for a strain that is maybe no more than 50% sativa, especially if it's your first time. This cannabis on SR, at least the top grade organic medical AAA+++ stuff, well, I cannot believe how powerful it is. I need probably 1/4 or less the amount I used to smoke from stuff bought locally maybe 10-15 years ago. So I would say also take it easy on your first smoke. I'm talking like one small toke and then wait at least 5 minutes before going again.

Sometimes with these strong strains, especially the sativa dominant ones, if you've not smoked ever or in a long time, it can give you a bit of anxiety or panic. So that's why I recommend starting slow. Once you've eased into it for a few sessions you can take more. It's just the mind has to relax and realize there is no reason to panic. Then it's all good and smooth sailing. It also helps to have some stuff laid out ahead of time that you want to do, like watch a funny movie, have some drinks or snacks all laid out. Because once you're super high you can still do all that stuff but it's much more of a pain.
