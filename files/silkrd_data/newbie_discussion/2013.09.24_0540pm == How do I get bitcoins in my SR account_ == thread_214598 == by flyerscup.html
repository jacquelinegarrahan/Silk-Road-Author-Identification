Post by: Yoda on September 24, 2013, 06:35 pm
You have some reading to do on localbitcoin.


No.  Well, I guess you could skip escrow with your Localbitcoin seller... but I don't recommend it.

On localbitcoin... you can make a deal to meet in person, right?  You make a buy, he should load the coin in the localbitcon escrow, you get a code... when you meet him and show him the money, he releases the coin to you, which are then deposited into your localbitcoin wallet.  He will then get that code sent to him, which he must show to you to prove that he released the coin.

You then log onto localbitcon and transfer your coin to where you want.   Directly to SR if you want as long as you did this anonymously, used tor tor logon to your localbitcoin account , didn't use your real phone, etc. etc. etc.

If you make a bank deposit on localbitcoin... it too will be transferred to your account on localbitcoin.

no, don't ever give your sr wallet address to the localbitcoin seller.

you get a wallet as part of your localbitcoin account. what happens is when the bitcoin seller releases your btc, it shows in your localbitcoin wallet, you will get a notification appear near the top of the screen to tell you this. right by where the notification shows up on the localbitcoins page, you will see a link that says wallet. click that and it will have on the left side of the screen a box to paste in the wallet address of where you want to transfer your funds to. just paste your silkroad wallet address into that, type in the amount of btc's you want to transfer and click done. the funds will show up in sr usually within the hour but can in some cases take up to six hours. they will always appear tho as long as you done it right.

I have no idea where you're located, but I highly recommend you use a known exchange like CampBX. They let you set up an account with nothing more than a screen name and an email address. You can then make deposits with Postal Money Orders and include no more information than your CampBX username and email.

Download a good Bitcoin client, I recommend MultiBit. Once your deposit reaches CampBX, you can then buy Bitcoins, which you would immediately send to your Multibit wallet, so that it is kept safe and encrypted on your local machine.

When you get ready to buy on SR, just send the Bitcoin the address shown on your SR My Account page.
