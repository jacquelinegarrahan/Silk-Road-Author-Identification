Welcome one and all to SR - Read the Rules - Stay in Escrow (unless you trust the vendor or the listing asks you not to and you MUST HAVE that item) and as always remember that anything less then a 5/5 hurts a vendor and their rating.

At the end of dealings with a vendor - if you got legit product; safely and securely - chances are that is a 5/5 transaction.

If it took 2 days longer then you hoped....or the item was not "as strong" as you had hoped - its still a 5/5 transaction.


If you got your coins took and got a bag of flour in the mail 2 months later - then we are looking at a 1/5

TAKE THE TIME and actually write some feedback - THAT is what REALLY helps other potential buyers when looking at a listing.

It is a bit annoying when a buyer gives a vendor a 4/5 but then only says "its ok" in the feedback......


And to all you buyers that have the "no on is perfect - no one deserves a 5/5!" - get over your self and learn how the system works here - there is no doubt (and plenty of threads, by both vendors and buyers) to back up the fact that the rating/feedback system is flawed.

None the less this is the system we have to work with - learn how it works and what is expected as YOU a buyer and your time here will be smooth as SILK!

Welcome one and all!
