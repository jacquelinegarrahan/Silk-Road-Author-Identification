Title: Re: Question about LSD/shrooms dose
Post by: hchoman on June 14, 2013, 03:56 am
Once you ingest tryptamines your body immediately starts to build a tolerance as it is metabolizing it. For me, 3 days and all tolerance seems to have cleared.  So if you start with say a half tab, then when you re-dose you have to take at least double the first dose.  Although for most people this doesn't work, including myself. Its either all at once or not at all...my advice, for newbies is start slow. I cannot imagine anything worse than having my first psychedelic be too intense.  Get used to the feelings and effects, then go for broke!
