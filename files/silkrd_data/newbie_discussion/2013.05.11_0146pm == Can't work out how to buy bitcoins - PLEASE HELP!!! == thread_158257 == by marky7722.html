If you are in Canada you can use 'www.cavirtex.com'.  In the states I'm not sure, the market seems to be all over the place and I don't know where users are buying BTC right now.

One thing I will warn you about, you asked 'Can someone sell me bitcoins'.  Please be very careful about buying BTC directly from any user.  Buying BTC directly from another user involves you sending your money to them and hoping they send you back the BTC.  This is probably the number one scam I see people complaining about on the forums.  They send some guy a moneypak code and never see the bitcoin show up and the vendor stops responding to any attempts at contact.

If you're in the uk, go to bitbargain. co. uk and register an account.

Also, check out mtgox.com if you're outside the uk.

Never respond to people offering to sell you coins privately. Very risky.
You're better off finding a reputable site that sells coins.
