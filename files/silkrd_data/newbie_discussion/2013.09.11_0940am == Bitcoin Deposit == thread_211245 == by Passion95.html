Title: Re: Bitcoin Deposit
Post by: cbdx_en on September 11, 2013, 12:55 pm
Try not using your sr wallet directly. Download bitcoin client and let it sync with the network.
After your wallet is synched and encrypted you should do all your transactions from there.

Get your bitcoins there on your personal wallet and from there transfer them wherever you like.
This will allow you to have full control of your coins and you know exactly where the delays are and how many
confirmations left for the transaction.

Try not to send all your coins at once. Send 0.1 see if everything is OK and then send the rest.
