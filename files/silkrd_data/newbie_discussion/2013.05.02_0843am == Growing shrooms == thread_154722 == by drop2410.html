Title: Re: Growing shrooms
Post by: cheaplime on May 15, 2013, 02:22 pm
Growing Cubensis Mushrooms is a very fun and satisfying hobby.

It is also extremely easy, and if you are in the UK is a great excuse to buy high-end jam. (bon maman jam jars are about the only ones that are the right shape!)

Thoroughly research the links provided above and get a pressure cooker if you don't already have one.

When you come to inoculate do not skimp on cleanliness. Disinfect your surface and yourself and wear gloves and a face mask. Be meticulous, systematic and swift.

Pro tip: place tea light candles in the area where you are inoculating your jars. The hot air rising removes airborne contaminants.
