Title: Re: How to trust a vendor in SR
Post by: SandStorm on May 27, 2013, 11:13 am
Since one of his suggestions is for you to pay him with bitcoins I assume it isn't bitcoins you are buying. Therefore you really shouldn't pay to his bank account, I don't know how the bitcoinvendors are doing their thing on SR so I wouldn't comment on that.
On his silkroad vendor profile *he has listings of products and you have the "add to cart" thing. When you use this to pay for your product it will stay in escrow. This means that the payment will not finalize until you have received your product and have finalized it manually or you have waited 17 days for your order to auto-finalize. You should avoid letting the order auto-finalize so if time is running up and you aren't receiving your package you should click the resolve option on the order to get more time so you can find out what is gong on.
