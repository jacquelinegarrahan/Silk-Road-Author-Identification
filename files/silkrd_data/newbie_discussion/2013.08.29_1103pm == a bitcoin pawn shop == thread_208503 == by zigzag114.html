Title: Re: a bitcoin pawn shop
Post by: unbeknownst on August 30, 2013, 01:33 am
Quote from: cryngie on August 30, 2013, 12:52 am
I cant see how it wouldnt work BTC arnt very hard to understand at all

The actual currency isn't important. BTC prices can be indexed to another currency so fluctuations are not a deal breaker, same as on SR. You'd never be able to compete with ebay for cheap stuff for one thing, and I don't think it would be feasible for many more practical reasons. There's all the shipping involved back and forth, for all kinds of oddly shaped crap, and the good folks sending their sparkly drum kits and flying V guitars in wouldn't have a clue how to pack it, so you'd have a lot of losses that way. And we're talking about dealing with used goods being pawned by drug users for drugs. I cringe to think of some of the shit that would be offered! Who is going to want to buy said stuff online without being able to examine/smell it firsthand, and how could the shop afford to provide a guarantee, without which no sane person would spend their money on such a site? How would the shop owner appraise everything, or determine if items were worth accepting? The potential for scams would be huge for not much margin. A shop with only high dollar pieces might work, but such places already exist.
