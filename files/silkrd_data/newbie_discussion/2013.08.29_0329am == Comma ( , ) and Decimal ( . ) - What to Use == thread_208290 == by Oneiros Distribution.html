Title: Comma ( , ) and Decimal ( . ) - What to Use
Post by: Oneiros Distribution on August 29, 2013, 03:29 am
Just a minor thing which I've been thinking about is most of Europe utilizes Comma ( , ) while the United States and a couple of other countries use Decimal ( . ) as their preference. Now since Silk Road does not give an option on what to use and simply utilizes a Decimal I've decided to switch over from all instances of Comma to Decimal as it's not very likely to get changed either way.

For a map showing preferences around the World: https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/e/ed/20130228DecimalSeparator.svg
( Blue = Decimal / Green = Comma / Red = Eastern Arabic numerals / Grey = Data Unavailable )
