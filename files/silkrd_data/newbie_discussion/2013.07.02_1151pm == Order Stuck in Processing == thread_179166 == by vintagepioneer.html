Title: Re: Order Stuck in Processing
Post by: Red Rover on July 03, 2013, 12:37 am
If you placed the order before the vendor ran out of stock, then he should have your order reserved for you.  You don't take orders on product you don't have.

Have you messaged the vendor?  If so, how long ago?

Also, please tell me you didn't FE.  You didn't, right? 

If it does turn out that the vendor ran out of product, he should give you the option to either cancel the order, or wait until he restocks.  But you really, really need to message him.  If he doesn't respond in a few days (72 hours MAX) you may have to go to the resolution center.
