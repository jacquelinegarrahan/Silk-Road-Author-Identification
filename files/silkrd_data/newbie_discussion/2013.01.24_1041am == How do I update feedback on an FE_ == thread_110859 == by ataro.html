Title: Re: How do I update feedback on an FE?
Post by: Jasper Jenson on January 24, 2013, 03:28 pm
Click on 'account' on top of silk road page. Look for the 'feedback' link on the right of the next page. Once you see the feedback you have given you can adjust the score and add/edit feedback.

On the 'account' page you can also see purchasing stats as a link in the menu. Read the buyers guide too. The 'settings' if you click on it allows you to change prices to $ or BTC.

Also, be very careful if you do FE. Start out small, with an amout you can afford to lose. Buy samples, 1g weed, etc until your stats are good just in case someone fucks you.
