Title: Re: 25i-Nbome question
Post by: macdrizzle on September 26, 2013, 08:26 am
There can be a big difference in the dosage depending on the source, the form, and the preparation.

There is a source, one of the better known ones, who sells only the freebase powder. This free base is a light tan powder, which dissolves decently but not great in cold water. Some small yellow specks don't dissolve (indicating some impurity or impurities, I suspect). It is quite a bit less potent than the HCl salt. I'd say maybe >50% less effective by mouth and ~50% less effective by other routes. Dosing starts @ ~500 micrograms (0.5 mg) and dosages up to ~5mg are common. Multiple (large) males have snorted up to 11 mg of this freebase powder in a night causing a strong trip without any problems arising. It has also been "too much" for a small person to take <5mg of it (causing an overwhelming trip but no physical health problems). It's said to be 97%+ purity, but I know of no confirmed tests of that.

The HCl salt is probably just about as common to find, possibly more so. It is usually a white powder. Dosage reportedly starts @ ~300 micrograms and a goes up commonly to about 1.5 milligrams - 3 mg by various administration routes. Usually it's advertized as 98%+ in purity, but I have seen some of the HCl salt come back testing @ about 91% pure.
The HCl salt dissolves freely in water iirc, and I'd suspect a range of other solvents as well. To be put onto blotter paper, it'll need to be put into a solvent first.

Sometimes 25I-NBOMe (freebase [as well as HCl salt?]) is complexed with cyclodextrins (most commonly HPBCD [hydroxypropyl-beta-cyclodextrin]) to improve the solubility in polar solvents. This makes it easier to put the freebase on paper and to dissolve it in solvents for other dosage preparations. I'm not sure exactly how that effects the potency, but it likely goes up in efficacy.
