Title: Re: local bitcoins .com
Post by: Croskin on July 27, 2013, 05:37 am
It is true.  Localbitcoins.com runs their escrow through their own website. To get their funds released to them, they have to send it to your specific wallet address for the account on your website.  You can ask them to do a transaction outside of the website, but then you lose your escrow protection and will most likely get scammed out of your money.
