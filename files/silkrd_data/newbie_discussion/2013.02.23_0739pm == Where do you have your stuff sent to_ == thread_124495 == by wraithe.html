Title: Re: Where do you have your stuff sent to?
Post by: homertortoise on February 23, 2013, 08:33 pm
disclaimer: never made a purchase on SR (not yet anyways...) However, I've done a lot of research on these boards and can likely summarize what most will say.

> For small amounts used for personal consumption, most on here would likely advise you to send it to your home address.
> For larger quantities, or more frequent orders, you may feel safer having it sent to a PO box or a mailbox at a mom & pop shop.
> Mom & pop shop mailboxes appear to be what most on here recommend because they usually have less security.
> If you go the mailbox/PO box route, don't use a fake ID because then you could get in trouble for mail fraud or identity fraud, etc.
> Most will tell you NOT to have orders shipped to an abandoned house or something like that because the orders are far less likely to arrive.
> Though not the majority, quite a few members seem to use a drop-house where they pay someone to accept deliveries for them. This is risky, but appears to work for some. Others believe that doing so is pretty shitty of you because you're putting others at risk.

hope this helps!
