Title: Re: PGP, FE, Escrow and how to use them? any idea???? what are they
Post by: scout on May 22, 2013, 04:26 am
For protection.  PGP helps you encrypt your sensitive information (like your address), and escrow protects your funds until you have received & are happy with your order.  FE = finalizing early = releasing the funds to your vendor before you've received the order ... this is something you should NOT do.

Please take some time and read the SR wiki - it will help answer all of your questions:
