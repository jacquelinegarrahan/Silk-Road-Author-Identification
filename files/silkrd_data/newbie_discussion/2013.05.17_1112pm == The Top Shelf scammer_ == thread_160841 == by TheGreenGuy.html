Yeah, TheTopShelf got me too. I am out $140 to this fucking scammer... but a look through the posts in the other forums (just search under his name) shows that I am in good company. Many other people appear to have lost $600 chunks after FE'ing, some lost thousands to this guy.

Maybe the rule of thumb is to just stick with vendors who respect the escrow system... or find a seller who has dealt with you honestly with good service, and perhaps for them only, finalize early.
