Post by: PotatoConnoisseur on March 26, 2013, 01:28 am
Things rarely get seized but sometimes it happens, once something gets seized authorities sometimes won't pursue it beyond a letter from customs. However people certainly do get busted, look up the statistics on drugs found in the mail. It isn't a lot compared to how much is sent but it still happens.
I can think of some examples but would rather not mention them specifically.
The best thing you can do if you plan on ordering large amounts of things or from overseas frequently is to work on some good drops. For small amounts of domestic products it doesn't matter as much, the cops might knock on  your door and ask you about it but they won't really give a shit about an eight ball of coke or a quarter of weed unless you make it really easy to bust you and admit to everything right away.
Every drop has weaknesses but the idea is to make it harder for them to bust you, if they're willing to put int he work you will get busted. But you'd really have to be fucking around with bulk for that to be a problem.

Be safe and don't be careless, carelessness is what gets people busted.
