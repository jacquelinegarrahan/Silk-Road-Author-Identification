Post by: wwizard on May 01, 2013, 05:05 pm
Not havin FE'd is already a good move as a newbie, but some people will still scam you by letting you stay in escrow.. As a newbie, you have no good record, so the staff may be favorable to the vendor... In some cases they offer 50% refunds but if they never sent the product they still effectively scam you, and you leave 5/5 because you think it got caught by customs.
That's why you should always mention if you got a refund in your feedbacks, and look if there's a lot of "never arrived but 50% refund". Good scammers selectively scam by offering refunds on never sent stuff.
Of course, there are legit people, and some dodgy looking vendors deliver, the forums are also very important in choosing a vendor.
7g is a lot for a first time.. Try to buy 1g first, most people don't scam for small amounts.
