Please excuse me if this looks too pessimistic. I hope it will help others here though.

This post is just to confirm what I think most SR members have been seeing lately, that is, things are more difficult at the USA customs that what they used to be just a  few months ago.

After several successful purchases at SR and similar sites, finally I had two packages intercepted.

First one with 500 g of a certain precursor ( not liquid, solid ). Tried to contact carrier several times. Nobody knows. Tracking stopped at USA customs. Never got any paperwork. A few months ago already.

Second one, this time it was 1 g ketamine, and this time I received a letter from the US Customs and Border. A bit inaccurate, they say they seized approximately 3 g of ketamine, given it a value of $ 7.98. Just received the letter today.

They ask me to fill and return the last page, I have to choose one option. They gave me five options. I think the best thing to do is just to ABANDON it ( option # 3 ).

They say also that besides the forfeiture there might be a civil penalty. Two options follows, I might get more information included, or being prepared and mailed soon. NONE option marked, no info included, so I hope it ends here.

That's all folks.
