Post by: wonderlandkidz on July 15, 2013, 06:17 pm
 Just dealt with this situation this morning and have some information to add to the discussion that I feel will definitely help ease some people's minds. I went to check my DCN tracking and it had stated that it was missent.  :o Naturally I instantly got quite worried and began investigating around. Thankfully SR Forums are incredibly informational and a lot of the posts that I read on this subject helped a lot!! Thanks to all of u that took time to share very helpful information on these threads!
 When reading a post that stated that it may be a fake name issue and/or address mix up I couldn't contain my suspicion any longer and called the post office that it last showed up at. I very nervously gave the lady my alias, address, along with my throw away phone # and DCN and waited in suspense. She called back and told me that what happened was the machine sorting the mail scanned the return address, which was in the city of the post office I was talking to, instead of my address. They had already realized the issue and had put it back to be placed in the correct spot. I was told it is now going to be sent off later today to the correct place and I should get it within 1-2 days. WHEW!!  ;D  They were very helpful and nothing seemed sketchy at all, which is always a big relief in our line of business!!   ;)
