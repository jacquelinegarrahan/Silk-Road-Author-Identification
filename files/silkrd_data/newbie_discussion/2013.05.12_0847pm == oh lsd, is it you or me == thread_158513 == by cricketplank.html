I only took MDMA on my own once, and while it was nice enough, I had a great urge to be with people, and at the time, I couldn't be, so it was a bit frustrating. On the other hand, with LSD, it got to the point where I could only take it on my own, when I had a completely controlled environment so nothing 'external' could go wrong, phone off the hook etc.. I did have some amazing experiences tripping out to music. For me, it tends to go wrong if I have things to worry about. I'm an anxiety sufferer, so I have to be careful. Funnily enough, my best trips were at Glastonbury festival back in the day. There was so much crazy shit going and you were kind of cut off from real life anyway so nothing really mattered. I think that 'nothing really matters', happy-go-lucky mindset is kind of what you need for a good trip.
