Title: Re: Storing/Shipping/Handling Acid
Post by: dipset on January 25, 2013, 04:31 am
LSD is very stable when on blotter paper or solid crystal form, less so when in liquid solution, and more susceptible to degradation as such. Acid will keep and retain most of its potency, for a very long time if stored properly. Avoid heat, light, air, and moisture. No particular container is necessary, I avoid foil due to its heat conductibility and the freezer isnt really a requisite, but plastic bags and an opaque container/layer are just fine as long as it is in a reasonably cool storage area.
