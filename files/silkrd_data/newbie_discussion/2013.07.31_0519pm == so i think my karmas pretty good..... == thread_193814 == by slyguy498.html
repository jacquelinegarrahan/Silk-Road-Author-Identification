you get to be able to "give and take karma" to another member, when you reach 100 posts.   Making posts that are informative or helpful will get the clicks of a few people ... there are also 'karma whoring threads' where members go around boosting each others +karma ... but it's a scam, coz ... it just is! 

That's how some 'pretty random'  people have +karma of over 500, sure there are a few members who have rightly deserved this, (i.e. Pine, a truly wonderful member, runs the PGP club here) but it will show in the amount of posts they've made ... Or, maybe If they''re DPR!  lol!


Karma doesn't really mean much except you've said stuff that resonated with people, and as you can see with myself for instance, also 'rattling some cages' as well, when the bullshit starts to fly!  IMO people with about 50/50 good/bad karma are probably the 'most honest' with their opinions, as someone with something solid to say, will definitely get a response.

So,  Go forth and post sensibly, and help others in your travels, and you will be well  karma'd ... and dont worry if you get 5-10 neg K points one day, there are some sad souls floating about, with multiple accounts, who sometimes go on a mission to get somwone lots of bad karma!

  At least there's a rule where you can only give any one person, a karma point, up or down, once every 72 hours! A very good idea, as it stops pissed off people, and people "on drugs" from doing stupid stuff with the karma, and THEN it WOULD BE pretty useless ... once you read around the forums a bit, you'll start to see the  patterns!


Take care out there, and do your research on the vendors etc HERE on the forum, and you'll come out on top
