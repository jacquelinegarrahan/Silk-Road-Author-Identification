What type of product are you interested in purchasing? I could recommend some vendors for you.
My advice is to always check the forums for information on vendors and products before you purchase.
Never jump on an item because "its such a good price" before purchasing especially if you FE.
Scammers use lower prices, a perceived "great deal" to lure customers into FE.
There are some good vendors that I will FE for, still a potential risk though....
If you never FE you will negate risk of being scammed and you will have the protection of SR escrow! The SR team is very good imo!
If you do FE, never FE more money than you're willing to lose!
If you do FE and lose money, don't stress it to much. It will only do you more harm to stress about your loss.

Also, don't FE until you get used to the swing of things around here.  It's never worth it and escrow is always a better option.
Don't sign for anything you aren't expecting to sign for.  Actually, don't sign for anything EVER.  If you are asked to sign for something, it may be a controlled delivery.  Just tell the postal worker "I was not expecting this and I will not sign for it.  Return it to sender, please"    Yes, you lose drugs, but you also cover your ass.

If you're short on an order or want an amount that isn't listed, contact the vendor.  If you have any questions, contact the vendor.  It's what we're here for.
