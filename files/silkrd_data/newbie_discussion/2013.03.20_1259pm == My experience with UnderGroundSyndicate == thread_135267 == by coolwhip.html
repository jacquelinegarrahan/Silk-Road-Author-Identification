Title: My experience with UnderGroundSyndicate
Post by: coolwhip on March 20, 2013, 12:59 pm
Posting here because it's the only place I can post things (Mods please move this to the Rumor Mill), as well as the listing was deleted so my feedback no longer shows up. I don't think it's fair to other users to not have a heads up.

So I ordered something from this dude UnderGroundSyndicate, figured hey 99 rep, he must be doing something right.

I was extremely displeased with the quality of the package. There was no attempt to make it discreet. The package had something labeled "Research" on the top, which ordinarily wouldn't be an issue except it arouses more suspicion if there already was some. I'm not overly worried about this, I was more worried at the lack of smell proofing the package. A dog could smell this a 1/4 mile away upwind... If you're in a situation where discreetness is a must, he is not your guy.

That being said, what lacked in the shipping was made up for in the quality. VERY pleased with the huge shard and plenty of medium sized ones I received. Good quality stuff right here. Weight was spot on.

tl;dr Buy for quality, not for discreetness.
