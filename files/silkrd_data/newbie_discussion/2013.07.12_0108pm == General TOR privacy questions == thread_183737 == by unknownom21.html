Title: Re: General TOR privacy questions
Post by: BillWithers on July 12, 2013, 01:13 pm
Only the tor entry node knows your IP address, or whatever IP address you're coming from. For hidden services such as SR you don't come out an exit node so there is no risk there. If you're accessing Tor from your own internet connection, your ISP (and any eavesdroppers such as the NSA) definitely know you're hitting Tor entry nodes (and that's true whether you're using Tails or not - there are ways to prevent that though such as a VPN or proxy).
