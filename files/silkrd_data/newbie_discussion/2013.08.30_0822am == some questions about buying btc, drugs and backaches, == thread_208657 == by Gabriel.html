I am not techie enough to answer your first few queries without engaging in more research. The physical queries you posed I have a much better chance at giving you an accurate response.

(I find it odd that pot increases the tension and pain in your back.)

The strychnine in the blotters is a poison, you realize this? Ok for the best acid trip I have found that getting baked before hand is a must. The strychnine causes muscle tension, jaw clenching, teeth grinding, perspiration. To get ready for those sides, I recommend pre dose,  take your vitamins. Seriously. So many back problems are related to B Complex deficiencies. Take your Vit. C, your potassium and magnesium. I would also say have a handy muscle relaxer so when you are ready to sleep, take either a Soma or a Zanaflex. My preference is the Zanaflex 4mg.

Also, having some great candy on hand is one way to keep that jaw clenching under control. I like Skittles and Starburst for this purpose. Make sure you have some fresh orange juice on hand, or some juice high in Vit. C. Be aware of your physical self, hydrate as you feel your body ask for moisture.
