Title: Re: How long is the average wait?
Post by: TNS on August 10, 2013, 11:12 pm
Just a few facts I recall from the postal service. Please note, as they have recently changed things up, my knowledge may be a bit dated. I am not a postal employee but have made friends with many.

First class parcels can take up to 5 business days, Priority is 2-3 (on a rare occasion 4).

The difference in transit time is processing priority at sorting facilities or airports.

Express mail is awarded highest priority and always is sent directly to the destination sort facility. In layman's terms it's the first thing they load on the plane/truck and the first thing they sort when unloading. These packages are given first priority to keep with their next day guarantee.

Non Flat-rate Priority is next.

Followed by flat-rate Priority.

Then First class letters then parcels.

Parcel post is last. Usually put in wherever it can fit.

There are space and tonnage procedures too but I don't know in detail what they are. Plane specific stuff that programs run through to ensure every last cubic inch and ounce of weight capacity is utilized.
