Title: Re: Question about LSD units
Post by: the dude abides on February 24, 2013, 03:47 am
Yeah, every listing will refer to hits/tabs/blotters. This means 1 single square of blotter paper - not a sheet from which you can tear many individual hits from. Consequently, the ug refers to the dosage in each individual hit.

For example, a listing detailing "5 x 110ug tabs" would get you 5 individual dosages. Obviously you don't have to take just one, it depends on your tolerance and experience, however the general idea is that one is a solid starting point.

Hope that helps :)
