Title: Re: Order Marked "In Transit"
Post by: Generation Y on February 06, 2013, 10:03 am
It depends entirely on the seller. For example, if a seller requires you to FE they will have to mark it in transit before you can finalize, which means it will be marked as in transit long before it is actually sent. I would advise you to read the sellers profile to see what is written on there, and also to have patience. If its shipped an hour after you order or two days isn't too important, unless your an addict ordering your next heroin fix of course.
