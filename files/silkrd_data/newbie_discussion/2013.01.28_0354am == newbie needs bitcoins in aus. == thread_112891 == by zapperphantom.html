Post by: Wadozo on January 28, 2013, 11:46 am
On the order form from Spendbitcoins, you need to add the Bitcoin Address found under the ACCOUNTS tab on SR's Homepage. That is the Bitcoin address, not the Wallet address. Please note - After every transaction you make, clock on the "GET NEW ADDRESS" tab to receive a new address from SR. Use them once only. If you have coins left over in your account, they will still be there after acquiring a new Bitcoin address. The correct way of getting your coins and where to keep them is this -

Quote
  You should never transfer coins directly to your Silk Road E-Wallet just in case the site is ever taken down by LE or Hackers. If that happens, there would be a good chance your coins would disappear forever. You need to download a Bitcoin Client, either a Web based one or a Desktop one, and transfer your coins from where you buy them to this wallet. A good one to use is Electrum - http://electrum.org/
Once you have this downloaded and installed, transfer your coins to one of the generated Bitcoin addresses. Then when your ready to make a purchase on SR, transfer the required amount of coins from your Client to your SR wallet containing your Bitcoin address. Always send a little more coin over in case of a price rise so you don't end up short when your ready to order. After every purchase you make on SR, request a new Bitcoin address. Using the same address more than once could potentially link you to more than one transaction should something ever happen to you. It's all about minimizing your risks, just in case something does go wrong.
