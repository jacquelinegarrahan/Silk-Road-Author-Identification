The point to focus on is whether you believe that the resources that would be invested into such a sting for this environment would be approved by the powers that be in order to try to convict an end user, and that's a determination that no one else can make for you, you weigh the risks of whatever you're going to do, and you decide whether the gain is worth the risk, and you try to mitigate the risk as much as possible
