Title: Re: Music + Drug experience
Post by: TR0N on April 15, 2013, 09:31 pm
One time, I did an obscene amount of mushrooms and listened to an album called "Bigger Fish Frying" by a western Canadian artist called "Longwalkshortdock". He has music available for free online, bit this album in particular is what you want. I'm looking forward to his next one.

The music itself is unlike anything I have ever heard. It is a blend of electronic, ambient, vocal, and organic elements that combine to make something one might call, "ambient electro pop" .. or something... but it is entirely its own thing.

By the time the final note had played on the final song (fittingly titled "Life Is a Sine Wave") I had learned much about life and my place in it.

It's cool music to listen to sober, but when tripping it takes on a much more profound tone.
I guarantee it will captivate the shit out of anyone while high, no matter what your musical tastes.
