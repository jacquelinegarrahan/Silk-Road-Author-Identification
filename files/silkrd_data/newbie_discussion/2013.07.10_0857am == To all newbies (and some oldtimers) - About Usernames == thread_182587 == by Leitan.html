Title: /!\ To all newbies (and some oldtimers) : About Usernames /!\
Post by: Leitan on July 10, 2013, 08:57 am
Hi everyone,

Today for the fourth time in a month i had to send a message to an user, because simply googling his username linked to a Google+ account with location and pictures.

DO NOT USE YOUR REGULAR PSEUDONYM ON A HIDDEN SERVICE.

It takes 15 secs to 5 minutes to link it to your regular identity for any LEO.

Don't use either a name linkable to any of your close friends, relatives, old schoolmates..

You can find plenty of names that are vague enough not to give any information on your person/location yet unique enough you could remember it !

Take care,
Leitan
