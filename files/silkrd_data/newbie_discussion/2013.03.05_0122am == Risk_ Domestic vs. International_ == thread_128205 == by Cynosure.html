Title: Re: Risk: Domestic vs. International?
Post by: oldtoby on March 05, 2013, 02:16 am
To consider:

- on interception, would you be burning your real name, real address? (if yes, strong factor in favour of domestic)
- country of origin - Germany - not bad AFAIK - could be worse (eg: the Netherlands)
- country of destination - U.S.. a generally suspicious lot, but could be worse (eg: Australia)
- type of product. I'd be content to order tabs from overseas if reports of stealth were good. Powder and pills, less so depending on vendor feedback. Weed, no.

I'm not you, so I can't weigh these for you, but these are the factors I'd consider.

Title: Re: Risk: Domestic vs. International?
Post by: funeightyone on March 05, 2013, 03:29 am
I would say stay within the US. I've done both. The shipping time is murder. In my experience it's been nearly a month each time - which is nerve racking. Then there's the issue of customs burning your home address and name. The chances of getting burned domestically are very slim and usually only happens with weed or with a terrible vendor (you can rule that out with feedback - specifically comments about stealth).

But, in my opinion, the biggest factor is the spirit of this issue: we need to support domestic vendors where possible so that prices/products/services will improve domestically. If we support international vendors, we will keep having the same issues. But the more domestic items we buy, the better the supply will become domestically.
