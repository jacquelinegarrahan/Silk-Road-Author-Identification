Title: Re: I can't do anything
Post by: onufri on May 09, 2013, 06:22 pm
My impression from lurking is that the likelihood that you will be subjected to a controlled delivery — in which case the presence of any marijuana or paraphernalia would land you up shit creek without a paddle — is pretty low if you're just buying small amounts. It would be a considerable waste of resources on the part of law enforcement to go after you, considering there is no way you could lead them up the foodchain to the dealers that they really want to catch.

With international shipments, it's more likely that your import will be caught, but the standard procedure is for Customs to send you a 'love letter' telling you to knock that shit out.

Low risk does not mean no risk, however. You'll have to choose between the anxiety that you might get busted and the hassle of keeping your house weed-free.

You could also try sending to a friend, if you've got friends with clean premises who don't mind receiving your goods.
