I just gave BST a try.  Hard to believe but we don't have a single BofA in our city so had to do the cash in mail (CIM) option.  You just email them and let them know how much you want to buy along with the wallet address you want it sent to, and they will email you back with confirmation and mailing instructions.  They lock the price/transfer rate in at the time of the email, so you have 12 hours to send the cash, which is checked via time stamp on the envelope.  I sent the cash along with the email address I used to place the order to the address they gave me.  I used USPS with tracking/delivery confirmation and made up a return address.  I sent it Wed and it should be delivered this afternoon.  I'll post an update on how this works out later today.
