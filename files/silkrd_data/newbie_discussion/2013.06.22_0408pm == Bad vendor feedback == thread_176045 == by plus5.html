Title: Re: Bad vendor feedback
Post by: frank-butcher24 on June 23, 2013, 07:28 pm
The vendors allegedly maintain a buyer blacklist in the vendor forum, but how well-used it is or whether it's officially supported by the site admins is questionable. We buyers don't know much about it really but I suspect it isn't as big a deal as it's made out to be.

If you are unsatisfied with the quality of service, it'd be best to contact the seller first before leaving bad feedback. If you can't come to an agreement, take it to resolution. But avoid getting into that situation in the first place by only buying from good sellers.

For any popular item, you can easily avoid being sent bad stuff. Just read feedback on these here forums. I have been here for a very long time and have never, not even once, done a deal that was not perfect.
Title: Re: Bad vendor feedback
Post by: mcguire39 on June 23, 2013, 07:38 pm
Vendors can't leave you any feedback currently, good or bad. Some vendors do say if you give less than 5 they will not sell to you again. I'm sure some vendors do share a 'do not deal with list' but I doubt all vendors participate.

I think it is wrong to leave bad feedback for a vendor without contacting the vendor first to explain the situation. Yes they may not do anything about it and then the bad feedback would be warranted. If you're using your real name and address I would be hesitant to get into too much of a heated argument with a vendor; nothing good can come of it.
