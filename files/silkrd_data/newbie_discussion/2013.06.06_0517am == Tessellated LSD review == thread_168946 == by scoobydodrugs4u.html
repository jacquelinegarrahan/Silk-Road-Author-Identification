Great acid and hope to get more soon

Quote from: beefyfrog on June 06, 2013, 05:33 am
Im interested in getting some acid. But have never experimented with anything other then weed and maybe a few pills. Would you recommend 200ug tabs for a first time trip? Or should I try mushrooms first like everyone keeps telling me

You're going to get a wide array of responses to the question of "mushrooms or LSD", and none of it can really predict how your brain will react. Personally I recommend LSD, as it will be easier to control your dose. If you have ~200µg tabs, for example, you can easily just cut the paper to take a smaller hit. For your first time, I recommend you stay at or under 150µg, possibly even under 100. This will get your mind's feet wet, help you acclimate to the weirdness of tripping, and will probably get you really excited to trip balls later. Make sure to wait a couple weeks between your mini-dose and your first big trip, otherwise you're just wasting good acid.
