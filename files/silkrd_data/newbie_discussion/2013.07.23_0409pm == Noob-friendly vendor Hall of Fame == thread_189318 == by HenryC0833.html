Two categories.

NON-SENSITIVE ITEMS.   Good way to build up your stats, and practice your encryptions.

PURVEYORS OF FINE CHEMICALS.    Once you have one or two transactions under your belt, go for the gusto. 
1.  Aidoneus.  Huge buzz over on the grown-up forums about his clean, hard-hitting MDMA done up in stealthy little capsules.  The word you hear over and over again is "professionalism."   He really wants you to be happy.  (Extremely happy!)  Definitely worthy of noob coin.
2.  BlueViking.  A well-established vendor who's  in with the in-crowd, having received a coveted endorsement from the LSD Avengers.  The Viking is the nicest guy in the world.  Patiently answered a noob question I sent him, which I realize now was a little inconsiderate.  I didn't mean to!  And he was nice about it.  So first chance I got, I sent in an order.  And he filled it, lickety-split.  Great guy, quality product.

OK!  This is the Hall of Fame thread.  Don't be all bitchy here, this is for positive experiences with vendors who deserve our business!
