Title: Re: High price for seller fee worth it ?
Post by: Aussie bob on April 05, 2013, 11:50 am
Difficult to say Kirkoff, it would depend on how much money you were hoping to make. A good place to start would be checking out vendors offering similar products and trying to get an idea of how much money they may bring in (one simple way is multiplying feedback by purchase price divided by the age of the oldest feedback). Vending is more than a money commitment, you'll have to commit your precious time as well.   

   Remember, although the vendor bond may be expensive, it's there to weed out the scammers and wasters, and once you've proven yourself you'll get your bond back. Many vendors have been lucky in this regard as SR is giving back the amount of btc paid, and with the recent inflation of btc prices they are making a serious profit off of it.

   Good luck, AB.
