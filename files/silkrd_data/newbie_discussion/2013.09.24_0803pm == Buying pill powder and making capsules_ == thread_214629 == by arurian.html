Title: Re: Buying pill powder and making capsules?
Post by: fenmet on September 25, 2013, 09:31 am
I've thought about this. It seems like measuring out powder for the individual pills would be a time-consuming pain in the ass. The only way I've thought of to get around it would be-- well, have you seen how sort of primitive, a couple-hundred-at-a-time capsule fillers work, where it's filling one-half of the capsule to the top and then capping? I figure, you calculate the volume of that capsule half, figure out the volume of the dosage you're going for in pure powder, then cut it with starch or sugar or whatever so that the right dosage fills the half-cap. For example, if the half-cap is 1.5 ml and the volume of the ideal dosage is .5 ml, you add enough sugar/whatever to fill the other 1 ml.
That way you don't have to measure for each pill. Hopefully that makes some sort of sense.

Make sure it is powdered pill and not pure alprazolam.
