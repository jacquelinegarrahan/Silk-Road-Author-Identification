There is no PIN discussion place left to newbies like me...

Here is the summary of this problem.
There is only one way to get your PIN reset without knowing the old one and that is to use your five (5) attempts to get a PIN message from support which cannot be opened for a time.

First the waiting period was 7 days, after complaints of the members they made it 3 days; The problem is that it seems that time varies to people. I myself in wait 3 days and still cannot open the message in order to answer it to reset my PIN. I also am not alone in this situation.

I've a guess that maybe new accounts have to wait longer than the new 3 days period or something.

Whatever, still the only way to get your PIN is to wait as long as it takes.

Since it is a security issue, I also have a simple solution instead of disabling that option, to this problem for support: You could make the bitcoin deposit option in a way that requires to type your PIN. You may do that by requiring the member to type their PIN in order to see their wallet address, or you may just find a little different way to get this option since the wallet number could have been saved by old member so that may doesn't help them... So, even if you don't remember your pin, unless you wouldn't have to worry about your money and the currency changes anyhow.

Also it would be nice to get an open explanation of this problems from the support. How long should we wait? Does time varies? It is just annoying.
