Title: Re: how do you FE?
Post by: Sam Glad on July 25, 2013, 04:30 pm
The fact that many successful transactions have required FE doesn't not make it a good idea to do so. 

I've driven many places without wearing my safety belt and never gotten into an accident.  So yeah, you can go ahead and not wear your safety belt.

You see what I did?  Finalizing when the product arrives is your safety belt.  If the product you receive is not as advertised or worse, never received, you have the option to resolve that transaction as long as you have not finalized.  You can go thousands of miles without NEEDING your safety belt, but when the time comes that you need it, you'll be damn glad you were wearing it.
