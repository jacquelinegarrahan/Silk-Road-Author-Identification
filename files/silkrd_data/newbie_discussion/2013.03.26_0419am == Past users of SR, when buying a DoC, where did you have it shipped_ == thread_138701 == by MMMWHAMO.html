Title: Re: Past users of SR, when buying a DoC, where did you have it shipped?
Post by: newbottles on March 26, 2013, 08:31 pm
Always ship to your real name and a real address.

Nothing wrong with either option - private residence or a legit PO Box.  Just don't try to get all shady with fake names, abandoned addresses, etc. 

The fundamental idea is to hide in plain sight.  It just looks like another piece of mail in the system.

If you can make sure you get a high volume of legal packages, that certainly helps.  Buy your groceries on Amazon for example.

Try to avoid getting a lot of packages from foreign countries if possible.  If you stick with domestic you are much less conspicuous.

Always use PGP for your address!
