Post by: dildobuggery on February 28, 2013, 07:04 am
Well, I'm pretty new too and here's how I did it. First, you gotta get bitcoins. I used a website called btcquick.com because they will take credit cards but it's not the best exhange rate and you can them lots of other places. Then use blockchain.info and get a free wallet. When you get your digital bitcoin certificate from whomever you bought it from (my guy emails me with a link to download pdf files with the certificates on them). Take the "private key" number, copy it and import it into your blockchain wallet. Then you take the silkroad payment number (listed in the "Account" link of your Silk Road profile) and enter it into the "send bitcoins" area in your blockchain wallet. Within an hour or two, your bitcoins will be in your SR account. Does that make sense?
