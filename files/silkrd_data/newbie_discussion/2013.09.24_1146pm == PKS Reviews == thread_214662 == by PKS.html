Welcome to my review thread.   #WELCOME

I will be reviewing anything I order on the SR here, using a couple of categories. 

Safe Assumptions:
-Anything I'm reviewing, I've tested.
-I did not FE for any of these orders, ever. 
-I will never be purchasing Cocaine or Marijuana through the Road.
-I do not review shipping times.  Not only are there too many factors out of a vendor's control, but I order my drugs long before I need them.  If you know you want drugs, and you have access to the SR, then you should be able to obtain them well in advance.  If you can't plan that far ahead, maybe you should rethink casual drug use.

How to use this review thread: 
I'll be making use of hashtags (#) for tagging parts of my review thread.  Each product will have it's own header (#MDMA, #LSD, etc) and you can find a list at the top of the products section.
Additionally, you can see what sections are called at the top.

Explanation of my reviews: #BUTWHATDOESITALLMEANBASIL

*       Poor
**      Below average
***    Average (or, what I've come to expect from SR.  Usually this is above street average)
****   Above average. 
***** Amazing.  Excellent.  Buy now!

Price: How does the price compare to other vendors.  I won't be comparing to local prices, strictly to other vendors on the Road.
Quality: Was the drug what I was expecting to receive in the amount advertised? 
Stealth: How good was the packaging?
Bonus: I'll something add (or remove) a * for whatever reason.  Usually customer service.
