Post by: JeanRob on June 20, 2013, 09:48 pm
I have found Bitinstant to be both fast and reliable.

I prefer to use their service where I deposit (i.e., pay) cash at a local Walmart or CVS and send it to my SR bitcoin address (i.e., wallet). This only takes a couple hours from start to finish. Bitinstant charges 3.99 percent plus a flat $3.95 charge.

It may be more secure to use other, intermediate services, such as Dwolla, Virwox, or Mt. Gox. However those services often require you to have a verified account, and they take an additional commission.

I note that Silk Road allows you to change your bitcoin wallet address at any time.
