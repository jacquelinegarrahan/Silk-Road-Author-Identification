Title: NIK Testing Kits --make sure your drug is that drug
Post by: Railgun on May 12, 2013, 12:32 am
I'm new here, so I apologize if you guys know this already, but I see a lot of advertisements for organic powders/MDMA/etc.  It's virtually impossible to tell what you're getting or what it's cut with in black trade (even on the street).  I'm only here for the ganj, but I realize a lot of people have other tastes.

I highly recommend NIK kits, which have a reagent that reacts strongly in the presence of a drug (it's used by LE as far as I know).

They are available on clearnet:
 http://www.amazon.com/NIK-Drug-Test-Kit-LSD/dp/B002ATUECS

is one for LSD, but they have others for cocaine/ketamine/opiates(and heroin)/meth etc. 

I am not so sure if I'd order these at the same time as a shipment if you buy large quanities, as having an LSD testing kit with a controlled delivery of LSD probably voids plausible denial.  However, I'd most certainly urge doing so maybe a few weeks prior to your original purchase and keeping the reagents.

 I believe only the reagent liquid is needed, so if you can house the reagent (normally strong acids) in a plastic container (do research on types of plastics), it most likely will blend in with your other fridge wares. 

Note: THESE ARE ONLY IN THE US.
