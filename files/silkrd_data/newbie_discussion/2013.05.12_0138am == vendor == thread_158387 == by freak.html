Hi freak,

Further to scout's post, you must pay a $500 bond which is refundable once the terms of that bond are met. This only needs to be paid once, it is not an annual fee.

The requirements for getting the money back can be found here:
http://dkn255hz262ypmii.onion/wiki/index.php?title=Seller%27s_Guide#Bond

You should also read the Vendor F.A.Q. and the Seller's Guide as they contain necessary information that you need to know before you consider becoming a vendor.

Vendor F.A.Q.:
http://dkn255hz262ypmii.onion/wiki/index.php?title=Frequently_Asked_Questions#Vendor_questions

Seller's Guide:
http://dkn255hz262ypmii.onion/wiki/index.php?title=Seller%27s_Guide
