Title: Re: What exactly are the karma points for?
Post by: Razorspyne on April 01, 2013, 06:53 am
Karma's programmed as a feature in SMF-run software like Silk Road forums. It can be made invisible at admin's discretion but on this forum (like most) it is available for viewing. whitenegro, Kinetic and NCChronic pretty much answered it. Constructive or positive post, in theory, attract positive karma, while trolling will see an increase in negative karma. So a high neg to positive ratio will often indicate a troll, meaning you might be given a heads-up if you you look at the Karma beforehand. A high postive/low neg ration tends to indicate the opposite. Sadly some children press shiny red buttons on everyone here just for the hell of it and neg everyone on a page. You get that here b/c of the demographics.

And yeah, I'd LOVE to trade them for bitcoin...... ::)
