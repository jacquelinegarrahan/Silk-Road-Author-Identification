Title: Re: Could someone recommend a wallet?
Post by: Remember My Name on August 26, 2013, 03:30 am
I used LocalBitcoins.com and that site comes with a Wallet which the funds are transferred into upon the purchase of your BTC.

I then transferred these funds to Blockchain.info, from there I transferred to EasyWallet.org, and from there to my SR wallet. Always used a new identity when transferring between each wallet.

Be aware that most sites have a small fee which is applied to transactions where you are transferring BTC.

Hope this helps.
