Title: Use duckduckgo.com for browsing, not google
Post by: MedicineWoman on June 19, 2013, 11:30 pm
Whether you're on TorBrowser or not, switch from Google to duckduckgo.com!

When you use google, and the web site your visiting has google analytics set up, a LOT of information about you is being tracked. Read more at:
http://donttrack.us/

I am not affiliated at all with duckduckgo.com, just amazed at how many folks don't know about it yet.

Google, Microsoft, F-book, and many other companies share information with governments the world over. Not duckduckgo!
