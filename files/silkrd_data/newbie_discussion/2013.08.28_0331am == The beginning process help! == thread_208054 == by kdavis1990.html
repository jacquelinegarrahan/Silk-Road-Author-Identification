Title: Re: The beginning process help!
Post by: ds on August 29, 2013, 05:25 pm
LE will be able to look at your bank account and figure out that you've purchased bitcoins, but it's not illegal to purchase bitcoins.

They won't be able to show that you've deposited them to SR because SR "tumbles" coins on the way in.

If LE asks why you bought bitcoins you can say you wanted to donate to a cause/website that accepts bitcoin donations, or you lost a bet to an online friend and paid him in bitcoins or whatever.

If they somehow know you deposited bitcoins to an SR account, you can say you just dumped to SR and out to a wallet again to make use of the tumbler.
