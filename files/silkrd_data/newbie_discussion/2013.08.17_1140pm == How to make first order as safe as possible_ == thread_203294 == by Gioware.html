Title: Re: How to make first order as safe as possible?
Post by: a1b2c3 on August 18, 2013, 09:57 am
To be fair it really is a case of using common sense.

There isn't really much more information anyone can tell you (unless I'm very much mistaken) that's going to make it safer for you.
Do your research on your vendor in the forums, as well as his feedback page.  Make sure positive feedback is UP TO DATE - plenty vendors once were good and then turn bad.
Dont believe a vendors hype - of course theyre going to tell you their stuff is good and bla bla bla.
People say NEVER finalize early and I think that's a good rule of thumb however some vendors will refuse to sell you without FE if your a noob.  So try not to FE (personally there are some vendors Id take a chance on - but for the cautious Id say dont)

As has been mentioned, buy locally so customs are cut out.
Apart from common sense things like this there really aren't any magical tips that will guarantee everything goes perfect, but Id say you stand a very good chance of getting what you ordered and not getting busted, buying from a high ranking local vendor with good up to date feed back in forum and on his page. - Good Luck  :)
