Title: Re: Newbie wanting to try marijuana
Post by: JoeHartley on February 01, 2013, 08:42 am
Hey,
First time I tried smoking weed it was through a joint. I can see why some people would recommend a pipe for a first timer, as rolling a joint oneself takes a bit of practice. However, as others have said, in my opinion the smoke from a pipe is too harsh for a beginner, and may have you hacking-up a lung which could put you off the idea of smoking bud for a while. A joint is a nice way to start off as its a smoother smoke, and if you feel you're getting too high you can simply put it out, just as you can stop hitting a pipe. Plus, a single hit off a joint is much less strong than a hit off a pipe, so a joint may allow you to ease into the high more smoothly, and not get hit in the face by it ;)

Whatever you do, definitely don't try a bong first; it's too difficult to measure the size of your hit and will definitely have you coughing like an idiot! At the end of the day you don't have to worry too much, it's definitely not going to kill you no matter how much you smoke. Oh, and as someone already said, a sativa strain is better for a first-timer as it's less 'stoney', but an indica will do if you can't get the former.

Good luck,
