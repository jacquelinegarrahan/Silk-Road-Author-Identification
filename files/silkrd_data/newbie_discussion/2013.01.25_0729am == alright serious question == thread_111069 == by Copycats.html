The standard BTC client as invented by "Satoshi Nakamoto" is completely open source, as is the whole protocol. When Mr. Nakamoto (ifthatisyourrealname) released the software, he also released a pdf of an academic paper detailing its design.

The source code and this design paper are basically the most intense Bitcoin-related material you can dive into. They can both be found at the SourceForge page for Bitcoin (where it was originally released) here:

http://sourceforge.net/projects/bitcoin/

Just click the Files tab.
