Title: Re: Dugs in Mail
Post by: stenr on August 05, 2013, 05:20 am
Just don't sign for anything that you aren't expecting to sign for and you won't have any problems. They can't prove you ordered the drugs as long as you take the proper precautions. (encrypt your address with pgp when ordering, only order from reputable vendors ect) If you buy domestic, the chances of any problems (package being seized) significantly decreases as your package doesn't have to go through customs. If your package does get seized, whether or not you get a controlled delivery will depend on the contents and the amount. If you are only ordering personal amounts, you have very little to worry about.

Hope this helps, and good luck!
