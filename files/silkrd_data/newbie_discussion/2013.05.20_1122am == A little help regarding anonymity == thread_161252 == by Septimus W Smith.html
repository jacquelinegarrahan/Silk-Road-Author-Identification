I think you r being paranoid. I was like that too my first time, but once you get into the system you start to realize how safe it is.

After 15 successful buys this is my system. I get on localbitcoins, find a reputed vendor online, bank transfer to his account, get the coins. The whole process takes 15 mins max with a good vendor. Then I transfer the coins to my bitcoinqt wallet ( i dont trust any web service to hold my coins). Then SR will tumble it for me and at this point the coins cant be traced back to your account.

Simple, fast. Yes there is a very remote chance that someone could connect the dots but can you see how much resources would take for LE to do that? do you really think they can be bothered for a small amount of drugs? I dont think so.
