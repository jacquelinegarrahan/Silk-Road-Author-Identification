Hi Minny,

Which Bitcoin client are you using? If it is a desktop based client then the reason that the transaction was only sent today is because the client has to sync with either the full blockchain (in the case of the bitcoin-qt client) or a partial blockchain (in the case of MultiBit and a few others). Your client only caught up with the block that your transaction was included in at 2:32pm.

It can take up to 24 hours for your Bitcoin to reach your Silk Road account, however it normally takes a lot less time than that.
If your BTC is not in your Silk Road account within 24 hours, feel free to send a message to SR Support on the main site when it is back up with the details of your transfer so as they can figure out where it is and manually credit it to you.

Silk Road is currently under a DDOS attack, and this kind of limited access is unprecedented in Silk Road's history. It has been online since February 2011 with minimal down-time (~3%), meaning in the 26 months it has been in existence it has been up for approximately 25.22 of those.

Dread Pirate Roberts will continue to persevere in his/her efforts to get the site back up, and has a contingency plan in place which you can read more about here:
