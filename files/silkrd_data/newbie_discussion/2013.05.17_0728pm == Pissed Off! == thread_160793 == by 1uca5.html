When I leave feedback I do so in a way that that helps other potential buyers with similar tastes gather appropriate information; the same information I would look for when researching vendors.

While researching vendors, I personally never spent much time reading the 5/5 rankings.  With the exception of the lengthier ones, the 5/5 generally seem to be lack detail and are very homogenous.  Instead I look for the lower ranked reviews.  To me, this paints a much better idea of what I might be getting into or what someone can expect on a bad day.  It also helps give perspective to that particular vendor.
