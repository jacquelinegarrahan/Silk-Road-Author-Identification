Post by: sleeptight on June 03, 2013, 11:58 am
Quote from: world wide what on June 03, 2013, 11:40 am
How long does an order ususallly stay in 'Processing' for?

If it is a large vendor, it usally just takes 1-2 business days, but maybe your vendor has specific shipment days, you can look that up in his profile info. It is also possible that your vendor requiers you to FE, due to you buyer statistics.

These are things that you should look up before you order, so always read the info on the vendors page.

If none of this applies I would wait another 2 days and then contact the vendor.
