Post by: GlenRunciter on July 02, 2013, 09:00 am
Well first of all I'm a little dubious of what quite constitutes as "speed" around here.  The word  'speed'  to me is a very loose, generic term for any upper. (other than coke)  I know others have more precise definitions.  So the exact ingredients in your "speed" will tell us a lot about what to expect.   But since we don't know the actual components, we will have to talk in general terms.   Generally speaking, what you described sounds very typical; you had a generally average response to the "speed" in my opinion.   The feelings of re-dosing will be evident and hard to ignore.  For instance, if I had a bag of speed I would likely finish it off over the course of 12-14 hours, knowing I wouldn't be sleeping that night.   That's fine, I go into it with that mentality.  Coming down with benzos and alcohol is also very standard (although I hate to encourage anybody to mix benzos and booze, just a rough combination in excess.)    You also experienced vasoconstriction as you so acutely observed, an extremely common side effect.  To remedy this I usually take an aspirin which works as a blood thinner, mainly as a preventative measure.  Lots of water intake should help too.  I would strongly discourage the taking of beta-blockers, as studies have shown they do not play nice with stimulant compounds.   However, alpha-blockers would be fine, and I would keep some around for an emergency.    All in all, it sounds like you had a very typical speed experience, and handled yourself nicely......cheers, man.   8)    (My opinion is based on my own years of usage and that of my friends....others on the thread may have completely different viewpoints on this.)
