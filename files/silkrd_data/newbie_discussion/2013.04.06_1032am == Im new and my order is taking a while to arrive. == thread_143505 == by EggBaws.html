Hi EggBaws, welcome to the joys of SR :) Very glad to hear your five other orders came through, that's a great start. As to your question it would really depend on a few things; Have you ordered from overseas or domestically? Has this vendor given you a time frame as to when it should arrive? You can place the order in resolution anytime in the seventeen days, and that also includes an option to extend the period in which the funds stay in escrow, but try not to let it auto-finalise. Good luck, AB.
