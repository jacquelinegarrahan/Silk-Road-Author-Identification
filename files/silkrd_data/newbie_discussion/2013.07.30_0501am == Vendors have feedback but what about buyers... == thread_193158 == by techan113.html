Title: Re: Vendors have feedback but what about buyers...
Post by: postrex on July 30, 2013, 06:20 am
This is definitely an awesome feature about ATL.  However, buyer stats are difficult in that it is quite easy to ditch a buyer account after a bad review.  Plus, we want to encourage people to retain the same buyer account to at least some extent.  A single bad review on a buyer profile is likely to scare a hell of a lot more vendors away when compared to say a 5% refund rate.  Buyers feel safe keeping the same accounts here because their buyer stats aren't revealed unless they initiate a transaction.  And even more so, it's numeric and impersonal data.

Anyway... I think SR's lack of buyer feedback works well.  It makes it much more of a market place and less of an ebay.  Atlantis is a lot more like ebay then a marketplace at the moment, but that may change as it continues to grow and age (and the vendor buy-ins increase).
