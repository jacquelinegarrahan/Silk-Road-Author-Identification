I think vendors do this to stop anxious newb customers drawing attention to the package by checking its tracking status every ten minutes, through Tor, especially.  The tracking is to protect vendor from fraudulent no show claims mostly, to prove that it was sent, not to reassure the customer. The customer has the protection of escrow.
