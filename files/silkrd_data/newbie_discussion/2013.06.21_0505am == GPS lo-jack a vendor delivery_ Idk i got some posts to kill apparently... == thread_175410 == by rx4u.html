Post by: thcaddict on June 21, 2013, 10:37 am
the only time the anonymity seems to fade is when you are trusting the vendor with your name and your address. But this is the safest bet to make sure that you received the package. Using fake names, abandoned houses, or neighbors address is only asking for trouble and it immediately becomes very suspicious. Keep it simple, use your real name and address and it makes nothing looks suspicious and everything looks legitimate especially if the stealth is done correctly. Make sure you don't send your name and address to your vendor in plain text. Please read to the security forum and read up about PGP encryption so you can learn to encrypt your messages to your vendor when making purchases. This insures that only the recipient you want to view the message is going to be the one reading it. The security subforum has tons of information and if you spend a little bit of time there I'm sure you'll learn quite a bit more than you anticipate.
