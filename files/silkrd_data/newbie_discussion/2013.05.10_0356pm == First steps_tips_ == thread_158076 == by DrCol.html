Title: Re: First steps/tips?
Post by: luxuries on May 10, 2013, 04:24 pm
I believe the usual way of getting the ball to roll is to offer free or cheap samples, preferably to people who have been here for a while (ie. not a newbie like me  :) ). It's not great, but people will be reluctant to trade with you as long as there is no info about your product and practices and so on. It's also a good way of sorting out any kinks with your packaging and such before you really get going.
