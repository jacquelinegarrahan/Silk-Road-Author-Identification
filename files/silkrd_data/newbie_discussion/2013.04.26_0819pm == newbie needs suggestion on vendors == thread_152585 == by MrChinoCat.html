Title: Re: newbie needs suggestion on vendors
Post by: heavysmoker on April 28, 2013, 12:02 am
Alot of vendors ask for (require) FE because they have been burned by scammers in the past.  If you do decide to go with a vendor that requires FE i would suggest making a couple smaller orders first.  That way you can gain a little of the vendors trust and any good vendor will allow you to stay in escrow for larger purchases in the future. 

I wouldnt worry about the required 5/5 either.  If a vendor does something that would warrant a rating of lower than 5/5, chances are you wont want to do business with them in the future anyways.
