Title: Re: Websites for drug production?
Post by: jdtram27 on February 22, 2013, 12:46 am
So, there are plenty of places you can find synthesis's. The real problem is getting reagents. Sure you may find some precursors but you will be damn hard pressed to have everything you need. MDA and the like are much more difficult to synthesize than the nasty meth you see getting cooked inside of walmart on the news.

Its pretty clear that you dont possess the general or organic chemistry knowledge to even know what to search for to find the synthesis you are after. Plus, the synthesis that is actually available to you will most likely not be spelled out in some paper on say.. rhodium or the hive or sciencemadness. You have to know what kind of reaction you need to perform etc and then look those up individually. There is also a huge problem that 99.9% of people do not even realize exists.. Paper chemistry is really easy.. Once you get into the lab and start putting dollar signs on everything and start realizing that many reactions are difficult to get to go to more than a 50% or so yield. The financial loss is compounded by the fact that you now have to SEPARATE your products from any side reaction products, as well as any unreacted starting materials.. Which only means more solvents, etc.. Which all gets difficult to remove without a rotovap etc..

 Chemistry is a rather difficult to subject to master, and without a university education(and chemical connects), one will most likely find failure in their path, hopefully not one that ends with an explosion in your face.
