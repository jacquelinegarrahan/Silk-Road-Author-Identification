Title: Re: When buying...
Post by: CLK on September 11, 2013, 10:34 pm
Put your address in the space provided, click order, then wait for the vendor to accept the order (on the order page at the top), and finalze once recieved on the same orders page.
DO NOT finalize early unless the vendor is 100% trusted, many try to scam newbs like me & you and when you FE you lose your chance for a refund and protection from the SR staff etc. Be safe!
