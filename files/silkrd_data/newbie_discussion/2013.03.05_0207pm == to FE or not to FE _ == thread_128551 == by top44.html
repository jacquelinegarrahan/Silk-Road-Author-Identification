Title: Re: to FE or not to FE ?
Post by: ANewLife on March 05, 2013, 03:12 pm
Well my personal story,

I have bought a variety of products in the last 15 months (about 45 in total) of which I FE'd 3 times.
2 times I lost my money and had no recourse to be able to get my money back and the other one took forever (about 6 weeks and I still wasn't really satisfied with the outcome) to be sorted out.
Every other transaction I have done without FEing, have all worked out professionally & efficiently. If a vendor insists on FE, there is probably a reason and guaranteed it won't be in your favor. You can't get much help from SR, because you have finalised the transaction.
If you are looking to save 10%-15% to buy from someone insisting on FE, you need to weigh up the person and their operation and decide if it is really worth it.

But even some of the highest rated Vendors (ETM, MIMM, Nevita) have been well known for pulling scams on unsuspecting clients.
 Escrow is there for a reason. The sooner everyone refuses to FE, the sooner SR can develop a great marketplace otherwise it will just be another BMR.
