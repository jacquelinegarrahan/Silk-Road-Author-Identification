As a vendor, and a new one, all I can  say is that processing an order is completely up to the vendor.  My own philosophy as someone who has been in this business off the road for decades is to follow your instincts, if an order does not feel right, or anything brings doubt, it should be canceled.  I am not saying this is the case with your issue, just offering one viewpoint on the issue.  I would continue to talk to the vendors you do business with and not completely shut the door, there is no telling what circumstances could be playing a role there.
