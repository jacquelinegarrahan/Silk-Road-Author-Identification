I haven't experienced any cases like this tbh, but I'd be really surprised by such bad manners. In general all the vendors i've been in contact with are really nice people who wants the best for their customers. There might be some assholes out there, but this is what the resolve option is for. If you feel unfairly treated you can always take it up with SR Support, and they will have the last word on whether you were treated fair or not, and make sure you get your refund if the vendor refuses.

Also, after you've made a purchase, your bitcoins will stay in escrow until you release it to the vendor, which you do by finalizing the order, and if you respect the system (as in, don't finalize early ...you'll see alot of people using FE, which means you hand the bitcoins over to the vendor BEFORE you've got the product), you will have full control of your money until you've got the product.

So as far as I'm aware, no vendors will have any interest in scamming you unless they want a very short life here on SR.
