Title: Re: Why is weed so expensive here?
Post by: VaultTec2277 on September 30, 2013, 04:11 am
In my part of the world SR prices are considerably lower than most of my friends are accustomed to paying.
In fact, the only reason I don't get my weed through the SR is because I lucked out and hooked up with a guy who gives me a bulk discount, his prices match SR for the most part, but I can have my weed the same day.

The reason that prices increase with bulk is because it's less work, and you can move more product with less risk.

This isn't exactly a novel concept. It's just how retail business works.

If you hold out for only selling small amounts at market value you have better profit margins, but you move less product by only attracting the end user. If you sell bulk cheaper then there is room for private retailers to sell your product elsewhere at a mark-up.
