This is what a lot of people call "WoW" blotter (no not World of Warcraft), White on White meaning White LSD crystal on white paper.  Seeing as how you didn't know it was paper one would assume this will be your first time taking acid?  Assuming it is legit, prepare yourself for a strong, long lasting substance.  Once its "on", its on for a long time, and much like mushrooms, there is no off switch :P  Plan to take it on a period of time of no stress, and in a comfortable environment.  After the trip you will have learned something introspectively, whatever it may be, you probably won't ever forget it.
