Quote from: Mitsubishi Man on March 28, 2013, 07:20 pm
What are benzos exactly? I could do with something to get me asleep after MDMA. Are they safe?
[/quote benzo is the layman term for benzodiazepines, they are a class of sedatives that have a variety of effects. the most common ones are xanax (alpazolam) valium (diazepam) klonopin (clonazepam) restoril (temazepam) ativan (lorazepam) and the most infamous of all rohypnol (flunitrazepam) these are just the most common there are close to 100. they are very safe in moderation but are physically addictive long term. temazepam is my personal choice for stimulant comedowns as it is one of the more sedating and hypnotic benzos.
