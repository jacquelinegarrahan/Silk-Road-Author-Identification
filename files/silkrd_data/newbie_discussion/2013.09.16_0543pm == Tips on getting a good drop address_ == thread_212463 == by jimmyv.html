Title: Re: Tips on getting a good drop address?
Post by: anonypunk on September 17, 2013, 08:18 pm
If you think of it like it's supposed to appear then there should be no worries.

Think of it like you are getting a post office box. Get a big one if you can afford it but most small orders will be folded in half and fit nicely if it comes in a priority envelope
Don't order from overseas so you'll never have to worry about customs. I'd personally Never use my friends addresses since really all they have to say is that they haven't seen the package.

Most of all. Get your security straight on your end and don't let anyone (else) know that you buy drugs online. That's fucking stupid.
