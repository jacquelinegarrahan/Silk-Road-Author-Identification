Title: Re: Interest check: 5 tabs of GANDALF 150UG LSD for 50 domestic shipped.
Post by: Sooperknot on May 25, 2013, 06:40 pm
For blotters, I don't want express shipping.  IMO the best thing is a plain flat envelope that looks exactly like a letter.  Because it IS a letter.

The absence of tracking does create some problems for the seller.  You can still deal with ripoff buyers by blacklisting them and tarnishing their rep.  Lower postage costs, partially passed on to the consumer, will compensate you for some of the inevitable losses.

It's emotionally difficult to just let it go when somebody burns you, but from a business perspective sometimes it's the most profitable thing to do.
