Post by: tree on July 02, 2013, 10:53 pm
I'd use PGP every time you have to give your address and avoid vendors with keys smaller than 2048bits because they're not really secure anymore (and also avoid vendors that don't use PGP). Use gpg4usb for PGP, it's really the best.
Don't finalize early (FE), it's not a security issue but you're way more likely to get scammed that way. You should encrypt your hard drive with truecrypt too, and keep any silkroad related documents encrypted.
TAILS is not necessary but it's safer than using TBB on windows or on other OSs, you don't absolutely have to use it but it's a lot better. If you want even more security, use whonix over linux.. It's more complicated than TAILS but even more secure. Forbid scripts globally on torbrowser is a good thing to do too.

To optimize PGP and to use it anonymously, add the following to your gpg.conf file :
throw-keyid
trust-model always
utf8-strings
no-greeting
no-emit-version
no-comments
no-mdc-warning
armor
