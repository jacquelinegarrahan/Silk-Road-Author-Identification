Title: Re: I thought I would be ordering within a couple of hours after downloading tor
Post by: rxsunshine on March 28, 2013, 11:18 pm
dl gpg4win and make sure to check gpa on the installation. then when dl, open gpa and set make your pgp key. then you just copy the vendor pgp and import it from clipboard. then check the vendor pgp and type your address. then click encrypt. then copy and paste that in your address box on your order.
btc, you need to set up an account with mtgox. but first make a tormail email account to set up your mtgox account with. after you make a mtgox account, go to bitinstant and make a deposit to walmart or cvs or other local location. use your real name bc this is not illegal in any way and a fake name to do something legal is just asking for problems. then it wil tell you to print out your deposit slip. take it to walmart and go to money center and pay them. they will send it to your mtgox account. if they ask, tell them you are paying a bill. your money will be in your mtgox account before you get home. then transfer it to an instawallet. remember to save your instawallet address and key. VERY IMPORTANT!!
i usually send it through a couple instawallets before i send it to my sr account.
hope this helps you out man. i think its accurate to the best of my memory right off hand.
