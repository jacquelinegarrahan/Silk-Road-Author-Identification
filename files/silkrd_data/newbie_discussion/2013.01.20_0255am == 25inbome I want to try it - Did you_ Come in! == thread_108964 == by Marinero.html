Title: Re: 25inbome I want to try it - Did you? Come in!
Post by: strangeman on January 22, 2013, 09:37 pm
I have a fair bit of experience with LSD and I've recently tripped twice on 25i. There are some similarities in the experiences: the visuals tend to be comparable in terms of complexity and their fractal quality, though I found most form constants on 25i to be relatively transparent, whereas I've had shit completely block out my vision and consume my entire consciousness on LSD. I once had a concert poster come right out of it's frame on LSD, and then I fell forward (felt like falling down a well) into and through it. I've haven't yet experienced anything that immersive with 25i.

LSD feels "harder" as well in terms of the level of intoxication you experience. 25i is more functional, I find I can get things done when I need to without having to fight the drug. I'm pretty functional on LSD, too, but complex actions require an immense amount of discipline and willpower. I can just kind of ignore 25i while I get shit done.

25i has a reputation for not being very deep, but I think a lot of it comes down to the user. LSD is pushier, it will force you to face yourself in ways 25i won't necessarily. Still, I've found I alternate between introspective, even dark, moods on 25i and a more laughing, "fuck it" feeling. I've had some interesting insights on 25i, usually at the tail-end of the trip, and I think the lightness of the headspace might be good for exploring more painful or anxiety ridden issues for some people than LSD.

Oh, and I'd be careful about going solo (especially with high dosage) on 25i until you know how it treats you. A lot of people out there have had nasty side effects (including death), and nobody really knows why yet. I suspect some of these Chinese labs are pushing dirty synthes with toxic by-products, but it also could be that 25i is just dangerous for people with certain genetic make-ups or health conditions. I've never had any problems beyond very minor vasoconstriction (tingly feet, numb face), but it would be a good idea to have somebody on hand to call 911 if you stroke out or some shit.
