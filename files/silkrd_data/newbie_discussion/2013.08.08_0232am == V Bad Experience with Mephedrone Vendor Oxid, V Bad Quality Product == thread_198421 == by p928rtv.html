Post by: p928rtv on August 08, 2013, 02:32 am
I recently purchased 5gms of mephedrone from vendor Oxid. Based on my experience, I strongly recommend against dealing with him. In summary:
* He sent me product that was not mephedrone, and that was extremely weak and had little effect
* When I gave the product a poor evaluation of 1 in 5, he was most abusive,  he falsely accused me of refusing to FE, and he refused to discuss my complaint.

In part, what I learn from this is that in future, I should try the product before finalizing. For anyone interested, more details are below, along with the message exchange I had with Oxid.


THE PROBLEM IN MORE DETAIL

I purchased 5gms of mephedrone from Oxid. It arrived promptly, and I then finalized. Upon trying the mephedrone some hours later, I found it wasn't mephedrone. I am familiar with the drug, and closely related cathinones, and am certain about this. I took a 200 mg dose, and it only had a very weak effect. It was certainly not as advertized: "pure Mephedrone 99.8%. The purest explosive quality on SR."

I therefore posted a score of 1 in 5, noting the prompt delivery, and the poor quality of the product ordered. I immediately received an abusive response from Oxid. He accused me of being a scammer, a dishonest person, a blackmailer, a bullshitter, and so on. He also said he had alerted other vendors to me, telling them I should be avoided because I had refused to FE (which is false, he never asked me to FE), and that I am a "waste man". He said that I must correct or remove my bad score, otherwise he would contact SR staff. He made no attempt to address my concern about the product.

In our subsequent correspondence (see below), he continued to be abusive, and to avoid addressing my underlying concern about product quality.

Oxid has unusually high satisfaction scores (perhaps the best of any mephedrone vendor), and comments made about him are perhaps unusually short. It may be that he is gaming the system in some way.

I believe that Oxid's response to me was wholly inappropriate, that he sold me bad product, and that his product descriptions are misleading and inaccurate.

p928rtv
