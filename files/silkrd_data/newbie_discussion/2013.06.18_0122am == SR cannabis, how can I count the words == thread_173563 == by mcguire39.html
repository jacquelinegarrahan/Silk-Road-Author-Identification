Title: SR cannabis, how can I count the words
Post by: mcguire39 on June 18, 2013, 01:22 am
Picked up some sour diesel. Sheesh, this stuff is powerful. I think it's mostly sativa and it definitely shows that. I remember back in the day we would buy 'nugget' weed, and it was pretty good, but man two small rips off this sour diesel and I am high as a kite. After you learn to just relax and let go with it, it clears way into a surreal state of mind. You can examine minute details of a problem or your past in great detail. Especially those little memories from many years ago that you may have tucked away in a corner of the brain. That can be good or bad. But pleasant memories of your past can just linger in the mind, and it's almost like reliving the moment. Even if your eyes are open you can still see the image.

Oh man Neil Young Down by the River just came on. I've always liked that song but this time I got a warm chill, if that's possible, all over my body and I felt as though I were floating a little. Damn this is awesome.
