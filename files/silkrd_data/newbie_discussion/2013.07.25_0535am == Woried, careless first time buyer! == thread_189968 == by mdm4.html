Title: Re: Woried, careless first time buyer!
Post by: kimlee on July 25, 2013, 05:47 am
Im fairly new but from what Ive been learning, you want to stay as anonymous as possible (obviously).  So, you shouldn't be using your bank account to buy bitcoins.  Although bitcoins are legal, using them sends up a red flag or could be used against you later.  Its doubtful your local law enforcement has the resources to hunt you down over 5g, but you never know. USPS money orders seems to be the best choice because you can make up a name and address (although the address has to be real) and they are much cheaper than money grams.

When purchasing, make sure you will not have to sign for the package, because the only way you can get in trouble with Johnny Law is if you sign for and accept the package.  So, if you order and you're sure you wont have to sign for it and there's a mailman trying to get you to sign, refuse the package and eat the money.  Otherwise, you should be good.

Im sure there are some veterans who might say Im wrong on some of this but this is what Ive picked up so far. Enjoy you're package.
