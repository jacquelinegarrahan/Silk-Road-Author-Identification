Starting with the type of weed you're looking for, there are two main catagories: Indica and Sativa (& many hybrids of the two).  For pain, you probably want and Indica which is generally described as a "body" high whereas Sativa is more generally described as a "head" high.  Good hybrids hit you with both.  Asking a vendor which catagory their product fits in is a good way to know what you're getting, or look for anything Kush, as Kushes are mostly Indica and best for your pain management.

For a n00b, I'd recommend (Clearnet Warning) leafly.com/explore.  This site has a large variety of marijuana strands and aggregates the reviews of multiple users to describe the effects.  You can even look for strands based on certain effects such as pain management and causing sleepiness. 

Never ordered bud from SR, so I can't offer you any vendor advice.  There are many ways to get BTC.  I'd suggest doing a search on how to get one & you can decide which method you'd like to go with.  Some are more anonymous than others. 

To minimize the smell, edibles would be your best bet.  It's not the easiest to make your own, but there is a plethora of information out there on how to make cannabutter that you can then replace regular butter in any recipe.  Other options I met recommend is a going to your local headshop and looking into a vaporizor.  It leaves a smell that is commonly described as burnt popcorn, far from weed smell.  A vape also maximizes the THC you get from the bud.  It's a good economical method.

Post by: 1682_anon on August 15, 2013, 01:44 pm
I can't really advise about who you should buy from or how to get BTC, but I can tell you about how to use weed.

 Firstly you should get a bong and a herb grinder. In my country they are available at adult stores, but you can also get them on SR. I recommend a double-chambered bong. It filters and cools the smoke better.

Start by filling the chambers of the bong about half to two-thirds full with fresh, cold water. Doing this will give you much nicer smoke and avoid the harsh throat hit.

 Next, grind up some weed with an equal amount of tobacco. It doesn't need to be perfect, just roughly equal parts. You can use cigarettes or rolling tobacco, whichever brand/strength you prefer.

A dose of pot smoked through a bong is called a cone, because of the thimble-like gadget that you pack the drugs into. Pack the cone, get a lighter, ignite it, and hold it at a downward angle towards the cone. If your bong has a small hole in the neck, cover it with your thumb. Inhale. Keep the lighter ignited. You'll probably cough the first few times, but soon you'll get the hang of it.

As you inhale, the drugs with burn up. You'll see the smoke bubbling through the water. When the cone is empty, release your thumb from the hole on the neck of the bong. This will make you inhale the remaining smoke. Hold it in your lungs for a few seconds and then exhale.

I recommend just starting with one cone at a time, as more than that is likely to make you nauseous the first few times (it did for me).

Occasionally you will need to clean the bong, particularly as the cone and stem get gummed up with resin and shit.

Hope this helps. :)
