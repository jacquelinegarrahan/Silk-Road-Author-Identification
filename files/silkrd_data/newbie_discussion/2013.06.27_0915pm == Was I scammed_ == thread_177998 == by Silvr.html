Title: Re: Was I scammed?
Post by: captainjojo on June 27, 2013, 11:32 pm
If your order does go to resolution because the order never arrives, either you or the vendor indicate what % of a refund you will be getting.  If you propose 75% the vendor can either accept it or reply back with a lower % refund.  This goes on until you both agree on a number.

If you cannot agree on a number and it ends up going to SR, unless there are unusual circumstances, they will usually go with the refund % that the vendor states on their profile.  If the vendor has no stated refund policy SR will probably go 50/50 unless they believe the vendor scammed you. Simply not receiving an order that wasn't tracked isn't enough to say that the vendor was scamming you.

To be honest, you should always go with tracking, it's better for both parties.
