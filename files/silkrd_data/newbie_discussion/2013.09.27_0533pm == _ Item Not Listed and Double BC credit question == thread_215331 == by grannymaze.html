I had to reply as Ayeseadee1080 is one of the top vendors on the road. He is my go to guy and has never let me down.

First, not a good idea to put items in your cart until you are ready to purchase (btc already in your account) as things can change while you're waiting for your deposit to go through. What usually happens is the item you put in your cart is still fair game for someone else until you place the order and if the item is in your cart and it's the last one then they get it because they are ordering now.

Second, ayeseadee1080 is probably the most honest guy on SR. He was my very first vendor because when I sent out PMs looking for help as a newbie he was the only one who replied who wasn't rude or sarcastic. Walked me through the process, answered all my questions and took my order (my very first SR order) without making me FE. When delivery was delayed because of USPS I PM'd him and he immediately tracked the package and gave me it's location and status. He has rave reviews going back his start on SR a long time ago. If I had to pick one vendor on SR for trust it would be him.
If it says "No active listings" on his profile page that means something has happened and he had to go somewhere or do something and he has taken down his listings so orders don't come in and sit around waiting for processing.  I just looked at his profile page and that is the situation. There must have been an emergency. He will get back to you as soon as he gets back I'm sure. Don't worry you didn't get ripped.

Yes the exchange vendor who paid you twice would be out of luck if you chose not to return the second payment. But you don't want to start out on SR by getting a rep as a scuzball. You should return the payment and keep everything in balance. For the most part we look out for each other here, that's part of the reason for the forums. Do the right thing.
