Post by: goldenrod on May 21, 2013, 05:35 am
It's an operating system you can store on a USB and when you start your computer it boots from this. It is used because it leaves no traces on the hard drive of your computer. Normally if you delete stuff from your computer it never really disappears so this stuff can be retrieved if your computer were seized. But using Tails when you close down your computer anything you used this OS for is gone forever.
