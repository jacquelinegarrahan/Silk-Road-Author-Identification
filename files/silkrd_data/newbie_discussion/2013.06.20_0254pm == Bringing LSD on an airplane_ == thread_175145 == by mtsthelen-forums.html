Post by: TriedItAll on June 25, 2013, 12:23 am
I have brought acid on planes as well as other drugs.

For LSD, it is by far the easiest.  Carry it in your pocket, in your sock, in a book, in an envelope, it really will not attract attention because it is paper.  TSA is concerned with metallic and sharp objects, liquids over a certain amount, gels, and things of this nature.  They are not there to hunt down and confiscate your LSD and it is highly unlikely that they would think twice under the xray and they certainly wouldnt feel it in a pat down.

For Cannabis, it is certainly more risky due to its size and smell, but I have successfully put 1/8's under my balls or near the privates where they dont really grab with any force in a pat down.  Key is to also wrap it up so that it won't emit any suspicious weed smells.  Probably would do the same with shrooms

Things in powder of any kind, I would say the same thing, OR, even better and safer, if you have a 5HTP bottle or a vitamin bottle or anything that comes with capsules that you can remove the contents from and then fill it with your product, that is really nice.  Then you just stick the pills back in the bottle with the other legit pills or just your product pills.  There is no way in hell they would detect that or ever ask to rifle through each individual pill in a vitamin pill bottle...at least HIGHLY unlikely.

If you can, try to put these types of things in your checked luggage as they get way less scrutiny.

It is never a guarantee, but these steps are probably in the 90-95% success rate if you are not stupid about it.

Good luck to you.
