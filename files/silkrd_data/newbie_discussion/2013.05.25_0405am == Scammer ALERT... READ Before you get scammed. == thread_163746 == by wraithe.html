Title: Scammer ALERT... READ Before you get scammed.
Post by: wraithe on May 25, 2013, 04:05 am
Tip for Newbies NEVER FE.  Ever.   If you FE and get scammed, SR cannot help you.  If a vendor is asking you to FE I ask that you post in this forum below so the community can look into it

FE = Finalizing Early,  You should only Finalize when you receive the product.  SCam Vendors will offer you free shipping, discount, or more product. DO NOT FALL FOR THIS!

Report any suspicious vendors to SR using the REPORT VENDOR, it also helps to alert the community below is a thread I have for exposing potential scams.

http://dkn255hz262ypmii.onion/index.php?topic=162720.0

trusted vendors in the past have turned scammer.  When you FE, you take that risk.  Are you gonna get scammed for FEing for a trusted vendor? likely no, but in the event you do you lost all Resolution Options with SR.
