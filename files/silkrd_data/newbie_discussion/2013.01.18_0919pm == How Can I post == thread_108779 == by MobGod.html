Hmm, well this is a newbie forum so it does seem us more experienced "newbies" should help out.

Well if you have things you want to sell on the actual silk road website, you have to first make an account there. This isn't the SR website, go to the SR website. Make an account. Then you have to purchase a vendor account which I believe costs $500. After you do that, you will be able to list your drugs and such. But only serious vendors should purchase a vendor account, as the price is high. Only purchase one if you plan on selling consistently and not just because you have 10 or so things to get rid of.
