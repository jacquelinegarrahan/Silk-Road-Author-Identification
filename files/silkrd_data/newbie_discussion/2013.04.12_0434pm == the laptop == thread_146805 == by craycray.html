I think your safest bet is just to use Liberte on a usb stick for all of your actions, then stash that while you're waiting to receive.  If you want to make sure your windows machine is really clean, do a deep format and reinstall the OS.

Just because you have truecrypt DOES NOT mean you are safe. There's lots and lots of information that can still be pulled and if your encryption isn't secure enough it could be cracked in the snap of a finger if they really wanted to see what was on your hard drive. I'd recommend what hojasanta said clean your entire hard drive do a fresh install of windows and then only go on tor through a linux live usb such as Tails or Liberte.
