Quote
Yeah they sells pills and H so maybe the pill supply  is hit/miss. Other than that looks legit.

Exactly. The pill vendors sell their supply, disappear for a while and then come back. It sucks for them because it causes them to fall down in the rankings for not generating sales.

One thing though: please be careful with anyone claiming to sell H here. H vendors are the ones I've seen the most scam complaints about; I'm not sure if they are scammers or if they're just dealing with a bunch of crazy junkies (probably a little of both). Like I said, trust your gut and don't talk yourself into making a dumb choice.

If they ask you to FE, just say no. They should cancel your order. That's the first sime of a scammer; it'll say nothing on their page about FE, but you'll get an email from them asking you politely if you wouldn't mind FE'ing. Don't fall for that.
