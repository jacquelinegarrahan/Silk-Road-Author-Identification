I'm still not sure that dogs can smell shrooms but regardless use a good smell inhibitor. 3M moisture barrier bags(the metal ones) are a big plus. A little cost but in real terms you're obviously growing them so you're sitting on profit. Pick up 100 on eBay for $200 or so and you'll make it back in an ounce and change with reduced risk to your operation. Use gloves when handling anything that is going in the mail (especially if you've been printed before).

Think long term, good luck.

Some security advice for those that are new that I have read.

Finger prints/Palm prints
Your ID can be identified just from palm prints.
Do not attempt to burn or use acid on your prints. To remove your prints from your hand you need to get 3rd degree burns.
Partial prints can also be used to get you too.
I have put 10 layers of glue on my finger. Pressing the mirror with that finger after it dried.. No print. I than went and took the glued finger and rubbed it over other fingers. The oil from the other fingers got on the glue. Upon reapplying the finger to the mirror I was still able to make out a good part of the print but hard to see. I was able to make out about 80% of the print.
Medical gloves can still have a print go through the latex, also if you touch the outside of the glove it can reapply the print somewhere. I would recommended a fine cloth glove that is than covered by the medical gloves Also wipe down the gloves with an alcohol wipe to ensure no spread of prints.

Prints on paper/cardboard
From what I have gathered if you have got your prints on such objects you are shit out of luck with removing/destroying them. You can attempt to smear them but most attempts will be futile. Prints on such objects can last upwards towards 40 years.

Hardware ID/ Macadress
