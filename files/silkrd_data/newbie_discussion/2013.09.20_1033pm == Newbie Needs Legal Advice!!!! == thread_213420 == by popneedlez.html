The reason why they may have not arrested and book your friend is because the LE investigating him/her determined that your friend was not a threat to society and shows no flight risk. Now that does not mean that your friend is going to just get a slap on the wrist. Even people charged with felonies may only be issued a court date as long as they are determined non-violent and will not cause any harm to society.

Here's the best way to access how much trouble your friend is in. If the summons is for municipal court, then he is in little trouble and may only get a misdemeanor charge or a city violation. These generally result in fines and community service. Also, with the help of a lawyer they can get these charges sealed or expunged.

Now if the summons is for magistrate court, Then your friend is in bigger trouble. This court deals with felonies and jail time may be a possibility. Hopefully this isn't the case.

The lawyer is a good idea for many reasons. The main one being that they know the law, procedures, and how courts work better than you. Also, the best part that lawyers (especially good ones) have connections to politicians, law enforcement, judges, and prosecutors. They work with these people on a regular basis and may have befriended them. The benefit to this is that your lawyer will be able to put "feelers" out and direct you to take the right steps while suffering the least amount of consequences. Never get a public defender, they are usually fresh law school grads and are overworked and under appreciated. They may work up to 50 case at one time which tells you they aren't going to put in a lot of effort for you. Poor bastards, I hope they make it out of there one day but I digress.

Anywho, sorry for the late respond and I hope this info helps you and your friend. Stay safe :)
