Title: Placebo Effect on SR
Post by: SimpleSky on September 21, 2013, 06:26 pm
I see several listings where a fair percentage of the buyers are complaining about bunk material while others exclaim that it's the best dish they've ever licked.
Taking into account the placebo effect, and given that 39% of taken placebo ends in an expected effect (especially pain related effects - aka opiates) I'm highly skeptical of these listings. I know a few people who've even taken bunk acid and had a trip.
I'm fairly certain that most people are getting the same product. I doubt that these dealers have a stash of bunk and a stash of live-lead and play eenie-meenie everytime they send a shipment.
What do you guys think? Is it possible for a seller to put a purely bunk product up and get the 39% 5.0 because of the placebo effect? Think they scammers might even be betting on it?
It's almost my nature to see a few bunk claims and a few "amazing stuff" claims and give the seller the benefit of the doubt. However, statistically - we should be thinking about the placebo effect and consider that it's all bunk, the amazing stuff is just amazing because of placebo.
