Title: Re: Anyone do sports betting with bitcoin?
Post by: SmokesHisBroccoli on June 13, 2013, 09:41 pm
Yeah they accept only bitcoins and payout in only bitcoins.  It does take a number of confirmations in the blockchain before you'll see the bitcoins though.  So if you want to bet the 9:00 EST NBA game tonight, you'd have to move quick to send bitcoins to your account because it takes about 3 or so hours when xfering coins in and out there.  When the game is over they are pretty quick to payout your bets too.  If you're betting a ton of games like I was a few months back you'll notice they payout in waves.  Like on a college basketball or football saturday if you make 15-20 bets like I was (I don't recommend this, just pick a few and bet em big), I would notice some games were over for an hour or two before I'd get credit for the win.  They're not as quick as a typical book with payouts.  There were a number of times when I had my entire balance pending in bets and wasn't able to bet a later game because they hadn't credited my earlier bets even though the games had been over for a good hour or two.
