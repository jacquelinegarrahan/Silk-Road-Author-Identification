Title: Re: How long is a deposit supposed to take?
Post by: I_hate_scammers on April 27, 2013, 09:38 pm
Quote from: Llama Socks on April 27, 2013, 05:54 pm
Made a deposit a couple of minutes ago and it isn't showing up yet. How long do they usually take?

What kind of deposit?

A cash deposit to somewhere like BitInstant? Within 1-2 hours or less.

A bitcoin transfer? Could take around 1 hour or more, depending on network traffic. You can enter the btc address you transferred the coins to into blockchain.info and it will tell you the status of the transaction and how many confirmations it's got. When you hit 6 confirmations, it should be complete.

I've had transfers take over 2 hours when the network is very busy.

Did you pay the 0.0005 btc processing fee? It's not required but it does give you priority in processing (or so I'm told). It still takes a good 45 min to a hour with that.

EDIT: That's on average. I have had to wait overnight for a transfer to complete, but that's more rare.
