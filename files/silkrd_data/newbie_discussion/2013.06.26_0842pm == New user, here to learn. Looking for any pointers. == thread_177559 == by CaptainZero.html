Post by: 77 on June 28, 2013, 04:06 am
I'd advise you to never try to buy BTC on SR. It's the easiest way to scam a buyer.

Definitely keep up with threads on your drug(s) of choice, as well as vendor review threads. Read older posts in big threads to get a feel for who's been around a long time, plays nice with others, and isn't prone to rants and freakouts. If one of these people reports a scam, recommends a vendor, etc, you can give their opinion more weight. When someone with 51 posts, 50 of which are spam, starts a thread or posts in ALL CAPS about being scammed, or starts dropping rave reviews all over the forums for a newer vendor, you can and should ignore them. You get the picture - do your homework so you know who to give a bit of trust.

Never trust anyone here completely. Accounts get hacked, or bad shit happens IRL and a vendor needs $$$ fast and scams in desperation. Don't risk more than you can afford to lose, especially if you buy here for resale offline.

Don't be a dick. Karma isn't a number under your name. :)

That's about all I got. It sounds like you already have the right idea and are proceeding with due care. Keep that up, and you'll be alright.
