Post by: The Bubble Dreamer on September 10, 2013, 04:03 am
Hi!
I also find 2cb in gel caps to take a bit long to come up. You can also just throw it in water and drink it, it's a bit bitter but not that bad. For me the duration of the effects are never that long, I guess 4 hours max. Also the effects come in waves: you may think you are back to normal, only to be tripping hard again several seconds later. Part of the fun :-) . I also feel it's easy to be 'distracted' from the 2cb; I have been having conversations while on 2cb where I hardly noticed the effects while I was talking, but as soon as I was alone (going to the toilet, looking at my phone, etc) I realized how hard I was tripping.
I never had any problems falling asleep after 2cb, it's actually quite nice to lie in bed with some closed-eyed-visuals. 2cb is stimulating but not so much in a physical sense (and no jaw clenching)
Finding your ideal dose it a bit tricky. 20 mg is my dose for going out to a bar (I don't drink alcohol, but 2cb works well for me when going out with people who do), 25 mg is my dose for clubbing. And 30 mg was way too much (as in needing a good friend with me, which I had, so no problem).

And while 2cb is really nice on itself, I love to combine it with MDMA or methylone.
