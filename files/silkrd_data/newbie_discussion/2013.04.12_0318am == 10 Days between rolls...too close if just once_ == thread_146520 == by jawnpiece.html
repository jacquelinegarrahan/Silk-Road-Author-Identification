You sound a lot more knowledgeable about Molly than I do, but maybe my anecdotal evidence can help you decide one way or another.

I'm about 155lbs. During a music festival in Vegas last year (you can probably guess which one) my friends got me to roll for the first time and we did it for three straight days, averaging about 250mg each roll... while drinking copiously. I did not realize how bad an idea this was at the time, but it actually ended up being the best roll I've ever had since. However, my most recent experience rolling was two times in three days (again averaging about 250mg each time) and although the first roll was pretty solid, the second sucked; I was anxious, the roll was mild and lasted only about two hours, and I felt down before we even left the club.

My uneducated guess is that 10 days between rolls sounds like a lot but, to reiterate, my knowledge of Molly isn't very deep. Hope that helps even a little, happy rolling!
