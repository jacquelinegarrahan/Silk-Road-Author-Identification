Title: Re: Newbie LSD Thread
Post by: incogmagnito on September 29, 2013, 07:49 pm
set and setting are the two biggest factors that determine your trip.

dose and product quality do add some aspects, but mostly determine trip's depth.

benzos are indeed useful for ejecting from a bad trip, but this should not be taken lightly as bad trips happen for a reason and typically one needs to work through that material to get some great insights for personal development. Many a psychonaut would say that their "bad" trips (or bad parts of their trips) were actually their best ones in terms of personal growth. I certainly would.

LSD does not cause bad trips, you do. Ejecting from it is like trying to run from yourself.

When you done with your 50 come over here - all questions LSD have been in discussion there for many months already: http://dkn255hz262ypmii.onion/index.php?topic=45270.0;topicseen

By the way, doses are often very personal thing - some people have hard time imagining ever going over 200ug, some people find that their entry dose.

Be safe and happy,
