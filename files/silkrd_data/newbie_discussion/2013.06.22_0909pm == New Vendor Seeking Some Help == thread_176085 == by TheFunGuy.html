Title: Re: New Vendor Seeking Some Help
Post by: one2bcurious on June 23, 2013, 08:33 pm
When I buy from a new vendor, I start with something small.

I suggest putting up some auctions for people to spend less than $50, closer to like $30 would be good.

Also, sell them cheaper to draw in new customers.  If I remember correctly, you are selling 10/325 norcs for $10 (what these cost on here is ridiculous).  There are sellers with more experience and feedback selling them for cheaper.  Also, there are lots of comparable items like oxycodone, oxymorphone, and hydromorphone that come out cheaper when you do the calculations (I know the comparison of opioids and how to calculate the cost per 10mg across all of them  There are sellers that have them as low as $5 per 10mg so you are double that).  You may have to take a loss but that is what you should be willing to do when starting out to gain business.  That is going to make it harder to get customers.  I would post a listing and make it clear that it is a sale instead of standard pricing.

Maybe list them at like $8 or even $7.50 and draw some people in.  You have started a business and this is what businesses do to draw customers in.  When you have competition that has better feedback selling the same thing for cheaper, you arent going to get much business, if any.
