The guns on BMR are wildly expensive - in the range of 3x retail, not to mention that being stealthy is a whole lot harder with a firearm than with small quantities of recreational chemicals.  The legal issues also get a lot nastier a lot more quickly and will go federal almost from the start.

In the vast majority of states in the US it's still legal to buy guns face-to-face and there are tons of classified ad sites that make it easy to find any number of people happy to anonymously take your cash.  With the notable exception of the more exotic stuff (full auto, destructive devices, etc) or buying from some random guy on a street corner there isn't even much in the way of underground stings.  It's a lot more complicated if you're in NYC, Chicago or DC but in most of the rest of the country the availability, selection and price of firearms is just a hell of a lot better and safer outside the bounds of this type of marketplace.
