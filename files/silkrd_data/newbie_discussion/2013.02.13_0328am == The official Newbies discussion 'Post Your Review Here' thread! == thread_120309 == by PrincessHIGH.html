Title: The official Newbies discussion 'Post Your Review Here' thread!
Post by: PrincessHIGH on February 13, 2013, 03:28 am
As you know as a newbie you can't post on anywhere on the forum apart from the 'Newbies discussion' board until you've reached 50 posts. You may wish to post a review to a vendors review page located within the 'Rumor mill' discussion board but you can't as don't have the privilege to. Some of you have already posted your reviews here, and some vendors have taken the initiative to create their own 'newbie review threads' too (which are both great!), however the main source of information of vendors reviews are located within the 'Rumor mill'. We feel your frustrations and we want to help you, feel free to post your reviews here and we will transfer them on your behalf to their original vendor review thread within the rumor mill as we believe your reviews deserve to be heard!

Here's the rules:

1. Firstly go to the forum homepage and do a search of the vendor to see if they've got a review thread, on the rare occasion they may not have one, we can get in touch with the vendor and ask if they'd like one, however if they decline we have to respect their wishes, this would be very rare but it could happen, in 95% of searches you'll easily locate the vendor's original review thread.
2. You need to write this sentence  on your post before you write your review 'I give permission for my review of 'insert name of vendor's name here' to be posted to their original review page'.
3. We will quote your review to the vendor's main review thread with a link back to your original post here to prove it's genuine.
4. If you're a new vendor and would like a review thread to be created in the 'Rumor mill' on your behalf please get in touch with one of us via pm with proof you own a SilkRoad vendor account.

If you've already posted a review here which you would like us to transfer on your behalf please post the link here, and feel free to post your review both in the 'vendors newbie review thread' and here if you'd like us to transfer it to 'vendors main review thread' as well.

That's it, post your reviews freely (don't forget to give your permission) and we will take care of the rest. Happy travels everyone :)
