There are a few different types of buyers on Silk Road.  Two of these types of buyers are small fries and big guys.  Small fries have no reason to sign and they usually order domestic.  Big guys have to sign because all express mail from many international countries requires a signature or the vendor sends with tracking which requires a signature.  If you're a small fry then you can keep chanting don't sign don't sign don't sign.  If you're a big guy then you have already signed and you know that it is a requirement for many types of shipments from many countries.

I sign when I expect to sign.  I don't sign when I don't expect to sign.  To each their own.
