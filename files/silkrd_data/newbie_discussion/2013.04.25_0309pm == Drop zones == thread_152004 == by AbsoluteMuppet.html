Title: Re: Drop zones
Post by: barnargulus on April 25, 2013, 03:50 pm
Most vendors prefer you use your address.  You're trying to make it look as normal as possible, they don't search your things from amazon, you never worry about that do you?  So just treat it as a normal delivery, never sign for it, and don't order beyond reason.  The people looking are looking for the vendors not buyers.  The more out of the ordinary you make the delivery, the more likely to be noticed.  If you are buying en masse, maybe rethink because you don't sound too experienced, and that is a quick way to get noticed.
