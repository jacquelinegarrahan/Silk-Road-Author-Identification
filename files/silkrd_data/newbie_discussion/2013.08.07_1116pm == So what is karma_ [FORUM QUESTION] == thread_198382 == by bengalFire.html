1. How do you get karma?

People click a "thank" or "discourage" link next to your name.

Quote
2. Does it mean anything in practice?

Not really, although some people care a lot about meaningless internet points. :)

Quote
3. If karma is dispensed by people; when can I give it? Also, it there a quantity limit (to either good or bad)

You need 100 posts before you can give karma, and you can only give someone plus or minus karma once every 72 hours.

Bonus: you also don't have to fill out captchas after 100 posts!
