Title: Re: How much methadone do I take?
Post by: captainjojo on September 28, 2013, 05:11 am
Staplaytion3, you need to me a bit more careful.  Methadone isn't like other opiates, it has a very long duration and it takes about 3 hours for it to hit its peak taken orally.

What you have just done is how many users overdose on methadone.  They take some, don't get high, then take some more.  The problem is that methadone accumulates in your system and stays there for up to 36 hours.

About the only thing saving you right now is the fact you already have a tolerance.

The long duration is what makes methadone a perfect substitute for Heroin and other Opiate addictions.  Once a user has determined their stable dose they only need to take it once a day.

An H user taking 1g a day can get by feeling just fine on 40-60mg of methadone once a day.  Someone who has never taken opiates before could actually die on just 10mg.

I've done them all, H and Oxy's of all types and Methadone.   They only way you can take methadone and get an immediate high is via IV.  I've used MSeller's stuff that way and I usually take 40-100mgs IV'd and get a nice rush, but unlike H I don't keep taking it.  With H or Oxy's you can take some, then take some more an hour or so later without worrying too much about build up.

The 40mg you took of methadone would have been better done all at once (and no more), you would have started to feel it at around 2-3 hours, but it wouldn't have been a major high, more a nice buzz and feeling good.

The other thing is you need to be taking Methadone a few days in a row before getting the full benefits.  Taken as a substitute to stop withdrawals it usually takes several days before that 40mg once a day actually lasts all day.  When you go on a meth program they usually start you at 30 and bump it every 1-3 days until you are stable.
