Make sure you have these things in your vicinity before you take your trip:

1. Good friends that you trust. Don't do your trip - especially your first - alone. Have someone with you who you can rely on to be by your side if you feel uncomfortable.

Title: Re: First LSD Trip Advice?
Post by: adamiz on August 16, 2013, 03:54 pm
Since the tab is 110ug you maybe feel nothing so tremendous different. Maybe a positive "energy" if I might say.

Don't expect that the substance will kick in before 30-90 minutes. I always had this feeling at my first tries that the lsd didn't work. For the first hour don't do anything different than usual until you feel it. And with such a dose don't expect to have visuals. Especially like seeing dragons and elves as some say who has never tried. Lsd is NOT about visuals.

When you start feeling it (again it is only 110ug so it will be a light experience) I will first advice some things NOT to do.

Don't be nervous. If you feel nervous then don't do it all.
Lsd lasts for hours, so try and do it when you know that you have all day free.
Don't do it late at night. Try to take it as early as possible so you can enjoy the day.
Don't stay in front of a screen except if you are into writing.
Don't spend your "quality" time entertaining yourself with tv, video games. It might be fun but there are better things to do.

Now advices.

Music is one of the most addictive things you can do while on lsd. At least from my own personal experience.
If you play any kind of musical instruments then definitely spend some time there. It will be an new musical "research" not matter how experienced or not you are in music.

Nature is by far the best place to be while on lsd. Try avoiding staying inside. Even if it rains :)
Swimming as one above me suggested, is an amazing feeling as well.

There will be also a lot of thinking during these hours. Try to spend your thoughts in nice matters and not so much in personal problems.

Lsd will definitely give you some physical energy. I tend to make looong walks in the forests or the city and just "connect" with the nature or socializing. I have found myself many times as well to clean the house and perfecting every detail. I don't do this, only on lsd :P

Food.. Food is a tricky part at least for me. Either I "forget" to eat or when I am hungry I prepare some special dishes. Although -at least again for me- when I prepare for example something hot or with meat, when I start eating it I don't like it. I have noticed that I can only and really enjoy eating fruits and vegetables/salads. I guess while on lsd my body wants only natural raw things... Don't know..
Another thing about food. If you are going to take the blotter do it after 1-2 hours you have eaten a good meal/breakfast. Don't do it with empty stomach and don't do it immediately after eating. It will take longer time to kick in.

No matter in what situation you are, being amongst friends is a good idea. If they could have lsd as well even better.

You mentioned that you don't do other substances. But just for future advices if you get involved with others here is my advices.
If you have weed, well this is your call. A spliff is always good :)
There are many combos you can do, but my favorite is to take dmt while you are on lsd. Although when I take lsd or generally anything I tend to stick only to one substance. Expect lsd-dmt. This is great.

If you like the experience you had then here comes another advice for future tries.

Don't do it often. Lsd builds a tolerance that last days or weeks. Once per month maximum if you want to really feel it everytime.
Don't be afraid to raise the dose or even double it.
And last thing that I am trying to do when I plan my lsd day is to write down something I want to do or think while tripping.

That's all I have to say for now. Just don't worry, lsd is one of the safest substances.

Take care my friend, enjoy your experience and if you really like the moment don't hesitate to share your experience.

All the best

Adamiz
