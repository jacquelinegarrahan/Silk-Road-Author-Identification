The forums aren't meant as a place for commerce. Silk Road proper, http://silkroadvb5piz3r.onion/, is.

Please check the following pages for info on what the standard ways around here are:

Buyers Guide - http://dkn255hz262ypmii.onion/wiki/index.php?title=Buyer%27s_Guide
Sellers Guide - http://dkn255hz262ypmii.onion/wiki/index.php?title=Seller%27s_Guide
The Ordering Process - http://dkn255hz262ypmii.onion/wiki/index.php?title=The_ordering_process

Silk Road has systems in place that protect both parties in their endeavors to participate. Directly trading between individuals isn't safe, and generally won't happen.

Generally speaking, if you don't have the money to get started selling, then it's probably not a good idea. You have to be able to handle the risk involved, when you begin selling you have obligations then to meet your sales. It is a business. At the same time, there is a great deal of power that comes with this type of involvement... you take great risk at both the buyer, and your own. The same goes on the other side, as a purchaser, you do so at a risk. Going around the systems that are there to try and reduce the risk as much as possible, only serves to harm both parties involved.

Save some money, you can purchase bitcoins via cash through a few different methods. Bitinstant - https://www.bitinstant.com, allows for places like moneygram. https://localbitcoins.com allows connecting with individuals in your area. There are many others, and if you are interested in that avenue I also suggest visiting the bitcoin wiki proper to learn about it: http://en.bitcoin.it

You don't have to buy 1.00 bitcoin, you can purchase smaller amounts. Most places will do much like normal currancy, anywhere from .01 and up. Its strongly suggested to start small anyways, so that you can learn and develop your understanding of the concepts here.
