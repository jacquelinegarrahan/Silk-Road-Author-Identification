I've done it probably 15x, and you don't have to use your real name, or address.

Post by: PavlovsD on May 01, 2013, 08:40 pm
I used Bitinstant for my first two bitcoin purchases. Since then I have used Blue Sky Traders because of their great reviews in the Rumor Mill Forum.

You send them an email with your request and your SR wallet. They reply with instructions to make a cash deposit in their bank of america account (so there needs to be a branch near where you live).  You go to the branch, fill out a deposit slip (none of your info is required) and hand it to the teller with the cash. The coins are in your wallet very quickly.
