edit - what it says in plain english is, heroin hydrochloride monohydrate (C21H24NO5Cl.H2O) is sparingly soluble in acetone, freely soluble in chloroform, insoluble in ether, insoluble in hexane, very soluble in methanol, and freely soluble in water.

as for heroin base (C21H23NO5), it's soluble in acetone, freely soluble in chloroform, slightly soluble in ether, slightly soluble in hexane, sparingly soluble in methanol, and insoluble in water.

oh and fwiw, the coke doc from the same source says coke hcl is very slightly soluble in acetone. how "sparingly soluble" compares with "very slightly soluble" is something for a chemist to answer but to this dumdum it doesn't sound like an acetone bath would hurt a horsey.
