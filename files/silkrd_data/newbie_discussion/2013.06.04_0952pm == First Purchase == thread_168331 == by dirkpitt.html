I think its fairly safe ordering from vendors who have a large amount of transactions, and a high rating. Go through the comments in their history and look up their name on the forums for their review thread, it would be impossible for someone with 200-300+ transactions and pages of good reviews to be a scam, without it being littered with people who got ripped off.
