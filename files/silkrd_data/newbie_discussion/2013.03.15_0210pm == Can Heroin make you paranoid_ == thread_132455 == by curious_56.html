Title: Re: Can Heroin make you paranoid?
Post by: Jasper Jenson on March 15, 2013, 05:03 pm
You aren't likely to be 'skipping' on heroin. More likely you will feel pretty laid back, maybe even 'nodding'. It's a really hard drug, and you definitely don't want to overdose. Be very careful and start very small. You can always take another chase at the dragon, but you can't undo it once you have smoked too much. I have never felt too paranoid on heroin. I even did some (snorted) at work a few times back in the day. I had a job that I just sat around on a computer, and didn't have to interact with people.

Try looking at Erowid for more info, or search the harm reduction section here for more info. Be safe, and hopefully you have fun. Remember too that this shit is very addictive...

JJ
