Title: Re: is this seller legit
Post by: Identity on March 28, 2013, 04:41 pm
First I would look at the vendor's profile on SR to read their various T&Cs and information. This should (a) give you a good idea of how professional they are and (b) hopefully answer your questions on FE and shipping

The next step would be to search for them on these forums for reviews. Many vendors have direct links to their forum threads on their profiles. Read the various feedback and try to get a feel for how legit they are and what the product may be like. When reading reviews check the poster's post count, karma etc. to get for a feel for if the review is legit.

If you are happy that the vendor is professional, has good reviews etc. the final step would probably be to message them before ordering especially if you are new. You can ask them questions about FE and shipping if they haven't been answered anywhere else.

Tl;dr Do your research and you shouldn't have too many issues.
