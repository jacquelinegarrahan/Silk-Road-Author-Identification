Title: Re: Do prices on SR change as the rate of bitcoin?

Quote from: chronicbro on February 08, 2013, 01:36 pm
Just wondering if prices on here change as the bitcoin rates do? Or do the sellers need to update the prices themselves? I guess what I'm asking is if it changes automatically as the price of bitcoin rises or drops.

Sellers can choose whether to set their listings in bitcoins or USD. If they set their listing in bitcoin (say 2btc) then they will get 2btc when they sell. If they set their listing in dollars (say $100) then they will get $100 when they sell. Setting listings as dollars ensures vendors that they get what they want for their products regardless of bitcoin rate fluctuations.
