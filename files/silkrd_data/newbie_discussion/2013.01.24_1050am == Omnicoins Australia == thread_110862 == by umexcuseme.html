Title: Re: Omnicoins Australia
Post by: vousetici on January 25, 2013, 12:25 am
Hey umexcuseme
I was about to use omnicoin today and I was put off by one small detail. The require you to send a scan or photo of your deposit slip after you've made your deposit. If you have a way of anonymously uploading and sending files then you should be ok. I haven't figured out a way to do this and I can't risk my anonymity.

I've used www.spendbitcoins.com a couple of times and they haven't let me down. They charge 10% on top of their rates so they're not as cheap but they are quick and discreet. They don't require a copy of your deposit slip unless the bitcoins don't make it to your wallet which has never happened to me.

Both sites require an email address but that's easily overcome by using an anonymous email service (there's plenty of options if you google).

Good luck.
