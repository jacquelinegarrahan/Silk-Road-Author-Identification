Post by: MrRoxy on March 15, 2013, 08:46 pm
Just a little side note, but it's never good to get drugs sent via UPS or FedEx (I'm a UPS driver)

UPS has the right to go through your package without reason or consent. If it gets damaged, they'll open it. If the label gets torn or unable to be read, they'll open it to find information on the sender/receiver. They don't need a warrant or probably cause, it's much better to go with USPS.
