Title: Re: My First Psychedelic Adventure
Post by: Icon on April 06, 2013, 11:40 pm
1.5g mushrooms will not make you feel sick at all. With 1.5g your thoughts will be altered and you will see brighter colors, halos around light fixtures, mild euphoria generally a good time that isn't hard to handle. You won't see anything really trippy.

Now if you ate all four grams I think that would be a good amount. You will see tesilations and things will have trippy visuals. You might feel a little neausia for the beginning but it passes after 30 to 60minutes. Eating more than 4g the neausia will become more of a problem.

If its your first time don't smoke weed while coming up on the shrooms. It will take the experience into the stratosphere. I had a friend who ate 3.5g and he was still coming up on them (he had done them one other time) and it really overwhelmed him.

It sounds like your plan is perfect dude. If your really worried just take the 1.5g. I did that my first time and I had a really killer time. Since you have a sitter take advantage and eat all 4g. You will have a great time.

Mixing psychedlics with weed has gotten me in trouble a few times. The amounts are hard to gauge and its easy to over do it. I would say that after the peak of 1.5g you could totally hit a pipe and it would make it more interesting. With the 4g dose you don't need anything else.

Grind it into a powder and mix it into something you like to eat, I use ice cream. If you want to super charge the shrooms drink orange juice.


On the topic of Salvia. Most people do it once or twice and are scared shitless by it. Its like doing anything scary. You have to do it many times before you start to relax and have fun and play with the trip. I was one of the weirdos who had a great time right off the bat, so I've always liked salvia. If someone really wants to explore salvia. You have to go balls deep and commit to doing it many times, then its a real blast.

Oh I forgot to talk about what to do for a bad trip. Really there is nothing you can do. If you have a trip sitter that person's willingness to help you is your antidote for a bad trip. I have books that doctors and nurses use for treating patients who come in on drugs (just like in pulp fiction). The section on psychedelics is really really short. The only thing they can give you if you went to the E.R. would be Valium. For people who are freaking the fuck out on a bad trip all the E.R. can do is try to calm the person and give them a Valium. The LD 50 for psychedelics (which is half the amount it takes to kill someone is really really high for psilocybin and LSD). These chemicals cannot kill you. For psilocybin the LD 50 on a rabbit is 12.5mg/kg so if you weigh about 70Kg that means you would need 875mg. Keep in mind thats 87% of a gram of pure crystalline psilocybin!!!!!

Psilocybin only makes up about 1% of the dry weight of a mushroom.  1g of mushrooms contains 10 mg of psilocybin.
