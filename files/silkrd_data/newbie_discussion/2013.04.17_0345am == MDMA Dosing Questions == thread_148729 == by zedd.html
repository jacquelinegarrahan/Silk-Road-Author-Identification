could be a matter of purity

i've done lots of mdma, as in many times and (sometimes) in large quantities. if it's pure and unadulterated, you could easily take half a gram or more over the course of a night (i would not recommend doing that with pills unless you are 100% positive they contain no other agents because that's how you OD on random things you never intended on taking).

if you didn't feel 130mg your first time, either you didn't measure it properly, or it's not very pure, or it could simply be a matter of mdma not being very effective for your chemistry. mdma works by releasing the seratonin already in your brain, and certain medications and/or drugs you may have taken in the past can fuck with that. also since the mdma itself doesn't actually make you feel anything (other than the lockjaw and body "high" associated with methamphetamines in general), if your brain for whatever reason doesn't have a lot of seratonin in there then you won't have such a strong euphoric effect. this is also why you should wait at least a week but better 3 or 4 between rolls -> give your brain time to replenish its stores. i think certain vitamins are good for that as well, but i don't remember off the top of my head.

i would just go ahead and try 180mg. or take 130 again, and then another 50 or 100 if you're not feeling anything. your gf should be fine with 120-150. put some good music on and have crazy sweaty sex for 3 hours straight. good times. also if you rail it then you can get a faster and somewhat stronger initial high. i recommend doing a few fat lines every 90 minutes, gives you a nice rhythm of peaks and plateaus. if you have pure crystals just keep doing it until you get bored/tired/run out. also make sure you smoke a ton of weed when you're coming down.

also, don't listen to people's advice. do your own research and find out what works for you.
