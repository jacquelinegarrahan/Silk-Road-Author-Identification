Title: Re: What happens if customs take your incoming package? (Australia)
Post by: JabbatheSlut on June 14, 2013, 03:11 pm
As to "what happens ..." I would say that Customs will keep your package of contraband and then decide whether or not to charge you with importation.

and "what should I do ... " - if the worst comes to the worst and you end up facing charges, remember that you are under no obligation to answer any questions and the "burden of proof" lies with the authorities ...

"To convict you of a Importing or exporting a border controlled drug or plant charge, the police must prove each of the following matters beyond a reasonable doubt:
You import or export a substance; and
The substance is a border controlled drug or border controlled plant; and
The quantity imported or exported is a marketable/commercial quantity."

Should the worst happen, say nothing, politely decline to answer questions and seek legal advice immediately.
