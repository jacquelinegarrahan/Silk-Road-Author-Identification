Title: Re: Coke Dosage
Post by: tomroberts on July 20, 2013, 10:52 am
I have a fair bit of experience with coke (having spent a fair bit of time in Sth America in the past).

Key points are:
 - You don't know what 'your' dose is until you test out yourself.  My advice is that everyone is different, and most batches of coke are different (excluding those which you have purchased which come from the same brick).
 - Start small, and be prepared to use some of it to work out what is good for you (i've seen some people break a gram into 8 lines, whereas others may get 20 out of one gram and still get a similar effect)
 - Buy from the same people so you know the quality is good

In my experience a set of 4 small lines taken 2 x 2 is a good way to get more in, and prolong the effect, rather than doing 2 big ones!

Bottom line - some of that gram you've bought should be viewed as 'calibrator' material, no matter what your level of 'experience'

Can't say much about speed comedowns, but unless you've worked through 2g+ in a night, there isn't too much of a comedown on coke...
