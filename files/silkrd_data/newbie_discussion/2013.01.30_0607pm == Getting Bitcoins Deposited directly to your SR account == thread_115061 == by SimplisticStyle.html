Title: Re: Getting Bitcoins Deposited directly to your SR account
Post by: wonko on January 30, 2013, 07:56 pm
You can put your bitcoin address into blockchain.info to see what is going on. You should see any transactions that are pending for that address.

Click on the transaction itself and you can see how many confirmations you have had. SR will require 6 confirmations before the coins show up in your wallet, after that it takes 15 minutes or so and then you will find them in your account.
