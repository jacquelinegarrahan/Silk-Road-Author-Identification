Post by: unbeknownst on August 23, 2013, 04:22 am
Much more tolerant LE attitude towards weed. Especially in BC, its a similar situation to many areas in NorCal where there are whole neighborhoods growing weed, with the law turning a blind eye. In Vancouver (Hongcouver!) in particular, there used to be whole extended families brought over from asia who were set up by ethnic gangs to grow in multiple houses. Some of the small towns are largely propped up economically by pot. I grew up on the BC border and the beasters were everywhere. Generally the stuff that is (was?) exported is pretty mediocre, airy, not trimmed well, not the worst schwag, just blah. Back in the day, BC bud was considered a step up from mexican brick, but was acceptable and cheap enough for the kids in more rural areas. Haven't seen any in quite awhile, so maybe the average quality has improved to compete with the improved access to high class weed and medical stuff in many places. That being said, there is plenty of GREAT weed grown in Canada. Canadians love their pot and appreciate quality. The current successful push for state legalization has some of its roots in BC over the last 20 years. Google Marc Emery. I knew some people who were running a legalization campaign in a western state some years ago and Marc sent them a huge check!
