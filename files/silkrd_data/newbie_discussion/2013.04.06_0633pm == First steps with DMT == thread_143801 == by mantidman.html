Title: First steps with DMT
Post by: mantidman on April 06, 2013, 06:33 pm
Never in my life could I have imaged what DMT has begun to show me.... I would like to personally thank JOR and BLIM for the beautiful DMT extract freebase they have offered just recently.

The first day I was able to get some into my body I was so uneasy.... I thought my heart was going to explode out of my chest... it felt like it was beating a thousand times a minute... I thought I was going to DIE!  I really did and had to make the decision if I wanted to go then or not!

I had the extreme idea that if I wanted to I would be able to blast off into the universe with just another toke on my layered with cannabis ash DMT pipe....  I made the deliberate decision to stop and hold off for a little bit... I really wanted to go to that place and other level but did not want to force it.... I wanted to walk through the gate not run..... little did I know.......

It was not long before I was back at it..  ( NEXT DAY ) I put some more DMT in my old mans pipe... ran the lighter across the top real quick to melt it onto the ash..... waited a minute and then....

Oh...........my..........fucking..........GOD!!!!  How is this even possible?  How could I even be in this place at all...... I thanked whoever had led me to this place over and over...... sitting in the lotus position I felt myself vibrating with electric energy through every part of my body..... I had the VERY real auditory distortion of sound that I have never heard before......  of course I have heard sound distortions before and the fusing of one sense with another but THIS was VERY VERY different...... 

The high pitch ringing, vibrating in my ears, head.... it sounded like a very high tech engine that was going faster and faster..... spinning over and over.... faster and faster...... quicker.....  and then............BAM............ and I do mean BAM! with all capital letters.

The good and bad, the pro and con, the in and out..... the GEOMETRIC PATTERN OVER EVERYTHING!    It was like taking 1,000 hits of very potent acid......

On another toke of DMT it had my mind just reeling...... I had to actually get off the chair I was on and sit on the ground.... fuck man if someone had seen me I think it would have looked like I was fucking fried and I was!..............BUT only for the 5 or 7 min. it lasted......

I have used the DMT a number of times since I got it..... I am always kind of nervous before I use it.... It has the feeling of the unknown for sure....... a thing of beauty and a thing of the unknown.  Something to be treated with respect for sure.... not your everyday let's go get stoned kind of thing.... be warned.

Thank you SR for being here...... I really don't know if I would have ever come across this absolutely interesting substance if you were not here......

For anyone who want's or need's a wake up call.............  DMT just might be it......
