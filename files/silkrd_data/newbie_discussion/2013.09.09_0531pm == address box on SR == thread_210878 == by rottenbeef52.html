Title: Re: address box on SR
Post by: The Bubble Dreamer on September 10, 2013, 12:11 am
You could do that, and the seller will likely just copy&paste the (encrypted!) address, but I prefer not to do enter any additional information. First of all, it makes life easier for the seller, second, if by accident the seller includes this non-address information on the envelope / label, your package could be compromised. Better just put the address and send a message to the vendor, where you include the transaction URL.
