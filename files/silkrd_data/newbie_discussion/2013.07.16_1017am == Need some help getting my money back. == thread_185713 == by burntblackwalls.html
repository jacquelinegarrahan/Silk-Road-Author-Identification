If the vendor cancels your order, it won't affect your stats. If they cancel it after it's marked as "in transit", you will be able to leave feedback for the transaction as well as get a refund. Your stats will only be affected when you have a dispute in the Resolution Centre and are awarded a refund of some sort from the vendor or Admin.

See below for more info. -

Quote
  Buyer places an order

Seller cancels the order. A full refund is given to the buyer.

Buyer cancels the order. Four days after the order is placed, if the order has not been market "in transit", the buyer may cancel the order.

Seller ships the product and marks it "in transit". Regardless of the outcome after this point, the buyer will be able to leave feedback.

Seller cancels the order. A full refund is given to the buyer.

Buyer finalizes the order. Payment is released to the seller. The buyer may finalize any time after the order has been marked in transit, though they should not do so unless they have received and are happy with their product. Once the order is finalized, it will not be possible to obtain a refund.

Buyer sends the order to the Resolution Center.

Buyer and seller agree on refund amount. Same effect as the buyer finalizing except they are refunded anywhere from 0 - 100%.

Admin decides refund amount. Same effect as the buyer finalizing except they are refunded anywhere from 0 - 100%.

Buyer does nothing. The order will automatically finalize and release payment to the seller 17 days after it was placed.
