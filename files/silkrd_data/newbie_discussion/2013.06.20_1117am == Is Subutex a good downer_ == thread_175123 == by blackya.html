Oh god I can't imagine why anyone would voluntarily take buprenorphine.

Admittedly I was taking it as an alternative to methadone for a couple of years but I can assure you that even if you do not have tolerance to opiates it does not produce anything remotely like an opiate high.

Another weird feature is that increasing the dose does nothing to increase any effect due its properties as an opiate agonist-antagonist. Whatever you do, never take buprenorphine with any opiate and definitely do not take it if you have a habit because it causes immediate, extremely unpleasant withdrawal symptoms.

Speaking as someone who has used and abused bupe as much as possible I would recommend staying as far away from it as possible. The only thing it is good for is pain.
