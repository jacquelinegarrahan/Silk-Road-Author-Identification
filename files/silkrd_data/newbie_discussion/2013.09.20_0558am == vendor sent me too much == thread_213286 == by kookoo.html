Title: Re: vendor sent me too much
Post by: justaperson on September 20, 2013, 06:56 am
Sounds like you lucked out. You can message the vendor notifying them, but then he may expect you to provide some sort of compensation. If he does, you don't really have to do anything, but there is the slight issue of that vendor having your address, assuming that you used your real address. Can you trust this vendor not to somehow out you in retaliation? It's unlikely, but it's something that can happen. The moral thing to do here is to provide notification, and that's all.  Remember, it's the vendor's mistake, so you are not obligated to provide any kind of compensation as you never agreed to do so. If you were to provide the wrong address, for example, a vendor would not provide a refund on product already sent out. We are all responsible for our own mistakes.

If you're feeling REALLY guilty about it, you two could arrange for it to sent back, but honestly for that amount it probably isn't worth bothering over.
