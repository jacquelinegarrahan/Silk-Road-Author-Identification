Post by: TheEmeraldTriangle on February 02, 2013, 07:22 pm
As a vendor with over 300 transactions myself, let me advise you to take a few deep breaths. You say your vendor has a good rep. Accepting that as fact, it's unlikely (but not impossible that) s/he would deliberately rip you off. S/he may not be answering due to a sudden personal problem that barely allow time to get orders processed every day. Have you checked the vendor's profile page? For some of us popular vendors who don't frequent the forums very often, and participate even less often (there are only so many hours in a day) the new blanket 50 post rule etc. has cut us off from the forum boards buyers have created to connect us to you folks in an more informal way. So the only way we have left to communicate with you buyers is through our profile. We certainly can't PM everyone, even if we had a list, and we don't. (SR doesn't provide us one, and keeping that data local is a security risk.)

Where shipping is concerned: Take a look at the "Shipping" forum board and you'll see a thread about how much longer the Post Office is taking to deliver packages since the holidays. Why? Nobody knows for sure. What I can say is that my buyers are experiencing this themselves. Last week we had a Priority package take 11 days to arrive at the buyer's house. Just the other day we had a buyer post feedback that it took 7 days for their order to arrive! Our own experience: We had an Express package from a SR vendor we use regularly take 5 bloody days to arrive. It religiously takes that vendor's PRIORITY shipment's 3 days to arrive!

So how about a drink, and a bit more patience. Hopefully things will turn out right in the end.
