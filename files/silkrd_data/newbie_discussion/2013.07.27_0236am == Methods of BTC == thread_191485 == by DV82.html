Post by: odd on July 27, 2013, 02:44 am
go to localbitcoins.com and register there.  next click on buy bitcoins.  from there you will a couple different options.  you can buy bitcoins online and there will be a list of all online merchants offering btc for the currency you are paying with.  next to the merchants name will be their limits (how much or little you can buy at once) so just look for one that is in your range.  next to the limits will be what kind of payment they accept so you would be looking for someone who will accept wire transfers/bank transfers.  Vendors there accept all kinds of payment, some accept okpay, some accept cash deposits in several different banks, paypal, moneygram, and western union.  sometimes you can use a credit or debit card through moneygram or paypal and never have to leave the house.  Another option is to buy them locally from localbitcoins.com which usually means you'll contact the merchant and set up a time to meet and make the transaction.  Hope that helped!
