Title: Re: LSD Pricing on the Road
Post by: Drippy on March 21, 2013, 10:27 am
Those of us in the USA who are involved in the psychedelics scene will definitely find LSD to be more expensive than we're used to.  A year ago I would've never said I'd pay $10/hit, but w/ the current scarcity of quality LSD (IMO due to the explosion of nbome family chemicals) I've changed my mind. 

I will vouch for Tesselated having some EXCELLENT LSD.  I had a very hard time clicking the buy button and paying the seemingly absurd price, but his claims of 200ug/hit are definitely not bullshit.  I gave a quarter tab to a friend at a show who had to work the next day, and he could clearly tell he'd eaten L.

I just find it frustrating that there are very few people offering quantity discounts.  I'm more than happy to pay $12.60/hit for a quarter sheet of 200mic product seeing as that's the equivalent of paying about $160 for a quarter sheet of standard (100mic) blotter (about $65/strip), but not getting a discount for anything past there makes me cry a lil on the inside.

For the love of god, someone please hit up their chemistry buddies in India and source some of this rose crystal that's been floating around for the past few months.  We need a decent bulk connect on the road ASAP!!!
