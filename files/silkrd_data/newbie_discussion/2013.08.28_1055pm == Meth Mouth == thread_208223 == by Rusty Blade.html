Title: Re: Meth Mouth
Post by: junjo on August 29, 2013, 07:03 am
From my limited use of meth I'd recommend 3 things:
1) Brush/floss your teeth normally (twice a day)
Most people forget to do this in the depths of a meth binge
2) Avoid sugary drinks other than gatorade/powerade, since those are good for electrolyte replacement from all the water you piss out while on meth
3 (most important IMO) ) Have a stash of chewing gum on you
The worst after effect I got from my early meth use was awful pain in my teeth due to grinding them constantly under the influence of the drug.
Once I started chewing gum, the grinding stopped immediately.

I highly doubt that meth itself destroys your teeth. It's just the simple combination of poor hygeine, bad diet, and teeth grinding/clenching that ravages your mouth after a while.
