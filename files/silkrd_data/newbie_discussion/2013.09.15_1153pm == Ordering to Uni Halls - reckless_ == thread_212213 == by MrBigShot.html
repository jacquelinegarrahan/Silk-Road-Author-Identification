Title: Re: Ordering to Uni Halls - reckless?
Post by: Tiger on September 16, 2013, 09:33 pm
Chances are, someone who lived in your flat in the past will still be getting (junk) mail sent there, I'd just use their name.

People recommend not to do this on the basis that the postman will know that person doesn't live at that particular address and will get suspicious, but that's definitely not going to be the case for halls.
