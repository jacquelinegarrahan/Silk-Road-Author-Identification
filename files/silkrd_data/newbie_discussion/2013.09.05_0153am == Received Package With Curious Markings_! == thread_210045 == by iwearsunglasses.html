Post by: RedNeckHippie7 on September 05, 2013, 09:37 am
Do the envelopes have the little bar code strips on them. Usually somewhere ner the bottom. If they do. This question mark is due to those and this vendors should source another decoy envelope system. These are used by companies such as Credit Card, marketing, banking, etc to literally watch payments as they come across the country or globe. In the case of financial institutions they use this to forecast sales, income, payments, ect. If it does have this. It may be throwing a flag due to the address not going to or from where the code says it should. And this is a increase in risk. I wouldnt suspect negligence on the vendor as much as lack of knowledge. Check the envelopes for the codes.
