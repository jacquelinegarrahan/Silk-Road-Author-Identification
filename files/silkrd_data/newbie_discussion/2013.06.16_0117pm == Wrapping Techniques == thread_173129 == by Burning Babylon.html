Title: Wrapping Techniques
Post by: Burning Babylon on June 16, 2013, 01:17 pm
The purpose of this thread is to discuss various ways of wrapping products, in most cases the first layer on the product before any stealth or Moisture Barrier Bags are applied (although some vendors use that as the wrapping itself) but it can also be for various containers. I suspect very few vendors use any Wrapping Techniques beyond a ziploc bag and those who do will most likely not post, so this will most likely be more of an intelligence thread for the future if we can afford some of the more advanced equipment.  :)

Now there's various types of products that can be wrapped and I would make a distinction between the following forms:

* Solid: Shapes like for example hash bits or ecstasy pills who have fixed dimensions.
* Powder: A powder like for example pure MDMA or DMT.
* Fragile: Shape that can easily be crushed or malformed like for example mushrooms or cannabis buds.
* Liquid: For example alcoholic beverages or dissolved GHB.

Other than that I'm going to format my listings somewhat similarly to my old thread Heat Sealing Equipment; http://dkn255hz262ypmii.onion/index.php?topic=160856.msg1139175 :

* Name: What kind of name the product goes under, pretty straightforward.
* Shipping: To what countries the product ships to.
* Link: Where to acquire the product(s).
* Price: The current price of the product.
* Type: What kind of types the product can wrap according to the categorization I mentioned previously.
* Size: How much space the type of wrapping takes up dimension-wise.
* Closure: The mechanism used for closing the wrapping.
* Extra: Any additional information and comments, possibly a review of the product itself if the user posting about it has used it for a while.
