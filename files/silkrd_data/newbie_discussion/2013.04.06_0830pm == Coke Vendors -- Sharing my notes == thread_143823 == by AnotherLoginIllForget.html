Just to help any people new to the SR cola market, here are my experiences with the cola vendors I've dealt with so far.

NOD
An 8ball from him was the first order I'd ever placed on SR. I've picked up from him 2 more times. The prices, as everyone knows, are quite high, sky high. Customer service is beyond excellent. As slow as TOR is sometimes, he gets back to you pretty quickly and doesn't just write little blurbs. They are thought-out responses that show that he cares about his customers and his business. I've only ever picked Express shipping and if you make the cutoff, you get it next day. It's that simple. The product comes in one big beautiful rock and little size lines give me great, smooth euphoria, no jitters, and the kind of comedown that I like (yawn, yawn, 15 minute nap, back to normal). People (including me) obviously are willing to pay the price that he sets, and I have absolutely NOTHING BAD to say about him except that somewhere inside me still cries a little bit when I pay up for his 8ball :) In any case, if you're financially ok, Nod should be one of your top choices.

SPACEWRANGLER
This is one cool dude. My first order from him was just for some Xanax bars. I had just placed an order before he took down listings and he pm'ed right away to let me know about his inventory issue. He is super friendly and sent me another msg once my pack was in the mail. The second order I placed was last week for some cola and xanax bars. Same little inventory issue with the Xanax but he threw in some addy's to make up for it, which was class. His stealth is fucking amazing and actually kind of funny. I saw it and it made me laugh. The cola comes in caps so for all of you that are obsessed about getting stuff in rock form you may be disappointed. HOWEVER, the cola is visibly shimmery, and the high is smooth and euphoric with a nice comedown. I hate to say this because I really like SW but I would rank his cola just a tad tad tad tad behind Nod's. But because of the price, I would say SW is the better deal. I can't stress how pleasant it is to do business with SW.. really cares about his customers and I want him to succeed so that the cola market on SR has more competition (which means better prices for the consumer).

DRAGONCOVE
Sends Tracking Number right away. Bought 1g from him and product comes in little rocks and powder. People seem to be pleased with his product but, at least for me, his cola was not even close to Nod's or SpaceWrangler's. In terms of transaction and delivery, though, he is spot on and professional.

UGS
Got 1G from this vendor. Shipping was on point; stealth could use a little work. No vacuum seal. Comes in a rock. The cola was good, but I would rank him behind Nod and SW.

So, I guess the final word is if you have $ burning your pocket and want your gear RIGHT AWAY, go with Nod. But in terms of value, quality, and increments (meaning SW handles smaller orders whereas Nod only does 2G and 3.5G), SpaceWrangler should be your go to guy. Both of these vendors (Nod, SW) are legit; just depends on what exactly you're looking for and how much you're willing to spend.

NEXT UP TO TRY IS COCAEXPRESS, who has marketed his new cola product brilliantly. The prices are the best out of all these vendors but I cannot vouch for the quality as I have not yet sampled it. I hope this helps everyone.

FE means you have to finalize before you receive it. It will say "processing" until the vendor moves it on to "In Transit."

A lot of vendors require FE from new buyers to cover their ass. I don't necessarily agree with it but there are also plenty of other vendors who don't ask for FE from new buyers.

Most vendors ask you to use PGP, but only a few 100% insist on it. If you want to figure it out, search for some tutorials, whether on clearnet or youtube. It's not that difficult once you get the hang of it.
