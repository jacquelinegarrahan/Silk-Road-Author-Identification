Well... according to the wiki

http://dkn255hz262ypmii.onion/wiki/index.php?title=Ratings_%26_Reviews
Quote
Vendors are weighted (and ranked) by the following two factors:

    Total sales
    Freshness of those sales

The weighted sum of a vendor's ratings are displayed next to their name and on their homepage. The relative weighted sum of each rating group (1 through 5) is displayed in a vendor rating widget that looks like a bar chart when browsing items and on a vendor's page as well. These are meant to give you a quick impression of a vendor's quality. If you want to dig deeper, you can click on the widget and see a full breakdown of a vendor's ratings. In the top right, you'll see the weighted distribution of ratings (same as the widget) and the unweighted distribution. The unweighted distribution is a simple average of all ratings with no weights as described above. Below this are all of the reviews that have been left for the vendor, organized by each rating group. You can sort these by either their weight or freshness.
