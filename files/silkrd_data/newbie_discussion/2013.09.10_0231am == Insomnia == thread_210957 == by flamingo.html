Same boat, and I agree with the longer posts in here. Fact is, without determining the real cause of your insomnia just throwing random hypnotics at it might even make it worse. Most benzos prescribed for temporary relieve end up giving you rebound insomnia that's worse than the baseline. You might have a messed up circadian rhythm due to a melatonin imbalance, or simply not enough physical strain on you during the day, could be a plethora of different things. Read up on sleep hygiene (no, it got nothing to do with showering :P).

Try to follow the guidelines found on the big reputable sites like WebMD, work out, add a little weed.

When I was worst off my sleep deprivation really fucked with me so I started self-medicating with benzos. Worst mistake I could have made.
