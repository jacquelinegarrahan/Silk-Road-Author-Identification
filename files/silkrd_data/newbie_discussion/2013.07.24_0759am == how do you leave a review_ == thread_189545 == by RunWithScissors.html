Title: Re: how do you leave a review?
Post by: CannabisConsumer on July 31, 2013, 12:15 am
Keep in mind that leaving a review also implies finalization.

Finalizing releases the funds to your vendor as they are held for you by silkroad acting as a middleman(escrow is the name for this). The longer it takes you to finalize after recieving your package the more and more (rightfully) pissed the vendor will be at you.

Tl;dr Don't finalize before you get the package but as soon as you get it and you're sure it's what you paid for(don't wait a week to trip on the acid you bought before finalizing) then FINALIZE.
