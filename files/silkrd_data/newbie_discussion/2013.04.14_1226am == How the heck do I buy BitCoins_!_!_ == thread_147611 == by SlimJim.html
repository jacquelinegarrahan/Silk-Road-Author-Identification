i would not use western union or do a random bank transfer to another country because the fees are outrageous - unless you live in an un-populated area, there WILL be someone nearby who is trying to sell coins for cash. its the safest and quickest way of getting btcs

Post by: ilovelucyshow on April 14, 2013, 02:25 am
Using BitInstant is a very good way of procuring bitcoins for those in the US.
It goes without saying that one may be best off using fake information when funding though Bitinstant. I personally wouldn't feel secure trasferring straight from Bitinstant to SR, but everyone has their own risk tolerance. I'd rather move the funds through an additional wallet, then on to SR.

If you use bit instant, go to Wal-Mart. They are the least likely to ask questions. Fill out the Moneygram form in advance, and remember your info!

Oh, if you're using fake info on Bitinstant, be sure to go to an anonymous wireless access point to get the payment slip. It will work through TOR, but your fake info will possibly get flagged for fraud sooner, and you'll have to make up new fake info. (Unless you just wanna do that every time anyway)

I know it's intimidating at first, but you can do it. Take your time, and work through it.
