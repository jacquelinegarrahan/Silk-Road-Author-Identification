Title: Re: buying bitcoins with credit card
Post by: gr8ape on July 18, 2013, 06:36 am
A business accepting credit cards as payment is very risky. While bitcoin transactions are final, credit card transactions can be disputed and reversed. This means a scamming buyer could pay with a CC, get BTC, and report to his card issuer that it was a fraudulent purchase. The card issuer would then take the money back from the business. This leaves the business out BTC and payment.

Good luck.
