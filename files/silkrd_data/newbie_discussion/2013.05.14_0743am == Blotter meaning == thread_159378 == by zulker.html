Title: Re: Blotter meaning
Post by: zetterberg99 on May 14, 2013, 08:02 am
The drug lingo is so interesting to me, and definitely varies based on your location.

In my experience "1 blotter" refers to a single tab of acid.  Usually about 1cm x 1cm in size or less.  Multiple tabs are sold on larger pieces of blotter paper which are divided into a grid pattern (some are perforated others not.)  Typically a sheet if LSD is 100 tabs (although a 900 hit sheet would be something!)  I'm not sure what vendor you're looking at but I would saw "5x5"=25 doses is a pretty safe bet.
