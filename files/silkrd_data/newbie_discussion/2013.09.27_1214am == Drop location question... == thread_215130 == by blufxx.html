Post by: gn0ssos on September 27, 2013, 12:24 am
You'll hear a lot of different things here, but I personally use my own home address and real name. I just feel most comfortable this way and what's less suspicious than that? I only order small personal amounts and I never order internationally, so I feel pretty safe. Honestly I think it's gonna look a lot more conspicuous for you to be rummaging around in some other house's mailbox, whether it's for sale, abandoned or whatever. Also keep in mind that it's generally the same mailmen on the same route every day so they know what names should be going to what houses. I just wouldn't feel comfortable doing that but do what makes you feel safest...

I read a post the other day where someone was saying they get their orders delivered to a hotel. They call ahead and be sure they have the package and just act like they're staying there. Not sure of the details but it seemed to work out for them. Once again, do what makes you feel the most comfortable. This is just what works for me.

Post by: aghostlyfart on September 27, 2013, 02:51 pm
If you poke around the forums, and trusted vendors page alot of people actually recommend using your real address and your real name. Theres lots of reasons for this because the PI's actually look for mail out of the norm and thats a good way to have a failed delivery or even a controlled delivery. Honestly SR is pretty damn safe IMO as long as your encrypting your address to the vendor and what not. Do your research first especially about the vendor. As long as you're not ordering LBS of shit you should be safe you know? Plus vendors are obviously good at what they do, i've seen some sneaky shit in my experience ;) Don't risk not receiving your product, and taking money out of a good vendors pocket by getting a refund for a delivery problem that you created. It takes alot of work for PI's and piggies to work together to make a CD. They have to first go through millions of things of mail, pick out the fishy ones then they have to then sort through the fishy ones and make a decision based on it manually. On top of that they have to then get a warrant to search the package as its even illegal for them to check your mail, then they have to inform the police who ALSO have to get a warrant and research the address and the person. You're looking at 1-3 weeks of warrants and shit for them, surely they aren't going to waste our tax money and their time cracking down on someone who ordered a few grams of a particular substance for their own use IMO. The more paranoid you are, the more likely you are to get caught from my experience think smart, use your heart :)

Post by: IngenPR on September 27, 2013, 04:54 pm
In the shipping section of the forum an intern postal inspector answered lots of questions.

PO Boxes you need two forms of ID to set up usually. They can just as easily be used for a CD. Try and stick to personal use amounts. Use a real name, real address. Make it look like regular mail. The smaller, less out of the ordinary it looks the better. You don't want it to garner ANY extra attention for any reason. Mailmen will notice that a house that hasn't had mail to it in months suddenly gets packages.

Oh, and NEVER, NEVER sign for a package.
