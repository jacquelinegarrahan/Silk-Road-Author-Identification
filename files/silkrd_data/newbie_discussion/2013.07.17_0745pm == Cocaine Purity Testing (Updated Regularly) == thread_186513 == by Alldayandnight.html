We would rather not disclose all of our testing methods but I can tell you that we are very knowledgeable on the subject. One of the main ways we test is by using hot plates. This isn't the only way we test or the best way but it just gives you a little insight on what we do.

Cuts can normally consist of:

Quinine
Lidocaine
Procaine
Lactose
Caffeine
