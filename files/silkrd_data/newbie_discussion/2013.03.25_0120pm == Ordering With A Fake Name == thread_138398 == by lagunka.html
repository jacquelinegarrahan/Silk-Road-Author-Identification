Title: Re: Ordering With A Fake Name
Post by: testingtesting12 on March 25, 2013, 01:49 pm
There seems to be quite a lot of conflicting suggestions about which names to use on your packages. I'm sure that, if you were going to use a fake name, a fake magazine subscription wouldn't hurt. However, I've started using my real name on all my orders- I doubt it increases the likelihood of your getting arrested given that anyone could have used that name, and I think it certainly does increase your odds of actually getting your drugs if they end up at the post office.

What does seem like good advice is to wait before opening your package. I know it's exciting but just set it aside until the evening or the next morning. In this way it'll be much harder to get caught "with" drugs if the police have had their suspicion aroused.
