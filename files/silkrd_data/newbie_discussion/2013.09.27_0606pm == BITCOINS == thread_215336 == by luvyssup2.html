Yes, the bitcoins (BTC) fluctuate like any other currency around the world. 

A good time to buy then is when they're cheap :)  Like they used to be like $10 per btc... now they're $130 per btc.  So clearly if you bought a bunch when they were $10, you made a bunch of profit if you held onto them.  Think of it like playing the stock market in that sense. It's difficult to predict when, for that sort of scenario... other than that, just buy them when you need them to purchase stuff like 88fxstc stated.

As for when to buy, prices usually spike toward the end of the week, so I find it's better to buy them  early in the week, but again, it really doesn't matter most of the time.

I have been fucked when I purchased coins at a high price and the exchange rate plummeted. I'm not sure, but I think that happens in listings priced in btc.
