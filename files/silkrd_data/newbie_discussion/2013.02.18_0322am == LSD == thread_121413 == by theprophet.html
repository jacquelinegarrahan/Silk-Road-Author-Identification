Without any real lab-tests run by people receiving them there is no definitive way for either us buyers or LD to know without a doubt that there is a issue with ug distribution. I'm tempted to find a mail-in lab, but then I'd have to sacrifice a few tabs and then there is the question of degradation in shipment, etc. For the batch I got I would think 2 would be a decent visual trip and 3-4 would start to get into heavy mindfuck zones. The 1.25 I had left me tripping for a solid 12-14 hours, warm afterglow, was doing yoga stretching half the day, good times had by all.

I was even going to order a third round but his page was down for a while due to some SR glitch involving vendors going into vacation mode or something, I'm so non-technical that it all means nothing to me. Anyway, I enjoyed the experience on LD's tabs and while it's just my opinion I do not think LD would purposefully or knowingly distribute tabs of a low dosage or of uneven distribution. If anything it could be related more to his source, but I think I caught rumor of production moving in-house to off-set these very risks, but I can't confirm anything as I'm just a know-nothing tab-eater. I think he's a little swamped with orders/messages, but was kind enough to respond to me for my first order and in such a way that I fully believe he believes in the product, which for an L vendor means that any issues will eventually be worked out. Unless you're Tony76, but he was the kind of doctor that didn't take his own medicine, which doesn't rank high in my book.

Which is just to say, I've had such success with LD that I have no problem investing in a third, fourth, or fifth order in the future. In the meantime I'm waiting for another molecule to float in off the breeze but until then I'm totally satisfied with what I've received.

If you have questions I'd check out the Rumor Mill and spend some quality time reading through the LSD Avengers thread. I placed a small order with Tessallated just to see, I like to keep 2-3 good lines open in case one falls away from SR and also just to see the difference in quality, shipment methods, customer interaction, etc.

Anyway, yeah, read through the Avengers, there tends to be a LOT of discussion going on in that thread regarding what's going on in the here and now. It's the first place people go to talk about anything and it's pretty accurate. I only wish the crowd here at SR was a little more forgiving as it seems like a vendor can be totally shit on for small discrepancies. One or two orders out of thousands that aren't perfect and you better believe that those will be the one or two most vocal people out of the group. Singing someone's praises?
