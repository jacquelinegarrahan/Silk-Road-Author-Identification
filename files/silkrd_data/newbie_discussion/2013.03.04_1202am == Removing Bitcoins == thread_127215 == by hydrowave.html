hi,
i would open a wallet at one of the following two sites:

https://instawallet.org 
https://easywallet.org

at any of those two sites a bitcoin wallet will be created for you instantly upon visiting, along with an adress
note: the url created on that site is the key to your wallet, so make sure you save that one!

then just go to your account page on sr, and there you should see the field "withdraw bitcoins", enter the bitcoin wallet adress in the appropriate field,
the amount you want to withdraw, as well as your silkroad pin code (the 2nd pass phrase you had to create when you registered your account)
hit withdraw, and then just wait until your coin are transferred to your wallet (can take anything from 30 minutes to six hours)

when they arrive at your wallet, just transfer them back to your respective adress at mtgox.
if you are a vendor more measures might be advisable, like washing your coins as mentioned above - but i can't help with that,
