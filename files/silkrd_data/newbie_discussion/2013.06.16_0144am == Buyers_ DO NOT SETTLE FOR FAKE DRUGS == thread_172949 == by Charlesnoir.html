Title: Buyers: DO NOT SETTLE FOR FAKE DRUGS
Post by: Charlesnoir on June 16, 2013, 01:44 am
To Noobs and Full Members Alike,

Be sure to test your order thoroughly  prior to finalizing or leaving a review for the vendor.

If it does not test as authentic product, or if the effect is not what an experienced user knows to be the effect of said product, contact the vendor with your concerns.

Most importantly, even if you have finalized, but know that the product was sub-standard or downright fake, please alert other buyers with your opinion. 

In every category, buyers are being scammed every day with fake and potentially harmful substances that are advertised as the real thing. 

Even vendors with great reviews and many transactions can be guilty of this practice. 

The only way this will change is if buyers SPEAK UP! 

Don't let your newness or reputation of a vendor allow what you know to be true go by without saying something.
