Title: Re: east coast vendors
Post by: bolorolo on August 16, 2013, 01:57 pm
hey so i wanted to update the thread with things ive learned after 30+ packages in a little less than 2 months

- it is less about the vendors location than it is about the actual vendor and the vendors processing time. fast shipping is useless if they take 3 days to process
- of all my packages, i've never gotten anything sooner than 2 days. my west coast weed purchases usually come within 2-3 days if i use the right vendor

all in all, do your research on the vendor, try them out and see who meets your needs the best

also at first i was a little apprehensive about international orders, and i receive these in 5-7 days tops
