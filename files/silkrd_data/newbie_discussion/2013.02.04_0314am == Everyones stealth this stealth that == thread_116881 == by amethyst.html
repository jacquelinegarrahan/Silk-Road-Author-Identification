Title: Re: Everyones stealth this stealth that
Post by: National Direct on February 04, 2013, 03:58 am
Not much needs to be revealed to set out guidelines.

Firstly, Domestic: I don't think there is one vendor here that doesn't ship within their country. From my history of ordering 80+ packages from many vendors, I feel that some do a great job with disguising products as legal products of different types just shipping domestic (some of these methods clearly cost up to $10, esp. for large orders)! For me, If I'm ordering within Canada (where I am from) All I ask is to receive a discreetly labeled package with no odour coming off the package prior to opening it myself.

Internationally, There is no "perfect solution" but disguising products can work, and often allow a package to be opened at customs and still delivered without suspicion. If you want to avoid customs as much as possible, flat-pack. With crystalline compounds and powders, but the order in a bag or other containment vessel that spans a good portion of the exterior packaging (IE: a half ounce of ketamine or MDMA can be crushed down to fit between a greeting card with a thickness of 1-2mm when measuring the internal packaging. Sandwich the product between two pieces of non-corrugated cardboard, such as those from greeting cards. Tape the product to the cardboard to ensure that clumping, and uneven surface area distribution occurs at a minimum when in transit. Don't forget the Triple-Vacuum Seal any products that are known to be tested for. This also works to prevent product from settling or feeling like a powder at all!à¸¿0.00
