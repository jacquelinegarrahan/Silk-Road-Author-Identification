Hiya,

Anyone who is using SR or the net for substances ought to read 'Drugs 2.0 The Web Revolution That's Changing How The World Gets High', written by Mike Power, published UK by Portobello Books.

Chapter 12 is all about SR, but more importantly it looks at the interaction between LE, RCs and the web. Serious book, serious journalist.

If you aren't so permanently twatted that you can't have sex, or unfotunately have medical issues, then most of us are going to have kids one day. The author draws the first speculative conclusions about the drug world they are going to grow up in in the future. Kinda important.

Great read too.
