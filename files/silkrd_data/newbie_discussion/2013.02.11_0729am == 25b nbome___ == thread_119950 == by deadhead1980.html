No don't do that, don't exceed the dose you want to take otherwise you are asking for trouble.

The idea of taking multiple drops is so that you don't get one drop that is a lot more concentrated than the others. Instead your dose is spread out over three drops instead of one for the same final amount.

Basically, start out with a dose in mind and stick with it. If it is not strong enough, given you mode of delivery, then up it a bit next time and see if that is better.
