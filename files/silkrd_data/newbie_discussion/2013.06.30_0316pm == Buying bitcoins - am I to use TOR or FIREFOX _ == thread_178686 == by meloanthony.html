Title: Re: Buying bitcoins - am I to use TOR or FIREFOX ?
Post by: boaclon224 on June 30, 2013, 03:28 pm
There's nothing unsafe about using Tor, so long as you're using HTTPS or hidden services. So bank transfers using it are fine You may get a call from your bank asking if you're aware someone accessed your account from another country - tell them you're using a proxy and they'll leave you alone - they don't mind, they'll just be pleased they don't have to open a fraud case.

Do be careful to check the HTTPS connection is OK though (no broken padlock symbol or scary warning page), as it's possible a tor exit node could be trying to scam you.

But as ben78ynos mentioned, you don't need to use Tor to buy bitcoins. It buys you more anonymity, but obviously if you then just put your bank details into a form then that service still knows who you are. Also some bitcoin exchanges forbid Tor, because they want to look like they aren't helping people like us.
