Title: Re: Questions about how your purchases are shipped
Post by: klemmo on August 05, 2013, 06:29 am
1) Maybe/maybe not. You can always request this by PM

2) One order per vendor - stack up as many items as you want & click update. Just your name & address goes in the box here - other details (items,amounts,shipping etc) are at the top of the order.

Remember to ENCRYPT your name & address with the vendor's public key first

Discretion (stealth) will vary. Some consignments will be immediately apparent, some won't. Get to your mail first.

Return address will be fake if the item consigned is not legitimate. Home & Garden purchases, for example, may have legitimate return addresses.

1. You should contact the vendor individually to ask about combined shipping options. Vendors have different ways they deal with this. Some could create a custom listing, some have combined shipping built in. You really have to ask.

2. First, USE PGP. Just do it.
Second, you should use the cart only for one vendor. This makes it so you don't force them to try to decode three different PGP keys or whatever. So basically, take all the items out of your cart, start one with all of the items from one vendor. Encrypt your address with their pgp key, and send the BTC. Repeat this process for each individual vendor.

"what kind of discretion have you all experienced with vendors, like what sort of things to they do, its highly likely someone will be opening my mail illegally?"
I'm not really sure what you mean by this. As far as stealth goes, you should look at the comments that customers put on the listings. Lots of them usually comment on stealth. Some of the vendor's profiles will even talk about stealth.

"and finally, is the return address they use a fake address or a PO box or what"
They wouldn't use a fake address. That raises major red flags for the postal service. I've heard of a vendor using a comic book store. It really varies. I'm sure some use PO boxes.
