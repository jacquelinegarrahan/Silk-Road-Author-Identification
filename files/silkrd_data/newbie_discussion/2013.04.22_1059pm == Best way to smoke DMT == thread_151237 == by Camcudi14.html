I had a link to a highly rated forum posting a while ago but lost it. :( Will update if I find it...Basically...

You get:
• Vial with side 'outlet'
• Rubber stopper w/ hole
• Breather tube to insert in said hole, and
• Some vinyl tubing to attach to the vial's side arm

You just heat up the DMT using a lighter till it vaporizes making sure not to overheat, after completely vaporized you can just inhale from down the vinyl tube. It is important to be careful with the vapor as it can be quite hot and isn't all that pleasant (harder to keep down than a puff of weed, it can taste kinda like burnt plastic). It's important to hold as long as possible for the most effect. The stuff hits you pretty quick and it can be debilitating so be aware of that. Haha

Also, meth pipes work, I guess there are also special DMT pipes too that have a bend in the tube.
