Post by: Vinospear on June 08, 2013, 11:04 pm
Give it a try or two - you will not be addicted and probably wonder what all the fuss is about.

It's not actually a super-strong drug - you can function in public, although I wouldn't recommend doing a big rail of it right before a job interview.

The addictive qualities are subtle and over many months of use - it can definitely hook you, and potentially destroy your life in some cases, but it's more of a stealth attack where you barely notice it getting slightly stronger over an extended period of use. This can be very dangerous, but you won't wake up the day after your first try saying "I gotta get me some more of that!".
