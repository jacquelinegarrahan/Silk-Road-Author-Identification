If you don't have 0.001g scales....... buy some! If you're working with RC's on an anywhere-near regular basis, it's only a matter of time before a mis-eyeballed dose fucks you up. Or you get some bromo-dragonfly or something by mistake and snort a massive line.

Like Not_a_sheep says, start with 1mg or so just to check that you've got the right compound. Work your way up dose-wise.

Until you get some scales, the liquid measurement method is fine. 250mg in 250ml water or alcohol should work fine (1ml = 1mg).

Title: Re: 2CB dosing
Post by: Tripazoid on April 18, 2013, 06:45 pm
If you keep your liquid in the fridge, it's gonna last longer. If you keep it in the freezer, it's gonna last longer still. Probably years.

Or you could make your 250mg 2cb : 250ml solution, measure a portion of it out (say, 50ml). Then turn the remaining unused 200ml back to powder by evaporating the water off. Powder will keep a long, long time if dry and stored well. I've had RC's in the past that were still fine after 3 years.

Or buy some scales :)
