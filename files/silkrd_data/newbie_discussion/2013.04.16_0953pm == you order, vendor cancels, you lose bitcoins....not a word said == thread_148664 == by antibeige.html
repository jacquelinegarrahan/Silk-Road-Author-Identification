Title: Re: you order, vendor cancels, you lose bitcoins....not a word said
Post by: nanpa2001 on April 16, 2013, 10:14 pm
Hi, the vendor didn't keep any of your money.

The product you attempted to purchase was hedged. That means your purchase is pegged to the US dollar the moment you finish in the shopping cart. In the event of a refund, those escrowed dollars are converted back to bitcoins at whatever the current weighted average is for bitcoins, minus the SR hedging fees.

That is why some of your bitcoins were missing. On an unhedged order, this isn't an issue at all.
