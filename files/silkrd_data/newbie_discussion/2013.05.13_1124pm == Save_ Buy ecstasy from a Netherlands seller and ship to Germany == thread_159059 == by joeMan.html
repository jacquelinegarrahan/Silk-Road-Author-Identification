On the original topic: I think the chances of passing through customs without being intercepted is actually pretty high as long as the order is just a letter (not a package), MBBs are used, professional outside package (not handwritten or anything like that), real return adress is used, and that the receiving address is not flagged by LE or customs already.

Nevertheless, it is of course still possible that a shipment is intercepted even though all these measures were taken but the probability is just much lower.
