Title: Re: White MDMA vs brown MDMA
Post by: wolf2wolf on July 25, 2013, 02:57 am
In it's purest form, MDMA/HCL should be a white crystalline solid. Brown or any other color variation, usually means there is at least some impurity left over from the synthesis. It doesn't take much for the color to change though, so it's possible to have very high purity off-white, grey or tan MDMA. It's also possible to have white MDMA that is not that pure and cut.

Basically, you shouldn't draw too many conclusions on the color alone. Get a testing kit or, if you can, send it to lab to see exactly what you have.
