Post by: YayAreaPlaya on June 27, 2013, 03:34 am
Some of the more potent opioids like oxycodone/morphone and hydromorphone have pretty short half-lifes.  If you get your dosage right with Dilaudid, you'll have a fantastic body high for a few hours, without the laid out, zonked effect you get from higher doses or more intense opioids.

Of course, take too much and you'll just end up laying on your bed for the night.
