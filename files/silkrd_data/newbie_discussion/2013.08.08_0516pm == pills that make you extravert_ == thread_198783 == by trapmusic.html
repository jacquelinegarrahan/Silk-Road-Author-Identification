Title: Re: pills that make you extravert?
Post by: postrex on August 09, 2013, 12:13 am
Quote from: trapmusic on August 08, 2013, 10:16 pm
alright thank you all for these suggestions. I'll definitely try some out. There is just one more question that came into my mind:
Why do meds that are made to get hyper people to stay calm (so to talk less etc.) also work to get people to be more outgoing? That are two opposite effects? lol
It's a misconception that stimulants make you hyper - they will provide increased energy to an extent, but increased hyperactivity will only occur with larger doses.  Stimulants medications administered in more moderate doses work by making everything seem more interesting - whatever you are doing you will be engaged and interested.  Basically, think of something you love doing and can be engaged by for hours.  Take some Dexedrine, and you will feel like that about almost anything you do.  That's why these medications work so well for people with ADHD - they makes it easier to concentrate on a given task because it is simply more engaging and stimulating.

Higher doses will of course produce more profound effects, up to and beyond increased hyperactivity.  But for social situations, 5mg (or even just 2.5mg) of pure Dexedrine is plenty for most people to feel engaged and social.

If you're ever curious about Dexedrine or stimulants in general, feel free to send me a message on SR.  Am happy to answer questions, as I consider stimulants the second most interesting drug-class (#1 being psychedelics).
