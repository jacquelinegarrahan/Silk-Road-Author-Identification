Title: Re: newbie question: postage options
Post by: james23456 on July 01, 2013, 01:35 pm
Well you have to choose a postage option, otherwise your item won't get posted. Each postage option varies from vendor to vendor. PM the vendor first before purchase if you don't understand a particular vendor's postage options. Hope that helps.
Title: Re: newbie question: postage options
Post by: snark on July 01, 2013, 02:09 pm
You do need to choose one, but not every vendor charges postage fees so if you really don't want to pay the extra you can always shop around for another vendor. Keep in mind some just roll their postage fees into the cost of the product as well.
