Title: Re: Packages have not arrived yet.. Should I worry?
Post by: jekyll on June 08, 2013, 02:40 am
People establish and use drop points in different ways.  There's lots of different opinions about this issue in the forums.  Personally, if I got a love letter from Customs, I wouldn't receive an international package at that address again.  But I probably wouldn't worry about domestic packages.

Some people would never ship to their own address in the first place.  I order everything online anyway, and I get packages practically every day, so I don't have a problem ordering SR stuff to my house.  But domestic is much safer, so I rarely order internationally.

I have used drop points occasionally.  I live in a population dense area, and for me, the easiest drop points are neighbors.  I have a couple of neighbors who work long hours, so every now and then, if I need to order to a drop address, I have my package sent to a neighbor and then liberate it from their mailbox before they get home from work.  There's no room for mistakes with this method though.  You have to know when your package is coming and be able to watch for it and collect it before the neighbor gets home.
