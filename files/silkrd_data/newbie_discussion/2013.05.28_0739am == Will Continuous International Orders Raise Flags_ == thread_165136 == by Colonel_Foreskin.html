Title: Re: Will Continuous International Orders Raise Flags?
Post by: android465764E on May 28, 2013, 08:51 am
It will only raise red flags if you get caught. That is to say if one package is discovered, packages from the same location thereafter will come in for extra scrutiny.
If you order 5 packages and the first one is discovered, the following packages will more than likely be seized also.

The Netherlands is a place that is well known for drugs, and is considered by some to be a risky place to order from.

Personally, I have no problem with ordering from abroad, but I prefer to space out my orders, and try not to order too much at once.
