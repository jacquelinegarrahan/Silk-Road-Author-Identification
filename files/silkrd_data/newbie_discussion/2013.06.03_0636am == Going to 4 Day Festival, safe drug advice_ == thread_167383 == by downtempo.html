Title: Re: Going to 4 Day Festival, safe drug advice?
Post by: MO2021 on June 03, 2013, 06:55 am
If you're going to be taking MDMA two night within the four days I'd suggest thinking about spending day 3 sober (except weed and maybe a couple drinks) to recover for day 4. I'd also recommend watching your dosage/redose rate on night 1. Between the late nights, sleeping outdoors, and a good comedown waking up at a festival can be hell, and you don't want to ruin day 2. Basically just pace yourself and remember to eat and stay hydrated. I just did a 3 day recently and made the mistake of going too hard too early on night 1 and ended up passing out early and ruing a lot of day 2. Its a marathon not a sprint.
