Title: Re: How to make chrystal meth?
Post by: JoeHartley on February 05, 2013, 09:58 am
Step 1) Learn to spell crystal.
Step 2) Making meth isn't that hard, to be honest if you can follow a cake recipe you can make some shake-n-bake at least. Making really pure meth is a-whole-nother story. And, without sounding like too much of a  dick, if you can't spell crystal then I doubt that you have it in you to produce some high-grade shards. However, if you're certain you want to try, studying up on some basic organic chemistry is the place to start; acid-base extractions, distillation, simple stuff like that. Reading Uncle Fester's 'Secrets of Methamphetamine Manufacture' is also good, but it can't be used as the end-all guide as it does contain some errors. Strike's 'Total Synthesis 2' is also a must-read, and his 'Sources' book is also worth a look too. Archives of 'The Hive' and 'Synthetical' (both of which were clandestine-chemist forums) can be found on the deep web, and are also extremely useful. But even after all this, even if you find a method which you can reasonably carry out, it's unlikely you will have great success until you understand the chemistry inside every reaction of every step of the process. Then, if you're skilled, you can make adjustments to the method based off your practice and experience, and create something truly brilliant ;)

Main point being; there is no easy method. Study hard and you might make it!
- JH
