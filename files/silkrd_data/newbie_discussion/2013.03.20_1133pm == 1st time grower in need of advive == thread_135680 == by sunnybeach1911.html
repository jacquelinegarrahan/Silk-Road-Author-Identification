Title: Re: 1st time grower in need of advive
Post by: xpsbud on March 20, 2013, 11:56 pm
Buy best seeds you can find and don't handle them after germ, use the correct method - no paper towel. Avoid clones unless you really know the grower well, you don't want to start off with someone's problems - disease and bugs. Make sure your area has fresh air and proper circulation. Use a fan to help strengthen stems and use it early on. Use a good carbon filter. Keep humidity correct. Don't over water! Don't over-or under fertilize! Don't take your plants too early, lots of weight forms in the last couple of weeks. Flush when the pistil are a little brown. After your plants stop growing when pistils are mostly brown harvest.

No. That's not possible. Not one bit. The air won't magically combine with the toilet water and flow away. (Ō_o)

You need an extraction fan and a carbon filter. The fan sucks the stinky air through a tube into a carbon filled box, of sorts. Which cleans the air, removing the smell.

If you're worried about the smell, which tbh you should be, then you must get a carbon filter. It's essential. And don't just vent the air outside without a filter either. The whole street will be able to smell it.

Keep your light on during the day. Off at night. Depending on your cycle.

If you're worried about choppers picking up hotspots with thermal cameras, then there are solutions to this. For instance, instead of HiD lighting (hps etc.. ) you can use CLF bulbs, which run at a MUCH cooler temp.
