Title: Re: Mt Gox to SR
Post by: lelmeriodici on March 27, 2013, 03:25 pm
Assuming you are in the US....

1.  The laws vary wildly from state to state, you would have to consult with an attorney to know for sure.

2.  99% of the courts in the US don't have time to prosecute someone for putting money into SR (where they might just be buying a book :P)

3.  You ought to keep some Bitcoins in your Mt.Gox account, just as an investment and a thin facade to your true operations, and spend some on legitimate things (like gift cards: http://btcbuy.info/).

4.  When you want to transfer Bitcoins to SR, send them first to a free Blockchain wallet, then "shared send" (0.5% fee, breaks the chain of transactions) them to an Instawallet (instawallet.org) YOU MADE IN TOR.  Then send them to SR.  It takes a bit, but its pretty bulletproof.  You could also use Bitcoin Fog (http://fogcore5n3ov3tui.onion) to break the chain of transactions, it costs more though (1-3%).  If you wanted less security you could skip the Instawallet.
