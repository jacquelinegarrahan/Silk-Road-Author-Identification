Title: Re: sam giancana scamming
Post by: captainjojo on May 02, 2013, 02:32 am
Four days after your order is placed you can cancel it if the vendor hasn't placed the order into transit.  Once they place it into transit you have to wait until the resolve link becomes active, at which point you can ask the vendor for a refund.  Make sure you do not let it auto-finalize or you will lose your money and there will be little anyone can do about it.

If the vendor has already placed the order in transit you can ask them to cancel it OR PM SR support/admin on SR and explain the issue and ask them if they can cancel the order (also, click on the 'Report Vendor' link in their profile to report what you say has happened).

If you have FE'd then there isn't much anyone can do for you now.
