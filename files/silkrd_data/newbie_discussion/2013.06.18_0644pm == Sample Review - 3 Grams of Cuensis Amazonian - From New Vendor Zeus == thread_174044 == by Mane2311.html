Title: Sample Review - 3 Grams of Cuensis Amazonian - From New Vendor Zeus
Post by: Mane2311 on June 18, 2013, 06:44 pm
OK. So first off, massive apologies to Zeus for taking so long to getting round to writing this review. And a massive thanks for being one of the "Chosen Ones" and receiving a very generous sample of 3 (3.1 to be exact) grams of dried, powdered Cubensis Amazonian shrooms for just $1 postage! :D

Please excuse me if I leave out anything important, I've never written a product review or trip report before.

COMMUNICATION - 5/5

Zeus had great prompt and polite communication throughout. No problems here :)

DELIVERY TIME - 5/5

Germany -> UK. Pretty quick. No complaints :)

PACKAGING AND STEALTH - 4/5

Packaging and stealth was excellent; double vacuum sealed, so I even found it hard to get into and it was well hidden in the envelope between paper.

My only reason for giving less than 5/5 here is because the envelope had printed on the front the return/senders name and address. This was also printed on paper inside the envelope.

I would obviously highly recommend to new vendors not to do this as obviously the receiver can deny knowledge of the package contents whereas the sender can't! I've subsequently received other packages from the E.U. which also have this. Is it a postage requirement there or something?? Even if it's a fake name and address I'd recommend not doing this if at all possible.

PRODUCT - 5/5

As I said I've never written a trip report before and tend to take them with a pinch-of-salt because i believe that who you are can have almost as much effect on how you feel as the substance you ingest, but anyway here you go: Overall they were great. I have a reasonably amount of experience with hallucinogens, but not a great deal with mushrooms. So I originally started of with about 1.6-1.8g sprinkled on a pizza (not baked on, just fresh on top of pre-made one I cooked) and then topped up just eating more of them throughout the afternoon.
As it was going to be a nice sunny day ,the plan was to go to a local park and drink and smoke spliffs with some friends. In the end the weather wasn't quite as good as I'd hoped and I ended up hanging out with some people I didn't know too well but this wasn't a problem. I was all smiles and plenty of spliffs were smoked and beers drunk ;)
I had a wonderful afternoon. Very nice floaty, full-body feeling you get with proper more "traditional" trips like acid (as oposed to RCs). Visually very nice, my favourite part being the beautiful trails that followed everything around, like the birds flying across the park.
So overall I had a great time. Thanks again Zeus I'll def be back when I was some more shrooms :)
