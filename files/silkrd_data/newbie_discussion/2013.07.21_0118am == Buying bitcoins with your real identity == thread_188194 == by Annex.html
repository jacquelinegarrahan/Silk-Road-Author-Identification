Post by: atwas99 on July 21, 2013, 02:38 am
My way probably isn't the most anonymous, but here it is

Bank account >> Dwolla >> Campbx >> Bitcoin Fog >> Bitcoin wallet >> Silkroad

1) I have my bank account connected with Dwolla. From Dwolla I withdraw funds from my bank account.
2) Then I withdraw funds from Dwolla into Campbx. In Campbx I use my transferred funds to buy bitcoins. 

3) Once I've bought some bitcoins, I transfer the BTC to Bitcoin Fog (http://fogcore5n3ov3tui.onion)
4) Then I withdraw the BTC from Bitcoin Fog to my Bitcoin wallet.

Finally, from my wallet I transfer the BTC to silkroad

I've found this way to be the quickest and easiest for me
