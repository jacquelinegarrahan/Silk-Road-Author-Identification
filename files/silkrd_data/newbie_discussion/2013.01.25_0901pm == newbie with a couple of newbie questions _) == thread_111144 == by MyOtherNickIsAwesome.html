Title: Re: newbie with a couple of newbie questions :)
Post by: emptystomach on January 25, 2013, 09:34 pm
Congrats on taking the plunge and making your purchase!!  I'm about 8 purchases deep myself.  And I've been reading this forum and the subreddit for weeks.  What I've learned about FE is to never do it.  Especially for your first purchase.  There are enough vendors to choose from that don't require FE, that you can stay clear of the ones that do.

As far as "in processing", that varies from vendor to vendor.  But there's no need to worry about your money.  It's in escrow.  If you're not comfortable with how long it's taking for him to ship it out, then send a message asking what's up with the order.  I had this happen twice - one vendor said it would be another few days til he could ship, so I asked him to cancel my order (the coins are put right back into your SR wallet).  The other time, the vendor apologized and said he got backed up on orders and shipped on the 3rd day.

Note that many vendors promise "fast shipping" on their profile page.  And it might be their goal to always ship immediately.  But you know how the game can be. . . things don't always go according to plan.  I would offer this advice though, if you're in a hurry: pick a vendor that offers express shipping.  Not all of them do, so you might have to click through a few pages until you find one.  But I've done this a few times this week and gotten my order within 48 hours.  Totally worth the extra $$  because I am trying to party hard tomorrow night.

Good luck man
