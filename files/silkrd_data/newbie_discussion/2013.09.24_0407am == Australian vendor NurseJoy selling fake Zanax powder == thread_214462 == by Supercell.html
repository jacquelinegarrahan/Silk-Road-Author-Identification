Title: Australian vendor NurseJoy selling fake Zanax powder
Post by: Supercell on September 24, 2013, 04:07 am
After previously purchasing alprazolam powder from the vender NurseJoy and receiving a bitter tasting powder with a consistency similar to talcum powder, and having an effect that was no doubt that of alprazolam, I felt pretty confident ordering again. The order I received next however, was certainly not alprazolam.

My first alarm call came when looking at its consistency. Instead of the very fine powder one would expect, it had a gritty consistency, with large, half peppercorn sized solid clumps in it. The best way to describe what it looked like would be a crushed up pill. I then tried roughly four mg of the powder to find a slightly bitter, but a far different tasting powder to alprazolam.

The effects started to hit about twenty minuets later and far from the relaxing, clear minded experience I'm use to and look forward to with alprazolam, the best way I could describe this was not pleasant and similar being stoned on weed. I felt panicky and my partner commented on the redness of my eyes. I bring this up now because I've noticed in their reviews, several others expressing doubt about whether their "alprazolam" is even a benzo.

A lot of customers of theirs still seem satisfied, so whether those people are inexperienced with Xanax, or, it's only a certain percent who receive the false product I don't know. If anybody else has had a similar experience, then please share it and for those considering buying alprazolam powder from NurseJoy, please be careful.
