Post by: iliketurtles on March 06, 2013, 09:59 pm
Your best bet would be to become friends with a real estate agent.

However, my preferred method is to ask a *friend* if you can simply have some mail sent to *their* house under *your* name, because *your* housemates go through *your* mail. So when *your friend* receives mail in *your* name at *their* house, *they* don't know what's in it, in case anything goes down for *them*.
