Title: Re: Cocaine to the UK from Europe
Post by: bastrak on September 05, 2013, 08:51 pm
It's most likely either delayed in the post or HM Customs have intercepted it, which would usually be due to insufficient stealth.

In the latter case you should get a seizure notice from Customs in the post and claiming it is yours once you get the notice would obviously be a bad idea.

I have never had a seizure but given the amount I would have thought it unlikely any further action is likely. I'd leave it a month and use a domestic supplier from then on. I usually go domestic anyway. It's a lot less worry as domestic UK mail is not screened for drugs at all.
