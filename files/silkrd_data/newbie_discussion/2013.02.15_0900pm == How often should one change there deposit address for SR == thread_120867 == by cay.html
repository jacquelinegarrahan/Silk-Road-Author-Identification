I know SR shuffles money around, but I thought I'd ask if anyone has a policy on this to follow. I was thinking about every 3 or so transactions.

You should use a new Bitcoin address for each and every transaction. Using an address more than once will give LE an opportunity to link several transactions to the one buyer should they ever come to the attention of  LE. It's so simple to hit "get a new Bitcoin address" for each transaction and minimize any chance of multiple transactions ever being linked to you. :)
