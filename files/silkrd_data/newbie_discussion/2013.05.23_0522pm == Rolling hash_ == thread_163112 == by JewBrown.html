Hey JewBrown,
first of all, if the hash is good it will be easier than if it's a bad one. So what you got to do is this:
get a spoon, put a piece of the hash into it, put your lighter under the spoon (it will be black btw, so consider that your parents/whoever don't get mad about that spoon), warm it a bit up and then chop it into little pieces with your finger. Then you should have a nice amount of hash pieces which are pretty small and then you put them just into your joint like you do it with your weed.
Enjoy the smoke.
