Post by: CrunchWrappedCoke on August 22, 2013, 03:56 am
Okay, to start, no need for SWIM bullshit here.

How close is that?
Regardless, address it to the person. The goal is to make it look as normal as possible. Someone might see the fact that that person doesn't live at that address and wonder why it's being sent there. Thus drawing more attention. The goal is to be normal and ordinary in external appearance.

If you're sending weed, make sure you don't just put it in a plastic baggy and drop it into a box. Find some method to minimize/eliminate the smell. Don't make them sign for it either. If it goes wrong, deny any knowledge of the incident.

Post by: galileosophia on August 22, 2013, 04:05 am
Use their name.  While it's awful to get a package intercepted, the great thing about mail is that anyone can send you anything without you requesting it, so there's always plausible deniability if it's addressed directly to the person.  If there's deception involved, i.e., a fake name or something is used, it looks worse if something goes wrong.
