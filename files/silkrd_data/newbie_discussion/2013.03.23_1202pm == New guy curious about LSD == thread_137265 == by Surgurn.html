Post by: oldcactushand on March 23, 2013, 12:24 pm
I would recommend Tessellated. Jesus of Rave has vials of what is apparently some of the best LSD in Europe if not the world, but you'd have to buy a bit more, so probably not worth it if you've never tried acid.

I have only made 5 purchases on the road, 2 have been from Tess. Incredibly fast shipping both times, great comms, does not require FE, and the stealth is amazing. Really amazing. He/she is very open to new buyers and never asks for FE. Good prices (relative to other vendors), great product. The dosage of these tabs is not advertised since lab results have not come back yet. They were originally listed at 200ug, and many of the reviews attest to this dosage.

That said, before making any purchase I'd say it's worth doing your own research, don't just take my word for it, check out Tess' review thread. There's also a group called the Avengers who work to provide everyone with reliable information about LSD vendors, I'd check out their thread too (at least the first page).

As for dosage... LSD is several times more potent than 25i, hence the lower dose. And they are really not the same drug. I'd recommend taking 100-200ug for the first dose. You will not be disappointed with a 200ug dose, I'm sure. And if you are, then you'll know for next time.

Good luck on SR. Just a few weeks ago I was in your position. Have ordered 4 times with absolutely no hiccups. These forums are essential if you do not want to get scammed IMO, so read read read the reviews of a vendor before buying from them. If there aren't any reviews, be very careful. Hope this helps.
