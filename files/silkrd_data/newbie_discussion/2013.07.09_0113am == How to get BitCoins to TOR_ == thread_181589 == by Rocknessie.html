Title: Re: How to get BitCoins to TOR?
Post by: milkabuki on July 12, 2013, 09:59 am
Go to your SR account and look for something like 'deposit btc'.

it'll take you to a page that warns your to know your PIN as it's irretrievably linked to your SR wallet.

It gives you the address to your SR wallet you can send your btc to. Copy that address, go to your UK site wallet and send your btc to the address SR gave you.
