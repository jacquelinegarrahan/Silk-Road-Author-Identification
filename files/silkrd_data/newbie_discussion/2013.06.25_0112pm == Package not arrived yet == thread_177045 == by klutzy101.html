Title: Re: Package not arrived yet
Post by: mcguire39 on June 25, 2013, 01:38 pm
It is tough waiting. The first couple orders are the most nerve wracking until you build up a little stash so that the arrival of the next order if it takes an extra week or so isn't so bad. For sure usps has some stuff royally screwed up in some parts of the country. There's articles indicating that for some areas where they closed the sorting center, the mail (even local in that town/city) gets sent to a sorting center that could be many states away and then comes back, and other weird things like that.

I try not to sweat it too much. There's pretty much three main things that could happen.
1. It arrives later than expected. This is still a good outcome; vendor might have shipped it late, but it could easily be usps being slow too.
2. It gets 'lost' in the mail and never arrives. This sucks but such is life. Good vendors may reship or offer other condolences. But let's not forget it cost the vendor too, if they genuinely sent the product and it got lost, I really think if they go 50/50 on a reship that is plenty accommodating.
3. Vendor didn't actually ship it. Unfortunately that is life. Win some lose some. Mostly win provided you pick vendors carefully. If you don't FE you can always run it through as a dispute. Or if it isn't worth it to you to dispute just take the hit and move on. Life is short.
