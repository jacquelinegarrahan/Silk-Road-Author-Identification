Title: Re: E-cig with Hash oil...
Post by: zebra420kitty on June 04, 2013, 06:50 am
quite easy to do this actually.

Things you need.
1 bowl of water.
1 microwave
ammount of bho/whatever
PG/VG mixture. I use 50/50. some use higher pg ratio to vg. some use only vg.
1 glass container with a lid

get your bowl of water and put in the microwave for 2 min. The water will most likely be boiling. let the water cool for a minute or so. get your glass container put the pg/vg mix in there and then add the bho(or what have you). use a paper clip to get the bho in there. dipping it over and over will make the oil fall off of the paper clip. if you are lazy just use a lighter to heat the paper clip. now put the cap on the mixture. Set the bottle in the bowl of close to boiling water. wait for it to cool down. once it has cooled down get your glass container and shake it while you heat the water again. now put the container back in and rinse and repeat until your hash is completely dissolved.

Generally takes a long time. i did it for 3 hours yesterday and still was not able to get it completely disolved yet. i am having some slight issues getting it as potent as i want. but ill come back here and update.

Also, i have read that you can use a crock pot on the lowest setting and just set it in that for 24 hours. then let it cool and you have your final product. but i dont know about this one since i have never done it
