OK dude, so on Silk Road, click on "account" then on the right of your screen you'll have "account links" go to "account history". There it should say whether recent orders have been finalised and, crucially whether your BTC has been withdrawn to a wallet that's not yours. Sounds like you may have been pished and like someone's finalized your orders and then withdrawn your bitcoin. So whilst your sellers will have had their coin, hopefully, you may have had your money withdrawn. That may not have happened but if you scroll through account history and also "recent orders" links then you should have a better idea of WTF is going on.

Then change your password and pin code. Immediately.

In future DO Not go on any silk road link that isn't the "very big 5 inch Pizza" as I call it, this one http://silkroadvb5piz3r.onion/

Make sure you only ever log in to that one, memorize it - I keep mine bookmarked and go in via that.

Don't create accounts at other online markets with the same credentials as you use on Silk Road.

Good luck and let us know what happens.
