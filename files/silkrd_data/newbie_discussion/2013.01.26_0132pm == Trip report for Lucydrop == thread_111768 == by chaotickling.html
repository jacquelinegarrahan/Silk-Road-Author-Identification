Yes, paper.
Finalizing early (FE) is the act of releasing funds to the vendor before they've shipped the product. This reduces the risk that the vendor will not be paid by a buyer claiming that they didn't receive the product. With less chance of being scammed by a buyer, a vendor is then able to keep prices lower for the customer. However, there is always the chance that the vendor will leave town with your money and not ship the product. That is why many people advocate not using FE and instead remaining in the silkroad escrow system throughout the entirety of the transaction. It's a bit of a balancing act on how much you trust your vendor and their track record.
