Title: Re: $$$ from Dwolla -> MtGox -> SR
Post by: Icon on April 02, 2013, 11:43 pm
This is a common way to get coins, but I'd advise against it. When I joined this place I spent months reading stickies and many posts.

The reason why this is not good for SR users is because you are using Dwolla. To use Dwolla you are transferring funds from a bank account! This will compromise your anonymity. If someone really wanted to they could trace the bitcoin purchase all the way to your bank account. Bitcoins are not automatically anonymous.

From recent reading on the security form people have been having really good luck with this scheme, I have not used it myself yet.

Obtain Cash---->Do a cash deposit to Bitfloor (using a wallet that is not your SR wallet)------>When coins are in wallet send them through a coin tumbler (bitcoinfog or blockchain info mixer) to another wallet (this help to anonyomize your coins)------send to SR wallet.

It might help to send the coins through a few more coin tumblers.

Here is the block chain info mixer address:  https://blockchain.info/wallet/send-shared
