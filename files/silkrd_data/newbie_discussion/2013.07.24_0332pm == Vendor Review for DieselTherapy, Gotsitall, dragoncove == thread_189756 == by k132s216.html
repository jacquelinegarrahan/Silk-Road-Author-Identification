Title: Vendor Review for DieselTherapy, Gotsitall, dragoncove
Post by: k132s216 on July 24, 2013, 03:32 pm
So I've sampled a good deal of the #4 H available domestically. I thought you guys might be interested in hearing my opinions on different vendors gear/service.

DieselTherapy 3/5

So I ordered a half g of DT's cheap H.

Shipping: 2/5
I placed an order on a Saturday, priority. He marks my product in transit on monday which was cool. Then on wednesday he messages me saying he lost my adress and I had to give it to him again which I did. I expected extras for the delay but alas none came. So I expected to get my package by saturday, but DT didn't actually ship until thursday, so I received it on monday. Overall took like 8-9 days for a priority package which is okay, but not what I like.

Stealth: 5/5
Mylar was used which was a pleasant surprise. Overall stealth was fine for domestic.

Communication: 5/5
DT promptly answered all my PM's.

Quality: 1/5
Now here's the problem. I started off with a 150mg line snorted and felt almost nothing. So I opted for a 150mg shot, once again almost nothing, so I did another 200mg shot. Achieved a small buzz after using ALL of the product. Maybe his new stuff is better but that cheap H is NOT worth the price.

Dragoncove 5/5

I've had 2 g's from DC and he is definitely a reliable vendor both took 2 days after being marked intransit to reach my hands. Don't hesitate to give him your business if you're looking for a gram shipped priority M-F.

Shipping: 5/5
Ordered on a monday, was in transit by tuesday, in my hands friday because thursday was 4th of July. For free priority that's pretty sweet.

Communication: N/a
Never had to send him any messages.

Stealth: 5/5
Was well done for domestic.

Quality: 5/5
I've had mixed results with the quality. The first gram I got from him was ridiculously potent. Smelled super dank and was overall just amazing. The second g I got was the same stuff but I ordered right when he took down the listing so it seemed like my g was cut. It had the same smell but was way fainter and didn't dissolve as cleanly.

GIA 5/5

Shipping: 5/5
Express is always overnight.

Communication: 5/5
Always quick to reply and courteous.

Stealth: 5/5
Equal to DC's

DC or GIA are my go to guys. GIA is a little more expensive but quicker.
