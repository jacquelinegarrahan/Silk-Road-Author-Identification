My advice is to ask your hotel/guesthouse to recommend a motovcycle driver, and hire him for the day. Most drivers will offer to get you drugs or take you to brothels. Post by: pingpo_125 on March 03, 2013, 07:19 pm
Having lived in Thailand for the last 6 yrs and a frequent visitor to Cambodia mainly to Phnom Penh I've witnessed the changes for the worse over that time.
5 years ago you could get a gram of meth, china white heroin from one of the dealers on the lake side for $50 and quality was pretty good and 'I'd say the China back then was close to uncut. Alot of changes since then especially last 2 yrs and the Americans building a big ANTI CORRUPTION centre and leading an anti corruption drive. Where as before the police on the lake side would take a pay off from the local dealers all that stopped and alot of dealers got locked up along with some of the senior people in the police force.
god bless America, the American paid for all the electronic finger printing equipment that is installed at the airport and border crossings, I believe all the data goes to the big US embassy there.
Prices these days are $100 for a gram of China white and Meth, if you get to know one of the local dealers and buy often you might get it down to $80 but it will of been stepped on a fair bit now days.
Luckily police stings are a lot less common than in thailand, a few months my guy there took to me to this place to buy some meth and we are outside these houses and 2 guys came up asking what i was doing there, undercover police guys and I said just looking for a guest house and have gotten lost. They said this place is well know for methamphetamine so go now! If that had of happened in thailand the cops would of waited for you to buy the stuff first then busted you and you'd of been marched to the nearest atm to empty your bank account.
I think your best bet is still the lakeside, there are one or 2 guesthouses still open there and i'd book a couple of nights in one of them and you'll be offered stuff from the people working there and tuk tuk guys outside. At least that way you aren't walking in and out of the place and doing a deal on the street.
Wouldn't bother with the random tuk tuk guys who are always pestering you to buy weed and most of it is shit stuff they buy for 30 cents and ask you $20 for.
