Title: Re: First time depositing
Post by: sinister-breaks on August 09, 2013, 11:08 am
No worries we all went through it, sadly the ordering of coins and awaiting them to hit your account takes much longer then ordering from SR itself, and its a royal pain when you realize your short on postage and have to repeat the whole process for something like 0.02 of a coin!!

My advice is get your order in your cart, check the final amount WITH postage, then order the correct coinage. Make sure currency is set to your currency not bit coins (Account > Settings), so you get an actual idea of how much you need to pay for (remember coin price fluctuates constantly so don't order coins based on those values).

Its all a learning curve, first order, order small, go with a trusted vendor, do not use ANYONE that requires FE, some will especially with a new account, DONT DO IT until YOU are comfortable with ordering, thats my best advice!

My first order I was asked to FE early, it was 2 weeks late,and I nearly walked away from SR, don't go through it yourself, there is no need!!!
