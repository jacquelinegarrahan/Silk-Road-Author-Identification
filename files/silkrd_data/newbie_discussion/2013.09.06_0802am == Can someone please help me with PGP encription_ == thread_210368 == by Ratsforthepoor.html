morning, i have a mac and had issues also but now i'm all sorted. this is how i do.....

1) open TextEdit and type your message.
highlight the message with cursor ( as if you're going to copy/paste)

2) go to TextEdit dropdown menu and go to the Services dropdown menu*.
here you should see and choose a option OpenPGP: Encrypt.
(*if you have not highlighted your message this option won't appear)

3) then a small Choose Recipients GPGServices window should appear.
choose/tick your recipient.
importantly in this window (below recipients list) the Secret Key should show your own details.

4) click OK and Choose Recipients GPGServices window disappears. immediately your message in TextEdit gets encrypted in front of you.
generally i attach my Public Key below my encrypted message. done simply by opening and choosing your own name in GPG Keychain Access. press command C (copy) and then in TextEdit under the  encrypted message press command V (paste).

5) now highlight and copy all the TextEdit text (encrypted message and your Public Key) and paste into your PM and send.

6) below is my Public Key if you want to post a encrypted message and i'll decrypt it.
remember to attach your own Public Key.
