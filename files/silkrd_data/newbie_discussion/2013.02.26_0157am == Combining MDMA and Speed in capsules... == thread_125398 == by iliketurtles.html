Title: Re: Combining MDMA and Speed in capsules...
Post by: MDUK on February 26, 2013, 03:26 am
You won't get through 10 hours on MDMA and still be high at the end of it, your body will develop an acute tolerance to it after 6-8 and at that point all any further dose will do is increase the negative neurotoxic effects.

Personally, I don't have a whole bunch of experience mixing speed and MDMA, but what I do when I want the experience to last a little longer is this:

1-Get some decent 5-HTP - 5-HTP is a seratonin precursor, and will increase the amount of seratonin in your brain that can be released by the MDMA. This is something you have to be careful with, as it can lead to seratonin syndrome if you take too much of it with MDMA, and it will increase your sensitivity overall.

2-For 3-5 days beforehand have 200-300mg 5-HTP in the morning. On the day of the festival cut that back to 50-100mg.

3-You'll be a bit more sensitive, so cut your initial dose down maybe 20%, you might find that the sensation is a bit different, as dopaminergenic response isn't significantly altered but seratonin levels go higher, so you get more of that "everything is awesome" feeling.

4-Take doses maybe 50% of your initial dose every couple of hours - you should be able to go for most of the festival this way, you'll still develop an acute tolerance late in the day but it'll buy you an extra couple of hours.

5-Have 200mg of 5-HTP when you get up the following morning, and again for the couple of days after that. It'll really help with the comedown.

You should be able to find 5-HTP online or at supplements places, but it can be a bit rare - once I start selling on here I think I'm going to start packaging 5-HTP capsules in with my MDMA capsules as a comedown solution :D
