Yes, but this isn't Silk Road's responsibility to teach you how to use it, it is your responsibility to seek out and learn.  It takes at least 6 confirmations for the funds to credit your SR account after a deposit has been applied to the address used from your SR account. Each Confirmation takes 8-12 minutes, so about an hour in most cases.  I've heard of it taking upwards of 20+ confirmations for people's SR accounts to be credited with the funds. You can simply check the blockchain address you applied the credit to(the address you deposited to) via https://blockchain.info  and it will tell you how many Confirmations it has, and how much money was credited to that address from your deposit.
