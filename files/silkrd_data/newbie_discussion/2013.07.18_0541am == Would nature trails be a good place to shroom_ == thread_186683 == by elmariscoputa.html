Title: Re: Would nature trails be a good place to shroom?
Post by: gr8ape on July 18, 2013, 06:07 am
Yes, nature trails can be awesome. However, psychedelics have a way of magnifying the little things, so there are some preparations you can take to make sure they stay awesome.

Strangers (people not in your group) will probably freak you out a bit when tripping. And I don't mean yell and scream and run away, I mean fill your head with ego thoughts. In my experience, tripping around strangers has not affected the strangers at all; most people don't even notice that you are on drugs. Instead, your head is filled with thoughts of what those people think about you and other such social nervousness. And then it is just awkward.
Try to pick a place that not many other people will be there. Your group should stick together. Like respect1 said, avoid children.

Bring water. Maybe some munchie food, but definitely water.

Climb a tree.

Take your time.

Remember to sit down and rest at some point.

Listen to the sounds of outside. As hard as you can.

Glowsticks, unless you don't want extra attention. Glowsticks on strings for a good time.
