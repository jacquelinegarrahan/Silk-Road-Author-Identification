Post by: JumboMonkeyBiscuit on May 14, 2013, 11:44 am
Bitcoin traffic can be followed by using the blockchain info. However i have not been able to find anything that says they can determine exactly what the purchases were following the flow of the coins. Someone please correct me if I am wrong? The safer you can be the better. Bitfog basically works like a giant bank deposit that mixes everyone's money together and then returns your money in smaller increments to scatter the blockchain info. My suggestion.... Create a clean user name, one that is only used for transferring bitcoin in and out of SR. Go to bitcoin.org and get a paper wallet for offline storage as well.....Hypothetical here.... If you receive a deposit from your bank account purchase of 10 bitcoins into your wallet and you want to get them to the road. Have your coins posted to your offline paper wallet from the bank deposit. Transfer them out in smaller increments to your clean user name. Then transfer to your purchase name. SR has it's own built in sort of tumblr as well. You don't have to pay a fee to have them tumbled somewhere unless you really feel like you are safer that way.

Just our two cents
