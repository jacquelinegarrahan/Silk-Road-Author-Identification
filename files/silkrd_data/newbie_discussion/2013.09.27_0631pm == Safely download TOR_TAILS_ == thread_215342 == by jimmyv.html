Title: Re: Safely download TOR/TAILS?
Post by: IForgotMyFuckingPassword on September 27, 2013, 07:41 pm
The problem is that the external application can reveal your true IP address, not the IP you're connect from through tor. That's what the warning is about.

A simple solution is to just disconnect from the internet when the download completes and then open and install them. Reconnect when you're done. You don't need an internet connection to install TAILS or TorBrowser.
