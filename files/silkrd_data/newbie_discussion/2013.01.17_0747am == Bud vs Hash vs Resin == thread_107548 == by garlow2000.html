Post by: ProudCannabian on May 02, 2013, 07:23 pm
keif/kif = loose trichomes knocked or sifted from dried weed
Hash = pressed trichomes
hash oil = oil extracted from weed using a solvent of some sort

These are terms that are hundreds of years old, and hash and keif could actually be over a thousand.

Now we have modern extractions that people call this and that.  Back in the 80's, 90's, 2000's resin was known as the black shit at the bottom of your pipe.  Pipe resin.  Uuuuugh.  Now any oil that is concentrated enough to be solid matter is called resin, budder, concentrate, etc.

BHO = butane honey oil
HO = honey oil (sometimes used interchangeably with BHO, straight honey oil can be achieved through extraction and filtering without using butane)

Feel free to add any if you know what you're talking about.
