Title: (Guide) How to buy bitcoins
Post by: Loggan92 on August 15, 2013, 08:51 pm
Step 1:
Have a hi-res scan of your ID or passport (300dpi or more)
Have a scan of your proof of residence; this can either be an utility bill or a residence certificate (In sweden you can simply call what is equivalent to the IRS and request information about yourself where your residential address is also listed)

Step 2:
Register at https://www.mtgox.com , then go to https://www.mtgox.com/forms/verification and upload your scans.

Step 3:
Safest way is to wait until you get a verified account, this will take max 5 days, after this you need to fund your account by visiting https://www.mtgox.com/trade/funding-options , if you live in Europe choose "Europe Bank Transfer (Europe)" then simply copy the information exactly, go to your bank and send the money (In Euros).
If you live outside of the EU you have to choose regular "Bank Transfer" and send the money in American dollars (USD)

Step 4:
Wait until they money arrives and then buy your bitcoins.

Hope this helped
