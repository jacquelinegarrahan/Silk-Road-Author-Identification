Title: Re: MDMA and Alchohol?
Post by: Annex on July 23, 2013, 02:24 pm
Hi Quietguy,

Personally, I've done both together quite a bit. Its definitely not something that will kill you, like say xannies and alcohol might. 
Having said that taking any two or more substances in conjunction with one another is almost always going to put more stress on your liver, kidneys or other vital organs. It's really up to you. The only drugs you really shouldn't take with MDMA are any sort of Selective Serotonin Reuptake Inhibitors (SSRIs; antidepressants) and for two reasons;
1. you probably won't feel the affects
2. you may develop Serotonin syndrome... which can lead to death

MD is great becuase it's pretty forgiving i.e. it is almost physically impossible to die from overdose because the dosage needed is so high. People do however fall off the bandwagon when it comes to water, often to much or too little. Don't go crazy, if you're dancing and sweating and drinking whilst on MD, it's advised to drink 1-1.5 bottles of water each hour.

Hope this helps, holla if you need more info.
