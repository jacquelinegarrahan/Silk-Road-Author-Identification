Miss Demeanor turned out not to be a scammer (like many had feared) so I wanted to jump on here and write a positive review of my experience to help build her reputation back up.

I was amongst the group of people who had their orders cancelled after being marked in transit. Mine was in transit for 10 days then was cancelled for no apparent reason. I received a full refund but no message from the vendor to explain why it was cancelled. I jumped to conclusions as it seemed that nobody else had received their orders and sooooo many people had FE. All signs at this point were negative and I really did not expect the goods would come.

Now I eat my words. Miss Demeanor came through with the goods. My package arrived safely yesterday, approximately 11 days after being marked in transit. Stealth was good and the product was appropriately sealed and hidden inside.

It seemed so odd to me that a vendor would cancel all orders in Escrow and give a general reason to other people that it was "too hard to keep track of the orders" but obviously I, (and many others) were completely wrong.

Miss Demeanour provided a good service. Almost immediate response to messages, fast packaging, fast shipping, good stealth. Product looks good and it on weight but I am yet to try it out. My order turns out to be a freebie too as the cancellation refunded what I had originally paid. Can't complain about that - everyone loves a freebie!!

Miss Demeanour - I apologise for leaving a 1/5 rating after my order was cancelled. If I could update this now to 5/5, I would happily do it. I now have full faith in your service and will be back again for business soon :) ...I really do hope that you turn out to be one of the trustworthy vendors on SR and stick around for a long time.
