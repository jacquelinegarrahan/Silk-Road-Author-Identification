Title: Re: Just about to make my first purchase, but still have a few questions...
Post by: Jack N Hoff on July 04, 2013, 03:01 pm
1.  No.  You're fine.

2.  TAILS is a hardened Linux operating system where everything goes through TOR.  Not technically necessary.

3.  Type your message in the big white box on the left, click the check mark next to their name on the right side under where it says "Encrypt for:", click the button on the top left that says "Encrypt" and in the white box will be your encrypted message.  You can now copy it and send it to the vendor.

Jack
