Title: Re: Is the SR ranking system broken?
Post by: vertigo2020 on September 30, 2013, 01:38 am
The entire ratings and review system needs some work.  DreadPirateRoberts has a topic going on right now in the main forums about this, and everyone is chiming in.  I thought about it some and I think here are the core issues:

1. No clear definition/enforcement of rating/review standards. 
     (Does a package that arrived late but had high quality constitute a 5/5?  Should vendors be allowed to request 'good faith' 5/5 FEs?)

2. There is no differentiation between the purchase and the product.
     (Are we reviewing the overall experience with the vendor?  Or just the product? What about communication and shipping?)

3. Vendors are requesting FE too often and without cause.
     (Even 'good' vendors end up asking for FE, and that leads to false ratings "aka 5/5 FE" which are bad for the community)

4. Buyers are allowing FE's to happen too often and without cause.
     (In the spirit of faith in the system we've all FE'd a 'trusted' vendor and faked a review/rating, which again, is bad for the community)

5.+ ? (Please add in your thoughts)

Personal anecdotes:

 - Vendor Name: V3SPAR
 - Product: Weed
 - R/R: High, with perfect 5.0 score, positive feedback in reviews and product comments - no suspicious behavior
 - Result: No ship, no tracking.  SR resolution with 50% refund.  Vendor claimed buyer scam, but did not provide any shipping details.
 - Note: * Left bad taste in my mouth for SR; I receive various SR products to my secured ship point without fail; this was first no-show/no-ship.
             * I feel like the vendor was selectively scamming; and was poor in communication.  But R/R was high with perfect 5.0 :(

- Vendor Name: Haizenberg
- MDMA & LSD Blotter
- R/R: High, 4.9 score, mixed feeback in reviews and product comments - some late-ship reported
- Result: Eventually ordered three times from vendor:
     (1) LSD Blotter: Asked for FE based on positive reviews, and I obliged; product arrived in 1.5 weeks from EUR, but had no drug effect
     (2) MDMA Messaged vendor about order; without asking he sent a free blotter to me as apology; along with high grade MDMA; arrived in 2 weeks
     (3) LSD Blotter: Based on positive experience, I ordered additional blotter from different brand, vendor has been communicating in process
- Note: This vendor maintains a good amount of criticism, and R/R show mixed bag, but I had a great experience every time. What gives?

Theres more examples like this but I would love to see SR make some editorial and admin changes to prevent situation 1 from happening more and giving 'good' vendors like in situation 2 more exposure and positive credit. 

Anyway my long winded 2 cents.
