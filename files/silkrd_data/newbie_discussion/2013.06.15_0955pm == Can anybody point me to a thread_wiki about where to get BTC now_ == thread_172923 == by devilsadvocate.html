I was having the same problem and needed to get some coins on fast. So I tried  LocalBitcoins (dot)com.
You can find someone in your area that will do pretty much any kind of transaction you're comfortable with. Including face to face, and bank drops. They also have an escrow service and feedback rating or each broker.
I don't see why you need to pick someone local, unless you want to do a face to face though.
I really like the whole bank deposit thing and found a guy that had my coins moving on blockchain less than 10 minutes after I put the funds in his US Bank account.
He did charge 10%, but having an honest broker that lets me deposit coins the way I like is worth it IMO.
