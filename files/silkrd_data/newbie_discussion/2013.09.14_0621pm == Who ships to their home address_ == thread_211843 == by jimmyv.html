Post by: captainjojo on September 14, 2013, 10:52 pm
The general rule is:

If you do not have access to a safe drop box, an address where you can have the package delivered and pick up safely, then yes, use your home address and real name.  But, change the spelling of your name just a bit.

Change 'John to Jon' or 'Steven to Stephen'.  Change it just enough so you can say 'Hey, that isn't me, its not spelled right' but not enough that the post office might get suspicious or not deliver it.
