Post by: Timbucktoo on May 06, 2013, 06:16 pm
You should really limit it to once a week, and even just once or twice a month.  This gives you enough time to reach normal serotonin levels after taking MDMA.

You probably aren't going to stop taking it for the time being, until you are out of the area at least.  In that case, you should know that the come down—when you stop taking it—is going to feel like shit.  Emotionally and physically, you are going to feel like shit for at least a week.

Binging like this can really fuck up your body's serotonin regulation.  Your body will get used to the excess serotonin and will produce less to counter that.  It's not something to make a habit of binging on.

Even if you're not doing permanent damage, you're still experiencing diminishing returns each trip. Hey I am going to second most of what has been said here. I have been a long time user of various drugs and you must reserve the greatest respect for them or pay a heavy toll. That said, 4-7 days of once daily use is likely to set you up for one hell of a hangover, even after sleeping for 12 hours strait, you will wake up and feel like the earths gravity has just increased by 4 fold, and have a banger of a headache (it will be severely worse if you haven't stay properly hydrated and eaten well). Then it is quite probable that a depression will set in, hopefully you are not having issues with such kinds of mental health, as that can set off a really bad one. Then after a few days you will start to feel normal again. No lasting damage unless you do this stuff regularly like binging once a month for years, that could start to cause some change in Neuroplasticity (physical changes in the Brain, but in this case not good), causing all kinds of issues from memory on up. But that is only in hard core use for prolonged periods. Remember the Brain is a very resilient and complicated Organ. Also MDA and MDMA are more complicated then acting on just the Serotonin Receptors, they also hit Norepinephrine and Dopamine ones, thus the Euphoria and Stimulating effects etc. In short, I would not recommend more then once a week in the Summer, or during Festival season, and then break for the rest of the year, and as long as you stay healthy and fit you will be fine.

At least that is my non-expert opinion ;-)
