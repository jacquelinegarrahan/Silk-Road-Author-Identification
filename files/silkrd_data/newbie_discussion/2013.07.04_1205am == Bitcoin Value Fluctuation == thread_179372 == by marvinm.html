Title: Bitcoin Value Fluctuation
Post by: marvinm on July 04, 2013, 12:05 am
As if the buying and selling of drugs and using the mail system for delivery wasnt a risky enough business unto itself, trying to deal with the drastic fluctuation of bitcoin just takes things entirely to another level.  I love the principals of silk road.  I love that the community seems for the most part to be a team working towards a common goal, and i feel privileged to consider myself a member of said community.  I have bought and sold amongst in black markets for many many years, and we are all aware that this type of trading is always accompanied by substantial risk.  However i am recently forced to contemplate not only the risk of snitches, thieves, spies, thugs, and cops; but now the additional stress of my currency not sustaining its value. 

I remember watching the news while third world countries such as Libya were undergoing rebellion or Iran hit with massive sanctions, and their currencies became instantly worthless and millions of people lost their life saving in the matter of hours because they were unable to convert to a stable currency in a timely manner.  I recall thinking that was a horrible scenario for the masses that wanted nothing to do with the political agenda but were still subject to its repercussions simply because of where they resided: guilty by association.  Granted i do not have my life savings invested in bitcoins, but shit when 10 grand turns into 6 grand in 2 weeks time, it certainly makes an impacting difference in my life.

Anyway, I suppose the best thing to do right now is sit and wait.  Perhaps even invest more?  They say buy low sell high right?  Bitcoins are at $77 usd today, two weeks ago it was $120.  That is 40+%.  Logic tells me that the value is bound to go back up, at least to 100, and if i were to invest now that it would very likely pay off.  However logic is something that often just floats around in the back of my mind.  In the front of mind things function a lot more passionately, and it is difficult to get past the anger of my current loss.  And there is always the possibility of more exchanges being taken down by federal agencies for tax evasion reasons causing the value to fall even further.  My fucking paradox.

Anyway, I am more or less just venting.  I will not be investing.  For me, I think the best route is to sit tight and not spend dollars or bitcoins rightnow and just hope that the market recovers in a timely manner.  A month from now I will either be regretting that I didnt invest, or regretting that I didnt spend.  I will not risk venturing in either direction at this time, therefore minimizing my loss and at the same time minimizing my gains. 

I am interesting in reading others' thoughts on this.  Please, vent with me.
