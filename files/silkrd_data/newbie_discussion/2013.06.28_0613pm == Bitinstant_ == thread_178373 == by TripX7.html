It's to my understanding that all you have to do is:

1. Go to the bit instant site.
2. Click new transaction
3. Fill out the info using a fake name / email ( I've heard that it is fine to use your real name and that some places require ID but not all. The choice is yours on that one.)
4. Select the store you wish to deposit your money at.
5. Either print out or copy down the info provided by the downloadable form.
6. Head to that location, fill out the moneygram form with the info from the bitinstant form, and deposit your $.


Once all that is taken care of it's just a matter of waiting for your coins to reach your account and you're all  set. :)Hope this info helps and if I left anything out feel free to correct me everyone.
