Just keep checking up on things.
Make sure you're place of res is clean.
Clear all of your computers that you've used Tor on. Back everything up that you need (that isn't illegal)
wipe all your drives just in case. Make at least 7 passes with a disc shredder to make it impossible to recover any data.
Reload your software, and all of your backups.
If they come a knockin', deny deny deny. You never knew anything about the package. For all you know, someone shipped it to you or your friends or whatever, in an attempt to get you in trouble, or in hopes they could come claim it from you.
Remember: your name on a package doesn't mean shit. You don't know anything about it.

Hopefully you did it all for nothing, but it's better to be safe than sorry.
I'd try to continue to get in touch with your seller and only login from coffee shops.
