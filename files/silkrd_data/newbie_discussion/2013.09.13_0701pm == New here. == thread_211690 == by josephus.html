Post by: Mister Mortimer on October 01, 2013, 02:56 pm
welcome to SR!

domestic is obviously the safest way as you don't have customs to contend with. some people use drop houses, others use PO boxes, some use their own address - that is your call. a PO box on fake credentials would possibly be the safest bet, but you need to make sure there's no CCTV there.

as you've said, start small. once you've found a vendor you are happy with (read, read and read some more on the forums), make a few smaller purchases, not just one or 2, but several - these will build your buyer stats up. if you only make a couple of small purchases then go for a big one the chances are the vendor will ask you to finalize early, as you'll still be seen as new and won't have decent stats to back your honesty up. finalizing early leaves you open to getting robbed - always stay in escrow, no matter how trustworthy the vendor seems. after a few small purchases PM the vendor about a bigger order (or 2, or 10) and go from there. make sure they are willing to let you keep in escrow, if not message another vendor.

basically it's all about the stats, the better buyer stats you have, the more likely you are to be accepted on a big order in escrow

i can't emphasize enough - READ as much as you can on the forums, about encryption, bitcoin, and especially feedback of the vendors you have your eyes on. you don't have to read from the start of the thread if it's like 50 pages long, but you should at least go back a couple of months worth of posts to see how they're currently doing, how they handle their customers etc.

hope this answers some of your q's.

have fun, and stay safe! :)

Morty

7. Treat your vendor with respect and FOD <-- Finalize on Delivery
