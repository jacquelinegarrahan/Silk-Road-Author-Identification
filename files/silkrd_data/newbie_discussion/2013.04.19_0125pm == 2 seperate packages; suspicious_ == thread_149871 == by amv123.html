Post by: safras on April 19, 2013, 07:13 pm
In my opinion there's nothing suspicious about packages bearing your name and address being delivered to you. I've ordered more than one thing from here before as well as things from Amazon etc. at the same time.

Just make sure you don't order two things that you might not be able to tell the difference of when they arrive. If only one arrives then you might not be able to tell which vendor you should finalize. Or even if they do both arrive you might want to know which one is which and vendors probably don't want to be quizzed about that kind of thing.
