Title: Re: ALASKA LSD REVIEW
Post by: Tessellated on August 19, 2013, 09:16 pm
"Quality" of LSD generally indicates its strength. Lower quality LSD may contain unconverted precursors in it but in microgram doses they should have little or no effect. A trip being good or bad is more likey related to set and setting than the quality of the LSD.

That being said, others disagree with me and think that impure crystals can cause negative side effects.

I have seen LSD crystal ranging from ochre in color to the whitest of whites. I personally cannot tell the difference in the type of trip, just potency. My current batch is white as driven snow, the same amount seems stronger.
