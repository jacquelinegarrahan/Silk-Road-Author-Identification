Welcome!

1. YES. PGP will save your life. Plus, cryptography is cool. Learn it! There is a newbie PGP club thread stickied at the top of this subforum.

2. DO NOT use a fake name. That is just one more thing that could go wrong. While it COULD work, if shit goes wrong just because you used a fake name, it would suck. It does NOT give you any additional protection, you already have plausible deniability from ordering to your house. THEY have to prove you deliberately ordered it. Using a fake name gives them ammo to use against you.

3. NEVER FE. Unless you like gambling with your money, this is a bad idea!
3.1 Do LOTS of research on the forums before picking a vendor. This can save you from getting bunk product or scammed.
3.2 If you are in doubt, ask a question! Someone on here probably knows the answer.
3.3 NEVER FE. Emphasis on that.
3.4 If shit hits the fan (lets hope it does not), do NOT say anything to the cops. They will screw you over as much as possible. Superglue your lips shut if necessary, this will save your life. I had a situation where if I had just kept my mouth shut I probably could have saved several thousand dollars, gotten a job, and not had to spend the better part of a year without any weed at all (that was the worst part).

If you have any questions, feel free to pop me a message!
