Title: Re: what if i dont have another address to use
Post by: cantharidin on March 29, 2013, 05:07 pm
Newbie here on SR, so take this for what it's worth. Not so new to the overall scene, so maybe it's worth something...

Drops are desirable (and necessary) for pros who are running lots of packages and big volume into their addresses. For a personal user, I think the security provided by SR's normal packaging standards (and you should research any vendor to make sure they are using best practices) is perfectly fine and safe.  Now that everyone shops online, the world is SWAMPED with small packages being delivered by all sorts of means. No one has the time or inclination to worry about your specific little package. As long as it doesn't reek of weed (and probably even if it does), your chances of having a problem approach zero. My opinion is that you place yourself at more risk with suspicious activities like 'anonymous' PO boxes or dead drops.  Nothing is 100% guaranteed, but I would argue that you're much much safer in the SR system and receiving the occasional package at home than you would be under any other scenario of buying drugs that I've ever encountered

SR is providing a fantastic service by making a marketplace with open communication among and between buyer and sellers. We are all safer for it.  God bless the internet...
