Title: Thanks to DPR and the SR community.
Post by: Thekla1 on May 09, 2013, 11:50 pm
Hi everybody,

I'm a bit old school (read - I am actually getting old), don't do social networks, twitter, and all that crap. I have been using SR for 5 months and having spent a lot of time on the forums where other people have put time in to post I just thought I would say hi.

I came about this place when my girlfriend hinted that she would like to try acid, about two years ago. She is a lot younger than me (I am a lucky bastard), and I didn't have a clue since it was years since I hung with the kind of crazies who had access to things like that.

Careful internet research, a chance conversation in a bar, and frankly big cojones on my part, and I took the plunge into what seemed like the Wild West.

I have been astonished by what I have found here, not just the great gear (that's nice!), but the fact that Silk Road works, the vendors I have used are totally reliable, the forums are full of people with the same belief in freedom that I have and look out for each other so much, and particularly by DPR's commitment to what is going on here.

So I would like to thank you all, vendors, buyers, mods, DPR, everyone involved. It has been said before, but the first time I used Silk Road and the postman popped his unknown pleasures thru the letterbox, it truly felt like the future.

And the acid? Blew my girlfriends brain to fucking pieces. What a night.

Take care all. Thanks for reading.
