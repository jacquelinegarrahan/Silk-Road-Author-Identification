Post by: Annex on July 29, 2013, 10:28 am
Yes, anatomy does take a little patience. I should know, I am studying to be a doctor.

Anonymity on the other hand, is not guaranteed with the use of bitcoins... ever. Every transaction is logged and every coin is unique. So it can always be told that a coin that ends up in a drug dealers hands came from you if you gave it to them. The idea of the tumbler is to split up your coins and 'wash' them through many many transactions so that it obscures the path. It never actually guarantees your anonymity.

Ideally you would buy your coins anonymously (cash deposit at bank).

The absolute best way with coins that are in a real name is to use a fogger and do it that way because they have a pool of bitcoins and will put your bitcoins in, and take the same amount ou, minus the fee
The fee is also randomly set between 1-3% on the most popular site so as to add further obscurity.


Really its all about weighing up risk and reward for both you and LE. You need to ask yourself how much effort would LE put in to find your coins and link them to you and your drugs. In most cases, its too much to matter unless you are moving serious quantities.
