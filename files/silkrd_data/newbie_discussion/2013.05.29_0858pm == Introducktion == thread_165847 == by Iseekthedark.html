If I recall 2c-t-7 is easier to over dose on than other RCs. Please be cautious.

I recommend trying 4-acO-DMT.

MDA and its analogs (MDMA, methylone, ...) are not like tripping. Any event where tripping, dancing, or alcohol is acceptable ecstasy (MDMA) is probably good. I do not recommend taking it alone. It is a couples drug, and a party drug. The experience is drastically different in a social situation.

MDMA is the popular rave/club/party drug and most e pills are mdma. MDA is more trippy in comparison to mdma, however I still have not had mda yet. I have had methylone though. Methylone is roughly 3x more speedy and 'meth' like compared to mdma. Or, another way to think about methylone is it has 1/3rd the serotonin dump as mdma.

MDMA does have a physical risk. imho it is best to take mdma no more than once every 3 months.

Oh, and candyflipping (mdma+lsd) is AMAZING. I highly recommend it.

Ketamine is kind of like being launched into similar to an ego death, but without the intensity of tripping. Ketamine is addictive so it is best to try it and then stay away from it imho. In comparison, ketamine is more like a meditative ego death than tripping: like stream entry/cessation, but without the after glow. (I've only had k once. Best to take this comparison with a grain of salt.)

I don't know why, and I have no idea if it is the same for others, but while meditating I get stuck and can not achieve access concentration if I have pot in my system from smoking within the last 48 hours. Meditation can be hard at first but I highly recommend trying it.
