I just created a Silkroad vendor account a few weeks back, and I have to say there are a few pros, but more cons compared to BMR. Here's a honest chart of the 3 largest marketplaces out there:

Silkroad:
Pros:
- Very big userbase=lots of sales fast
- Pretty good layout
- Easy to withdraw from account

Black Market Reloaded:
- Great layout (Easy to make listings, adjust etc)
- SUPER fast! On SR it can take several minutes to load a single PM on its slower days. On BMR, I have never had to wait more than 30 seconds
- Cheap fee's
- More freedom on what to sell (True freemarket)

Atlantis:
- 2 payment options
- Cheap fee's
- More freedom on what to sell
- Super fast
Major con? Next to no userbase.

You can all hate on me for posting this, but the honest truth is that Silkroad is just to slow from a vendors view. The amount of time it would take to read the amount of messages I get on BMR to read on SR would be insane, I wouldn't even do it. Until DPR invests in more server space, SR is doomed to be going offline very often.

BMR compared to SR:
 My personal favorite is BMR currently, I believe Atlantis has potential, but I am going to wait for them to grow for a while. On BMR, the server is blazing fast and is using less than 1/3 of the server space. Its pretty obvious SR is using a very big chunk of theirs. Fee wise: 2-4% on BMR compared to 10% on SR, much nicer. You also have the freedom to sell whatever wares (Not including CP) you want, while some of you disagree with this, its not truly a freemarket unless you are free to sell what you please. SR has a much bigger audience, which means products sell a lot faster on here. BMR has a reputation of "scam city", while yes there are scam complaints, that is going to go hand in hand with any anonymous market. The main problem is people FE for obvious scam attempts, the FE syndrome going on at SR needs to stop, escrow is there for a reason. Use it!

I will be selling prints and commercial bud+some high grade meth on my SR account here, and I sell some various other wares on BMR (Including firearms full escrow, feedback proving success to AUS+UK :))

Send me a PM there if you have any questions, or wait until SR is back. I cant wait for SR to come back, as I need to mark a few orders as shipped but well... Yeah.
