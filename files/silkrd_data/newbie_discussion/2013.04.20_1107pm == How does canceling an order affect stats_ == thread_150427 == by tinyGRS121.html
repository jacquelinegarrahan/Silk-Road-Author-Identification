Title: Re: How does canceling an order affect stats?
Post by: DriveHard on April 21, 2013, 12:45 am
Cancelling an order does not affect stats, refunding an order will. A cancellation assumes nothing was sent, nothing was received. You'll be fine.

I have had vendors cancel orders because they suddenly ran out of stock. It's very easy to do when you have multiple listings for different amounts (like 1/8 listing, 1/4 listing, 1 oz listing, etc). Vendors usually put their total amount divided by the amount in the listing rather than designating stock for specific listings.
