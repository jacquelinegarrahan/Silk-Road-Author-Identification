The Karma feature is meant to be a tool that enables you to see who is serious, and knowledgeable (They should have loads of positive karma), and who is just trolling and speaking about thing they know nothing about (They should have massive amounts of negative karma).

Unfortunately a lot of the users abuse this feature and hand out negative karma to everyone the disagree with, or simply because they have a bad day.

Overall the karma thing can give you a hint on who is legit and who isnt as scumbags tend to receive lots of negative karma, but on the other hand, during the last server-maintenance we started seeing pissed of kids and other cunts give all the mods negative karma simply because they were frustrated that they couldnt get their drug!

You will be able to hand out karma once you reach 100 posts, but there are some restrictions on amounts of karma points you can hand out per day, etc.
