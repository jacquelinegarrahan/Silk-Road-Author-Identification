Grower/vendor here.

What you are asking to do is much more dangerous then selling on SR. Meeting someone to make a FTF cash transaction for your buds is about as dangerous as it gets. You could either be meeting with a cop, or you could be the cop looking to meet with a SR vendor.

By using the SR to sell then your customers never need to know who you are, whether they are a cop or not (LE bitcoins are good too!). You don't have to sell eighths if that is too much work. There are many buyers looking for bulk all the time. This means less packaging materials, less stress, and less time consuming. Selling in smaller amounts directly to end consumers means you will likely make 2x the profit you are currently seeing. Selling in bulk will probably fetch you 1.5x what you are currently getting. The stress of moving your product disappears as there is constant demand. Keep the packaging equipment and materials at a separate location so if the grow is discovered they don't find more evidence against you.

Bank accounts aren't the only way to cash out bitcoins. Do some research and you will find many alternative solutions. You can trade bitcoins for almost anything.
