Title: Re: Hedge buyer account
Post by: Propylhexadrine on April 03, 2013, 12:29 pm
If BTC crashes, then SR would have to provide its users with more BitCoins in order to maintain the value of their accounts. This would open up their capital reserves to a large amount of volatility. I mean, the compensation for lost value has to come from somewhere. If SR hedges its buyers accounts, then SR has to pay for it when a crash comes. The only way to really make it worthwhile would be to charge a very large premium for the service.
