A tip for transporting safely is to use MBB bags, heat sealed.

It's a standard for my dispatch on all products, and allows for effective sealing against dog detection.

Alternate (if you don't have resources for a sealing unit and access to these bags) is to seek our "smell proof" bags - so-called bear proof or branded "smelly proof"....these are often sold at head shops and very reasonably priced. They are virtually undetectable in trial tests too...not so good for my business as stealth requires flat, seamless packaging....BUT for a one off, travelling idea...I think they will give you much needed security and concealment.
