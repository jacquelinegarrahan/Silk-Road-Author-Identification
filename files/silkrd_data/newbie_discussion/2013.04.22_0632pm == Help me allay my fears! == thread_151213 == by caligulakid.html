Title: Re: Help me allay my fears!
Post by: safras on April 22, 2013, 07:18 pm
My first order didn't arrive for over 10 days (was international though). I was convinced that it was not going to arrive and had already considered that money "lost", and then one morning I got up and there was the package sitting by my door. You can't believe how happy I was on that day. If it hadn't arrived I might have never have come back to SR.

Four days is nothing and not cause for concern (especially if they fall over a weekend). I'd give it a few more days and then contact the seller if you are still worried.

The other thing to consider is some sellers mark it as "in transit" either as soon as they have printed your name out and stuck it on the package (but not necessarily posted it), or some just mark it as "in transit" early so that you can finalize early if you want to. The fact it said "in transit" on the 18th does not mean it actually went that day. It might also have missed the post for that day and been collected the following morning depending on how your country's postal system works.
