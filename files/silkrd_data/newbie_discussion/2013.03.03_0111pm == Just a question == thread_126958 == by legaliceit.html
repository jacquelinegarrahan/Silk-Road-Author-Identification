Title: Re: Just a question
Post by: jägermeister on March 03, 2013, 01:47 pm
hi,
you'll be able to finalize as soon as the status of your order changes from "processing" to "in transit".
in your case of FE being required, the vendor will obviously only put it into that category as a pretext for you to be able to release the funds before he actually ships them.

the auto finalize option on the other hand only shows how much days are left, until the funds are being released from escrow automatically, if you do not happen to finalize on your own.
