Title: Re: Can whoever I buy bitcoins from send straight to my sr address
Post by: Aussie bob on July 17, 2013, 08:44 am
Not recommended, but yes you can do it. It's unlikely they'll hunt down the details of your wallet address before transferring coin, but there will always (forever & ever on the blockchain) be a record of those coins you bought being sent to that particular wallet. Safer to have them sent to a wallet, preferably then again to another wallet by a different wallet provider, and then on to SR. The transfer of btc to normal clearnet wallets is pretty much instantaneous, and the fees are very very minimal. It'll prob add 10-15 minutes on to the time it takes to get to your SR wallet, and you'll be much more secure. Sorry for blabbing on, AB.
