My thoughts: Not available for where i live.... Pity as it seems the most used method on SR.

4. Virwox.com allows you a multitude of options amongst them the option of buying with a credit card.
My Thoughts: After a lot of research i like this method the most. With the use of a pre-paid credit card this can be quite anonymous. I have used many different methods to purchase BTC.  If you are looking to get some almost immediately or same day completely anonymously, I would suggest localbitcoins.com   The reason is that you can purchase them from people with cash in person (ANONYMOUS), Dwolla (NOT ANONYMOUS), Paypal (NOT ANONYMOUS), Bank Deposit (ANONYMOUS). 

Another really great one I have just come across for both vendors and buyers is https://coinbase.com   You can link up your bank account and buy Bitcoins as you need them at the going rate with no fees.  You can also send and receive BTC to and from this wallet.  If you need to sell the Bitcoins to dollars, there is just a 1% transaction fee plus a .15 bank fee, and then your USD go right into your bank account.  Obviously this is not anonymous, but if you use it wisely, you will have no trouble. Here is how:

It is not illegal to purchase bitcoins, so once you have them, send them through another free wallet for a tiny tiny BTC transaction fee, and then from that other wallet to your SR account.  Then change the BTC address you just used on your SR accounts as well as your intermediary wallet to cover your tracks. This will add just a little extra security in the digital trail.

But if you need to go completely anonymous on this and don't mind paying a bit of a premium, localbitcoins.com is by far the best thing out there for document-less cash transactions.
