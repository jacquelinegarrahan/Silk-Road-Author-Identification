Title: Re: new to SR please help.
Post by: yabbadabbadoo on September 25, 2013, 05:59 am
Localbitcoins with a cash deposit at a major nationwide bank works pretty flawless for me.  Check out their reviews and use the escrow system they have, its there for a reason.  Expect to pay a small premium for anonymity though and figure it into your transaction.  Receiving them to your localbitcoin wallet will be pretty quick but expect a delay when transferring them to your SR account.  Sometimes its just a couple hours, at other times it can take many hours.  Strong passwords and different passwords than you normally use are strongly encouraged.
