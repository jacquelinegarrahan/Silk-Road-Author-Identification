Title: Re: Do crystals define how high?
Post by: farmer1 on January 27, 2013, 10:27 pm
The 'crystals' are called trichomes. They are what hash is made out of. Their abundance can make for some great cannabis.  :)
They aren't the only factor contributing to the high you feel when smoking cannabis though.

Depending on when the cannabis is harvested the grower can control the type of high to some degree. He does this by gauging the appearance of the trichomes.

Giving the plant what it needs during flower allows it to produce an abundance of sticky, bloated, fat trichomes. Some strains produce them better then others.
