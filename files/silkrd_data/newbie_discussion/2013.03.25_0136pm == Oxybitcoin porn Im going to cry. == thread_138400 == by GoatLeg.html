Post by: XpoUj897Tt on March 26, 2013, 11:26 am
If you want bitcoins quick then goto Blockchain and use the Barclays Pingit app from your phone.

You'll need to set up a wallet first and keep a record as you go along of your passwords and account details. Print these details off and keep them somewhere safe as bitcoins are next to impossible to recover once you've lost access to your account. If you're making a one off purchase then try to purchase only the bitcoins you require as it is harder to convert them back to cash.

Also, don't be tempted by offers to send you free bitcoins for completing surveys, watching videos or from a bitcoin faucet. The value of the bitcoins gained will be less then the fee for processing them and will leave you out of pocket when they transfer over to your Blockchain wallet.

Before attempting to make your first purchase though make sure you've learnt how to use PGP first as you'll usually be asked to encrypt your address before you send it to the vendor.
