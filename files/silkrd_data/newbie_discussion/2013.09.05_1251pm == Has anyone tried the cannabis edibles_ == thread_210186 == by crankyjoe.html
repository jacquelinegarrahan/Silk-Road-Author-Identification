Yes I love the edibles!  I'd check out the vendor QualityKings and his Cheeba Chews.  I've ordered from him a few times and his shipping speed, stealth/packaging and prices are all the tops.  He charges about $33 for 2 chews which is $16.50 a piece and the best price on SR.  These chews won the 2011 cannabis cup award for best edibles.  They are potent!  They come in quad dosage and deca dosage (4 dose and 10 dose respectively).  They are very small about an inch and a half long by about half in wide and half inch tall but they pack a serious punch.  Seriously if you have no tolerance for marijuana I'd suggest starting with just 1/4 of a quad dose chew.  If you have a tolerance start with 1/2 of a quad dose chew or 1/10 of a deca dose chew.  Of course you can't OD on marijuana so eat as much as you'd like.  But I've heard stories of all day everyday smokers puking and just getting messed up in general off of a full deca dose chew.

Oh and I almost left out one of the best parts.  With the quad dose you can pick from Sativa, Indica or Hybrid.  So unlike other edibles where what you see is what you get, with these you can pick the type of high you want (and I can vouch that the Sativa chew is def. a mind high and a bit of a "rush" and the Indica chew is def. a body high which will make you sluggish and relaxed with the Hybrid meeting in the middle)
