Title: Re: Shipping times from Chemical Brothers (to Spain)
Post by: forehand on May 09, 2013, 11:34 pm
These are my experiences on intl shipping.

You could check these things out on the postal service site in NL and Spain. Seriously. It will give you just as good information as asking anyone here. Delivery times internationally are notoriously irregular and varies greatly if you dont use some sort of express tracked service or pay-extra-delivery.

I have had packages sent at the same time by the same person arriving 5 days apart! Also packages have taken from 2 to 7 days from the same source with the same postage. I don know about NE-->Spain (my two favorite countries btw) but i suppose that if you use regular shipping you will not get any wiser from others experiences since its so varied and random sometimes.

I would think that you will have your order in time though but why not check out the postal service delivery times between your countries? 10 days should be enough. Ask the vendor what it usually takes and then hang back and wait for your order to arrive.
