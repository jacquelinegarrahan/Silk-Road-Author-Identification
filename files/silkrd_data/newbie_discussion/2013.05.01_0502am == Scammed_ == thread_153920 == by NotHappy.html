I put up a 100% request in the resolution center.  I contacted Djabbar and explained the problem.  Djabbar insisted that the hash is not adulterated.  I explained the problem to Djabbar again and asked for a full refund.  Djabbar refused and offered a 0% refund in the resolution center.

Try the test yourself if you've ordered "Commercial Hash (plaque of 200 gram)", take a small piece of hash leave it sitting in a cup of hot water.  If it starts dissolving after a few minutes, then its adulterated (cut).

This sort of behavior is abusing SR and vendors and we should all be aware of the vendors who mislead and scam us.
