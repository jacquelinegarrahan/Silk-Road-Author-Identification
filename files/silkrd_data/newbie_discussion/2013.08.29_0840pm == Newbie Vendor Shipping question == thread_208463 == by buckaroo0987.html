Title: Re: Newbie Vendor Shipping question
Post by: farmer1 on August 29, 2013, 08:46 pm
Use a real address that is in the same physical region as where the package will be dropped. Do not use your own. Any packages returned to this address are to be considered a loss.
Make it believable and something that customers will feel safe having on their package.
Use a label printer.

Change it periodically, especially important if any of your packages have been returned to this address or intercepted.
