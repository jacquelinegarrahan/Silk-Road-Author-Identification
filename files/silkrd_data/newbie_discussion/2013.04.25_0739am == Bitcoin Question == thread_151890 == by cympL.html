You can easily and safely send bitcoin from an exchange like bitinstant directly to SR. The only thing you should watch for is to be sure the deposit address is copied over exactly.

I would check your history in Bitinstant to see what the address you sent it to was and confirm it is the one in your SR account.

If your BTC isn't in your account by now it is not likely to show up. Usually only takes an hour (a few at most). It is always best to practice transfers with a few fractions of a BTC to make sure you have the process down before sending a larger amount.
