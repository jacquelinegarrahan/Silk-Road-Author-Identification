Title: Re: My first order, question about escrow...
Post by: ragemyfaceoff on March 05, 2013, 06:44 am
You have no reason to be worried. Sometimes vendors take awhile to confirm and mark the purchase "in transit". If they take too long (3-4 days?) to mark confirm the order, you'll have the option to cancel, meaning that you'll be able to actually click cancel. You dont release your money to the vendor until you click "finalize", which isnt even an option until the purchase is marked in transit.
