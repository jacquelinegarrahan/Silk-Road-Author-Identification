By your first sentence, we sound alike  ;)

The site itself is pretty safe, it's the shipping you really have to worry about. Always buy from vendors with good feedback, and never finalize early.

As you'd expect, domestic packages are a lot safer than international (no customs).

Make sure to use PGP when ordering, you don't want to send your personal info unencrypted.

Also, no fake names, ship this as you would any ebay package
