Title: Why vend here if you have TRUE Top Shelf weed?
Post by: slimcharles on February 12, 2013, 12:12 am
Lots of weed vendors claim to be selling top shelf weed.  My question is if they really have top shelf weed, why risk prison or the hassle of BTCs and shipping since if it is top shelf you can sell it to a dispensary?

Let's say that you can profit more by selling here, then it would have to be a lot more to offset the risk, and I doubt that the profit margins vending here are that large.

On the other hand, it could be argued that most of the weed vendors are middle men and so, the above point applies only to growers but not to resellers.  However, based on personal experience, I can comfortably say that all the weed I've gotten from resellers here have most definitely NOT been top shelf.  The weed has been pretty good but no way has it been dispensary top shelf.  Not even close.

I'd go further and say that the best weed I've gotten off the road has consistently been from growers themselves but so far none has been really top shelf.  (Actually, there's been one reseller that I've ordered from that has provided true top shelf, Novartis, but that's because, I think, they actually purchase it from the dispensary, and consequently, their weed is pretty expensive.)

Getting back to my question, I don't think you would vend here if you were a grower and had top shelf.  So it would be unlikely to get top shelf from a grower here.

The most likely place to get top shelf weed here then would be from a reseller but none of them have any. 

Why can't we get any top shelf weed here?  My love for weed is making me contemplate a move to CA or WA.
