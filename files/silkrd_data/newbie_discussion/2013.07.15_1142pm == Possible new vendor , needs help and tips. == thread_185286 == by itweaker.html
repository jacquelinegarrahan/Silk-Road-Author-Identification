Pony up the $500 bond.
Post your public key on your vendor page.
Create an official review thread in here so buyers can review your business and products.
Put a link to your vendor page in your forums sig and send a message to vendor support to be added to the vendor roundtable.
Offer some free samples in here to help get the word out and establish some feedback.

Right now the forums are your best friend. Use them to learn how to vend and promote/defend your product when needed. If you're vending on SR and have no presence in here you are handicapping your business from the get-go.
