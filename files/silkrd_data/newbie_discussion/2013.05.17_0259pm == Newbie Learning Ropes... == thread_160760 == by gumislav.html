Here's how localbitcoins works, if you do it right:

You and the seller meet up in the lobby of a local bank, where everybody is safe.
You show him your stack of cash money.
He uses his smartphone to transfer to your wallet.
You use your smartphone to check that the transfer is complete.
You hand over the cash and the two of you shake hands.
Neither one of you just did anything remotely shady or illegal, that's why you can do it in a bank lobby.
