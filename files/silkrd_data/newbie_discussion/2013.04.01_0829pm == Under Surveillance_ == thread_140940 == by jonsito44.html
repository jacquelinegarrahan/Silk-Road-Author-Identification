Post by: CRWHITLOCK on April 02, 2013, 09:10 pm
Saying "she knows she's being watched" sounds very suspect to me. The whole point of LE surveillance is so the subject of investigation doesn't know that LE are watching them. Care to share any insight into how she "knows" she is being watched?  The likelihood that your friend is under LE surveillance as a small time "user" is very slim. If she is dealing to support her habit, that's a whole different story. If she receives a lot of packages in the mail that have drawn suspicion, they would probably just get a warrant to open one of the packs and then either seize it or set up a controlled delivery. If the amounts she's ordering are small, personal usage numbers, it's very unlikely LE would waste their time on her. That being said, you didn't provide enough details for anyone to give you a very accurate opinion on what's going on. I would suggest your friend is either paranoid, or selling to pay for her habit. To think she is under active surveillance for strictly "using illegal substances" is not rational.
