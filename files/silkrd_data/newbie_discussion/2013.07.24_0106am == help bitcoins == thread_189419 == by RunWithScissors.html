Post by: isallmememe on July 24, 2013, 01:17 am
once you type in the amount you want, you then get the sellers bank details and a reference message. you use those to set up the bank transfer on your online bank account or go into your branch with the details. remember you only get 90 minutes to make the bank transfer and then get back to a computer to click the 'mark payment complete' button. its the blue button further down the page.

btw you can't message the seller until you send trade request.
