This is the first thing you should do. Go to your profile and update your signature to include links to your vendor page and later include your forum review page. You will want to have a forum review page created in the Rumor Mill section of the forums, not the newbie section.

Then you want to create a forum post soliciting your samples for community members to see what you are offering.

After you have found your samplers, you want to create some personalized stealth listings so each sampler engages in an SR transaction with you and you can establish feedback on both SR and on the forums.

This is a good sampling, out of 10 samplers, hopefully you will get 4 or 5 good reviews, 2 or 3 okay ones, and a couple who just don't do what they claim they will...

Good luck to you. If you need a review thread created message me and I can help you out.
