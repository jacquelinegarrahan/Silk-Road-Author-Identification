Title: Re: Idiot Noob here. Best ways to purchase Bitcoin.
Post by: jimmymcnulty on April 18, 2013, 02:31 am
If you need a wallet apart from SR, I love blockchain.info/wallet, even though they go down every so often like right now. But they have convenient integration with BitInstant for really the easiest anonymous cash to BTC system there is at the moment. But it is expensive, BitInstant takes 3.99%, and ZipZap takes a $3.95 fee. (ZipZap is the go between BitInstant uses with MoneyGram who actually *does not* take a fee, presumably due to an arrangement with ZipZap. When you think about it the chain is fairly long: MoneyGram > ZipZap > BitInstant > Mt Gox > to your wallet of choice.)

If you don't have a need for an external wallet you can use SR as a wallet and direct BitInstant to deposit there. Just, you know, caveat emptor: SR could go poof whenever along with your BTC so, be risk aware.
