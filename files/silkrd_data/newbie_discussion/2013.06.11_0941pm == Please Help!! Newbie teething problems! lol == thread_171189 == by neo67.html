I know this wasn't directed at me. I've been here since the beginning though...

You can sort listings by Vendor Rating, Most Popular, or Newest...

Newest - pulls up the latest most recently listed items by ALL vendors in that category.
Rating - pulls up the vendors by rating, ascending from 100...
Most Popular - pulls up the items that have been selling the most.

It's up to you, really, but I always sort by newest... cause I know a lot of the vendors in the categories I'm looking through.

For someone NEW, I'd say... either Rating, or Most Pop. :P
