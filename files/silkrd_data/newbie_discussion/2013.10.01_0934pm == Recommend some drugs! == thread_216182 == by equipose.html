Post by: clearnet on October 01, 2013, 11:17 pm
I know it's not something you'd have to buy on the Road (although I would if a vendor was offering powder) but when I was in your shoes a few years ago, the next drug I tried getting my feet wet in on my quest to break into hallucinogens was DXM - dextromethorphan. Despite what a lot of less experienced users say (or users that don't happen to like dissociatives), DXM is much more than just getting fucked up on cough syrup. A good place to start is William White's DXM FAQ and the wealth of knowledge on Erowid. The next place I'd check out is the Dextroverse forums - if there was a capital of the DXM universe that would be it.

DXM is a very complex and extremely interesting, mysterious, and many-leveled drug to many different people, and personally I found getting experience with dissociatives helped prepare me for psychedelics like mushrooms and LSD immensely. Read up on it, if you're interested give it a try, and let us know what you think. If you have any questions about it I may not have all the answers but I am very experienced and will do my best to help you.
