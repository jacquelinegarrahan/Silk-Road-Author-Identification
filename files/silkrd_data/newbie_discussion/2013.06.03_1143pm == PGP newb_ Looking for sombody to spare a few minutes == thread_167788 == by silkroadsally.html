I'm a newb to, so i hope im doing it right, but this is what i do. I copy the PGP that i want to use, open a txt on my desktop, paste it in, save.

Open GPA, import, click on txt and click ok. It should load in the info and give you a profile in the main page.

Next you go into clipboard within the program, type your message, once done click encrypt, click the profile that you just saved, it will make it into another PGP that looks alot like the one you used that was the other persons key, only this one has your message in it to. You send that PGP, person uses his own key to read it.
