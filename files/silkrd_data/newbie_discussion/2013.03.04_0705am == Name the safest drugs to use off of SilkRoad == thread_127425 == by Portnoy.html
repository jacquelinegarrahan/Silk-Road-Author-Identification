Nothing annoys me more than people who say that marijuana has no side effects.

It can and will lead to paranoia with a lot of its users -- I'm not here to preach to you all, you can do whatever drugs you want (this is The Silk Road of course), but do not give this person the impression that it's as safe as aspirin. I know, it sounds like I'm being a wowzer, but I'm speaking from personal experience of myself and friends, smoking weed for an extended period of time (I'd say longer than 12-24 months) will leave you with long term issues. I smoked daily for 3 years, and now deal with paranoia and anxiety that I did not deal with before that. Before I started (at the age of 18), I was outgoing, social, and had great confidence -- after smoking for a couple of years I became an introvert, socially awkward, and thought everybody else was talking about me behind my back. Now not only did I deal with that, but so did some of the people I was smoking with and some of their friends. So, speaking from personal experience -- hydroponic marijuana can definitely had negative, long term side effects.

I've smoked bush recently though and it's been great, none of the negatives associated with when I was smoking the potent hydro.

As far as safest drug, I would have to say MDMA -- it's not going to give you visuals (so inexperienced psychonauts needn't worry), if it's clean and in a decent dosage (big enough to get a proper roll, small enough to not put you into come-down territory), it makes you feel great and very sociable and isn't as long lasting as say, meth or LSD. Perfect for sharing a night with a (group of) loved one(s) or even at a party, however -- like other drugs, abuse can lead to permanent damage to your brain. I stick to the once every couple of months rule :)
