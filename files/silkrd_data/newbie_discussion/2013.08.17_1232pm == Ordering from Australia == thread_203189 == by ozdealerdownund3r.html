Title: Ordering from Australia
Post by: ozdealerdownund3r on August 17, 2013, 12:32 pm
Hi,

So I wanted to put my thoughts down on how it has been ordering from Australia. As most are aware, our customs are pretty tight. So getting anything in or out of the country is difficult. What makes it worse is that we have the highest prices in the world and have the most country hops from origin to destination. This means we pay the most for stuff which is cut back significantly.

Since jumping on SR I have made a few sample orders from the US, UK and Netherlands and so far no mail has gone missing however there are some now which are taking what seems like forever.

The quality has been fantastic on everything I have delivered and in our market have grabbed everything for a bargain.

Finding bulk suppliers is hard though. It seems like the most people are willing to send per delivery is 25g if not 14g. (There has been an exception). So getting large supplies here is difficult. I think there needs to be a market for sales in the range of 100g-1000g. I have already asked the 3 vendors I have bought from and they either can't supply the amount of don't offer wholesale.

Overall I am quite happy with the whole SR experience and dealing with reputable vendors make the experience enjoyable.

I have loaded up significant number of bitcoins and looking for a few bulk vendors so PM me. I am interested in
1. Your customer feedback
2. Speed in getting deliveries here (currently Budwork UK has that down pat with 4 days)
3. Quality of product
4. Price

In that order :)

Thanks for reading and be safe.
