Title: Re: New Requests - Contact Lenses
Post by: JabbatheSlut on June 14, 2013, 05:14 am
Just thought of something else ... you need to be pretty well-informed to buy contacts online because one of the most important factors is the Base Curvature (BC) of your eye.

The only way you would be able to find that out is to get checked out by an optometrist. 

There are other measurements as well - like diameter, etc., so it would not be a good idea to buy or sell this kind of thing without specialist knowledge.  I buy lenses online, but then again I know all my optical measurements.

If you buy contacts with the wrong BC, etc., you simply won't be able to wear them. 

See a professional instead.
