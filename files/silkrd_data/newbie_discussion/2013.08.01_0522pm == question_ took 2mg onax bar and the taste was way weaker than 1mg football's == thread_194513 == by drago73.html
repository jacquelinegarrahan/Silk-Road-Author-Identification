Title: Re: question: took 2mg onax bar and the taste was way weaker than 1mg football's
Post by: alwayswearsunscreen on August 01, 2013, 07:32 pm
What dissolves in a xanax pill is not the alprazolam itself, but the binding agent which constitutes 99% of the pill. Since you mention that this pill did not dissolve, you know at least that is different. If the pill is pressed tighter and in a different binding agent, then comparing alprazolam taste is an invalid measure of potency since only the surface is accessible to taste.
