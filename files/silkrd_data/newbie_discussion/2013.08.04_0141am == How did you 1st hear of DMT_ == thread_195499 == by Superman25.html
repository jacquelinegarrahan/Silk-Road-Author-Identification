It might have been Erowid.  But I remember as a young child, reading The Hardy Boys Detective Manual, which had a big section on drugs and narcotics.  They listed the various classes of drugs, and their "street names" which were hopelessly out of date.  I remember the section of hallucinogens, and there was an alphabet soup of drugs there.  LSD, MDA, STP, and I think DMT might have been in there too, I don't recall.  When I grew up and went to college the only hallucinogens were LSD, Peyote, and Psilocibin.
