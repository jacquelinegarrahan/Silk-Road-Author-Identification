As with any new psychedelic, it's usually a good idea to start with a low dose to see how you react to it. With 2C-B, starting low happens to work particularly well for many people. Approx. 20mg is the standard "full" dose. 10mg is a good "social dose" which often acts as a social lubricant (friendly, talkative, horny etc.) without the distractions of a full trip.

2C-B does work quite well with MDMA. A good time to take 2C-B is 2-3 hours into an MDMA trip. This protocol was the original combo for psychedelic-assisted psychotherapy. It helped patients to integrate insights from the MDMA phase of the trip.

Dunno about throwing up on 2C-B. I've never thrown up on staggered doses up to 150mg. But everyone's different.... :)
