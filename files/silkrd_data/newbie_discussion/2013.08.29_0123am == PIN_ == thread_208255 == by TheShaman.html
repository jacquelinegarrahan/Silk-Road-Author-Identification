You will have created your PIN when you created your account.

If you can't remember it, you can ask for Admin to reset it, but they will wait 72 hours before doing it. This is because the PIN is a security measure to stop phishers and hackers from getting into your account and emptying it.

Sorry mate, if you can't remember your PIN you won't be ordering tonight.
