Title: Re: Want to do mushrooms and LSD, which one first?
Post by: Agria on June 21, 2013, 06:04 pm
I think it really depends on the setting. LSD is a much more social drug and (in my opinion) is much better in social situations. On the other hand, I find mushrooms are better in a solitary setting where you can freely explore your thoughts and consciousness without being bothered. In addition, the duration of a mushroom trip (~6-7 hours) would probably be better for a newbie. An individual that isn't well versed in psychedelics can easily get overwhelmed in a 12-hour LSD trip.

Have you considered 2C-B at all? That's a nice and easy trip with lots of visuals and not as much mind fuck as a conventional psychedelic. Kind of like dipping your feet in the water before jumping in.

Also, I don't suggest you take DMT. I feel as though people underestimate that drug. It's a powerful, powerful hallucinogenic that you can easily get overwhelmed on.

No matter what you should use a sitter. Hope my advice helped!

Regardless of what anyone says to you here, including me, read about the drugs you want to take, and decide for yourself.  You are the only one who really knows what is inside you, and what you are capable of.

2CB can be pretty gentle, and is one of Shulgin's favourites.  Check out erowid.org for info and trip reports on all these substances.  I find trip reports fun to read, and they give you a good spread of what can happen with each drug, from good experiences to bad.

Don't worry about addiction with hallucinogens - they almost all have a tolerance of a few days to a week (except DMT, which could be done daily) so you can't effectively become addicted due to the lack of frequency, and there is no chemical addiction associated.  DMT is released into your brain naturally every night when you sleep.

I too think LSD is easier to handle first, but do not expect a very visual experience on a low-low/intermediate dose. It's still a beautiful and highly recommended experience.
