There's an idea similar to that, which involves planting plug computers such as http://www.plugcomputer.org/development-kits/dreamplug/ (http://www.plugcomputer.org/development-kits/dreamplug/) in random places around a home city, and using them as stealth-mode bridge relays or even hidden servers via public wifi in range of these tiny computers.

You can literally plug a plug computer into a random office or restaurant wall outlet, in an out-of-the-way corner, and they might not even be noticed, yet are fully fledged computers capable of acting as a bridge relay or hidden server.

It's pretty cool.
