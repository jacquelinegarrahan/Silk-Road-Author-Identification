Post by: Illuminatrix on July 24, 2013, 03:33 pm
LSD down in Newport Beach visiting a very wealthy friend of mine. We decide to go for a late night stroll to 7-eleven (it was probably around 2am or so).

We start walking, and my thoughts are getting twisted. Very twisted, you know, typical hallucinogen stuff. We are at a corner, waiting to cross the street. A car drives by us, and as the car passes us I get the most intense tracer I've ever witnessed. Instead of typical multi-colored tracers following objects, I get an entire life experience.

In the .3 seconds that it took for that car to speed past us, the image that followed was the entire life story of someone I don't know. I witnessed their birth, first steps, first day of school, their entire high school career, college, and their death in a car accident shortly after they graduated from college. All of this in the blink-of-an-eye tracer that followed a speeding car. Every thought that wen through their head, the intensity of his first kiss to the boredom of his high school classes, just waiting for something fun to happen. I witnessed his entire existence.

Me: "DUDE!  DID YOU FUCKING SEE THAT CRAZY SHIT!?"
Friend: "Yeah man, the guy driving that sedan wasn't wearing a shirt!"
