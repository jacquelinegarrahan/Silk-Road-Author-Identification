Post by: rustykoin on July 05, 2013, 01:15 pm
Keep your torbrowser.exe file on a small USB, the whole Browser Bundle I mean, along with any PGP keys and applications of that nature.

Download CCleaner for free from ccleaner.com . This is an application that makes it easy to clean up the registry and get rid of temporary internet / log files files.

It wouldn't hurt to do a wipe and reload the hard drive operating system about every six months to a year to keep the operating system running smooth but that's just a maintenance issue.

That's all I can think of at the moment. But you're good. Stay safe.
