Title: Can't Register????
Post by: Squirrel on April 26, 2013, 10:18 pm
I consider myself a somewhat astute user and am a member of many a fora.  However, when I tried to register for this forum, I got the dreaded error indicating that I had incorrectly entered the Captcha shit.  Well, I tried 100 times before I finally did a search and found an old post from a current member that was having this problem and here is the fix if you are using TorBrowser:

1-Go to the orange TorBrowser box in your upper left corner and click it.
2-Select Options on the right side of the drop-down box and options again from that drop down.
3-Go to the content tab and de-select the 'Enable Javascript' checkbox.

Voila.  You will now be able to use the stupid captcha and register.

Your welcome.
