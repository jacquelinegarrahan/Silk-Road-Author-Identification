Title: Re: Real or Fake Addressee
Post by: crunchytuna123 on March 04, 2013, 02:04 am
Real address.

Reasons why:
-A lot of orders do not get delivered because people use fake names.
-Most vendors have good packaging and the chances of getting a package intercepted is quite low. (especially for items that are sent in an envelope
-Either way, you aren't in possession of the item. Anyone can send you anything in the mail. If you have a package intercepted and the police contact you, DO NOT talk to them. They probably don't have much evidence against you as long as you use SR in a stealthy and secure manner.

MOST IMPORTANTLY: Always send your address using PGP.
