Get a bitcoin wallet (online or desktop). It will take some time to synchronize to the BTC network at first. (Maybe a day or two..) It's a public record of every block created, (200k or something?)
You get an address.

Then go to a site such as Blue Sky Traders and put in a cash bid for BTC. Contact them, and provide the address (like and acct #) that you would like your BTC deposited into. Deposit the $$ into the account that is e-mailed to you the day of trading. You will then receive BTC.

Then, send BTC to the address provided on your SR "account" page. After it has been verified by the network- then you will see a balance in your SR account. Then, use like any other online store.

from one newbie to another.. I hope this helps!

PS- if you can figure out PGP encryption, BTC should be a piece of cake.
