Look at how long they been vending and see how many transactions they have. Look at there latest feedback and see if they have a forum review thread listed on their page and if so you should be able to at least read it but not post yet until you get the 50 post count. It's against SR policy to FE and vendors are supposed to have at least 55 transaction before even asking buyers to FE. It's easier to scam you when you are new because you have not proven yourself as established buyers yet and no body will take your side so don't FE
