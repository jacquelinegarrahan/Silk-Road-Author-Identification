Title: Re: Sublingual Cocaine Use - Share your experience !
Post by: smilingorchid on July 10, 2013, 11:52 pm
Cocaine is probably my favorite drug.  I have never acquired it on SL but can imagine the quality is much higher then my current dealer.

Some things to note if you are doing it for the first time.
* It's amazing, there is a strong feeling of euphoria that comes on almost instantly.
* It will keep you awake for a little while
* It's hard to stop doing more - what I mean is you may do a little, one or two lines, as you come down you will want to re-up. This is contrary to a drug like LSD where after I start to come down I am just done.
* Be careful if you are drinking.  You will not feel drunk, nor will you throw up from being WAY too drunk.  Most people I know who got sick while doing cocaine got sick from drinking not from the coke.
* You don't feel "fucked up".  The beauty of coke to me is that it is a totally functional drug.  Unlike cannabis, MDMA, or alcohol, you will feel almost normal with a lot more energy and ready to take on the world.

I recommend splitting a gram with a friend or two on the first night, take it slow. Draw out lines about 2.5 inches long and as thick as an earth worm.  Another option is to use a key, just get a small pile on the end of a key and snort that.  It's a little more present but not as much of a rush. Lastly to me one of the best parts of coke is the ceremony around cutting a few lines and drawing them out.

Have fun
