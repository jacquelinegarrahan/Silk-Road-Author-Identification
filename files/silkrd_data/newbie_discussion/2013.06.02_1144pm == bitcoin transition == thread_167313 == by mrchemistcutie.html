Title: Re: bitcoin transition
Post by: RobLoblaw on June 03, 2013, 12:20 am
heyy, welcome
it takes a while to go your wallet to bitbargin (i dont know what that is) and then to SR
each place you send your coins to the transaction has to make a certain number of confirmations, usually around 6-10
the more places you send them through, the longer it will take
usually you can see the coins in your wallet at 1 confirmation but you wont actually have the coins until all the necessary confirmations are complete. this stops double spending
on average it takes about 1-3 hours to get them into your SR account but i have heard it taking up to 24 before

i know it can make you nervous with your money just "out there" floating around. best advice is to fuck off and go do something
the refresh button is your enemy and only makes the time go slower
