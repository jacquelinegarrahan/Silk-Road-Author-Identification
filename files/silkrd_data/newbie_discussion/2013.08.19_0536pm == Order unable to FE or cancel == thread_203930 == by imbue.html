Title: Re: Order unable to FE or cancel
Post by: Galaxy123 on August 19, 2013, 05:43 pm
You can't FE until the vendor has placed the order in transit.  Once they have placed the order in transit the spot where "cancel" is will turn to "Finalize | Resolve".
Title: Re: Order unable to FE or cancel
Post by: odd on August 19, 2013, 05:53 pm
First and foremost NEVER EVER EVER FE.  If you do you lose your bitcoins and if you don't recieve your product there's nothing protecting you.  When you place an order you can not cancel it you must wait four days before you can do so.  Maybe you were on a phishing site when you canceled your previous orders before 4 days?  If you wish to cancel your order i suggest yoou message the vendor and explain you would like to cancel the order and ask them to not send you anything and to not put the order "in transit".  I hope everything works out.
