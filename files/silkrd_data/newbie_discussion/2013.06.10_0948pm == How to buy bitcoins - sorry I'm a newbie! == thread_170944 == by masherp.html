Post by: hexdef on June 11, 2013, 02:20 am
In order of ease and/or processing charges:

coinbase.com- the best in my opinion, tie your bank account to coinbase, buy bitcoin, transfer to your SR bitcoin address. I think it takes a few days to tie the coinbase acct to your bank account.

bitinstant.com-find what you want to buy. put this amt. in the bitinstant web interface and your SR bitcoin address. print out the generated invoice. take it to a moneygram, cvs, etc.. pay clerk in cash and they call in the order. order generally shows up in your acct. in about 1 hour. see blockchain.info for more on how long it takes.

mt gox- pain in the ass in my opinion, but popular/secure. probably the most secure if you think you may store bitcoin in your account for a while. get a yubikey if that is the case. may need to send them a utility bill.
