Post by: Praetorian on August 02, 2013, 08:48 pm
Orbot is very secure.  Orweb browser works for that.  Orbot = mobile Tor.  It is still Tor.  It is still anonymous.


Getting busted and having your phone taken and having them be able to get into your phone and look through your stuff, would be the fault of you for not rooting your phone, and taking necessary steps to ensure that your phone is clean and clear.  Any good dealer/distributer has 3 phones.  The real-life phone, the orbot phone, and the burner for local distribution.

All of this information is readily available here in the forums; if you have less than 50 posts you won't be able to reply to those threads, but that doesn't mean that they do not exist. 

Patience is a virtue.
