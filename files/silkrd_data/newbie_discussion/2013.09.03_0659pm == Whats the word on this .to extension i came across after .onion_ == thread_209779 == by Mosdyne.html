using proxies to access tor, even if over tor, is not recommended, and may be able to trace back to you in some cases. I suggest you memorize the URL, it's 3 simple parts

silkroad
vb5
piz3r
then the .onion

as for if you should be paranoid, don't be, you only accessed it over tor, however, still, don't use it. LE can pull info from proxies almost as they please (info like your username, what said username looked at, etc). as long as you didn't order anything, message anyone, finalize an order, etc, you're fine, and even if you did one of those, you're likely still fine
