Not sure if you are referring to police follow ups from SR purchases, or for raids in general...... If you mean raids in general - here is a story for you. A chick I know owns a house and rents out 3 rooms. All of the tenants are a bit dodgy, but for the most part decent people. Some work. All have previous dealings with the cops but nothing serious - no jail time, only personal use and traffic offenses.

Anyway, the chick that owns the house is right into salt water fish tanks, and has big barrels to hold the water, constantly getting packages in the mail for fish stuff, tank chemicals etc etc One of the tenants was a fairly heavy meth user. Would deal but only to friends in small amounts etc.

Police raided and on the paperwork, it indicated they thought the meth head tenant was manufacturing meth at the house! Not sure how the cops got the info, but they did a full search on ALL the rooms in the house, including shed and everyone's cars.

The meth head was not cooking there, but did get busted with a bong and weed, and a sml (.5g) amt meth. The chick who owned the house obviously kicked the meth head out after the raid.

Since then there has been no hassles at all - the cops have been called since then (by neighbors due to loud music) and no dramas at all.

As the house has always been a share house, no one is sure if the cops were watching the house, or were just interested in the meth head.

But yeah, no dramas since the original raid. (VIC)
