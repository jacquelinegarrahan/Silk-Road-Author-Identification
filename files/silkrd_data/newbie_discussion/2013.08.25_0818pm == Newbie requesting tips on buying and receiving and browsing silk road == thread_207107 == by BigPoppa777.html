Title: Re: Newbie requesting tips on buying and receiving and browsing silk road
Post by: Capital Gain on August 25, 2013, 08:21 pm
Stay in escrow for ALL your transaction. Never FE until after you inspect what you ordered.
Truthfully state your feedback, whether it met your exception or if it fell below average.

Make sure you use a real address and name for packages, fake name = lost packages.
PO box should be fine, just make sure you wait for your mail so no one jacks your package.

That should cover everything for now, the other stuff you will learn once you familiarize yourself with SR.
