Title: Re: Mardi Gras Recommendations!!
Post by: greendye on January 28, 2013, 10:09 pm
I'd consider bringing some opiates or xanax along, in case things get too intense.  Mardis Gras seems like a fun time, but it's gonna get weird at some point if you're tripping, no way around it.  Might be good for your nerves if you're able to pop a bar or do a line of oxy if you feel yourself losing control.

Also, if you feel the trip veering towards somewhere you don't want to go and start to panic, just say to yourself when you took the drugs ("I took mushrooms at 7 p.m."), note what time it is now, and remind yourself that you took a drug and this experience will not last forever.  It will be over around 5-9 hours after you dosed.  This really does help.
