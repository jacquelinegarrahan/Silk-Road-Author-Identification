Quote from: Xennek on March 28, 2013, 01:52 am
Anyone experiencing missing / delayed transfer of funds from SR to BlockChain.info? A transfer I made nearly a day ago has yet to show up and it is worrying/ pissing me off.

If you did a withdrawl, they sometimes take a while (a few hours) to show up in blockchain as they go through the tumbler. The tumbler sends the bitcoin through many addresses to hide the origin and destination of the transaction. Also make sure to check the destination address and not the originating address. If the bitcoin does not show up in more than 12 hours, contact support. Hope that helps.
