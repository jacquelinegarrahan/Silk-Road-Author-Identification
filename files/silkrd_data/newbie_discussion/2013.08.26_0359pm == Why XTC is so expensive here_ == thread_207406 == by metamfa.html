Title: Re: Why XTC is so expensive here?
Post by: unbeknownst on August 27, 2013, 04:43 am
There seem to be a lot of questions of this nature being asked by newbs, and in every case the answer is the same. SR isn't some overstock discount drugs warehouse; with a product like MDMA of which there is a finite amount of precursor and a huge demand there isn't any incentive to let it go cheap. There is a minimum cost to the vendors and shipping the stuff into the wild blue yonder to god knows who (essentially upfront) adds to the risk and end cost of product. Even given that, pills are not any more expensive here than irl on average, considering the quality is more consistent, and you can at least rely on some feedback whereas on the street you have to take someone's word. There are bulk deals on here for less than 5 a pill, granted in large quantities, same as you would encounter anywhere. Who is going to sell you pills for less than 10 without buying in bulk, not worth their time if they sell 1000's a week. As far as making money, I frequently see people pay 20$ or more gladly for hq rolls, so it's all about selling in the right places. Like has been said E is a drug that begs to be sold in certain situations like festivals where everyone needs your product immediately and there is a lot less supply. There really aren't that many habitual users of this stuff who want it all the time like with coke.
