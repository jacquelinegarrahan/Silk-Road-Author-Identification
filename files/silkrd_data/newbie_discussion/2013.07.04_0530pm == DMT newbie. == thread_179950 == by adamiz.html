So you got a glass dick and some powders but donno wtf to do.

Put a lil bit of the powder into your device (dmt is actually active in consciously aware humans at 5mg vaped).
Hit it with the coolest part of your torch until you see it start to melt and bead up.
Let it pool, then gradually increase the heat.
You do not want it to boil or SMOKE BLACK.
Allow the molten liquid to start to vape slowly, then hit it with a bit more heat.
Maintain proper temp on the bowl till its all vaped.

Now load it up and hit your friend, then have them hit you, most of the time 2 lungfuls is all you need.

I usually hold my first lungful for 70% of my breath time (like the longest  Ican hold my breath underwater)
Then shoot for blastoff on the second hit, which is maximum capacity of lungs that I can hold for 100% of my breath time.
If you are not astral light by then, you should wait 45minutes to three hours to let your brain and body metabolize it, and give you a baseline worth trying again.

Good Luck! See ya on the other side... maybe ;)
