Ok. In my experience there are 3 levels of drug smuggling for festivals.

Level 1 - Personal use
Level 2 - You, friends, some light selling
Level 3 - Enough to remove sobriety from the festival

You can smuggle, similarly, in 3 levels.

Level 1 - Underwear. Easy, just throw it in there and keep it warm.
Level 2 -  In your stuff, not in the trunk. Just throw it in a big duffle bag of clothes. They don't have the time to look too deep. They mostly just want to grab the idiots who make it obvious. Avoid the glovebox and trunk*.
Level 3 - The trunk. Let me explain this one. Every car in the US and I believe EU is required to have a spare tire, jack, and the necessary tools to change a tire. Most people have no clue these huge items are hidden in their trunk. Pull everything out, take out the lining and fill every spare space with as much fun as you can. I have seen people go so far as removing the spare tire. Note that every car is different but he point is there are some crazy clandestine spots in them that are hard as hell to get to. This is in some ways the safest route of transport.

Hope this helped.
