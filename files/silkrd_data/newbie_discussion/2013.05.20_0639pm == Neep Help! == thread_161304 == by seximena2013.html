Title: Re: Neep Help!
Post by: britkid55 on May 20, 2013, 07:31 pm
here is an relatively easy guide to get you set up...you seem somewhat familiar with the process but here it goes: if you go to blockchain.info/wallet you can create your own wallet. Once you create your own wallet you will see on the right side of the screen easy deposit and then follow the directions. You already seem familiar with moneygram (the service used to transfer cash to bitcoins). once you fill out the zipzap form you will get an email and pdf file either print that or write down relevant numbers and take that to cvs/walmart/where ever and once you pay itll transfer directly to your wallet. From there you just go to sr account, click on account, make deposit and itll give you an address (long string of numbers). Go back to your wallet and choose option send money type in your address as seen on your SR account and in an hour or so itll show up. I did my first deposit yesterday make sure you only transfer a small amount first to make sure it shows up. Change your pin and passphrase to avoid phishing and scammers (unless you want to risk losing all your money). hope this helps! Good luck!
