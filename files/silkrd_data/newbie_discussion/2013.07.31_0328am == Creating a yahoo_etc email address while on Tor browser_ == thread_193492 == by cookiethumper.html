Title: Re: Creating a yahoo/etc email address while on Tor browser?
Post by: fatbrian on July 31, 2013, 03:52 am
I could never get a tormail address, so I created a Yahoo mail account (that I only access through Tor).  It wasn't easy.  I had to answer tons of questions and they keep on wanting me to give them a mobile phone # and stuff, I just left that off.  I made up various info for the security questions and kept notes of what they are.

They autodetect what country you're accessing them from and try to suggest what location you're in, etc.

When I log in to access it, that's a hassle too, because with Tor they see me logging in from around the world, so they assume my account has been hacked.  I pretty much always have to jump through hoops to prove I'm accessing the email legitimately.
