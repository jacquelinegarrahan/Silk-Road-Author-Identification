Title: Bitcoin success for this newbie!
Post by: osbourne on July 23, 2013, 08:23 pm
After some lengthy (and tedious) forum searching and review scanning, I purchased $200 of bitcoins from cashintocoins.com.  I shot the site a email last night and got a quick response from Will with the needed deposit information.  In the morning, I drove to Wells Fargo to deposit my $ into the account number provided.  I had to provide NO ID of any sort.  Made the deposit and got my receipt.  I emailed Will back with a picture of my receipt and received an email from him about an hour later saying there was trouble with the transfer.  I went to blockchain site and it was down for maintenance.  I replied back to Will's email explaining the site was down.  Once the site was back up, I created a new address on blockchain and asked him to try to deposit again.  In less than 1 hour, my coins were transferred.  Then, I transferred the coins from blockchain to my SR account.  After staring at my SR account for 20 minutes and seeing no coins, I left it alone.  Checked it 3 hours later and the coins were in my SR account and I placed my first order!!!  Finally!!  Now, more waiting for a package :o
