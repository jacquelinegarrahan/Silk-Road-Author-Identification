Title: Re: New here
Post by: Candy on July 12, 2013, 11:03 am
Becoming a vendor is quite easy. Under Account settings on the marketplace there is a link called "become a vendor".

All you got to do is click that link, pay the vendor fee (I believe it is 500$), and you have officially become a vendor.

Usually you get a full G, or sometimes a little more.

The best way to start out as a vendor, is to find a couple of respected forum members, and offer them Free/very cheap samples, and if your product-price range is reasonable that will usually get the ball rolling. Also being friendly, and having good costumer support is an important thing, if you want to become a successful vendor.
