I know most of you were probably nervous and didn't know what to expect, but most likely ended up having a night filled with euphoria, who knew one girl could make so many people happy! My first time with Molly was probably one of the best nights of my life, I went to my first rave on the first of this year, with my ex girlfriend. Up until this point I had only done MDA and didn't know the difference between the two, but boy, Molly made me feel like a little kid in a candy shop! Everything was just amazing, I took around 300mg, and for a short period, right after the come up started feeling tingling all through out my body and felt really shaky, luckily my ex helped me out and told me to just go sit down and drink water and that what I was experiencing was completely normal, so I did and I sat down for about 30 minutes, and these thirty minutes were amazing, I could literally close my eyes and feel like I was one with the music. After getting up, me and my ex went out into the huge crowd and danced our asses off! I literally had never been that happy in my life until then, which is kind of sad, but oh well, all in all it was an amazing night, and I had a more positive outlook on life and the whole universe since then.
