Title: Re: should I be worried about a controlled delivery?
Post by: Rotaluc on September 12, 2013, 08:57 pm
Do not open the package. Write "return to sender" on the package. Wait for a couple of days.

If the feds come knocking, tell them it's not the package you expected (you're an avid Amazon shopper, yes?) and wanted to return it, just had not gotten to bringing it to the post office yet.

If you've already opened it, same story, tape it back shut and write "return to sender" on it. Tell the feds its contents did not match with what you expected.

If the feds try to incriminate you and say "but these are drugs, didn't you see! why didn't you report!" you tell them you figured it was a dietary supplement, diet pill, caffeine pill or whatever. Even the thought of illegal substances never occured to you.

If they still don't buy that, stick to the fifth amendment (or if you're not a US citizen, the right to remain silent).
