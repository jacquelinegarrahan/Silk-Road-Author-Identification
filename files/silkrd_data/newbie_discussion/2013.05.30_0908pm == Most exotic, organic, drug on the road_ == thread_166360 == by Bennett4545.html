My lay-man's and simplified understanding of what an MAOI does is it keeps your body from breaking down, and "washing out" many things in your bloodstream/cells.  That's why the DMT acts for so much longer- because it doesn't get "cleaned out" of your system.  The problem is that with MAOIs, things that aren't usually toxic to the body become toxic because they aren't broken down, and their affects last for way longer.
