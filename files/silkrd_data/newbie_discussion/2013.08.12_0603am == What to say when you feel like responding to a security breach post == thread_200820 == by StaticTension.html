A disturbing trend I'm seeing is whenever there is some sort of security breach involving a vendor or a buyer with the potential of being discovered by LE either through an intercepted package or the money trail is people will always say 'OMG dude Bitcoins are not Illegal to buy'. 

That's a really retarded argument. No they are not. However go ask 10 people tommorow in your outside adventures if they know what a Bitcoin is and see what answer you get. Trust me LE sees a male under 35 buying bitcoins and they already have a tip on you they're not going to assume that you're setting up the next Bitcoin Trust.
