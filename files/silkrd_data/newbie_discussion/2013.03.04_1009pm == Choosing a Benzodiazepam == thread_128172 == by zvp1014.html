i would recomend ativan (lorazepam) 0.5-2.0mg or valium (diazepam) 2.5-10mg for acute anxiety management i.e. "panic attacks." Klonopin (clonazepam) is used for long term therapy for treatment of persistent anxiety from drug and alcohol withdrawl and in pulmonary patients who suffer from anxiety due to hypoxia (low oxygen).
the idea with long acting benzo's like librium and clonazepam is: you take a specific dose twice daily until desired effect and then maintain that dose. you must taper off  when discontinuing after long term use. they have long half lifes. be careful not to take too much! start with 0.25 to 0.5mg clonazepam initially. it does have a strong amnesia effect with me...especially with tequila, coke, bud, and beer! so i must admit it is one of my favorites for i resort weekend when i am not driving ;)
