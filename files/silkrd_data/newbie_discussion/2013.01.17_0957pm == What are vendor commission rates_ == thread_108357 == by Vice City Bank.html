Title: Re: What are vendor commission rates?
Post by: midwestmedical on January 18, 2013, 01:09 am
SR's Sellers Fees are:

10% of the first $50  so $5
8.5% of $50 - 150     +$8.50
6% of $150 - 300      +$9
3% of $300 - 500      +$6
2% of $500 - 1000     +$10
1.5% for everything over $1000"

so for a purchase of $1000 the total is 5 + 8.50 + 9 + 6 + 10 for the total of 38.50

The fees are for each item sold.  They are not based on the vendors gross sales.
