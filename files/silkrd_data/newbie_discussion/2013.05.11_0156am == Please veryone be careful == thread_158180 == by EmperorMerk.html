Title: Please veryone be careful
Post by: EmperorMerk on May 11, 2013, 01:56 am
I can't post this on drug safety on account of my posts, but I guess it's more relevant to new users anyway. Please, please think long and hard about the doses you are taking with the drugs available.

Two nights ago, me and 4 other friends each had 2 tabs of Tyl3r's 1.2mg NBOMe blotters each. 4 of us were fairly use to the effects, one had never done it before and insisted that he had the same amount. This guy is incredibly healthy and sensible most of the time, everything was going well when he started repeating, "I need to jump! I need to jump!" He started running down the street (he was a representative track runner, had no chance of catching him but tried).

He started jumping people's fences, on one of them he miscalculated the jump and hit his head on the side of the pool. We're burying him in a few days. The tabs themselves are chemically safe, but I'll say again, think very long and hard about the doses you are using. I lost a friend of 13 years because of a stupid mistake that could have easily been avoided, and even though I'll never know any of you as well, I care about you all the same. Please stay safe.
