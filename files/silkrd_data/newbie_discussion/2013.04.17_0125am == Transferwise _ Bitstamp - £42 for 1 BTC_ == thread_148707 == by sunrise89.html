I use Transferwise > Bitstamp.

Transferwise cuts down the cost of exchanging money to Euros (for Bitstamp) at the same time as sending faster payments. I sent both a bank transfer and a Transferwise transfer to Bitstamp at the same time and the former took 2 days whereas the latter took 4 days.

To transfer money with Transferwise, you will need to set up the payment from Transferwise to Bitstamp of the desired GBP to send which will then be converted to Euros and a small transfer charge will be taken. You will then have to pay the invoice with your selected method which include UK Bank Transfers and Debit cards.

With the routing number problem, try and look for your international payments page. They require and BIC Code and an IBAN which you are given by Bitstamp.
