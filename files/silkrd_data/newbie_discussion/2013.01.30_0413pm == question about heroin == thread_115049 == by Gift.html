Post by: mrlavish on January 30, 2013, 05:02 pm
#3 Heroin refers to a base Heroin, usually it is smoked, it can be IVed and snorted but you must add a citric acid of some type. Black tar and #3 are not the same thing. Black tar actually is a "crude" process that doesn't really fall into the #3/#4 category, as it has it's own refinement process. Black Tar is Black Tar.

#4 Heroin is processed to a salt Heroin HCL, and is probably considered the "purest" and most refined of the Heroins. Now Among #4 you have Colombian Brown, Afghan brown, and then China White.

Now here's the deal 99% of the stuff being called China White on SR(or real life) isn't China White, it's Afghan or Colombian Brown with a cutting agent(many say Fentanyl)making it whitish in color. As far as I know the only true China White being sold on SR is coming from Smiles Awhile and he is not based in the USA.

China White only comes from a particular area in Southeast Asian called the Golden Triangle, Burma, Laos, Thailand etc.. This is where #4 China White is produced, and rarely seen in the U.S. . True China white is considered the purest most refined of all the Heroins. It for all intents and purposes looks like a high quality cocaine, as it is light fluffy and pure white.

Go read the reviews of Smiles Awhile and see how glowing they are.
