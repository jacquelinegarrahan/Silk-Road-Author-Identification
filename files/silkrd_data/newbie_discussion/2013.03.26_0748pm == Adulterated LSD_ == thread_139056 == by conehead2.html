Title: Re: Adulterated LSD?
Post by: Mercked on March 26, 2013, 08:19 pm
You can distinguish between LSD and DOX with Marquis and Ehrlich's Reagents.
DOX will turn the colour of the corresponding 2C-X compound with Marquis. Eg- 2C-B and DOB will turn green.
Ehrlich's Reagent will turn purple in the presence of indole alkaloids.
The relatively high dose of tryptamines vs LSD/ DOX means it is unlikely that your LSD is adulterated with another tryptamine.

In summary- if Marquis is negative and Ehrlich's Reagents gives a positive result then you most likely have LSD.
