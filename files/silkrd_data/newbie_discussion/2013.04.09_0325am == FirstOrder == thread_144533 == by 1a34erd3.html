Title: Re: FirstOrder
Post by: martine on April 09, 2013, 04:12 am
1oz is not really much to worry about - it's probably going to be fine and arrive 2-4 days after it's marked in transit. Some vendors mark in transit before actually shipping to throw off LE, others who are trustworthy just do it at night and ship in the morning. USPS priority will be 3 days to almost anywhere in the US, and it it's more you probably know why already (Alaska, remote location etc).

I've mailed 1oz before with way less stealth than SR vendors use and a handwritten address. there's always some luck involved but it's not really enough to worry about. Keep a clean receiving location & deny deny deny.
