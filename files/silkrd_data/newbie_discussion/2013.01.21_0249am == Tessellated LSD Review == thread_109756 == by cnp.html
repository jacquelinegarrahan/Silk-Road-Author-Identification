Title: Tessellated LSD Review
Post by: cnp on January 21, 2013, 02:49 am
So, I finally got a chance to sample Tessellated s LSD.  Six people total, including me.  My impression is below, and the collective impression from my friends follows.   

Mine:  Mixed, given the rave reviews that the forums have provided, but that may just be me.  After a hit, the most I got out of it was borderline; slight tracers, and, if I concentrated *really* hard, a little bit of motion.  Mostly just anxiousness and disappointment.  This is coming from someone who has fairly extensive experience with hallucinogens, but no tolerance buildup to LSD given the time that has elapsed since I last took it.  In terms of my physical tolerance, this will give you an idea:  the last time I took it I had a mind-blowing, textbook experience that was shatteringly positive and visually interesting, off of a single hit of street acid of questionable quality --it's low.  But Tessellated's just didn't fit that category, and certainly not at the 200ug range.  It's easily identifiable once you've landed there. 

** HOWEVER, like I said above, this might just be me.  I was with five other friends who all gave it an A.  Six people, 5 positive, 1 negative.  This is important!  The evening was fun; we sat around the fire and drew for hours, and we all felt very open and warm.  Everyone reportedly found themselves in the "lsd space".  Just not me. 

As far as my own personal tolerance, the only explanation I can provide is my recent experimentation with DMT which may have had a detrimental effect on my ability to appreciate and experience other hallucinogens, this vendors LSD included.  I've never heard of a physiological correlation, but it's still a possibility. 

Given that I've ordered quite a bit from this vendor, and after reading a recent, negative feedback item on his wall,  I'm eager to continue exploring under the assumption that it's an issue with me, not it --especially given the level of enjoyment my friends got out of it.  Next time I will take two hits rather than one, and take notes during the experience.  His communication and stealth is the best I've seen on the Road, easily.  He is still an A+ vendor in my book.  I give him the benefit of the doubt.  I'm just disappointed after two weeks of anticipation buildup. 

And so, though this may be a bit of a contrast to the multitude of positive reviews, it is honest and thus relevant.  Thought I would take the time to write it out. 

Will report back after the next trip with an update. 

Peace

I received Tess's product within the time frame stated. Packaging was superb.

I dropped one tab under my tongue T = 0 and stepped outside to start my day. The plans were to go to an IMAX theater and some museums around it. The movie started at T + 40 minutes and lasted for 45 minutes. The come up was awesome. Coming up on one tab while watching astronauts fix the hubble space telescope in IMAX 3D was a great experience. There was no body load at all. And if there was, a tingling throughout my body overshadowed it.

At T+2:00 I found myself sitting at a patterned table inside a restaurant. If I looked at the table through my peripherals the pattern would dance and combine within itself. I was feeling giddy. At 3:30 we entered another movie and scenes of the ocean and clouds had that self-similar fractal nature that comes with LSD.

At T+4:00 we were walking around outside and everything felt crisp, clean, and magnificent.

We began to smoke medical marijuana at T+5:00 and had a grand time. The one tab allowed a clear head space and room for great conversation. We were smoking with others that were not on the acid and could have a clear two-way conversation with them. When smoking while sober there gets a point where I cease to be social or interested in most things. Smoking on one tab was one of the most fun I've had with marijuana for a while. I could take bigger hits without coughing and overall felt very fresh. I could feel my stomach churning and making noises starting at around T+5:00 but it was nothing out of the ordinary for LSD. By this time everything had a clay/cartoon character to it and focusing on objects caused them to wiggle and dance.

Overall, it was a very positive experience. One tab was not overwhelming and allowed me to walk around a busy college campus/city and interact with others face to face and without sunglasses covering my eyes. I was able to fall asleep at T+15:00. Although it should be noted I have trouble falling asleep when smoking large amounts of weed.

I have tried street LSD 2 times and SR LSD 2 times. The last SR LSD I had were the Dalai Lama blotters from Neurotic Narcotic about 11 months ago. They were listed at 250 ug and one of those gave me a stronger experience than one of Tess's tabs. Both Tess's tabs and the Dalai Lama's beat the shit out of street acid. The feeling from these is a pure, expansive feeling that cannot be compared to the hot-flash/uncomfortable feel of the street acid I have tried.

This is a great product from a great vendor. A friend I was apprehensive about trying LSD but eventually decided to drop with us. He had a wonderful time and remarked how surprised he was of the mellowness of the drug. Highly recommend one dose to introduce others to LSD and more if one really wants a strong experience.
