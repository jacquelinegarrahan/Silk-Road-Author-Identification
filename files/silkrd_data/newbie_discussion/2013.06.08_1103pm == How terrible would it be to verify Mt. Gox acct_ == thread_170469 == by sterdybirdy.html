i am not an expert. also i don't know if you are asking about verifying at Mt. Gox or about bitcoin transfers? Perhaps you're asking about transfers out of Mt. Gox to an address?

Not knowing specifically what you are asking, if you are worried about your transaction being pegged to an address, you can send your coins through a mixer so that the transaction is distributed and harder to trace.

Really for practical purposes, unless you have a small fortune in bitcoins-- enough that the transaction would raise some peoples curiosity examining large transactions or large amounts of coins in a single address---i would not go crazy.

The act of verifying an account with Mt. Gox is not setting yourself up for anything. I've done it. What i do not do is send coins straight out of Mt.GOx to another address.
