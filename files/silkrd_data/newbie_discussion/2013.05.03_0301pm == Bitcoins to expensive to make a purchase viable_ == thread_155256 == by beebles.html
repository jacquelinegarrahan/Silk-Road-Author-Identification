Title: Re: Bitcoins to expensive to make a purchase viable?
Post by: Thrakmar on May 03, 2013, 03:22 pm
You would get a decent amount of LSD for 2BTC. I'm sure you can find a few tabs for far cheaper.

By the way, the price of a bitcoin does not affect the price of items on SR. SR prices rise and drop to make the price exactly what the vendor specified. For example:

Let's say 1g of MDMA costs $200 and 1BTC is worth $100, which would make the MDMA worth 2BTC. Then tomorrow the price of a bitcoin rises to $200 per BTC. The MDMA would automatically recalculate it's price and would now be worth 1BTC.

The SR wiki is a good place for information like this. Give it a read before making any purchases :)

Title: Re: Bitcoins to expensive to make a purchase viable?
Post by: TemaculaSunrise on May 03, 2013, 03:35 pm
Change your SR settings to show in USD or whatever your currency is, not in BTC.  Also, you can buy BTC in fragments so if the price of BTC is $120 and something is $60, you can just but 0.5BTC.  BTC price on SR is based off MT. Gox trading average, much easier to see prices in USD than BTC.
