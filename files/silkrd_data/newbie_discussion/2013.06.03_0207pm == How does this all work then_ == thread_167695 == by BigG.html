Hi BigG,

If you are interested in selling on Silk Road please have a read of the Seller's guide:
http://dkn255hz262ypmii.onion/wiki/index.php?title=Seller%27s_Guide

You will be required to pay a $500 vendor bond to purchase a vendor account before being permitted to sell anything here, and this is fully refundable once you meet the terms of said bond.

You can read more about the bond here:
http://dkn255hz262ypmii.onion/wiki/index.php?title=Seller%27s_Guide#Bond

You will be required to post your product to customers with their security and safety at the forefront of everything you do.
