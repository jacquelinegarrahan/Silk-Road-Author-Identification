Title: Re: PGP in messages??/
Post by: Poisonouss on March 12, 2013, 08:02 pm
You don't need to use PGP for messages. Use it for your address unless you are paranoid and want to annoy vendors, just a heads up. You import their public key, encrypt a message to them, then send them the encrypted message. Your public key is only needed for them to reply to you in PGP, which as I said before is unnecessary.
