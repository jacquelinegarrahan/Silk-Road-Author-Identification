Suboxone will stop the cravings but it is an opiate in itself so you are just trading one addiction for another.  However, the subs are a lot easier to quit than the H.  If you end up trying out Suboxone, start out with the lowest possible dose that you can tolerate.  A lot of Dr's prescribe 2 x 8mg films to start out.  If your H habit is not that bad then I would try to start out with like 4mg or even the 2mg film.  You have to taper down in very small doses also.  When I quit Subs, at the end I was only taking 1/16th of a 2mg film or .125mg.  Wellbutrin also seems to help a little bit.  If you want more info about the subs just send me a message.
