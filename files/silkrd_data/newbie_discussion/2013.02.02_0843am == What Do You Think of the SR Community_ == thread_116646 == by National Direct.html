Well here is where we hear from our new members on their first impression of our community. I think SR is incredible, with a majority of buyers and sellers who respect each other. Although I didn't list it, to me the SR Community is like alcohol: Anyone can access it, it can calm u down, it can fuck you up, it can ruin your day (wtf is my mail?!), we're friendly, but some of us are assholes. Prohibition didn't work on alcohol, and won't for drugs. Most importantly, alcohol can be made in almost every part of the world's land. All you need is sugar. Like a bar's social scene is lubricated with booze, the SR community comes together to help each other make the right choices. SR brings opportunity. Psychedelics can humble your soul and open your mind, and narcotics can be a fun vacation from the stresses of life. We should always realize the consequences of our actions on ourselves and those close to us.

I hope all you new users can find common ground on opinions, have some meaningful trips, and make some friends along the way. Always be safe and do your research!
