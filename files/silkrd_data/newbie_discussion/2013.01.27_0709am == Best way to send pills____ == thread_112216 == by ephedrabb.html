Title: Re: Best way to send pills????
Post by: #slothrop on January 27, 2013, 07:25 am
You're probably not going to get caught/arrested, but that's like saying your not probably not going to get kill running a red light. Packaging is a sensitive issue for discussion because each vendor has his/her own packaging method. If that method gets "found out" (i.e. discussed) it basically means that any LE who might be reading and happens to want to make a bust has just been given a leg up in making that happen. I'm not a vendor, but that's the rationale that makes sense. Of course if you want to learn about any vendor's packaging it's as simple as placing an order. And we all know how easy that is. If you want to discuss your own packaging publicly there isn't anything wrong with that. Just don't expect a whole bunch of people to jump on board such a thread.
