Post by: novakane on May 12, 2013, 03:41 am
Well as with all things, tolerance comes the more you do it.  But yes, with the configuration of my Persei right now, anyone would get pretty high after one to a few hits and it tends to last a while.  The Persei specifically is variable voltage by changing out the battery combinations.  There are different ohm cartridges for different power ratings for different people.  5 ohm cartridge at 7.4v (strongest setting) will knock out most people after a couple hits.

So for me personally, I'm hitting my pen quite a bit throughout the day more than just a few times a day, but not everyone would have to do that.  To maybe answer your question better, I filled .7g of oil in a cartridge a few days ago, have been using it nonstop, and have probably gone through <.2g so far.  mileage may vary.
