Title: Re: Bitinstant is Back Up!
Post by: Cynosure on March 08, 2013, 08:07 pm
For those of you unable to submit bitcoins because Bitinstant is down, check out Bitfloor.

I had been trying to use Bitinstant for a couple of months but it was always down in 3-5 day sessions every time I was going to place my order.  I got frustrated and said "fuck them".  Someone recommended Bitfloor and I had a great experience.  You simply sign up (e-mail required), type in the dollar amount you want to transfer, and they will give you an account number (which can only be used at Bank of America) and some information.  From here, you just tell the teller you are trying to pay an out of state bill.  Be sure to pay the exact amount (for instance, if you order $500 worth, your price to deposit will be $501.xx)  Hand them the cash and fill out the form with the information you got from Bitfloor.  The money will literally be in your account 10 minutes later.  Then all you need to do is buy bitcoins.  There's two options, but I went for the immediate marketplace option.

Of course, I send them through 3 Blockchain wallets before sending to Silk Road (which caused me to lose some money). 

After it was all said and done, $631 and some change to BoA yielded me around $575.  The entire process took about 2-3 hours (most of the time was spent waiting for my Blockchain transfers to confirm).

I highly recommend it.  The exchange rates are a bit high but there wasn't any hassle.
