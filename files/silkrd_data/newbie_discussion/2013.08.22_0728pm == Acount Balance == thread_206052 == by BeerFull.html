Title: Re: Acount Balance
Post by: mcguire39 on August 22, 2013, 07:40 pm
If you give a decent transaction fee like .001 it seems to rack up the confirmations pretty quickly. Then once it hits the six confirmations you're just waiting for the next round of processing for SR (almost seems like it is a batch job that runs a couple times an hour maybe). I tried a couple deposits with a decent transaction fee. The first got into the account within about an hour, the next in about 90 minutes.

Post by: mcguire39 on August 22, 2013, 10:02 pm
To assign a transaction fee you have to do that separately. Like in Electrum for example it lets  you specify the fee. Not all transfer services let you specify a fee. Some have just a fixed fee. Some assign no fee at all (i.e. SR). Transactions with no fee do still get processed but it seems the confirmations take longer.
