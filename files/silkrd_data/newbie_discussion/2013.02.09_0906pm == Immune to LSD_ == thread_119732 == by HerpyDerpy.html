Title: Re: Immune to LSD?
Post by: trippylizard on February 09, 2013, 09:35 pm
The light in the fridge isn't on when the door's closed, dude! Plus it's only ultraviolet that'll destroy it, not regular light... and a fridge light is like, thousands of times weaker than sunlight is, anyway. ;D

But if you didn't wrap it in anything, then any moisture or condensation on the shelf/whatever will have leached the LSD out of the tab, so they'll likely be weaker than if you'd wrapped 'em proper and stuck them somewhere cool and dry like between the pages of a book.

He might just have a higher threshold for LSD than you two, or a longer than usual recovery period for his brain to get back to normal. Give it a month, then try him on three tabs at once! :D
