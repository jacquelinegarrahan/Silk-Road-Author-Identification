Title: Re: GPU Privacy Assistant (GPA Encryption) Help on sending it?
Post by: Yoda on September 24, 2013, 09:00 am
In GPA:

-Select "Clipboard"
-Write your message in that clipboard
-Move clipboard aside and copy your key from the Keymanager/Keyring. 
-Go back to the clipboad and post your public key below your message/address
-Select "Encrypt" from the menu bar... be sure to select that vendor, not yourself.  (When you call someone on the phone you dial them, not yourself... but they need your number to call you back, right?)
- copy and past that entire mess on clipboard

When you order, a box will ask you for your address... paste all that in there
Title: Re: GPU Privacy Assistant (GPA Encryption) Help on sending it?
Post by: Blueness on September 24, 2013, 10:56 am
You don't need to give out your own key to send your address, or any message to someone; the other person only needs that if they need to message you. All you need to use is the vendor's public key: encrypt your message using it in the way Yoda explains (which it sounds like you've already done) and paste the block of text it turns into, into the address box. Everything including -----BEGIN PGP MESSAGE----- and -----END PGP MESSAGE-----
