Title: Re: How much does the quality of DMT affect the experience?
Post by: PotatoConnoisseur on January 27, 2013, 01:47 am
It's really easy, googling it will bring up a lot of different techniques. Personally I like to start with mimosa hostillis powdered root bark and do large scale acid base extractions. The easiest and quickest way to do it is to use a straight to base method where all you have to do is mix some distilled water with your bark then raise the ph (I use sodium hydroxide although it's important to note that a wash is probably necessary if you use such a powerful base. Nobody wants to smoke NaOH). You then add your choice of non-polar solvent, I use naptha, and shake the shit out of your container. It'll get hot, you'll want to do this in an hdpe plastic container so the high ph doesn't immediately destroy your container, it'd be one hell of a mess. After leaving it and shaking it for a couple of days (always vent after shaking) you separate the clearish naptha layer from the gross blackish layer, make sure no black is in you're naptha. Then just throw it in a really cold freezer, leave it for a couple of days and use a filter to get your dmt once it freeze precipitates out.

But yeah you should probably google it if you want details.
