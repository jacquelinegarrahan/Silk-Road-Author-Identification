Title: Re: Best Practices for Purchaser of Marijuana
Post by: Blue Dragon on March 25, 2013, 02:53 am
Hi there,
There is a thread on shipping practices, that offers good advice on also receiving parcels. Speaking to your vendor, and letting him know your set up he may be able to give good advice, as they have shipped hundreds of parcels and surely have "ways" As for the name, using a name that has been established and you have received mail for in the past is a good idea. Fake or not. If you throw some random name out there, it is likely to be "undeliverable" or draw more unwanted attention as your mail carrier will not recognize it.  Hope this helps, if only a little:)
