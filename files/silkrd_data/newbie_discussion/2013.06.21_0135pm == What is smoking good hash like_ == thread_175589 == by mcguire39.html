Title: Re: What is smoking good hash like?
Post by: NewStem on June 21, 2013, 03:00 pm
To me, good hash is dreamy and noninvasive on your ordinary cognition. This is in comparison to pot, which at its worst, will warp the way I think in the direction of anxiety and paranoia. At hash's worst, it will put you on the couch and you will not get anything you intended to do done that day.

One of my favourite things about hash is the head-spinnies you get from the first hit in a long time, especially with tobacco. It will drop you for a good 10 minutes before your blood pressure has caught up with your brain.

Whereas schwaggy weed is fun because you can roll joint after joint, bad hash is no fun at all. Tastes bad, gives ya headaches. That's why I'm glad for the Road- I haven't picked up local hash for years because it's all cut-up soap bar.
 
Ultimately I like weed better because I like the way it changes my engagement with the things I do and I build up a very fast tolerance to hash, so I view it hash more as a treat.
