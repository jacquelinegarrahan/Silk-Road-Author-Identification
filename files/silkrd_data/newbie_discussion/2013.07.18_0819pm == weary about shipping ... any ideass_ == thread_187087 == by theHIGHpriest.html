Title: Re: weary about shipping ... any ideass?
Post by: Demonology on July 18, 2013, 10:05 pm
Agreed. Time and time again, it's been recommended NOT to use a false name. That looks suspicious, and is going to cause more time to be spent attending to that letter. The aim is to blend in, and get out of the hands of the postal workers as quickly as possible. There still are MANY routes for plausible deniability even while using your own name. If you're set on not using your real name, it seems the best option is to set up a P.O. Box with a fake name, but for that you'll need fake verifiable identification, and likely more than just one; this also brings about a whole host of other laws being broken were that process found out.

Also, there's always having it sent to a trusted friend's mailing address (ask him first!), and possibly, if he knows the risk involved, paying him a small amount for the service (in cash, obviously). The risk is much lower to both of you as well because the drugs will not be kept at his address, so if LE do investigate, they won't find anything incriminating (find a friend that doesn't do illegal things), and there shouldn't be a trace back to you in that case.

But good luck, and welcome to SR! I hope you enjoy this place as much as possible  8)
