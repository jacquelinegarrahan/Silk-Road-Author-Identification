Title: Re: Stupid Question???
Post by: Blueness on September 25, 2013, 09:31 am
LSD is a very fragile molecule which breaks down very rapidly at temperatures much lower than is required for combustion, so it almost certainly wouldn't work at all; you would just destroy the LSD.

I think myths like this pop up because unknowledgeable people have an odd feeling one day when they're smoking, and it reminded them of LSD and they would like a way to explain it away. But, well, our minds are strange with placebo effects and nostalgia and completely unphysical LSD associated flashbacks, so a different feeling can't always be explained away by what strain it is or whatever. Somehow it reminds of people who believe in ghosts.

Could also be related to anti-drug propaganda trying to get people to believe that untrustworthy dealers spike their weed for mysterious unknowable reasons.
