Title: Elvis' DMT Review/Full Trip Report
Post by: Splint on March 05, 2013, 10:17 pm
Short:

I ordered 3 items at the same time, Elvis was the first to mark his in transit and it arrived 2 days later. Very prompt, good amount, great quality. The stealth could have been better. It was one of the most overwhelming experiences of my life, second only to a very humbling shroom trip I had a few years ago. It's a truly awe inspiring substance and I understand why so many people have a spiritual re-awakening. It can also be the most horrific 10 minutes of your life if you aren't careful. This isn't a party drug, it isn't something you take because your bored or you want to have a good time. It's a substance designed to destroy your reality, respect it.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

I had just finished an amazing night trying Underground's excellent MDMA crystals. My gf's passed out and has no idea I ordered some  personal psychedelic powder. I didn't want her to try it...there are just some people you know won't trip well. I check to make sure she's asleep, I pack a bowl with weed, sandwich a small amount of DMT smoke a lungful and lay back.

A minute later I have that familiar anxious sensation that tells me my perceptions about to be warped. Objects in the distance start to move and wave, pictures are slowly starting to blink down at me as the room begins to breath. "Oh wow", I think to myself astonished by the sudden rush I was experiencing. Once DMT begins it comes at you fast and hard. Small lights in the room intensify taking on different colors and odd shapes. Shadows move, dance and taunt each other while the silence DEAFENS ME. I'm suddenly aware at how quiet the room is and I start to get uncomfortable but at this point I'm already starting to come down.

Ok...not bad... I can handle that. Some music would be nice. Lets add some more on top, cover it and go for a full blow trip. I come across a song dedicated to DMT trips. A very light whimsical song that seems perfect for this kind of drug. I pause the video in full screen mode so all I have to do is smoke and press play.

First hit...yummy tasty weed. Second hit... nasty harsh dmt ("Hold it in as long as you can!"). Third hit ....getting that anxious feeling again, smoke feels harsher than ever have to close eyes to keep from coughing. Fourth hit.... entire room is moving, bowl looks out of place in my hand. Fifth hit...the bowl looks very unfamiliar but I still manage to inhale a lungful and press play. The smoke flows past me like thick cream, everything has a very odd glean about it like it's encased in shiny plastic. The room was gone before my head hit the pillow.

There's no way to describe with certainty what happened next. The room had melted into a black void and from where the ceiling should be manifested these shapeless mass of colors. Orange, yellow, gold, blue, green, white red was filling the room, I was forced to close my eyes because of it's intensity but they did not go away. They began forming intricate shapes. Patterns within patterns within patterns formed spiraling towards a center with these bright vibrant colors. It moved, swayed, morphed to my emotions and responded to the music. Every color had its own meaning but I was too overwhelmed to understand any. All I remember was red meant pain and it stayed in the periphery of the shapes.

"This is infinity, this is infinity, this is infinity", I begin chanting to myself staring into the center feeling my body merging with the endless spiral...it was as if my entire being expanded and became part of everything. Then I felt the presence of someone else.

Without a doubt this is where many say they saw god/aliens/etc. I had become part of the spiral and I stood staring at myself as the infinite patterns spewed from my mouth. I was opening and closing my mouth as hard as I could performing for the being that had sovereignty over the spiral. It was now my job and I would stay there creating the universe for this being, forced to be his puppet. There was no reason for it, no hostility, no promise of peace or any sort of deal...just complete and utter submission.

I don't remember much more after that.  When I started coming down I was once again uneasy with how silent the room was. The music had finished and I lay there in bed, my jaw sore and very confused. I didn't remember what happened, I just knew it was extremely intense and that I got completely lost in the trip. I didn't even remember the song I was listening to it was like I woke up from a dream (which, I figure, is basically exactly what happened).





The next day I decided I'd give it another try. I'm looking online for music and I come across the song from last night. As soon as I listen to it I get the CRAZIEST feeling of deja vu ever (almost tripped me out more than the drug itself). The trip comes flashing back to me in great detail and for a moment I almost feel like I'm high again. Now I can't listen to the song without thinking of the spiral and laughing

It was a powerful, positive experience for me. If anyone reading this wants to try DMT for the first time, be careful. You'll underestimate how powerful this drug is, but don't fight it because it could be one the most horrific trips of your life. Elvis will hook you up with some good quality stuff.
