Title: Re: bitcoin startup
Post by: lelmeriodici on April 02, 2013, 01:03 pm
Yessir

Make a MtGox account, Bitstamp account, or use an online wallet (like Blockchain.info). Go to bitinstant.com and select the appropriate settings. Go to Wal Mart and fill out the blue slip (pay a bill, at the customer service desk) with the information ZipZap provides. Hand it to them and pay cash. You can use a fake name at Wal-Mart since they don't check IDs.

Before depositing to SR, use Blockchain's Shared Send feature or Bitcoin Fog to make your transactions untraceable. Via those services, deposit to an account that you only use over Tor.

So....

Fill out Bitinstant forms > Fill out the MoneyGram slip and pay cash at Wal-Mart > Redeem BTC at MtGox or Bitstamp > Send to Blockchain wallet > shared send to an an instawallet (instawallet.org) or two that were created and accessed ONLY with Tor > then transfer to SR.

Its a bitch but its a pretty solid way of staying anonymous.
