Title: Re: Western Union or Moneypack? Localbitcoins
Post by: Hitch on August 14, 2013, 01:58 pm

   Localbitcoins is divided into geographic regions, hence the local. You'll need to state what country you're in for people to help. Localbitcoins don't need any ID, apart from an email address (don't use your real one) and you can access it with tor, but vendors can specify they only want to transact with people who have verified phone numbers or who aren't using tor.

   I don't know if it's possible in your country but in Australia we can make anonymous over the counter deposits into bitcoin vendors bank accounts, which is very safe and convenient. I'm pretty sure WU requires photo id for pretty much everything.
