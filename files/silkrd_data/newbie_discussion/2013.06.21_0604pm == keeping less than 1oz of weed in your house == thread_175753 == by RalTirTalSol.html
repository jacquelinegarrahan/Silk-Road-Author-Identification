Title: Re: keeping less than 1oz of weed in your house
Post by: dmc002 on June 21, 2013, 06:37 pm
It's not very dangerous at all as long as your neighbors don't suspect you of suspicious activity and you are not already a suspected drug dealer/user. No matter what the officer who comes to your door and asks to take a look around tells you, the only way the police can search your house is if they have a warrant. The only way they can get a warrant is if they have a legitimate reason to believe you are committing crimes in your house. Getting suspicious packages from around the world is not enough for a warrant.

But if your neighbors have been complaining to the police about you such as saying your dealing/doing drugs at your house then the police could have grounds for a warrant. This is the way most drug dealers where I live get busted. Your neighbors are spying on you whether you know it or not. Be aware of this and take appropriate precautions.

Another way they might get a warrant and search your house is if you are already under investigation. In that case it's probably best if you didn't order drugs through the mail at all. At least for now.
