Post by: onetwothree on February 22, 2013, 09:02 pm
Quote from: Qthello on February 22, 2013, 08:43 pm
I guess I was hung up on encrypting. I have the usb set up with liberte and what have you. Now I guess my question is, do I access the silkroad through that, instead of the torbrowser on my man drive? Also, do I want to truecrypt my actual C drive and usb with truecrypt?

You want to boot from your USB drive and access SR from there, yes. You may want to have a truecrypt container on your USB drive to store persistent data (not terribly familiar with liberte). The point of the USB drive is not to use your C drive anymore. So, no, you don't need to encrypt it, just get rid of what's already there and don't use it anymore when doing these activities.
