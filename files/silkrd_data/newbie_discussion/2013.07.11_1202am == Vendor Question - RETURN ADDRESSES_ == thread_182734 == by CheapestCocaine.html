Title: Re: Vendor Question - RETURN ADDRESSES?
Post by: jlranier on July 11, 2013, 12:12 am
It seems to me that using a return address in a box in a different area than the actual address could potentially bring some scrutiny, but that may just be me being paranoid. I just think that if I were a postal employee one of the first things I'd look for in profiling would be a series of packages from the next town/county/state over being sent from my location, because my thinking would be why are all these people coming here to send out their mail?

I'd recommend picking up a P.O. box and using that as your return address with real info or at least use separate addresses for sending/receiving/buying/selling. Hope this was helpful in the least and good luck!

I've watched the guy collect mail from the blue drop boxes. He pulls out a tall yellow bin of mail, slaps an empty bin in the box, throws the full bin on his truck.
