bitcoins arent illegal....use your real name and a regular browser to purchase them, welcome to the road, ALSO heres an important thing you need to do


you see that blue S near the top left corner, click that, forbid scripts globally, and then click it again and click options, once in options, click on embeddings and check the following boxes:

forbid java
forbid adobe flash
forbid microsoft silverlight
forbid other plugins
forbid IFRAME
forbid FRAME, now click ok

now in the top right corner it should say "TorBrowser", click that and go to options on there and click on content, uncheck the box that says "enable javascript"


once you've done that youre actually anonymous unless you post your address on here
