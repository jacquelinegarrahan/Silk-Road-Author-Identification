Post by: unbeknownst on August 23, 2013, 03:04 am
Go to localbitcoins.com. There are lots of vendors using every conceivable type of payment method, including WU and moneygram, and even better, anonymous cash bank deposits. There is a rating system showing how many transactions they've completed, and their feedback rating (obviously don't use anyone who doesn't have perfect feedback). It's been the go-to site for a lot of folks here for awhile, and haven't heard of anyone getting scammed so far. I've used many different sellers and had only stellar results. This site is generally much faster than exchanges, much more private and imo much safer than buying from someone on the forums.
