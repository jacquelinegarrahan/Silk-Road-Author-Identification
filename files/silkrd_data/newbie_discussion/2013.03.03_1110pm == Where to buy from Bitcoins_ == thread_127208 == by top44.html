Title: Re: Where to buy from Bitcoins?
Post by: respirer on March 04, 2013, 12:09 am
The available ways to obtain BTC must differ from country to country. You're probably not in the US, but in case you are (or in case this is helpful to other readers), I've found the easiest method to be 1) create a wallet at Instawallet.org [or Mt Gox or wherever you like]; 2) schedule a deposit at BitInstant.com, using the CVS/Moneygram option, with your instawallet  as the receiving address; 3) go to CVS and confirm over the Moneygram phone, then give your cash to a helpful CVS employee. Finally, send your coin from your instawallet to your SR wallet's address. (A downside of this method is that BitInstant takes a cut, but it's worth it to me to see the BTC appear in my wallet within the hour.)

Use fake name, address, email, phone number, etc. But keep it all written down or memorized, because you'll have to supply it to the MoneyGram people.

I hope that's helpful to somebody!
