Title: Re: Why can I post new topics but can't reply to a topic
Post by: lelmeriodici on April 01, 2013, 05:01 am
.to wouldn't be the problem, and besides, that site is not phishing.  You can use it to get to a Tor Hidden Service (.onion site) without installing the Tor Browser Bundle.  You add .to to the end of any URL.  There's also a service called tor2web that works.

So...

silkroadvb5piz3r.onion becomes silkroadvb5piz3r.onion.to
silkroadvb5piz3r.onion becomes silkroadvb5piz3r.onion.tor2web.org

I have an extra SR account I use just for browsing, and it's that account that I use through these services since I'm paranoid.
