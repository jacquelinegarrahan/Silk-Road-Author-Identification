Here are a few reasons:
- SR charges a fee on every order (5-10% depending on the goods I believe)
- Packaging items takes up time: Time is money
- If packages get seized through postal/customs your out your product
- Time consumed answering PMs, creating item descriptions etc etc.

Its much easier to deal in RL, as you spend a lot less time per deal, but overall the personal risk decrease+extra cash makes up for the time spent.
