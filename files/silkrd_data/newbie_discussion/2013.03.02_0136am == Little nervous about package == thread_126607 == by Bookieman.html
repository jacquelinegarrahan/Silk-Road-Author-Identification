I've never had it take more than 2 days for an express and that was only usually involving weekend. The post office doesn't usually just confiscate things. They ask you to come pick it up and sign for it and things. More than likely he didn't ship it when he claimed he did or something came up and delayed him a day or two. The escrow system works in your favor in that regard even if its a bit slow.
