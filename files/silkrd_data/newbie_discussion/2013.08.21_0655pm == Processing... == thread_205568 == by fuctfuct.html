Post by: unbeknownst on August 22, 2013, 04:39 am
Vendors get backed up with orders and whatever, sometimes it takes a few days to leave "processing", especially over the weekend. While the absolute best vendors will always ship same or next day, taking a couple days for an order to mark "in transit" shouldn't be cause for alarm. Messaging them every six hours just slows them down even more, so just be patient for awhile. And yeah, really great sellers will give you something extra for the inconvenience. I once got an order doubled for a three day delay!
