Title: Re: Bitcoin transfer not showing
Post by: leavesbrown on April 02, 2013, 01:07 pm
Bitcoin transactions have to be processed 6 times by miners before they are transferred. This is to prevent them from being spent multiple times during processing.
If you go to blockchain.info and copy-paste the receiver address (your SR account #) it will show you the progress of the transaction. Once it's verified 6 times they will show up in your SR account.
