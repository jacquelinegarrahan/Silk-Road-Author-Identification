Ketamine is nice for pain relief, but you have to keep re-dosing because it's so short acting.  I used it when I broke my ankle.  Methoxetamine provides pain relief that is very similar to Ketamine but is much longer acting.  Only downside is that MXE is a research chemical so it depends on how you feel about that.  The only reason I mention these over an opiate for pain relief is that they don't cause physical dependence.  Psychologically they can be highly addictive though.  Also, they are extremely cheap relative to opiates on SR.
