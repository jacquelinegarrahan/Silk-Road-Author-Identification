Title: Re: seeking advice - how to find a trustworthy vendor?
Post by: Shades on July 26, 2013, 10:43 pm
Hey there man
I've got a bit of advice since im really familiar with the site and how to judge vedors. Theres a couple ways you can go about checking
A) If you click on the vendors name, it will bring you to their 'vendors' page. If you read the top, it will give a percentage (97 and higher is very good), this means 97% positive feedback. In the same paragraph at the top, it will say this vendor is in the top 'blank' percentage of vendors on the site. This stat isnt as important but worth a look
B) Message the vendor you are currently looking at buying from. Sometimes they'll reply, sometimes not. if there is a response, use your judgment to think if they are legit or not!
C) Check out the forums thread and the feedback on the product your looking at. Sometimes people will write experiences, or simply put 5 out 5 ( good) or less 2 out of 5 ( not recommended)
Hope this helped a bit
