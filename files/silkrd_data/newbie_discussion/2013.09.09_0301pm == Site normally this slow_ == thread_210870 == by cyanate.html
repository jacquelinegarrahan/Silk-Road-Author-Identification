Title: Re: Site normally this slow?
Post by: KinkyJohn on September 10, 2013, 03:54 pm
Been using SR and Tor for about 2 years and it's much better than it used to be, although it does remind me of using the internet 20 years ago. If you can't bear it, sometimes reconnecting helps as you can get stuck with nodes on the network that have poor connectivity or are flakey, and make sure to keep your client up to date. The best solution though is to run your own relay that is on most of the time since it's more secure and gives you a solid connection to the network.

Since that's not feasible for most people it's probably best just to look at it as the price you pay for a much safer way to get at the things you want.
