Post by: GGGreenbud on July 31, 2013, 10:05 pm
 The original Nichols paper on LSZ(SS-2c), is on erowid, but it isn't really very valuable without a frame of reference.  It is a potent 5HT1a agonist, unlike LSD which is a 5HT1b agonist,  there is data
integrated from the original paper compared to other psychs at:
http://www.ncbi.nim.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC2814854/  (clearnet)
there are 42 drugs profiled at that site.  One interesting thing to note, is that LSZ is a potent agonist at
3 or 4 HT receptors, while LSD is potent at 7-9 receptors at equivalent levels, I'll display the
computed values, these are values based upon order of intensity, with 4 being the highest. 
each drop in 1 number(ie. 4->3->2->1->) is at least a 10-fold decrease, because it is based upon
a log graph of intensity at each receptor.
SS-2c: 4.00 5ht1a, 3.22 5ht1b, 2.82 D3, 2.45 5ht7, 2.44 5ht6, 2.32 5ht2a, 2.17 5ht5a, 2.17 5ht2b, 2.03 5ht2c, 1.74 D2, 1.72 Beta1, 1.62 D4, 1.16 5ht1e, 1.14 D1, 1.00 D5, 0.67 Alpha1A, 0.57 Beta2, 0.20 H1
compare this to the original:
LSD: 4.00 5ht1b, 3.77 5ht7, 3.75 5ht6, 3.73 5ht1a, 3.70 5ht1d, 3.64 5ht5a, 3.54 5ht2a, 3.16 D3, 3.11 5ht2b, 3.11 5ht2c, 2.93 Alpha2A, 2.62 5ht1e, 2.55 D2, 2.39 D4, 2.34 D1, 2.05 D5, 1.54 Alpha1A, 1.40 H1, 1.39 Beta1, 1.05 Beta2, 0.65
and Good 'ol Shrooms!
Psilocin: 4.00 5ht2b, 3.40 5ht1d, 3.37 D1, 3.03 5ht1e, 2.88 5ht1a, 2.83 5ht5a, 2.82 5ht7, 2.82 5ht6, 2.67 D3, 2.52 5ht2c, 2.19 5ht1b, 2.14 5ht2a, 1.77 Imidazoline1, 1.74 SERT, 1.57 Alpha2B, 1.36 Alpha2A, 1.03 Alpha2C
  You can see here that it is in some respects similar to both, yet different.
I have tried the compound at 75ug, it is definitely active, but an associate of mine took a larger
dose with an SSRI and had problems with nausea and vomiting.  The same subject had no adverse response to AL-LAD or LSD.  I suspect that the decreased breadth of activity makes this compound not as easily tolerated as LSD, although it does seem very promising.  I had good visuals and low body load at 75ug, but I have not seen very many reports, only 3 or 4 with low dosage and one with high dosage.

Post by: JohnBishop on July 31, 2013, 10:45 pm
LSD, Mescaline, Psilocybin

These three are all you need IMO everything else is not as good for one reason or another. Between these three there is an amazing range of length, intensity, and "type" or "feel" to the trip. The problem is, they are all illegal.

Safest one to procure IMO would be Mescaline which you can order online from Peru. You can order the dried green cactus chips from a Peruvian Torch or San Pedro cactus. You can easily grind this, mix some water to make nasty little dough-like cactus balls, and eat them with gatorade chaser. Or, if you are so inclined, you can do a fairly simple A-B extraction to get all the way or at least part of the way to pure Mescaline. All of this info is readily available.

The newest RC that looks promising to me is 6-APB but the prices are too high and/or I can't find a reliable source. Technically I believe its still legal in the U.S.A. at least.

All of this is strictly my opinion and understand that my bias towards psychedilics is of a spiritual nature and less of a party-nature.

In regards to OP: LSZ doesn't seem that great don't waste your time on it  :P
