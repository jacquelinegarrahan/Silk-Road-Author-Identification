The best deals come from vendors who take measures to not get fucked over.

Post by: redalloverthelandguyhere on July 23, 2013, 12:14 pm
I think vendors are more interested in how much you have spent rather that number of transactions.

Lets say you bought 50 ebooks for 0.01 btc. That would come up as 50 transactions, 0% auto finalize and 0% refund. On the surface it looks like a good customer, but vendors can see your stats including the amount spent.

I've always thought vendors ought to put up a minimum spends before you have escrow. Putting up a good number of transactions might be a way for scammers to hit and run.

And I agree with the vendor of two weeks not yet being 'trusted'. Until a vendor sends out 30+ listing to established members, I'd not buy anything myself. Its too easy for new vendors to set up dummy buyer accounts, praise their own product and then hit customers with FE demands.

I would research any vendor by simply looking at the vendor page (recent feedback) and look at the forums for good reviews from established members who basically tell the truth over quality and so on.

One other point....

I notice that my spend stats have now just gone to a year. Spends before that are not there. Maybe this is an SR security thing and I guess the last years spends are good enough. I can only think its a security measure as some have been here almost two years I guess and if any LEA set up a seller accouint and tried to lure people in with free samples they would not see all the spends. Not that any resellers would go for free samples I guess.

Good luck regardless.
