I suggest using tails. Google tails and and follow the directions to download the image and burn it to a dvd. Tails directs all internet connections through tor and erases all traces of everything you've done when you shut down. For more info search it on the forums. Tons of info here.

As far as vendors stealth. Read the discussions and feedback on their page. You can also read reviews on the forums here.

Escrow is built into silk road itself. When you make a purchase your funds go into escrow, essentially a middlepoint where your funds are held incase there is a problem. You dont have to do anything special to use it.

I dont use twitter or reddit. These forums are your best source for news. And as far as logging into twitter using tor, please dont do that. Or facebook. Dont log onto ANY social media using tor. Esp if you have another tab open buying drugs.
