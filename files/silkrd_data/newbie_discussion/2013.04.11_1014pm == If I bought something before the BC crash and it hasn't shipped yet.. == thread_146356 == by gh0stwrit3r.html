It depends on the seller. Many sellers will cancel sales that have not been shipped as they could stand to lose a fair amount. I personally am a small time seller, and have shipped all orders that came if before/during/after the crash. In fact i have to orders on me now that i will be shipping this morning in the expectation that they reach the buyer by Monday.
