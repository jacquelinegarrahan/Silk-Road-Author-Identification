Title: Re: HOW DO I ORDER?
Post by: galileosophia on August 21, 2013, 12:32 am
First you need to transfer some bitcoins to your account.  If you click on your account link you'll see a bitcoin address for you to transfer to.

Once that's done and you have BTC greater than or equal to the cost of what you want to order, you can add that product to your shopping cart.  Then click on your shopping cart and the final step will be self explanatory. 

Make sure to re-read the Buyer's Guide found on the right hand side when you log-in.
