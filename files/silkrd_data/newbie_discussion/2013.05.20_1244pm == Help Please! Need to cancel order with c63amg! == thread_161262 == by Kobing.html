Title: Re: Help Please! Need to cancel order with c63amg!
Post by: nightelf on May 20, 2013, 02:34 pm
The vendor has 4 days to mark the item "in transit". The vendor can also "cancel" the order during those 4 days. The buyer can cancel after 4 days as long as the seller has not marked the item "in transit" which means "shipped".
So, if the item is not marked "in transit" after 4 days then you can cancel the order. The seller has good feedback and reputation, chances are high that he ran out of product after your order was placed.
Good luck
