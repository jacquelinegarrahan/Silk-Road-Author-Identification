Title: NorthWestDirect Review - alternate newbie thread
Post by: norseman17 on January 17, 2013, 06:23 pm
NWD has been great to me in the past offering a sampling of four different strains, all dank and packaged nicely, and I left a review on their NWD official review thread.  However, now since I'm a newbie technically I cannot post in the general forums anymore until I become a junior member.. soo for any Newbies who have bought from or who are wondering about NorthWestDirect hopefully this thread helps.  Here's my review of NWD!

Strain: BlueBerry

Shipping/Packaging:  Item was shipped Saturday and received across the country on Thursday with excellent stealth.  there was padding in the package and multiple layers of color barriers and finally the bud was vacuum and heat sealed. 

Looks/Smell:  the Blueberry came in one nice size nug just over .5 g that was darker green in color with some nice tricome covered red hairs.  The bud was sticky when broken apart and a very sweet berry-y aroma that was deliciously enticing. 

Smoke/Taste:  This blueberry smokes really smoothly with an amazingly sweet aftertaste after the exhale.  I could easily smoke this bud all day long and never get tired of the classic blueberry taste!

High:  The high was nice and stoney and took affect almost immediately after smoking a morning bong rip.  A+ buds for sure every time with NWD for good prices!
