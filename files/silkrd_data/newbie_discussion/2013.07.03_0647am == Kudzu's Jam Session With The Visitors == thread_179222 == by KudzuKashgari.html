Title: Kudzu's Jam Session With The Visitors
Post by: KudzuKashgari on July 03, 2013, 06:47 am
Part 1:  Independence Day

It was July 4th, 2012.  I had smoked a big cigar to celebrate the evening, and retired to my futon on the porch.  I lie down, restless, and try to get some shut eye.  As I begin to drift to that thin line between sleep and wakefulness, I start to feel strange.  It was like the sensation you get when you have a strong emotional response to music, but without any sound: it stimulated me directly, in my nervous system.  Shocked and slightly perturbed, I sit up and open my eyes.  I look over to the dog sleeping on its bed and am surprised to see that it is sleeping soundly.  Relieved that the dog isn't scared, I decide that there probably isn't a reason for me to be either.  So I lie back down and close my eyes again.  The sensation comes back stronger.

Part 2:  Them

The sensation was like my nervous system was an instrument of immense complexity, and there was something playing it.  The feeling causes me to black out, and what I recall next could not be remembered until after I woke up the next day, as though it were blocked externally or internally.  I come to my senses for about 2.5 seconds in a very dark place.  In front of me just out of arms' length are three beings who stand at about 1 - 1.25 meters tall.  Their skin has the texture of gun metal, but with the color of titanium.  Their heads are over sized in proportion to their bodies, and their eyes are large and almond shaped.  Behind them is a black dome which is about one meter in diameter, and one meter tall.  I hypothesize that this has some important function in the space that I find myself in.  Though standing, I feel completely paralyzed, but I begin to regain my senses, though still hypnotized.

Part 3:  The Light Show

Suddenly, the being farthest to the left starts to emit a glow from where the pineal gland would be in a human.  The light was green, and it looked like molten glass.  It became extremely bright, and I could feel a similar sensation as when I was falling asleep.  Then the being in the middle did the same thing, but with a yellow color, and a different sensation that layered on top of the first one's frequency.  The being farthest to the left did the same, but with a red color.  As soon as all of their 'lights' were on, a thread like molten glass being blown was emitted from the green being, then the yellow being, and then the red being.  As soon as they all touched my forehead, I blacked out and woke up the next morning none the wiser.  Someone I know happened to ask me what my favorite colors are when I made my way to the kitchen.  " Red and yellow," I told them.  Knowing that red, yellow, and green are my favorites, I wondered why I would leave one out.  This triggered the whole memory back, and I had to go sit down to make sense of it.  I have had several other notable experiences with these 'Traveling Musicians,' and am very open to chat with anyone who replies to this with an interest in the subject.
