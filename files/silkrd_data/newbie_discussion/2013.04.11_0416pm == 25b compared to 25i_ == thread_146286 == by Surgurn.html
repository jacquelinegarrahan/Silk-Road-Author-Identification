Title: Re: 25b compared to 25i?
Post by: l1llykins on April 11, 2013, 05:19 pm
25b has less body load, not as speedy as 25i, and has a nice mixture of visual and audio distortion. It's more potent so start with 2mg. Come-up is only 40 minutes so you can take another tab around the 30 minute mark if it's not as strong as you were hoping.

I find it very euphoric, a good deal of music appreciation and empathy, better visuals than with 25i (though I never took anywhere near your doses, lol).

It's one of my favorites next to 25d.
