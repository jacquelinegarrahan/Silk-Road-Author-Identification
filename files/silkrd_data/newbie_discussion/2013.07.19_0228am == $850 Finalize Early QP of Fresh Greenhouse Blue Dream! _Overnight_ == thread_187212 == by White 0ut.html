Don't FE unless you can afford to lose the BTC's. It's that simple.

It's too bad "finalize early" is named the way it is. It's hard to grasp as a newbie. What it reallly amounts to is "payment before delivery."  That means you absolutely have to trust the vendor is truly going to deliver the goods.

Would you buy an ounce from your street dealer, by putting the cash in his mailbox, and trusting him to deliver to your door later in the day? Maybe you would, because you see that guy day in and day out. But an SR vendor is invisible, untouchable not just by the law but by his buyers. That's a critical difference. Hence the mantra:

DO NOT FE
DO NOT FE
DO NOT FE UNDER ANY CIRCUMSTANCES, unless you love to gamble.

For any newbies who haven't heard about it yet, the most infamous scammer on the Road was Tony76. Search and see how an uber-trusted, well-established vendor ran a "special", which required FE. He gathered a *lot* of money, then ... disappeared!

Well, we've no idea if he disappeared. The nature of this beast is that it's trivial for a vendor to reincarnate as Tony77.  :)
