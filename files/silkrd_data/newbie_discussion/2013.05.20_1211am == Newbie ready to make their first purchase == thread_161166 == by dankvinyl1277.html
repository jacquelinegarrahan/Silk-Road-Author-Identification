Title: Re: Newbie ready to make their first purchase
Post by: quixotist on May 20, 2013, 01:28 am
Regarding finalizing early, it depends on what you're buying and the type of person who buys that thing. If you're buying a small bag of weed or some MDMA then you're unlikely to be asked to finalize early because most newbies buying weed or MDMA are real honest newbies. In this case, if you're being asked to finalize early then the vendor is likely scamming you.

However, if, as a newbie, you're buying in bulk or buying opiates or cocaine then you're more likely to be an old scammer using a new account to get their goods cheaper by causing trouble for the vendor. In this case you can see why some people outright refuse to send to newbies until they've received full payment.

I've finalized early in the past but only with trusted dealers who do top notch product and aren't known to be selective scammers, and only for small amounts.

With FE you pays your money and takes your chances, never FE more than you mind losing and if you must do it, do it intelligently after a lot of research.
