Title: Re: Renting a mail box? (Ireland)
Post by: ethic on April 22, 2013, 04:48 pm
I use a PO box. I've been researching on how they would do a controlled delivery at a post office. Basically, if your package does get intercepted, they'll usually put a note in your PO box asking to pick up your package at the front desk, which does require a signature. If this happens, disregard your package, it's lost. Whatever you do, don't sign anything and don't open your package until you're in a safe place.

Post by: nontaken53 on April 24, 2013, 06:08 pm
I'm not an expert, and only human. So don't blindly trust me :)
My 2 cents:

1. I've read several times that packages or big fat letters sent from a foreign country to a p.o. box raise a red flag. So it's actually riskier than sending it to a normal private street adress.
Just read those threads about mail security in the security forum.

2. If BIG orders are your problem, there's an obvious solution: several smaller shipments. I'm sure you don't need to smoke 500g of weed all at the same day, do you?
So how about 1-2 small letters per week instead of 1 fat package every other month?

3. Maybe you know someone you can totally trust, and who is not involved in any illegal stuff (especially drugs), that lets you use his adress?

4. Maybe there is an abandoned (but not totally wrecked) house near you? Read: dead mailbox. Put a name on the doorbell and mailbox, and have it sent there, secretly pick up the mail at night etc. Details: security forum + google.

5. As you seem to either smoke a lot or sell a lot, have you ever looked into growing yourself? It's MUCH easier than you probably think. And for a small growbox/-tent the ROI would be reached after about 2 harvests. Hell, if you wouldn't need much, you could even grow in a PC bigtower (youtube -> pc grow) right under the noses of your parents if you filter the exhaust properly ;-) Also, growing can be a very nice rewarding hobby and fun! I now understand why some people love gardening. Sitting right next to my plants on the balcony, seeing them, watching them grow fatter, touching them and smelling them is awesome! Not sure how much sunlight an irish summer provides. In germany it's hardly enough to grow automatic strains, but it works somewhat (=you get typical outdoor weed of medium potency and not the highest yield).

5.b Maybe you could persuade someone else to start growing? Or he/she provides only the location while you take care of the plants every 2-3 days?

6. How hard/possible would it be for you to actually find a local dealer/friendofafriendofafriend to get rid of the mail problem?

7. Ever thought of buying hash, skuff, oil, budder, honey, extracts etc. instead of weed? Packages would be a LOT smaller.

8. Have it shipped to your workplace  :D *jk*


Also, to help you better: what exactly is your fear regarding shipments to your parents? That your parents find out you're a stoner? That your parents get in trouble with the police? That you get in trouble with the police? Anything else? Do you know a fellow stoner IRL? Maybe he could receive the packages?
