Title: Re: Buyers! What do you want to see from a Vendor?
Post by: jlranier on July 08, 2013, 03:02 pm
I'm a new buyer and I have to say the sheer volume of great offerings on here is almost overwhelming. What I've been looking for though is good reviews of stealth (Need to use proper equipment, mylar/mbb bags etc. and avoid contaminants) and quality combined with price. I know that seems obvious but I am surprised at what seems like a lack of truly competitive pricing on the road. Some things are cheap as dirt and others seem high. I'd wager that if you could vend somewhere in the middle so that you had competitive prices but were beating the markups you'd quickly earn a good reputation and alot of business. Quantity is another thing I look for, because I can see myself sometimes wanting to splurge but most of the time I just want something in enough of a quantity to last me a few times and then move on. If you could offer a wide selection of quantities I'm sure you'd win some fans. Other than that, I just look for fast response times if possible and PGP or some type of encryption for sure.
