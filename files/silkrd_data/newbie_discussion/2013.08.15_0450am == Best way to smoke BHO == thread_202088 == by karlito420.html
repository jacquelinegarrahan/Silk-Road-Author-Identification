Title: Re: Best way to smoke BHO
Post by: farmer1 on August 15, 2013, 05:06 am
You want a fishbowl and titanium nail. These will fit on a bong but you must take into consideration the bong's design and the diameter of the stem. Adapters are available for almost any configuration. Also get a butane torch and a stainless steel dental pick.

If you are going bare budget and don't mind a little waste then two butter knives, a stove, and a bong will do the trick.
