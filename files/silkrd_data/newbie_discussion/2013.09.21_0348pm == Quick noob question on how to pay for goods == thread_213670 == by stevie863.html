Title: Re: Quick noob question on how to pay for goods
Post by: beezerbuz on September 21, 2013, 04:01 pm
I believe there is a read me somewhere on the main page of the SR, but the quick answer is that you transfer it to your SR wallet that is created with your account.  Then you order, it then goes into escrow, then finalize.  There are more details in between these steps so make sure you read up.
Title: Re: Quick noob question on how to pay for goods
Post by: Yoda on September 21, 2013, 04:02 pm
On your SR page, under account, you'll have a btc wallet address... you must send your coin there.  Once you order something, the funds will automatically be taken out and put into escrow for you.

But more importantly... is your VirWox account associated with your real name/bank account in any way?  If so, you should run your coin through a tumbler/mixer to be safe.
