Your BTC address should still be in your SR Wallet. Go to www.blockchain.info (clearnet) and type the BTC address the coins were going to into the search box and hit ENTER. That will tell you information such as how many confirmations you've had, if your coins have been transferred into your BTC address, etc. If you can't find a transaction relating to the BTC address you've used, then no coins were sent by the Bitcoin provider. If it does show up on blockchain.info, contact SR Support as there may be an issue on SR's side.
