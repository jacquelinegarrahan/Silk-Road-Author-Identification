Post by: PsychedelicSphere on May 13, 2013, 03:33 am
My opinion:

Psychedelics are DEFINITELY not for everyone.

Are you mentally stable? Any psychiatric conditions in your family? This can be bad for a would be LSD user.

I cannot stress enough, do not fight the drug. Do not try to control it. No matter how deep you go, no matter how long you swear you were sitting there for, you are under the influence of a drug and it will eventually pass through your system and things will return to "normal."

I say "normal" because, depending on you as a person and how you think, you might not look at the world the same. I'm not saying you will come out a completely different person, but it will definitely give you new perspectives and make you think about things different.

Remember to keep an open mind, and avoid any situations that could turn bad... Trust me the smallest thing in the world can be scary on acid.

For your first time I would recommend doing it somewhere you are most comfortable... If you don't live with your parents I suggest for a first trip there is nothing more relaxing than staying home.

My favorite thing to do is....
I set up all my music before I start tripping. I put music on inside, and on outside in my backyard(where I spend most of my day on LSD)
Make sure you make a playlist of all your favorite songs, if you would like some suggestions I would be glad to give you some.

If you are really into electronic music and dubstep, and are tripping at night, I recommended glow sticks, and lots of them hahaha.

Some people say looking in a mirror is scary, but personally I like it.

I could write for days about LSD, but if you have any specific questions just ask.


What are your plans for when you trip?
