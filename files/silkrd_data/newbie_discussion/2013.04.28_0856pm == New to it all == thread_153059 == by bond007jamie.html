Bitcoins are a virtual currency. You pay someone real cash, and they sell you bitcoins.
The coins are stored in a "wallet" online, you then transfer the coins into your Silk Road wallet.

The rates changes constantly. Minute by minute.

How to buy coins depends on where in the world you are, to some extent.

I'm in the UK so I can only talk of UK solutions.
I use bitbargain .co .uk with Barclay's Pingit. Quick and easy.

My advice is to find a seller/method that you understand, and make a test purchase. To make sure you understand the process.

It can appear complicated to begin with, but the best way to learn is to just give it a bash.
