Title: Re: What is a typical response time when messaging a vendor?
Post by: Anasterion on February 27, 2013, 11:40 pm
I'm not 100%, because I've only made about a dozen purchases thus far, but I've gotten responses from every vendor I've purchased from within two days. I don't recommend you purchase from someone who won't message you in a couple of days, because if they won't message you back promptly before buy from them, they likely won't be different after you have already placed an order and are waiting for your package to arrive. Also, from what I've read, good reviews mean very little, without feedback from the community, because a person could just create dummy accounts to give themselves good reviews, if they had the time and funds available to do so. There have been a few scammers that have been exposed and banned from what I've read, for doing this. I'm not saying the guy you are messaging is a scammer, he could just be going through some personal stuff, or is having an off couple of days. That being said, its just good practice to be safe, to only purchase from vendors that don't require FE, and to find vendors that have been vouched for by longstanding forum members.

Just my shiny two.... Hope it helps. :-)

Anasterion
