Title: Re: newbie questions
Post by: hogfan on May 09, 2013, 08:50 pm
These are both great questions. I may know a little about what you are saying emotionally.

MDMA or ecstasy are both good, as well as mushrooms. Being inexperienced with drugs just means you need to test the waters before going all in. Start with a small amount and concentrate on what you are feeling. I don't recommend doing them alone, it's nice to be able to share them with someone whom you trust and are close to. It doesn't have to be sexual or freaky, can just be a buddy that you are close to and can talk about what the drugs are making you feel like. Makes for good bonding with someone.

My only recommendation is you use a name that is actually associated with the address. The flag is using a name that is not a resident, the post office picks up on that almost immediately. That doesn't mean it still won't make it to your address, but they may notice the 5th or 6th different name at an address and get suspicious.

Hope this information helps you on your journey, my friend.

MDMA/ecstasy could definitely help your emotions come out, if only for a few hours. I second the recommendation to have people around while you trip, both for safety purposes in case you do happen to have an adverse effect, and to truly experience the extreme love/empathy towards others that ecstasy tends to bring out, even towards people that you don't really care for.

Personally, LSD makes me see pretty colors and makes me really talkative but doesn't really affect my emotions or make me introspective. I've only taken weak doses, though. Read up on some experience reports on erowid.com if you haven't already to get a feel for what reactions people typically have.

Have you considered DMT? There's a movie called DMT: The Spirit Molecule that you could try watching to see if the drug could be useful for you. It's a powerful hallucinogenic that many people say has changed their lives for the better.
