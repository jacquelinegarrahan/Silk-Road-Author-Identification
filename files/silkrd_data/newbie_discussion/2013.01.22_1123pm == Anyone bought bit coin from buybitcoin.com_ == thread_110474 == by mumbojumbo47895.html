Title: Re: Anyone bought bit coin from buybitcoin.com?
Post by: ilovebeees on January 23, 2013, 02:25 am
I haven't used buybitcoin.com but from what I've read I wouldn't use it. Quite a few claims of people getting scammed by them. Like glasshouse said, you can buy bitcoins from a reputable vendor on SR like Sugar Mama. That method is pretty simple, just purchase a MoneyPak from a store with cash, add a listing of bitcoins for moneypak, and put your moneypak number in your address section when checking out. May take a day or two to get your coins depending on how busy the vendor is (they can only process a limited dollar amount per day with moneypaks).

I prefer to do cash deposit at a bank branch, fees are generally lower than other methods and I get my coins within an hour or two. Lately I've been using bitcopia.com which lets me do up to $1K per day.

I certainly don't know every vendor and good business that sells bitcoins, I would recommend searching the vendor or business here on the forums and checking their feedback before sending anyone your money.
