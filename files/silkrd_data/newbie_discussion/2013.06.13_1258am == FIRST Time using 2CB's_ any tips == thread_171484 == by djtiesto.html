Title: Re: FIRST Time using 2CB's? any tips
Post by: ScienceFTW on June 13, 2013, 03:10 am
AliceinHolland's 2C-B pills reagent test as 2C-b/c, so they're most likely 2C-B, indeed.
2C-B isn't like MDMA at all. Euphoria, but much less loving. Far greater enhancement of sense of smell, and a huge visual component - colors are amazing. Trees, bright signs, etc. Take Shulgin's advice and have somebody give you a car ride. Unlike MDMA, it doesn't interfere with sexual ability at all. Definitely some visual distortions and warping.

I took 13mg at 200+lb for a first go. Pretty fucking amazing. I'd start with 13mg, regardless. Look at erowid's dosage guide. Don't start high, 1/2 or 1 pill and don't take any more regardless of how long your comeup is. You can always try a higher dose in a week.
