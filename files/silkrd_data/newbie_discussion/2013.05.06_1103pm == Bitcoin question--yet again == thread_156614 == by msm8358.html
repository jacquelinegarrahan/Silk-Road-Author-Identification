Title: Re: Bitcoin question--yet again
Post by: nuclearDelta on May 06, 2013, 11:22 pm
Look up some of the bitcoin guides on youtube, they can't fail you. The app is essentially a wallet that holds coins that are sent to you. coins are sent via an address that is unique to you (kind of like an account number). An essential thing you need to know are that once you have the app the address is unique to that app, so loosing it, deleting it, ect. will make you a poor sad person who has lost all of their cash. There are alternative online BTC wallets that you can use that help to prevent this but even then you are still subject to phishing and other scamming attempts.

tl;dr
Wallet holds coins and has an address that is unique for receiving coins (Like a bank account number, except you are the bank)
