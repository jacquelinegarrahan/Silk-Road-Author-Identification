They can only see what you see under your purchasing stats. Total transactions, total spent, refund rate, auto-finalize rate, and length of membership.
Title: Re: What can vendor's see on your profile?
Post by: nanpa2001 on April 14, 2013, 12:25 pm
They can see exactly the same thing as you when you look at your purchasing stats. In addition, they can only see these stats when you make an order from them. After the order is confirmed, they are no longer able to see your stats (unless you then go into resolution).
