Title: Re: Anonymous way of operating and buying btc
Post by: awhiteknight on January 20, 2013, 02:59 am
Bitcoins aren't as anonymous as you think. The entire ledger is in the public and a lot of companies who deal with bitcoins have to keep records for legislative reasons (MtGox certainly do). You should really use #bitcoin-otc and deal with individuals rather than companies with anti-money laundering policies, never mention bitcoin and use the same vendor as often as possible. Use a "real" Bitcoin client and when you're sending make sure you do it through Tor so it's never tied to your IP address.

If you were heavily into tinfoil hat fashion then you'd get your Bitcoins by posting used bills and a bitcoin address to some post office box. I've not found anyone running such a service yet though, if I did then I'd be using them because it's what everyone should be using.
