There is currently no way of leaving feedback for buyers, but an auto-finalisation will affect their pruchasing statistics and show up there when they try to purchase something from any vendor in future.

Excellent, glad to hear it!

Regarding your situation, I would advise not refunding money unless it is a request that has been asked in the resolution center. If you believe the buyer did indeed receive the product, and have proof of this, provide it to the administrator presiding over the resolution.

You can read more about how resolutions are decided here:
http://dkn255hz262ypmii.onion/wiki/index.php?title=Frequently_Asked_Questions#How_are_resolutions_decided

However, if it is simply a case of the buyer messaging you AFTER the resolution process has already taken place, I would strongly advise telling them that the issue has already been resolved in the resolution center.

If you get a reputation as being soft on people who are trying to scam you then you will become an easy target for others. I'm not saying that that's what is happening here, but it has to mentioned.

Buyers can edit their feedback at any time within a 3 month period from the date they finalised the order. After this, the ability to remove feedback is gone as the item is removed from their 'view feedback' list.

Regarding how a 1/5 may affect your feedback score, you can read it here:
http://dkn255hz262ypmii.onion/wiki/index.php?title=Feedback_Score

Libertas
