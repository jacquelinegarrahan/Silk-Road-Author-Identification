Title: Re: depositing with credit card
Post by: MilesStandoffish on February 08, 2013, 09:48 am
From what I've observed, yeah, you're not going to get any transfers from your bank card, or CC, or PayPal or other conventional credit-or-debit-based means. The simplest way is basically to sign up with a bitcoin purchasing service (Blockchain and Mt.Gox are good for exchanging currencies but buying them there isn't really something I've been able to see done so far?), and then transfer from there to another wallet or to SR, and just provide a link to a bank account from which to draw funds.

It seems a fair amount of people have gotten some use out of Coinbase, a company in SF that handles BTC purchases from USD. Granted, it seems to take about a week for the requested transfer to go through, but that could just be a routing/security/banking issue. (I really need to read up on their FAQ again.)
