it's just supply and demand. you may think you have plenty of product but at the end of the day you can only send so much of it out. this may seem like it's not a limiting factor, but if you were to try to sell adderall on here for what you may find it on the streets for you will soon be so inundated with small orders that you'd have to either seek out help (a risk for you),  raise your prices, or sell in larger quantities.
