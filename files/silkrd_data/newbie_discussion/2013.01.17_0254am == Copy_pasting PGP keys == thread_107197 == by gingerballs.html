Post by: motek on January 17, 2013, 04:16 am
an easy way to practice gpg, is,  first open a page with wordpad

then  open your GPA clipboard ...in this you should have inpoted both your public and private keys  (to find the clipboard using windows, just type it into the search box of the start menu)

write something on your worpad  and copy and paste it onto the GPA clipboard, then click 'encrypt'...it will ask you with what key, click on your own, and you will see the message encrypted, which looks just like a 'key' on the clipboard.

copy and paste that message to the wordpad,  and  clear the clipboard.

Now copy and paste the encrypted message back onto the clipboard and click 'decrypt'  (this is the same as if you had just received an encrypted message and needed to read it, copy and paste it ontp the GPA clipboard) . . . it will ask you which key to use,  so click on your own, 

And presto  you should be reading cleartext  ;D   It's that easy

To encrypt a message to a vendor,  copy and paste their public key into a folder on your computer, then with GPA when you click import, it will ask where from and offer you the options, so you click on say 'documents' if thats where you filed it, open the document and click 'import'

A pop up will tell you you have successfully imported a key....cool  8)

Then,   write in clear text on the clipboard whatever you want to say, and make sure to add your own PUBLIC KEY to the end of the page, so they can send you encrypted messages!                  ( andONLY your PUBLIC KEY! folks have accidentally sent BOTH public and private!  :o)
 
then click "encrypt" and it will ask you which key you wish to use, click on the vendors key and press 'encrypt'   Voila!  You will see an encrypted message you now copy'n paste onto a SR message and send it  ;D

And wait for a reply      I hope this helps someone...sheesh I might as well use my 'spam' for good and the fight against evil  ::)
