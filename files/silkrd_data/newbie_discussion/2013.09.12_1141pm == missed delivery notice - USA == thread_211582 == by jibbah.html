I'd say go collect it - it's not like the delivery guy asking you to sign, it sounds (to me) like just standard post office procedure. It would be a shame to have your goods sat in some PO depot going stale, wouldn't it?

On the other hand, I can't speak from a specifically drug/sr perspective, so perhaps wait until somebody who HAS experienced this replies.
