Title: The most important lesson a SR noob can learn
Post by: El Duderino on April 09, 2013, 10:20 pm
Long time listener, first time caller, not yet @50. To all noobies (with, or without boobies) live by these two simple rules & your journey on The Road will be as safe as it possibly can be.

1) Always use PGP encrypted messaging to send your address

2) Don't Finalize Early (release funds from escrow at the request of the vendor before you have received your product - many require this as a condition of sale before they will actually ship).

Pretty much everything can be sourced from a vendor that will not have Finalizing Early as part of their policy, even if it costs say 20% more that's less than the potential cost of getting ripped off. The most reputable ones generally actually forbid their customers to FE & it's there for everyone's protection.

Imagine you were buying stuff in real-life & a trusted dealer you've used for months said "hey, this time can you give me the money now & come back tomorrow to collect the gear"? That's what FE is on SR.

Not everyone who asks you to FE is a scammer, but every scammer will ask you to FE.
