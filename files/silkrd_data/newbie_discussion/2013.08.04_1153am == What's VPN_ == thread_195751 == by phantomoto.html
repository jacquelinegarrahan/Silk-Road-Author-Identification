Title: Re: What's VPN?
Post by: DaveDoe on August 04, 2013, 12:06 pm
Virtual Private Network

Its software that encrypts all of your outgoing traffic and sends it to a private network, it is then decrypted and sent out as normal traffic but from the VPNs network. Incoming traffic gets sent back to the VPN and routed to you, hopefully without revealing your ip or anything like that. The advantage of a VPN is if your traffic is being watched thenall they can see is a strongly encrypted stream of data to the VPN.
