Title: Like weed and X? Try shrooms.
Post by: BenjaminFranklin on February 02, 2013, 08:43 pm
For those of you that like the effects of MDMA or simple pot and have never tried shrooms now is the time to get into the game. The most pleasant mind-opening experience you can't imagine. Feeling like you've got a lot of personal questions to sort out in your head or want to explore the origins of life? Go for a trip. I would recommend Loopyslo, good first order from him with quality weight, product, and OK packaging.

Start out with 2 grams. Don't eat for three hours prior to taking the mushrooms to avoid nausea.  Chop it up finely into a cup of orange juice. Drink it all down, don't take your time. Sit back and wait for the fun. *Sometimes you've got to wait a couple hours. You're body isn't exactly the same each time. So whatever you do, don't get bored and then leave the house thinking it's over, it will come.

Once you've gone through the two grams step it up to at least 3.5, I like 4. You'll get a "total melt" and will find a massive creativity you never knew you had that coincides with an amazing body high.

Cheers.
