Title: Re: Deposit not working? Help :)
Post by: John The King on August 21, 2013, 03:51 am
When I transfer BTC from the place I purchase through to SR I check [CLEARNET] http://blockchain.info by searching for my wallet address. Here you can see your transfer pretty much instantaneously. By looking at the details of your transfer, you can see the amount of confirmations on the transfer. In my experience the BTC doesn't show up in my SR wallet until I have 7 confirmations. If you don't have any confirmations, blockchain will give you an estimate on how long it will take for the first confirmation. It's not always accurate, but if you're really impatient you can refresh the page every few minutes.

Typically for me, once I get the first confirmation it doesn't take much longer to get the confirmations needed to show up in my SR wallet.

You can read up on it in the SR wiki here: http://dkn255hz262ypmii.onion/wiki/index.php?title=Frequently_Asked_Questions#Why_isn.27t_my_deposit_showing_up_in_my_account.3F
