Post by: tbart on July 27, 2013, 02:10 am
you might check with your neighbors and even ask the carrier - if you can't be there, leave a note in your mailbox explaining your pkg is missing  (and a printout showing the package shows delivered).

not sure what's going on with the PO, it may be budget constraints from sequester, or whatever, but we've had the same carrier here for 11-12 years - super lady, and always gets our mail right. Up until about 6-8 months ago - all of a sudden we'd find a lot of mail in our box that was meant for our neighbor - we'd go over to their box to put their mail in it and find some of our in their box. I noticed a few weeks back she seems to be hauling ass, when delivering, so it may be she's been given more territory for her route and has to cover more ground in the same work shift

if your carrier delivered it to the wrong house, odds are 50/50 he'll remember your pkg and which mail box he put it in - from what our carrier explained to us one time, she sorts the mail for her route before leaving the post office, putting it in cardboard trays set up by the way she drives her route

fwiw
