About MDMA, there is a lot of information on MDMA available. Check out erowid (clearnet warning) http://www.erowid.org/chemicals/mdma/mdma.shtml

How much MDMA you need for a good roll depends on your body weight and gender (and on tolerance). 1.5mg to 2mg per kilogram of body weight is a good rule of thumb. So, if you are a 75 kg male, 1 gram should give you at least 6 rolls.

This is not to say it would last you a week! If you roll several nights in a row, you will quickly be depleting your serotonin and it will be a waste of the MDMA. To really enjoy it, try to have at least 1 month in between rolls.
