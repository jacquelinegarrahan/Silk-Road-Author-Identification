The point is: it doesn't matter how small or even legal an individual order is, it NEVER makes sense to transmit personally identifying information unsecurely.  Why? Because IF the SR servers were to be accessed by LE, say, then one single instance of an unencrypted address is enough to link your identity to your SR account ... and from there to all the other illegal things you do here.  Thus, what nawlins is suggesting defeats the WHOLE POINT of ever encrypting anything.
