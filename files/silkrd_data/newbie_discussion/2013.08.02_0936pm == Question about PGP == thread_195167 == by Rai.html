Title: Re: Question about PGP
Post by: Tiger on August 02, 2013, 09:45 pm
You can't encrypt your address until you've picked your vendor and imported their public key. There's no point doing this so far in the future in case they change their key.

It's asking for your password because you're decrypting the message, using your key, when you encrypt a message with a vendors public key the vendor will have to put their password in to decrypt the message.
