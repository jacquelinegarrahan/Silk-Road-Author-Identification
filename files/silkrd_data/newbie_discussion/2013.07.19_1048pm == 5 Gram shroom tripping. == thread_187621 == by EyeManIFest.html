Title: Re: 5 Gram shroom tripping.
Post by: Ryi27HfQ on July 19, 2013, 11:18 pm
I did 4.5 or 4.75 ish once.  It was intense but not life changing.  The colors were intense, and I could not grasp a thought to save my life.  My mind just started running at a million miles per hour, but each thought had my complete focus for the moment I had it.  Then, that thought would be gone and I couldn't reach back in my memory to try to structure together some sort of chain of thought.  Just a string of intense thoughts, one after another, for hours. 

It was a hot day, and I couldn't tell if I was feeling short of breath and sweaty because I was hot or because I was dying.  I spent what felt like a long time grappling with the genuine thought that I might actually be dying.  I just couldn't tell if something was actually wrong, or if it was the drugs.  Finally I came to terms with things and decided if I was going to die, it was a good day to die and a nice place to do it.
