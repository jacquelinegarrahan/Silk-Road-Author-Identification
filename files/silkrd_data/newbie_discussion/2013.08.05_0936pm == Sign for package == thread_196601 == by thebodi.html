I'm sure someone more experienced than I could answer this better, but this is how I would handle.

If the vendor is certain that he didn't use enough postage, then I wouldn't worry and go get it, and then I'd leave him a 1/5 and NEVER order from him again as there is no reason for that non-sense. Concurrently if he verifies that he did in fact use "Certified Mail" then the mail man is required to obtain a signature. Hence why they use this service when you are being served a court supeona.

If he did use enough postage (as he should have of course) and he didn't use actual "Certified Mail" (most mail is not certified by the way, they charge extra for the service) then you should be concerned and I personally wouldn't go pick it up.

But just my opinion so I would seek more advice before you make a move.
