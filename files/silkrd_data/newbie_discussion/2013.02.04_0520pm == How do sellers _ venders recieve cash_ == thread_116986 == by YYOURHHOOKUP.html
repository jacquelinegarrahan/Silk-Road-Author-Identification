Title: Re: How do sellers / venders recieve cash?
Post by: mindmatter on February 05, 2013, 08:58 am
It's simple to sell your coins at an exchange like mtgox. But selling your coins anonymously is a different matter and like packaging methods, you won't find much public discussion about it.
The only public methods I've heard of is:

Sell coins to a vendor for cash in the mail
Or to buy physical goods with your coins like gold or more drugs and sell them locally.

It will take a bit of time for you to find a comfortable way of cashing out. And expect to pay a decent fee to get your coins turned straight to cash without needing to provide an ID.
