hello blackya

FE means to finalize early. the term finalize is used when talking about finalizing a transaction on SR. you place an order, seller ships order, you receive order and then finalize it releasing your funds from escrow to the seller. when you FE, your releasing the funds early, before the seller ships. this is done for many reasons but not recommended by SR.

escrow is any third party that holds money for 2 sides making a transaction. in this case its SR. its used to protect buyers from getting ripped off.

pgp/gpg is a form of encryption for text and files. on SR its used to send sensitive information from buyer to sender, usually shipping addresses.

its very important you read the silkroad wiki, its fully of information that will make the process easier to understand.
