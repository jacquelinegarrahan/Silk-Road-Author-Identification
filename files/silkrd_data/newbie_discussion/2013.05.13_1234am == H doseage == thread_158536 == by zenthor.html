Post by: captainjojo on May 13, 2013, 02:18 am
Given that you haven't done any opiates in almost a year, it would be safe to say you no longer have any tolerance, so I won't go into the differences in strength between prescription drugs like Oxycodone, Hydrocodone and other Opiate derivative's, suffice it to say you should do a google search on Opiate equivalence tables.

I prefer to IV my H. The bio availability of H taken via insufflation (snorted) as opposed to IV'ing is less.  In other words you get more of the drug by IV'ing it than by snorting it, so doing 10mg or H by snorting it is not the same as doing 10mg by IV.

All that being said, it is always safer to start small.  I would make a small line of 10mg and snort it.  Wait at least 15 minutes before trying more.  Do not keep snorting more to try and get a better buzz, if you do too much you will start feeling nauseous and possibly start vomiting.

I wouldn't do more than 50mg in one sitting the first time.  The problem with H is you never know the strength from one batch to the next.  So you might get high as a kite on 10mg from one batch and nothing from the second.

The most common reasons users OD on H is they get a new batch and take their regular dose, only to discover the new batch is two to three times as strong.  So they nod off and stop breathing.

Always start small on a new batch, at most 1/4-1/2 of your normal dose.

Well, I can't say from experience, but I can give you a logical, "SAFE" way to calculate it.
Morphine dose by IV is normally about 10mg for a fairly strong hospital dose.  Pure H is considered 2-3 times stronger than morphine, so if you DID get some pure stuff, and did NOT have any tolerance, then you would theoretically only do about 3 to 5 mg for IV.
For nasal use, morphine is around 20 percent bio-available.
So you need 5 times as much, nasally, as you would need compared to IV.
Close to the same with H.
So looks like a careful starting dose of morphine HCL would be 40-50 mg, while a starting dose (for PURE H) would be 10-20 mg, if you are certain it's close to pure.  If not, start there and work your way up, allowing enough time for it to fully absorb into the membranes before doing more.
If my calculations are off my much, please feel free to correct me.
