Title: Re: Be careful all newbies just been burnt
Post by: BlackIris on June 08, 2013, 05:48 pm
Quote from: Gunit666 on June 08, 2013, 04:07 pm
Not sure about fake sites, but I have only used my pin once the other time I sent funds from a different wallet to SR one  :-\

The *only* way someone could withdraw funds from your SR account is if they had your PIN. So you either inserted it in some phishing site (or similar thing) or you have a keylogger into your PC (in this second case changing PIN will do nothing, the keylogger will simply record the new PIN away) without knowing it.

I suggest you to scan your PC well if you are sure you didn't use a phishing site or gave away the PIN in some other way.

If you use Windows it is always best to use a VM dedicated to ToR and SR and used for nothing else, and even better (much) if it is a Whonix linked VM (there's a tutorial very easy to follow about this in the security section). Alternatively be sure to have a good anti-virus/firewall (please not the windows one, a good one free is ZoneAlarm) and malware blocker/finder (plus naturally an encrypted partition/content in which you use ToR).
