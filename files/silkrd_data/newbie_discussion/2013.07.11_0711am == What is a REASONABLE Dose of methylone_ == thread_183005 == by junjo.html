I've got quite a bit of experience with methylone and assuming you're average in terms of weight 200mgs is a good dose. Although some might consider this heavy. You could start out with something like 150mgs and do smaller 50-100mg bumps after an hour to extend the trip or you could do this with the 200 your call. Methylone seems to be a bit shorter of a duration. Some also describe it as less intense. You can also take rather high doses of methylone 3-400ish but it would be much more unpleasant and I'd always rather wish I took more than less. Stay safe!
