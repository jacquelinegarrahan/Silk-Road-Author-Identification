Title: Re: buyer infos for seller accounts
Post by: scout on February 26, 2013, 12:09 am
No, they cannot see the ratings you've given or the deposit amount.  A vendor you place an order with CAN see the feedback you leave for them, but cannot see the feedback you've left for any other vendor/product.

They can see your buyer stats -- number of transactions, total spent, auto-finalize rate, refund rate.
