Title: Re: How many of you FE for weed?
Post by: DutchMagic on June 28, 2013, 05:55 pm
Personally as a buyer I would try to find a vendor that does not require you to FE.
We ourselves do not require anyone to FE, and we actually advise against it clearly on our profile.

However, I do understand the FE requirement from a vendor's point of view. Some vendors cannot financially afford to have many orders in transit. You must understand that having $10-100k of orders in escrow/transit is common amongst large vendors. FE would help maintain their liquidity. Also asking NEW buyers (say <10 purchases on SR) to FE weeds out the scammers.
