before i encourage experimentation with any psychotropic substances i advise doing your homework on the chemical composition as well as physiological effects of any you choose to indulge in. With safe use these substances can improve the quality of your life on many levels (some would argue every level). If misused there can be consequences on both a physical or mental level. To use irresponsibly is to give our respective interests a bad name and hinder any efforts to reduce the stigma that exist in conventional society.

That being said have some fun! 

well if you wish to start out slow and easy i would advise Lsa (baby wood rose or morning glory seeds) are good introductions for tripping, both for a psychedelic state and a slight physical discomfort which a few worthwhile substances have.

mushrooms of most kinds rock as well and can bring you pretty far and are great for getting ones feet wet. Always make me feel silly.

L S D has a great deal to offer in terms of diversity of experience, although can be quite uncomfortable to those who become anxious. Using proper education and moderation, i plan to use it my whole life, like a few others.

mescaline is hard to find, i advise just extracting it yourself if you can get the cactus mailed to you. but mescaline is by far my favorite, it is sensual, euphoric, forgiving, subtle, and expansive. I owe much awareness to the cactus <3

ketamine when used with caution can also be quite expansive. It provided entirely new interior planes of awareness for myself, but also has a reputation for encouraging irrational beliefs when used compulsively.

4 aco-dmt is quite lovely, very sparkly and hallucinatory although i am yet to know what damage continued use it may have. quite fun though it merrits at least one or two goes.

many 2-c research chems are worthwhile but can be quite uncomfortable i suggest after a few years of mushrooms and acid maybe look at these.

d m t is quite powerful and its existance in every living thing has some implications, or at least one may be driven to extrapolate of of such an astounding fact. try it, expect nothing, accept everything, and be critical of any assumption.
 mdma and mda are not so much hallucinitory although i have had auditory and visual hallucinations off both i count them because psychedelics etymological roots are, mind manifesting, which i interpret as awareness or mind in a state of becoming. this takes place as a unique sensory or psychological environment, and i feel both mdma and mda provide this. try it out in small doses and gradually raise them based on comfort.
