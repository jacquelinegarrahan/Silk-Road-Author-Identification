Title: Trip Report (DOM) Grand Wizards Lair
Post by: just4djh on April 27, 2013, 04:33 am
Dose: 5 MG
Dropped 531: Metallic taste
621: Begins to kick in minor nausea+sweaty palms (Note: nausea is worse with cigarette)
641: Trip begins listening to kid cudi in woods and the leaves are dancing to the music. I lick finger to check wind direction, there is no wind. I look at the rusted wheel in the Wild West and I can see the cracks and rust in detail, it is bright orange.
715: I walk into my house and get something to eat, I stand by the table and begin to eat some fish. As I look up I feel the anger from the baby in a picture I look away and remember I'm tripping, but I can't shake the feeling that all the pictures in the room are staring at me. I look away and hurry out of my house.
730: Chilling w/M walking to gas station, time has sped up at this point and the ground is green and I see green snowflakes on the ground, yes green mofo snowflakes.
???: Walking back through the shortcut in the woods to my house. As I enter the woods I feel as though I'm walking through a portal,  As I look down I see grass and little flowers that seem to need to be cut around my ankles, but I do not feel them. I reach down in a futile attempt to grab them and am having trouble discerning the real from the unreal. The simplest smallest newborn plants seem to be bright vibrant flowers.
847-917 Watching Adult Swim in my room or at least trying too, but the light from the T.V keeps spinning and it is making it very hard to concentrate
1200 I turn off my TV and try to go to sleep, but there is a crack in the left side of my ceiling or at least I think there is, almost like something is lifting it up from outside. I begin to stare at it when out of the corner of my eye I see something coming out of where my light is and I hear mechanical whirry noises like something is coming out of my ceiling.
0200 Still can't sleep so I go have a cigarette on the porch, as I walk back inside instead of trying to go back to sleep I stare at the kitchen tiles and the white circles on there begin to mold together and form the picture of a turret. I have to break the image back down into the basic circles so I can break the trance I was in.

Body weight- 147 LBS
Age: 18
Note: M also took a tab, however he ate coconut pie thirty minutes before and did not feel anything until the next day when he explained that all he felt was minor nausea. (comedown?)
     Also any advice involving DOM would be greatly appreciated, I could not find very much information through Erowid about the drug other then the duration, safe dosages and a couple of trip reports.
