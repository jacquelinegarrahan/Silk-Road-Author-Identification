Post by: Cawla on February 06, 2013, 02:40 pm
It depends... the stealthiest package ever would be also impossible to determine from a normal letter. The main thing you'd have to worry about is all your parcels looking the same, as at some point when your business is growing bigger and bigger, LE are going to decide to make a small order from you to see how you package, so if all your parcels are the same or similar, they'll just pick those out of the mail. If you were "flying under the radar" and not worth LE ordering a package from you specifically (I wouldn't be surprised if they did it at randomly anyway), you wouldn't need to change it until one got intercepted...
The best stealth is to have your packaging look exactly like every other non-conspicuous letter, so that even if LE knew EXACTLY how you were doing it, it's not worth their time to open, for example, every plain windowed envelope... The thing to remember is they're smarter than you, but they have limited time and resources. That's our ONLY advantage, and both sides know it.
