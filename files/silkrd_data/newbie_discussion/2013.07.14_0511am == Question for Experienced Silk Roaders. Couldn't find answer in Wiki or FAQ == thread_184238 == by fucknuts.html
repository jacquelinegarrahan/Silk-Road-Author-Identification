Post by: jack909 on July 14, 2013, 05:51 pm
I'm still listed as a newbie, as I've only been an SR member for 2+ months.  However, I have spent almost $3K during these past 2 months, and if I were you, I would just finalize and take the hit since it's only $30.   After reading many vendor profiles, it seems as though they pay special attention to buyer stats (as they should), and I wouldn't want anything to negatively impact them.  You can always explain what happened in your feedback for the purchase.   I'd also be a little concerned at the fact that they haven't logged on several days.

For myself, I follow a very simple rule of thumb when purchasing items on SR:   If I can't afford to lose the cost of the item I'm purchasing, then I simply don't purchase anything at that time, and wait until I can afford to lose it.  It truly is a shared risk on behalf of the buyer and vendor.
