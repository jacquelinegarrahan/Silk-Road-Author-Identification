I applaud your admission of fault regarding your overreaction. Takes a big man to own up to that sort of thing. And I doubt there are very many people on SR that haven't been in the very situation you found yourself in.

As for getting clean, if I may make a suggestion... Suboxone, in my opinion, is a much, much better option for kicking the dope. From what I've experienced in my own struggles when I've let it go too far, methadone works but then there's a huge issue with kicking methadone. It's almost as though you replace one demon with another.

When I was at the depths of my abuse, I went to a doctor who specializes in opiate addiction recovery. He prescribed suboxone strips and told me that I should plan to stay on them for six months, starting out with two (2) 8mg strips per day. I'm a bit of a stubborn guy and chose to be more aggressive about it, and instead went with one strip per day, and did it for only a month. Then for the next month after that, I would do a strip whenever the itch got bad enough.

Everybody is different and I don't think there's any one recipe that can suit each person. But I do know that if you truly want to kick the stuff, it can be done. Surround yourself with people who care about you and your health, and be open with some of them about your issues so that you have somebody you can talk to when it gets hard to cope. The desire to get loaded will always be something to live with, but how you manage that will determine your success.

All of this coming from a guy who currently uses probably doesn't amount to much, I suppose, but I'm on my own path. I've found that I can use on occasion and kick the stuff before I let it get out of control. Took years of epic fails to figure out how to manage my own use, but for the time being, what I do works for me.
