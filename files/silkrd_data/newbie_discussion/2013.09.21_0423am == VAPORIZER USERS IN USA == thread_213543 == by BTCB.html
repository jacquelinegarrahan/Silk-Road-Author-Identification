Post by: Sugarleaf on September 22, 2013, 10:25 am
I'm based in the UK, but I'm a huge fan of vaporisers. Specifically, the Magic Flight Launch Box. I vape or eat edibles exclusively, no smoking for me! I highly recommend the MFLB and all of their accessory products. The customer service is fantastic (even internationally!) and they really do stick to their word in regards to their warranty - they even replace your box if you somehow accidentally break it! They are a fantastic company and I've had nothing but positive experiences with them. Not only that, but their products are very high quality and well made, I actually enjoy the feeling of holding the box, seeing some beautiful light and fluffy green slowly brown as I take hits, it's even better to watch concentrates bubbling away in there! It's a pleasure to clean, too.

Personally, I use mine nearly daily, usually with my bong, the water pipe whip attachment and the power adapter. The wooden grinder they sell macerates the bud to a really fine grind which greatly improves the hits. The concentrate trays are just amazing and clean up very easily with a torch lighter. I love vaping BHO, honeycomb and oils with mine.
