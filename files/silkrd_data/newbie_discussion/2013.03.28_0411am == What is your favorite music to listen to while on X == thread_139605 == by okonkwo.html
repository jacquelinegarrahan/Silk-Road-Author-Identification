Title: Re: What is your favorite music to listen to while on X
Post by: spokeknife on March 29, 2013, 05:36 am
Most dubstep is cheesy, lame, lowest common denominator dance music, appealing to infantile punters who cant wait more than sixty seconds for a bass wobble drop. Awful muck. Avoid like the plague. Stuff like Skrillex makes me cringe.

Anything that is longer, involving, slowly changing, some soul and groove to it. New York deep house, some of the Detroit House (not techno) stuff too - Omar S, Kyle Hall, Moodymann, etc. Producers like Ricardo Villalobos or Petre Inspirescu also for me walk a nice line between head and feet. Breach and Joy Orbison for UK based, garage tinged house.

For pure out there insanity, Shackleton produces some of the best music. His tracks are pretty uncategorisable. Anything he touches is gold. If it wasnt associated with naff, hippie dross, you could call it 'trance' music. Whenever I listen to that stuff, on MDMA, I am somewhere else. That doesnt happen that often any more.
