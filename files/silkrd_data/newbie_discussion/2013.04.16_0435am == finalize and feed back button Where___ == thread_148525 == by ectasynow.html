The finalize option will be available once your order has been processed by the vendor. It can take a little while. Once finalized you'll give your feedback (or you can wait until shipment has arrived). All of this you find at the top of the SR-page once you press the "orders" button.
Title: Re: finalize & feed back button Where???
Post by: dothedamnthing on April 16, 2013, 04:57 am
You will be able to finalize as soon as the order is marked "in transit." This doesn't necessarily mean it's actually in the mail, of course, but most reputable vendors won't mark an order in transit until they've actually shipped it. The feedback page will pop up immediately after you finalize.
