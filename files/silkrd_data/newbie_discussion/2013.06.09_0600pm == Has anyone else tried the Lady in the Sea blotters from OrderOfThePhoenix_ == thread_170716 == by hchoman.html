Title: Has anyone else tried the "Lady in the Sea" blotters from OrderOfThePhoenix?
Post by: hchoman on June 09, 2013, 06:00 pm
A couple weeks ago I came upon a listing for a 5-strip of "Lady in the Sea" LSD blotters from OrderOfThePhoenix so I ordered a strip and last night I decided to give them a test run. Upon careful consideration and meditation through out the day I had decided to take 2 blotters, listed at 100micrograms each. Took sub-lingual about 5:30pm with some cannabis about a half hour in.

About an hour in really started to notice the effects setting in. It was almost as if I had a super-drunk feeling yet I still had full control of my body and speech. I decide to take an evening stroll around the neighborhood and enjoy the scenery listening to some music.

The peak was intense but not too overwhelming. This lasted a good 3+ hours. Then as nightfall set in my trip started to take on a very insightful tone. I laid on the trampoline in the back yard and watch the stars do their thing for a while.

This was by far the cleanest LSD that I have ever had. No jaw clenching, no random headaches that come and go and no intense muscle cramping.

About 2am I had decided that the ride must end and took 10mg of Valium, this brought me down like landing on a big puffy cloud. Woke up this morning after about 6 hours sleeping and felt AWESOME!

Just wanted to put my 2 cents in and see if anyone else had any experiences to share?
