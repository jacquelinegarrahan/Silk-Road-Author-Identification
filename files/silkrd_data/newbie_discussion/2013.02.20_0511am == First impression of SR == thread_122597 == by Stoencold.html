Title: First impression of SR
Post by: Stoencold on February 20, 2013, 05:11 am
So I'm completely new to SR, I've known of it for about a year, but I've lived abroad, with a decent dealer literally living next door, with good prices, good products and a very friendly tone. So I haven't found the need to use SR, for as long as I've known of it. And I wasn't really positive to the concept. That is, until now.
When I first heard of SR, and checked it out, I have to admit. I was actually repulsed by the idea of how easy it could be to buy hard drugs. I was a textbook "weed is ok, anything else is going to ruin your life. And I hate that it's on the same black market as something as harmless as weed" type of guy. I was under the impression of SR being filled with dumb people, thinking that they wouldn't get caught, throwing an ounce of weed in a letter, and shoving it in a mailbox. "sleezy scumbags", from the biggest criminal organizations out there, was the other extreme, I thought the site could be filled with. I had been on the site before, spending 10 minutes browsing some products, not taking the time to read customer reviews, product info or really anything but the pictures of the product.  I was very quick to judge, and denied what my friends said about SR being really professional.

When I came back to my Country of origin, I found myself really bored, and wanted something to browse, to make time pass. After smoking crappy hash, and getting sick of watching watching youtube vids of people smoking the same quality I got used to, abroad. I found myself downloading tor, and browsing SR. It didn't take long before I actually started reading about the different products I was looking at. And although the info varies from vendor to vendor. I very positively surprised by the professionalism of a lot of these vendors. Anywhere from the packaging they've described to quality control, and customer service. Taking the illegality of the products I've been looking at, it's obvious that you're not going to have reviews from New York Post, and so on. But the amount of feedback given in the customer reviews, almost seemed overwhelming. I was especially impressed with vendors, having comments about vendors making up for mistakes. Like taking a day longer than planned to ship the product, being compensated with significant overweight. And the overall will to give the best service possible, from the vendors.

I have yet to fully figure out how to get bitcoins, I was introduced to it at the same time I started browsing SR again. I'm very new to the concept, and bitcoin merchants, but I love the concept of the anonymity of the currency. But most of all, I love how the value of bitcoins work, compared to any other currency out there. I really do like the challenge of figuring all of this out, and am looking forward to make my first order on SR.

Now, over to the thing about SR that really completed my first impression of SR, the forum. I was amazed by the community that the forum is providing. Unlike the usual forums I hang around in, people seem far more willing to help others. Whether that's helping a newbie through his first order, to offering full assistance to help someone learn about PGP encryption. Not to mention the most amazing and detailed product reviews, that you won't find on the trade market. I haven't been through the entire forum, so I can't say this for sure. But I have yet to see the immature posts you very often find in forums. And it's obvious to me that the people here, are serious. Although I've smoked for over 3 years (Yeah, I know I'm still a rookie compared to a lot of you), I've only been buying from friends that have been selling a bit on the side, from having a legit 9 to 5 job. I have to say that I'm amazed by the customer service the vendors here seem to provide. It has blown my mind, reading about the lengths some of these vendors are willing to go, in order to give the best possible service and product.

This was just my first impression of SR, I've been looking at if for 4 days, and it's clear to me that there's a lot more to learn about this community. If anyone else would like to share their first impression of SR, go ahead and throw it in here. Also, if you have any questions that this post didn't answer, feel free to ask them. I want to reach 50 posts on the forum, and be given access to post on the other forums. But I don't want to spam with meaningless posts, just to get the amount of posts up. I'll be updating on my experience with SR, as it develops.
