Post by: luxxiaxx on April 18, 2013, 08:00 pm
100mg by definition weighs exactly 100mg... milligrams is a unit of measurement that conveys weight, just like pounds/lbs or ounces/oz. What weight a scale displays depends on what unit of measurement its capable of & set to display, but most scales being used t100o weigh drugs are going to be set to measure weight in grams, in which case 100mg will be displayed as "0.1". 99.5 per cent purity --  & the entire matter of purity in general -- is completely irrelevent to how much a substance weighs.
