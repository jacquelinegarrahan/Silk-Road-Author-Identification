Title: Re: what's a good drug for a beginner?
Post by: Duckman on February 09, 2013, 09:33 pm
Quote from: rebatugam on February 09, 2013, 09:14 pm
I've also had some "mental health problems" and some say you shouldn't experiment with drugs then, but I don't give a fuck about that.

There is a lot of evidence supporting the notion that drugs, including weed can accelerate the onset of mental illness.

What this means is that most people can take drugs in moderation and suffer no ill effect, however a person with a history of mental illness could do themselves considerable harm as if you make your condition worse, the effects are irreversible and so if you stop taking drugs you wont get better.

Everybody usually knows one guy who has managed to "fry his brain" and often whats happened is the guy hasn't really taken as much as people think he has, its just that he had a pre-existing mental health problem and the drugs then accelerated the onset of this problem.

If you are going to take drugs then you need to do a lot of research as to how it will effect you personally.

If a person had heart problems then they would have to accept that there were some things in life that they just couldn't do due to medical reasons, unfortunately the same applies to mental health issues.

I smoke a lot of weed, its one of the softest drugs available, yet there is overwhelming evidence to suggest that it is not good for people with certain mental health issues.

Ultimately its up to you.  Unfortunately if you have a history of mental illness then you have to be a lot more cautions then people who have no such history.
