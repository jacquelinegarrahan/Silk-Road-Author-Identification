Samples could help your business, but definitely not required.

First, get to 50 posts so you can post a review thread for your vendor profile in the Rumor mill forum. Or you can post it in the Newbie Discussion board and then request a move once you reach 50 posts.

Just two basic tips. No worries, if you provide quality product and quality customer service, then your business will eventually kick off to the point where orders can become almost overwhelming.
