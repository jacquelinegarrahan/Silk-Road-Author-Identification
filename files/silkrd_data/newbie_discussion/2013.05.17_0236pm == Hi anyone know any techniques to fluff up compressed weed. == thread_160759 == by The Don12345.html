Title: Re: Hi anyone know any techniques to fluff up compressed weed.
Post by: HellInaHandBasket on May 18, 2013, 06:12 am
Not sure if its been mentioned but a solid container with the buds in it and a vacuum sealer to make a vacuum in the chamber.

My old vac sealer came with a special container for soup or whatever and its perfect for it. Has a little tube that runs from the machine into a port on the lid of the container. Might be able to rig up a ghetto version if your creative.

Or just use a coffee grinder to chop the buds after pulling apart if its for perso smoking. Just remember to scrape the dust out of the grinder as mix it through the chops. Its actually some really good 'emergency' smoke on its own too as its all the goods that's been smashed off the buds so is potent but harsh and difficult to work with sometime. The grinder will fluff it right back out though and is the most effective thing I've found. No good if it's for sale but just for yourself should be sweet as.
