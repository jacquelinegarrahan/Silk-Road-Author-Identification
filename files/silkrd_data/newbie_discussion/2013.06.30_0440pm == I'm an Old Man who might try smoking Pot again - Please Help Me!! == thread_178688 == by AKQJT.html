What you call a "wheelchair" I believe the current term is called "couchlock" if i'm not mistaking what you're trying to convey.

A sativa is more of a "stimulant" high. There are over 400 diferent chemicals which get you high called "cannabinoids" the main two are THC(delta-9-tetrahydrocannabinol) and CBD (cannabidiol).

THC is psychoactive while CBD is anti-psychoactive. Yin and yang.

THC is the main "high" and it is a stimulant. Cannabis effects are all detemined by ratios of different cannabinoids(Like I said there are 400 of them which is why all cannabis is different. Even the same strain grown twice, hell even two seeds from the same mommy grown in the same conditions)

Anyways so THC is the main "high" while all other cannabinoids I think of as "high modifiers".

CBD, the other main cannabinoid, is anti-psychoactive. This chemical is sort of the yang to the yin of THC. It produces an opposite, yet balancing out effect to complement the THC. It is very good for treatment of psychological disorders as well as many physical ailsments. I won't go too deeply into medical use as it doesn't appear to be what you're interested in.

Anyways sativa has a high THC to CBD ratio. This leads to a very energetic, creative, uplifting high.

Indica has a high CBD to THC ratio leading to a more lazy, couchlock, sleepy high. Or a "wheelchair" high I think...

No plant in the world is 100% indica or 100% sativa due to uncontrolled genetic mixing due to them being plants in the wild and all.

Also don't believe the paranoia bullshit, in reality cannabis paranoia is not caused by potency. Infact I only recommend top notch quality bud. Ever. Paranoia is caused by harvesting buds too early. You see when buds are harvested early(typically by mexican shit weed growers and mids growers or novice dank growers) it will have developed it's THC but not the CBD to balance it out. This causes a fucked up THC to CBD ratio, THC alone causes paranoia. CBD counteracts it and soothes you.

This is shown when people take marinol. Marinol is a pure antificial THC pill, noone in history has ever died from cannabis in plant form due to all cannabinoids balancing each other out but four deaths have resulted from marinol. It is known to cause paranoia and a plethora of other "problems" which anti-cannabis advocates scream from their soap boxes.

Anyways so i'd recommend buying bud from some of the vendors on the road. Always buy dank, become extremely familiar with SR before using it.
