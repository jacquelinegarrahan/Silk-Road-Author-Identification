Post by: Burning Babylon on June 29, 2013, 01:01 am
With the term Postal Spam I'm basically referring to all types of advertisement like catalogues and free samples meant to be delivered to postal boxes. Postal Spam is the cheapest way of getting mail delivered to an address and this is going to useful for the project/thread Mail Forwarding and Virtual Office Providers - Concept and Thoughts: http://dkn255hz262ypmii.onion/index.php?topic=161081.msg1143744 to see how the different providers work when getting mail. Mail could be sent manually but this would be horribly expensive in comparison and I'm planning on doing this experiment on a fairly large scale if possible.

I'm going to list all regions in the world and classify them based on if they are Multiple Regions, Active Regions or Inactive Regions. I will post a link along with a short description of the kind of Postal Spam along with any other relevant information if there is any. For those who want to help please post as many links as possible and I'll update in time, also if a link becomes dead or some kind of offer expires please inform me of that as well. :)
