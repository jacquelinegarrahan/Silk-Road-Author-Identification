Title: Re: Keeping items in cart for a while
Post by: MrVidalia on February 03, 2013, 11:32 pm
It is not a problem.  But do not be surprised if the items go away or the cart behaves somewhat strange. E.g. login and cart icon says 3 times, click it and only 1 item is there and icon now shows 1 items. This is because the item was already sold out or the vendor took down the listing.

I recommend you add the vendors to your favourites list. It is more likey you can find them in the future this way.
