I can't comment on the vendor in question but when it comes to checking vendor rep in general most should have their own review thread and there are other threads dedicated to specific drugs you should have a good read of.

It is worth mentioning though the vendors ratings on the main site can be manipulated with spurious feedback, this tends to happen more when a vendor is first starting out and if you see lots of "FE for trusted vendor 5/5" type feedback posts it pays to go digging on the forums to see what others have posted to see how legit this is. The rating scale only works when used in tandem with the forums by buyers.

Also be cautious regarding new vendors offering too good to be true prices, one MDMA vendor whose listings were very competitively priced appears to have disappeared over the weekend, don't know what happened yet but anyone who FE'd can be sure they have lost their coins.

Title: Re: where to check vendors rep
Post by: MaoZedong on January 20, 2013, 09:08 pm
There are three main ways to evaluate a vendor's rep all based on the forums and ideally all should be used in conjunction. If noone is posting about them in the forums then they are probably not a good vendor to deal with.

1 search for the vendors name in the forum search and read all posts with that vendor's name in them
2 read all the posts in threads about that vendor
3 click on the forum profile of the vendor in question and click "show posts" to see all of the posts that vendor has made

if the vendor is reputable and cares about the community all of these searches will turn up positive results
