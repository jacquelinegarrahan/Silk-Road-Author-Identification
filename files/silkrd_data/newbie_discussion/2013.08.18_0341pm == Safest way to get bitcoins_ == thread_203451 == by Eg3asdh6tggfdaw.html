Title: Re: Safest way to get bitcoins?
Post by: galileosophia on August 18, 2013, 07:46 pm
I can heartily recommend #bitcoin-otc on freenode.  You can find reputable sellers on there who allow you to make cash deposits to their bank accounts in exchange for BTC.

If you connect to freenode through Tor, use an e-mail account you signed up for using Tor and pay for BTC ONLY using cash, you are operating pretty low under the radar.

I have purchased from there many times and although there's a bit of learning to get used to in order to start interacting with people in the channel, it's nothing too complicated.  If you want more advice about it, please send me a message.
