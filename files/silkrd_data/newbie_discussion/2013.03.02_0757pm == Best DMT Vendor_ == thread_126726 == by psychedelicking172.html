Title: Re: Best DMT Vendor?
Post by: moonmanZ on March 03, 2013, 04:48 am
The colour depends on the initial plant material, whether defatting was done as well as how much it was done, and also possibly age (as it absorbs moisture over time). Mimosa Hostilis plant source will produce a white powder, if all the possible steps are taken, while Acacia Confusa will yield a slightly brownish hue. If proper defatting is done, the colour will be less tainted. Additional steps may be required in the case of Acacia, though it's no less quality a source - in fact, its yields are higher as a % of plant weight. It's been a while, but the moon-lab may pump out a yield on contract for a customer who, it's suspected, would then provide it through these services.
