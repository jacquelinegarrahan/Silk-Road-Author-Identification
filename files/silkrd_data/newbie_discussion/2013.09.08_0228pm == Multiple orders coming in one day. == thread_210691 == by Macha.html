Title: Re: Multiple orders coming in one day.
Post by: ppanth on September 08, 2013, 03:08 pm
It happens, I mean unless you are dealing with shitty vendors, its not a big deal. Consider how many people order from amazon, and ebay in a day..more then a few..and amazon sends multiple boxes all the time.

There is a mailman who was posting in the shipping forum last month, basically he said they just want to deliver your mail and get it done..unless its leaking or reeks to hell and back.

I have had multiple priority packs show up on the same day, and once 2 express came at once..they were not suspicious looking, and they were delivered just fine. Sometimes its from ebay, the road, amazon, and really thinking about it, with proper precautions there is no reason to worry.

And of course vendors use return addresses, they just are not theirs. No return address would fall under not slightly odd, and the idea is to blend in.
