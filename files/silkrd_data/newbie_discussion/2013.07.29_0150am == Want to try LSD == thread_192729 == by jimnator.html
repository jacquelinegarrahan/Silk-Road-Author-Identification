Title: Re: Want to try LSD
Post by: Dr RA on July 29, 2013, 02:27 am
Different drugs affect different people differently. Maybe not a very helpful statement, but very true.

There is no way to tell what 1/2 a tab will do to you without prior experience. Personally I would say it's unlikely you will get "out of control" on a half tab unless you have no psychedelic experience and/or extreme sensitivity. I don't know what the dosage you order is like but a standard hit of acid is about 100ug. 50ug is a pretty light dose in my opinion. 100ug is usually a fine place for beginner to start. Not nearly enough for some people however.

My advice is you can always take more, but you can't take less after you've taken it. Start slow, explore.
