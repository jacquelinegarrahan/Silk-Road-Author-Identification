Post by: kgfcfdq on February 28, 2013, 04:39 pm
Bitcoins can continue to deflate almost indefinitely. It may be that in future orders are mostly in bitcents instead... I can't remember exactly how far it goes, but you can get like 0.000001 BTC or something. This is not possible with current physical currency.

SR listings are tied to a dollar value and drop in price (as in, the number of BTC being charged will drop) along with the rise in value of BTC. There is an option in setting to change it to display prices to you in dollars on the road.

Maybe there are some sellers who are keeping the bitcoin price the same on the road, meaning their prices are really increasing, but I haven't noticed that. The products I have bought have almost halved in BTC price since I first came here a couple of months ago, and most of the stuff I look at still seems realistically priced (apart from prescription shit, but that doesn't interest me at all).

I don't think there's any reason to think it's a bubble or that it will be bursting any time soon. My only concern would be the SR team getting raided or something and us losing this fantastic resource. I think BTC values would probably take a substantial dip if Silk Road went down.
