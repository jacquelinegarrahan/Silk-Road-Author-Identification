Title: Re: White Vs Brown Vs Tan MDMA
Post by: MDUK on February 04, 2013, 04:11 am
Quote from: astor on February 04, 2013, 03:21 am
Read the reviews, since they will be more informative than the color.
Up to a point, these things are obviously subjective and depend on your capacity to produce seratonin, as well as how much you've eaten, how much you've drunk, whether you've been drinking alcohol, method of consumption etc etc etc.

Whilst the colour should never be used to judge whether a product is "pure", the "tan" or brown colour of street MDMA is typically caused by impurities that could be removed via acetone washing/recrystallisation - that however reduces yield and takes time, so a lot of producers won't bother, or won't continue the process once the product is "good enough".

At the end of the day, no substitute for a marquis test - I take a couple of samples and test every order I get in, bear in mind that even if what you're buying is crystalline there are a vast multitude of white/off white chemicals that could be mixed into a batch.

To be honest, I wouldn't say no to tan MDMA by a long shot, you're unlikely to notice the difference when taking it beyond the placebo effect, but if you're unsure about anything you buy invest in a marquis testing kit, your body will thank you ;)
