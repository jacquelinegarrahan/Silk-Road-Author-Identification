Title: Re: PO Box OK?
Post by: CannabisConsumer on July 01, 2013, 03:11 am
A PO box has its ups and downs.

Pros are the the vendor will not have your address which is good considering extorting and threatening buyers is on the rise.

Cons outweigh the pros and my opinion and the biggest con would be that you are foregoing your plausible deniablility.

Getting caught and a controlled delivery happening at your house is bad enough. At that point you already look like you did it. You could always get off on plausible deniablility though which is basically "I don't know who shipped those drugs to my house with my name of it Mr. Judge, someone must have spent $20-$500+ on drugs for me for no reason and I have no idea who it is.

A PO box would forego these opportunities unless you are fucking awesome at it. Getting a package full of drugs and going to pick it up even a couple day later is definitely not something you could deny. "Oh yeah I just so happened to visit my PO box when I got a package that I had no idea existed"....yeah no, not going to hold up worth a damn.

If you ran a legitimate business or some shit that gives you a reason to check mail ever X days, then you'd have an excuse to go there, otherwise keep it at home.
