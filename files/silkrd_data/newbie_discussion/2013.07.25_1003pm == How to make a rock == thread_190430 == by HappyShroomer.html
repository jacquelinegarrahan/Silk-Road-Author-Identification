That is easy.  Use a pyrex beaker.  Heat the cocaine in some water until it turns into oil and add about 25% baking soda slowly while whipping it and stretching it across the bottom.  I always used a metal pot with water and a cloth in the bottom and put the beaker inside the pot of water.  When it combines on the bottom and is spread nicely then put it on a flat surface in the freezer.  After it hardens in the bottom you can dump it out on a coffee filter.

If it is for personals then you can just put some ammonia in a shot glass and add a gram of cocaine.  Filter with a coffee filter.

The most simple way I ever cooked it up for personal use:


Get a small pyrex glass bowl.  Put in about a cup of ammonia (about 1/4 bowl full) in the bottom.  Throw a couple grams - a 8ball in the ammonia.  Stir it up in the ammonia for 10-15 seconds..  Stick it in the microwave for about 20-30 seconds (DO NOT LET IT BOIL - This will make it splatter all over your microwave!).  You just need the ammonia hot enough to melt the coke.  Check it.  Keep zapping it for another 10-15 seconds till all the coke turns into OIL on the surface of the ammonia.

THEN - Take out the bowl and slowly swish it around for several minutes.  As it cools you will slowly start to see those oil slicks start to turn into white rocks.  JUST KEEP SWISHING!  The oil will start attaching to the stones that are starting to form.  Swish til there is NO OIL left.  Stick the bowl into the freezer for about 5-10 minutes and make sure the ammonia has completely cooled. 

Then take a coffee filter and filter out all the rocks!

You are going to want to rinse the shit out of these rocks to get all the ammonia off.  That stuff is nasty.

Ammonia crack doesn't taste as good as baking soda crack, but it's so easy and fool proof.  SUPER EASY for anyone to do.  Good luck and be careful.  I ended up addicted to crack for 3 years because someone taught me how to do this with my coke one day.  It ruined my life.

You've been warned about this stone so sweet.
