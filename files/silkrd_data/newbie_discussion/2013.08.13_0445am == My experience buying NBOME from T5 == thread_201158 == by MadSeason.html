Title: My experience buying NBOME from T5
Post by: MadSeason on August 13, 2013, 04:45 am
  Having never tried the stuff, I decided to buy 50 from T5 with the change left in my SR BTC wallet.  On the profile page, it states over and over again how potent these tabs supposedly are.  T5 states," If you have never tried NBOME, then DO NOT take more than a half a tab your first time."   I took that seriously even though I have tripped a thousand times on Cubes, DMT, Acid, and 2ci.

   So first, it took 17 days for the stuff to reach my mailbox even though I live on the border of US and Canada (shipped from Canada) .  That in itself is just asinine as in this day and age, it wouldn't take 17 days for NBOME to reach me from Mars.  That wasn't really an issue though, just a minor annoyance. 

  Me and a few buddies decided to go camping to try these so we would be away from prying eyes in case the stuff made us go crazy or some shit.  We did as the profile page said....we ate a half a tab.  After an hour, all we had was a bad taste in our mouths.  No bright colors, no trails, no nothing.  We decide to drop 1.5 more in the hopes we could salvage the night.  After another hour, we seen some trails and got the giggles a bit, but absolutely positively ZERO movement of any object whatsoever.

  At this point, I'm the laughing stock of the evening and get the, "I told you so's" over and over again.  It fucking sucked hard.

  From two tabs of this crap, we tripped about the same as 2g of shrooms....hardly what the profile states.

  But you know what?  I wasn't even upset afterward.  Shit, I spent $35 to find out that NBOME from T5 had about the same effect as drinking a pot of coffee, no biggie.

  So I PM T5 and explain that I had my doubts that his tabs were 1000au, but I still gave him a 5 feedback and told T5 I wasn't upset, and wouldn't change the feedback....I just thought they should know in case they had a bad batch so they could change orders if necessary.

  T5 PM's me back and apologizes and doesn't understand how it happened.  We PM a few more times and T5 offers to reship and I accept of course because I want to see what NBOME has to offer.  I offer to send T5 the remaining 42 tabs of junk in return, which T5 declines. 

  It's 20 days since that conversation and no reship in sight.  I don't really care about that aspect though.  What pisses me off is that T5 gave his word and broke it.  Not only that, but waiting on drugs in the mail is stressful as hell.

  So....Mr./Mrs T5....If'n ya ain't gonna stand by YOUR WORD, then don't offer reships because those of us on the other end just end up getting stressed that LE got the mail and we end up waiting for the knock at the door.  Also, in the future, put a damned return address on the envelopes full of drugs you send to people for crisake.  Not having one on international mail is like putting a fucking neon sign on the package that says, "IM DRUGS".

  I'm not calling T5 a scammer at all.  I'm just calling T5 a lier, which is worse than a scammer in my opinion.  Why peeps bring the bad karma on themselves is beyond me.
