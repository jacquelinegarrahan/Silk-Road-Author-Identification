Title: Re: Being a relay (TOR)
Post by: SelfSovereignty on February 12, 2013, 10:36 pm
You do NOT want to be an exit relay.  Google / startpage / whatever "tor exit node child porn" and read in horror at what happens to exit nodes in many countries.

Being an in-network-only relay node is safe.  Just make sure you've got a firewall and no malware on your system, because you're likely to get a bunch of retarded script kiddies scanning you a dozen times a day (shouldn't do any harm -- never did to me, anyway).

Just enable the option in Vidalia, and see if it works automatically.  Most routers have support for UPnP (Universal Plug & Play) these days, which lets programs dynamically tell your router they need an incoming port open for connections.
