Title: Re: FE impossible
Post by: MedicineWoman on July 26, 2013, 05:23 pm
Search the forums for "finalize early". It's almost always, always, a bad idea.

When you FE, you are prepaying for your product, BEFORE it has arrived.

By doing FE, you effectively waive your right to properly resolving any issue that arises down the road. Such as, your product never arriving.

This is why "NEVER FE" is written countless times in this forum.

Peace,
MW

Most vendors have rules about new customers, because they can't afford to get scammed. But if you approach them, 99% of them will NOT require FE on you for a very small quantity, because it's less for them to lose if you flake out. Do this until you build up your stats. Once you have a solid history of buying, and you've been on the site for more than a few days, vendors won't require FE. They'll realize you have more to lose by scamming them than by being decent; you'll lose your good stats and have to start all over.
