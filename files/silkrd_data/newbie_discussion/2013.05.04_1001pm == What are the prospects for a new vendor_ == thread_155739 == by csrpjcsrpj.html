If you make/grow/synthesize whatever it is you want sell and are fairly intelligent you'll do just fine.

If you're reselling items bought from SR, you're going upstream and face lots of headwinds.

Every day a new reseller seems to pop up, and most get disgruntled and end up breaking bad and walking off with stolen FEs.

It's a hard way to make an easy living. It can be done, but it gets harder each day, and now that bitcoin doesn't just simply rise every single day, you have to add on that headache now too. If you time it wrong it could wash away any profits you've managed to squirrel away.
