Title: Re: My order hasn't arrived - How do I cancel it?
Post by: slowcrash on May 17, 2013, 06:17 pm
My understanding is that if there are problems with your order you would hit resolve to go through their resolution to escrow process. I would only use the resolution process if the order doesn't show after giving it enough time with the understanding that sometimes there are delays in delivery beyond the vendor's control. I would be patient. If the vendor has good feedback and is established, you shouldn't have any problems. But if it doesn't show after a reasonable amount of time, they do have the resolution process and the resolve link should be active before it auto-finalizes.

Post by: Libertas on May 17, 2013, 07:17 pm
Hi inlikeflynn,

Vendors have 4 days to process an order and put it 'in transit'. You can not cancel the order until this 4 day period is up, and you can not cancel it once it is put 'in transit'.

If your order has not arrived after 10 days, put it into resolution.
