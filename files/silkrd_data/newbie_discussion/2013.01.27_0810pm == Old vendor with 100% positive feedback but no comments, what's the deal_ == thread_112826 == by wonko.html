Title: Re: Old vendor with 100% positive feedback but no comments, what's the deal?
Post by: wonko on February 03, 2013, 01:33 pm
Tried them last night. Wow!

A brief report:

Packaging was very discrete and fast, up to the high standards I have come to expect from SR vendors. Musent kindly packed in an extra .5g and I decided I would take the entire 4g.

I took 2g to start with and then the remaining 2g over the following 20 minutes so as to not challenge my digestion too much. Over the first hour had the typical shroom coming up effects, then in the next 30 minutes everything exploded in unimaginable beauty. I found myself lying down saying 'wow' over and over and thinking 'this and every thing is incredible'. Some of the most intense mushroom visuals I have ever experienced. By the time I checked my trip timer again 5 hours had gone by.

I've been taking mushrooms on and off for a decade or so but not so much the last couple of years, I'd forgotten what a truly wonderful experience it is. Absolutely fantastic, thanks Musent.
