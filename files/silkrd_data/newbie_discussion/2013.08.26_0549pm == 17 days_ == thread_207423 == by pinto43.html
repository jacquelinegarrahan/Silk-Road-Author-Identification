Title: Re: 17 days?
Post by: hchoman on August 26, 2013, 06:19 pm
The fact that you can see the 17 days means the vendor has marked your order in transit which means it has shipped. If you got priority shipping expect to see it is 2-3 business days. If its regular mail usually like 5-7 business days. The 17 days is the start of the auto finalize countdown, it will change daily. When you receive your package click on finalize in your order screen and leave feedback, you don't want your order to finalize at any cost as it affects your buyer stats.
