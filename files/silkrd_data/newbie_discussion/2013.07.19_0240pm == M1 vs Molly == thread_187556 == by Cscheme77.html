Main differences

Effect: M1:MD

Comeup: M1: 20-40 mins range, food depending
MD: 45-1:30, depending on person

Duration: M1: 1hr - 1:30, much shorter lasting, kind of drops off near the end
MD: 2 hr - 5 hr, longer lasting, fuller in effect, has a pleasant down slope in the end

Effects: M1, more stimulant, euphoric, energetic, clean, urge to dance and party and fuck
MD, mildly stimulating, deeper euphoria, empathy, urge to dance and party and fuck, "beautiful" potent

Repetitive dosing: M1's dosing is limited by intake. After the first half gram or so, many of the euphoric qualities fade, and many of the stimulating qualities continue
MD, repetitive dosing with real molly can work for days if done in reasonable doses. The euphoria can be felt for a while. I've done it at 3 day events before and rolled real hard all three days. M1 requires at least a week to get similar effects of euphoria and enjoyment.
