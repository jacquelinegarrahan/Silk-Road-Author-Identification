Title: Re: What is the conversion from USD to BTC? I know it fluctuates....
Post by: dontek on June 21, 2013, 12:51 am
At the top of SR, click on ACCOUNT, next page, far right, click on SETTINGS and change it to USD. It will change all of the price displays to USD, with the exception of shipping, which will always be in BTC. Rather than worry about doing conversion rates in my head for the shipping, I just assume to add $10 to my order unless it's something crazy.
