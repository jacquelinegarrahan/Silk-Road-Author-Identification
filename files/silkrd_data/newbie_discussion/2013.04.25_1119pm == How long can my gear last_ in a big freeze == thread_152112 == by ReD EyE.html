Post by: Jack N Hoff on April 25, 2013, 11:41 pm
You don't need a freezer.  Freezers are bad for weed.  The trichomes fall of the weed easier.  A freezer is good if you plan on making bubblehash with the weed for that reason.  Let the weed cure in mason jars.  It lasts for years.  You don't need a freezer for the hash either.

Think about biology. Plants have cells. If you freeze a plant, those cells break. That's why you can't freeze tomatoes, they get all mushy and gross. I am just speculating but I think the biggest risk to freezing cannabis is that you risk loosing some of the good THC on the container you are storing it.
