Title: Re: How often is too often to message a vendor regarding tracking?
Post by: PrettyinGreen on July 17, 2013, 02:13 am
I know it's nerve-wracking waiting on an order to arrive, but also remember that just because a package has been marked "in transit" does not mean that it was actually mailed that day.  In fact, I know that some vendors print their labels one day and mail it on another. 

In regards to your other questions, I would say what you do from here on out depends on several things.  To start with, is this your first order with this vendor?  Secondly, what kind of feedback does he/she have?  Thirdly, has the vendor been online and read your messages since you sent them?  The answers to the above and whether or not you receive your order and or hear back from your vendor in the next couple of days will significantly impact the decision to go to resolution right away or not.

Hope this helps a little bit.  Keep us posted on the outcome.
