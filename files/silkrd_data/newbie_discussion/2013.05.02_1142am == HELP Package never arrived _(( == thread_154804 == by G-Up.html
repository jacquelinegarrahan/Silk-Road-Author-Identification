Does the tracking have the location it wad delivered to? Or does it just say delivered?
It is extremely unlikely that a domestic order got seized. Even with crappy packaging, orders should still be ok. There is no customs of checks for domestic mail. Only mail coming in from overseas gets checked. I have sent out hundreds of orders within Australia and all have arrived (except 3 but I think the buyers may have been trying to scam me, or they entered their address wrong).

Don't finalise until you are happy with the outcome. Send a message to the vendor and explain what has happened and work from there. They may offer a reship or refund. if you accept their offer, then finalise after that. if you are not happy, Silk Road admins will decide on the refund. Unfortunately for a new buyer, it is unlikely you will receive much of a refund, if any at all.
