Title: Re: Experiences with 2C-P
Post by: hwallclimber on February 08, 2013, 09:29 am
Yes, there is. Personally, I can only speak to B and P, maybe someone else can chime in.

For my money, 2C-B is literally the most pleasant experience on the planet. Big doses, small doses, it's just wonderful. Visuals are kind of fun, not super hallucinatory like mushrooms or something, but very fun. I enjoy the visuals on the B more than acid. Also, the closed eyes are very good, especially if you push the dose. It's great for taking in a show, just dance all night, body feels great. Sex is amazing on it. And it was one of the drugs they used for psychotherapy back in the early 80s; it has that same dissolving power of personal boundaries just like MDMA (except it lasts a good 4-6 hours, depending on the dose), so enjoy a good conversation on it!  It takes a good hour or so for it to kick in for me, and, as with pretty much every other psychedelic, smoking cannibas is always recommended!

P is a whole 'nother creature. It took a solid 4 hours or so before I even felt anything, but once it kicked in, I was tripping for a good 8 hours, lingering effects for another 4. Much more in the department of open eye visuals (see my above description). I've only tried it once, but am very much looking forward to doing it again.

And if you're into the science/pharmacokinetics of these things, we can get into that too if you want.

And as with all psychotropics, erowid is a great place to start!

Title: Re: Experiences with 2C-P
Post by: hwallclimber on February 08, 2013, 09:48 am
For me, even with a well-timed booster dose or two, molly never lasts more than 3 or 4 hours, with acute effects definitely gone by hour 3. Seriously though, I cannot recommend B enough. It's not the same as a good trip (several of my friends call it rolling instead of tripping on B), so there's still a place for tryptamines and LSD in my life, but for a good time, call B, she won't let you down.

And yes, in general the dose-response curves on the phenethylamines is much steeper than the tryptamines; the recommended dose for P is around 10mg, but when you read the "bad" trip reports on erowid, they start at around 14mg. The cool thing is that it takes so long to kick in that depending on the setting, you can realistically forget you took anything, which is awesome IMHO.

P lasts even longer than acid for me, but again, that 4-hour come up is weird. I took it with my friends who were all doing B, and I dosed up hours before they did and I still was the last one to start peaking!
