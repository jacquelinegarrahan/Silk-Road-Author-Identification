Title: Re: Silk Road [Qeustion] FE order what is this?
Post by: 420KING on July 10, 2013, 01:10 pm
Finalizing early means releasing funds from the escrow directly to the seller as if you already received your package.
Pretty much just go to orders, and click finalize. and if you don't have any feedback to give just write (FE FOR TRUSTED SELLER) and then once you receive your package you can update it with your review.

Sellers do this so that they can have funds available to 're-up'

Title: Re: Silk Road [Qeustion] FE order what is this?
Post by: Mr Hazlo on July 10, 2013, 02:59 pm
There's nothing inherently wrong in Finalizing Early, just do your research about the vendor and be sure you can trust him. However, I think for a first order would be better to order from someone you can stay in escrow with, to minimize all the anxiety you get from a first order. I have ordered like 10 times in about a couple of months and I've finalized early in two orders, and I haven't had the slightest problem. But, the ideal is to be able to stay in escrow (one of the virtues of SR).
