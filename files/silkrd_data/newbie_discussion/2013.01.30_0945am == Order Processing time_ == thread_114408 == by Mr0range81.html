Your coins automatically move into escrow when the vendor processes your order. Think of escrow as the 3rd person who holds the funds for both you and the vendor, you can release the funds at anytime once in escrow but strongly advise against doing that until your shipment has arrived and you are happy with the purchase. Any issues cannot be resolved once you have finalized, that is why finalizing early is not a good idea.
Title: Re: Order Processing time?
Post by: thebigjc on January 30, 2013, 11:22 am
Any payment you make to any vendor automatically goes into escrow. That's how SR works.
Unless you are dealing with a trusted vendor (how you trust a vendor is worth its own whole forum, but sometimes as a newbie you have to take a risk to prove yourself, too), never finalize early (FE).
When the gear arrives, you click finalize and the vendor gets the money. If it never arrives, you can resolve, which usually means asking for a refund/ reship/ or just extending the time to give it more time in the post.
If you finalize early and the gear doesn't show, SR can't really help.
If you stay in escrow and the gear doesn't show, you have options.
Find sellers that will let you stay in escrow - they are out there.
