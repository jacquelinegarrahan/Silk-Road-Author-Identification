Title: Re: Unread sent messages.. unread from me or the vendor??
Post by: ladyjane on March 19, 2013, 09:04 am
Quote from: top44 on March 18, 2013, 02:56 pm


Also i want to ask something silly. If i change the deposit address, will i lose the remaining USD of my balacne? hell no, right?

No that just changes the address for depositing bitcoins, and wont affect your actual balance in SR wallet.
However it seems to be generally recommended not to keep BC in your SR wallet, and move them to another wallet in the unlikely event SR goes down, or your account gets hacked.

I would just give it another day or two and see if it's been sent out of if you get a reply. Some vendors get many emails and orders each day so can't always reply straight away. Also if your last message to him was just confirming the order you actually placed, you might not get a reply as its not needed for the transaction.

hope that helps :) its probably a good idea to read through the forums a bit more too.
