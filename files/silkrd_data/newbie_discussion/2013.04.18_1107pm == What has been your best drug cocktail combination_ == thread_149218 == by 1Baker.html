A strange, but delightful mixture I discovered a few years ago was raw DXM hBR powder (600~ mg) with a few geltabs of LSD. My friends and I dubbed it "see-sawing", the experience was such that it was difficult to tell what substance you were on, rather than an obvious perceptual interplay.
