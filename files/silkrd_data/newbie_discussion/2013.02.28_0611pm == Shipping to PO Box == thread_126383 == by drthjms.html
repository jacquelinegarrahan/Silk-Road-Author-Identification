Title: Re: Shipping to PO Box
Post by: cake on February 28, 2013, 06:47 pm
PO Box? Not.

Do you plan on paying cash (bit weird) for a PO box that will only receive occasional packages that you anxiously check for?  Or if you use real bank details to pay for the PO box, that's a trail back to you too. Might as well use your actual address, and so as not to alarm the post man, your actual name, maybe slightly misspelled. Also if you get caught with a PO box there's no denying it through "I don't know why I was sent this!"

Anonymity is more important on the financial transactions with currency to bitcoin to SR.
