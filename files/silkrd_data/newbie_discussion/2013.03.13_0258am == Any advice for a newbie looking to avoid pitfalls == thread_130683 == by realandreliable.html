The hard part is changing your BTC into your local currency. In the US, the IRS doesn't need to justify to a judge why they should audit someone. One solution I've heard of is to cash out anonymously (not sure how to do this step), take your cash to a casino, play for a few hours, and report your large sum of chips as gambling earnings and pay your taxes there. Not sure how well it works or anything though.
