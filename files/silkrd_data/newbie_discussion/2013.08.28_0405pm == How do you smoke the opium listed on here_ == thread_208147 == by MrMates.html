Title: Re: How do you smoke the opium listed on here?
Post by: pills_where? on August 29, 2013, 09:50 am
A few friends and I got our hands on some opium several years ago.  I talked to an OG drug user, and got some advice (since it was the first time I had seen opium) and she instructed me to do the following (which worked well.  I took a nail with a very flat head on it.  Put that on a table sharp  side up near the edge of the table.  Attach the opium to the sharp end of the nail.  I would then light the opium and put a glass above it to catch the smoke.  Put the glass on the table, and let the smoke fill the glass.  I would then slide the glass to the edge of the table allowing me to insert a bendy straw into the glass without losing the smoke.  Then I would simply breath in what I felt like breathing.  and not lose the rest of the smoke in the glass.
