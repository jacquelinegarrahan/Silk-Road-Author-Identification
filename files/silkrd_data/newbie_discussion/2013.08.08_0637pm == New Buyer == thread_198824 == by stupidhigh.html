Title: Re: New Buyer
Post by: microbabe on August 08, 2013, 07:04 pm
Hey

PGP encrypts whatever your sending.  Only the vendor (or whoever has the specific PGP key you encrypted with), can decrypt your message. Meaning law enforcement or any other snooper can't view what you seeing.  This is just an extra measure of protection, but I would go as far as to say that PGP is REQUIRED! It is very easy to use, just do a quick google, theres a few good youtube videos.  You can start using it in under 15 mins.  I personally use GPG4win.  It's free and easy man. Trust me, use it.  I can't stress this enough.
