Post by: Euphoria4907 on August 31, 2013, 10:25 pm
I'm pretty comfortable, a few rules of thumb that I always go by:
1. Always look for sellers with great reputations. Not only just a good rating, but sellers who have many transactions and good feedback regarding their customer service and communication. You want sellers who will put you and your safety first.
2. Make your first orders domestic. Without customs, the chances that your product does not arrive to you are next to none. Also keep international orders to a minimum, the DEA won't come bust your door down for a few pills, they will just shake there finger and say "tsk-tsk".
3. Do not put yourself in a place where you have to FE, and look for sellers who offer refunds.
4. A certain seller might offer a given product cheaper than another, but the extra money may well be worth it to buy from a better seller.
5. Don't accuse the seller of doing something wrong, it will piss them off.
