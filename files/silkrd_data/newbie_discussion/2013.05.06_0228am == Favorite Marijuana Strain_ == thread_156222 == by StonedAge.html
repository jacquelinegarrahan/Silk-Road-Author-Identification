Post by: AstralMorrison on May 06, 2013, 02:41 am
My absolute favorite strain is this premium stuff I get from Seattle, my dealer simply calls it "Seattle". Not sure if it's the same strain, but I also bought an O of stuff from Seattle (same dealer) still in the medical bag which was labeled "Dark Horse", it was completely purple, unlike the usual Seattle stuff I get, and I didn't smoke any of it so I don't know. But this shit is seriously the craziest stuff on earth. Literally covered in trichomes, it's a fresh, healthy looking green and it doesn't even feel like regular bud, it feels slightly damp and almost like a "real" plant. The high is extremely psychedelic; OEVs all over, puts you into a deep thinking state, as well as being euphoric as fuck. Only downside is extreme dry mouth, but not too bad on red eye and munchies (which some may consider a good thing anyway).

My favorite commercial strain is Jack The Ripper; along the same vein as my Seattle but less potent and a ton more red eye and munchies.
