I don't think anyone will send you a free sample... As for getting bitcoins, it depends on where you are to find a seller. I do advise using localbitcoins.com to get bitcoins (since you're not in the US) rather than buying from a seller on here, you could get scammed. Localbitcoins helps you find bitcoin sellers near your area and you arrange a meeting where you pay cash, and the guy sends bitcoins to your wallet right in front of you. It's a lot safer than sending money to someone you don't know in my opinion, and also cheaper since the sellers on here take huge fees.

Post by: martine on April 06, 2013, 06:16 pm
It depends what drug you're looking for but lots of vendors have "sample" listings. They're usually 1-2 dose quantities. If anything goes wrong they're usually small enough that you don't have to worry about cops (it just gets nicked @ customs in most countries), and the monetary risk is small.

Vendors know that people won't drop a grand without sampling the stuff first - you can also PM a vendor and see if they'll do a smaller quantity, but I'd wait until you have a few transactions under your belt. Buy an ebook or something from the site first to get an idea of how it works. Use PGP, etc.
