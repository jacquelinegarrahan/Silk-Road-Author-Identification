Title: Re: music problem - playlist whilst on your drug
Post by: TR0N on June 01, 2013, 08:35 pm
Music is everything on my trips. Recently had a great trip to the "Mad Blunted Jazz" collection.

Whenever I know I'm going to trip, I usually crawl torrent trackers for a day or two and look for something interesting. Depending how I'm feeling, I might use keywords like "ambient" or "chillout" or "electro" or "psytrance". I'll download a bunch of new stuff, and sample a few tracks from each collection I download. When I feel I've found a collection that is put together well, is in the right vein, and would be good to trip to after auditioning a few songs, I just throw the whole collection into a playlist. This way, I can quality control my experience and at the same time I've only heard bits of a few songs, so all the music will be fresh and new to me. This strategy always produces great results for me.

If you really want to trip yourself the fuck out, throw on some Shpongle. Lol.
