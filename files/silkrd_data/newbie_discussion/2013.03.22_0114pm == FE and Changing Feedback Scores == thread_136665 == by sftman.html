Title: FE and Changing Feedback Scores
Post by: sftman on March 22, 2013, 01:14 pm
First thing I want to say NEVER FE.  But it you're one of the people who have finalized early and regretted it, you can change your feedback later even if you FE'd.  Just go to you account page and click on the view feedback link on that page.  From there click edit and you can make any changes you want.  Many people don't know about this feature because it's hidden on the side of the account page.  If you've been burned, it's at least some consolation that you can make the vendor pay for it in his ranking and score.  It seems like a lot of bad vendors who require people to FE have great scores just because a lot of people give 5 feedback in the hope that they will get their order, but then don't change it later when something goes wrong, as it often does with those vendors.

If it was a stealth listing your feedback comments may not show up on the bottom of the vendors page, the the score still is included in the calculations of his % rating and ranking.
