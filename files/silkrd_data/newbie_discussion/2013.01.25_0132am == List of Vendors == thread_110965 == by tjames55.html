Title: Re: List of Vendors
Post by: #slothrop on January 25, 2013, 02:04 am
I'll reply to your first post with a first post of my own.
My thoughts are a) such a list would be so long it wouldn't really be all that helpful b) because new vendors appear all the time and old ones occasionally drop out for weeks, months or all time, such a list would be difficult to maintain and c) part of the fun of SR is getting to know the site and the vendors who populate it, that a list would not only ruin this "fun" but perhaps be counterproductive, if not downright dangerous. Look at the the accusations that get leveled at the LSD Avengers list. They may be few and far between, but a list that attempts to describe the whole site would be a far messier situation (assuming that you're looking for more than just a list of names, which doesn't seem worth the time it would take as far as I'm concerned). To the best of my knowledge, no such list exists.
