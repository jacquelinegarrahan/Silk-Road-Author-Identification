Title: Re: how long bitcoins take for arriving from localbitcoins.com to SilkRoad account ?
Post by: psychedelicmind on July 13, 2013, 08:50 am
As well as the website, the blockchain is a log of all bitcoin transactions. You can also have a wallet on blockchain.info. So yeah, if you send the coins from LB to SR, the transaction will be on the blockchain.

You can search for your transaction on blockchain by putting the destination wallet into the search function on the right hand side of blockchain.info. You will see it under the 'Home, Most recently mined blocks in the bitcoin block chain'. When you put in the destination address, you can see how many confirmations your transaction has had.

I hope this answers your questions ;)
