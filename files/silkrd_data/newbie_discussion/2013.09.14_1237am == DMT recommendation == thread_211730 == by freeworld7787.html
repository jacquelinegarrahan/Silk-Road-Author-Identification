Post by: soviet on September 14, 2013, 05:58 am
I can personally vouch for DRshrooms' DMT. Buy the white stuff.

Firstly and most importantly, you need to practice your smoking technique and get used to the experience overall with smaller doses. Especially if you have never smoked anything before. As tempting as it sounds, don't try to blast off on your first try. I can almost guarantee you won't be able to.

When you smoke the DMT, you need to VAPORIZE it. This means you need to build yourself a vaporizer. Fairly simple, you just need to be creative.
A guide on how to build "The Machine" : *clearnet* https://www.dmt-nexus.me/forum/default.aspx?g=posts&t=25974
There are a few vaporizing methods on YouTube.
My favorite: I essentially made a light-bulb vaporizer (*clearnet* http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wmixODIvAtM) out of a round-bottomed glass flask.
DO NOT use the sandwich method where you place the DMT in a bong/pipe in between 2 layers of weed. There is too much risk that you'll burn the precious DMT. (The flame should never be in direct contact with DMT. It will burn it and destroy it.)

When you heat it up the powder will turn into liquid, and then as you heat it more it will begin to smoke very slightly. This is when you know it's vaporizing, so this is when you inhale. (Long, deep inhales. As many as you possibly can as quickly as you can). It will taste utterly disgusting btw, but it's worth it.

As for how much you should buy, it depends on how many people are doing it with you. 15-25mg is a normal dose. 35-45+mg is a breakthrough dose. It would be very wise to invest in a milligram scale. (You can find one cheap on Amazon.) Dosage varies from person to person. I recommend you buy 200-300mg, so you have enough for you and your friends to practice your smoking technique on. Buy white DMT. I don't think there's much difference with yellow DMT and white DMT, it's just that I can personally vouch for white.

All the information you're looking for is all over the internet. Do your research. Good luck!
