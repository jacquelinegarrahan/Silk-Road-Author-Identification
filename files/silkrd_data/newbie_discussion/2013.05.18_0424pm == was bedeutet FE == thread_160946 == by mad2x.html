I stuck your post in Google Translate, so hopefully I got the right meaning :P

FE means Finalize Early, which means that you release your funds from escrow (SilkRoad's safe keeping) before you receive your item in the post. Some vendors require this, and for new users it may be difficult to purchase without FE. It isn't recommended you FE as it puts your money at risk, there can be no dispute if the item is lost in the post!
