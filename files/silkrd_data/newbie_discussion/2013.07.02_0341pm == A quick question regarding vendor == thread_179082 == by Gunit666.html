Glad everything turns out well. Just want to share a piece of information which might be useful. Few days ago I posted "We're going on holiday from this date to this date" and keep my vendor page online while we're out of town, because we want customers to know that we're on holiday, and not disappearing. When I'm back, my vendor page is on vacation mode (not listed). This is a proof that SR care about buyers by preventing them from placing an order while the vendor is on vacation.
