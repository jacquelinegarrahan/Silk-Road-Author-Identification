Post by: AussieMitch on February 06, 2013, 11:29 am
By itself Alprazolam isn't particularly dangerous in regards to overdose potential but mixed with stimulant drugs it can kill you.

I've had one friend die from a combination of Alprazolam (Oral) and Methamphetamine (Smoked) and then a couple of years later I had a second friend die from a combination of MDMA (Snorted), Ketamine (Snorted) & Alprazolam (Oral). I've also had an extremely bad experience using Alprazolam to try and take the edge of a MDPV binge, it didn't do what I thought it would (Relax me) and instead made me feel extremely dysphoric and depressed, and worsened the heart palpitations I was having from the MDPV.

BE CAREFUL WITH THESE COMBINATIONS!!
