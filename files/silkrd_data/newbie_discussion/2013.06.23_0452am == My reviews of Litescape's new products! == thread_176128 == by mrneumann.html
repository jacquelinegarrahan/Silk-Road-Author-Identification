Title: Re: My reviews of Litescape's new products!
Post by: mrneumann on June 24, 2013, 10:01 pm
Now for my review of the cannabis enhancer. It arrived with the pills in the same style packaging (and rubber banded together by the post office) from the same "company" sender label. The packaging was clean with no flaws in anyway. After I ripped open and dumped out the package, a brown vial fell into my palm. The labeling was clean, boasting the wonderfully creative title "Everlasting Coughstopper" a creative play on words from Charlie and the Chocolate Factory. That name became even more appropiate after I unscrewed the bottle and took a whif of the sweetly pungent aroma. Reminiscent of Willy Wonka's chocolate, the smell had notes of melted chocolate with a slight herbal aroma as well, like a very light sage or turmeric. The color and cosistancy also reminds one of a melted chocolate. The lid to the vial contained a useful brush applicator, like a paint brush; convienent for glazing bowls with this beautiful smelling liquid. One vial was amost as wide as my palm, and with the diameter of a penny, holding enough for many many bowls.

I hurridly sandwiched the enhancing liquid between a bowl of ganja. A friend sat with me to share the weed and offer his oppinions for the review as well. Almost immediately after the first light, I noticed how hard you need to smoke it, and how gentle and forgiving it is on your lungs. The sticky liquid helps pack bowls tight, and doesn't allow for too much airflow, but it's okay because you can honestly take an entire lungful of smoke without so much as a tickle, as if you were breathing in air. The bowl also needs to be burned a little hotter to ensure proper vaporization. As for the taste, it leaves subtle herbal flavor, like incense or burning herbs. It isn't the most pleasant smoke, but the taste is more than bearable and it will be washed away completely with a small drink of water. The smoke from a stronger weed will also be efficient to completely mask the taste. But like I said, it is not an awful taste, and may even be a pleasant addition to their sweet mary jane for some.

Lets talk about the high. One bowl split between my friend and I would hardly be enough to put me where I want to be. This was most certainly not the case when the coughstopper was added. Personally, I dont know if it was just because of the higher smoke intake to the lungs, or if it actually increases cannabinoid effenciency to your receptors, but I can assure you that it this product is no placebo. I did not tell my friend about it's effects other than mostining the smoke and easing pain on the lungs, and he was visibly more high than he would have ever gotten with a single bowl. In conclusion, I personally believe this product to be absolutely worth it; it will help save you money on weed by enhancing bowls, and will protect your lungs from the wear and tear of harsh dry smoke and coughing. Overall I give this product a 5/5 all the way around. An excellent seller with a unique and helpful product.

5 stars all around for an excellent vendor!!



Side notes:
1) Do not use with a one-hitter or anything with a very small bowl. It packs so tight using a dugout that smoking became almost impossible. Eventually I figured out how to smoke it, but it does not pack as cleanly as a larger bowl would have.
2) I smoked a small amount on a cigarette (I do not smoke cigarettes often) as well as out of a freebase pipe, to make sure it was not laced with anything psychoactive like synthetic cannabinoids. The conclusion I came to is that the blend alone is not noticeably psychoactive, though i had quite a buzz from the cigarette (it could have been from the nicotine alone, as my tolerance is almost none) it went away fast and felt almost nothing worthwhile from the freebase pipe.
3) Do not try to smoke with cigarettes, it is a messy waste of product and a cigarette. Overall not a good idea, but thats why I'm reviewing, so others can learn from my experiences.

I hope everyone enjoyed my reviews. If anyone has any questions comments or critiques, please post them here or PM me. Thank you for reading!
