Post by: mcguire39 on June 12, 2013, 07:17 pm
It'll be fine, IF you copied exactly your SR deposit address. It does seem to take several hours. I believe that has to do with the # of blockchain verifications required, i.e. making sure that the majority of servers agree with the transaction.

Spend this time getting what you want loaded into your cart, and make up your order message, practice encrypting it, etc.,  then when the BTC arrive you can use it right away.

Post by: TheGhosst on June 12, 2013, 07:18 pm
Every transaction must clear a set number of confirmations before the funds are confirmed, and show up in your account balance for that BTC address.

What you do is visit: blockchain.info (copy and paste that url)

Then search for YOUR btc address(that you sent the funds to), and you should see the pending transaction and the number of confirmations the trans has. :P

No worries!
