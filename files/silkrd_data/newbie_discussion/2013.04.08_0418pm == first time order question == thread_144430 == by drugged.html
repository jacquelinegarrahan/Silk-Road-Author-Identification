Title: Re: first time order question
Post by: NoName4534 on April 08, 2013, 04:40 pm
Eventually the order will be marked as "in transit" and you will have the option to either finalize the payment, or to go to dispute resolution if the order does not arrive.

The order will also autofinalize after a fixed amount of time so that the seller will still get their money even if you've forgotten to finalize. An autofinalize is a bad thing to have on your account though.
