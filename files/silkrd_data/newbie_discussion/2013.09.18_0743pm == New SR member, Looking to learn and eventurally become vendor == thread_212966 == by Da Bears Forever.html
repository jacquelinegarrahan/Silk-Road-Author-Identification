Title: Re: New SR member, Looking to learn and eventurally become vendor
Post by: mountaincedar on September 19, 2013, 02:35 am
I would suggest that you learn from being a buyer.  Make small purchases from a number of vendors who now sell what you want to offer.  Make notes about their communications, shipping methods and speed, stealth methods, etc.  Read the vendors' home pages.  Get an idea of how you want to present your self and what will be your terms of service.  Learn PGP. Your first priority is the privacy and safety of your buyers.
