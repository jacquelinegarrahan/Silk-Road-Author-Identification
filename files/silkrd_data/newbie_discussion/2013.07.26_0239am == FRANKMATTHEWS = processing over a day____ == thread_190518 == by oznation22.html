Title: Re: FRANKMATTHEWS = processing over a day????
Post by: odd on July 26, 2013, 04:25 am
hey oz everything will be just fine.  The first time is always incredibly stressful.  I was so afraid i was getting scammed my very first online drug purchase.   Really you've got it good.  When i started doing this silk road wasn't around yet and you would just send your cash to a complete stranger and hope your product would arrive.  We didn't have things like escrow, marked transit, tracking numbers.  You just sent off your money and prayed lol.  At that time to it would take almost three weeks for an international order to arrive.  Just remember your safe and protected as long as you didn't FE.  Sometimes it can take a day or two for a vendor to respond to a message.  Remember he is not a local dealer who handles 5-6 deals a day.  He's processing orders in vast amounts reading/answering emails, packing and sending packages (which can sometimes mean driving around to several drop boxes and can take a few hours) plus still deal with their own daily lives.  This is a pretty demanding job and remember they don't notifications on their smart phones when they receive a new message.  So in the future i recommend sending one message and waiting a few days for a response before sending another one.  Just be patient and breathe and enjoy the silk road :)  Hope i could help.
