Title: Re: How do I know if a seller is serious or not?
Post by: p3nd8s on February 04, 2013, 09:36 am
You need over 100 posts to leave karma. To see if a vendor is serious, here's a checklist:

1. Make sure they posted their PGP key on the listing/vendor profile as well as the forum.
2. Make sure they have a forum review thread, read the reviews. If they don't have a review thread, that's a big minus.
3. Do a search fo the vendor name on the forum and see what people are saying.
4. Karma is not an indicator of trustworthiness as some people leave bad karma for comments, view points, or products that they don't agree with (i.e. cyanide).
5. Obviously check the reviews on the item you are interested in as well as the vendor profile reviews for all items.
6. See how long they have been in business and the number of transactions along with the total rating (0-100).
