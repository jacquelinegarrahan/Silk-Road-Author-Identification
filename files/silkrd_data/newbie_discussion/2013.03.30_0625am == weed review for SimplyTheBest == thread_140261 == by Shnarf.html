I bought a 1g sample of Agent Orange, hoping it would be more loyal to his name then all of the Orange Bud strains I've found on here so far.
Because of these disappointing experiences I didn't have high expectations on this order either.  :(

I don't remember how long it took to arrive, but it was surprisingly fast.(2-3 days, faster then orders from neighbor countries sometimes)
As soon as I got it unwrapped I knew this was the shit, never smelled actual orange smell like that on a strain!
It tastes as delicious as it smells too.
On top of that, the gram was 0.8 overweight.

Bottomline; there is no doubt in my mind I will be ordering again.
