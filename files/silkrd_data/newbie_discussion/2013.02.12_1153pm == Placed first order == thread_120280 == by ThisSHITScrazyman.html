Post by: The_Real_Batman on March 06, 2013, 03:49 am
Well usually SL will send out an automated message after 7 days of you have yet to finalize. Which they assume, you either haven't received your package, or you did and haven't finalized. In the case that you haven't, they open up the option to resolve. Usually before you do this, you would contact the vendor of the issue. Usually they can resolve this themselves, if not SL will try and play the middle man to resolve it for you. If you've read proper reviews of the vendor, you shouldn't have a problem. Of course I understand your concern though.

Best of luck.
