It is our hope that you take into account our foresight to have such contingencies in place in order to continue operations, albeit with a modest delay, and our willingness to compensate for our error without prompting that will instill in you a measure of trust and forestall a loss of confidence or loss of credibility.

As there were no other large to bulk sized Adderall suppliers on SR from whose sales we could make accurate projections of demand from, we entered new territory without having  such information and we simply were found to have underestimated.

Experience is an austere teacher, because she gives the test first, the lesson afterwards.
