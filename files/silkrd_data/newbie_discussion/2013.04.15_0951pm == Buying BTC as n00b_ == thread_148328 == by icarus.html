Post by: lettsindig on April 16, 2013, 09:00 am
maybe others have posted similar,same or even better solutions, but here's what I know to work and what seems easy and good to me:

make an account on www.bitstamp.net , click Deposit and select method (I use international bank wire transfer,  easy and fast), select amount of euro/dollar to send them and click Send.
Then they give you their bank details,  account number etc., and a message you include when you send the money which includes your unique account ID.

Wait a day or two after sending wire and it should have arrived, and the Bitstamp account has been credited so your account has a dollar/euro balance.  Now you can click  "Buy bitcoins",  I usually do it the simple and default way which is just entering the amount of dollar I wish to buy bitcoins for, and it buys bitcoins at market value until that amount has been spent,  this process is usually done in a minute from you click Buy.
Then the balance in the account shows the Bitcoin you just bought.
Now those bitcoins can be withdrawn to the Silk Road bitcoin address that is listed under 'Account', and after sending bitcoin from Bitstamp to Silk Road it usually takes an hour or few until your Silk Road account has been credited and you are ready to spend the funds on drugs.

Maybe not the safest or otherwise best method but, what Ive been using for over a year now I think, many times and has never failed me yet.  Also I dont think your real name is required, as I have set up transfer from foreign bank accounts sent from other names, they just included the 'Message' with the transfer so Bitstamp knew to credit my account, and I always got the money.



in short just setup an account with bitstamp.net and click to create a deposit, send the funds according to the details they gave on the deposit page.  then short wait before the cash is in ur account which u can buy bitcoin with, which u can then choose to withdraw to your Silk Road bitcoin address, which should have your account credited within a few hours and ready to start spending. 
select a drug off the menu and enter the address to have it delivered to,enter pin and click Send.. lil more waiting and dinner is served!
