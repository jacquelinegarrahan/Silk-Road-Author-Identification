Post by: lssb2132 on July 16, 2013, 04:44 am
Assuming green plant growing means weed, then that's also a great idea. There are never enough domestic vendors for weed, it's better now but a few weeks ago the weed listings for domestic were super dry. If possible I'd say do both, either on its own is fine too though. If/when you do start up a vendor account it never hurts to make an offer in the product listings forum, they don't have to be free either just discounted is good. Just make sure if you do them to give them to people who can show you their previous reviews so you won't be sending out product and getting nothing back for it. It shows good will and an intent to be a reliable/long standing vendor when doing offers imo. As for vendor stuff I think just learning PGP is a must, many buyers won't touch vendors who don't use PGP because it shows a lack of understanding/care sort of. Also be completely honest about your product quality and try your hardest to get actual pictures of the product you will be selling.(A lot of vendors just rip pictures of google images etc and that can raise some red flags sometimes)
That's all I can think of at the moment, if you have any questions or whatever I'll try to help out.
