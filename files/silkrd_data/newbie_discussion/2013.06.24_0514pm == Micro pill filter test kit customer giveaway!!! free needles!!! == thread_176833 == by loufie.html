The information should be based on what your carrier is offering you. Assume you have already worked out your chosen methods of shipping to different destinations, and possible additional services some customers may require - tracking, registered mail, express etc.

I would make a clear separation between Domestic & International - and try not to offer too many options;

Domestic - Express Post - 0.10
International - Mail no tracking - 0.20
International - Express tracking - 0.40

Have a look to see what other worldwide vendors are doing with this, too many options can become confusing. Also you must remember that the service you are using must be accurately represented in the title, more detail is then usually listed in the body of the product listing (if required).
