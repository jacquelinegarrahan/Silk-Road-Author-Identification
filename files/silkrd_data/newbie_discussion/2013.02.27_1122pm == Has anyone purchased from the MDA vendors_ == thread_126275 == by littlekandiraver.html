Yeah my first SR order was 1g of MDA from the ILF.  Was my first time trying it, I've had X and molly before.  ILF is a great vendor, very good communication and I got my order in 3 days.  Great packaging.  I have tried it twice so far, the first time was about 80mg (not sure, I didn't yet have a good scale) and I felt it pretty strong for 8-10 hours, much longer than I was expecting.  It was different but I enjoyed it overall.

When I get the chance I intend to try a real dose of about 100+mg and see where that takes me.  At my doses I feel like I got a lot of body high but not much visuals.  The first time I did see minor visuals and watched some trippy videos which definitely showed me the stuff was working.  The second time I got no visuals at all, just the body effect.  I look forward to seeing where a higher dose takes me.

I highy recommend ILF.  Very nice person and great transaction.

I also give a +1 to The ILF.  Based on what brain receptors the drugs have the most affinity for, MDA is more visual than MDMA, MDMA is more of an empathogen (love drug).  But both have both effects just to different degrees.  And of course individual experience depends a lot on the skill and integrity of the chemist (meaning is what you think is MDMA really MDMA, vs. MDMA + impurities + adulterants like caffeine?)   So that's how pure M.D.A. and MDMA are supposed to be different based on their chemistries, but individual experiences may differ from that.
