 My first ever drug experience was LSD, and I've been a regular psychedelic user since then for the almost the last two years. I dosed with three of my friends at approximately 11:35 PM, all taking one blotter, but two of my friends got theirs from a different source. The come up was very gradual and extremely gentle, and I spent it playing Pokemon Crystal on an emulator on my phone as we walked to a park nearby to lie down and look at the stars and just chill out and stuff. I finished beating the Goldenrod City gym and was noticing some effects, particularly a warm lightness to my body, and glows and auras around lights. We walked back to my friend's house after about thirty minutes and put Pink Floyd's Animals on the turntable, and decided it would be fun to play the original Star Fox on the SNES. I didn't play myself, but I watched my friends take turns. Staring at all the ridiculous combinations of rectangles and squares that make up all the on screen objects made me realize how much more I had come up than when I was lying down outside. Everything was glowing dimly and warmly, and because the game has a lot of frame rate issues, I was perceiving myself slowing down and speeding up the game in my head.

Probably about an hour into playing, I was noticing lots of time distortion, much more apparent visual effects, as well as the most relieving and relaxing body high. However, one of my friends (who was having his first psychedelic experience), starting feeling nauseous and ran to the bathroom. He didn't end up throwing up, but he was a little startled and claustrophobic feeling, so we decided to go back outside again. After we managed to collect ourselves enough to go back outside (we all had the giggles), it was around 2 AM, so we had to be much quieter, which in turn made it harder to control our laughs. The night sky was extremely clear, and though the moon was about a half moon, it was still very bright. All the stars created a sort of blanket across the sky, it felt as if we were in a dome or massive planetarium. I was having extremely lucid, intense, and impressive visuals, both OEV and CEV. The stars were all connecting with each other, dancing and wiggling, and we saw several shooting stars, all of which trailed incredibly.

We walked around some more, mostly letting our friend who was having his first trip lead the way, and it was amusing watching him figure out what it was like to trip. We returned back inside after about thirty minutes, though it felt like several hours. We decided that it looked like a lot of fun to build play with legos, since we were all feeling very imaginative and curious. It turned out to be one of the most enjoyable and hilarious things I've done on drugs, tears were streaming down my face nearly the entire time from laughing. I started to realize what a happy environment we had created, that it was just positivity and good vibes; we were just having pure fun. Legos are just pure fun, and it makes so much sense that they've become what they are to our culture. Like, just imagine the ridiculousness of the fact that we have computer games that you just build legos in. I digress.

I was still feeling some noticeably powerful visual effects, even after about three and a half hours in, and it was intensified once we smoked weed. We started cleaning up all of our legos and dismantling our absurd little creations after we realized how exhausted and tired our stomachs and lungs were from all the laughter, and also that we were pretty hungry. We spent a good thirty or forty-five minutes looking for real food, not just snacks, and wanted to make pancakes but were held back from the fact that all the milk in the fridge expired weeks ago. We all decided to wait until McDonald's opened up for breakfast at 5, and then drove to the one nearby (I do NOT condone driving under the influence of drugs, it's a very risky and dangerous decision to yourself and others around you. However, my friend who took a hit from the other source said that it was not nearly as intense as what I was feeling, and though he was definitely tripping, he felt capable of driving safely. Additionally, he's always seemed to require bigger doses of things to feel significant effects).

A lot of the visual effects had faded away, though my perception was still noticeably off, particularly judging distance and vibrant colors. We drove home and listened to more records and just hung out and talked about what we had all been experiencing and reflected on things, and decided to smoke more weed before lying down to go to bed. This was at about 6:30, and I think it took me an hour to finally fall asleep, after finally losing interest in the different chirping of all the birds outside.

Overall, it was a wonderful experience, one of the best trips I've had. I definitely want to experience dosing more next time, as this was already so powerful at just a single hit.
