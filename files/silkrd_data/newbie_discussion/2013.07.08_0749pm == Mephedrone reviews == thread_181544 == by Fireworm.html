Title: Mephedrone reviews
Post by: Fireworm on July 08, 2013, 07:49 pm
My first three month report on SR is that the Amazon-for-drugs tag is well deserved in terms of most areas. The one glaring problem, no doubt obvious to all, is the one that we all have when buying in the prohibition regime elsewhere, namely that quality can be very varied, even if the dealer is easy/honest to work with as the two in this report have been.

As a fairly hard-core mephedrone user from the UK legal days, injecting IM, I can say that nothing I so far have tried of the three lots I have had is up to the quality of the legal market, and some is very bad. I see reports like this as key to the efficient development of SR, and I intend to keep this thread updated with the various excitements I trip over in the meph market here.

My opinion is only that, but it's based on experience using the material without other substances in IM in doses varying from 75 to 220 mg.

LIMETLESS.

The cheaper stuff from Lim does not give the euphoric lift of the real deal, but is very speedy. Fine if you want to rush around, and not too bad on hangover. 4/10
The better stuff lim offers is much more fun and I got a real whiff of the old days, but again a bit on the speedy side. 7/10

SONICDRONE
This material is the worst I have tried and has a come-down worse than MDMA. I don't know what it is but its relation to mephedrone is tenuous. Speedy. 2/10

It does occur to me that if SR offered a testing service for dealers to send samples and they linked the report to their marketing it might be welcome to everyone; scammers would not be so delighted of course, but it would be a further boost for the integrity of the SR project.
