Title: Re: Using a dead mans identity to order from SR
Post by: JabbatheSlut on June 21, 2013, 03:09 am
If you're still getting mail for the guy you could, I guess.

I think it all depends on what you intend purchasing, where it will be shipped from and how severe the penalties are in your local jurisdiction.

For instance, if you were in Australia and you were to receive a small quantity of just about anything from a local vendor, then - so long as the address you receive the goodies at isn't a bust waiting to happen - then you could probably get away with using any name including your own.

If, however, you were receiving anything from overseas, the simple fact that you would be effectively "importing" increases the potential penalties and thus, the risk.

It is always preferable to be safe.
