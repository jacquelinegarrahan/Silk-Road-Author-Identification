Title: Re: Should your SR username and your forum username be seperate?
Post by: Smarties on March 26, 2013, 02:22 am
It's totally up to you. Smarties is my allias user name. I have another forum user name which matches my real account name on the Road. I only use that one for writing reviews of sellers products - so they know it's me.

Think about it any vendor you've ever used obviously knows your full name and address so I prefer to use an allias 90% of the time just to retain that added bit of anonymity.

Not that I have any evidence of this actually occurring but say you write an awful review of a vendors product or you have a major disagreement with one on here what's to stop a really shady vendor grassing you up to the police for drug possession? Double standards yes and also an extreme case it were to happen BUT you can never rule it out and you can hardly retaliate because you don't know where they live like they do you.

You can have as many accounts as you want both here and on the Road. I've been thinking about using a second Road account for my overseas orders. Basically due to international orders having a higher chance of being stopped by customs I would rather have a separate account for that so it doesn't effect my stats when making domestic orders.

The fact that you get allocated a new IP address every time you log onto Tor would mean that your two accounts on the Road would never be able to be linked together anyway.
