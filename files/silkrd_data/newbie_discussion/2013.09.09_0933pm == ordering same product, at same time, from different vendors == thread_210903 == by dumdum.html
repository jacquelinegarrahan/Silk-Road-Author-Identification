Title: Re: ordering same product, at same time, from different vendors
Post by: The Bubble Dreamer on September 09, 2013, 10:22 pm
I personally am a bit wary to have multiple orders arrive at the same time, because I think it reduces plausible deniability: If one package is intercepted, it is easy to maintain you have no idea where it came from. However, if more packages are delivered the next day, things may become more difficult to explain. I am by no means a lawyer though, so maybe this is just paranoia. And of course, in the case of small orders, the chances of interception are very low.

And my idea is: if I am consuming more than I get delivered by placing the next order as soon as the previous order arrives, I should probably cut down a bit :-)

That said, I also wouldn't ask the vendor, and Feesh suggestions are very good.
