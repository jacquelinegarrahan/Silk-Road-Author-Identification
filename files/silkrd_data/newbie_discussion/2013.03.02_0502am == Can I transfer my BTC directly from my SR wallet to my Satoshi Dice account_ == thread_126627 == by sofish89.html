whats a satoshi dice account?  I don't think you understand how SatoshiDice works...

you can send funds directly to the satoshi dice address for the odds you want to play, and you will receive your win or loss back to your SR wallet.  But why not use your own wallet to play SatoshiDice?  It would be faster, since your win/loss transaction won't have to go through the SR mixer.

But I wouldn't try this if I were you.  SR uses a mixer, so each bet would take a good bit of time to make it's way back to you.
