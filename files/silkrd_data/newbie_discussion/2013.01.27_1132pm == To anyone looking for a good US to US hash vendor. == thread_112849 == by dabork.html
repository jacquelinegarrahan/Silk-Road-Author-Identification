Might as well make my first post here something useful.

If you're looking for domestic hash but don't feel like dropping 35 bucks a gram check out UndergroundSyndicate's Moroccan hash.  I got a gram for my first purchase on the road and was completely satisfied.  I ordered it on a Monday night with priority shipping for about $26 total and had it in my hands (and under a cup) Friday afternoon.  Packaging was completely inconspicuous with no smell to speak of until I could actually see the hash.  The hash is hard but not brittle and can be sliced or shaved easily with a good razor.  It's also not too sticky, which made it very easy to manage.  The smoke is pure white and smooth as butter.  Slight hints of spice in the smoke and in the smell.  Very mellowing high without any real couch-lock.  I smoke about half the gram in small amounts, and will get a chance to smoke a nice chunk of it once my birthday comes around, which was the whole reason I bought it.  Overall, I had no complaints at all.  I had my insecurities about this site just like most people but if this guy is any example of what to expect from the high rated vendors around here, I will certainly not think twice about ordering again.  It's not the most potent hash here, but it did it's job just fine for me, it's also at $22/g right now, so it's close to the cheapest you can get.

10/10 all the way around, from packaging to product.
