Title: Re: Package marked delivered - never arrived
Post by: captainjojo on April 28, 2013, 09:03 pm
Did you ask the attendant where you PO box is if there is any mail that wouldn't it into the box?  That might be the reason it was marked delivered but not found.

How long has it been since it was marked delivered?  If it's only been a couple of hours it may be it was marked delivered when it was taken by the delivery person and may still be in transit to your box. 

That happened to me one time.  I checked the tracking and it said it was delivered, yet there was nothing in my mailbox.  It was delivered a few hours later.

It may also have been delivered to the wrong address and the person receiving it hasn't sent it back or just threw it out.

Finally, one other option is you have been scammed by the vendor.  They could easily send it to themselves or send an empty envelope to an address within your zip code, knowing it will be delivered. This is unlikely, considering the amount involved, not really worth it to go to all that trouble.
