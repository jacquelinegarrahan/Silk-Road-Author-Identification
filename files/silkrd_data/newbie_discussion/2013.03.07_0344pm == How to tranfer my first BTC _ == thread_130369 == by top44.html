Title: Re: How to tranfer my first BTC ?
Post by: letmelive1 on March 07, 2013, 04:18 pm
It Depends,

I like to ONLY send SR my BTC when ordering..if not i hold it in my Wallet....

BUT if you are looking to order ASAP, then sending it directly to SR would save you time and a small fee. (very small like .00005 btc)....

Remember, it takes time to "confirm" the bitcoin transaction, could be 10 minutes, could be 3 hours. So if u need something quick obviously its easier to go right to SR's wallet because its only 1 transaction.

Never share your SR pin, and NEVER sign in with it. The sign in's with the pin are phishing scams.
