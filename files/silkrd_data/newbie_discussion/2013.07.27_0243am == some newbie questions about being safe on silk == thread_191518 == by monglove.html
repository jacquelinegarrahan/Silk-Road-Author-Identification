Title: Re: some newbie questions about being safe on silk
Post by: gitlow on July 27, 2013, 03:42 am
There's no reason to fear having Bitcoin purchases connected to your bank account. They are not illegal, or even shady. The only concern is where you send them. Don't sens them directly to Silk Road. Move them around first. You're running them through BitcoinFog, which is good.

Don't sign for any package. That means don't use shipping options that require signature. Once you've signed for it, you've claimed ownership.

Ideally, don't open package for a couple days. I realize this advice is laughable if you're sick. It also means finalizing before you've verified contents, but that's OK because you can edit your feedback later if necessary (you should finalize immediately on delivery, for the vendor's sake). If no cop comes knocking in a couple days, indulge.

And as always, don't talk to cops. Ask if you're under arrest. If yes, ask for a lawyer then shut up. If no, ask if you're being detained (not the same as arrest, but you still can't walk away). If you're being detained but not arrested, politely inform cop you have nothing to say, and mean it. If you're not being detained, wish cop a nice day and close door/walk away.
