Title: Re: Approaching a vendor about resolutions
Post by: midlandsmafia on January 29, 2013, 11:35 am
If your package is coming internationally, I would suggest extending it. If it still hasn't came either extend again or If you think its taking to long ask about refunds/resends.

If it is domestic , message the vendor and ask them about their policy for refunds/resends.

Your vendor may have tracking info on the product, so it might be a good idea to ask them if it is normal to take this long to your country/city.
