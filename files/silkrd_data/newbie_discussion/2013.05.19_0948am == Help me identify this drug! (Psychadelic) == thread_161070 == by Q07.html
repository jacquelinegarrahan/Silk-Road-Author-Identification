Title: Help me identify this drug! (Psychadelic)
Post by: Q07 on May 19, 2013, 09:48 am
Ok so was interested in trying DMT, my friend said he could get some.  He got a 1G vial that according to the guy he got it from it's DMT deposited on an herb of some sort (I forget what it was called).  This was a while ago.  I now know from the road what DMT looks like and that what I got MIGHT have been changa.  I'm suspicious that it could have been Salvia or something else.

The material is definitely plant based and similar in size  to oregano maybe a little smaller but almost pure black and oily looking like it's had a lot of something dark deposited on it.  It smells kinda like nail polish and artificial fruit flavor, but not quite as bad as that sounds.

I burned a small amount like a pinch of the stuff with a direct lighter flame using a carb and it blew me out of the universe.  I forgot who and what I was or anything I was in the void.  All the objects in the room were 2 dimensional and laid together like a ream of paper.  I was one of the "papers" and the whole thing was moving forward like a slow river.  There was some more uncomfortable visuals and the first bit of reality that materialized was my friends face.  Once it was fully formed I figured out who he was and then realization of what just happened and WTF was going on.  The whole room was still tearing like an oil painting on high speed.  I felt this strong impulse to lean forward/throw my legs up (I was sitting in a chair).  I laughed because I knew how ridiculous it was but gave in and did it anyway. 

The visuals faded pretty quickly at that point and the whole thing lasted less than 10 minutes.  It was fairly uncomfortable and certainly didn't leave me begging for more.  Very intense. 

My wife tried it and enjoyed it quite a bit, she says all the objects were coming apart like decks of cards.  I have at least one dose left that he left me but I want to try salvia for comparison before I try it again.  I would try DMT as well but don't have the cohones to order some at this time.
