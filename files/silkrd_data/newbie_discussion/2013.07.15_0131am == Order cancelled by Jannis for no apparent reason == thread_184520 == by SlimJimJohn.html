Title: Re: Order cancelled by Jannis for no apparent reason
Post by: The Missus on July 15, 2013, 01:39 am
Well, it's probably because you're a newbie. A lot of vendors don't risk placing orders for newbs because they want to stay protected and avoid serving LE or getting scammed by new time buyers who threaten leaving 1/5 unless they get free product. I suggest making yourself known on the forums, it makes a huge difference. Try placing an order with vendors that will work with new buyers and don't require you to FE. Also buy small at first, test out the waters. And always read the vendors profile page to avoid wasting time. Check them out on the forums as well.
