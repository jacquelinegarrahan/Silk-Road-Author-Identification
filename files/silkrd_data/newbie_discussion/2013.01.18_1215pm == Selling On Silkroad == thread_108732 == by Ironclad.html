Title: Re: Selling On Silkroad
Post by: MaidMandy on January 18, 2013, 05:25 pm
You have to upgrade to a sellers account to be able to put up listings. To do that, click 'Account' >>> Go to right hand side of page, see list. Click 'become a seller'.

It will cost you approx $500 to get a sellers account, and you have to have that in bitcoin to pay for it. There are numerous threads on the forums on how to obtain bitcoin. Just do plenty of research and be sure you know you're being safe when it comes to getting the coins to SR from the clear web. :)
