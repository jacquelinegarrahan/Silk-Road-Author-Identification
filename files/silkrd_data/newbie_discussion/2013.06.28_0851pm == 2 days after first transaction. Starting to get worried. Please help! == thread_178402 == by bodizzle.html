Post by: YolkyPalky on June 28, 2013, 11:25 pm
There is a really good read about CD's (that's Controlled Deliveries for fellow Newbies) "stickied" in the Shipping section, titled: Topic: Profiling postal package 101 straight from the feds.  Worth the read.

Anyway, a CD is no small task, many people are involved behind the scenes, not to mention getting a search warrant to search your premises after you take delivery.  That's really why they want you to take the package, it allows them probable cause to search your home, computer, etc.

However, I highly doubt they're going to jump through all those hoops for a measly 1 gram of coke for an individual user.  No, they are targeting the bigger time resellers and dealers to make it worth the effort.  And anyway, a vendor worth his salt should be able to easily stealth ship such a small quantity like that.

I could be wrong, maybe LE would do a CD for 50 pills of oxy, or a few joints, or 1 gram of coke, but I got to believe they'd rather spend the energy getting a much bigger fish that won't be able to walk so easily with a competent lawyer.
