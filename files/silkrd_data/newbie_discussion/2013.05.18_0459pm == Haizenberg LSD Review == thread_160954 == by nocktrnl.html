** Haizenberg LSD Review. **

I am very satisfied with Haizenberg.

Shipping to Midwest USA from The Czech Republic took 13 days.
I tried his product last Friday, the same day I received it.

His product is very good, I ate a five-strip and had a very insightful trip.

I ordered 15 hits of his Gandalf blotter, I decided to test it out with five hits.
I held the five-strip between my lip and gum for about 40 minutes until I finally swallowed it, there was no taste to speak of.
The come up started after about 20 mins, I peaked at about T+ 1:30 and that lasted for awhile.. I would like to be more specific about the duration, but time is just one of those things that stops mattering when your in the middle of a good trip.

Haizenberg's acid feels very clean, with no notable body-load. I was very clear-headed with OEV including kaleidoscopes of color and tracers, color was greatly enhanced. The trip was separated into two distinct halves for me. The first part very introspective and visual, the second part euphoric and outgoing. Its quality acid. I havent had any this good since we got a couple sheets of WoW unperf from Colorado 2 years back, that was awesome LSD! And Haizenberg's LSD is awesome!

Haizenberg's communication was quick. But I ordered 10 hits and another 5 hits at the same time, I sent him a message requesting they be combined into one envelope, he replied promptly in the affirmative but when the LSD arrived it was in two separate pieces of mail. Meh... not relly a big deal to me, I understand that Haizenberg is moving a large volume of mail, its a lot to keep up with. I'm just happy he took the time to read my message and respond to it, and impressed that he was very quick about getting the LSD into the mail.

The packaging.. not much to say about the packaging. The stealth is good enough, it got here, but it is immediately obvious where the LSD is once you open the mail and take out its contents.


*Trip Report*

The first part of the trip was difficult, I am not in a good place in my life right now so when I became introspective I had several unpleasant personal issues that the acid forced me to think about and deal with. The good news is the acid also allowed me to look at my life from a different perspective and resolve my internal conflicts, I have been much happier this week than I have for some time. I realized I was depressed and had let my apartment and appearance fall into shambles, my apt was a perfect representation of my mind-Dirty and cluttered.. I got a start on cleaning my apt while I was peaking, a week later I am well groomed, and my apt is clean as is my mind. There were lots of visuals, the flies had looong streaks of tracers behind them.

During the second part of the trip my apt became too overwhelming for me, that's when I ran into the neighbor kid. We climbed on top of our apt building and monkeyed around on the roof for a bit, then we climbed down and went to a nearby bar. We sit at the bar, I'm not digging the vibe in the bar, it seemed like everyone was there to eat and just socialize within their own little groups. I convince my neighbor to walk downtown with me so we can do some "urban exploration" I.E. find an even taller building to sneak onto the roof. My neighbor bailed on me when faced with locked doors I climbed on top of the elevator to access maintenance hallways that I supposed would lead to the roof. No longer having an accomplice I felt disinclined to continue my exploration of the high-rise I was in, I exited the building and began walking around downtown aimlessly just soaking up the sights, everything was beautiful. An actual friend called me(not just an acquaintance like my neighbor) and invited me down to a club he and his wife were at, I walk back to my car and drive to the club. I stayed at the club for several hours drinking, socializing, and people-watching until my friend said he was leaving at which time I had him lead me to my apt in his car(There were lots of police lights and people downtown, it was all very disorienting, I felt it best if I just concentrated on driving behind my friend instead of trying to forge my own way.). I got back to my apt, popped some benzos, smoked some weed, and went to bed. Woke up the next day and continued improving my life. I had a blast, I love to urban explore but I almost never do it, I'm glad I took a break and did it. Everything was just so great.

It was an awesome day! And I appreciate Haizenberg facilitating that, I will be purchasing from him again.
