Title: Re: What drugs don't show up in hair tests?
Post by: CannabisCrew on April 07, 2013, 06:39 pm

Any drug that enters your bloodstream can be detected by follicle samples often for up to 90+ days! The REAL question is whether or not the panel of tests will reveal the drug in question as there is always a margin/variance for false-positive results.

Most labs test for Cocaine (Cocaine & Benzoylecgonine), Marijuana, Opiates (Codeine, Morphine & 6-Monacteyl Morphine), Methamphetamine (Methamphetamine/Amphetamine & Ecstasy), and Phencyclidine (PCP). These five drug classes are mandated for testing by the Federal Government.

Anything other than those and you would need specialty labs to conduct the specific testing -which are expensive and stand virtually ZERO % chance of happening!
