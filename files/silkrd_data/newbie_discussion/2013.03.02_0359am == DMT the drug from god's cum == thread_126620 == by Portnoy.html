The trip is intense, but the scary part is when it's first kicking in (this is just for me, I don't know if everybody experiences it) my heart beat goes up REALLY high (or it feels like it) and I'm scared that I'm having a heart attack. I'm in my mid fifties, so that experience may not happen to other people.
Several times I've woken up afterwards and not had any memories of the trip itself, I wonder if my consciousness didn't want to accept the visions (or something) or wondered whether I really did black out.

If I remember correctly, you soak the herb in a acid or basic solution (it's been a long time) and extract it with naptha (hexane is better cause it doesn't smell as much-naphtha has a very chemical smell and you have to use a couple of quarts, so that's a lot of smell around-anybody near would notice it)
You dry the naphtha, and get the white or off white color powder. You get quite a bit.
Sorry I don't remember the extraction better it's been at least 5 years.
Before I got high (small hits that really didn't 'rock my world'), the colors around me got really intense and I thought I saw a little dwarf like being with a cartoonish grin sitting up on my bookshelf.
Something else, when I got some good hits (and not passed out) I would immediately (5-10 seconds max) see everything in geometric shapes (you'll know what I mean if you experience it) and I'll think 'here I go again!!'.
It's pretty easy and cheap to extract, but the heartbeat thing (for me) takes away most of my desire to use it, might try it again someday. I don't care too much about the sudden rush, to me it's like jumping into a cold lake (or something) it's quite a shock.
