Title: Re: Free samples???
Post by: See3PO on August 28, 2013, 11:16 pm
Lots of vendors give out free samples. It is usually if they are new or sometimes if they have a new product they haven't offered before. I recently gave away samples of MDMA to get started. If you want to find them, keep an eye on the forums. DO NOT go around just asking for free samples from vendors if they aren't advertising it!! It gets annoying and most established vendors have better things to do than weed through a stack of messages from beggars. Anyway as for your question, it can be a good indicator of quality, or it could be a way of a vendor gaining trust to plan a scam. Just be careful!
