My advice would be to follow this easy Ten Step Method:

1. Walk or drive to the nearest Walmart, CVS, 7 Eleven, or Walgreens, go to the ATM

2. Withdraw the amount of money you wish to convert to Bitcoins in cash

3. Walk/drive to the nearest McDonalds, Panera Bread, or Starbucks

4. Use their public WiFi to access https://www.bitinstant.com (https://www.bitinstant.com)

5. Select Cash Deposit as the "From"

6. Select Bitcoin to Email as the "To"

7. Print to file (use CutePDF if using Windows)

8. Walk/drive home, print the invoice from BitInstant onto paper

9. Drive/walk back to the Walmart, CVS, 7 Eleven, or Walgreens, hand them the invoice, and pay them the cash.

10. Deposit the Bitcoins you should have received at your Tormail address to your Silkroad receiving address, and enjoy!

PROTIP: If you are extra paranoid, a bonus step would be to use a public wifi network (such as that of McDonalds, Panera Bread, or Starbucks) to register a NEW Yahoo.com email address, then supply that to BitInstant to send the Bitcoins to, then never use that email address again for anything else.

I hope this helps you.
