Title: Re: Questions about security
Post by: NoQuarter_merchant on July 29, 2013, 02:24 pm
- How often do you order?
Collectively, our team has ordered several times with no issues.  We order from vendors whom demonstrate the ability to understand stealth shipping, PGP, and overall security.

- What are the risks for a buyer to be harassed by the police if the packaged is intercepted ?
The risks are huge.  Your name and/or address is on the package!    The big question I would ask is, what liability are you at if you were receiving the package.   It would be easy for me to plead ignorance.   I remember a story about a woman who received a fedex package with a bunch of weed in it, and a guy came to collect it later. She called the police.   The way that worked is, most people wouldn't open the package, and the dealer would walk up to the house late and say "Hey, fedex made a mistake, it's my package".  The point being, how would the investigators prove you actually purchased drugs?   

- Can you confirm that the main risk on a transaction is that the package is intercepted?
No.  Even buying drugs in the USA is a crime, regardless of whether or not they are delivered.  If you're not careful and you make mistakes in the buying process, you can give out information that will screw you.

- How many orders have you done without a package intercepted?
I've never had a package intercepted, as a vendor or as a customer.   

- Is that some have had intercepted a package without problems with the police?
Certainly, this happens.  Understand also that mail isn't 100% perfect.  Sometimes packages go to the wrong address, they get lost, destroyed, and sometimes people break into the packages to steal contents.
