Title: Re: Newbie...need help with Bitcoins????
Post by: Newguy321 on August 02, 2013, 09:01 pm

Stay AWAY from MtGox.com. They are not a bad place to get a idea of the going rate for bitcoins but do not try to buy from them. Like others have said localbitcoins is a good place to take a look around. They have an escrow system ( I think) and that helps to protect you from scamers.

Another piece of advice is that once you are ready to make your first purchase NEVER FE (finalize early) for your product. This is when you buy a product and the vendor wants you to finalize (pay him) before you get your product.

The escrow system on SR in designed to help protect both the vendor and the buyer so use it as it is intended and you will have a lesser change of getting scamed. Some vendors will prey on noobies who get wrapped up and just ready to make a deal.

Finally, if a deal looks to good to be true or the vendors price is a lot lower then others just don't do the deal. You will pay more by using a well known vendor but it is worth the investment. If  you need anything or have questions PM me and I will try to help.
