Hi Kingdingeling,

Vendors have 4 days to process an order from the time you place it. As you have found out, if they do not mark it 'in transit' by the end of day 4 then you can cancel the order.

Nobody but the vendor knows why they didn't accept the order, but hopefully you'll get your order sent out this time.

And Happy Birthday! :)

Post by: BlackIris on May 22, 2013, 08:34 am
How much it takes to confirm varies between vendors, but the average is usually in the 48 hours.

I don't know why your vendor didn't confirm the order in 5 days, maybe s/he had some problem somewhere.

The only advice I can give you is that if s/he asks you to FE this time for whatever excuse the vendor can come up with, to don't do it (this is a rule you should always take in mind, never FE - finalize early - if you can avoid it). Better be safe than sorry.
