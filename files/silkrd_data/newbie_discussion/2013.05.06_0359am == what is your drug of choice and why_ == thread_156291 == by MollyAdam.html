Post by: entreterra on May 06, 2013, 12:56 pm
LSD
 
It is one of the most spiritually and psychologically therapeutic experiences to be had for me;

when an experience is over I feel totally satisfied waiting at least a month of several months before taking it again;

taking it every several weeks actually reduces the desire to take other more destructive substances (note LSD has been shown to help alcoholics kick the habit);

it's a great release and rejuvenation of energy for me: many times I feel as though a weight has been lifted;

once LSD is laid on blotter, it cannot be cut (unless of course someone shittily adds some RC, but what I mean is that you can't stretch out the product to make more money once it's already been laid) so there is a sort of intrinsic necessity that when the LSD laid blotter is passing from chemist, to distributor, to dealers, you won't get LSD that's been cut to shit so some idiot can make a few extra bucks by stretching the product as happens with many substances, you're getting LSD that at worst is a little degraded since its a fragile molecule.

in all it's intensity it is a safe, gentle molecule, physiologically speaking.

Even bad trips are really the most therapeutic and important at times (as with most psychedelics).
