Post by: ShamelessHarvey on May 31, 2013, 01:31 pm
You should be vacuum sealing at least once. And you also probably want to look at using a vapor barrier material like foil bags or Mylar (MBB). Those food grade bags do not stop particles from getting through and can lead to dogs finding your package. Also, wipe the outside of whatever pack you end up using with isopropyl alcohol to kill any trace particles before putting it in the box/envelope.

Just my .02btc
