Although I would love to shamelessly self-promote and tell you to buy what I'm selling now (which are Indica dominant CA strains), it does sound like you'd be best served by a Sativa-based strain.  CannabisCup's listing looks like a good choice.

However, rather than fully going by what everyone says, you should try both, i.e., Sativa and Indica and do a little experiment.  Smoke each on separate occasions, both by yourself and with other people, and assess your experiences.  Only then will you know what you really like.  I say this because each person reacts to weed differently and Indica can be a very good choice even when you want to remain active, as long as you control the dosage.
