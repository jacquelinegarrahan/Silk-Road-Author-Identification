Title: Re: Vendor threatening to blacklist anyone who does not leave 5/5?
Post by: JeanRob on August 01, 2013, 11:02 pm
I've been a SR member since January, 2012. I've spent about $3,800 on 51 transactions.

Generally the best rule of thumb is to find reliable vendors and then stick with them.  I generally am buying vicodins. There ARE good vendors, but they are always running out and/or raising prices. I find that the best prices are from brand new vendors--and that is where the risk comes in.

I too am amazed at the good feedback some people give when the product never arrived, the seller would not communicate, and no refund or ship was offered. Still people say, not seller's fault 5/5; or USPS fucked up, 5/5; or seller seemed like a nice guy, 5/5.

I suppose buyers are afraid of offending the sellers, but WTF? Don't be afraid to leave bad feedback! If you're wrong, you can always go back and change it.

Just don't finalize early. If the 17 day auto-finalize deadline is approaching on an order that hasn't arrived, don't be afraid to hit the "resolve" link. The few times I've been to SR resolution, SR management has been entirely fair in resolving disputes.

I do not suggest being nitpicky. But when you get ripped-off, don't hesitate to rate the transaction accordingly.

JeanRob
