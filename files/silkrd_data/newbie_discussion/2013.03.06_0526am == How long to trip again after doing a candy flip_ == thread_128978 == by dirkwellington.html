Conventional wisdom is 2 weeks.

The MDMA is because of the serotonin levels returning to normal, but the LSD is because it builds tolerance extremely quickly. So if you wanted to do it once a week, you'd have to progressively do more of it over time.

I wasted my last two tabs of acid because I wanted another trip really badly but didn't want to wait. Just do another drug while you're waiting.
