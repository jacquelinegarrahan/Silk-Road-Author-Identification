Title: Re: Any Non-Newbie Members Here to Share My Pain?
Post by: Thestral1 on February 09, 2013, 04:50 am
Quote from: iLegalBusinessConsultant on February 09, 2013, 04:42 am
do we need to use PGP for forum posts? thx in a advance for the knowledge

PGP (encryption), in general, should be used for sensitive information. Such as when you send your address during the order process, for example. That way if SR became compromised the only person that could see your shipping address would be your vendor (they delete your address upon shipping, but it also helps to become friendly with your vendor as well). I'll occasionally encrypt messages to vendors in SR if it contains sensitive info, such as anything that could even roughly pinpoint the area I live or personal details about myself.
