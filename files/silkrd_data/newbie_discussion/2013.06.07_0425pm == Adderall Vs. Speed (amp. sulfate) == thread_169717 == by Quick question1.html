Adderal is a mix of 25% Levoamphetamine + 75% Dextroamphetamine whereas (European) Street Speed is composed of 50% Levo- and 50% Dextroamphetamine.

Levoamphetamine is responsible for most of the physiological effects (dilated pupils, grinding your teeth, ...) also it releases mainly Noradrenaline.
Dextroamphetamine is responsible for most of the psychological effects (concentration, energy, confidence, ...) and releases mainly Dopamine.

so more dexamph=more fun, less side effects.

In your case i would say stay with the Adderall.

Post by: shermanhex4sun on June 08, 2013, 03:46 am
I take adderall daily, it is prescriped.

Adderall can be called "speed." Speed is a vague and generic word. It can mean any one of the 4 amphetamine salts found in adderall--- it really depends on what the speed is made up of.

If the speed is only say, Amphetamine Sulfate, one of the 4 salts in adderall-- then you will experience the effects that this compound tends to produce. i believe Amphetamine Sulfate has a less noticeable effect in regards to the bad feelings that addreall can bring-- the increased blood pressure; nervousness, etc.

When i see "speed" advertised on SR, it is often Amphetamine Sulfate.

I will say this, and this is speaking from experience: if you abuse adderall long enough it will be very hard to feel motivated without it. It has taken me years to function on a normal dose of adderall. DO not binge on this stuff.
