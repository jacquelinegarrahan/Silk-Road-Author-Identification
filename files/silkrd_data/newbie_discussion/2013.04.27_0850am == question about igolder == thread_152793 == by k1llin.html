Title: Re: question about igolder
Post by: dothedamnthing on April 27, 2013, 10:05 am
Your buddy is right. You definitely should not be using that site, or any website-based encryption tools for that matter.

I'm assuming you are talking about GPG4USB. Once you have generated your key, you can click the "Manage keys" tab at the top, and then check the box next to your name/key and click "Export to clipboard." You will then have your public key copied and be able to paste it wherever you need to. Check out astor's tutorial if you have any other questions ----> http://32yehzkk7jflf6r2.onion/gpg4usb/
