Title: Re: Favorite way to get bitcoins
Post by: photonsounds on January 26, 2013, 08:20 pm
1) bitmining costs more in your electricity bill than it does your revenue. And the power usage draws attention to your home. Look up a "bitcoin mining calculator" if you don't believe me.
 

The easiest way I have found is
1) create a throwaway email (hushmail is a good one.)
2) order a BTC --> email with Bitinstant and pay with moneygram with fake information.
3) put your coin in a throwaway wallet(such as blockchain.info)
4) ANONYMOUSLY send it to SR (blockchain.info has a built in fogger. but SR does also.)

Steps 1 and 2 are NOT ACCESSED THROUGH TOR. Feel free to use a[n in country] proxy of some sort, good on you. But accessing this with tor, from what I have read, CAN draw attention to you...attention that you don't want in your life.
