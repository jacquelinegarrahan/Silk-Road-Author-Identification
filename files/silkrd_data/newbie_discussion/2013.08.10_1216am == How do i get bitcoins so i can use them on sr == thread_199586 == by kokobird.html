I've used both Coinbase and Cashintocoins, and both are very simple to use. Coinbase you attach your bank account and directly purchase bitcoins, but until you can verify your information further it takes 3-4 days for the transaction to clear. Cashintocoins is a simple cash deposit at one of several different banks, send a deposit slip to them with your email and wallet address and you can usually have coins that night.

Let me know if you have any questions about either method.
