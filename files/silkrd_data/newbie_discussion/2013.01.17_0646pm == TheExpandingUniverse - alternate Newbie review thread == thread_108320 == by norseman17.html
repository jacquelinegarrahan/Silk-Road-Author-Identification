Title: TheExpandingUniverse - alternate Newbie review thread
Post by: norseman17 on January 17, 2013, 06:46 pm
I want to start a review page here for all the Newbies who've received samples and can't post on the normal thread!

TheExpandingUniverse hooked me up with a sample of their sour diesel which was shipped Saturday and arrived Thursday (today). 

Shipping/Packaging:  Shipping took about two days longer than other vendors I've received from recently but was top notch in quality.  The package was inside several layers of packaging, vacuum seal, heat seal, and mylar lining, you couldn't ask for better packaging!

Looks/Smell:  The bud came in two nugs and for a .5 g sample it weighed in at just over .64 g.  The smell was overwhelming even for this small amount as soon as I opened the bag.  There were aromas of super sour lemons and that gasoline like smell you always get with good sour d!  Both nugs were covered in trichomes and sticky as fuck!

Smoke/Taste:  Smoke is typical of a very high grade cannabis with a high thc content.  There's a very sharp feeling in the lungs when you take a deep rip from the bong but the taste of the smoke of pleasant and lemony. 

High:  Intense high that hits you quick and lasts all afternoon, I'll definitely be buying from TEU again soon!  I want to try their pineapple!

Post by: jaythejoy on January 18, 2013, 08:18 pm
I also received a sample from TheExpandingUniverse of .5g of bud.

Strain: Pineapple Kush
Weight: Bag was overweight
Packaging: Stealthy. Couldn't smell anything from the outside. Different packaging material (wont go into details). Bud was a little crushed due to soft packaging material.
Shipping: It took four days to arrive to the east coast. Package was shipped First Class Mail.
Appearance: Frosty dark green nugs. Many orange hairs. Well-trimmed with little to no leaf. Crystals were a mix of cloudy/amber trichomes with intact heads. Nugs are small, tight, dense buds that are coated in trichomes. Bud is well-dried and cured.
Smell: Fruity/Sweet/Skunky. More berry/minty than citrus flavors. Distinct pineapple after-smell. Smell is evident but not overwhelming.
Taste: Sweet fruity taste similar to smell. Has a distinct kush smell to it.
Effect: Strong body buzz. Overall good medicine for a good price.
Overall, good bud. Appears to be quality indoor bud with a tight trim. Bud are small, but that is probably due to a small sample order. 4/5

Thanks TheExpandingUniverse!
