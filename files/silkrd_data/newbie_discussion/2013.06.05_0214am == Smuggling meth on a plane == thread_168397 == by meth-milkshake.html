Title: Re: Smuggling meth on a plane
Post by: Charlesnoir on June 05, 2013, 05:52 pm
DO NOT CHECK DRUGS IN LUGGAGE!

It is far safer to carry any substance on you.  Airport security can open your luggage after you check it.  Dogs are more likely to be sniffing the checked bags and not in the terminal (I don't know if it is legal for dogs to smell check passengers though I have seen them in the passenger areas).

Your pill idea is one I use frequently.  It works.  A pill bottle, especially for a prescription medication or even a nutritional supplement will do. Have a couple of different pill bottles for vitamins, meds, etc. You can even empty out some of whatever is in the capsules of the bottle you are using and fill it with your shit.   Keep it with your other toiletries in your carry on.

With the introduction of the full x-ray machines, storing any substance on your person is not advised. 

I don't know about traveling internationally.  I have only done this domestically, but have been successful with this technique several times. 

Bon Voyage!
