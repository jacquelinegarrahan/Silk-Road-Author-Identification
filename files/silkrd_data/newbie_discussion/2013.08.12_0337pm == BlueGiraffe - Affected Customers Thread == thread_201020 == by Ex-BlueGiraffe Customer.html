Post by: spanky loc on August 13, 2013, 06:21 am
I created this account as a result of the blunder that BG made. Also made a new account on the main site to match. I will only access the old account to finalize when the order that's in transit shows up, and trust me, I wish I didn't have a parcel on the way. I won't be accessing that account again after that.

I'm frustrated to lose my good stats that I had built up over several months. I never let a transaction auto-finalize, and what refunds I had were the result of banned scammer vendors. The stats spoke for themselves, and it's irritating to have to start over from scratch.

Even more irritating is having to spam the forums to get back to full member status. TOR is slow at the best of times, and it was a total waste of my time to have to cruise thru the newbie forums to get to 50 posts, and as you can see, I'm still not back to 100 (I think this about my 80th post).

I do believe that the likelihood of anything coming of the compromised info is low, but it sure has made me carefully analyze my situation and reconsider whether I'm keeping safe. Like many here, I have a lot to lose if I were to get busted somehow. I do believe that SR is still a far better option than RL dealers.

If a street dealer got popped and all his calls and texts were examined by LE, there would be no possibility of a warning like BG provided after his screw-up, which is an important fact that helps me keep things in perspective.

None of this is to excuse the colossal nature of the security breach, and I sure wouldn't make a purchase from BG again, if and when he resumes sales.
