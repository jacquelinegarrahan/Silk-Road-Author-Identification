Lyrica definitely has some abuse potential. It's "Schedule V" in the US, which means diddly but notes the potential there.
As a psychiatrist once described it to me, patients tend to like Lyrica because it causes effects liken to feeling "stoned".

Unfortunately, though Gabapentin (Neurontin) has similar albeit lessened recreational value, one really needs to ramp up the dose (talking 2-3 times more) to get the "good" effects out of it.
Tolerance for both of these pharmaceuticals builds up uber rapidly; from personal experience, at least for Pregabalin. Sucks.

As for intended purposes, I found that Lyrica is extremely efficacious for generalized anxiety, and from what I hear it is helpful for those who suffer from Fibromyalgia or nerve pain conditions.
