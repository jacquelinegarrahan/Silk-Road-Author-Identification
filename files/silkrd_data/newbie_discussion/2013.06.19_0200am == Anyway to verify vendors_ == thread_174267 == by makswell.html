You said it yourself, it doesn't work. First, if you FE, you forfeit your right to resolve any issues with the vendor with Silk Road staff. This is your biggest insurance against scams, don't squander it. Next, the forums have everything you could wish to read on nearly every vendor, and if they don't, then start a thread. Vendors with a forum presence are extra reliable. LSD and MDMA quality and purity are tested independently by the LSD and MDMA Avengers groups (look for their threads). There are massive threads reviewing cocaine, heroin, speed. Research research research! Talk to vendors before buying, ask them questions you have. Notice if they're courteous or if they're assholes. Notice if they're bullying you into finalizing early.

You got the power bud, use it and you will have many happy transactions.
