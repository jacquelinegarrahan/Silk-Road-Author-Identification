We are all ignorant about something. You do have to get BTC and I recommend trying Craigslist or localbitcoins.com because you can meet with someone in person and pay cash for BTC. If you use localbitcoins and get them to follow LBC procedure, they will transfer the BTC to your LBC wallet and that can be done instantly, so you have your BTC at the time of transaction. Hopefully you are close to a larger city as being in a remote location can make it difficult to obtain BTC at times.
