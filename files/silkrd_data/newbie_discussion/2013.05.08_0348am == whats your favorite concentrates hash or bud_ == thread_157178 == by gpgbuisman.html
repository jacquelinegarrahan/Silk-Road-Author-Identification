wow tbh yall it sounds like what you got was badly purged iso, not bho..  i know a lot of "medical" clubs sell qwiso as bho and just sell horrible concentrates in general, most of the worst bho ive had has been from dispensaries.  i have also seen people cough up black/grey shit/bits of blood when smoking terrible qwiso..  its kinda sickening really because bho, when purged properly (this is not something you need a vacuum to do btw you can purge EASILY using just heat), is very smooth and while it might get you coughin/sweatin it's actually much better for your lungs (from what i understand, i dont like to spread misinformation so if i'm wrong call it out).  really the worst that i've seen happen due to a bad purge was a fat dab making crazy fireworks shot hot oil into my friend's eye..

ice-wax, which is just real full-melt btw (no matter what anyone says, it IS bubble hash), is not going to be as potent as it would be if you'd solvent-extracted the same material.  that being said, it can still be very potent, but it won't hold its own against a real good oil. 

as far as co2, i've had plenty of shit that people called co2 oil but i've never seen anyone make it nor ever really been convinced that people are doing it a lot.  the fact is, if you can get ahold of some n-butane and a nice extractor with a recovery system, what comes out is just as clean as any co2 oil would be (again my understanding), and co2 is much much more expensive and difficult to work with.  i make my own concentrates all the time and the only special thing i do is use a micron filter instead of coffee filters, i have a friend who washes hers in denatured alcohol to get the last of the waxes out afterwards but i've never really done this step because i don't like waiting for it to freeze, seperate out, etc before you can just drop a bhomb.  and i get ooey gooey sap consistently that is smooth and dabbable all day long. 

its a bummer that yall have had such bad experiences with it.  like i said, when done properly, there will be virtually no butane left in your extract, and doing it properly doesn't require anything special..  you inhale more combusted butane when you smoke a bowl id think than you ever would dabbing and that butane in your bic is FAR nastier..  i think there's a lot of misinformation out there about butane extracts, and extracts in general, and a lot of is fear-based because ppl think it's some wild new thing.. i know folk who've been dabbing many times daily for 8 or 9 years and many, many others have been doing it much longer out on the coast.. and we're all doing just fine  :)  ymmv
