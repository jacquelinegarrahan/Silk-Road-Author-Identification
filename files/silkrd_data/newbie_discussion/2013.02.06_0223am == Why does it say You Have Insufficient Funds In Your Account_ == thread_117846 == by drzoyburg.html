Title: Re: Why does it say "You Have Insufficient Funds In Your Account"?
Post by: SelfSovereignty on February 06, 2013, 03:32 am
It's because Silk Road is rounding.  Bitcoins are divisible to 8 decimal places; it's rounding that to 2.  It says you have insufficient coins because it's lying to you about your balance, basically.
