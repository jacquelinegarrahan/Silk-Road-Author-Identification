Hey new here was wondering if a vendor had say 14g of Silver haze listed does that mean he only has one 14g bag or is that just his asking price for 14gs?
Title: Re: If a vendor only has one of the items on listed that mean they only have 1 half?
Post by: SoAlone on July 23, 2013, 10:09 am
WYSIWYG as they say.

That would be a listing for 14gs. for - whatever they have as the price.

Click on the vendors name, and you'll see their other listings, in case you were looking for a different amount, they may have other listings.

Also, if you wanted to know how many 14g orders you could purchase from them, just click "add to my cart" - and you'll see the box for 'quantity'. Next to that box it will show 'quantity available'. If more than '1', then that's how many you can order at once.

Hope this helps.
