Title: Re: Vendor feedback ratings
Post by: JackBlue on February 24, 2013, 07:17 pm
You're right.  It is kind of screwed up.  But it is what it is.  Since the default rating that is pre-checked when you finalize an order is a 5, it's gotten to be pretty much customary for buyers to give five ratings.  As a vendor, I can telll you that because of the easy grading system here on SR, a 4 rating is noticed and really can hurt a vendor.  I recently had someone give me a 4 because my weed didn't smell like skunk and he thought all strong weeed was supposed to smell skunky.  I'm still answering questions almost daily from customers wondering what happened to my perfect (100) rating.  It's almost like I have an STD or something.  If you give a vendor any rating besides a 5, expect some push back.

So you really do need to go through and read the actual feedback comments to really get a sense of the vendors.  The feedback scores are pretty much meaningless.when the majority of vendors have (100) scores.  Yes, it's a twisted system, but it is what it is and you won't make friends trying to change the grading system to a more realistic one.
