Title: Re: first order questions!
Post by: See3PO on August 14, 2013, 09:16 pm
1. Your computer is NOT safe, no matter what you think. Read about Virtualbox and set up a Linux virtual machine, at the very least.
2. SR handles escrow. There are no external sites or services needed.
3. What do you mean "SR encrypt my adress once"? Even if SR did this, you still shouldn't trust it. ALWAYS use PGP!!
4 & 5. You can use your own address if you can't get somewhere else to have it sent. Just make sure the name is a person who actually lives there.
Title: Re: first order questions!
Post by: Newguy321 on August 14, 2013, 09:19 pm
1) TOR is safe but I would read through some of the stickies up top to help with security issues.

2) SR is all the escrow service that is needed.

3) Yes. PGP offers a extra level of protection. Just think if anyone got your address all kind of bad things could happen.

4) I use my own address all the time with no issues. If you were making a large purchase I would probably use a drop.

5) That is between you and the vendor. Different vendors do things different ways. To me a real address and real name is fine as long as the vendor uses good stealth.
