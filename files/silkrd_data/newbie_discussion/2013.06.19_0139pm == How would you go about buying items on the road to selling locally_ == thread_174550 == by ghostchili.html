Title: Re: How would you go about buying items on the road to selling locally?
Post by: Luvs2Trip on June 19, 2013, 02:43 pm
To sell on 'the streets' you do need to know a few people, that take the drug you sell, to get you started!

If you have quality stuff you can then start with the people you know who will likely do the drugs with people they know who will then ask where they got it from and will eventually also end up buying it from you!

Then they cycle continues like that!

IMO you really do need to start selling to people you know first because then you can build up a chain of customers that are all linked together by people you can trust! Never sell to anyone that cant explain how they managed to get in contact with you. never sell to people who randomly approach you. Confirm everybody's story of how they heard about you before selling to them! If you follow this then you can keep relatively safe.

Molly is ok for the profit margins but requires a larger customer base than other drugs because it isn't moreish meaning that repeat custom is not likely (Some people will be able to make a gram last a weekend). Drugs like coke are the opposite... if someone takes one you can almost guarantee they will be back as soon as its gone!

Remember tho that its a risky game to play and its your freedom on the line! Better to be to paranoid over not being paranoid enough!
