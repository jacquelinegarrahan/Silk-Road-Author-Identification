Post by: shuvool on May 02, 2013, 05:38 pm
I've often heard of crystal meth referred to as "poor man's coke".  Not having done coke before, I can't compare, but in my teens I spent about a year doing a LOT of meth.  There's definitely a large effect from the meth, both physiologically and psychologically.  It doesn't take much to give you the effects (until you build a tolerance) and the effects last quite a while, but it is extremely addictive, and it's not hard to tell if someone else is on meth or coming down off of it, so you can't always use it when you have other things to do (esp things that require focus and changing tasks frequently).

I wouldn't recommend it as a replacement for coke, because the effects I've heard from coke really aren't the same other than they both speed you up, but the meth rush lasts a lot longer so the next day you're not going to want to do very much (other than smoke or snort some more meth)
