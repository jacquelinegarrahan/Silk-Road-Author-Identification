Title: Re: buying guns
Post by: heavysmoker on October 09, 2013, 05:41 pm
If you live in an area where guns are available it doesnt make much sense to buy a gun on the darkweb. 

Its all about supply and demand, in areas where guns are outlawed or for people who are not allowed to buy a gun for whatever reason, the demand for guns is high and the supply of guns is low leading to a high price point.

You are also paying for the vendor to source unregistered weapons and the risk involved in sending the weapon to the buyer.

If you can get guns for 200$ that are selling for 5k, why not jump into the market yourself?  It sounds like you could make a killing as a vendor.
