Title: Re: What is your preferred type of Address for SR deliveries?
Post by: FugNuggetz on January 28, 2013, 11:59 am
I usually go with the 'hide in plain sight approach' and use real everything. Whether the package has your name on it or not there is the same plausible deniability if it gets intercepted. Think about it, what is to stop anyone from sending one of their enemies an envelope addressed to them with drugs clearly smeared all over it? I feel that the more normal your mail seems then the more likely it is to get delivered. You will not likely be questioned about any suspicious packages but if shit ever hits the fan deny anything when it comes to soliciting or receiving said packages.

Also if you have a package that must be signed for (unavoidable in some cases), many types of apartments have front desk personnel that will sign for all deliveries since they sort the mail themselves and put it in your box or have you pick it up from the front office. These are ideal places to get things delivered as a controlled delivery would be much more difficult for LE to execute, and your signature is never on anything.

I'm no expert! But that's my two cents.
