Title: Re: how to buy btc with credit card ?
Post by: noumen0n on August 07, 2013, 01:27 am
There's really no reason to buy bitcoins with a credit card because of the insane mark up and extra fees that come with it. I can only think of a few situations that would warrant a credit card, and the main one would be if you're broke and can't wait until your next paycheck or whatever. If that's the case, it's likely you have an unhealthy addiction and shouldn't be spending money you don't actually have.

Anyway, lectures aside, virwox is the only other legit btc vendor I know that accepts credit cards. But be prepared to pay up the ass for it from the multiple currency conversions... I want to say you receive 3/4 of your initial deposit but I'm not sure. You'll also have to factor in the 48 hour hold virwox puts on your first transaction for verification purposes. Although after your first couple transactions, the deposit is basically instant. Not worth it unless you have money to blow and need your bitcoins asap.
