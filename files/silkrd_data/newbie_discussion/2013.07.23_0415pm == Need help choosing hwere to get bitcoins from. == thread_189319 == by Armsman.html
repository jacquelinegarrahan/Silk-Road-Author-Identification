Title: Re: Need help choosing hwere to get bitcoins from.
Post by: Crepuscular on July 23, 2013, 05:59 pm
Depends a bit how busy you are getting your coins and where you are located.
The cheapest option is usually to get an account at one of the exchanges (Bitstamp, Mt. Gox or similar), provide the proper validation they need to keep their back free from money-laundering charges (like proof of identity and proof of residence), wait out the whole tedious process, send money there by wire-transfer or SEPA, buy the coins at current market price and transfer them to the wallet you prefer.
Often, you can find a local vendor selling coins from day to day at a significant markup (often 8-10%), but it's easy and convenient. You can also try to see if https://localbitcoins.com/ works out for you.
