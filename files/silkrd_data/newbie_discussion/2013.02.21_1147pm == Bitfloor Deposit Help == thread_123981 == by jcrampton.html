Title: Re: Bitfloor Deposit Help
Post by: MarcoRuas on February 22, 2013, 12:21 am
Regular deposit slip>>>Fill it out >>>LocalTill,LLC ///Account #. Give them the deposit slip and the cash and then your good. No ID was asked for.

I was using Bitinstant before things got a little funky with them. Read around the forum and learned about "Bitfloor.com".
Opened a an acount using only an e-mail address. Deposit money in Bank of America. Money goes into your account1,2,3.
Then you have to trade (purchase) to get BTCs ,either by Market order or limit order. Market order,the transaction is quicker in terms of the actually purchase but the minimum amount is 5BTC at a time. Limit order you can trade for any amount(1,2,3,4etc.),but it takes 24hrs and you get a better rate of exchange. Hope this helps.

Quote from: jcrampton on February 22, 2013, 11:45 pm
I too am wondering the exact process once you have the BTCs on BF getting them  from BF to SR. Can anyone clear this up?

Once you have the coins in your BF wallet, go to your account tab in SR. There you will find your SR bitcoin wallet address. Copy this address ,then go back to Bitfloor,click on the withdraw link at the top , and paste it to the out going wallet address. There is also a space asking how many BTCs you want to transfer. It takes a little time for the transfer to happen, so be impatient. Hope this helps
