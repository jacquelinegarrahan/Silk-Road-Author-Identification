Title: Re: Which EU vendor for speed?
Post by: jägermeister on March 17, 2013, 07:53 pm
amphetamine paste is nothing more but adulterants that are being added to toxic & uncleaned amphetamine base.
you can identify it easily by it's moist & clumpy appearance, as well by it's penetrating smell of cleaning agent.
also it is drying in nothing more but a few minutes (if the paste were pure unadulterated amph base it would never dry that fast).
And when paste dries, a lot of the amph base material is evaporating along with the adulterants. just let some dried paste sit for a few days, and you would see it having almost no effect anymore - compared to amphetamine salt, which is pretty stable material.

real amphetamine for "consumption" (like in pharmaceuticals) has to be finished by making it into a sulfate, that is, the salt of sulfuric acid.
that's an important cleaning step, that is not being done with base, and can also not be done afterwards because the base is already suspended in those adulterants - so this stuff most certainly is not being sold by accident where someone just forgot to do the final process, but quite deliberately.

i guess most vendors may not even know this themselves, and are just selling whatever they can stock - the problem are the manufacturers who try to make easy money at the expense of our health - and unfortunately it seems their plan is working out well, as some people seem to be snorting almost every shit they can get their hands on without thinking twice.
something has got to be done about this stuff!!
