Title: Re: Well, I guess I can only post here for now- How do you all feel about that?
Post by: John Humphreys on February 05, 2013, 05:23 am
I can see the reason, but as it stands there are some unintended consequences...

Previously the number of posts was a somewhat useful metric for how new a person was to the forum. Now new people feel obliged to quickly spam 50 messages, which means that having 50 posts doesn't really mean anything. A person with 50 posts is now just as likely to be a new person who has spammed "hello" 50 times.

Another unintended consequence is that this encourages new people to write anything on any topic, even when they have nothing to say. Not sure if that will improve the quality of conversation in the long run.
