Title: Re: Did I screw up my BTC transfer?
Post by: thebigneely on March 24, 2013, 05:24 pm
I just want to add a resolution to this for any future readers who may be having the same issue.

20.5 hours after I initiated the transfer, the BTC showed up in my SR account. I contacted both SR and blockchain support, but haven't gotten a response from either yet, so I think it just resolved itself

To summarize what I did so that to reassure future searchers:
- Paid cash at MoneyGram location for BitInstant to Blockchain.info wallet
- bc.info wallet to different bc.info wallet via regular send feature
- second bc.info wallet to SR wallet via send-shared feature
- From there the BTC went to a different address and my SR wallet showed no transactions whatsoever. The transaction to the wrong wallet racked up about 175 confirmations before it moved on elsewhere in different amounts. I had pretty much given up hope at this point, but I guess this is either how SR's tumbler works or Blockchain's send-shared or both.

Hope this helps you, person from the future.
