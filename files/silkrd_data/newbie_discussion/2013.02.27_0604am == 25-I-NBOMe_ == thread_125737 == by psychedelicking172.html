Title: Re: 25-I-NBOMe?
Post by: lemonz0r on February 27, 2013, 02:19 pm
Tolerance takes about two weeks to go away, and pretty much prevents you from using other psychedelics of the same class (LSD, shrooms) for at least the first week.

No-one can give you solid answers on its safety, since there has been very little research done on it. Regular use is not something I'd advise, though, for this reason. However, anecdotally speaking, many people have tried it a few times and been fine. I'd say there's no real chance of OD on 1200ug, but I wouldn't dose over that myself. Having dosed 1200ug, I see no real need to, either. If you want to go further than that, switch to another psychedelic that can be dosed much higher safely.
