Title: New here, looking at novelty ID's, do they pass this test?...
Post by: lolidkwtf on May 23, 2013, 08:51 am
I have been looking around for a good novelty ID, some of the ones on here looked pretty good. The state I was looking at was Arizona, so ill use that as an example.

All of the ads for novelty ID's on here say they have..
- Hologram image
- "Grand Canyon State" down the ID 3 times in faint but visible text
- The little outline of AZ if you shine a light through the back
- Scans (Obviously thats a big one)

And I know that there are some checks that are done on the information thats on the actual card, but that has nothing to do with whoever makes it, thats more on the shoulders of whoever fills out the form

But... I ran into something new today..

In very very very.... VERY VERY faint text, it says "GRAND CANYON STATE GRAND CANYON STATE GRAND CANYON STATE" on the id. It actually looks like just a little of scribbles, until you hold a magnifying glass over it. If you have a new AZ license, and its a horizontal license, then it's under the signature, if its an older one, it may be positioned vertically to the right of the headshot, (On the actual blue part, at the very edge)

Anyone have any of the novelty IDs from anyone on this site? If so, can you check to see if it has this?

FYI: This is where I found it, so if you don't have AZ, check your state on this site, then look at the novelty ID you got here.
