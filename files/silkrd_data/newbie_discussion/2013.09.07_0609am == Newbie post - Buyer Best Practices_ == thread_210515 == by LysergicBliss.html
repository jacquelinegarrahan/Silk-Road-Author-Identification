Title: Re: Newbie post - Buyer Best Practices?
Post by: SmoothUp on September 07, 2013, 07:45 am
Do some detailed research on the vendor you want to buy from. Means: General vendor page, product page, vendor reviews, vendor discussion and (here in SR Forum) the vendor's forum topic.

If you just want to order "two pills at most" there really is no need to use a fake name or address in MY opinion. This would just increase the risk of a non-arriving letter. If your letter comes from another country and customs find out it's a fake name/address you will probably get in trouble when recieving the letter.

Only use fake data if you know exactly what you do! Everyone can send a letter with drugs in it to your address and your name. Just keep your apartment drugs-clean/hide them safely.

Cheers
