At this point i believe the other exchanges have pretty much closed up shop for the anonymous buying of bitcoin.  I do believe there are a few exchanges out there where you can buy your coins at market price but you have to hand over lots of identifying information.  Since buying bitcoins is not illegal in theory there is no harm in doing this.  If LEO are eventually able to trace those coins to you and the purchase of something illegal will it become a problem.  But at this point in time i can pretty much promise you if you are buying BTC with your real identity there's a good chance some government agency now has some interest in you.
