Title: Re: Shipping to a re-shipping company
Post by: SemiTranslucent on July 13, 2013, 08:55 pm
Quote from: kaphir on July 13, 2013, 04:14 pm
I have taken the risk, and ordered a small amount to the re-shipper address. will update you when it arrives.

As previously stated, pick the right vendor with a reputation for stealth, and make sure you let them know that your product IS being bought internationally.  If you just buy it domestic they will use a different form of stealth then on international orders, and so it wont have X-Ray protection etc...

The difference between shipping an order through customs internationally, and through domestic, is pretty huge, and if you are re-shipping to a tight-customs country, the stealth might not be adequate if the vendor was simply shipping with domestic stealth.  Just a heads up for next time you get an order, let your vendor know that you are re-shipping, and I am sure they will be more then willing to accommodate you with more stealth.
