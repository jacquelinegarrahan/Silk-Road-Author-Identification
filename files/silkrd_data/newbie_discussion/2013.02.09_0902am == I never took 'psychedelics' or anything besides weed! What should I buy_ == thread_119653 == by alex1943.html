There is always a chance to get caught when ordering something illegal through the mail. If I said there wasn't a chance, that would be ignorant. The vendors I listed are great and have sent me stuff flawlessly, besides SuperTrips, which is why I recommend waiting a few weeks if you do order from him, after checking the forums and reviews. He has a 100% refund/reship policy that he abides to, which is very nice. I do not know your countries legal system, but if you got caught ordering small, personal sized amounts, I don't think you would get in HUGE trouble. That is not to say that the legal consequences wouldn't be bad. Do some research on your local laws.

Post by: lemonz0r on February 09, 2013, 03:43 pm
I wouldn't say MDMA is exactly 'psychedelic', but it's what I would recommend as the next thing to try. I can't imagine having a bad time on it, as long as you're vaguely sensible with your dose (~120-150mg for a first time).

I've also tried 2c-b - although only once, so far - and it seems like a great introduction to psychedelic visuals - the headspace is very clear, so you're unlikely to panic. At at ~20mg dose, I only had quite light visuals, although it was still a lot of fun.

I can't comment on the classic psychedelics like shrooms or LSD yet, although from what I've read, low doses of either would be a good start, too. Once you're used to more altered states of mind/visuals, I'd recommend 25i-NBOMe. I took half a 1200ug tab, then the other half a little later, and had very little anxiety, but a long and interesting trip - my first real one. At that dose, there is quite a lot of mindfuck - I got stuck in thought loops, for example - but the visuals are mind-blowing. A lower dose (~600ug) may be better to start with, though.
