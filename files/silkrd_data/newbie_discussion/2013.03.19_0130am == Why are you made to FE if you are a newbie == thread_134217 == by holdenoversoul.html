Title: Re: Why are you made to FE if you are a newbie
Post by: liberta709 on March 19, 2013, 01:45 am
Some vendors will make you FE if you have no buying history, or if they are trying to scam you.

If a vendor requires you to FE, then don't buy from them. Simple as that. Rule #1 of SR is NEVER FE. I never have, and never will, it takes away the buyer-seller protection that helps make silkroad so successful.

If your a newbie, usually just start by buying a small quantity of something. I dunno, like an 8th of cheap weed, or a sample of something. This is low risk for the vendor, and they should be happy to make a deal.


Think of this like ebay. If your making a large purchase, no one is going to sell to a buyer that has less than 10 transactions, cause scammers are everywhere. If a seller forces you to FE, this should be a big red flag.

Title: Re: Why are you made to FE if you are a newbie
Post by: liberta709 on March 19, 2013, 03:31 am
Well it's obvious how a seller could be a scammer.

But anyone, literally anyone could make an acc on SR. They then buy something from a vendor. They wait, oh say, 5 days and their purchase arrives. But they can "claim" that the package never arrived. If this happens, I guess there are a few things that can happen. The vendor can resend the product (unlikely unless the buyer is reputable), have to deal with a negative review (which sucks for the vendor), or just accept the fact that the buyer was a dick and essentially stole from them. I assume this would be an example of a buyer being a scammer, because I'm sure shit like this does happen. This is generally why it is recommended to buy smaller things when your a newbie to get your rep up, so vendor's can see you're actually trustworthy.

I'm sure some vendors have been fucked over by this so many times, that they may require people with 0 purchases to FE, to protect themselves. If that is the case, and you have 0 purchases, just find a different vendor.

My first purchase was like, 1/4 oz of weed for ~1 bitcoin. Pretty small simple purchase. Slowly I gained a higher reputation, have made multiple purchases, and now I can buy large quantites of stuff, and I appear more trustworthy to vendors. Ya dig?
