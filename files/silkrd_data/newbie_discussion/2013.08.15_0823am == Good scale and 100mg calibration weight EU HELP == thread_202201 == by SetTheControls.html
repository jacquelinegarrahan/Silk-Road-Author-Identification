You can get a cheap milligram scale on ebay for like $20. It'll be good to within 5mg. You can pay a couple hundred dollars for a top of the line cheap scale and get maybe to plus/minus 3mg and then there is a biiiig jump in price for the quality lab scales that cost thousands that are good to a milligram. It isn't just a matter of money, it's also technique. You can't just toss crap on there and take it off. You have to use gloves and tweezers and a level and microcurrent shield and a bunch of other stuff. So the question becomes can you live knowing that you're within 5 migs of the dose or are you really anal, wealthy and have loads of patience?
