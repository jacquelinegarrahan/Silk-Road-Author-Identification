Title: Re: help has my package been seized first time so need advise
Post by: jlranier on July 12, 2013, 05:59 am
Not to incite you to panic or be offensive or anything but it was really stupid to check the tracking info let alone post it and you've put both yourself and your vendor at risk. If I were a vendor I personally would exercise caution around you in the future based on that alone. There are some great threads in the Shipping section about this same topic and reducing your chances of a seizure. For one thing, never check tracking unless absolutely necessary/you think you're being scammed. And even then caution should be exercised, it shouldn't be checked over tor, and it should be checked sparingly. I hope things work out for you, they probably will and if it got seized then they'll likely dispose of it if it's a small amount like has been said. If it was a large amount cover your tracks, clean house and deny everything. Don't talk to anyone other than your attorney. In the US at least, the burden of proof is on THEM, not you. You don't have to prove you didn't order it as much as they have to prove that you did.

Whether this works out or not please exercise more caution in the future. This is a high risk community in all honesty and we do have to look out for one another on some level, no matter how naive it may seem to think that people in the "underground" do this we do. By using safer practices in buying and communicating you reduce risk for yourself and also do us a solid by reducing risk for us.
