Lets say If it's only a gram of cocaine you should be fine, the worst case scenario that could happen to you is getting a letter saying your 'suspicious package has been seized and destroyed' or something along those lines. After it arrives go and collect it from the mailbox, always watch for the law when going to collect the package, you never know. This is probably the oldest trick in the book when dealing with higher quantities of drugs, it removes some of the risk - but it may not always be the case. Or if you are extremely paranoid, pay someone to go and collect it for you from the mailbox; I'm sure you'd be able to find someone willing to collect a package for you for $10, just go and find some crackheads. (Maybe a bad example). Also make sure, you don't get too cocky and start getting delivered package, after package after package to the same address. Three times is usually enough - then switch, and so on.

I found a case where a guy received a package and threw it into the trash because he had been tipped off about LE discovering the package.  His throwing the package away was used to prove "constructive possession" because it convinced a jury that he knew there were illicit drugs in the package.  He got convicted.

In another case a couple received a package and placed it in a couple of black bags and set it down in a mobile home they were staying in.  No other drugs were found and they didn't talk.  They got convicted, mainly because (1) they had the tracking number written down and (2) placing the package into multiple black bags helped to prove constructive possession.

Most people here aren't getting into trouble because of these things: (1) they buy domestic which is harder to catch.  (2) east coast customs isn't checking packages and they just aren't having any bad luck that their package went through the xray.  (3) small amounts and you get a love letter (note: a small amount is 1g not 28g).

If you get a controlled delivery then chances are you're fucked.  Very few people have a delivery address that is clean and has a person that won't talk after being told they are going to jail.  The police will put you into jail for simple having a package delivered and they'll let the DA/jury figure out if you're guilty or not.  Making bail and hiring a decent attorney can be very expensive.  The process can take a long time.  Oh and police will not hesitate to claim that you said things that you didn't.  If they think you're guilty then they justify it to themselves I guess.  They want to "win."
