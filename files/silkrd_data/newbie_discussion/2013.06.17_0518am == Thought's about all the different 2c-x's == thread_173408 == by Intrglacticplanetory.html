Post by: Air///cooled on June 17, 2013, 08:43 am
I have a little experience with 2c-x's. I've had b on a few occasions, i twice and loads of e. For me b was really superficial as a psychedelic. It was lots of fun though, like a night out type of drug. The i was a out the same, only more visual, but still pretty shallow. 2c-e was, and still is, my favorite 2c. It is capable of depth, offers stunning visuals, and has a very nice body load until you get into heroic doses. The highest up I've gone was 45 mgs and it was very intense. Completely immersive. The body load was at the border of being unmanageable though. Shivers, leg tremors, severe uncoordination. I enjoyed it thoroughly though. I imagine an i/e or b/e combo would be nice. I've only mixed it with m1 and psilocetin and both were amazing.
