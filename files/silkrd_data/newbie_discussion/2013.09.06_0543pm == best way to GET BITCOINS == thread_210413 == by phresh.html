Also, if you want to break the link between your bank account and SR, send your bitcoins through bitcoinfog. SR's tumbler only hides which transaction(s) your bitcoins went to (it's still obvious they went to SR), whereas using a 3rd party tumbler hides the fact that your coins went to SR at all.
