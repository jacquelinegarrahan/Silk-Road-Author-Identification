Title: Re: Bitcoins. Did i do a good job or fail? Help rest my mind.
Post by: captainjojo on April 24, 2013, 01:56 am
You should not have sent the BTC directly from where you purchased them to SR.  You should always have another wallet in the middle.  Buy the bitcoins, transfer to your own personal wallet THEN transfer to SR.  To be even safer, transfer to your own wallet, then to a second wallet you also own that is running on another computer or in a VM.

You should make sure that your wallet is set to run over TOR, further hiding yourself from being traced by your IP address. (You do that by going to Options/Network and setting the wallet to communicate via proxy using the TOR port number)

While it is unlikely that someone is going to trace your transaction to SR, better safe than sorry.
