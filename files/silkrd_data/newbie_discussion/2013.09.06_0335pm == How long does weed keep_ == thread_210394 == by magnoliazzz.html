Title: Re: How long does weed keep?
Post by: cbdx_en on September 09, 2013, 12:39 pm
if you have a small piece of stick (stem) from your weed while its still moist cut it and store it into your fridge.  If your buds get dried out just cut a small piece from the stick and place it in the jar / zip bag and the buds will get re moisten very very fast.

potency will not drop even if your buds get dried out completely (assuming proper cure took place before the weed was sold.) 

Placing your buds in the fridge while they have enough moisture in em will cause mold and probably destroy your weed. Just dry it enough (<30% moisture) and then it will be fine.

Every time when harvesting my babies I keep one of the stems just for this reason.
