I hit the bank earlier and made my cash deposit to get my BTC and they were in my wallet by the time I logged on from home.  That only took 5 minutes and I was in and out with no worries.  LOL, I was given a whole nickel from the change on my deposit (It was $XXX.95 total) and when I walked out of the bank, some guy was pulling the old "got any change?" scam in the parking lot so I tossed him my shiny new nickel and told him good luck.  I got a good laugh on my walk back to work from that.

It's almost surreal that it's this convenient as long as you pay attention to what you're doing.  I hope we all focus on our own risk reduction and the vendors do too so we can all enjoy this awesome opportunity.

Best of luck to everyone else going through their first time(s)
