Title: Re: Is this working for newbies?
Post by: tree on May 28, 2013, 08:27 pm
There are a few things that you could improve to have buyers... First of all, Canada isn't a huge market and I'd recommend doing Canada and US. You also don't specify how you ship it, which is really important for weed. You should say if you vacuum seal it, or use MBB (MBB recommended) for example. You should also show pictures of your actual product and not just plant products, have amounts in listings name like "7 grams of medical grade xxx", and have longer descriptions, like what the high, taste, etc. is like.
And finally, your listings are all for big amounts and not everybody is ready to pay $200 on a completely new vendor. Try to make listings for a couple grams for people to know what your stuff is like, and do some advertising on the forums!
