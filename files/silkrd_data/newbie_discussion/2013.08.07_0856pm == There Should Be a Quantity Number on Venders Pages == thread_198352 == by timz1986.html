Title: Re: There Should Be a Quantity Number on Venders Pages
Post by: Thekla1 on August 07, 2013, 09:08 pm
Hi.

There is a quantity number displayed, to see it add the item to your cart, on the next page it will display a default quantity ordered of 1, and next to that the number of that item available. If you go back from there, it will show an order in your cart, but if you were just checking then clear your order quantity down to 0, and update. Your cart will then be empty. But things move pretty quickly so if there is little available of the item and you don't order there and then, it may be gone when you go back.

Hope this helps,
