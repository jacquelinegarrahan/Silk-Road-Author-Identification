Post by: SorenBowie on February 27, 2013, 06:19 pm
There are no dumb questions!

This whole process takes some getting used to :)

To find your address, sign in to SR.

Then click on "Account" up at the top of your screen.

On this page there will be a box that says "to deposit bitcoins, send them to this address:" about midway down your screen. If you are signed in, this box will hold your address. It will look something like this:

15z7MJKBWd9JuTT2sbwCRqDoF5JqEnnUei 

It used to be that, from the account screen, you had to click another link that said something like 'make a withdrawal" to get to your address but not the case any more.

hope this helps!
