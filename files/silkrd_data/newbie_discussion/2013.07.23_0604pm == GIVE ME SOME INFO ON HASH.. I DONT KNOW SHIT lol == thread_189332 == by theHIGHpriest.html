Post by: Sam Glad on July 24, 2013, 05:33 am
hash i made from compressed trichomes off the marijuana plant.  by weight, the trichomes are the richest in active cannabinoids of any part of the marijuana plant, so collecting them for smoking greatly reduces the amount of plant material that needs to be burned for a pleasurable high to be achieved.

there are several ways of collecting these trichomes.  most involve special screens with holes of exact specifications.  You can get hash with inferior screens, but it's going to have more fibrous and leafy material in it than those designed for only a trichome to fit through and little else.  some methods use cold temperatures to make the trichome brittle and thus easier to break free of the leafy matter.  other methods involve just rubbing the plants in your hands and scraping the accumulated thrichomes off.

the smoke contains concentrated cannabinoids, so the high is the same.  it is more potent, though, so easier to inhale more of the drug than you would smoking bud.

Post by: jardallah on July 24, 2013, 06:58 am
Nowadays there are four main ways to extract hashish.

Rubbing is the classic method that has been abandoned nearly everywhere due to commercial and quality reasons.

Dry sieving is sieving dried plants, usually per beating them with sticks like a drummer and collecting the powder which is then sold as Polm / Pollen which is slightly pressed or its sold as harder pressed hashish. Normally in high quality hashish the resins will turn black through oxidation which shows in pressed hashish being dark to black on the outside. You can usually tell of the shininess and wetness of its good product.
Polm is usually divided into qualities, since they sieve plants more than once. Obviously the first sieving is the best and the third or fourth is used for Standard. Often Standard gets thinned down at the farm with other material - in the old days it was Henna nowadays I think they like bird-sand, but not sure hvent smoked cut product in ages.

Wet sieving is used in the Ice-o-lator process which is a sieving process with extremely small holes being used in connection with bud / trimmings being put into ice-cold water together with ice-cubes. This hardens the trichomes and makes it easier to let them pass thru the 25+micron sieves.
Ice-O-Lator is usually very pure but can have disappointing taste since the less plant material you get the less actual taste you have - in addition the broadness of the cannabinoid spectrum becomes decreased - in the best case you have product that gives you exactly what you want and only that, in the worst case it gives you nothing. This is a typical problem for all hq hashish, also probably being caused by small holes letting through less ripe glands.

Oil extraction: Usually using coffee-filters this method uses different gasses to extract the fluid resin from the plant material. Stuff will be called Dab or BHO or Wax or Glass or whatever shiny name they give it :P This kind of hashish is mostly created in the "medical Marijuana" scene, which is really just a name for high-end junkies. I would keep away from that stuff, the body cant metabolize the cannabinoids quick enough, 11-thc-ooh will go into your fat in masses and you'll be stoned for months after stopping, possibly get dopamine-deficiency and other shit.

In any event, if you've never smoked hash go for an easy polm.
