Post by: GrimWaldo on June 23, 2013, 05:46 pm
For proper drying of wet 'shrooms:

Place 'shrooms in a collander. Place that on top of an open tub of dessicant (like Damp Rid). Get a small fan to blow across the collander. Takes 24-48 hrs, 'shrooms are properly dried when they snap like a cracker when you try to bend them.

Another method is to get a few cellulose furnace filters, stack the sqaure filters with the 'shrooms sandwiched inbetween them, and strap the filters to the output-side of a cheap floor fan/box fan (bungee cords are good).
You shouldn't try to go more than 3-4 filters deep, and again the 'shrooms are only dry when they snap like a cracker!

Post by: TheFunGuy on June 25, 2013, 03:28 pm
@ Jason Bourne,
Here is an easy guide to the Lemon Tek:  :)
"1) Pull apart mushroom capsules.

2) Dump the powdered shrooms in a glass.

3) Cut up 2-3 limes/lemons, squeeze the juice (i just use my hand) into the glass of shrooms. Your goal in this step is to fully submerge the shrooms with lemon juice, so if you need more than 3 limes/lemons, go for it. I recommend cutting the limes/lemons into 4 pieces each, makes it easier to squeeze. Remember, if it a bit of shrooms is sticking out of the juice/isn't fully submerged, you need more juice.

4) Let this concoction sit in the glass for 20 minutes, stir every 5-10 minutes with a spoon.

5) Once 20 min is up, drink that stuff down. Your goal here is to drink it as fast as you can without keeping it in your mouth any longer than you have to.

6) Trip balls"
