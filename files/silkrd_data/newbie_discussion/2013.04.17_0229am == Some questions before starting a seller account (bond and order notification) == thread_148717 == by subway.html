Title: Re: Some questions before starting a seller account (bond and order notification)
Post by: ReD EyE on July 05, 2013, 08:32 pm
You are required to pay a bond when opening a seller's account that will be refunded to you after you have demonstrated that you are an asset to the Silk Road vendor community. To be eligible for a refund, you must have completed at least 30 transactions totaling $1,500 or more and have been a vendor for at least 6 weeks.
