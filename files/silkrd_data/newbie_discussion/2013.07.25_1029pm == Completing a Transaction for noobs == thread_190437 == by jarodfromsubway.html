Jarod,  Sending your public encryption key to the vendor isn't necessary.  The only time i would send it at all is if there was a dispute and you wanted the DCN(tracking) number from the vendor.

Silkroad has a built in escrow service.  I don't think you can use an outside source for escrow.
Title: Re: Completing a Transaction for noobs
Post by: HappyShroomer on July 25, 2013, 11:16 pm
Silk road automatically escrows for you. After you place your order it will say "In Process" and when the seller ships it he changes the status to "In Transit" and you will also see a selection that says  "finalize and resolve" when you receive your package you will log on to SR and press finalize. If you don't receive it,and you can't get any response from the seller you can have the resolution center get involved by pressing resolve.
