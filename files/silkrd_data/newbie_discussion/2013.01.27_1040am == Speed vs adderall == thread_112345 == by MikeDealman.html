I have only tried pharmaceutical d-amphetamine, and only extended-release versions. There are instant-release versions available in prescription form, and from what I've heard IR versions are better for recreational use (more euphoria, maybe some empathy, etc.) compared to XR versions, although I have not had the chance to test this out myself.

Street speed (refers more to d-amphetamine than d-methamphetamine from what I understand) obviously doesn't come in XR preparations, but probably there's a higher chance of impure product. However, no intentional binders and fillers (unless purposefully cut) will be found in something bought on the street, so it is more readily useable for different routes of administration (IV, insufflated).

I guess what type of speed you use is up to you, and might vary depending on what you want to use it for/how you want to use it.
