Title: Re: Placed 1st order 25 hours ago
Post by: 77 on July 10, 2013, 12:55 pm
Agree with dolphy - in my experience so far, anything up to 48 hours or so isn't too unusual. I've also found that some vendors are known for turning orders around very quickly. I've had an order acknowledged and marked In Transit in less than an hour, and it did ship that same day. It's typical in my experience for a vendor to mark In Transit as soon as they begin their own process, not necessarily when it ships. This is fine, as it serves as a way for the vendor to keep things straight themselves.

And it bears repeating for anyone making their first order: Never FE! You may choose to do so in the future if you become a regular with a vendor, but even that can be risky, as you never truly know the vendor's circumstances. Have fun and be safe!
