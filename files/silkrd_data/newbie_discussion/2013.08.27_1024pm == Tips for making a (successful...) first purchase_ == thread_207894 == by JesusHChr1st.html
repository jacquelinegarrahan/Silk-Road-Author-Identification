x2 on this, and I'll add

1.) Learn to read the reviews.  Lots of recent activity = good.  Lots of recent FE's = bad. 
2.) The better vendors will state their policies on their profile pages.  Read them.  Now read them again.
3.) Lots of fish in the sea - if something seems off, then go elsewhere.
4.) Small quantities a few times makes everyone more comfortable and most vendors seem to love getting return customers.
5.) Read the forums to keep up with who is doing well (and who isn't) - and be sure to post your own impressions.
6.) Keep SR and SR-forum ID's separate (...as I am doing)
7.) If the vendor can't be bothered to use PGP then be wary - particularly as the substances involved move up the, umm, sensitivity scale.

I've had very good luck so far.  Establish yourself with the established names and your odds of happiness increase substantially.
