Post by: zipp on February 15, 2013, 07:58 am
Have you tried www.virwox.com ?

They accept PayPal, Skrill, Credit Cards, Neteller and they do accept uKash as well.  If you are going to deposit via uKash however, I would recommend depositing your uKash vouchers into a Neteller account (www.neteller.com) first, and then from Neteller, deposit into VirWox.  The fee is lower this way, and VirWox has no deposit limit for Neteller.

Hi rayzzz !

Fellow french buyer here :)

I personnally used "bitcoin-central.net" and i was pretty satisfied. You WILL need to make a "money transfer" to Paymium (the company owning bitcoint-central) bank account in order to get BTC. It usually takes me 24H to transfer money to bitcoin-central.net and convert it into Bitcoins.

I also think it's worth mentioning that bitcoin-central.net offers double-authentication (just like in Gmail) so it looks really secure to keep your Bitcoins safe.

As far as i know, you can't really buy Bitcoins fully anonymously in France yet. Got to rely on SR's Bitcoins tumbler :)
