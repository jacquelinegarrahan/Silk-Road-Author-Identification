Title: Re: fastest way to cancel an order?
Post by: BillWithers on August 06, 2013, 03:56 pm
Right you can't cancel the order unless it is still 'processing' after four days.

You'd have to ask the vendor to cancel the order if you want it cancelled before then.

The time delay in part helps protect vendors. The vendor might have your order packaged and ready to ship, but not update the status to 'in transit' until after it has actually been dropped. It would suck for the vendor to have shipped it, then when going to mark it in transit, the buyer had cancelled the order.
