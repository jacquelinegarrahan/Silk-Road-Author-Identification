Title: Re: Cola tips?
Post by: wireless214 on September 23, 2013, 02:38 pm
In my very limited experience, I've learned that a great majority of the "pure" coke on SR is anything but. Be careful and do your homework before going with a vendor. Order small first to sample. Though I have also run into vendors sending great samples and when you go back for more, they send lower quality stuff even when they say it's from the same batch.

As far as how to goes, I'm far from an expert and will check back for any tips that anyone else leaves, but make sure it's dry, chop/grind it into as fine a powder as possible, and start out really small. You can always do more, but once it's in your system, that's it. The better the coke, the less you'll need. And with coke in general, you get a better effect with smaller amounts spread out and redosed periodically. A lot at once just gives you a headache. Small, match sized lines. Start with one. Go to two when you know how you'll react, then another every 30 mins to an hour.

Another reason to start slow with EVERY batch is that until you test it with an EZ test, you won't know the quality or if there are active cuts. Your reaction can differ every time. It pays to play it safe and will make it much more enjoyable.

More experienced users feel free to correct me and/or add as you see fit.
