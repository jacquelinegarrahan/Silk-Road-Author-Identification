Title: Re: Best dutch drugs exctacy?
Post by: Leukart on April 25, 2013, 01:49 am
You should always do a bit of research on the vendor before ordering. Many will suggest "the best stuff ever, 100%" which will mostly be untrue. Most synthesis processes will yield 76% or more typically 84% MDMA. This is not to say that the remaining 15-25% is gunk! It could partially be MDA, MDEA or other related yeilded products - and no doubt some gunk.

Honest providers will have lab tested at 76% or 84%. If you're doing a pill for the first time, and you have reasonable feedback  from those around you who have taken before, then 1 pill is a good start. If you're talking powder, I would suggest 120mg (keeping in mind that at an 84% yield this is about 100mg) is a safe and reasonable start.

Have fun, and keep safe!
