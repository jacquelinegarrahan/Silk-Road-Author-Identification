Title: Re: Help getting started selling
Post by: mcguire39 on June 21, 2013, 02:38 am
Hi and welcome. PGP is fairly straightforward, but as a vendor you will be dealing with it on pretty much every order so it's good to become proficient through practice.

It really takes a good, saavy businessperson to be a good vendor. You have to manage all aspects just like any other business, but the customers are even more picky, and attention to detail for safe shipping is a must.

There are people who will give you say moneypaks or other forms of stuff for bitcoins, or you can sell your bitcoins for that. But right safely extracting the BTC can be tricky in large amounts I would imagine.

Of course you'll need supplies like dcn labels, valid (but not yours) return addresses for the packages, some way to print the shipping labels, etc.
