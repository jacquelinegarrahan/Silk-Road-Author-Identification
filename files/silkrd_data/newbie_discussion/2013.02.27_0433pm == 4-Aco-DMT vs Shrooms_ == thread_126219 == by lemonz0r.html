Title: Re: 4-Aco-DMT vs Shrooms?
Post by: strangeman on February 27, 2013, 05:09 pm
I haven't had a chance to try Shrooms yet, but I've had a few goes at 4-Aco. My experience was that it had an intense body high, gave me a mild case of the tryptamine stupids but was very low anxiety and mellow. It wasn't especially visual for me, at least not around 20-25mg. Not particularly insightful or "deep" in my experience, but ideal for watching AdultSwim. It did make shit really funny. For my wife it was intensely visual and had a fractal complexity not unlike LSD, but only in a natural environment.

Most people will say that Shrooms are a deeper, more spiritual/intellectual experience than 4-Aco, but I couldn't say one way or the other.

One word to the wise, store it in an amber vial away from heat and especially moisture. The shit degrades if you look at it funny.

dngdwn: I get nauseous from tryptamines generally, but 4-Aco only every gives me a mild bit of it during the come up. A little weed usually tamps it right down.
