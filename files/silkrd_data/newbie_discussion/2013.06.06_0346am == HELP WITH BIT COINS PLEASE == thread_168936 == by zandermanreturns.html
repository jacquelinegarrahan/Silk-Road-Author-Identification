Title: Re: HELP WITH BIT COINS PLEASE
Post by: BenJesuit on June 06, 2013, 03:59 am
1.On your SR account, click account.
2.You'll see on that page "click here to make a deposit".
3. After you click it, it will give you a bitcoin address. Will look something like: 9GBkNjncB1ZjiHdDaRPBZdBRKwmAb3TA2R (<-not valid, just an example).
4.Your BTC receive address is case sensitive so copy and paste it to a notepad program or something. Do not transcribe it by retyping. An error equals lost coins.

5. Go to any number of cash to BTC exchangers and this is the "send to" BTC address you will input.

If you decide to use an intermediate wallet to further obscure your transaction, then that wallet's address becomes your send to BTC addresss. SO say you use easywallet.org via TOR as your receive wallet from the cash to BTC exchanger. You would then send to SR's BTC receive address for your account from there.

Pictorial:

[CASH 2 BTC xchgr]------->[SR Recieve BTC Address on your account page]

or


[CASH 2 BTC xchgr]------->[online or offline wallet's recv BTC address using TOR]-------->[SR Recieve BTC Address on your account page]

Easy peasy.
