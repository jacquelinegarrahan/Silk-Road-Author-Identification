Post by: super-silk-woman on April 16, 2013, 06:03 am
Watch out for Black Market Reloaded (BMR) they have a different system over there and a lot of the feedback's are made up or generated to look real. There are "vendors" there who make a "living" off these multiple accounts switching in between them when one goes bad. Its not hard to see when you start researching  the feedback. It becomes obvious that the feedback is from a group of the same users all leaving each other positive feedback. like a giant circle of vendors leaving each other positive feedback for transactions that never happened. Sprinkled in between those are a few forced ratings (and forced FE ->Finalized Early thus releasing the funds to them) from real customers. The key is they all want you to FE. No scammer will take escrow. I have seen a "vendor" who ONLY asked for the shipping to be FE and they ONLY robbed that. I guess when you add up a ton of shipping charges it can be a small fortune to a scammer. I have found only 1 person on there of any worth (enki) who also has a SR vendors account here but I dont what name they use and they do escrow. That is the only vendor in my experience that was honest. All the rest that I thought for sure was legit robbed me for more then I want to admit to anyone. Bottom line dont assume other sites work like SR!
