Suggest 1.5-2g make tea if u want it to kick in faster with less tummy upset and get a touch more visuals out of your trip.

Warm water maybe half cup or so. Do NOT boil or microwave.

Half a lemons worth of juice or equal amount worth (half a lemon) added to water.

Toss in your shrooms. Let sit 30 mins, stir often like every 5 mins. Strain.

Drink tea. Effects will be generally felt within 15 mins.
