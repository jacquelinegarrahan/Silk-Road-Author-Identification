Title: Re: Stealthy shipping advice please?
Post by: SlimeGuy on May 25, 2013, 09:42 pm
Why not use the real company name? The fake company name is going to be suspicious for a postman, no?

I don't know where you are but it's better to be not suspicious rather than try to hide yourself in any country with a decent legal system. It's not illegal to be sent drugs (though it is a little suspicious) but once you've gone through all this trouble of mail handlers it gets really suspicious. I wouldn't want more people handling my orders either. The safest thing you can do is get it sent to a different address where the item won't be stored so if that address gets searched there's nothing dodgy there.

Title: Re: Stealthy shipping advice please?
Post by: imoscardotcom on May 25, 2013, 11:55 pm
Be safe, but don't over-think it. The more complex you make it, the more room for error. Choose an address that you know you can reliably have things delivered (and an address to which you have had things delivered in the past) and, most importantly, keep your order size within reason. While it certainly isn't out of the realm of possibility, the chances of LE doing a controlled delivery for a personal amount (e.g. an amount that would only last you a couple days) are very low. Most LE departments simply don't have the resources to go after people for personal amounts, especially when the post office just confiscates small amounts they find (unless of course it's a repeat offender). Most seizures of personal amounts come from people being pulled over. It always amazed me that people would drive with drugs on their person or in their car knowing they had a warrant or suspended license.

My Dad was a LE officer for over 35 years, so I would ride around with him on occasion, so I was privy to some LE tactics and witness to a few arrests that went from simply having a warrant to a full on intent to deliver charge due to having drugs on them. One of the more "facepalm" moments (and the moment I realized I'd never be a cop) was when I was riding around with a state cop. He pulls over a truck for having a tiny crack in the windshield, which leads to him finding out the guy has a warrant for failing to appear for a speeding ticket, which leads to him finding meth and weed in the truck.

Ok, I feel like I've sufficiently deviated from the OP. Sorry. :)
