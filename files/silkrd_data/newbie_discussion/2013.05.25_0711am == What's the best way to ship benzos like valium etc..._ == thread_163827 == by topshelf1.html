Title: Re: What's the best way to ship benzos like valium etc...?
Post by: SoAlone on May 26, 2013, 12:45 am
When you say they come vacuum packed, you mean the blister packs? Because those can rattle pretty loud. Unless you can stop the rattling, you can always make the return address; "Bob's Baby Rattles" :)

Otherwise insulate with bubble wrap, or have even gotten with several pages of newspaper circulars wrapped around. Recycle!

If you have access to some other sort of lite foam product, like you'd see on a portable air conditioner filter, that would work too.

With the foam and or bubble wrap, you probably want a sturdy piece of cardboard, or at least index card thickness to make a little protective sandwich.

Good Luck~~!
