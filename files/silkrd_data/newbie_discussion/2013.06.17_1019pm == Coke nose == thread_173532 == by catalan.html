Well first off, cocaine is probably the worst drug you could be doing considering you had a rhinoplasty. Second, you should absolutely wash your coke with either acetone or ethanol to remove any cuts because you don't want to be doing more damage than necessary. Lastly, although you said you have used saline sprays, make sure you continue to do so. In my experience, a saline wash after a coke session makes a huge difference. I can comfortably breathe through my nostrils meaning sleep is much easier at night. Leaving coke in your nose dries out your septum, irritating and eventually deviating it. I recommend you go back to your doctor and tell him the truth. Doctor patient confidentiality was made for these exact scenarios and you shouldn't be afraid to utilize it.

Also, taking cocaine orally is definitely a waste. No idea if its true, but I've heard it can cause stomach ulcers because of how corrosive it is. Then again, I figure that must be for large quantities because regular coke users ingest a good amount from the drip and it doesn't seem to be an issue.
