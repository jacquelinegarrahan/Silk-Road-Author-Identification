Title: Re: What is a good starter Psychedelics ?
Post by: PsychedelicSphere on June 03, 2013, 03:31 am
I think shrooms would be good for your first time, but you have to remember everyone is different and everyone has different reactions to different drugs. I find it comforting to have someone going through, somewhat, the same experience you are. So I would suggest for your first time have two close friends you are very comfortable with. One to trip with and one to trip sit.

I love the outdoors and really think that's where psychedelic experiences should happen. Indoors is just a different feeling and pretty much a whole different experience in itself.

You must realize how confusing things can get on psychedelics. If you are tripping hard enough simply conversation can get too confusing. Be prepared for this.

Remember to embrace everything that happens in the trip. Just let everything happen and don't fight it.

Make a nice playlist of relaxing music that just has good vibes to it. If you are looking for music that will help you get lost inside your own mind I would recommend some Pink Floyd.

For your first time tripping try your hardest to avoid anyone you don't know. Stay away from any authority, parents, anyone that you don't want to be around lol.

If you find yourself in a bad place or having a bad thought, simply change the setting. It might be as simple as changing the song that is on, or maybe all you need to do is walk away by yourself and look at some flowers.

I wrote this up pretty fast and skipped a good bit, but if you have any specific questions please ask!

~PsychedelicSphere

Post by: lanman on June 03, 2013, 09:10 pm
I would also recommend mushrooms. I would recommend that you take them infused in tea to reduce the chances of upsetting your stomach. Just break apart the mushrooms into small pieces with your fingers, put then in a teopot, pour hot water, cover the pot, and let it brew for at least thirty minutes (you don't need to keep heating up the water).

Also, my recommendation is to take mushrooms in a quiet place, either by yourself or with a close friend who is also taking mushrooms, or with a sitter. However, the sitter should be somebody who should not be intrusive, only to support you if necessary. I would avoid taking mushrooms in a place with a lot of distractions or people, or with people who are taking other types of drugs.

Bon voyage!
