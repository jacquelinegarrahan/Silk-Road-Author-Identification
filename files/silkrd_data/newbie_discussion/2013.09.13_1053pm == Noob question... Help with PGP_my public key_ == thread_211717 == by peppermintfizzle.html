how to obtain public key. open GPG Keychain Access window:
1) find your own name on the list.
2) click it (highlight) and press ctl + C (copy)
3) when you press ctl + V (paste) your public key will appear

whenever i send a encrypted message i paste my Public Key underneath.

hope this helps.
