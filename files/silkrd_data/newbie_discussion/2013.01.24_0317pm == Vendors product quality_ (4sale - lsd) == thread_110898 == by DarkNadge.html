Title: Re: Vendors product quality? (4sale - lsd)
Post by: Anyo on February 03, 2013, 06:54 am
I took half of 4Sale's 150ug hit this past Tuesday for the first time. I was chasing an “ego death” as a cure to my long battle with social anxiety. After two long hours, I had hardly felt anything, and thought that I had only talked myself into feeling anything up until that point. So I took the other half. Right when I did, I felt it hit me all of a sudden. I was in awe.

Throughout the experience, I kept coming back to a certain feeling that felt so second-nature to me. It was pure joy/happiness/fun. I felt that I had lost touch with this natural feeling for too long from years of conforming to a rigid pattern. The re-realization of this feeling still hasn’t left me after about four days. Although I don’t feel it as strongly now as during the trip, I now know what to look for in life. I’m much more sensitive to this feeling in my everyday life. This is the beauty in it. Maybe it is the goal of the cosmic game of consciousness- to experience this feeling of fun.

I started to make much more loose word associations. At this point, I also started seeing the simplicity in the things the mind goes crazy over, which caused me to laugh from a very deep place. It also hit me that this substance helps to distance you from your thoughts, while smoking pot just makes the mind overactive.

The days following the trip, I was living life mostly free from identification with any self-made identity. I would see the identities come and go, or quickly snap out of it if I did happen to fall for it. For the first time, I felt facial muscles relax after years of tension, and it felt so relieving- my eyes, cheeks, forehead.  I noticed muscles in the scalp area especially that were now relaxed on a regular basis, and it made me feel happy. Hopefully a few more doses will lessen the weakly resurfacing patterns of old behavior, and help completely relinquish identification with the mind.
