Title: Advise - proper way to message vendors
Post by: resalesam on January 28, 2013, 05:37 am
Is it acceptable to use the message vendors without GPG for information about their products? I understand from what I have read about personal/shipping info in GPG of course.
Title: Re: Advise - proper way to message vendors
Post by: farmer1 on January 28, 2013, 05:54 am
No problem messaging most vendors in clear text. If your message is compromised then it doesn't matter if they don't know who is talking to who.

The occasional vendor is a stickler on this and won't respond unless it is encrypted. Check their vendor page to find out. Most of us find it annoying to decrypt non-secret information though.

I recommend to always encrypt identifying information (you address). This is a personal decision though as it doesn't effect the vendor's security, so most of us don't require it.
