Title: Silent Darkness
Post by: badniff on February 20, 2013, 11:22 am
It's like the hard mode of tripping. Every sense distracts from the complete tranformation of mind that psychedelics can induce. Everything you see has associations, your home contains emotional energy and the music you listen to is the expression of feelings and thoughts. These things shape an experience, not necessarily in a bad way, but in a sometimes predictable way.

When you are in the dark, without sound, everything changes. You will  meet fears, past experiences and people you didn't know you valued. I hope everyone tries this at least once, for the amount of personal development one undergoes in the sensory deprived state is monumental.
