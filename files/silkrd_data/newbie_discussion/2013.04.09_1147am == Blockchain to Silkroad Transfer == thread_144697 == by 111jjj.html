Title: Re: Blockchain to Silkroad Transfer
Post by: fosters on April 09, 2013, 01:04 pm
Bitcoin transactions are non-reversible. A Google search will have revealed that very quickly.

When you say a small amount, how small do you mean? Silk road only displays up to 4 decimal places in your wallet. ie, if you sent less than 0.001 coins to SR they are inaccessible. This could be the reason for no show.

Alternatively use block explorer and search for your receiving address to see if has been seen on the network.
