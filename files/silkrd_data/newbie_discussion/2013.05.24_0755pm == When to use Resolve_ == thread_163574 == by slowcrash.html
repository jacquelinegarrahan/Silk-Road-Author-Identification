Title: When to use Resolve?
Post by: slowcrash on May 24, 2013, 07:55 pm
Tomorrow's mail is the last chance an order has of arriving before auto-finalizing. The vendor states in their profile that shipping does take sometime and is not consistent. The feedback backs this up, everybody seems to get their packages eventually. There are only good mentions of the vendor in the forums and they post in the forums also. According to the vendor's terms, I would not be eligible for a reship or refund as I do not have tracking to prove it was not delivered. Which would seem as less offensive to a vendor - having 5/5 feedback stating product was not received or having it go to resolve while waiting for it to show up in another week?

Hi slowcrash,

You should enter resolution if your order has not arrive once it has been 10 days from it being marked 'in transit'. Once you enter resolution the finalise period is automatically extended by 5 days, and you can request a further extension of necessary. Vendors do not consider it offensive for an order to be brought to resolution - that is the normal procedure if an item has not turned up yet.

Don't let it auto-finalise as this will negatively impact your purchasing stats.

Libertas
