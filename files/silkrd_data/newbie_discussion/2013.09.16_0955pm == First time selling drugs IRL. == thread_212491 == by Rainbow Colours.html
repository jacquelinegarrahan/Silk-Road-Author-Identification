Title: Re: First time selling drugs IRL.
Post by: Cornelius23 on September 20, 2013, 11:31 am
Yes, dealing is stressful enough even for people who do know what they're doing. However...

Firstly, observe how your own dealers operate. If you don't have enough experience of buying from other dealers, what are you thinking?

Secondly, don't sell anything that you wouldn't use yourself. Ideally, test every batch, in the form that you're going to sell it, personally. If you don't have enough experience of what you're selling to tell what's good product and what isn't, what the hell are you thinking?

Thirdly, if your only motive it to make money with no regard to quality or the effect on your customers... you're not worth thinking about.
