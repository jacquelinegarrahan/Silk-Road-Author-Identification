First off, good luck with the FE. I admire someone that can go totally against the vast majority of buyers on here as well as the buyers guide. Kudos for the balls to march to a different drummer. <thumbs up>. ;)

Secondly, you should wait until (if) you receive the product before leaving a rating or review. Once you post them, they are permanent and not editable.

What you can do now is go to the vendor's homepage and open up a discussion if you want to leave some kind of feedback without leaving a permanent rating/review.
