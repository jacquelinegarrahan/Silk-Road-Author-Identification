Title: Re: Transferring btc from Mt. Gox to a SR wallet
Post by: android465764E on May 11, 2013, 05:44 pm
Log into SR and click "account" at the top of the page.
Copy the wallet address to your clipboard.

Go to mtgox (mtgox.com/trade/funding-options) and click "withdraw funds".

Enter the amount you want to withdraw, and paste your SR wallet address in the field next to "bitcoin address".

Choose the options you want below, and click "confirm".

Funds will appear in your SR wallet after a few hours, depending on what options you choose on the previous step.
