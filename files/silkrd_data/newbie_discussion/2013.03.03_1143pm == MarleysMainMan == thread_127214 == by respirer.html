I'm not really a newbie to SR; I've made several purchases, and in a couple of cases I've posted reviews on the main forum review threads. But I haven't made a lot of posts, and suddenly I find myself locked out and unable to post more reviews. So if I have something to say that seems like it could be helpful or informative, I guess I'll post here until allowed back into the regular forums:

I had an excellent experience ordering weed from MarleysMainMan recently. I ordered in a week with a long weekend, but even so received my package on the sixth day after ordering. The long weekend added a day, almost certainly, and it would be understandable if my particular order added another (I asked MMM to combine two strains in a half-oz order, which he was good enough to do). Stealth was excellent, and the product was slightly overweight. Both strains (Amnesia and Green Crack) are great, quite potent. The Amnesia affects me, at least, more like an indica than a sativa. The Green Crack might be my favorite of all the sativas I've tried, and if MMM has a steady supply, it could easily become my regular smoke.
