Remove syringe from spoon, hold upright, tap with finger to release air bubbles to top. Depress plunger until the liquid beads at top- forcing the air out.  Tie up limb and inject. 
Some tips: I found that serious users like my friend from Europe to the time to add far more water to their hits, and dilute more, even if the dose stayed the same.  While there could be any number of reasons for this, I simply found it to be a far greater rush and ease of withdrawals. Some may say the gear was just better those times I increased the amount of water and time necessary for dilution, or maybe experience allowed a better injection, but from now on no matter what type of gear, I will always endeavor to add more water for a bigger shot.
