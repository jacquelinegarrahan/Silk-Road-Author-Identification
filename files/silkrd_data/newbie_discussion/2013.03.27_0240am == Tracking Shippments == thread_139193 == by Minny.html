Title: Re: Tracking Shippments
Post by: ladyjane on March 27, 2013, 01:25 pm
On the vendors profile there is a button under their name 'send a message' to contact them if you need to. wait an extra day or two if it was only sent out today though.
Not every order posted to you will have tracking, it depends on the type of postage choice. Most vendors will describe the mailing options in their profile or listing and whether or not it comes with a tracking number.
