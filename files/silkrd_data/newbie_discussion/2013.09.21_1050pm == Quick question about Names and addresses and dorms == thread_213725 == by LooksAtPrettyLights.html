Title: Re: Quick question about Names and addresses and dorms
Post by: Yoda on September 22, 2013, 05:48 am
Yes... there is a possibility that your package will be flagged somehow.  The person named does not live at that address.

Say the mailman that delivers in that area has been doing so daily for the last 20 years... this guy knows who lives where, it's his route.  He may think there is some kind of error since you haven't changed your address to that one, nor even put your name on the mailbox... so he may just drop a notice saying come pick it up in person.  (this is also a reason why you'd not want to send shit to an abandoned house)

Then lets say you do go to the post office to pick it up... you show them the card and your ID, but your ID still has your parents address... the postal worker may be a dick and say that your ID doesn't match up.

I'm not saying this is 100% or anything, but it is a risk. 

People constantly ask this question... "can I deliver to my dorm room?"  Yeah, why wouldn't you be able to?... what's the difference really?
