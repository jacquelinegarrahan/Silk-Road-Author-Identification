I FE'd because the vendor asked me to as it was my first ever SR order.  The vendor has an extremely good reputation and has contacted me to say when the goods are being shipped. 

I realise I have put my money at risk by doing this and perhaps I should have done more research first but from looking at vendor reviews this seems to be fairly common practice when they are dealing with new users.
There's a problem with what you're saying though.  If you think about it, feedback on vendors who require FE is almost meaningless.  The reason the feedback is so great for FE vendors overall is because buyers write the feedback immediately after they placed the order.  They are hoping and praying that the vendor doesn't rip them off, so of course they are going to write great feedback.  Yes, after being ripped off a buyer could change the feedback, but I'd guess 90% of people on SR aren't even aware that you can change feedback later on FE orders. And the people that do know often don't spend the time to go back and change it.

So don't rely on feedback comments for FE vendors.  They werre most likely written after the buyer checked out and mean nothing.  If a vendor has been scamming FE for a while, some of it may have hit his overall rating score, but that may not show up for a while because of the inaccuracy of the feedback.
