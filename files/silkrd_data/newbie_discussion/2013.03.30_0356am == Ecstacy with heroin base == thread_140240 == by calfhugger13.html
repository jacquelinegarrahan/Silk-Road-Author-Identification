a small dose of a strong opiate goes very well with PURE MDMA, to much of either muddles up the experience. your never going to find a pill that contains both thats a very old myth, heroin is expensive, almost useless orally and most wouldn't actually like the combo. if you have a certain roll that is less stimulating and more relaxed then normal its likely you got PURE MDMA for your very first time and are used to MDMA with AMPHETAMINES. PURE MDMA is much like pure COCAINE, its slightly stimulating, very euphoric and very different than cut product. do you have any tolerance to opiates or mdma? one of the most euphoric combos i have ever done was mdma+lsd+oxycodone+nitrous oxide, i was rolling around on the floor for hours in total euphoria. i would not recommended a opiate mdma combo outside of a home setting you wont want to dance.
