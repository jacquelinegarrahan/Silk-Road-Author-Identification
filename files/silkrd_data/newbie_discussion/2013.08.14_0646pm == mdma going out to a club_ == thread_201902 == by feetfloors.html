All I can say is stick to a safe, comfortable dose and make sure you drink plenty of water. Hydration is key when it comes to rolling. If you become dehydrated, not only are you putting yourself in danger, but it lessens the effects of your roll. Dehydration makes things not as pleasant as far as effects go and can make the comedown/hangover much worse. So just make sure you are safe and you'll have a great time. And if you're doing it this much that frequently, I would advise taking a break for a good while. At least a few months, you don't want to lose the "magic".
