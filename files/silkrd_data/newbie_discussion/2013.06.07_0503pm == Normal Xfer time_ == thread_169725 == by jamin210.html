Title: Re: Normal Xfer time?
Post by: speedygonzales23 on June 07, 2013, 05:32 pm
Once the transaction is confirmed it will be a matter of 10 minutes.

The time that the transaction takes to be confirmed depends on the speed new blocks are generated on the bitcoin network and if you did pay fee or not for the transaction. Since a transaction requires 6 confirmations to be considered confirmed, typically you have to wait until 6 new blocks are generated on the bitcoin network.
