Title: Motherof2 Vendor Review
Post by: KarlMao on May 14, 2013, 02:26 pm
Ive had the pleasure of sampling Motherof2's two new strains: Cherry Pie and Purple diesel.  They arrived in multiple stealthy sealed vacuum packs, but when I cut open the bags, my entire house started smelling like the bud. 

Cherry Pie:  Has a very pungent and slightly minty scent.  These are some of the frostiest nugs I have ever seen.  While breaking a nug apart, my fingers became sticky immediately.  A squeeze test noted great density and also a hard spongy rebound, definitely AAA grade. There is just a thick layer of trichromes covering every corner of every bud.  Smoking is great, it burns slowly and nicely.  However, vaporizing it was perhaps way more efficient, as I got knocked out in a couple of vape hits!!  Definitely a great bud.

Purple diesel: This bud is actually purple! It smells very fruity and strong.  The nugs were so frosty they were almost hard to break apart.  A couple of bong hits and vape hits also put me at a very good buzz.  I got a bud the size of a tic tac box, thick and dense.

Definitely these two strains are some of the best bud I have ever had.  If you want something slightly stronger, definitely go for Cherry Pie.  However, the purple diesel is more fruity tasting and overall a more sativa buzz. 

In addition, Motherof2's customer service is top notch.  My first order did not go as well as I hoped but she made it up in a very professional manner.  Thanks!
