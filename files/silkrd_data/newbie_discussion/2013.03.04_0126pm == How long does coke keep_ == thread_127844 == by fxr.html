Title: Re: How long does coke keep?
Post by: PrincessHIGH on March 04, 2013, 11:14 pm
Storing coke in dry uncooked rice will keep it fresh and dry because rice absorbs moisture. If your coke is slightly wet or gummy in consistency chop it up, spread it, and leave it under a lamp or warm plate to dry out. High purity cocaine hcl is pretty much inert chemically, providing you store it in a cool dry place, it can last for years. However, as nearly all coke is cut and is frequently cut with other things such as sugars that are not so inert your coke might not keep for as long as it's meant to. The only way to accurately predict your coke's 'best before date' is to send it off to a lab for analysis so you know what's exactly in it. Hope this helps you :)
