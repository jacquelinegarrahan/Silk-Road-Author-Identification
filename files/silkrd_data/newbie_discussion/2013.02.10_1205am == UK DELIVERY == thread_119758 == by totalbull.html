Post by: Ron Swanson on February 10, 2013, 09:58 am
haven't had any issues with vendors in the EU, Canada or USA. well lost one from mr ouid but that was a case of his shipping method being identified and a whole lot got seized his side of the pond. it wasn't tracked delivery so he said no refund or anything. i ordered one a while after from puff daddy to the same address just to test and it got through fine so i don't think i'm on any watchlist.. maybe steer clear of those vendors shipping out 1000's of packages a week as they'll get profiled quicker. the same thing happened to budmail in the past.
be careful if you're trying to import cartons of cigarettes though, they're getting stopped more than illegal drugs. here tax evasion and terrorism are the main priorities. gotta love the system. i wouldn't order anything massive from USA because they frequently open those (clothing size packs) up to see if they can tax you on the contents. an oz or 2 should be ok but be prepared for it to be compressed.
top tip: every time you order research the vendor, their shipping methods and success rates to your country. the forums are a goldmine and a lot of vendors/substances have dedicated threads.
