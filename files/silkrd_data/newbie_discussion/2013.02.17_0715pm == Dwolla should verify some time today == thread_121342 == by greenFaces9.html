Thanks !! +1

You can do Dwolla > MtGox > SR but recently (past year or so?) Dwolla decided it wanted to make sure people were extra verified before they could send money to MtGox (for obvious reasons). That means you have to be verified in Dwolla to get money to your account, and then be verified with MtGox in order to send money directly from Dwolla to MtGox.  There are two solutions:
1) Verify yourself with MtGox by sending them proof of residency (utility bills, etc.) as well as a photo ID (Drivers License/Passport). Note however that this means Dwolla and MtGox have your name, bank account number, social security number, date of birth, mailing address, etc. instead of just your bank account number.
2) Route the money through a third party (BitInstant) to circumvent this verification requirement because Dwolla will send money directly to BitInstant without a problem compared to MtGox.

In the end, it comes down to your requirements for security. If you are okay with Dwolla and MtGox knowing all this extra information about you then you can go ahead and get verified. Honestly it probably does not matter that much since the bitcoins you buy will be put through a tumbler on SR anyway but it never hurt to be on the safe side.
