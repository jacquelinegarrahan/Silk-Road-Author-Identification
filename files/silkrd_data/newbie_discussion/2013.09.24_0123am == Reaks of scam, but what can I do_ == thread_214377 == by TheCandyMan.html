A few days later I checked the tracking number, and the order had confirmed that it reached it's destination. Days continued to pass, and I was under the impression he was just going to let it auto-finalize since he had not replied to anything since day 1.

When I logged back in, he had added it to the resolution center, saying he would take half the money back since he did not receive it.

Now I'm in a really sticky situation. I have detailed logs containing order info and tracking labels for all of my transactions, including this one, which indeed confirms delivery a few days after it shipped, just like all of my other orders to date. I also ALWAYS copy and paste the address provided by the buyer, so in the off chance that he did mess up his address, that's on his shoulders.

I did my part 100% and am out a significant amount of product, and feel I should not have to refund half of his money when the tracking confirms the package was delivered succesfuly.

But then there is my flawless reputation that I have gone above and beyond to maintain. If I don't refund his money I'm sure he is going to take it out on my reputation, and I don't think that's fair when I have a tracking number confirming it was delivered.

What do I do?!
