A buddy is a pro photographer and his advice for anyone is to take the photo in natural light! Also, use some nice clean A4 paper to carefully lay the product on. Write your vendors name also.

A lot of people take photos of products indoors leading to some grainy photo in which you could not tell a xanax from an MDMA tablet.

Traders should make the effort to get a decent product image sorted out.

Don't forget to strip the meta data before you upload.
