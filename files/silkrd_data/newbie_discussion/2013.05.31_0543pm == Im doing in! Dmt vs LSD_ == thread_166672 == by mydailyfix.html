Title: Re: Im doing in! Dmt vs LSD?
Post by: TR0N on May 31, 2013, 07:37 pm
IMO, if you've never done psychedelics before, I would NOT recommend you start by freebasing DMT. DMT is the most powerful thing I've ever. I imagine it feels a lot like dying, and can be scary and overwhelming if you're not used to the psychedelic experience. Launching yourself into DMT space for a first go around could very well scare you away from the whole idea of psychedelics.

The way I see it, (and also quite literally), DMT is the father of the tryptamine family. It is "the main vein" as Terence McKenna puts it. It is the most powerful psychedelic of all, and you should work your way up to it slowly, not start there.

My recommendation, would be that your start with mushrooms. Mushrooms are a lot of fun, and you remain "in this world" for the most part, there is just an added quality to the reality you are already familiar with. All the action happens in your head, and a mushroom trip can be very powerful and profound, but as long as you dose properly (3.5-5g) you will experience a psychedelic journey that doesn't take you TOO far from home, which is why I believe it would be the perfect introduction to psychedelics.

Jumping into DMT is definitely not recommended. Get your feet wet with mushrooms, work your way up to LSD, and if you're still enjoying yourself, then think about DMT.

My .02 BTC
