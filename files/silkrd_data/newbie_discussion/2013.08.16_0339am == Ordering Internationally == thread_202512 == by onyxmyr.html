It seems like there is always somewhat of a greater risk when importing foreign substances. Think about it. It makes an extra stop at US customs when it is incoming. A stop that domestic packs do not get.

I'd imagine the risk is lower if you ordered a minuscule amount of substance versus a bulk quantity. I've ordered from EU once and India once, and both arrived without issue.

There is always a risk when ordering illegal substances...both internationally and domestically. You will be waiting longer for your international pack to hit home plate, and that makes a lot of buyers uneasy and causes anxiousness.

Good luck, and be safe.
