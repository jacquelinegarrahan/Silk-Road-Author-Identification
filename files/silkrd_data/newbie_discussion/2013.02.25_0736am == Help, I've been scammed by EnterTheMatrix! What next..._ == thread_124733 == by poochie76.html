It is always disappointing to see a vendor go Rogue.

I have a question regarding what happens to a Demoted Vendor's Account.... which buttons will disappear?
As you  may know, when you upgrade an account to Vendor Status, you instantly gain more Links and Buttons.
My assumption is that a Demoted Vendor instantly loses these buttons and may not even have access to
some of the required pages for re-ship or whatever your case may have needed at the time.
So if in your case, your Item is Processing.... you should be able to cancel and it wont effect your refund rate.
If he marked it in transit.... and by SR rules he deleted your shipping info.... There may not be anything that can be done
unless the x-vendor answers your messages.

Unfortunately, due to the nature of how Demoting a Vendor works.... there aren't many Human Beings who would continue
to send people's product out knowing that their Vendor account is Permanently dead and cannot be recovered.

So, again.... unfortunately, due to the nature of Human Kind....
The x-vendor takes YOUR btc and transfers it to a new name, pays the Vendor Bond...
Then sends all leftover BTC to a bogus Buyer account.
Makes Listings for brand new product and sets the price to .03BTC.
Orders 300 separate transactions from himself.
Raises the price of his product to actual normal drug prices.
and will be vending again in 15 minutes with 100% feedback from 300 transactions.

Newbies are EXTREMELY susceptible to this trick.
Just cause a vendor has 300 Transactions don't mean they were Real transactions.

Follow your vendors in the forums.
Watch how they behave in the forums.
