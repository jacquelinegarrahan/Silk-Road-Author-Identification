Post by: Luvs2Trip on June 12, 2013, 02:11 pm
If you log on to SR and then click account at the top it will give you a bitcoin address (on you first time it will have a link that says 'click here to make a deposit') You then send your bitcoins to that address and they will show up in you SR account within a few hours (Has been know to take up to 24 hours)

Then you place you order and it automatically deducts the coins from your account! Then just sit back and wait for the messy times to arrive through your mailbox!!
