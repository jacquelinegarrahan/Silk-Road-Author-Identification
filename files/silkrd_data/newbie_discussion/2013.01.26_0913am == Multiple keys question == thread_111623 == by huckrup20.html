Not sure if you're still looking for an answer to your question, but here's my answer :
Yes, you can create as many personal key as you want. Each personal key is a pair of key (private key and public key).
The key you publish / give to people is your public key. You hold on to your private (secret key), never reveal it to anyone,
it's the key to read any message sent to you. Hope this helps.
