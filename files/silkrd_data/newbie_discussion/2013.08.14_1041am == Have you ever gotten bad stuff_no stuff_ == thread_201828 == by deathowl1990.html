Title: Re: Have you ever gotten bad stuff/no stuff?
Post by: mykat on August 14, 2013, 11:31 am
Hi there,

Welcome to SR, and yes drooling over all the shiny stuff makes you want to jump right in and buy  8)

I would start small, you don't say what you want to buy but advice is the same regardless.  So no need to say.

Find a vendor with good feedback.  You should especially look at the most recent feedback, so click the vendors name and go to his main page.  Look out for lot's of ppl saying "FE" or even worse feedback saying it took too long to arrive etc...
when you find one, with a product you want, check out the feedback for it.  Some vendors have too many listings and don't have everything in stock...  so your order could be delayed :(

Check that the vendor does not want you to FE (finalize early).  if they do then find another one.  If you FE then you are on your own, and you don't need that for your first buy.

never spend more than you can afford to lose. EVER :)
Start small and see how it works.

As long as you never spend anything you cannot afford to lose, and use vendors with a high rep and feedback that makes you comfortable, then you will be fine.  Also staying domestic is the way to go. 

take the plunge dude...
