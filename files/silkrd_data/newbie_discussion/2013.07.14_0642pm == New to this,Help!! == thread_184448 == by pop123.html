What Is Bitcoin?

Bitcoin is a digital currency powered by a peer to peer network, used primarily for internet transactions.
Benefits of Bitcoin
- As anonymous as Cash
- Impossible to charge-back (Unlike paypal/Credit card)
- Not controlled by a government
- Impossible to inflate due to printing

Lets Get Started With Bitcoins

The first thing we will have to do is get a wallet, witch is a program to store our bitcoins(online bank). There are desktop wallets available, and there are online wallets available.
The most popular Bitcoin wallet(Desktop) is available at http://bitcoin.org/en/download
Go ahead and download the client for your OS.
Once the client has installed..
Start > All Programs > Bitcoin
And Launch the software, once you have launched it you will see the program.
Note your bitcoin address, as you will need that for your bitcoin transactions.

Site for wallet: blockchain.info
Site to buy bitcoins: mt.gox

When you have the bitcoins in your wallet on blockchain, you click on you silkroad account
than you create a deposit adress, after doing that you send the bitcoins from you blockchain adress to your silkoad deposit adress

after that you have to wait till silkroad have shows the bitcoins on you balance.
