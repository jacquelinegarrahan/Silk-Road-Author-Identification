Sorry to hear you had a bad first transaction. 

When I started buying here back in Feb/March, the stuff was pretty decent.  I noticed that after my first few orders, I was getting either very heavily cut dope, and on several occasions now, All N-ISO. 

It is very frustrating to go through everything to buy dope;  Bitcoins, checking vendors, worries about security only to get sold a bag of cut. 

I hope that you contacted the vendor with your concerns.  The ones who are doing this will just keep selling this garbage as long as they can get away with it.  Being new, I understand wanting to build a good reputation as a buyer, but it is important to let the vendor and if you are willing, the other buyers via the forums, that you know you were sold fake dope. 

Unfortunately, as you have seen through your research, there is nothing that can be done to turn N-ISO into meth, or even salvage any meth re-rocked with it. 

Be careful when reading 'reviews' in the forums as well.  Vendors selling this crap have multiple forum 'members' who post to say how great the latest gear from so-and-so is.  Check any forum poster's history before buying on their recommendation. 

If you read the review forums for awhile, you'll see what I'm talking about.  For example, a Jr. member who spammed to 50 in 10 minutes starts raving about what a great vendor this or that one is and how great their gear is.  You may also notice the identical writing styles. 

If a vendor can sling $100+ grams of N-ISO that cost him pennies and get away with it, what happened to you will happen to many more people.
