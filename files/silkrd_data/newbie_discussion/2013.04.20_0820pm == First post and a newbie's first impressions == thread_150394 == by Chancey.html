Title: First post and a newbie's first impressions
Post by: Chancey on April 20, 2013, 08:20 pm
Hi all and its my first post here as you can see  :)

Well I'd say I have an average amount of tech-savviness but to be honest reading through the various forum sections and threads has ultimately left me feeling quite bewildered and somewhat depressed.

The whole process seems to be incredibly complex. I fully understand the reasons why that needs to be the case in order to protect buyers and vendors alike from LE and scammers etc but its so complex nonetheless. What with the PGP encryption thing, finding a trustworthy and value-for-money bitcoin vendor, then a drugs vendor; then the delivery arrangement process - real name v fake name, which address etc. There seems to be so much conflicting advice over all this which seems to change on an almost daily basis, plus who to trust and who's a scammer and the whole fluctuating bitcoin/vendor situation.

All I'd like to do is to be able to but some psychedelics in as safe as possible way for medicinal purposes in my case (no joke), and not recreational, without minimum fear/risk of being scammed and/or busted. I guess I just need some kind soul to take this UK based newbie under their wing, or a little sage advice at least, not that I would expect either as this is really just an opening post giving my initial impressions on the whole SR thing...other than all that it seems like a fantastic community and resource which I'm delighted to have come across :D
