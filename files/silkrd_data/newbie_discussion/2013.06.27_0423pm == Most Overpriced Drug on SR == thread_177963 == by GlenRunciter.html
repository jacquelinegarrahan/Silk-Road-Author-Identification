Title: Re: Most Overpriced Drug on SR
Post by: NovaScipio on June 28, 2013, 02:23 am
Depends on how the venders get their supply.

 I could imagine that coke and meth vendors get their product from trafficking organizations and those groups have pretty wild expenses (street muscle, mules, bribes, lawyers, weapons, risk, buying the product from the producer). It would be ignorant to say the cost of those groups' expenses don't get passed onto customers on SR. Then you have to include the vender's time spend packaging and shipping product. Unless you live next to and know the producer, expect a far heftier price.

 Drugs like weed, mushrooms, and other easily produced goods can cut out the trafficker, thus passing wild savings to customers when the vendor produces the product them/him/hers/self. All you would pay for would be shipping, materials, producer and packaging wages.
