Title: Re: First order.
Post by: lemonz0r on February 09, 2013, 06:27 pm
No, most vendors will work with newbies :)

Some require things like finalizing early (which I wouldn't do except under exceptional circumstances), or won't give you a full refund if you're new, but - depending on what you're buying - it's usually possible to find a vendor who'll treat newbies well. AFAIK, post count has no effect on whether vendors will deal with you or not - only your purchase/refund history.
