Title: Re: Tor browser and clearnet
Post by: Libertas on May 17, 2013, 07:29 pm
If you want your browsing to remain anonymous then you SHOULD be using Tor for browsing clearnet sites. However, it is inadvisable to log in to your personal accounts via Tor (email, social networking etc.), and do not access your Mt.Gox account over Tor or they will flag your account for verification.

But yes, you can absolutely use Tor for safely browsing clearnet websites - just not the other way around!
