Title: Re: Does this sound like a good vendor for a first time buyer?
Post by: JackBlue on March 20, 2013, 03:40 pm
Lots of vendors will sell to new buyers without FE.  NEVER EVER finalize early.  You lose all of your power in the deal and are at the seller's mercy.  And never believe any of the feedback scores of vendors who require FE.  All of the ratings were submitted before the buyer even knew how fast it shipped or if it shipped or if it was any good.   And if you look at the feedack from buyers who FE, they very rarely update their feedback later.  So bottom line, dont' believe any of the feedback for vendors like that.  Just look a little harder for vendors who don't require it.  New vendors aren't allowed to require FE, so maybe it would be a good match to try out a new vendor.  Also, since a new vendor has just spent a substantial sum of money in a vendor deposit and probably don't have a lot of feedback yet, they are probably more likely than a typical vendor to try to make sure his/her first customers are happy and write good feedback.
