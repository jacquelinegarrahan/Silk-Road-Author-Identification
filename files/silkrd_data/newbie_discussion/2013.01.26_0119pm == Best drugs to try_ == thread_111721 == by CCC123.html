Title: Re: Best drugs to try?
Post by: DiddleMyThoughts on January 26, 2013, 02:06 pm
Psychedelics are not for the faint of heart. I usually see them as a catalyst, something that works with whatever is already in your own mind. With drugs like MDMA and coke, youre pretty much guaranteed a good time wherever you are .. unless youre overdosing or some such other extreme nastiness..

Psychedelics will almost always amplify your own inner self and expose your ego raw, pulling you apart and handing you your arse as you fumble about the inner workings of your mind. Start off slow and low dose, you can always add more but you cant take it out once its in your system!

I wouldnt recommend harder psychedelics like DMT or high-dose shrooms unless you have a fairly good mental constitution and do not suffer from any mental illnesses. Some people have bad trips but you know, they can be a learning experience too.

Set and setting are paramount. Go to lengths to ensure you have a good headspace and external environment; ie. music you enjoy, dim ambient lighting, good friends you trust who are on the same drugs and dosage.. it always helps to have a tripping partner who you can bounce ideas off and who you arent afraid to get a bit messy around.  In a comfortable environment man, you can have so much fun with these drugs! Creativity is off the dial, embrace the random, commune with nature, get in touch with yourself and free your mind from daily constraints.

They can open your mind and your soul to the unique, the weird, the sideways and the strange.  I fucken love psychedelics and in particular, I love DMT. That is another topic in itself though..

Theres plenty of reading material out there in the clearnet, I read alot about this stuff because theres alot of questions yet to be answered.. its an exciting place to be; on the frontier of psychedelic experience.. but, thats just me..

Carve your own path man but remember, we cant do this alone :)

Peace,
Diddle.

Post by: bokchoy on January 30, 2013, 06:41 am
Psychedelics are my personal favorite. I was kind of anxious going into my first ever trip (shrooms), having no clue what was going to happen. I've always heard about what it's like to trip, but until you experience it yourself you really have no idea. The small amount of fear I had soon faded as I realized how amazing the experience can be.

I'd say shrooms will be easier to handle than acid for your first trip, especially since you can more accurately measure out the dose yourself. Shrooms and acid both vary in potency, but at least you know shrooms are relatively close to the same almost every time. To be honest, knowing what I do now I'd say 2C's would be a really good introduction to psychedelics for someone who has no experience with them, specifically 2C-B. Great body high, awesome visuals but nothing too intense, and it doesn't have as much potential to screw with your head like tryptamines do. If 2C-B just isn't quite your thing but didn't scare the shit out of you, go for some shrooms or acid. Everyone is different, and there's plenty of people who hate 2C's with a passion but LOVE shrooms or acid.

If you do like, good! After trying it some more and becoming familiar with the whole tripping thing, MOVE ON TO DMT!!! I cannot recommend this enough, it's ridiculously intense but the most beautiful thing you will ever experience in your life, as long as you can handle it.  ;)

On a side note, ketamine is pretty amazing too. And is less likely to scare the shit out of you as long as you don't k-hole your first time. Almost everyone loves it!
