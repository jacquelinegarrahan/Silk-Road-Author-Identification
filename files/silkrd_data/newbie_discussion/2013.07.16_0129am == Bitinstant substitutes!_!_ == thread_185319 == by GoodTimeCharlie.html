Localbitcoins is ok. I started there and made a successful transaction so, I'll vouch for it. The key is to do your research though... Try to either find a seller who will sell locally (if you live in a good area... Always be safe) or, look at the other payment options. Some take bank deposits (not very anonymous... but usually reliable) some take money orders, some even take electronics. CampBX is another decent one too I suppose. Another option is going on the Bitcoin OTC IRC chat and finding a person. either way you have options.
