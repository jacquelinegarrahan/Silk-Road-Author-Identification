Post by: sportshort on July 09, 2013, 10:06 pm
Once you're logged into SR, click the 'account' link at the top of the page. In your account page you'll see 'To deposit bitcoins, send them to this address' followed by a string of numbers and letters, which is your bitcoin address. Send coins to that address from wherever they are currently residing. Don't panic if they don't show up right away. After each transaction on SR, it's a good idea to hit the 'get new address' link on your SR account page, and a brand new bitcoin address will be generated for you. Good luck and be careful.

Not p2p but PGP! Google PGP. I myself use GPG4win. It's basically a method to encrypt. You can find guides on the internet everywhere.

http://www.reddit.com/r/SilkRoad/wiki/guides/multiplayer_dreams_/guide_1 Look here under 'encrypting your address'. It basically makes sure that whatever info you send (your address in this case) can only be read by a person with a proper code. That's why most vendors have a PGP key you can use to encrypt a message to them. Only they can decrypt it.
