Title: Re: Bitcoin price seems to be really hiking at the moment.
Post by: smellywelly on March 21, 2013, 08:32 pm
I found an answer to my question in another thread.

On the face of it, we see BTC as our currency - or our price if you will. The vendors actually specify their price in USD however, and the BTC price that we see fluctuates with the market.

Therefore, the price is always the same. Now I feel like I'm getting somewhere :)
