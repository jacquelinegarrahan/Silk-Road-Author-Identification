Title: Emerald's "Royal Afghani" review
Post by: roubignol_01 on June 05, 2013, 11:06 am

Hi …

I successfully ordered some Alibaba from Emerald, some weeks ago and was pretty pleased with the product. Decent quality morrocan hash, commercial quality i'd say. Good potency, average taste.
For my second order, i decided to step up on the quality scale, and went for the Royal Afghani.
Took a week to arrive, but i'm living in a very isolated area. Stealth was good.
The hash came flattened, not a concern if you ask me, and a bit overweight, i won't complain about that either :).
Pretty soft, a little dry, reminds me the 70 micron Bubblehash i used to press some years ago… Despite it's name, we're not talking about anything close to common afghan black hash (which is a good thing in my opinion, I tend to think you get used very quickly to afghan hash, while good quality morrocans are more consistent on the long run). This is some damn fine hash, and something special compared to the more common hashes you get from morocco. Smoke is smooth and clean, the taste is spicy, with pepper undertones.
Effects are very strong. I mean VERY strong ! A really powerful head high combined to a warm and mild body stone. No need to supercharge joints…
I'm not sure i've sampled any morrocan more potent than this in my 20 years of hash addiction,
and i've sampled quite a lot of qualities.
To summarize, if you're looking for top quality morrocan hash, Emerald delivers, and his Royal afghani is a real treat…
