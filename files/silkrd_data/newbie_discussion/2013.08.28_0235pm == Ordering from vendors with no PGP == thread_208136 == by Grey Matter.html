Title: Re: Ordering from vendors with no PGP
Post by: bitchaser on August 28, 2013, 03:50 pm
I have been wondering about this too...
PGP is definitely an extra blanket of security, but your personal details are only held in Escrow until the vendor accepts your order, and then they are deleted. The only risk is if hackers or the authorities happen to get into silk road while your order is still processing, cause your name and addy will still be in the system. So its unlikely youre gonna get screwed not using it, but its a better safe than sorry thing I guess.
Privnote is not secure at all and has been known to pass details to authorities - you might as well just send your address to the feds and let them know what your up to.
