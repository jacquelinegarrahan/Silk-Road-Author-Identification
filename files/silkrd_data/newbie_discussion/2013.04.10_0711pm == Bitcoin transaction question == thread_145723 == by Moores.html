Title: Re: Bitcoin transaction question
Post by: lagunka on April 10, 2013, 07:38 pm
Sending money to your blockchain wallet is practically instant in most cases, however there can be a few hour delay when sending coins to your SR account. This is totally normal. As long as you see the transaction on the blockchain you're all good. And as far as the insufficient fund notification that's because there is a very small charge every time you transfer the coins. I.E. if you only have 1.2 BTC you can only send 1.195. If that is even the fee i just know its very small.
