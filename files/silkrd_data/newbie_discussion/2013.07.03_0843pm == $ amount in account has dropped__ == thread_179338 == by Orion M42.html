No, you have it backwards I am afraid.

If you have 1BTC in your account today, and 1BTC is worth $100 today, then you have 100 dollars worth of BTC.

Tomorrow, if 1BTC is worth $95, you still have 1BTC in your account, but you only have 95 dollars worth of BTC.

Don't feel bad, everyone else who has BTC is seeing the same thing happening. Next week you might have 110 dollars worth. Or you might have 80.
