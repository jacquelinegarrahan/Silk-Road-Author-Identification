Title: Re: Noobs do not FE No matter what
Post by: MachineElves on September 14, 2013, 06:24 pm
Good advice to ALL.

NOBODY should EVER be asked to FE.

(Only exception would be if YOU are asking the Vendor to do something outwith his regular process and you mutually agreed to it).

The ESCROW system's PURPOSE is to protect BOTH the buyer/seller and is the ONLY way that you can take an issue to the resolution center.

New buyers should always initially buy small; FE on delivery and get their reputations up.  Also, speak to the vendors and build up a rapport and their trust.

Vendors often PM other Vendors for feedback reviews on a "new buyers" previous transactions, which can help them decide whether to carry out a deal or not.

In an anonymous world, trust is the only thing we have and ESCROW is the "safety net" used if that trust is ever broken on either side of the fence.

Good Thread and as stated, VERY important advice.
