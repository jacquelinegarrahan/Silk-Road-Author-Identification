yes, in a perfect world when a vendor has marked an order in transit what that SHOULD mean is your order is packed up, labeled, and in the mail and on it's way to you, and all your personal information was destroyed.  Unfortunately the reality of things is sometimes vendors get really lagged or something goes wrong and they get incredibly behind, or they are just starting to go rogue, and in order to shut people up and stop getting 50 messages a day they will mark an order in transit although it hasn't been sent yet.  This buys them a few days with that client before they start asking questions again.  The point I'm trying to make is SR asks that all vendors delete their clients address after they SHIP the package not after it's marked in transit.  Your vendor may have had all intentions on sending your order at a later time once they had gotten caught up so he marked it in transit to buy a little time but then realized that wasn't possible and then canceled all orders and deleted all info.  I don't care if a vendor holds on to my info for a few days to make sure i get my order.  But if you make another order with them 3 weeks from now and they say "don't worry i have all your info on file"  then I'd be fucking worried.
