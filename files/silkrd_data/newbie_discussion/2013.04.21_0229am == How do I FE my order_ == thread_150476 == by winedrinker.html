Title: Re: How do I FE my order?
Post by: 88fxstc on April 21, 2013, 02:36 am
Hit the "orders" link at the top of any SR page to bring up your pending orders . When the vendor changes the status of your order from "processing" to "in transit", it will give you a link  saying "finalize" so you can FE.  Vendors who require FE will mark the order as "in transit" B4 it is actually sent to make the FE option available to you, the buyer.
Hope this makes sense.  Good luck!

Title: Re: How do I FE my order?
Post by: Everdred on April 21, 2013, 03:36 am
If you really want to finalize early, yes. Keep in mind this will make you completely vulnerable to any scam or other funny business. By pressing the finalize button you are transferring the money to the vendor and it is more or less impossible to get it back if, say, the vendor never ships your order.
