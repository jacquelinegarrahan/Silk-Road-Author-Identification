Title: Re: Must leave 5/5???
Post by: sedgeandbee on April 12, 2013, 07:42 am
Because if you're dealing with an established vendor, there is almost no situation where you would need to leave less than that.

Some problems arise that are totally out of anyones control. Legit vendors recognize this and are typically more than willing to work with you to resolve the situation to your satisfaction. I've never ran into the need to leave less than 5/5, and I don't expect to. These ratings greatly affect a vendor, and a few high users who are angry that a problem occurred and are unwilling to work toward resolution can quickly damage a vendors rating.

Title: Re: Must leave 5/5???
Post by: sedgeandbee on April 12, 2013, 08:09 am
Not necessarily. The crux of the situation is that there are SOME situations where a lower rating is warranted. These are few and far between, as legit vendors will bend over backwards to satisfy their customers. People get impatient, don't go through the proper resolution channels, don't communicate and then leave a rating that wrecks an otherwise flawless record. This isn't an accurate assessment of a vendor and creates an inaccurate appearance.

You can leave a text comment along with the numerical rating, which can reflect that you had a problem but the vendor resolved it, which people do all the time when leaving 5/5. So, if going through reasonable procedures does not seem like something a person can bother themselves with, based on these comments you can choose to go through a different, less established vendor.
