Weed is a tough one, because of the smell.  It's extremely important to find out how it is packaged as to mask the smell.  The "stealth" when it comes to weed is first masking that damn funky odor, with other drugs where smell isnt a factor, its more about concealing it to make it look like the drugs arent there.  Try your best to find out beforehand how the parcel will come packaged, and what is done to 1) mask the smell, 2) make it look like theres no weed in there to anyone who opened the package.
