Do your research - there are heaps of vendors out there who will claim their stealth is superior, and on the occasion it arrives, you wonder just how the fuck it got through customs?
Order the smallest amount for a first order - just the same way a dealer on the street can fuck you over, in fact even more so on here, you gotta realize this shit is illegal, so any mo fo can screw you over - you gotta be prepared to take the occasional loss until you find 2 or 3 reliable, honest vendors, whom you never piss off by messaging too much, you are always grateful to, and if anything ever fuck's up you keep it in house unless it's clear they aren't willing to work with you - EG - 50 % of the loss is yours, 50% is theirs.
