Regarding moving Bitcoins around like this, I agree. I use a service called Bitcoin Fog. Basically, you open an account on Bitcoin Fog and send your Bitcoins to this Bitcoin Fog wallet. When Bitcoin Fog receives the Bitcoin it is added to their pool with your balance marked in your Bitcoin Fog account. Then you set up a withdrawal: tell Bitcoin Fog what wallet to send the Bitcoins to and how many Bitcoins. Then you tell Bitcoin Fog the period of time to send it, between 6 and 97 hours. You see, Bitcoin Fog anonymizes the Bitcoin trail by sending Bitcoins in random amounts at random times within a set time frame until your requested total withdrawal is sent. Also, it charges a random fee between 1% and 3% to further anonymize.

Pretty cool, I have used it once. It takes a little patience but you get some added security. Do some research yourself and see what you think.  :)
