Title: Re: what is the difference between indica and sativa?
Post by: android465764E on May 26, 2013, 10:18 pm
Indica typically gives a strong body high, heavy limbs, couch lock etc.. If you just wanna mong-out, stare at the TV all night then fall asleep, indica is the way to go. Great for videogames, movies, chilling out.

Sativa typically gives a strong cerebral high, makes you feel happy, energized, creative, talkative and expressive. You're far more likely to go walkabout and chat, or paint a picture etc..  while high on sativa.


Some strains are a mix of the two, some are almost entirely one or the other.

Personally I prefer Indica. I want my weed to knock me on my arse after a long day,  not force me to get up and "do stuff". lol

Title: Re: what is the difference between indica and sativa?
Post by: DoctorFate on May 26, 2013, 10:49 pm
Sativa - heady high (day time energetic stress relief)
Indica - body high (night time sleepy pain relief)

Sativa's tend to be better when you want a buzz but have to deal with stupid people and need to be able to think clearly.  Indica's are better for when you don't want to think and need to manage pain or nausea issues.  Either are great as they get you high :).

If your wondering how you can identify them then it can be hard but in a perfect world Sativa's normally smell sweet and fruity, Indicas will normally smell strong and have a sweet or sour earthy aroma.

Of course with all the hybrids out there identifying only by smell isn't good but generally will give you an idea.
