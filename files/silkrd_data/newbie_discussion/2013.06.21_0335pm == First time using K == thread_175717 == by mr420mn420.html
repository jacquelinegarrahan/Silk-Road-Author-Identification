Title: Re: First time using K
Post by: catalan on June 21, 2013, 03:45 pm
The more you do, the deeper you go...

Even as a K veteran, I'll often only want the mild sensory effects that come from a bump or two.  I usually find my senses get muddled, such that I can *feel* things that I hear (like music), as though they have form and shape.

Lines lead to much greater disconnect between self and reality, which I think can most succinctly be described as "powdered confusion" (it's as though something profound is just beyond one's reach, and nothing really makes much sense - disorienting, but fun).

Much more than that, and you'll likely "k-hole" where you zone out entirely and are left alone to your crazy confused thoughts.

I don't find it a very sociable drug (meaningful interaction is bloody difficult), so if you're going to be around a bunch of people who aren't familiar with K you might want to steer clear - they might get freaked out by the confused, barely communicative, dribbling mess.

I'd also warn against combining with alcohol, as I invariably find that leads to the prompt evacuation of my stomach.  Of course, for that matter it's not really wise to combine with ANY form of CNS depressant.
