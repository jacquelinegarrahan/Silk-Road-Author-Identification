Title: Re: Easiest Method of Buying Bitcoin
Post by: ace02 on August 02, 2013, 05:24 pm
cashintocoins.com is pretty great. The guy who runs it is really friendly and responds to emails really quickly. They've been a little strained by the increased demand since bitinstant died so he runs out of BTC before the end of the day sometimes but he's super reliable.

To get BTC you make a cash deposit either at Wells Fargo or Chase. Then you take the receipt from that deposit, write the date, your order number and the amount deposited on the receipt, take a picture of it and email the picture to the guy with the wallet address you want to send the BTC to. Usually, the coins show up in my account within a few hours. The longest I've had to wait was until the end of the day once when he was really overwhelmed. The main plus to using cashintocoins is how awesome the customer service is.

There's also BTCquick.com. You do have to verify your identity by uploading a picture of a photo ID. You can use credit or debit and it actually is pretty much instant after you get verified.
