Post by: Nobody@Home on July 27, 2013, 03:11 pm
Southern Ireland or Northern Ireland.

If the former then yes they are stricter and slower for sure. If the latter no problem IMO/IME.

I recently posted someone some green to a Belfast address which is then picked up and transported to a Dublin Mail Box they then send a PIN by Text and you go collect your parcel. The reason for Belfast route is it's much faster that passing through Eire Post/Customs 2 days rather than 5-8 days. I don't remember the name of the service but if interested PM and I'll ask my friend.

Edit: EU to EU Customs most definitely exist but it would seem they concentrate on hotter regions but seizures do happen.
