Levamisole is a nasty cut, and is 90% of the reason I don't really do coke anymore.

It's gotten ridiculous. It's almost to the point that you have to actually visit a source country to see anything like the quality we used to see on the street.

It's odd, since coca production has increased exponentially with the advent of the war on drugs. The more intense the war gets, the more coca goes into production.

I'm not sure where all the good yay is headed. I know it's not to the streets of the US.

It seems like it's mostly a middleman problem. Those at the top of the food chain don't like cutting agents, as it degrades the name/value of their product. In some circles, stepping on the brand will get you killed, or at the least get your line cut.

But, these days, ripping off people is the norm, not the exception. And since the war on drugs in the US has gotten so out of control crazy, there is no motivation for the scumbags in between to keep it clean. The more crap they add, the more weight they can  slang.

They know that people will devour whatever does hit the street, since the streets are a desert in the past few years.

I keep wanting to order from overseas. The cartels aren't stupid. They saw what was happening, and set up shop in Europe, Africa, etc. I guarantee you that somewhere in fucking Mali, some warlord is sitting on stacks of diamonds, and I don't mean gemstones.
