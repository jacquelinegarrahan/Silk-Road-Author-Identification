Hay Lawnmowerman,

Welcome to the site, 1stly do a search through the shipping section of the forums. There are a number of posts on the best methods of receiving goods and how the postage system works.

Generally you get a key and open them yourself unless the package is so big it doesn't fit, then you have to go to the counter.

My 2 cents on deliveries:

1) PO Boxes are OK but a risk that you run into a smart postman when sorting the mail
 
2) Send with your name to a friends address and ask them to collect for you - They can claim it's not for them if the cops turn up "sorry officer not even my name on the package"

3) Best option for successful delivery - Your address with your name. Anyone can send you mail, so if the cops turn up just say "No not for me officer, I wasn't expecting this package. No idea what it is"  This is the option I use and never had an issue.

By the way domestic orders are by far the most successful in terms of delivery.
