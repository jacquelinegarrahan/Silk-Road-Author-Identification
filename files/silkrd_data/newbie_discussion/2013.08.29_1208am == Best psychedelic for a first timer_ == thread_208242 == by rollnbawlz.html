Title: Re: Best psychedelic for a first timer?
Post by: FubaredUK on August 29, 2013, 12:27 am
My first psychedelic experience was with magic mushrooms (Psilocybin)

They grow in fields locally so you can harvest them yourself for free! (they are usually growing around late September/October here in UK)

My first time I only took 12 and about 2 hours later (after I thought nothing was going to happen) I was having mild distortion hallucinations and general giddyness. (was only 15)

The thing with hallucinogenics is the emotionally rollercoaster that comes with them, I love the visuals but all your feelings are maximised/exaggerated.
So if you find something funny you will laugh until your belly aches, but if something worries you it can turn into fear/paranoia and "thought-loops" can be hard to break out of.

If you do go with mushrooms make sure you're 99.99% sure they are the right kind. Also you can start with a small amount and build up.


LSD is the most famous one but there's no way of knowing how many Micrograms are on a tab and once you start you're in for at least an 8 hour ride.
I'd recommend doing it somewhere safe, at yours or a friends home and have someone you trust with you just to reassure you if things start getting a bit intense.


I've not had a chance to try DMT yet, powerful and short lasting.
