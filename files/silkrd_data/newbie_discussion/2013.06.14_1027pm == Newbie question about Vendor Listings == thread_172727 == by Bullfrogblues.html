Title: Re: Newbie question about Vendor Listings
Post by: letsgetfunky on June 14, 2013, 10:44 pm
"You may have noticed the (100) next some vendors names, this is their Feedback Score. This is a quick way of finding out how reputable a vendor is, a step to take before reading some of their actual feedback. This score is a weighted average, based on many aspect of their transactions. The weights are determined by a few factors, and here they are, in order of importance:

    1.How active a buyer is in rating other vendors
    2.The age of the rating
    3.The price of the transaction

In order for a vendor's rating to appear next to their name, they must be reviewed enough for it to be a meaningful average. This requires roughly 10 reviews per month, but can be more or less depending of the weights of the reviews. " - Taken from the Silk Road FAQ
