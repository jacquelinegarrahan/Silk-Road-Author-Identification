SR recommends to never FE. if you do and your package doesn't arrive they won't be able to do anything about it. when you FE you are given a sense of security and it holds vendors accountable. it would be like meeting someone on the street who says they can get you mdma but you have to give them your money first and wait for them to come back. its a risk. plus vendors and coming and going around here pretty fast. this isn't exactly a steady 9-5 job. as long as your money is in escrow it is safe. if you have a dispute, SR still has the ability to work with you and the vendor to come to an agreement. the ratings on SR do hold some value but they often dont give you a full picture of a transaction. they could be faked as well. i never consider a "5/5 FE" a positive rating. check all the ratings on the vendors main page, don't just look at the ones from the product page you are ordering. always visit a vendors forum page!! i believe it holds much more information then the ratings section on SR. check to make sure the people commenting have a decent post history and talk to other buyers if needed.
there are some vendors who do require FE for all orders and some for only large orders etc, no one is saying they are bad vendors or you should avoid them but if you use them you taking a risk, a risk most people would deem unnecessary figuring there are tons of vendors who never require FE. If a package is sent with tracking i dont see the need for a vendor to FE ever.
its really up to you and whether or not you want to risk loosing your money. not saying its going to happen but it could and you won't have the support of the SR staff.
