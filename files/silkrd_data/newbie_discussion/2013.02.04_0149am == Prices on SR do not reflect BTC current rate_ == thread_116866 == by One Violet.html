Title: Re: Prices on SR do not reflect BTC current rate?
Post by: MyOtherNickIsAwesome on February 04, 2013, 02:00 am
I understand your concern, but you have to understand that SR is an independent market, and the individual vendors decide whether they wanna change their prices according to the BTC prices every time there's a change. If you're looking at a certain item try PM'ing the vendor asking him about the price.. Most of them are normal people who will understand your concern..

But with that said, don't expect SR to follow the btc exchange market promtly.. everyone can't be online 24/7 adjusting prices accordingly.

And there's usually plenty of vendors out there for whatever you could desire, so if you disagree with the price see if someone else can give you a better offer
