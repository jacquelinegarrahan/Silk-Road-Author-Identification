Title: Great LSD
Post by: BCF11 on September 26, 2013, 10:46 pm
I've done LSD from a few different suppliers before, along with tons of research chemicals. Psychedelics are great almost no matter what you take, but nothing can beat good clean LSD.
The greatest experience I have ever had with a drug was when a friend and I each took 3 tabs of Tessellated's White Label. About half after taking them things started getting weird. About an hour in I had to sit down because the sky, the people around me, the ground, everything was overlaid with crazy geometric patterns of color and light, and I needed to get a grasp on things. I looked over to my friend and his face was stretched and breathing, he had lines like electric tattoos pulsing and squirming along his entire body. I could tell by the look on his face that he saw the same thing in me too. This wasn't even the peak.

What happened that day was absolutely amazing, and I have serious respect for the people who risk their freedom to bring this wonderful molecule to people.

What would you like to say about LSD?
