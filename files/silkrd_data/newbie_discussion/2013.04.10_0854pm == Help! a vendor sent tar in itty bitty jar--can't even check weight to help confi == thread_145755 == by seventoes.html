Post by: fumanchu on April 10, 2013, 09:21 pm
Put it in the freezer for a few hours until the tar solidifies...

Then take it out of the freezer once its completely frozen and smash the little plastic vial apart with a hammer or something... then you will be able to peel the tar off the plastic in one clean piece if you do it quick enough. It's important you take it out only once its completely frozen, and then immediately smash it open and peel it out... if it gets too warm from being at room temperature or from you handling it, it will begin to melt back into the sticky mess it originally was. Time is critical here.

I dunno about heroin, but I make hash oil from time to time, and I use the freezer method with hash oil as its extremely sticky and messy..
