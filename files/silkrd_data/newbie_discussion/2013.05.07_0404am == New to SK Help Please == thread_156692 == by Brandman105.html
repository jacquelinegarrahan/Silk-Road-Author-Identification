Post by: neuromancer on May 07, 2013, 05:01 am
The prices are generally fixed by dollar amount, so as the value of BTC fluctuates you'd need more or fewer BTC to buy product.

If you're looking to buy $100 of stuff, expect to deposit $110-120 to account for cash deposit fees, trading fees to convert to BTC, SR markup, and of course to have a cushion for the volatility of BTC market value in the time that this could take.

If you don't have said $100...then unfortunately there's no quick way you can get that except investing in a smaller amount and praying the price goes up...
