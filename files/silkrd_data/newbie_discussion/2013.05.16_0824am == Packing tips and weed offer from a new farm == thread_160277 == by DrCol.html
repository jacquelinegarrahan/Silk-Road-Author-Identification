Good start on the vac seal, and mylar.

Always wear 2 pairs of latex gloves when packing. One pair has been shown to leave latent prints.
Always, when packing marijuana, wash each vac seal with soap, or wipe it down with rubbing alcohol in between vac seals, and before heat sealing in mylar as well.

Practice these measures and youll be safe and secure. Much love.

If you introduce enough solvent to the package, it's going to take up pretty much every molecule (that you want taken up) on that package (it's nowhere near as chemically advantageous for those molecules to stay on that mylar than it is for them to dissolve in a solvent in which they are soluble). I just can't comment on what solvent dissolves all cannabanoids that a dog could possibly pick up on. You've got THC, CBD, CBG, CBC, and CBN (the ones I've heard of) - maybe more than that. You need to find a common solvent, or a couple, that are OTC and common, that dissolve all or most of those. Then you are good to go. I'm not sure if there is anything behind IPA/bleach or whatever else people use, except the fact that laymen know that it "kills" germs. I don't have all the MSDS's on all the cannabanoids, so like I said I can't comment, but you're probably better off with the IPA and/or bleach dip than without it. Take as much as you can off every one of those seals. It seems to be working for most vendors, thusfar.
