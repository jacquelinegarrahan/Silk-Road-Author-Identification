Hi! You have to enter your address as you would see it on normal mail, so also include your name. You can use a friend's address with his name on it then, but it'd be better if he agrees to it for obvious reasons. Using a friend's address is actually better than using your own.
You should maybe try to buy an e-book or a lottery so that you get comfortable with how it works, and with the lottery you even have a chance of wining something! Your record will also look slightly better if you have at least 1 transaction and even only $10 spent.
And don't FE.. There are a lot of vendors that don't require FE for newbs.. I could refer you to some if you can't find any (it depends on what you want too)
