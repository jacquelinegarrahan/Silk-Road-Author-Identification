Title: Re: NEW GUY WANTS TO BULK PURCHASE. MY CHANCES?
Post by: Nakorx on June 27, 2013, 08:14 pm
FE means Finalize Early, in other words, the vendor wants you to bypass the Escrow system and release your money to him before he sends you the goods.
As a newbie, you will come across this a lot, at least for 5 or maybe 10 transactions.
My advice is to avoid FE if you can, but as a newbie it maybe impossible, it depends on your purchase, or drug of choice.
So do your research, check the vendor out on this forum, there is a thread for nearly every drug and other categories too. Also the feedback on the Vendor's page and sometimes the item page. Take your time, buy small at first and as someone said above try and build a rapport with the vendor/s you wish to ultimately purchase bilk from, and then you will find it much easier.
And has someone said earlier, if you can choose a purchase with a tracking option, then it will much easier not to have FE because they will know, via the tracking, whether the package reached you or got seized by customs. You will probably have to sign though, but that never bothers me, but I am in UK so different rules apply I believe.
