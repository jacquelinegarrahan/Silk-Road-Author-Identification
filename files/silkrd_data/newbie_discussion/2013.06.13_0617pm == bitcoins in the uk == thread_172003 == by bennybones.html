Shills are secondary acc./s (accounts) that a vendor will use to artificially boost feedback. It is not uncommon for some vendors to use them. They may decide to use shills when they are starting out, or when they are losing a lot of custom suddenly, in an effort to restore faith to prospective buyers looking through this feedback on their vendor's page. Additionally, they may use a shill on the forum to put in a glowing recommendation of themself on their review thread. They typically contain a handful of posts, and are obvious.
