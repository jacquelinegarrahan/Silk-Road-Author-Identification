Post by: neuromancer on May 06, 2013, 07:36 am
Weed is, for me, the best music enhancer. I didn't listen to electronic music until I started smoking pot. Now, ~10 or so years later, electronic music of some sort (and I don't necessarily mean modern EDM like Swedish House Mafia, which I actually find pretty awful) is probably close to 90% of my music collection.

My friends and I, for the past ~4 years, have gathered to share music with one another on a regular basis. Everyone brings a set of 3-6 songs and we all get super blazed and listen to music on quality hi-fi equipment with some sort of interesting visualization on. It's a great experience and we've all been able to get into some stuff we normally would never have come across.

Shrooms and MDMA are awesome too, though for different reasons. Some of my favorite music is the sort that is reminiscent of the afterglow of a mushroom trip. Heh, I wish there was a name for that genre. (Ulrich Schnauss, Emancipator, Joey Fehrenbach are a couple of artists that come to mind)

Yesterday my fiancee and I rolled face and discovered some great music. Check out Efdemin's self-titled album (awesome dark techno) if you're feeling adventurous and willing to go off of a complete stranger's recommendation.
