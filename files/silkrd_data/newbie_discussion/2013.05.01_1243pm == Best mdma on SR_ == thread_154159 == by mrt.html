Title: Re: Best mdma on SR?
Post by: Aidoneus on May 20, 2013, 05:50 am
Without getting extremely technical...

MDMA, when encountered in the wild, is found as a salt - MDMA.HCl. When the salt crystals form under perfect chemistry, the resulting product is 84% MDMA and 16% Hydrochloride (Hydrogen Chloride).

So 100% 'pure' MDMA found in the wild is actually only 84% pure (MDMA).
Assuming 84=100, then product that is 86% pure MDMA is really only 72.24% MDMA, at least 16% HCl, and 11.76% nonsense/fuck up. LOL

Course... if you happen to encounter MDMA in freebase form, you can find truly 100% pure product (or as close as possible eliminating trace elements like dust). But this is a liquid... kind of.
.Whish.
