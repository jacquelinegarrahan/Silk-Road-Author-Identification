Title: Re: SOME BASIC ADVICE PLEASE
Post by: cosmictourist on September 29, 2013, 12:24 pm
Yes, when you buy bitcoins via a bank transfer, those bitcoins are linked to your account, as well as all subsequent transactions - so, sending them to another wallet is absolutely futile. Bitcoins, like tor, are only anonymous if you use the technology in the correct way.

The solution is to use tumblers. If you read the SR wiki, you will learn SR uses a tumbler to break the link between your bitcoins and drug purchases, but SR can do nothing about the link between your bank account and your SR wallet - you need to use 3rd-party tumblers for that.  blockchain.info provides one, in the form of "shared send", and I believe they only charge 0.5% commission. Another option is bitcoinfog, which provides a hidden service as well as a clearnet site, but charge 1-3% comission. Both tumblers introduce a delay of at least approximately 8 hours.
