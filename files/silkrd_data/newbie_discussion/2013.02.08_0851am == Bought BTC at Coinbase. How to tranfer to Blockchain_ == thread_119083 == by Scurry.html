Title: Re: Bought BTC at Coinbase. How to tranfer to Blockchain?
Post by: MilesStandoffish on February 08, 2013, 09:24 am
If you click the PENDING sign, it should tell you how long it'll take until the coins you have purchased will be part of your account/wallet at Coinbase. From the time of purchasing BTC to when they're available in your Coinbase wallet is about a week. (I forget why the delay. Probably has to do with the whole bank account thing.) But once the 'pending' transfer period has elapsed, you can then send them to Blockchain or SR, and that particular transfer should only take a few hours...
