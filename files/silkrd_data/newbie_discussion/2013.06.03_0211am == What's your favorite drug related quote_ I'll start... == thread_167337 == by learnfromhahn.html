A kind of spammy thread, but figured it would also offer a little insight into our drug usage from another perspective, here's one of my favorites thus far, a simple, classic one by Alan Watts:

"Psychedelic experience is only a glimpse of genuine mystical insight, but a glimpse which can be matured and deepened by the various ways of meditation in which drugs are no longer necessary or useful. If you get the message, hang up the phone. For psychedelic drugs are simply instruments, like microscopes, telescopes, and telephones. The biologist does not sit with eye permanently glued to the microscope, he goes away and works on what he has seen..."

That's how I look at all drugs, it shows you where the window is and might crack it open a touch, but its your job to throw that window open and take a deep breathe of air.
