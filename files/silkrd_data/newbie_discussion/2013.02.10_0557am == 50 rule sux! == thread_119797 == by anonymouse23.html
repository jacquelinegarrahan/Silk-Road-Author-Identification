There are never any USD involved. You can only convert them through other means. When you transfer them or order something as you say, it just tells you what they are currently worth in USD so you have a better idea. It uses the average price from the markets that buy and sell the coins. Like right now if you ordered something for 1BTC it would probably say something like ($23ish USD) next to it. It's not converting it. Just helping you keep track of what you are spending.
