I'm only a newcomer around SR myself, at least as a participant - I've visited here or there in the past but my personal availability of privacy and freedom was at a low (but she's gone.. ^.^) and now I'm finally entering what I've wanted to be a part of for a while now.

As for the point on why some of what is going on isn't necessarily said... its not always easy to really be able to say what truly is going on. On the surface, a DDoS is a broad statement... it really could be just about anything, from anyone - a competing market, LE (law enforcement), or otherwise. Stating who, or what it is, isn't always possible - and to say that it is or isn't (and then be wrong) can also be something of a demoralizing situation. That information is in the power of the admin to be told or withheld, and personally when I've had to deal with it IRL I tend to lay on the side of information that I see available here regarding it. Keeping people informed, without having to divulge information that honestly I usually didn't even know myself, when battling similar events. The internet in its current state doesn't make it hard for someone to "become" someone else, and push blame to another group.

I feel the important thing is that the presence IS there, and that they make themselves known that we are here to fight. We know when something is going on, and we know when progress is being made... or when it isn't. The struggles are not after-thought press releases... and that is not an easy thing to maintain.
