Title: trip report; Tessellated
Post by: knihT on May 23, 2013, 04:07 am
Background;
Light pot smoker
Done shrooms once before
Stimulants here and there

Amount taken for first trip;
2 hits. Original plan was 3 hits, I wanted to get hit hard for the first trip. In hindsight I'm incredibly glad I took only 2 tabs, decided to drop it down after discussing w/ some people online and realized that the people I spoke to in person have probably never had shit as potent as this.

Trip;
-Duration: Around 17 hours
-Probably, to date, the most powerful experience I've had. There was a good 6 hours of intense tripping, and then 11 hours (until I passed out) of still being affected by it. Music, movies, games, I felt so much more connected to it all, in an artistic way. I had a notepad file up the whole time, trying my best to record my experience. Ended up being over 3000 words, my fears, my understanding. A very unique and moving experience. Overall I am incredibly satisfied with how everything went, ordering, shipping, stealth, packaging, and the trip itself. I went into it hoping to come back with understanding of the experience and my own thoughts and feelings, and I got just that. At points I was scared, at points I was having a wonderful time, for 17 hours I had a different perspective on life. Mind boggling to see just how much of an impact two little cotton blotters can have on how you view the world.
