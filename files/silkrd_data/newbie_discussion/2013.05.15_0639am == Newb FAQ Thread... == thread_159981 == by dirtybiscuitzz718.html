Title: Re: Newb FAQ Thread...
Post by: Jizo893 on May 15, 2013, 11:50 pm
I'd just like to add one little gem for those starting out on the Road.. yeah i am still in Newb status,  but have been active on the road for almost a year now and have some pretty reasonable stats. I just cant be arsed posting BS posts to get my status "upgraded".. i am not that concerned about it..

ANYWAY... I know how awesome it is to receive your first few successful orders and the subsequent urge to want to tell everyone about it, but when you post Vendor feedback after Finalizing, please DONT put your country of origin in it. EG : " really great service, product excellent and only took xx days to (country) "

Its hard enough with LE scrutinizing our methods, without giving them an indication of which source countries may be "hot" . I have seen LE profile a source country which i used to purchase from with no issues, then crack down on that particular country's mail to the point where its no longer viable to order from there. I know you may think you are doing other buyers from your country a favor by quoting where you are in the feedback, but trust me its just tipping off the feds. In addition, dont mention anything about Vendor packaging, aside from the quality of it. I recently read in one of the threads someone painstakingly detailing a Vendors stealth method.... i dont think he is going to be too happy about that.

As has been noted multiple times across all the threads for Newbs... READ up and study before you make that purchase. Understand and accept the risks that we take here.

Enjoy and be safe.
