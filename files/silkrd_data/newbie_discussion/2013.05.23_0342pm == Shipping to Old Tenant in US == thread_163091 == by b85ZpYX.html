Title: Re: Shipping to Old Tenant in US
Post by: captainjojo on May 23, 2013, 03:53 pm
So long as the old tenant hasn't paid for an address redirection, then the item will probably be delivered.  When I first started to use SR a couple of years ago I did the same thing, used an old tenants address and I received what I ordered.  Unfortunately it's not a good idea to do so if you are ordering priority because you may end up having to show ID if it ends up going back to the PO for pickup.

Just keep in mind that the vendor may not refund you anything if the package isn't delivered and they find out you weren't using a real name.
