Title: Re: NSA program X-KEYSCORE collects nearly everything a user does on the internet
Post by: ALsupremeZAteam36 on July 31, 2013, 05:48 pm
Quote from: MauryDrovich on July 31, 2013, 05:38 pm
what's onion.to? what do you mean by that?
Onion.to is a clearnet service. It basically means that you can browse darknet services, such as SR, through your regular web browser like Google Chrome. It's done by adding ".to" after the onion address like so:

http://silkroadvb5piz3r.onion.to/

However, when doing this, you negate all the encryption that Tor provides, and thus make yourself much more susceptible to things like this X-KEYSCORE.
