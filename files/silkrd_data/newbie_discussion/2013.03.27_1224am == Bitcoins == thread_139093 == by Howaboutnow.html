Hi buddy,

I used to run a Bitcoin trading business on these lines. I think your best bet would probably be to become accustomed to working as a Bitcoin trader, which would also give vendors an opportunity to get to know you. You can register on localbitcoins.com and other similar sites to accept payment by cash/wire transfer for BTC. If you have the funds to spare you can also set up an SR seller account and offer the service direct to buyers - don't let the fact that there are others doing this put you off, you can establish a niche by accepting payment methods they don't or operating in an area others don't as I did with the UK (at the time!).

You will reach a stage where you will need Bitcoins in large quantities and will have to approach vendors directly. In the mean time, I suggest you do as the other poster said and try to build up a good rep doing small sales. If you need any more info or help getting started, let me know.

All the best,
