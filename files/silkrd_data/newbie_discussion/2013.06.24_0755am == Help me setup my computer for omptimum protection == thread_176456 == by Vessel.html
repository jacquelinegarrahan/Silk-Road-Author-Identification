Step by step (simplest version) :
- Install Tails
- Copy the TOR address  http://silkroadvb5piz3r.onion/ in your sticky to avoid phising.
- Access Silkroad
- Slowly work up your reputation as a buyer. Buy local, buy small.
- Learn PGP (encryption). It's difficult but it's worth it. Use it to encrypt your address before sending it to your vendor. Some people here use Privnote. Not sure about it's security, but it's better than using no encryption at all.
- Don't use abandoned house, your stuff will not be delivered. It will bounce back to you seller, and it will be bad for everybody. Use a valid address, don't sign the receipt.
- After you receive your stuff, finalize your order on the Silk Road. Leave a comment. The number of finalized orders in your stat will be your credibility when you buy from a vendor.
