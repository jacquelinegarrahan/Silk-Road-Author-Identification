you go to localbitcoins.com, register, put in your postal code, choose a seller, meet up, buy using the localbitcoins page or your wallet on your smartphone.

or you buy them online using one of the many payment options and the escrow system

it is really simple. the site guides you while doing your order, which is not more complicated than buying stuff from amazon.

just give it a try. once you did it you'll wonder what you were worrying about.
