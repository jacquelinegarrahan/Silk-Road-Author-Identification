This is not a silk road issue, but a tor issue. See here https://metrics.torproject.org/users.html

The linearity of the increase implies that the quadrupling in users is not extra human users, and it's suspected that a botnet is behind it, the evidence for this being the total bandwidth of the network has not increased: https://metrics.torproject.org/network.html (scroll down to "Total relay bandwidth in the network")

Whatever these new users are doing is causing the hidden services to be slowed to a crawl, while accessing clearnet sites appears to be unaffected.
