Title: Re: How do you all handle your drugs?
Post by: soviet on July 27, 2013, 09:27 am
The safest way to store any type of drug is to keep it in a cool, dry, and dark place. Some may even require refrigeration.
For weighing I recommend a cheap (milligram) scale with a dish from Amazon.com
As for capsuling, if the amount of product you're moving isn't too high then you could buy/make a little funnel. If you're moving a large volume of product you should invest in a capsuling machine.

In my experience a bit of waste is simply inevitable.
