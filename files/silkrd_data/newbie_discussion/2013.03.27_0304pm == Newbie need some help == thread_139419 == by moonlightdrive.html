It sounds like you got it down. 

Yes, you need to enter your SR address into the ( Bitcoin Address) field on BitINstant.  Just copy and paste.  Then after you fill in the rest of the info- you should be able to click to the next screen which usually asks you where you will be depositing the $$.  You can select any location because BitInstant deposits will work at any of the listed stores. 

  Then once you print out your order page, take this with you along with your cash to the store and get a (Blue colored) Money Gram slip and fill it out with your info and the account numbers from the order page.  You should be able to pay without being asked any questions.  If they do ask you why- you can just tell them you are paying a bill (bill payments can be done without showing ID BTW).

  Once you pay and get your recipt, the BitCoins can take a few hours to show up in your SR account; but once they do, you can buy whatever you'd like. 

  You can also transfer your Bit Coins to other addresses and tor sites, and then re-deposit them back in your account for more anonymity; but there is plenty of info on the forums how to do that. 

I hope that helps and also DON'T FE unless it is absolutely necessary.  Try to buy from vendors who let you stay in escrow and there are PLENTY.  Most scams on SR happen because a buyer FE'd and the vendor runs or says they sent the product; but nothing ever shows.  SR will not be able to help you or your case if you FE and need assistance.  It is the best protection a buyer has.   

Good Luck!
