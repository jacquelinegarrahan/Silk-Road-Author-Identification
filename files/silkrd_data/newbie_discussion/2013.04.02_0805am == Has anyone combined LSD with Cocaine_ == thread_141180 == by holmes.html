Title: Re: Has anyone combined LSD with Cocaine?
Post by: CRWHITLOCK on April 02, 2013, 08:55 pm
It's a terrible idea. When you "come down" from a psychedelic experience, your exhausted for a reason. Your brain and body have just been through a very strenuous activity. Thinking that doing coke as your coming down will help you is absurd. You will crash 10 times harder when the stimulant wears off than you would have if you just let the psychedelic wear off on it's own. A psychedelic trip is not something to play around with, and adding a powerful stimulant like cocaine is just asking for trouble. Your natural brain chemistry will be totally thrown out of whack, as will your physical body. You are likely setting yourself up for a terrible crash, and a few days of deep depression.
