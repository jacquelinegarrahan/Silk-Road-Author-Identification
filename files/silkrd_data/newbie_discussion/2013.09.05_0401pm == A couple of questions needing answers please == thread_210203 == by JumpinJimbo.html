Title: Re: A couple of questions needing answers please
Post by: The Bubble Dreamer on September 07, 2013, 10:28 pm
That's not really the whole picture. Escrow means that there is a independent third party that the buyer gives his money to, and this third party will give the money to the seller, once the transaction has been confirmed by the buyer. In case of an unresolved conflict, the third party will decide if the money is returned to the seller or given to the buyer. Here, the independent third party is Silk Road. [Clearnet warning!] http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Escrow
