Title: Re: LSD Vendors = Where they at?
Post by: unbeknownst on August 23, 2013, 02:51 am
The NBOME series, commonly called 25-b, 25-c, and 25-i are RCs of the phenethylamine group which includes 2cb, ecstasy, amphetamines and most of the other RCs of recent times and have effects somewhat similar to both ecstasy and LSD simultaneously. They are very powerful by weight and as such are frequently sold on similar blotters to acid; as these chemicals are dirt cheap, unscrupulous street dealers often try to pass them off as acid for much more than they are worth. And a lot of inexperienced users can't tell the difference. LSD and many other "traditional" psychedelics like mescaline, psilocybin and DMT are from a wholly unrelated group called tryptamines. NBOMEs are still tons of fun, they have some crazy properties, just be aware you can get a whole sheet for like $50!

http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/25I-NBOMe

As for the AL-LAD, I don't know the specifics of its manufacture but it is one of several possible analogues to LSD. It was discovered by the famed chemist Alexander Shulgin.

http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alexander_Shulgin
