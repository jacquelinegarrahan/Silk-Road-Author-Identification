Title: Re: Risks of International packages?
Post by: mixmasterrick on June 18, 2013, 01:38 am
as someone who deals primarily overseas (europe and asia shipping to USA), I can say a few things:
UK is not anything to be worried about. the problem countries (from what ive seen) are the Netherlands and China.
specifically packages that are heavy.  any package over 500g (half kilo) will be inspected much more closely.  So if possible, stay away from those 2 countries (esp when dealing in larger weights, or high amounts of pills) and you should be fine.  most packages when coming from some place that is not considered a "drug producing country" pass through in 2-3 days, with no problems whatsoever.  the vendor should also realize the package is going through customs, and to take the proper precautions when creating it.
