If you search the net you can find all the resources you need for both of these.  Both will take some practice to get good quality and quantity.

DMT is in just about every living thing in different quantities, there are a few different plants people use for extraction purposes, as well as different techniques for extraction.  You can do a simple extraction in about a weeks time.

I've seen vendors selling ayahuasca in capsule form.  This would be super easy to do...  Just take your plant that contains the DMT, and your plant that contains the MAOI grind them up and put them into separate capsules.  You can buy the plants and needed supplies online.  Some testing would be required to find the proper potency.

For mushrooms you need the spores, something for them to grow in and feed off of(grains work well).  Basically you inoculate your substrate(food for the mycelium) with the spores, and keep them in a proper environment for them to grow.  Once it has taken over the substrate you birth them(give them a bit of light and air) so they grow up and out of the mycelium.  You need to make sure to keep everything really sterile.  I lost my first grow to mold, second grow was okay, but I didn't get much until after the 3rd grow.  There is a learning curve involved, as with anything.  They take 3 weeks to a couple months to spawn in the substrate(depending on the size of the container).  Once they are birthed it takes a week tops for the first flush, and you can usually get 3 to 5 flushes from each grow if you keep the environment in check.  Look into the monobucket technique, it is super easy, and helps keep your environment sterile :).

A close friend of mine just finished a batch of mushies.  It probably costed him under $100, and he wound up with 4+ ounces.  I'd like to start growing some again, once I have a bit more space.
