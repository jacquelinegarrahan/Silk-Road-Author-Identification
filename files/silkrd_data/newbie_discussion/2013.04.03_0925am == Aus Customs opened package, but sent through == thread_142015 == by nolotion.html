Title: Aus Customs opened package, but sent through
Post by: nolotion on April 03, 2013, 09:25 am
So I recently received a package of 150 x modafinil tablets, which were taking slightly longer than I expected to arrive. When they were delivered I noticed a big green 'Opened By Customs' sticker on the back of the package which scared the shit out of me. Then I realised that if they'd found illicits the most likely action would've been to just destroy them.

So I opened the package and inside was everything I ordered, plus the standard love letter from customs informing me that they'd opened the package, but it was all above board and they hadn't removed anything. I've had these letters on several other occasions when purchasing other (legal) things, but one difference about this instance was the inclusion of a stamp. On both the love letter and the outside of the package was round stamp which said 'CUSTOMS SGF 80' which I haven't been able to find any info on so far.

The internal packaging was also intact so it doesn't appear that they even removed the items from the parcel.

This whole thing has got me more confused than anything. I'm not really concerned about LE busting down the door or anything like that, and I don't believe I should be. Has this happened to any other Aussies? Or can anyone shed some light on this situation?
