Doubtwater: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/25I-NBOMe. Tons and tons of posts about nbomes on SR. People have very strong and divided opinions on the stuff, its very powerful and has strange, unexpected properties. For anyone wanting to try it, order a 5-10 strip and start small, like 1/2 tab first time, even if you've done other hallucinogens. Since it is a relative of 2cb/mdma type RCs, it has some similarities to those effects, with the addition of visual and mental effects somewhat like tryptamines, just not as deep or profound. A major downside of 25-x drugs is the aftereffects can last for days, and even one decent trip can build a huge tolerance to other RCs/psychedelics that can last a week or more. I've heard reports of SR users not being able to trip on legit LSD or DMT for a while after doing this stuff. I rarely hear of anyone having a "bad" trip, but lots of people seem to hate the stuff even when it is not terrifying. I guess LSD lovers find it shallow or something, if you like death trips this is not for you.
