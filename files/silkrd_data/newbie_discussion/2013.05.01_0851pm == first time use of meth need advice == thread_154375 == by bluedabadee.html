Quote from: JustWokeUP on May 01, 2013, 09:19 pm
doing molly but saying hell no to meth is a fairly arbitrary distinction if you ask me the A in MDMA is "amphetamine"

MDMA is 3,4 - Methylenedioxy-N-Methamphetamine, so yes, the A is for amphetamine, but there's a huge difference between Methamphetamine and MDMA.

Small differences in molecular structures can cause massive differences in the effect of the drug.

The difference between Ethanol and Methanol is just a carbon and three hydrogen atoms. But that's the difference between getting tipsy and going blind!
