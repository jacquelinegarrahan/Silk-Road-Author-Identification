Post by: Aussie bob on July 17, 2013, 08:16 pm
Firstly, most people here dislike fe'ing a great deal. I personally think it's ok for  trusted vendors. Just wanted to get that out there, obviously it's your decision, but you are creating more opportunity to get scammed when you fe. Research your vendor thoroughly, is what I'd suggest.

   That extended throat clearing over, go to orders section in your account, next to the order you want to fe will be a button that says finalise. Just click it. Note, your order must have been marked as in transit by the vendor before you can finalise.

   Good luck! AB
