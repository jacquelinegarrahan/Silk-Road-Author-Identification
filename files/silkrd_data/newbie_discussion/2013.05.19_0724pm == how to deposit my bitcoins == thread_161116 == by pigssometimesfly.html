Title: Re: how to deposit my bitcoins
Post by: lanman on May 21, 2013, 06:49 pm
You first transfer your BTC into your SR wallet. SR knows the amount of BTC that you have in this wallet, and they will deduct BTC from this wallet once you make a purchase, and put them in escrow. If you don't trust SR, then don't transfer BTC to your SR wallet until you are ready to make a purchase!
