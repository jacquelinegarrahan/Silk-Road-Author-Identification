Title: Re: New to SR
Post by: sativanist on August 12, 2013, 12:54 am
Best advice is to read through the forums, learn as much about SR as you can. I have been here several weeks and have only scratched the surface.  Don't expect others to hold your hand and spoon feed you. You will learn quicker if you do your own legwork.

Then start looking through the listings of stuff for sale, check out the different types of vendors in all categories, learn what to expect. You will minimize your chances of being scammed if you establish a relationship with a few vendors you trust.

Start with small orders, finalize promptly and provide a review of the product. Until you have built up a book of ordering history, you might want to use newer vendors. Don't FE, there are new vendors coming in all the time.

Start reading and learning, then you can help others. SR is a big community and we share information on these boards, so post when you can.

Enjoy!!
