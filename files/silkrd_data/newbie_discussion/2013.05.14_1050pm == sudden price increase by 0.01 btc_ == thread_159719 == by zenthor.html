Post by: drbrule on May 16, 2013, 05:50 am
On a slightly unrelated note, when calculating the proper amount of bitcoins to buy, don't go buy the "Last Price" like on Mt.Gox, SR goes by the Weighted Average. They can vary greatly, but they almost always vary slightly, and it can make a big difference on how much money you actually end up with.

I can't tell you how many times I've made sure I had at least $20 extra, transfer my funds to SR, and find out I'm literally less than a dollar short, wondering where the fuck my money went.  ::)
