The outer coating is pretty easy to remove. Place the pill in your mouth for a few seconds and cover it with saliva. Remove it and use a tissue or piece of paper to wipe away the outer coating. Then all that's left to do is crush it up as finely as you want it. You're snorting a substance. It will not be comfortable.

Be sure to let it dry prior to crushing after removing the coating.
