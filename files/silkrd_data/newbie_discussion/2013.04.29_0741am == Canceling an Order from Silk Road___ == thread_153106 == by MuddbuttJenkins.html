Title: Re: Canceling an Order from Silk Road???
Post by: Tessellated on April 29, 2013, 07:52 am
You will get the option to cancel after a certain number of days(I think 4) if the seller has not marked it as shipped by then.

If you can contact the seller they may accept the cancellation, but if they ship it within the 4 days then they really do not have to accept a cancellation.

That being said most vendors are willing to cancel orders(Even though it happens way more often when the price of bitcoin is going up).
