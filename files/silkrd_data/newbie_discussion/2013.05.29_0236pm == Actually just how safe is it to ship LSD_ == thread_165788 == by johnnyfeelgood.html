Title: Re: Actually just how safe is it to ship LSD?
Post by: Buttercup. on May 30, 2013, 01:09 am

I would wait, especially if you're still living in the same area or at the same location you were when you were arrested to begin with. Seriously. Yes, LSD is one of the safest drugs to order if you're a wide-eyed innocent with a squeaky record and a mailbox that gets stuffed full with letters and eBay packages and catalogs. If you're on probation FOR SELLING LSD it's not safe. What if the vendor you order from gets arrested the week you place your order, or what if customs - after a tip off - starts paying extra-close attention to his or her mailings? Because you're on probation you wouldn't be able to deny any inquiring LEOs entry, or even demand a warrant. They'd have your computer in no time.

If you're still not sure, go look at the sentencing guidelines that apply when a crime is committed by someone already in custody. I highly doubt you'll regret waiting to place an order. SR will still be here when you're free again.
