Title: Re: Why is FE bad or good?
Post by: samesamebutdifferent on February 17, 2013, 12:00 pm
Please, before you do anything further on SR make sure you READ as much info on the forums as possible, I really mean that.

If you FE you release the funds to the vendor and from that point forward you will have no recourse to SR resolution center if you run into any difficulties e.g. your shipment does not arrive, what you receive is not as described etc.

The escrow system is there to protect both you and the vendor, if you go outside of escrow SR resolution cannot and will not be there to help you.

If you FE you assume all of the risk, if you choose to do that live with your decision.
