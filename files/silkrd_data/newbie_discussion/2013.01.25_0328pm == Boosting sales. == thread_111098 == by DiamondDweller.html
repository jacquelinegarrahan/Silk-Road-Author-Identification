Title: Re: Boosting sales.
Post by: 00OOIlI00lO1O0 on January 27, 2013, 06:31 am
Open up your sales to beyond Europe. If you can package well, you can ship anywhere. I don't think US customs scrutinizes EU stuff too much, but it depends on which country you're shipping from.

How do your prices compare to other sellers of the same product?

You might want to sell smaller sample-sized quantities. Sometime people have 1btc left, and they can spend it on your gear, giving you some feedback.

Perhaps consider listing some other non-steroid items that you know will sell just to build up some reputation.

-Give out free sample to people with some feedback in return for good reviews.
-Keep your forums activity very active in order to increase popularity.
-Contact your buyers after you have shipped the product in order to help them until they give you a feedback.
-Having a good stealth package, there are many good reading material on the forums. IMHO better packaging= fewer problems + more popularity.
