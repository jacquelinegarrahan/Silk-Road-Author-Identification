Title: Re: New vendors
Post by: Derry on August 26, 2013, 05:14 pm
Just an advice for the future: you will eventually stumble on a vendor who is "perfect" for you ( good price, quality etc.) but will require FE. IF you'll want to buy from him, check his reputation first. 1) the should be real reviews, not "FEing, will post review when i get the order" and 2) take into consideration how old the reviews are. Only trust the vendor if the reviews are 1,2 days old max. If they are older, there is a chance the vendor plans on leaving SR and is just cashing in orders without actually sending them.
But again, the safest way is to NEVER FINALIZE EARLY.
