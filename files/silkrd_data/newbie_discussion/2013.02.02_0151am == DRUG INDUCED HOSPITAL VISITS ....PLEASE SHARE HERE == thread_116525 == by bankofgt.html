Post by: Ranxerox on February 28, 2013, 01:04 am
MDPV. Panic attack. I was convinced I was in cardiac arrest.

MDPV is a strange drug, and not a terribly useful one. Smoked, the first couple of times are like magic. Beautiful, clear-headed euphoria, aphrodiasis. But it burns out something in your brain, rapidly downregulates some receptor, or something, and all the good stuff goes away after a few days of use. If you try to get the nice effects back by going up on the dose, all you get is anxiety, and if you push it to far, you get a weird panic experience like mine. Some people have had visual hallucinations.

Recreationally, MDPV is a useless and somewhat hazardous drug. A shame, because the first time you use it, you think you've found the holy grail. Nope.
