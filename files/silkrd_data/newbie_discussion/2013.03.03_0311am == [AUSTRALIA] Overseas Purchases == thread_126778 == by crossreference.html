Post by: nanpa2001 on March 03, 2013, 05:50 am
Before considering all those things, you need to work out which vendors are actually going to send the drugs you have purchased.

Most of the vendors primarily sell to countries in the EU, and their stats are buffed by the positive reviews from European buyers. Without proof, but with anecdotal and personal evidence, I think a few of the leading European vendors do not send every order they get from Australia, but only send a fraction. Their stats take a hit, but the hit is overwhelmed by the positive from Europe.

I think that a lot of people assume that their drugs were taken by customs, when the drugs were not sent in the first place. For example, I ordered in a large quantity to my address. The amount I ordered, on 2 occasions from 2 different vendors around the same time was such a large amount that it certainly would have warranted investigation by the Australian Federal Police. At the very least it would have meant a knock on the door, or an order for Australia Post to withhold my mail for inspection by the AFP. None of those things have happened, and I have continued to receive drugs.
