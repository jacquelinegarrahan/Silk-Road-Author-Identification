Title: Re: How to delete a post?
Post by: PrincessHIGH on March 18, 2013, 09:20 pm
To delete a post: At the top right hand corner of your post you'll see a 'Remove' link with a red cross before it, click on it and your post will be removed.
To delete a thread: At the bottom of your thread in the left hand corner you'll see a 'REMOVE TOPIC' link, you can also lock the topic by clicking the 'LOCK TOPIC' link next to it', whichever you prefer.
Hope this helps you :)
