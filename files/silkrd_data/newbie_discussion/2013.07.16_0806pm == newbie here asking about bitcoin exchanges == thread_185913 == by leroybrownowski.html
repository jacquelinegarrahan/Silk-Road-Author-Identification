Post by: PowerToCharm on July 16, 2013, 10:45 pm
Traders on localbitcoins.com accept all manner of payment options. I've seen one or two that take Paypal, but it's rare. Many use dwolla or okpay, cash in mail, money order in mail, cash deposit to bank branch, electronic transfer to bank, western union, moneypack, vanilla reload card, etc. Every trader is an independent entity offering whatever exchange rate and payment method he desires. But you the buyer are safe because funds/coins are held in escrow through the site.
