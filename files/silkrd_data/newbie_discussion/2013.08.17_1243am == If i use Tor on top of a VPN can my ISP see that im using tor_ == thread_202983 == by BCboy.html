Title: Re: If i use Tor on top of a VPN can my ISP see that im using tor?
Post by: StaticTension on August 17, 2013, 01:53 am
It's pretty simple, just pick a VPN service that you think you can trust and sign up for it using a prepaid card from a public Wifi spot. After that you just connect to your VPN either through their preconfigured software or manually by going to your network > connect to a new network > connect to a VPN > enter VPN IP info(this is if your using windows). Once that's done then you just start ToR as usual. You may want to go online first and run a check of your IP to make sure you're connected to your VPN.
