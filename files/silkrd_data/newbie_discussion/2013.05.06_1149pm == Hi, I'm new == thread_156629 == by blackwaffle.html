Post by: android465764E on May 07, 2013, 11:51 am
I've used my home address before now as-well as various drops. Not had a problem thus far.

The escrow system is in place to protect you.
You pay for the goods and the money is held in 'escrow'. ie. It's neither in your wallet nor the vendor's wallet.
When you receive your goods and are happy, you finalize and the funds are released to the vendor.

Finalizing early, or 'FE' for short, is when you release funds before receiving your goods. Some vendors ask for this for various reasons, especially from new members. There are vendors who won't ask for it though. Be aware though, if you do FE and you get ripped off, SR will NOT be able to help you.

General rule of thumb - remain in escrow. It's for your protection.
