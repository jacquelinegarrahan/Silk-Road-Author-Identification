Title: Re: Purchasing as a newbie
Post by: rwitherspoon on August 22, 2013, 08:49 pm
Ignore this lottery crap.  Your buyer stats will show how much money you've spend as well as the number of transactions.  The vast majority of vendors will do business with someone who hasn't done a single transaction.  Many will not ask you to finalize early.  I've never been asked to finalize early...unless something truly shitty happens leave 5/5 feedback as soon as you get your order...if you have negative stats that's when you'll really have a hard time getting sellers to accept your order.  I'd be more concerned about finding the vendor that YOU are comfortable with.  Look closely at the feedback regarding shipping time, stealth and product quality; as well as the seller overall stats on their page.
