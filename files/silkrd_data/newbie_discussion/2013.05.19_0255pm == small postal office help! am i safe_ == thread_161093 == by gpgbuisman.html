If you use an established trusted vendor you will be fine.  The smell can pass through the bags over time, but you have to have faith that these guys are pros.  Small post office, or large post office shouldn't make a difference.  Mail goes to a forward sorting area, shipping facility, processed close to you, and then off for delivery.  The process shouldn't change that much for a small time.
