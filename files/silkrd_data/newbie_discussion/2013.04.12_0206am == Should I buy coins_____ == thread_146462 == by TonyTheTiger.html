I'd buy now but just because you'll spend less per coin. Once you have it it doesn't matter if it goes up or down, the price of stuff on SR is tied to the dollar, not to Bitcoin.

For instance, if you bought bitcoins when it was $100 for 1 bit coin and you planned on buying MDMA worth 1 bitcoin then you've spent $100.
If you buy bitcoins when they are $200 for a bitcoin, then the price of the same MDMA would be 0.5 bitcoins, which is still = $100. The only difference with the 2nd option is that you've had to buy $200 of bitcoins instead of $100. If you were planning on keeping some extra BTC anyway then no hassle.
