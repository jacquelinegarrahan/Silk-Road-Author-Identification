Title: Re: vendor issues
Post by: frank-butcher24 on August 04, 2013, 07:34 am
Chill out. The vendor has your order. Soon, he will either mark it as in transit, or he will cancel it. If he doesn't, you can cancel it yourself after 4 days.

You don't need to spam his inbox with your messages, and if he is a busy vendor he probably has a policy of not responding to messages like that.

The only thing that you should check at this stage is his vendor profile page, to see if he requires early finalisation for new buyers. If he does, out of courtesy he ought to send you a message telling you so, but he may not. If he does require FE for buyers like you, then you need to wait until he has marked it as 'in transit', and then you need to finalise. FE at your own discretion. We tend not to like FE around here but is is unfortunately a necessary evil for many new users of the road.
