Title: Re: cheapest/easiest way to 5 transactions?
Post by: kittiesandpuppies on March 02, 2013, 09:55 pm
Well, there are a variety of ebooks and lotteries that are only a couple dollars.

But for someone making you fe because you are new, they can see that you only spent 10$ in those transactions, and know that it was not a "real" order.

I would NEVER fe on SR, as a new buyer or for any other reason.  TOOOOOOO many stories of people getting ripped off, even by reputable vendors.  Its just too easy, especially now that many vendors are not tracking packages, and are not reshipping or refunding for orders that dont show.  All they have to do is say they shipped it, and then threaten to put you on a black list if you do not still leave positive feedback.  Also start with small orders, they are less likely to ask for fe if its only a gram or two.

Keep looking for vendors who dont require fe.  I have never been asked to fe, even after a bit of hooplah with one vendor.  The one time I was going to volunteer to do so for free shipping, the rapport with the vendor went down hill, and I never did it.
