Is it legal to possess sassafras oil in the US?

Yes, and no. It is fact specific and depends on the quantity. See 18 U.S.C. 2D1.11

It is listed as a precursor for the manufacture of amphetamine and methamphetamine.

It appears that the possession of 320 kg or more is illegal per se. Otherwise possession of any amount when said person "knows, or has reason to believe, that it will be used to manufacture illicit drugs."

See also 21 U.S.C. 802

Quote from: Unity on May 12, 2013, 01:54 am
What are the penalties for different amounts of nBOME?

Uncontrolled at Federal and State level, though arguably may contravene the Federal Analog Act under certain circumstances given its structural and functional similarity to controlled substance 2C-B.

Read more: http://www.drugs-forum.com/forum/showwiki.php?title=25I-NBOMe#ixzz2T2dwDeN1

21 U.S.C.A. § 813


United States Code Annotated Currentness
Title 21. Food and Drugs (Refs & Annos)
Chapter 13. Drug Abuse Prevention and Control (Refs & Annos)
 Subchapter I. Control and Enforcement
 Part B. Authority to Control; Standards and Schedules
§ 813. Treatment of controlled substance analogues


A controlled substance analogue shall, to the extent intended for human consumption, be treated, for the purposes of any Federal law as a controlled substance in schedule I.

CREDIT(S)

(Pub.L. 91-513, Title II, § 203, as added Pub.L. 99-570, Title I, § 1202, Oct. 27, 1986, 100 Stat. 3207-13; amended Pub.L. 100-690, Title VI, § 6470(c), Nov. 18, 1988, 102 Stat. 4378.)
