Title: Virtual office?
Post by: flightwave on January 27, 2013, 06:25 am
Has anyone ever used a virtual office to pick up? You know, one of those places that offers you a mailbox and a business address. So let's assume you have a cheap shell company registered to this address you can simply use:


Product returns
Shelf Co. Ltd
Suite 6100
Corporate-Dicksucking Plaza
Des Moines, Idaho
 
Seems a lot less suspect than using a P.O. box if you can justify paying the $100/month for it (say, if you're a reseller). I'd assume it's much harder for them to do a controlled delivery in cases like this.

Plus, it's insurance in case a seller ever gets busted with addresses he should've been deleting/clearing.
