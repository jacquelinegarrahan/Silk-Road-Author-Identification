Yes, you should click "resolve" and extend the auto-finalization date.  That'll give it a little more time for the package to get to you, and it will NOT affect your stats at all.

Title: Re: What should I do?
Post by: scout on June 01, 2013, 05:21 am
Your buyer stats will not be affected negatively at all by taking it to resolution.  The only time it would affect your buyer stats is if you:

a)  let it autofinalize
b)  receive a refund from SR

DO NOT finalize early!  Stay in escrow and just extend the auto-finalization!
