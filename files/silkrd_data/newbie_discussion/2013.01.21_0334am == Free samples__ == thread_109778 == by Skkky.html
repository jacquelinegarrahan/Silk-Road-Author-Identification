Title: Re: Free samples??
Post by: Thirdplace on January 21, 2013, 04:59 am
Most vendor's offer samples when they start business.  Some offer them for free, some charge and that's fair.  A vendor should never feel obligated to donate their product to anyone else.  If someone wants a small amount of product they can purchase it.  It's often the case that a new vendor will give discounted 'samples' to established members that can verify that they received the product and remark on it's quality and the service.

Most established vendor's don't give out samples because they have no need.  Their product and feedback speaks for itself.  Some newer vendor's don't have special deals because they're confident their product speaks for itself.
