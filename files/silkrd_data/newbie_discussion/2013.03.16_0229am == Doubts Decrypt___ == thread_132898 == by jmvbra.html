Title: Re: Doubts Decrypt???
Post by: SudoMan on March 16, 2013, 02:49 pm
The Vendor usually posts their public key. You want to add that to your keyring. Then open up a TextEdit type application and begin to write the message you wish to encrypt. Right click the message, copy to clipboard and encrypt/sign. Here you will chose the public key you wish to send to, then sign it with your public key. The Vendor will need your key to read the message and reply using PGP encryption.

There are TONS of threads on here that can help you better understand PGP. Also YouTube has a great wealth of information (that is where things really made sense for me)

Anyways, best of luck with your travels!

If you are dubious about PGP message your vendor and politely ask them if they accept Privnote. Privnote is a 3rd party website that encrypts and deletes the message after the recipient views it. Personally, for the illegal actions we are doing on this site, I would recommend PGP and NOT a 3rd party for encryption, however the choice is yours.
