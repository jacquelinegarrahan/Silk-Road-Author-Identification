Title: Re: How do you keep your stash fresh/as potent as possible for as long as possible
Post by: dirac on September 13, 2013, 03:05 pm
I just use an old tea container out of the sun and keep powders in their baggies. For weed, 420science sells these awesome glass jars which'll keep it sticky for a solid month and pretty good for up to four or five months. Cute designs too, and I've had them fall out of my bag onto asphalt when I'm biking and it was hardly scratched. The smallest jars fit an eighth nicely.

Tabs are the hardest I think... I fold them so that the print touches (so if it leaks it just likes into itself) and then wrap them in paper and then plastic, put them in a DVD case and keep them refrigerated. It seems to work pretty well.
