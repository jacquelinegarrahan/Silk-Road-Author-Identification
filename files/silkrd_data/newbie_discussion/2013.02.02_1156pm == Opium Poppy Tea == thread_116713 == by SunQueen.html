Why not harvest the opium from the pods? There are lots of tutorials on this online. Basically you score the pod with a sharp knife in the morning, let the opium  bleed out, and then scrape it off the pod later in the day. Do this every day until there's no more. Collect all the opium in a jar or something, and there you have it. Eat it, smoke it, whatever.

There are many alkaloids in opium. One of them makes you nauseous. Many are pain relievers. There is a chemical process for separating out each alkaloid. If you are truly interested in refining your own drugs from raw opium, again, there are a lot of guides on this online... it will require a chemical that is difficult to get without attracting the DEA's attention, though.
