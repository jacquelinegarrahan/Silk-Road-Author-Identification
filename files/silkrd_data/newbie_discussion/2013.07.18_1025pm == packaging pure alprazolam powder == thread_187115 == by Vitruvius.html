Post by: white1 on July 23, 2013, 12:32 pm
I have found the best way to deal with Alp powder is to mix it with Everclear alcohol, must be 90 proof, fuck isopropyl  alcohol or any other shit. Turn it to liquid form. I am trying to finish the  2nd gram I bought 6 months ago. The shit goes a long ways. Anyways, if you got the bag split in 100mg lots, just put 100mg in a small vial or bottle that's leak-proof and add 1 ml THATS (milliliter) of Everclear to the 100 mg (milligrams) of Alp. I use a syringe from and old ink jet printer refill kit that I sterilized to measure the Everclear. Hold the vial or bottle against a light bulb for a few minutes until it feels warm to touch and shake it up. If it isn't fully dissolved and you see particles,  repeat process. The powder should fully dissolve from 160 to 180 degrees. When it's liquid suck it back up into the syringe.Before hand you should count how many  drops  come out of 1 ml of liqiud from your syringe. Then do the math. Dispense it  drop by drop onto a candy or some form of paper, Ive used everything from small squares of rolling papers with the gum edge cut off  to plain paper towel squares. Also used different  candies that will hold the liquid without losing their color or shape. Experiment. But be safe. Hope this helps.
Title: Re: packaging pure alprazolam powder
Post by: Crepuscular on July 23, 2013, 07:02 pm
I agree with white1. If you have it completely dissolved in liquid (propylene glycol or strong ethanol does the trick), dosing is much easier. It is not really trivial to mix it equally with an inert filler and distribute over caps - if density or particle size distribution is not equal, you risk getting VERY different amounts of active drug in each capsule.
