The "Shitman Review" severely tightened up CD control in the UK, making things much, much harder for all us hardworking chemist-shop connoisseurs. For starters the old trick of running a CD script from a private GP through a laser printer was totally blown out, but more significant, pharmacists became much, much stricter post Shitman.
