Hi changemonger,

A vendor has 4 days to process your order; the option to cancel will become a clickable link when that 4 day (96 hour) period is up if they have not marked it 'in transit' by that time, and you will receive your Bitcoin back immediately. Please be aware that if the order was hedged you will receive back the USD amount (in Bitcoin) that the item cost, minus the ~4% hedging fee that is charged on all hedged transactions.
