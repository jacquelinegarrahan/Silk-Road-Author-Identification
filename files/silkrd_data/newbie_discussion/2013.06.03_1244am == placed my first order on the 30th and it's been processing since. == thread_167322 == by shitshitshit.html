Hi shitshitshit,

Yes, you will receive all your BTC back. However, if the item was hedged you will receive back the item's USD value at the prevailing Bitcoin rates at the time that you receive your refund. The Bitcoin value has dropped a very small bit since the 30th so you will likely get a small bit more Bitcoin than you paid out; please note that the ~4% hedging fee will be charged on what you receive back, if the item was indeed hedged.
