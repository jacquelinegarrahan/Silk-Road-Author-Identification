If you have not paid the bond then you do not have a vendor account. All sales must take place through a vendor account, so you will not be able to sell anything until you get one. However, the bond is refundable once you meet its terms (which you'll find at one of the links below), which essentially means that a Silk Road vendor account is free!

I would recommend that you read the SR Wiki - specifically the Buyer's Guide, and the Seller's Guide - to get a better idea of how everything works.

SR Wiki:
http://dkn255hz262ypmii.onion/wiki/index.php?title=Main_Page

Buyer's Guide:
http://dkn255hz262ypmii.onion/wiki/index.php?title=Buyer%27s_Guide

Seller's Guide:
http://dkn255hz262ypmii.onion/wiki/index.php?title=Seller%27s_Guide

F.A.Q.:
http://dkn255hz262ypmii.onion/wiki/index.php?title=Frequently_Asked_Questions

Bond & bond refund:
http://dkn255hz262ypmii.onion/wiki/index.php?title=Seller%27s_Guide#Bond

I would also recommend doing your research in the Shipping and Security forums. Anything can be successfully sent and delivered within Europe (and indeed, worldwide) once you take the necessary precautions.
