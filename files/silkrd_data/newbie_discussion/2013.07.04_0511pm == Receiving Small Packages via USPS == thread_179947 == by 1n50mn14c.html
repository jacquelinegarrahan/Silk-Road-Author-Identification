Post by: Mr.Charlie on July 04, 2013, 05:31 pm
I used to use a PO box with no issues for a while.

Someone recently was telling me they used their last name but a different first name, that way the mail man will assume your brother or something is staying with you. It's worked before, but might not be a permanent solution.
If using a P.O box or friends house wont work, then use your home address, but when ordering make sure your house is clear of ALL paraphenalia and drugs, especially if your ordering bulk amounts (~20+grams)
