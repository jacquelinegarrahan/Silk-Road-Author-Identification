Title: Re: .001 g scales
Post by: simplemechanic on June 09, 2013, 04:11 pm
Like one poster said, get the gemeni 20x.001 scale off amazon for ~$20. I tested it with 3 different calibration weights (2 10 gram weights and 1 5 gram) bought from different sources (to avoid gemeni altering the results somehow - yes I'm that paranoid) and it was within .002 of each calibration weight. I also tested the two 10 gram weights that were provided, and again within .002 grams. Don't spend $100 on a scale that will perform just as as well for a normal user. That .002 difference is not quite withing the .001 specs, but you also have you consider the face that there might have been a slight magnetic field effecting it, some oil from my hand got on the weight, some of the weight oxidized and changed its mass, etc. Basically, you can trust the gemeni from amazon to .002 grams (maybe even .001 grams with proper weighing techniques). When I'm weighing out meth, I want 30 mg. 28mg or 32mg is okay (I usually don't even weigh out to exactly a 30mg read anyways), but the difference between 20mg vs 40mg on a .01g scale is completely unacceptable. Although I have a 20x.001g scale, a 200x.01g scale, a 5000x1g scale, and a 200,000x100g scale. If you're going to spend $200 on drugs, you might as well spend $100 on proper scales so that you don't overdose.
