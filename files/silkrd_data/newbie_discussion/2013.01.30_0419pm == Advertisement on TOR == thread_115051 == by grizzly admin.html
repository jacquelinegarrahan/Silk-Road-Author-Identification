It's not a secret either. :) Minimal price or so called bid is 0,0001 BTC per view (single impression) and there are only three ad spots there. With future rotation in mind, so technically there are more.

Basically if someone places an ad with default bid (0,0001 BTC per impression), and than another someone bids 0,0003 BTC for his/her ad, the latter is shown. The one with 0,0001 bid just waiting for that other campaign to end though. And if no-one bids more meanwhile, his/her ad is shown.

Simply put, minimal price is still 0,0001 BTC per single view until someone is willing to pay more. In the end market itself dictates the price. It's a sort of generalized second-price auction.
