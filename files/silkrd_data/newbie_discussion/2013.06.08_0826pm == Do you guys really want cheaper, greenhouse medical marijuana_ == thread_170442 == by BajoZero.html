Hey Newbies  ;D
In the past, I've focused on only top quality, indoor grown, small crop, organic medical marijuana.  That shit is expensive.  There's simply nothing more that I can do to bring down my prices for buyers.  But I've noticed: 1. Everybody seems to want cheaper weed, and 2. The weed that most SR buyers ultimately buy tends to not be top quality.

I'm not going to stop offering amazing bud.  But I know that lots of people can't afford amazing bud, and just want bud.  So I'm trying to determine whether or not I should stock lower-quality, greenhouse grown weed.  If you buy weed on SR, please let me know what is your interest in this by responding to this poll.
