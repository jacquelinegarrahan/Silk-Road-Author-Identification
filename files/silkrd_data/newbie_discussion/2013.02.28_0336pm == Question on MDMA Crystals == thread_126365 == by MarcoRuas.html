Title: Re: Question on MDMA Crystals
Post by: exis on March 01, 2013, 07:05 am
Cool, dry, dark place is always good, but don't put it in the freezer.

Some silica gel (those packets in electronics that say "do not eat") will help absorb moisture.
Its very stable, I've had pills over 5 years old that have been as good as the first ones.

Title: Re: Question on MDMA Crystals
Post by: mushmold on March 01, 2013, 07:23 am
As long as they have been well cleaned, dry, dark and relatively cool  (<30c) and they will last for years. Never refrigerate, and if you freeze layer in multi zip-locks with silica gel packets in the innermost, and when you take it out of the freezer let it come up to room temp before opening (freezing is still not recommended).
