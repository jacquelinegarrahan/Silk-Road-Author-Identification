Title: Re: 20% of MDMA?
Post by: catalan on June 19, 2013, 09:38 pm
Pure freebase MDMA constitutes only 84% of the hydrochloride salt, so if you have 80% it's pretty damn pure.  But how do you know your batch is 80%?

If you want to find out exactly, the best thing would be some form of fluid chromatography to separate the molecules followed by mass spectrometry to identify them.  Most chemistry labs would be able to perform this for you, with varying repercussions depending on your jurisdiction (in the Netherlands, for example, there are state-sponsored programmes that will give you a full report of your sample's content).
