Title: Re: LSD vs Shrooms
Post by: Dovah457 on February 15, 2013, 04:43 pm
I like LSD better for three reasons:

1) Like someone said before, shrooms can be more dark and mystical. This can sometimes be awesome, but I found that it only takes one or two bad images/thoughts to get stuck in my mind and kinda haunt me for the trip. This usually is nothing more than a recurring thought that I have to put down, but its distracting and worrisome if the dosage is high. I still love shrooms, but I have to be super careful of my environment when I take them

2) The method of taking. Putting some paper or liquid on your tongue is a lot easier and less disgusting than munching on some dried stems, especially if you have that nasty fucking shroom shake XP

3) The duration. I always feel like right when I'm about to settle into a nice, long hallucinogenic experience on shrooms, they fall off and I come down. Good LSD keeps me going for 8-10 hours depending on the dosage.
