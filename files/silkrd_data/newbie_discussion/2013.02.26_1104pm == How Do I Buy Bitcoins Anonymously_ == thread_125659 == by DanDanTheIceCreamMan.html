Title: Re: How Do I Buy Bitcoins Anonymously?
Post by: Ranxerox on February 27, 2013, 12:39 am
With any service that uses Moneygram to accept cash, you give info over the phone, so there's no way they can check it, and you can give false info. When I did this at CVS, the clerk who rang up the whole thing didn't ask for info either, she just took my money. The problem, obviously, is that if anything goes haywire with the transaction, you're screwed, because they're very likely going to ask for ID, not just the receipt, before they refund your money.

Now, what service uses Moneygram? Bitinstant does, but their Moneygram -> BTC-e has been down for a week, and isn't up at this point. Even if it were, I'm not sure I'd trust it for a while. Ziggap uses Moneygram, too. I've used them via WU, and it worked great, but not with cash/Moneygram.

Hope this helps!
