Post by: Yousaf on July 13, 2013, 12:40 am
I've snorted Ketamine a few number of times, the rest of the hundreds of dozens of other times I chose the route of intramuscular injection.  If you are an experienced Ketamine user you will understand why IM (intramuscular) is the only way to go.  Usually the dosage for racemic for someone with no tolerance is 100mg, for the S isomer a dose of 50mg should induce a psychedelic trip.   The trip on racemic for me feels very euphoric and drunk, while the trip on S-isomer is truly psychedelic, ego loss, you feel like you're on a roller coaster that is slow moving, and your limbs are dragged across the room.  There is a usual after glow with Ketamine, but if you use it everyday or very often, it will become an addiction.  I think I read somewhere that it acts on some of the receptors that opiates work on.  I also think snorting ketamine is very stupid and habit forming.
