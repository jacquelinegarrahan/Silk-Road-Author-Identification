Post by: snuff420 on September 05, 2013, 08:32 am
Although Bungee54 has announced that he will soon stop selling speed, I will finally go ahead and post my review.  Given the current speed famine on SR, especially for those of us here on the west side of the Pond, I figure there must be one of two of you freaks out there who can use this info.

Based on his excellent reviews and (alleged) test results in the cocaine market, as well as on one positive review from an acquaintance in the real world, I purchased some speed from Bungee about a month ago and offered to FE voluntarily.  This is not something I have done previously in my first purchase with any SR vendor.

The stuff was at my door in the western US less than a week after I clicked buy.  I was expecting the quality to be decent but not great, because Bungee had both recently announced his plan to exit the speed market and dropped his prices (on speed but not on coke).  Even so, when I opened the vac-sealed bag, I was a bit surprised to find a mustard-yellow clumpy paste with the strongest smell of any speed paste I've seen.  I decided to put it aside until I had a chance to properly clean it up.

Over the holiday weekend, I finally got around to cleaning up Bungee's paste, in the following sequence.
  1) The wet paste was weighed at 27.7g (11% over the 25g promised in the listing).
  2) After drying over vacuum, the weight dropped to 24.7g.  The liquid filtrate was yellow and had a smell that was strong but still well short of the paste itself.
  3) The dryish paste was then washed thoroughly with (anhydrous) acetone and vacuum filtered again.   The filtrate was an orange yellow, even darker than the paste and just as smelly.  The washed powder was now completely white, but it was still quite wet and clumped.
  4) Finally, the white powder was lyophilized for an hour at room temp and between 10 and 20 mbar.  The product was a fluffy, snow white powder that looked something like powdered sugar.  The final weight was 19.8g.  This is 20% below the listed quantity.  The lowest I've ever obtained by this method was a loss of 6%, and that was for pharm-grade powder.  The amount dissolved away into acetone is therefore probably only ~15%.

Alas, the mass spec was off-line.  I apologize for the lack of legit purity analysis.  I'll do my best to get back in for MS results, but that will likely not happen until around the end of the month.  By then, Bungee may not be offering this stuff any more.  For what little it's worth, bio-assay indicated a pleasantly clean product with potency that the subject could not distinguish from a control sample of an equal pharmaceutical grade racemic amphetamine.  No pure d-amphetamine control was available for comparison.

In summary, Bungee54's speed looked and smelled unexpectedly nasty out of the box, but don't judge a book by its cover.  With a simple acetone wash and thorough drying, this stuff was up there with the best at a nice price.  Any of you aspiring speed vendors out there, I recommend you consider following the procedure before you sell your product.  It doesn't require real lab facilities, just vacuum and acetone, and it will probably pay for itself.
