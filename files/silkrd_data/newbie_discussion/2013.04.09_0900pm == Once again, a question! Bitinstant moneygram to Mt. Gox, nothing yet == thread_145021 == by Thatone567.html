Post by: SFdirect on April 09, 2013, 09:37 pm
I dont hedge my sales. What is means is that:

Example 1

Bitcoin increase during escrow  =  Seller, not you, make $$$$

Example 2

Bitcoin Decrease during escrow  =  You make out like a bandit. When the seller transfers to fiat currency, the $100, when received is worth $10, Seller loses money.

Hedgeing = Price is fixed to fiat currency. When you make a purchase for $100 the seller receives $100 regardless of bitcoin price, but it cost the seller a fee that is passed to the consumer.

Hopes this helps,
SFd
