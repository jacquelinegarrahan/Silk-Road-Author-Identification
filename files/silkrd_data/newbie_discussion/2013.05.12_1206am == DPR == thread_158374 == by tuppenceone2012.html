There's no way for SR to FORCE vendors not to ask for people to FE.  All SR can do is advise over and over again that people ALWAYS STAY IN ESCROW and DO NOT FINALIZE EARLY.  SR has made that position abundantly clear, but it can't eradicate FE'ing, I don't think.  All it can do is discourage finalizing early - which they / we do already.  If a buyer or vendor choose to ignore that, then they do so at their own peril.  The escrow system is in place for the protection of both the vendor and the buyer - there is no reason to choose to leave escrow or go around it.
