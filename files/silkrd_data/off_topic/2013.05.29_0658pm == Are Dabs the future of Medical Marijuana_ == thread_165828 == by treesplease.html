Title: Re: Are Dabs the future of Medical Marijuana?
Post by: HEATFan on May 29, 2013, 08:24 pm
Unless you are a MMJ patient you don't know what you need. Everyone is different. Dabs will definitely become more popular but flower, edibles, tinctures, and other forms will never go away. Different ailments require different forms and doses of medicine. I would actually say that dabs are used more recreationally rather than anything medically applicable. But with that being said, dabs do have the potential to cause less harm as it is vapor and not smoke so it could be beneficial to someone who is not able to inhale many carcinogens. To answer your question - no, I do not think dabs will be "the future" of medical marijuana but rather that it will co-exist with every other form currently out there.
