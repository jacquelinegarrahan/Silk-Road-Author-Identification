This is going to be an odd one. More of an anecdote than a recommendation.

I'm on acid, and for some reason am watching Lost. (spoilers follow) And it happens to be the episode where Sayid has been captured and is being interrogated by the "others". In the past, I think. Anyway, the lead interrogator, in true 60's hippie style, isn't going to torture Sayid. He gives him LSD.

Interrogator and co. approach Sayid, bound, outside, and question him. Well, Sayid says all this crazy stuff. He's from the future, etc, etc.. Interrogator looks at the others and says "I think I gave him too much."

Sayid's already looking terribly amused, but now breaks into a huge grin, and I'm grinning, too, watching this. "No," he says. "You used exactly the right amount!" Which both Sayid and I find to be the funniest thing we've ever considered. ROFL.
