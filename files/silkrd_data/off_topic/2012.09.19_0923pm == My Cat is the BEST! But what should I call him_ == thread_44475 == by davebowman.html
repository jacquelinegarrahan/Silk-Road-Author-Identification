Title: My Cat is the BEST! But what should I call him?
Post by: davebowman on September 19, 2012, 09:23 pm
I found a stray kitty last weekend and now were best friends. I haven't committed to a name for him yet but so far I'm teetering between logan (as in weapon X, the ole canucklehead) and black peter (as in "the people they know, but the people don't care, that a man can be as poor as me. Take a look at poor peter, he's lying in pain. Now lets all go run and see.) He's the cutest guy ever. He doesn't play much but I think hes just settling in. Like, most cats LOVE string, and if you make them chase a string they get really happy! He just ignores string, but he does chase elastic bands. Also that seemed to confuse him. He sleeps at least 20 hours a day, but at night he comes and sleeps on my bed. He's been coughing and sneezing, probably has the kitty flu. There was one day last week that it rained really hard, and he was probably just cold and hungry, and contracted some sort of illness from exposure, but now he has a warm cozy home, and I am nursing him back to health. I think I like logan as a name, because of this healing factor which seems to be at work. But generally, I think cats are the BEST! When I come home he follows me around my bedroom rubbing his face on my legs and hands, and he says, "meow!" which on various websites which claim to be authorities on cat interpretation, means, "What's up? Where have you been? I've missed you!" and that's exactly how I feel about my cat! So obviously we understand eachother. How do all of YOU feel about cats? And what should I name this cat? If someone has a REALLY good suggestion I will take it into consideration, but only if its really good, like good enough to make me say, OH SNAP I sure wish I thought of that first!
