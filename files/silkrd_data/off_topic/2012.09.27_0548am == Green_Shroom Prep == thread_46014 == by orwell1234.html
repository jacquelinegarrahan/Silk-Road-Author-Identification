Here is my recipe for cannabutter. I always choose the amount of butter so that I get 40 grams of end product per gram of hash I use. Considering that butter has about 20% water, you need to use 50 grams of butter per gram of hash. Heat some water in a pot (the amount doesn't really matter) and dissolve the butter in it. Wait for the water to start simmering and add your finely chopped hash. Stir until it has dissolved. Let it simmer for half an hour while occasionally stirring up the mixture. Pour the mixture into a container and let it cool at room temperature. This is important because if the cooling process happens too quickly, the butter will not become a solid chunk and it'll be harder to retrieve from the mixture. After everything has cooled properly, you can take the layer of hardened, cannabinoid-infused butter off the surface of the mixture. The mixture now only consists of water, cellulose from the hash and some water-soluble shit from the butter.
