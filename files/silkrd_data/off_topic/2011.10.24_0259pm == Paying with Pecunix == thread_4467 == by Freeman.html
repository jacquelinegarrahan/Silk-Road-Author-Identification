I've never seen a seller briefly take control just to send a payment. create a new account for each deal, and ONLY fund it for that deal. then the seller logs in, changes password and email settings, transfers funds to their account, and leaves that account empty... BTW pecunix can and WILL disable an account suspected of illegal activity or tampering. this is why the account email settings are changed from inside the account, so that you can't report your own account as stolen, or taking part in illegal activity anymore (email address is account number). this protects the seller from you knowing what their real account number is.
