Title: Re: lucid dreams and shit..
Post by: mahalo on August 15, 2012, 11:47 pm
In a lucid dream you are usually fully aware of any desicion and choices. Therefore you can consciously talk to dream figures or choose your dream setting. Being caught up in a "given" scenario and experiencing it stronger than usual is imo either drug related or, more likely, due to the state of falling asleep as CaptainTrips said. In this state, when mentally not "fully calm" you tend to wake up and nod off again and again leading to what seems like weird reality perceptions. If you are able to stay calm enough to enter the observer mode you might actually be able to either clearly distinguish that you heard the music in kind of a pre-dream scenario as you were falling asleep instead of in reality, or you might be able to induce a lucid dream by staying in the observer mode. Your explanations sound more like a mixture of perceptions not exclusively relating to any possibility leading to actual lucid dreaming.
