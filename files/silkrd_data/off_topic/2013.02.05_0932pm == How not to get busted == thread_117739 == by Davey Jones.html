Title: How not to get busted
Post by: Davey Jones on February 05, 2013, 09:32 pm
This is what I've done over the years; never keep the same cell phone for too long, keep one phone for normal everyday stuff and the other for business.  Once the number for the business phone gets used quite a bit, throw entire phone away, get another, repeat process.  Never deal with new people even if they have cash, how did they come so close anyway?  Anonymous thru the mail works good, but its not the only way.  Create layers between you and the street.  I would never want to be the one in the club handing half grams to people in the bathroom.  Keep a set of friends that don't get high, they know you at least well enough to say you don't even do the shit much less sell it, basically establish a cover.  Maintain a legit job and keep the business away from work, keep up appearances, the wrong disagreement with a co-worker may get you busted.  Doing business out of home address?  Yeah right.  Someone elses house is always best, or a rental space obtained with a false identity.  Storing shit at home, another mistake that gets people busted.  There's a hundred ways to hide shit off site and a hundred more to transport, but I can't give all my secrets away.  Have fun
