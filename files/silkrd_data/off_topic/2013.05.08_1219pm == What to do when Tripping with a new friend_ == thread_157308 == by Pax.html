Title: Re: What to do when Tripping with a new friend?
Post by: TrashBox on May 08, 2013, 12:54 pm
My suggestions :)

Make sure she is in a comfortable environment, like her house.

Have her invite over a close friend that isn't going to trip with the two of you, but is willing to hang out in case she freaks or gets scared. A more familiar friend can help ground her.

I think music is important, so see if she has a favorite playlist or something.

Speak to her about using a benzo in case of a bad trip. Then have one on hand just in case.

Good luck and have fun!
