Yes.

But benzos are situational- use only when needed.  If you use them daily thinking this is the solution to your "problem" you will build up tolerance, and be in trouble.

Also use the smallest amount that works for you, and the benzo that works for you.  Get one that has a high anxiolytic effect, with a low "drowsy" effect.

Get a benzo that has a short half-life to reduce long-term tolerance.

I use Klonopin/Clonazepam @ .25 mg.  For me it is a strong anxiolytic, and gives me MORE mental clarity, vs less (due to not being overwhelmed by anxiety/anxious thoughts).


Per a remedy for your self confidence, I think if you use a benzo to move your life forward into things that give you more self confidence, that can work; also if you use it to gradually acclimate yourself to more and more uncomfortable situations can work too;  but as I said don't think of the benzo as the solution, or that can get you into trouble- abuse and tolerance.
