Title: Re: Is the weed worth the price?
Post by: redalloverthelandguyhere on April 24, 2012, 09:43 pm
Home grown cannabis is WAY too expensive and the price has crept up from £90 in the 90s to over £200 on the street.

Anyone smoking a fair bit of weed ought to grow for their own personal use. For the price an oz costs here you could buy a grow tent, light, extractors, filter and other assorted bits and pieces.

Some folk are lucky to know a few growers but outside of that the average punter is paying top dollar because small growers make the profit selling outside of friends and family. Weed grown on a small scale by a grower who has 15 years or more growing under the belt is worth the high price.

Also many of us here NEVER see good hash outside of Amsterdam (and even there you have to be careful) so its 'worth' buying 3.5 grams of hashes that are for us rare treats! Sure they are pricey but I bet even the farmers in remote areas of the globe are demanding more dollar. Hash is a one off that differs from harvest to harvest. I do a gree that some prices are just silly. I've seen hash going for 5 bitcoins - not ice-o-lator hash just imported. No way is a gram of ANY hash worth almost £15.50. That's just greed IMO. I could not see any vendor buying hash that cost almost £300 per ounce. Ice-o-lator hash (water and ice extracted hash) is almost pure THC glands so maybe that would bring a mad price.

The BEST hash is the growers own made from trimmings and small airy buds. I prefer dry sieved hash to the water extracted.

I admit the best deals here are for 100 grams and above with the hash.

One things always makes me amused. The fact remains that bud contains about 20% resin glands at most. So if you see a gram of hash on sale for 3 BTC - and a gram of bud going for 3BTC also - the hash should always be a better bargain! Science says so! 80% of the bud is just carbon based nothingness!

Sometimes the UK gets cheap and nasty imported weed that has obviously been used to make hash prior to it being compressed. Even some Dutch weed has been sifted of hash prior to it being compressed. The resin heads are missing and only the stalks remain which still leaves a decent weed.

Some sellers will shake an ounce of the glands - and sell on the weed which will be minus 3 grams or so on average for a short shake to grab the best glands.

Good weed is worth the price. You are paying for all the hours a grower puts in plus the risk also.

Commercial weed ought to be dirt cheap really.

Then again I think that if a joint is the same price as a pint of beer then its good.

Sorry for the long reply but its like pillow talk here sometimes. All the confessing and reminiscences.
