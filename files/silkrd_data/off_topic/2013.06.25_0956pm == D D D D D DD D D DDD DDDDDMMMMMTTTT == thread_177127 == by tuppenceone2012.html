You can eat small quantities (80mg to 150mg) by dosing with an MAOI about half an hour earlier. It gives a good, long ayahuasca-style trip, but usually with no unpleasant somatic side effects (like nausea and diarrhea).

Or you can vaporize and inhale it using a good vaporizer, a glass pipe, an ordinary pipe with the DMT sandwiched between layes of an innocuous herb like parsely, a water pipe or The Machine.
