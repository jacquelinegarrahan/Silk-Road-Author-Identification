Title: Re: Drugs and Magic
Post by: powersmoke on August 04, 2013, 07:36 pm
Quote from: fiendish on July 25, 2013, 12:08 pm
I've always reckoned there's a connection between the two. Certainly had some interesting experiences on different drugs that I would characterize as magical. Very interested to hear from fellow fiends as to your thoughts, magical experiences and general words of wisdom.

I dont know that this experience qualifies as magic, in the Harry Potter sense of the word.
But to those of us there that night, this experience was definitely magical in a way that we felt a very real connection to each other, the forces of nature that were all around us, as well this planetary 'ship' (thats not too cheesy, huh?) that was transporting us across the cosmos at that very moment.


Anyway--heres my story, and I'm stickin to it.
If i start draggin on, or repeat myself, just tell me to move on, you've heard that part before.  ;-)

Several years ago, my buddy and i rented a cabin on a large lake for an entire summer.
We were both thinking this would probably be our last hoorah, and were intent on making this summer a blowout.
We figured our days of partying and zero responsibility were numbered as we both had recently hit 30 something, we both recently finished up college and had new jobs, he was getting married in the fall, and my girlfriend had just told me she was pregnant.....
So we rented this place right on the water for a couple of months, spent days skiing,  fishing, and drinking, and the nights were dedicated to various forms of perversion and madness. 

Short story long---
One afternoon, after we had been on the lake all day drinking, i pulled my boat up in the area that had a nice bank to beach on and a wooded area so the girls could go pee...
My buddy got out also, and was staggering around in the woods when he starts whistling and yelling for me to get my ass over to where he was.
 As i got there, i noticed we were standing in an area COVERED in mushrooms....uh oh.
We began filling them up anything we could with shrooms. Ice chests, backpacks, towels--did mention it was COVERED??

We spent the next month on another planet. We had so many, we tried them just about every way imaginable.
We made the usual kool-aid, but also baked them on pizza, made spaghetti sauce,  sauteed them on steak chicken etc, boiled them and used the liquid to make ice cubes----we had a LOT of shrooms.

So....one night we were trippin pretty hard, when this late evening summer storm comes up.
We sat on the dock for what seemed like forever watching HUGE bolts of lightning striking out on the lake.
We could see the rain coming across the lake, headed towards us and just before the rain hit, we got hit with this wall of cold wind that was strong enough that kinda freaked us out at first. Once it started raining, the wind calmed down, but the lightning was still going pretty constant, lighting the whole lake up every few seconds.

We sat there in the pouring rain, in complete darkness other than the lightning, and in complete silence, other than the occasional thunder clap.
The power and beauty of mother nature had us mesmerized for what seemed like hours.
Truly, that experience was one of the best I've ever had on psychedelics.
The feeling of the rain, the lightning, the thunder--all of it was amplified by the shrooms to the point of making it seem almost like a religious experience for me.
And somehow while this was happening, without us saying a word, the other three there were experiencing almost the same thing I was, and having the same feelings I was having.

Is that magic? I dont know.
 It sure felt magical, though.

I have not really ever tried to explain that night to anyone before because i didnt figure they'd get it.
And I'm sure I am not doing the experience justice now.
But I have a feeling that if whoever reads this has done shrooms before, they probably understand exactly what I'm talking about.
