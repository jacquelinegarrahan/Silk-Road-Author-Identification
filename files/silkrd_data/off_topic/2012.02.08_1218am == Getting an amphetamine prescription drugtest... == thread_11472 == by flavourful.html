Post by: le botbahn on February 08, 2012, 09:54 am
Will they take away my prescription if they notice I have been on adderall recently without a prescription?

  -Depends on what kind of communication/relationship took place prior to being prescribed & tested. Are you sure it was a drug test and not a thyroid test (this is usually standard with prescribing stimulants)?

- If you test positive, then yes, you may, and likely will get cut off. Amphetamine can show up on a urinalysis anywhere from 1-5 days after the last dose. Your result depends on how frequently you have been using it, as well as how much time passed between your last dose and the piss test. If you weren't taking tons of it and didn't take any for 3-5 days before being tested, then you may have passed it. There are guidelines for what qualifies as a positive drug test. If a test does not give results higher than the guidelines, it does not qualify as a "positive" test.
 

also can anything else happen other than losing my script?

  -Not sure what you had in mind, but probably not. It's won't be that big a deal for you. The worst I can think of is that it would go in your chart/medical record and they refuse to renew your prescription. If you do fail but have already established trust w/ him/her, might want to consider being frank/honest about your use and why when the subject comes up, it may go a long way. Good luck.
