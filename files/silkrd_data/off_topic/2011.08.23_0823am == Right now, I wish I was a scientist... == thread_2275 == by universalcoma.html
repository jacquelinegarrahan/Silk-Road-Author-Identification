Title: Re: Right now, I wish I was a scientist...
Post by: wowzers on August 24, 2011, 05:15 pm
chemist friend here. it'll dissolve in ethanol. use 100%. get a 1mL syringe (with 0.1mL graduations on the side), dissolve your stuff up in 10 mL of ethanol. the resultant solution will have a concentration of 5mg/ mL. take some rice paper and draw accurately a grid with 4cm2 squares. now, add 0.1 ml to each square. this much liquid will saturate the square so add it dropwise and let it dry (absolute ethanol will disappear quickly under the heat of a lamp) between drops. each blotter square will have a dose of 500ug (prob not more than +/- 50ug if you're careful). whilst this won't be super accurate this is a lot safer than eyeballing sub mg doses.

one last thing- keep your solution in the fridge and sealed, if the ethanol evaporates obviously the concentration will increase and your next batch of blotters will have higher dosage.

Ethanol may not be necessary depending on what kind of 25i-NBOMe you have - is it a freebase or HCL salt? If you have an HCL salt then it is really water soluble at around 8mg/ml.

If you have the freebase, your best bet is 100% alcohol. Everclear should work if you cant get 100% alc.

To make blotters:
1) Measure out a proper liquid dose.
2) Squirt it on a piece of paper.
3) Let it dry.

The blotter will have to be fairly larger than your typical LSD blotter but it will work just the same. Hope that helps!

Title: Re: Right now, I wish I was a scientist...
Post by: Fred Flintstone on August 26, 2011, 05:38 am
Trying to eyeball a 0.7mg dose is asking for trouble. Powdered substances are not easy to divide and get accurate measurements, especially when you are dealing with sub mg doses. What looks like two even clumps of nbome could be way off - if some of the powder clumps together then two even piles could be very different in weight. Imagine two even looking piles of nbome next to eachother: one pile has a compressed clump in it and the other pile is fluffy - the fluffy one may look like more but could weigh half as much as the first pile.

Just dilute it in a solution for easy measurement or buy a mg scale - although I doubt a cheap $20 .001g scale would be sufficient for such small doses.
