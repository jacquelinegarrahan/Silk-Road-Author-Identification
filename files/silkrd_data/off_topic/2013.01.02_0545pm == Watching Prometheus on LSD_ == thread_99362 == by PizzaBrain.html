Title: Re: Watching Prometheus on LSD?
Post by: Rowsdower on January 02, 2013, 10:31 pm
I find I can watch just about anything on acid and have watched even gory horror films and stuff like that, so if you think you'd be into the movie when you're sober give it a shot on acid and you might like it even more albeit in a different way.  Some movies and genres are more enjoyable than others though for sure, and I wouldn't recommend horror movies although it does make for a different type of trip/mood which can be interesting in its own right.  Some of my favorite things to watch on LSD are really bad 80's and 70's dubbed kung fu and B movies, especially stuff directed by Godfrey Ho or movies like the American Ninja series.  Oh and the Room from 2003 starring, directed, written and produced by Tommy Wiseau.  If I watch those type of movies on acid I'll laugh my ass off so much it feels like I might die, but it's fun as hell and makes for a great time.  Acid makes anything visually trippy so the movie doesn't need to have high budget effects to be entertaining during a trip, in fact I find the lower budget older stuff strangely more endearing while tripping.

I haven't seen Prometheus but I agree with the dude who recommended watching 2001 : A Space Odyssey while tripping.  I saw that once during a trip and had an awesome time, it didn't even feel like a long movie when tripping and I found all kinds of weird levels of meaning in there that I never really thought about otherwise.  It actually felt like a deep experience although of course I'd seen the movie before that.  I think at the time we also put on some music by Giorgio Moroder and it went surprisingly well with it, but maybe we were just tripping really hard.  I remember how we thought we were geniuses for discovering how well it synced.  Blade Runner is another good one imo, once watched the first half hour or so during a trip but it freaked out or bummed out my friend for some reason so we turned it off.

I like to put on blacklights, blacklight posters and things that glow under blacklights when I'm about to trip if we're at my place, and I own stuff like lava lamps and psychedelic rugs that I will decorate my place with for a trip.  Any musical instrument can be pretty cool to play with, I have some analog synths I sometimes really like to play with when tripping.  Also, jello is fun to play with and handle while tripping, I like to make a batch beforehand and I also find it a tasty form of nutrition during the trip itself, so maybe buy some nice colors of jello.  I know it's weird for an adult to eat jello but don't knock it til you've tried it on acid.

Being outside listening to music and walking around is great especially during good weather and if you have interesting surroundings nearby or even a half decent park or forest.  I have friends though who say they get uncomfortable around other people if they're out in a public place on acid, but I don't have that problem, it could be because I've taken it so many times I can handle it somewhat or at least know what to expect from it.  My favorite way of tripping is to take some friends, weed and a music player to a trippy quiet nature spot on a nice day, and then spend a few hours listening to music outdoors before you go do whatever later on.
