Title: Re: Adderall vs Modifil
Post by: Coherence on August 05, 2011, 10:29 pm
I might be biased (full disclosure: I use modafinil every other day, and I sell some), but I think that they serve different purposes.

Modafinil removes feelings of tiredness, so if you're already motivated to get things done, but you are just too tired to bother, it might be useful.
If you're just being lazy, modafinil sure won't pick you up as well as adderall does. However, for pure sleep-fighting purposes, I consider modafinil to be a far superior and far cleaner alternative to amphetamines. Furthermore, it has been shown that sleep-deprivation on modafinil requires less sleep afterwards, i.e. you won't be as broken after an all-nighter on modafinil than after one on adderall or caffeine.
