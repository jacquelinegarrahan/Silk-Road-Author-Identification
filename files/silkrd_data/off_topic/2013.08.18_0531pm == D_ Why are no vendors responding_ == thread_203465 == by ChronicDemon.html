Hate to say it, but a lot of vendors won't answer your questions.

Did the vendor have a PGP key in their public profile? Did you use PGP when you contacted them? If you didn't, then that might be a reason why they have not contacted you back. A lot of vendors are strict on their communication and if someone does not use PGP they will simply discard your message.

A lot of vendors get swamped with orders, and they simply do not have time to answer your questions. Some vendors on here are strictly business and do not have time for small talk. You have to realize it might take multiple days before your package is put into transit if the vendor is busy. Perhaps they have fallen behind on orders and will get yours out as soon as they can.

Good luck with your orders!
