Post by: Mister Dank on April 05, 2012, 06:13 pm
Ship from a different Post Office EVERY time. LE will buy from you to see where you are shipping from - that's just common sense. They will also re-order from you and try to catch your package at the same post office before the DCN gets scanned. Your package will get lost and the LE buyer will leave you shitty feedback.

All the top vendors on here are cops who don't know shit about weed. I got in an argument with one in particular who swore that triple vacuum sealing was the right way and that the smashing that the weed went through after all that vacuum sealing didn't hurt the product - it does.

I package my weed in a hard case that is sealed THEN vac packed. They can't smell shit through it and the hard case helps conceal the weed from x-ray.

But MOST IMPORTANT: Keep changing post offices!!!! Try to use big city ones if you can - or just send FedEx overnight.
