Title: Oz's Shake -> Dry Ice Hash
Post by: Spedly on March 13, 2012, 06:24 pm
Today me and a friend made dry ice hash with two ounces of Green Crack shake that my friend bought from Oz. We got about 5 grams of grade "A" (160 micron bag) and 5 grams of grade "B"(220 micron bag). Not bad! I tried a little of the grade "A" afterwards and what a nice, level, floaty buzz I have. Tasted like a cross between weed and oil - no real "hash" taste to it but that's OK. Good stuff!

This was the first time I've ever made ice using the dry ice method. What a cool way to make hash. Very quick and very easy!

Anyone else done this? My friend and I are DEFINITELY going to be doing this again!!!
