Title: Music Animation Machine (videos designed to be watched on hallucinogens)
Post by: mamad on August 12, 2012, 07:30 pm
In the 1970s, Stephen Malinowski took LSD and had an experience while listening to music and watching the sheet music score that led to a project he called "The Music Animation Machine."  In 2005 he started posting his videos on YouTube:

    http://www.youtube.com/user/smalin/videos

Malinowski stopped taking hallucinogens before he started working on this project (and, last I heard, hadn't yet watched them under their influence), but the videos are nevertheless amazing to watch when you're high.

If you watch any of Malinowski's videos under the influence of hallucinogens, report your experiences here!
