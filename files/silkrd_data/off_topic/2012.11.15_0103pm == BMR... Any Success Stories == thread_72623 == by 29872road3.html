I have a seller account on BMR under the same name and have a lot of my products listed. I have had it up and running for 3 months and These are my findings.

#1) No where near the traffic. For every 200 items I sell on SR I mite sell 1 on BMR
#2) I get a few messages from people wanting a freebie to leave feedback, however BMR does not support setting up a freebie sale or a giveaway.
#3) It is not as well policed for scammers as SR is.
#4) The BMR board is as dead as BMR.
#5) Support is very responsive. I do get a fast response if I make a suggestion or ask how to do something.
#6) I like the escrow system, it has separate escrows for shipping charges and item charges. Which means you could have them release the shipping escrow once you ship the item then once it is received have them finalize the item charge escrow.
#7) I dont like they way they display the escrow. (SR is much better at that them BMR)
#8) I like the way the vendor can manage the items and the way it lists the items on the vendor page.
#9) I saw no pickup in traffic on BMR while SR was down so I am not sure people are or were switching but you never know.

All in all it has some good points and some bad points. I keep my listings there just in case and if I get a sale, great. However, I am not counting on it in my bottom line.
