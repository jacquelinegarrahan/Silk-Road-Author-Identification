Title: Re: First time buyer on SR
Post by: captainjojo on May 14, 2012, 08:19 pm
If you are a new buyer with less the a certain number of transactions or have a high refund rate the vendor will ask you to finalize early (FE) which means finalize the transaction before you get the goods or even before they will ship the goods.

Look around, you may find a vendor that will allow you to keep your order open until it arrives, but I wouldn't hold out too much hope if you are truly a new buyer.
