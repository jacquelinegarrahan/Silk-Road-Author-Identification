Post by: bp on July 10, 2011, 05:19 am
My first time was 30 years ago.
Took purple pyramid gel at break between 2nd and 3rd period in 9th grade.
By 4th period (just before lunch) I realized I wasn't going back to class after lunch.
Our bleachers looking over the (smallish) football field was where the stoners/smokers hung out at lunch.
I remember really tripping by this time and was seated towards the left (looking out at the field) and seeing the grass swirling in unpredictable patterns. I looked to the right, up at the top row and the person sitting there had a beam of ??? light? energy? whatever in colors and intensity coming down on him. Whoh!
As the (hour or so that seemed like forever) went on the dynamics would change. people would change seats, some guy trying to fuck with me was telling me that a dog that had wandered onto campus and laid down was dieing, and the seats kept changing......but every now and then i would look up at that uppermost right seat and who ever was in it was in that beam!
The seats kept changing and suddenly I realized that I was in the uppermost right seat! I looked up and the beam was ???????? coming down??????enveloping??????me.

The whole pattern of how the constant movement of people throughout the hour suddenly appeared to me and my being in that seat in that beam seemed natural, inevitable.

Best trip ever! Other than the dickhead about the dog, who got up and wandered off of course, it was.......innocent.

I had some really good innocent trips (sort of, staying out all night at 16 without permission) but later doses became hard to find.
I wanted them so bad it became an obsession. When I finally got them maybe I took too much.

I had a couple of really bad trips that didn't put me off but made me cautious about how much to take.
I later heard DMT takes you in so far so fast that maybe the fear can't set in and you come down way faster. I've been curious but still cautious.
The last time i tripped was a few years ago and it may have had some shit in it but it was way harder on the body than I recall as a kid. And I've done the meth for days thing too so I know how hard it is snap back from a busing yourself but this was hard. Maybe I'm to old for that shit these days.
