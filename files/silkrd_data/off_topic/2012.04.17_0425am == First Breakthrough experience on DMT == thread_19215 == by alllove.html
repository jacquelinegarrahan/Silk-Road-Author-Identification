Title: Re: First Breakthrough experience on DMT
Post by: Edawg420 on June 13, 2012, 07:57 pm
Just saw this post and I had to make a reply.  I was introduced to DMT about 2-3 months ago here on SR.  I have done my research on DMT through erowid, and have watched that DMT Documentary on Netflix.  IDK why but I had a huge boner for psychedelics so I decided to make a DMT purchase.

I have since then made MANY DMT purchases from SR and I probably won’t waste much time with other psychedelics anymore.  DMT has hands down the most amazing, spiritual, Zen, calming, and meaningful trips I have ever experienced.  The first purchase was changa and this was extremely easy to smoke and has a STRONG psychedelic trip.

I just packed a dry bong up with the changa and gently lit the herb.  Remember, DMT vaporizes at a very low temperature, so less fire is better.  I slowly chalked it up, ever so gently pulling on the tube.  I did this 2 times as quickly as I could.  I like to think of smoking DMT as a 30 second window, you have 30 seconds to smoke as gently and as fast as you can, while holding in the smoke for at least 4 seconds.

Soon as I put the bong down I started doing what I call “Phasing".  It’s like I am this big instrumental gong and someone comes buy and strikes my center with the mallet causing an enormous ringing/vibration.  These vibrations emit from my center EXTREMELY fast, yet with a warm glow like feeling.  Anyways, I was kinda freaked out at first so I started fighting the trance like state by opening my eyes.  This was when I remembered that I was looking to enjoy this and I couldn't help but to flash back to all the other times I had tripped on mushrooms or acid.  Then, all of a sudden, this knowledge comes over me and I knew that all those experiences in the past were just preparing me for this moment.  So tripping balls, I re-close my eyes.

Upon doing so I saw the most beautiful entity of all.  It was definitely female or at least she had that presence of it.  She had six arms and was swaying to the music I had chosen for myself (Animus Vox - Glitch Mob).  She was sitting cross legged over a stone slab and on the slab was the most intricate lines that where changing constantly.  As I sat there I had MANY thoughts RAPIDLY shoot through my brain.  I was thankful for the music because it was almost a reference to my reality/time.

After enjoying her dance and me trying so hard to understand what it is I am seeing, I knew I was starting to come down.  Longing to stay with the entity longer I just laid there with my eyes closed, literally laughing out loud and thanking her for such an amazing experience.  This was my first experience with DMT. 

There are a lot of visuals I can't really describe, because they go extremely fast.  It seems we only remember the last little bit of it because like someone posted earlier, it is almost like our body treats the trip as a dream.

This experience is not a breakthrough, although I felt it was when I did it, it really carried no meaning to me.  It wasn't until my 4-5th time I had a breakthrough and let’s just say I had a conversation with an inner entity through thoughts alone, meanwhile he was showing me shapes and designs that would appear from his hands in thin air.  This was all with my eyes closed (friends told me) and I would have sworn they were open because I was THERE.

I am sure I sound crazy, but I can assure you I am not.  I am about to graduate University with a bachelor's in CIS and I consider myself a very down to earth person.  In fact before my experiences with DMT I was Agnostic if not an Atheist.  Soon after my experiences, I knew something was changing inside me.  Every time I came back I knew that this place I perceived was something more than just a hallucination.  It’s almost like that movie Inception, once I was awoken to this dimension, I have desired nothing more than to return again.

I keep meaning to type up my next couple trips but it seems the first one is the most vivid in my head.  I think it is because the changa had MAIO's, which allows for a longer, more memorable trip.

Final Thoughts

All in all DMT is EXTREMELY powerful stuff.  Even in the smallest doses, it can be the strongest trip you will ever experience.  Although it is overpowering, it can be a very beautiful and spiritual adventure if you just let go and accept you have no control.  I always look for an entity, there is always one somewhere, sometimes even hiding, but they are there to guide you through and show you the most beautiful things.  I have never had a bad trip but I think you honestly have to be an evil hearted person to experience one.  DMT shows you what you need to be shown, that's at least my take on it.

Well I have wasted enough time re writing my 1st trip report LMAO, none the less I hope you all enjoyed my experience.  If anybody has any questions please PM me.  I LOVE to share in people’s thoughts and experiences.
