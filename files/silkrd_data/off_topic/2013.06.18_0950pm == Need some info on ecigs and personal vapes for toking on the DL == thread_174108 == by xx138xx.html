Post by: Drippy on June 19, 2013, 10:00 pm
There are plenty of options out there for this, but it all depends on how much you're going to spend and what you're going to be filling it w/.  If you have access to BHO, I'd suggest one of the following (in order from most to least expensive).

- Thermovape's new line called Cera.  There's a pretty much bomb-proof, and the best portable vaporizer I've ever used by miles.  They have carts available for concentrates, tincture, and flower that you can purchase separately - you get one of the 3 with your first order.

- If you need maximum portability, either an Atmos Raw or G-Pen mini will do the trick.  These are tiny, all-in-one solutions that won't give you any hassle, but also won't give you many options.

- If you want to save some money and get what I think is a better setup than an Atmos/G-Pen, e-mail Thermovape and ask if they still have the parts to build you a LV (low voltage) Revolution cartridge for concentrates.  They ran out of the properly branded parts, so it will say the wrong model but functions the same as the ones they used to sell.  You'll have to choose between Dart or Window style for loading the cart.  Dart splits in half w/ a dabber on the mouthpiece end and open chamber leading down to the ceramic element on the battery end, while window is a two layer stainless steel outer sleeve that rotates to reveal a window above the ceramic element.  I prefer the Dart, but it's all personal preference.  This will cost about $50, and it can by screwed onto any e-cig batter that has a 510 threading - which is pretty much all of them.

If you don't have access to concentrates, your best bet is probably to just buy an ego twist or vision variable voltage battery (these both are variable voltage, and have ego as well as 510 threadings - both the main standards) and purchase pre-filled carts on SR.  I wouldn't normally suggest pre-filled, but IMO most of the oil on SR looks like rubbish and it wouldn't make much of a difference which you went with.
