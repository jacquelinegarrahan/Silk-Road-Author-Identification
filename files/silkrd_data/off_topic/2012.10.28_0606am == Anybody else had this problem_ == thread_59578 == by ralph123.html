Title: Re: Anybody else had this problem?
Post by: CaliTrees on October 28, 2012, 07:46 am
SR is just a bitcoin wallet.  It is not like mtgox and it does NOT convert your bitcoins to usd.  It does have a feature that will show you the value of your bitcoins in USD though.  That is why the USD will change but the amount of btc in your wallet does not change.

When you make a purchase, depending on the sellers settings, the coins may be hedged.  That means the transaction will be tied to the USD (minus 4%) and at the time of finalize the amount of bitcoins the seller receives will be based on the bitcoin rate at the time of finalize as opposed to at the time of the order being placed.
