Flight between countries can be found for extremely cheap prices through Ryan Air. It's a no thrills type airline, but great value IMO. I remember flying from London to Ireland for 4 quid + 10 Quid tax. Hostels are a good place to stay as it's a great way to meet others who can point you in the right direction about, for example, best places to eat at and best pubs/nightclubs in town, etc. There are websites such as www.lastminute.com (cheap flights, hotels, etc) which offer great deals also.
