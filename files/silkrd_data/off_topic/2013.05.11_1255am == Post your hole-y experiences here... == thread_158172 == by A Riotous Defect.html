Title: Post your hole-y experiences here...
Post by: A Riotous Defect on May 11, 2013, 12:55 am
There's a thread for for other drug experiences, let's see what crazy shit comes out of the rabbit hole....


I got a gram of MXE a month or so ago and spent the following week using it off and on for its pain relieving and anti-depressant qualities ( I shattered my ankle in a car wreck and painkillers only serve to dull the pain, making walking still bothersome ) and after about two weeks of maybe using little key bumps every now and then to keep me spry I was down to my last point and I wanted to make it a good one.

I took the point and waited a couple weeks to lower my tolerance, and then blew the point and waited for *it* to happen.

I remember being upstairs at my girlfriend's apartment, she was sleeping 'cause she had an 8 A.M. class in the morning and actually gives a shit about school so I was letting her sleep downstairs.

The come-up was gradual, not staggered like I normally feel when I use K - for me, K feels like a roller-coaster while MXE feels more like a smooth drive down a highway. The pain in my foot went away as I was playing Skyrim and I sort of phased into my character, the computer becoming an extension of my body, and I really felt a part of the game, just going around dungeon crawling and such. That lasted for about five or six minutes, and then I could tell something big was going to happen.

I wanted to hole while holding my girlfriend for some reason, so I get up off the chair and immediately fall to my feet and forget how to walk for a bit. I thought about the muscles in my leg and how they move against one another to make motion and thinking of the physics of human motion reminded me how to walk.

Next came the stairs.

FUCK.

I've been on K a few times, and had a string of DXM induced experiences when I was younger, and stairs have always been my deepest, darkest enemy. If you had saw me go down these stairs you probably would have thought I was some drugged up robotic ballerina trying to tiptoe down them. I had to stop several times because I was afraid I was going to fall.

I got down, shambled to the bed my girlfriend sleeping in and she had it on Comedy Central and it was an episode of the Colbert Report where they were talking about Bitcoin and I remember thinking for some reason this was meant to be seen by me. All I could hear before I laid back and succumbed to the hole was "DON'T BUY BITCOIN, DON'T BUY BITCOIN, DON'T BUY BITCOIN' and I closed my eyes and was inside this bleak stark white room with Colbert and the economist he was talking to about bitcoin. They were talking to each other but their words were coming out like "ra ra ra ra ? ra ra ra ra ra? ra ra ra ra RA ra RA?" but I could understand what they were saying perfectly. Then the world went dark and I couldn't see anything, but I existed, in this formless black void and I remember thinking, "Fuck, so this is what death is like. This is going to be boring as hell." My consciousness floated around for a while until I saw myself laying next to my girlfriend limp and lifeless, her still asleep and my inner self starts freaking out, like, "You've done it now! You're dead!" and then the void starts spinning and it's like I'm a tornado tunnelling into myself again.

Then I opened my eyes, stumbled to the kitchen and fixed myself a glass of orange juice and wrote down everything that had happened so I wouldn't forget it.


Anyway, I love weird experiences, and as much as I love to trip balls dissociatives most assuredly provide the weirdest experiences I've ever had. It was like embracing Thanatos and then casually letting him fuck me in the ass, with a reach-around of course.

Hope you folks have some awesome experiences to share too :]
