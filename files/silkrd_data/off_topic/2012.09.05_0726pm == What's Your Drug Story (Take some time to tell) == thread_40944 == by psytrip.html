It was an unseasonably warm night December 13,19XX and I was preparing for a short outing before going on vacation to visit family in XXXXXX. A friend of mine picked up some pot from his uncle, and we decided to smoke it. This started a once per week smoking session that quickly escalated to daily after school special. Not quite 6 months later, a different friend brought over some shrooms. He was afraid to try them, but not wretched. I ate that entire bag of shrooms, and tripped nuts for the first time on a Tuesday night while finishing a school assignment. I got very unique comments written on that assignment from the teacher the next day.  Anyway, the daily pot smoking continued, and shrooms were added to my schedule twice per month. This continued for about 4 months before Lucy joined the party. LSD became my Thursday night routine. I would eat 2 tabs of acid on the school bus ride home, and trip till the wee hours. Made for interesting Friday mornings in homeroom. The shrooms were passed on to a friend who was just beginning to get interested in getting higher than pot (Disclaimer: Marijuana is NOT a gateway drug, it just happens to be more readily available to individuals who would have taken other drugs had they been around, and the previous statement should NOT be used to further the bullshit propaganda campaign of "gateway" drugs). As the LSD remained regular, the pot died down for me. it started causing some paranoid feelings that I won't get too specific about to a group of robots today.

This continued on like this until I met Molly. She mecame my life. Three times per week we would dance, then two weekends per month, I would cheat on her with Lucy. Of course you cannot sustain that level of use of Molly, but I was a dumb kid then and nothing anyone said mattered. I put her down entirely after about 6 months of that rollercoaster, and returned to the land of pot and LSD. The LSD supply then dried up in my area, and was replaced by prescription opiates. I don't have any regrets in my life, but the closest thing I do have to a regret is that epic struggle between good and evil with the opiates (closest I have come to being addicted to anything save nicotine) The opiate battle raged on for the remainder of my high school years, until one fateful night, there was a mishandling of an order that went down between a fried dealer and a completely drunk wretched. Pills were mixed up, and too many were taken. Three days of my life I will never get back or even remember.

Alcohol and LSD it was now. And that is the way it remained for years, with the acid supply waxing and waning with the changing seasons. I prefer to trip during long winter nights, but the supply is at it's best during the long hot days of summer. Then one day, My good friend Bourbon kicked the living shit out of me and almost left me for dead (car crash). That was when I realized, I only had one lady that I needed in my life and that lady was Lucy. Yes lately I have ignored her for some shrooms, and 4-aco compounds with occasional visits from Molly, but by in large, Lucy has my heart, and I know she will never leave me.

I left out a lot about cocain, meth, heroin, and ritalin. I tried coke, meth, heroin...not my thing. Ritalin WAS snorted by the truckload though, but I never REALLY enjoyed it, but it was a cheap (free) high any time I was in a drought.

I'm sure I left alot out, but you should get the basic idea of my drug use history. As for identifying information, if my parents are on this forum, they could probably recognize this story, so hi mom, hi dad!
