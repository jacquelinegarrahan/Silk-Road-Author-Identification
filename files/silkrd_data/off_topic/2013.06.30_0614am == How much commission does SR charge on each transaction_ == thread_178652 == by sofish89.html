150 - 300 -> 6%
300 - 500 -> 3%
500 - 1000 -> 2%

Title: Re: How much commission does SR charge on each transaction?
Post by: astor on July 01, 2013, 12:44 am
Keep in mind that if the product costs $155, the commission isn't 6%.  It's

$50   * 0.1     = $5
$100 * 0.085 = $8.50
$5     * 0.06   = $0.30

For a total of $13.80, which would be 8.9% in this case.
