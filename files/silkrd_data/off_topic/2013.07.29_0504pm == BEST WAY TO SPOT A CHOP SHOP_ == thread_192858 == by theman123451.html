There are lots of nooks and crannts in vehicles. Just spend an hour checking out your own vehicle and look for places you can easily stash anything that needs stashing.

Asking a chop shop carries risks as many are known by the cops and there might be a relationship there.

D.I.Y.

There are plenty of books and videos on how to find a stash in the vehicle.

The bigger smugglers, such as those running the Mex/USA border via vehicle, use sophisticated lock mechanisms to open up stashes, usually in the boot. Cops are onto that but there are too many cars to check so the cartels accept a loss using some disposable to drive through a few kilos at a time.
