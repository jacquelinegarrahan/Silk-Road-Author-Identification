Title: Re: Adderall Resistance?
Post by: Fred Flintstone on March 04, 2012, 08:36 pm
For someone who seldom takes adderall, a 50mg dose should cause quite a bit of euphoria. As Shredder mentioned, your post reads like it was written on adderall - so I bet you are feeling something. I am not a big fan of adderall but have still taken it quite a lot for school and work. Used to take upwards of 200mg a day during finals. Adderall does not feel the same every time. Some days, 50mg may get you extremely high while other days you feel very little. Perhaps it was just one of those days where 50mg did not hit you very hard.

Another possibility is the "honeymoon" period is wearing off. The first X number of times you take a drug, it is new and different so your body doesn't know what to expect. It is usually those first X number of times taking a drug where that drug is the best it will ever be. Once your body has kind of gotten used to the effects it just isn't the same. This is not the same as physical tolerance although they are related. Tolerance is probably not an issue seeing as you haven't taken any in a week and a half.
