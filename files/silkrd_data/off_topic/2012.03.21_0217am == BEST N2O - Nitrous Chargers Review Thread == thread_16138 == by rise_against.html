The iSi brand that we get here in australia is far from medical grade...
While it is true that not not all food grade nitrous was created equal, that doesn't mean that the high will be "better" than with a different brand.
They all contain the same amount of nitrous.

Some of them have small (I mean miniscule) amounts of oils that are used in the process of compressing the nitrous into those tiny canisters.
These oils build up on the inside of the dispenser when lots of nitrous is passed through it and probably on the inside of your lungs when you inhale... you should consider making a filtration system.
