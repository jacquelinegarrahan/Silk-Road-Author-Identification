Post by: le botbahn on September 02, 2012, 01:43 am
Mix it with polyethyline glycol or vegetable glycerine (double check on that one). You can find it in the laxative section of the.bb pharmacy.

Mixing it in a small amount of alcohol wont me you.drunk if thats your concern.

An alternative option is to mix it in alcohol at a predetermined concentration per mL. Using a syringe, measure out your desired dose and slowly drip that volume on to homemade blotter paper (any paper or tissue works). The alcohol evaporates, leaving the alprazolam.embedded in the paper matrix. Now you have portable tabs (like acid blotter). The same concept could be applied to candy smarties or other items (use your imagination).

If someone can share their knowledge on alprazolam solubilities I'd really appreciate your input!

But just an example, take 100 mL alcohol. Add to it 100 MILIgrams alprazolam powder. Thoroughly mix. Now you have 1 miligram alprazolam per 1 mL (cc) alcohol.
 
To make.a 1 mg Alprazolam paper tab, simply apply 1 mL alcohol/alpraz. solution and let it dry. It may require applying 0.5 ml, letting it dry, then adding another 0.5 ml depending on the size of tab.

I.d like to know the solubility of alprazolam because if you can make the solution more concentrated the process becomes easier.
But the above 1mg/ml is a good starting point.

I wouldnt trust your scale to weigh out single mg doses unless you paid 10,000$ for it.

Any any event, be sure to understand what you are.doing before doing it and.seek help if any doubt. Feel free to pm.me.any.time if you need help :)

Post by: mercurysolid on September 11, 2012, 01:01 pm
It's quite difficult to dose capsules using the powder because measuring precisely 1 or 2 milligrams requires expensive equipment with very high precision, and is time consuming.
Using lower precision equipment - hence less costly - one sensible method would be make a concentrated solution in a solvent like dichloromethane, but ensure it is a known concentration, say 20mg /mL. You could then use an insulin syringe to deliver 10 units of solution into each capsule. Allow the dichloromethane to evaporate before sealing the capsules and you have 2mg capsules of alprazolam.

The group I experiment which found that the most consistent effect with alprazolam powder sourced on silk road was to place the powder directly in the mouth and wait a few minutes for absorption. It doesn't taste terribly nice, but it's nothing compared to san pedro cactus soup or laudanum. A couple of minutes after administering this way sipping milk or a gently fortified wine seemed to assist onset.
  Swallowing powder wrapped in paper had variable effects from person to person, and for any given person from occasion to occasion. It was generally accepted by the group to be less efficient than simply putting the power directly into the mouth with no real focus on swallowing it. I suspect the varying conditions of the stomach in response to the time of day, previous food eaten, and current acidity affects alprazolam's efficacy if it's being swallowed, but I say this acknowledging that I'm not sufficiently familiar with the molecule's properties to suggest a mechanism for this, or produce evidence to support it being true. It's merely a consensus observation amongst my fellow travelers.

According to the person most familiar with alprazolam, dissolving it in warm vodka and consuming 3mg of alprazolam in solution gave sensations and onset very closely mirroring having taken 3 mg in pill form.
