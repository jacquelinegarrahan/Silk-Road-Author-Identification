Title: Re: Bonnaroo
Post by: reg on May 27, 2012, 10:56 pm
And a few tips:

- During the first day a lot of sellers will walk around the camps and offer stuff - from my experience these guys are pretty legit - just make sure you taste the product if it's coke, molly, etc..

- The majority of drugs are sold outside of center Roo (where the stages are). A lot of dealers will post up by the porta potty's and on the walkway - this is your best chance of finding something.  This is where you should head when the sun goes down and there are big shows about to start. Stay away from people offering crazy good deals as it's prolly junk or fake.

- You'll get searched before going into center Roo.  You can take backpacks and usually they will just glance inside them.  When I went I just put my drugs in my wallet.  Just observe the lines and see which searcher is the quickest and just letting people through.

- You will also get searched before going into Bonnaroo - it's pretty easy to get stuff through though.  I just bought a hollowed out salt container and put everything in there.  But just make sure stuff is hidden.

Good luck!
