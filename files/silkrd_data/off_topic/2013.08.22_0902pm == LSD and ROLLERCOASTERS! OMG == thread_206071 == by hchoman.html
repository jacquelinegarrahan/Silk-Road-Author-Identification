Title: LSD and ROLLERCOASTERS! OMG
Post by: hchoman on August 22, 2013, 09:02 pm
A couple days ago I traveled to the most popular amusement park east of the mississippi. I love riding rollercoasters and have my whole life and have been to this particular place many times. One of my bucket list items was to combine a day of thrill rides and LSD. The morning of, I retrieved my stash of blotters from various vendors and knowing how important dosage and quality would be for such a wild day. Seeing as how I would be with a group of people (some family) I needed just enough for a good time, but still able to function without detection. After careful consideration, I decided to go with 1 approx 130ug White Lightning tab from TheDrugFederation, as I was very pleased with my previous experiences with this product.
So I dropped about 11am and entered the park around noon. The come up was a little uncomfortable as I am surrounded by many loud noises and lots of people. I was the only one in my group to not ride the first coaster, I just told them I needed more time for my lunch to settle but I just wanted the L to settle in first because I had one of those "what did I do" moments. As they rode I settled my breathing and did my best to relax.
Off to ride #2, still feeling a tad uneasy I decided to give it a go as this ride was not real intense. It was amazing! From there it was all pure bliss. We moved on to other rides and every single one I had the feeling I was flying. Words cannot describe what going up 200 feet, straight down, multiple inversions and pure coaster speed feels like on LSD! We rode lots of rides that day, even the new one and stayed til the park closed at 9pm. I was still feeling the effects when we left pretty good for 9 hours in. After a couple hour ride home I was still not back to baseline, but was exhausted. I took a 10mg Valium and a nice hot shower and went to bed, awoke the next day feeling like a million bucks.
This day will definitely be one to remember. This isn't for the weak or faint of heart, I would only suggest experienced trippers try this. Like I said earlier, know your body and how it reacts to different doses because I would figure too strong of a dose would be too much to handle. Sunglasses helped hide my dilated pupils and having my iPod helped for waiting in lines which was the worst part of the day.
Cannot wait to do this again!
