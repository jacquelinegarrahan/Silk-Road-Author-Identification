That said, mining pools do exist.  However, the work factor has now become so large for bitcoin mining that it is literally out of reach of the individual computer user.  In order to mine coins effectively, you require expensive hardware like an FPGA mining rig.  Bitcoin mining by individuals was possible in the very early days, but this is no longer the case, at least with the stock computer hardware that most people have.
