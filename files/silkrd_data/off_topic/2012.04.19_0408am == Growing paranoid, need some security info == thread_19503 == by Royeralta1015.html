Have no record of Tor anywhere on it. If you still want to use the Silk Road, have a friend let you use his laptop. And obviously clear your house of any and all drugs and/or drug paraphernalia. You could technically make the claim that someone shipped something to your house as a cruel joke. If the package does show up, you throw it away somewhere that is nowhere near your house. I suggest driving at least an hour in one direction and throwing the whole package in a giant dumpster, although I doubt it will arrive at this point. Take a friend's car to that location and wear a hoodie that covers most of your face. The important thing is to cover your trail everywhere you go. I hate to say it, but you may want to destory your computer entirely and take every piece and do the same thing I suggested with the package. Take the pieces somewhere far, far away in a vehicle that has nothing to do with you and throw them away. Burn them if possible. Clear yourself of any and all evidence.
