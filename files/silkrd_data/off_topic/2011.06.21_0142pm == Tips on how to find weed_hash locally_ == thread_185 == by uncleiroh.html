Title: Re: Tips on how to find weed/hash locally?
Post by: tofuu on July 10, 2011, 02:07 pm
Actually it couldn't be easier....I've had to move with my job over the years and before SR I always had to find locally.  Simply go to the local Craigslist and post an ad in the Rant section or something.  Basically you need to tell the story you're looking for someone to help supply you with personal, small amounts of 420 for medical purposes.  It helps with my back pain and it may do something for you like this also.  Short and quick, but new to the area and looking for someone who might be able to help you to relieve some of the pain that 420 helps to heal.  I hate to say it this way, but you have to play up the sympathy  angle.

Use a new email address and screen the responses pretty good - offer to smoke with them first, but I guarantee you will get numerous replies - some hate against drugs in general, a few that scream at you for being LE, but always a couple thrown in from genuine caring people looking to help someone in pain.  I have done this countless times in countless cities and it always works.  I usually try to pick out the most "normal" of the bunch and become a regular customer.

Because you're initially looking for a smaller quantity and not hard drugs, and you've made the story it's for medical reasons to help with pain , LE will leave something like that alone....
