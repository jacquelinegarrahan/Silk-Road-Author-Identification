Title: Re: Estimating Silk Road Volume
Post by: a_blackbird on March 04, 2012, 09:33 pm
Place an order.  Record the order number.  Wait a week.  Order something else.  Record the order number.  Compute the difference and divide by 7.

Obviously, this does not account for cancellations / refunds, and it makes no distinction between a 0.01 BTC order and a 100 BTC order, but it'll tell you roughly how many transactions are occurring on a daily basis.
