Title: Re: How to take MDMA
Post by: beergut100 on July 16, 2011, 11:07 am
I always do it orally and never snort and find that an 8th (125mg) of a gram is a decent dose for starters and about the same as a strongish pill. I will often "top-up" during the night, but nowadays try never to go over 1/4 gram in one night ( I once did a full gram in a night and it was far too much for me and stopped me from peeing for 2 days!). Personally, I just eat it washed down with a drink, but I have put it in capsules in the past or dissolved in drinks. Takes a little longer to work in a capsule, though, and I feel it isn't usually really worth the effort. If you want to have a few handy 'doses' (useful at a festival, for instance) and be sure of not accidentally having too much, then gelcaps can be a good idea. It will be just as strong no matter how you do it. To fill the capsules, use a tiny funnel if you can find one or a sharply creased square of paper. If filling caps, I generally get a sponge scourer and make a cut in it to hold the cap. This gives you two free hands for the fiddly business of filling it.
