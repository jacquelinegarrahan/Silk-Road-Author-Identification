Title: Re: Best way to preserve MDMA?
Post by: Ballzinator on January 13, 2013, 10:52 pm
Quote from: TK1991 on January 13, 2013, 10:36 pm
Hey Ballz (and rest of thread) what is the storage protocol for storing ketamine and dmt?

I just keep everything in my fridge but I was curious if there were any substances that don't like the cold?
I don't know about ketamine but I store my MXE in a simple baggie in a dark place (drawer). DMT is pretty stable from what I've read, especially in salt form. Freebase I'd store in an air-tight container in a fridge.
I've never heard of any drug that is damaged by low temperatures but if you do store them in a fridge, do so in an air-tight, waterproof container. The reason behind this is that when you take them out of the fridge, the cold drugs will attract lots of moisture from the air so leaving them in a waterproof container until they've warmed up is a good idea.
