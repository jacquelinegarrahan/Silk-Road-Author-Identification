if you have trouble keeping a good ratio on IPT, most other sites are not going to be good for you as IPT is one of the easiest to seed on. just a tip with getting a good ratio quickly, download the most popular stuff( TV shows, Movies) as soon as they are uploaded so that you can upload to everyone else who wants to see it.

general/0-days means it has all sorts of things on there that are uploaded to the site the day they are released. it means it has tv shows, movies, music, programs, etc. as opposed to a site like what.cd that is only music, or BTN which is only TV. now, p2p means that the tracker has both scene releases and p2p releases. scene releases are releases from scene groups that have to hold to a specific set of rules created by that scene. scene releases are originally uploaded to "top sites" which are ftp servers( usually a minimum 10tb with no maximum size. i've been on some that were 15tb and some that were close to a petabyte because of their archive) and from there, people with access to those servers will upload them onto torrent sites. this behavior is strictly frowned upon by the actual groups that are involved in the scene. now, p2p releases are release from normal people, an example of a p2p release would be anything from axxo, or imagine( who was recently busted btw). p2p groups are not involved with "top sites",  and do not have to adhere to the same rules as scene groups. if you want more info about the scene and that stuff, i suggest you read articles from torrentfreak.
