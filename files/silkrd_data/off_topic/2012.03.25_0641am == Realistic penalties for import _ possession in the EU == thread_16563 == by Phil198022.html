Just in case - which I really don't hope - a package gets caught up in customs and you are charged with import of say some amount like 3 grams of Cocaine (just an arbitrary example), or 50 pills of mdma or whatever, so basically an amount that can be realistically considered for your own consumption, but that isn't negligible either, in which case the law suit would probably be dropped alll together, what are the realistic penalties one can expect in the EU if you have no prior convictions, above 21 years old? Of course in the case of customs you could always refuse to accept the letter and say you didn't order whatever it is and know nothing about it.

Still, in the case of possession are we talking about a financial penalty, perhaps some social hours, psychiatric evaluation etc. OR a short jail sentence? After all, in addition to possession in no small amount (like 20 consumption units or so) you have gone against import laws, which I assume aggravates the crime in the eyes of the state leaders and judicial system. Anybody have any idea what is approximately to be expected in the rather unlikely case one is apprehended with importing by mail say 3 grams of coke, or meth, or any other drug below the "night geringe Menge" ("no small amount" = amounts that exceed the amounts listed in the table below) at which point one would probably go to jail I guess?
