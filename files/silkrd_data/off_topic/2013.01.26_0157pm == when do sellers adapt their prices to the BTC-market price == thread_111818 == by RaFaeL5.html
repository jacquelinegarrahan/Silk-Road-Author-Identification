Title: Re: when do sellers adapt their prices to the BTC-market price
Post by: acider on January 26, 2013, 02:18 pm
When they create a listing they fix the price to USD. It's not mandatory but there are serious reasons to do it, and still haven't noticed anyone who doesn't.

Also the hedging option is not only for the sellers, in case you get a refund it will be in USD and not the exact amount of bitcoins you spent.

Hedging you SR wallet is not possible, who would pay your loss when btc price falls? SR? Of course not. It's the nature of the coin, if you don't like the risks buy as much coins as you want to spend here, and order asap.
