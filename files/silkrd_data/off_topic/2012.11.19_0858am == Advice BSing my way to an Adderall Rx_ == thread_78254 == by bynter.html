Title: Re: Advice BSing my way to an Adderall Rx?
Post by: asdfsquared on November 20, 2012, 04:41 am
Wait till you're 18 then go to your family practitioner. Your parents can't get your medical records from the doc once you're 18, and they wouldn't be able to find out. Honestly, even if you're not 18, it's unlikely they will find out. Why would they have any reason to ask your doctor?

The longer a doctor has known you, the more likely they are to prescribe you controlled substances. Just tell them you have a really hard time focusing on things, and a friend recommended adderall. Say that you did your research on the ups and downs of it and other medications and would like to try it. Be up front about it, or the doc will be actually more suspicious, and also more likely to prescribe something like strattera.

I'm not going to give you any more advice, since I personally don't agree with taking adderall (even for those with severe ADHD) at all in childhood or adolescence.
