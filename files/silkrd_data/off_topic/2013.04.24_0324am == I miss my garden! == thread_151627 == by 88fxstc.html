Title: Re: I miss my garden!
Post by: northstars on April 24, 2013, 04:39 am
Uh, growers here. 

We made our own strain from Blueberry and OG Kush seeds. It's been four generations from seed now.
So we called it kushberry when people ask.   Some purple leaves and its large producer.  The stink is just stupid.

Before this we had white rhino and early girl.  They were great, but tiny little dwarf plants with rock hard nuggets.
We did hose outdoors too, but had mold problems in the thick buds and it spread fast.

We are excited to try out a few new strains for sale here on the road.  That Barney's farm LSD strain is just killer.
I bought the red diesel from Mrouid Canada vendor, it was very unique.

Our indoor setup is two large rooms , veg and flower. We got a work area for trimming and smoking, mixing up food. Our canister air filters work awesome. Not a smell in or outside.
We cut clones once a month and keep a cycle in three stages of growth. So there is always a crop to cut each month or so.

Trimming is the part you must remember hating?
I dread it each month.  It's not something you can hire people for really.
We get through it by taking breaks to clean the hash off the scissors and fingers to smoke it. It's always deadly when it's fresh resin like that. wet and gooey for hot knives.

You should do a garden outside.  It's easy and fun summer project.  Very rewarding.
