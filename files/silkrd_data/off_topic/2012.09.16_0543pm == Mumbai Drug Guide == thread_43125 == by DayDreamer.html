I thought I'd post a small guide to drugs in Bombay for such people. This is mostly based on personal experience, and may not be applicable in all cases.
Any suggestions / corrections are welcome.

BOMBAY DRUG GUIDE

WEED
Weed is easily available. Not good quality compared to what i hear of purple haze, skunk and the likes.
Street Price : 4-7 USD /ounce or so in the city.There are many places outside the city where you get it at 1/3rd the price. This is for personal quantities. Bulk is even cheaper.
Sources : MANY small dealers scatterred all over the city, mostly in obscure slums.
While scoring weed, keep in mind that the dealers pay the LEOs a monthly bribe in return for permission to operate. your chances of getting caught in the vicinity of source are nearly 0, although not ruled out completely. always keep an eye out and be aware. Ignore the people staring at you. They know why you are there, they know what goes on where you're headed. They will not bother you. You are a paying customer, and they are poor people. And they want you to come back again.

Consumption:
You have to be careful where you smoke. Smoking while driving through the city in a car is OK 99% of the time. You have to watch out for roadblocks, where they mostly check for DUI. They do so by sticking their head in the car to smell your breath, so if you have been smoking, they will smell the weed instead. Take appropriate safety measures / precautions, and you will be absolutely fine.
Smoking outdoors is very dependant on where you are, what time of day it is.. etc, but in general, LEOs are not specifically on the lookout for people smoking joints during the day.

Outside the city and on national highways, no one gives a fuck. The only times you will have to be careful is while crossing the state borders, especially on the way to goa.

What to do if busted :
In the city, you will have to wiggle your way out by paying a bribe. They will not put you in jail for smoking a joint, even if they threaten to, which they will at first.
In the end you will need to negotiate the amount to pay as bribe, but the subject needs to be brought up by YOU, not the cop, and not very directly. From experience, if caught with one joint, you will end up coughing up about 100  USD equivalent in rupees in cash, if you are an Indian, and about thrice as much if you are a white foreigner. This is usually settled on the spot, however it is not uncommon to be asked to go to the police station to "negotiate". Most police stations have no CCTVs. Lower level LEOs in bombay are underpaid. They WILL take bribes.

HASHISH
Three varietes : bombay black,  kashmiri , manali in order of their potency / desirability / price.Do not smoke bombay black. It's not worth it. Manali is available for betwenn 40 - 70 USD. the dealers operate by taking orders on their mobile phones. They will deliver to an area of your choice. Relatively safe. You can only get in touch with them through friends who already know them. Getting during consumption caught carries same risks as with weed and the same precautions apply.


COCAINE
Very easily available, considering the mumbai Police does not like the drug.
They assume that you have to be really rich to be doing charlie.

Quality : highly variable, even from the same dealer and for the same price. The cheaper one is always cut.
Street Price : 50-130 USD/gram

What to do if busted :
Option 1 - Do not get busted. Getting out of this will be difficult and very expensive. The best precaution is to keep your transaction with the dealer as short as possible, in a public place.
You will need to keep an eye out for police vehicles. Individual LEOs on foot will not be any trouble.

Consume indoors only.
Scoring late at night ( when it is actually needed!) is very risky and i would not advise it. Pre planned purchases are safest.

Option 2 - If you do get busted, follow the same procedure as with weed, but it will be much harder. Good luck.


MDMA
Quite easily available, good quality every single time i've bought it.
The dealers of drugs such as MDMA, LSD, etc are usually well educated, well informed decent looking typical working class people.
The best way to deal with them is to meet up as buddies over a smoke, have a chat, assess the situation, then do the exchange, say thanks and be on your way.

Do not carry MDMA into a club. Have it just before you go in. In fact make sure you have nothing illegal on you whenever you are inside a club.

Street Price : 80-120 USD /gram

LSD
Verly low availability in Mumbai. Very easily available in goa.

Quality : Highly unpredictable. The strength is rarely known,even to the dealer, as the acid changes quite a few hands before it hits the street.
mostly available as blots.

Street Price : 20-25 USD / blot. Very expensive imho.

Remember - Keep the transactions with dealers as short as possible, in a public place,during usual hours, surrounded by people doing usual things. keep an eye out for brown coloured uniforms.
Consume in a safe place. within the city do not snort coke outdoors... very risky. go to a friend's place, or a hotel room.
MDMA is best consumed at a house party. LSD most effective outside the city, openair.
