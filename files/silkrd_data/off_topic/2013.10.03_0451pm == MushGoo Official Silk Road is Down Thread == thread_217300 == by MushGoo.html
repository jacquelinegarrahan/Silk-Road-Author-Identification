After all, any order that takes place under these circumstances wouldn't have ever occurred had the marketplace site never existed to begin with.

SO, to reiterate/clarify - you pay me by whatever means we decide here, then, when the site is live, I will make a listing for you to buy using a coupon [so you are not charged anything extra]. This will ensure that all SR fees for orders which may occur during SR downtime will be received by the SR team once the site has gone back up. I'm sure that the SR team works really hard in this sort of event, so I may charge another 2-10% [depending on order] in addition to the normal SR transaction fees to thank the team for their hard work bringing the site back up [not to mention to help cover all the lost commissions that occur during downtime!].

I hope this makes sense, and I hope it never comes to this! :)
