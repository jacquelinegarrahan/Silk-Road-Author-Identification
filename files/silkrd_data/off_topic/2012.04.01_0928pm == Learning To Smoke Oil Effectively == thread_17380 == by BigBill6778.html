Title: Learning To Smoke Oil Effectively
Post by: BigBill6778 on April 01, 2012, 09:28 pm
OK here is a lesson on how to smoke OIL

first------thing acquire great oil
second-get a ladies hair pin or bobbypin (this is a flat pin great for scrapping the sides of a gram vial
third----get a plastic pop bottle with cap and melt a hole in the side about 2" from the bottom
four-----take a cigarette or cigar and light it
five-----take the flat pin and remove some oil from the vial
six------now wipe the tip of the pin on the heater of the cigarette and insert into the side of the bottle with the cap on tightly
seven--you will notice the smoke bellowing inside the bottle once the smoke starts to slow down remove the cigarette and twist off the cap   and inhale deeply with your lungs
eight----repeat steps 5-7 no more than 3 times and then see what it is like

Oil is a very good smoke as it takes (16 grams of weed to make 1 gram of oil) so when you consider the out come you are smoking 1/2 OZ of weed for every 1 gram of oil
