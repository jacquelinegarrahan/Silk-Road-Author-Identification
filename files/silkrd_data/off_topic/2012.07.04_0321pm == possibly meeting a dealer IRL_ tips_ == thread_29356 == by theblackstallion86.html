anyways, what i'd do if i was going to meet a total stranger to buy drugs:

1. turn up at least 20 minutes early and case the area looking for anything out of place. this includes keeping an eye on people looking out of windows - are the just having a look or looking for someone? is there anything shiny next to them that may look like a camera lens? and of course the totally obvious old guy with a mustache reading a newspaper on a park bench. generally people loitering without a purpose.
2. keep an eye out for CCTV and cars/SUV's/vans with blacked out windows, particularly if they have a good view of the meet spot.
3. keep out of site until you can see them turn up so you can see whether they're turning up heavy handed or not.

Title: Re: possibly meeting a dealer IRL? tips?
Post by: jameslink2 on July 04, 2012, 07:03 pm
theblackstallion86, I have done this before and here is what I did. Remember, this is VERY dangerous!

#1) I gave him a fake name. No need to give out your real info.
#2) I chose the location, a restaurant at lunchtime. Lots of people around and a generally safe environment. One without any cameras in the parking lot.
#3) Take only a little. A small joint in a pack of smokes. If you are going to do a deal it is best to get to know him/her first and do the deal a little later.
#4) Get there 45min early. It was in a shopping center so I went around and checked out a couple of stores and wandered for about 15min casing the place.
#5) Go into the restaurant 30min early and choose a seat near a window so you can watch the parking lot and see people coming in and out.
#6) Order a drink and tell them you are waiting on some one.

Watch how s/he arrives, what they are wearing, do they have anyone with them, are they the kind of person you expected, etc. If anything looks even the slightest bit out of place, simply pay for the drink and leave. If they walk up and ask if you are XXX you can simply smile and say no.

The best advice is dont do it! It is a major risk.
