Just curious, I can't be the only one. I'm the kind of person who loves drugs but doesn't often have connections where I live for most substances of any quality, so aside from great weed I used to have looong dry spells. When I found my way here I was overjoyed to the point of tears so I started stocking up! I am not a pessimist who thinks the road will be gone tomorrow, but it is a reality that LE could put the regulatory squeeze on some of the tech that keeps it flowing, like BTC. Since drugs are more affordable in quantity I found myself justifying large orders thinking I could just keep all this shit in the freezer or unload some at festivals to recover part of the cost. On top of that I have had some amazing experiences with vendors who have given me way more than I purchased :0. Astonishingly, it cost only a month's disposable income to have lots of everything rare and illicit I ever wanted to try. As I have sold drugs before irl and hated the lifestyle, I ultimately decided that I don't really want to sell this shit to unknown kids; too risky. So I have given quite a bit away to friends and gone on as much of a binge as I can get away with! In past times I would have killed to be in this situation, what can I say, life is hard.
