Title: Re: Vendor Ranking
Post by: Silpheed on January 30, 2012, 09:54 am
On SR, click on the Drugs category in the left sidebar.
http://silkroadvb5piz3r.onion/index.php/silkroad/category/70

In the box next to "sort by", select seller rank and click go.

This will sort all the items according to the highest seller rank based on feedback. You can click on their names to see their overall rank on SR. Right now the top 5 sellers in the Drugs category just happen to be the top 5 sellers overall as well.
