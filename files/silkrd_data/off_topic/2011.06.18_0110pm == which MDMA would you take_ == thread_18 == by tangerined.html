Ironically this can be a blessing in disguise.  Most of the time "baby laxative" is really "Mannitol" which is a known diuretic.  In the case of MDMA and its analogues this is a helpful substance in preventing cerebral edema (build up of water in the brain).  While many people never experience this brain swelling, it certainly can kill people and in the case of MDMA it has more than a handful of times.

I guess the moral of the story is, if your drugs are going to be cut with something, better mannitol than something else.
