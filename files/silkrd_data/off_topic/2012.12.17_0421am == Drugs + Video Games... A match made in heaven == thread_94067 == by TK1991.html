Post by: TK1991 on December 17, 2012, 04:23 am
*DISCLAIMER* Cannabis will be smoked upon every play through of every video game ever made by anyone anywhere, therefore it will never be mentioned.

Game: Journey
System: Ps3
Drug: Ketamine
I can honestly say that Journey is an understatement, they should have just been blunt and called it "What a fucking trip." There is no text in the game, no dialogue, and no direction. You appear as a sand dwelling humanoid creature in flowing garb, most notably some sort of inscribed scarf. As the name would suggest you travel a great distance presumably searching for the past of your people, or anything that could explain the game itself (as I said there are no objectives shown or explanation of the story, let your internal dialogue go crazy here) but the part I found the most entertaining was that was there was a kind of online feature to this journey. With no warning during game play what so ever another entity entered my game (the ketamine definitely helped to mystify this) and proceeded to explore with me. There are no mics or text chat, the only way to even remotely communicate is by pressing your action key (which shows a small blip above your head). I found this endlessly entertaining in the sense that when frustrated nothing could be seen besides a fury of blips and characters running in circles. The scenery and different environments explored are truly breathtaking, found myself saying, "wow" alot. A very relaxed game with an awe inspiring aura of mystery, wonderment, and beauty.
Long and short-
AWESOME for any lower scale hallucinogen (LSD, mushrooms, 2C-B) I dually enjoyed ketamine as it really made the game that much more epic (it's beeeaaaaaauuuutttiful) but would definitely be frustrating for anyone on any sort of stim.
4.5/5 Visual
2/5 Gameplay
5/5 Drug compatibility
