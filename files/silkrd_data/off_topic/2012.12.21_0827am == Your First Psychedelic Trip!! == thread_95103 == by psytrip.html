Title: Your First Psychedelic Trip!!
Post by: psytrip on December 21, 2012, 08:27 am
Share your first psychedelic experience in this thread. I know the first psychedelic is a good trip or a bad trip..but every one has a bad trip, share it. I was lucky to have a very good trip in my first psychedelic trip and the substance was lsd. It was a 350 micro of a good blotter paper, that was shared by me and one of my good friend (I call him Jim Morrison), and probably he was responsible for making my trip a very good trip. We had good hand rubbed Indian hashish with us to smoke while tripping, so we were prepared. We bought some cigarette pacakets, a juice bottle, some eatables, and were ready to go.

We sat in our room closed with a psycho painting hanged in front of  the wall, connected the ipod with the speakers, and we both started chewing the blotter and started making a joint. The music was on (The Doors), and Jim Morrison was by my side  :) :).. We ingested at about 9 pm and it started in 15 minutes. The vibe, whole body, every tissue was dancing with music. We smoked the joint. There were good closed eye visuals, which we both were enjoying together throughout the trip and out of body experience was there, and believe me the eyes closed, we both were dancing together sometime on the mountains, sometimes on the beach. The walls melted sometimes, and sometimes, everything was infinite. At around 12 pm our ciggerete, our juice were finished. And damn, we were thirsty. Though there was a shop just below our room, but it was like traveling from the Moon to Earth. We both went out of the room, went to the shop and bought all the required stuff. Everyone down there were amazed to see the way we were talking and the way we were behaving. We grabbed our stuff, and immediately went to our room, and all of a sudden (because the music was on), we both were on the top (flying high again). We rolled a joint, and rolled again and enjoyed a lot. The night passed by. At around 5 in the morning, we slept and it was the best sleep of my life. I got up at around 4 pm, and my mind was crystal clear, I was crystal clear in fact (loved the feeling). And couldn't wait for my next trip.

And a lot of trips after that, some good, some bad, some worst  :-\. But the first trip is always a first trip.

Title: Re: Your First Psychedelic Trip!!
Post by: A Riotous Defect on December 21, 2012, 09:35 am
I've only done one psychedelic and it was 25i, so it wasn't really that mind-blowing an experience, but I did enjoy it a lot. Basically I was at a friends apartment and I had been doing a lot of methylone and I had 25i tabs that I hadn't done yet and I was trying to calm down my drug use so I vowed that that night would be the last time I did any of the batch we had got. So I take one of the tabs, go outside and manage to come up right as the sun is coming down, it was beautiful. Notice just a slight bit of mindfuck, but not too bad. After that, I start getting nauseous, so I go to vomit, and I just remember staring at my vomit for the longest time because it was pretty in a morbid sorta way; it looked like little micro-organisms swallowing each other up and then splitting and changing color. Immediately when I stood up, I noticed that the world had gotten a bit more wavy. I stare at myself in the mirror for a while as I wash my hands and brush my teeth, and remark at how beautiful I thought I looked, because everything just had a wonderful oil-painting look which washed all my blemishes away. This made me feel really happy and I immediately start laughing uncontrollably, which continues as I exit the room. For the next couple hours or so I literally *can't* stop laughing. It was wonderful. My friends leave the apartment to go to the store or something so I'm left alone, and I did a little inward thinking about some issues in my life and some stuff I was coming to terms with, and genuinely felt that the 25i helped me mentally conquer some of the things I had been putting off for a long time.

Then I go to check my blood sugar and realize that I don't have any strips for my meter, so I have to spend ALL my money on them ( testing strips are sold in boxes of 50 and 100, and they're roughly 1.5 USD per strip ) so me and my friend start talking in line about how we're going to go down to the hardware section and break open the box ( they had it in the little plastic things that stop you from stealing them ) and laughing about everything. So me and my friend are bullshitting and this lady in front of us looks increasingly more and more fed up, but that just adds to the fun of it.

So we buy the shit, I check my sugar, and it's high as fuck. I didn't realize that the complexed blotters apparently increase my blood sugar, plus since the bodyload of the 25i was so different, i couldn't tell that my sugar was high but I didn't want it to get low throughout the trip so I had been eating and drinking sugary shit all night to stop myself from falling into a coma from low blood sugar. But I take my insulin and everything gets hunky dory again.

We get back to the apartment and me and a friend talk drug pharmacology which I enjoy discussing immensely, and I have a good time just shooting the breeze with 'im. At about t+8 or so I feel a bit tired, but still sorta odd, so I check my sugar again and it's fine so I go to bed without the aid of any benzos or anything. Very nice closed eye visuals, nothing too pushy, but I could visualize many nice things in my head as I was falling asleep.
