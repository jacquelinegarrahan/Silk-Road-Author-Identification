Post by: blackend646 on April 30, 2012, 12:33 am
Easiest method for me is to go to bitinstant.com and type in however much money you are going to spend, and then go to the bank and deposit the money into the account number they give you. Bitinstant works really fast so by the time you get home the money should already be in your Mt.Gox account. From there its a simple click to turn the money into bitcoin, then send it to another wallet, and from that wallet to silkroad. Whole process usually takes about 15 minutes- 2 hours plus however long it takes you to get to the nearest bank.

(You do not have to give your name when you make a bank deposit)
