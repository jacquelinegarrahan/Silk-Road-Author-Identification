Post by: sollemnis on June 14, 2012, 04:12 am

Secondly, I rarely see arguments here, except of course with Mr Dank, but I'm sure you all agree with me its banter. Nobody takes him seriously.

Honestly whenever I need advice I get it from you guys! I'm a laid back guy, try to help out where I can, try to point people in the right direction as to what drugs to use and what doses are best. I've learned so much from this community! Before joining SR, I was settling for street crap... now I get  CLEAN drugs that are always on weight and very well packaged.

I've always loved to discuss my drug experiences are hearing about others, here I've read a ton of reports! I now know (from this community) how to keep myself safe from LE and the best to get BTC. I've messaged a ton of people about things I'm unsure about, always received an honest and helpful answer.

This community is HUGE, what is it? 20,000 members? I have a few people on here I'd call friends, always help me out and comfort me.

Before SR I was a little down about myself, mainly due to my upbringing (ANTI - DRUGS) sort of family. I had to hide my drug use back as a teen, would have been grounded for months just for using a bit of Coke  ;).  When I moved in on my own I had total freedom, but my friends mainly just smoke weed, not my thing really... use it with MD every now an then but that's about it. It comforts me that on here people are in the same boat as me, we use drugs from time-time and it comforts me knowing others are enjoying what I am.

Lastly, a BIG thanks to DPR, we represent freedom, the right to put substances in to our bodies. Why are drugs illegal? Because the media hype about under-ages kids taking an OD... here we are intelligent enough to know how to dose, if shit hits the fan there's always somewhere here to advise you what to do. I've never known such a big community where all the users are friendly. - Obviously we get the odd troll - but lets face it they're ignored.

We are the new generation, fuck the street dealers mixing their drugs up with harmful stuff - clean drugs, tight community, helpful guides and good honest people.

I LOVE SR.
