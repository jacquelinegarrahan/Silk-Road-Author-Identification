Title: Re: how to find dat cali connect "commercial dro"
Post by: Jack N Hoff on April 27, 2013, 12:22 am
It's cheap in northern Cali.  Get to know the growers in Humboldt, Redding, Eureka, Ukiah Mendocino area.  Go to the medical marijuana meetups.  All the people I know of charge $1,000 to $2,400 for quality pounds out there.  $2,400 being for original GDP right from the tall ass man who invented the strain himself.  It's a tight knit community of growers.  It doesn't get cheaper if you buy higher quantity than a pound from what I've seen.  If it's a thousand a pound then its 40 thousand for 40 pounds.  At least that's how it is with all the growers I know out there.  It's pretty common for growers to carpool from northern Cali down to San Fran to go ship out packages.  San Fran isn't a weedy area like northern Cali and there are soooo many shipping points :)
