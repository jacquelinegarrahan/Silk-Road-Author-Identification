Title: Re: Help
Post by: foldingpaper on November 25, 2011, 09:26 pm
First off, know that it will take a few days and some effort on your part.

Do you have a bank account ? MtGox (https://mtgox.com/) and Intersango (https://intersango.com/) take deposits via Bank Wire and exchanges your fiat currency for Bitcoins. Fees are minimal, and this is the preferred method.
Do you prefer using PayPal ? You can use VirWox (https://www.virwox.com/) to buy SLL (Linden Dollars) and use these to buy Bitcoins. Fees can be as high as 40%.
Do you need to use another method ? Visit Get-Bitcoin (https://get-bitcoin.com/) and the Bitcoin Wiki (https://en.bitcoin.it/wiki/Buying_bitcoins) for various options.

Do you reside in the US ? Use MtGox.
In the UK/EU ? Use Intersango.
Other ? If the above do not work, search for a local trader at TradeBitcoin (http://www.tradebitcoin.com/).

For general advice, visit the Silk Road Wiki (http://dkn255hz262ypmii.onion/wiki/index.php/Main_Page) and search the forums.
