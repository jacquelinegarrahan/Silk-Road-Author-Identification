If you guys ever feel a bit chatty and would like to talk with people from all over the world, feel free to visit us on

http://krautchan.net/int/

Every poster has a countryball, so you see from which country he is. We have posters from nearly all countries, even from countries which you probably never heard of. We are always open for new people.
You can learn much about other countries and the people there are smart and very friendly. kc/int/ is also called pure love.

Don't think it is a chan for retarded people like 4chan or something like this. Just give us a try, you will not regret it. In my opinion it is the best international chan, that's why we have many active posters from all over the world.

You can lurk with Tor, but posting works only without proxy, because we want to know from which country you're posting.  ;)
