Title: Re: First DMT trip - any advice?
Post by: ruddyRudeman on September 12, 2011, 05:03 pm
Tried some a week ago. First time was a fail, I tried an exotic way of evaporating by putting a can into boiling water. It didn't vaporated really good, so just got slightest visual distortions with no mind blowing. Next time tried the light bulb and that was it! It smoked really intensively and easy, and I could make about 3 full breaths before I knew another one will pass me out completely, then leaned back on a sofa and had a really intensive and scary experience. I am had many acid and 2c- trips before, but none could scare me this way. Anyway I considered it a good experience.
Be sure in your smoking method! One can waste most of the stuff with improper use.
Have someone to take a smoking device from you, or have it stand at something like a table or a chair. First time you would probably forget about it and leave in your hand. It may cause some trouble.
Try to open your while tripping. There is an opinion that DMT is a closed eyed substance, but sometimes open eyed visuals are even cooler.
You can be vague in dosing and measure the dose by full breaths (about 2-3, held for as long as possible), but remember that there may be lots of material left unsmoked, especially first time, when you don't quite feel the substance behavior.
