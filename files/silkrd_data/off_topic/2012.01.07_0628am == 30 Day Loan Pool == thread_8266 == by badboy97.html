Let me explain to you a little bit about how to read this data.
At the top you will see the name of the pool under the starred line.
You will see what the rate is for a full 30 day loan.
You will see what the current debt is for the entire pool.
Total amount borrowed for that given pool.
Amount repaid.
What the early and late factors are for any given loan within the pool.

"Early Factor" is the bonus you get multiplied to your rate ex. 20% x .975 = 19.5%.  This will cause a rating bonus of 0.5% per day for early payments.

"Late Factor" is penalty to your rate for being late 20% x 1.05 = 21% and cause a penalty of 1% per day.

Lastly you will see the loan factor.
For each completed loan your pools rate will increase or decrease by a factor indicated for that pool. If you had a factor of 0.98 for a completed loan you can see the rate change permanently for the whole pool.

Completed Loan
20% x 0.98 = 19.6%            -0.4% on your rate for entire pool.
This would be your new interest rate after your first loan for the entire pool.

Default Loan
20% x 1.10 = 22%            +2% on your rate for entire pool.
This would be your new interest rate if a loan goes into default.

Each pool has a collection of members.
Each member will get the rate and fees associated with the attributes of that pool.

Anyone can join anyone other pool.  In fact I actually recommend to my borrowers to look at the ledger and join pools to build better rates.
All of these emails are actual verified emails.  Not sure if the users check them often.

If you would like someone to join your pool please email me the email address of the account and I will email them and see if then want to join your pool.

I encourage anyone to make very large pools.  This will be the most efficient way to root out bad users.

Also keep in mind that I cannot force users out of a pool.  They must request to transfer.  So if you add a bad apple to your pool it could cause permanent damage.  Keep that in mind before sending an invite to your pool.

If you decide to leave the pool you are in to make a NEW pool, your rate will increase by 15%.  This will discourage pool sharks, who seek out vulnerable pools.

If you join another persons pool.  The rate for the pool will remain the same.  So a lot of early members might have the best pools.

20% of your balance is actually due on the due date.  This is your "minimum payment" of your loan.  You can keep your loan going for another 30 days by "rolling" your 80% you still owe to the next month.  This will be a good way to build credit cheaply and quickly.

There will be 24 hours of grace period after the due date before your account goes into default.

If someone in your pool defaults. Someone in your pool must pay off his outstanding balance in 30 days, or the pool's rate will drop. 

The amount you borrow in usd is the amount that you must repay.  If you borrow 50btc at $4 a piece.  You must repay me $200 plus the interest you owe. 

Example:
50btc loan @ $4mtgox exchange rate with 20% interest for 30 days.
You pay off 5 days early.
Rate 20% multiplied by early factor .98 is 0.5% per day early.
0.5% multiplied by number of early days 5 = 2.5% discount.
You would pay $200 multiplied by rate of 17.5% = $235
You must purchase the $235 in bitcoins and send to my account.
I will take note of the time you transferred the bitcoins.
Make sure you transfer them quickly before any rate change.

This will keep my purchasing power high, but I will risk losses due to deflation.

There can be as many pools as you want and you can make new pools anytime.  Just email me badboy97@tormail.net
