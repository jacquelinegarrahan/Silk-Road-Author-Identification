Title: First Oxycodone experience
Post by: jimnator on March 12, 2012, 02:17 am
I bought 2x Oxycodone HCL 30 mg few days ago. I took 1/4 of 30 mg yesterday.

After 30 minutes, my heart began to beat REALLY fast. I was really troubled and called the police to send me an ambulance. While talking to the woman, my heart began to become more stable, so I told her to cancel the ambulance and I will call back again if it happens again.

Few minutes later, my body became warm and relaxed. It felt good... Then, a funny analgesic effect happened. I went to my room, I could barely walk and feel my body, I was totally wasted lol! A friend called me and I was so fucking high couldn't find words and I was talking very slowly, he laughed at me. There was a light euphoria effect which was pleasant. The analgesic effect was very strong. I went to the kitchen get water and I almost fell on the ground, was really high. At the end, I felt sleepy, but I heard it's not good to sleep on opiates incase you puke and you swallow.

At the beginning it really sucked, but when the rush came in, it was pleasant feeling. I don't understand why people are addicted to it, I didn't find it addictive, it was just pleasant, but I wouldn't do it everyday.

For the side effects, I had itchy skin and constipation, that's it. I'd give that drug a 7/10.
