Title: Re: K2 Weed. Is it really that different from other Strains?
Post by: DrGonzoII on June 08, 2012, 04:00 am
Well.....There is K2 the Marijuana strain. And then there is K2 the synthetic cannabinoid blend(s).

If you are talking about real weed, the answer is yes and no. All the different strains of real marijuana have subtle differences (taste, aroma, different ratios of THC/CBD/...etc) but its like being a wine connoisseur, in the end, it all gets you drunk/high.

If you are talking about K2/spice/herbal potpourri, then the answer is: YES IT IS WAY DIFFERENT FROM MARIJUANA.
Herbal incense/cannabioid blends are sketchy from the start in that 99% of them wont tell you the chem whats in it (unless you buy from a blend maker are a clearnet forum) and there are literally 100s of different Research Chemical Cannabinoids in existence with more being made each day. And all of them have radically different effect profiles. Plus, Unlike marijuana, it is definitely possible to cause serious issues to your brain and body (possibly even death) by using these substances (THEY ARE CALLED RESEARCH CHEMICALS FOR A REASON)
you can find a lot of good info about them at the following clearnet site:

http://www.drugs-forum.com/forum/forumdisplay.php?f=336

I hope that this answers your question.
