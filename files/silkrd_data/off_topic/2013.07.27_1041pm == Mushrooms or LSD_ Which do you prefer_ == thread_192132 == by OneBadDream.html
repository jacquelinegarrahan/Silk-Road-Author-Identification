Title: Re: Mushrooms or LSD? Which do you prefer?
Post by: wretched on July 28, 2013, 12:16 am
It really depends on several factors. For me, first is the day of the week. If I were wanting to trip today, it would be LSD. If the trip urge hits me on Wednesday afternoon, then it is shrooms for the obvious time constraints. There are also different experiences I might be looking for. If I am having some emotional issues I am dealing with, then shrooms seem to be the answer for me. If there are complex non emotional issues I am working out, then it is LSD all the way. If I want to wander around in the woods, then the only choice is LSD, but if I am going to be trapped indoors, then I'll take shrooms. These are not the only two options though, so don't focus all your energy on choosing between them. Silk Road has plenty of variety and the experiences are wildly different.

But my advice for the moment is the shrooms for the simple reason that you haven't had them yet. You can't make an informed decision on which is best for you until you have tried them both :)

Title: Re: Mushrooms or LSD? Which do you prefer?
Post by: anothergirl on July 29, 2013, 12:35 am
I'm tempted to say "mixed".

For me, shrooms give me a serious body load (head drooping like a zombie, almost drooling sometimes) and they make me want to dance or at least shamble rhythmically. Also, I notice that shrooms have more of a confusion factor that can be fun. On high doses I've found my self curled up dry heaving mumbling the same nonsensical phrase for hours (not a bad trip but a cherished memory), which would never happen to me on L (most I've done is 1000 mics)

LSD, on the other hand, is more visual and less mindfucky with no body effects except the vascular constriction. On REALLY high doses things might feel intense and I might breath heavy or feel a sense of energy flowing, but this is uncommon.

As I live in a locked down conservative country with strict laws and don't have the balls to order shrooms, my experience is relatively limited (I went through 100 tabs of L in the last 6 months (about 20 trips), but only had a dose of shrooms that really did it for me maybe 5 times.

So what I prefer is to take some syrian rue tea before a mild dose (2-3g) of shrooms (since they're relatively precious in terms of availability to me), which mitigates the nausea of a higher dose, and then maybe 300 mics of L right after they're down the hatch.

This makes me want to dance or at least nod my head to music, while the LSD provides the crazy CEV music video.

And I can hang out in a well lit room where L has a chance to shine for a few minutes, then go into a really dark room where the shrooms make everything cloudy and gray-scale around me.

Mixing really has become the way to go for me, unless I'm in public. I would never shroom in public due to the physical signs I'd give off.

Speaking of public, I've become such an acid head (I never tried any drugs until a year ago, unfortunately  late in my life (late 20s)), that I plan events around my tolerance levels. So far my greatest accomplishments while tripping are a day of roller coasters and skydiving.

But if I had to pick one, in a country where they are both easily available to me, I'd have to say LSD by a hair.
