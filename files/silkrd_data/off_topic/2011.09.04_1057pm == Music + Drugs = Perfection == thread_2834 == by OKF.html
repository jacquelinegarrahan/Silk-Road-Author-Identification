Post by: cosmicamerican on September 06, 2011, 06:03 am
i am a big fan of both music and drugs independent of each other, but together they enhance one another and can make for some incredible memories.

my fav drugs are psychedelics with acid being my absolute fav.  my fav music to listen to on acid is pink floyd (that's a given), jimi hendrix, lynyrd skynyrd, the beatles (my all time fav band), etc.  i listen to music a lot on mushrooms too.  i enjoy smoking weed when i can (my job does random drug tests, boo!) and when i do i like to listen to pretty much anything.  i like everything from james brown to hank williams sr.

i remember tripping on acid at a park with my then gf and we had a beatles mix cd playing.  "i want you (she's so heavy)" came on and i remember thinking, "wow!  this has got to be the heaviest song i've ever heard!"  of course, it isn't the heaviest if you are talking about metal or whatever, but to me it's still the most acid oriented song i know of.  "across the universe" is my fav song ever, esp. after hearing it on acid; it's just so beautiful.  lastly, "i'm only sleeping" is a wonderful song that i think was written either on or strongly influenced by acid.
