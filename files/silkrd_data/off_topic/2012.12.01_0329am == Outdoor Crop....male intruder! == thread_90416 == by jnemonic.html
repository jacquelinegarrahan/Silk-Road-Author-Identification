Title: Re: Outdoor Crop....male intruder!
Post by: CaliTrees on December 01, 2012, 03:55 am
Should you cull it?  Yes.  How far can pollen travel?  Miles.  Will your girls be safe?  I can't tell you for sure unless you cull it.

Perhaps look into selective pollination for a couple seeds.  Can you harvest some pollen safely?  You may be able to pollinate a single branch on a selected (your best) female and have a bunch of seeds for next year without fearing a fully seeded crop.
