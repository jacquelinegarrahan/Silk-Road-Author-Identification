If you use it multiple times a week for months on end -- you will literally want to kill yourself. Extreme depression, lethargy, anxiety, loss of sex drive, etc... Seems like you'll never feel better.

If you respect the substance and only use it infrequently, you will feel just fine. (I recommend at least 30 days between rolls)

If you take too much, you may feel a little down or hazy for a couple of days, but it isn't bad at all.

It's definitely something I believe every human should experience at least once. :)
