Thats how a number of drugs are sold. Its called a metric or "Mexican" ounce, and you'll never get more than 25g (since its how they break up kilos evenly). In fact, in some areas its even 24g, from multiple unrelated dealers (so its not just one guy fucking with his buyers over a single gram). I suspect its because it can be evenly broken down without half g's involved (so a half oz would be 12g, a quarter would be 6, etc). Stupid, I know, and I thought I was being shorted the first few times it happened, but everyone does it like that. The ounces are also known as "pieces."
