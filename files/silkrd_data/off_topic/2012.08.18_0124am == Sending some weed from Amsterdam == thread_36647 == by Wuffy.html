Title: Re: Sending some weed from Amsterdam
Post by: rightonion on August 20, 2012, 04:33 pm
Came back from Amsterdam a few weeks back, successfully posted back 13g in one package. Combination of the above posts really.

Best to use a DVD case for maximum space rather than a CD case. Then if you want to go the low tech route just get yourself some smelly proof baggies, which I have used in the past but tend to be best for hash. If you load up one of those with 10g of Super Lemon Haze from Greenhouse you are in trouble :P

My advice would be to get some anti static ESD bags from ebay or the like. If you can source 3M manufactured bags these tend to be the best, make sure they are heat seal able. Then buy yourself the cheapest pair of hair straighteners your can, mine cost Â£4, and take that lot in your bag. They work an absolute dream. Very easy to seal up and no smell leakage. My parcel of 13g of bud was waiting on my doormat for a week and still no smell when I opened it and that was with a single layer. Once sealed place inside the DVD case, might require a bit of crushing. Simply pop that in a jiffy bag and you are golden.

As for the actual postage, for me it cost me 4eu to send it priority to the UK. Easiest post office to post from in the center for my money is the one behind the royal palace in Dam Square. Forgotten the name but a quick Google search you should be able to find it. They asked no questions simply weighed it up for me and then placed it into the post box.

Hope that helps.

Best of luck to you. Nothing better to ease post Dam depression then arriving home to a fat parcel waiting for you :)
