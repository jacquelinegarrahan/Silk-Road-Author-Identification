Post by: redalloverthelandguyhere on July 20, 2012, 12:05 pm
I have done a little bit of time inside.

Visiting!

UK jails are not so bad. My buddy got into a Cat C prison as he was done for fraud.

Worse thing about UK prisons is the younger lads, many fucked up by circumstances more than drugs. Coke does not help you work out anger issues.

There is some bullying, not as bad as USA jails. For instance the fear of getting one up the ass is a myth. It happened way back I guess but most gay men in a prison full of men, some who aint been laid for a decade, its all consensual.

Main thing for younger men who do time is NEVER take any favors inside. If you use drugs use jail to take a break especially if you use H or crack.

Don't show weakness. There are cases of younger prisoners forcing some to get stuff inside.  Even people's mothers have smuggled as if their son is targeted it is bad. Easy answer though, report bullying, but NEVER name the prisoner. If you can handle yourself then take care all the same, thankfully it is rare for ordinary prisoners to be hurt. If bullied badly smash your cell up. Repeat if needs be. Write also, create a stir, the prions do take bullying seriously but obviously you cannot cry to the guv and name names.

Main thing is to take no shit but in an amicable manner.

Keep away from drugs - many prisons have gangs and individuals who can target families of prisoners outside. If you don't do drugs you won't be seen as an easy mark. H costs a lot inside, you might think a 100mg bag of H is nothing but inside that shit has to go a long long way. H is the biggest temptation inside. My buddy did not partake but others did and came out to a habit well established. Outside the H is way cheaper.

Most prisoners use H for depression and issues. That is my experience with many opiate addicts.

Another thing, going to prison your relationship will suffer big time. If you value your women enough then best avoid things which can bring big sentences. I avoid crime and stick to working for a living. Pay is shit compared to crime but crime pays and crime fails. When it does prison is the most they can give to you.

Best thing about prison is that prisoners are made to behave as they will get out of jail VERY soon. Not unusual to get 5 years and be out on a tag a year later. This stops a lot of bullying in prison and the opportunities to get educated are there. I would jump at that chance. Many men come out of jail different men, better men, not because of prison but despite it!

Education is the way to go.

Also, prison gives 3 meals a day, cleans your laundry, gives free electric, TV in most cells, games also. Mobile phones all over. No worry about paying the bills for many but my buddy had a mortgage and lucky his women worked or the house would be lost.

Crime is tempting. But prison takes you away from your loved ones.

At night time, when everyone else is free, your locked up and I guess being denied love is punishment. Most prisoners harden themselves to that but it hurts.

All in all UK prison is good, USA prison is a lot worse depending on the location. Race is a big issue in the USA and the UK also has this to some extent.
