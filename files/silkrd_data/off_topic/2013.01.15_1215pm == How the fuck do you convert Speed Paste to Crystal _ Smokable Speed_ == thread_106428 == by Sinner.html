Title: Re: How the fuck do you convert Speed Paste to Crystal / Smokable Speed?
Post by: ch0sen on January 15, 2013, 02:08 pm
First off I am no chemist or an expert and I don't even play one on TV.

But

My understanding is you use acetone because the speed will not dissolve in it but many cuts will.  Do a few rinses with that.  Then use denatured alcohol because the speed will dissolve in that but not the cut.  You can then filter it and get cleaner dope.  Now this process is described in detail many places on the clearnet.  So if you are gonna do this, definitely take the time to read about the process and do your homework. 

Because that is paste you may have to do recrystallization.  That is an entirely different subject and is way more involved.  There is tons of good info out there covering these subject all the way to reductive amination.  Again, you will need to do a lot of reading, or even consult someone with a chemistry background before attempting any of this.
