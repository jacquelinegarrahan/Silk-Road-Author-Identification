Title: Re: How to hide drugs in your luggage?
Post by: Sname on July 16, 2012, 06:15 am
I am sure you can get some good ideas through an in depth google search, and people here may have some clever tricks as well. That being said, the safest thing to do is to find someone you trust who lives near the festival and mail everything ahead of time. As a general rule, prescription pills and medication, including small amounts of liquid medication, are not scrutinized by TSA and are permitted for travel. Vitamins and supplements are also allowed on the plane. Therefore, if you can disguise anything inconspicuously in gel capsules which can be stuffed in old pill bottles, that should suffice for some of what you are bringing. When I go through this, I just pick a friend and mail everything USPS priority. I would urge you to do the same. However, if you can't find a mailing address by your destination (even a hotel may hold your mail for you), then you should listen to other responses from people more experienced than me. Hope I could help some though.
