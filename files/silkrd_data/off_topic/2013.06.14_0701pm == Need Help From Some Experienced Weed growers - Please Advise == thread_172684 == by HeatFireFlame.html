Post by: bigbabbablue on June 14, 2013, 07:23 pm
Are your plants straight in the bottom? If so you probably dont have to use fertilizer.
Less is more..
Dont water it too much. And really start using fertilizers when the plant need some..
If you use too much fertilizer the earth can get very salty. The roots will die, and the plant, too.
So for your problem you could mix up some more, and store the rest.
If its no bio-fertilizer it will be usuable at least a couple of days.
