Title: 10g shroom trip report.
Post by: 2marijuanaman on July 11, 2013, 12:23 am
Well that was a very interesting trip. I ate the 10grams of mushbuds shrooms on pretty much an empty stomach, and heres what happened.

About half an hour later I knew I was gonna have some kind of intense experience. The only more intense psychedelic trips I've had were once when I took a 10-strip of acid (that was just insane tbh... not sure if I leaned from that one) and strangely once I had an eigth of golden teachers that was incredibly strong (maybe my mindset was just really sensitive to shrooms that day?)
So it was four o clock when I took the shrooms. I rolled around on my bed for (2 hours? There was time dilation) This part of the experience was the most visual, and I felt very connected to the universe. I felt like everything was at my fingertips and the world was my oyster(dont even know what that really means) but that thought came into my head and it just cilcked.
I checked the time and around 7:00 my trip for some reason turned bad. I felt like some energy wanted me to confront my suicidal thoughts which I dont always think about. I realized the reason I wanted to commit suicide didnt really have to do with my life situations, just my mindset. I for some reason thought that if I committed suicide, I'd transcend what I view as a narrow-minded, controlling society, to enter DMT hyperspace as a light-being. Some energy warned me I hadnt learned everything I needed to, and that a natural death would eventually allow me to enter a hyperspace/void where I would see my true self and then carry on from there based on choices. Thats all I was told on the subject from the energy. I was frustrated by this and tried to communicate to the energy that I WAS ready to move on past my earthly form and that I felt I wasnt learning anything. Then a bad feeling washed over me, and I endured an uncomfortable, yet bearable, state of mind for about an hour. I soon learned I could use this breathing technique to conquer it, so I started meditating. I meditated for maybe 3-4 hours, and when I opened my eyes the visuals were pretty much gone. I saw more CEV's than OEV's during this trip. In a way I kind of wish I'd opened my eyes more, but I had to meditate to overcome a bad trip, and I did.

I ate a meal, watched some breaking bad on tv, and went to bed about 12 hours after dosing. I feel like I want to try more shrooms (next time Id like to try 15 grams, maybe with acid too?) I also had a strong urge to buy DMT which I'm now placing an order for. Will dmt work if I sandwitch it between tea leaves, and just let it cherry a bit?

A day later I realize that the trip taught me to let go and meditate. I still have a subtle sense of calm, and I catch myself blankly staring at the sunset as I type this. I still have thoughts of suicide, but decided that if I'm really gonna kill myself at a young age, I need to be fair and give it a good 6 months to think about. And if I still want to do it after 6 months of real thought... well.
