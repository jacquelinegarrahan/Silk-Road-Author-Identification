Title: Re: LSD question
Post by: blockrockinbeats on February 13, 2012, 12:08 pm
I'm not an expert, but I think you need at least 2 hits to be really in the "tripping balls" zone.

There have been times when I only took 1 hit and just felt a bit wired, strange and uncomfortable for the whole night. I didn't really enjoy it.

I need at least 2 hits to experience the serious euphoria, open-eye visuals, and deeply philosophical head space.

Obviously it can depend on how much each hit is dosed at, whether the acid has degraded, the user's frame/build, whether you've eaten much prior to dropping, set and setting, etc.

It sounds like what you had probably was acid, perhaps just a mild/weak dose. The giveaway is usually the onset and duration. LSD generally take 15 mins to an hour to kick in, and lasts for a solid 8-10 hours.
