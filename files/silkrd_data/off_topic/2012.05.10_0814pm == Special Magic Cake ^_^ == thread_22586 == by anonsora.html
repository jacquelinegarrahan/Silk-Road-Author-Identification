Post by: deadfuture on May 10, 2012, 08:35 pm
Yup just cook the weed in the butter.  Break up the weed really fine first. melt the butter and add the weed.  Keeping it on LOW heat is extremely important so you don't burn the weed and lose the THC.  The longer you cook it the more THC you extract but a minimum of 45 minutes to an hour should be used. I've seen people say they cook it for 2-4 hours before.

Also I'm not sure about the previous posts.  When I cook cannabutter it really stinks up the whole kitchen so I wouldn't bank on it being odorless.

Once you're done cooking it you have two options.  To strain the weed out using the cheesecloth or leave it in.  I've done both with equally good results but only left it in when I broke it up so much it was more of a weed powder when I cooked it so you didn't break a tooth on a stem or something.

After this just cook the cake as normal using the butter.  Just make sure to look how much butter the cake mix requires and use that amount to cook the weed in.

This. Or mix it into chocolate pudding.

What I do is make 2 chocolate cakes each in a 9inch baking pan, on one of the cakes use a spoon (while it's warm) to scoop out the cake 1-2 inches from the edge, all around, making a crater in the middle of the cake (but not scooping so low that there isn't a bottom).

Then fill the crater with chocolate pudding, the kind in a can from the baking section. Put the other cake on top of it and I like to use cream cheese icing all around that, slice up strawberries and put them on top and use a cheese grater on a chocolate bar to get those curly chocolate shavings of deliciousness on top too.

Post by: Meister on May 10, 2012, 08:42 pm
If your making the cake I have above, with pudding, then what you do is heat up vegetable oil, mix in your weed (grind it up as much as possible). Then I mix the pudding in with the vegetable oil and stir, after it's stirred well let it cool a bit and taste it, if it tastes good your fine, if not then I add more pudding and/or condensed milk and heat it up again stirring. Doing it this way your not really heating the weed, so you don't have to worry about losing any thc, if you reheat after the weed is added make sure it's on very low heat.
