Title: Re: Buying extacy in Amsterdam
Post by: j789745 on November 28, 2011, 10:58 pm
@White Light - I've spoken with many Dutch people. I backpacked around Europe a few years ago and ended up spending a significant amount of time in the Netherlands. Amsterdam isn't really a dutch city in culture. It is an international city, where people come from all over Europe and the world and communicate in English as the primary language (almost all dutch speak English, but in Amsterdam it is the first thing you'll hear from people until you get further away from the central train station. While it is absolutely true that many Dutch hate the drug and sex tourism (and the reputation they get as a result), Amsterdam is different. The people there know they need it for their economy to thrive. The people in Eindhoven, Utrecht, Den Bosch, Rotterdam, etc - they're the ones who don't give a fuck about drug and sex tourism because their cities can survive without it. Amsterdam can't.
