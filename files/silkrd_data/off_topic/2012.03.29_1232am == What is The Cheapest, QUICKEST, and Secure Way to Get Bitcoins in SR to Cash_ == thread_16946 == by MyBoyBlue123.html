He s asking to get fiat with BTC not the other way around.

No one here should be using gox unless you are just a casual buyer. They will eventually lock the account and require AML verification. Not only do they comply with all LE request (all exchanges most likely will) but they also go out of their way to help law enforcement agencies.

You should limit BTC > USD / EUR  transactions at exchanges as much as possible as these are highly scrutinized. USD / EUR> BTC is mostly ok in small amounts like 3 digits a month.  If you had to use an exchange you should look for a small independent ones like get-bitcoin.com or spendbitcoin.com who likely don't keep the best of records or papertrail. 

Otherwise you should try to stick with p2p. bitcoin-otc was an excellent suggestion. You can also look on bitcointalk forums marketplace or make a post of your own.

The best way to cash out big amounts is to save up a good amount of BTC then f2f trade USD for BTC on a laptop in a public place. Just make none of your online identities are linked so they have no idea who you could be then f2f isn't a problem.Pretty mu ch every other way, if you move big amounts, you could be easily caught (doesn't mean they will bother, but you're not safe.)
