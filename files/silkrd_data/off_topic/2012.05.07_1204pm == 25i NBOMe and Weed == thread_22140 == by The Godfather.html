Title: 25i NBOMe and Weed
Post by: The Godfather on May 07, 2012, 12:04 pm
Greetings all,

Last night my fiancee and I tried two tabs of DudeRugs's 25i-NBOMe - the visuals on that alone were quite nice but nothing much really. When I felt the drug starting to wear off I decided to smoke a bit of weed. I usually have three to five hits from my mini bong to get a good effect from the weed but I decided to only have two hits shared with my fiancee who only had a few puffs. Minutes after smoking the weed I felt a lot more fucked than I normally feel on weed, obviously, but I thought the 25i was almost completely gone! We turned off the lights and tried to get to sleep, my fiancee told me that she was feeling very high from just those few puffs and me, well, I was on another level altogether! The visuals after smoking the weed were INSANE compared to just tripping on the 25i.

When my eyes were open I could see the curtains swaying from side to side and if I looked straight up at the roof I could see what looked like a laser from a night-club shining on the ceiling in little hexagon shapes with other odd shapes here and there. When I closed my eyes it was like watching a movie in my head! Normally when I close my eyes the things that I see are not very clear but these visuals were as clear as day!

Has anyone else tried this and what did you feel from doing it? Anything similar to this?

Cheers,
The Godfather
