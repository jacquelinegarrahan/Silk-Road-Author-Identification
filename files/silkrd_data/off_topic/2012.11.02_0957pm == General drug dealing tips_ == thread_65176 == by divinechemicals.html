Title: Re: General drug dealing tips?
Post by: Phox on November 02, 2012, 10:12 pm
Hello divinechemicals,

I understand the potential to make a very nice profit by purchasing MDMA on SR in bulk and reselling locally.
However, if you are asking this you are already off to a bad start.

You need to already have a good, small client base that will buy a large portion of the MDMA. You may be buying the MDMA for $27 a gram and are doing the math to find what your profit can be when you raise it to a great profit margin, yet this only includes you making 100 sales of a Gram of MDMA. What is safer is to already have a small base of (3-6) people to sell your 100g to for them to sell. Just starting off you do not want/need to have to find 100 different people to sell to if you have no previous customer base. This is very risky. If you have to go looking for business, you're asking to get busted.

As far as the generals of dealing -

1. Get the money first, no fronting.
2. If it feels wrong: just don't do it. Greed isn't worth jail/injury.
3. Do not drive with the product in your car/have it known where it is at any time.
4. Do not use a bank checking/saving account. Get a safety deposit box and keep it in there.
5. Switch your burner phone regularly and meet at new places of your choosing.

There are more. Just use common sense and don't end up in jail!

Title: Re: General drug dealing tips?
Post by: thesearstower on November 04, 2012, 04:29 pm
Teach yourself how to be cool under pressure. People tend to lose their heads, and if you're able to keep yours, it'll help you stay alive and/or stay out of jail.

Take the first money you make and put it in the 'Lawer Up' fund. This isn't re-up money, and this isn't rent money. This is only for when you get pinched. Make sure that you have a name and phone number of a lawyer who specializes in defending drug dealers. A good lawyer will save your ass, so long as you have money to pay him/her.

Don't talk. To anyone. About anything regarding your operation. Ever. This includes, but is not limited to: friends, fuck-buddies, customers, wannabes, running buddies, or your mother. Dealing is exciting, and you'll want to tell people. Don't.

Don't talk to the police. Ever. End of story.

Good luck.
