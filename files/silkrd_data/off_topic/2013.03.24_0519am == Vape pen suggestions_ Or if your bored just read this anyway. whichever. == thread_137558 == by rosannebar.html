Post by: CoinBoxsWeedBox on March 24, 2013, 06:44 am
Smoking hash in glass is best done by smoking down a bowl until you have a layer of pure gray ash at the bottom of the bowl (sometimes you have to add a bit more to smoke ontop to build the layer of ash). Throw your hash onto the ash screen, and bring the herb iron, lighter, etc.. close but not to the hash. Vape pens fall into differnt categories. Some, are meant for herbs, and can handle hash. The other are for budder, oil, or shatter. If your looking for a concentrate pen, check out the e-skillet. For herb check out the e-blunt (I think that was it's name).

If you want to vaproize out of glass, theres a newer vape called the vape exhalecloud that attaches to glass.
To smoke DMT search 'The machine DMT' to find the best way to smoke DMT. have fun!
