Marijuana is seriously awesome.  It has different smells, looks, textures, tastes, effects.  From energetic, narcotic, euphoric, psychedelic, and mixed highs.  What more can you ask for from a plant?  Not to mention it's health benefits for people who are sick and even though the smoke being carcinogenic, it doesn't cause cancer. 

It goes good with everything and anything.  Even smoking it with shrooms, acid, or even coke is amazing. It goes well with almost any drug to be honest(except alcohol for me I get vertigo). Smoking for any type of pain, nausea, or no appetite.  For anxiety and depression as well as creativity and opening up your mind.  The point is, it is such a safe drug compared to most anything else and I feel it really is good for you as long as you don't smoke ounces a day lol. 

Not to mention it is seriously awesome for harsh come downs of any drugs and especially hang overs.  Bud doesn't give you a horrible widthdrawls like other drugs do.  When you don't have any bud for the day or a while it sucks but you live.  I think that is one of the best things about bud.

There's nothing like smoking and chilling either with your friends, loved ones, or dogs/pets in your comfy house watching tv, movie, listening to music, playing games or what ever.  It makes the moment so much more memorable and fun.  It's awesome.

I seriously don't know what I would have done with out bud in my high school years.  I had a very tough child hood and high school was a nightmare.  I'm not saying what I did was right but in my situation I had absolutely no other escape or medicine to help me through those tough times.  Depression, anxiety, huge family problems, friend problems, and puberty. I was just a total mess.  I know self medicating isn't the best idea but if it wasn't for bud to take a huge load off me every night and day I don't think I would've made it.  At least not as easily.

I have great respect for this plant and I seriously hope it becomes legal in the US this year.  I'm praying. 


I have no bud right now and am feeling really good on DXM lol so thought I'd share.   I honestly didn't think I would ever try DXM but it's pretty crazy.  The light doses mimic ecstasy but with a lot more "drunk" to it and not nearly as much euphoria. 

Always wanted to see if others felt the same way about bud for a while now and finally got to writing this.

Let me know!
