I have read that too...Only thing is that is not what a "Thumbprint" really is...Nobody in their right freaking mind actualy sticks their thumb in a  pile of crystal and rubs it on thier tounge...Or presses a vial to their thumb and licks that!!

A "Thumbprint" is actualy the veteran guys lay out their crystal to lay and before adding it to the solvent they eyeball and put aside a nice chunk of crystal of aprox 1 to 2 milligrams and give that to the newbie as their thumbprint...at least thats my experience!!

Sticking your thumb in crystal is like 10-100 Milligrams...Thats just insane for a an inexperienced person, and no "Family" Member that I knew would ever want to fuck someone up possibly for life doing that!!

I have also seen two other versions of the "Thumbprint"...

In one a person puts his thumb on a freshly laid, dry sheet of WOW and traces around it with a pencil, then cuts out the outline of his "Thumbprint" and eats that...usualy the thumb covers about 1500-2000 Mics of Tabs (Depending on thumb size of course!!)

In the other someone dips his thumb in the solvent containg crystals about to be laid and either licks that, or presses it to a blank sheet of blotter creating a "Thumb or fingerpriint", cuts that out and eats it(also usualy about 1500-2000 Mics depending on how big and fat your thumb is!!)
