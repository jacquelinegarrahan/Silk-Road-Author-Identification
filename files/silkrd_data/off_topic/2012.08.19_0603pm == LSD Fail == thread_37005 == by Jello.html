Wrap it in plastic, then in foil, and put it in the fridge.  Freezing it over a period of time may degrade it, but doesn't always.  Some say the foil contact can taint it, some disagree.  But the foil will keep out the light, and the plastic will keep out the moisture.  Fridge is cool enough.  The butter compartment on the door is as good a place as any.
