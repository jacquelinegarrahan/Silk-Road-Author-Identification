34tuforlunch, what was your tolerance to opiates at the time you did the 100mg?

The symptoms you are describing are those of a mild to moderate overdose.  You have to be careful with things like H and other strong opiates, over a short term usage pattern they are cumulative.  Unless you are used to doing 100mg of H or a similar opiate a day, you did too much.

This is one of the more common ways people overdose on opiates.  They chase the rush.  They do a bit, get a nice high, then keep doing more until they pass out or in your case start feeling nauseous and then vomit.
