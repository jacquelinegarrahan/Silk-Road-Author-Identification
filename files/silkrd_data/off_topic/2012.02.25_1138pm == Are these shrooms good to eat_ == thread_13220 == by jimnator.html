Title: Re: Are these shrooms good to eat?
Post by: novocaine on February 26, 2012, 01:41 am
without looking a the pic...
Ask yourself...
a/ Is the vendor reliable? (sold shrooms with good feedback)
b/ Are they wild or specifically grown from known strain?

Because if it was me I would not trust anyone with wild picked shrroms and on the flip side I would not trust myself giving wild picked to anyone.

Research edibles and poisonous mushrooms and note the similarities and the proximity they grow next to each other.

Im not saying the vendor doesnt know his shit but I am saying it is easy to fuck up.

A couple(1?) of chinese nationals died in Aus recently because they ate death caps that looked liked an edible.

It is doable for an amateur to ID a mushroom when it is fresh. (spore colour, bluing if active, gill colour etc)  It is a lot harder to do once dried.
