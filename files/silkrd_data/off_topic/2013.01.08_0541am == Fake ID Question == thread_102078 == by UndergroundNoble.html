Title: Re: Fake ID Question
Post by: tmt971 on January 08, 2013, 06:35 am
there are absolutely no scanners that check whether or not an id is fake ... UNLESS a cop does it. nobody besides government officials have access to that info. the info that comes up on a scanner at a store or device is more or less as follows: name, dob, address, identifying stats, and whether the id is expired or not.

real story friend told me: "i went to the bank to make a withdrawal. they ask me to scan my actual id to verify my id. i accidentally scanned my fake id. all the teller told me was that the id was invalid. the reason it was invalid was because the birthday wasnt the same as my account." moral of the story, even a bank doesnt have access to that info.

ps: ive done extensive research on fake ids
