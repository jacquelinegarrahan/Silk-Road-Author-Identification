Post by: CoinBoxsWeedBox on March 23, 2013, 09:39 am
Yes, rigs for flower are for flower specifically, and vice versa. The fats & tars in the Trichome are mostly hydrophobic, so when you keep the rig for oil specifically it never builds up a grungy taste. You could turn the thing amber with reclaim, and still on taste the slightly bit of hashyness in the water.

In a perfect life I would smoke flower 35% of the time, bubble hash 35%, ice wax 20% of the time and shatter / defatted shatter 10% of the time. That changes progressively, depending on my needs.

Out of Ti, Quartz Glass, or Boro their are two clear choices, Quartz or Ti. Borosilicate (glass) is mostly ignored because it shatters / cracks after consistent use of a real torch. Quartz does not. I prefer Quartz to Ti as though it takes longer to heat it heat to a slightly lower consistent temperature, preserving flavor! Also Ti requires 'seasoning' for the same reason cast iron pans do, they absorb the oil when heated.
I would recommend the quartz honeypot (which can be bought seperately from the mini before mentioned), or a Quartz thermal DI nail. Having tried all ranges of domesless, and non and about every Ti or quartz nail out there. The only one I havn't tried is the E-Nail by Task Rok.

I like all ranges of strains. I think Gage Green Genetics, Kingdom Organic Seeds, DNA Genetics, and a few others are masterful breeders. I love Hindu Kush, Grape Stomper OG, Inferno Haze

Goldstiens tube are nice, fritted percs are clever. They clog rather quickly, making grunge off a constant purchase. The pulls is a tad heavy for my lungs once it begins to clog, because my lungs are a tad weak, though I love the diffusion. Very fun to watch indeed :)
