Post by: captainjojo on June 07, 2012, 10:05 am
How did you transfer it?  Was this a BTC transfer from a personal wallet to your SR wallet address or a transfer from a third party exchanger to your SR wallet?

Did you initiate the transfer before or after SR was down for maintenance?

If started the transfer just before SR went down, it is possible the money arrived, then was lost when they went down, as it looks like they did a restore from an a backup that was several hours old.

A number of people are posting about orders the were closed and finalized now re-opened or BTC that were received now gone.

If it is the case that you sent the BTC just before the site went down I suggest you gather all the information you can and have it ready to submit to SR if necessary.

If you sent it after SR went down and you are using your own wallet, make sure the block chains are up to date.  Your wallet won't send anything until it has a complete block chain.
