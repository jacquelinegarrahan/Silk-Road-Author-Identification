Title: Re: ESCROW AND 100% REFUND
Post by: Moon Fried on November 27, 2012, 12:25 am
After 10 days or so you'll get a message from SR support reminding you to finalize or resolve before it auto finalizes. So if you have one day left before it auto finalizes and you still don't have the package, you can click resolve instead of finalize and you can extend the date or work something out with the vendor to get a refund.
