Just get a waterproof lockbox or something, put your stuff in and throw it in a creek/pond near your house.

SWIM's Uncle's Pet Unicorn told me his Puppy psychically messaged him and told him that his great Grandfather used to store his drugs under a step in the back garden, they're great for getting up easily, especially ones that stick out a little as you can get granite blocks as steps that are FAR too heavy for even 2 people to use, and use a car jack to lift them up.

(The SWIM statement was a dig at how much it's popped up recently, not me being serious)

Also a note, whatever you store them in outside, make sure to include a lot of rice, or if you can get it, silica gel, this will absorb any moisture that gets in.

Also, if you have an attic and a good neighbour, it's a good idea to take a few bricks out in an inconspicious place so you can easily access the drugs, but they are not on your property.

I'm not sure how it works with warrants, but I'm sure if they suspect the drugs are in a different address they will need a fresh warrant, which gives you time to have them destroyed, don't quote me on this though!

Oh also, as a sidenote, you should try to keep 2 stashes. One that has a decent portion of cash and drugs, and a second main stash, as most times if you're raided and they find it, and that's what you're suspected of, they're not gonna carry on searching.

I've seen a really nice setup where a guy had solid wooden flooring that could come up and had a safe in the floor, where he kept loads of cash, scales, baggies, and some drugs...then he kept a second stash with his main supply and a third stash where he kept his money (if they come in with sniffer dogs you're fucked, so best to keep money separate).

But these are only recommended if outside storage isn't a possibility. If you're planning on using stash houses be very careful, several years ago I got SEVERELY burnt by a stash raid...a guy I used was partying really hard on my gear and got raided, but the only thing he had on me was my initial and a phone number for a burner...but he had the information for my other stash houses, so gave them up.
