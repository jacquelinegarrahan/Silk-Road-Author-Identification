Strictly in the technical sense, look for something like marhyde paint stripper. That stuff is extremely nasty to car paint, it will make it bubble and peel right off anywhere it gets on the paint, even a little pindrop. I don't know if it's the same stuff anymore with the VOC regulations and whatnot, but 15 years ago, that stuff was great for paint job prep.
