Title: Re: effects of low-dose mdma
Post by: dew72 on October 04, 2011, 02:36 am
I think mdma's anti-depressant effects have more to do with it offering a therapeutic experience that can change your outlook on life.  The studies with PTSD sufferers used full doses in conjunction with therapy sessions - mdma can be a powerful tool for opening up and addressing internal issues that you subconsciously block yourself off from.  It is not so much what the mdma does, as what it allows YOU to do that can lead to positive changes IMO.

But that said I have no idea what a daily tiny dose would do.  I kind of suspect the effect would be negligible or negative.  Personally I think mdma is best used with long breaks in between.  There are definitely negative physical side effects, but this way they are outweighed by the positive therapeutic experience.
