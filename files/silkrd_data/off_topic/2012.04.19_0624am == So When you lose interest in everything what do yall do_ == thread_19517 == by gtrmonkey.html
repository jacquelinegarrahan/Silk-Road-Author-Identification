Post by: Regicide on April 20, 2012, 06:02 am
There's a misconception that people who do drugs only once a week have no problem whereas someone who uses them everyday has a problem.

The fact is, some people get consumed by the anticipation of doing drugs, and the once a week user may spend the whole week thinking about the trip on the weekend. This could in fact be more of a problem than a moderate dose daily user, who is already high and doesn't spend their time worrying about the weekend, because they can enjoy their time in the present. Everyone is different, and of course many people do drugs occasionally with little thought about it before (at least no more than going to a concert for a band you love). Basically you need to watch out how much the thought of using the drug as well as the acute drug use is affecting you. I think that it is important to give this some thought, as I have quite a few friends who spend way too much time thinking about getting high next, even if they know the next time will be in 5 days. If this is your problem, a long break may be the only solution.
