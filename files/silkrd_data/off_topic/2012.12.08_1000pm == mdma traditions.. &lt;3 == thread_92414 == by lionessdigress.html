okay, so i can't really say that i have a d.o.c., because i love all, indiscriminately... but there's something about that molly.  :-*

i'm excited that i have found the silk road, as i can now have a safe and reliable vendor to buy from without needing to drive into an unsafe area of my city to be ripped off by some random scrub raver. i never wanted a physical drug dealer for these kinds of drugs, so this website really is my personal utopia!

i digress.

tl;dr - i think i have waited an adequate amount of time since my last roll and that my non-drug using girlfriend and i will be on the same level when we take this mdma. (which i am sure is going to be much purer than anything we've ever had.)

sidenote: recommendation on attack dose and booster amounts? i was thinking 150mg each with 75mg boosters an hour and a half after initial dosing. what do you think?

since i am waiting, i've begun compiling all that is needed for us to have an epic night. we are each taking st. john's wort and 100mg of 5-htp a day for two weeks before the roll. two days before we will stop taking both, so that they do not diminish the roll at all. we will post-load with 200mg of 5-htp when we are coming down.

we eat relatively healthy, but i will make sure that we do vegetable juices a few days building into "d-day". something nice and healthy to eat before we drop, which i'd like to do around 6pm. i will also be juicing fresh grapefruit juice to drink during the roll. it REALLY IS amazing and spikes you up when you are dropping a bit... if you haven't had FRESH grapefruit juice, try to! you can taste the difference, 100%.

i bought 25 glowsticks off of amazon for us to do as we please with them. i wish that one of us was capable of lightshows, but we aren't-- one of the ONLY things i miss about raves! but anyway, a bunch of sticks will be in the bathtub with us, lighting up the bubbles i am going to use. incense will fragrant the air and candles will light up the bathroom.

when we get out of the bath, i assume we will be starting to come up. time for oil on the bodies (helping one another, of course), soft bathrobes, and menthol lotion on the feet before the fuzzy socks go on!

after that, it's time for nature to run its course. we have a blacklight and a strobe. i am going to connect my mac to the tv for visualizer purposes, but my itunes doesn't have such great music on it to go with the situation. any recommendations of mixes on soundcloud that i can download would be GREATLY appreciated. any suggestions for music sites in general? let me know!

plenty of soft blankets to roll in, a big fluffy cat and gooey stuff from the toy store to play with, maybe even some body painting. we will have whipped cream, lollipops, gum, and maybe even some poprocks! pacifiers in case of severe clenching, pot, and a pack of newports. she has a small studio space set up in the house with a decent soundsystem, a keyboard, and a synthesizer that we can play with. i think we're going to have a blast!  :D

any other suggestions? what do you do to get ready for your mdma sessions!?

Post by: catfishinmysocks on December 11, 2012, 06:33 am
I don't really buy into pre-loading / post-loading (all that much). I eat well and take vitamins anyway and have never felt any worse after using MDMA than I would otherwise, nor have I noticed any benefit when I have tried to pre/post-load.
There's not much science to support it, at least not properly tested, and contradictory opinions. 5-HTP for example, when taken before you've fully come down could cause serotonin syndrome. However, since loads of people do this and have not experienced serotonin syndrome it may suggest that 5-HTP does little.

MDMA is a substance which gives very varied and subjective experiences at the same dosage. So if you think popping supplements for weeks will make you roll harder, perhaps you will.
