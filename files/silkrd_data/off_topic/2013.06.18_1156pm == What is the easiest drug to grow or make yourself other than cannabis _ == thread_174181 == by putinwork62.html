Title: Re: What is the easiest drug to grow or make yourself other than cannabis ?
Post by: redfunguy on June 20, 2013, 12:55 am
Mushrooms are your best bet.  Way easier than weed (I've grown both) and can be close to as profitable if you can move large amounts.  Start with PF tek and move on to casings.  Make your own pf jars, dont buy them from a vendor or your profit margin will go way down.  At most you will need a couple hundred bucks for tubs, spores, jars and other supplies.  If you are going to do it long term or for profit then drop a couple hundred on a good pressure cooker, it will save you a lot of wasted materials by cutting down on contaminated jars.  Use sporeworks and spores101 for your spores.  Penis envy is a good "cash crop" strain, big fruits and people have a good time on them.  For info on how to grow use shroomery.org and shroomtalk.  A month of incubating your jars and you will have fruits a week after birthing your cakes into the fruiting chamber.  Use the dunk and roll technique to get multiple flushes from every cake.  Good luck, it is great fun.  It's magical to see the little pins come off the cake during fruiting for the first time knowing those little things will turn into big, healthy mushrooms.  Fresh air is a must so you will probably want to grow where you live or where you can hang out every day for a while.
