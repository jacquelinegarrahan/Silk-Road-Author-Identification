Title: Any one of you guys ever try to write a book?
Post by: monrovia on May 27, 2013, 06:06 pm
To give a synopsis of the story in one sentence, "It's about a young, white collar suburban college graduate who's addiction to heroin led him up the ranks of (city edited to protect myself)'s criminal underworld, but not without facing do or die situations, pivotal decisions, remarkable characters, and his conscience which follows him through it all."

I'm still in the brainstorming process. I'm trying to get the bare bones of the storyline in place, chapter by chapter, until I start actually writing the first draft. I might post some updates and excerpts so I can can some encouragement/criticism from the community, as any and all of it would be welcome. I'm desperate to to perform a creative art, and with my firsthand knowledge, along with the research that I can do, this has potential to be a very realistic crime novel. My bigger challenge will be making the plot as entertaining as possible.

The main character is loosely based off me, the situations presented throughout the book will all be influenced by my own life and things that I've seen other people have to deal with, as well as some complete fiction just for the purpose of entertainment.

Basically a young, average man who makes a little more money than the average man his age has a well hidden secret(which I've already given away.) He eventually gets lucky and is faced with an opportunity to move up the dope ladder in a major US city. He takes it, and things start to go really well. His customer base is expanding, he's making money/doing dope for free, and gaining the trust and respect of his new group of friends. Throughout the book, he endures conflicts with police, associates, rival dealers, his family, and himself. I haven't decided yet, but I have an Idea for a hell of an ending.

Anyway, comment if you'd like. I wanna hear if you think I'm a fuckin idiot or if you think I might be able to catch the interest of a few people with this idea if I do things right. I don't really mind if I get all the way through this and never get published. This is a personal project that I'm doing for personal reasons, and I could give a fuck whether people find it entertaining or nite, although it would be a nice bonus :)
