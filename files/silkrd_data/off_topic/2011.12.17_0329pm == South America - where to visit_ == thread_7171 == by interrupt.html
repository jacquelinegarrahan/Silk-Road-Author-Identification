Title: Re: South America - where to visit?
Post by: Julio on December 19, 2011, 07:16 pm
I'm a 6 foot and look white and I never got taken advantage of. But just like everywhere stay out of shifty areas. Medellin is great, lots of younger people. All the nightlife is in La Zona Rosa, bars and clubs every block. If you go in December through January its just one huge party. I've been to the coastal areas such as Santa Marta, Barranquilla and Cartagena. These places are great if you stay in the tourist area.

Like any county the best friend you can make is a younger cab driver.
