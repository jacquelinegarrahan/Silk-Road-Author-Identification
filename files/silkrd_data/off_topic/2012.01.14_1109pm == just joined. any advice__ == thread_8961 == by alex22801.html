If you want to have an extra degree of separation between your personal address and your product, get a 24-hour access UPS box (preferably the biggest one they got).  Make sure that if your package does not fit in your box, you won't have to sign for it in order to get it from behind the counter.  Pick up at inconspicuous hours like 5-10 pm local time.

If you want plausible deniability, buy a domain name and ATTN: a phony title like CEO at your UPS box address.

Or you can just use your real name and real address - chances are no one cares what the fuck you do to yourself.
