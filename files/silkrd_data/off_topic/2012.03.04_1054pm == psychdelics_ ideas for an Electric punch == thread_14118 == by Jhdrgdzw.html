Quote from: Jhdrgdzw on March 05, 2012, 12:11 am
LSD works awesome its what the hippies used, but what about all these new modern chems like 25 i, 2 c c, 2c p etc are any of these guys good for dosing either water base or alcohol base drinks or does that even matter?  :D
I've not tried 25I-NBOMe, but have heard its effects favorably compared to LSD many times.  It is water soluble as far as I'm aware, but I think it may only be sublingually active (have to hold it in your mouth for a while, don't swallow).  So that's probably not the best choice for liquid dosage; blotter would be better.  Research chemicals like 2C-I, 2C-E, 2C-B, 2C-T-2, 2C-T-7 would make good candidates for this experience.  I always dilute my doses of these chemicals in a glass of juice.  Mescaline may also be a good choice.

Personally, I'd rather just ingest my LSD in blotter form: it's convenient, tiny, and can be kept protected from degradation by wrapping in foil.  Once you go mixing such a fragile chemical with various liquids, you should go ahead and dose right away, before it starts to break down.
