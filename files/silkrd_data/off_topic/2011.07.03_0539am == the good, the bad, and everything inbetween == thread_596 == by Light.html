Post by: Light on July 03, 2011, 05:39 am
So I figured there are some people on these forums with a broad range of drug experiences, the tales of which could be very entertaining to read, not to mention offer some valuable information to those seeking to explore those particular drugs.

For those that are interested, post a list of drugs you've tried, along with whatever details you feel pertinent... like how many times, and how you'd rate it. Here's my list:
Marijuana, appr 30 times, never really been a fan
Salvia, 3 times: cool shit, weird out of body experiences, though intensely uncomfortable
LSA (no not LSD): 2 times, mild experiences, not worth the nauseau
Mescaline, 3 times: Incredibly energetic psychedelic, brewed a tea from San Pedro and drinking it ruined the experience
Mushrooms, 4 times: Fuck yeah mushrooms. Taken up to 5.2g. They're notoriously difficult to handle, but nothing compares.
MDMA, maybe 15 times now: Cured my depression, nearly saved my life. Pure MDMA is incredible, the peak of which is a moment of inexplicable, euphoric epiphany. Definitely something everyone should try.
Methamphetamine, 1 time: HOLY FUCK
Oxycontin, 3 times: just made me itchy and lethargic
Ketamine, 1 time: weird, dreamy, trippy, euphoric. overall fun. I'd like to hear what others have experienced with K. Any input?
DMT, probably 30 or 40 times: this is the true universe. The DMT breakthrough experience will unveil the truth of reality, that there is an underlying current not visible to the sober mind, and the cosmos is ours to explore. 10/10 you must experience it to understand
2C-I, 12 times: doses have ranged from 10mg orally to 30mg insufflated, this is one of my favorites. Lucid, beautiful visuals, euphoric and peaceful.
2C-E, 15 times: same dose range as 2c-i, but different experiences. Can be more difficult during the come up, but 2C-E is pure musical heaven. I've rediscovered music because of it, even had multiple ego deaths from snorted doses. You can get lost in a song with 2c-e, amazing stuff.
2C-D, 5 times: tried 3 nootropic doses, but tolerance develops too quickly. Psychedelic doses are extremely light... eh, not a fan.
4-AcO-DMT, 4 times: THE BEST DRUG EVER. Like a superior, cleaner mushroom trip. Beautiful, intense visuals, especially when paired with a MAOI.
Desoxypipadrol, 6 times: most powerful stimulant in history, will keep you awake for 36 hours. Be careful, this shit can kill you at 10mg but be perfect at 7mg...
5-MeO-Mipt, 10 times: Fuck yeah, Moxy. Like the tryptamine version of MDMA, its euphoric, tactile, and gets visual i the end.
GHB, twice: Like being drunk, but without any of the negatives. Fun as fuck.

let's see what you've got.  ;D I'm interested in hearing about some real LSD stories, plus other RCs.
