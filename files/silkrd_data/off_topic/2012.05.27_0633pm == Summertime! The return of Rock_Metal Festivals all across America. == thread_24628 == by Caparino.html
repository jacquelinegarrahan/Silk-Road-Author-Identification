With June right around the corner, Warped Tour, MayhemFest, and Summer Slaughter are soon to arrive to a city near you. I'm not sure about MayhemFest, since it's the same crappy main-stream bands headling again this year, but I'm sure to visit Warped Tour and Summer Slaughter for a couple of my favorite bands.

Warped Tour: After The Burial, Born of Osiris, Chelsea Grin
Summer Slaughter: Veil of Maya and The Contortionist.

All Metalheads weigh in with your favorite bands!

By the way, if you haven't heard of them yet: I recommend you give After The Burial a listen. Their vocal message, guitar shredding amazingness, and instrumental synergy is rivaled by no other band. Just my opinion of course, but give these couple of songs a listen: Cursing Akhenaten, Drifts, Berserker. If you're a fan of amazing riffs and breakdowns, these songs provide amazing examples of both. In addition, they're fucking inspiring and provide me with a motive to lead a life without mediocrity of any sort.
