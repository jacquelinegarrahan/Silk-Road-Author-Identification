Title: Re: Debating on taking MDMA first time
Post by: mochill on October 23, 2012, 03:58 am
It depends on which particular pill you bought from ST. Is it the 120mg ones, or the 200mg one? For a first time, your typical dosage should be 100 mg (unless you are <75KG in which case 80 would be good). You will SERIOUSLY enjoy it. Seriously.

You might not want to do it alone. While I'm sure there are many who enjoy rolling alone, there's just a spirit of camaraderie and together-ness that binds people who are flying high. Think about all your pot buddies, all the people you enjoy hanging out with getting high. Now multiply that awesome closeness by 100 and mix in some killer Electronic Dance Music. That's how epic it can be. My first time I tried it, I was extremely glad to be around thousands of people feeling music coursing through my very soul.

The first time is always the MOST magical, just like LSD. You COULD do it alone... but you SHOULD have a Plan E ready. Plan E would be going out to a kickass club playing awesome new music (EDM baby) that you can dance to with a couple of friends who are rolling with you. Buy a pack of menthol cigarettes (as mentholy as you can get it's sooo goooood), and get a bunch of water to drink. Don't do alcohol, don't smoke weed, don't take anything other than water. Split a pill to start, finish the other half 3 hours in. You need friends because talking is awesome and partying is even better.

MDMA is a social drug. Do it with people! There are many many guides on the forums on what to do high. I've outlined some basics (1 pint of water an hour is good) so do some research if you want to really roll hard.
