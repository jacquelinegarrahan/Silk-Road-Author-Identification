amount for a proper trip:
acid- 1.5-2.5 strong tabs (usualy in the 100-150ug range)
shrooms- 2.5-3.5 totaly dried grams. 30-35 fresh.

duration:
acid: comes up within an hour, lasts about 8-12 hours. you peak at around 3-4 hour mark. then for me it comes in waves. starts with a real pyschadelic mental head space and then you seem to 'earn' the most beautiful fluid visuals by the end.

shrooms: havnt done in a good while, comes up in around an hour aswell? from memory. far shorter duration maybe 4-6 hours? different aspect visualy- less fluid visuals more distortion. head space is more head fucky for me, for lack of a better word. less motor control and the like than acid.

acid- deeper thoughts and concepts. more intrinsic visuals. head trip much more challenging but rewarding.
shrooms- deeper head fuck, visuals are less constant- distort more and have their own distinct characteristics.

overall- i choose acid over shrooms. all down to the situation though and personal preference. Also, people react differently to the same drugs, so just try both and decide. both are worth it.
Title: Re: Acid or Shrooms?
Post by: v01d on January 02, 2012, 08:07 pm
Acid: Around 10 hours total I'd say. Peak is 4-5  hours or so. Acid is more of a rational trip I'd say. The headspace is clear, and you can think and function better than shrooms. 2 tabs would be great. (Around $20)

Shrooms: Around 8 hours. Peak is 3 hours or so. It's more...confusing, and can be a difficult headspace. You're more likely to be couch-locked, and less in control. 2.5g-3.5g.($25)

Summary:

Acid lasts longer, and you are more in control and less likely to have a bad trip, but if you do it lasts awhile if you are not good at getting rid of them.
Shrooms are shorter, more expensive, and don't last as long, but some people consider them more spiritual and say you take more away from the trip.
