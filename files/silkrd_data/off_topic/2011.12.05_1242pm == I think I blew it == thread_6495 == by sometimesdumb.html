Is it a narcotic or just an antibiotic?

If you falsified a narcotic prescription that is a fourth degree felony, the lowest felony in the code. The maximum jail time is 18 months, up to $10000 fine (I'm not sure of the amount) loss of driver's license for 6 months plus DEDR penalties and other assessments over $1000. But a first offense is eligible for diversion programs where you would end up without a conviction record. A first offense of this type would not mean state prison or county time. Also in  depending on the facts this type of case may be downgraded to where other lesser charges could be negotiated. You could also be charged with theft/fraud offenses. It all depends.
