Title: Re: Favorite DMT Albums
Post by: mrroboto187 on August 17, 2011, 03:00 am
Fleet Foxes - Helplessness Blues

Took pharmahuasca for the first time and listened to this album with my audiophile headphones (I HIGHLY recommend good, comfortable headphones if you enjoy tripping and listening to music). Had a 3 hour eargasm. Yes, I listened to the album about 3 full times...

They definitely are in the know when it comes to DMT, the lead singer keeps referring to an imaginary place (Innisfree) throughout the album. If you pay attention and especially if you're in that mindstate you get the subtle hints he's throwing out there. Grown Ocean is probably my favorite track off there but seriously, they're all amazing (except the horrendous sax solo at the end of An Argument, though I think there is a really deep meaning behind it).

Definitely check it out  :D
