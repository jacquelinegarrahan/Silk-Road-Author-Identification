Title: Re: purchasing yayo in amsterdam
Post by: WhereAmI on October 23, 2012, 11:37 pm
I lived here for nine months and I really wouldn't suggest getting anything from any of the street dealers. Most are homeless guys trying to rip off tourists and I can't tell you how many I met who had been sold sugar or salt or sand. And it's super sketch. Your best bet is to go to one of the regular clubs like the Winston on Cheeky Monday for DnB night, Trouw, Paridiso, Studio 80 and hang out around the smokiing area and look for people who may have extras to sell, usually if they're rolling is a good way to tell if they have a good contact. Luckily good drugs are very good in Amsterdam and can be affordable. I was good friends with a drug dealer while I was there and was so spoiled. I would expect to pay 60-80 euro a gram as a tourist, though wouldn't be surprised if it was higher because that's the non-Dutch tax. Also, try anyone working at a hostel. Look for ex-pats from England or Spain. They usually know where to get the hook up. Hope that helps!
