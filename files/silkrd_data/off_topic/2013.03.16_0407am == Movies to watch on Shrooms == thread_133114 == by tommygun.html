Title: Re: Movies to watch on Shrooms
Post by: The Scientist on March 25, 2013, 03:56 am
When I recommended Bambi, I had only seen clips of it during the tail-end of an acid trip, and I assumed it would be perfect for tripping, because it is one of the most artistically beautiful films ever made.

Yesterday I watched the whole film during the height of an acid trip, and I never cried so hard in my life !

And I started crying even before Bambi's mother was shot dead. The whole movie, from the opening scenes, is imbued with a sense of melancholy that is subtle when you're watching it sober, but is really brought to the surface when you're tripping. Even the 'cute' scenes seemed very sad to me.

Although it mad me sad (to the point of almost having a bad trip), it was the most beautiful film I've ever seen. No other film can beat this in visual beauty, as far as I'm concerned. Especially when you're tripping. Modern special effects seem cheap compared to the beautiful artwork of early Disney films.....

I still recommend Bambi, but be prepared to cry a lot.
