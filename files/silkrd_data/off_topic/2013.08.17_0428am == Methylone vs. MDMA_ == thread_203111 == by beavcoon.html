Title: Re: Methylone vs. MDMA?
Post by: Jack N Hoff on August 17, 2013, 10:40 am
Methylone is bk-MDMA.  It is simply MDMA with a beta ketone.

Some people prefer MDMA and some people prefer methylone.  The MDMA roll lasts slightly longer.  MDMA is more neurotoxic.  You should not reuse MDMA for a long time after use.  Methylone is speedier.  MDMA is about twice as potent milligram for milligram.  MDMA has more negative side effects.  You can take higher doses of methylone than you can of MDMA.
