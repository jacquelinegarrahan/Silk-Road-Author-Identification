Just a quick heads up, please make sure that it is a crystalline white powder if you do continue through with this. If the product you receive is of a brown, gooey consistency, this is 4-AcO-DMT that has been degraded into 4-HO-DMT which is a markedly different trip. Unfortunately this degradation from 4-AcO-DMT to 4-HO-DMT happens extremely quickly if not properly vacuum sealed, and because people buy bulk but can't sell it quickly, there are a lot of vendors peddling degraded 4-AcO-DMT in the brown, goeey form and stating there is no difference. This is entirely false.
