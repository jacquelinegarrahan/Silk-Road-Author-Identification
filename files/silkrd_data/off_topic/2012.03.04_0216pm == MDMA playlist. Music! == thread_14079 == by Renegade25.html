Post by: grandaddyturtle on March 09, 2012, 03:29 am
I like to listen to dubstep while rolling.  It's a very unique genre with a broad range of sounds, so even if you're not a fan of Skrillex or the "brostep" sound you should find something you like.  I'll try to cover a broad range here. If you have a subwoofer handy, or some good headphones with good bass response, I suggest checking some of these tunes out.  Even if not, most of them have nice ear candy in the mids/highs, although some of the "deeper" stuff might not.

Emalkay - "When I Look at You"
Plastician - "Japan"
Psy:Am & Stinkahbell - "Don't Tell Mum About Ibiza"
Biome - "Space"
Trolley Snatcha - "Make My Whole World"
Tek One - "Broken String"
The Others - "Africa VIP"
Ellie Goulding - "Lights" (remixes by Dream and Bassnectar)
AFK & Dks - "Shining"
Kryptic Minds - "Can't Sleep"
La Roux - "In For The Kill" (Skream's Let's Get Ravey Remix)
Eddie K - "Serial Killer"
Subscape - "Universal"
Freestylers - "Cracks" (Flux Pavilion Remix)

I could go on, if you're lazy/have ADD like me I'd definitely also get Rusko & Caspa's Fabriclive 37 DJ Mix.  Probably one of the best mixes of the genre, to come out, like ever.

I'd also recommend getting these songs in lossless FLAC/AIFF/WAV if you can, it definitely adds to the experience.  Especially with things in higher ranges, like white noise "whooshes" and hi hats.
