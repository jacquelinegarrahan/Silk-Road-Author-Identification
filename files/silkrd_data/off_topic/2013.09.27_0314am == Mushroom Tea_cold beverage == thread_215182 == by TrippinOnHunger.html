Title: Re: Mushroom Tea/cold beverage
Post by: XTCandLSDHappyME on September 27, 2013, 08:44 am
I find it is best if your mushrooms are dried out properly so you can crumble them to dust with your fingers. Put your mushrooms in a pan with some boiling water and slowly simmer for about 20 minutes. Estimate how much water will boil off so for example. If there is 3 of you then pour 5 cups of water into the pan and after 20 minutes boiling there will be 3 cups worth left. Once boiled strain the mushrooms out. use a colander or even a pair of tights or something so you can squeeze all the juices out of the soggy shrooms into your brew. Then thats it. Fill up your cups! You can add tea bags if you want and milk or whatever because the taste is vile but I don't bother usually I just wait for it to cool a bit and down the full cup in 1 go. Another alternative is to chuck some jelly cubes into the pan with your brew in and leave it to cool and set. You will then have shroom jelly which is much easier to stomach if you hate the taste. Hope this helps  :)
