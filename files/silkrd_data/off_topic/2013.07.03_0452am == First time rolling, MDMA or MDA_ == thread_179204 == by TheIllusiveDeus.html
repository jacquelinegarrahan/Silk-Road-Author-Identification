Title: Re: First time rolling, MDMA or MDA?
Post by: Dingo Ate My Drugs on July 03, 2013, 05:17 am
MDMA is amazing for dancing / partying or chilling out. It gives you a lot of energy and clears your head, but it's a different kind of energy than Amphetamine gives you.
Personally I live stimulants and being full of energy, so I enjoy 10mg high quality amphetamine with MDMA. It just gives me that bit more energy and makes me feel even better. Too much speed though will overpower and kill t he MDMA roll.

MDA I've only tried once (from Dreamension - Awesome vendor all round). It was nothing like MDMA really. It had some similarities. It made me happy, but it made me want to sit down and close my eyes and see things. Cannabis really intensified the visuals too. I closed my eyes and just smiled and saw colours and shapes. I could think of something and I would see it. I could control what I saw. I really liked it. I haven't been very far down the psychedelic path yet, but this was the best visual experience I had ever had.

MDA didn't "open me up" in the way that MDMA did though. MDMA enables me to talk to anybody and they feel like they're my best friend.
MDA made me not to social. But everybody reacts differently. My first MDMA experiences were my best ever experiences with drugs. Unimaginable, intense happiness and everything was just amazing and perfect.

Whatever you do, enjoy yourself. You won't regret it.
