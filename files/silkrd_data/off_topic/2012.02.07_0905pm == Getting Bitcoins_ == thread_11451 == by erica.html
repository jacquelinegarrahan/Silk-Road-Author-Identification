In my opinion this is the simplest way (if you're in the US):

Go to Get-Bitcoin.com and select the cash by mail option. Send them your cash in an envelope to the address they give you after placing your order using a fake return address and name to eliminate any possibility of the bitcoins being traced to you. When placing your order use the same fake name you used on the return address. Be sure to use a real, anonymous email so you can keep in touch with them. When using this payment method I just have the coins deposited straight into my SR wallet since the coins aren't traceable using cash by mail. It takes like 5 days for them to give you your coins but its worth the wait because it's so easy. I've had two successful coin purchases in the last 3 weeks using this method. There is a 5% transaction fee but its better than what you would pay at most other BC exchanges.
