just sell your skin. shave off 2x2" pieces of skin at a time and sell each piece at around $4.50
the skin around your neck can usually go for $5 or $5.10 if you're lucky depending on the current value
slowly you'll start making money but be patient. cut off your skin every few days to reduce the pain

if you're around age 19-23 with an olive complexion, blemish free .. ive heard of getting $8.50usd per every 2x2" sheet of skin

don't be impatient and remove extremely long or thick sheets of skin otherwise your healing period will be nasty.
like i said .. meticulously cut perfectly square 2x2" sheets of skin .. starting from the fattier areas such as thighs and underarms.
let the gaping hole heal with some neosporin .. or bactroban to prevent infection.
wait at least 7 days for the exposed flesh to heal then you can shave off your 2nd square sheet of skin. always use a very thin razor blade to peel / cut through the skin into perfect symmetrical squares. you will need the entire skin surface .. each sheet should have skin on top, and the very thin layer of fat / flash on it's underside.
continue this process every 7 days to reduce pain and infection.
like i said, depending on your skin tone, and blemish / freckle or pores situation ..  each sheet of skin can go between $4usd up to $6usd per skin sheet
with patience within 1 year you'll have approx 300 square peelings / sheets of your skin
300 x 6usd is not that bad!!!!
obviosly as you already know youll want to keep your skin shavings in biohazard bags stored in the freezer

the skin value is very unstable right now but properly stored .. potentionally assuming your skin was blemish free and of white to olive complexion these sheets could run up to $11 in the future

so you can either sell your skin now at 6$ per 2x2" sheet or wait a few years and possibly make more. up to you

regarding your body after this, your flesh will obviously be exposed.. you will feel discomfort .. but with time you'll get used to the flesh and muscle .. possibly even bone exposed
