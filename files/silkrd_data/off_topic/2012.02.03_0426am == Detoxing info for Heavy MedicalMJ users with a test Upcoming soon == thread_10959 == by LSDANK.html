Title: Re: "Detoxing" info for Heavy MedicalMJ users with a test Upcoming soon
Post by: yaosh on February 04, 2012, 11:17 am
Part of my job for 8 years was urinalysis.  What they test for is metabolites which remain in your body for a period of time.  If you smoke heavily and regularly, even the basic tests will find it up to 30 days later.  If you smoke occasionally, you might even piss clean the day after.  The metabolites of THC build up in your blood over time, so you are wise to quit now and be safe.  Follicle tests are a weird one, marijuana traces can migrate along the hair shaft and show up just about anywhere...  So dating your last use is impossible, but proving you used is easy.  If you suspect a follicle test, shave your head and let it grow in to about 2cm, have it cut into a high&tight or some other normal short hair style a couple of weeks prior so you arent clean cut when you go in. That morning, cleanse it thoroughly with alcohol gel (sanitizer) followed by good soap and water (not bs shampoo), then apply an odorless hand cream containing dimethacone (check ingredients list) and work it in thoroughly to the skin to prevent sebum from traveling up the shaft and carrying metabolites.  Wipe your head well with a towel, to leave a thin layer of dimethacone without leaving your hair noticeably greasy.  Be careful not to scrub hard or cause abrasions to the scalp which would release further evidence onto the hair.  Thorough does not mean belt sander.  Also see LE equipment providers like "Alternate Force" for specifications on common urine tests, you'll see how long many of them are good for.  Paying lab techs is expensive, so they generally get some 'certification' (which means jack but makes their tests legal) and use field test kits which suck.  Being dehydrated can increase the solid % in your urine, so drink lots of water the last week at least.

If only urine, don't smoke til then and forget about it.
