I think it usually goes for 1-2 metric fucktons. That's in GBP, though, idk what the American conversion would be.

Let's do some guesstimation, though. Currently, on SR, there's a ~5mg patch for $100, and a ~10mg patch for $230. We can conservatively estimate the retail value to be $20/mg. Since there's 1000mg in a gram, that would be a retail price of $20000/g. Now, hopefully the "bulk" would mean you get a lower price, and assuming this person is a "great" friend, let's say you're getting it for $5000.

We also must bring into question the quality of the product. Who knows what purity this product is at? IIRC the three DEA fentanyl busts I've heard about (1g each) ranged from 60%-80% purity. The lack of purity means it's worth less. It also might be "China White," which are any of the 12 (known) clandestinely produced analogues of fentanyl. As I understand it, these are commonly produced on the cheap in Russia, and would significantly reduce the cost of the product, which means it could be sold for less.

You might want to find more information about it.
