Post by: Internal Freedom on June 12, 2013, 02:37 am
Just use a normal pipe, don't melt under it unless you have an oil pipe.

I have a fairly good sized gandalf-pipe. I pack about 0.1 of weed and put the DMT on top. I use a butane lighter and keep the lighter flame about half a centimeter away from the DMT. After a while it starts to melt into the herb and then you can just smoke the whole thing normally.

The first time I did DMT I put some freebase into a pipe, lit the entire thing, and had an amazing trip even though I clearly burned it. I think smoking it in that way just results in a small loss of product, you still get the effects for sure.
