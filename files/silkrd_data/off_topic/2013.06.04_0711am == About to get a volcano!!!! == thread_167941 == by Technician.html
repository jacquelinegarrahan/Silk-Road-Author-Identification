Post by: Vape_Headie on June 11, 2013, 04:17 am
Sorry dude. A little late to the party, here. I've owned a digit for (I forget how many) years. You are making a wise investment. I still smoke the occasional blunt and bowl, but I've been a happy vap'r for years now. You will not be disappointed unless he's mistreated it (dropping it is the only thing I can think of). Fuck the EZ Valve. Take a half hour to learn how to use a solid valve. I (at the recommendation of Storz&Bickel) wash my valve parts in the dishwasher. They didn't suggest this part, but I like to soak them ISO for a half hour or so before I put them in. The valve comes out shiny and brand new. I would also suggest a second valve if you ever have an extra 50 bucks laying around. It's nice when you reup with some really good shit and you don't want to wait to clean the valve or use a super dirty valve. I'm going to stop gushing on this thing, but I love mine. I have taken very good care of it and it has never had to be serviced.

Now, for bags: I like the Volcano brand bags, but you can totally use a Turkey size oven bag from the grocery store. I don't like them because they are fat and you have to hold the valve while filling or it will fall off. They are MUCH quieter, though. If stealth is at all an issue, I'd say go for the oven bags. They are made for cooking a turkey, so you don't really have to worry that you're inhaling plastic from the bag. That's about all I've got. Oh, and I hope you like having company over because there will be plenty of it. Congrats, dude. Welcome to the club. Oh, also. For portability, Iolite is the bomb, although the Magic Flite Launch Box looks like it might be even better.

To canuckboy: I bought my Volcano because I was dating a chick with asthma. The fact that she doesn't smoke could be a selling point for getting a vape. There are also plenty of other viable choices that are much cheaper. But the Volcano is DEFINITELY the Mercedes of vapes. It's even German.    ;D
