Title: Lou Reed Appreciation Thread
Post by: ShieLdz on February 23, 2012, 05:06 am
I know this WAY off topic for SR, but I've noticed a few posts relating to Lou Reed and/or the Velvet Underground - must be that SR-people just dig Lou or something, idk - so i figured I'd make a topic, see if anyone wanted to share their Lou reed stories ...

For me, I can honestly say that Lou Reed changed my life. He (and his music) saw me through some pretty tough times. All in all   Lou taught to me quit caring about waht others (and myself) thought about me; I eventually accepted my [perceived] abnormalities, my anxieties, and my insecurities in a very existential way. so much so I almost began  treasuring my faults to point that I pretty made up my identity - because in the end now matter how much like an outcast you feel like, Lou will always there to let you know you're not alone...

a tad sentimental I know, but that's Lou. Oh, Lou also inspired me to learn the guitar....never seen him live though  :'(

Anybody else got love for Lou and the VU?
