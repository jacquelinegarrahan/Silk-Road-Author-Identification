for now though, I have come up with a ghetto way for smoking budder which I shall share with you all in case someone searches the forums in the future:

-Unfold the end of a paperclip to about a 45 degree angle.
-swap the folded-out end through the budder a bit to get a very small amount stuck on it.
-dangle the swabbed end of the paperclip into your $5 oil burner and just let it hang there.
-use a lighter or candle to heat up the bowl.
-you can either take your hand off the lighter and touch the end of the paperclip to the glass, or just wait until the inside of the bubble gets hot enough for the budder to drip off on its own in a sizzling, delicious, blaze of glory.

Got this stuff from Budderman, btw, and its fucking incredible.  its so pure it leaves almost zero residue in the bowl...no possibility for burnt after-taste like with some oils.
