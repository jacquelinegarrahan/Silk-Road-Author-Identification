Title: Hungry on LSD
Post by: Vape_Headie on June 26, 2013, 12:07 am
Does anybody else crave food during an LSD trip? It's been a few years since I last tripped on LSD (will soon thx to Tess, though), and I honestly can't be certain if it was real or RC as I didn't test it. Every time, whether I eat before or not, I always crave food. The second trip was the only time I gave into the urge. I wanted nachos :o, so I drove to the (very, very close) grocery store and wandered aimlessly until I happened across beans, grated cheese, and chips. I couldn't really read words, so I just went on visual. I actually made it out of the store with those specific ingredients. I got home and made the most disgusting plate of nachos I've ever seen. There were probably around 50-60 chips on a large plate, with half a family-size can of re-fried beans, and probably an inch of grated cheese. It took forever to microwave, but I digress. I started to eat it and got about a quarter of the way through and, of course, started to feel incredibly ill. Anyway, it turned into a real shit trip with me in the fetal position on my bed for the next 3 or 4 hours  ::) Since then, I've avoided food during these supposed LSD trips. Any thoughts? LOL
