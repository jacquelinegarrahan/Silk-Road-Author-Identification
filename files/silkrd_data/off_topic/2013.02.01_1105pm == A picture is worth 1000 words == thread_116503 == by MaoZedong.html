Title: A picture is worth 1000 words
Post by: MaoZedong on February 01, 2013, 11:05 pm
One thing that really grinds my gears is when I click on a weed vendors reviews and I start reading, and this person has literally written a NOVEL to describe each strain in this vendors sample pack. Like dude, I understand that all weed is different but I don't need you to describe the exact details of each of your sample strains that you get because when I place my order, it will probably be from a different grower/harvest/strain.

That's why with weed vendors, don't read into the lengthy reviews too much. Reviews should be based on their packaging and the dankness of their nugs and then the vendors should post a picture of their current strain. I only deal with one weed vendor and I trust that he knows his shit more than any other vendor on the road.

Yeah, just have wasted a little too much time reading silly weed reviews sorry  ;)
