Title: Giving blood seemingly intensifying psilocybin trip.
Post by: JonBones on June 22, 2012, 03:34 am
First of all, is this the right forum for drug related talk? I was thinking about posting on the drug safety board but it's not totally related.

I had this ounce of psilocybin mushroom a while ago. No idea on strain just some random shit I got from the streets. I dosed 2g's once after giving blood(like 5 hours after) and it was one of the strongest psychedelic experience I've ever had. As I was coming out of my magical sleep, I couldn't explain what I was seeing. It's like the parts of my brain involved in language were affected and I couldn't have an internal monologue. Totally the weirdest feeling(although not unpleasant) I've ever experienced.

Since then I've tripped on 3.5g's of the same batch and everything was less intense and seemingly the language parts of my brain weren't affected as much.

I'm just curious if anyone ever tripped after giving blood and noticed a difference? I hadn't taken mushrooms for a couple years before that though, maybe that could explain it. The trips before were lsd ( like 3 one hit trips and two 2 hit trips, lsd in a sugar cube, no idea purity or amount, which is one of the main reasons I'm now on silk road).

I'm probably going to go back to give blood just to test it out and see if I can verify it does intensify trips. Anyone knowledgeable in neuroscience/medicine have a theory?
