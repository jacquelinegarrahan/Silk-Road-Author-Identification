Depends on your lifestyle. If you want to trip with friends and they're more available during night/day, then that answers it for you. If you want to trip alone, then pick a time that you'll have the lowest chance to need to function: days off of work (or even the night before a day off of work, if it's a long-lasting psych), days off of school, that sort of thing. And for me, I have to have some visual stimulation if I'm beyond functional, but everyone's different. Though going for a walk during a bright day on a mild psych can be fun, too.
