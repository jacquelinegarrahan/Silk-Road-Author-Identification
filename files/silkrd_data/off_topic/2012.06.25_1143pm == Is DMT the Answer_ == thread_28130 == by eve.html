My Short Answer: Load 5-10mg, smoke. Later... Load 20-30mg, smoke. Go upwards by 5mg for subsequent doses, if necessary.

Dose will vary, but 75mg is far too high for an initial go at DMT. The 'standard' breakthrough range is usually from 20mg to 40mg. This is best achieved through a vaporizer, but a meth pipe will work. Look into "The DMT Machine" it is very simple to make and works well.

Smoking technique is everything with DMT so it may not work perfectly on round 1. It helps to load the DMT evenly instead of one big pile and to apply heat (not too much) evenly.
Also, for my first time I smoked a small (5-10mg) dose to get my bearings with the substance before loading a breakthrough dose.

Anything above 50mg is probably wasting some DMT, likely by overheating.
