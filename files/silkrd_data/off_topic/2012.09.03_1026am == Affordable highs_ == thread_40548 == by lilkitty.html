Post by: Ben on September 06, 2012, 12:24 am
Well, pot does not have to be smoked to be effective. You can eat it and end up with similar results, although they will likely take quite some time before you notice them.

As for the problem you suffer from, you'll probably be best off with medication for both. This could include something like tramadol for the pain and xanax for the anxiety, which may be an interesting experience if used at the same time (i never have).

I suppose a warning is in order though, as mentioned the combination of alcohol and benzo's, while synergetic in hyptnotic effect, can be very dangerous. Considering how many people do this anyway and are perfectly fine, the practical risk probably isn't that large. One downside i found about it is that it can give you complete anterograde amnesia, which can be quite alarming to some. This already happens at relatively small doses, larger doses of the combination will put you at risk for more severe effects like respiratory depression that can eventually result in death.

Many sedatives have this problem though. Alcohol alone can put you in a coma for a couple of days if taken rapidly in large amounts. One way to limit this risk is by not drinking spirits straight, but limiting yourself to beer, wine or mix drinks with similar alcohol content. This will not reduce the effects of equal doses, but it does provide the benefit that you are more likely to fall asleep spilling your last glass instead of just passing out with no idea of when(/if?) you'll come to.
