Hi youngblood,

You can see the feedback for an item as long as the vendor still has that item listed. If the vendor leaves up your custom listing (or perhaps has more than 1 listed, despite knowing that only one person will buy it), then everyone will be able to see it.
When a vendor de-lists an item, the feedback associated with that item cannot be viewed by buyers (in my experience, at least - I'm open to correction on this, but I'm fairly sure this is the way it works at the moment.)

I agree it would be better if the feedback remained, but I think the general idea behind this is so that people can see recent feedback of items that are still listed.
