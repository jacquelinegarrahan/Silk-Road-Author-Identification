Title: Re: any experienced Mescaline users here?
Post by: sharkonwhisky on April 30, 2012, 10:09 am
Mescaline is pretty special, very warm and benign, quite distinct in nature from the tryptamines, I feel it would be really hard to have a bad time on it. Just hands down great stuff. I would definitely recommend access to nature, preferably on a beautiful day, to get the most out of your mescaline experience. Pure mescaline is more stimulating than ingesting in the form of cacti, most people seem to prefer pure mescaline but some prefer the whole plant experience...makes it much harder to ingest a full dose though. Peyote in particular has many more alkaloids than San Pedro so it will be quite a distinct experience from pure mescaline, while being very much in the same ball park. The different forms mescaline comes in...hcl, sulphate, citrate, acetate etc, will only differ in their molecular weights, so each require slightly different dosages to get to the equivilant place. Hcl is more potent by weight than sulphate for example so less of it is needed.
