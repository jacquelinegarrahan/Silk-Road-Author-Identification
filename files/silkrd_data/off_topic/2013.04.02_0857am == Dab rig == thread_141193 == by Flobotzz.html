I have a ROOR rig with a glass dome and Ti nail. It's a 45 cm. Not too big, not too small. Works perfectly for me. I would recommend a dome for a dab beginner. Quartz all the way. It holds heat well, taste the best, and just generally are easier on the throat (plus no seasoning the nail). Next onto glass:

Post by: wtfisgoingon on April 23, 2013, 03:13 am
Ive been making and smoking honey oil since '02 and always just use homemade portable hot knife tip.

1lb propane tank - any hardware store or walmart $5
pencil flame tip for soldering - $10-15
kitchen knife - heat center of knife til red hot bend it until pointing nearly straight down
big safety pin - stretch out and curve the sharp pointy tip slightly
empty bic pen (melt the air hole closed)

The tip of knife will easily hang off tip of torch which will keep it red hot. tank lasts 45-50 hours.

1g oil is approx 150-200 hoots. True budder is best concentrate (REAL budder not the crap "budderman" sells lol). 1g budder will get a noob FRIED bout 45-50 times.
