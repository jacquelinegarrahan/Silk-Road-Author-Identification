Title: Re: Grow question concerning jiffy pots
Post by: AfternoonDelight on May 03, 2012, 07:50 pm
If the roots are coming out of the bottom, you'd best be repotting soon or you could get root rot.
I would just take the jiffys and bury then 2/3 into a larger pot, like around the size of a 2L soda bottle.
Also, put a fan on them to strengthen the stem for outdoors, if that's what you're planning.
Title: Re: Grow question concerning jiffy pots
Post by: cindylove on May 03, 2012, 11:32 pm
5in sounds a bit tall for a week old plant. Make sure they are getting enough light and that the lights are as close as possible without touching the plant. You might want to add more lights to increase the intensity. Also, a good tip for using fluorescents in to mix the yellow and white lights to make sure you plants recieve the full spectrum of light they need.
