If we're talking illegal drugs only, my list would be as follows:

1. MDMA (see above)

2. LSD - not a huge fan of psychedelics, but acid opened up some doors in my mind that I never had access to before. I wouldn't call it a particuarly life-changing experience like many people would, but I'm very glad I got to experience it for myself.

3. Amphetamines (i.e. adderall) - Longer-lasting and cheaper than cocaine and much less neurotoxic than meth. It's my go-to "get some shit done" workhorse.
