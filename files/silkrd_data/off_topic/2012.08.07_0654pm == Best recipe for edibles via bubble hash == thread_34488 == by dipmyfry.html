Use either, but clarify the butter before you merge the hash.  Clarifying is the process of removing water and milk-based solids from the butter, and the technique is simple:

1.  Place the butter in a small saucepan over medium heat
2.  Bring to a gentle boil, lower the heat if it is a "rolling" boil
3.  It will foam, wait till the foam gets thicker, the ,melted butter turns slightly brown, and there are milk solids on the bottom
4.  Skim the foam off the top
5.  Carefully pour the melted butter through a cheesecloth to capture the milk solids
6.  Voila, you have clarified ghee that you can use for cannabutter, or to melt your hash inside of


As for recipes...  I like cheesecake, or just spreading the butter on toast. :)
