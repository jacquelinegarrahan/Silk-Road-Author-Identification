Title: Re: Weed nearly ready - How to dry and cure?
Post by: UKGrower on July 11, 2012, 02:27 am
55 is a little on the low side, and the longer you can extend the drying time, the better (within reason, of course).  I aim for around 60-65, or I should say I hope for 60-65, since I don't really do anything much to control it.  Your rh will probably rise as the temperature drops anyway. I find that any dark cupboard in my house is usually around the 18 degree/65 rh mark, although your mileage may vary, depending on where you live.  Still, 55 isn't too bad, as long as you jar the buds whilst there is still some moisture in them.

One other possibility for lack of smell is harvesting too early.  What method are you using to judge harvest time?  Scope, guesswork or breeder time?  It's probably the heat that has caused your issues, but proper harvest timing can definitely affect how strong the weed smells.
