Enjoy. It's a fun ride.

The psychosis is every bit as fucked up as you have heard, but in my opinion well worth it. Try to keep dosage small and resist the urge to just keep bumping. That might not be possible, but at least try.

Two disclaimers...

1) Don't mix and match on MDPV. Pepper is fucked up all on it's own. But it's manageable. If you start adding substances, you go off the rails real quick.

2) The psychosis is subtle at first. You are going to think you found the motherload of awesome drugs. And that everything is fine. The shadow people will drift in slowly, so at first you will just pass it off. By the time you have a break, it will be a big one. But as fucked as that experience will be, it will be mind opening as long as you can attribute it to the MDPV and not believe any of the shit you think while you are on it. I have a friend who went bat shit. In one of his hour and a half showers while on a pv binge he had the eureka moment that all of his troubles were caused by a friend of his who hit him with a bat in 5th grade, 27 years earlier.

Don't believe the MDPV.

But don't buy the hype either. It's a good ride.
