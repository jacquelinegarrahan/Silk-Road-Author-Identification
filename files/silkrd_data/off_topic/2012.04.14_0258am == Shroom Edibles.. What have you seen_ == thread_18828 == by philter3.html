Most of us have had "Shroom chocolates"... these are basically shroom flour (ground fruiting bodies) mixed into chocolate and poured into a mold.

I myself am fond of "choclatl funghuasca".. which is basically shroom chocolates with ground syrian rue seeds mixed in for good measure. The harmine potentiates the psilocybin. The taste is strong, very bitter and powerful. It makes your testes quake in fear. I'm a fan.

I also am fond of Trippin' (Reese) Balls. Trippin Reese Balls are the classic chocolate peanut butter balls, but instead of margarine you use real butter (I will make them with cannabutter one day) and coconut oil. You then mix in the shroom flour with the powdered sugar and ground graham cracker crumbs. Roll, freeze, then dip in mixed chocolate & parafin wax.

I also have a Blue-My-Mind-Berry Cheesecake. This uses Blue Berry Topping as the carrier medium for the shroom flour (I am nervous about letting the oven work on the actives).

I've also decided this harvest I'm going to make some No-Bake Cookies (aka Cow Patties After The Rain). I think these might be quite stellar.

 What else have people seen, liked, and would like to taste again? Anyone want to give me some ideas? If you suggest it, and I like the idea and try it.. I will feed it to you free of charge :D

Onset is generally not much effected by turning the shroom flour into an edible. Either way you are eating the fungi.. it's all being churned into a big trippy mass in your stomach anyway :D

The edibles thing has three purposes... 
  1. Is cause it's just really neat and cool (or so many people think)
  2. It can be a useful concealment/camoflage tactic
  3. Some people have real problems with the taste/texture when doing large enough doses to really trip hard.

 That said a cook who knows what they are doing can put enough trip into a small amount of food (enough to hold in your palm) to have you chewing the carpet for hours. And it will taste like something off a summer church buffet picnic table.

Joe,
 Try using a dedicated little automatic coffee grinder for grinding your dried shrooms. The finest grind (espresso) setting is best.
 It's best to keep your initial powder grind out of your main food blender until you've started your other ingredients.. that way you don't leave powder in the crevices and cracks.
 Basically grind the shrooms separately, reserve the powder, add the most liquid ingredients of the smoothie first, then the shroom powder and the solids. This is best from a cooking perspective to keep your gear clean of residuals.
