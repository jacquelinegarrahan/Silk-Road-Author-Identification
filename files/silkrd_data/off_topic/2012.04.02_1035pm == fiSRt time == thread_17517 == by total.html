Title: Re: fiSRt time
Post by: boringflooring on April 02, 2012, 11:07 pm
I would message the vendors you're thinking about buying from first.  Just ask them some basic questions.  It's really just a way of introducing yourself and gives you the opportunity to present yourself as a legit customer and not some scammer.  I have good stats, but I still like to message a vendor I've never bought from before.

I personally never finalize early.  For my first transactions i purchased from vendors who welcomed newbies and didn't require early finalization.  Depending on what you're looking to buy, you might have limited options.

Be patient.  Most people, once they figure out how everything on this site works, are so excited to place an order that they don't do all their homework as far as researching a vendor.  A lot of people get scammed this way.

It can take some digging to find a vendor that fits all the parameters of what you're looking for (product, pricing, shipping location, FE, etc), but you  can usually get what you're looking for as long as you're patient.

Good Luck!
