Post by: 328502E on April 12, 2012, 04:48 am
Here are a few popular mixes of EDM.  Listen to a mix and see what kind of music you like, then look up the individual songs.

A State of Trance - Melodic / Classical Trance, this is a popular mix hosted by Armin Van Buuren

International Departures with Myon and Shane 54 - Available free online (google Myon and Shane), these mixes are groove driven and generally melodic.  I recommend this.

Global DJ Broadcast - Another major radio, this time by Markus Schulz.  Trance that is slightly more Electronic

Identity - Produced by Sander Van Doorn, this is my personal favorite.  More electronic than trance

Others you could check out - Corsten's Countdown, Future Sounds of Egypt, Trance Around the World (A&B, very popular)
