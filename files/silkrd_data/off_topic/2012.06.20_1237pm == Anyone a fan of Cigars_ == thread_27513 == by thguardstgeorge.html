Post by: northsidepk on June 20, 2012, 12:47 pm
I am quite the cigar aficionado, been smoking for about 5 years, mainly social events, sports events, vacations, BBQ's etc..

For Cuban Cigars (CC) I prefer Partagas, Punch, Juan Lopez, Ramon Allones, and Montecristo

For Non-Cuban (NC) I prefer Drew Estates Liga Privada, Berger & Argenti, Arturo Fuente, Illusione, Tatuaje, and La Aurora

I have a local smoke shop that has a really comfortable smoking area with HD TV's, pool table and bottle service so I hang out there too some.

As far as personal taste in the type of smoke, I typically go for the more "full" flavor profile but enjoy a medium body cigar as well.

I shy away from mild smokes...to me its like smoking air.

Try the forums at www.cigarbid.com to chat more about cigars if you'de like
