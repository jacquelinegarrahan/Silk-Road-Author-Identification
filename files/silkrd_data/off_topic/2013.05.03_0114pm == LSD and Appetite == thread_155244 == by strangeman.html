To get our  minds off all the SR drama, I was hoping some experienced LSD users would be kind enough to share their experience with appetite/hunger while on LSD. My wife and I, both experienced trippers, disagree on what constitutes a "normal" appetite while on LSD.

She never has an appetite when she trips. It even seems to suppress her salivary response, so that she can't even chew and swallow food without lots of water on hand.

I, on the other hand, can eat normally after the come up is over. On one occasion, I devoured a foot long spicy meatball sub two hours into a 300ug trip. One of the best meals of my life.

So, am I a freak?
