Title: The Gentlemen's Agreement
Post by: Dutch_moroccan on November 17, 2012, 02:46 am

There is a disparity in all things in the world, dividing the haves from the have nots. It doesn't matter whether this is in the realm of the legal system, political influence, finance or criminal activity. Those in places of power hold onto it through networks. They realize that by agreeing to support one another they strengthen their own individual positions and keep others from getting in and taking a slice of what they've already stolen.

I don't blame them for this at all. But I think there's much to be learned. Most of us don't have the ready liquid cash to outsource our problems away or further amass personal fortunes. But what we can do is provide a latticework for ourselves.

To this end, I propose a criminal enterprise for the rest of us. The Gentlemen's Agreement (not that it's open only to men, I just liked the sound of it) is a concept in which people with needs and skills would be able to find one another.

Maybe it's the need of an alibi, a dead drop, money laundering, hacking, forging or counterfeiting, muscle, guns, black market sourcing, buying or selling, storage, any etc. you can think of, really, the Agreement would be a common way to organize and find support beyond any of our individual needs, playing on various strengths.

I've been thinking about the concept for a while, but how would such a thing be set up? I'd be interested to hear ideas.
