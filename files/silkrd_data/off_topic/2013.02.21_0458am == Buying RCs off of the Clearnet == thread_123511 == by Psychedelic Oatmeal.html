Title: Re: Buying RCs off of the Clearnet
Post by: oldcactushand on February 22, 2013, 12:22 pm
I recently bought some nbome from clearnet. Seems like you basically have to do your research on the vendors in much the same way you would here. As far as I understand, nbome family is considered an analogue of 2c family in the USA, and therefore the european vendors (at least the one I bought from) are generally unwilling to send it there. Many people on a forum I was reading were insistent that being caught with nbome would be treated exactly the same by LE as being caught with LSD. Basically, the law is murky, and the cops are treating like it's illegal.

It's several more times expensive on the clearnet as well, and there is much less of a community gathering anecdotal data on strength, quality of service etc. On top of that, if you do find someone to send it to you, the packaging probably won't be as stealthy as an SR vendor, just through lack of experience if nothing else.

Good luck.
