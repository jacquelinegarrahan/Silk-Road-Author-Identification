I recently ordered a 0.5g sample of EnthusedUK's bud. I believe it was the Eye strain. I'm not able to access Silk Road at the moment and I can't remember the actual name of the strain.

Shipping
I ordered on the Thursday evening and it was dispatched on the Friday via Royal Mail 1st class delivery. Now this would usually be delivered the next day but because of some very bad weather (lots of snow) it did not come until Monday. Still very fast delivery, only 2 working days. When the package arrived it was small enough to fit through the letterbox and didn't smell at all. From the outside nobody would've been able to tell what was in it. When I opened it, the packaging was similar to other vendors I had received items from on Silk Road. It was nicely hidden and took a while to find the product. Like I said, if somebody random was to open the parcel they wouldn't be able to find the product.

Product
Although the packaging was smell proof, as soon as I unwrapped the bud the smell was so strong. Probably some of the nicest smelling bud I've come across in the UK. I have yet to weigh the bud but I'm not too bothered about the weight as it was a sample I delivered but it looks to be on point.
The actual product is very nice. I smoked it and it produced a very nice high and also tasted beautifully. Once again, probably some of the best bud I've come across here in the UK.

Communications
Throughout the process I had sent multiple messages to EnthusedUK and received very prompt replies almost every time. The times I didn't receive prompt replies was when the message was sent at very inconvenient times, at night for example. The team were very helpful to me and came across as very friendly. I'm grateful to them for allowing me to sample their bud.
