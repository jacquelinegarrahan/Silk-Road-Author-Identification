Title: Re: which is better MDA or MDMA?
Post by: lesseroftwoweevils on November 29, 2012, 06:30 pm
I wrote about this a few days ago:

"A few nights ago, I dropped .250 grams of my MDA order from the ILF (very legit vendor, I highly recommend) after a two-month break from MDMA. Here are my takeaway thoughts:

1) MDA is a much more psychedelic/less euphoric version of MDMA. I knew this was to be expected going in, but the visuals were much more "trippy" than I had imagined, producing some nice CEV's t:4:00-t:9:00 after dropping. The euphoric impulses also felt somewhat muted, lasting only t:1:00 - t:3:30 and did not lead to the personal introspective thoughts I've come to expect from MDMA.

2) The MDA experience is a two-parter; the first is like a normal roll and the second is somewhat similar to a mild LSD trip. Although the first "euphoric" stage only lasts a few hours, the second stage lasted 2x as long. Hence, MDA felt much longer lasting than MDMA, as I still had lingering visuals and huge pupils 20+ hours after dropping.

3) The MDA hangover is absolutely terrible, much worse than MDMA's. I've never felt that mentally broken before, it was as if I was experiencing temporary brain damage. I struggled to put simple sentences together, and it felt like someone was driving a pick axe into my skull. Just thinking about it is giving me migraines...
 Now granted, .250mg is a heavy dose for me and I wish I had halved it, but I've had pleasant comedowns on MDMA after using .400mg's in a night. This experience was in a league of its own.

Final thoughts:

Would I do MDA again? Probably, although I'd take it in lower doses/combined with MDMA next time and in a different setting. It seems ideal for use at a festival or psychedelic show, but definitely not in a solitary setting, as the therapeutic self-reflection felt toned down vs. the one experienced on MDMA. Just not worth the mental anguish I felt coming down.

Overall, an interesting experience, even taking into consideration its negative aspects. I'd recommend any seasoned molly veteran at least give it a try, although I caution anyone buying it as a replacement to MDMA, as the two are definitely not one in the same!

Anyways, thanks for reading! If you have any personal insight to add, it would be very much appreciated."
