Title: Re: I'm on LSD right now.. Am I doing this right?
Post by: NeuroRelativizer on May 18, 2012, 06:19 pm
Personally, when I'm alone on any psychedelic I meditate on my music unless the experience compels me to do something such as dance or draw. Nothing I do, however, pulls me away from a solitary meditative state of mind.

With friends, I focus on the reverse, being completely in the group rather than in myself, constantly communicating to diffuse my state of consciousness into the group as they do the same, and so we reach a common place where we may have the same thoughts simultaneously.

Exactly what you do depends on the individual experience, but that's how I generally guide it.
