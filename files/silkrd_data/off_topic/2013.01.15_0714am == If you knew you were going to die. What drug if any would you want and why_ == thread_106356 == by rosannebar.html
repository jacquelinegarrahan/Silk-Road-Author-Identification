Title: Re: If you knew you were going to die. What drug if any would you want and why?
Post by: ominuby2 on January 19, 2013, 03:13 am
Death is easier to pontificate about when it's not immediately in front of you.  It surprises me that so many people say they want to do LSD to more clearly understand the experience.  When you're actually forced to face the reality of your mortality, things like 'clear white light' and 'creator' can start to sound like euphemisms for something that's too awful for most people to talk about.  Death is, after all, permanent.   

Then again, according to record, Albert Hoffman asked his wife to give him LSD shortly before he died.  Gotta respect that. :-)  So obviously it can affect people differently. 

As for me, it depends on what kind of death was coming.  If it was going to be painful, terrifying or unpleasant in some other way, I'd take a heroin overdose instead-- to die on my own terms, quickly and painlessly.
