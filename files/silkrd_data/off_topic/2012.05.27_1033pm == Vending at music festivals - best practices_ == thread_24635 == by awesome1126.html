From friends experience, the hardest part is getting it into the festival. But after you've achieved this then its quite easy tbh. Title: Re: Vending at music festivals - best practices?
Post by: psychedelicmind on May 28, 2012, 10:26 pm
I have brought lots of drugs into festivals in the past. To bring them in, I used to carefully open my food packaging, then put whatever I had into it and then glue the food packaging closed again.
My reasoning for this was that even if you were pulled aside by LE, they couldn't go through your food because that would ruin the freshness of your food. Then for added safety, i'd put the package with the food in a bag with other foods, so they definitely wouldn't go checking every bag. Always worked for me.

As far as selling goes; I used always talk to people first and try an get a feel for what their vibe is. I always let them mention drugs first. Once they mention drugs, I'll ask them what they take and if I can supply what they are looking for, I offer it to them.

Hope this helps.
