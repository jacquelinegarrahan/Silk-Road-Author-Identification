Title: Re: what should be done about recreational drugs?
Post by: wretched on January 09, 2013, 05:19 pm
I vote for complete legalization with regulation on the sales. But NOT exactly government regulation. More of government licensing based on regulations set by the "public drug retail board" which would be an organization paid for by business men and women engaged in the retail of recreational drugs. The licensing requirements would be based on product testing and labeling requirements, harm reduction and addiction counseling. It would be illegal to sell drugs without this license, and the penalties for doing so should be as harsh or more so than current drug policy. I think there should be an age limit on possession also, but not because I think that people under let's say 18 shouldn't be able to use drugs, but because I think the children should be able to argue that "it's legal" to parents who do not want their children participating in drug use.
