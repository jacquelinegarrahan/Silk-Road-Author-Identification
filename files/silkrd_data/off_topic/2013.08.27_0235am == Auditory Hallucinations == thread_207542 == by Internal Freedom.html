Title: Auditory Hallucinations
Post by: Internal Freedom on August 27, 2013, 02:35 am
Up until a music festival that I attended several weeks ago, I had never experienced an audio hallucination. Even after doing LSD, 2C-B, Shrooms, 25i, DMT and many other psychedelics DOZENS of times.

I had done MDA the second day of the music festival and on the way home that night I heard all the songs I had listened to that day playing full volume while I was in the car. It was amazing because I was still moving to the music and enjoying it even thought it wasn't actually there and no one else in the car with me could hear it.

Then I experienced another audio hallucination while taking mescaline two weeks ago, while I was lying down with my eyes closed admiring the patterns appearing, I heard the song "I don't want to set the world on fire" by the Ink Spots played FULLY from start to finish. Every single word and note in the song was perfect. Im really starting to enjoy these considering I only recently started experiencing them. Does anyone know any other drugs which may help me to experience intense audio hallucinations? I want to try and keep my MDA trips to a maximum of a few times a year and Mescaline is expensive as shit and I cant do it very often.
