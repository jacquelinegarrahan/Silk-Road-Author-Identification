This is exactly right.  Smell molecules can permeate through materials (sometimes even glass!) over a pretty short amount of time.  If vacuum sealing was infinitely effective like some people seem to believe, then vendors wouldn't triple-bag it!  They would just use one bag.  Triple bagging provides three times as much plastic material for the smell molecules to permeate through, theoretically allowing three times as much time in transit.
