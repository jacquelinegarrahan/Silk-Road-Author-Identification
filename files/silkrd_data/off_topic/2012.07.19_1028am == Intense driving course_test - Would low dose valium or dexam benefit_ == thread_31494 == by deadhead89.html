I wouldn't suggest taking Valium before a driving test, especially since it can slow your reaction time. However, like BanWork said, if your anxiety is bad enough to where the Valium may put you at baseline, then you could try it.

I don't recommend taking any amphetamine, as this can increase your anxiety; not to mention making you more irritable (like it does to me). I'm a very impatient driver, especially on dexamph.
