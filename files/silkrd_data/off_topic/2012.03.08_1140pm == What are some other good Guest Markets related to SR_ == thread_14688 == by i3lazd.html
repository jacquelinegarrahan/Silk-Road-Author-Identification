I also use Black Market Reloaded: http://5onwnspjvuk7cwvk.onion/

It's still a lot smaller than SR, and the selection there obviously reflects that.
The biggest difference between the two is that BMR does not have a seller fee, only a small commission on sales. That means a lot of their sellers are either scammers or technically incompetent, but the escrow system works very well for buyers wise enough not to finalize early. It also means sellers with small quantities can still afford to bring their products to the market.
