Title: Re: Tripping 2 days in a row?
Post by: fire on May 04, 2013, 09:11 pm
2C-B resembled acid in many ways, just much less of a mental trip, with an euphoria very much like MDMA not as intense. Since 2C-B has a steep dose-curve, and many people report significantly different reactions to doses. For some, 20mg is too strong while others will not feel much with under 30mg. I also heard that the same person can react much differently to the same amount on two different occasions.
You should try it on its own sometime. It'd be safer to find out your ideal dose in isolation, and not a day after tripping on something else that tickles the same receptors.
