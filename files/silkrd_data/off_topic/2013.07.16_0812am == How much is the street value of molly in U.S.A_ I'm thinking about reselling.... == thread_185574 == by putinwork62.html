It all depends on your market.  The USA is not one homogenous area.  I have noticed, however, two things worth mentioning:
a.) Always come at a new customer with a higher price.  They will almost always try to negotiate a price as they buy larger quantities and as long as you're giving them some significant discount for bulk they will compare it to the original price you started off selling to them at.
b.) Get rid of customers who keep trying to low-ball you when your offer is already reasonable.  Some individuals just want to take take take and if demand is high enough for your product then either i.) drop them or ii.) make them come to you.  I have some customers who keep thinking I'll come to wherever they are on a Saturday night (when I want to be partying) to give them 2 pills.  They do not get responses to their texts from me.
