Title: Re: does anyone know of any good way to disquise the tor logo on my laptop?
Post by: Raoul Duke on March 20, 2012, 08:49 am
move the tor folder to a hidden location (maybe rename it and stick it in Documents folder), then create a shortcut of 'Start Tor Browser' on your desktop or wherever. rename it to whatever you like then right click>properties, then click the 'change icon' button in that window - change the icon and you have one really hidden tor browser.

or just stick the whole folder on a USB drive and keep it on you at all times
