So I thought I would make a little thread for people selling at clubs and raves and anything like that who want to share tips and tricks.
I started chopping all ages raves back when I was about 15 so I would say I am fairly experienced.

I find one of the safest ways to sell is to work with some one who is also selling. At the end of the day you will usually make the same amount of cash because you are not competing and the chance of getting busted is decreased by a lot. One person handles the money the other handles the drugs, this will confuse most security guards just make sure the person that is holding the drugs does not interact with you to much during the night. The only real downside of this is if you are selling to people who are not regulars in your scene they may not trust you or think you might just run of with their cash. I also recommend the person holding the drugs be female, security is usually male and the chance of you getting a heavy pat down is less.

When holding keeping your drugs properly hidden will save you a lot of cash, in the last 2 years I have seen probably around 2000+ caps of molly get flushed down toilets.  Keeping a bag of 100 caps in your pocket is not advised, The best place for men to hold is in the crotch pocket of briefs. heres an example http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WxNs3WGbNNw

For women the best option is to usually modify a bra. the best way to do this is to cut a small hole at the top and to slip whatever you are carrying behind the cushion. I personally keep a large baggie filled with 10 smaller baggies  that usually have 10 caps each in them in my crotch. I take out 1 bag at a time and put them in a pocket or something. That way I have easy access and if security catches me the most their getting is 10 caps. When taking a bag out or adjusting them always go to the washroom stall and have someone keep 6 for you.
