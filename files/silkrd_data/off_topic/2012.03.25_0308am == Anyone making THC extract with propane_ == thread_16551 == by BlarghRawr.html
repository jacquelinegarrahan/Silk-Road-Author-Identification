I used for extraction mix of absolute alcohol and di methyle ether in proportions 6:1. 15 minutes at room temperature. if you will keep longer it will not improve extraction, but will increase amount of unwanted substances in extract, like chlorophyll, beta carotin and others.

for 250g of dry weed take 450ml of solvent, keep in solvent for 15 min, then filter and recover solvent by distillation with condenser. no heating more then 90C.  250g of dry weed will give about 1.4 g of extract.
