Title: Re: How to get stoned?
Post by: gharyeee on September 09, 2011, 12:27 am
Each type is a little bit of a different experience to me. Bong for relaxing, joints for relaxing and doing something, pipes for anytime relaxing, vaporizers for relaxing and being productive, and edibles for getting destroyed. You might be able to get just as stoned from packing bowl after bowl in any of those devices, but I usually lose focus because I'm happy with my high. When you eat an edible you go all in.

As profspudhed said, edibles will also react differently with your body than smoking will. Dosing edibles can be a bit tricky as you see 2x, 5x, 10x, 20x edibles and many work on different scales and definitely different quality. I've found chocolates to give the heaviest high. Something with thc binding to fats/chocolates, definitely not my area of expertise.

In my experience using a volcano vaporizer, it almost always gives a much lighter high. I don't get that "couch lock" feeling where I don't want to move anywhere. I can be active and productive and high as a kite. Epic quality bud through a vape will make me feel like I can still be active and productive, but I'll be higher than a kite and definitely can't perform as well. By far my favorite combination is an amazing sativa through a vape, but if you want a "heavy stone" munch on some edibles or smoke some hash oil.
