I've saved my sheet for over 6 months using this method and no degradation what so ever.. have even done it for over a year on other sheets... I wrap them up in tin foil so they have no exposure to light, put them in a baggie... then vacuum seal that so no oxygen can get in.... and then lastly I put them in the freezer because it is consider a "Dry" environment!! Just make sure that when you take it out the freezer you allow it to adjust to room temp or else due to rapid change from cold to hot moisture will ruin your tabs!!

This prevents the tabs from the 4 things that cause LSD to degrade
1)Light Exposure
2)Oxygen
3)Heat
4)Moisture
