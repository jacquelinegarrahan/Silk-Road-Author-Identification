Title: Re: Online Pharmacies
Post by: quinone on December 02, 2011, 08:24 pm
There are legit Canadian online pharmacies, and it's not too hard to weed out the scammer's. 

You need to verify the pharmacies license, the site may have it posted directly, or you might have to contact them, either way get the pharmacy's licensing info and any 'accredited' organizations they may claim to be members of.

Then go to the Canadian International Pharmacy Association's website:
http://www.cipa.com/

Compare licenses, most scammer's are so stupid they don't even know the CIPA exists, and their license won't be in there.  It's as far as I know the only third party organization in Canada that represents the legit association of licensed retail pharmacies.  If they don't belong to CIPA steer clear.

The downside to 'legit' online pharmacies is that legit ones actually require a physical doctor's script, so unless you are just looking at online pharmacies for cheaper drugs there's really no point I see in using online pharmacies.
