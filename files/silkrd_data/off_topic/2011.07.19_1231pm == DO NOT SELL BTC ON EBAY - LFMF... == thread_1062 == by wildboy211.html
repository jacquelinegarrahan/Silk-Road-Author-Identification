Title: DO NOT SELL BTC ON EBAY - LFMF...
Post by: wildboy211 on July 19, 2011, 12:31 pm
I put 2.25 BTC up for sale on ebay (it was what was left in my wallet), put it up for auction, and i was ecstatic when it sold for $50.00. I got my paypal payment right away from a buyer with 50+ feedback (this was actually my second auction, first auction sold to a seller with 0 feedback - so i cancelled). LFMF - dont sell BTC on ebay. I still see alot of people selling them on there - although looking at some of them now, i see some people are sending out letters or pennies to the customer to show that they shipped something - clever.
