Post by: M4y0rMcCh335E on July 19, 2011, 06:33 am
Yea, I love bass and can't get enough of dubstep now.  Shrooms would be cool for a first psychedelic experience. I saw Umphrey's Mcgee for the first time at a music festival the first time I took shrooms and I now know the meaning of face melting, haha.  Just make sure that you at least take an eighth if you want some visuals and that you have a trip sitter with you too, because even when you're in a good mood and have no bad vibes, the shrooms can still turn on you believe it or not.  I would recommend acid for your first trip though, that's the route I took.  I think music sounds a lot better on the acid trip rather than the shroom trip, granted shrooms still make music sound amazing, and you're not going to get any real hallucinations (if that makes any sense) on acid until you take the higher doses.  You'll still have a lot of visuals and see changing colors and stuff and still trip from a tab or two your first time, but it's a lot easier to handle and control compared to shrooms on the lower doses.  I would highly recommend listening to Deadmau5 during your first trip for a little bit, especially on acid.  His music is just so... full. Anything from his new album to his other hits are just amazing and are completely mind blowing when you're tripping and if you're by yourself, definitely sets a good atmosphere for meditating.


I do have to say though, I like skrillex as a musician and he has some good songs, but he sucks as a dj.  He messed up a couple times when I saw him, lol, and he dropped a lot of stuff that wasn't even his.
