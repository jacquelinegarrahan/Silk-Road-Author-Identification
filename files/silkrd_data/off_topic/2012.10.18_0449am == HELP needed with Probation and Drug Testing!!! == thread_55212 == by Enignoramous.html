Well they tested you once already right? Did they use a standard 5-Panel test they do in the office or did the office send it out to a lab? If you have THC in your system at the time of taking the test, it's going to obviously come out positive and it doesn't matter how much is in your system because a positive is a positive.

You said you haven't smoked in a month right? You should be completely fine if it really has been a month...Google is your friend and you can research the amount of time THC is likely to stay in your system (usually 30 days or less depending on your body because everyone's body is different) and the cutoff levels for the drug test they are using. Just RELAX!  :) You'll be fine if it's been a month.

I've been on both Probation and Parole and in both cases they would switch off between using a standard 5-Panel (and in my case they would use a 12-Panel) and sending it out to the lab depending on the person who was giving me the test.

My advice? Don't smoke while on probation...I don't know what you're situation is but I had 5 years of my prison sentence suspended and I damn sure didn't want to go back so I stayed away from ALL drugs.
