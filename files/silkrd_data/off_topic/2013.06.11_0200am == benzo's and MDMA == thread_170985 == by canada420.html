Title: Re: benzo's & MDMA
Post by: Dingo Ate My Drugs on June 11, 2013, 02:36 am
Well if you have panic attacks regularly, MDMA probably isn't he best idea.
It's because MDMA is a stimulant and benzo's are a depressant. The benzo's override stimulants and that is why you can't feel the MDMA.
If you are OK to stop taking benzo's, probably wait at least a week before having MDMA.

If you overdose on stimulants and go to hospital, they will give you benzo's to calm you down.
