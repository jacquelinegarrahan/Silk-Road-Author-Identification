Honestly, I don't think too many people worry a whole lot about who they're selling to.  I've been at shows and had people handing my business cards saying to come pick up a bow or two before I head home lol.  I'm sure getting 25 makes it a lil more difficult, but definitely far from impossible.

How long are you willing to stick around town?  Shouldn't be too difficult to socially network yourself into contact w/ some better distributors, especially if you're of the right mindset.  Go to some shows, hit up some dispensaries, and talk to EVERYONE.  Just be forthright about what you're doing - you're from out of town and came to find herb.  People in CO (especially in Boulder/Ned) are used to this type of thing, and worst case scenario they're gonna look you up and down then say they don't know.

Here's a couple suggestions for places to hit up:

- Concentrate (harhar) on Boulder/Ned, but don't forget about Denver.  FoCo is probably worth driving up to and putting some feelers out, but is a bit of a trek for a small town.  Plenty of powder substances up there if that's your thing though - Snort Collins :)

- Go to any jamband/bluegrass/electronic shows you can find, and smoke some herb w/ the "locals".  Keep in mind a true "local" in CO is rare though - it's one of the only places I've ever been where a super common question when introducing yourself is "where are you from?". 

- Hit up the First Friday art gallery openings in the Santa Fe area

- Head to local headshops and clothing stores.  Grassroots flagship store would be a really good place to start.  If you're of the same mindset as those kids, you should have no issues finding a connect.

Basically, if you don't suck at talking to people and have utmost heady vibes to coallesce w/ the light beings of the front range ::rolleyes:: you shouldn't have any issues figuring it out.
