The advantages of the ccartomiser systems is that the cartriges are cheap, when the atomiser paks up you just fill up another. also the advantages off the cartomiser systems (the disposable ones) is that you can fill with drugs without fear of damaging a costly atomiser.

As for the site. Totally wicked is by far regarded as the best. I was one of thier first customers. and in fact I am smoking my first tornado e-cig I bought with them right now. yummmm

Remember you will need to buy a usb charger with your batttery. its always best to have 2 batterys and two chargers on hand and about 5 throw away cartomisers.
