Best trick I have came up with, put the drugs in your hand and put your phone over them, drop the drugs into the hand of the person who is buying while you drop the phone in there hand. Act as if they are looking at your phone (which happens a lot at loud clubs) and them have them hand the phone back. If you wanna make it even better have one person hold the drugs and do the drug exchange that I described and have another do the money exchange. Club security looks for money exhange the most. So if you have one person with the money and one with the drugs, the drug guy will be more stealthy because they will be only handing over a phone and will likely not get suspected. The money person probably will get caught a few times but all they have is money which then proves they are not distibuting :) Hope that helps!

further advise when being a runner in clubs:-
- guard against moisture getting to your goods, the silica absorbing thingys would help but adds to the bulkiness of your stash....too many plastic bags increase threat of moisture.
- pre pack them into little bags to quickly dish out w/o getting your whole drug stash out, ie to do discreetly.

Title: Re: Tips for selling in clubs without getting caught
Post by: oldschoolclubkid on January 20, 2012, 10:32 pm
Selling in cubs is very dangerous! Bar staff love drugs and love to catch people selling so they can just take there stuff for free (other option is they call the cops).  But I did it very successfully for many years back in the early 90's.  I used a team of three people one being me.  One person would make the sale and take the cash always at the bar when ordering drinks so you had an excuse to have money out.  The second person on the team then would catch the customer walking away from the bar and great them like an old friend shake hands and hug and pass the pills.  The phone thing mentioned earlier would work great too in today's day and age when everyone has one.  Now the third guy is the most important one of all hes your runner.  the number one and two guys need to be carrying a very small amount of cash or product depending on which side they are working.  Small enough that the product could be dropped into the drink number one is carrying or small enough that the amount of cash on number two would not indicate hes selling.  Number three guy makes this happen by making cash drops and picking up new product, so hes in and out of the club a lot.  best if hes a smoker and needs to step out for a cig.  My guy wasn't and always used "damm its smoky in there I need some fresh air".

I actually got pulled from a club one night had 4 in my pocket and dropped them into my beer bottle, I walked to the back door with the bouncers and let them pat me down go through my pockets and wallet all while holding my beer.  Needless to say I didnt drink that beer until very late in the night when we were about to leave to go to an after party which I was not going to be working then,  And I split it between myself and a friend it was 4 hits!

last tip NEVER and I mean NEVER use and sell at the same time in a club you will get sloppy and lose money and or product and more than likely be noticed by the staff.
