Post by: couchman420 on January 14, 2012, 07:27 pm
Here is a link to one of my experiences with 25i-nbome:

http://dkn255hz262ypmii.onion/index.php?topic=7975.msg72398#msg72398

I wouldn't recommend taking as much as I did, or smoking/vaping it your first time.

If you bought your 25i in power form, chances are it is in freebase form, you are unfortunately in a bit of a predicament. Freebase 25i is water insoluble, and inactive in buccal/sublingual/nasal/oral, at least that is what I am told from the big and dandy 25i-nbome thread on bluelight. Also, in that thread, there are a few different ways to get it into solution to aid in dosage measurement and/or laying blotter.

As far as the experience goes, during the come-up there is a small bit of anxiety which will increase as dosage increases. Nothing too bad, just a wee bit of... mental unsettling, if that makes sense. The heart of the trip is where all the awesomeness happens. You'll get lots of tracers, enhanced vibrancy, and color distortion. There is a small stomach-in-the-throat feeling that increases as dosage increases. You might also get sweaty hands, at least I did.

As far as safety goes, you *can* take quite a bit of this but that does not at all mean you *should*. I read a report of someone accidentally taking 30mg and he is still alive with no long-term effects.

Hope this helps. If there is anything else you need to know, don't hesitate to ask.
