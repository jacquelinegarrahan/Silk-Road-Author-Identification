Title: Re: What is the strongest hallucinogen/disociative?
Post by: Dissec on January 14, 2012, 09:45 am
Have you considered salvia? It is pretty powerful, and legal in some states still. It's main active chemical (Salvinorin A) is the most potent naturally occurring hallucinogen.

5-MeO-DMT or regular (N,N-DMT) are both very powerful as well, like salvia they can put you in a different world. After the breakthrough some effects linger for about an hour or so, feeling almost like a small LSD trip (I have nothing else to compare it to but a weak LSD trip).

2C-T-2 (and 2C-T-7) is (are) pretty cool, cartoon like visuals in some reports, some other 2C drugs are also pretty interesting including their nBOME counterparts that are in some cases far more potent. ETH-LAD is an analogue of LSD and is more powerful (ug for ug), so I feel it may be worth mentioning.

And for LSD, it is very powerful, and it takes so much to OD you could take a whole sheet without putting your health in danger (from dosing, I can't speak for possible emotional damage or self-caused injury.) That would probably be a very powerful experience, I'd believe more powerful than DMT (N,N or 5-MeO), if you were able to get a thumb print even more so.

Datura sounds very fucked up to me, I'd give it a small dose try because it is very powerful, if you're just looking for amazing power that is. Same with nutmeg, I never hear good trip reports about these 2, people always end up naked knocking on random people's doors it seems like.

All in all, I'd say DMT is the most powerful per price, dose, and availability. Its pretty cheap, easily available, and you wouldn't have to consume alot of it for desired effects. I'm sure there are some research chemicals that would blow our minds away (which I'd love to try if we've got anybody sitting on small amounts of these), but when adjusted for availabilty and pricing I'd say go with 5-MeO-DMT (or if you want something a bit lighter, go N,N-DMT).
