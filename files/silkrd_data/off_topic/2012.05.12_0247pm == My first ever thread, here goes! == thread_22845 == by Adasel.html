It's sort of like a mix between guitar hero and tetris. You take any song from your music collection and import it into the game, and then the game generates a track that syncs up to the song. Title: Re: My first ever thread, here goes!
Post by: PriscillaMarie90 on May 12, 2012, 03:39 pm
Well I wouldn't say it blew my mind or anything, but I play League of Legends ALOT... Haha it's really addicting, it takes alot of strategy and knowledge of the game and it's characters.

You have to pick from a big list of champions, and every one of them is unique but they all have certain abilities and whatnot. Then when you play, you're placed on a map that has 1 Nexus at each end (that's your "power source" or whatever), several Inhibitors (smaller versions of the Nexus) and several Turrets (turrets help defend the nexus and the inhibitors, they stay in one place but they deal damage to enemies if they come too close). Basically the goal is to destroy the other team's nexus by destroying their turrets to get inside their base. You're on a team of either 3 versus 3 or 5 versus 5 and each team has an equal army of Minions(little guys that run around and deal damage to enemies but are not played by a human) to help you win. You have to stay behind your minions so they will take the damage from turret hits while you destroy the turrets. You also earn gold during the game by killing enemies and whatever, and you can buy things during the game that enhance certain abilities, so you have to know what kind of abilities your champion has and how you should build them.

I dunno, I like it alot because you don't have to dedicate a /ton/ of time to it or anything. I mean each game lasts about 30 minutes to 1 hour, and then you're done, there's no quests or anything time consuming like that really lol.. It does get kind of boring after a while, but I don't have alot of time to play anyway, so I like it.
