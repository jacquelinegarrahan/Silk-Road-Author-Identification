Title: Re: finalilizing the order
Post by: OuterLimits on February 05, 2012, 04:13 pm
The option to finalize doesn't appear until the seller has marked the order as "in transit". This usually means that the seller has shipped out your order, unless it is required that you finalize the order first before shipping. After it is marked "in transit" you can finalize the order before you receive it if you wish to do so for trusted sellers or wait until you receive the product before finalizing the order which releases your bitcoins to the seller.
