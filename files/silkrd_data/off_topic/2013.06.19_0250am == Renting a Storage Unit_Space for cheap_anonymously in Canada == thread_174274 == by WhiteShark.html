Post by: P2P on June 19, 2013, 03:34 am
You should not be doing such things in a storage locker space. Dogs are taken through those periodically (it is way too obvious of a place for a cache of illicit goods). The same goes for the basement (I'm assuming this would be in another person's house), but even the basement would be a lot safer than what you have been doing.

And you are correct, you definitely do not want any records that could be tied back to anything you have been/are/will be doing that is illegal.

I would say go with the basement, if you truly want to stay away from home. If you are a vendor doing good business, 600 shouldn't be too bad of an expense. Consider it overhead, add it to your bulk cost. It shouldn't hurt you too much. It's well worth keeping the salary and infinitely worth it to keep the freedom.
