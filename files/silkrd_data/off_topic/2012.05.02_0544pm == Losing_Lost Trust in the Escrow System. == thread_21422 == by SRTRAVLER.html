For the purposes of this discussion, lets say everyone knows how to use bubble bags, and/or advanced hashmaking techniques and these two hashes are being made by the same person, using the same technique.
Theoretically, lets say the bombass weed has 25%THC and other related cannibinoids, and the schwag has 15%.
1oz of BOMB would yield approximately 7g of THC and related cannibinoids if extracted 100%,
while the 2oz of schwag would yield 8.4g of of THC and related cannibinoids. Not a huge difference, EXCEPT you have plant matter in some cases (the final few passes using larger micron screens), and cell walls from the trichomes in ALL cases, unless you're making oil.  So from the 2oz there would be more cell walls contributing to the hash to yield the 8.4g than from the 1oz of BOMB.  NOTE: these gram estimates do NOT take into account the weight of those cell walls, so the weight of the hash from the 2oz would likely be more than that of the 1oz and would therefore be less potent gram per gram.

IF they had the same ratios of THC to related cannibinoids you could POTENTIALLY have better hash from the schwag.

What makes weed schwag though?

1) Sometimes it's harvesting too early/late.
If you harvest too early, the trichs will not fall off very easily, and you will get very little hash.  Early harvested pot/schwag is really only good for oil, and even in an indica you will get a sativa-like high due to the low THC to cannibinoid ratio.
If you harvest too late, you will get a LOT of trichs, as they're all stretched out and brittle, but you wind up with a heavier stone as the cannibinoid to THC ratio has a higher percentage.

Properly harvested weed makes the best hash due to a balanced THC/cannibinoid ratio and well formed trichomes that haven't spooged all over the leaf, losing potency.  You're looking for a T shape, with a reddish tinge running about halfway up the stalk of the trichs. (use a jewelers loupe)

2) Sometimes it's the strain.
Obviously some strains are better than others, they produce not only different ratios of cannibinoids, but they also produce more or fewer trichomes.  That's why hash makers tend to use specific strains rather than any old strain they found a seed in.  If you've ever bought really, really good weed it takes a lot less to get stoned than the equivalent weight in schwag. To top it all off, there are some cannibinoids present in some strains that are not found in significant amounts in others - this greatly affects the quality of the high, and the final product when you're making hash.

If you use a bubble bag, with the smallest micron screen, and do a first pull off of schwag, and one off of bomb bud, you will get higher off the quality bud, and you will likely get a better yield of trichomes.
