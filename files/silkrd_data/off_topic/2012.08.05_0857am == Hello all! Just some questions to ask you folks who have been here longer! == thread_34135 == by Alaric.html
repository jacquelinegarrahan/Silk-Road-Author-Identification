i've been here six months but i'm still a newb at some things, i'll try to answer some of your questions the best i can, since no one else has

1. the rate changes all the time. i think you can see what the current rate is at https://mtgox.com/

2. the silk road is anonymous but to further protect your anonyminity you should encrypt your messages/addresses with pgp... which is really something i need to learn how to do already. >.>

3. ug (micrograms) is the unit of measurement used to measure lsd. iirc, there are one million micrograms in a gram.

4. escrow is where your money goes when you make a payment. it's not yet in the vendors account and not in yours. if you finalize, it's no longer in escrow.

5. make your first purchases from someone with a solid reputation (fans, sales, feedback). you'll probably be asked to finalize early for your first few purchases, you shouldn't have to but it can be hard to avoid as a new member.

6. don't beg people on the forums for bitcoins.
