the "whats the worst thing you've snorted" thread reminded me of one of the more retarded things i did as a teenager, but it didn't involve snorting so i thought i'd make a new thread.

i had heard about people taking bong rips through liquor and such, so i figured it would be pretty cool if i took a bong rip through soda.  so we poured some cherry pepsi into the bong, i took a rip, and then everything went white.  i fell on the floor in a state of extreme nausea and confusion, wondering if i was going to die while my friend was laughing his ass off.  i didn't realize at the time, but figured out after, that you're basically just taking a big lungful of smoke mixed with CO2 from the soda, in essence, suffocating myself.

i can laugh about it now, cuz i was a dumb ass kid, but just in case anyone out there hasn't ever thought of this:  DON'T TAKE BONG HITS THROUGH A CARBONATED BEVERAGE!!!
