Title: Re: Distinguishing new drugs in multiple simultaneous deliveries
Post by: hoobydoobydoo on January 05, 2013, 06:38 pm
I personally would recommend ordering more slowly.

As a noob I had a problem where I ordered Ketamine from two vendors at around the same time.  Then they ended up being marked in transit on the same day and when I received the first one I didnt know who it was from.  Boy did I feel stupid.

I just finalized both orders out of embarrassment.  Both arrived eventually.

Anyway, certain vendors will mark things and others won't.  You are safer off ordering more slowly or ordering things that look distinctly enough for you to tell apart.  You can always PM them with the location it was sent from and ask them to verify but some of them might not want to do even that.

Just my $.02
