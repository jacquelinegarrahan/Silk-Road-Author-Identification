I can only base this on small scale lab synths, yields tend to go south when working with large quantities. If you are careful with your methods and patient you should get near to the yields I describe below. I will be basing this purely on the amount of sassafras you have available as including everything else turns into a lot of math. My yields are based on typical routes of synthesis.

Synthesis
Benzoquinone Wacker Oxidation
Reductive Amination of MDP2P with Al/Hg + Nitromethane


Yields:
Safrole from distillation typical 70% - 70L
Wacker Oxidation Safrole to MDP-2P typical 65% - 45.5L
MDP2P density 1.2g cm3 - 54.6Kg
Reductive Amination of MDP2P with Al/Hg + Nitromethane typical 80% - 43.6Kg MDMA
