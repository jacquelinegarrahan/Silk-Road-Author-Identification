Title: Re: Doing shrooms in NYC
Post by: MollyBloom on March 21, 2012, 03:23 am
I would advise against Times Square. Way too many people, and that's just begging for a freakout. I nearly freakout even when NOT on psychedelics, AND I'm definitely no stranger to the crowds. I would also definitely advise against going to Times Square or down to the village (east or west) on the weekend. Trust me on this.

I would say for harm reductions sake, you should stay inside until the shrooms have kicked in, and THEN see how you feel...especially since you've never done them before. You might find that watching the bathtub fill up is the most beautiful thing you've ever seen :)

For the most part, being out in public and coming up on a drug you've never done, and don't know your reaction to could be a recipe for a very uncomfortable time. Wait until you're an experienced psychonaut (and know your reaction to mushrooms) before you go making big plans for a public outing

If you have roof access, I recommend going up there and watching the sky. That's pretty fun.
I did shrooms last week at a friends place, and we stayed indoors...it was one of my most fun mushroom experiences ever (thanks Galindoi!)

We mainly laid in bed and listened to music. We also taped some big sheets of white paper to the walls... and would occasionally get up and draw all over them with colored markers. The next day you have some really fun art.

Either way, have fun and best of luck!

Post by: MollyBloom on March 21, 2012, 08:08 pm
I doubt you'll feel anything much off just one stem. You need .25 - .50 g for most people to feel even a hint of anything.
But every body is different... so meh

I still say chew your dose indoors, wait 45 minutes and see how you feel, then go explore if you're feeling froggy

I say this not only because of the inexperience with your reaction, but also because of the puke factor.
You don't want to be vomit-tey on the street, or subway, or even worse....a cab. Eww.

Make sure to eat them on an empty stomach, and don't be surprised if you puke a few times anyway (especially if you take a high dose as someone suggested above). Don't fight it...that just makes it worse. You'll feel heaps better once you puke.
A few tablespoons of ginger or a few ginger candies really help with the nausea you might feel.
