Title: Re: 2c vs lsd
Post by: strangeman on May 29, 2012, 05:15 pm
It's funny how different these drugs are for different people. A lot of people get side effects from 2c-x's, like splitting headaches or muscle spasms. I don't get any side effects at all from 2c-b (at 30mg anyway). I get nausea and occasionally pukeyness from LSD, most people don't.

My attempt at a comparison based on my own experiences:

The visuals on 2c-b tend to be more prevalent at comparable dosage. I get a lot of laser-light show type visuals on 2c-b, and the closed-eye visuals are crazy intense. That said, I think the visuals on LSD are more subtle but more intense in some ways.  It's a little hard to explain if you haven't done them both, but there's a hyper-intensity to the visual experience of LSD that I haven't yet found in anything else. It's like you were living your life at 480p and BAM, shits is 1080p all of the sudden. Also, I think the visual fingerprint of LSD and 2c-b are different. With 2c-b, the form constants I got tended to be repeating geometric patterns, colorful but not real complex. LSD form constants have a bit more variety for me, but tend to be complex fractal patterns with more texture and depth, but not so sparkly. Some people report melting on 2c-b, but I didn't get that so much.

The body high on 2c-b was really nice. At times the body buzz is almost overwhelming, like 2-tons of pillows smashing you into the couch. LSD has a similar body euphoria for me, but it takes longer to kick in and usually starts as an intense restlessness, rather than the relaxation I get from 2c-b. Sex is great on both (for me anyway, some people lose all drive on LSD). They both make me crazy horny, but it seemed like 2c-b was more erotic. You are definitely more in tune with your body on 2c-b, and less likely to get distracted or weirded out during sex. Food is nice on 2c-b. I eat just find on acid but some (most?) people find their hunger-drive totally shut down.

It seems like the headspace is the biggest difference between the two. 2c-b was very clear-headed for me. I'm pretty much useless for the first two or three hours of an acid trip, I can get "stuck" for a good 15 minutes on something as simple as putting my fucking clothes back on at higher doses of LSD, but 2c-b wasn't debilitating at all, just a little demotivating. 2c-b was emotionally neutral to mildly euphoric. I was giggly and easily amused. LSD has a harder edge, can push you to places you don't necessarily want to go, feel more overwhelming, etc. I can't even begin to describe the weird shit that gets into your head on acid, the language of a sane person really can't do it justice.

TL;DR: You'll see shit on both of them, but LSD tends to be a more intense ride and 2c-b is more body-buzz dominant, very little mindfuck.
