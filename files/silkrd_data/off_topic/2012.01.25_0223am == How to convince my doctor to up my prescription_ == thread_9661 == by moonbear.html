Title: Re: How to convince my doctor to up my prescription?
Post by: mrgrey on January 25, 2012, 04:42 pm
dude if you reported your script lost last month i would not ask for anyting for atleast a few months.  doctors arent stupid and vyanese is a schedule 2, which means docs are very wary about writing them as they can lose their license.

your best best is to make this like a 3 month mission.  in the first month you go back, and say everything is good.  the second go bnack and say how its not working as good as before., that everything is still working but you notice towards the end of the dose that its effects are wearing off.  then in the third month go in and repeat the same thing and say its more noticble now. etc...

as long as a doctor has a chart and history documenting this, he is pretty covered and will be more likely to help ya out. 

also its good to act like these things are really making your life much more productive, and now that they arent working as good your overall life quality is suffering and you would just like to get back to where you were.  dont ask for a higher dosage, but maybe ask if there is something that can help make the medicine more effective.  and be prepared to take a couple other suggestions and try them before getting what you want.

you gotta play it slow or docs know whats up
