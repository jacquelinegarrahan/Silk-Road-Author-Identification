Title: Spice (herbal incense) and Disassociation
Post by: CiscoYankerStuck on January 24, 2013, 03:17 am
I've noticed spice (the herbal incense kind, not DMT) doesn't get discussed here very often, but I'm curious to know about others' experiences.


I spent several months smoking spice daily, going from 1 bowl a day eventually to 5 bowls a day to compensate for tolerance.

I noticed it had very potent disassociative effects on me, among other very bizarre effects. And when I started smoking multiple bowls a day the disassociation hung with me throughout the day even when I wasn't high. I have not done any classic disassociatives, so I don't know how it compares, but my experiences matched word-for-word with the definitions of disassociation, derealization, and depersonalization.


My question is, who else has experienced this? And how does it compare to other disassociatives?

As amplifying information, I'm referring to the much more recent types of spice being sold. The US federal government banned the original 5 chemicals used in spice back in 2011 and then the local government in the area I was in also banned many more chemicals detected in spice, so frankly, I really don't know what was actually in the spice that was having these disassociative efffects on me.
