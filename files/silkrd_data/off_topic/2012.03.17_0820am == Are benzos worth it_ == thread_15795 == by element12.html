Title: Re: Are benzos worth it?
Post by: microRNA on March 17, 2012, 09:43 am
I agree there is definitely a very legit use for benzos when needing to sleep, psychedelic comedown, anxiety... but there is also definitely a recreational component for many users as well. Some people just react differently or have varied preferences and will find benzos more enjoyable than other people, or will find specific benzodiazepines more enjoyable than other benzos. Many people probably really enjoy the way that your stress just floats away and you have no more worries or distractions from ones day after using them. There are more powerful benzos that cause noticeable intoxication as well, resembling that of alcohol which is also a GABARa allosteric modulator. I prefer the buzz over that of alcohol certainly and find myself much more sociable, so that is also another use in ways. Valium is one of the less intoxicating and euphoric of benzos... people just seem to find alprazolam and clonazepam (or nitrazepam if you can get your hands on it). once you become more experienced or take a sufficient dose, the effects are certainly noticeable beyond sleepy relaxation.

Its interesting you mention the dangers of benzos which is certainly true. The sad thing is benzos are actually fairly safe by themselves even if people repeat dose as you mentioned. Their are two main problems outside the low intrinsic toxicity though which increases their danger. One involves the idea that benzos are safe relatively, especially compared to that of say barbs (also GABAR) or opiates, which I think kinda lures people into a false sense of security. Because they are safe individually and a commonly used medication, people dont really consider the dangers of mixing them sadly. Also, the inherent decrease of ones inhibitions, and especially memory as you mention also, results in people either deciding they should take more, or not having the rational thought processes to stop themselves from taking something else in addition, say alcohol or opiates which is extremely dangerous and combining factors in many deaths.

After a summer of liquid caps my group of friends made from powder alprazolam, I kinda have tried not to abuse them too much anymore. Mainly after psychedelics or to make opiate withdrawal more bearable. One of my favorite combinations is definitely a benzo and some good coke though! The edginess is mellowed out but really potentiates the feel good vibes from both. Im not really a upper kinda guy unless got some work to do...

Hope I helped answer your question in addition to my ramblins
