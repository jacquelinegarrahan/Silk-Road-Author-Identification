Dilution is your friend brother, good luck

Do be careful, most all places do check for over dilution at this point, and depending on how severely over diluted it is they will report to your employer/potential employer that you dilute positive/dilute negative. Usually just ending up in requiring you to retake the test. Dilution also becomes severely less effective for chronic users. As far what they mean by heavy users, they mean habitual use, not quantity.

BTW fizzy there are certain metabolites that in any quantity are considered positive because there is no known pathway from any combination of anything that can cause these.

If your a prospective employee you wont have any recourse, but if your an employee already you can sometimes challenge the results, but they may require you to do hair analysis or other things. Really depends who we are talking about that is requiring the test, certain GOV offices are pretty strict and go a bit nuts.

Best of luck and hopefully you pass clear and free man!
