the thing about most of the 2cs is the clearheadedness which can make some people believe they are not tripping or the trip isn't as strong. learn the language of your drug before dismissing it.

a few more experiments, maybe change your setting and ask yourself questions, perhaps write or draw to figure out more about how the drug is interacting with your body. You should generally feel a body high, not unlike MDMA with perhaps a slight uncomfortable feeling as you come up in waves. The visual trip itself can be minimal unless you allow yourself to lose focus and see what the drug has to offer through its language. as you begin to communicate in the language of the drug, you'll be able to learn to focus your/its energies to get the most out of your trip.

good luck, the 2cs are a great family if treated with respect and understanding.

Title: Re: 2c-i just not doing it...
Post by: jollygiant on March 11, 2012, 05:20 pm
Don't rely on eyeballing RCs. It's inconsistent for research and can be dangerous (you have some leeway with 2c-i).

At higher doses, 2c-i can become a bit uncomfortable, there is an obvious speediness and perhaps muscle tightness.  I found this to begin at 20mg or higher on 95%+ quality but YMMV.  Smoking some weed helps potentiate results just remember to keep your head straight if you do, weed can make you feel dumber or more confused.

it's a subtle trip that creeps up slowly, around 4-5 hours to peak at 15-20mg doses. Finding some nice art to look at or even just textures like asphalt or a carpet, could help direct some of that energy.

2c-i feels like an old friend giving you a hug. Let go, embrace, feel its energy, then share yours, see how it changes as your mind becomes more centered. I've heard it referred to as an "ego reshaping", I can agree with this term. It can also be a helpful empathogen.
