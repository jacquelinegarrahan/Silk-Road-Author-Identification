Title: Re: Making my own pills
Post by: Walnutst on April 04, 2012, 02:43 am
If your just doing a small amount then a pollen press will be sufficient. Manual presses don't create enough pressure to keep the pill from crumbling tho. For a nice solid pill you would want something automatic or semi-automatic. And just a heads up,  if you buy a pill press off eBay chances are you will have LE knocking on your door. There are whole divisions of LE that are dedicated to browsing the shopping carts of eBay buyers.

Want to know a fun game? Go search for someone who has bought palladium chloride (an essential component for E manufacturing). Look what else they bought. Probably a scale, some glassware, other chemicles such as mercuric acid. You'll even find people who buy glassware and 3 or 4 lithium computer batteries. Hmm wonder what they're doing? If we as regular people can see that what do you think LE can see. Ship a pill press to your house and somebody is guarantee someone will take notice.

What I did, and recommend to you: deposit money to your PayPal account using a moneypak. Make sure the PayPal contains none of your real information and don't ever log into it from your own IP address. Public computers are best. Find a press which will allow for local pickup. Take a cab to and from the shop so that your license plate is not accidentally recorded by a nearby camra. Take it home and enjoy with piece of mind that you'll never catch a manufacturing charge.

Just my .02.

Title: Re: Making my own pills
Post by: Walnutst on April 04, 2012, 04:11 pm
In my experience the pill is more compact with a combination of active ingredient, binders, and fillers. You can get these imported from the UK for about $20 per kilogram plus shipping. Most machines can adjust pill width so you kind of have to figure out what ratio of the 3 compounds you need through mathematics. Example: if you use the press and the pill that comes out weighs 0.5 grams, make sure you load the machine with a ratio of 150mg of mdma and 350mg of a filler/ binder mix. MAKE SURE YOU MIX ALL THREE COMPONENTS THROUGHLY BEFORE ADDING TO THE MACHINE. Everybody has there preference for the ratio of filler to binder so search the web to and test some ratios to see which works best for the machine you have (different ratios work better at different pressures)
