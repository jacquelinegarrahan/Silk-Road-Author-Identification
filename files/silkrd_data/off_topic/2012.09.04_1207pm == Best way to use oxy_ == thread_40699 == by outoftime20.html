Title: Re: Best way to use oxy?
Post by: eth0s on September 04, 2012, 02:18 pm
This depends largely on the form of Oxy that you are getting.  Instant release or Exteneded?  The difference here can be told in the pill labeling.  All ER (and brand name is always OxyContin) pills are marked OP in the USA, and they cannot be smoked or snorted due to a gelling agent that was put in place to discourage illlicit use...they will literally clog up your nose.  If there is no marking for some reason, the consistancy of the pill will be like wax vs powder when crushed.

There is a method of making the OP pills snortable though, I have had mixed results.  Basically, grind the pill up into a fine mesh, then microwave on a plate until all the powder has turned brown.   Take out, stick in the freezer, then pull out and scrpe off the resulting charred stuff.

If 100MG isnt having any effect orally, then 20mg isnt going to do much snorted...but may just be your best bet.
