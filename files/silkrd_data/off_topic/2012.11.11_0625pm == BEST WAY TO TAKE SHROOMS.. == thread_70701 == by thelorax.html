Title: Re: BEST WAY TO TAKE SHROOMS..
Post by: railingcapz on November 12, 2012, 04:13 am
When making tea I always let the water cool down a little before adding your dried shrooms, I forget the psilocybin degradation temperature I'll have to find it later on. When you trip on shrooms it's not really the psilocybin that makes you hallucinate, once it enters the stomach it is converted to psilocin which then enters the bloodstream to the brain and you begin to trip. When placed in very acidic/basic conditions psilocybin is converted into psilocin. I prefer to squeeze some fresh lemons into a small cup and weigh out 2g of dried psilocybe subaeruginosa (p.subs) - these are one of the most potent mushrooms from Aus. Once the dried (or finely chopped fresh) mushrooms have been added to the cup place a plate or clingwrap over the lid so it becomes air tight. Psilocin is very unstable and reacts with oxygen, so to ensure you get maximum potency, make sure its air tight! Let it sit for 10 minutes or so and its ready to be consumed. I prefer to prepare another cup with my favourite tea and a dollop of honey, and then pour my lemon/psilocin mixture into the tea and quickly drink it. The beauty of preparing your psilocin in this lemon mixture is you can add it to any drink of your choice and you don't need much lemon juice to get good results. If you intend to try out this method please use fresh lemons, and none of that preservative/additive filled crap from the supermarkets. Using this method is like smoking amphetamine rather than snorting it: it hits you quicker and harder, but generally doesn't last as long, so its down to personal preference.

Very quick, very easy:

1. squeeze freshly picked lemons into a cup
2. add 'x' amount of dried/fresh mushrooms
3. Make the cup airtight with clingwrap/lid
4. Wait 15 minutes
5. Consume straight from the cup or pour into a drink of your choice (drinking the straight lemon mixture actually has a nice zing to it and isn't as bad as most think)
6. Sit back and enjoy the ride  ;)

Hope this helps
