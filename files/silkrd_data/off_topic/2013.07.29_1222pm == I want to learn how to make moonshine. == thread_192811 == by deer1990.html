Post by: mcguire39 on July 29, 2013, 02:57 pm
Probably have to search around for plans. I know I've seen one some years back that used mostly copper tubing such as from hardware stores. Then I think just refrigeration grade lead free solder. Some of the larger diameter copper tube esp the thicker wall is hard to bend; might need a mandrel bender to keep from crushing it. Or some people I have heard put sand in and cap the ends when bending and supposedly get good bends that way. Usually discard the heads and tails to get rid of the methanol, octanol, and other non ethyl alcohols.
