You stand patiently in line, as you get closer rumors start to be overheard. Your anticipation grows and grows. This is amazing. This is going to be amazing. This is life changing. This is it. Any moment now. Closer and closer. Your life's anticipation has grown and accumulated to this very precise moment.. and.. it's begun. Silence. Maybe, maybe not, I don't know. Somehow I want this all to make sense but it doesn't.. it just doesn't. All that's left is the deep unrepairable void of nothingness.
