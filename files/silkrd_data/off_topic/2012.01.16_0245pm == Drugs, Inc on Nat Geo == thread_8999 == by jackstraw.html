Has anyone been watching the Drugs, Inc series on National Geographic Channel?  I saw the one on Hashish and learned a few things I didn't really know.  They follow it from the pollen to it being smuggled and consumed.   They also had one on Ecstasy.  Showed the trees being illegally cut down in Cambodia to get the sassafras oil and even had drug labs in Amsterdam showing how it's made and pills being pressed.  They don't really show too much of a drugs are bad bias it's more just information.  But they do show the consequences of the drug industry and some of that speaks for  itself.   I found the cutting down of majestic trees in SE Asia so ravers can dance is kind of disturbing.  Gonna watch the psychedelics one next.
If you have the chance it's pretty interesting stuff to watch.
