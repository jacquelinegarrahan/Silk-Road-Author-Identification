Very nice.

The majority of NBOMes range from 9¢—30¢ for the powder, and from 32¢—$1 per hit with the blotters. Reliable powder vendors seem to be Someguy9191, and Chelonii. For tabs, there's Righteous, Family, UndergroundSyndicate, and Silo.

They can be marketed as LSD, or as general acid substitute, and can be sold for about $5 a hit locally, or up to $10-$15 per hit if at an EDM event.

Essentially, no matter what you do with them, they're profit, so long as you sell them for more than a dollar a hit.
