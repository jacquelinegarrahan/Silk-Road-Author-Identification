No matter what the means. You'd do everything in your power for them because THAT'S who we are.

 At the core there's not much different about any of us at all, but somewhere along the way we've stopped introspectively calculating what is right and what is wrong and why it is so. It's an old way of thinking. It's as outdated as those silly laws and yet it is the backbone of humanity. The thing that's kept us striving as a species.

SR to me, is a compassionate effort to fill this void and ripen the age. It serves as a voice that speaks words louder than what any one person could do.
