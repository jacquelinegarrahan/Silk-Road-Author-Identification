Title: Re: How to store 2-c family chemicals?
Post by: degr3e on May 17, 2012, 01:43 am
I've always felt throwing drugs in the fridge for storage was unnecessary. Unless you live in a very, very humid and hot area, then you should be fine wrapping the blotters up in foil, putting that in a plastic bag, then putting that in a mason jar or something similar. I have kept Acid like this for over a year with no noticeable decrease in potency whatsoever.

However:

The 2C's are NOWHERE near as unstable as LSD is. I kept my 2C's in the bags they came in between a book and that was it. If they were synthesized right and have no impurities, then I see absolutely no reason why any of the (common) 2C's would decompose at all over time.
