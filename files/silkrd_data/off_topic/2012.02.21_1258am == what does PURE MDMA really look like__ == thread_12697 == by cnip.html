Title: Re: what does PURE MDMA really look like??
Post by: wowzers on February 21, 2012, 12:12 pm
Pure MDMA is either sparkling white or completely clear crystals depending on the size of the crystal and which solvents where used to recrystallise.
The majority of the MDMA sold by the big vendors on here is very pure, despite sometimes being coloured.

Coloured MDMA is a result of the chemist not washing and recrystallising the product properly at the end of the synthesis. As the impurities are often highly coloured, it only takes a fraction of 1% of the weight to be an impurity for the product to be coloured so some dark brown MDMA can still have  purity close to 100%.
