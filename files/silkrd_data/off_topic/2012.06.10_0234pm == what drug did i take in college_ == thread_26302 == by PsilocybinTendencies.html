Maybe the drug you took in college was mephdrone (4-mmc) at least that's what the euphoria and other effects you described sounded like to me.
It has been around the drug scene since about 2007 (although it was first synthed in 1929 and was "rediscovered" in 2003.......kinda like MDMA's history) and It has been used as a "replacement" for MDMA in a lot of pills. As for the mild visuals that you described from your recent report. I figured that I would mention that MDMA can become visual in high enough doses, due to one of its tertiary metabolic pathways converting it into MDA in the body. This only happens at high enough doses to flood the primary metabolic pathway though (but keep in mind that everyone has different natural enzyme levels). but I have had it happen to me from a single bean (200mg mdma) from holland in the past.
