Dude, I think you might've just broke the surface. I'm a huge comedy nerd, so I could go on and on. Have you seen any of Louis CK's specials? Shameless and Hilarious are fucking killer. Find some Bill Hicks stuff from before he turned bitter, epic mind blowing stuff when you're high/tripping. Besides being a genius comic, he puts some mellow musical interludes between the bits on his albums, so it's great to listen to when you're nice and baked.

If you've never listened to Ricky Gervais's podcast, you should definitely check that out. Him and Steve ridiculing Karl never gets old. Doug Loves Movies is a great, and free, stoner podcast. Really funny, though it helps if you like movies, like a lot.

Man, I could just go on and on. Since the '90s, alternative comedy has come a long way from those old Jerky Boys and Sandler albums I used to listen to. Much fertile ground, many great comics. While you wait for further suggestions, I'd heavily recommend checking out Marc Maron's WTF podcast. It's great in its own right as funny and thought-provoking comedy, but it's also great for getting introduced to new comics. And for getting a sense of the history of comedy (actually very interesting and entertaining subject matter), and for building your comedy brain.
