Title: MDMA & The Outdoors
Post by: AnimusVox on August 24, 2012, 08:17 pm
While I realize the very large majority of individuals taking MDMA love the rave scene, indulging in eclectic light shows and incredibly loud music, I've found my calling to be when taking MDMA and enjoying it outdoors with a few close friends. A beautiful evening with a full moon and a bright, starry sky provides a setting so much more blissful and I am able to enjoy it completely differently. Coupled with instrumental music (Explosions in the Sky, Boards of Canada, Ludique, Bonobo, RJD2, Emancipator, Little People, Trifonic, and even classical music such as Ludovico Einaudi) and great conversation with your close mates, it provides for the most wonderful time. Has anyone else found to enjoy this setting more than the traditional rave environment? I can't be the only one!

Also, hippyflipping outdoors = absolute perfection.
