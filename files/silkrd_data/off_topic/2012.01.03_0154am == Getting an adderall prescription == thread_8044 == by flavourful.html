Post by: whirlwind on April 08, 2012, 09:04 pm
It's important to be subtle in my opinion. The place you have to worry about being busted for lying is in the DSM IV (if they have you take it. its a ~500 T/F question personality test that can diagnose anything form depression to schizophrenia to anxiety to ADHD. If you for example answer in a way that makes it seem like you have another condition such as social anxiety, the doc will be reluctant to give you the amphetamine script until that issue is resolved.
They will look for signs in your childhood. It could help to fabricate some symptoms. according to their diagnosis criteria, ADD is something that youre born with, not something that develops post childhood.
