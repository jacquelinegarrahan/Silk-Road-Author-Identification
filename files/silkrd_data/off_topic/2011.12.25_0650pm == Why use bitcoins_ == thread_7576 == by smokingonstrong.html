Title: Re: Why use bitcoins?
Post by: Asbury on December 25, 2011, 07:01 pm
It would be harder to safeguard both the buyer and seller if using a prepaid debit card. The escrow system would be tricky.

Example 1: Somebody buys a large amount of drugs, receives them, seller gets invalid or too little value of a prepaid card.
Example 2: Buyer sends the prepaid card first for a large amount of money, seller sends no product and keeps the money.
Example 3: Buyers sends the prepaid card first, seller acknowledges shipping product, buyer uses the card before the seller cashes it.
Example 4: Buyer can use stolen CC data, buy a prepaid card to send to seller. Seller is unknowingly involved in credit card fraud and selling drugs
