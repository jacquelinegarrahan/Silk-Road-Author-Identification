Title: AUSSIES!!! Nimbin Mardi Grass is happening on May 5th & 6th 2012
Post by: mardigrass on April 20, 2012, 02:29 pm
If you are an Aussie and a weed connoisseur then you should know about Nimbin and its annual Cannabis Law Reform rally.

With a lot of opposition this year it seems that it still will be going ahead so if you can make it then come and show your support for the cause and, of course, pick up some nice bud. It will be happening on the 5th & 6th of May so try not to spend ALL your cash this weekend at the 420 sale. :)

Be a little careful trying to take pounds of weed home with you though (unless you can hide it very well) as you never know if the coppers will be randomly stopping cars coming out of Nimbin and being such a small town unfortunately there are only a few exit routes. We haven't had trouble in the past but each year they say they will be getting tougher and one day it probably will happen.

Anyway, for more info check out the website at nimbinmardigrass.com or have a quick read of the Wikipedia page at wikipedia.org/wiki/MardiGrass
