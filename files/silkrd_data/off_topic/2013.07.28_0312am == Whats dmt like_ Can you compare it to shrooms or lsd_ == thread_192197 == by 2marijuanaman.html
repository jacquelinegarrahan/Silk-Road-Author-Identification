Title: Re: Whats dmt like? Can you compare it to shrooms or lsd?
Post by: BoNgOn on July 28, 2013, 03:35 am
It's a very hard question to answer, but in short there are a couple of levels of high dependent on dose.
So generally 10-30mg is, at least within the people I know regarded as a low dose, high doses can be as little as 30mg in some users though but most 'break-through' with a 50mg dose.
With low dosages it will resemble a typical psychedelic trip OEVs and with me anyway very distinguishable DMT CEVs. 
With any sized dose there's an intense rush that feels something like a plane taking off/ shot out of something vibe.
With a 'break-through dose' upon smoke exhalation you will be almost instantly taken to your own world where you could see anything.
 
3 tips:
Hold the smoke in as long as you can this will be the biggest variable with reasons the same dose doesn't feel the same.
Find or research a decent smoking method for DMT. 
Direct flame to the DMT can also ruin it a bit.

The whole experience is very life changing.
I reckon you might see benefit for all that pent-up anger posting. =)

Title: Re: Whats dmt like? Can you compare it to shrooms or lsd?
Post by: Internal Freedom on July 29, 2013, 02:26 am
In short, No. Its absolutely nothing like either one (atleast in my 40+ breakthrough experiences with it 8) )

I did 4 hits of acid once and basically saw rainbows of colors radiating off everything in the room and couldn't speak, move, or think clearly for several hours.

I did 15g of shrooms once and temporarily lost my mind, forgot who I was, and saw the entire world spinning and the floors moving right before my eyes.

Then I did DMT and I was shot into a big empty room full of hundreds of faceless people dancing while the walls, floor and ceiling all changed colors. Then I appeared on the top of a mountain above the clouds and slid down the mountain watching various animals slide down with me. In my most recent experience (3 days ago) I saw a large green cyclops holding a spinning globe which looked like earth while squares and triangles of all different colors started spewing out of floor.

In every single breakthrough experience, I have actually believed I was there and fucking LOVED everything I saw, all the different people, shapes and extremely vibrant colors. Whereas with shrooms and acid I just sit there watching things morph, move and change. Perfectly aware of where I am and whats going on.

Medium doses of DMT give fractal/morphing visions similar to a hit or two of acid which is nice. But if you're doing DMT then dont waste it and not do a breakthrough, its pointless in my opinion  :P
