Title: Re: Advice on Where/How to get rid of Dutch Molly in SE USA in major city
Post by: XxWINxX94x23 on December 01, 2012, 07:30 am
I would agree with the suggestion to go to clubs/raves and either wait for people to ask you. or just walk around saying "molly?!?"

If you think that's too sketchy, have a friend (who doesn't have anything on them) ask people if they are looking to buy molly, then have your friend signal for you to come over and do a quick transaction.

Then, when summer/festivals come around, literally just "open up shop" and have a few friends sell the stuff and rake in the cash at those large scale events. Security tends to be pretty lax at large festivals. People sometimes don't have connections to good quality MDMA, if any at all. So there is a market.

Also, start to get to know your customers, maybe try to become friends and assure them that the quality is superior to anything else (which it most likely is if you bought it on silk road). It would be a great way to make friends in your new location and also establish some solid connections. People know other people, and you could probably end up selling alot to one person, so they can re-distribute it to others themselves, and you not have to worry too much.
