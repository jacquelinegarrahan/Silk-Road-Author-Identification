There is a fantastic film by Jacques Tati called:

-----"Play Time"-----

It is an avant garde comedy from 1967, very much different from any other comedy I've ever seen. (7.9 rating on IMDb). It is THE BEST FILM I have ever watched when high. I could hardly breathe through the laughing fits. I cannot recommend it highly enough.

And YOU MUST WATCH IT IN HD, preferably in a cinema with 70mm, or 1080p on a big screen.

It is a movie about a weird futuristic society, and it has no main character and no conventional plot. A good tip when watching it: it is like watching ants running around, each doing their own thing, you can study this ant for a while, and then look an another to see what it does. Every scene in the movie is like that. There are no close-ups, everything is filmed at some distance, and the screen is filled with weird things that people do. For example, the funniest thing in a scene can be some person far in the background talking to a cardboard cut-out; just the way a person walks; a strange chair that makes a weird sound; someone doing something mundane in a rhythmical manner with very comic effects.

It has been released on blu-ray, so go get it and watch in on a big screen. I say again:

IT IS THE BEST FILM I'VE EVER WATCHED WHEN HIGH!

Dikebog
