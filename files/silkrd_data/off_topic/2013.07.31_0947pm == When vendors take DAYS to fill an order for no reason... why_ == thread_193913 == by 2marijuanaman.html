Theres all sorts of reasons why it might take a while - i've had a few vendors run into personal issues they had to resolve which slowed down their work. You've gotta respect that.

Alternatively - they might have forgotten - that can happen. No harm in sending a message to double check your order is ready to go if its now beyond the timescale they specified on the page? A decent vendor wouldn't resent you for that.

And finally - theres always the chance they already sent it but havent marked it in transit yet. I've had things arrive on the same day they were marked in transit a few times, meaning they'd obviously been sent earlier.
