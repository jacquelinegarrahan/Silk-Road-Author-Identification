Title: Re: Buying bitcoins
Post by: rekline on February 27, 2012, 06:46 am
For my first and so far only bitcoin transaction I used get-bitcoin.com

First, fill out the buy form.

Leave "maximum price" blank if you want your transaction guaranteed.  "Maximum price" is the maximum price your willing to pay per bitcoin.  If the cost of bitcoins is higher than your chosen 'maximum price' at the time your bitcoin buy is processed, your transaction will be cancelled and your money refunded.  Since bitcoin prices fluctuate and get-bitcoins doesn't provide instant service (transactions take at least a day depending on how fast you provide payment) its best to leave these option blank.  If you're in no rush and/or are unwilling to possibly pay slightly more per bitcoin, choose a maximum price your willing to pay per coin and insert that number here.

In order to get your "bitcoin address" login to SR and click "account".  On your account page copy the number that follows "to deposit bitcoins, send them to this address:" and paste it where it says "bitcoin address" on the get-bitcoins buy form.  This way, your bitcoins will be sent directly to your silkroad bitcoin wallet. 

Choose whichever payment method you prefer including western union (note, western union charges around $20 in fees, plus get-bitcoins charges a 5% commission). 

After submitting the order, closely follow the payment instructions given to you by get-bitcoins.  These instructions will be based on your method of payment.

Please note:  Fees will be subtracted automatically from your bitcoin buy (get-bitcoins has a 5% commission I believe).  Do some math ahead of time and plan accordingly.  In order to cover commission fees and still have enough for your purchase, spend a few extra bucks on bitcoins .  Also, in order to calculate how much cash you must spend in order to get the correct corresponding amount of bitcoins (depending on how much your silkroad purchase costs), use a bitcoin calculator prior to buying bitcoins

This is the easiest method Ive found but Id love to see some other suggestions, especially those that are instant. Im still new to the process.

Hope this helps!!! :)
