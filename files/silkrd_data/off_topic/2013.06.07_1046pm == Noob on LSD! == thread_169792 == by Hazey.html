Title: Re: Noob on LSD!
Post by: livestr0ng on June 08, 2013, 09:17 am
There's a lot of hype around bad trips. I once had a very experienced tripper explain to me that sometimes "bad trips" are just misinterpreted. For example, let's say you're tripping. You begin to think of yourself as a selfish person. You become disgusted by yourself. You assume other people are as well. Before you know it, everybody is out to get you and maybe you panic blah blah blah. Maybe it was just an important lesson that got taken out of context. Maybe the acid helped you see you're a little selfish and its something you may want to work on. That's just an example of how psychedelics can create a bad trip from something fairly small. Almost if not all bad trips are from panicking so stay calm.

REMEMBER: the three most important things are set, setting, and dose. That goes for pretty much all drugs. It's not  just "my opinion" its widely accepted. Anyway, if you go for it, have fun!
