Title: Your thoughts on "minors" using SR?
Post by: sorasgaze on November 16, 2012, 04:03 am
Hello everyone, I was just wondering how many people under 18 were using SR, what everyone's opinions were about that age area, I noticed some discussion about it in another thread where some were saying kids shouldn't be messing with SR and it's a big risk factor not knowing about Tor and security. My thoughts, well, meaning "kids", I'm talking about high school+ so within the age range of 15-18 is mainly what I meant. I think if they know what they're doing and are smart about their actions then it shouldn't matter. I started using SR when I was almost 16 (I haven't started being active on the forums until recently), I'm probably gonna get shit for that but it's being honest and I'm sure there's a ton of others younger than me on here for sure, and having a right experience when trying different substances along with tons of research and help from sites like Bluelight and Erowid, along with the SR forums is the right way to start if you plan on doing anything. Even when I was 14 or so and was planning on smoking weed for the first time, I did a ton of research on Erowid to get an idea of how I was going to feel. Before trying anything else since that point, I'd read over 100 sources about each substance I planned on taking. Not going to keep rambling or post my whole life story involving drugs, but I will mention I've always imagined myself growing up homeless and addicted to something "heavy" such as meth, I never knew why but I always had an urge to feel good and get away from reality without the thought of consequences. SR and research changed my view on everything, I've tried numerous different things without developing any addictions and I owe this all to places like Erowid, Bluelight, and even though I didn't have access to it at the time, SR forums is a big help to me now as well.  Silk Road in my opinion is a more efficient way of trying anything, cleaner drugs, and if they have the ability to access it in the first place, I'd guess they'd know to do a good amount of research on what they're doing. It makes the future look pretty good in my opinion.

I say this with almost everything I post, but I'm so sorry for going off-topic and rambling, I get sidetracked extremely easily and go on and on.
