A Jeffery is a fictional preparation which includes every known traditional (older) drug wrapped in a weed joint.

According to the movie it's from, it includes acid, cocaine, ecstasy, herion, and obviously weed, and possibly other drugs such as DMT sandwiched inside the joint.

The movie that referenced it was about this record company employee who is a complete nerd who proposes to his boss, a rapper, to invite this rock star to agree to a record deal.

Hilarity ensues.

Needless to say, the nerd is given the "Jeffery" by the rock star, who may or may not be strung out on heroin.
