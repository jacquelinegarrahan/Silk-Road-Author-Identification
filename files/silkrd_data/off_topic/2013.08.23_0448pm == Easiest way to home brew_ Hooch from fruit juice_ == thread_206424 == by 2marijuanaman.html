When I was inside I learned the same way, didnt drink it though as thought prison was a great opportunity to rid my body of all toxins..however upon release I took my basic knowledge and honed it, this is what i came up with and it tastes damn good.

40 oranges, 1-2 kilos sugar, yeast, large glass/plastic container, sieve, muslin or silk and other plastic pots.

I squeezed the oranges and mixed in 1tsp of yeast,sugar and then left in the container with a a small prick in the lid to allow gas out. Every day i would turn the container upside down for a few hours and then open it a few times a day to let out built up pressure. after 8 days i filtered the scum off the top and then decanted it back into container. I added some more suger and left for 3 days. on day ten or eleven i did a final filter with cloth and presto had a reasonably strong drink. you can put things in to stop the brewing process so the sugar doesnt get used up and to keep it going sour. add sugar or water to taste. yummy.
