Title: Re: Bartering
Post by: Appa on May 06, 2012, 02:52 am
Many people will not be comfortable with anonymous drug bartering, and for good reason.  The way things are on SR, the vendor can be held at least somewhat accountable for mistakes/scams, in the sense that his rating will be destroyed or he may never be welcome in the market again.  Obviously this already has its flaws, so the likelihood that someone will mail drugs to your address in hopes that you'll mail some to theirs is pretty low, and that's assuming they're willing to give out their address in the first place.  Would you just give your name and mailing address to the first person who wanted to trade with you?  Avoiding security risks is one of the main reasons people are picky about who they buy from (rating, feedback, packaging, etc).
