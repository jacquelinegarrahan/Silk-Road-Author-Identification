Title: First DMT Trip Walkthrough + Futuristic Visions...
Post by: btcfreedom on September 15, 2011, 01:26 pm
Last week I tried DMT for the first time, and did a nice bit with me Ubie. About 150mg to the face, mostly in two hits that were HUGE.

I coughed them up like I was smoking a big ass cone of chronic.

Upon take - off, I was ego-less. Upon breakthrough to DMT hyperspace, the room lifted up and away with the walls. The power of this drug is unreal.

The windows became liquid streaks of light and the dog was a flat, breathing white mass on the floor. His existence made no sense to me. I dared not

look at his face, as he was way too complex for the state I was in. Instead I turned around and looked into the room I was in, and it hit full force.

I grinned so deeply it felt like my body was smiling. The patterns are so incredibly powerful and pervasive, as well as color and light. Whole surfaces

appear to move very far from where they were.

Could not move due to the onslaught of the high I was experiencing. I saw beings as people say - but they were friendly. I conversed with them,

but about what I don't remember....Deep understanding of civilization, why we're here, and ancient beginnings of life. DMT is that deep, deep shit.


**SO, moving forward - hours after this experience I had a fucking crazy vision. Going to share it below.**


What if we pioneered a way to move things across TCP/IP - and wrap packets while we do it for encryption? Sounds like some out-of-movies shit.

Imagine the possibilities !? Obviously it would be exploited so firewalling with dmz would be necessary....the repercussions wouldn't be a fucked up OS -

rather an ass kicking by someone hired to do so, teleported over TCP/IP. Or perhaps if it's fast enough, fire a bullet into the receiving end! LOL

Would we need a sort of "pod?" I don't know I'm not there yet. I think we could stand under a light source that would act like a giant scanner.

We already harness the technology to optically recognize and process, why can't we take that further and duplicate / transport ?

You would be scanned, encryption validated, and whatever you have on you would go with you. You could scan an object and have it show up across

the world in GT/sec. This could prove to be incredibly groundbreaking, yet incredibly dangerous. It would require particle physicists, nuclear physicists,

and extremely smart people way above myself. They'd be using matter theory applied to TCP/IP protocol, which I'm not sure would get along too well.

Silk Road would become so fucking active their servers would explode with the amount of orders placed. Vendors wouldn't even have to offer shipping.

Just purchase Ye Olde TCP/IP Teleporte. Buyers could validate themselves with any way they choose...a fucking encrypted scratch on a soft surface.

Pay via bitcoin and be leaving feedback in 15minutes while you're getting high. I think I'm going to order more DMT now.


Meanwhile my digi-heads and silk-knurds get at this and give me your input. Is it really unfeasible? Is what's holding us back government funded labs?

I want their equipment. I promise to read the manual.
