So, when I first got introduced the dark-web and sites like this one... I had NO problem with that... but, one time while I was home(holidays) several states away from my operation... it got raided. My mentor took the fall; but he actually only got charged on the IRS/money side of it. Not even for laundering... cause... we weren't. But, he had at least 60k in cash "unaccounted" for and the IRS chewed up him good for that.

No DEA was involved; just Sheriffs dept. And they were pretty cool about it; left our mothers alone, and didn't scour the property to find out outdoor stuff. But they took all of our clones, dried/trimmed/cured/prepared product. And no one got charged on any of that(they probably sold it. lmao)

After that, I was forced to get into the Social/dealing aspect of it full-on. I took what I could salvage from my outdoor, and other money I had saved, and I started buying off other growers who knew I was 'cool' ... but as I said, the prices for LBs had come up dramatically. When I was growing, 2500 was standard. 2k for a good friend. By the time I was dealing, 4K+ had become the standard. I knew people paying 4800-5k for a lb at that time in upstate... it has since come back down, but it's around the 3600-3900 range.

Being introduced to this site has gotten me prices much closer to what I used to sell Lbs for. And for an ex-grower, this had been brutal up until SR, really. Some of the same people I was selling lbs to for 2k, were asking 3500 from me. OR MORE.

I have made so many 'friends' in so many AWESOME vendors here, that I really do feel like I'm back where I once was. For 5k I can get 2lbs of some "on the trimmy side" dank, year round, right here on SR. And for 5500, I can get 2lbs of the 'drop your jaw from 10 feet away' stuff pretty much whenever also.

The REAL money-maker here for me though, was MDMA and Bitcoin. I made 30k in profit off coin this past year. If I had sat on all the coins that have made their way to my suppliers... I'd be a multi-millionaire, ten times over. Hindsight... but also, MDMA is dirt cheap out of NL. And if you're not a complete moron, use one-time receiving addresses, international-express, and use the 'right' vendor... you can very quickly turn 2k into 10k in a week. This is what I started doing the moment I found SR.

Honestly... Bitcoin + Escrow + USPS = Genius.
