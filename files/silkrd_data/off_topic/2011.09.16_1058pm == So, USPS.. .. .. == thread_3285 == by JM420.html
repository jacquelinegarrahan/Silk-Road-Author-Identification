Title: Re: So, USPS.. .. ..
Post by: RickyRango on September 17, 2011, 04:25 am
It's not as hard as it sounds.  I've gotten weed, coke, lsd, opium, and ecstacy through the mail, and half
of the shipments were international.   

The very first principle is to avoid the USPS's red flags that they use to quickly profile possible
contraband.  They're basically looking for shipments from individual to individual, poor packaging,
a return address that doesn't match the area it was sent from, excessive tape, basically anything
that doesn't look "professional."

You can get around that by shipping in a brand new box, use printed labels, list the shipment
as a business sending something to an individual (or better yet another business) use a real
return address that you have no connection to, and basically make the package look pretty.

As for the inside of the box, make sure it's tightly packed, with no rattling around.  Triple vacuum seal
any drugs, and make sure the drugs are concealed inside another object.
