Has anyone watched these TV shows? I watch the Canadian version of it called "Border Security: Canada's Front Line"

It's actually really interesting/informative show. I've seen a couple "Border Wars" episodes but I like the Canadian version better because it shows more aspects of what they actually do, including postal service! It's good to see how they go about cracking down on illegal packages because it gives you an idea of how they're working on the inside. I would recommend the show to anyone but I'm sure vendors could use this information to their advantage because you get to see how they work on camera. When I first saw the show and seen the postal segment I thought, "Wow, I can't believe they're actually showing what they do to crack down on packages." I'm going to start watching more episodes and hopefully they have more segments of how customs works because it gives you a really good idea of what they do behind the scenes.

Has anyone else seen these shows? I know there is an Australian version of Border Security and also the US's "Border Wars." I'd recommend watching because they're just interesting in general and also can be informative!
