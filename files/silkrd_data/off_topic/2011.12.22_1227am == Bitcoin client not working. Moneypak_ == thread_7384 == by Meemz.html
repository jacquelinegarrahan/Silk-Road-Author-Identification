There you go. ^^^

Back up your wallet to the desktop, delete it all, open up the client and verify that it opens, then quit it out, COPY your wallet out of your desktop, PASTE it into the directory, then start the client back up and use it as usual.
I say to copy and paste it incase you mess up and delete the wallet by accident. Do everything you can to not lose your wallet while you are working on this folder!
