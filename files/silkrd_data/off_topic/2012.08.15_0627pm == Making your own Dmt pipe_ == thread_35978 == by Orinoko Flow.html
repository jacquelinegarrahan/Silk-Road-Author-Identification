Title: Re: Making your own Dmt pipe?
Post by: Slugger on August 16, 2012, 09:10 am
I'd make a Machine pipe. Made specifically for smoking DMT. It's the easiest and the best way to conserve your spice. I would say go on over to DMT nexus and read up on it but I can kind of give you the idea of it here.

You just need a glass bottle, copper scrubber(like the ones for cleaning dishes), and some sort of straw. You make two holes in the bottom of the glass bottle(diamond drill bit recommended, but you can just use a screwdriver and hammer if you get lucky). One is a carb and the other hole is where the straw goes in (this is where you suck in when you smoke). You must torch the copper scrubber to get all of the crap that's on it off. Best to put it fully inside an open flame. Keep burning it until smoke stops rising and you should be good.

To smoke it, you open up the copper scrubber, put some spice inside of it, then stuff it inside the mouth hole of the bottle, with a metal wick coming out of the bottom. So you can just put the flame right on the tail for a few seconds, then start toking.

DMT nexus will definitely have better information and walkthrough.
