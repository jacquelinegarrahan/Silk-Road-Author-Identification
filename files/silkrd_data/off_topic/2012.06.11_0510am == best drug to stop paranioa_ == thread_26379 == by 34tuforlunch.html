In seriousness,

1) Most benzos will do the trick, and kill your experience also. If xanax is too expensive, source phenazepam or etizolam. Be careful though. He also took 10mg etizolam to abort a psychotic pentadrone session, and his body was depressed to the point of not being able to respond to external stimuli; chest rubs and cold water proved futile for several hours until he woke up highly disorientated later. In times of distress, we can make irrational decisions to make the paranoia go away. The best idea is to have a pre-measured dose of the benzo before even starting.
 
2) GHB will work also. Alcohol is not a good substitute but in times of distress it can be better than nothing (make sure to check beforehand if there is an interaction with the agent that will be causing your paranoia and the alcohol) even if it acts as placebo. 

3) A desperate labrat will self-administer heroin or any other downer, but what a waste. It's an option, nonetheless. 

4) Paranoia is often intensified by irregular heart beat. Medications which stabilize the heart (usually end in "-lol") can slightly reduce the symptoms (please check interactions).

Overall there is no drug that will allow you to get high as fuck and magically erase your paranoia. If a substance is causing you to be paranoid every time you dose recreationally, it may be worth considering (1) reducing the dose (2) finding a different substance. However, if you insist on using this substance, the least you can do is to be prepared when shit hits the fan - so that the paranoia does not cause harm to you or expose your activities.
