Hi imghost9,

1. Once an order is in resolution it will not auto-finalise. It will remain in resolution until the buyer and the vendor come to an agreement on how to proceed, or an administrator makes their decision on the resolution (which is final).

2. If you want access to your Bitcoin at all times, use the original bitcoin-qt client which you download onto your own machine, routed through TOR, and with three strongly encrypted wallet backups; one on a USB stick, one in the cloud, and one attached to an email that you send to yourself. I would advise using a brain-wallet if you want the ultimate security solution.

You can also use Multi-Bit or Electrum if you do not wish to download the entire blockchain. Again, ensure you back up your wallet and private keys.

3. I cannot help you on this point as I have no experience using Dwolla or BitInstant.
