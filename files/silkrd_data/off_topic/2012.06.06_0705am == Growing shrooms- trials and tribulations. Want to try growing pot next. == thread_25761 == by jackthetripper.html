Post by: UKGrower on June 07, 2012, 10:16 am
The grow kit you listed says it contains everything you need to grow, yet it fails to mention extractor fan and carbon filter.  Airflow is the most important aspect of growing, and a good filter will stop you getting busted or robbed.

Soil growing is easier to learn, more forgiving of mistakes, and cheaper on the initial equipment costs, so if you are on a budget consider putting your money towards good extraction, rather than hydro equipment.
