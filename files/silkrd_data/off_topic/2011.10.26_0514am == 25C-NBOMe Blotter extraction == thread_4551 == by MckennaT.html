Title: Re: 25C-NBOMe Blotter extraction
Post by: kilomonster on October 28, 2011, 07:46 am
I would think that your NBOMe is already a salt, probably HCl.  I would agree that the best thing to do is order 25mg. That amount is too small and you will probably wind up wasting something there.    If you are dead set on doing it, I think the first thing you should find out is if NBOMe's are freely soluable in isopropyl alcohol, and if it is, get yourself some 99% IPA from somewhere like radio shack because that kind will be dry enough that you won't have to worry about excess water.   Don't bother with salt/base conversion.  You always  lose some product when you do that.

Use 1-2 mL of 99% IPA, no more if it's freely soluable.   Pull all your IPA into a syringe.  Get a large sheet of glass and lay out a lines worth of dextrose/powdered sugar.   Squirt your IPA solution onto your sugar.  warm the glass from the bottom with something warm to evap the alcohol.  If the alcohol is not dry (water free) you will be left with a sticky mess.  If its dry, you should have a line of NBOMe and sugar.   Scrape up and chop with a razor blade.  Snort.

I'd still say all this is a waste of time and your probably likely to waste more product trying to extract it than you would just going buccal.   You're just dealing with too small of an amount really.  But there's an answer that is along the lines of what you had in mind.
