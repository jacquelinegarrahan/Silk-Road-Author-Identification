Title: Re: DMT vs. 5-MeO-DMT
Post by: microRNA on March 14, 2012, 11:23 am
they are two completely different experiences. as was stated, dmt is a very visual compound yet 5-meo basically completely void of visual activity. the feelings of each resemble one another in a way, especially given they are both tryptamines but strangely they are more dissimilar than anything. mainly 5-meo is like a blank peaceful, spiritual for some, void that is just possessed by like a presence and psychedelic mindset. but dmt is characterized by a very colorful active realm of many entities with a much stranger other worldly feeling to me. i dont really enjoy 5-meo for some reason idk, like it is too spiritual and almost not meant to be experienced or something, at least certainly not abused. produces a surreal experience certainly, but nn-dmt is more recreational in some ways.

both are extremely potent and it is not like one produces stronger effects overall, just when compared mg to mg. dmt will certainly produce just as if not more intense and crazy a trip then 5-meo... it just takes a little more of the drug. starting doses with 5-meo are around less then ten mg if I remember correctly, but for dmt its around less than 50 mg. if you took equivalent dosages however, the experiences strength would certainly be similar potency if you understand what i mean.
