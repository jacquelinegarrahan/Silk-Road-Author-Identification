OK, point taken BUT
1.  Most of the scams occur because people go out of escrow.  If a seller requires early finalization, you don't have to order from Him.  You never HAVE to finalize early.

2.  Use and read the feedback.  There are vendors on here with stellar records.  Once you establish a relationship with a vendor, it is smart to stay with that vendor.  You are then working in escrow with a vendor that you have an established relationship with.

3.  If you want to order from a new vendor, start small.  It makes no sense to order 3 ounces of weed and then to finalize early.

I guess I would agree that there is a lot of scamming and a lot of potential for scamming.  BUT I also have several vendors in my saved list that I have a high degree of trust in them.  They are responsive to messages, their packaging is excellent and they are here for continued business over the long haul.    There are some very mature and professional vendors on these boards.

Overall, I would say SR is a very positive experience but you do have to do some due diligence to find out who is solid
