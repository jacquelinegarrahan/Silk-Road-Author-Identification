My method is using a cigarette rolling paper, I tare it on my scale.

I add 100mg (Obviously you can add any dosage you like) onto the cigarette paper then poor it into my caps with my custom made funnel.  8)

It's quite easy to build a paper funnel and it works very well.

Cut out a circle (experiment with sizes, a swisse mens ultivite container is the perfect size for me), cut out a quarter from the circle and tape the remainder together and snip off the end with scissors. It also helps to have a stoker, I use a blow gun dart. You can also reinforce your funnel with tape, but they're so quick and easy to make it's not really necessary.

This method is quite tedious and does take time, patience and a steady hand. But it works for me and if the caps are going for $30 each it's time well spent.

I can fill about 30 per hour.

There are also other methods which involve determining the capacity of your capsule then mixing your molly accordingly with a filler... sugar for example and filling the capsule to capacity, it works well if you have a capsule machine, although depending on how well you mix your M and F you may have inconsistency it's also not great if you intend to break open the capsule and snort.

But really, unless you want it down to the mg there's no sense in weighing out every Molly cap like that.  For other powders like the 2c series or 4FA i will because the 2c its just good to be exact on and my 4FA is pretty clumpy and i often cant tell the difference between 20 and 50mg in a size 3 cap.

But check it out:  Toss a half dozen(or more) caps on a scale and get an avg mg per cap.  they should only be off by 2-5mg anyways with halfway decent caps.  size 3 is by far the easiest, btw.  Separate them, hold the large end between your thumb and forefinger and 'scoop' at the molly.  it should clog up the front portion of the cap, tip up and tap to settle the powder.  repeat.  Now weigh the cap.  by subtracting the difference you can find how much MDMA is in the cap. 

It does take some getting used to and you will need to weight every(other) cap for a while till you get the hang of it but before too long you will only need to check every 10th cap or so and you will AT LEAST triple your production speed.
