Title: Re: Ideas for cool Tor services

Quote from: lesseroftwoweevils on November 03, 2012, 02:08 pm
I don't understand how that would be either practical or safe. What exactly would you need to store on Tor that you couldn't to 100x better using the clearnet? 
Documents, your GPG keyring, backups of your bitcoin wallet, maybe some useful shell scripts. All in a securely encrypted volume of course.

The advantage of a hidden service is that you can store your files there, without the ISP knowing where they're located. Law enforcement would not be able to demand the service to hand over the documents, and require you to hand over the passwords under key disclosure law. Then you have a backup in case you have to wipe all your disk in an emergency, or you can access them from anywhere in the world if you somehow lose or abandon your computer.
