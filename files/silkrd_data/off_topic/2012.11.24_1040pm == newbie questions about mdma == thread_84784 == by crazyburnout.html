Title: Re: newbie questions about mdma
Post by: Agria on November 25, 2012, 08:06 am
MDA has a higher efficiency stimulating the 5-HTP receptor than MDMA does so the effects are much more visual and last a fair bit longer. MDA is also much less predictable than MDMA, so the effects from person to person can very widely (positive or negative). It's also quite a but more toxic and places more stress on your central nervous system and cardiovascular system.
