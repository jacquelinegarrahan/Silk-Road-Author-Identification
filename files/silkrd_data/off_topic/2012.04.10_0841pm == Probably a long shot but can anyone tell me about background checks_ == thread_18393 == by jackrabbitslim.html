In addition to the poster above.

They will also run a credit report/history on you. Under the reason people with good credit can manage money and wont get themselves into situations that could be solved by selling secrets/doing illegal things.

If your going for secret clearance they will also start digging into family history unless your going for the very entry level clearance.  Under the same principle as above, if your family members get themselves into trouble, and you know secrets you may be inclined to 'sell' them to help the family member out.

Secret clearances your friends will get DOJ/Home Land Security agents that come and interview them (they'll likely call to setup an interview). These guys are highly trained to detect lies and will be carrying, but they are usually actually friendly.. DO NOT lie to them or advise your friends to lie, they are looking for lies, or reason to think your deceiptful. They usually are looking for what type of person you are, such as responsible and if you are trustworthy.

This is pretty spot on. I went through several federal background checks in the past 6 months for financial licenses I hold in different states and for a concealed handgun license and they were both pretty thorough. The first thing they will do is run credit as it's the simplest and most cost efficient way to weed applicants out being that a standard tri-merge report only runs about 19-22 dollars. Typically the only thing you can get away with on credit reports are medical collections, but any late payments, alias names or derogatory trade lines will indeed need to be explained. Residency history for typically the past 10 years will also need to be furnished and depending how important of a public position you are looking to hold extended family members will be interviewed. Like Raven said, be 100% truthful or it could come back and bite you in the ass. I know with what I do I have to maintain a certain amount of surety bonding for each individual state and any sort of misinformation can invalidate that thus rending my licenses useless.

Best of luck on whatever it is you are seeking.
