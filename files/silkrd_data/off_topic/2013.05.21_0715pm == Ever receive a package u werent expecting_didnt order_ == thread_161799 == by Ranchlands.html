Hmmm...

This is indeed very strange. It is not a lost package that then miraculously got through because you never ordered it as you say, it is obviously not an error because it's impossible someone for an error took your real name (and misspelled as some users - IMO wrongly - do) and address precisely, seemingly not a gift because you say nobody knows about you and the SR.

I can think of two cases, both of which not so good: the first (and more plausible) is that some vendor you've ordered in the past has still your address and mistaken it for that of someone else in shipment; the second is that it is a trap somewhat.

I would bet on the first case and I would not be so happy about it. You could try asking the vendors you made business with if they still have your address memorized somewhere but naturally it will be very difficult that someone of them in this case will admit "yet, sure, we have it memorized" openly, but at last maybe the fact you mentioned that you know it will be enough for him/her to cancel yourself from the list.
