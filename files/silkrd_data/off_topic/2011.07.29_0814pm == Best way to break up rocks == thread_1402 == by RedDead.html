Put the rock on a flat surface (mirrors work).  Place a credit card or some other plastic card over the rock.  Press down on the card with the side of a bic lighter and rub the lighter over the rock under the card.  Remove the card, and use it to further break up and seperate the rock into lines.  Cards work better than razors for sticky coke.
