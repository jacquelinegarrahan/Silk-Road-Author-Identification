Title: Re: Tips on Growing?
Post by: ready2roll on September 23, 2013, 05:37 am
Youre gonna need to start off by making sure if you use a tent or just the entire room that it can get 100% dark. That is one of the most important steps.

Lighting wise if you can afford it and deal with the heat look into MH/HPS (if on a tighter budget just go with HPS). LED's are very expensive and hard to find good ones. If that setup is too expensive look into CFL's. 2700K for flowering and 6500k for growing. For 3 plants you will need at least 300 (?) watts. Thats my guess. The more actual watts the better.

Circulation is also important. Get a big fan for the room and use it.

An automated timer for the lights is a must. Either 24h or 18h on for growing and 12/12 for flowering.

If odor is a concern look into carbon filters.

There are tons of guides online (grasscity, rollitup, icmag, etc) about germination and planting. read everything you can.

Best of luck to you!

Post by: mollyfan on September 23, 2013, 08:00 pm
also be careful about growing indoors if you are in a state that doesnt allow it, i just moved out of CO where its legal... and i had a pretty large crop (45 plants) and they were all hydro. i started off with soil but i found that it was just better for me to use hydro

i had quite a few patients so i was able to grow that much... but if you are in a state that doesnt allow green to be grown, if you have a nice amount of wattage coming from your plants the electricity company will know

but if not! heres a small tip from me :)

i would suggest for the veg. state that you use MH lights and keep them on a 18 hours of light and 6 hours off.

also i used foxfarm soil and nutes but be careful... if you are using foxfarm soil they have MORE than enough nutes for a while so DO NOT FEED THEM FOR A BIT! i would normally start feeding them (giving them nutes) when the perlite in the soil turned white and not that off greenish mucky color

and also all plants have suggested veg. times... so check online for the strains specific requirements... another thing is FLUSH! in my studies i have noticed that plants using soil almost NEVER use more than 20 % of the nutes that you give them... and that shit just stores itself in your soil which causes all kinds of problems later... i made it a point to flush them 2-3 times during veg and 2-3 times during flowering

when it comes time to flip them into flowering, i would use the 12 hours light and 12 hours dark schedule and it never did me wrong... also be careful with using soil... MAKE SURE YOU KEEP YOUR GROW AREA CLEAN! during the flowering stage spidermites can be a pain in the ASS! so take GREAT care of your plants and they will take care of you! if you have any questions about growing then go ahead and message me and i would gladly help you!
