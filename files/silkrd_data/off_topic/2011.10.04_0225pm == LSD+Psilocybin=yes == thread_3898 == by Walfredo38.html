Title: LSD+Psilocybin=yes
Post by: Walfredo38 on October 04, 2011, 02:25 pm
So the old lady and I had some free time the other day and the above drugs.  We didn't have all day however but a decent chunk of time to spend.  Now I have a stash of Mayan LSD and that day had a nice big shroom leftover from awhile back that measured 2.75 grams.  Not enough shrooms for each of us, and I didn't want to take too much LSD and be flying when I had to be somewhere that night.  So we each took a half tab of a 200 microgram Mayan.  Enough to feel it only a little.  We waited about 1.5 hours then split the large 2.75 gram mushroom between us.  About an hour later I was in scientific terms 'tripping balls'.  The peak lasted almost 3 hours of which the visuals were as intense as if I were fully saturated with LSD.  The spiraling snakes in my head were magnificent and just like that 3 hours later they were gone.  I was still weird and had an afterglow with huge pupils, but not tripping anymore.  I went on about my evening as planned with a sensational 3-4 hour trip with the old lady one lazy fall afternoon.  The moral to this story is LSD+shrooms much like LSD+MDMA is amazing.
