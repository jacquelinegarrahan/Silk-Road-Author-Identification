If you have been scammed and are trying to avoid that, I would steer clear completely of Moneypak to Bitcoin transactions.  Sure there are reputable vendors that will do it, and I have myself done it successfully.  However, once you get into that sort of transaction you have NO way to protect yourself, and you are in a spot where it is very easy for a reputable vendor to decide to keep your Moneypak and give you jack shit in return, it happens.

Link your bank account to Dwolla, transfer cash to Dwolla, transfer cash from Dwolla to Mtgox, buy your bitcoins, transfer them to your SR account.  All in all this process takes about 5-6 days, so plan ahead.

Dwolla did just add an instant deposit feature which will mean buying BTC instantly!  There is a $3 per month fee for this service, but that is already cheaper than buying the $5 Moneypak.
