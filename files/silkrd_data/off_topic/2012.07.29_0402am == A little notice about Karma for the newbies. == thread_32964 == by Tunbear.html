Title: A little notice about Karma for the newbies.
Post by: Tunbear on July 29, 2012, 04:02 am
Hi there folks I recently hit full member and can now give karma, and I thought I'd make this thread for users who can't do so, because hopefully, if you sweated it as much as me, you won't do afterwards.

First off, I believed the karma points were given on a per user basis, and that to add them you had to go in their profile. Because of this I held the karma points in a higher regard than what they deserve, and therefore held users with such and such points in either a high or low standing accordingly.

This is not the case. Karma points are awarded per post. Each post has an agree or disagree link underneath - all it takes is a bad post or a bad viewpoint for a user to get a lower rating.

To be honest I reckon nobody sweated it as much as me, I was so obsessed with maintaining a good number because I didn't want anyone to think I could turn out to be a shitty vendor.

But just in case anyone was sweating it, I hope this alleviates any fears :)
