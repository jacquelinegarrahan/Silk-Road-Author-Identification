Me: And that made me think of my bedroom again, and I opened my eyes to make sure it was there, and then I wanted to get back to Donald Duckn and the grid people, but it was fading rapidly.

Salvia frightens me more than DMT to be honest. It's like spice compared to marijuana in my eyes.
