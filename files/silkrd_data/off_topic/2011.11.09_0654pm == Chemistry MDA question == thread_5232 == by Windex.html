It's quite possible. And they have both been made and tested. The 2-methoxy was named MMDA-3a by Shulgin and is a psychedelic listed active at 20-80 mg.  (outside tor link) http://www.erowid.org/library/books_online/pihkal/pihkal134.shtml
The 6-methoxy was named MMDA-2 (2-methoxy-4,5-MDA = 6-methoxy-3,4-MDA by a different convention) and is active at 25-50 mg. (outside tor link) http://www.erowid.org/library/books_online/pihkal/pihkal133.shtml

They are both virtually unheard of on the market. So you better be a chemist or find one willing to synth it.
