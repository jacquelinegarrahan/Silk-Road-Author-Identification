Post by: prometheus247 on December 18, 2012, 01:22 pm
+1 to Okamiyasha for a wonderful post!

Just two quick points, psychedelics it's just one title given to a class of drugs. The association immediately done by the uneducated mind (like myself before trying them) is that they are all about illusion and nothing is real about the experience, which is completely wrong. There is no way to prove this to someone except with experience, but I believe a more accurate definition to this class of drugs is "entheogenic" which comes from the greek words "en" and "theo" which literally translate to "within" and "God". And this is precisely what psychedelics/entheogenics are all about - it put you in touch with the God within you.

The other misconception here is that LSD is some chemilca, and DMT is more natural. Wrong - there is some chemical work done to arrive to what we call LSD, but the substance is derived purely from nature, ergot fungi to be precise. Quote from wikipedia:

"LSD is an ergoline derivative. It is commonly synthesised by reacting diethylamine with an activated form of lysergic acid. Activating reagents include phosphoryl chloride[67] and peptide coupling reagents.[68] Lysergic acid is made by alkaline hydrolysis of lysergamides like ergotamine, a substance derived from the ergot fungus on rye, or from ergine (lysergic acid amide, LSA), a compound that is found in morning glory (Ipomoea tricolor) and Hawaiian baby woodrose (Argyreia nervosa) seeds."

Albert Hofmann in his book "LSD my problem child" goes in depth explaining how on a molecular level all psychedelics including DMT and LSD are related between them.
