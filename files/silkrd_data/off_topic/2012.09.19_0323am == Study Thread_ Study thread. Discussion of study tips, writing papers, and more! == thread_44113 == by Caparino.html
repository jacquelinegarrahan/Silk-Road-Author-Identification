New semester at college. More fresh pussy from high school, more parties, but there's always that thing you have to do to get good grades... We all hate it, but it has to be done :(

I'll start.

Your brain is usually most active during a certain time of the day, schedule your studying time for this. Are you usually up all night for some reason

Schedule a time? What are you daft?! No actually, I'm not. Setting a time mentally prepares you to be fully engaged into studying without your mind wandering.

Turn off the television, computer, and music while you study. Why? Two reasons; first your brain may be more stimulated when you have these things on but won't be able to absorb the material as well as it would otherwise. May be hard at first, but it gets easier after a couple times I promise. Second, it's scientifically proven that electronics emit beta waves; beta waves make your brain more dormant so you don't want that during study time... I suppose the tin foil hat on your head might take care of that though  :P

Eat some quick brain food to boost your study yields and occupy your manual fixations, albeit shortly; apples, bananas, and nuts provide you with B vitamins, the "brain" vitamins, and nuts also occupy your manual fixation--apples also release choline which help process B vitamins faster.

That's all I got for now, keep 'em comin!
