I've made it twice. Once with 80% Polish spirit, and once with plain old vodka. Both times I ground it in a herb grinder, both times I left it for a couple of weeks, shaking it occasionally when I remembered to. Both worked absolutely fine. Put less herb in if you use vodka, or use twice the amount of vodka, as obviously it's half as strong in alcohol.

Title: Re: How to make a THC tincture?
Post by: khlong boat on July 16, 2011, 03:31 pm
I love a good tincture. Everclear will work. Just grind up the bud, put it in a jar, pour some Everclear in, and let it sit in a dark place. Shake it up a few times a day.

The amount of bud you use vs. the amount of Everclear you use and the amount of time you let it sit will all play a factor in the potency of the end result. I'd say let it sit for at least a month, if not two.
