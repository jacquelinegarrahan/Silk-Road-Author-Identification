Post by: aciddeath on April 21, 2012, 12:26 am
I don't know how much 'preparing' yourself you can do for a DMT experience.  Make sure you have a quiet place and the 'correct' type of music for your experience. Measure your dose as accurately as possible

I have never done it before but I will tell you a second hand experience of my friend who recently smoked it.
He was suffering with depression. According to him he had been drinking excessively and was generally down with 'many dark and evil thoughts'. He basically felt like he was at the bottom.

From what I gather he initially smoked what he estimated to be 15mg of dmt. He did not break through.
Roughly an hour later he smoke what he claimed to be roughly double that amount while listening to Shpongle (heh) laying in his bed with an experienced dmt friend as a sitter.
Within 50 seconds he said he was completely out of his body traveling through what he described as 'inter-dimensional wormholes' that he could navigate at will. While navigating the inter-dimensional wormholes he came across a being that he knew could see him and that he could see. He said they made conversation but neither of them could understand exactly what the other was saying.
When he returned back to earth he said the experience felt like hours despite only being out for a few minutes. He described physical and mental drain.
He also said that when the experience ended he felt as though all of the angst, worry, fear, and evil that had been plaguing him had been washed away. Weeks now and he's maintained an entirely different outlook on life.

Then a few days later a family member of his tried it. The first dose did nothing.
Then a larger dose did nothing.
Then an EVEN LARGER dose just "made him feel a little light headed"
Absolutely nothing. He was and is not taking any other drugs but alcohol and marijuana (and neither of those at the time)
Crazy how either brain/organs/soul put on the brakes... or that he is physiologically not susceptible to its effects.

I can't wait to experience DMT
but I want it to be right
