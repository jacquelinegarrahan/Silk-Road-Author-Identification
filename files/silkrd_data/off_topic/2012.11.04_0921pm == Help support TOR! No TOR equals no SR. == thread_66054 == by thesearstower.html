Title: Help support TOR! No TOR equals no SR.
Post by: thesearstower on November 04, 2012, 09:21 pm
Hi all! I thought that I'd use my 100th post as an opportunity to ask everyone to give a little back to the community if they can. SR can only exist because of TOR, so it's in all of our best interests to keep the TOR network running smoothly. A larger and more robust TOR network means faster browsing & greater anonymity for us, while also ensuring functionality for those around the globe who dearly need TOR's privacy protections.

If you've got extra time, a twenty-dollar bill, a few BTC, or a fraction of BTC to spare, consider donating to one or more of these organizations.

Tor Project:
TOR PROJECT DONATION PAGE: https://www.torproject.org/donate/donate.html.en
TOR PROJECT VOLUNTEER PAGE: https://www.torproject.org/getinvolved/volunteer.html.en

Exit node & relay providers:
RISEUP.NET DONATION PAGE: https://help.riseup.net/en/donate#bitcoin
TORSERVERS.NET DONATION WALLET: 1KLhvgi9exzvea7fr1CNdHroDC28SkkvEL
TOR.NOISEBRIDGE.NET DONATION WALLET: 1PFH8NPWu2g6TdRQsirTPGpbnPBhkzQMvM

Guides to running your own relay:
AT HOME: https://www.torproject.org/docs/tor-doc-relay.html.en
IN THE CLOUD: https://cloud.torproject.org/index.html
USING RASPBERRY PI: http://www.instructables.com/id/Raspberry-Pi-Tor-relay/?ALLSTEPS

Donating your hard-earned money is not as much fun as buying drugs. I'll give you that. But if you're keen on this whole Silk Road 'thing' that we've got going here, then just think of this as 'doing your part' to keep things up and running. It only takes a moment, and it helps everyone. And while we here are using TOR to protect ourselves from governments with opressive drug laws, lots of people are living underneath much more pervasive opression, and TOR is their gateway to things like free speech and free association that we take for granted.
