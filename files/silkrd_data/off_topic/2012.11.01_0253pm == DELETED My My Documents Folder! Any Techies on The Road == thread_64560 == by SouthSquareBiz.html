There is a free program called "recuva", distributed by piriform, the same people that also made the program CCleaner. It allows you to easily recover deleted files.

http://www.piriform.com/recuva

Warning: You should not write data to the disk, there is a risk of overwriting your deleted data, causing them to be destroyed permanently.

A good option is to have another computer with recovery software installed scan your hard disk for deleted files. Or if you really have to, just install recuva and scan your disk directly and hope that installing the program doesn't overwrite the deleted files.
