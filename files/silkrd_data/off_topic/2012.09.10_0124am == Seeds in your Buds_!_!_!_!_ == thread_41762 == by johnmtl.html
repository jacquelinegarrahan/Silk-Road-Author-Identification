Post by: farmer1 on September 10, 2012, 07:40 am
'Mexican' bricked weed usually always has seeds in it. I assume in this case they are growing from seed and some males get through.

A hermaphrodite is another cause - the plant self pollinating. This could be from stress or other factors like unscheduled lighting.

The plant uses energy to create seeds. It would otherwise direct this energy towards bud formation which is what we want. We want a virgin female for the most potent smoke. Sensimilla is a mature virgin female cannabis flower. The word sensimilla comes from the spanish phrase 'sin semilla' (without seed).
