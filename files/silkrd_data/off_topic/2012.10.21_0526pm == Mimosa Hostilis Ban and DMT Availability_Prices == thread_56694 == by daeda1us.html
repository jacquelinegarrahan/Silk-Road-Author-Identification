Title: Mimosa Hostilis Ban & DMT Availability/Prices
Post by: daeda1us on October 21, 2012, 05:26 pm
Mimosa hostilis root bark is the most commonly used plant in DMT extraction because of its legality and relative high concentration of DMT compared to similar plants. Up until about a month ago, MHRB could be purchased openly and legally. In September, sonoransong.com was raided by the DEA and all of its MHRB was seized. The government began holding shipments of MHRB in customs. As a result of these events, just about every seller (that I know of) has ceased selling to the US and it seems inevitable that MHRB will be banned at some point in the near future.

My question is how do you guys think this will affect the supply, demand, and pricing of DMT on SR?

I don't question that a lot of DMT vendors have their own private sources for MHRB but at least some must have used legal, internet sources. Likewise, people (such as myself) who extract their own DMT don't really have that option anymore. My exhaustive searching has yielded little but some ridiculously priced Tor listings (I saw 10 grams/$40 on here. $40 used to get you 50 times that.) I think that, at least initially, there will be less listings and the existing listings will go up in price. My ultimate hope is that there becomes a black market demand for MHRB in light of the ban and it becomes readily available to purchase here. In the meantime, if anyone is feeling generous enough to share their own MHRB source with me in PM I would greatly appreciate it as I am currently shit out of luck.
