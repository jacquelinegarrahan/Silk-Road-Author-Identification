Title: Re: Where does the best of each drug come from in the world?
Post by: Limetless on May 07, 2013, 04:10 am
You are all being extremely narrow with this.

Cocaine - Peru/Bolivia/Columbia - First isolated/Synthesized by Richard Willstätter in 1898 (there was some minor production in Dutch Indonesia in the first decades of the 20th century)

MDMA - Globally. The idea that it all comes from the Netherlands is a crock of shit. Most MDMA in the U.K for example is actually done in clandestine labs within the U.K. The reason why it can be done globally is that there is so many different routes and there is always one that enables you to source all the precursors and reagents within a country legally.

Cannabis - Globally. Cannabis is one of the most resilient plants in the world and can grow anywhere (much like the Opium Poppy) but obviously different agricultural conditions will influence quality. Arguably the best naturally grown Cannabis is found in Afghanistan and the best quality of where it's cultivated is California and British Columbia.

Heroin/Opium - Afghanistan, The Golden Triangle, The Golden Crescent, Columbia and Mexico. The only negating factor for this is that the growing conditions in Afghanistan are ideal and thus the best quality comes from there.

Meth - Mexico/USA/Australia/South-east Asia. Meth, much like MDMA is easily produced but the only difference is that it's not caught on universally so it's nearly always produced in the locality that it's used in and if it isn't it'll be only in a neighboring country (an example of this is the Meth trade between Mexico and the U.S)

Amphetamines - Globally. Like MDMA and Meth it's easy to make clandestinely and there are many ways to make it that allows Amphetamine production to be easily done anywhere.

LSD - North America, Northern EU and India. LSD is not dependent on farming like Cocaine or Heroin however you need decent links to get the precursors and skilled chemists to make it so although it can be made anywhere the specialists that do it generally come from those places.

Ketamine - India/Mexico. India is still the largest Ketamine producing country in the world despite the law changes however recently it has begun to be produced clandestinely in Mexico instead of importing it from India for the reason stated.

Synthetics - China/India. Can be produced cheaply and quickly there because there laws haven't caught up with the west with only the ones sanctioned by the U.N (2C-B for example) being sanctioned against.

Benzodiazepines - India/Pakistan. Most generics are produced there and shipped around the world because of the relaxed laws on this.

Steroids - Same as Benzodiazepines.

Anymore ask and I shall say.
