I'd buy some peyote when I get some btc. I haven't seen it listed, but I don't frequent the psychedelic category often. I've been on hiatus for a few years, but it might be time to hop back on the train. 8)

I'll prepare it like I did San Pedro. Cut off the spines, puree the cactus, boil for at least 2 hours or until there is no more foamy gunk on top, filter with cloth and squeeze out as much of the liquid as possible, and drink the bitter green extraction. I didn't get sick and had a great trip. Ecstatic waves with visuals similar to acid, but more stimulating and longer duration. Lasted about 18 hours total including comedown. Definitely more like LSD than shrooms. Another time I drank cactus extract, I ate some shrooms. I was on another plane for a few hours.

Most of the alkaloids are in the skin and the layer just beneath. I used more cactus the second time, but had a milder trip. San Pedro potency can vary a lot making accurate dosing difficult until you get to know your cactus.

Mescaline is a psychedelic phenethylamine alkaloid, and can be found in Peyote and Peruvian Torch and San Pedro cactuses, and also in Acacia berlandieri.

There aren't many listings of Peyote or mescaline, but listings do exist. Just keep your eyes on the Mescaline category (which currently has 2 listings):
http://silkroadvb5piz3r.onion/index.php/silkroad/category/40

As for past listings of Peyote:

In June, July, and September 2011 I saw some Peyote seeds for sale from Germany in the Home & Garden category.

In July there was some San Pedro and Peruvian Torch powder for sale from the US in the Psychedelics category.

In September and October there was some Peyote seeds for sale from the USA in the Home & Garden category.

I don't think I've ever seen Peyote buttons for sale.

As for past listings of Mescaline:

In June 2011 there were 4 listings in the Psychedelics > Mescaline category, all for Mescaline HCl from the US.

In July 2011 there were 2 listings in the Psychedelics > Mescaline category, one was for "dried peruvian torch powder (2% mescaline)."

In September 2011 there 2 listings in the Psychedelics > Mescaline category, both for mescaline acetate. And later another listing for just mescaline.

In early October 2011 there 4 listings in the Psychedelics > Mescaline category, 3 for for mescaline acetate and 1 for mescaline. Later in October there were 3 listings for mescaline acetate and 2 listings for Mescaline HCL. In mid-October there were 7 listings for mescaline.

In early November 2011 there 3 listings in the Psychedelics > Mescaline category. In mid-November there were 5 listings. In late-November there were 6 listings, including mescaline acetate and mescaline citrate. In late-November there was a listing for "mescaline sulfate or chloride."

In December 2011 there were 1 or 2 listings in the Psychedelics > Mescaline category.
