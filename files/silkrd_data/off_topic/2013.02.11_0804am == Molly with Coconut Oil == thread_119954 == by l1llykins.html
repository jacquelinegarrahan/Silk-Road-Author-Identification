Title: Molly with Coconut Oil
Post by: l1llykins on February 11, 2013, 08:04 am
I decided to try some canadian brown sand with coconut oil. I scooped some coconut oil into the capsule, sprinkled the sand over it, then topped it off with more coconut oil before capping.

I ended up having a steady come-up and peaked at around 30 minutes. Then I rolled for a solid 3 hours (I normally crash around or before the 3 hour mark). It was a steady decline to baseline after that, about another 2 hours before I was left with a nice afterglow.

Anyway, it was a smooth ride and I'm definitely going to try it again, maybe swirl the stuff into the coconut oil for a more even distribution and maximize absorption.  8)
