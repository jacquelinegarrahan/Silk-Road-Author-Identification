Title: Re: How long does it take you guys to get your BTC to your SR wallet?
Post by: fred5 on September 22, 2012, 07:31 pm
Usually about 6 to 10 confirmations in the block chain, or about an hour or 2. But don't freak out if it takes a bit longer... think my longest SR deposit wait has been 6 hours. Bitcoin transactions are sometimes subject to strange happenings, they can get trapped in orphan blocks, which slows them down, I've also seen one take 2 days for a single confirmation. It did eventually come through, have never lost a coin in the void :) You can also plug your address into the search at http://blockchain.info and track it's progress. If it doesn't show in 24 hours, message support on the main site and they'll take care of you, they're awesome like that :)
