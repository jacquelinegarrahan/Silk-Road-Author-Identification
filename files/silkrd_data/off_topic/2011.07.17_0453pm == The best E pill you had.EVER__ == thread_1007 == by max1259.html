 We got out from the pub and just couldn't stop saying how good we felt,drove up to the rave with out heads out the window screaming like bloody banshee's,what a night,what a rush,only thing is no E has even come close to that since,not even pure MDMA crystals,like i said always something missing,nothing has that BIG Eurphoric hit or really happy tripping your balls off feeling,or maybe it's just me but a lot of people have said it.
