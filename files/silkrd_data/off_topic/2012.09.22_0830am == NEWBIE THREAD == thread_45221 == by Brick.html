Post by: ZenAndTheArt on September 22, 2012, 01:33 pm
FE = Finalize Early
LE = Law Enforcement

If you finalize early you are giving your money straight to the vendor before you have received anything. This means that it could be hard or impossible to get it back if something goes wrong. If you use escrow you effectively give that money to Silk Road, who holds it until you receive your item and are happy with it, at that point you should be fair to your vendor and finalize as soon as possible.
