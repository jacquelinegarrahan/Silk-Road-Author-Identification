It doesn't really matter with having the same SR account and forum names. What does matter though is if you're a vendor, to have two different accounts on SR. Do not use your buyer account as a vendor account, as those you may have bought from in the past may have your personal information and it's entirely possible they may out you as a vendor to LE.

As long as you keep information to a minimum on both the forums and SR, you should be fine with using the same username on both. A lot of vendors do it, and so do buyers.
