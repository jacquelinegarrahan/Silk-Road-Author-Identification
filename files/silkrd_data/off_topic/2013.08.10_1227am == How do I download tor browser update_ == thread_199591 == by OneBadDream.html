Quote from: isallmememe on August 11, 2013, 08:52 am
when i dl'd a new tor bundle it didn't have my bookmarks so i deleted it again. anyone know how to import them?
In your outdated Browser Bundle, press Ctrl+Shift+B and go to "Import and Backup" -> "Backup..." and save the file somewhere.
In your fresh Browser Bundle, press Ctrl+Shift+B and go to "Import and Backup" -> "Restore" -> "Choose File..." and open the file you have previously saved.
