Title: Re: Whats the best method for putting powder into capsules? Or any method?
Post by: cantharidin on April 26, 2013, 07:57 pm
Get a plastic 1 milliliter syringe. Use a razor blade to cut off the end that the needle attaches to - now you have a small cylinder with a plunger. Pull the plunger back to a specific volume marking - experiment to find which mark corresponds to the weight that you want.  Tap the open end of the cylinder into a pile of our powder until the cylinder is full of powder. The syringe will fit right into the capsule half and you can press the powder out gently with the plunger.

You'll be surprised at how reproducible the weight is if you take even minimal care to tap into the powder more or less the same each time.
