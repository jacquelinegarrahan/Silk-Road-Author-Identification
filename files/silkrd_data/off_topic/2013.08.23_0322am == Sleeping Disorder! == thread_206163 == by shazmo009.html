Title: Sleeping Disorder!
Post by: shazmo009 on August 23, 2013, 03:22 am
Hey guys, I have been having this problem in my sleep for about 5-6 years now and up until now I have just let it go as I am kind of used to it now.

This is a long post so if anyone has any experience please read...

Basically, its sleep paralysis. What happens is when I am lying in bed going to sleep sometimes (maybe once or twice a week on average) my body falls asleep before my mind. I know it sounds crazy but its a surreal and scary feeling. Anyone that has had this will understand. My body feels totally paralyzed but I can think at the same time. So imagine lying in bed, cant move your body but you can think at the same time...My breathing goes really shallow and I start to panic because it feels like I am going to stop breathing. I try to moan to get my partners attention. Sometimes it works and she wakes me out of it but others she says I wasn't making any noise. Sometimes I can move my toes slightly but I really have to strain to do it. Its really fuckin scary but I have sort of gotten used to it over the years but I always get that initial panic. Once I snap out of it and sit up, occasionally when I rest my head again it will happen straight away and I can feel my body becoming paralyzed again. How I stop this is I have to get up out of bed and go do something for a couple of minutes like have a cigarette or something to eat. Anyone else have any experience with this?

Also, I get another thing that feels like electric shocks running through my brain. Its only 1 really quick one and my head will twitch right off the pillow. Then maybe 30 seconds later another one. It happens over and over and its not painful but it is in a way o_O If that makes sense...Its just like a shock to my brain...I even went to my GP and they could not explain this one...I might have to go see a specialist. If anyone has any similar experiences please let me know because im trying to find a solution to these...

My GP put me on Endep which is 'Amitriptyline Hydrochloride'. I didn't have any sleeping troubles for the first week but into the second week, wouldn't ya know it...My issue comes straight back so this Endep is not the answer. Someone help :(
