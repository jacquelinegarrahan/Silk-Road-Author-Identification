This. Only really need a pencil, or pen, though. Take off the stem, and the top of an apple is a perfect natural bowl, poke the pencil straight down where the stem was, stopping at about halfway through the apple. Poke a 2nd hole from the side of the apple (wherever you want the mouthpiece) to intersect this hole, then a third hole higher than this for a carb. Then poke down the hole for the bowl again to shove the apple bits further down so they don't block your holes for your carb and mouth piece.

+1 for green apples, give more flavor than red.
