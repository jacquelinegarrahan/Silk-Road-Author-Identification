Title: Re: anyone care to teach us about hash?
Post by: jmoney113 on March 10, 2012, 06:05 am
Hashes have great variance, simply because there are so many ways to extract and process the trichomes into a purer THC product. I'm not an expert on Middle Eastern hashes, but I have heard they hand rub their product over fine silk screens in order to attain the purity they desire. Obviously the finer the screen, the finer the end product. Most people judge hash quality on it's meltiness. Full melt being the top quality most go by, is pretty self explanatory. If the hash bubbles and melts completely with no ash, then you have yourself a very nice quality hash. Then it goes down from there to half melt, quarter melt, and anything lower than that isn't really worth mentionioning. Generally it's bubble hash which uses those grades, as bubble hash is filtered through several different size micron screens before the final product is finished. Again, the finer the screen, the meltier the product. The reason being, the finer the screen, the less plant matter that goes in, and the higher concentration of trichome heads, which results in a very melty hash. Dry sift, cold water extraction, ice water extraction, etc. There's numerous ways to get a very refined product.

Then theres your hash oils which again vary with extraction method. The most common hash oil is BHO, which is simply extracted with triple or quadruple refined butane through some type of tube made out of non-reactive material like a stainless steel turkey baster, or a custom glass extractor. Then you heat or vacuum purge the product to evap all of the remaining butane, and are left with a golden gooey/hard/buddery/waxy substance, that varies greatly with ones technique and even environmental factors such as heat and humidity. Then you can go into CO2, ether, or even hexane extractions, which I'm not familiar with.

Best way to smoke hash: (like a Canadian)

Bottle tokes, or rather "bots" or "bods" depending on your vocal dialect.

Break up your hash into little ball like pieces and set them out on a tray like surface, like a book or cd case or cigarette pack. (Canadian 25 packs are best for this, I believe in America cigarettes only come in 20s)

This works best with dark hash that does not crumble easily.

Now take an empty bottle with a cap and burn one hole in the side large enough to fit a cigarette through easily.

Light a cigarette.

Pick up one bot at a time on the burning tip of the cigarette, let the smoke start to stream, and then insert it into the bottle through the hole with the cap on. When you see the hash has finished burning remove the cigarette, unscrew the cap and inhale, or leave the cap on and pass the bottle to your friend. Then repeat.

This takes practice but its awesome. If you don't like cigarettes you might cough or get dizzy.

If you do this in your car or bedroom you can pass the smell off as cigarettes, don't attempt while driving. Also be careful not to drop bots on your pants, most of my pants have burn holes in them from this.

Btw the process is referred to as brewing, 'brewing bots.'

The most efficient way to smoke hash is doing blades.  No waste.

First I use a marble slab to place a bunch of little pieces of hash, just tiny 'lil things big enough to hold in your lungs without coughing out all your smoke.
I use a propane plumbing torch to heat up a couple butter knives and place a plastic cone made out of a pop bottle-top in my lips, and gently do a tap-twist to one of the hash bits to connect it to one knife.
I then place it under the cone (no touching!) and squash it down with the second red-hot blade while inhaling. This all takes about 3 seconds.  While holding that smoke in, I then scrape the knives together to get rid of the charred remains, and place the blades back into the flame to heat back up for the next one.  After about 20 of these I'm ready to take on any task, or sleep, depending on the characteristics of the hash.  :P
