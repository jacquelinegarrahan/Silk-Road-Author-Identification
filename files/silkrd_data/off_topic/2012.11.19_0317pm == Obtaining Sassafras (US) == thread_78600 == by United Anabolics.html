Title: Re: Obtaining Sassafras (US)
Post by: K5blazer on November 20, 2012, 02:11 am
A lot of the "synthetic" oil still contains safrole. If you do some googling there is a DEA document where they use a standard marquis reagent test to test for the presence of safrole, it gives the results of color change based on percent content. So you can buy a small sample and test it. Also if that does not work you can always harvest or obtain the bark yourself and then steam distill it to get oil.
