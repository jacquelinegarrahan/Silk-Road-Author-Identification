Post by: A Riotous Defect on December 20, 2012, 03:38 am
I agree with abby25, the college crowd is definitely your best bet. At my college ( and every single college I've ever visited ) there are fucking DROVES of people looking for good weed. And there's a lot of them that get weekly money from their folks that SOLELY goes towards weed. Much more demand than supply. However, if you're not the kind of guy that would fit in on a college campus, then I doubt you should go there - people get sketched out quick at colleges and you never know what could happen.

But the MDMA market at colleges is booming as well, especially if you have a college in a city that has a nice rave scene. But with MDMA, you do have to be careful - the penalties are usually higher for distribution and there WILL be times when you might have to fight out of getting robbed.

What I did when I dealt at my college is sit at one of the smoking gazebos ( my college didn't allow smoking except in designated smoking areas ) and shoot the breeze with people. Obviously it was easier for me because I went to the college, but sitting out there later at night from around 8 pm to 12 am, I usually found at least several people looking for some weed. And most of the people that are looking for weed ( if they're not narcs ) are usually pretty docile, and the reason they're looking is just 'cause they don't know anybody that has any, or don't have any connections at all. Stick to naive people - but don't go after people that have a sketchy feel to 'em. There are a lot of kids in college that will get pinched and sing as soon as they figure out they can save their asses and stay in college. Be careful. It can be very dangerous.
