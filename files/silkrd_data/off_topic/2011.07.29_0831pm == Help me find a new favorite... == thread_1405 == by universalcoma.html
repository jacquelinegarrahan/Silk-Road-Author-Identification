Title: Re: Help me find a new favorite...
Post by: y00d0k0b0z on August 01, 2011, 11:43 am
To preface my long and rambling post, I started out as a regular user of E in the early 2000s and after a few years of blissful conversations with friends/significant others, raves, and solo rolls I started making the same progression as you.  It's been an extraordinarily fun journey and I have yet to try anything that I can look back and say "Ooh, branching out and trying that was a mistake."  Oh, except for salvia; had one very uncomfortable trip with my girlfriend at the time.  We both lost all sense of trust in one another and weren't able to talk about it, and in retrospect, if we had forced our way through those emotions and talked it out, we might still be together now.  That should have been an ecstasy night, not salvia.  Oh well.

Anyway, I haven't had a chance to do a "common" dose of 2C-B yet, but having ordered some from here I'm looking forward to having a couple of days off work to do so.  I tried a 12mg dose a couple of nights ago and found it to be a really mellow yet friendly experience compared to LSD and shrooms.  I'm can't wait to try a stronger dose and combine it with MDMA. 

Even at a low dose I had a noticeable sense of physical well-being and empathy; the patterns on my stained concrete counter were breathing and shifting in a beautiful way; but I didn't have any open or closed eye visuals. Took a couple of somas after 3 hours or so as I was coming down to facilitate sleep since I had work the next afternoon and woke up feeling fine.  An interesting note is that while on it, I didn't feel a desire to have taken more for a stronger trip.  From reading other trip reports and my one low-dose experience, it seems like a versatile substance that can be lots of things for lots of people (somewhat similar to MDMA).

The other 2C-series is also interesting, but I personally prefer the shorter trips of 2C-C (which I've tried also at a low dose and it was similar to my recent 2C-B experience, but it lacked something that made me interesting in ordering more).  The 8 hour trip that faded in and out on 2C-I was a bit long for my taste and also didn't have the warm fuzzies that I got on 2C-B.

Also if you like MXE, I'd definitely see about getting some ketamine to try.  Some of my most memorable drug experiences have been had on K.  I haven't had any profoundly spiritual experiences yet, but even more-so than with psychedelics I've had really odd thought patterns that after the trip make a lot of sense and have given me a different way of looking at life and myself in the long-term.  And, it's a lot of fun in smaller doses :D

And as a last thought, once you're comfortable with substances individually and have done your research, combining them - ie MDMA and LSD) can lead to ridiculously good times.  One of my best friendships that's been going strong 10 years now all started the night I first tried that combo and started talking with somebody I had just met the day before.  Hopefully your acid arrives soon so you can give it a go ;)
