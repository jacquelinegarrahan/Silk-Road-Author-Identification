I believe what you are refering to is how a certain variety  of weed (strains are for fungi, plants are varieties) expands in your lungs after taking a hit. I might be using the wrong example but, think of it as heavy and light smoke.

A dank cheese variety might be a heavy smoke, when you take a normal hit, about a second later it feels as if you took a hit 3x as big, almost like it exands in your lungs.

I generally find the danker, indica varietis to be the lung expanders whereas sativa's seem to be much ligher. Most likely has to do with resin content being slighty higher in many indica varieties.
