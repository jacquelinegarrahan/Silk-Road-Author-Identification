Title: Why are these forums so awesome?
Post by: SavoyFan on March 05, 2012, 07:28 pm
Like many of you I have been part of a wheelbarrow full of different forums. From Xbox to Internet Marketing to Pickup I have seen a variety of different people interacting through the internet. That being said, I have never encountered a better group of people then my fellow Silk Roader's on here. You guys are amazing! I was thinking about this last night as I fell asleep. I was trying to figure out first; what exactly I liked so much about these forums and secondly; what was responsible for this. Here are some of my conclusions. Probably not groundbreaking by any means but I think it sums up some different ideas well.

I think these forums are remarkably judgement free. This strikes me the most as I read through here from Rumor Mill to Off Topic. I see so little judging and people putting other people down it really astounds me. Everyone always seems so positive and happy, I love it! Maybe it's because people are high all the time but I don't think it is that. For me at least, my happiness comes from knowing that a subversive community like this not only exists but thrives.

What I was really thinking about though was why is this community so close knit? Again not groundbreaking but I really came to the conclusion that our underground culture is really responsible. Everyone here is taking some degree of risk and we are skirting our respective countries laws. This really seems to all put as together in a very small boat that encourages cooperation. I think it is hard to judge one another when we are all to some degree fucked if ever found out.

Wow, this whole post sounded alot better in my head than it came out on paper. Disappointing to me but I gave it my best shot. What I really wanted to say is that I love these forums! They are the best I have ever been a part of. As a general rule I do not really like small communities but if I could live with all you guys in the real world I would do it! As long as you didn't steal all my drugs lol

One final note, I had the idea for this post and did the subsequent posting completely sober. I know you guys would not judge me but I think it adds a hint more credibility that my un-inebriated brain did all this

~SavoyFan
