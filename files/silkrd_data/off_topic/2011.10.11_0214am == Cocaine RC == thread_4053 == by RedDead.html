i was looking at it myself, its not actually a cocaine RC its a methylphenidate (ritalin) analog, in fact you may have experienced it in a way already, much like cocaine when ritalin is taken with alcohol the way in which the liver metabolizes it changes and rather than creating an inactive metabolite it creates ethylphenidate an active and possibly stronger metabolite, cocaine and alcohol yields cocaethylene as a metabolite which is actually said to be more euphoric than coke itself. so basically if youre a fan of a bit of ritalin and some booze then this one is probably for you but i wouldnt go expecting it to be too cocaine like and it doesnt have any anesthetic properties so no numb face obviously.
