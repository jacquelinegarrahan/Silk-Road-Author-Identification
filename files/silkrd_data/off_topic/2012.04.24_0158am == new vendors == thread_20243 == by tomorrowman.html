When someone orders from me (when I have stock! thanks 420 sale!!) they KNOW it will ship that day or the next day AT THE LATEST. That I will msg them if something happens or just msg them to thank them for their order (not as much now, too busy!)
I am sure there are plenty of 'professional' buyers on here who will tell you that price is not the only factor.

Good Luck!!

Title: Re: new vendors
Post by: cerealbox on April 24, 2012, 02:17 am
If you can offer something unique that others can't, you'll get noticed, IMO. The users of the SL aren't one-off drug addicts, but rather responsible, mature dabblers. Offer a price and a product that can't be beat and you'll get some loyal customers in no time. It's basically monopolistic competition. Distinguish and differentiate yourself and you'll be somewhat of a price-maker. Sell a homogenous product and you'll be a price-taker.

Source: microeconomics.
