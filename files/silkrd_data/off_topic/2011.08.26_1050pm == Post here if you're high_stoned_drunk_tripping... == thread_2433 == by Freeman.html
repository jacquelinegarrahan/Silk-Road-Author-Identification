well you guys it really depends on your psychedelic experience.

you can break it down into 3 classes

like this :

-lightweight - little to no experience with psychedelics, probably took shrooms once or twice, but has no idea what hardcore tripping is, more likely to freak out or not be able to handle the intensity of the trip. for this person i would advise small doses  like what watmm said, to become familiar with it, and lots of meditation prior to smoking it. respecting the spirit is always advisable. just continue double dose after you become familiar with one amount.

-middleweight - moderate experience, has taken shrooms multiple times, maybe lsd, or mescaline, once or twice. most likely this person has had one or two difficult experiences but they managed to overcome them and continue to do psychedelics, that kind of experience helps a ton when doing dmt because you need to be used to your mind being completely blown away. 50mg of pure dmt would be a perfect starter dose. changa is better because it smokes better and more evenly(effects you better and doesnt taste so much like shit)

-heavyweight - Basically someone who has done every psychedelic they can get their hands on, someone who loves to trip and has done it a multitude of times. I dont even really think i need to explain this any further, you folks know who are ;) perfect dose for a heavyweight would be 75mg 100mg. yellow is more intense, white fluff is smoother. i would do 100mg of white


***DONT EVER OVERDOSE ON DMT***

make sure you weigh your dose accurately, dont be scared to smoke it all once you have weighed it and know it is right.

i would never advise going over 100mgs until youve smoke 100mg dose at least 5 times, then move up slowly. if teleporting is your goal, you need to ease into it, never dive in head first.
