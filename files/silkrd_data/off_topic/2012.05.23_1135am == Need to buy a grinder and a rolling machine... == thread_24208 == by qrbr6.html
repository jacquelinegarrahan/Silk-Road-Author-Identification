Title: Re: Need to buy a grinder and a rolling machine...
Post by: Meister on May 23, 2012, 03:19 pm
I can roll by hand but I still use my little 'rolling machine', it's the cheapplastic kind. I get perfect joints from them every time. Most important part is grinding your weed, people who use a grinder often grind it up way too much and it'll roll too packed and you won't be able to hit it. Those that break it up by hand often don't break it up enough and get a chunky smokeable joint, but it doesn't stay lit properly. So you have to find that middle ground.

Then, with the roller open, you dump it in, close it and roll it a few times to get any excess out. You want the roller facing in a direction where you pull the bottom roller towards you with your thumbs. Then you lightly lick the strip on your paper, put the non-licked side into the crease of the roller with the licked portion facing you, and pull the bottom roller again towards you with your thumbs several times.

For a grinder, it depends on how much you want to spend. A space case is awsome and with multiple chambers you get way better consistency and can choose how much you want it ground up. I like mine just barely ground up, some people who use smaller pipes want it ground up quite a bit.

The cheap metal ones they sell in gas stations, I've heard of people who push down really hard to grind on these and metal flakes end up in the weed. Not from the grinding spikes but from the edges of the grinder itself. The titanium grinders and such you won't have to worry about that. They also make wooden grinders, never tried one but I like the idea of it.
