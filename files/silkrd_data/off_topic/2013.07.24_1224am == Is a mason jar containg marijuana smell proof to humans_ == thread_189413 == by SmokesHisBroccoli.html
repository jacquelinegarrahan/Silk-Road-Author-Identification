Alright after spending some more time on the internet looking for other solutions, here's what I have come up with. 

First, I double vacuum seal the bud (going to take a shortcut and use cheap ziplock hand held vacuum start kit for like $3 as opposed to a food saver since this isn't for long term storage).  Next I take some baking soda and pour some of it into the bottom of the mason jar.  Then I close the mason jar up and rubber band some dryer sheets to the top.  Finally I keep the open box of baking soda in the cabinet along with a couple of dryer sheets. 

So basically my original plan but adding some vacuuming sealing and baking soda which seems like a good odor neutralizer.
Title: Re: Is a mason jar containg marijuana smell proof to humans?
Post by: tbart on July 24, 2013, 02:56 pm
it helps if you vacuum seal it - one way, the way the folks that can food, is to place the jars (with the mj already in it) in a pot of hot water, immersing about 75-80% of the jar (they make special tools that look like large pliers to handle the jars with) - basically you're heating the air in the jar, then when you put the lid on tight, and then when the jar cools, the air inside cools and contracts (becomes denser air) and that pulls the lid's seal down even tighter

they also make an adaptor for the lid with a small hand operated vacuum pump. A lot of folks in the tobacco forums like to cure their tobacco in mason jars and this is what they do
