Post by: tedka on June 10, 2012, 11:55 am
from the popular series of macgyver drug paraphernalia:

materials needed:
7.62mm (or larger) rifle cartridge, discharged and cleaned.
a tool to drill metal, one very thin, one about half as thick as the cartridge used
a tool to drill wood orplastic, for creating the chamber that holds the amount you intend to snort
a little round piece of wood or plastic, the diameter of the hole you drill in the cartridge, about 3cm long
a little piece of cork, to close to top. can be whittled from a large cork.   

steps:
drill a hole through the rifle cartridge, just below the point where it starts to get narrow, large enough to give the little round piece of wood/plastic a tight fit

drill a very small hole in the narrow upper part of the cartridge, where the bullet used to be (for airflow when you snort out of the top)

whittle a cavity in the middle of the wood/plastic piece to hold the amount of substance you intend to snort.

shove the little stick with the hole through the large hole in the cartridge. make sure it fits rather tight and doesnt turn too easy/freely. turning it should require some manual force to avoid accidental filling of the "snorting chamber" with material

take a large natural or plastic wine bottle cork and whittle it to a smaller cork that fits the tip of the cartridge nice and tight. ugly, lazy ass version: fill top with gluegun glue and pull it out when cooled :)

remove the little turnable stick with the cavity and fill the cartridge with the substance you intend to use and reinsert the piece.

tilt the cartridge and turn the turnable piece from 6 to 12o clock. you can make markings on the "chamberpiece" where is up and where is down to avoid confusion

remove the cork from the top and hit it


ps:
if you cant find old rifle cartridges; try stores that sell clothes and accessories for kids who want to piss off their parents, they should have fake ammo necklaces/belts.
