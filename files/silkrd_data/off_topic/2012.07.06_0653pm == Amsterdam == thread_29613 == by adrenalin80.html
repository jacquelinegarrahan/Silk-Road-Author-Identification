I certainly wouldn't have any idea how to find drugs anywhere besides my own town simply because I just don't know anybody there.

If I were you, OP, I personally would probably smuggle them in myself. Here are two ideas you might want to consider:

1. Contact the hotel ahead of time and see if you can ship yourself some packages for when you arrive. As long as you only ship a small amount and put it in with a lot of other legitimate items, you will probably be okay. This is assuming they let you ship something there, though.

2. Put the powders inside capsules and hide them somewhere. If you have an Adderall prescription, putting it inside Adderall capsules is probably really your best bet. Otherwise, you could probably put some of them in a B-Vitamin capsule container. I took a capsule of B-Vitamins (legitimate vitamins; I didn't smuggle anything) with me on an international trip, and while they did a complete search of my person and both of my bags, they didn't actually check what was inside the capsules (that would be impractical, no?).

Or you could just stay sober in Amsterdam. You'll most certainly be able to find weed up there (though I hear those places are getting less friendly to foreigners), and while the drugs will certainly make it more fun, you can still have fun without them.

Whatever you choose, I wish you the best of luck, adrenalin80.
