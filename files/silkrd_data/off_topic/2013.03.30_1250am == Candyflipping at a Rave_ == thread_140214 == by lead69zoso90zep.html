Title: Re: Candyflipping at a Rave?
Post by: hee57 on March 30, 2013, 01:54 am
Few notes. Take in mind this is all personal recommendation. I'd say do 150ug (half a tab) and 100 mg of mdma. That may sound light, but both of these drugs play off one another. One of the biggest issues people have with candyflipping is that the mdma overpowers the lsd. You obviously have enough acid where that wont be an issue. What's the highest dose of lsd you've had and think about how you functioned on that. Then remember that the effects increase exponentially as you up the dose.

tl;dr I wouldn't dose that high, but if you think you can handle it feel free. Should be fun no matter what.
