Title: Re: could use some help on my first transaction :)
Post by: phubaiblues on May 23, 2012, 11:59 pm
Yeah, when you're a new buyers, without enough purchases, they can and do, require that you finalize early.  You can either just put FE, new buyer in the feedback area, or just leave it blank, and yes, later you can go to 'Accounts' and see the 'View Feedback' and you are allowed to edit it.  The only real help you can give yourself, is to study up in these forums, what is said about the vendor.  Blow off the flamers and idiots, and see what other buyers say about the vendor...in *recent* threads too.  Some of the worst scammers on here had extensive, wonderful, glowing praise all over the place on here...until they ripped everybody off.  See 'Italian Pilot' or 'Tony76' or...well, you get the idea.

Tracking numbers, ah, they're more for the vendor, IMO, to protect him from false claims by by sleazy vendors.  I don't like them, but that's just me...mostly u just got to give it a little time, think of something else ha ha....

But yeah, it's in transit, should be here soon, then u go back and edit the feedback, mention if shipping took to long, and how quality was, if it's not pharmaceutical, and don't describe shipping methods that might reveal too much about vendor. 

Just part of the process.  Once you've been here a while, you can stay in escrow.
