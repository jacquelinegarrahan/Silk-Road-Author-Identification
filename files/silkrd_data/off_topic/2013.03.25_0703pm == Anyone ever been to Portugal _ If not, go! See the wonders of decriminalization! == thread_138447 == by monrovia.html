Title: Anyone ever been to Portugal ? If not, go! See the wonders of decriminalization!
Post by: monrovia on March 25, 2013, 07:03 pm
I had an awesome experience when I went there. In case you don't know, ALL drugs in personal use quantities are decriminalized there, and the policy is actually working quite well. In the capitol city I visited for a few days(I was in the Azores, not the mainland) there were tons of kids who would smoke hash/weed(mostly hash, being so close to morocco and all) in the open at nighttime, and no one would bother them if they weren't causing a scene. I also had the chance to get my hands on some fine Moroccan hash from a friendly, dark skinned, Portuguese speaking(obviously) kid who was nice enough to provide me with a gram of hash for 15 euros as long as I smoked a tiny bit of it with him. It was just really cool to hang out with some locals for a few minutes and try to communicate through the minor language barrier we had(I speak very little Portuguese, and he spoke a little English.) The attitude of police over there are very different than in the US as well. No cops on every corner trying to bust you for speeding. I think I could have counted the number of speed limit signs the whole 2 weeks I was there on one hand. The 2 times a saw a cop that I can remember was when we got into an accident the first day we were there, and when we were parked a block down from a police station at a coffee shop. We were parked illegally, and a cop walked right by us on his way to get a cup for himself. He obviously saw us, but didn't say a word. I hated my country(USA) just a little bit more than I already did when I got back. It has since become a goal of mine to move there permanently at some point of my life. It's where my ancestry is and it's just a naturally beautiful place.
