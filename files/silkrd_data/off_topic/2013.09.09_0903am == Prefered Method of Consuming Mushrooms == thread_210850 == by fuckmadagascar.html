Title: Re: Prefered Method of Consuming Mushrooms
Post by: underbelly on September 09, 2013, 09:14 am
Mushie Smoothie!!  ;D

My fave method which causes no nausea and no puking is to grind up dried mushrooms in a coffee grinder.
Get a fruit smoothie, anything fruity or berry tasting (raspberry, mango, passion fruit, pineapple etc). Pour in half the cup, add the mushroom powder, then pour on top more of the smoothie. Stir with a spoon and drink it slowly or fast.
You WONT taste mushrooms. The fruity goodness completely masks the texture and the flavour of mushies.

I dont take them any other way and you will always trip hard cos you are consuming the entire load.

Also there is no loss due to heat or cooking as psilocybin is fragile.

PS if you have fresh mushrooms then just blend them with a smoothie and drink.

Just make sure its really fruity, I tasted this sweet non fruit based smoothie in Thailand and it tasted like crap.
