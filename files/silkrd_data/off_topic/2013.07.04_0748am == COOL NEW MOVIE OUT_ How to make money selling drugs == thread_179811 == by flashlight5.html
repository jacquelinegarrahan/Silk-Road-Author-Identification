As a cannabis smoker, the game is different but people will shoot you for a kilo of bud. Drugs = money + no police. That is tempting for the gangs of robbers who prey on drug dealers.

I jokingly mentioned to watch Blow, or Scarface. Honestly, that's just not how it's done these days. The cartels are the only ones fucking it up for the rest of us, here in America. The Mob is pretty hands-off with drugs(American Mafia, I mean). They used to leave it to the cartels and the hood. Every time someone in the Mob gets involved with large drug trafficking, there's a lot of unnecessary blood shed; so those days are long over.

I'm not at the top, by any means, but I'm well versed in the trade. Most of what these trafficking organizations have done since the 80's and 90's is take the movement of, and the distribution of drugs out of the hands of the Cartels. Mainly, by sourcing to and from online shops and discussion boards like this. Jack N Hoff seems to understand, albe-him a troll(lol) --that this is the way things are done. And have been done for some time now(long before SR).

Silk Road is the tip of the Iceberg glimmering out of the top of the deep-sea...

From my time in the game, I will tell you... there are TWO definite black markets. The violent, dying out, black market of the past... and the non-violent, growing, black market of today and tomorrow. I cannot by any means go into detail, but a lot of powerful money has gone into the drug trade; to fund labs, grows, shipments, etc. and hurt the Cartels where it matters most. Some of the very same people who have made the necessary political pushes to legalize and decriminalize cannabis in states across the nation.

Violence in this game is the past; not the future. It takes a lot of strong will and courage to make that true.
