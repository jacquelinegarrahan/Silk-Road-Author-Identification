Title: Re: I wanna make THC pills with hash oil, anyone know how?
Post by: Ballzinator on February 16, 2013, 08:17 pm
You're right about the tetrahydrocannabinolic acid (2-COOH-THC) having to be decarboxylated into THC. 2-COOH-THC decarboxylates rapidly (in a few seconds) at 106°C but if the temperature is too high, the active ingredients will evaporate. So there are two options depending on if you have a good digital food thermometer or not.
If you have one, you can simply put any kind of oil into a pot with your extracts and keep that at just over 106°C for like a minute, stir that shit up and you're done.
If you don't have a thermometer, you need to mix the oil and extracts in a heat conductive container (so preferably metal) and put that container into a boiling water bath in a pot. The problem with that is that the temperature will never exceed 100°C and the decarboxylation process will take much longer. You'll probably have to let the container in the water bath for about an hour.
