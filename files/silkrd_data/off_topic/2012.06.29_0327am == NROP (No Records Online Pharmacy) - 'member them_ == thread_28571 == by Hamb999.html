Title: NROP (No Records Online Pharmacy) - 'member them?
Post by: Hamb999 on June 29, 2012, 03:27 am
Anybody remember the NROP's back in the mid-2000?  It was mostly norcos and phentermine I think - at least that's what we bought.  You filled out an online questionnaire with basic medical questions.  With phentermine, for example, we listed a high BMI and that we had been prescribed it before, etc.  The site would say that a doctor would review and call you and you had to list a primary care doc, but they never checked any of these things which were fake, of course.  They shipped from very shady Florida (mostly) pharmacies via fedex and DHL.  It was pretty expensive.

There were also sites (I seem to remember one called Friendly Clinic) that required verified medical records.  Some sent a Nurse Practitioner to your house to do a physical exam.  They were a lot cheaper (once you paid the up front fees for the physical) than NROP's and I think you could get stronger stuff or more of it.  I never worked up the guts to try to fake medical records, but I was tempted.

There was a review forum, much like this one, called drugbuyers.com, i think.  I tried that URL and it says forums are closed.  Likely the victim of the DEA crack down on online pharmacies and pill mills.

Anywho, 'member them?
