Well i basically listen to the same bands no matter what psychedelic im on. But i arrange my playlist order differently.

Here are some of my favorite albums/songs.

The Jimi Hendrix Experience: Electric Ladyland

-One of the greatest albums of all time. We all know his hits like voodoo child, all along the watchtower, etc. But i consider "1983, a Merman i Should Turn to Be" (13 minute song) and "Rainy Day, Dream Away/Still Raining Still Dream" (1 song spliced to into 2, 8 minutes total)  Psychedelic Masterpieces. They both make you feel like you're in a dream.

"Long hot summer night" is more of a blues psychedelia, just a great song in general.

A few songs off his other albums: "The Stars that Play with Laughing Sam's Dice" (STP with LSD) is a mind fuck, "Room Full of Mirrors", "Ezy Ryder", "Power of Soul", "Third Stone from the Sun", "Purple Haze" (ofcourse). Can't go wrong with any of them.

MGMT: All of their albums are loaded with neo psychedelic audio paintings. My favorites are" It's Working" (which is about MDMA), "Electric Feel", and "4th Dimensional Transition" (They did alot of DMT)

Pink Floyd: "Dark Side of the Moon". "Us and Them" was the first song i ever tripped to. Listening to it sober can bring tears of joys to my eyes.

Tame Impala: Another Neo Psychedelic Band. "Innerspeaker" is their only album to date, one of my all time favorites. Check out the song "When the Feelings in the Core", one of my favorite songs to trip to.

Cream: "White Room", "Strange Brew" "SWLABR", just to name a few.

We should check out each others recommended songs, tripping or not, and leave some feedback :D Im always looking for new music.
