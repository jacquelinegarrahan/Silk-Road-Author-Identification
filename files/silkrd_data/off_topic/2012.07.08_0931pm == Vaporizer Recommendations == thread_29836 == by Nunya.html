Post by: dawnglider on July 08, 2012, 10:19 pm
I own a Magic Flight Launch Box - you get a great heady high from vaping with this. :D

You don't need to use much in the tray, which might save some people on bud. It runs on batteries and comes with 2 AAs to start you off, though I ended up buying more powerful batteries (IMEDION 2400 mAh) due to the original ones losing their capacity after a while. :/

The dimensions of the box are 7.5cm(L)x4cm(W)x2.3cm(D).

As for odour control, the mflb is pretty damn good but it does give off a burnt popcorn-like smell if you hold the battery in for too long, or let any vapour escape... Nothing an open window or some air freshener can't fix.

Actually using it is a bit of a learning curve for some - you have to control the temperature with your tokes (ideally speaking, you want to use it like you're sipping out of a straw). There's a nice tutorial on vaporpedia.com.

For vaporiser size, I guess the Iolite is the closest matching. There's also the VaporGenie which is a lot cheaper, and functions as a pipe too.
