Title: Re: Prices for opiates skyrocketing?
Post by: watertiger on February 29, 2012, 01:40 am
I'm not sure about lots of opiate vendors disappearing, but you're definitely right that prices have skyrocketed lately.

It's a supply/demand thing. If a vendor lists a batch of opiates and finds them snapped up in a day or two, it is human nature to list the next batch at a price that's a few dollars higher per pill. My sense is that there's been a very slight correction lately -- crossing the $1 per mg threshold seems to be resulting in a sales slowdown -- but the general trend over the past few months has been for the SR price fpor opiates  to increase, both in absolute terms and (especially) relative to prices IRL.

So first off, OP if you are not a regular user, then PLEASE reconsider your use of opiates and just appreciate that the prices of opiate are a barrier to your use. And well, that cheaper option is diacetylmorphine surprisingly.

OP, if you start with H, to be honest, the most important thing is to set your daily limit and stick to it. Given you will be receiving a bag of powder, instead of already pre-measured doses, you need to keep track of how much you are using or you can easily do more than you realize and quickly increase your tolerance.

That being said, I have received one order from wumg (now W007), tony, psilocin and have been very satisfied. You have no idea how great it feels not to blow through a few hundred dollars every week, instead I spend less than 100. Thats 1/3 of the cost I have been spending which is amazing! I have just been using small bumps to take the edge off the pain and keep the sickness at bay. But when I want, I can actually feel something from it too which oxy was losing its magic.

Like Habitat said, if you are already using OC daily, you are in wayyy over your head already and switching to heroin is not going to be a major change and your downfall UNLESS you start to IV or abuse the dosing. <SO ABSOLUTELY DO NOT EVER USE IV> Snorting, I have found the effects to be very similar to oxy, except H is slightly more relaxing and sedating and the half-life is a bit shorter, 2-3 hrs for morphine compared with 3-4 for oxy so I find myself wanting that next bump a bit sooner...  still find myself to be able to restrain myself and say no, tell myself "you need to wait a couple hours" which I couldnt do easily with oxy. I respect the H much more and still posses the fear I am going to do too much and overdose, but with oxy it was just do as much as I can afford...  I was worried I would have the intense desire to just use the H and get high everytime (which I do sometimes) but I am also able to use less just to feel normal.

With any opiate you are going to suffer withdrawals, period. Even tramadol. I have seen that there are many personal differences regarding which opiate is worse to detox from, but some find H to be worse than OC, others the other way around. At least with H the half-life is shorter so the withdrawal wont last quite as long. Not like you are switching to methadone and gonna increase your w/d by weeks of hell.

Dont take it orally, because you will be wasting 1/3 to 2/3 of the H given the horrible bioavailability. Also, I would not recommend you smoke the dope, I tried it for the first time yesterday after receiving psi's gear since its #3... and the rush and actual high are much more noticeable and I feel more prone to abuse, plus it wore off more quickly and about 4 hours later I felt much more shitty than I normally ever do and had to snort a bump to mellow back out. It took such a ridiculously small amount though, it was surprising (and I probably wasted much of the smoke not inhaling all given the learning curve)

If you are concerned about customs though, I would say you have a greater than 95 percent likelihood of success, so dont worry. W007 and psilocin have extremely clever shipping methods, to the point customs could open the package and still not find the goods. I would definitely go with Tony or wumg though, their H is incredible and both are really nice guys. Both arrived within a week and psi just over a week.

Good luck!
