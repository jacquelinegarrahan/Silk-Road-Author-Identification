Title: Re: Best Sugar Solution For Weed??
Post by: Elwood on June 12, 2013, 02:47 pm
There are liquid ferts that you mix with your nutes during the flowing stage to add sugars (Botanicare "Sweet", among many others) that are supposed to enhance the taste of the final product. Many people add a bit of molasses to their nutes as well. I think if you grow the right stuff, and properly flush at the end, you don't need those additions. Many will disagree...

Title: Re: Best Sugar Solution For Weed??

Be careful with saccharide solutions. This promotes rapid growth of unwanted molds and bacteria that can infect the entire plant, from the roots to the trichomes--just like the widespread E. coli outbreaks in spinach, where consumers were unable to effectively wash the affected products.
