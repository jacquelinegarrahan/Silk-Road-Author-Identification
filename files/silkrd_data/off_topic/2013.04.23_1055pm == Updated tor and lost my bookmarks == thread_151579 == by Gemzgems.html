Title: Re: Updated tor and lost my bookmarks
Post by: Yoda on April 24, 2013, 06:08 am
You're not supposed to extract new TBB in same location as the old.  Just rename the old TBB and move it or whatever when you have to update.

Since you have the old TBB:

- I'd say first DL the new TBB again, and extract in a different location... you can move it to your preferred default spot later
-Open/run this new TBB
-Go to:  Bookmarks > Show All Bookmarks > Restore > Chose File
-Find this location in old TBB (the one with the bookmarks): TBB\FirefoxPortable\Data\profile\bookmarkbackups
-Select most recent backup, hit enter or whatever
-Your new TBB should now have your bookmarks

Now you can rename the new TBB to something different from your other TBB's and move it to your default location where you like it.
