When your Internet service gets shut off, they don't do it like cable where they actually send someone out to unlock the box and connect the cables. It's possible that they're just screwing with you through their DNS server. It's also possible that they're just really dumb and are only blocking port 80 (which is for HyperText Transfer Protocol, eg HTTP, eg clearweb). Tor connects through a different port entirely, and I believe it can be changed in settings. Skype also connects through a different port. I don't know, though, since you said you couldn't play online games - are you talking about, say, Steam or something, or just browser games?
