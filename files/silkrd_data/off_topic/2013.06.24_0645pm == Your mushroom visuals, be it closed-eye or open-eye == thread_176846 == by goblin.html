Title: Re: Your mushroom visuals, be it closed-eye or open-eye
Post by: NewStem on June 24, 2013, 10:57 pm
I ate an eighth of shrooms and walked around my suburb with my two buds. Streetlights had rainbow halos, friends' faces were reflected koleidoscopically. For a large part of my trip I was quite anxious because when I sat and looked down at the pavement I would see it as an ant would see it, valleys and mountains and rivers- but when I looked up, I still felt like an ant, the clouds racing across the night sky. My favourite moment- at about 3 in the morning, when the rabbits here are at their peak of activity, one particularly friendly fellow hopped along our trail for quite some time. We stopped to get close, and surprisingly, it didn't run away. I looked into its eyes for quite a while and it felt very intense- everything around this rabbit was morphing and refracting, but the rabbit, specifically its eye, was preserved in perfect, realistic detail. I will never forget that rabbit.
