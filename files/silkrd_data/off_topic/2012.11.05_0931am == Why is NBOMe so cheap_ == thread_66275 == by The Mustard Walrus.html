Title: Re: Why is NBOMe so cheap?
Post by: thesearstower on November 05, 2012, 09:43 am
None of the NBOMe chemicals are federally scheduled. Legal drugs stay cheap.

They're very potent. Like 1000 doses/gram. So you don't need to make much in order to have a stockpile.

Also, they're very new and very exotic chems. There isn't a huge market for them (except when people are pretending that they're LSD). Lots of questions still about safety..

tl;dr: Supply & Demand.
