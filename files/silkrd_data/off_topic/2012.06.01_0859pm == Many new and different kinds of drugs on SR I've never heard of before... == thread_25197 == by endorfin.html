Title: For most of the "alphabet soup" drugs, the books 've never heard of before...
Post by: Jsu9466j on June 14, 2012, 09:08 am
For most of the "alphabet soup" psychedelic drugs, the books PiKHAL: Phenethylamines I Have known and Loved, and TiKHAL: Tryptamines I Have Known and Loved" are really good information resources. They were written by Alexander Shulgin, the chemist that first synthesized most of those drugs. They have details about synthesizing each drug, dosages, and effects/trip reports. The man tried a LOT of psychedelics. You can read it online free, I believe I was on erowid when I was reading about 4-FMA and the 2-X family last week. Cool stuff.
