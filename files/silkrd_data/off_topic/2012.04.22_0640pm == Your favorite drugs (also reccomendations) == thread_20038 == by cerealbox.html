I like lists. Here are my favorite drugs, in order.

Zoloft + Abilify - without this, I'd probably have killed myself by now.
MXE - my new favorite. I love this shit.
Magic Mushrooms - wish they were more visual for me, but mostly it just makes me go sort of insane and get stoned. Need more experience with it.
DXM - I had a very enlightening experience on DXM in which I resolved (successfully) to do better in school and forget my old friends. I didn't find it particularly fun though, just sort of weird and odd. Also makes music sound tinny and distant to me.
Pot - it's good to get a buzz, especially with some alcohol and just chill out. On the other hand, I'm not a huge fan of it like some other people are, I feel like I'm turning my mind off too much.
Booze - it's legal and it lets me enjoy time with my friends and get past my social anxiety. However, I drink too much and am probably fucking up my liver and if I could use other drugs out in public I'd quit booze entirely for probably my benefit.
2c-b - not a very interesting or enlightening high. I also don't like that it makes magic mushrooms not work for like a week after using it. However, the feeling of being on 2c-b, being shitfaced and stoned off your ass is a very interesting high.
Opiates - would be up there with booze and pot if it weren't, you know, addictive as fuck. I don't feel good with opiates, but I don't really get "high" per se. Not interesting, not enlightening, not a very good contribution to my life.
MDMA - I actually had a bad MDMA trip when I took it. I think my pill was too strong.
PCP - I reluctantly put this on the bottom of my list because when a PCP trip is good, it's really good and some of my most memorable and productive drug experiences have been on PCP. However, when it's bad it's really bad and I've heard too many stories of really unpredictable PCP behavior that has made me swear off it. It's actually the only drug I've sworn off of besides tobacco. Speaking of which...
Tobacco - I nearly killed myself with this shit. So much wasted money for nothing, so much difficultly just making it through movies because I needed a cigarette. So many years wasted not working out or being physically active and therefor probably being depressed because of lack of physical exercise, which probably led me to being a bit fat which probably made me not get laid. Fucking cigarettes.

Anyone have any suggestions on what drug I should try next? I've already ordered some Ketamine and 3-MeO-PCP. I'm also interested in LSD as I'd like a very visual experience, but have yet to have one with any of the drugs I've taken.
