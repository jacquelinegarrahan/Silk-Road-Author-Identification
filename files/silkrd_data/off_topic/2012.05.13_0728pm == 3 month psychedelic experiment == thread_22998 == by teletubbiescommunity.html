25i-NBOME - Visual swirling, rainbow bands of color on white backgrounds, lots and lots of noticed patterns and textures, but not too many hallucinations, at least for me, aside from really amazing sound->color synesthesia when music is played.  Also, it's highly confusing, and you'll feel lost and confused on it no matter what, so give 500ug a try to understand the confusion, and then redose when you're comfortable with being confused.  It removes your psychological defense mechanisms and doesn't teach you anything that you aren't prepared to search for on your own, so don't go in expected to be guided by the chemical.  It goes really well with MDMA, though. :D
