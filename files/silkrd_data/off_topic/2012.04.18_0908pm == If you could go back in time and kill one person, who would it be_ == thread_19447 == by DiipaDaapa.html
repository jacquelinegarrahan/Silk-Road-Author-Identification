Title: If you could go back in time and kill one person, who would it be?
Post by: DiipaDaapa on April 18, 2012, 09:08 pm
Weeks of continuous sobriety has again made me think how shitty the world is. So lying on my bed, trying to get some sleep, I started thinking what I would do with a time machine - and killing historical figures is what I thought of.

I wouldn't go for the most obvious choice and kill off Hitler. There's no guarantee that the end result would improve the present day world - I mean what if WWII was fought 30 years later with nuclear weapons instead? The religious characters are also very tempting to me, considering the immeasurable suffering their followers have caused in their name, but again the changes could easily have too dramatic consequences to the present day. What I ended up with was Harry Anslinger, because he's the reason why we're all outlaws here. Because of that one American no country in the world except the USA has the power to legalize recreational drugs without inducing the wrath of the UN.
