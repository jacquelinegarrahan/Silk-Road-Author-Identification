dosage wise I would recommend 150 to 200 mcgs, that will give you a full scale trip with open and closed eye visuals.

My last order where these:

Pro tips for your first time are: Two days of your free time, one for the experience, one to relax afterwards. Use it between 8 am and 12 am, that guarantees you will be able to sleep at night. You will need to be somewhere where you can @totally@ be yourself eg. where you can sing, dance, cry and laugh. Where you can be without any disturbance from the outer world. Bring a good friend, someone you trust and someone who can hug you if necessary. This is very important, because LSD can show you things that were buried in your subconsciousness. Physical closeness eases such experiences a lot and takes the edge of them.

Hmmm, basically that's it. My biggest mistakes were not having a sitter when I really needed one and ingesting 200mcg at 9pm when I was already really tired.

Good luck tripping, let us know your your trip went.
