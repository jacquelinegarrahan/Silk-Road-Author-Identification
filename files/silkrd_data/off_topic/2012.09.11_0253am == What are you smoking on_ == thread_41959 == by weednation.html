Snoopish -- You should try a sativa!  Your experiences in the past with weed that causes "couch lock" have probably been with an indica strain.  Those are the ones that keep you stuck to the couch, not wanting to move, not wanting to do much of anything -- they make you sleepy, really relaxed, etc.  Sativas, on the other hand, are more "uplifting" and "energetic" -- they can make you feel more creative too.
