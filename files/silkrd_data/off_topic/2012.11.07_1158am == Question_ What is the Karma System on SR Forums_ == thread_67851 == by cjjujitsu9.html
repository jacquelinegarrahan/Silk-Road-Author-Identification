Title: Re: Question: What is the Karma System on SR Forums?
Post by: psychedelicmind on November 07, 2012, 12:18 pm
The Karma feature is merely a feature of the forum that lets people give approval/disapproval of a comment by giving +/- karma.

So, if you agree with a comment, you give positive karma and if you disagree or have a differing opinion, you give negative karma.

My impression of it, is that most people don't really give a shit about it.

You will be able to give karma after you have made 100 posts.
