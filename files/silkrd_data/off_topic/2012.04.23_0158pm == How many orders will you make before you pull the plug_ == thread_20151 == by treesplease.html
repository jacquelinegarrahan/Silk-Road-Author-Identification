I get the logic. I don't agree with it. I plan on using this site for as long as it's safe. And then probably still afterwards.

But let's put it this way: you'll tolerate a 50% chance of getting caught, but not a 60% chance. If there is a 1% chance of getting caught every time you use the silk road, you might determine before hand that you're willing to make 50 purchases, but not 60.

To use a different example, the marginal risk of smoking a cigarette is very low. One extra cigarette has a very low chance of killing you. But if you used that excuse forever, "one more isn't going to make a difference" then eventually there is a very high probability of dying from smoking. One might decide that they've taken on enough risk smoking and quit - they may even know how much smoking it will take them to quit before hand.
