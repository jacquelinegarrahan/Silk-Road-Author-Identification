Title: Re: Stories about making drugs
Post by: lesseroftwoweevils on March 13, 2013, 04:13 am
Just because something is legal to acquire doesn't mean you're off the hook. Make sure to look at what's under the DEA precursor watchlist and NEVER buy suspicious chemicals together with the same name/address/credit card #. Many people have made this mistake before and received a knock on their door by law enforcement. 

The biggest culprit here is Ebay/Amazon, which will give up your past order information to the feds if asked to. A better option is a small chemical supply shop. Even better is dealing in cash off the net.

You should also become intimately familiar with the science behind your desired synthesis/extraction, which will help increase your % yields and potentially save you from getting a pair of handcuffs slapped to your wrists (be aware that there are many precursors you can make from less-suspicious chemicals).
