Post by: Ahoyhoy on August 28, 2012, 11:55 am
I bought a 12v electric grinder in Amsterdam. It has 3 settings and the third (highest) results in a powder which is good, but with very sticky weed can clog up around the roach and render the joint difficult to smoke. Also, the blades can get a bit sticky after a while but you can scrape the stuff off the blades and smoke it like a hash joint. Despite its limitations I love the little gadget but tend to use the second setting which seems perfect. A good purchase at 20 euros.
