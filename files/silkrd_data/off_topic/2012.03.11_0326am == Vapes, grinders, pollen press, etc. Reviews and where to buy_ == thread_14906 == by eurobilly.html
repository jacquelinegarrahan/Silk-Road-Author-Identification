grasscity is always good for glass, grinders, etc.

you can get a cheap pollen press on ebay for 5 bucks, like i did.  works fine.  but if you spend a little more you can get a little better torque for a firmer press.

if you've got a lot of money to spend, you can buy an ill bong specifically for oils...these guys have a bunch: http://www.aqualabtechnologies.com   This involves heating a titanium "nail" or "swing" with a blow torch and then dabbing your oil on the red hot metal.  for me, this is a little too much dangerous stuff to be dealing with while stoned or otherwise intoxicated, so i just use a cheap oil burner (meth pipe) for smoking oil. 

make sure you buy glass online from a superstar headshop because too many sites will poorly pack the product so that it shows up broken.
