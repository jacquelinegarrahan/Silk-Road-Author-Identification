Title: Re: What is your favorite psychedelic and why?
Post by: drugfather on December 26, 2011, 11:29 pm
5-methoxy-dimethyltryptamine (5-MeO-DMT)


Most all other psychedelics or dissociatives alter the current reality. For example: LSD changes perception on colors and sounds or might make you see something that isn't there... but that's all an alteration of reality not a change. For both 5-MeO-DMT and DMT, your complete reality is changed. You more or less enter a different realm all together all which is met with colors, visuals, sounds. You're left with no sense of the current reality you reside in. It's almost as if you become a different being in a different reality all together.

It's the most intense tryptamine I've tried to date.

Truly a life changing experience.

-DF
