Some of the 2C-x compounds might be up your alley...

2C-P is similar to LSD in duration (perhaps a bit longer actually), and can be had on the cheap. It's very psychedelic, but it won't be the same "feeling" as LSD, just as shrooms are significantly different feeling from LSD.

The DOx compounds are supposed to be somewhat similar, but they're all ridiculously long lasting. DOC is the shortest of them all, and it can last 18-24 hours (i believe).

There just isn't anything in the world that's going to give the same vibe as LSD. It's such a unique molecule that only its direct analogs are going to give the same experience.

And they're entirely unattainable. 

But DOx and 2C-x compounds are still very much psychedelics, and very cheap. Might be worth giving them a shot.

euphoricknowledge.com has some great vendors which will blow the SR prices out of the water. You can pick up 2C-x, DOx, and DMT anologue compounds for 10 times cheaper than SR. If you're comfortable going that route, of course :)

Edit - when I say cheap I mean buying the equivalent to 50 doses worth for 30-40 bucks... literally less than a dollar a trip.

Out of all the RC psychedelics the cheapest and closest to acid I've found are NBOMe's. Personally, I'd pay any extra amount for actual LSD though. DOC and DOI are cheap but they're pretty intense in effects and duration and I would not recommend them unless you know you want a VERY intense and (physically) stimulating experience. It is really a crying shame how many people have been sold DOx as LSD because about all they have in common is they will wake you up and make you hallucinate but they are totally different drugs.

If you like shrooms but want more bang for your buck, 4-AcO-DMT is quite inexpensive per dose and as close to psilocybin as you can get and unlike psilocybin it is legal most places. You can buy a quarter gram for 100 dollars or less at RC sites which is approximately 20 doses.  But you will need a milligram scale if you're buying in that amount.
