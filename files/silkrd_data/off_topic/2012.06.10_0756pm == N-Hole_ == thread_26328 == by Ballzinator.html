Of the better-known ones at least. By that I mean 2C-B, 2C-C, 2C-I, 2C-D, 2C-E and 2C-P.
There's also the 2C-G-X and the 2C-T-X series and a few obscure ones like 2C-F, 2C-N, 2C-O-4, 2C-SE... you get the picture.
2C-B is used as an Ecstasy replacement due to its empathic and entactogenic effects.
2C-C is slightly more psychedelic than 2C-B and rather short-acting.
2C-I is just trippy as fuck but also seems to be a good party drug because that's what it's being used for.
2C-D, 2C-E and 2C-P seem to have the same effect profile but get more potent, long-lasting and visual toward 2C-P. They all bring on a nice philosophical, creative, hyper-analytical mindset as well as sensory enhancement and nice visuals.

Long story short, yes, you're right: 2C-E is more spiritual and 2C-I is the most visual.
