Post by: awesome1126 on July 18, 2011, 02:07 pm
I use an Extreme-Q, but I'm soon moving to a Silver Surfer as I don't really use the bag function on the Extreme. At the 300 dollar price range, it's all about what kind of functionality you want. The Extreme-Q comes ready to use the whip, bags, and vapor-bong on an 18mm ground glass connection. The SSV comes with only enough to use a whip, but it's by far the best whip vaporizer (a little more efficient, especially with the hands free option, also very dense clouds of vapor, some people prefer this). If you want just a unit for bags, the herbalaire is the way to go here. If efficiency is your game, take a look at a Purple Days or a Myrtlezap. If it's portability, the Magic Flight Launch Box is the best portable smoking unit I use :)

Hope this helps.
