Title: Re: Favorite music to listen to while blazin/ trippin?
Post by: Dazarius on January 07, 2012, 02:09 pm
I think I like all you've already mentioned but wanted to say I love two great albums which I fully understood only through drugs. I know it depends, we all have somewhat of different taste for music but yeah, I didn't really get these albums before acid experience.

One is Animal Collective - Merriweather Post  Pavilion, it's very good when you're peaking on like...4 or 5 tabs of acid (I mean, those SR tabs which are claimed to be around 80-100mcg). Especially I like side B on vinyl (Daily Routine & Bluish) and definitely smoke some cannabis too while this. 

Second is MGMT - Congratulations, its amazing, like full trip guide. Literally, if you're not experienced and feel that you need a sitter for your acid trip but can't find a one then this is it. It's not for the intense part of the trip but best thing you can do, is listen side A and B before peaking and then C and D when coming down.

Didn't find any pictures of song descriptions on the backside of vinyl case but they are all like this one about the first song. -

"It's Working: Love? Or Confusion? Reality? Contusion? Subterranea or Sub-fusion. The Quest begins."

Definitely check this out, listen "It's Working" and "Congratulations" which are the first and last songs of the album. I'm pretty sure you'll understand the album is a full trip by itself.

Also, if you consume cannabis by eating and get more spiritual and peaceful high than by smoking then Congratulations is one of the best album through all the day.

But yeah, there are lots of music and for me it's the one of the most interesting aspects to think or discuss when combined with psychedelic experience.

And btw, Fleet Foxes - Helplessness Blues is pretty good a day after if you get that peaceful afterglow while sun is shining through you and by all that pure energy you just want to live and love.
