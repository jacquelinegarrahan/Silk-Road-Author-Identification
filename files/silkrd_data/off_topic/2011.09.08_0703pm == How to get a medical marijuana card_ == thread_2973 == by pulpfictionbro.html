Title: Re: How to get a medical marijuana card?
Post by: gharyeee on September 08, 2011, 10:45 pm
I believe it will depend what state you are in, but marijuana can be used for a variety of conditions. These include migraines, ADD, chronic pain, depression, anxiety, anorexia, and much more.

Marijuana dispensaries verify ID and call/check online to verify your doctors recommendation on your first visit, so a fake likely wouldn't work past this point. Look up local places that provide recommendations or evaluations. Some places take 5 minutes of paperwork, 45 minutes of sitting in a waiting room receiving coupons for nearby dispensaries, and about 15 seconds with the doctor signing your recommendation.

Check your state laws for details. It should state what medical conditions qualify for medical marijuana use.

Ah, and once you have your rec it's pretty much "Patient X is eligible to get high." You get to choose your own meds.
