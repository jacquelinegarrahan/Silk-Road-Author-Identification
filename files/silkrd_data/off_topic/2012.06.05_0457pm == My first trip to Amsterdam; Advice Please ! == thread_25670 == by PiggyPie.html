I've been about 20 times over the years, a few tips for you.

Stay away from the tourist coffee shops, the local ones hidden away up the side alleys etc. sell better weed and are cheaper.

Dont go behind central station - junkie madness, likely to get robbed.

And avoid bikes and trams, as when you're stoned out your face they are fucking dangerous. I've been hit by a few cyclists and nearly got trammed once.

But you'll have a blast that's for sure.
