Title: Re: Need Some Good VIBES!
Post by: Edawg420 on May 10, 2012, 12:42 am
So i finally decided to stop being a pussy and smoke the DMT.

I started out with my 2 ft bong and emptied out all the water.  After i put a little bit of pot in at the bottom of the slide and packed the rest up with Changa.  I sat down and meditated for a second and the lightly kissed the changa with the lighter.  I SLOWLY burned the changa trying to make as much smoke as i could as i chalked it up.

Once it was fully chalked up i blew the little bit i got in my lungs as i chalked it up out.

Then i committed and took a slow but steady rip from the already chalked bong.

Held that in for about 5 seconds then blew it out, only to immediately follow up with the last amount of smoke in the bong.

After i had taken that last hit i could feel a rush coming on.  so i put the bong down and sat back in the recliner. 

At first all i could hear was like this ring or vibration buzzing in my head.  A rush of extreme excitement came over me and felt like i was moving at extreme speeds.  At this moment i knew i was in for the ride and i should just enjoy what i saw.

At first i felt like i had lost complete control of my body.  I must of looked like i was in a coma.  Couldn't feel my heart beating, my chest expanding with air, or just me being there what so ever ( my buddy said my eyes were in the back of my head).  There was a big ball of white light above my head behind me if that makes any sense, and i was really afraid to be sucked into it so i struggled to move and opened my eyes.  Once i opened my eyes it looked like the world around me was electrified, patterns changing at INCREDIBLE speeds all over everything around me. 

I thought i was wasting the trip so i closed my eyes because it was more bearable now that i had a better grasp on my body.  I have to say the body buzz is intense.  Felt like i was kinda of drunk or a centripetal force was holding me to the seat or pushing me backwards.

Once i closed my eyes again i saw the entity.  I don't know how i knew, but it was a female.  Her face was a blinding white and blue light that i couldn't quite see, but i saw she had six arms almost identical to Shiva.  I was listening to some trance music in the background and she was swaying and dancing to the music.

Below her she had a tablet it looked like that she was floating over and it looked like a square maze that was constantly changing lines.  Some very earth tone colors were on the tablet but it also looked electric for some reason.  At this point i felt like i was kinda good on the whole trip so i opened my eyes again and saw the real world again.

Still the carpet, table, walls, and couch where changing patterns very rapidly but i had complete control over my body and speach again.  i Laughed out loud and couldn't stop saying OMG OMG that was so fucking insane.

Honestly, the most extreme psychedelic i have EVER done and i know i only just tapped into the first plateau. What would have happened if i was sucked into that white light?  IDK but i think that's what breaking through means.  Honestly more happened but it was so indescribable i am not going to try.  What i recorded here was the best explanation i could give.

I look forward in traveling again but i think i want to try ingestion, i hear the onset is much more relaxing and you can spend some time exploring and understanding what is being shown to you.

All in all DMT is amazing and has some sort of weird power to it.
