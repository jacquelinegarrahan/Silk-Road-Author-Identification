No offense but this just looks like a link-to-link list of stuff you find on Wikipedia when searching for nootropics, etc.

At the last office I worked at, some frat-guy types got hold of some Nuvigil (Armodafinil) and jokingly called it NZT.  It kinda annoyed everyone else :(

Some of the chemicals you list may have nootropic properties, but I posit that you can't just go combine every drug with such a property and expect them to just "increase" one aspect of their impact.  For example, one of your chemicals serves as a full Serotonin agonist.  Another serves to block reuptake of Serotonin.  Yet another (Sam-e), tends to function as a precursor to this neurotransmitter.  Just those three, taken together at a normal dose, would probably kill you due to Serotonin syndrome.  And that's just the first item on the list. 

Also, many of the things you list are just consumer supplements that can be bought on amazon, at the drug store, etc.

Finally, don't forget about homeostasis; you don't get something for nothing when it comes to medicine, unfortunately :(
