Post by: profspudhed on March 07, 2012, 08:30 am
its been done many times before, we call it a botnet, there are even a few miners who have infected their entire offices with trojans so they have many systems cpu mining when the offices are closed. the most common miner used for trojan botnet mining is ufasoft since its very small, needs minimal configuration and can be hidden fairly easily, at one time someone on here was selling a trojan for just that. ive also heard about others who have set it up to use only a certain percentage of cpu power so that the average user wont notice that their machine is mining away for someone else while theyre using it.

on the subject of mining software ive used several in my time, im using a 5850 and a 5970 and it seemed that phoenix 1 with phatk was the fastest, cgminer had the best features but was a little slower and somewhere in between those on speed is diablominer which im currently using as so far its proven to be the most stable option (cgminer is pretty much as stable but slightly slower) this is all with 11.7 drivers on, i did mess with the drivers not too long ago trying to squeeze a bit extra out of it, i failed and went back to 11.7 but ive ended up messing it up a bit and now pheonix wont mine well at all for some reason, it used to get about 415MH per gpu but for some reason its dropped to 250-300, i really should format it and reinstall everything but that means not mining for a few hours while i get it all reinstalled, i hate downtime and as diablo has been so stable i havent bothered for what will be a less than 100MH/s increase from using pheonix, but its also a while since ive updated the miners so they may have improved a little since i updated last.

@freefyte1

i think the 5850 just trumps it as they tend to clock slightly better but in all honesty if you can get a voltage unlocked card then pretty much any of the 5xxx series are the golden mining cards, good luck finding one for a reasonable price now though, i got mine just before the BTC hit the big time, i paid £99 for it, a few weeks later when id convinced a friend to mine they had gone up to £140 and finally stopped at £180!

and dont go to deepbit, they charge a fortune in fees and certainly arent the best pool out there, i was using arsbitcoin but it kind of fell apart when they fell into negative smpps buffer but there are plenty of other good pools who wont rape you on fees like bitcoins.lc, btcguild, abcpool and a few others, they may not all be totally fee free but theyll be less than deepbit any day of the week. also its a problem if one pool controls over 50% of the total network mining power and deepbit has come scarily close to that a few times.

out of interest how have you got your pheonix and card setup? if you havent already id suggest dropping the memory to 300MHz and adding the flags "-k phatk VECTORS BFI_INT WORKSIZE=256 AGGRESSION=13 FASTLOOPS=false" that seemed to give me best speeds and if im honest it was slightly faster running the 10.10 drivers with sdk 2.1 but that may have changed with recent alterations to the phatk kernel

Title: Re: BTC Mining
Post by: profspudhed on March 08, 2012, 03:34 pm
im not as familiar with 6xxx series cards but most all radeon cards benefit from using -k phatk rather than poclbm, if you have your card overclocked and memory clocked down to 300MH then up your aggression to 12 or 13 and turn fastloops off, oh and make sure youre running the older drivers package (i use 11.7) and sdk 2.5 if youre using newer drivers with the 2.6 sdk then youll need to clock your memory up rather than down and then drop the worksize to 64. thats about all i can think of as far as optimizing your pheonix line, for any more speed than that youre going to have to push your clocks up. 

on that subject if you havent yet got your 5xxx series card be careful when buying, some cant have their voltage increased so it limits your ability to overclock the card (i know many of the xfx cards suffered this problem) some of them can have the bios flashed to allow overvolt but its best to get a card that does it without a mod if you can
