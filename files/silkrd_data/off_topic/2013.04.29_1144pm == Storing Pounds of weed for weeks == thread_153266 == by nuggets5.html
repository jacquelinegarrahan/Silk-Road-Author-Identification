Title: Re: Storing Pounds of weed for weeks
Post by: ruby123 on April 30, 2013, 02:16 am
Cannabis flowers are best kept long term stored in a cool/dry environment. Vacuum sealing helps to preserve the moisture content, however it is not necessary. The buds will gradually become darker, but potency will remain intact. I have kept weed for over 2 years in jars and plastic bags. The bud must be cured properly for long term storage ,otherwise you will end up with moldy schwag. I am vaping on a strain called Dutch Treat currently which is around 1.8 years old.
