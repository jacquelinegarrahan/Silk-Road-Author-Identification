Post by: sollemnis on September 15, 2012, 07:24 pm
Personally I'd advise you try Mephedrone. It's my favourite drug... Wonderful Euphoria, music appreciation, rush, talkativeness and HUGE self confidence boost.

It was originally an MDMA replacement... IMO it feels a lot different to MDMA, but with some similarities.

The similarities are:
Music Appreciation
Euphoria
Empathy - Now with Mephedrone the 'empathy' aspect is slightly different to MDMA's... MDMA is a feeling of love and compassion or others, you love everyone, you feel peace with the world bla bla. But with Drone its more a feeling that everyone is fucking AMAZING.

Mephedrone has similarities to Coke and Amphetamine as well, these being:
Feeling like SUPERMAN
Being very talkative (Personally it makes me more talkative than Amphetamine does)
Stimulation.
Improved self confidence.

MDMA in its pure form isn't very stimulating... In fact, it makes me feel quite relaxed... which is why it is known as the 'relaxing Amphetamine'. But Mephedrone is VERY stimulating... The rush is quite intense too, almost overwhelming euphoria.

Now for the dose...

The two ROA's I've used are oral and nasal.

Snorting 100-150mgs of Drone (As a first time user) should put you in a good place. It hurts a bit, but to be honest, you soon get used to it. I'd then do 50mg top ups every 20-30 mins to keep the buzz going.

Parachuting it works well too (I find that this ROA is much more MDMA like). I'd do 250-300mgs... This should give quite an intense but not too intense an experience.

What I like to do is parachute 200mgs wait 15 mins then do a 100mg line. This gives the extended duration of taking it orally, and the more intense experience of taking it nasally.

Unlike MDMA, re-dosing brings back the peak effects! Though if you want to play it safe, don't go over a gram :).

Oh and this stuff is even more amazing when combined with M1.
