Ah, Topamax.
It can make one a) stupid, b) forgetful, c) not-hungry, d) forgetful, e) migraine-free (for some people), f)  seizure-free, in really epic doses that no human being can tolerate, apparently, g) predisposed towards metabolic acidosis, h) prone to kidney stones, i) prone to paraphasias, j) did I mention stupid?

Most everyone loses weight on it. Some to the point of concern.
Affectionately called "Dopamax" by those who know it. (The joke is that people lose weight on it because they're too fucking stupid to find food. I wish that were an exaggeration, but, having become lost on the way to the grocery store while taking it...)

Amazingly, it's been approved in combination with phentermine for weight loss in the US. Unbelievable.

It does have some intriguing off-label utility for compulsive disorders - binge-type behaviors, in particular - cutting, gambling, binge-type drinking, binge-eating, and for OCD. Maybe a role for sensory integration in autistic spectrum disorders; not sure, I am underwhelmed by what I've read and observed.
