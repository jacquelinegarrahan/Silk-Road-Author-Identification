Title: Re: Small hint for druggies in Australia to hide your drugs
Post by: OzFreelancer on November 10, 2012, 03:36 am
In Victoria, a warrant to search premises for drugs or for evidence of the commission of drug offences can be obtained from a Magistrate by a member of the police force. The Magistrate must be satisfied there are reasonable grounds for the belief that there are drugs on the premises or that drug offences are being committed.

The warrant may authorise entry by force if necessary onto the specified premises and search of those premises and of any persons or vehicles found there.

So, in Vic at least, I would not follow the OP's reasoning.  You will probably just really really piss off your flatmate.
