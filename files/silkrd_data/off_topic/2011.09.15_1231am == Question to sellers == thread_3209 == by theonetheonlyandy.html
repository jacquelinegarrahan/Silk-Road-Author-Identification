Post by: btcfreedom on September 21, 2011, 02:11 pm
I do not profit from my work here. In fact I am here solely to redistribute my BTC and buy other goods with them.

This has worked out pretty well, but as a seller I can say it's not like the street - where you get rapid infusions of cash after unloading your gear.

There is a due process for transactions; competition, and of course finalization. Sellers work very hard (or they should) for maximum bang for the BTC.

I initially put in an investment of about $1,000 to get the ball rolling, and I don't even want to repeat what I've made back so far... it's sad.

Take the ubie for example...with postage and such I'm breaking even, with about 2 extra dollars on the side...I only bought a 50 pack of them.

With BTC fluctuation, regardless of hedging, this 2 dollars is unstable. Multiply that 2 dollars times a few transactions, and you really don't have much

of an offset. But the feedback and happy customers are worth it. With other items I've listed, I've actually lost money in efforts to compete.

So as you see there are many obstacles :)

But that's not the point. :) I love everyone here and being here for people.

So I'm here to stay, unless commission deep dicks all of us.
