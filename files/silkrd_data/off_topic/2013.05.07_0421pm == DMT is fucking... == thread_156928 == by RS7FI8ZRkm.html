This is misinformation. DMT can be sored well in an inert setting.
Even if you do leave it out, it only degrades into DMT N-oxide, which is still psychoactive, and can be made back into DMT.

Your best bet for storage would be to take your DMT freebase and react it with fumaric acid to make DMT fumurate. Vaccum seal this, and put it in a mason jar that can be sealed. Mix some baking soda and vinegar in another jar, wait for the reaction to finish, and "pour" off the co2 into the mason jar (To remove oxygen and create an inert atmosphere)
Then all you have to do is seal your mason jar and pop it in the freezer. I've kep quantities of DMT like this for years, and they still work.

You can convert your DMT fumurate back to a freebase and vaporize it, or you can smoke it as a salt. The benefits of a salt is that if it's pure enough, it can be injected, or shorted, or plugged, if you're into that.

It can also be mixed with Dimethyl sulfoxide (DMSO) and then applied to the skin as a topical cream. It will be like injecting it, with dosage being similar.
