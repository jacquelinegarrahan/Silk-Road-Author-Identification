Title: Re: Apparently I do not understnd the bitcoin thing
Post by: TalkingHead on February 01, 2012, 08:38 pm
First of all you can search on "bitcoin" and get lots of hits or look in the security forum - page 3 maybe? - and there's a thread there. If you're in the US start an account with mtgox or tradehill, then go to bitinstant.com where there are instructions on how to fund those bitcoin exchanges and others with cash or otherwise. You can set up an account at Dwolla and they will take money from your bank account, though it takes a few days to do so, then transfer it to where you want. For cash, you set up an order at bitinstant and then it opens a window for CashTrust, you pick a local bank and make a cash deposit, no ID needed. Once you have your bc, transfer them to SR, which can take a little time for it to show up. Do your own research though first, think it all through carefully, don't forget to account for the fees in most of these transactions and good luck.
