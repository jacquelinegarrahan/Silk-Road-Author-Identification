Title: NBOME series and HPPD - A Cautionary Tale
Post by: element12 on January 12, 2013, 05:08 am
Not sure if this belongs in the official NBOME discussion thread but I figured it made enough sense to make a new thread.

I had been doing pyschs for 2 years before I tried NBOME.  I loved it - it was cheap, I had wonderful afterglows, and I made really interesting art while tripping.
So, I decided to get 50 tabs of 25C, and some 25I considering how cheap it was.

This summer I took 2 tabs (enough for a +++ trip) every two weeks (enough for tolerance to wear off).  Now I've noticed that it had lasting effects.  Whenever I get really sleepy, or drunk or high, a small spinning kaleidoscope of colors appears far off in the center of my vision.  It's fairly easy to ignore so it hasn't affected work or anything but it is definitely noticeable and definitely real.  Sometimes its fun but sometimes I feel kind of fucked up that I've permanently rewired my brain with this drug.  I decided to lay off NBOME until it goes away, but it's been several months and I'm not sure it ever will.

I've taken shrooms and acid plenty and smoked plenty of weed too and nothing triggered HPPD except NBOME.

I'm not saying it's a horrible drug and you shouldn't take it but I guess reminding everyone that it is not a totally harmless psychedelic and it should be taken, like all other drugs, in moderation.
