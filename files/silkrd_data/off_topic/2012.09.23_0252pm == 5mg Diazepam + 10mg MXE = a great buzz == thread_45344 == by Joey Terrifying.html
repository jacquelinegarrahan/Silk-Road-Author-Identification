Title: 5mg Diazepam + 10mg MXE = a great buzz
Post by: Joey Terrifying on September 23, 2012, 02:52 pm
i enjoyed it greatly.  i felt the familiar walking-on-clouds feeling i get from valium, mixed with the intellectual stimulation i get from MXE, but not enough from either to render me incapacitated.  I was quite functional, just euphoric....I dove right into a creative effort that I had been working on recently and finished the whole thing in 2 hrs which felt like the blink of an eye.

a few days later i tried .5mg Clonazapam +10mg MXE and it just wasn't the same.  i felt, i dunno....heavier?  i guess valium is just the benzo that suits me.

i usually don't like to dose heavy with either of these drugs, but i also feel that low doses are not enough.  i thought that didn't make sense until i did this valium/mxe low dose combo.  they totally completed each other.

just thought i'd share with y'all
