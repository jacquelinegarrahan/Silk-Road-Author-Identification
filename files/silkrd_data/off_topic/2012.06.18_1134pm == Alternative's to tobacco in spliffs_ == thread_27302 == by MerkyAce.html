Title: Re: Alternative's to tobacco in spliffs?
Post by: Ben on June 23, 2012, 12:51 am
Frankly i don't believe the 'no tar' part of that. Any smoldering vegetable matter will produce tar, tobacco does, lawn grass does, anything else you can cut from a plant that burns does.

Herbal cigarettes may be free of tobacco and nicotine, but the mainly dangerous part of the smoke, tar, will always remain to some degree, no matter what other plant material you substitute for the tobacco. Obviously the pot itself will also produce tar, which is fairly evident when you roll a joint with pure cannabis and look at the tip after smoking it.
