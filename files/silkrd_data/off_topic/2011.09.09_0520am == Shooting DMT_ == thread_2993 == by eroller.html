Agreed - injection of freebase is a very bad idea - you want to use the fumarate or HCl salt. The dosage will be less than smoking freebase, calculated per body weight (if memory serves it's something like 0.3mg/kg - 0.4mg/kg). Dr. Strassman did quite a bit of work on this (hehe). You're going to want someone with you. Lay down. The effects will come on instant and strong and since this is your first time you might not get the needle out of your arm. Here's a decent article on IV preparation: http://forums.mycotopia.net/dmt-spice/19925-dmt-hcl-i-v-expirements-uh-whoa.html. Be safe man, and know what your doing.  It's one hell of a ride!
