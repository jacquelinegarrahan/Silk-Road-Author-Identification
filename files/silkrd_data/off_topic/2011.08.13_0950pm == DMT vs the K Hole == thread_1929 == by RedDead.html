I recommend DMT over K or MXE (or anything else for that matter), with the caveat that no matter how hard you try you will not be able to communicate the quality or intensity of the experience to others (unless perhaps they've been there themselves).

Title: Re: DMT vs the K Hole
Post by: Tryptamine on August 15, 2011, 07:01 pm
Quote from: RedDead on August 15, 2011, 06:34 am
Thanks guys for the replies. I'm afraid I'll freak out the first time if I'm alone. How do I prevent a bad trip if I'm alone?

In my experience there are two ways to have a bad trip: 1. You don't get enough to 'break through' and you're just intoxicated for 15 minutes (which isn't bad but it's disappointing which feeds back and can lead to a 'bad trip'). 2. You attempt it twice within a short period of time; the second time will be disorienting, with a bunch of random sounds going off in the left side of your head, and you'll be really disappointed.

So vaporize it (ideally in a vaporgenie), take it ALL on the first hit (or two), and hold the hit until no vapor comes out. How fast you do it, how much gets destroyed in the process, and how well you hold it is more important than how much you pack into the bowl.
