Post by: John Keats on March 07, 2012, 05:30 pm
If you'd like to learn some of the basics and history of art forgery, I would recommend reading "The Forger's Spell: A True Story of Vermeer, Nazis, and the Greatest Art Hoax of the Twentieth Century" by Edward Dolnick. It's a very entertaining book, very informative as well.

If you're looking to enter the art forgery buying/selling business you'd have to be either very rich or very connected (in most cases both). That's where I don't have much to help you with.
