Post by: g01d3n on January 22, 2013, 06:35 am
Send it all to me; I'll keep it fresh!

Just kidding, I guess it really depends on how long you plan on storing it. Keeping them in mason jars should be fine for a few months; just to sure to "burp" them by opening them every so often to let excess moisture out. For a longer term storage, consider putting them in the coolest part of the fridge, and leave them alone until you have a need for it (constantly pulling it in and out of the fridge may cause excess moisture to build up).

Title: Re: Storing Brick Weed
Post by: BusinessMentality on January 29, 2013, 03:47 am
I was in your situation many months ago and from what I have learned from many friends who have been growing 10+ years is Rule #1 keep it in something air tight if you plan on having it long-term, if you are really going to be holding on to your weed for awhile, Vacuum seal and place into a Mason jar, to be specific, don't get the twist top jars, get ones with a snap lip of some sort that have a rubber seal under the lid, those are much better than twist top and make sure it's a good quality jar and the rubber isn't loose/crappily installed.

Also, keep your weed in a dark and humid place, I personally I don't have any humid places in my house so I keep my big orders of things vac sealed, inside a snap lip jar inside a black thick garbage bag to deflect light.

Also, NEVER freeze your plant, don't put it in a freezer/fridge ever for it can affect the potency harshly, some people might disagree with me so I will let you do the research on that for yourself.

Aside from that you are all set! happy storing!
