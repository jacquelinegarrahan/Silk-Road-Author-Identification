Post by: greenturtle on January 10, 2013, 02:09 am
What really helps me fall asleep is reading while laying in bed with only my lamp that sits on my nightstand on. Never take your laptop into the bed with you, whenever I do this I end up staying up all night reading on the computer, except the bright screen makes it so I stay awake.

Another thing you can try is spending an hour a day working out, whether you go to the gym or just go for a jog around the block it should help. I work out Monday through Friday every week. If you are not on a schedule then get on one. I find when I am on vacation it is hard for me to sleep because I stay up late and sleep in. As long as I am on schedule, in bed by 10PM and awake at 430AM, it makes it easier for me to fall asleep when it is time.

Hope this helps,
