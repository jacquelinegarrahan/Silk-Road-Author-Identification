Post by: alexduz on August 13, 2012, 12:51 am
I've had lots of fun watching television while tripping, but it's seldom had much to do with the content.  It becomes next to impossible to follow a complicated plot, but subtle things (nuance, innuendo, etc.) come right to the surface (and can be hilarious).  Unfortunately, bad acting becomes more obvious too, and many things on television seem hideously overplayed.  But it's all amusing, regardless (though I tend to feel the need to do something else after ten or twenty minutes; I don't think I've ever watched anything all the way through).  I'd say: watch anything (except perhaps the news; that can be distressing).

I've only watched a couple of movies in a movie theater while tripping.  One of them was wasted on me because it was very plot-centric and that part of it went right over my head (I don't even remember what movie it was).  But the other was fantastic: City of Lost Children.  That was many years ago; I recently rented the DVD, and was surprised to find that it was in French (with subtitles); I'd completely forgotten that!  But it's incredible, visually, and is more like a dream than a story, so you don't need to know what's happening to know what's happening.  My one suggestion would be: try to time it so that you're well into your high when the movie starts, because the opening minutes ... well, I'm not going to say more, so that I won't spoil it.

But the reason I'm posting a comment here is not TV or movies, but music videos by Stephan Malinowski.  His YouTube channel is
Code: [Select]
<a href="http://www.youtube.com/user/smalin">smalin</a>
He invented this kind of music video to watch on acid, and for me, they're right on target.  Everybody I've shown them to so far has liked them (high or not), and the one guy I know who watched some on DMT said they were awesome.  And they're short (just a few minutes, most of them); I've sometimes watched the same one a few times in a row, and I've never gotten bored in the middle.

Malinkowski also has a channel with 3D videos (you need special glasses):
Code: [Select]
<a href="http://www.youtube.com/user/musanim3D">musanim3D</a> (for me, the 3D effect is fun, but when I'm high, the videos look pretty 3D even without the glasses).

2. Apocalypse Now Redux... sure, you can watch the original, but if you like film, watch redux... better.  This movie will pull you in and eat your soul.

3. Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind... lighter fare than the first two, but still a very trippy story and very emotional.

4. Donnie Darko...  Tripping on LSD opens a wormhole in your soul.
