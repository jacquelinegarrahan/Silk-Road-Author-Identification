Title: Re: Getting a medicinal permit in California. How do?
Post by: NYslice on June 15, 2012, 06:45 am
To get your 215, just go to a cannabis Dr. for your script.

They're easy to find in CA. Make an appointment if you want but they'll probably take walk-ins.
Mine did. I went in on the way back from the DMV as soon as I got my license. Not even photo ID yet... just the paper card with my sig.

You'll get a physical evaluation. MAKE SURE TO SAY YOU HAVE NO HISTORY OF HEART CONDITIONS when they ask.

It's just for chronic back pain, spasms, anxiety, sleep, appetite... whatever ails you. I suffer from ailments due to a serious accident, so I'm legit.

Make sure to get a handy card for your wallet. It saves me a lot of trouble because I can't keep my paperwork with me.
The card ( it's paper, not laminated ) covers you for possession and especially when you want to become a member of a new club.

Edit; Cost me about $100
