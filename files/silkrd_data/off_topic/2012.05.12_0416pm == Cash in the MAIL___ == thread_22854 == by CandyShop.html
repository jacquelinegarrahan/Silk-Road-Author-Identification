Title: Re: Cash in the MAIL???
Post by: j0blo on May 12, 2012, 04:28 pm
If you can't send a money order, be smart about sending the cash.

Put in inside an envelope with some business-looking documents and the cash discretely tucked in the fold.

Write a return address on the back of the envelope where it is sealed, and put Scotch tape over the address/seal - it will be obvious if it's been tampered with.

And remember - there's no guarantees of any kind when sending cash in the mail! Do so at your own risk!
