Lincoln

Silver Linings Playbook
This movie gets a 4/5 from me. I have not saw a movie yet that I did not like with Brad Cooper. Again he plays a role with humor and drama that most everybody can relate to. In this one he plays a character who is going thru a hard time when his wife leaves him. Her husband had died and she had turned to a life of drunkenness and sluttiness. He had just gotten out of a luny hospital. His wife has a restraining order against him but he is still obsessed with her but the new girl does a great job of filling in the gap.

This one is a real tear jerker for the chicks, A great date movie and good entertainment for the family. Highly recommended.

Next up is Quentin Tarantino's Django Unchained. This one has a huge cast of top actors including Jamey Fox, Leonardo DiCaprio, Don Johnson, and Samuel Jackson. There are lots of others. This one is set back 2 years before the Civil war. Fox plays a slave who is freed by a bounty hunter. Together they ride thru out the old west collecting bounties for wanted crooks.

This one has the familiar touch of Tarantino's satire like movies with all the special kinds of music he loves to include. It last dam near 3 hours and has lots of action and gore as such is his MO. I really liked Samuel Jackson's part of playing the House slave to Di Caprio being the master of a plantation in Mississippi.

It didn't say but the way Don Johnson's part was played it put me in mind of him being Nathan Bedford Forrest. Django and Dr.King Shultz, the bounty hunter who freed him, rode together from Texas to Gatlinburg Tennessee looking for wanted outlaws and finally Djangos wife. They were separated at an auction. Lots of explosions, gunfire, and close up bloody splattering gunshots to the head and body in this one. I really like a lot of Tarantino films like Pulp Fiction, Death Proof, the Kill Bills, and others but I would like to see him change up his style a bit. 3/5 for this spaghetti western

FLIGHT

Watching him take control and fly inverted, with all the passengers getting sick and in awe with terror in their hearts was absolutely the best part of this film.

After that it kind of went down hill from there with the captain waking up in the hospital with minor injuries. Now the film goes into a rut being all about addiction.

I have to give it a 2/5. It's starts like a BOSS but ends with a whimper.
