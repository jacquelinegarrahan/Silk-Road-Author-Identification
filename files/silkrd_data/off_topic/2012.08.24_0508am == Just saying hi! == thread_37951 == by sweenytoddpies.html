Title: Re: Just saying hi!
Post by: Spunkaroo on August 24, 2012, 09:34 am
Hey man, welcome aboard.

Seeing as you mentioned Lucy I just wanted to say this before you get burnt. Look at all the feedback for the vendor you're interested in, check the Rumor Mill sub section of the forum for the vendor's thread, as well as doing a search in the other areas, and start small (don't go in for a sheet to begin with.) Lucy seems to be so sought after here that scammers tend to use it to their advantage. People go absolutely ape shit when a new vendor with good prices comes up, but the number of times this has turned bad is amazing. Check out Kat and GummyStars for examples of what I mean here (a lot of reading, but hey, at least you know what to look for.)

As for general advice, all I can say is this: Lurk on these forums, lurk on these forums and lurk on these forums. When you think you've done enough lurking, come back and lurk some more :P The information you can pick up here even without posting is amazing.

Finally, if it seems too good to be true, it probably is. Words to live by here on the road.
