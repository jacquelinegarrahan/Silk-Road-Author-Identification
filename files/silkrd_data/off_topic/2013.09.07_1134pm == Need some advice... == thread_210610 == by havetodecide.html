Post by: Remediless on September 08, 2013, 07:59 am
The general advice if you're thinking about fleeing is... don't. You have to break all contact with absolutely everyone in your life and you can't see them again, ever. As in EVER, not even funerals or anything. You have to spend the rest of your life looking over your shoulder, never fully trusting anyone and being ready to flee at any given moment. Which means you'll never have "real" friends again... because even going for a few beers and some lines of coke and a few spliffs at a new buddy's is risky because you could accidentally 'fess up you're on the run then they talk to someone else and... next thing you know you're in prison and doing way more time than you would have done in the first place.

It sounds like a horrible existence being on the run, more so than doing the time in some cases. Do the time if at all possible.
