Here in the UK an ounce (28grams) of green is usually between £180 ($270) and £240 ($360). Very little of this is medicinal grade or A+ quality, it's what many would refer to as mids. Anything half decent will be at the top end. The lower end of those prices is usually for 'home-grown' which just means poor weed generally. Most are heavy on the stalks/sticks and the weed is quite thin and patchy, certainly no dense buds. Often this is so sticky it's impossible to grind or even get out of the bag. Basically it's crap grown by amateurs that just want some quick cash.
You can see why so many UK users of SR discuss foreign vendors because they hugely undercut prices while delivering much higher quality.

Although I don't use coke myself I know people who do. It averages about £50 ($75) a gram but the street stuff over here is rarely even 20% cocaine. Some reports put it as low as 5%.
