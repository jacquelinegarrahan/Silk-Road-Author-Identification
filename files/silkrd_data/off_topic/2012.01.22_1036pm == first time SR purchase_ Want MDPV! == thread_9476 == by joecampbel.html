MDPV is a new drug but, after having some experience with it when it was legal up to September 2011 in the US and UK, I can offer some advice for those willing to 'go there'.

The stuff is potent as hell.

I mean it blows all other stims out of the water.

The first time I tried it, I bought it in a head shop (while it was still legal, under the brand name "White Rush", when it was being sold legally as a "bath salt"). The pack was 250mg, a quarter of a gram, and being more used to coke and speed, and knowing my stims in general, I took a regular line. (A long rail). I wish the guy behind the counter who sold me the stuff had warned me but the sleaze bag didn't give a fuck.

Anyway, it was an instant mind fuck.

But in a good way. The aphrodisiac effects of this drug are fucking insane. That's typical with stims but MDPV is out of control. Problem for me, after I'd banged my girlfriend 3 times and taken the whole 250mg over the course of 6 hours (taking it like an idiot at a rate I was usually accustomed to with coke or speed), I then entered into a 3 day waking nightmare of mind fuckery.

Seriously, if you've never taken MDPV before, tiny doses are your friend.

I spent 3 days awake, no sleep, paranoia, hallucinations, all kinds of terror. In fact, it bordered on psychedelics. After 3 days of no sleep, I found my mind dreaming while awake, such is the body's need for sleep, and it continued to the point where the waking world got transformed into a dream. Sure, this might sound like a nightmare but it was actually one of the most transformative drug escapades of my life. There was no physical fear for my body, (heart rate normal, blood pressure normal etc) the whole experience was entirely psychic. And despite the terror, I learned a lot of shit about the nature of the reality we perceive from the whole crazy binge.

Needless to say, after that first exposure, I approached MDPV from then on with a healthy respect. 150mg for a first time user is enough to keep you stimmed for 24 hours. (It's too much).  75mg is easily enough for a good night out. The effects last and are persistent.

Do not re dose after that amount no matter how "moreish" it might seem.

Over time, I got used to the stuff and can say it's better than coke, meth, speed and all other stims. You just have to treat it with respect. One of the funny things about MDPV is the comedown. There is no crash per se. When you've had enough, you know. And the lingering effects of the drug in the comedown phase lead to a new stage of the experience... total mental clarity. You see shit clearly. It's almost like acid. The comedown itself is "energized wakefulness" as to the nature of your particular reality.

I never found the need to take it again for many weeks after. Once a month is a healthy treat. The experience, for me, was so total, that it's not something worth doing all the time.

MDPV is potent shit but definitely a drug experience worth having at least once in your life if you like stimulants and are partial to a dopamine flood in your synapses.

Approach with caution. It's not for everyone and I agree with the above poster when he said "Just like LSD.  2 people can cut a line or take a hit from the same source of MDPV as another person, and one can go to a party and then to work, while the other may go to the Psychiatric unit in the ER."

Stupid people overdoing it and winding up in the ER (as usual 5% of user idiots of any drug) got this drug a 1 year ban by the DEA pending "investigation" which means we'll never see it available legally again. I wish the assholes that ban shit knew the real statistics of the typical ER. 60% of admissions on a Friday and Saturday night are alcohol related and yet no media outlet creates a shit storm looking for that drug to be banned.

Be careful out there.

That's my mind dump.
