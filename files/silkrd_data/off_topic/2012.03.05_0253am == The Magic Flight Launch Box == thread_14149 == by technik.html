Title: The Magic Flight Launch Box
Post by: technik on March 05, 2012, 02:53 am
Hey guys,

I'm sure some have heard of it. For those that haven't, the magic flight launch box is the smallest portable handheld vaporizer on the market. It a little wooden box with a plexiglass? sliding glass panel that has a heating element inside. It works by sticking in a label peeled rechargeable battery into it which purposely creates a short that heats up the element (and your herbs) to 350? degrees vaporizing the plant material. The price is ~100.

Now I haven't used one yet, but i've read many rave reviews on it on in the internet. I just ordered one off of amazon from some guy packing the mflb with a badass airtight waterproof lockable vapecase. For around $120. I've wanted this thing for a year now and finally had the funds to buy it.

I'm not a seller or promoted advertiser, I'm just a user letting people know about this thing!

From what people say, it creates little smell and is easily palmed and used anywhere even inside around other people.

I'll come back and update once I get mine.

Post by: jackstraw on March 05, 2012, 03:05 am
MFLB is a really amazing device.  At first it seems overpriced for what it is BUT the thing has a LIFETIME warranty which really speaks well towards the company.   That screen is so thin that you really need to be careful cleaning it and I recommend only using the brush they provide.  That said if you do happen to rip the screen they will send you a new one after you send them a picture of the MFLB and the serial # on it.  You will get your new unit before you even have to send the broken one in.   Check out fuckcombustion.com  for ANY vaporizer questions.  That site is the bible for vape enthusiasts.
Only thing is there is a learning curve with the Launch Box....you basically are controlling the temp by the way you are inhaling.  So it's not the best if you are trying to get a few people high.  It's great for super stealth and quick hits.   Was using mine in a movie theater last week with no problem.   My favorite vape right now is my Arizer SOLO.  It's portable and battery powered and works like a DREAM.....but I still love my MFLB for sure.
