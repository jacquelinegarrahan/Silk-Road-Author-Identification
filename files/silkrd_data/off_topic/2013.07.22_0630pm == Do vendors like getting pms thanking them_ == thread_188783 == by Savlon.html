Title: Re: Do vendors like getting pms thanking them?
Post by: GrimWaldo on July 26, 2013, 01:19 am
As a buyer, I usually just opt to leave feedback in the vendor's forum review thread. I consider it more helpful to all parties involved. Potential buyers receive the benefit of my purchasing experience, and the excellent vendor receives an appropriately glowing review that is accessible to the public and helps attract customers. Win, Win!

However, I was recently so happy with a purchase (and therefore, the vendor) that I sent my first-ever PM to them lettin' 'em know just how happy I was with the mechandise, and they rather quickly responded in kind. There are some genuinely nice folks here on SR, and it certainly doesn't hurt to let a vendor know they've made you happy... but I'm sure many vendors have already over-loaded message boxes. Besides, it's more proper, productive, and beneficial to all involved if you sing their praises in the open forum.

If you'd really like to help them, that's the best way to do it.
Hell, do both!
