Even sites that used to take Western Union have stopped recently or started taking validating really seriously - otherwise they loose their merchant account. Btinstant was the last big hopeful on my list but their system keeps going up and down and I've heard they are having some legal problems. So my noob are dreams of easily buying bitcoin online looked crushed: After a few days I came across two different methods, and used both and both really suck:

Method 1) Use cashU  to buy BTC from the nordic bitcoin site - but buying cashU certificates isn't easy because they mostly sell in shops. Enter: www.pcgamesupply.com who sell them online and then email you the codes. So 1) open a cashU account 2) open a pcgamesupply account (with telephone  confirmation nightmare) buy cashU card - buy bitcoin from nordicbitcoin. But! Wait for it - pcgamesupply only allows you to buy $60 in a 24 hour period - and you are charged a $7 handling fee. So for $57.00 you get a total 3.48 BTC. How am I going to buy an AK47 and some crack with BTC 3.47?

Method 2)  Enter Virwox, my savior? Just buy SLL (linden dollars) and convert them to Bitcoin! No problem, here we go. 1) create a stupid second life account because virwox won't let you register without one 2) Create account with Virwox  and confirm 3)Buy SLL (linden dollars) transfer to Bitcoin! Great!? No, Not great. Virwox will only let you spend $52.00 a day on Linden dollars AND charge a fee to buy Linden AND charge a fee to transfer to BTC AND charge a withdrawal fee AND only sell full BTC / SLL no decimals so I can't get rid of the remaining Linden dollars in my account or the USD change (every penny counts) total 3 BTC for $52.00 and all that fucking around.

Bottom line, no one sells BTC online using a reasonable method - the sites that did are closed or have stopped due to chargebacks and fraud. It's even impossible to use online Westernunion cause the BTC vendors want your drivers license and proof of address and take like a week to do it. And who the fuck wants to give this shit to bitcoin merchants? You must be joking. Most want counter to counter WU / MG transfers and if you are going to do that you may as well just go deposit cash at a bank which is still the best option (not for me)

So I'm going to cave in and am now going to register with CaVirtex and will send them my ID and stuff (after draining my chequing account - just in case)  so I can buy via their online bill payment system (great exchange rate as well). So how does this end?
