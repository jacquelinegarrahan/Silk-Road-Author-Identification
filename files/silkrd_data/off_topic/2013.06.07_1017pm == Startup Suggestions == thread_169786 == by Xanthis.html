Well the result of the implementation of vendor creation fees/bonds has been interesting.  I've been here since the road started, and at the beginning (before there were fees to become a vendor), certain products were much cheaper/more available, such as pharmaceutical amphetamines (Adderall, Dexedrine, etc).  Before the change, there were plenty of 'vendors', who I assume were really just regular joes just selling their unused scripts, so you could get 30mg IRs for 3 or 4 bucks apiece.  Now?  Expect to pay 3-5 times that, which is pretty obscene.  On the flip side though, it seems there are fewer scammer vendors and 'pointless' vendors (e.g. people who would just put up listings for nonsense to send out a message).
