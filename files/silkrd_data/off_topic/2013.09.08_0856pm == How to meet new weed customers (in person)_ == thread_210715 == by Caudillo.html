I'm inexperienced, but here's my thinking, and it does go along livestr0ng's information.

Start with people you know and can trust. If at all possible, turn them into business partners of sorts - they put you in touch with potential buyers, but you only sell to your friends, those in your contacts list. 'Cause you don't want to be caught selling to an undercover cop. Have trustworthy friends that accept the buyer's cash, collects product from you, and delivers it. A mailman of money and drugs, right in your contacts list.

And by trustworthy friends, I mean ones who would take a bullet for you, then take another if asked. Everyone can turn on you, but finding those who would be the last to turn on you are the ones you will look to first.
