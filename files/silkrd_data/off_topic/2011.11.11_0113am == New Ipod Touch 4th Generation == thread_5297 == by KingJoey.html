Hey every1 wants up?

I just purchased the new Ipod Touch 4th Generation, 32GB. I know its not really related to SR but this machine is really amazing and I just wanted to share.

I have had several Ipods in the past but this one is amazing. It is basically a mini I tablet. It comes with a good camera, it can take HD video in good quality, it can hold tons of songs, it has the same types of apps and games offered on an Iphone also. Now my phone is an Android but I have noticed that the Ipod apps are much better. There are also alot of free apps which is really cool.

One my my favorite things about the new Ipod touch is that it has wifi. The new Ipod touch is perfect for watching movies, listening to music, playing games, and even doing productive things using the apps.

Since it has wifi you can also use it for navigation purposes but its not that great unless you can be mobile while maintaining a wifi connection.

But one of the downsides is music prices. Since the Ipod makes it so easy to listen to music, watch videos, tv shows, and play games you will need to budget alot of money to pay for these things. I was looking at a few songs and they seem pretty expensive, around $0.99 each. If I was buying just a few that wouldnt be bad but I want to buy like a 1000. I want my favorite tv shows like South Park and Two and a Half Men by the season. I want new movies at affordable prices! I also found around 40 games I want plus at least 100 apps. When all was said and done I calculated that the media cost for my $320 Ipod was going to far out weigh the actual cost of the Ipod, which I consider to be very expensive.

I would really like it if some other users could post good sites for getting free Ipod apps, music, videos, and games. I am going to try PirateBay but this will be the first time I have ever used PirateBay for music. In the past I have had mixed results with PirateBay.

The cheapest Ipod Touch 4th generation is the 8GB that cost $199 before tax and shipping. In the past I have owned 8GB Ipods and found them to be plenty big but with all the options now adays I am worried that a 32GB might not even be big enough. If you can afford a 64GB I would recommend buying it.

Here are the prices from Apple, 8GB Ipod Touch $199, 32GB Ipod Touch $299, 64GB Ipod Touch $399.

For a college student an Ipod is probably the 2nd best purchase you can make after a high quality laptop.

If I could afford the Iphone I would purchase that because the Iphone is basically the Ipod touch with phone capabilities. But the downside of the Iphone is that if you are purchasing just one line you should expect to pay around $100 per month. If you can convince your family to convert to Iphones than you should be able to find a decent family plan for around $200 a month.

I hope this helps anyone who was thinking about purchasing an Ipod touch. For those of you who havent had the opportunity to use an Ipod touch I highly recommend this machine. If you enjoy technolgy then you will enjoy all that the Ipod has to offer.
