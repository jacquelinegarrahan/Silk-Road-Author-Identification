Most of the best drug movies have been mentioned (Fear and Loathing in Las Vegas, Withnail and I, etc.), so here are some others I think were missed:

A Clockwork Orange
http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0066921/
Alex and his droogs drink milk laced with synthemesc, vellocet, or drencrom before committing their crimes in this classic Kubrick satire. "Synthemesc" is synthetic mescaline; "vellocet" is methedrine/speed, and "drencrom" is adrenochrome. A classic.

Rules of Attraction
http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0292644/
Bret Easton Ellis, author of American Psycho, Less Than Zero, and The Informers among others, has called this one of the best movies based on one of his books.

The Big Lebowski
http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0118715/
The Coen brothers' offbeat comedy classic is hilarious with a joint, white russian, or nothing at all.

Weeds
http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0439100/
This one doesn't fit since it's a tv show, but season 1-3 feature some of the funniest pot-related moments around as well as light drama. Along these lines, I'd also recommend the American version of the British show Shameless (the British version is probably as good or better, but I have never watched it).
