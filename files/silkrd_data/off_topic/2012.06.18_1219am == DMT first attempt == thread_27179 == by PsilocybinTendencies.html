Title: DMT first attempt
Post by: PsilocybinTendencies on June 18, 2012, 12:19 am
I just tried DMT for the first time.

Result? Almost got somewhere, but didn't break through as expected.

purchased this powder off a vendor decently reputed vendor. then bought one of those rose stems they sell for meth-smokers, because I don't have a vaporizer and didn't have the money to invest in one. I saw where there were instructions to take like a glass bottle with some steel wool, but I didn't fully understand how it worked in the end.

I have zero experience smoking chemicals in a manner such as this.
but I had someone who is very experienced with smoking meth with me, and he applied his meth-smoking knowledge to my attempt at smoking dmt.

I measured myself out 50 milligrams, give or take, put it into the stem, and heated it from the bottom, letting the flame get close, but not touch the glass. twisted the pipe back and forth as I was doing this until the vapor in the pipe was thick and sucked lightly. took in as much as I could handle, exhaled, repeated.

The taste was just god awful. Easily the worst tasting thing I've ingested in my life so far, but that's probably not saying much.

I felt it come on quickly though. It overpowered my whole body, and made me dizzy. I had to lay down.
For the first couple minutes, it made me really nauseous, like I was about to throw up.

After that subsided I just plain felt weird. Trip was definitely the correct word to describe it, but it the substance definitely has its own qualities.

I felt incapacitated, like not in control of my body, and thought "here we go, yessss." laid back and waited for visuals.

nothing.

just a strong as fuck body buzz for about 15 minutes at most (maybe, I think it did effect my perception of time). All in all, I did not enjoy the feeling.

I figured maybe I didn't do enough. My chemical smoking experienced dude said it looked like I had a bit of a puddle left over, so I figured maybe the taste was so appalling the first time that I was unprepared to take in enough to make the trip work.

So I waited 40 or so minutes. And tried again, adding about 30 more micrograms to the leftover puddle previously described.
This time I inhaled deeply, three good hits.
I wanted to puke really bad. Noxious tasting shit.

It hit me harder this time. Noticeably so. I still feel it a little bit about now maybe 40-50 minutes after the second attempt. But still no visuals :(

What do you suppose went wrong? I'm guessing either the DMT sucked (I doubt this based on what I've been reading on the forums and such), I did something wrong in taking it (much more likely, I'm a complete noob to this substance), or I have a strong genetic resistance to visuals (it takes me ALOT of acid to get visuals, and I took about 13 mg 2c-p quite some time ago and didn't hallucinate, and I definitely felt this experience pretty good...).

Post by: ultramega on June 18, 2012, 02:44 am
50mg should send you into space, i really don't understand why you didnt get any visuals.

personally i think a meth/freebase pipe is the best device for DMT but you need to know how to use one to avoid wasted DMT, burning DMT and painful hits.

here is how i do it:

20-30mg in a freebase pipe just like you have
heat the bottom of the pipe for a second with a torch style lighter (don't use a normal lighter!) so the DMT turns to liquid
let the DMT cool and solidify again, should only take a minute.
now go lie down somewhere and let your body go limp, make sure you are comfortable in this position and you're not too cold/warm.
make sure you have somewhere to place your hot pipe and lighter or have a sitter to take them from you.
when you're ready in one motion bring the pipe to your mouth and the lighter below it and start inhaling slowly, the key is to start inhaling before it beings to vaporize as if you vape a load of it and in hale a huge load in one go it will burn like hell and taste like shit which will make inhaling more and holding it much harder.
once you have inhaled for about 2-4 seconds (it should of completely vaped clean) put the pipe and lighter down while continuing to inhale slightly, then hold it for as long as you want or can.

25mg is generally enough for a breakthrough dose for me.
