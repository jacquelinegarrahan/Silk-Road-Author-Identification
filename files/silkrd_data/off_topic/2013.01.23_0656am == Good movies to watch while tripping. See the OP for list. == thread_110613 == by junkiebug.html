Post by: lilacstar on January 25, 2013, 08:21 am
Hmm, I don't know, most of the movies on your list seem kind of heavy. Which wouldn't be enjoyable really. Especially during the first half of the trip.

I know that my first trip was extremely childlike and I decided to watch Elf. Literally the most hilarious movie on acid I have to say. I'd definitely recommend a light comedy like Elf, or Zoolander, or anything of the sorts. But it really depends on what kind of trip you have, whether it be more childlike, visual, or introspective.

I know that some people said Fantasia is awesome while tripping because of the music and visuals, but I didn't get much visuals my first trip so that movie did absolutely nothing for me. Turned it off not even 5 minutes in.

So yeah, try something light. You might be able to watch a deeper movie like Inception, like Donnie Darko, like Memento, like The Matrix, during your comedown. That's always when I've been more introspective and wanting to think new things. It also helps watching a movie you've never seen before so you can really follow it and not get bored.
