Title: First outdoor grow, need advice.
Post by: Steelo on July 07, 2013, 04:23 pm
I'm about to attempt my first cannabis grow, and I'm going outdoors. I've watched a video on youtube which seems to be pretty helpful, however it really seems a bit basic, and I want to see if you guys think this is effective.

-First, find a north facing patch and dig it up, leave for a week or so.
-Lay down organic fertilizer pellets containing feather and fish meal, composted seaweed, poultry manure and blood and bone.
-Lay down powdered Lime on top of your fertilizer and turn soil into itself with a garden hoe.
-Leave for another week, and in this time germinate your seeds and keep them in small pots, watering once in the morning and once at night until they have 4 leaves before moving them into your patch.

The guy in the video says this is all he does as far as fertilizer and nutrients but it just seems a bit basic. Will this yield good results? Any tips or advice will be greatly appreciated.

That would be a straight organic grow depending on the fertilizer pellets you use.

Keep it simple for your first grow. :) If you have any questions ask away. I have grown a large amount of weed indoors and out.
