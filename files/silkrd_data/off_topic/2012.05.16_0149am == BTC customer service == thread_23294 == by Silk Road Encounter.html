Post by: philter3 on May 16, 2012, 10:36 am
For cash into BTC the absolute best way to go is Bit-Instant (i.e. deposit cash with an invoice number at specific major banks) and they send you BTC at your Mt Gox account or at your account at other exchanges. Processing time is a few hours at most. VERY FAST.

Taking money out is trickier. If it's small amounts obfuscate and tumble your BTC *****thoroughly***** then cash out through a wire transfer from an exchange to a bank account.

Don't ever deal with exchangers here on the Road. It's an out of escrow transaction (you know that whole NO FE STAY IN ESCROW mantra everyone keeps screaming??) and you are almost certainly going to get screwed over at some point.
