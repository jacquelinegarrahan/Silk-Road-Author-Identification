Random drug testing does suck. I don't know what kind of store you work for, but if you're feeling ballsy, you could always talk to the person who is directly in charge of you. Let them know that you are a hard worker, and your history with the company, as well as positive reviews from coworkers, that you are a serious worker, and assure them that anything that you do outside of work is your own business and that it in no way effects your performance. You do not have to go into any details about which drugs you use, or the frequency that you use them.

I used to work for a HIV/AIDS clinic and did this once word got around about drug testing. You have to be as professional as possible and be sure that you are going to the person that is directly in charge, not your manager/supervisor, ect. From what I've read, it sounds like it's a small mom and pop type of store (family owned, not a chain/corp). Go to the owners and be honest with them.
