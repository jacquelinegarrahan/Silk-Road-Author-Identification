Tolerance is different for everyone my friend. You may not be so 'lightweight' to shrooms as you think, you could have just been in a bad setting and let paranoia over come you, making it overwhelming. Torrance mostly depends on BMI and metabolism rates, also how often you take the drug and cross tolerance.
If you have a low BMI, like me, you are most likely going to have a lower tolerance compared to someone who has a high BMI. But this works in your advantage because it means you dont have to spend as much money on drugs :)
remember always start on a very low dosage when trying new drugs, you can always work your way up and you have nothing to lose :) For example, start with 0.10g of shrooms and then just work your way up until you find a comfortable dose!
