Title: Fronting product?
Post by: wright on April 19, 2012, 04:57 am
Just made our first listing public and have to say, incredibly excited to be joining the community.

As a seller here, it seems one of the great benefits is that product doesn't have to actually be fronted directly to buyers without at least proof of funds for payment. But the logistics of obtaining bitcoin anonymously are obviously slower and less convenient than cash transactions and may introduce a lot of situations where someone that has the means for payment may still end up with slightly less than what an item costs due to currency exchange fluctuations, transaction/exchange fees, etc. and would take a considerable length of time to just get what may amount to $0.50 or less.

So the question is, after a seller builds a solid client base here with lots of repeat customers, how often does it come up that a small portion of the payment gets loaned as a customer service request? And is there any mechanism for accepting slightly less for an item or would it have to be a new custom listing?
