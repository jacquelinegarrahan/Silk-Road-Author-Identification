Post by: PriscillaMarie90 on January 09, 2013, 10:38 am
The first time I was on probation, I was drug tested regularly and I know that my P.O. used a drug panel cup. If your P.O. uses any kind of panel drug test, dip sticks, ect. then the synthetic urine should be perfectly fine. The only way they can tell that it's fake(and i don't even know if they really can or not) is if they send it off to a lab for testing.

Urine doesn't have to be kept in any specific condition that I'm aware of to remain useable. Keep it in a zip-lock bag, a water bottle, whatever you want as long as it won't spill.

Just make sure that nomatter what you use(someone elses urine or the synthetic urine) you keep it VERY warm when you go to use it. Most drug testing cups have a black sticker on the side that will detect the temperature of the specimen, and if it's not warm enough (or too warm) it's going to look very bad and will probably get you in alot of trouble, or at the very least, re-tested.
