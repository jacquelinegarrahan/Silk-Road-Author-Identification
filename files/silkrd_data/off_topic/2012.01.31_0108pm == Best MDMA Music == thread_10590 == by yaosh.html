Post Molly's favorite albums here, along with a little description like follows:

[ Euphoria - a Decade of Trance ]
Chill, old school club trance anthems/dance electronica.  Real good pace through the 3 CD set perfectly guides your roll, GREAT for groups, keeps everyone on the same wavelength.  Starts out pumping you up a bit and I swear right as the roll hits almost every time, it cuts to smooth epic trance.  It picks up tempo gradually, declines gradually, then picks up again and goes into dance mode.  The 3 CD set runs for 4 hours and loops well.  Wouldn't roll without it.
