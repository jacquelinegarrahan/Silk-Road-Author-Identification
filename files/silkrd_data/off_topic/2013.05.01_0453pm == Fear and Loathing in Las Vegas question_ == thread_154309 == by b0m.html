Title: Re: Fear and Loathing in Las Vegas question:
Post by: TylerDurdensPenis on May 02, 2013, 02:18 am
Yeah, it's adrenachrome. Total BS, but it's funny in the movie. Hunter's lawyer was talking to (or about, I forget) his dealer about getting some of that elusive, rare adrenachrome. It's supposedly so hard to get because it can "only come from the human adrenal gland".

Then later, there is a guy on the phone talking about some brutal murder where they cut out the person's adrenal gland. Later still, Hunter & his lawyer are in the hotel room, and they have a vial of adrenachrome. The subtext is that he put in an order for it, the dealer (or the dealer's thugs) went out and killed someone to get their adrenal gland to harvest the adrenachrome, and the guys got high off of it. In other words, some poor sap got murdered just for Hunter to get baked.
