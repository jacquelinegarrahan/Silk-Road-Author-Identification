Post by: Hungry ghost on August 20, 2013, 07:35 am
OS : operating system, like Windows, Linux etc. Lots of people use secure OS like TAILS, often from within:

VM: Virtual machine, running your OS from within an VM adds an extra layer of security

LE: law enforcement

FE: finalize early, in the SR escrow system, your payment is held until you finalize, when you receive your order. FE is when you release payment before order is sent, normally due to new/untrusted status.

PGP: proprietary encryption software. Used interchangeably with:

GPG: open source version of same. Used on SR to encrypt mailing addresses so only vendor can read it.
