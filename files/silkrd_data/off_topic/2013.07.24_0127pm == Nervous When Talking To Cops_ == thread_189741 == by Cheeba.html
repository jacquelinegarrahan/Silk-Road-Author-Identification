Title: Nervous When Talking To Cops?
Post by: Cheeba on July 24, 2013, 01:27 pm
Hey guys. The other day, I had a run-in with an officer who was called to my house. I was expecting some friends over, so when I heard a knock at the door I assumed it was them and immediately opened it for them. What I saw before me was a police officer with what looked like a permanent scowl tattooed on his face. He immediately started questioning me as to who I was. I had been waiting several days for a package from SR and I assumed that they must have intercepted it and have come to arrest me. The cop started mentioning that they'd gotten a call for something different that I'm not going to mention, which seemed to rule out SR. He quickly and commandingly asked if he could come in, and out of shock I stupidly agreed to let him.

I was so shocked that I didn't know what to do exactly, and he wanted to look around the place. I'd been looking at SR a minute beforehand and still had it up on my computer, so I was sweating bullets like crazy. He started questioning me really hard about the reason why he was there, and wanted my ID which I'd left in my room and had to go and get. I think I was visibly shaken, as I was stuttering a bit when talking to him and looking around nervously. Luckily when I was back getting my ID, he didn't take it as an opportunity to really search my house hard, and when I gave it to him he looked through some of the rooms in my place, was satisfied and decided to leave. I was physically shaking for about 10 minutes after he left, and I felt nauseous for a good few hours afterward. Luckily I was just on the Silk Road forums, so it wouldn't have been immediately obvious what I was up to even if he did enter the room my computer was in.

It occurred to me after that, that I've never really had any run ins with police before where I was considered a suspect for anything and actually had to deal with their cop manipulations.  It made me realize how incredibly ill-equipped I am to actually speak with or handle cops when I have anything to hide. And it made me think that there must be some way to get more experience with it so that I feel more comfortable the next time something like this happens.

Does anyone here have extensive experience speaking to cops that are trying to bust them? How do you get more comfortable with that? I'm pretty worried about this happening again in the future, and I want to know what I can do to get a handle on this.

"I'm not going to give you a ticket today but you might not be so lucky in future, you can't be going #km in a #km/hr zone, have a good day sir"
