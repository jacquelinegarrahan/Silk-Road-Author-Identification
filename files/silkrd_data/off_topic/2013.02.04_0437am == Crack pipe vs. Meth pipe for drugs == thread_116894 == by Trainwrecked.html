Title: Re: "Crack pipe" vs. "Meth pipe" for drugs
Post by: gestaltassault2 on February 04, 2013, 04:52 am
crack pipe = a straight glass tube which one puts a piece of steel wool inside which the crack rock will sit on. The user then sucks the flame of the lighter through the front of the tube where it directly comes in contact with the crack.

meth pipe = a straight glass tube with a bubble on the front end. The user loads meth into the bubble and then heats the bubble up from the outside with a lighter until the meth starts to vaporize. With a meth pipe the flame never directly touches the meth. The point is to bring the meth to a temperature where it vaporizes without burning it.
