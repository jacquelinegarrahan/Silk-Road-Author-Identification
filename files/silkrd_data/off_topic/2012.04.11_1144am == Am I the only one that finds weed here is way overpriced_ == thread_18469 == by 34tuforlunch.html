Title: Re: Am I the only one that finds weed here is way overpriced?
Post by: UKGrower on April 12, 2012, 06:21 am
When I first encountered this site, I did think the weed was pretty expensive. However, since considering becoming a vendor on here myself, and running through the figures, I realize that there is a big difference in the prices you see as a buyer, and the amount of cash that ends up in the vendor's pocket.

First, there are the site fees.  For small orders, you are looking at around 14% loss before your bitcoins can even leave the site.  At this point, you could theoretically just dump them onto an exchange and cash out straight to your bank account, but most prefer to cover their tracks by laundering their coins, then cashing out via more anonymous methods, which can be expensive, and somewhat unreliable.

Then you have all the expenses associated with packaging and shipping, and the other expenses associated with this method (occasional fraud, funds tied up in escrow etc).
