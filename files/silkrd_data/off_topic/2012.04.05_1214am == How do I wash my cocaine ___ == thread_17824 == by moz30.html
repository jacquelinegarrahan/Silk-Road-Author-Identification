Title: Re: How do I wash my cocaine ???
Post by: element12 on April 05, 2012, 05:27 am
Found this:
-Dissolve the freebase in acetone. You want it to be clear. If it's cloudy, filter again.

-Next prepare a solution of 1:2 concentrated HCL in methanol. Use the aqueous HCL/methanol to acidify some acetone and then add this solution to the cocaine freebase/acetone solution.

-Stir and allow the cocaine HCL to crystallize.
(Optional - one more acetone & filter wash at the end)
