Title: Re: Which benzo should I try first?
Post by: anton on May 29, 2013, 01:23 pm
Benzo's are ALL different. They have different half life's and the effects are all different.
I would recommend starting on a short or medium half-life benzo with a small dose + caffeine. One effect this class of drug produces is sedation/sleep so it is good to choose one the has more musco-skeletal relaxing profiles and less sedation. For your first time take a half dosage and give it 2 hours before upping. Do yourself a favour and no alcahol, as you can loose your mind with no memories in the morning.

http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_benzodiazepines will provide you with the best information!

My personal favorite is Lorazepam (Ativan)
Alprazolam (xanax) deserves an honorable mention, as does Temazepam (Temaze)
