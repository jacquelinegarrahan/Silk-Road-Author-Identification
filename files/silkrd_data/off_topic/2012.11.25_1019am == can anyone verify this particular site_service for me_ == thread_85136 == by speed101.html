Title: Re: can anyone verify this particular site/service for me?
Post by: doitclean on December 08, 2012, 01:07 am
I tested WHMX.   I was scammed.

I paid to Acrimonious2 escrow.

WHMX said that he used UPS, through Mail Box Etc., to ship.

WHMX said that the package was seized by customs, and that I would be receiving a letter (at my shipping address) from customs informing me of the seizure.  I never received any letter from customs or anyone else.

WHMX sent me an invoice with MailBoxEtc., that allegedly showed the date of shipping and the date that customs seized it.  The invoice was phony.

I asked WHMX to provide me with the UPS tracking number.

WHMX said that he did not have a tracking number.

I told him that all UPS packages have tracking numbers.

He ignored.

I have requested a return of my money from Acrimoneous2.  They have not yet responded.

Bottom line:  scam.

Advice:  There are many excellent vendors of drugs on Silk Road.  They best ones have a long track record with hundreds of purchases.  I have never had a problem with any of them, although I am usually careful about reading up on them before I buy.  If someone was really selling  Counterfeit Money, they would have an enormous positive following.  That fact is that there is no Counterfeit Money available anywhere on Silk Road or Tor.
