Title: Re: need concentrate knowledge
Post by: sl1pknot on May 23, 2012, 03:22 am
Yes get a super torch crack lighter. Lol. If your local smokeshop doesn't carry them, Bed, Bath, & Beyond carries nice torches for cooking crème brûlées. Tasty stuff too haha.

DMT/meth/crack pipes work well. They look sketchy or fun, whichever you think.
Even a long thin glass tube that can withstand heat will do. (My long vaporization pipe broke, but the lengthy part was still in tact and I can still dab concentrates onto it easily.) Or a specialized concentrate pipe/water pipe works the best IMO. Just use a torch and heat a quartz or titanium nail piece til it's red hot and dab your oil/whatever onto the nail to vaporize it. Those of us lucky enough to be around medical marijuana/dispensaries can find pieces like this in stores easy enough, otherwise look online.

I try not to directly smoke oil with flame as it burns a little, vaporization is best.
