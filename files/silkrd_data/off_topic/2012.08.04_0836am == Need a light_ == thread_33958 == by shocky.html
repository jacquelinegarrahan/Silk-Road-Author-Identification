Post by: shocky on August 04, 2012, 09:18 pm
It shines a really bright light on it, causing an increase in heat to whatever absorbs the light. If the lens is focused 100%, a small dot of light appears and it's REALLY hot. If you defocus it a little, the spot grows bigger and cooler.
So if you put DMT in your pipe, a solid layer of it, then shined the sun on it in a larger area, it should only get hot enough to vape it and nothing more.
