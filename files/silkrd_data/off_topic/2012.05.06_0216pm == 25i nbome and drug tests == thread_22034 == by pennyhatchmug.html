Title: Re: 25i nbome and drug tests
Post by: GreenPEAs on May 06, 2012, 11:55 pm
Any of the DOx / x-APB / MDxx chemicals will test positive for amphetamine and you won't be able to get out of it with a GC/MS in most cases.

The NBOMe series isn't fully understood in regards to metabolism in the human body but it seems likely the ring would be cleaved at the nitrogen and so you'd be dealing with the benzyl ring and 2C-I in terms of metabolites. Those shouldn't show up on a drug test - if something does show positive, demand that GC/MS like I said before.
