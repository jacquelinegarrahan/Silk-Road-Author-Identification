You should set up your own VPN in Iceland, google how to do this. Sorry can't write it all out here would take forever.
Also look for "logwiper.sh" it's the handy Debian log killer the NSA left lying around one of their Flame/Stuxnet control servers.

Is it safe? Depends what kind of a target you are. Are you selling mass quantities of dope? Then no. Are you running this site? Then no. Otherwise it's good enough to fool your ISP into only seeing a vpn connection and not a direct Tor connection. Also look up how to control DNS leaks through your VPN (if you use windows) but Torbrowser already does this.

Should also look at fixtracking.com the site set up by DuckDuckGo that recommends privacy addons (for regular, non critical browsing)
