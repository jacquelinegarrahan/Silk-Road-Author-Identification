Ok, I recently figured this out.
1) copy and save the desired vendors key
2) write your message in text edit, and highlight it, then choose services and encrypt
3) choose your key, it's the one that looks like it's priv but is your public.
4) highlight and copy that text and then send using the key that you imported just paste the whole thing in the address box
