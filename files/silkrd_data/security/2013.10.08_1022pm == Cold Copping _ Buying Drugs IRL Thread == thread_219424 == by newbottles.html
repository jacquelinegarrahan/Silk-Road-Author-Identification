Title: Re: Cold Copping / Buying Drugs IRL Thread
Post by: Yoda on October 09, 2013, 06:21 am
Depends on your locale, depends on what you're looking for.

If you're looking for heroin or crack/coke... having a ghetto nearby is greatly advantageous:   

In this case, to make things super easy your first time, all you need to do is drive through with some white people in the car... the crowds of people just seemingly standing around will come to life shouting out to you what they have.  Just stop and let them walk/run up, confirm what they have, ask for what you want etc., and just make the swap.   Obviously don't give them the money to just run away with it, let them show it to you as you're handing the money. 

-The bigger the crowd on the corner, the better... gangs making their money dealing don't want their customers getting ripped off, that would drive away all the customers once word gets out... they want customers to know that their spot is legit, and use force to keep things this way.

-Go there at reasonable hours... like don't go at 1 AM, you're just going to get robbed.  Even street dealers want to catch sleep or want some time to go kick it etc., don't expect them to be out 24/7.  I'd say not try to go pick up past 10 PM, and maybe you could go as early as 8 if you're lucky... outside of that, you're pushing your luck. 

-Don't get cornered... scammer robbers may try to get you to pull into some dead end alley or something similar, then proceed to rob you since you are surrounded with no way out. 

Pull through their spot enough, they'll give you their number... maybe they even have someone doing delivery service that they deem you worthy of.  Still, don't really trust them though...

Quote from: newbottles on October 08, 2013, 10:22 pm
Where do you start location wise in your area?

Depends on what you're looking for really. It's different from city to city.

For instance:

SF - Heroin & Crack - Mission Street
       Crack              - Tenderloin
       Pharms           - Pill Hill (Leavenworth&Golden Gate around block up to Eddy&Hyde)
       Weed/Shrooms - Haight & Ashbury

Oakland - Pharms    - Pill Hill (Between 27th and 35th & MLK and Broadway)
               Crack      - West Oakland Park

Berkeley - Weed/Shrooms/Psychedelics    - Telegraph

There might be more in these areas I'm not remembering at the moment.

Quote
How do you initiate the conversation?

Best to let them initiate it. You don't want to come across too desperate either since you'll be too easily fleeced.

Quote
How do you manage risk/reward, for example with a middleman/runner?

You don't. You NEVER allow your money to walk. Make that point up front. If they try and insist anything else ... fuck it they're playing games. If they've got it on them and they're going to sell to you, they'll do it right there and be chill about it.

Quote
What are the common scam tactics?

Oldest trick in the book is convincing you to have to go somewhere with them and springing on you that you need to give them the money so they can go get it. That's why as soon as you hear anything like that you make your point up front your money doesn't walk.

Quote
How do you manage risk/reward re: LE IRL buying?

The UC I've encountered on the streets have always seemed really obvious for whatever reason. All the real slingers seem to spot them and melt away. ;D

But it's usually better to let them offer it to you even though that's not always possible if you look too straight. But if someone offers something even if it's not what you're looking for you could ask for a referral.

Quote
How do you get to that ideal connection that keeps you well but off the streets?  Meaning the man that you get on your SMS and connect, or that man that delivers to your home, etc.

In short, you spend lots of money ... well lots of money for the streets anyway where they're used to people buying dime bags. Again it depends what you're looking for, but generally whenever I've dropped a bill or two or up on something I've often gotten hooked up so I don't even have to go down there anymore. Sometimes they come to me or I go to them. But you want to make sure whoever it is you're buying it from is legit first. Like on your 2nd or 3rd order ... though sometimes that's not even possible if you're in a strange city like I've found myself in a couple times but still gotten hooked up when I dropped a couple bills.

Quote
