Title: Re: Bit Instant - no idea what I just did.
Post by: TalkingHead on April 23, 2012, 12:25 pm
The standard way to do this is after you have filled in the info for a cash deposit -> Mt. Gox on Bitinstant a new window will open for CashTrust. You pick the bank and branch (not too near your house), go there and fill out a standard deposit slip. All you have to put in there is the account # given to you and the name CashTrust Holdings, Inc. You don't need the invoice #. That's it. Usually it will be in your Mt. Gox account within the hour.

Mt. Gox now has a new feature where you can set up the transfer there but I don't like having to do any more than necessary there, mostly because you can't access your account through Tor. Also, make sure that you set up your Mt. Gox account with a fake name and Tormail email address.
