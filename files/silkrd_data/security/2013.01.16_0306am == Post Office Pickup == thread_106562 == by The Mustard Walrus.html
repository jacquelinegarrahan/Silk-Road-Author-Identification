Ok, so you live in an apartment? Do you think this order was large enough to NOT fit in your apartment mailboxes? If so, that right there would be taking away some of my paranoia. One time I had a package say "out for delivery" very early in the morning and it should of came that day no doubts. Ended up not coming. Tracking then updated to "notice left" but no notice was left! Turned out that it looked like the PO was backed up and never had time to deliver. I looked things up and it auto updated to Notice Left. Package came next day no problem.
In other words, always give it that extra day. USPS could be weird and screwed up sometime. Like with me, sending my package the COMPLETE opposite way after it was in my area! I hope I'm ok too! Pray for me and I'll pray for you! lol :P
