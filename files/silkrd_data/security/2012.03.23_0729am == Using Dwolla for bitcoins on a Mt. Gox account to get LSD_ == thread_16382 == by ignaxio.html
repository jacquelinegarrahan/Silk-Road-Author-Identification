Title: Re: Using Dwolla for bitcoins on a Mt. Gox account to get LSD?
Post by: spd on March 24, 2012, 02:49 am
When sellers say they don't accept escrow, they are basically asking you to "Finalize Early".  Usually when you purchase an item here - the funds are taken out of your account, and put into escrow - then there is a button marked "Finalize" to click once you receive your order.  Once you finalize, the transaction is done - you can't file a complaint/ask for a refund/etc. 

When you finalize early, you finalize before the seller ships your item.  So if it never shows up, you dont have any recourse.  Overall, you want to avoid it if at all possible, but for your first buy or two, that might be tough. (Sellers are hesistant with new buyers - because some buyers will also try to scam by claiming to not have received product.)

i dont know envious' policy on that, but his seller page probably will let you know.  If you do finalize early with any seller, make sure to research them a bit here in the forums first
