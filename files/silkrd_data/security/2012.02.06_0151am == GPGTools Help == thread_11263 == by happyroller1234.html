- public / private key {public + private = key pair}
   - you will only have the other party's public key they keep both, and their private is private.

- you own created keys, yes you also have both but you ONLY EVER GIVE OUT THE PUBLIC portion.

- "Now I'm just confused about the private vs. public key"
   - public + private = key pair
   - you need your private key to decrypt messages sent to you by the 2nd party who encrypted the message with your public key.
   - if its someone else's pub key you imported into your keyring you will only have the public key part cos they have the private cos it belongs to them
   - hand out your public key for people to send you encrypted messages {when gpg/pgp msg recipient = you.}

- try r/click or Option or alt and click, to export your own key - DON'T EXPORT THE PRIVATE WITH IT, JUST THE PUBLIC
