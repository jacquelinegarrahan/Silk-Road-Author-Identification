You only need the sender's first and last name, the tracking #, and the city, state, and country the sender put for their address.

If you use a "test question", for transfers under $500 you can use a fake name and they won't check your ID. The sender would fill out their money transfer form with the test question being set to say, "What color are my eyes?" and provide a fake name & address for the receiver. He'll then tell you the fake name & address he used, and the test question answer "Blue", and you can pick it up using that fake name at any WU/MG location in your city.
