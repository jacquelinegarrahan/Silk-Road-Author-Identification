Title: Re: Leaving money in your SR account??
Post by: wetdog on January 15, 2013, 11:56 pm
I would advise having a wallet outside of silk road.  This way if it ever goes down you won't lose out.  You can go into account settings and provide silk road with an outside wallet address.  This way if the site ever does go down they will have a place to send your coins to you.  If you go ahead and create an outside wallet for this purpose then you might as well use it all of the time.  I would advise using blockchain.info.  If you go to that address via TOR or clearnet the site guides you through the entire process.  Pretty simple.  I would highly recommend doing this.

Title: Re: Leaving money in your SR account??
Post by: peeweed on January 16, 2013, 12:55 am
Most people suggest that before you send funds to your SR account, always get a new BTC address.

It only takes a second, and does make it more difficult to record a "pattern".

If you are a low traffic customer, it is probably overkill, your real risk is receiving the product.  Dealers etc would be more at risk to money trails etc (that is how a lot of the old mafia got busted via taxes and money trails). 

Yet it is sooo easy to do, you may as well change it every deposit.
