Title: Re: is there a waya to use a debit car or cc to put bitcoins on?
Post by: SelfSovereignty on December 30, 2012, 07:34 pm
No.  The issue is that a bitcoin transaction cannot be reversed.  No matter what the circumstances are -- nobody has that power.  You can give them back, but you can't undo the transaction.

Accept payment in forms other than cash, and you get tons of people claiming fraud and canceling the payments after they get their bitcoins -- since there's nothing that can be done about it after that.
Title: Re: is there a waya to use a debit car or cc to put bitcoins on?
Post by: astor on December 30, 2012, 07:35 pm
Generally no. Too much chargeback fraud. Bitcoin transactions are irreversible so there's too much risk in getting scammed. Cash deposits and bank transfers are the most common ways to get bitcoins.
