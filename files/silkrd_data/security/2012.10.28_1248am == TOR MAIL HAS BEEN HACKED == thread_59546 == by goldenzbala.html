25 characters, upper/lower + numerics works out like   this;

26 uppercase + 26 lowercase +10 numerics = 62.

62^25 = 6.4534542777x10^+44 possibilities.  This works out to about 148 bits of entropy, which is considerably more than the AES128 and IDEA, and 3DES ciphers, which feature 128, 128 and 112 bits of entropy respectively.
