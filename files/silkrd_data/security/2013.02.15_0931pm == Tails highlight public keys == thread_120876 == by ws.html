Title: Re: Tails highlight public keys
Post by: SorryMario on February 16, 2013, 01:09 am
Use the cursor to highlight the first dash in the "-----BEGIN PGP PUBLIC KEY BLOCK". Now hold down the Shift key and click on the very last dash in "END PGP PUBLIC KEY BLOCK-----" Voila! The whole thing is highlighted

You can also do it by holding Shift and pressing "End" to highlight an entire line, then (while still holding Shift) tap the Down arrow on your keyboard (each tap adds another line to the highlighted part) until you've got the whole message highlighted.

Title: Re: Tails highlight public keys
Post by: talawtam on February 17, 2013, 08:36 pm
Click Applications/Tails/Configure persistent volume.

When setting up you can specify what will be saved and what wont.

Follow the onscreen instructions and click GnuPG when you have the option. From now on when you import a pgp key they will be saved... you can access them from System/Preferences/Passwords and Encryption Keys
