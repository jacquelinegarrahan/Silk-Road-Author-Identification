If I were in your shoes, I'd do the following:

First, I'd try to find out whether or not they are licensed to do business in your particular state.   In August 2012, Solidtrustpay just got fined $60,000 by the State of Arkansas, for doing business in that state without a license.

See: http://www.securities.arkansas.gov/!userfiles/Orders/2012/SolidTrust%20Pay.pdf   ** Clearnet .pdf file **

If they're not licensed in your state (and they may not be) then you can ask to have your money withdrawn without fees.  If they give you a hard time, tell 'em you'll file a complaint with your state's regulatory officials.  Get you rmoney back, and then try some other method. 

In the worst case scenario, given that it's only $58, you may have to write it off.
