Post by: bluedev1 on September 05, 2013, 08:15 am
never let emotions dictate your buying/selling strategy.   if there is a good reason to sell, then sell.  but if you have no idea why the price is dropping, then you do not sell, you hold on (if you can) or buy more.  there is a reason recurring-buy strategies work great for people who are confident in long-term value growth of an investment.  that is, if you invest $100 every week, no matter what the price of btc, some weeks you will buy high and by the end of the week you'll have lost some value.  but over the course of a year or two or five, you will have a winning strategy because you're averaging out the volatility.
