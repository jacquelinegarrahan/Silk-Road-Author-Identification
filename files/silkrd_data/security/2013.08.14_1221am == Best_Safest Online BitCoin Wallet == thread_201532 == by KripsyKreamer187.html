Title: Re: Best/Safest Online BitCoin Wallet
Post by: Wadozo on August 15, 2013, 02:46 am
To be honest, Web Based Clients are not safe at all, such as Block Chain or Coinbase. Your trusting a third party to look after your wallet and the coins in it. They could disappear in a flash meaning you're likely to never see your coins again.
Go with a Desktop Bitcoin Client such as Bitcoin-Qt with Armory installed over the top for added security or even Multibit.

You can create as many Bitcoin addresses as you like to keep in your Wallet.
