Title: Re: First time buyer -- Escrow?
Post by: Wadozo on September 29, 2012, 05:33 am
Buyers with less than 5-10 transactions are not forced to finalize early. However, most vendors will ask you to FE with less than 10 transactions or been a member for less than 1 month. Whatever you do, don't get sucked in to FE. Just shop around and send some vendor's a quick PM if your interested. Once your up and going it's a lot easier compared with those initial first few transactions. What product are you looking for in case I might be able to help with suggesting a vendor?  :)
Title: Re: First time buyer -- Escrow?
Post by: Rowsdower on September 29, 2012, 11:25 am
I finalized early for my first transaction before the vendor could even ask me because I figured there's a lot of buyer scammers here and I wanted to show I'm legit.  I just went with a trusted domestic vendor and made a relatively small order.  Remember if they screw you over you can still leave them 1/5 bad feedback, and most good vendors don't want that.  I wouldn't FE for more than I'd be willing to risk losing though just in case.
