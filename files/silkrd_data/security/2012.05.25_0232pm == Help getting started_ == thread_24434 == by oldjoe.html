Ok karma is related to The forum only and does not impact on your ability to use SR. Appears you can only give karma once you pass the 100 post limit and you are limited to giving one per day. Anyway you probably don't want to concern yourself with that.

The best thing you can do is lurk the forums and read the sticky posts in each section to familiarise yourself with the site.

You are going to need bitcoins, no two ways about it.

If you are in the UK/EU contact vlad1m1r he will take cash in the post and convert it to bitcoins anonymously. Using moneypak or similar will no doubt end with you getting scanned as a new user.

Order small at first as you have no feedback buyers will be wary if large orders from an unproven buyer.

You will need bitcoins so if you want to use SR get familiar with them. Ultimately you are not going to buy loads of cheap drugs on SR, what you will get is a quality product, a secure anonymous and safe service. You pay a premium for this compared to street prices but the benefits outweigh the negatives considerably.
