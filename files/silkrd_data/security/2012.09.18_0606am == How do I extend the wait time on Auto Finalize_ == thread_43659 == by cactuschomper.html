Title: Re: How do I extend the wait time on Auto Finalize?
Post by: shiznit on September 18, 2012, 06:34 am
you cant extend it without entering into resolution.
in there you cant request a refund, or extend the time for how many days you want. it also allows you to write a message to the vendor on that request. he can then accept your suggestion, or have his own suggestion like + or - more days..    either way its easy to get sorted, and if not, SR Admins can see everything and will decide if you both cant :)
dont let it auto finalize, or it looks badish on your stats.
