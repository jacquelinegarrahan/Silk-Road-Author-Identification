I've been doing a lot of reading lately and have discovered that tails is NOT what you want to be running for this purpose. Liberte Linux is your best bet.
As for tails I also have had several connection issues, I'm betting that your problem is that Tails is not syncing its clock to the network or that the sync fails. I'm not sure why this happens but when it happens to me I just reboot until tails syncs up.  Once it successfully syncs tor will start up successfully. When it doesn't sync tor gets stuck on "Loading authority certificates" or something like that.
Anyways to be honest I do not know nearly enough about this to really be giving you advice, I've just had a similar problem.
