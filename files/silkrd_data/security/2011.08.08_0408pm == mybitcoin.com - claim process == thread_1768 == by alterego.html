Title: Re: mybitcoin.com - claim process
Post by: Kind Bud on August 08, 2011, 10:12 pm
Sounds like you are out of luck if you traced the addresses and did not turn up anything.

Silk Road is very good about tracking down lost bitcoin, but he does get busy so be patient.

The key to recovery is the deposit address. In theory only you and Silk Road know that address (it's public, but only the two of you know it is a Silk Road address). If bitcoin was received at that address Silk will make sure it ends up in your account. Before you message Silk make sure you see it in the block explorer and you have given Silk Road inc 12 hours to confirm the transfer. If it is not in the block explorer, than it is an issue on the sending end, Silk Road does not have it, and there is nothing he can do.
