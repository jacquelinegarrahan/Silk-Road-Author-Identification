Title: Mozilla Collusion Add-on
Post by: Enemy of the State on June 26, 2012, 08:52 pm
Just thought I would post about the Mozilla add-on Collusion. It's a program that shows the third parties that are collecting information on you as you browse. I don't know about you but I wanna know who's watching me as I surf. Ghostery is a good program but Collusion blows Ghostery away. They give you a tree that works in your toolbar so you can have an up to the minute picture of where your info is going....or not. When I'm stealth there isn't a single stalker, but when I'm au natural it gets scary. Not into paranoia shit, just common sense security.

Any way heads up. Let me know what you think if ya wanna think about something like this.
