Title: Re: Post for setting up and using pgp?
Post by: phubaiblues on July 23, 2011, 05:10 am
You're doing fine...it's just tricky at first...once you get the basic idea, it seems kind of easy and even fun...but lots of people seem to go thru what you are going thru.  I did too.  In that tutorial I posted a link to, there is a site that you can send email to.  First it's good to get the idea: You make a key for yourself first, you will have a public and a private key.  Your *public* key is so I can send you messages, so you give that one out freely.  Then I give you my public key, and you use that to send *me* messages.  The private keys are how we decrypt the messages.

Have you installed gpg4win (if you have windows?)  then you'll have Kleopatra or PGA as your key manager.  I"d make a file on your Desktop with your public key, and post that, and somebody could use it to encrypt a message which you could decrypt.

Anyway, get your regular browser, and get started here: http://www.gpg4win.org/  They have a tutorial that walks you through it, or several have been posted on here...

Here's the beginning of the tutorial...easy does it, it works: http://gpg4win.de/handbuecher/novices_5.html
