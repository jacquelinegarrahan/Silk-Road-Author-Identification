Just gonna point this out as it seems to be a common error:

      Inflation is where a currency loses value (compared to goods and services) over time.

What the BTC is experiencing is deflation.

The BTC is actually designed to deflate over the long term due to the limited number that will ever be produced, filling a increasing demand. That's why it can be split into such tiny fragments. The theory is that eventually we will be using 0.1, and 0.01 and 0.001 BTC.

I don't think the current rise is part of this trend tho.
