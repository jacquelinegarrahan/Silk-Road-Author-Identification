Keep in mind its the Christmas season and mail delivery is going to be delayed.

With that said, yes, I would definitely stay alert to the possibility that they did intercept one package, then started monitoring your mail to see what else might show up.

But honestly, I would have to lean towards it simply being delayed or possibly diverted by some crafty USPS employee who either thought you might be receiving money or took one expecting money, discovered it was something they could have fun with and kept their eyes out for more.
