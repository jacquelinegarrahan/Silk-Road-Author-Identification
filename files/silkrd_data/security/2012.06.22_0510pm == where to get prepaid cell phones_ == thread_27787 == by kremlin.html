If someone is living in the UK or US, he can easilyget a phone anonymously, there's no need to buy one from SR.
But if you live in some other country, the UK phone you bought from SR will be pretty useless because the money on it will be quickly depleted, especially because roaming costs a lot, after that you can't top up the SIM card anonymously from an other country without a proper bank account.

A phone can be bought anonymously where I live, but the SIM card needs to be registered.
Second hand phone shops sell/buy cheap phones here, they also have SIM cards that were left in the phone by the former owner, it might worth to ask around for it, this is one way to get SIM cards in my country.

The other way is to give a false name and address when registering  (by phone) a pay as you go SIM card sold at supermarkets. The card won't be verified, but it's gonna be activated so it can be used anyway.

3rd way is to take a bum to the shop with you, and buy the phone under his name.

There are verified SIM cards sold on local auction websites and classified ads, might wort a look.

It's not a good idea to buy a phone / sim card / private bridge / VPN / computer / secure USB  ...
or anything else from Silk Road that links your real identity with SR.
