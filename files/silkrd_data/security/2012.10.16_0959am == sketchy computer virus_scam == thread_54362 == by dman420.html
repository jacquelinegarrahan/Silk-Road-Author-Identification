Title: Re: sketchy computer virus/scam
Post by: dumbnerd on October 17, 2012, 02:00 pm
This is a fairly common ransomware infection that is making the rounds. I assume it's getting dropped heavily by someone who's bought a nice chunk of a botnet or could just be largely in the wild - haven't had enough time to see any patterns. Either way always good practice to scan for rookits after it's cleared.

First you'll need the Kaspersky Rescue Disk (http://support.kaspersky.com/viruses/rescuedisk) - boot to that and remove the inital ransomware infection.
Once it's removed the inital infection, load in to Windows and run: Combofix, Malwarebytes and TDSSKiller. Google these, easy to use.

Also performing a system restore will do nothing at all to fix this!
