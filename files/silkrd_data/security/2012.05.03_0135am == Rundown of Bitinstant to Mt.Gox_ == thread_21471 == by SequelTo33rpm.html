Title: Re: Rundown of Bitinstant to Mt.Gox?
Post by: Hawk23 on May 03, 2012, 02:03 am
MtGox > Add Funds > Bitinstant > Scroll down and copy Exchange Account #

Then go to:

Bitinstant > Pay From: "Cash Deposit" > Pay To: "MtGox" > Enter Destination (Exchange Account) > Enter Amount > Enter Email

When you hit 'send funds' you must choose a bank location. It will then create an 'invoice'. Print it out and take it to the bank. Fill out the deposit slip with the name (trustcash or some shit..), account number and deposit amount.  You must deposit the exact amount on the invoice. For example, if you want to deposit $300, then bitinstant will make you deposit $301.23 or something like that.

I just did this from memory so there may be something I left out. But you get the idea. On a recent deposit my Mtgox account was credited in 10 minutes. It's never taken longer than 20 minutes. Good luck and happy trails on SR. Hope this helps.
