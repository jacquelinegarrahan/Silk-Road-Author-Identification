Post by: theotherotherside on May 10, 2012, 01:36 am
Credit cards and Paypal are reversible and not to mention trackable, so it's generally not possible to buy Bitcoins using these methods. A scammer could reverse the transaction once the unreversable bitcoins are received.

You could try the bitcoin IRC channel, they'll do paypal trades. Just make sure you wash them after using something like bitcoin fog.
