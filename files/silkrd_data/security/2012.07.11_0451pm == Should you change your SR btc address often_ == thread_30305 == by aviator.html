It should automatically change every-time you make a deposit, but I'd also recommend scrubbing your coins on the way in. I know of BitCoinFog which is public, there are also a few others. Using multiple wallets isn't really a good method for scrubbing your coins. You should obtain them as anonymously as possible, as well as scrub to be the most safe.

The fact SR keeps the addresses active presents a very large problem if the SR server is ever compromised, they can easily link BTC Address->UserName.
