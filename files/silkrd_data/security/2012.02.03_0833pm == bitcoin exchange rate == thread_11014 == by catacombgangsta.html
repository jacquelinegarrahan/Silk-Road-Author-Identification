Title: Re: bitcoin exchange rate
Post by: Silpheed on February 04, 2012, 07:35 am
You can see the value of bitcoin at:

http://bitcoinprices.org/
http://www.bitcoinwatch.com/
https://mtgox.com/code/data/ticker.php

On Silk Road if an item is priced in USD then the site uses the "avg" in that last URL to determine the price in bitcoins, which is the 24-hour weighted average price of bitcoin on MtGox. "last" is the last successful transaction (considered the current market price), and "sell" is the lowest price someone is willing to sell bitcoin for

If you buy bitcoin for lower than the "avg" then you'll be saving money on items.

If 1 bitcoin is worth $5.86, then $1 is worth .17 bitcoin (1 / 5.86).

As for obtaining bitcoin, you might want to look into bitinstant.com.
