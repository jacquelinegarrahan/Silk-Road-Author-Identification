Title: Re: Getting onto the SR is all of a sudden a tortuous process.. Help?!
Post by: CaptainSensible on September 23, 2011, 04:15 am
The Tor network goes through these periods where all the relays are overloaded with too much traffic. Also, the server that Silk Road is using probably can't handle me, you & everyone else trying to connect to SR at the same time.

Trying a new identity doesn't do much to help the problem with a slowed down connection.  This has worked for me in the past: on the Vidalia control panel click on the green onion to stop Tor. This breaks down your current circuit of connections.  Wait a few seconds & then click on the stopped Tor icon (the red "X" over the onion) to restart Tor and it will build a new, hopefully faster circuit. 

Good luck.

Or you can just go into the network panel and close the slow circuits yourself manually, it then builds new ones. Keep doing that until a faster one has been established that works for you. You can view the speed of each tor node. Close any relay circuits that are operating at kilobyte speeds.
