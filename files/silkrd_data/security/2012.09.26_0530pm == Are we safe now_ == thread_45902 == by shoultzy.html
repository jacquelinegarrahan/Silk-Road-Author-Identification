Title: Re: Are we safe now?
Post by: nardical on September 26, 2012, 06:53 pm
I've only been here a few days, but I think I can answer some of your questions. I highly recommend going back several pages on the Security subforum and reading through as many threads as you can.

1. From what I've read, you have a better chance at receiving your package when using your real name and address. You don't have much of a defense if you're caught picking up drugs from a PO box or someone else's house. I've also read to write "Return to sender" in big letters on the package and letting it sit in your house for a day or two before opening it. However, I'm sure LE knows about that trick.

2. You can put Liberte Linux or Tails OS on a USB or MicroSD and boot your computer from there. I believe both of them store data in RAM, so nothing incriminating is left behind when you shut your computer down. A good tutorial I used was: http://dkn255hz262ypmii.onion/index.php?topic=15383.0
However, if you're just buying personal amounts, using Liberte or Tails isn't necessary, but you can never be too careful. I would recommend encrypting your hard drive with TrueCrypt.

3. I'd like to know the answer to this, as well.

4. As far as I know, the most important thing is buying your bitcoins anonymously (I found Shroomeister's method to be the easiest: http://dkn255hz262ypmii.onion/index.php?topic=36048.0) and learning to use PGP/GPG.

Again, and I can't stress this enough, I'm new as well, so take my suggestions with a grain of salt.
