I wouldn't recommend it if you plan on buying on SR in the future. They'd have you on record, and it doesn't matter that they're bought for actual legal purposes because they have to assume  that you'd tumble them through a few wallets to get to SR. They can't ever know for sure that they're going to drugs though. It shouldn't really pose a security problem or raise any red flags too high, but like a said, they'd have you on record and that is the only reason I'd say not to do it. Down the line there is a very small chance it could bite you in the ass, but in reality if the legal system has got you, your bitcoin trail won't matter too much, it'd just be another piece of evidence in the trial against you.

Basically, your decision should depend on how heavily involved in SR you are and whether you mind being in the database. You might just go to multiple banks anonymously as suggested above for the peace of mind.
