And obviously, don't use a membership card if applicable (like CVS ExtraCare, etc). Wait maximum 24 hours for the card to activate. (12 hours for me)

Using the Tor network follow the steps below:

Create an email address at hushmail.com or any other email sites.

Go to centregold.com and create an account there using the email address you made. For the phone number and address, just put in some bullshit. I used the address of some random post office and their phone number. You may see some warning about it not being on public records, just ignore it and continue.

Go to libertyreserve.com and create an account. Link your Liberty Reserve to your centregold account. Then, using the credit card you bought to buy some Liberty Reserve money.

Next, create a Mt. Gox account and transfer the Liberty Reserve funds to your Mt. Gox account and buy some Bitcoins.

Finally, you're able to buy drugs without any evidence (most likely)! (Just don't have login info for any of your accounts on any websites anywhere)

Also, they can't prove it was you who ordered the drugs if they ever intercept the package. Who knows, it could be your neighbor using your name and address with plans to "steal" the package before you checked your mail. ;)
