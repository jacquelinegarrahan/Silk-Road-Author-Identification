Title: Re: Getting pills when I have a RX...
Post by: Appa on March 15, 2012, 06:38 pm
Technically, you could get in as much trouble as anyone else.  An prescription only legally allows you to have a certain amount of whatever drug.  It will not protect you from going "behind your doctor's back" to obtain more of the drug than he recommends.  This is not to say that, if you were caught, a cop wouldn't fall for the "I have a script!" line.  So once the pills are in your possession, it might be easier to defend.  However, having a large volume of pills is usually considered "intent to distribute," which is a felony (in the US), so stay cautious.

Domestic shipping is generally safer than international, since it doesn't have to go through customs.
