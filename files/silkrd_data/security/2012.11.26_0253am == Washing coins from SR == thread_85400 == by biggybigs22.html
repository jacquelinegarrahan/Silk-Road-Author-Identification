Title: Re: Washing coins from SR
Post by: woahmang on November 26, 2012, 03:34 am
A rule of thumb is that any service that's not compatible with SatoshiDice runs a shared wallet and is therefore also a mixer. If you send 10.5 bitcoins in to some service then send the exact same amount out then that might be obvious, but if you send them as 5, 5, and 0.5 back to different use-once SR addresses then you're probably fine.

If you're really paranoid just send them through different services across borders, specially ones who are quasi-anonymous (I'm looking at you betsofbitco.in).

edit: oh, getting coins out of SR? If you've got a large volume that may be more tricky. I'll retract my earlier statement and let someone who's experienced at that pipe up.
