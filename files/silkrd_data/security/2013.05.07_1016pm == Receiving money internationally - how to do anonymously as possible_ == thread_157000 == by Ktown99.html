Title: Re: Receiving money internationally - how to do anonymously as possible?
Post by: Baraka on May 08, 2013, 04:31 am
Whether you want to exchange cash for BTC, or BTC for cash, you can do it locally with trusted traders. You physically receive or hand over cash in exchange for BTC. No middleman, no government, no LE, no nothing. Even in a worst case scenario where there are eyes everywhere, you can't be connected to anything but the actual monetary exchange transaction. That's why it's untraceable and it can't be banned unless physical cash is banned.
