Post by: FramedAgain2222 on May 07, 2013, 01:11 am
You have a couple of options.  Either get a wifi switch/router or get a USB wifi dongle.  The dongle will probably be cheaper but you'll get more general use out of the switch/router.

USB wifi dongle route: plug the dongle and the 4g into the desktop and torrent Connectify hotspot.  Use it to turn your wifi connection into a hotspot and share your 4g connection.  Then your laptop wifi can pick it up and use the connection that way.

Switch method: use the 4g on the desktop and send a physical line to the switch's WAN jack.  On the pc, share the internet connection over the lan link.  Configure the switch to use that as the source of internet and turn on wifi.
