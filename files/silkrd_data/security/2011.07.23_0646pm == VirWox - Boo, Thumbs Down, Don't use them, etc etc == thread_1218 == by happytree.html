While VirWox does cost more than using the dwolla route, you save the 3 or so days that you would otherwise have to wait if you're coming directly from a bank account.  When I need funds ASAP, it's still the way I go.  Instead of using PayPal though, I use MoneyBrookers with a pre-paid VISA card.

If you're getting more than just one or two bitcoins, it isn't really all that bad.  On average, after all the conversion fees I end up paying about $16/bitcoin there where dwolla --> mt. gox usually costs me around $14/bitcoin (this is an average over the past month).

In addition, as others have mentioned, you don't want to try sending directly from virwox to your SR account.  Go through either a bitcoin wallet on your computer or an online one with mybitcoin.com or somewhere else.  When using this method, I can have funds into SR in under an hour, usually.  Longest it's taken is 3.

Edit: Just remembered that my first BitCoin withdrawl from virwox DID take 48 full hours.  I was not a happy camper.  I've done 10 transactions since then and all of them have been much speedier.
