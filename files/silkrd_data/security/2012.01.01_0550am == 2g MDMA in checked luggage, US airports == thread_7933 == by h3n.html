Personally, I'd rather pack it in my carry-on - capsule it up in some vitamin capsules and store it in a bottle with some real vitamins.  It's not going to show up as anything suspicious on the X-ray scanner, and as long as you don't set off the metal detector, you're not going to have to worry about TSA going through your backpack.  Even if they do a bag check, they're not going to open your vitamin bottle and look at every single capsule.

Of course, you didn't say where you're moving to - the customs inspectors on the other end might be a different matter - but they're going to have a chance, if they want to, to look through your shit regardless of if it's carry-on or checked.
