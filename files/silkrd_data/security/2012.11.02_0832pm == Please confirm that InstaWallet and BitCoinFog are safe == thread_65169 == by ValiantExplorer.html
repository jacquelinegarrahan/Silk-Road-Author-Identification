Title: Re: Please confirm that InstaWallet & BitCoinFog are safe
Post by: thesearstower on November 04, 2012, 06:48 pm
Any time that you place your coins in a wallet that is not personally controlled by you (meaning: BTC client downloaded on your computer), you're risking someone walking off with your coins. There have been several high-profile hacks of BTC exchanges.

That said, InstaWallet & BitCoinFog have been dependable in the past.

Your best bet is just to buy the coins right before you use them. This minimizes your exposure to BTC value fluctuation and decreases the amount of time that you're vulnerable to an e-wallet hack.
