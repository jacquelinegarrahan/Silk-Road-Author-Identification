Title: Re: UK BTC through mt.gox - real or fake registration details?
Post by: UKMJ on August 28, 2012, 08:54 pm
depends on what you are going to be doing with the account.

If you are going to use the account to only buy coins to spend on SR sign up with fake data. There is no point using real details and then making cash deposits to the automatic deposit machine, if you use real details you might as well do a bank transfer direct from your account. If you are just a casual buyer, i.e. buying for personal consumption and not buying too much then you could use real details and buy coins and send them to SR directly without too much concern. LE are not going to be tracking your coins from mt gox to SR and then intercepting your post to make an arrest. But for extra security use fake details and anon deposits.

Mt gox will ask you to prove your ID if you want to withdraw large sums or if you access the service through Tor. Whatever you do do not access your mt gox account through Tor, that way lies trouble,

I have heard mention of the removal of a cash deposit service but no evidence of it.
