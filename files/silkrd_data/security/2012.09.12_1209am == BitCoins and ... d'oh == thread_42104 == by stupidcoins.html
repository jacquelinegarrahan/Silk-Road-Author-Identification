Don't worry about it.  There's a reason the SR has a built in BC tumbler.  The coins CAN be traced to the SR.  They CANNOT, however be traced to any illegal activity.  After all, purchasing items from the SR isn't illegal.  Only purchasing illegal items is illegal. 

They can't even trace the BC from your SR BC address to your vendors due to the tumbler.  You're fine.

Personally I've always been rather wary of non-SR tumblers because i'm not certain they can be trusted and what's to say they won't keep your coins once they're in their system?
