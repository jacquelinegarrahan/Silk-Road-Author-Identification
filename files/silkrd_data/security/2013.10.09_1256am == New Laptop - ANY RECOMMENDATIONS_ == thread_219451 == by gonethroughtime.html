Title: Re: New Laptop - ANY RECOMMENDATIONS?
Post by: 7Kz2 on October 10, 2013, 02:52 am
Don't search "blackhat" vpn exactly, because none will advertise them as such. I just refer to them as "blackhat" because they're mostly used in hacking communities. 
A good VPN will surely NOT be hosted in America or other country with strict internet laws.  Logless VPNs often come from Eastern European countries, the most common I've seen are from the Netherlands, Ukraine, Romania, and Russia.
If they accept credit card, it is very suspicious.  If they offer any kind of reoccurring payment do not use it as that means they log payment info.

The most important thing is - don't be cheap.  There are tons of great fast VPNs for $4 that'll hide your IP but get you arrested in a second.  Also learn how to use them properly. NEVER login to an account with a VPN that you logged into with your real IP.  That'll make it practically worthless.
