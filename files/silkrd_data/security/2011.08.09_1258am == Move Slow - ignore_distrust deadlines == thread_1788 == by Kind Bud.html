Title: Move Slow - ignore/distrust deadlines
Post by: Kind Bud on August 09, 2011, 12:58 am
Don't rush yourself or let yourself be rushed by others.
Rushing anything for any reason should tip you off that you might be  dealing with Law Enforcement or a scammer.
Panic and deadlines are very common - Ignore them completely.

A time constraint mixed with doing something out of the ordinary is a huge red flag.

Most things  happen at a reasonable pace and you should stay in communication but don't rush faster than what you promised.
