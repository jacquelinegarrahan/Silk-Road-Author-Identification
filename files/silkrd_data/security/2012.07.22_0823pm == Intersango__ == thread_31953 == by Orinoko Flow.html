That is a question you need to answer for yourself. In certain jurisdictions, getting caught with a small amount of weed will not have serious repercussions. If you are not concerned with getting caught, I guess you could leave out certain security procedures. I wouldn't recommend it, but I'm a privacy geek.

I would not access Intersango using TOR. You probably used a bank transfer to get money to Intersango. That means your account can already be linked to your identity. If you use TOR to access it, it might indeed raise eyebrows. Just buy the coins, send them to another wallet, optionally you can launder them through a mixing service, and then send the coins to Silk Road.

However if you got your money to an Intersango account in an anonymous way, then I do recommend you use TOR to access your account. That way the BTC cannot be traced back to you, and you can just send them to your Silk Road account directly.
