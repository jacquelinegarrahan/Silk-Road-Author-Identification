Post by: mdmamail on March 31, 2012, 08:26 pm
Remember it's not illegal or suspicious to buy bitcoins. It's what you do with those coins afterwards that makes you anonymous, just move them around a few addresses to create some plausible deniability in blockexplorer and good to go. If anybody asks you sold them to somebody, no way to prove you didn't especially if it was a cash based transaction on the street or by mail.

If really, really worried after every transaction and your balance is 0 truecrypt wallet.dat, wipe it from your hard drive and start up bitcoin-qt and make a new wallet.dat again. Now no transaction history kept locally
