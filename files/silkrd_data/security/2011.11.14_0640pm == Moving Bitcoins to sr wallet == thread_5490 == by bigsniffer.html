Title: Re: Moving Bitcoins to sr wallet
Post by: vanilla on November 14, 2011, 07:16 pm
It kinda sounds like you are doing it backwards. You want to move coins from your local wallet to SR, correct? If so, go to your SR account page and copy the address listed there. Then go to your wallet and click 'send coins', paste the SR address there, enter the amount and click send. Once you do this your coins should be on their way and arrive in your account within a few hours at the most.
