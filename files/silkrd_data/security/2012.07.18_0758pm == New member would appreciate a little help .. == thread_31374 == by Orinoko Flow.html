Title: Re: New member would appreciate a little help ..
Post by: Un-invited on July 24, 2012, 07:53 pm
The first time I put money into my SR account, I used a MoneyPak to Bitcoin exchange within SR. I'm not saying this is the best or most reliable way, but since I'm a horrible noob, I figured it would be more anonymous than myself trying to fail at being undetected.

Go to a store that sales Green Dot MoneyPaks with the amount of cash you want to deposits, be generous since the prices fluctuate quite a bit and vendors will charge a fee. The salesclerk will give the card back with an activation code, which is the same as the scratch off code on the back of the card. *Make sure you get a card with a scratch off on the back, and not a pre-paid debit card*

Just in case this needs to be said, don't activate the card. It's temporary and does not have personal information attached until it is activated, at which point they send a "permanent card".

The hard part is finding a vendor you can trust, because really, you're taking a chance that they don't just run off with your money. Do your research, and research very well. I, personally, used Sugar Mama and had no problems whatsoever with this vendor. Bit coins within 24 hours, but just because one transaction goes well, doesn't guarantee a future transaction. Like I said, research, research research.

If all goes accordingly with the vendor of your choosing, they will deposit bit coins directly into your SR account. I would advise lurking forums and learning as much as you can before placing an order, make sure to check out Rumor Mill vendor threads.

Hope this helps, good luck and welcome!
