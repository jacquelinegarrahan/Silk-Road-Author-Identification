Sometimes the vendor will list on their page what their preference is for PGP communications.  Generally, for general questions, plain text is fine, in my opinion. 

Always encrypt your address.

Point 2:  NEVER FE AS A CONDITION OF THE SALE, FIND ANOTHER VENDOR.  Be prepared to get burned and be sure you are able to afford the loss if you do FE.  Don't whine if you get burned from an FE transaction.  If I like the vendor an have done business in the past, I may FE as MY OPTION.  But I never will when asked to.
