Alright mate, I've been doing this for years a lot of the dealers in my local area when they "blow up"/get bigger they tend to get noticed by law enforcement and then get raided and the pigs find lots of weed, coke, scales e.t.c and a shit ton of folded up money = dealer behind bars for a long time.

at the end of the day a lot of people will tell you different things and "good ideas" but at the end of the day it's your stash and whether it's worth £/$100 or £/$1000, don't come up with some half-assed location or technique which ANYONE can find accidentally.

You've actually pretty much already got the best method I've used successfully over the years:

Quote from: Heyenezz on January 20, 2012, 07:48 am

----In a jar or airtight box in the middle of a lake/stream/swamp (you could memorize GPS coordinates and keep a GPS with you to find them)


I use a slight variation where I vac seal the contents and place inside a BLACK (colour is v.important so it doesnt stick out) plastic bag, then find a local park/forest/nature spot...bury the shit, whip out phone, store GPS co-ordinates, (I also take a photo of the area so when I come back I can find it in a few seconds...but if your extra paranoid best not do this).

Also try and make sure the spot is about 20-30 mins away from your home so nobody "local" sees wtf your doing digging around.

I can assure you if you do this technique properly it works a treat.

Up a tree = kids playing around can find/dog walkers can see easily.
bushes = people walking dogs..leads to dog having extended sniff of the bushes
under a rock = if there's a day of strong winds e.t.c say goodbye to your stash
fake bird nest = best hope a real bird doesn't land on it!
In a fake hornets' or bees' nest = whoever owns the land might call a pest controller..

drainage pipe idea = not a bad idea..but can be quite bulky..which might lead to other problems
Inside a hollowed out tree trunk = also not a bad idea just make sure that tree is completely secluded..

hope that helps!
