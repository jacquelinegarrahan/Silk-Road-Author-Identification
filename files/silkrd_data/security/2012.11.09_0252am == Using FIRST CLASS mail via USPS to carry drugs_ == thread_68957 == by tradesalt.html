Title: Re: Using FIRST CLASS mail via USPS to carry drugs?
Post by: paxpax on November 15, 2012, 04:09 am
Tradesalt -
 
I may be mistaken, but an envelope with a stamp does not qualify as first class mail. It would need to be processed by the post office before is carries a legal definition and protection form unlawful search. However, your person and belongings are protected form unlawful search as well. Unless you are arrested or an officer has probably cause they can not search you or your belongings without your permission to begin with.
