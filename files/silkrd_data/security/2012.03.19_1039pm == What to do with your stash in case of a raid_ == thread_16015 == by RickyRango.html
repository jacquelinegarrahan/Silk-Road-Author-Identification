Post by: coptcha on March 21, 2012, 05:43 pm
As with all advice here, it depends on where you live. Different places have different rules, so on and so forth.

BUT - in general for almost everywhere I know of, if they get to the point of raiding you, you are already pretty fucked. If they can raid your place, they have either obtained or found a loophole around whatever permissions and protections that would otherwise keep them out.

You could try flushing it, tossing it out the window, chucking it into an already-lit fireplace and so on, but if they are raiding you they are probably already anticipating that and have plans to intercept such things. As for shoving it up your ass or swallowing it, if they have permission (warrant or whatever local thing) to break into your house and look for drugs, said permission likely covers any small area capable of containing one dose of drugs. With drugs being as small as they are, that pretty much means anything. Including your ass.

In my opinion, you are talking about a situation where you are already very, very fucked. Your best bet is to A) be very polite, sit where they tell you, call them "sir/ma'am," keep your hands visible, sit where they tell you and so on. No sarcasm, no namecalling, etc. Be as nice as possible and try really really hard not to piss them off.

B) Politely, decline to comment about anything without a lawyer. If you are not in a country that has the legal right to keep your mouth shut, well, keep your mouth shut anyway. Don't be rude, but be very firm. "I'm terribly sorry, officer, but I really won't talk about this without a lawyer."

C) When in doubt, remember rule B.

D) If these other rules don't work, remember rule B anyway.

E) If rule B doesn't work, it's because you forgot rule B. So go back to rule B.


If they kick your door down, they are gonna find your stuff. Your own words, however, are absolutely going to be more valuable to the prosecution.

They will probably have it and in most countries it's a public record. Read it, see what that guy did wrong and learn from it. Then do that for a dozen other convicts. And post what you learn on the forums so other people can be safe too.

Make sure the remote ignition requires a deliberate action and is always on hand, on your keychain, an app on your phone, can be activated by a quick dial from a phone, or whatever better you can think of for your life style.  A 5-10 second ignition sequence should allow for enough complication to avoid accidental destruction and short enough to pull off by the time your face is smashed into the floor.  There is still the possibility they discover your destruction and method and charge you for that but the odds are much slimmer and the charges almost certainly far less sever.
