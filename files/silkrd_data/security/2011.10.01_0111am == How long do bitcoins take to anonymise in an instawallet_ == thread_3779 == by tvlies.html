Yes that will most likely be more than enough. It doesn't necessarily take 6 hours. It can be far less. After 1 hour just go to 'blockexplorer' ( <--- google it), and tap your btc wallet address into the search. If you can see that all the BTC has been sent out (and new coins come in), then its had a stage 1 wash. Now send to new wallet. (only do this twice, 4 times seem overkill).

Just be aware that instawallet may keep http logs, to you might want to reset tor exit node between wallets. I think there will be more free wallets than instawallet soon, so it will be best to pass between at least 2.... insta + 1 other.
