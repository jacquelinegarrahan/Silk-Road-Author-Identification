Don't mind the part about it not mentioning mac, it works. 

Download the iso.
Insert a blank disc.
Start Disk Utility.
From the File menu, choose Open Disk Image and select the ISO to be burned.
In the list of volumes, you will now see an item representing the ISO file. Select it.
Click the Burn button and follow the instructions.

Post by: FiveSeven on January 31, 2012, 10:22 am
it's worth noting it's almost impossible to be 'traceless' with just an encryption of a HDD or files. The most important thing to get is, chances are if you have police accessing your computer you're already in a heap of shit. You can hide your SR activity but not the fact you've used tor or an encryption program (again this is for methods of casual encryption). Your best defense is not getting to the point where your house is compromised. Put most of your effort into that.
