There's quite a bit of feedback from people who FE'd and can vouch for the seller. However, I've read in a couple of places that you should NEVER FE. What's the deal here? Is it common for new buyers to FE?
Title: Re: FE-ing as a new buyer?
Post by: Oldtoker on November 15, 2012, 07:02 am
It shouldn't be but, it is quite common.  If your buying weed you can usually find a couple that still won't require FE but, I'm not sure on the other items.  If I were you, I would make ten small transactions in order to get your transaction numbers up.  I know when you first arrive on the Road it's like a kid in a candy store.  But, go slow at first.  Get your transaction numbers up and get a feel for how things work.
