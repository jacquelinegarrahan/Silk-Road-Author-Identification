Post by: CaptainSnowFlakes on June 07, 2013, 03:05 pm
Just create a decent sounding fake name, phone number, DOB, address in the local area of your deposit,(apartment building preferably) and tormail for your fake name. Now try to remember this info. You may need to give the teller your phone number in order to pull up your transaction info on the computer. Also, if they cannot figure that out sometimes they will ask for all the rest of your information in order to pull up the transaction. Just be sure you remember it in case you are asked. If they ask for ID just find another location and tell them that you are using this service because you do not have these types of things. Works for me every time. Also, make sure you save all the transaction info bitinstant and zipzap give you in case you have to make a claim due to their error. Good luck!
