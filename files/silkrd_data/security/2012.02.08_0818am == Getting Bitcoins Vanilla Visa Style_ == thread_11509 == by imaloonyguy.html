Quote from: imaloonyguy
...what should i set my maximum too? im having problems finding the price of bitcoins. if it is 5.75 right now would i set my maximum at 5.95? or how do you do it....
You can leave the Maximum Price text box empty (or enter "market"); either way, you'll get BTC at the market ask price less commission.  Otherwise, you can find a near-real time price quote (US$ and Euros) at Bitcoin Charts [ bitcoincharts.com ] in the "#bitcoin-otc ticker" section.

Quote from: imaloonyguy
..and do i just set the address to the one on my account, or the bitcoin program?
It depends on where you'd like the coins to go.  I prefer to use my own local wallet because: 1) it simplifies the resolution process should your coins not show up, and 2) I can tumble the coins (or not, at my option) before moving them to SR.
