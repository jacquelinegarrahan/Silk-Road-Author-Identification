Title: Never forget where you hide your stash!!!
Post by: mito on July 30, 2012, 06:07 pm
I was just remembering something that happened to me many many years ago.

I had hidden this small baggy of marijuana in the pocket of a shirt.

One day I had to travel to another state so I caught a domestic flight.  This was in South America where security isn't as tight as it is in the US and A.

I had completely forgotten about this tiny baggy of marijuana and packed this shirt in my luggage.

I arrived at my destination and went straight to the hotel.

When I was unpacking I saw this baggy and froze. 

It had passed x-rays and wasn't detected.

But I had run a huge risk of getting caught.

So always remember not to forget that hidden stash.

You could get in trouble for nothing!!!!!!

Imagine the TSA finding drugs in your luggage that you are not aware of.

What are you going to say?
Someone put it there?
It's a prank?

Will you get in trouble nonetheless?
