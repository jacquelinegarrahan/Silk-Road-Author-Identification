Title: Re: YOUR security setup.
Post by: Euphoric on March 26, 2013, 02:14 am
I wouldn't list any exact specific setups, but I'll give you some general suggestions:

If you are just buying from SR and not vending, then an anonymous operating system is probably the best option Both Tails or Liberte are good choices that allow you to boot from a CD or USB key to access SR securely through TOR without leaving traces on your primary operating system that you have installed on your computer.

Tails: https://tails.boum.org/

Liberte: http://dee.su/liberte

I don't find a VPN neccesary for SR access since TOR encrypts all data that is sent between you and SR since it stays in the onion/tor network and not on the clearnet.


If you have any persistence storage, or use TOR from the operating system installed on your hard drive or VM then you definitely want to encrypt the file system.
