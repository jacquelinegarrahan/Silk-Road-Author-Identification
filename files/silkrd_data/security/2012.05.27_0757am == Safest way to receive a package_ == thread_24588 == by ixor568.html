Hi Ixor,

There seem to be two schools of thought on this - one is that by having contraband shipped to your home address you don't have to trust a third party and you can also try to claim that any packages containing illicit items are unsolicited (although in the UK at least this would not be an automatic defence and the burden of proof would be upon you to show that's the case).

My own opinion is that you could use a little social engineering to have a package delivered to a communal block of flats or University accommodation with easy access and no CCTV. Of course the Police could stake the address out 24/7 and there's a risk that one of the other residents will get to the package before you do but I would say it's safer than having controlled drugs posted through your own letter box.

Another method used by one of my "associates" is to grow your beard out a little, dress untidily and pitch up at a church of a Sunday and make it known you're out of work and homeless - wait for some elderly do-gooder to offer to help and say you could use some help in having a "reply to" address for your job applications.

In this case my right and honorable friend was receiving stolen ID's so it was unlikely the old timers would have got into any trouble but if the thought of someone's Grandma being carted away for receiving heroin is too much for you, remember you can always come forward and take the rap if she is caught.

I realise this is a bit ruthless but there's no harm in considering all the options!
