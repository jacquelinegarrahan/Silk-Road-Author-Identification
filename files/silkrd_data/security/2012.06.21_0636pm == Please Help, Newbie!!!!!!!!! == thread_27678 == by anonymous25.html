Title: Re: Please Help, Newbie!!!!!!!!!
Post by: Green on June 21, 2012, 07:45 pm
It is not illegal to purchase Bitcoins using your bank account, but it can draw unwanted attention to yourself if people wonder why you are buying these Bitcoins and what you will be doing with them! You ideally want there to be no trace of Bitcoin purchase because it may cause trouble for you later down the line. I guess it depends on how much precaution, be it necessary or uncessary, you want to take. I personally would be too paranoid to buy bitcoins with my bank account.

When you get your Bitcoins, it might be a good idea to transfer them through: Your chosen exchange>instawallet>BitcoinFog>Silkroad. That way it will make the destination of your coins harder to determine and it wont create a direct link to you and Silkroad.
