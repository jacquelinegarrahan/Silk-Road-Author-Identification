This has worked for me:

- Put order through Bitinstant on clearnet to deposit to my personal wallet
- Deposit cash at CVS/Walmart
- Receive funds within 60-120 minutes
- Open Tor browser
- Transfer coins to anonymous wallet, new registration every time (this site offers free tumbling service, others prefer Bitfog but it's slow and has a higher charge)
- Create new Tor identity after transfer is complete
- Create new bitcoin address on SR
- New identity
- Transfer to SR

Personally, I have used my real info for MG and Bitinstant. Buying bitcoins isn't illegal, and you can launder/tumble them after they are in your possession.

I'm sure others have methods, perhaps even more secure, but this has always worked for me.

Good luck!
