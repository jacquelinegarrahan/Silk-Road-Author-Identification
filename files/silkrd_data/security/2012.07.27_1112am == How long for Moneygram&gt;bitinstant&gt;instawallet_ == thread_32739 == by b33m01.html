Title: Re: How long for Moneygram>bitinstant>instawallet?
Post by: goblin on July 27, 2012, 06:37 pm
SR hides the outgoing bitcoin transactions, not the incoming ones. And if there's a purchase, and coins are in escrow, you might say that it's mixed there, but not before, nor after. Before, you have a path that leads from an online wallet to your SR address (not hidden), and after, if you're a seller, the newly-acquired coins that now sit in your account, are hidden (mixed) when they are withdrawn to wherever it is you send them to cash out. But even then, it's wise to use a mixing service to completely obliterate any traceable path that may lead to you.

goblin
