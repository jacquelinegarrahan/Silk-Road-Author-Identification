Title: Re: tormail and safe-mail
Post by: Nightcrawler on October 07, 2013, 09:42 pm
Quote from: CarlYoung on October 07, 2013, 05:28 pm
Why isn't safe-mail safe?  What is good to use now?

It has been reported by sources that I trust, that Safe-Mail turns suspicious emails over to the authorities, which implies that they are scanned for certain keywords. Some people have speculated that Safe-Mail is actually run by the Mossad -- whether that is true or not I have no idea. If you _do_ want to use Safe-Mail, you must bear in mind that the metadata from those emails, i.e. the header information, e.g.: To:, From:, Subject:, etc. are all scanned/recorded, even if the emails are PGP-encrypted.
