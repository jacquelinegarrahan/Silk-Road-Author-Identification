Post by: aciddeath on February 23, 2012, 12:53 am
Grub is a boot loader only
You need to tell your BIOS to initiate the boot sequence from the USB. Perhaps USB is absent from your boot sequence or below the bootable volume your box sees first (CD -> HDD -> USB -> Network boot)
Chances are either 1) usb made incorrectly (non bootable) 2) your BIOS isnt recognizing the USB drive (unlikely)

If I remember correctly (haven't used my mac in a while) you either have to hit and hold "c" as you bootup or "option"
one of those should let you get to a boot menu to choose the USB
