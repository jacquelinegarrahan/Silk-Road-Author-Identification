Post by: Just Chipper on August 09, 2013, 04:17 pm
Quote from: pathenry76 on August 09, 2013, 05:04 am
Why is silk road off limits via phone if tor is running properly?

It's not Tor you have to worry about. It's the insecurity of the phone itself that's the problem. I use Tor on my phone tunneled through OpenVPN to browse sites. I NEVER login to anything SR related. This way if my phone is ever compromised (LE, angry GF, etc) I have nothing linking me to SR on my phone, keeping my plausible deniability intact. Simply knowing what SR is and viewing forums does not constitute a crime. Now if they have record of you logging in to SR and ordering something on your phone, you have no plausible deniability anymore.
