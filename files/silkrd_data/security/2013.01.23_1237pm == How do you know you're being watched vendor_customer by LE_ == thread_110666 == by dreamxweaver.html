I struggle to see how you could suggest buying drugs off the streets or from friends is even slightly safer than buying from a website on the Hidden Web, considering one is completely anonymous if done correctly as compared to the potential of being caught in possession with the drugs either whilst making the purchase or driving home with them. Most of the answers you seek to the questions you pose are unknown to any of us, unless your in LE. All we can do is control our own actions and the decisions we make. It's important to keep educating yourself on how to remain anonymous and access/use SR from a secure set-up. Minimizing your risks in everything you do is what you can control. If LE are planning to arrest you, there is not much you can do. Once your anonymity is compromised and you've been identified by LE, you're in deep trouble. This is why the best security tactic you can use to keep yourself safe is to maintain your Anonymity. Encrypted HDD's and USB sticks, using PGP, etc, etc are all pointless if LE has identified you and are monitoring your activities. There are basic principals one has to follow to give themselves every chance of staying safe. Things such as using common sense, think twice before you act, make sensible decisions your comfortable with, be cautious with the information you give to a vendor (always Encrypted with PGP) or post on the forum, your choice of vendor, the delivery address you use , the quantity of each order you make, etc, etc. Truth be known, you won't know for sure your being watched until the day your caught and arrested. People may think they are being watched but that could be partly due to some paranoia we have all experienced at times.
