Title: Re: ordering drugs to my own address
Post by: timekey on September 17, 2012, 03:34 am
Domestic packages aren't inspected by Customs -- you don't have to worry about that if you select a vendor that ships from your country.

Read the reviews of vendors to ensure that the vendor practices good packaging techniques (e.g, double/triple vacuum packing) to keep the package discreet. Good packaging won't release a smell.
