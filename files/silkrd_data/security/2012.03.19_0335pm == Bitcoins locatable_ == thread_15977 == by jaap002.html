Post by: vlad1m1r on March 21, 2012, 08:19 pm
Hi buddy,

Suggest you take a look at Bitcoin's page on this topic:

https://en.bitcoin.it/wiki/Anonymity

In brief you can hugely increase your anonymity by :

- Using a new Bitcoin address each time you receive money (most bitcoin wallet software does this for you automatically anyway)
- Moving your money regularly across several wallets.
- Encrypting your wallet.

If you're feeling ultra paranoid you can also use a Bitcoin mixing service like BitCoin Fog which for a small commission will take coins you deposit and pay them back to you in random amounts at sporadic intervals, I suppose it could be called money laundering. I would only recommend this for large amounts of cash.

I could be wrong but I don't think you need to worry too much about this - even if someone could prove that the 20 dollar bill in your wallet was the same one used to buy a line of coke three months ago, how would they prove it was you holding the cash at the time?
