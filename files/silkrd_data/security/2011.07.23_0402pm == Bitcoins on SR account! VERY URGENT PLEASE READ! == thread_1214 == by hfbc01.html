Calm down...and breathe.  ;) Go to your SR Account, and check under 'Unconfirmed'. It may be listed there. Unconfirmed pretty much means the transaction is in progress. Sending bitcoins to your SR account is not immediate. My bitcoins usually hit 'Unconfirmed' status within 12 hours. And it can take up to 3 days for complete transaction in my experience. There are lots of factors...depending on country, payment method, the site you're withdrawing from, etc..  I'm not familiar with Bitcoin7. Check hxxps://bitcoin7.com/index.php?show=withdraw_funds. They have a table based on destination/source and payment method. Considering it's only been 5 hours, you can probably take a deep breath.
