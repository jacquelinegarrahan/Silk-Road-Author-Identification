I wrote you a little guide mate.

Step 1. Copy and paste the vendor's PGP Key into a notepad and go to file > save as > 'name it anything you want'.txt' to your desktop (hypothetically)
Step 2. Open up your PGP software and click on 'import key'
Step 3. Select the .txt file you previously saved to your desktop.
Step 4. A prompt will come up that says: '1 public key imported, 0 private keys imported'
Step 5. Open the clipboard on  the PGP software and type your message or address.
Step 6. Click encrypt and select the vendor's name from the list of PGP keys
Step 7. Type in your password when prompted
Step 8. Copy and paste the encrypted message to your vendor.
Step 9. Order your drugs and enjoy  ;D
