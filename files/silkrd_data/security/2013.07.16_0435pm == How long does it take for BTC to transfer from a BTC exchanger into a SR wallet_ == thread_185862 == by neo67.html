Just go to this website (clearnet) - https://blockchain.info/ - and type your BTC address into the search box and hit ENTER. You will need approx. 6 confirmations before your coins will go through and then you may have to wait a little longer before they show up in your Wallet. It varies but it can take anything from an hour up to 6+ hours.
