The issue is not MtGox, it's a combination of the time it takes for MtGox to initiate the transfer and then it takes time for SR to confirm the bitcoins, which can vary in time, it then takes SR a bit to tumble the coins.  So no, it is not unusual for it to take 3hrs, but most times it is pretty good, around 2hrs, sometimes less.  MtGox is pretty good actually and rates seem to be better.  Tried TradeHill and found I was getting hit with way too many fees so sticking with MtGox.
