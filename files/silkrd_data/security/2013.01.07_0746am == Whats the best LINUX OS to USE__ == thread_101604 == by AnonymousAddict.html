I know of 2 different Liberte USB boot implementations - Liberte Linux and Tails.

You'll find much discussion comparing the 2 both on here and on clearnet, and just when you've thought you figured out which one is more secure based on the info on the net, you find enough convincing counter-info to put you back to square one in terms of choosing between the 2..
