Title: Australians keep it down!

Lately I've noticed more and more asking of Australians, for details about postage, lost orders, success rates etc. This doesn't worry me much, phishing for information is unfortunately a common practice on the internet.

What IS worrying is that there seems to be a reasonable number of folks willing to volunteer their success/failure rates with no regard for WHO you're giving this information too. Fair enough we're all anonymous here, but, and this is also VERY unfortunate, the police aren't actually as stupid as they seem and they're often quite fucking clever at gathering intelligence. Even if they're not the ones doing the phishing, they're still being given information here that I personally don't want them to have.

"Total Orders Calculated: 351 (39% mention the orders orgin)

Arrivals: 333
Non-Arrivals: 18

95% arrivals
5% non-arrivals (could be combination of scams, lost items or seizures)

82% non arrivals were from EU
18% from USA

49% arrivals were from EU
22% UK
23% USA
6% ASIA + OTHERS

stats by Jones32."

Sorry Jones, but even if this is only a fifth of what has actually come across the border, they NOW KNOW that 351 have definitely been made, and 333 odd have arrived. I know all this info is contained throughout the forum and anyone can compile the evidence... SO WHY MAKE THEIR JOB EASY? I'm a fan of helping the community and where reasonable I will give out some minor details about my SR shopping, but even then anyone who believes me with 100% faith is rather foolish.

We have the forum so users can stay informed and it's up to the individual user to make up his own mind, using the information gathered herein!
