Post by: phubaiblues on December 04, 2011, 11:06 pm
It can be a bit overwhelming, all the terminology makes it seem confusing.  Since you got here, you've managed TOR, so you are already getting the hang of things.  You'll need to study up a bit on bitcoins, obviously.  That is the currency we use here, and it protects us from each other and LE (law enforcement).  I suggest going thru the Security forums--as I've done--focusing on bitcoins: what they are, and how we get them.  Tumblers are just way to make them less tracable.  A good 'tumbler' is instawallet.org ...  I'd study up, buy a few bitcoins, and send them to instawallet.org, so you can see how it works.  To buy, after you read thru the forums, I recommend either get-bitcoin.com, which is fairly quick, or dwolla and tradehill.com, which takes longer.  You'll have to read up on them.

Key is referring to PGP.  Another area we've all had to learn and study up on.  I'd go here: http://www.gpg4win.org/documentation.html  That is excellent documentation, and from that site you can download and install everything you need, and then practice up a bit.  You will download Kleopatra--or PGA--and then you will make a matching key pair, which includes your private key, which is for you only, and your public key, which you can give to anybody, so they can encrypt messages to you.

Obviously it would take pages to explain it all, so this should get you started, and after a day or so of study, you'll get a better grasp of this.  Don't get discouraged, most of us have had to do the same thing, and, if people see you are studying and trying, then they will be much better about answering the many questions you probably will have.

Best wishes.
