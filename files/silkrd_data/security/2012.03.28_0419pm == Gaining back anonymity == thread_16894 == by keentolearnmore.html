As far as having purchased Bitcoins with your own bank account you haven't done anything illegal.  But it's easy to trace the path of those Bitcoins directly from your Bitcoin exchange account to SR.  So if someone does a little investigating it can be seen that a person with your name, bank account number, and address sent Bitcoins to SR.  But... all is not lost, there are things for sale on SR that are perfectly legal, like art, and computer accessories. 

"So if I have my packet delivered to a friend's house is it still relatively safe to go through with an order?" 
This is about the only way you can safely proceed.  Your address may be flagged just to see if illegal substances are being sent to you.  If so, you'll have a hard time denying it's yours, since the fact that you sent Bitcoins to SR is known.  Use PGP when communicating with sellers, and have your purchases sent somewhere else than the address associated with your bank account.
