Title: Re: Shipping to Biz
Post by: CrunchyFrog on November 16, 2011, 01:40 am
I'd guess personal use amounts wouldn't be a problem.  If it's more than personal use, you could be at risk of having your business assets (if any) -- equipment, accounts -- seized in the event of a bust.  Having it addressed the same as the mail you already receive at the business would arouse the least suspicion.  Although, I'm not keen on the idea of having employees (if any) in the loop; they could inadvertently sign for something they shouldn't or open the parcel "for you" for some reason.

(Disclosure: I'm neither a lawyer, employer, nor business owner.)
