Title: Re: Intersango!
Post by: Santander on September 18, 2012, 04:34 pm
Doing a bank transfer is not a good way of buying btc as it is not anonymous.

A generally accepted way of buying btc anonymously in the UK right now is:

Register for MTGox with an anonymous email address (tormail.org). Don't use Tor to connect to MT Gox, they don't allow that. They may freeze your account and it adds unnecessary attention. Goto Starbucks and do it over a normal internet connection.

You don't need to "verify" your MT Gox account when it asks. Make your default currency GBP when signing up.

Within MT Gox account, goto "Funding Options" and copy down the details for a UK bank deposit (account holders name, sort code, account number and your accounts reference code).

Put on a hat and goto your nearest Barclays and pick up a pay-in envelope and the little slip that goes inside it. Fill it out with the MTGox details, stick the cash in, put the envelope into the quick pay in box. You can take the envelope and slip away and fill it out elsewhere if you wish. The slips asks for the name of the person who paid it in. Either leave this blank or put a fake name. It's not required.

Shortly you will have cash in your MT Gox account anonymously. Go to a free wifi place again - Costa this time (randomness is good), log in to MT Gox and buy bitcoins with the cash.

Then it's mixing time.

Good luck
