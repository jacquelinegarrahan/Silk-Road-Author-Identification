Title: Re: how do i purchase bit coins?
Post by: Hamb999 on August 20, 2012, 09:09 pm
I'm going to assume you are in the USA.  Here is how I fund my account.  This is just how I do it and I find it easy and within my comfort zone of anonymity.  No ID needed and I give a fake name, but I'm sure I'm on camera in the CVS.

Create a MtGox.com account
When logged in to MtGox, select funding options under the Buy & Sell along the left side.
This will take you to the Add Funds page
At the 'Choose Funding Options' pick BitInstant
Write down your Exchange Account and follow link to BitInstant
At BitInstant.com, choose CVS (or 7-11 or Walmart, etc.) the Pay From drop down box
Leave the Pay To box as Mt. Gox (unless you have another BTC wallet)
Be doubly sure you put the correct Exchange Account
Enter the amount you would like to send
Push send and you will be taken to the ZipZap website
At the bottom of the page, choose Create Payment Slip (the Payment Slip is a bill that can be paid at any MoneyGram location)

Go to CVS
Pick up the red phone.
Enter invoice info from my Payment Slip.
Had some trouble entering the amount.  Enter the whole dollars, then push *, then cents, then push #.
Gave them a fake name, address and phone number.
Go to the CVS counter and tell them you need to pay an overdue bill today.
Clerk will ask you if you are Mr. Dimmydummydummerdorf.  Say yes.
If the clerk asks for a CVS rewards card, decline
Give her the cash - have exact change ready.
Sign the receipt - scribble it.
Given a receipt.

By the time I get back to my office, a Bitinstant email is usually waiting.  It shows up in my Mt. Gox account within a few hours.

I like the fact that the printout says it is a bill payment.  The lady at CVS didn't bat an eye.  She seemed familiar with ZipZap, so maybe people use it regularly for bill payments.

Any moneygram location will do, so I can mix it up.
