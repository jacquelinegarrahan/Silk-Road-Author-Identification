What are your asking for is kinda of hard to sum up in a post. I'm in the same position as you. All I can tell you is do alot of research and creative thinking. Once you think you have everything figured out then go back  and start from scratch because you probably left something out.

In terms of cashing out Bitcoins every vendor has their own methods and there's no right or wrong way to do. There is however a stupid way which is to cash out by using your bank to accept the deposits. That's just asking for trouble.
