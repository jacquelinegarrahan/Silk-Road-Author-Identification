Title: Re: Bitcoins via wire -> blockchain
Post by: acider on January 08, 2013, 04:17 pm
Yes you can.  By wire you mean bank wire transfer right? That's the way a lot of people do it. You can send them anonymously to SR or use another wallet between like instawallet (use it behind tor). You can send them directly to SR, but the mixer usually breaks the total amount in smaller and does many transactions, while SR is sometimes a pain to get a deposit (takes some time) so I would prefer sending them first to instawallet and then to SR with one big clean transaction  ;D
