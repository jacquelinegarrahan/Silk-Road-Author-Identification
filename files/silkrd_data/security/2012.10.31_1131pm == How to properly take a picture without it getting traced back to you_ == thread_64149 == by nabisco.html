Title: Re: How to properly take a picture without it getting traced back to you?
Post by: Reece on November 01, 2012, 01:41 am
First, after taking the picture and placing it on your computer, delete meta-data associated with it. (Google)
Then you must upload to an anonymous upload service that doesn't record your IP (couple TOR hidden services provide this).

As far as the taking of the picture, do so against a white wall. If paranoid, take picture against a different colored/textured background such as taking a picture of your product with a poster board behind it.
