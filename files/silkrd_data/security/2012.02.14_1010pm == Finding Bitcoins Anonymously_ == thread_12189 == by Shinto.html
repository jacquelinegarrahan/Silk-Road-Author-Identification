Title: Finding Bitcoins Anonymously?
Post by: Shinto on February 14, 2012, 10:10 pm
I don't know if you've noticed, but this topic may seem trivial to those whom already have a plan that effectively works when getting bitcoins to Silk Road anonymously. So, for those of use with minor experience or none at all when it comes to buying said bitcoins, it can be a frightening concept to grasp. (With anonymity that is...)

Obviously buying bitcoins is easy and simple for anyone to do if you choose to reveal your identity.
However, we (buyers & sellers) don't want to do so without anonymity.

So when it comes down to it, you want to have a sort of split personality. One for the innocent you that only websurfs and and does minor actions on a computer, spending a little money online (legitimately). Then the SR you that seems to be a complete different person that cannot be backtracked or traced.
The innocent you does not matter, as long as you stay clear of anything illegal.
The SR you needs 110% anonymity to seem like a different person. To do so we use TOR, proxies, passphrases, encryptions, e-wallets, hidden addresses, and much more.

Some of us are new to the online game of being anonymous with money. It is completely different than just being anonymous alone. When involving legal tender of a major leading government, it can be a bit intimidating to those who have only run the streets before.

Well, to not make this any longer I'll ask what you do. From point A to eventually point Z.
How do you go about being completely anonymous?
How do you have your comp setup?
What software and/or hardware do you use?
What services do you use and if any different than the norm, how?
Share your complete plan of anonymity here for all those who are new to this. Lend a helping hand and expand.

The future is Silk Road my friends, let's treat it with care.  ;)
