Title: Forums Online Status
Post by: abuttelmao on January 24, 2012, 03:01 am
This may seem small and unimportant to some, but when it comes to security, every little bit counts, right?

I've noticed that most people let the forums show when they're online - you can look at the bottom of the main page of the forums and see all the online users, and when you click on someone's forum profile it will show them as online.

This seems a bit unsafe to me - it makes linking a tor client to an SR username that much easier for LE.  Because of this I've disabled the forums showing my online status.  If you're wondering how to do this, go to Profile > Accounts Settings and uncheck the "Show others my online status" box.
