Title: Re: Electrum wallet help needed
Post by: eddiethegun on July 15, 2013, 05:09 pm
Click the little triangle next to Main Account to drop down the receiving addresses. Use the first one listed and use a new one each time you do a transaction.

Make sure electrum is connecting to the network (otherwise you won't be seeing the new coins in your wallet).

And don't forget to write down or (preferably) memorize your seed phrase. That is your backup.
