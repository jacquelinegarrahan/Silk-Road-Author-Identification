At the risk of revealing my status as an old fart, I'll never understand this literal infatuation between the younger generation and their phones.

Maybe it comes from growing up when there were no mobile phones, I don't know. It has actually been reported that officers are using these to image phones as the result of traffic stops.

As far as this old fart is concerned, your secure communications should be conducted at home, where the cops would need a warrant to enter.
