Try sending an email to Trustcash, give them your transaction number and the amount and as much info as you can. I have not been able to use the service myself - different bank, but I had a similar problem when I first tried. They are the people that handle the bank deposit and pass it on to Bitinstant, so they should be able to tell you more. Your order will not be lost, since they will have a copy of the details.
As for the second point, I have seen  the maximum time of 2 hours quoted many times, after which you should start to panic. (Well don't panic, but time to start asking questions)
