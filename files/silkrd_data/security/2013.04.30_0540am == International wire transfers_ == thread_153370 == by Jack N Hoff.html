Outgoing transfers will ALWAYS require an ID. Otherwise some bloke could just run into the bank pretending to be you and initiate a transfer anywhere. Wire transfers internationally are also a huge red flag for banks as it is the primary means of fraudsters who steal bank info online getting their hands on cash.  You need an ID to even get a balance inquiry at 99% of banks nowadays.
