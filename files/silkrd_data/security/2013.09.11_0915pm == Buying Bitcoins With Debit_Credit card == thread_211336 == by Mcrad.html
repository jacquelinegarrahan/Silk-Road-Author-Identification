Title: Re: Buying Bitcoins With Debit/Credit card
Post by: mmmmonkey on September 11, 2013, 10:10 pm
You're limited to the amount you can buy but here are the two fastest services that I use.

btcquick.com - lets you buy bitcoins from your google wallet (usually credit card attached to account). Initially it'll let you buy only $250 worth of coins, but it's done and sent to my SR wallet within an hour usually. Once you reach a certain number of transactions, you will be upgraded to a higher level account and you can buy more.

quickbt.com - lets you buy up to 0.3/0.4 of bitcoins through online banking. I think this may only be a Canadian thing though.

Just stock up and let them sit until you have enough. I can't say which place is best for larger bulk amounts though. Cavirtex (who I was doing business with before) has made it so difficult to get bitcoins fast (usually having to wait at least 4-5 business days now) that I don't deal with them anymore.
