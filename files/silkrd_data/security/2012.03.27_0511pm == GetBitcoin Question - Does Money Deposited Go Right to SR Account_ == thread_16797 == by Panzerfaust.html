Title: Re: GetBitcoin Question - Does Money Deposited Go Right to SR Account?
Post by: Diamond on March 27, 2012, 05:41 pm
Forgive me if I've read this wrong, but you might wish to familiarize yourself with bitcoin and how it works before sending anyone any money.

When you send funds to get-bitcoin, you also give them bitcoin address in which to deposit your coins. This can be any address that you control. For instance:
 - An address in your Bitcoin software on your machine
 - An e-wallet, like Instawallet
 - A receiving address from your SR account
 - A receiving address from your Dwolla or MtGox account (I'm not that familiar with services like that, but I assume they work like any other service, providing you with a bitcoin receiving address)

You CAN give them the receiving address from your SR account and the coins will go right there, but that's not recommended. You will want to have them sent somewhere else first and then do some mixing to enhance anonymity.
