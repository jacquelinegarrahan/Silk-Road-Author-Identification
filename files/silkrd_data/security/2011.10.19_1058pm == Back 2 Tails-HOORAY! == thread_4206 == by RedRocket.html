Post by: CaptainSensible on October 22, 2011, 08:05 pm
Easier is not always better.  "...tails is the only distro advertised on the tor website...so that must say something."  Yeah, it says the Tails people work with the Tor people. 

Look over the Liberte Linux site carefully, click on many of the links the developer, Maxim Kammerer, has listed.  He references many of the known security flaws of Tails, and if you're concerned with staying as anonymous as you can on the Internet you want a product developed by someone who recognizes the anonymity problems with both Tails and the standard Tor browser bundle. 

Read the bottom part of the page at http://dee.su/liberte -- the section marked Privacy-Enhancing Live Distribution.  It's all about how Liberte addresses many of the security flaws in Tails. 

Use the best tools you can when you don't want your activities on the Internet to be tracked
