Title: Re: Easier/Quicker Way To Get Bitcoins Without Going To Bank?...
Post by: Silver on August 15, 2012, 02:07 pm
Plenty of ways, few if any of which are anonymous and none of which are really worth the risk.

The whole point of cash over the counter is that it is anonymous. Don't need to give a name, contact details, anything, just tell them this money goes to this account for....whatever your imagination can come up with. If they question it, "I dunno, I'm just running errands for a friend."

Bank transfers, Paypal, credit card and the like all leave a paper trail. If your bitcoins are traced through the network to or from SR, your name will be on the other end of that trail. Game over.
