Title: Re: How do you use your moneypak?
Post by: scout on February 23, 2013, 05:43 am
Are you saying you want to become a vendor here and accept MP as payment for your products/services?  If that's what you're saying, that wouldn't work - unless you are a bitcoin vendor, you cannot accept other forms of payment.  If you did, then those buyers wouldn't be able to use the escrow service  and wouldn't be protected.  And if you, the vendor, were to be scammed, SR support wouldn't be able to do anything to help you because they do not support out-of-escrow dealings.

Post by: sleepyeyes2k2 on February 24, 2013, 08:41 pm
Transfer to PayPal.  PayPal can verify with a MoneyPak, and can deposit funds to it.  So, just find one of the many ways to turn BTCs into Paypal currency, and you're golden.

Make sure it's a stealth account (PP).  And, use that MoneyPak to verify, and nothing else.  Nothing linking you to the account.  And, if you don't know how to proxychain a socks5, you're going to screw up the anonymity side of this, anyway.  So, just make sure the BTCs aren't "dirty."  Forget what I said about "stealth account" unless you know how to anonymize your IP address.  Don't ask; there are wikis for that.
