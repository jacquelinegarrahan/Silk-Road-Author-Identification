Try localbitcoins.com. Some of the established sellers on there will let you lodge money to their bank account, which means you can stay anonymous by giving a fake name at the bank.

I set up my localbitcoin.com account from an internet cafe, made contact with a seller, got his bank details, lodged the money using a fake name, sent the btc seller a message saying money was in his account, then he released the bitcoins to my local bitcoins account. I transfer it from there to SR, all from an internet cafe, keeping me completely anonymous!

Hope this helps ;)
