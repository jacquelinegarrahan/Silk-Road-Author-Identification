Post by: Baraka on May 16, 2013, 09:38 am
Five, simple points:

#1. If anyone other than you has physical access to your computer, always use whole disk encryption (like TrueCrypt) with a strong passphrase. ALWAYS.
#2. Using anything other than a strong magnetic field to try to erase or kill a hard disk is a waste of time. Three words: large, neodymium magnet. Just watch your fingers.
#3. If you have Tor installed, but no bookmarks, use Tormail for e-mail and no one knows your passwords, you most likely have nothing to worry about here.
#4. If #3 is right, then you can either do nothing, or report your computer stolen. If LE initiates a search on your computer without establishing probable cause, most of the evidence can be tossed.
#5. Never try to negotiate with a psycho ex-girlfriend.
