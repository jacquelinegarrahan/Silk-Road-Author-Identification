I would say never meet anyone in person. If you wanted to do something locally, it seems like you'd want to do some sort of pickup. Receive half (if trusted) or all (if not trusted) the BTC, go plant the package somewhere, leave detailed instructions PGP after you're clear, and have them pickup and pay remainder, if any.
