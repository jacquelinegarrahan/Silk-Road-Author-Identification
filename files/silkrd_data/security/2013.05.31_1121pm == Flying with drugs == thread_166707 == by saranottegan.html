I've flown with personal stashes regularly and never had a problem. As long as the quantity is closer to personal than distributable I don't think there's much to worry about: most frequent business travelers are carrying at least one or two prescriptions, as well as a mountain of favored toiletries, and x-rays can't tell the difference between a pot of eyeshadow and any other sort of powder.
