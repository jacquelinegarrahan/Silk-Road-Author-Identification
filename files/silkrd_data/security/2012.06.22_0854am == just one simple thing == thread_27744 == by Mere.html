I read a lot of the security threads as often as I can and I just realized I haven't seen anyone ever mention this simple advice.

Always tip your mailman around the holidays.

It might not make a major difference, but it's just a good idea to have the guy that delivers your gear to think the best of you.

I grew up with a mailman as my Dad. I heard stories growing up. The mailman knows more about you then you could even realize. Let him know you appreciate him/her. A card is not enough, put a $20 in there.   :o
