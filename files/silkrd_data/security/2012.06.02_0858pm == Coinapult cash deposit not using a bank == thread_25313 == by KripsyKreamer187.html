Title: Re: Coinapult cash deposit not using a bank
Post by: nimbus on June 02, 2012, 09:24 pm
Yes all those other options like Walmart, CVS, etc are MoneyGram. After setting up your order at BitInstant, you go and fill out a send money form using a fake name if you want, and include the business name/account number(s) you will get during the order process, deposit whatever the amount is (stay under $900 to avoid them wanting ID), wait while they enter all the info, and you're done. Coinapult will email whatever email you provided to BitInstant within a matter of minutes, with a link in it that will take you to a page where you can send your coins to any other BTC address. Remember to write down your 4-digit PIN too.
