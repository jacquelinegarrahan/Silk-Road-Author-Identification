I should imagine it's down to a couple of things. 1) AFAIK the SR BTC price is aggregated from a couple of sources (at least, I'm sure I read that when there was an announcement that they'd stop using one of the market's prices, MTGox I think), so the average is then bought down by cheap rpices on some other market, maybe not in the UK. 2) The SR exchange rate is not (again, AFAIK) 'live' and constantly being adjusted in response to instantaneous changes, so there is likely to be a lag between market prices and SR prices. 3) There seems to have been some leaning on UK markets, at least that's the impression I get by the removal of some of them (like the removal of any way to buy from the UK through Blockchain), which creates a 'sellers market' I guess....
