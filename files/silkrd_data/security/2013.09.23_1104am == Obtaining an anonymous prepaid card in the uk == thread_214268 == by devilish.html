Title: Re: Obtaining an anonymous prepaid card in the uk
Post by: This_is_not_SOCA on September 23, 2013, 07:35 pm
You sirs are looking for the IDT Prime pre-paid Mastercard. Available from multiple outlets for a fee of £5. Can be topped up anywhere there is a Paypoint sign.

They can be registered online at which point you provide a name and address, if you like.

Be aware there are piss-taking charges involved whenever you spend cash that you have loaded on the card, the same piss-taking charges are also applied for NOT spending the cash you have loaded on the card. That said they are as anonymous as it gets - obviously CCTV in the newsagent where you buy them is a potential issue as is the place you top them up.

Title: Re: Obtaining an anonymous prepaid card in the uk
Post by: This_is_not_SOCA on October 02, 2013, 10:09 am
No problems. yes you can register anything you like.

Remember though that some payment processors (like Worldpay for instance) will do a bunch of checks when you make an online payment one of which will be to check the address exists and perhaps even to see if the name and address you supply can be correlated with other records such as electoral role or other credit cards etc. They then provide a risk rating on the transaction to the vendor. Just use a valid name and address in that case.
