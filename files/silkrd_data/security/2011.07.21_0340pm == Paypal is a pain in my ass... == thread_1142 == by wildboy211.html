Post by: CaptainSensible on July 21, 2011, 08:22 pm
The issue of buying bitcoins with Paypal has come up in a few other forums.  Since you've said Paypal will still let you send money to another person, you have a way to buy bitcoins.  Sites like Bitmarket.eu let people buy & sell bitcoins; many sellers accept Paypal. 

Here's how it works: you buy your Bitcoins on Bitmarket.eu and pay for them by quietly sending the seller payment through Paypal.  You use the "Send Money" option on Paypal, and NOWHERE do you indicate that the payment is for bitcoins.  Under the "Send Money" tab you check the box for "personal payment" (I think that's what it's called).

Your transaction record will look something like this: "Personal payment of $60.00 made to SomeGuy@gmail.com"

I don't like Paypal's restrictions either, but since you have money tied up there you may as well use it.
