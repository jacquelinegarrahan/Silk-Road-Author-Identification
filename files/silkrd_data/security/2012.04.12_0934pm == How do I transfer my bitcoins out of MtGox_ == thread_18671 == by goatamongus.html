Hi goatamongus,

I don't blame you for the confusion as the "withdrawal" bit for MtGox is actually hidden away!

First go to your SR account and find the ID for your wallet there, copy and paste into a text document.

Next log in to your MtGox account (do not use Tor for this) - Click "Trade" then "Funding Options" then "Withdraw Coins." Under the drop down menu choose "Bitcoins" as the Withdrawal method and then paste the address for your SR wallet in the box next to "Bitcoin Address". Finally enter how many Bitcoins you want to transfer over to your SR account and click go.

Anything else you need, please feel free to get in touch!

V.
