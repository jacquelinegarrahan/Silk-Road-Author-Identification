Hi willwham1,

Yes, I'm afraid buybitcoin.com is a less than reputable site. Most people get scammed outright, and those that don't get charged an astronomic rate for their Bitcoin.

People need to do their due diligence before buying Bitcoin - here's a link (clearnet) to an article about Bruce Wagner, the owner of buybitcoin.com, which you would have found with a quick Google search: http://www.metafilter.com/107065/Bruce-Wagner-and-the-Bitcoin-Scam

A simple search here on the forum for "buybitcoin.com" would also have saved you from getting scammed by Mr. Wagner. Always always ALWAYS do your research before making any transaction where you send your money first, whether it's for Bitcoin or anything else. You're essentially trusting a complete stranger with your money. If you wouldn't do that with a stranger on the street, why would you do it over the internet?!

The mind boggles.
