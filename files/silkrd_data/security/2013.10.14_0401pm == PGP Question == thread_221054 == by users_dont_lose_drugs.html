GPG4USB has two main panes. The editor pane is on the left, while the keyring pane is on the right. Choose your key from the right-hand pane, and right-lick on your key. Choose append selected keys to text, and your public key will appear in the left-hand editor window. In GPG4USB, you can distinguish your keys (i.e. ones for which you have the private key) by the fact that your keys will have a little gold and silver key between the checkbox and the key name.

Hope this helps.
