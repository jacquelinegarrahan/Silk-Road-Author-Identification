You can get bitcoins ananymously in the UK one of two ways.

Mtgox - set up an account on mt gox under an assumed name. Make a cash deposit at a Barclays bank directly to Mt gox, takes up to 24 hour then your funds will be available in your mt gox account.

Intersango no longer allows anonymous cash deposits.

Finally you can buy bitcoins directly from vlad1m1r, you send him cash in the post and he sends you bitcoins anonymously. Vlad1m1r is pretty active on the forums and has listings on SR.
