Post by: Chip Douglas on July 08, 2013, 11:36 am
LocalBitcoins.com is a very good option. It took a while for me to get the hang of it, as with most things. (AADHD-Pi)

Since most of you probably are comfortable with BoA deposits, like BST does. You can usually find at least one vendor doing BoA deposits, as well as Wells Fargo, and Citibank.

I paid $9 over the going rate per coin, but it was worth it to me. You may find better deals. Also there's the 'meet in person' option.

*Hint - if you don't see any BoA 's or other banks you may want on that first list that goes by your zip code, click the link under the last listing that says, "Show All In USD"  and then you'll get a much longer list. It doesn't matter what zip code when you think about it, because you're doing a bank deposit.

They have ratings next to sellers just like on SR+Ebay. - It's actually a very good site. - No need for BST anymore. Not for me anyway.

Also wasn't BST always closed on Mondays? Either way. LocalBitcoins is it for me. You can message back and forth with the seller to clarify any special requests they might have. - Though the one I found was easier than any other btc purchase I've made, and I've been buying on the road since April 2012.

Good Luck all. - Hope this helps
