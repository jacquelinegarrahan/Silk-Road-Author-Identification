A Tormail account is exactly like a normal webmail account like Hotmail, Gmail or Yahoo. You can send or receive email from within Tormail to/from other Tormail users, and you can also do the same to normal clearnet email addresses.

-- Some general Tormail advice --

However, there is an issue like has already been mentioned. You shouldn't trust the Tormail operators as a matter of principal. Could be they are nice guys, but who really can know.

You should use PGP when possible, and also sometimes you should setup Tormail accounts for a single use (to prevent network analysis).

Use the non javascript version of Tormail, turn off Javascript and use Squirrel Mail version.

To switch between Tormail accounts, you should logout, switch your Tor identity and clear the Tormail cookie from your browser. Then wait a few minutes before logging in. This prevents association of Tormail account ownership, or at the least it prevents it being used as circumstantial evidence in a court of law.
