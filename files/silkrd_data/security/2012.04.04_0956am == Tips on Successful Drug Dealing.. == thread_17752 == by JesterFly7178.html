Post by: Raoul Duke on April 04, 2012, 10:18 am
dealing IRL? a few rules i wouldn't break when i dealt. most are obvious but still doesn't hurt pointing them out. i used to sell coke and weed:

1. don't deal from your house, car or your local pub/hangout - always get out of your hood. customers homes is usually best if you know them. if you don't know them take a big mate with you.
2. don't deal with idiots - any loud mouth muppet with a total disregard to subtlety is near enough certain to get you pinched
3. get a throwaway mobile phone for your customers. change it every 6 months or so. NEVER take 2 phones out with you - you get caught with 2 phones while you're carrying and you are fucked. throw away the whole phone as well, don't just get a new sim and use the same handset - you can get caught out on the IMEI number. turn your phone off when you can't be bothered to sell anything. this is especially true if you are selling coke.
4. once you have a customer base you are happy with do not take on any more customers unless they can be vouched for by 2 people you know well
5. don't arrange to meet 2 different customers at the same time and same place.
6. don't go on deals stoned - paranoia is a bitch that being stoned does not help.
7. when getting to meets park up at least a block away and walk the last bit - you don't need your car getting recognised.
8. don't tic. to ANYONE. mates are more of a pain in the arse to get cash off than customers.
9. if you have been given a bad batch by your supplier take it back as soon as you find out - you don't need your product being given a bad name.
10. when going to a strange place for a meet turn up early and case the place to make sure you aren't walking into a robbery/sting. stay out of sight until your meet turns up to make sure they don't have a couple of heavies with them to do you over. also keep an eye in the sky for CCTV
11. don't underestimate the 'enemy' - it's only arrogant idiots that think the police don't know what to look out for
12. don't keep anything at your house - i used to pay a mate to keep my stash in a locked box round their house.
13. don't sell short weights
14. don't be late to meets. if you are going to be late send your customer a message as soon as you know you aren't going to be on time
15. make up a nickname for yourself just for your customers, just in case any of them take it upon themselves to start asking around after you
16. probably most important - if you go to a meet and something doesn't feel right GET THE FUCK OUT OF THERE.

and by the way - it's normal after dealing for a few months to shit yourself every time you see the polizei ;)

i never broke any of the above rules and i managed to deal for a good 8 or 9 years. never seen the inside of a police cell :)
