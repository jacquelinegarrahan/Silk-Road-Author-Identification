Bulk is riskier.  If all you're ordering is small amounts for personal use, you're more likely to end up with only a love letter than the sort of major investigation a bulk interception could trigger. Also, as long as you're padding your mail box with dozens of other uninteresting packages, a few deliveries a week from SR won't stand out.

Imo the safest route is to stay small, and make sure your mail always has at least a package or two (or more) waiting. If you're constantly receiving small items from eBay or Etsy or whatever anyway, any illicit packages won't stand out so much.
