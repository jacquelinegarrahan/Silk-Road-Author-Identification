Title: Re: Depositing into MtGox via Bank Transfer
Post by: dudeguy551 on April 17, 2012, 05:11 am
Log onto Tor.

Go to bitinstant and start cash deposit at bank order.

Find the bank near you at their cash processor, not too near.

Present the teller the letter and EXACT cash.

By the time you get home you should have email stating the order is completed, it usually completes in 30 min. Otherwise call from a payphone or just your cellphone if you aren't paranoid.

CONNECT to instawallet.org and copy down the URL. Do this in your clipboard and on paper, once you lose the URL you lose your coins.
ALSO copy down the bitcoin address, on paper.

Log OFF tor.

Connect to Mt.Gox and you will have the USD, make a trade for bitcoins. I always wait for a good deal.

When completed, send the BTC to the instawallet bitcoin address. DO NOT CHECK IT YET. Verify on MT.GOX.

EXIT browser.

LOG ON tor.

Check instawallet and proceed. Otherwise, wait ten minutes, if no BTC has arrived, log off tor, onto Mt.Gox and contact support. Never log on to that instawallet with your real ip.

Wait an hour or five for the BTC to go into the instawallet and then transfer to SR bitcoin address.
(If it's a large sum of BTC, separate into several random amounts into other instawallets, write down URLs and BTC addresses, then repeat for SR)

Enjoy.
