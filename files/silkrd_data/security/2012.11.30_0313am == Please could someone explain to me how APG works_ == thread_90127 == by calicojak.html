Post by: Party Girl on December 01, 2012, 01:42 am
Just Google:  "apg encryption" and start learning.   (About 285,000 results (0.42 seconds)    ;)

The secret key decrypts messages sent to you using YOUR public key which you must provide to another party in order for encryption to work.  If you want to send an encrypted message back, you will need their public key so they can decrypt it on their end.
