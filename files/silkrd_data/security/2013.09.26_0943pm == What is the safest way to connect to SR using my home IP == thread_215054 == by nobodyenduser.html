Simply using the latest version of Tails should be enough if you only purchase small amounts. You should be more conerned about the security/stealthyness of the seller. When his packages smell like a cannabis plantation then that would be a problem.
The connection between you and the Tor entry node is encrypted anyway, so as far as I know they won't even know what you do with Tor. They just see that you use Tor, which is not so unusual these days.

If you want to hide that you use Tor, then you could tunnel it through a VPN in a country where the cables are not monitored by your adversary, and whose secret services don't automatically share data with your adversary.
