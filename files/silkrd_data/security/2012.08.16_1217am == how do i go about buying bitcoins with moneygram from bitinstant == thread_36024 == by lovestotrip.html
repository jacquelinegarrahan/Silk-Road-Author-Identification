Bitinstant doesn't require any info except a bitcoin address and email address.   Here's how I do it, not saying it's the right way:

1.   Order bitcoins through bitinstant with MoneyGram (SR bitcoin address, get a new one each time, and my tormail address).
2.   Go to moneygram place that has tellers, or go to a 7-11 that has one of those fancy looking ATM's with a receiver.   Either way you'll talk to a real person.   Give them a fake name, address, and phone number (i actually use a real phone number but that phone is not tied to my real identity.   Moneygram has never called)..
3.   Bitcoins show up in your SR account a few hours later.   Yay, time to buy some droogz!!  :p
