Well theres the feedback system to help you find a trusted vendor. If the site was fully compromised this would be a moot point however, but look beyond the words. Look for feedback using varied slang/abbrevs/dialect as spoofed feedback would likely have similar wording on all posts.

Also the more you can distance yourself from your own address the better. Use public wifi, or crack some wep if youre tech savvy. If possible deliver to a friend or neighbor and have them bring it to you. 
I mean its a risky endeavour any way you look at it but unless they can prove beyond reasonable doubt its you, which is expensive and time consuming when cryptography is involved, youll be ok.  Keep your orders small and low key items until youre confident.
