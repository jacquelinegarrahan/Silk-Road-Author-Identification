Title: Re: Question about new bitcoin address ...
Post by: Raoul Duke on June 04, 2013, 12:46 pm
not sure about the history of addresses, i don't think so, i would imagine only you and SR admins know the history.

the block chain itself is public, so anyone can see what transactions are going on. it's a pain in the arse to follow the trail but it's doable. that's why people use tumblers and bitcoin fog.

SR supposedly has a tumbler built into the deposit bit, so your coins get jumbled with a load of other peoples before they hit their final destination (your wallet), in effect making it impossible to trace your deposit to your account. they would be able to see it was sent to a Silk Road btc wallet address, but not where it ended up in SR
