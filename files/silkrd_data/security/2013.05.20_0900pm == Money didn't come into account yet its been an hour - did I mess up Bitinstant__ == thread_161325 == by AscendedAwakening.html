Unlike a lot of other users, I have never had a problem with BitInstant. If you have to fill out the MG form, the account number is the actual account number you are given on your order form. If you are doing a deposit to a bitcoin address, it can take up to 24 hours like they say (never been that long in my experience) - but, if you didn't use a bogus email, check for your confirmation. Zipzap and Bitinstant will send you confirmations when everything goes through and is processing.

When I deposit directly to my personal wallet, it's literally within 60 minutes, but if you deposited directly to SR (not recommended) it can take longer.
