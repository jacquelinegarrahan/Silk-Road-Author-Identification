I would say an ACH transfer is not nearly as ideal as a cash deposit or payment since it would definitely allow tracing the deposit back to your account. But that's unlikely to happen unless somehow the BTC gets traced back to the seller, who then says, well I sold them to this cat on localbitcoin, then they trace it back to you through the ACH transfer.
