Title: Re: TAILS help
Post by: Raoul Duke on July 01, 2013, 11:49 am
the F8 key gets you to WINDOWS boot options - you need to enter the BIOS before that. depending on the make and model of the PC it's usually F1, F2, F10 or Del, or some have the options to hit Enter to 'enable temporary boot device'. as soon as you turn the machine on it should have 'Hit XX key to enter startup options/BIOS' or something along those lines, usually just under the bios version or the corner of the screen. once in the proper bios you should have 'boot options' where you can choose what boots first - this is where you add your USB stick as the top boot option

Hope this helps! come back to me with the make and model of the PC if you like, i should be able to find which key you need to press to enter the bios
