Post by: CaptainSensible on February 23, 2012, 08:12 pm
Can't do it via ATM, but there should not be an age requirement.  All you're doing is making a cash deposit to the account of Get-Bitcoins.  Last time I did it I just walked in and laid down my money with the deposit slip.  No ID required. 

Hint: to speed things along I scanned a copy of the deposit receipt and attached it to an e-mail to Get-Bitcoins.  That way they have more proof that I deposited the cash.  Last time I used Get-Bitcoins there was no confirmation e-mail from them and it took almost a day before I saw my Bitcoins.  A little nerve wracking but it all worked out in the end.
