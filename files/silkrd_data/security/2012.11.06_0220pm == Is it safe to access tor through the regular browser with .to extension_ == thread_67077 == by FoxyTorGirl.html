Unsafe.

The owners of onion.to can read all of your traffic as cleartext and know your real ip (if you use a normal Browser). It basically breaks everything Tor offers. If you have logged in to Silk Road via onion.to, i recommend you change your password.

But if you are just reading SR forums with it you should be fine.
