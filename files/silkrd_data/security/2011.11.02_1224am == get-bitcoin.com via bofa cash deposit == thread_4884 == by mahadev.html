Hey don't worry! I did a BoA deposit for get-bitcoin earier today. The information that their website doesn't give you is that you need to use an out of state deposit slip (unless you're in CT) and it's a CT savings account, so you need to mark that on the form. I didn't have to show ANY form of ID, and the attendant did not bat an eye, I think this is a common thing for the cashiers to process.

Again, they never asked for any form of ID and there was absolutely no fee.
