As much as I like to encourage people to use PGP, I must ask: Why didn't you have this up and working already before you set up shop?  Is your packaging also going to be arranged at the last minute as well?

Ditch GPG4WIN, and use GPG4USB instead. For the record, your ostensible buyer cannot send you encrypted messages without your PGP key.  Nor can you send encrypted messages to them without their PGP key.

Read the documentation at the GPG4USB site: http://gpg4usb.cpunk.de/index.html
