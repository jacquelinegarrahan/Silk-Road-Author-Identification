Title: Re: BTC & Paypal
Post by: Bitshopper on November 23, 2011, 09:32 am
It makes absolutely NO sense to sell bitcoins for Paypal. Paypal does not approve of Bitcoin sales using their service. Paypal will allow the buyer to get a refund for up to 180 days. That means whoever sent you bitcoins has to worry for a solid half of a year that you're going to scam them and ask for a refund for something you already received. Paypal will side with you and the person who sold you the bitcoins will have been successfully scammed.

This is why you can't use credit cards to buy bitcoins either. Bitcoins are non-reversible transactions. Paypal and CC are reversible.
