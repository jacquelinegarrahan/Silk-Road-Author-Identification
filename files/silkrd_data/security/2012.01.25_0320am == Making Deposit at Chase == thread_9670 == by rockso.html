While I was typing this exodusultima made a post, but since I already typed it enjoy :)

I used get-bitcoin the first time I bought bitcoins and was very impressed with there service; I opted for the Bank of America deposit method although I assure you the process is the same despite the different bank (which I did not have an account with btw). Basically when you place an order at get-bitcoin they send you an account number to make the deposit to when you make your trip at Chase. At Chase you fill out a despot slip, take it to the teller, hand her the money, she gives you a receipt and you walk out. Next you either scan or take a picture of the receipt the teller at the bank gave you and send it to the email address provided to you by get-bitcoin. It is incredibly simple and pain free, I should also point out that no ID is required for making a deposit.

As for the address where you want to get the BTC sent to, go download the bitcoin wallet app and use an address you generate there.  This method is much safer and guarantees an extra layer of security for you, ensuring you remain anonymous.  This also ensures you have access to your BTC in case something goes wrong with your SR account.

Let me know if you have any more question. Cheers.
