Yankee - the "clipboard" in cleopatra is your windows clipboard.  when you copy and paste in windows , you're copying to your clipboard, and pasting from your clipboard.

So: type your message in notepad , select all(crtl+a)/copy(crtl+c) - and then right click kleo - clipboard - encrypt - when you're done encrypting, your encrypted message is ready to paste -  crtl+v
