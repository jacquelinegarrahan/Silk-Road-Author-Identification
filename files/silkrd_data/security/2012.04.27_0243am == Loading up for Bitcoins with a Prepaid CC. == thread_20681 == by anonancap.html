Title: Re: Loading up for Bitcoins with a Prepaid CC.
Post by: railingcapz on April 27, 2012, 06:20 am
I too had a look into this, but currently as it stands (due to vast amounts of credit card fraud) 99% of the bitcoin companies will not accept pre-paid credit/debit cards of any form. Most will simply ask for Western Union or a Wire transfer/cash deposit.

For now pre-paid CC's are not used (that I'm aware of). In saying that, going to a bank anywhere in your state, making a cash deposit straight into the BC company account and then tumbling your BC's via wesbites like BitFog (for a maximum 3% fee) will secure your anonymity. Remember - bitcoins are bought for perfectly legal things, the chance of the authorities using cameras to identify your face and then trace your BC's after being tumbled is close to 0. That's my understanding of it anyway.
