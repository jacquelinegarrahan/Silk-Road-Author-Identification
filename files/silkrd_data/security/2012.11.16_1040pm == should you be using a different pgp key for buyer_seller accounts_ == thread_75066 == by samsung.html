The reason you should have two separate accounts with different PGP keys is so that your delivery address isn't associated with a vendor account. If you used the same PGP key then the two accounts could be linked together. However, I doubt any of the vendors you purchased from would ever notice. You just better hope you didn't make a purchase from a LE or compromised vendor account :o

I think it's a very small chance your info has been compromised. However, always use different PGP info in the future  :)
