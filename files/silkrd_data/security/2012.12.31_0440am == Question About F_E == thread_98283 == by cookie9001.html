Title: Re: Question About F/E
Post by: kingdedede on December 31, 2012, 05:22 am
Many people will tell you NOT to FE. I'll be one of those people. As princess said, if you FE, it's at your own risk. You won't be getting any money back if you get scammed.

Don't get me wrong, there are some legit vendors out there who do FE, but there's also other legit Vendors who work within the escrow system. I, and almost every other buyer, will recommend finding a vendor working in escrow because in the long run, it's not worth it to FE.
