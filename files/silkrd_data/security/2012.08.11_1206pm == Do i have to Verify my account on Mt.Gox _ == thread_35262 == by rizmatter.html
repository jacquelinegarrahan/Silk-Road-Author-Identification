Title: Re: Do i have to Verify my account on Mt.Gox ?
Post by: sourman on August 11, 2012, 01:12 pm
If you are using a third party such as bitinstant to fund your mt gox account, you won't need to submit anything unless you're depositing thousands of dollars (or fucked up and used tor to access the account). The account verification thing is usually a problem for users who wish to withdraw lots of btc from mt gox:

"All [withdrawal methods such as sending btc] are subject to a withdrawal limit of 1000 USD per 24hr period and 10000 USD per 30 Day period"

So basically, if you buy a shitload of btc on mt gox, you'll need to verify your account before sending them onto SR or other sources. Otherwise, there shouldn't be any problems with verification.
