Title: Re: Fast, efficient way to transfer credit card to Bitcoin?
Post by: dogmaticCAT on September 02, 2012, 06:14 am
Credit card payments can be subject to a "charge-back" initiated by the cardholder.  If you were a bit-coin issuing authority, you would unnecessarily risk every coin you "sell" to a credit card user, because he could call his card company and issue a charge-back.  The bit-coin issuer could put a hold on the bit-coins, to keep himself safe, but I think 90 days is the window of a charge-back, and that's a stupid long hold - aka not economically feasible to use credit cards due to fraud. Debit/Bank might work, but I'm unaware.
