Just mention the coins not showing up in your wallet, though; he doesn't need to know about the Road part.

I'm sure they also have customer service pages, but the benefit of a complaint on a forum full of BTC customers is that the BitInstant people have a bit of pressure to resolve your issue quickly to reassure other customers.
