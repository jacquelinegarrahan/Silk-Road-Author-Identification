Title: Re: PrivateInternetAccess And Tor
Post by: Baraka on June 14, 2013, 06:40 am
Do you want to run Tor over your VPN? Or VPN over Tor?

Running Tor over VPN requires that you configure your VPN to use UDP. Otherwise Tor will be shit slow because Tor prefers to be tunneled over UDP.

Running VPN over Tor is just the opposite. Tor will only tunnel TCP traffic over it. If you don't configure your VPN to use TCP when tunneling its traffic over your Tor proxy, it won't work at all.

If you trust your VPN (private is top notch) and you've paid for their service with bitcoins then Tor over VPN is best and most versatile. This also has the effect of hiding all of your traffic from your local service provider. If you don't trust your VPN provider then run their service over Tor. It all depends on who you will allow to know your real IP.
