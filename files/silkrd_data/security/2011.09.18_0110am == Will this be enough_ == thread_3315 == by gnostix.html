See if I can answer some/all of this...

1. JAP / HSS / Ultrasurf are all VPN's.
There are pluses and minuses to each, like how Ultrasurf reportedly collects data on you like your IP, which sites you went to, what you clicked, etc.
HSS doesn't record this information but has ads built in (that can be easily blocked with firefox addons) to pay for it since it's a great/fast free service that hides ALL internet activity on your computer, not just your web browser like Ultrasurf and JAP.
JAP (aka JonDo) is a java bundle with its own browser with built-in apps. It has paid service and free service, good but reportedly user limits on free servers so can get crowded and slow.

2. InstaWallet is an eWallet (https://en.bitcoin.it/wiki/EWallet) available at instawallet.org

3. Tumbler --> any type of coin mixing / laundering service, potential fees or commission.
