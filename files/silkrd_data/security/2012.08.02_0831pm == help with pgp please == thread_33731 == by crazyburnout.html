Title: Re: help with pgp please
Post by: keynob on August 02, 2012, 08:51 pm
What are you using, GPG4Win? You should be entering plain text into something that's like notepad, and then clicking on an "Encrypt" button somewhere near the top. Then you select his Public PGP Key, which turns it into said "gibberish". Then just highlight it all, ctl+c and ctl+v it into the PM or email you're sending, and make sure you include your Public PGP key somewhere at the bottom, and you're done. He will reply with a message for you, you'll decrypt it using your Private PGP key.   ...etc, etc.
