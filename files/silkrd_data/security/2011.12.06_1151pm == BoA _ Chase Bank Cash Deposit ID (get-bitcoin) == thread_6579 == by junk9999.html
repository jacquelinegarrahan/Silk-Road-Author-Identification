Title: Re: BoA / Chase Bank Cash Deposit ID (get-bitcoin)
Post by: Dobbs on December 12, 2011, 08:52 pm
1. go to www.getbitcoin.com
2. order bitcoin choosing BOA deposit method
3.  Get email from get bitcoin LLC with deposit info (bank account number and name of company, I chose checking account on deposit slip) I'm not 100% sure if its an email or if it appears on the screen after I place the order...lol
4.  Take cash (exact change) and go to bank.  get deposit ticket, it will ask for all kinds of information you don't have.  ignore that.  just fill in the name and account number and amount. 
5. take cash to counter and say I'd like to make a deposit, and smile nicely.
6.  take receipt, I take a picture of it (not at the bank, at home) and attach it to the email I send back to them with order number
7. wait 24 hours max
