Title: People Are Forgetting To Properly Edit Product Photos!
Post by: dirtybiscuitzz718 on December 18, 2012, 06:05 am
This is just a reminder to any vendors, when posting pics of your product be sure to look it over real good when you think youve edited it properly. I just seen a listing ( I will keep this anonymous), for a prescription substance, the photo was taken of the bottle with the lable "edited". The name and addy and all the basic stuff was sensored, but what this particular vendor forgot is that those random numbers on RX labels all mean something.

 I was able to pull up a map with a pin point location of the pharmacy that dispensed this stuff. This can be incriminating on so many levels, and its not the first time ive seen this happen, so I just wanted to speak up and remind every one, be diligent in your efforts to remain anonymous. Ive sent this particular vendor a PM informing him/her of the error. Please if any one happens to notice little slips like this, lend a hand and kindly PM them.

 We have to work together to keep this community as tightly knit as it possibly can be, with the huge influx of new members and all. Much love every one!
