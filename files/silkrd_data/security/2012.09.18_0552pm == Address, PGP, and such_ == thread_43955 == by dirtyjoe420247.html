Title: Re: Address, PGP, and such?
Post by: Hamb999 on September 18, 2012, 06:45 pm
It is much much more reliable to send to a real address with a real name.  The package will be less scrutinized and less likely to be lost.  The flip side is that if it is discovered (weed is a little stinky, after all) and your real name and address are on it, you could get a controlled delivery.  Just don't ever sign for anything.

As WSG said, PGP does add security and it is probably worth learning eventually.  You can always use privnote.com in the meantime.  Pine (she is a forum user) has a thread that explains PGP pretty well.  Search her name.

Good luck with your first purchase!!

Title: Re: Address, PGP, and such?
Post by: treeline on September 18, 2012, 06:53 pm
^ what they said.  it's always best to use a real name and real address -- if you don't and something happens to the package, you won't have as much recourse with the vendor as far as getting a refund or reshipment.

as for PGP - definitely learn to use it.  it won't take long to do so, and there are many, many threads that can help you get up and running with it.  there are a lot of vendors who require that you use PGP to encrypt your address during the order process.  it's just safer that way!

good luck and be safe! :)

** as for privnotes -- i've noticed several vendors state on their vendor profiles that they will NOT accept privnote because it's not as safe as using PGP.
