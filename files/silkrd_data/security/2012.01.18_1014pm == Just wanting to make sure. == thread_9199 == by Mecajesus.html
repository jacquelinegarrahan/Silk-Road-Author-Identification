Post by: club on January 19, 2012, 03:40 am
Yep that your bitcoin address. You can test by going to http://freebitcoins.appspot.com . They give away 0.005 of a bitcoin to each visitor (youll need to sign in with a google account). Good way of verifying everything works.

I'd get a PO box. That's what I use, and it's worked well. Theyre dirt cheap. Some people think it's a bad idea, but it can't be worse than shipping directly to your home.

If anything ever was intercepted, most likely you will just get a love letter the first time around. But regardless, you dont want your apartment involved... I've heard a story by a guy who got busted for possession, and the cop let him go, but had to inform his apartment's leasing office. If this happened to you, even though you may dodge a legal bullet, the apartment complex could still very well evict you.

Besides, to me a PO box is just cleaner anyway. Thats just that many less hands the package will need to pass through. Plus that nervous walk into the PO is better than living in constant nervousness about what might happen at your home. Just my opinion.
