Post by: stolea2269 on July 30, 2012, 03:55 am
The least risky thing to do is send it to your real house, addressed to your real name.

That way you have plausible deniability if anything were to go wrong.

Think about it - anyone can send something to your house. But if they do see these drugs going to a vacant house, they're more likely to go wait and see who picks it up, and then you have NO deniability.

Look into PGP/GPG encryption as well.

Good luck.
