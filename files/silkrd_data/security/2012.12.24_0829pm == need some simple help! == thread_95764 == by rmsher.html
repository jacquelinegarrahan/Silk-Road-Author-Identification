Title: Re: need some simple help!
Post by: astor on December 24, 2012, 09:18 pm
I guess I could offer some advice, too. The cheapest way to get bitcoins is direct bank transfers to an exchange, but that's not anonymous and you'd want to mix your coins before using them for anything illegal.

Cash deposit to BitInstant has been the most popular method for months, but a lot of people are being asked for ID now.

An increasingly popular method, and probably the best way going forward, is to buy directly off other people on localbitcoins.com or bitcoin-otc. I've found people in my area who charge as little as MtGox price + 2%, and you can use a fake name, they don't ID.

Title: Re: need some simple help!
Post by: astor on December 25, 2012, 03:31 am
Instawallet is an intermediary like a mixing service, but nowhere near as safe. Some people believe it's enough to offer plausible deniability, ie, someone follows the bitcoin trail back from your SR address to an Instawallet address to the exchange. The exchange knows who you are but you can claim that you sent the coins to someone else, and as long as you access Instawallet over Tor, nobody can prove it's you.

I'm not particularly convinced of that method. I'd rather not be identified in the first place, which is why if I bought coins in a non-anonymous way, I'd properly mix them.
