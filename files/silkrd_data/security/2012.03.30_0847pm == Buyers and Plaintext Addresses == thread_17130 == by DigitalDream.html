I would say that about 1/2 of the SR buyers don't use pgp in my experience.  It would really only affect them if silkroad was compromised while their order was processing.  And honestly pgp is hard for some people to figure out, they all want to try a program like kleopatra that complicates everything.  People will not use command line for some reason.

I encrypt messages to buyers only if they provide their key, which is almost never.  If they want to talk about something I am uncomfortable with then I force them to provide it.  All of my pms are just, your order is on its way, thank you.  I have never done a behind the scenes deal or negotiated with buyers, I mean they ask but I don't entertain it.

About the other stats, meh.  Who knows, I don't really care about buyer stats from people ordering from me.  I mean it is great to see people with 50 txns, you know you will get paid on time.  I have yet to have anyone with any auto-finalize and only slight refund rates order though, so my experience in that area is limited.
