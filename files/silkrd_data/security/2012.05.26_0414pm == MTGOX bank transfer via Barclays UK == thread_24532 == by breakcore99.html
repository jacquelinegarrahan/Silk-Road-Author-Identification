I deposited some money to Mtgox over the counter at Barclays a few weeks ago - as I wasn't sure how it was going to work out I didn't go to my local one. I only deposited a small amount (Â£30) just incase it didn't work but it was all pretty straightforward - filled out the slip with Mtgox's details as per their website and handed the slip over with the cash at the counter. The slip asks for the name of the person depositing but I didn't use my real name and was just going to claim I didn't have any ID on me if they asked for any.. was seriously paranoid, haha! It took about four days for me to get the BTC, I raised a support ticket with Mtgox after three days asking when I could expect it but but their reply actually coincided with my coins being credited soooo patience is important I guess!
