When sending bitcoins, you should ALWAYS include the recommended "transaction fee" amount so that your transaction will start getting confirmations quickly in the bitcoin network. When you don't include it, there's no telling how long before will get confirmed. Confirmations require that one of the bitcoin network computers cryptographically verify a transaction against the entire ever-growing blockchain, and any transactions where the sender didn't include a fee will have zero priority.

Unfortunately, there is no way to "cancel" a bitcoin transaction. You're stuck waiting.
