Title: Re: Purchasing BitCoins?!
Post by: BTCmonkey on December 06, 2012, 02:43 am
Bitcoin can seem overwhelming at first, but it's really simple once you spend a little time with it.

I highly recommend going to Bitcopia.com. The site is incredibly easy to use and they accept cash deposits. Just read the section Buying Bitcoins in the FAQ. I think it has instructions there, but it's so easy, you really probably don't even need to read it. Just fill out the forms and place your order. Then you make a cash deposit at Wells Fargo for the amount they quote you. Your bitcoins get sent to your wallet address. Voila!

They're only open during banking hours though, so you'll have to wait til tomorrow. Email the dude if you want. He's always on top of it.
