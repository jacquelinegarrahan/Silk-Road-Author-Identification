Title: Re: Support for beginner
Post by: etsubucs31 on April 23, 2012, 02:29 pm
I suggest using bitinstant. Just go to bitinstant.com and select cash deposit to mt.gox. You obviously must sign up for an mt.gox account if you haven't already done so. After that you just put in the amount you want transferred to your mt.gox account. They will let you choose a bank near you to make the deposit, and they will send you an email with all of the information you need. You then go to the bank you chose and make the deposit into the account # that they emailed you. The bank will not ask for id or anything so you should be good. After the deposit just call the number that is in the email and confirm your deposit and the funds should be in you mt.gox account within a couple of minutes.
