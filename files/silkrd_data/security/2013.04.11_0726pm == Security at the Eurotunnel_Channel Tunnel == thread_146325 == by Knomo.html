Regular user, security is a lot higher than it used to be and all manner of hi tech shit is in use. They can x-ray a vehicle. Swabbing is common and I've seen two vehicles I have been in given the full treatment, practically taken apart and there is nothing you can except cooperate and you will get a cup of tea for being patient.

Even if your the most careful of smugglers, if you get a pull customs will usually find it.

I don't go through the tunnel with anything.
