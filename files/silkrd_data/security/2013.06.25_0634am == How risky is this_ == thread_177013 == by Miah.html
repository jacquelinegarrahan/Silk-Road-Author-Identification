Title: Re: How risky is this?
Post by: enpiping on June 25, 2013, 06:45 am
Whatever instigated that vendor's disappearance (if they were even supplied by the person you want to order from...) at your end is purely speculative; maybe they realized that the SR game was a bit too much work for them, or maybe they got into a situation where they couldn't keep up with orders and decided to call it quits.
That said, nobody will be able to give you a finite "amount" of risk that this order would entail and it's really up to you to decide how much risk you're comfortable with--you're ordering drugs on the internet, internationally.  Personally, if the stealth is good and you trust--to a degree--the vendor you're buying from, I'd be comfortable enough to order.  I have a friend who orders decent quantities of K off of the clearnet, internationally, and none of his packages have been seized.  However, judging by your language and rationalization, you seem to be pretty paranoid about it, so it might not be the best thing for you to do.
