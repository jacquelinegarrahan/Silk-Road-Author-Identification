Title: Re: bitinstant.com - confused about "destination"
Post by: Oldtoker on March 14, 2012, 08:46 am
Bitinstant is really nothing more than a service to move money.  You tell them where to get the money (for instance Dwolla) and then give them the destination to the exhange service you use.  Currently they only work with those four Exchange Services (CryptoXChange, Bitcoinica, MTGox
and VirWox).

So they basically withdraw the funds from your source (using my example Dwolla) and deposit the funds into your exchange service.  Once the funds are in the exchange service you can purchase your Bitcoins.  Once you purchase your Bitcoins you can then move them to SR usually through a mixer such as Bitcoinfog.
