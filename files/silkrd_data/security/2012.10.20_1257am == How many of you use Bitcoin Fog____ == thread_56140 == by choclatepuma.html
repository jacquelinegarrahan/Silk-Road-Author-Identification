Title: Re: How many of you use Bitcoin Fog????
Post by: fatoldsun on October 20, 2012, 05:13 am
Even though SR uses its own tumbler, at the end of the day, coins you transfer to SR will still be linked to coins leaving SR -- unless SR actually has a money _launderering_ service, your coins will eventually be traceable to some other SR users. This means that theoretically, if one of those users gets busted along with his bitcoin accounts, then your coins will also be tainted.

Using a Bitcoin Fog or Blockchain.info's tumbler before you even transfer to SR prevents this.
