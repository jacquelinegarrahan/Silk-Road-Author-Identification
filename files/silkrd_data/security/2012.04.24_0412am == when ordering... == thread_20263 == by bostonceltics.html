Include your key in the message if you want or expect an encrypted response.  Since you don't know what information the vendor may need to pass along to you, it's better to include it.

Your public key is open, used to encrypt messages sent to you by someone else.  Your private key is secret, and used to decrypt messages encrypted by your corresponding public key.
