Title: Re: cashing out bitcoins
Post by: digitbh on July 25, 2012, 02:02 pm
If you're familiar with Moneypak, than you can give someone BTCs for a Moneypak in exchange. You don't need a verified debit card as you can actually just send it to your PayPal. There would be no link to drugs through Moneypak.

Also, you can throw your coins through a tumbler and send them to another BTC wallet set apart from the SR. Afterwards, they will have little to no trace to any drugs. After that, go to bitmarket.eu, set up an account, and make a listing on their market stating how many BTCs you have for sale and how much you're selling one for per unit. bitmarket.eu is a nice site for cashing out.
