If you are in Canada we recommend using cavirtex (https://www.cavirtex.com/, no affiliation). They support bank transfers, interac/debit online, and 'anonymous' direct cash deposits at physical banks.

I would never get coins sent directly to a 3rd party bitcoin address, be it SR, Mt. Gox, or any e-wallet service even though most of them are trustworthy. Getting them sent directly to a wallet you have on your computer ensures you'll get your coins and have the ability to backup your wallet in case of any problems.
