Title: Re: Ordering Lb's of Cannabis Internationally
Post by: anarcho47 on February 29, 2012, 02:43 am
I have had a couple of bulk orders picked up (1lb +) over the past year.  It happens, it's higher risk when you are dealing with parcels because they are more rigorously searched, and the volume is lower than smaller item classes.

That being said, it can be done - you just have to be pretty fucking creative.

I do recommend splitting stuff up when possible.  Every QP I ship is split up into 4 packages and sent over 7-10 days.  Every one makes it, every time.  Even if one were to get lost, it's only 25% of the order as opposed to 100%, so it's a more recoverable situation.  I try to ship in 1oz-or-lighter packages when I can.
