I don't think instawallet expires....not with coins in anyway. You can't give karma until 100 posts.
Buying ebooks doesn't work, although I used it to get from 8 to 10 trasnsactions, but I already had spent a lot of money, which is what vendors look for too.
Buy your bitcoins, transfer to an instawallet. (make 2 copies of URL; if you lose it youre screwed!) Go bitcoin fog create an account. Go to deposit page, and find the receiving address. Copy paste it ( 2 copies again) and then go back to instawallet and enter it into the "withdraw" bit. Wait a couple of hours for it to show up. If you get worried there is a link to blockchain on the bitcoin fog deposit page; this will show your deposit as unconfirmed but on its way!
     To send coins from bitcoin fog go to withdraw page. It will have options to send to multiple addresses over a set time ( this is how it works) You can enter either your SR address or a new instawallet. It will then send your money bit by bit. It takes at least 6 hours.
