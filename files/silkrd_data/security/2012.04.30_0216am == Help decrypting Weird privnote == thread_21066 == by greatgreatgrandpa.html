You don't need a vendor's key for privnote...  You paste the address into your browser, and the message will appear on the page.  Once the page is closed, the message is "destroyed" and gone forever unless you've copied it elsewhere.

Like this:

https://privnote.com/n/tniweibhncepmfvx/#oyqvdqbatlmeeoxu

Just copy and paste this link into the address bar on your browser.  Not sure what you have in that long string of characters in your post...  Unless the vendor tried to encrypt the link?  Even so, it would appear in the "begin PGP message" block format.
