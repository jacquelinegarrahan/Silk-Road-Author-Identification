Here's an easy way to do it (if you're a vendor I'm sure you have a more better secret way  ;) )
1. Download/install Truecrypt
2. Encrypt your usb stick using Truecrypt but click the hidden partition
3. Put an easy crackable password for the visible partition
4. Make a hard uncrackable password for the hidden partition (google one they have generators)
5. Put embarrassing stuff in your visible partition like naked pictures of guy, nudes of your ex, whatever.
6. Put all your illegal stuff like TOR into your hidden partition

Now if your usb ever gets confiscated and they make you decrypt it or crack it cause you put such an easy password, they'll only get to your visible partition and you can say like "fine you got me, I have a problem, okay!!  :'("

I personally hide my encrypted usb anyway because the best way to get away with it is for LE to not even have access to it, yes this might be an inconvenience but I'm not saying stick it in a jar of peanut butter in a ziploc bag, I'm just saying hide it somewhere. You can even get one of those micro flash drives that are smaller than the tip of your finger and then just use an adapter to access it and when you're done browsing SR just put it away. Just stay away from terrorism and CP and the cops won't bother you  :D
