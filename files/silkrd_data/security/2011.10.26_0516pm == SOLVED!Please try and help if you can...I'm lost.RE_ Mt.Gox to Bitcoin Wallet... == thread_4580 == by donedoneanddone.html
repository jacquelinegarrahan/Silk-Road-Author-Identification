You do not see transaction because you did not made a rescan.
Ok, do rescan this way;
Close bitcoin client.
go to bitcoin client shortcut on your desktop. If you do not have it, then create it.
Right click of the mouse on shortcut and select properties.
There where write target paste this line:
"C:\Program Files\Bitcoin\bitcoin.exe" -rescan
Click ok and doubleclick on that icon.
After rescan is made, do not forget to open again properties, and remove -rescan (and ok).
