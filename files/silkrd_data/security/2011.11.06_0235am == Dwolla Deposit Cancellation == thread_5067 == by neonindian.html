Post by: anarcho47 on November 08, 2011, 03:42 pm
That depends entirely on what you have set up with your bank.

Usually, either way, the bank will let the transaction go through even if it puts your account into the red.  If you have overdraft protection feeds added to your account, that's what it's for - when too much goes out and not enough comes in.  If you don't they will almost alwaysa let the transaction go through if you have a decent banking history with them because they will ding you will a NSF fee for NOT having overdraft.

So I wouldn't worry about the transaction being cancelled, only that whatever you were going to buy on here is probably going to cost you an extra $35.00 :(
