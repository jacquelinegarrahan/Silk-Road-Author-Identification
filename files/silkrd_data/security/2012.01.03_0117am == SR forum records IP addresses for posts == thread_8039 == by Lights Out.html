Title: Re: SR forum records IP addresses for posts
Post by: Horizons on January 03, 2012, 01:31 am
Asbury can be a little too sharp in his comments sometimes. :)

But he's right. You're accessing these forums through Tor. The entire point of Tor is that no site you access through it knows your actual IP. Instead, they get the IP of whoever happens to be your Tor Exit relay for that session. The IP being logged is not your own, and you can't be identified by it.

If you're serious about this whole "buying illegal stuff on the web" business, you should really read up on what privacy measures you are or ought to be using. It's the only thing between you and a very harsh prison sentence, so you'd better know about every single loophole and how to plug it.
