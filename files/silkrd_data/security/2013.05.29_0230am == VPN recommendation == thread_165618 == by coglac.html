Title: Re: VPN recommendation
Post by: AustralianHash on May 29, 2013, 02:55 am
ACtually being in the states can actually be a good thing. What you really want is one located somewhere that the law does not require them to keep logs. Lots of countries do, which means they can track you after the fact. If no logs are kept, even if they approach your VPN provider, they will have no useful information to hand over. Which counts out every VPN in europe, they have to keep logs. Soon australia will also have similar laws, and the USA is working on it, but do not require it yet.

I think.....always do your own research, but that is the conclusion i have come to.
