There are a few ways you can get bitcoins in new zealand;

First you need to download a bitcoin client. You can find this at http://bitcoin.org and download it for whatever OS you are on (linux, windows, mac). Now extract the file and run the bitcoin.exe which is in the extracted folder. This will install bitcoin. Once bitcoin is installed open it up and let it synchronize with the network completely before attempting to buy bitcoins, this can take a while depending on your internet speed but I ran into seriously problems because I didn't let it synchronize before buying bitcoins (it should say down the bottom 'synchronizing with network block xxx xxx downloaded wait for them all to download.

Now you have a couple options to buy bitcoins;

1. You can use an exchanger on Silk Road. These are Silk Road users that will send bitcoins to your address for some form of payment, I think in New Zealand you probably will have to use Western Union or cash in the mail. One of the most popular exchanges with good feedback is Sugar Mama ( http://silkroadvb5piz3r.onion/silkroad/user/aace915f06 ) send her a message about buying bitcoins in NZ, you can search for other exchangers using the search function. Note Western Union may not be ideal for anonymity as I believe you need some kind of ID whereas cash in the mail will take longer but be completely anonymous (as long as it's not intercepted by customs, but I don't think it would be.

2. You can use an exchange like mtgox.com although this method is not anonymous as you will need to pay with your bank account, and may need to upload photo ID. Though this may not matter much if your only going to order a small amount of DMT one time for personal use, however I would advise you to be as anonymous as possible, contacting sugar mama is probably your best bet.

I have heard of a kiwi using a visa 'prezzy card' to buy bitcoins in an article here; ( www.newswire.co.nz/2011/12/silk/ ) though it doesn't say how he did it, if you can find info about this, this may be a better, less traceable option.
