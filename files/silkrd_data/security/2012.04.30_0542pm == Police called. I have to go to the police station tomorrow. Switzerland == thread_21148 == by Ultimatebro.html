Superderp said it right. They can suspect all they want, that's what they do. They all wanna be Dick Tracey and be the one to crack the big one. Say nothing. They will tell you why you are there. The cops can bend the rules with you because you don't know any better, the lawyer being there will prevent that.

If they have enough to charge you, they will. If they are trying to get you to talk its because they want to collect some easy evidence before they arrest you. Believe me when I say this, you talking can only make it worse, it will never make it better because these are cops, not judges. They have no say in anything, they simply collect evidence and charge people with shit.
