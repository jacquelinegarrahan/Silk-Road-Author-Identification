I don't see any reason not to send the BTC straight to Bitcoinfog - indeed using a secondary wallet wouldn't do you much good as any fool with access to the block chain would see the transfer of BTC from that address to Bitcoinfog.

This is a fairly secure method to obtain BTC but I would suggest you consider using cash in the mail / Moneypaks in future as that way it will be impossible to trace the Bitcoins back to you - the US vendors CoinBox and BlueSkyTraders can help you with this.
