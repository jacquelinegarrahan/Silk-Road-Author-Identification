Title: Re: I may have messed up with the method of gettting my BitCoin, where can i go now?
Post by: fatoldsun on October 20, 2012, 05:09 am
accessing Bitinstant through Tor only identifies the tor circuit used at that time. soon you'll have a new circuit that's not identified, because tor automatically switches circuits.

just send your coins through a mixer, or use the "tainted" coins to buy some legit products and then buy more coins in a secure way.

I've come to the conclusion, that buyers using PGP, and bitcoin purchase with bitinstant and fake names/addresses for those purchases are pretty damn safe. Be alert to whats happening to your vendors by watching forums and your vendor's page to see if something bad is happening so that you can be hyper alert at pickup time. Open and/or use the package away from the delivery site.
