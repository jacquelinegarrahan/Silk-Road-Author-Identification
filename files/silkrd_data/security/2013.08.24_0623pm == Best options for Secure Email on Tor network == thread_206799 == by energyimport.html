The alternative is to use a common email service through TOR. There are many. mailinator.com, safemail.net, etc.

Just use Silk Road's private messaging and use PGP for every message that contains any information that could identify you or trace anything back to you. There isn't really a need for email except your personal email which should never mention anything related to SR.
