No such thing as being "too cautious", but for the most part you don't really have to go through all the extra motions.  The real point of error is always going to be where the package is intercepted, which can happen no matter how many 100's of proxies you are going through, no matter how many bridges you're using, no matter how many times you encrypt your hard drive...if LE wants to bust down your door over 100mg of cocaine they're gonna do it.

That said, I think it's a wee bit overkill but if it puts your mind at ease go for it.  A friend of mine has ordered -easily- 50 times from SR and the only real safety precautions he takes is PGP for posting his address on a purchase.
