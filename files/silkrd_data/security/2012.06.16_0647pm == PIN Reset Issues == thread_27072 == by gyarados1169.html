Title: Re: PIN Reset Issues
Post by: cacoethes on June 16, 2012, 11:45 pm
They used to cut some slack depending on the circumstances, but that's a thing of the past, I believe.

You shouldn't even be able to read the PIN reset message they send until the 7th day (that's seven full 24 hour periods- even though it may say 7 days, you still have to wait until the end of the 7th 24 hour mark).

Once you are able to read the PIN reset message, you should be able to follow it from there.  Reset seems to be instantaneous.
