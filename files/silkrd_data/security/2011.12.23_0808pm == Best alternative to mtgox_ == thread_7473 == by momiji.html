Title: Re: Best alternative to mtgox?
Post by: CaptainSensible on December 23, 2011, 11:56 pm
You want to steer clear of MtGox.  The people running MtGox are working closely with the DEA to keep track of people who might be using Bitcoins for unapproved activities.  You can't log in to MtGox from Tor because all Tor exit nodes are rejected by the MtGox website.  Plus, last time I checked, MtGox required most users to provide copies of state-issued ID in order to do things like withdraw Bitcoins from the website. 

A lot of folks seem to like Tradehill, but I don't know if they also now require ID.  I know that Intersango does not require ID and can be accessed via Tor.  How safe it is to keep your 'coins there I can't say.  That seems to be the real problem ~~ the established Bitcoin sites seem to want users to give up all their anonymity, and the newer sites like Intersango are O.K. with anonymous users, but who knows if they'll just disappear one day with your money.
