Post by: LexusMiles on January 25, 2012, 01:54 am
Quote from: happyroller1234 on January 22, 2012, 08:50 pm
- BoA cash deposit > Getbitcoins > instawallet > SR

In all honesty, this is a pretty good system. Since safety is measured by degree, none of us are *absolutely* safe.

Add the extra layer of OS encryption is really only useful in the case that your PC is taken away as evidence.

If Silkroad becomes a regular haunt for you, then maybe just run truecrypt on you OS.

The main risks of not being inside an encrypted OS are:

[-] Confiscated PC as evidence
[-] Some friend (or enemy) snoops on your PC when you are in the bathroom or whatever
[-] Accidental discovery by family or friend

If none of those concern you, then simply continue as you are (nothing wrong with that).

Post by: CrunchyFrog on January 26, 2012, 03:28 am
Quote from: happyroller1234
...If I don't sign, they can't raid the house or get a warrant, right?...
The police are *unlikely* to raid a home without a warrant; it looks bad in court.  The police can get a warrant if they can convince a judge to issue one; (hopefully) difficult without probable cause.  So anything you can do -- or not do, like signing -- to reduce the likelihood of a judge concluding that probable cause exists should reduce the likelihood of a raid.
