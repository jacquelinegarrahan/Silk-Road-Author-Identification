I figured I better step into this thread and let everyone know the bitter truth. Instawallet.org and bitcoin-central.net (same ownership) were robbed and bitcoins were lost, how much is unknown yet. They currently have a claims process for all accounts found here:

***CLEARNET WARNING***
http://notice.instawallet.org/
***CLEARNET WARNING***

They even admit on that notice that their infrastructure was flawed to begin with, that's why they cannot reopen the site as is. The reason was that this site posts your BTC wallet, and password in cleartext in your URL bar. Now the SSL encryption "theoretically" ensures protection from a MiTM attack. Although if you've ever emailed, IM'd, txt'd, or opened it in a google/apple product the wallet and password have been indexed and are now in the cloud.

With that being said I hope for the best on everyone that has to go through the claims process, so far BTC return processes have been essentially nonexistent. Hopefully everyone will learn from this event that the only safe place for your BTC is in your possession.
