Easiest way to cash out: become your own exchanger.

Carding forums also sell reloadable cards, usually with accounts set up with fake ID, but you're chancing they might run away with your money or they might be informants, or maybe banks are tracing foreign cards (which they do all the time). Remember if you decide to declare any income it's for a service, preferably you're selling NDA protected software scripts or templates. Cash your face off who gives a fuck, it's now legal clean cash. I find it's cheaper to set up offshore corps, launder the coins to obscure the silk road link, and sell them direct to buyer avoiding exchanges wanting ID, then withdraw the funds through a local company paying the paltry 15% taxes. All the illegal methods cost almost the same and are much more risky.
