Title: Re: Question about shipping
Post by: killerbunnies on May 01, 2012, 02:19 am
I have other people let me use their addresses. Also, I always try to have product sent with NO signature required (meaning the package should just be left in the mailbox). When the feds seize a package containing drugs they usually attempt what is called a "controlled delivery". In the event of a controlled delivery they have a conditional warrant that specifies when the package is delivered it must be signed for by someone at the address, thus validating the warrant, and allowing them to execute the warrant ie. kick in your door, search through everything, and make arrests. By not having a signature required it is obvious when a controlled delivery is taking place. If a "post man" insists that the receiver must sign for a package that the receiver knows should not have a signature required it should be immediately obvious what is really going on. To avoid a controlled delivery in a situation like this all the receiver needs to do is deny delivery of the package. Say "that is not my name, I am not expecting a package". Then the warrant will be void and they can not do anything. I hope this helps a little :)
