Title: Re: Where can i stash my funds? any safe wallets?
Post by: woahmang on November 17, 2012, 07:43 pm
The safest method:

Keep funds completely offline then you can use the paper wallet generator on bitaddress.org. Save the web page to a flash drive, boot into an Ubuntu live CD (you may have a trojan) then generate and print out the wallet and keep it somewhere safe. Save the public address and get coins sent to it whenever (like when using localbitcoins to buy for cash), use blockchain.info or similar to keep an eye on the balance.

When you come to spend your coins you can import the private key into blockchain.info or the desktop client (both using TOR of course), then send them over to SR. This means you only have to trust SR and blockchain for 1 hour each, your computer can even have viruses on it and you still won't lose your coins.
