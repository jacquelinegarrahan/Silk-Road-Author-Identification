Title: Re: Band on the run....
Post by: ithasbegun on May 16, 2012, 07:21 am
It all depends. If the case is big enough they might issue an international arrest warrant. In that case you'll have to pick the country to which you flee carefully. When you get convicted and they come to the conclusion that they can't find you/are on the run they will just wait until you run into them, or they into you. Only after 20 years or more/less, depending on the country, they might cancel the sentence because it is to old.
You'd also have to be  sure your not in any biometrics database. Hand/Finger prints, Iris scan, DNA, Facial recognition.
All in all the punishment has to be pretty severe for it to be worth all the trouble.
