tnx
Just make sure your bitcoin software is configured to run within Tor, so your address can't be tied to your IP. You do this by going to settings > options then check connect to socks4 proxy and the proxy IP change to 127.0.0.1 and the port to 9050. Sending BTC drom your SR address to your personal wallet is masked pretty well by SR's built-in bitcoin mixer, which does indeed work.
