The IBAN (Short for International Bank Account Number) and BIC code are used when making wire transfers between different accounts across countries - so if for example I lived in Australia and wanted to send you some money, I'd need your IBAN to do it (which is actually just your account information put into an internationally standardised format.)

Neither of these are necessary for making a cash deposit in branch. Re: putting your name, there's no reason you can't sign another name. However as I've said in previous posts it is not illegal to buy Bitcoins and near impossible to trace them back to you once they've been put through a mixer such as the one built into SR.
