Post by: Banjo on August 17, 2012, 07:11 pm
Quote from: imaloonyguy on August 17, 2012, 06:29 pm
im soooo confused..... i downloaded cleopatra and made my own key using my silk road name but now how to i encrypt?? do i use the whole pgp block of the vendor im purchasing from?? i will give a coin or two for help please!!

No need to use Kleopatra for this at all.

Step 1.)
Copy the vendor's public key into notepad

Step 2.)
Save that text file onto your desktop, or somewhere you can find it easily.

Step 3.)
Open the program called GPA. In the window, click the "Import" icon near the top of the window.

Step 4.)
Select the text file you just saved from the vendor's profile.

Step 5.)
If everything went well, it should say, "1 key read, 1 key imported". Click ok. You should now see that vendor's key listen in your list of keys.

Step 6.)
Click the "clipboard" icon, which will bring up a new window. In that window, write your address.

Step 7.)
Click the "encrypt" button. It will present you with a list of keys. Select the key of the vendor you just imported. It will warn you about there being no way to know if that key belongs to that person. Just ignore that message, and click ok.

Step 8.)
You should now see your encrypted message. Copy that, and send it to the vendor when you make your order.

I broke this down into eight steps to make it simple, but it's really very easy, and once you do it a couple times, you should be able to do the whole process in just a few seconds.
