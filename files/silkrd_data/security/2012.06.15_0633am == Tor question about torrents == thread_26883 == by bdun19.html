Title: Re: Tor question about torrents
Post by: jochem on June 18, 2012, 08:12 am
Never use Tor for torrents.

Move to something else then torrents. Chances are that your ISP is not actively monitoring your connection, but is acting upon DMCA notices and stuff like that. Since you can see all IP's using a certain torrent, it's quite easy to automatically send these notices out (and they do, I used to get them all the time when I didn't filter out torrent traffic on my tor node). Use the direct downloading sites mentioned before. Or use Usenet, which you can get for a couple of bucks a month (and they often offer an encrypted connection for free, so your ISP can't see what you're doing even if they want to).
