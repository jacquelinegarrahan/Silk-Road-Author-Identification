The phone can be tracked even if it is on or off, unless the battery is dead or removed. I would report it stolen, if the cops take it seriously, they will try to find the phone / person who ripped it off.

By chance, is it an iPhone? You can report it stolen to Apple and they can do a remote wipe of all the data on it, unless the data is the important part of getting it back.

Call the cops and if they don't take it seriously hound them until they take some action. Contact you wireless carrier as well and tell them the phone is stolen. They might not give you the info, but if you can get an LE agency involved they (the carrier) will give the pigs the GPS coordinates and any WIFI IP address it is connected to.
