i just started using gpgtools as well. Use the gpgmail version that comes with it. You import the keys by saving them as a textedit file first of the persons PGP Key and then it will generate their key from there. After that you click on their info to show their email and open up mail on your mac, copy-paste in their email, and there will be an option to encrypt just below the subject. You select it and choose to choose just your key as the signature. Type in your message and send it off!
