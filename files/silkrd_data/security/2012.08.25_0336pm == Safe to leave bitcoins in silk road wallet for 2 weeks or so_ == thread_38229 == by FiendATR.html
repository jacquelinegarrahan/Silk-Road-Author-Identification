Title: Re: Safe to leave bitcoins in silk road wallet for 2 weeks or so?
Post by: CoolGrey on November 06, 2012, 09:25 am
Yes that is pretty safe. Nobody will steal your bitcoins out of your SR wallet if you keep the password secret. The value of bitcoin may increase or decrease a bit, so when you come back they may be worth a little less or a little more, but I don't expect the difference to be huge.

Only if something happens to SR, you may have trouble accessing your coins. The safest way is to store BTC is to have a BitCoin client on your own (virus free and encrypted, preferably Linux) computer.
