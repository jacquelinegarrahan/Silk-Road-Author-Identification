Title: Re: Electrum Bitcoin Wallet Question
Post by: Wadozo on June 20, 2013, 03:23 pm
Yes. If you have Electrum set up correctly, synced to the network and have numerous BTC addresses in your Wallet, there is no reason why you can't have your coins transferred to one of  your Electrum BTC addresses (use a new one for each transaction) and then transfer your coins to your SR BTC address (which you should change after each transaction also) when you want to make a purchase.  :)
