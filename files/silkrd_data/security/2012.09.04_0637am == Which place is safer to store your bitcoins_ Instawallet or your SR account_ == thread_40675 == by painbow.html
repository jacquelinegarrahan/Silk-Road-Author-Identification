Title: Re: Which place is safer to store your bitcoins? Instawallet or your SR account?
Post by: koooloap27 on September 04, 2012, 04:36 pm
I try to never have spare coins (market could fuck you over by fluctuating) but if I did I would definitely not keep them in my SR account! Just think about it... bitcoins = legal. SR = probably illegal where you live. If SR were to ever shut down or go offline even for a week wouldn't you want to know your bitcoins are safe in a instawallet, or even better a good ol' plain bitcoin wallet on your computer. That way even if instawallet goes down at least you can attempt to contact someone or even ask for outside assistance. By storing coins on your SR account I wouldn't think you would want to ask anyone for help or even find anyone who could help you.

I'd say do instawallet or bitcoin wallet
