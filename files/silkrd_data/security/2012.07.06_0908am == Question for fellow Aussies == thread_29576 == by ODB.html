Title: Re: Question for fellow Aussies
Post by: Gibbroni on July 06, 2012, 04:09 pm
It can be very very simple, or it can be a little more complicated.
Direct deposit from your bank account is obviously the simplest method....but considering SR, media attention, LE possibly targeting bank accounts from which the funds to buy bitcoins came, it's probably safest to go with cash deposit, ie spendbitcoins.
I never bother putting them through any other wallets. Bitcoin address can be changed every time. Nobody would know it's a SR bitcoin address.  I'm sure lots of people disagree on the matter. You're a first time buyer though, focus on keeping under the trafficable amounts in your area, making sure you get the right postage type, researching your vendors.  Bitcoins are fairly straightforward.
