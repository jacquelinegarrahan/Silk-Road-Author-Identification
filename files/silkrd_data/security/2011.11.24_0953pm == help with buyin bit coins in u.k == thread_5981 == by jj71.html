Do you have a bank account ? MtGox (https://mtgox.com/) and Intersango (https://intersango.com/) take GBP deposits and exchanges them for Bitcoins.

Title: Re: help with buyin bit coins in u.k
Post by: foldingpaper on November 25, 2011, 10:57 pm
For Intersango to identify your payment, you should include your personal reference which can be found on their website, in Account>British Pounds>Add Funds.

To deposit the money to the Silk Road, create an account (if you haven't already), log in, and navigate to your account page (http://ianxz6zefk72ulzz.onion/index.php/account). There you will find a Bitcoin address (a long string of random characters) which you must send your Bitcoins to.

To send the Bitcoins, go to Intersango.com, navigate to Account>Bitcoins>Withdraw Funds and copy your Silk Road address to the corresponding form field. Specify an amount and click withdraw.

-

If you want to make the transaction less traceable, send your Bitcoins from Intersango to an Instawallet (https://www.instawallet.org/) (don't forget the URL of your instawallet!) and from there send it to Silk Road.

Bitcoin transactions are not instant, they can take 2-6 hours.
