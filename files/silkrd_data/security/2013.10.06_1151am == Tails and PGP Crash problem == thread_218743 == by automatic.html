Title: Re: Tails & PGP Crash problem
Post by: GyroGym on October 07, 2013, 08:10 am
Have Gedit open, type whatever you want encrypted, highlight it all and Ctrl+X to copy to clipboard.

 Now left click your pgp icon beside the Onion icon. and go to sign/encypt clipboard with public keys.
 Choose what key to encypt and BAM.... the message will be encypted with the public key......go back to your email, IM, or forum msg and just past it in. (ctr+p)  [Thats all you gotta do, send the email as if it had normal unencrypted text inside.]

 To decrypt msgs its the same thing, copy the code to you're clipboard, highlight it all and cut it, and click on icon and it will have changed to a decrypt option.
 Everything runs off the clipboard. just remember that (ctr+c OR ctr+x to copy/cut any text to clipboard)

 Its very simple to use, just keep your gedit program running to keep things neat and tidy and use your clipboard.

Ctrl + C  = Cut selected text from gedit

Ctrl + P = Paste Text to gedit



 Also store all your public/private keys in your persistant drive. (duh!)
