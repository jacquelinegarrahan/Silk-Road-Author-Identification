Post by: MissNatural on July 10, 2013, 06:38 am
While that was most definitely stupid and getting a new phone/reporting your old one stolen would be smart, I highly doubt you will get in trouble for that picture, or the messages. The stuff can likely be tracked, but probably wont. I know people who have texted explicitly about drugs and been dealing large quantities for years and not been caught, or even suspected, Yes it is very chancy, but LE is not on top of all the latest technological opportunities that they could be, yet. Many LE departments are severely limited by funding which can prevent them from utilizing various resources.

On another note, what are they going to do with that text? They can't get a warrant to search your house based on a single text. It likely won't spark a full blown investigation. And it's very likely it won't be considered a major piece of evidence in any case unless of course they were already investigating you, which would mean they already have more pertinent evidence.

Just my analysis. Even if you get a new phone, your old records will still exist, even if you switch phone services.

I had a friend get busted for Heroin/Methamphetamine trafficking in multi-LB quantities and all they did was raid his house and charge him for the shit they found. They still never knew he got it all from SR, despite confiscating over half a million dollars in cash and drugs. Not even that seems to warrant a full blown phone-record search.

If you're not a terrorist, I wouldn't worry too too much about your phone records... but it doesn't hurt to be safe/paranoid.
