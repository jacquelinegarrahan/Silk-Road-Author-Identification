Buddha,

I don't know how far you want to take this but considering your business (pills & powder) I'd set up a New Zealand corp (very easy laws for hiding identity) which in turn would be the primary shareholder for a US shell corp you set up.  You can do all this online and anon.  For your US dummy corp I'd have the dba be something about herbal supplements.  With this corporate paperwork I'd rent a climate controlled storage locker or even a warehouse locker (think foodstuff storage for small grocery store suppliers) and stash all those bags with package and labeling along the lines of creatine or vitamins or choline (or a large mix of various supplements).

Done right it wouldn't be traceable to you plus you'd be able to pick your stuff up at a location where regular foot traffic (pick ups and deliveries) doesn't raise eyebrows.
