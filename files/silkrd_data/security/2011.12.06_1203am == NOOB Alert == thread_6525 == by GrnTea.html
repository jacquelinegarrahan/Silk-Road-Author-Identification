Encryption: PGP (google it)  I myself am a noob, but reading the accompanied documentation is a great starting point.
Basically in a nutshell, you create a pair of keys through PGP (Kleopatra is a GUI)  share your PUBLIC key with everyone so they can send you encrypted messages.
When they do so, you use your PRIVATE key to decode the message...simple as that
