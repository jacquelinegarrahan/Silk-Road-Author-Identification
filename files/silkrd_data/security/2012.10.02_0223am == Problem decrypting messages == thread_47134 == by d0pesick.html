just to clarify :
your friend will encrypt a message to you using YOUR public key. You then use YOUR private key to decrypt said message.
If you wish to communicate something to your friend, you need to encrypt your message using HIS PUBLIC KEY.
he will then use his PRIVATE key to decrypt your message.
