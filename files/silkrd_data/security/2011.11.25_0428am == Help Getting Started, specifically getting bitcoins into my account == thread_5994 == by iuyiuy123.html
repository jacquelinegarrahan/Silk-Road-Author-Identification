Title: Re: Help Getting Started, specifically getting bitcoins into my account
Post by: marooncode on November 25, 2011, 05:59 am
It takes time to get the ball rolling as I figured out by researching and reading up on things.

I personally use Dwolla to exchange money to send to Mt.Gox, where I buy my bitcoins. From there, I use several Instawallet accounts to move it temporarily, then to a tumbler (ex. bitcoinfog), let the bitcoins wash for a couple days, to another couple Instawallet accounts, then all of those finally to SR.

Dwolla-->Mt.Gox-->Instawallet (1->2->3)-->Tumbler-->Instawallet(4,5,6)-->SR

I only buy bitcoins and go through the process when I make purchases. I feel safer that way. Don't keep any money in Mt. Gox or bitcoins in a one specific location. If so, leave it in the SR account.

Good luck!
