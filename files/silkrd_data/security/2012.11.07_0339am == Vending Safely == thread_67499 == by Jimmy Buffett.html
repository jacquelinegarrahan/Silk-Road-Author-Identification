Title: Re: Vending Safely
Post by: CoolGrey on November 07, 2012, 02:07 pm
That's a broad topic and something you should research yourself before you start. Read the wiki and the seller guide.

But a few general tips:
- Use the proper Tor browser bundle
- Don't give out personal details about yourself.
- Use a clean computer (no viruses), preferably Linux
- Remove metadata from the images you upload
Title: Re: Vending Safely
Post by: awesome1126 on November 07, 2012, 03:19 pm
Packaging and shipping are going to be the first battle-fronts to get you busted. If you can do those things safely, a tight run ship is much easier to keep afloat.

Second, loose lips sink ships, don't tell anyone. Period. If you can't do this, don't vend.

I'd say the technology safety is third compared to these two things.
