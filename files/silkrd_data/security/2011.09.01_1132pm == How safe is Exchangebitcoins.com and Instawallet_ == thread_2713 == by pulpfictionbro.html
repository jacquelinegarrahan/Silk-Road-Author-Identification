Title: Re: How safe is Exchangebitcoins.com and Instawallet?
Post by: CaptainSensible on September 02, 2011, 04:37 am
I don't think the Bitcoins transfered to Installwallet are untraceable.  But since the ownership of the wallet at Installwallet is unknown and not connected to any site that sells a product, you could theoretically send Bitcoins to Instawallet for payment of any kind of legitimate service or product. 

My experience with Instawallet is that it receives Bitcoins pretty quickly, but it can take hours for Bitcoins to be sent out of Instawallet.
