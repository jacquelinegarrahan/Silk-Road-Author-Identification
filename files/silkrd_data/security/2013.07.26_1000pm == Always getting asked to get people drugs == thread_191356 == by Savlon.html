Post by: entreterra on July 27, 2013, 12:38 am
In respect to buying and selling for profit, here's my take:

I have always loved sharing good moleules with people. L, DMT, Mushrooms, MDMA, MJ, really, just good entheogens, whatever the substance might be. I believe these molecules are important for humanity on a much deeper level than just for the sake of fun; and I think many people agree with me.

The time period we live in is but a tiny slice of the entire span of the history of humanity. This period of prohibition where all drugs (except the ones manufactured for profit by pharmaceutical companies, of course) are demonized is so ridiculously incongruent with human history. Humans, different cultures, have been consuming various psychotropic substances to alter and expand their mind for thousands of years all across the world and still do so today in more removed settings from the modernized world (take ayahuasca for example or native american peyote ritual). Entheogenic substances are important. They are therapeutic, medicinal, and I believe they facilitate our evolution and spiritual development. Even in the scope of western medicine, drugs like LSD, Psilocybin, and MDMA have proven to be incomparably therapeutic for a range of disorders and illnesses.

I believe these substances are important and helpful for many people, but many people do not have these available to them. With SR, one can supply people pure products to provide these life-changing experiences.

However, these substances are costly. If I had piles of money, I'd buy tons of this stuff and give it away to anyone willing to partake with the right intention, in a heartbeat.

So, the next best option is for one to buy and sell for profit. This way, people can have access to these beautiful molecules at a fair price, and you can continue to supply. It's not a matter of selling for profit so that you can buy a new stereo system, or take a trip to Hawaii. There's no harm in using the process to add a little more padding to your savings, but the primary motive should be to be able share these molecules to those who would otherwise have no access to them or may try to buy them from someone who is selling them a RC.

And don't make it something where you sell to just anyone who is willing to shell out the cash. Be selective, in a reasonable way, and use your instincts as well. Mostly, sell to friends or people you feel comfortable with and around; you know, not people you feel are going to take the stuff and then stomp all over it and resell it. If you don't feel comfortable with a person, like they give off a negative vibe or they do things you are uncomfortable with (like asking for things through email, or being pushy or nosey etc.) then just don't sell to them, simple as that. I know the feeling of not wanting someone to go empty handed, but if they aren't showing you that they can be mature, discreet, and genuine, then you have nothing to feel bad about.

Anyways, I know that was a lot, if you read it, I hope it was helpful.

Friends, and even relatives, will 9 times out of 10 save their own skin even if they risk yours. This true story (with names disguised) should be an object lesson to all. It's the very reason I will never transact except on the Road, where my anonymity is guaranteed:

A friend of mine, call her Rosie, had a legitimate nursery. She grew flowers and trees, had 3 or 4 greenhouses, all legit. She realized, like Nancy Botkin, how much more money she could make by throwing down a few cannabis seeds.

She followed certain strict rules. ONLY sell to family, and friends she had known for eons.

She sold, for example, to her daughter, and her daughter's husband of many years.

One year, the daughter got a divorce. Out of anger the husband vindictively turned Rosie in.

By way of epilogue, the cops took every dime Rosie had. She lawyered up and beat a horrible rap, but she was left with no means of an income and no money. It would have been far, far worse to go to prison.

I get the impression that folks around here have never stepped foot in an actual prison. One day of it will make you pay any price to avoid it.

Walk with care, folks. Don't deal. Not even to your grandma. Faced with the option of jailtime or turning your ass in, what choice will grandma make? What about that friend you've known for a year, what choice will he make?
