Post by: morganeverett on May 19, 2013, 06:50 am
For basic starters do you regularly receive national / international mail? If all you get are utility bills and adverts then suddenly there are 10 big packages arriving in a day (exaggerating) it probably will raise an eyebrow.

Also consider getting multiple locations. Some use dead drops, some think PO boxes are good although others say no. There are a few threads on those. Whatever method you choose, it is advisable to not store your stuff at receiving location.

Say they performed a control delivery on you. They've been letting packets get through for weeks. Postman drops off package or asks you to sign for it. You open the door and SWAT team pops out and arrest you. If you have nothing else there, then they have no way of proving whatever contraband in that particular package is yours. Your lawyer can argue that you've been setup, that you have no illegal stuff at home since you are a law-abiding upstanding citizen. In this example they will have to follow you all day for weeks and check all those locations for caches. Plus you could have handed it off to someone else. And clean up after yourself. Trace amounts of substance could be argued to be merely contamination since paper money in circulation is cross contaminated frequently.

From what I gather (not having the finances to test it myself) big shipments get broken down into smaller packets to get past security more easily.

Lastly, WHY THE FUCKING FUCK DID YOUR FRIEND KNOW YOU ORDERED FOUR PACKAGES? If he gets busted and sings like a canary in exchange for less time (see prisoner's dilemma), (cops get a warrant?) they're going to try get the biggest bust per resource (if they don't have the resources to SWAT all his associates). 4 packages a week is a more tempting target over one they don't know the rate / quantity of. You might as well put A MOTHERFUCKING BIG RED NEON SIGN saying PROMOTION to cops / DA or whoever wants a piece of your flesh. The strict laws being what they are means they can accuse your quantity (assuming they have it in evidence) indicating distribution, even though it may only be for personal use.

At least that's how I would do it. Think from their perspective. Develop countermeasures accordingly. Try from their shoes again. Repeat until you cannot. Review it 1 week later. See if you spot mistakes and hope they didn't spot it. Modify & deploy. Repeat.
