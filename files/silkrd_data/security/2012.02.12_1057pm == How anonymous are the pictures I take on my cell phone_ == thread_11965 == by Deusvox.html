Title: Re: How anonymous are the pictures I take on my cell phone?
Post by: MiND.BLOWN on February 13, 2012, 08:30 pm
I would recommend using Irfanview, it can scrub exif data and has a lot of extra information about your image.
Imgur as an image host will also scrub any exif data, at least for the average viewer.

For the paranoid:
Imgur may keep an original copy/record of your image that includes original exif data. Clean exif data before using imgur.
Your phone may have an app that stores your pictures with third parties that you aren't aware of. After all the issues with apps stealing contact lists and logging data/keystrokes this becomes an issue.
