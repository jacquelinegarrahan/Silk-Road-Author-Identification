There are a few ways to go about it. There are vendors here on SR that can hook you up, look under the "Money" listings. You can also use a service to buy them, the bitcoin wiki (sorry, don't have the link at hand but you can google it) has a list of services that allow you to make cash deposits to their bank accounts in exchange for btc.  Make sure you check the reviews here in the forum before giving your money to any of those services, though. You can also go to virwox.com and use paypal or a credit card to buy linden dollars, then convert those to btc and send them to your wallet. I have found virwox to be the fastest way to go, but it is a bit of a pain doing all the conversions to get your money in and out. Hope that helps.
