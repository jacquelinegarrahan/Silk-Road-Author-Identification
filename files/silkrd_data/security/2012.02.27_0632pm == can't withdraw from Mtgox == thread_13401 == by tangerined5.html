MtGox is nothing but trouble.  They're working hand-in-hand with the DEA.  If they can't readily identify you then they're liable to freeze your account until you provide government-issued ID. 

Leech -- by wallet we mean https://www.instawallet.org/  You can get a Bitcoin deposit address & a link, or URL,  that points to the wallet.  You can then use this wallet as a step to getting Bitcoins to SR.  Send your coins from wherever you bought them to the wallet, & then from the wallet to perhaps another wallet, & then on to SR. 

** Just be sure to bookmark the Instawallet URL.  You must have that URL to get back to the same wallet.  It's not enough to just have the Bitcoin address for receiving payments.  **
