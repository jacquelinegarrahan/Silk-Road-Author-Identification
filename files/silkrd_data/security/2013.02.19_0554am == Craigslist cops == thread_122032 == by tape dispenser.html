Post by: sleepyeyes2k2 on February 23, 2013, 11:09 pm
Quote
I guess what I'd need to do is pay more than the cops - what do cops pay their CIs for low level ratting?

There's two answers to that question.  The first is that cops, when they actually pay a CI, pay out depending on the market.  If the CI is a crack addict, and a hit of crack has a street value of $20, then that's his price. 

The more complicated answer is: you can't pay more than the cops.  The cops can use their discretion to choose who they're going to charge with what crimes.  So, that hooker might not rat you out for $20, but she will to avoid catching a charge for felony possession.  Narcs make deals like that all the time, and more often than not, they keep up their end (even though it is EQUALLY true that all cops lie to suspects - it's like a paradox).  You can't compete with that. 

Not to be debbie downer, though.  When I made the decision to join the winning team (go drugs!), all I knew about how to buy and sell drugs came from my experience working with narcs.  So, I'm kind of down on street dealing, generally, just because I can't think of a single safe way anymore to buy or sell that way.  So, take my words with the appropriate dosage of salt.
