Post by: klaaat21 on May 14, 2012, 02:52 pm
In the US, as far as I know, all of the prepaid cards require you to register the card and provide verification of name, address, social, etc.
So even though we can all buy green dot money packs without id, if you want a prepaid credit card to buy things with, you have to provide identification.

So, even though buying bitcoins is legal, if you buy bitcoins with your prepaid credit card, you have created a paper trail that leads back to you (if the coins were traced backwards)
