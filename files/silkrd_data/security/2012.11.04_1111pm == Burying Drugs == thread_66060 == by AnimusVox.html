Well, The best thing is always a non-odor-permeable container.
I doubt soil is that fantastic an idea.

Get a container odors don't travel through, make sure it's totally sealed with rubber, and that the stuff inside is preferably independently stored. Then put that into some either well hidden, or inconspicuous location.

Scents higher up being more difficult to detect would make sense, as scents tend to rise like hot air.

Really though, keep your drugs in a place where nobody would ever stumble across them, and where (if found) they wouldn't incriminate you.

Never store all your drugs in one place, multiple caches is always a good idea. (I'm sure you already know this.)

I'd suggest transferring the drugs to a different location. Alternatively, you can put the pot under someone else's deck. Classic.

Good luck, and happy crimes!
