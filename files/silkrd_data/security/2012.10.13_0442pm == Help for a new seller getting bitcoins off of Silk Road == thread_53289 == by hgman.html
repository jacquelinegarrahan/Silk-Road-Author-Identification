Title: Re: Help for a new seller getting bitcoins off of Silk Road
Post by: CoolGrey on October 13, 2012, 09:13 pm
It will largely depend on the volume of bitcoins. If the amounts aren't suspiciously big, you can just send them to your preferred bitcoin exchange, sell them, and have the money on your bank account.

The bitcoin exchange cannot see that the address the bitcoins came from, is an address from a Silk Road account. However, if the SR server gets compromised, then whoever gets that information can tie you SR account to your cavirtex account.

A trick to prevent that is using Bitcoin fog. It is basically a BTC mixing service. It will bounce your btc through various wallets and mix it with other people's btc, before it returns the coins back to you. If you use that, it is very hard to connect your original SR BTC address to your cavirtex account.

You can find bitcoin fog at: fogcore5n3ov3tui.onion   

If the amount of bitcoins are really big, you will need more advanced methods. I'm no expert on that but I may have some ideas. Send me a PM if you are interested.
