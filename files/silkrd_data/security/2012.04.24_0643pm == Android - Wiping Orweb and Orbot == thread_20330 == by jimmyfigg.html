Hi buddy,

I've been interested in this too as I'm toying with the idea of selling Android phones with these apps and others pre-installed but enough of my plugging, onto your question.

First off, Orweb keeps no local browsing history so it would take an experienced digital forensics expert to extract any meaningful data.

Secondly, there is an application called Autowipe which will restore your phone to Factory settings (https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.vesperaNovus.app.AutowipeFree&hl=en)

Finally, what version of Android are you running? As memory serves anything from 2.3 above can support full device encryption which would make analysis of your phone's browsing history virtually impossible?

If you do have this feature on phone enable it, if not I would suggest you use an encryption app for Android to have Orweb and Orbot installed to a secure folder. More details on request.

Hope this helps buddy,

V.
