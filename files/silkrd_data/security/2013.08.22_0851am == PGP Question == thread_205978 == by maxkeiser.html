Post by: GrimWaldo on August 23, 2013, 12:33 am
Remember:
To practice your PGP skillz, send a plain-text e-mail containing your Public Key Block (the .ASC file you saved earlier) to adele-en@gnupp.de. She will respond with an encrypted message containing her Public Key. Decrypt it in gedit, save it, and import it into "System/Preferences/Passwords and Encryption Keys" and send her another e-mail encrypted with her Public Key. She'll exchange messages with you until you're comfortable that you're doing it right.
