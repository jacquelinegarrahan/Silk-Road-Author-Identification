Title: Re: Blockchain Shared Send
Post by: s1llyn355 on May 31, 2013, 08:21 pm
If you look at your own address on blockexplorer,
it will show you where your coins were spent.
The 'where' bit... is represented by the receiving address(es)

For example.  Here's a random address for demonstration purposes:
1NmP1XnJjCaWHSZETCFMTqpUW1fNqMwmmg

Go to blockexplorer and supply the address at the end of the URL.
Like this:
http://blockexplorer.com/addres/1NmP1XnJjCaWHSZETCFMTqpUW1fNqMwmmg

The website shows you the transactions associated with that address.
The incoming coins are labelled with "Received Address" and shows
the original address from which the coins came.
The outgoing coins are labelled with "Sent Address" and shows
the destination address to which the coins went.

Hope this helps :)
