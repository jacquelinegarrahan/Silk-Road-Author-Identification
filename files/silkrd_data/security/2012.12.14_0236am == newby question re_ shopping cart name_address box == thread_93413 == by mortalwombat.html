Title: Re: newby question re: shopping cart name/address box
Post by: someoneelse87 on December 14, 2012, 02:47 am
Only the seller should see what you have typed in there, not SR staff BUT it is always best to assume SR has been compromised & to encrypt your address so that you can be SURE only your seller can read your address. Sellers should then delete that message once your goods are posted.

Post by: monrovia on December 14, 2012, 03:10 am
Quote from: mortalwombat on December 14, 2012, 02:36 am
... etc. and secondly, does that field have to be plain text, or can I enter a PGP encrypted message containing my name and address, followed by my PGP public key?

Yea, you put the encrypted message in its entirety right into the text window. You don't put your own public key in that window though, because theres no reason for the vendor to message you back in PGP. If you send them a PM in PGP, then you can paste your public key right below the message.
