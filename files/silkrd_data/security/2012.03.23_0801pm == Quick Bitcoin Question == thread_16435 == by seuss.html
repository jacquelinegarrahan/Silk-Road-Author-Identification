See mtgox.com or bitcoinchart.com, lately its up/down around .5 BTC -> USD throughout the day. (+/- 10%)
If you use BitcoinFog to mask your BTC thats 1 to 3%
BitInstant / GetBitcoin charge you 5% or so, if you use BitInstant you then also have to pay MTGox fee or whoever you use (~1%).

So if your really unlucky expect 5%, then 11% then 3% off whatever you put in. (Likely you wont be that unlucky)
MoneyPak vendors can be shakey and around here usually 10% or more.

This is why bitcoin's such a better currency then credit cards which merchants off like .25 + 0.5 to 2% per transaction.  ::)
