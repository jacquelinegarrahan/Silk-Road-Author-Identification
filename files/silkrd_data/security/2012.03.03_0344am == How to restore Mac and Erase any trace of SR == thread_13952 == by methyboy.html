Quote
Radium1911- Really? Having truecrypt eliminates my need to wipe my computer?

At this point where you already have scrutiny on your unencrypted computer, adding TrueCrypt now won't help you. You need to have used it from the start.

Here's what to do, for you:

1. Back up your (non-incriminating) personal files.

2. Wipe your entire HD with DBAN. Use DoD 5220.22-M (7 passes). It will take a few hours. If you're very paranoid, after DBAN finishes, run it again.

3. Reinstall your OS (hopefully the disc came with your computer), and then add your personal data back.

4. Install TrueCrypt. Use a STRONG password that you don't and will never use for ANYTHING ELSE.

5. You may want to set up a hidden partition in the future, for SR and other incriminating stuff. But don't do it while you still have scrutiny on your computer.
