Title: Re: Paypal to Viriwox to BTC
Post by: GlenRunciter on July 11, 2013, 01:12 pm
The transactions fees are a pain in the ass....you get dinged 4 times I think:  1. Fee from Depositing Paypal $$ into your Virwox account 2. Transaction fee exchanging $$ into Linden Dollars  3.  Transaction fee exchanging Linden Dollars into BTC  4. Finally, a .01 BTC fee to Virwox when you withdraw your Bitcoins to your SR wallet

I haven't added up all of that yet, but I should...it's probably 10-12% 

Your first two Virwox withdrawals are subject to a 48 hour manual verification hold which is really annoying so plan ahead before ordering .....

Other than those annoyances, I find it very fast...very convenient, and it has been the only BTC exchange I've used.....(the convenience factor may justify the high transaction fees.)

Cheers...... 8)
