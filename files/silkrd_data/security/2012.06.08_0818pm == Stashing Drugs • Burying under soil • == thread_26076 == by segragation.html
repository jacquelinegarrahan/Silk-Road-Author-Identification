Post by: cache on June 22, 2012, 01:42 pm
Please try it with a plastic bag full of salt before you do it with drugs.
You would be surprised how easy it is to put your shovel straight through the bag without even noticing it.....

From hard experience your best bet is to get some 6" drainage pipe and two end caps. Cut a piece of pipe about 2' long and glue an end cap on to one end to seal it. Put your gear in several smaller bags, put them inside one bigger one and squeeze all the air out before sealing it. Seal that into another bag and tape the fuck out of it. Put this package into a bin liner, tie it off with a cable tie then use another cable tie to attach a 3' piece of parachute cord to it, make sure to knot this so that it cannot pull off. Slide the whole thing into your pipe, make a hole in the spare end cap and pull the parachute cord through. Knot this so that it can't pull out then cover the knot with epoxy resin to seal it watertight. Dig a 1' diameter hole 2 1/2' deep, put your pipe in and backfill around it with sand. Use soil for the top 6". Make sure to do it in a flower bed or somewhere where it will either not stand out or be easy to conceal.

You can also put an elbow to the end, and put the pipe horizontally into the ground with the top of the elbow sticking out, or almost sticking out from the ground, so you don't have to dig that much every time to access the opening, but the whole pipe can be deep enough so it wont be unearthed or frozen.
