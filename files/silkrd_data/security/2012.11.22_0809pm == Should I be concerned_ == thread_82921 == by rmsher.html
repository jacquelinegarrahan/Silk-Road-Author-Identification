Post by: Leapfrogger on November 23, 2012, 12:45 am
Quote from: rmsher on November 22, 2012, 11:28 pm
also, if im using the tor browser & i happen to open up IE at the same time and surf on some other sites while on SR on tor will it interfere with my anonymous status?

The short answer is no, at least not in the way you might be thinking. Some do advise against accessing clearnet sites at the same time you access hidden services because a fairly powerful adversary could look at the correlation in login times/activity on your SR and Facebook accounts (for example) and reasonably conclude that you're the same person.

It's not beyond the realm of possibility, I suppose, so it might be a good idea to at least disable the feature that allows other users to see when you're logged into the forum (enabled by default), and/or avoid Facebooking while on SR. You'd hate to get the two windows confused...
