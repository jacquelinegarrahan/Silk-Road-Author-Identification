Hi. I tried to get Tormail working with thunderbird at first; if you google you will get some suggestions. But have you considered that syncing your Tormail with mail application on your mac kind of defeats some of the object of having an anonymous tormail account. On the whole I decided it was more secure to only access my Tormail through Tor browser bundle itself.
