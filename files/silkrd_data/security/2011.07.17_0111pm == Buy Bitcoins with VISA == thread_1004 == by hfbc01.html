I've had similar issues when using VISA while traveling in certain parts of Asia and Africa.  You are incurring a lot of fees is the basic problem.

VISA is taking your local currency, converting to USD (2.0-8.0% fee, depending on your home country).  Then that money is being converted to Liberty Reserve (I don't know their fees but I am sure they are high (5-10% probably).   Basically, you lost 20% on the transaction.  And you don't own any BTC yet!

You would be better to go to TradeHill or Mt. Gox websites and see about sending a money order or cashier's check in USD there. 
Not as secure as having so many intermediaries, but *wherever you are* the exchange rates are NOT favorable.
I'm pretty sure you will also have a 5% "cash advance" fee on your card when your statement arrives.

Your only two choices are A. lose 30%+ on every transaction or B. have less security by sending a USD denominated negotiable instrument to Mt. Gox or TradeHill.
