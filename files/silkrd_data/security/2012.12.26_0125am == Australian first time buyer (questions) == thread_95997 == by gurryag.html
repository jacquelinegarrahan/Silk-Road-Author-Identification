Title: Re: Australian first time buyer (questions)
Post by: Wadozo on December 26, 2012, 02:04 am
Mate, no offense but don't rush things as that only leads to mistakes. You should continue to research things but if you must order, do this.

To simplify things on your first order, use the bitcoin address located under ACCOUNT on the SR site to have your coins deposited into. This address has to be added to the Spendbitcoins form you submit when placing your order. Normally you would have the coins sent to a wallet on your P.C and then transfer them to your SR address when your ready to order. By using your SR bitcoin address, it will eliminate some hassle for you and make it easier but don't do it again in case SR goes down. Just make sure that you order straight away after your coins are in (try to not leave coins on SR). Encrypt your name/address using PGP (Gpg4win or Gpg4usb). Spendbitcoins are reliable vendor who always come through. There is a lot to get your head around so please keep reading and learning so your prepared and understand about remaining anonymous and staying secure.
