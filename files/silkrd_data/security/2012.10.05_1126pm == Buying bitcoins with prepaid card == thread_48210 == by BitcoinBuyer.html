1. Went to InstaWallet and bookmarked the address. (I use a Liberte linux OS installed on a jump drive, really nice, there is a thread on it, I recommend doing it.) I also saved it in a file on my desktop, just in case Epiphany cleared the bookmarks for some reason (happened before). The address for instawallet is http://www.instawallet.org/

2. Go to Bitinstant (http://www.bitinstant.com/) and select the "Major Bank Branch" in the "Pay From" drop down box. For the "Pay to" box, put "Bitcoin Address".

3. Copy and paste the instawallet's address and plug it into address text field. Make sure you double & triple check and make sure the address is correct because if you mess up here your money will be gone entirely. Put the amount of money you want to send in the next box (self explanatory). Now, if you haven't already, I would make yourself a Tormail.org e-mail. It's really simple to sign up and its over the onion network, so you can feel safe & secure. Their address is (http://jhiwjjlqpyawmpjx.onion/). Just click the "Sign Up" button and make your account. Remember your domain name is "@tormail.org".

4. It should take you to a page that has a green button that says "Go" on it. Click it. It should open up a page from "TrustCash Holdings" or just "TrustCash". You will need to type in your local zip code and select a location you are comfortable with. Selecting a location close is ideal, unless you feel safer driving across town, it is up to you. Just print the invoice and take it into the bank specified, and do the deal.

If you are using Liberte, it may be a pain to print the invoice. What I did was, press print to file, then put the file onto a jump drive which I then printed from a computer that had printer drivers on it.

Identification is not required. Just go in there, and do what the invoice says. And yes, the invoice is the piece of paper that has a picture of the street where the bank is, and all other information. Just hand them the invoice, they may ask you a few questions (nothing personal, just like how much you are depositing, etc). Then hand over money, get the receipt, and head home. Job well done!

I checked my instawallet account after about an hour and a half, but it was there. From here, you can directly transfer the coins into your SR wallet, or run them through a tumbler like bitcoin fog. With bitcoin fog, you will get your coins sporadically over 6 hours (minimum). I just swooped them over to my SR wallet, though. I read that SR has an internal tumbler, but for some this is just too sketchy.

I say, if you are confident, and are only ordering a couple personal amounts of drugs, sending straight to SR is fine. But once you start doing orders $100+ I would most definitely bitcoin fog it.
