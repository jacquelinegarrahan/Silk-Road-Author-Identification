Title: Re: 6-8k bitcoins per month
Post by: monesty on September 07, 2013, 02:10 am
You could look at the sellers on localbitcoins that accept postal money orders. The MOs are refundable if lost. Each MO has a dollar limit but I forget what that is; probably $1000. Alternatively you could do a 'cash deposit to bank' where you deposit cash into the sellers bank account. No ID required to make a cash deposit.

There isn't any such thing as 'anonymous and cheap'. The more anon you want to be the more they cost.
