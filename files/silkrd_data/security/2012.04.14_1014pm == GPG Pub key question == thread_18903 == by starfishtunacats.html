It prob. just a feature of GPGTOOLS.  The email adr you add to the GPG key does not matter - it is just there for convenience.  Only thing you really need to make certain of is: 1.) that you can encrypt a doc. with your public key and 2.) that you can decrypt the same doc with your private key.   At some point in the decryption process you should be asked for your password.
