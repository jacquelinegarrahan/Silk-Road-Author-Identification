Title: Re: Could someone please give me Simple steps to using tails on a Mac??
Post by: ECC_ROT13 on August 12, 2013, 02:41 am
Tails is hard to get working on most of the newer Intel Macs.   They use something called UEFI, and Tails doesn't properly support it yet.

Oddly, it'll probably boot fine from a CD/DVD.   So if you have a CD/DVDdrive, and don't mind sitting through frickin five minutes of crunchy CDROM sounds, it'll boot just fine.

You can also find some folks on the Internet that have had luck getting it working with USB, but they had to make some changes to the USB filesystem, if I remember right.   I tried it, tried refid (which tried to boot it, but failed), and gave up and ended up just running Tails in a VM when I wanted to get online from a Mac.
