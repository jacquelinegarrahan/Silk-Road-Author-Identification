Title: Re: Why is it taking these Bitcoin dealers so long?
Post by: Sugar Mama on January 28, 2012, 06:33 pm
Unfortunately, we have only two choices: Processes moneypaks as fast as they come in and get shut down by big green dot (one reason new exchangers turn scammer). Or continue to process at the speed that we know is workable to allow us to operate for the long run. No one can cash out unlimited moneypaks so when demand shoots up it's either slow down processing or price everyone out. I wish I could give you a better answer but at the moment it's either be patient or find someone with availability. When bitcoin prices drop demand for bitcoins goes up.
