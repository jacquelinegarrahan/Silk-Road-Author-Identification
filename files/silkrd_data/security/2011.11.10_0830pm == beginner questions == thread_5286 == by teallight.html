Title: Re: beginner questions
Post by: Isa on November 11, 2011, 02:05 am
If you've gotten this far, PGP should be a breeze.  There are a few threads about encryption near the top that will point you in the right direction.  Once you have the PGP encryption on your machine, it's just a matter of importing the seller's public key, which amounts to copy and pasting into a text file and hitting import.  Then hit clipboard, type your address and then hit encrypt, choose seller's key, copy the entire encrypted message into the text box for your address when you check out.  I've read just about every thread on this forum and sellers have reported that it's very common to see plain text addresses, but why take a risk that's so easy to mitigate?

If you buy bitcoins with cash, there's not much tumbling needed.  Get-bitcoin.com makes it very easy and it's quick.  Have the bitcoin sent to an online wallet, then to SR and you're good.  If you are buying bitcoin from Mt. Gox with funds from your personal bank account or credit card, you might want to figure out how to mask your personal information.
