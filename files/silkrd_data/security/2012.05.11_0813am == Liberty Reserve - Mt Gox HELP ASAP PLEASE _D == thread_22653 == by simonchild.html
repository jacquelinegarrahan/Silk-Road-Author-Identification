If somebody would help me get through the mess of GETTING USD into Liberty Reserve or Pecunix, that would be great, but ironically I think I CAN tell you how to get it to Gox once you have it in your acct!! (more or less). Go to your Gox acct, click on Funding options on the left, then obviously select Liberty Reserve. Then you have two options at the bottom highlighted in yellow; "You can only deposit through our partners bitinstant and aurumXchange." Bitinstant looks like the easier one here, Aurum is far more advanced.. least I don't feel like going near it at the moment to figure it out.. so click on the yellow Bitinstant link..

So with Bitinstant, the front page does just about everything there for you. Select Liberty Reserve as your funding option on the left, and sending to Gox on the right. At the bottom, you're asked to put in your account number- That's got to mean your Mt. Gox acct number, just scroll up on your Gox login page and you'll see it at the top (in small font). Then enter the amount to send (looks like they charge you 3.99%), then your email address. Now since I can't figure out the initial getting USD in LR part, I'm not sure what happens after that, er after hitting the "send funds" button on Bitinstant. Somehow they've got to associate your liberty reserve account with the transfer to get the money out of it.. I'm going to guess this is on the next page when you proceed further with the transfer.
