For those, like me, who use tails, as our portable linux distro.  Will be glad to hear that yesterday they finally got it all upgraded and tightened up a bit.  I've used others, but I still like tails, since it cleans up after me, and leaves nothing for the next time....erases memory too, if you have to shut down in a hurry. 

I always update, as suggested, everytime I boot tails.  I also then get the needed urls and passwords from a keepass folder I keep online, along with my exported pgp keys.  First thing I do is hook up to router, next I adjust mouse settings so my keypad will work, then get updates and install keepassx, then get my files downloaded.  There are several good online storage sites...dropbox is best known, wuala is also popular, swissdisk too...make sure whichever one you want works o.k. in TOR...

Anyway, just wanted my other tails crew to know we got a new version out :)
