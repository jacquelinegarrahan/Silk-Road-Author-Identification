Title: Re: Does mtgox send the BTC directly to my SR wallet?
Post by: ld55 on September 19, 2012, 12:45 am
This is what I do:
1) In the clearnet, get bitcoins from mtgox, no need to take any precautions. Always use mt.gox without tor.
2) Through tor, make an e-wallet at http://blockchain.info/. Always access this using tor.
3) Create an anonymous bitcoin address in blockchain.info, and send the bitcoins to this address from mt.gox. This will pass the coins through blockchain's tumbler before depositing them into your e-wallet, leaving no trail.
5) Send them from e-wallet to silk road account.

Title: Re: Does mtgox send the BTC directly to my SR wallet?
Post by: technopium on September 19, 2012, 12:46 pm
If you are in the EU, I would search the forums on how to do what you are asking.  But in the US, I went to BitInstant.com and selected cash transfer and just put in my SR wallet address as the address to send the Bitcoin to (they will also send just to an Email addy) and then took the receipt to my local Walmart and paid with cash.  This sent the money to BitINstant which converted the money into Bitcoin automatically and then the Bitcoins were forwarded to my SR wallet.

Now that I know the process a little better, I would go through Coinapult ot Blockchain as someone has suggested to further anonymize the transaction rather than having Bitcoin going straight into SR wallet.

I was going to do a bank transfer to; but the more I read about it, the less attractive it seemed and the less anonymous it appeared than other methods.  There might be some quick and easy way to do a bank transfer, but I have not come across them yet.
