Title: Re: Simultaneous orders for the same P.O. Box
Post by: DwightEAnderson on September 14, 2011, 01:40 pm
You will either receive a LL (love letter), otherwise known as seizure letter, in most cases if the quantities are low.  Sometimes you will not receive anything at all.  If a seizure leads to other investigative operations, LEO would still have to assess and expend the money to surveil you, get warrants, etc. 

Since this is a small order, much of the worry should be absent.  It sounds to me like the quantity would not be worth pursuing and certainly not worth monitoring your mail for future deliveries.
