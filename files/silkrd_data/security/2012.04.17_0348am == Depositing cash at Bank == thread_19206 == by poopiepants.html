Post by: dudeguy551 on April 17, 2012, 03:59 am
1) Do you do the deposit at the bank you belong to?
While checking out they will likely send you out to their Cash processor who has a bank locating service built into their website. They will locate a bank they belong to you nearby which you choose.

2) Can this transaction be done at any bank?
Answered above but I'll elaborate. It depends on the site, I tried but never completed a transaction with TrustCash through Bit-instant and they had an account that could receive funds at any chase near me. You don't need a bank account there either.

3) Is there any other way to deposit the money besides going to a bank?
For pure anonymity, no. Technically they could go to the cameras and compare faces if they can make it out. Pick a run down old bank near you.
