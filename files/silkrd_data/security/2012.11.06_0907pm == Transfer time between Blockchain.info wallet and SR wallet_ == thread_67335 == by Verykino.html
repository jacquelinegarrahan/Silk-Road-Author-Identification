Post by: Nakorx on November 10, 2012, 05:22 pm
I don' t know if you got an answer to this but in case you haven't, and for the benefit of those looking at the question, the short non-techy answer to this is that not all e-wallets behave the same when it comes to showing your BTC deposit. Before any transaction is considered truly verified it needs at least 6 confirmations from the "network".
    Some e-wallets, like SR for example, wait until they receive all 6 confirmations before showing you that your BTC 's have been deposited and you can spend them straight away.
Other e-wallets, like Instawallet, show you that the BTC 's have been deposited straight away, but will not allow you to spend them until the remaining network confirmations have been received. I am sure people with far better knowledge  could explain this better, but in layman's terms, that is about the gist of it.
The time it actually takes to get the 6 (or more) confirmations depends on a number of things like how busy the network is, the speed of your machine, the relative humidity..............I am sure you get the idea!
If it gets to over 3 hours and there is no disclaimer saying it make take upto X hours before your BTC show in your wallet, then I would start to ask question (i.e.panic but not panic if you see what I mean)
Hope this helps.
