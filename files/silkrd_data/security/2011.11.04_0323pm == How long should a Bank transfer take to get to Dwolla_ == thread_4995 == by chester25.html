Title: Re: How long should a Bank transfer take to get to Dwolla?
Post by: happytree on November 04, 2011, 03:50 pm
The fastest method is bank transfer via Dwolla to Mt Gox. Basically, you log in to your Mt Gox, and "Add funds" via Dwolla deposit. It will ask you to pull from your Dwolla balance or your bank account. You select your bank account. This transfer will take (usually) a total of THREE BUSINESS days to get to Mt Gox and is free.

Otherwise, if you transfer from your bank to Dwolla. Then transfer from Dwolla to Mt Gox or elsewhere, you're looking at at least a week (business days).
