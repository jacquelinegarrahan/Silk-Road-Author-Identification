Post by: aciddeath on May 13, 2012, 07:11 pm
Here's how it works

1. bitinstant.com
Payment from (cash deposit) -> Bitcoin to Email (coinapult)
Destination Acct = you@email.com (I always use tormail.org)
Amount = Your choice
Notificiation email = you@email.com

Bitinstant will ask you for a pin number. MAKE SURE YOU REMEMBER THE PIN  YOU GIVE IT!! Or you won't be able to transfer out of coinapult

2. Deposit Cash

3. go home and check email address

4. Follow link in email to coinapult page. This is the page that has your coins
Enter the wallet address you want to send the coins to
enter the amount of coins to send
enter the pin number you created earlier
Coins go flying into your wallet

Things to note:
1. coinapult takes 1% fee
2. coinapult is buying at current market rate

It's my favorite way to get coins so far. 4.5% +$1 (bitinstant) + 1% on coinapult = 5.5% + $1 fee
it sucks but... easiest I've found
