Title: Re: Buying Bitcoins
Post by: divinechemicals on June 18, 2012, 10:14 pm
The process with MtGox and Bitinstant is very simple.

1) Set up a Mt. Gox account (do NOT access Mt. Gox with Tor or your account will be suspended, clearnet is fine)
2) Under "Fund" options, choose "Cash Deposit with Bitinstant"
3) This will take you to Trustcash. Follow the instructions here to make a deposit invoice slip. Personally I use Bank of America, it's easy.
4) Assuming you did Bank of America, you simply take that invoice to your local bank and the person at the counter should give you a deposit slip that you can fill out with the right information.
5) Within an hour all your money in your Mt. Gox account. Now you can easily buy the bitcoins and send them to SR.

Good luck and have fun! And always be secure. Switch up banks from time to time, never sign anything (you shouldn't need to, I never have), that sort of thing.
