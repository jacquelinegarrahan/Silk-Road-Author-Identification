Title: Re: GPG For Mac?
Post by: Cgault on October 25, 2011, 04:45 am
I am going to try and answer everyone with one general paragraph: GPG tools for Mac works well for me under 10.6.8 Snow Leopard. On Lion, everything works, encrypt, decrypt, and key management (generate, import, export, verify , signing) via the services menu if you set up for right click access., but the mail client does not work under lion. Big deal.

Everything that you send and what the other parties send to you uses the Public Key block. You have to import the other person's pub key before you can encrypt and send. THey need your Pubkey to do the same. The secret, private key is not for them, and you dont even see it mostly, you just create it with the initial pass phrase, and update it or revoke it with that pass phrase.

How do you get a persons pub key?
