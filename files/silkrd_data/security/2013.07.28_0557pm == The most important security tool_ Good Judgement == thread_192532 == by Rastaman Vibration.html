Title: The most important security tool: Good Judgement

Using good judgement is by far the most important security tool. Even PGP won't protect you if you are going around bragging about your underworld operations to everyone you meet. Don't forget, this is not a game. Your freedom is at stake here. Its important to be security minded at all times and to maintain a healthy dose of paranoia.

Keep you mouth shut.
Maintain separation between your clearnet presence and your underworld dealings.
Use extreme caution when giving out identifying information.
Be careful who you trust.
Stay informed.

Stay safe everyone. And happy 500th post to me ;)
