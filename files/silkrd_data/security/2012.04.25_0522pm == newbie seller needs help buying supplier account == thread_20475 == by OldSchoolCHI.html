Ok, google either "bitinstant" or "get-bitcoin"

They're very similar, so I'll walk you through the get-bitcoin process

1. Go to instawallet.org, you'll be generated a BTC wallet. MAKE SURE YOU KEEP THE URL, that's the password to your BTC. If you lose it, you lose your bitcoin. You will also get a Bitcoin address, that's where BTC can be sent to. Record that as well in a notepad file or physically write it.
2. Go to get-bitcoin.com, place an order for a certain amount of BTC, and choose Chase Bank Deposit cash option. For the bitcoin address, use your instawallet one.
3. Go to the bank with the account number/name, deposit your cash.
4. Wait a little, and in 3-24 hours you should have bitcoin in your instawallet
5. Then from instawallet, send your bitcoin to your SR account. You can find that bitcoin address by clicking on account.
6. It takes a few hours to get to SR, and boom you're done
