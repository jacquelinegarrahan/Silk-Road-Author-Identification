Title: Re: Best way to sell bitcoins in Australia
Post by: k1k1 on May 30, 2012, 04:38 pm
I can repeatedly link to #bitcoin-otc on the freenode-irc.
They also have a website www.bitcoin-otc.com where you can find the latest offers/requests.
For myself i see the advance, that there isn't a central authority (as e.g. mtgox stated that they would cooperate with cops/le) and you have a direct user2user transfer.
Of course this also brings some problems with it as fear of scamers, but their rating system works quite well.

For the anonymity you're on your own, it's possible to access freenode-irc by using TOR and all the people there knows to handle pgp because you need this to authenticate yourself with the bot there.
