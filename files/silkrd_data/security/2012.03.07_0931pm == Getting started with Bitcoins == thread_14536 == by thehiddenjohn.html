**all of this is done on a regular browser not tor network**Go to mtgox.com. Make an account then go to bitinstant.com. Pick the mtgox coupon funds option and then pick the option where u can deposit to bank. from there it lets u pick a few options (chase bank and bank of america being 2 of them). The drop off is ANONYMOUS and they dont require ID. Just print out the information they send to your email and use that to verify any information having to do with account numbers and such. The money will go to your mtgox account. (perfectly legal anyway). from there trade the mtgox dollars for bitcoin (takes 2 seconds). Create an intermediary wallet on blockchain.info. send ur coins to that address. now on the **tor network** deposit the coins from blockchain.info/wallet to your SR address. It takes a few hours to show up.
