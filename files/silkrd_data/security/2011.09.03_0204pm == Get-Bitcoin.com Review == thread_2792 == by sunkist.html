Title: Get-Bitcoin.com Review
Post by: sunkist on September 03, 2011, 02:04 pm
I recently purchased some Bitcoins thru this exchange (www.get-bitcoin.com) the other day and thought a review may be helpful to bring them some attention and for newcomers looking for an exchange providing fast turnaround.

This exchange guarantees 24 hour fulfillment or their services are free. My order was fulfilled in less than 3 hours - can't beat that!

I was looking to make my first Bitcoin purchase the other day, and I wanted to use a Green Dot MoneyPak as I found it to be the best balance of speed, affordability, and anonymity compared to other methods. Reading many forums, it seemed that the best exchange to use was bitcoinexchange.cc, but after I noticed they no longer had MoneyPak listed as a payment option I decided to contact them for clarification. They told me that they were backed up with MoneyPak orders and that it would probably be a week before they could process it. I couldn't wait.

Upon further digging, I came across Get-Bitcoin.com and saw a few (and by few I mean like 3 or 4) reviews where people were pleased with the service, but with so few reviews and this being my first purchased I must say I was a little hesitant. I'm glad I took the risk!!

Get-Bitcoin.com will definitely be my go to exchange for future orders. They charge a small fee of 5% for buying and 3% for selling - and for the most part that's it. There is a 3% surcharge for payments via Cashier's Check, Traveler's Check, Money Order, and Prepaid Debit Card. However, they also accept Cash, BankWire, Dwolla, Western Union, and MoneyPak. Cash, Western Union, and MoneyPak are your best options! Mailing address is in California, USA.

They have a review thread at bitcointalk.org which I will be sharing this review with. This was a great fast experience and I highly recommend to other SR users.
