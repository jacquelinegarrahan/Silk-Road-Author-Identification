Call up Apple directly, and ask to upgrade to Snow Leopard.  It will cost about $16.   If your system can handle it, go for Lion, but the thing is you'll have to get at least SL to get to the app store or run most of the apps nowadays.   You will be able to run most the latest apps, because 10.5 is too old.   You can get Snow Leopard only from apple usually, most of their stores dont' carry it.  Try calling some apple stores in your area, get them to special order it or order directly from apple.   A friend of mine had the same problem, is now living the good life! good luck.
