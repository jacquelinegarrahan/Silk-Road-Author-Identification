Nothing will happen, since you haven't even received the drugs then you can't be prosecuted for it if the police did decide to investigate your PO BOX. You could say "what? I had no idea anyone was going to send me that!". So relax. Your parcel will get sent back to the fake address and then binned.

Note that it is an imprisonable offense in the UK to open any persons post other than your own without a warrant from a Judge or permission from the Queen. Hence it is Royal Mail (under the Queen's protection). So noone will open it, it's already past customs. It will get binned.
