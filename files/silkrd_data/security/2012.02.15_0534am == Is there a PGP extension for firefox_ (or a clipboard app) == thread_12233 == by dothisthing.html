Quote from: dothisthing
...is there a windows program that I could load my keys into and then copy some text to my clipboard, press a hotkey and it will decrypt in my clipboard (or encrypt) or maybe I could highlight text and it will encrypt/decrypt in place?
Gnu Privacy Guard for Windows (GPG4Win) [ gpg4win.org ] has *it's own* clipboard -- as opposed to that of your OS -- that functions much as you describe.  There's even an illustrated "GPG (Step-by-Step: Windows Pictorial)" thread [ dkn255hz262ypmii.onion/index.php?topic=131.msg516 ] stickied at the top of this subforum if you need it.

If you do choose to try GPG4Win, I wouldn't bother installing Kleopatra or Claws as you won't be needing either of them for business on SR.
