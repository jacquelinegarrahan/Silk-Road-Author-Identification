Title: Re: How Safe is Linking a Bank Account to CoinBase.com?
Post by: uhohoreo369 on April 24, 2013, 05:32 am
I use Coinbase. The 4 day process period hasn't ever been a problem with me because I like to take my time with my purchases. However, Coinbase has had a lot of glitches in the past, but I haven't seen any in the last couple weeks. Hopefully they're finally figuring it out...... I have been using it for about 3 months now. Once my coin is processed in Coinbase I send it straight to my Blockchain wallet, which is where I keep it until I'm ready to buy some goodies.

One thing I would like to note is that Coinbase has a limited amount of coins it can sell a day, so the best time to buy them are in the morning. A couple times I have tried making a bitcoin purchase and it says they have exceeded their daily limit. This can be pretty annoying, but I just try again in the morning and it usually works.

It's definitely not for everyone, but for me it works just fine.
