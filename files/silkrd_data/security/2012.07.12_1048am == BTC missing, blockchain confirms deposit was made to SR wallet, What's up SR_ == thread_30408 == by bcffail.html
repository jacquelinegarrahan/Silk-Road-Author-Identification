Title: Re: BTC disappeared, bitcoinfog-->SR, what can I do?
Post by: Darkwave on July 12, 2012, 06:16 pm
To see whether it's bitcoin fog's fault or something else, go to blockchain.info and put in your SR address in the space in the upper right hand corner (search). If nothing has gone through, it will say 0 transactions, and you can be fairly sure it's bitcoin fog's doing. If however it says so many BTCs and the size of each deposit and the number of confirmations, then you will know it's SR's problem (and YOURS, too).

Darkwave
