Title: Re: Custom Seizure Notice
Post by: Glasskandi on September 14, 2012, 02:15 am
As I understand it, your address will be basically under watch now mail wise but it's not like they can open every single piece of mail you receive. Your best defense is to buy only from vendors who have THE BEST packaging. Also, as a precaution, when receiving any shipment 1) don't sign for it. 2) immediately write return to sender on it and let it sit by your door inside your place for a day or two. That way if the cops come knocking you can put into question whether or not you actually ordered that shipment.

However I am not in the AU so I dunno that much about how your customs/DEA works, so anyone out there can feel free to correct/add on to my advice.

Stay safe!
