When you transfer bitcoins to the wallet displayed on your account page, SR collects those bitcoins and tumbles them around before depositing them into your actual account. This means that it mixes them up with several other people's coins and makes several transfers to several wallets with varying amounts of coins in each transfer before giving you your share. This is a measure to make it more difficult to track your coins and find out where the money came from. It takes several hours, but your coins will show up eventually. Don't worry. :)
