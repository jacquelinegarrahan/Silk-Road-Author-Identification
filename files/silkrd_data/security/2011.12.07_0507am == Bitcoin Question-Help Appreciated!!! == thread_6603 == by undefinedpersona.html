1. GetBitcoin is quite popular here.
2. There's cash deposit (at Chase and BofA branches) and cash-by-mail.  Cash deposit will require you to e-mail them a copy of the deposit receipt, but is safe from theft.  Cash-by-mail should be done using FedEx or PriorityMail (USPS) for both speed and safety from theft, but will cost more than First Class mail.
3. & 4. If GetBitcoin needs to send you money -- a refund or when converting BTC to cash through them -- they'll be sending a check.  They may be amenable to sending to a name and address different from that of the original order, but maybe not.

Keep in mind, too, that buying and selling BTC is legal in most (all?) jurisdictions, however suspicious it may seem to some.  If you *do* use your real name and address, you may want to pass the the coins through an insta-wallet or tumbling service for anonymization before transferring to SR.
