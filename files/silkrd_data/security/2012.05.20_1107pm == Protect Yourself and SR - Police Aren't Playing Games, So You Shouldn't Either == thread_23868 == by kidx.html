I think it is important to distinguish between having "a lot" of Facebook friends and an excessive number of them. Personally, I don't use Facebook. I don't like announcing my every move, thought, and bowel movement to all my friends, family, and a handfull of strangers, and I don't like companies who build profiles on me and sell them to other companies that try to sell things to me. Someone with as many Facebook friends as Litz obviously talks too much. Having 4000 Facebook friends is a sign of carelessness and narcissism, and narcissist tend to believe that nothing will happen to them because of who they are, or something similar to that.

2) Litz was a well-known political activist. She had no business dealing drugs. I know that many of you have strong political beliefs as well, and there's nothing wrong with that. However, someone who starts organizations at their college, leads rallies, writes articles for newspapers that are politically dissenting of our government, is vocal on ending the war on drugs, goes on radio programs to talk about how to get involved with causes that fight the government, etc., shouldn't be surprised that she was being watched by either the local cops, federal government, big brother, or otherwise. What I'm basically trying to say is that if you have strong political beliefs, either be involved with the underground movement or the mainstream movement, but not both. The Silk Road simply doesn't need this type of exposure.

Now, I know that many of you might be thinking "So what am I supposed to do, Google my customers and not sell to them if they have too many Facebook friends?" Certainly not, but you should Google them to find out what information is available on them. Maybe you won't find much. Maybe you will find a ton of information. How many friends are too many? Well, I can't give a specific number, but I know that 4000 is too many for sure. But 500 may not be too many, as long as they weren't buying too much, and there arent' any other red flags, I would worry about it. The point is to research them in the first place to get a feel of who they are.

I'm not trying to screw with anyone's revenue stream, and I know repeat customers who buy larger quantities are hard to find. But if we want Silk Road to exist into the near future, we have to take action to prevent this type of exposure. Shit! Can we at least TRY not to announce this shit to the whole fucking world? She knows the cops are watching her, and she still is posting shit on the Internet? What will it take for this bitch to keep quiet?
