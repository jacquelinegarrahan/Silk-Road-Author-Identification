No, do not do this at all.

If you go to the door with marker in hand then that could feasibly be used as evidence you knew it was coming. If you know that you are having a controlled drop initiated either do one of two things. Answer the door, say you weren't expecting a package and THEN go and get the marker to write the return address on it and give it back OR just clean house and don't be in when the postman comes. If they are doing a controlled drop then they are watching your house, if they are watching your house they know you are in, if they know you are in and you don't answer the door all that does is make them aware you know they are trying to control-drop you.

Hope this is of some use to you.
