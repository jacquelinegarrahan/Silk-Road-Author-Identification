Can't use VoIP over Tor it's too slow. You'd sound like Darth Vader talking in a toilet bowl. You could however make your own software PBX using asterix for voip calls and connect to it over stolen or random wifi (through a highspeed VPN in Iceland or other free country.. not USA). Buy a VPS using bulletproof shady Ukrainian or Russian hosting. Or you could make a cheesebox, which is what the mafia used to build to run bookie lines. Basically you stay connected on the other end at all times and people phone in. The box is placed in an empty apartment with alarms/cams to warn you if it's compromised https://whyweprotest.net/community/threads/how-to-build-a-voip-cheese-box-for-anon-communication.70458/ or you hide it at a business and leech off their wifi, or in the basement of an apartment building and leech off somebody's wifi.

You can also run Ostel on your comp and make encrypted free voip calls over wifi/VPN that you change up (and change MAC address)

Depends what you want to do. Are you phoning Julien Assange to whistleblow secret NSA info or just trying to run a drug delivery line. You can easily harden an android nexus 4 to use wifi only calls with no SIM using Ostel or even Redphone + Google Voice, and make it have plausible deniability so if cops demand to search your encrypted phone you just show them the decoy install with no history or proof of you ever phoning anybody.
