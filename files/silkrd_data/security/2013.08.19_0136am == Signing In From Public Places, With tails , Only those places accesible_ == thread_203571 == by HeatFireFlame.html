Since they cant, in theory, tell where in Tor you go from home, if they investigate you or people using Tor in your area, they may still think you are of interest. In other words, unless they know where the vendor logged in from, they can't tell you are not that vendor they are hunting. So, I think using tor at home has risks about helping LE get probable cause to search your location. Very circumstantial though. They would need something more to connect you. To be safer, take steps to be sure you don't get caught with a Tails "persistant" volume with data about the vendor on it.

PC -> VPN -> Tor seems like the smart strategy so your ISP does not know you visit Tor. Buy VPN service with Btc anon.. See astor's "lets talk about security" thread for getting serious about it.
