For small amounts (<60GBP):
Set up an account on mtgox.com using a private VPN/public wifi. Do not use TOR or your home internet connection. Once the account is set up then there is an option to deposit funds into a Barclays account through a bank wire. You can take this account information, go into a branch and ask them to pay however much it into their account, be sure to give all reference numbers and ask them to type it in capitals. The account will be credited in around 24-36 hours and at this point you exchange the pounds for bitcoin and withdraw to a bitcoin address. Downside is they may ask for ID but you run that risk, this works for me in small amounts. Up side is they only take 1.5% for a cash deposit to the bank.

Larger amounts (>60GBP):
Search for Vlad1m1r in the forums or on the market. He offers a cash in the post for bitcoins service. For under 130 he charges 12.5% for under 1000 he charges 10%
