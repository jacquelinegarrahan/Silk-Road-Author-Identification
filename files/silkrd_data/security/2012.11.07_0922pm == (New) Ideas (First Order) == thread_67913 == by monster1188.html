Post by: fatoldsun on November 07, 2012, 10:01 pm
People routinely order internationally here. As long as the address isn't suspicious, and your vendor is professional with the packaging and sealing, you should be in the clear.

Shipping to a vacant apartment is a tipoff to the postal service, as detailed in posts about secure shipping... read the stickied posts in the Shipping forum:
http://dkn255hz262ypmii.onion/index.php?topic=18904.0
http://dkn255hz262ypmii.onion/index.php?topic=19010.0

In the US at least, you can't get arrested for somebody else shipping drugs to your house, if you don't touch them yourself. So wait a while before opening the package, and do so in a different location from the one where it arrived.
