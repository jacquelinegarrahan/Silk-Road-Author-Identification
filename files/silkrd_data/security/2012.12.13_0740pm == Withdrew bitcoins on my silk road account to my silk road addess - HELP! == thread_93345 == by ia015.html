Title: Re: Withdrew bitcoins on my silk road account to my silk road addess - HELP!
Post by: grahamgreene on December 14, 2012, 12:56 pm
Make sure you hit 'get a new address' before attempting to transfer any more BTC to your SR account, and do so every time your Bitcoin arrives. The main cause of 'lost' deposits is people not using a new address for each deposit. As you have already used the current address, you may experience the 'lost deposit' issue.

Technically speaking all addresses will continue working but by hitting 'get a new address' before every deposit, and waiting for your BTC to hit your account before clicking it again, you won't experience any issues with depositing coins.
