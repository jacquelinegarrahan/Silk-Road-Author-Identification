Post by: malacath on October 23, 2012, 04:37 am
You need to balance ease of access with level of security.

A super secure hiding spot that NO ONE would ever find would be to put your stash in a waterproof container and bury it off your property somewhere. BUT that would be difficult to access.

The best spots are inside common objects. When I lived with my parents I had a computer with a side panel that could be popped off so I would hide my stash inside the computer in an unused hard drive bay. I'm sure the cops know to look there but parents don't.

An airtight container, like a surplus ammo can works well when hidden in a crawlspace or basement. Buy several surplus ammo cans and store them together in the corner of your basement, fill them with junk and in one hide your stash. Intake vents work good to hide the smell (make sure its intake NOT an outlet!) A friend of mine actually installed a fake air return vent with a bathroom fan that sucked in air and vented it out a chimney on his roof, so even if dogs were brought in they would not be able to smell it! Just remember that vents are checked so you need to make it so if they were to take the grate off and look in with a flashlight, they would not find anything.

One of my craftiest hiding places in college was inside a radiator in the study lounge on my floor, it was usually empty and right down the hall and best of all was not even in my room, if you use at work you can hide your stash at your work.

Its great if you are able to hide it off your property because when found, it can't be linked to you.

Another good method is to only keep a day's supply in your residence, hide the rest in a public place. I have a friend that used to have about an ounce of H at a time, but only a bag a day in his house. He hid his stash in a mason jar inside the drop ceiling of the bathroom at his local convenience store. Everyday he would go in, use the bathroom (get his stash for the day), buy a coffee and go to work. If he ever got searched the most he would have is a bag, not a huge deal.

If you take your drugs orally or rectally, you can dissolve them in a water bottle (make sure you measure so you know how many mg per ml are in the water) and store in the fridge among other water bottles, mark the drug bottle so you can tell them apart and use a graduated oral syringe to take out the dose you need.

Finally, the best thing you can do is keep a clean house. This means clean as in no dirt or gross smells, also no drug references. If you are hiding a bunch of weed DO NOT have a big Bob Marley poster in your bedroom. Make your home as innocent as possible, hang a picture of jesus on the wall, make it look NOT like a drug user lives there (I know this sounds stereotypical and I know not all drug users fit the stereotype, but the police profile like crazy so take advantage of this.

If they do search your house, you need to remain calm and be polite. If they have a warrant they can search anywhere so don't try to fight them on that it will just look suspicious. If you only have a small amount of drugs in your house like I suggested, flush them as soon as you realize its cops at your door, a security camera is a good investment for this reason, you can get a cheap setup for about $100 if you have a decent understanding of hooking up cameras. You can also buy sensors that alert you when someone pulls in your driveway or approaches your door, this might be cheaper but you will have to look out a window to check if its the cops.

In a pinch you could eat your stash, but only do this if its amount you can handle! There is a good chance you will be with the cops for next few hours and if you start tripping in front of them that would be bad!
