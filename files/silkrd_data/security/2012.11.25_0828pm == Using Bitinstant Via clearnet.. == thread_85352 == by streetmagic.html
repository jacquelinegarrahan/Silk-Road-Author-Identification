Post by: w0ju2hnq on November 25, 2012, 08:42 pm
As far as I understand it, you pretty well need to use clearnet to buy bitcoin.  This is where mixing services like blockchain.info become handy.  Maintain a 'public' wallet that can be tied to your clearnet transactions and have a separate clean wallet on a different computer (maybe even on a different network) that you send your purchased bitcoins to via a mixer.  From there, there is theoretically no link between your bitcoins and your clearnet wallet/purchases.

Also, learn how to configure your bitcoin wallets to use TOR.
