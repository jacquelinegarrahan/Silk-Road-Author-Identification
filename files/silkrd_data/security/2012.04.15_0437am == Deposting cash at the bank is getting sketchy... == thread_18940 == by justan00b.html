Title: Re: Deposting cash at the bank is getting sketchy...
Post by: mdmamail on April 15, 2012, 04:41 am
Western Union (though risky/need ID)
Buy a lot of Moneypaks and trade them on bitcointalk.org or #bitcoin-otc IRC
Mail cash to somebody trusted, or even better get a money order and send it to them.

Send an exchanger you trust a reloadable prepaid debit card. Whenever you want to buy bitcoins just reload the card and they cash out.

Nothing at all risky with BUYING bitcoins. You could be buying stocks and bonds on GLBSE or MPEX or just speculating and trading.
