Post by: 328502E on April 24, 2012, 02:32 am
A friend can work, but it isn't ideal.  You may trust them now, but stuff happens IRL, and it's better to just not have them in the equation at all.  You also can only access your stuff when they are available.

I would suggest something off site that is always available.  When selecting a site, here are some things to keep in mind:
-You want something nearby, so you don't have to travel too far to get it
-It should not be regularly travelled by anyone.  You want something out of the way that nobody sees
-It should be well hidden.  Even if someone is in the area (which they shouldn't be), they shouldn't be able to see it, even if they are 5 feet away
-You should be able to access the area and the stash without arousing suspicion.  This might mean only going at night, when nobody is around to see you

I'll give an example of one such place.  Do you have any freeways near you?  How about an onramp - the kind that does a full 270 degree turn before getting on the freeway.  If it has trees around the edges, this is perfect.  Park near the onramp, and walk onto the ramp, finding a good place to stash it.  The place never gets pedestrians, and it's off site. 

Just keep your eye out in your day to day life for areas that are fenced off.  You'd be amazed by the sheer abundance of them.
