No prob. What you do is click Settings in the vidalia gui, then in the settings box, click on Network. Below you will see My ISP blocks connections to the TOR network. Enable that. Suddenly the Bridge settings section will appear below that. On the one line below where it says Add a bridge, copy and paste a bridge. These will look like this: 91.157.29.225:10089 After you paste one bridge, click on the green "+" sign and that bridge will be incorporated into the big box below that, and it will disappear from the one line. Keep copying and pasting and clicking on + until you have no more new bridges.

You find bridges by going to bridges.torproject.org. It's pretty much self-explanatory there. Also, and I don't know if you really get different bridges or not (most of the time you don't), you can send email from either gmail or yahoo mail to bridges@torproject.org. You get some bridges by return email. Don't ask me why those good folks at the tor project selected these two miserable email providers for this.
