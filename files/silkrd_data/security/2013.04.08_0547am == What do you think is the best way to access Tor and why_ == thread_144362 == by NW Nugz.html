Title: Re: What do you think is the best way to access Tor and why?
Post by: BenJesuit on April 09, 2013, 12:44 am
Quote from: Jello on April 08, 2013, 10:44 pm
Is using a live disk pretty much the same thing? Also how important is being connected to a VPN for security?

A VPN set up properly prevents your ISP from knowing that you're using TOR. Using TOR isn't illegal nor is visiting SR. It's not enough for probably cause. So using TOR through a VPN is for the very paranoid. It's just extra insurance.

Or think of it this way, piping your TOR use through a VPN to prevent your ISP from knowing that you're using TOR is just one less thing that could possibly be used against you. Would it really matter in a case that LEO found out from issuing a subpena to your ISP to divulge all customers using TOR and that you're one of them? Not really. They would need other corroborating evidence to wrap it all up. Which when you think about it, is the least of your concerns at that point. The other evidence they would have on you is sufficient enough to not have to be bothered proving you used TOR. 

If you have a VPN already that you use for torrenting, you can add your TOR activities to it. I wouldn't recommend paying for a VPN to use with TOR. It's overkill.
