I have no experience with this, but here's a method I figured should work:

- Get one of those anonymous Polish IBAN cards (or a couple, depending on how much BTC you must cash out - there's a limit on those cards). Check out SR and other hacker forums in onion land (the ones I saw on SR were overpriced).
- Sell your BTC for euro's on an exchange like Intersango (do not use the site through Tor, instead get a proxy in your country/someones wifi/prepaid 3G card/public internet). You can even automate this with the API's.
- Withdraw your euro's to your IBAN card and cash out at an ATM (use multiple, just to be safe). Because of the limit, go around 23:45 and use the ATM again after 0:00.

Although this probably won't attract much (if any) attention, I'd try to keep as anonymous as possible (wear a motor helmet at the ATM, for example).

The coupons (like money packs and Ukash) rely afaik on the fact that you're a 'store' and cash those coupons out. I dunno if European online stores can cash out money packs. I believe that Ukash got a lot stricter, which is why not many people offer BTC for Ukash anymore.

Anyway, good luck :).
