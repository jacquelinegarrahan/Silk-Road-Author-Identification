Title: Re: Can your encrypted vpn see your unencrypted traffic?
Post by: Psyche on September 21, 2013, 09:15 pm
Yes they can, it is encrypted from you to your VPN provider so that your ISP and other spying eyes can't see. Then it leaves the VPN provider on the clearweb like normal with no encryption without HTTPS/SSL.

Your VPN can only see as much as your ISP. Your VPN provider basically takes the same role as an ISP does.

Therefore SSL encryption is important.

Definitely get one in some esoteric country. Assume any USA or UK, other 5 eyes VPN is backdoored but also keep in mind that many VPN encryption protocols can be broken anyway.

Good luck.
