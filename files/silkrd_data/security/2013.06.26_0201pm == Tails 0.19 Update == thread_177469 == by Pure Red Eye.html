Title: Re: Tails 0.19 Update
Post by: Just Chipper on June 26, 2013, 06:55 pm
Quote from: PrincessButtercup on June 26, 2013, 04:32 pm
How did you keep the persitent folder?

First you burn Tails 0.19 to DVD (too big for CD now).
Then you boot from the LiveCD.
Insert your existing Tails 0.18 LiveUSB with Persistence.
Select Applications>Tails>Tails USB Installer.
From the pop-up menu select "Clone & Upgrade".
Select your USB from the drop-down menu.
Click "Create LiveUSB".
Click "Next".
Allow the application to finish, then shutdown PC, remove DVD, and boot from USB.

This will only update the boot partition and leave the persistent partition intact.
