Title: Re: Way to get BTC online with my credit card?
Post by: four2o on June 18, 2012, 01:55 am
I'm new here and I've been researching bitcoins a ton. I actually know of a way to use your CC or even Paypal to get bitcoins but it involves some work and it's not tested by me so take it for what it's worth. (I personally am going to just use BitInstant) Anyhow, here's how you do it:

You'll first need to purchase a different type of currency called SLL or Linden$. This is the currency used in the role-playing game called Second Life. To do so you would head over to https://www.virwox.com/ and create an account. Once created you have the option to buy SLL via a CC or by Paypal. Once you purchase SLL from this company you can then use the withdraw feature to convert your SLL into BTC.

There are some issues though. Firstly, the company enacts limits on accounts to prevent chargebacks. New accounts can only transfer 40EUR/52USD in 24 hours and 140EUR/152USD in 30-days. However, your account automatically gets its limits increased the longer you have an account. It works like this: the time starts from the moment you make your first CC/Paypal deposit. Here's how the limits increase:

10 days since first deposit: 60EUR/78USD in 24/h. 300EUR/390USD each month.
60days: 100EUR/130USD in 24/h. 1000EUR/1300USD each month.
90days: 200EUR/260USD in 24/h. 2000EUR/2600USD each month.

The second issue is that there are fees for this service. It's not too bad from what I can tell but obviously it's far more pricy than just depositing by cash via Bitinstant.

Keep in mind though there are inherent risks with obtaining BTC in this manner. You're essentially attaching your personal information to the SLL/BTC conversion. Hypothetically speaking though you could also hide your personal information when buying via CC or Paypal though by getting creative. For example, if you pay by CC you could use one of those prepaid CC's you buy at grocery stores. (I've noticed some of those prepaid CC's have issues with authorization though) Also, if you pay by Paypal, you could use a prepaid CC that offers direct deposit and then add the info for it under  the bank account section to make your Paypal verified. By doing this you could use completely fictitious information on the prepaid CC and under the bank account info and thus have an anonymous Paypal account to use for obtaining BTC.

I haven't tested any of this though. I'm just putting this information out there in case anyone wants to try it or knows a reason why this absolutely won't work for some reason or another.  :P
