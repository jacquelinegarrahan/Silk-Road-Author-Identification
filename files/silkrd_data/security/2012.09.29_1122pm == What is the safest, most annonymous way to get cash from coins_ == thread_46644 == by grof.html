In general, it depends on what country you're in.  You need to find someone, somewhere, who has your local currency and wants bitcoins.

I would check the following pages for the currency you want.

https://localbitcoins.com/   <- This is a directory of people who live near you and want to trade.  Depending on where you live, your mileage may vary.  If there are no official exchanges available in your country or at least available anonymously, there might be plenty of people waiting.

https://en.bitcoin.it/wiki/Selling_bitcoins <- Comprehensive list. If no one wants your coins nearby, use one of these vendors.  If your coins are anonomized, I wouldn't worry too much about merchants knowing your identity, because they shouldn't be able to figure out what your coins did.  I personally use a mainstream, taxpaying business on this list, because it is well organized and efficiently offers services at a low cost in my area.  You may have to shop around.
