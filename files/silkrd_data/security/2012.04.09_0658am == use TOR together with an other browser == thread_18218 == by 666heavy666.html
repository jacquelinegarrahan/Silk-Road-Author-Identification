Title: Re: use TOR together with an other browser
Post by: mdmamail on April 11, 2012, 02:32 pm
You could have problems if cookies get messed up, and a google cookie on your other browser somehow crosses into the Tor browser bundle. They specifically warn against this on their site.

Two browsers at the same time if they're different (not both Iceweasel/Firefox) and you are not logged into anything in the regular browser is fine.
