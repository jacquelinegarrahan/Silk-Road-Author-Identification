Here's what I do:

Dwolla -> Bitinstant -> CryptoXchange -> Instawallet -> Bitcoinfog ->  SR

Bitcoinfog is a mixer.  It puts your Bitcoins along with others into a big pot.  It then moves them around in different denominations and then moves them in different amounts into your specified account.  The whole process with Bitcoinfog takes about six hours with very little cost.  It's supposed to make it next to impossible to track your bitcoins. The only real drawback is the amount of time that it takes.  You can also specify that it take longer (which is supposed to be even more secure).
