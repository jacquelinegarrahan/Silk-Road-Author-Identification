Title: Re: How to save GPG/PGP key?
Post by: CoolGrey on October 21, 2012, 07:04 pm
Quote from: Nellion on October 21, 2012, 05:34 pm
Now that I have that figured out- what is the protocol for using GPG when placing orders? Do I just import their public key and encrypt a message w/ my address and paste the encrypted message along with my private key into the address box?

Do NOT share your private key with anyone. Give them your public key.

To message people, you need their public key. For them to message you, they need your public key. Private keys are kept private.

Apart from that, you got it right (important difference though). You encrypt your address with the public key of the vendor. Then you paste it in the address box. You can also put your public key in that same encrypted message.
