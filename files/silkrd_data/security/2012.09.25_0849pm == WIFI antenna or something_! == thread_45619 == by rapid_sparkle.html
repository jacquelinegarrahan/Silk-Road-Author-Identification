Title: Re: WIFI antenna or something?!
Post by: Evanescence on September 27, 2012, 11:01 pm
He's saying if you pay for a VPN, then in principle your identity could be subpoenaed i.e. credit card info, address.

However I think that's not really a concern because why would they have probably cause to get your info?

To me the advantage of a VPN is that the traffic coming out of your house cannot be profiled as TOR traffic.  TOR traffic profiling out of someone's house has actually been used as part of a bust before.

Theoretically TOR traffic can't be identified because it's just HTTPS encrypted traffic.  But in reality, there are sometimes tiny differences that can be used to identify it.  This is also one of the techniques used to block the Internet in China.
