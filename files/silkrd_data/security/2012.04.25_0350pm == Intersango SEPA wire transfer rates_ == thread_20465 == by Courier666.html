Title: Re: Intersango SEPA wire transfer rates?
Post by: Karlingo on April 29, 2012, 04:16 pm
0.65% is the trade fee.
When you add funds by SEPA transfer, Intersango charges a fixed fee of 5PLN (1.2€).
Some exchangers don't charge fees to add or withdraw funds, but they require you to upload ID documents first.

You can also use bitcoin.de or bitmarket.eu. On those sites you don't have to deposit money, you pay the seller directly but they provide an escrow service. The rates are usually a bit higher though.
