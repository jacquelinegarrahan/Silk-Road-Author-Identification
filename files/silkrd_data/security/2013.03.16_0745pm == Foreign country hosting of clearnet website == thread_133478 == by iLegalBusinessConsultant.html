Title: Re: Foreign country hosting of clearnet website
Post by: awhiteknight on March 18, 2013, 08:03 am
What I'd do:

Find at least two hosting companies and a registrar that deal in BTC or work with anonymous top-up credit cards, make your site incredibly cache-friendly and have your front-end website be a VPS which runs nothing but a Tor / I2P relay and a caching server.

If your front-end is seized then nobody can find out who you are or where the service is really hosted, if it's shut down then you just switch your DNS to a backup proxy.

Your main attack vector would be having your DNS seized, which is more likely the more infamous you are. So I suggest picking a TLD in a country which doesn't give a fuck about such things.
