This information is everywhere, and 10 minutes with the search engine would have provided you with the answer. If you're concerned about not being personally linked to your SR activity, then I'd advise that you spend some time on the forums learning, else you're probably going to make a mistake in your security setup.

That said, if you don't want a 'paper trail' from you to the coins, do the following:

(It is assumed that you will be connecting to the internet via TOR for all internetting (.onion and clearnet), and that you will be on a public WiFi network, and not your own, home network.)

1. Set up an anonymous email account through TORmail.
2. Go to bitinstant.com and select 'Cash deposit at Bank Branch' > bitcoin to email
3. Enter your TORmail address on the bitinstant site, and complete the transaction.
4. Follow the instructions on the TrustCash website, and select the bank branch you want to deposit in.
5. Ask a friend to make the deposit for you. Tell them it's for a bill. Or tell them it's for drugs.
6. Get a few instawallet addresses.
7. Claim your coins at your TORmail address.
8. Bounce them from one instawallet to the next (in varying amounts)
9. Send them through bitcoinfog (because you're paranoid).
10. Send them to your SR account.
11. ???
12. Profit!

(Alternately, you can make the bank deposit yourself, but then there's your face on the bank camera.)

Seriously, though. If you're concerned about your security, read the forums for a while before you order. It will help prevent you from making mistakes that might cost you your freedom or reputation.
