Title: Re: PGP/GPG Preferred email host?
Post by: doc on September 04, 2011, 08:30 pm
Avoid any provider that requires enabling scripts to use as doing so might reveal your real IP address. Practically all providers on the face of the Earth will comply with a lawful order to turn over their records. If your messages are encrypted then it makes no difference whether your emails are turned over to law enforcement or not.

Be safe.
