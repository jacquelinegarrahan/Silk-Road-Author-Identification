Title: Re: New to SR, need some advice on bitcoins
Post by: fumanchu on March 08, 2012, 09:09 pm
Yeah, fuck that.

You see, you want to make this whole process as anonymous as possible brother. That means none of your bank account info, no ID, no personal info, etc etc

If shit hits the fan and you're using Dwolla, you're going to be up shits  creek without a paddle.  Not to mention; Dwolla is fucking SLOW! Way faster (and safer) ways to obtain BTC while keeping your identity anonymous.

Try GB:  get-bitcoin.com  if you're looking for amounts smaller than 1k... Over 1k, they want identification (for fraud prevention purposes obviously).

Fill out the information on that main page and select your method of payment. I use cash deposit at Bank of America; no ID required. Create a fake email account (if you haven't already) and use that email address to fulfill your GetBTC order. Once you deposit the cash to their account at whatever bank, send em a picture of your deposit slip and voila!, within 24 hours (usually 6-12 or less) you'll have BTC in your wallet.

When you send the picture of the deposit slip; look around the forums for a tutorial on removing meta-data from images. Make sure you scrub the picture clean before sending it to anyone. Most cameras write meta-data to the file that can help identify who owns it, copyright information, camera model, tracking image use etc. This data will be written to all the files from the camera, so be sure you scrub that to ensure that there is NO way they can trace it back to you, should anything go awry.


I've used Get-BTC for over 3,500$ total and have been impressed every time. If you're new and don't want to get hustled by the MoneyPak traders charging 10%, I recommend GB for a good first experience.

Be cautious, stay safe.

Much love
