I suggest using Dwolla to make your deposits. You can link Dwolla right to your bank account, make deposits that take 3-5 business days, and there's only a 25 cent fee, which you really can't be beat. Title: Re: Using Mt.Gox for the first time. Still have a few questions
Post by: JungleRemedy on April 17, 2012, 01:45 am

My BitInstant transactions have always been smooth.
And I have always used the cash deposit option.

Here's how it worked for me...

1.  Entered all of my info into BitInstant (Mt.Gox Account#, email address, total sending).  BitInstant then produced a page to print out that has all of the bank deposit info.
2.  I took this to one of their sponsored banks and asked to make an out-of-state deposit.
3.  The teller filled in all of the info from the BitInstant print-out and I handed over the cash.  Never once was I asked for ID, nor was my name ever recorded.
4.  In a fairly short amount of time, maybe 30 mins or so, I got an email from BitInstant saying that the deposit was successful.  This is where BitInstant gave me a coupon code to enter into Mt.Gox.
5.  Entered the coupon code into Mt.Gox, got my bitcoins in Mt.Gox.
6.  Transferred from Mt.Gox to an instawallet, then to BitCoinFog, then to SR.

Hope this helps!   :)
