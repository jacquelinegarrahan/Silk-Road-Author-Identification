Learn how to use PGP first so you can encrypt your address and personal information when messaging a vendor. In the search box type Pine and  you will see many of his threads on how to use this. He is also more than happy to answer questions if you can't figure it out.

I would get a P.O. box to ship it to. Just because I have been to a lot of dorms and mail gets misplaced very easily. It is a fake I.D. so a P.O. box is perfect.

Bitinstant is a popular place to get bitcoins. You can use Moneypak exchangers on SR just research who you are giving your money to before you do the exchange. Some charge basically no fee and others are over 15% commission. Research and feel comfortable you know that you are making an educated decision. A lot of new people are in too much of a hurry and lazy to read only to find out that they got their money stolen and have to wait 10x longer than they would of if they researched for a few hours.

I have heard California and New York I.D.'s were the better looking ones, but I don't remember which vendor. They must be easier to make them look like the originals than some other states is my guess.

I would advise using your fake I.D. at places where you have a better chance of the bouncer not caring. So, ideally not at the hottest club in town. Liquor stores in the low income areas aren't going to hassle you about it where as in snob hill people tend to think they have to be super cop. Nicer establishments are targeted more often by excise so they are more stringent where as a smaller club just wants you in there to buy drinks, so they don't care as long as it says 21 and looks close enough.
