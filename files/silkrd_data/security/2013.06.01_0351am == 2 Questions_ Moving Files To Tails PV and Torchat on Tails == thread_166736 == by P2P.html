Title: Re: 2 Questions: Moving Files To Tails PV & Torchat on Tails
Post by: talawtam on June 01, 2013, 09:53 am
When you get to the tails boot screen (the screen where you can choose "Live" or "Live (failsafe)"), press the tab button on your keyboard.

At the bottom of your screen you will see some writing that ends with "quiet"

Press <spacebar> and type "truecrypt" (without quotes) and press enter.

When you are at your tails desktop go to Application/Accessories/Truecrypt

You can now decrypt your USB drive and move the files to your persistents partition (assuming you have created one)

When it comes to Torchat i can't really help you but i believe the Tails devs left it out for a reason. As far as I know Torchat is junk (according to some well respected forums members here) and isn't really that safe. I'm sure there are guides to installing it on your persistents partition but i wouldn't bother. I'm sure in future Tails releases they will include bitmessage when it becomes more established.

Hope that helps
