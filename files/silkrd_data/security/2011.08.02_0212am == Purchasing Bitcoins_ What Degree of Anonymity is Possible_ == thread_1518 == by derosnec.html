I would say localbitcoins with a cash meetup is better.  You can create an account using tor and only communicate using tor.  Some sellers block tor and require certain verifications like phone, but I doubt most do. 

I'd rather have some random guy see my face once a week than a bank camera.   Though outside of densely populated areas you probably won't find anyone selling locally so the bank may the only option.

But in that case, maybe it'd make just as much sense to open an account with an exchange and buy bitcoins there and send them through bitcoinfog to anonymize them a bit.  You'll probably end up paying less of a premium that way too.
