Title: Re: Paypal USD to bitcoin question!
Post by: sensa on October 27, 2011, 12:57 am
The least sketchiest method for using PayPal to get BTC would be using Virwox.  It's a website that exchanges currency mainly dealing with the currency used in 2nd life called lindens.  You can buy the lindens with paypal through the site then use your lindens and exchange them for BTC.  The whole process takes less than an hour to have bitcoins ready to withdraw to your address. 

Two drawbacks to this site other than the issue of paypal are as follows.  Firstly, their exchange rate data involving current BTC rates is a tad behind (ie tradehill showing average 2.8 while virwox is still at 3.2) leaving you with a possible inflation from 5-15%.  Secondly when you withdraw your bitcoins to your address it usually will happen instantly, but they require a manual step sometimes that could take as long as 48 hours.  I've spoken with a customer service rep there and he states that it's usually your first withdrawal that will take up to 48 hours and the rest after that are either instant or no more than 30 minutes.  Do a little google search for info on virwox or look up the thread on bitcointalk.
