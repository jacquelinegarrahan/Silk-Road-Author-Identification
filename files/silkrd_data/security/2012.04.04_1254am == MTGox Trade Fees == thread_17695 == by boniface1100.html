It all depends on how much you purchase from them, but I have found that the fees associated with buying Bitcoins aren't that bad (negligible).

The real cost of buying bitcoins from Mtgox is having to wait a week while your money is in limbo when you perform an ACH transfer from your bank. Mtgox makes money buy pooling ACH transfers with other customers money and investing them in short-term money markets. ACH transfers should not take over 3 business days, and a typical ACH transfer into MtGox always takes a week.
