Title: Re: BITINSTANT QUESTION
Post by: AdmiralSpanky on September 04, 2012, 04:37 pm
It depends upon what level of paranoia you sport. Personally, I like to fly as far below the radar as possible.

This is all in the stickies on the forum, but what I do is to get a Mailinator temporary email address and a Instawallet temporary wallet. From BitInstant with a cash deposit using fake info, I have the Bitcoins sent to the Instawallet address (BE SURE NOT TO LOSE THE URL!) and then from there to my SR wallet.

I feel like doing this is a lot safer than going directly to your SR wallet, but again, I'm paranoid.  :o

Open a tormail account and choose to send your BTC to email from BitInstant when you generate the invoice. Once you get your BTC in your email you can route them to your SR BTC address directly or via one or more BTC addresses depending upon how safe you want to be. Hope that helps!
