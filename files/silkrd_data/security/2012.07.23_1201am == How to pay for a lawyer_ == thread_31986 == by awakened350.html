Title: Re: How to pay for a lawyer?
Post by: digitbh on July 23, 2012, 07:18 pm
Get a lawyer that only requires payment after winning a case. They exist.

Also, only potential drug money will be seized in most cases. So if you have money in the bank, police cannot touch that (without probable cause). Keep records of pay-stubs to prove that the money in the bank is from a legitimate source. Or at least have documentation proving that the money is legit.

Really, you shouldn't keep any large amount of money on your person or in your home. It's a silly thing to do. Go open an account at a credit union or a bank. If you do large deals (excess of $1000 regularly), deposit cash in small amounts day by day. $200 would be a good amount to deposit.
