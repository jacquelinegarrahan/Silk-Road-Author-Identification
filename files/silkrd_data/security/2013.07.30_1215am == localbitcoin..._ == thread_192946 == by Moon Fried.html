Title: Re: localbitcoin...?
Post by: hmdd on July 30, 2013, 09:41 am
I have used localbitcoins for a while and always had perfect transactions.

The whole process goes like this

You request a buy/sale from a seller entering any important info into the box provided.

The seller will give you his bank details for the deposit and then will enable escrow and fund the transaction with the coins you requested. At this point your coins are safe and the seller cannot take the coins back without contacting an admin.

When you have made the payment you mark the transaction "payment sent" and the seller will be notified.

After that all they need to do is click release funds and your coins will be in your localbitcoin wallet instantly.

I think the whole localbitcoins system is very good. I done a few transactions on there and every one went without a hitch.
