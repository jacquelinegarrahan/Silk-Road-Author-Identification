Yeah, don't use your SR username, AND don't send your coins straight from Getbitcoin to SR.  Use an intermediate address to break the link between your Getbitcoin purchase and your SR purchases. 

Finally, if you don't have an email account just for sending & receiving private correspondence, then perhaps try a disposable account at Mailinator.com  The way Mailinator works is you make up any email username and have your messages sent there.  You then can check the messages without having to log in to Mailinator or create an account.  For example, you can list your email as DoubleDx2@mailinator.com.  Then you go to Mailinator.com and check the mail that's been sent to DoubleDx2@mailinator.com.  Mailinator is great for one-time messages that aren't too confidential.
