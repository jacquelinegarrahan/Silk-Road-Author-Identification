Using a prepaid card isn't as secure as a cash deposit unless you take certain precautions.

e.g. using fake documentation or alt information to acquire the card, using a fake address to receive the prepaid card, using dark glasses/hat for the benefit of CCTV when filling up the prepaid card with cash at a shop, filling out forms to acquire the card while wearing gloves etc.

Also, even if you achieve the above, you've got to consider that you should ideally be using it as a one time card because otherwise the transactions are being linked together.

You can do this anonymously with cards, but frankly it's not for newbie buyers due to either the level of capital you'll have to employ or the amount of grunt work you'll have to do.

So...

Either use CIM or use cash deposits at a bank or give the cash to somebody in exchange for bitcoin (P2P exchange). All those methods have minor caveats, but are infinitely better than using a bank transfer or credit card to obtain bitcoin.

now, if you're a vendor, you really should know your way around computers, and linux in particular, so you can properly protect yourself, but the bare essentials to buying on silk road are as follows:

know how to use tor.

know how to use gnupg (it comes out for windows, if you prefer)

know how to chill out and take things one step at a time.


may i humbly suggest that you go ahead and google tor and gnupg and start reading a little bit about it.

all you really need to know how to do, is encrypt a bit of text to someone /else's/ public key with gnupg.

that's it. it's for your mailing address.

another bit of help: use a friend's address, or a p.o. box.

Post by: raveryote on May 17, 2012, 07:59 pm
regarding the bitcoins, just keep a lot of them handy. learn everything you can about bitcoins.

i'd also read about similar technologies such as OpenTransactions and such, just so you have a very solid grounding in the technology, but you dont REALLY need to do that in order to simply buy from silkroad.

what you do need to know how to do, is how to buy bitcoins anonymously.

is there a public wifi near you?

use a mcdonalds or starbucks wifi with one of those unused laptops, and connect to mtgox and bitinstant.

create throwaway accounts there (do not use tor, use the public wifi!) and act like nothing is suspicious.

THEN, once you are done buying your bitcoins (more detailed instructions are in the sticky thread at the top of this forum) you need to launder them, you can just use several insta-wallets (again, more detailed instructions elsewhere in this forum) to obscure your trail in the blockchain.

then deposit your bitcoins in your silkroad bitcoin address to have them credited toward your account.

then you can chat with several vendors to see what you need to do to prove yourself.

personally, i'd use TAILS (a special version of linux just for this sort of thing) and connect to the SILC chatroom, and ask about it.

if you dont know how to connect to the SILC chatroom using TAILS, and are unwilling to learn how, then you probably shoudnt even be here.

you are going to learn about computer security really fast, so be prepared.
