Title: Re: getbitcoin question
Post by: Oldtoker on March 12, 2012, 07:20 am
I also have a Dwolla account.  Dwolla deals with money only, not Bitcoins.  SR deals only with Bitcoins.  So you must use an intermediary to transfer the coins.  As stated earlier you can: dwolla -> your bitcoin wallet -> your SR account.

If you don't have a bitcoin wallet setup I would recommend this: dwolla -> bitinstant -> bitcoinfog -> your SR account

You basically go to bitinstant.com and setup the transfer from dwolla through bitinstant to either bitcoinfog or SR.

I recommend bitcoinfog because it mixes the bitcoins (mixes them up with others coins numerous times moving them around some) which makes it much more (if not almost impossible) to track your financial transaction from your bank to SR.  Hope this clears it up somewhat.
