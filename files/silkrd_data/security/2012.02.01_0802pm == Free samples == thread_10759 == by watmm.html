Title: Re: Free samples
Post by: tordemon on February 02, 2012, 02:58 am
I don't think that's exactly how it works, ianfleming.

Free samples would definitely be entrapment, just as LEO selling them to you would be entrapment. The problem is that, in regards to entrapment as a defense, you have to prove that you wouldn't have committed the illegal act without the interference of LEO. That's somewhat difficult to defend seeing as we're on a website almost specifically for the purchase of drugs.

As I understand the law, it also doesn't matter if you sell someone the drugs or just give them to them; under the law they're essentially the same.

You always have plausible deniability when receiving through the mail, though. And, yea, they're much more interested in catching vendors than buyers.
