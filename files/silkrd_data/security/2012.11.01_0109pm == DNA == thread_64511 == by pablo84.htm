Title: Re: DNA
Post by: CoolGrey on November 01, 2012, 01:28 pm
It's a good practice to wear a hairnet and prevent hairs from falling into your shipments. However...

It is not at all that easy to find someone if you only have one hair.

First of all, hair does not contain DNA. If a hair falls out (or gets pulled out), some cells from the hair follicle stick to the end of it. Only those cells contain DNA. That is not much material to work with, and it happens that the lab fails to extract DNA from a hair. It doesn't matter how long it is, only the end of contains DNA. A hair that is cut off doesn't contain DNA at all.

Second, as far as I know, LE does not have a database of all citizens, only of those who have been forced to surrender DNA in a criminal investigation. And even if they did, it would take forever to find a match with the millions of profiles in it. I doubt they would go through all this trouble to locate a small time drugs dealer.

That said, if they do manage to extract DNA and they arrest a suspect, they can use it to verify by comparing the DNA from the suspect with the DNA on the hair. It can work against you as evidence, but I don't expect they will use it to find you.

Disclaimer: I'm not a forensics guy. This is all just my understanding of it, not an expert's opinion.
