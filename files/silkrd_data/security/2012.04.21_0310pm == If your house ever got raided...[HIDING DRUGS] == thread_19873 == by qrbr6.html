On another topic... I remember watching numerous youtube vids about how sniffer dogs are trained. The main thing to take away from these vids is that dogs can only be trained to seek out 1 specific scent. Ie. only MDMA, or only Amphets or only Weed. Dogs are not trained in multiple scents. However, the dogs are also trained techniques to give a "false positive" result. For instance, if the handler suspects there's drugs hidden under a floor tile, however the dog isn't trained in that scent, the handler will get the dogs interest in that spot by scratching and tapping it while whispering encouraging words/sounds.

Take this with a grain of salt and definitely do not take my word for it - watch the videos, read about it on the net and educate yourselves.

Also, masking the smell of drugs with a stronger scent like coffee or perfume will never work because of the way a dog's sense of smell works - it is completely opposite to a human's sense of smell. Humans will usually smell a combination of the scents that are in the air, like a smell soup of sorts. Dogs on the other hand smell each scent separately, and if they have indeed been trained to sniff out MDMA, they will completely ignore the scent of weed, perfume, your flatulence and anything else you tried to use to mask the smell. These additional smells will only make the LE more suss!
