Post by: pine on April 15, 2013, 03:46 pm

"Hope" is the thing with feathers

"Hope" is the thing with feathers—
That perches in the soul—
And sings the tune without the words—
And never stops—at all—

And sweetest—in the Gale—is heard—
And sore must be the storm—
That could abash the little Bird
That kept so many warm—

I've heard it in the chillest land—
And on the strangest Sea—
Yet, never, in Extremity,
It asked a crumb—of Me.

Hope is a delusion.

It is only when you realize you are in total control of your actions and the consequences thereof, this truth will become clear.

If you hope for a certain favorable outcome of a certain action, you failed to plan adequately and are instead relying upon things outside of your control.

A delusion

People fell for the Obama Hope delusion, because they expected a good outcome based on the actions of someone else. If you take care of yourself, are self reliant, with a multitude of (self taught) skills, there is no need for hope. I fell for Obama, and I took it as a learning experience.

A child hopes Santa Claus will bring them the newest hottest toy. The U.S. fell for a childish delusion that someone will come along and make things better for them.
