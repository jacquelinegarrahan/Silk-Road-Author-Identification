The Bathing Rice Crispy Meditation Technique.


The first time you lay back fully in the bath the foamy suds will gather around your head and start to pop.
this is the milk entering the bowl - an explosion of fizzy popping.
Close your eyes.listen to the popping,do not try to think that your a rice crispy - accept that you are a crispy.
Float in the bowl, as parts of your body touch the bath, think of how many crispy your in the bowl with - all bumping off each other.
Daydream as a rice crispy.
The popping will slow and fade into a muttering of your crispy brothers.

Conclusions :
Milk is warm.
For a second the feeling of the crispys bumping off me raised the question of a suppressed memory of a group teabagging incident.
I was however very, very stoned.
