Title: Re: Quick Poll: Why do you do drugs?
Post by: narcocapitalist9 on May 05, 2013, 05:54 am
I just refer to the "Action Axiom":

All human action is motivated by the desire to eliminate discomfort by use of reason to find the most efficient means.

Drug use, no matter whether it's an actual analgesic, or if it is the opposite, a psychedelic, or a stimulant, or narcotic, is all about eliminating a discomfort. With psychedelics, that discomfort is usually curiosity, and I am of the opinion that curiosity is one of the most painful discomforts that exists - knowing that there must be something else going on but not knowing what it is. With stimulants the discomfort is in feeling foggy and unfocused. With sleeping drugs it's feeling to wide awake and unable to sleep. All drugs have some effect at eliminating something or other.

That's why I use drugs. It's also why I am so excited about the existence of SR and a LE resistant drug distribution network. It's not just the cops, it's also all those doctors dishing out garbage drugs that don't solve problems (eliminate discomforts) adequately, or almost are worse than the disease (discomfort) they are aiming to treat. Citalopram and other SSRI's spring to mind for me with this. Ritalin is a really crappy stimulant too, so many more side effects. Though it seems a lot of people, especially europeans, like dextroamphetamine (and racemic as well, of course), i personally can't stand the sympathetic nervous system effects, vasoconstriction, blood pressure elevation, anxiety. give me meth anyday. or better still, let me make methyl-propyl amphetamine or even better than that, that rare and obscure russian stimulant called 'bromantan' (though i'd like to try a methoxy-, thio- or iodo- derivative.

Even though it has some unpleasant effects relating to vasoconstriction and inducing mild spasmodic effects, Bromo DragonFLY (i has it for sale) is a really good psychedelic, the thing I like about it is it doesn't scramble my ability to think like LSD and DMT do, though I don't mind either other of those. I would love to see what other non-halide substituted *FLY drugs are like someday. And I still haven't had that 25i-nbome or whatever it's called either, I see so much of it around I figure it must be good.
