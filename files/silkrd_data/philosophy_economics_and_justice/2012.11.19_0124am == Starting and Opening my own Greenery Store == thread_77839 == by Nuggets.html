Title: Re: Starting and Opening my own Greenery Store
Post by: opensky on November 23, 2012, 12:41 pm
Sorry for such a late reply, but I figured I would chime in.

I had someone close to me run a dispensary in CO. He said it was the biggest hassle he's ever had to dealt with in his career. You can't imagine the fees associated with it in comparison to selling it at market prices.

Just take a step back and realize you have to pay for rent, licenses, taxes, security, etc etc in addition to being paranoid about the DEA. Not to mention, extreme competition. If money making is as easy as growing a couple plants, you sure as hell bet that there will be tons of people trying to inch their way into the business.

Federal law is federal law, no state can avoid submission.

I don't want to discourage you, because no doubt there are shops that make it, but my buddy after having the life experience suggested I never try it. I trust him, he's been around for several decades.
