Post by: looselucy on November 29, 2012, 10:11 am
Here is the clearnet link: http://www.shroomery.org/11432/Lets-Grow-Mushrooms-Pf-tek-videos

Vid here describes a simple process using 1/2 pint mason jars, brown rice flour, and vermiculite along with a spore syringe to colonize about 2.5 quarts of material (takes 3-5 weeks depending on temperature, strain) whereupon the material is birthed and placed in a "terrarium". Here this term refers to a large plastic container with roughly 4" of perlite in the bottom that is kept moist. This helps to maintain conditions at 95%+ relative humidity. Another 1-2 weeks required for fruiting. In demo, narrator uses gloves, mask and a cheap glove box--another plastic tub that has a couple of arm holes cut out so as to keep the jars out of the "breeze" during inoculation (injecting .25 cc of spore solution into each jar). Gloves and mask a good idea IMO--box a bit of overkill but again may depend on where you live.  So figure on two months to get your first crop which will certainly be a couple doses plus. Simple mods such as adding casing layer will bump yields substantially but add time, complexity and risk of contamination/failure. IMHO well worth it tho as the bare cakes are somewhat higher maintenance and may only throw up a few shrooms at a time whereas the same cakes properly cased will throw up over 100 mushrooms at a time.  Nice thing about the technique described in the vid: requires no pressure cooler, just simple items found in any kitchen. The author hangs on the forums a lot and will answer any questions posted--pretty much a bullet proof tek that anyone with half a brain can do. Just can't rush things--plan on two months between start and first couple crops.
