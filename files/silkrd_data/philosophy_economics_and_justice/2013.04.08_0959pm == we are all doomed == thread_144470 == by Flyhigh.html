Flyhigh seems to have anticipated your question.  The extraction technique described above will certainly work if the salvia leaf onto which the extract is deposited were replaced by a sheet of tab paper.  If you're working from raw leaves, the sheer amount of acetone involved might make it difficult to produce potent tabs this way, but you could let the acetone evaporate down before soaking the sheet or even let it dry all the way and dissolve the residue in ethanol to lay blotter as with LSD.

I have always simply purchased enriched leaves.  I buy 50X standardised enriched leaves online for smoking.  I do occasionally make tea, but I much prefer the smoked salvia experience, especially when paired with DMT.  If you were going to lay blotter, you can find salvia extract already in an alcohol solution available for order over the clearnet.  For oral dosage, although it is not strictly necessary as with DMT, I would suggest a reversible MAOI such as harmaline to help activate the drug.
