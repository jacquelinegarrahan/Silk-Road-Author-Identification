Hey! I know astrologers like to assign all things to the planets according to their qualities.
Herbs, flowers, and also brain active substances are ideal candidates.

If you don't like astrology, please skip this thread.

I hope we can collect some ideas in this thread, and I'll start with my own ones.

Recently, I have been pondering on the correct assignments especially of psychedelics, it all didn't make sense until i got the great idea that psychedelics are never a single planet but actually provide a link between two planets (which describes their potency) .. more precisely, a link from neptune to one of the faster planets.

Why Neptune ? With psychedelics you tap into cosmic-oceanic consciousness, making it more concrete and accessible for you. The 2nd planet describes the mode in which this happens.

Psilocybine Mushrooms : Neptune - Moon
(you can shift realms and reside in them like on a platform. The experience is driven by your energy state and thoughts just follow. Shrooms also bear the moon signature: they like the wet, fast to grow, and fast to fade.)

LSD : Neptune - Mercury
(mercury is pretty versatile, and since it's rather easy to shift focus on LSD it contains the possiblity of many other drugs in one way or the other)

2C - Family : Neptune - Mars
(it's this shattering and pushy experience i had on 2C-B which made me think of mars.)

Mescaline : Neptune - Sun
(I have not tried M. yet, but the reports are all about heat and lucidity. Cactus also bears the sun signature: they grow in the heat, adstringent taste, rays+stings)

Other groups of brain-active chemicals, require a different category.
E.g. coffee perhaps Mars-Mercury.
MDMA perhaps Uranus-Moon but i'm not sure, that's subject to update.
There's a chance Opiates, especially unaltered ones like morphine, might be pure Neptune, similar to poppy itself.
But i haven't got much experience here.
Anyone knows about Dissociatives, MDMA, Stimulants ?
