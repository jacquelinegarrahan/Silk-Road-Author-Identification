First of all, really sorry to hear about your situation and hope you can beat the case.

The ALD-52 story doesn't sound very plausible to us, either.  Nick Sand & Co. did make that way back in the day to try to circumvent the early anti-Acid laws, but it didn't even work for them as a defense, so why would anybody go to the trouble of synthesizing it now?

Seems like your best hope is to get it independently tested via GC-MS so there's no doubt about what it is.  Have you talked with your lawyer about how to make that happen?  And if it's a non-scheduled RC, then it would seem you'd be in a much better position, as long as they don't have any evidence that you were representing it as LSD for sale.  This might be the first time we'd be pulling for somebody to not have Acid on their blotters ;).

Good luck.
