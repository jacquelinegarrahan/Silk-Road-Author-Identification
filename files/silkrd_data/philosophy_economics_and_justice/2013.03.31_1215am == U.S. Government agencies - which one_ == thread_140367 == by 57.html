From what I've read, local police are usually involved and USPS investigator/inspectors are always involved.  If it's a big bust, its a toss-up between the DEA or FBI getting it, they both have concurrent jurisdiction over these crimes.  ICE only gets involved (and usually briefly before handing the case off) when gear crosses the US border.
