A lot of people who claim to have seen ghosts and aliens happen to see them while their in bed, you know... suddenly awoken in the night. They're still in a deep sleep state and can have hypnogogic hallucinations. It's happened to me a number of times. One time I was really close to felling asleep, I opened my eyes and there was someone at the foot of my bed. He walked around my bed, towards me with a knife. It didn't quite seem real and I couldn't work out how I didn't hear him come on, as I hadn't yet completely fallen asleep. So I threw a shirt at him and he disappeared. It was scary as shit.

Another time, similar situation, opened my eyes and saw an almost transparent imagine of my room mate sitting on a chair next to me. It seemed like he was watching over me, but I knew my friend was alive and when I went to touch him, my hand went through him and he disappeared. Easily mistakable for a ghost if you don't understand hypnotic hallucinations... especially if you see someone you know is deceased.

Overall, I believe in spirits and energies being present but I don''t believe in the sensationalized expression of  ghosts in certain societies.
