Hallucinogens don't generally change your body physically in any way.  The mind can be a fragile thing, but I think most bad trips are caused by anxiety about the trip.  Children don't usually have this anxiety unless their parents are freaking out about it.  I had a friend who was identifying mushrooms once, and her sons both ate them behind her back, and spent the night tripping.  I think they were 6 or 7.  They were taken to hospital, but doctors just said keep them comfortable and happy, and they'd be right as rain by morning.
