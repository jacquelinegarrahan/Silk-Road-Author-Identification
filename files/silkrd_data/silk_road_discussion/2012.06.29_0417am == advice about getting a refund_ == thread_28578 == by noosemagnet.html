Title: Re: advice about getting a refund?
Post by: redalloverthelandguyhere on June 29, 2012, 04:35 am
You should have messaged the vendor before it finalised.

You ought to message them now to explain you finalised but not received the product.

I realise many want clean stats so finalise instead of resolve, but the resolve button gives an option to allow the vendor so many more days.

But with you finalising it is down to the vendor now. SR cannot step in after finalising. It would be too complicated a process.

Who is the vendor?

Some are very good if you got decent stats. They might resend instead of 50% refund. but you need to change the name and address if you suspect it is seized as customs must have a database of suspicious addresses.

Good luck.
