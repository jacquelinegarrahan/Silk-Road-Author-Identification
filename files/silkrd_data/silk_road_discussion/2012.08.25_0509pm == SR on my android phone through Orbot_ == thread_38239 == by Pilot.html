Title: Re: SR on my android phone through Orbot?
Post by: Silver on August 25, 2012, 05:34 pm
Key out of Orweb (I assume that's what you're using?) - just back back back key until it closes.
Restart Orbot
Restart Orweb

Sometimes it doesn't pick up first time, just keep trying and it should work. It did for me, at least.

Be warned though - Orweb holds cookies in your phone by default. E.g. SR login - it skips right to the homepage with you logged in. Be careful.
