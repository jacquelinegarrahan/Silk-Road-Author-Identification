All I know is that we started up here to try and do a good job selling product in the opiate and benzo categories.  As it turned out, we did become the #1 vendor and we've just been keeping our heads down and trying hard to make our customers happy and make a profit while doing it.   I don't know if in the future it's going to be possible for just two people to be the biggest and the best when there's a quarter of a million people to be served, but I do know that we'll definitely choose working to be the best over being the biggest and if that means we have to stop taking new customers, or start having a minimum order, or whatever, we'll cross that bridge when we get there.  But we're here to do a good job and to fill people's needs at a fair price.  We're not here for any other reason.  And we will continue to do that for as many people as we possibly can and still have an outside life.  But we're not going to keep working these hours and letting the rest of life get ignored and undone.  We're going to find a way to strike a nice balance and then just keep it that way.  We hope everyone can understand that.  I told Ben when we started that we had to take some days off or this thing would become like a monster and eat up every hour of our lives and then I just totally forgot about that and let it do just that.  Well, that's got to stop and there's got to be a better way and I'm really hoping to figure it out this week so I'll have it in place when Ben comes back to work.  Maybe other people who vend here are well equipped to handle 180,000 buyers, but we never thought there'd be anything approaching that number when we started and we don't have the ability to serve them all...so we're going to have to figure out just how many we can serve and do a really good job and then that's going to be our limit.

PLEASE DO NOT CANCEL YOUR ORDER RIGHT NOW IF IT IS "LATE"...I've had other things to deal with tonight with Ben and I am just now getting started sending out the shipping confirmations for today.  EVERY ORDER THAT SAYS "LATE" WAS SHIPPED.  So please don't cancel it and make me have to write to you for the money and keep track of that on top of everything else.

And please do not write to me asking about the $1 for 1mg xanax sale because I cannot do that until Ben is back and ready to work at the pace that that sale will demand.   We have the product and it's going to happen, I just can't tell you when.  As soon as I know, I will make sure everyone knows.

During this week I am also going to take a look at every product, policy, rule, regulation, whatever, that we have and decide which parts make sense and keep them and which parts don't make sense and get rid of them.   The person who died was very close to Ben and he is pretty broken up but he also told me that he is committed to this business and he will be totally ready to work when he gets back.  I was very happy to hear that and I was happy that he would even think to say something to me like that as I was dropping him off at the airport.  I am pretty close to the members of Ben's family and it hurt me too to hear this person had died, but nothing like it hurt him and so if you think that Ben has been a good guy and worked his ass off to get your orders out really fast and used really good packaging, you could send him a note, I set him up an account on the forum tonight, it's user # 12437 and he's called "BadBen" but that's just a nickname, he's not really a bad guy. :)
