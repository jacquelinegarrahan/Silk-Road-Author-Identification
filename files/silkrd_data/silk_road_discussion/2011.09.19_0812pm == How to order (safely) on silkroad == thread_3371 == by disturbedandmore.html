Post by: CaptainSensible on September 19, 2011, 09:09 pm
The US Post Office does have a way of checking inside packages, but the common wisdom seems to be if you order from a US seller (and you are in the US) the chance of detection is less. 

Yes, use PGP when sending a seller your address, just to be on the safe side.  You're taking a risk having your package sent to any address, so you have a reason to be nervous.  But using a trusted seller with a good track record means you are probably safe.
