Title: Re: FE problem
Post by: Oldtoker on September 17, 2012, 08:04 pm
There is no "Finalize Early" button because Vendors are not suppose to ask you to finalize early.  Many still do it.  Especially for those that have few or no purchases.  It's actually quite common.  Just make sure that if you finalize early that you don't give any feedback.  When you finalize just hit the "Silk Road" symbol in the upper left and it will exit you without requiring feedback.  Do the feedback when you receive the package.  Once you get a few purchases (five or more)I would refuse to finalize early any more.  There are Vendors who do not require it.  This is for your safety.

It's really up to the vender if he wants to request it, and he needs to message you if he wants to, no matter what it says on his vendor page.
