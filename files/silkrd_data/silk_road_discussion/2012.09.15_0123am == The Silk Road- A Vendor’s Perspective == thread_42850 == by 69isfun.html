Title: The Silk Road- A Vendor’s Perspective
Post by: 69isfun on September 15, 2012, 01:23 am
I’m keeping my SR username off the forums for now, so what you see here does not match my vendor user name.

I want to give a candid review from a rookie vendor (130+ transactions) relating my experiences with buyers here on Silk Road. Out of my 130 plus transactions, only two have gone to resolution.  And one was due to a shipping extension for international delivery.  I’ve had one buyer claim they never received a shipment when I’m pretty sure they did.  I sent them more product free of charge, giving the buyer the benefit of the doubt.  Perhaps they never did receive it?

So far so good.  I’m impressed with the escrow system, and I’m impressed with the Silk Road community.

Here’s to free markets.
