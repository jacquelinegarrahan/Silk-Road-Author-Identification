Post by: bladen on July 06, 2012, 07:37 pm
I am not defending this vendor's authenticity because I have no idea who you are dealing with, but I can give you some general knowledge that might help you out. These vendors are not machines, this website is not amazon.com. You aren't dealing with a corporation. Most of these vendors are small teams of people, or simply one person, and they ALL have lives outside of the silk road. They ALL encounter problems, small or large, and these problems can appear at any given time. The last thing a vendor needs is for us buyers to constantly message them, especially after our orders have been placed.

Check his review threads, read his feedback, open a thread about him in the rumor mill to see what other users have to say about him. BE PATIENT. Like I said, these sellers can get very busy at any time.
