Title: Re: How does one go about getting on the farmers market or similar?
Post by: OldGuard on February 09, 2012, 01:24 pm
  FM is a site that has been evolving for about 8 years now, it started out as a mailing list then a forum until it made its first selling site then changed its name when it started to focus more on resellers instead of being used by its creator for selling his product. There is actually two of them the FM for newbies to prove they can be trusted then the PFM  (private farmers market) for established clients.

    It is mostly a Cannabis site with only limited hallucinogens. It is nothing like SR as they are VERY strict in what is allowed to be sold on there with weed or extracts (lots of Maroc hash) so you will not find any of the hard stuff available there. Also they do not use Bit coins, it is pecunix or cash.
