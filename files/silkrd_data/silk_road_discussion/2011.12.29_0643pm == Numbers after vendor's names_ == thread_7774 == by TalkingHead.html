Title: Re: Numbers after vendor's names?
Post by: zomgwtfbbq on December 29, 2011, 09:29 pm
One other thing to keep in mind is the vendor's *recent* feedback.  It seems there have been cases lately of long-time vendors that had good reputations suddenly going tits-up for whatever reason - so even if someone has 99% positive from 250+ transactions, if you start seeing a lot of 1/5 ratings come in, or if the vendor hasn't done anything at all in the last couple of months, it may (and it may not - use your best judgment) be cause for concern.
