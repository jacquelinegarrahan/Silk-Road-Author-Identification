Post by: fatalz on December 31, 2011, 04:21 pm
Quote from: StonerGirl89 on December 31, 2011, 01:18 pm
made my first purchase 2 days ago, its still sitting in processing but the user has been on and active, is this normal? the product was pulled off the guys listings when i went to look at his page about a hour later what i bought had been took off his listings but anyways how do i finalize and when?

One reason why the listing disappeared would be that you bought the last of his stock. And you shouldn't finalize until you receive the product. A delay is probably either the seller is backed up on orders, or he is just getting the package ready to be shipped, and waiting on the post to pick it up before marking it as "In Transit".
But the way to finalize is to go to your orders (top of page on SR) and click finalize, give it a rating 1-5 and type in a comment/feedback about the product. However, you cannot finalize until the seller has marked the product as "In Transit". Once he does, a finalize link will appear. But take note that finalizing is final (hence the name), and once you do it you can't get that money back, which is why you should always wait until you receive the product to avoid getting scammed.
