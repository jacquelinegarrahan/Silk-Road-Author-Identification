Title: Re: Cost of vending
Post by: PepperidgeFarm on August 24, 2013, 09:17 am
You can choose to have the SR commission added to the listing so you do not pay that the buyer does. Hedging is roughly 4% "I suggest you hedge" Charge the buyer for shipping. Not sure of your shipping rates out there but make a small profit on your cheapest shipping. In the states it would be "first class mail" then on your next shipping option "priority here" make a little money "I mark that up $10 US" and then we have next day "express" I mark that up too $10 US" That covers your shipping. Now you have to worry about scammers. And there are a ton of them that will try to extort a new seller. I really suggest you sell small quantities and build a good reputation for selling a quality product and shipping hella fast. Thats the secret to repeat loyal customers. If you get them the product faster than anyone else you can sell for a premium. People are more than willing to pay extra for the security of knowing its coming and its coming fast. Selling your coins, that the tough part. That can really hold up your business. You really need to have 1 months worth of product on hand if your serious about being a vendor. It can take 3 weeks to get paid by some customers and then you have to convert that btc to cash which could be a difficult venture. Sorry I did not organize that better for you, but I think I hit all your points. You have any questions about anything contact me on SR PepperidgeFarm. Ill be happy to help you out a little. Remember the real secret is LOGISTICS.
