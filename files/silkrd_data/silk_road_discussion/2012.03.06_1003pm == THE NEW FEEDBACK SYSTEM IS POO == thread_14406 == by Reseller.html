Title: Re: THE NEW FEEDBACK SYSTEM IS POO
Post by: anarcho47 on March 07, 2012, 01:33 am
I am with Reseller here on some points, and for different reasons.

I have no problem with capping the displayed transactions at 300 - this is a smart move in my eyes.  I understand from DPR's point that capping the displayed transactions at 300 but then having 2 months of feedback history (which might contain 1000 transactions) is kind of counterintuitive.  That being said, I would rather see a number limit on displayed feedback (like 200 or so) than a time limit anyway.

I do think that getting rid of the time display and randomizing feedback is going to end up hurting buyers.  The feedback tells a story when it's chronological, and it's a lot easier to nip a scammer in the bud (as a buyer) when you can see what's been happening for the last week.  With the new system, the feedback that the scammer garnered in his quest to gain trust will appear to be new.  This is dangerous, and will probably cost a lot of buyers a lot of money.
