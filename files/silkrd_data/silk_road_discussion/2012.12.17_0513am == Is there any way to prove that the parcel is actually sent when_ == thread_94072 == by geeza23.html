Title: Re: Is there any way to prove that the parcel is actually sent when?
Post by: avast on December 17, 2012, 05:53 am
There is no real way to prove a package was sent without tracking. And unfortunately yes there are always sellers that selectively scam people. The best option in an instance like that is simply resolve the purchase (get 50% back) and if something smells fishy provide poor feedback.

Best recommendation would be to NEVER finalize early. Also stick with sellers that have been on SR for a long time or provide tracking information for packages. Reading up on sellers on the forums beforehand is also valuable to weed out selective scammers.
