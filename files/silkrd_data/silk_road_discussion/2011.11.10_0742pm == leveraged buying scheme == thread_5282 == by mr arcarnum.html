What you described is escrow.  It is designed to be that man in the middle.  It's a new thing, for vendors to request that it be lifted, bypassed, finalized early.   Escrow is 'the man in the middle.'  We don't need to find a new way to replace escrow, we just need to restore escrow, and find a new way for vendors to cope with deadbeats.  New people have just as much right to escrow as anybody else.
