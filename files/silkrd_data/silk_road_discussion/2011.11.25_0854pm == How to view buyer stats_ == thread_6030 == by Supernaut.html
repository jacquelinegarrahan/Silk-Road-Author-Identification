Buyer stats are worthless and here's why:

What scammers like 'williamgates' do is just finalize early without asking you to, and make it seem like the deal went well and completed, then a week later starts complaining and making demands lest he ruin your feedback. That way it stays out of the resolution process and he doesn't have to worry about having a refund on his stats. With me, I opened my big mouth and let slip that I no longer had his DCN, which just made it easier for him.

Another problem is that the buyer will demand that you cancel the order instead of him sending it to resolution. If you make the rookie mistake I did of obliging, you've refunded him and it doesn't show as a refund on his stats.

This site HAS TO HAVE buyer feedback for it to live up to its promise. Ebay has buyer feedback, so should SR.
