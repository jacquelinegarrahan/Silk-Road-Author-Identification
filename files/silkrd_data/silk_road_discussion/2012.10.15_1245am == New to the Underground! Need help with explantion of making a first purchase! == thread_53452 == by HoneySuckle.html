Your best bet is not to use money from paypal at all. You would be better off looking at the Account-area of your SR account and copying down the bitcoin address it lists.

That covers your first question.

2) No exchange will accept paypal, because paypal has a strong history of immediately undoing any transfer of funds should the buyer of bitcoins claim to have never received it.

3) A bitcoin wallet is, technically speaking, a collection of the cryptographic private-keys used in determining the ownership of a particular bitcoin address. Less technically, it's where all your bitcoins are stored.

4) No, but there is a thread created by shroomiester that is a great help.
