Post by: psychedelicmind on April 01, 2013, 12:04 pm
Lucky you, heading off. You will have a great time in South East Asia; first off, in the big cities, be VERY careful who you trust. I can't say that enough. Pharmacies etc. will hand out valium, viagara etc no hassle at all, but i'd recommend sourcing other drugs far away from cities in the more rural areas.

A few years ago, a friend of mine was in Bangkok and a taxi drivers asked him if he wanted some hash. He was delighted to hear of the hash as he is a very keen smoker. So, the taxi driver made a quick phone call and within 15 minutes, my friend had about an ounce of hash in his hand. Anyway, they were driving around and my friend noticed they were going the wrong direction for where he was headed to. A couple of minutes later, there was a Police checkpoint and my friend got searched and arrested for the hash. The taxi driver had arranged for this search and my friend had to bribe the police with a lot of money (Thailand is notoriously strict with their drug laws). Presumably, the taxi driver got a cut of the bribe money. It was a lot of hassle for my friend and the expense of it wasn't only out of his own pocket, which was a pain in the arse, as he had to explain his situation to his family. By the way, I am not trying to scare monger you or anything like that. I am just giving you a heads up to have your wits about you.

If you are into shrooms, I would highly recommend trying them in Thailand. They are really good quality and fairly freely available in the form of milkshakes in places where full moon parties etc will be held.

I don't really have much other advice for you, but I hope you have the time of your life over there. You'll be blown away by the whole place :)

If you'll only buy off a white guy,you prob won't find anything to buy in Cambodia...So here's some stuff i can add..In Thailand i was able to buy speed ,advertised as cocaine.From a street kid for $60 a gram.He also had smoke .I tried haggling but he stuck to his price..Didn't buy the smoke .But hace heard from many its usually dirt weed.

Was in Cambodia .Whats often heard or read is ,that alot of the tuk-tuk drivers will set you (foreigners) up ,ie (police bust) .I was their for the entire month ,Delt with several didfferent locals.And had no suck problem in Phnom Penh and Sihanoukville.I tried to deal only with young people,but a few times .I was referred to older guys (whi in fact had the weight and the harder stuff).Most Khmer's are just trying to make some
extra money.And by the end of your stay their ,you'll be so tired of people trying to sell you shit,sunglasses ,drugs ,gitls,trips to the sites ect.Be prepared to be stared at(sized up),its what everybody does.But you get used to it after a few days.

Ok so Cambodia,The best moment is right when you land ,and walk out of the airport(phnom penh).And get a tuk tuk or ride to your hotel..I was like holy fuck,i was by myself and equate it to landing on a different planet.

So the drugs ,you'll get offered pot if you walk along the riverfront,where the resteraunts are.Whoever offers it (tuk tuk and noto guys),also knows where to get opium,china white and most likely ice.Most of the dealer(tuk tuk drivers) are just north of the weekend market square.Think around the 6 blocks near KFC.,accross from the boats.Just walk along ,it will come to you..Prices are as follows and are pretty standard

China White (hard off a block like chalk) pure white or lite tan $100 gram ,you won't get it cheaper trust me.
Opium $25 or 30 gram.The'll try for $40 sometimes ,only go $30 max its everywhere..
Local smoke is used for cooking ,but can be purchased for $20 a quarter at happy pizza,i found it to be pretty medium 50/50 indica sativa.It was ok ,not bad.

There's 1 pharmacy that sells morphine sulphate capsules ($5 each),and dihydrocodiene($1 each) the rest in town don't because of foreigners buying and abusing them ,Ketamine (500 ml for $20) is avaiable at pharmacy on the outskirts.of main tourist area.It you want their location message me..I was able to find oxyfast oxycontin, at (1place)but they won't sell it to white foreigners,my german friend got it for me only once .Faked some symptoms and a story.

In Sihanoukville,( a place you must visit) IMO...Dealers sit out in front of Monkey Republic (backpacker resteruant),and sell opium and china white and smoke .You will be offered if you walk by.The quality and prices are the same.The pharmacy only has standard xanax and codeine.I ended up buying 2 1/2 grams during my entire trip..But i like to smoke it,if you shooting or snorting it will last you a long time .Was for sure the best i've ever tried.(harm reduction"tiny bumps " "not lines!")

If you ave any interest in meeting girls,make sure to bring collared shirts and nice shorts or pants.If you dress like a bum (i do all the time) only shorts and t-shirts.,you will be treated differently.Also if you do hit Sihanoukcille,go to the dolphin bar after !0:30 pm any night.The best party l.ots of college kids adults,and hot local girls,getting hammered.A friendlty attitude,and a collared shirt and your laid. "Edited" to add  Sihanoukville has like kinda a full moon party every night on the beach,with some people who dress up(costumes).Fireworks,guys who juggle fire,They also have boats that go out with Dj's and a party on board..They were advertising "Full moon boat party with western DJ's" .The day i left on a big sign ..The party kinda depends on how many people are in town ,it happens every night .But the weekends are always the best .

Most importantly ,especially in Phnom Penh.Be discreet about using your drugs.Theirs LOTS of cops ,in the riverfront..Hope some of this helps..
