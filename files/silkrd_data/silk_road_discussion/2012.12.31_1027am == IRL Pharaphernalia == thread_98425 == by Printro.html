So yesterday morning I had the horrific experience of a friend dropping, which resulted in smashing my gac pipe, (the pipe I have had since it was still legal to buy and sell in my state/province/ territory)

2 days in wired and looking to continue I had a small freak out.  How is it that I can buy my illegal goodies locally when needed, but I dont know where to buy a freakin glass pipe from!

Deciding that I couldn't let my world end in that terrifying moment, I hopped onto the web (using the tor network) to find where I could source a new pipe now deemed illegal to sell in my neighborhood.   Obviously buying and shipping wouldn't help the predicament I had found myself in for that moment.

And  for obvious reasons no one gave the names of stores away, there were a couple of hints, that led to a two hour mission while wired, running through the city to find one (oh heaven).

I did eventually find one and that has brought me happily to be able to celebrate into the new year (and not one of those BS valentines rose glasses).  It also got me thinking - how could we start up a network for others that find themselves in the same predicament?  Knowing LE's get on SR, how would/ could we set up a little, 'how to find bibs and bobs in your area network'.

Again I have no idea how to set that up without some LE reading and in turn our nice little stores getting done.  But wouldn't it be great to know where to go when your world crumbles like mine, or where to go when your on holidays and cant take paraphernalia on the plane?

How is that that we now live in a world where I can walk down the road and buy my party substances (granted it would be street shit, but will get me by nonetheless) and getting the paraphernalia is harder?

I see the massive issues:
We cant know who is an LE and who is a trusted forum member - thus putting stores in danger
Giving up any city/town information is dangerous and helps remove anonymity.

I see these issues and realise it might just be a dream, but has anyone else run into the same issues and been like, "faaark what do I do now"? 

or any ideas on making an extremely tight information centre - just for pharaphernalia?  I guess its too risky, I see that, just curious for your thoughts
