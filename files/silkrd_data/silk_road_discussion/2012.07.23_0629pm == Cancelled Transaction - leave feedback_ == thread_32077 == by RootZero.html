Title: Re: Cancelled Transaction - leave feedback?
Post by: digitbh on July 23, 2012, 06:57 pm
Just be honest with your feedback on how the transaction went (even though it was cancelled). Express your concerns about the various issues, if they were the vendors fault.

If the vendor has issues than other potential customers have a right to know. If the issues were on your end, and the vendor worked with you to cancel the order then give them positive feedback and say they're doing things right.
