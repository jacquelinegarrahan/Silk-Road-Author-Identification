Title: Re: just how easy is it nowadays to get bitcoins into my SR account
Post by: beargrylls on November 28, 2012, 08:48 pm
Its very easy... the easiest was is moneygram

what you do is get a block chain wallet, (free) and then go to bitinstant (there is a 3.99% fee here for using the service) and choose moneygram send to bitcoin address, add the address from your block chain wallet to the bitcoin address field, add your email (use tormail here if possible) then proceed to use the zip zap part of checkout, here you will see zip zap charges a $3.95 fee, and choose your cash deposit place. You will be sending a moneygram to the company zip zap who then pays bitinstant who then pays mt.gox and you get a mtgox gift code. Log into your mt.gox account and transfer the money in your account (it will still be in USD format now) buy the bitcoins with your funds (under "trade") on mtgox. and send those back to your block chain wallet address (you can choose for mtgox to add extra privacy by mixing in your order more for a 0.005BTC fee which isn't a lot so just do it) and then transfer the money from your block chain wallet to your SR acount...

These transactions make it hard to trace because it is going in and out of everywhere plus block chain and mtgox mixing up your transactions. Hope this helps!
