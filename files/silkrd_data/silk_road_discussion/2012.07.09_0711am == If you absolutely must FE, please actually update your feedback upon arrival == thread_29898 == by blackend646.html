Title: If you absolutely must FE, please actually update your feedback upon arrival
Post by: blackend646 on July 09, 2012, 07:11 am
Has anyone else ever been in this situation?

You see a listing for a product you've been dying to try, at a price you can afford and being sold by a vendor with 100% feedback. You start to get excited, check out the vendors page and see they have a sizeable amount of transactions. Finally! The product you've been dying to try (Ketamine & LSD in my case) is within your grasp.

You scroll down, and see the vendors feedback is a wall of "FE - will update when received". Nearly every single piece of feedback, days, weeks, or months old is exactly that.


If you really feel the need to bypass your only means of protection on here, that's your right. But if you don't update your feedback when you receive the product, or when the vendor screws you over, it messes things up for everyone. How is anybody supposed to know if a vendor is actually trustworthy if all of the feedback on his page is "FE WILL UPDATE LATER"?

 How is a legitimate vendor that you so generously decided to float your money to, and actually didn't decide to fuck you over supposed to expand his/her customer base if everyone who finalized early for them doesn't mention that they actually received their order?


tl;dr

If you are going to finalize early, which you shouldn't, please actually update your feedback when you do or don't receive your package.
