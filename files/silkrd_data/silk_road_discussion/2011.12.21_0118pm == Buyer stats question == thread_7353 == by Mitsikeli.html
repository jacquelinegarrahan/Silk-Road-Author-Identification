Title: Re: Buyer stats question
Post by: gainslayer on December 22, 2011, 08:45 am
I had the same issue after my first two orders -- one was refunded 100%, the other, completely successful.  This seems like it should be a 50% rate.  However, since my refunded order cost substantially more than my successful one, I ended up with around an 85% refund rate.  I believe the refund rate is calculated by dividing the (amount refunded) by the (total amount of all your orders).  Hope this helps!
