Title: Re: Is 2C-B that much better than all the other 2Cs?
Post by: DiamondSky on March 22, 2013, 06:36 am
To answer the original question, 2CB is by no means magical but it is fun when taken in the right context. My experience with it seems best described as a pleasant body high and at higher does visuals that are more like slapping on a pair of special effects goggles where you see the world diferently but don't feel the world differently. In other words you keep a pretty clear head but get some good visual fun. Taken as a replacement to LSD most people would consider it lame. Taken for it's own properties it can be quite fun. I personally enjoy it at the end of a MDMA roll. Takes the urge to drop more MDMA away and lets me ride out the roll into another sort of trip.

Normally when I dose it on it's own it's not going to make me have any world shattering revelations but it makes watching cartoons with the wife more fun. I've dosed it up high enough where it definitely gets mental and time starts breaking down while reality fractures into incoherent slices but even then it wasn't anything like a locked loop bad acid trip. I knew it was the drug, I knew it would pass so it was easy to enjoy it.

If you want more info I have a little 2CB thread that stays pretty active at http://dkn255hz262ypmii.onion/index.php?topic=106130.0. I don't sell the stuff or even suggest everyone try it but it is fun as long as you don't go into it thinking it's a cross between LSD and MDMA because it's far from that.
