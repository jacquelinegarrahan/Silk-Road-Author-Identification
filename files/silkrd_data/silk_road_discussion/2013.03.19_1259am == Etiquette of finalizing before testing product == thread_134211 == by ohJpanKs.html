It's your call and it depends on the drug.  For instance many MDMA buyers will do a marquis test before finalizing.  In general it's a good idea not to sit on your butt too long before finalizing, releasing funds helps the suppliers / vendors re-stock and helps with their cash flow.  Also be careful not to let your orders auto-finalize because that is a stat that vendors do not like seeing on your purchasing stats.

Title: Re: Etiquette of finalizing before testing product
Post by: Looker on March 19, 2013, 04:42 pm
If there is some sort of quick test that can be performed to validate that the chemical is what was advertized (MDMA, Ketamine, Opiates, etc) I will usually verify that the test checks out and finalize within 24-48 hours. Generally as soon as I receive it, but there are some times when I am not able to get to the destination the day that the package arrives.

Generally it's the responsibility of the vendor to get you the product. This said, that constitutes grounds for finalizing. Now if the vendor sent product that was inadequate for some reason (See Nomad's example) then that should be something you take up with that vendor to see what they can do to help correct the issue. If a LOT of users are seeing the same thing it will be very likely that it will show up in the forums and the vendor will either correct it, or just completely bail out.

I just try to use reasonably good vendors and extend them whatever courtesies I can to ensure a smooth transaction, if after that they do a crappy job I simply move on to another vendor, if it's bad enough I may comment in their review thread, or start another thread to warn other users of their service/product/practices.
