Title: Re: 1st time buyer, help?!
Post by: Mickie496 on August 11, 2012, 12:49 am
Unfortunately no, they probably will not trust you at first. What they'll want you to do is FE, Finalize early, where you pay before the product arrives. It's strongly discouraged on the forums as you're paying for something you don't even have yet, but it's pretty much unavoidable for a first time buyer, unless you buy some other smaller things of the Marketplace first.

If you go with a trusted and respectable vendor you should be fine, just check there long term AND short term trade ratings, don't FE to someone who is relatively unknown.
