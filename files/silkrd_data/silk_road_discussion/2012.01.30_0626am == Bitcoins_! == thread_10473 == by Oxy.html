Post by: Silpheed on January 30, 2012, 02:22 pm
Oh, and you can see the value of bitcoin at:

http://bitcoinprices.org/
http://www.bitcoinwatch.com/
https://mtgox.com/code/data/ticker.php

The price Silk Road uses is the "avg" in that last URL, which is the 24-hour weighted average price.

For example, if 1 bitcoin is worth $5.53, then about 0.1808 bitcoin would be worth $1 (1/5.53).
