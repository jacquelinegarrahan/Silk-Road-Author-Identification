Title: Be CAREFUL This Weekend People
Post by: OldGuard on April 15, 2012, 03:14 pm
With the start of the big sale only 5 days away I hope people will be careful when making their purchases since the last time we had a sale like this a number of scammers popped up and made away with a good chunk of money.

Be VERY leery of sales from new vendors especially those who want you to finalize early as that is what helped fuel the last cash grab. Do not let greed over run your common sense as the old saying goes  "if it looks to good to be true it probably is"  especially in the middle of a big sale.

And as always run if you see someone offering one of those lotto scams as they never seem to turn out good for those who participated except of course the seller.
