Quote from: lightonion on October 04, 2013, 07:48 pm
The one with the smaller escrow fees will probably win in the end because they are able to offer lower prices, and I might find something on one site that I can't find on another.

The commission fee has a subtle purpose besides generating revenue for the site. Vendors won't be able to make fake purchases just recycling his own bitcoins, thereby propping up his stats with fake reviews. The higher the commission fee, the less tempting it will be. Similarly, the commission fee discourages the behavior where vendor with bad intentions creates dummy customers buying from himself before going to "real vendors". These dummy customers would then show their perfect buyer stats making purchases and then claiming that their stuff never arrived asking for refund. A commission fee dampens such behavior.
