Title: Re: Who are some good vendors that sell QPs of bud for good prices
Post by: NW Nugz on April 08, 2013, 02:35 pm
NW Nugz is also offering higher end stuff at 850/Qp. I agree with what medicalcannibas420 was saying, of course. On top of it being expensive and time-consuming and nerve-wracking to produce QP's of great indoor, stealth packing and shipping and USPS shennanigans are a real pain. Plus there is listing and inventory maintainance, customer service and forum presence and ..........

That said, I think the OP may be someone who is willing to take lower quality to get the lower price. I think a lot of bigger buyers look for that as well. I'd guess there was more of that around in the Fall when North American outdoor harvests came in. I'd guess people who sell larger amounts tend to meet people who buy larger amounts and then we don't see those sellers with public offerings as often. In other words, I think if anyone had really good stuff that cheap they would get regular customers that would buy all they had before it got listed on SR publicly.

Also, If I am reading the tea-leaves accurately, SR is tapping a huge and growing buyer pool while the vendors supply is lagging behind that growth. I'd expect that to be more obvious in later Summer when demand peaks and domestic supplies usually tighten up. If these prices remain stable, I think growers will boost output (which takes time) and prices will eventually drop some, especially for lower grades of bud. I would also not be surprised to see massive supply arrive soon from mega-suppliers domestic or nearby foreign that have seen this thing recently and are gearing up to flood this market as best they can. What kind of mega-supplier will determine the quality they would offer. I have no idea which of the types, I imagine could show up, will, if any. Exciting times on the Road :-)
