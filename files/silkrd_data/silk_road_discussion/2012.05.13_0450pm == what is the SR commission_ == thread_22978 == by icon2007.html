Title: Re: what is the SR commission?
Post by: vlad1m1r on May 13, 2012, 04:58 pm
Hi buddy,

Welcome to SR!

SR's fees on seller's charges are as follows:

10% on the first $50
8.5% on amounts of $50 - $150
6% on amounts of $150 - $300
3% on amounts of $300 - $500

The commission is banded so for an item costing $110 (or the BTC equivalent) the commission would be $10.10 ($5 of the first $50 + $5.10 of the remaining $60).

This can be a bit involved but SR does give you an option to list a price before seller fees are deducted, so you can just enter the price including your mark up and let the website do the rest.

Please ask if you need anything else.

V.
