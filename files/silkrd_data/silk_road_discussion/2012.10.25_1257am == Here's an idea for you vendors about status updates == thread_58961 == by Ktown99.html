Title: Here's an idea for you vendors about status updates
Post by: Ktown99 on October 25, 2012, 12:57 am
It's great that you give updates on your vendor profile but what's the point in putting it there if it doesn't have a date associated to it?

"Hi I'm out of action for a couple of days. Will be back as soon as."

Would be better if it looked like this:

"24/10 Hi I'm out of action for a couple of days. Will be back as soon as."

If there's no date and you're saying you're out of action for 2 days how the hell do I know when those 2 days started?
