I'm a cocaine newb who's been trying some the last couple of weekends, and I have a few questions for any wise ones that might be reading.

For background, I'd never tried cocaine before a few weeks ago, but I was taking/addicted to adderall for the last ~1.5 years.  When I stopped adderall a month or so ago, I could take 75-90mg in a day with no recreational effect.  My only experience is with Tetravort's cocaine.

Also, I'm high on cocaine right now, so this post will probably be chattier than I normally would write.  Feel free to ignore me if I get too silly or something.  Maybe a true master can tell how much I'm on by the excess verbiage in this post.

ADDERALL AND COCAINE:  Is there a cross-tolerance?  It seems to me like there is.  Also, when I took adderall recreationally there would be a few hours of euphoria, followed by 3-4 hours of more productive time.  Cocaine seems exactly like the first part to me, and there's no second part.

COCAINE AND HEALTH:  How unhealthy is cocaine (separate from the levamisole issue)?  It actually seems healthier than adderall to me.  At least, if you go overboard on adderall, you could be (and I have been) up for days.  It seems like if you go overboard on cocaine, you can still fall asleep in max 3-4 hours (if you're not dead) and actually get restorative sleep.

Also, I haven't really experienced the speediness of cocaine.  It seems to speed my heart up for a little, but then go back down.  I'm a runner (on days I don't do cocaine) and I like to believe I'm pretty in tune with my heart, but I might be wrong.

COMMON COCAINE TROPES:  There are a few cocaine tropes I've heard over and over, but haven't experienced.  Is this because I'm using Tetravort's washed product (btw, this isn't an advertisement for tetravort, I've heard similar things about other vendors' washed product, e.g., godofall2 and roberte):

   Depression (Comedown): I've never experienced this.  There's some necessary "depression" just because you're like "Oh yeah, I forgot.  Reality isn't really euphoric all the time."  but it hasn't made me depressed, more like "Well, that sorta sucks.  Oh well, moving on..."

   Short duration: People say cocaine lasts 30m or so.  Last night I took somewhere between 0.5g and 1g at midnight, and with just one extra line around 3am, I was really high until about 6am.

DISPOSING OF STUFF:  I threw away a baggie the other day, but it then occurred to me that I was putting in a public place an object that has both: (a) cocaine residue and (b) my fingerprints.  How do people deal with this.  Do they wear surgical gloves every time they pour out a line?

OTHER PEOPLE:  The first few times I tried cocaine, I was self-conscious and didn't want to be around other people.  I thought they might be able to tell.  Last night was the first time I was around other people, but I still just decided to go to a strip club, since I was pretty confident they wouldn't mind even if they could tell.  (Btw, this turned out to be tons of fun:  Cocaine making me think I'm awesome + paying women to act like they think I'm awesome > disneyland)  Anyway, can people tell?  If so, will they care?  Most of my friends are pretty non-druggie, so I don't have much experience with this.

OK, I'll stop here before my brain comes up with 50 more things to ask.
