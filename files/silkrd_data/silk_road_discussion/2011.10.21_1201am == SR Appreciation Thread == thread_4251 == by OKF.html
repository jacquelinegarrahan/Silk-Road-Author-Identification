Title: SR Appreciation Thread
Post by: OKF on October 21, 2011, 12:01 am
I just have to get this off my chest, because i was worried when the site went down:

Thank you Silk Road, thank you for excisting.

This site means more to me than buying drugs. I'm not much of a drughead, i take some speed once or twice a week, i like experimenting with lsd or shrooms. I'm gonna try some more stuff in the future, but for now, that's basically it. For me, this site isn't about drugs alone.

It's about freedom, the freedom of choice. I want to decide what's good for me and what's not and do what the fuck i want [as long as i don't harm anyone]. Society and the government do what's best for the dumb mass, not for the individual. Because of the possible failure of a few, everybody else has to suffer.

I'm annoyed to death by this kind of treatment. If someone can't handle drugs, he shouldn't have taken them in the first place. It's not like anyone (at least in western society) doesn't know the risks. The prevention of a bad decision by another instance doesn't change anything, as long as the person doesn't know why. Let people experience, let them try, let them make their own mistakes, let them fail.
It's the only thing they learn from.

The Silk Road gives me the rare possibility of free choice, beyond the limitations of a regulating society.

That's why i love it and that's why it means more to me. It presents an outlook on society and what the internet has to offer for every libertine.

Thanks SR. I hope this place will live much longer, cause it's a pleasure to be here.
