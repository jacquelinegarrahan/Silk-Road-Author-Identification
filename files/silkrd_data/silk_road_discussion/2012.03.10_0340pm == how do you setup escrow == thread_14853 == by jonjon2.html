There's no setting up to it.  You'll see once you make your purchase.  As a new guy you do not want to purchase out of escrow unless your ready to lose your money.  There are vendors on here who will sell to newbies without requiring that you go out of escrow.  Once you have 3-5 purchases very few Vendors require that you go outside of escrow.  I was where you are now about a month ago.  I've made five purchases.  All have arrived in a timely manner and I was more than satisfied with the quality.  Just take your time and try to only purchase from well established Vendors.  Welcome!
Title: Re: how do you setup escrow
Post by: subtlety on March 10, 2012, 03:52 pm
Escrow is automatic. Your money sits in escrow until you finalize, which you should do ONLY after you've received your purchase (unless you know/are comfortable with your vendor.) This way, if you are scammed, you have some recourse to get your funds back.

If the vendor asks you to finalize before receiving, make sure they are trusted and search them on the forums. Some vendors require FE for new buyers... Should say so on there seller page.
