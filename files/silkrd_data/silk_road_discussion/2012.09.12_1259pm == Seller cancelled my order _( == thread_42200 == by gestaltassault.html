Hi gestaltassault,

As this is an agorist marketplace, vendors are free to sell to anyone, and decline any order they wish. I understand it can be frustrating but don't take it too harshly - there are plenty of reputable vendors out there that are willing to work with new buyers, especially new buyers that are also posting on the forums.

Don't feel too bad about the cancellation though! I recently placed an order to the value of about $25 for a small amount of viagra and had it cancelled by the vendor. I have perfect stats, over $8,100 spent and 40+ transactions so it doesn't just happen to newbies!!
Some vendors just decline some orders for their own reasons; perhaps they're out of stock of that particular item, perhaps they also sell locally and someone bought their entire supply, perhaps they just don't like to work with certain people or 101 other possibilities. They're free to make that choice, and don't have to provide a reason for it.

What exactly are you looking for? I might be able to point you in the direction of a reputable vendor willing to work with new members. Feel free to PM me if you don't want to post it openly on thread.  :)
