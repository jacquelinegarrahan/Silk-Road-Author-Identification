Title: The Silk Road Vault: An Archive of Drug Experiences and Monthly Writing Contests
Post by: AnimusVox on May 23, 2013, 08:15 pm
FIRST CONTEST TO START ON JUNE 1ST, 2013! ;D

Welcome to “The Silk Road Vault: An Archive of Drug Experiences and Monthly Writing Contests!” thread! The purpose of this thread is to spark the inner creativity within the Silk Road community and promote positivity and beauty in writing by sharing detailed accounts of our experiences on drugs purchased here on the Road. The inspiration of this project is the absolutely wonderful resource of the erowid experience vault. erowid has offered myself as well as tens of thousands of others with beautiful insights on drugs and the experiences associated with them, and I want to carry this paradigm over to the Silk Road. Once we start accumulating reports, the ultimate goal is to be able to establish a full blown archive of trip reports organized by substance, the vendor whose product allowed such an experience, and the type of experience (positive/negative/etc.). Months from now I am hoping to be able to provide a much more thorough and detailed resource of experiences granted by substances purchased from the Silk Road in the form of a Wiki. I plan for this to be a great resource for buyers and vendors alike (think of it as an extended review thread), but more on that in due time. :)

To kickstart this and get the creative energies and juices flowing, I will be running monthly contests where users are able to submit their experiences and win a prize based on a community poll and the end of each month. Each contest will begin at the start of each month and last until the end of the month. All submissions must be in by the final day of each month. At the beginning of the new month, I will have an updated thread that links all the submitted reports coupled with a poll that is open for one week where everyone has the chance vote and comment on which they feel is the most beautifully written experience. At the end of the polling week, I will close the poll and a winner will be chosen and a prize delivered!

On that note, while I greatly encourage individuals post their experiences on any and all drugs, the majority of prizes will initially be psychedelic in nature (LSD, MDMA, 2C-B, etc.). However, as we gain a larger following, it is my hopes that drugs of all types will be offered and contributed to the prize pool. It is also my hope to be able to run best of the best contests that include goodie bags of multiple substances.

To vendors and members alike: if you would like to contribute to the prize pool, please contact me here on the message boards or via Silk Road (same name, AnimusVox - donations can be sent to this Silk Road name as well if you would like!). This is a COMMUNITY project, and without you, this won’t be possible. Let’s turn this into something absolutely amazing and an integral part of the Silk Road experience! Also, suggestions are ALWAYS welcome! This will forever be a perpetually evolving contest and archive, so if anyone of you would like to recommend a change, feel free to make it known! :)

GUIDELINES FOR SUBMISSIONS

1) First and foremost, these must be YOUR experiences, preferably ones experienced within the past one or two months. If I, or any other member, finds that you plagiarized any bit of your report, you will be immediately disqualified and added to a list which consequently bars you from participating in any future contests. This also includes previous trip reports that you’ve posted here on the message boards! The point of this competition is to see a new and creative side of the Silk Road flourish. I WILL be checking all reports to see if there is any foul play. Trust me, Google is a powerful tool and I have zero tolerance for plagiarism.

2) Be creative! Be articulate! Be expressive! Be fun! The best reports are those that we are able to live vicariously through your words and fully immerse ourselves within your experience. Touch us with the sights, sounds, smells, thoughts, conversations, feelings, and everything else associated with the experience. There is absolutely no limit to what you can include, because this is YOUR experience. Make it a beautiful one!

3) There will be TWO threads for each month of submissions, one for the actual trip reports and then another for the voting and commentary. When submitting your trip reports, please only submit the trip report in the SUBMISSIONS thread. Commentary on reports is greatly encouraged, but in order to avoid clutter, I must advise that all discussion take place in the POLLING thread.

4) Within your trip report submission, please include the following items:
* Report Title:
* Date Experienced:
* Substance(s) Used & Dosage(s):
* Vendor Purchased From:

5) Last but not least, HAVE FUN, STAY ACTIVE, AND CONTRIBUTE! Involve the anonymous online world with your altered states of consciousness. :)
