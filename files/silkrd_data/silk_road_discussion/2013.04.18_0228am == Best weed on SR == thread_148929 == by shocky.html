I've gone through many of the US vendors and they are all top notch but have different strains at different times.  Which means you may want get one that has a potent strain your looking for. 

Do you prefer a head high or a cough lock body type high?  Do you want to just have a head buzz or do you want pain relief?   Sativa's will give you the former, indica's the ladder.   And then there are blends, which are much more common.   I like an indica dominant strain and I like it potent too.   

But I try not to smoke pot, I rather get hash from SR.  See even good pot's potency is kind of unpredictable, even if you have a nice frosy plant, every nug on there isn't going to be full of wonderful thc filled trichs.  And even if you get a wonderful nug to smoke, you still got all the plant matter to burn through.  Which is why I go hash, much easier to ship in large amounts and I can smoke a couple of hits of hash and get as baked as 2 or 3 bowls.
