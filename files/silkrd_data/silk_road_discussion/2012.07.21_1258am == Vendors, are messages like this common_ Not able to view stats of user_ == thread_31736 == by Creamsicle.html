Hey Creamsicle! Welcome to SR


First - you only get to see buyer stats after an order has been placed. Until then nothing.


Messages like what you received are somewhat common. As much as it would be nice customer service to help out, there really are dozens upon dozens of tutorials out there, so when I get these I typically don't response other than to suggest they take a look at the forum for getting the help they need.


Eventually you find that you are getting so many legit orders that responding to all these kinds of inquiries would take a staff of minions just to keep up. That's a sign that you've made it!


Last thing - read up on shipping methods to ensure you are protected. Use DCNs or Express, both of which provide you with assurance that the package has been delivered. Unfortunately this site is getting full of scammers who will claim they didn't receive your shipment (when they did) and then ask for a refund or reshipment.
