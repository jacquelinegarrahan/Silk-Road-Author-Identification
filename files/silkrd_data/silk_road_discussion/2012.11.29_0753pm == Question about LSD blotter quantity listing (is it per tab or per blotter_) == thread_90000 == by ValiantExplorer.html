Blotter as a whole just refers to the paper in general, it isn't necessarily a unit of measurement.  But if it comes with a quantification (such as one blotter), it refers to one tab, one hit, however you want to look at it.  I assume a ticket means the same thing, but I've never heard this term used for LSD before.

Each it is roughly one-quarter of an inch.  AKA 1/4".  That Erowid quote has nothing to do with four hits or anything.  It's saying that the paper (here referred to as blotter) is divided into squares roughly one quarter of an inch on each side.

And yes, as already stated, when it says "5 DaliLama tabs (300ug!)" it means 300ug per hit.  That order would theoretically contain 1.5mg total.  However, please understand that LSD is rarely tested at a testing center and few people have a solid point of reference for effects at a specific, given dose.  Therefore, these claims of 100ug and 300ug and everything in between, are usually overstated and exaggerated.
