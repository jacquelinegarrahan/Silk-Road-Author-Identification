Post by: mushitup on October 24, 2012, 08:38 pm
Dank is dank whether it's from Cali, Colorado, Canada, the Dam whatever.  It's all in the eye of the beholder, I could go on and on and say Co has the best buds ever but why the fuck would anyone believe me over their own tastes?  Sample the strains around and don't worry about where it comes from, great growers live everywhere in this world.

The one thing you need to worry about is how much you want and how often.  Some vendors have a constant supply, others offer up things here and there.  I've seen lots of dank on this site and it comes from all 4 corners of the damn planet.  Just find the vendor that has what you want in the quantities you want, and that just takes some looking around and trying things out.

If you want to save some dough and still get an idea of what's available check out Uniwiz's thread on MJ reviews.  It's at 90 pages of research already done for you (it's even formatted).  It gives you an idea of what's out there and from who but it's still up to you to find what you like from a vendor you can deal with.
