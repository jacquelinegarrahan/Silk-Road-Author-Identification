Title: Re: Any ideas for getting SR merch. whilst on holiday in Ibiza?
Post by: Drippy on March 04, 2013, 10:40 pm
I've had plenty of success in overnighting packages to hotels 3 days before I'm gonna be there.  Just make sure to call the hotel first and ask how they'd like it addressed.  I like to call once in the morning, and then again before I go to bed to get the info from two separate people.  Generally you put the name on the first line along w/ your room number, and then "C/O [insert hotel name]" on the second line.  Sometimes they'll ask you to include the dates you're staying on, or the C/O will be the person who does receiving at the hotel along w/ the hotel name.

Also, I'd suggest writing the name of whoever you're sending it to in HUGE letters everywhere on the package or coloring/marking it in an easily noticeable way.  Might seem like it'd raise a bit of suspicion when mailing, but it's common practice for people who mail a lot of stuff to hotels for their trips.  Hotel mailrooms are a fucking MESS, and I've had them say they don't see it before only to call me later saying they found it. 

I've never been to Ibiza, but if it's a nice hotel you're staying in it should be a breeze.  I've never had an issue doing this, besides when they can't find the fucking package anywhere lol - although they always have.
