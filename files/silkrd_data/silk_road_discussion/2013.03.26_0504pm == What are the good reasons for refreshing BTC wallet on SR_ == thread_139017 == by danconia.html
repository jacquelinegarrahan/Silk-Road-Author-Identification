Title: Re: What are the good reasons for refreshing BTC wallet on SR?
Post by: Oompaloompa on March 26, 2013, 10:38 pm
Its worth refreshing, or rather replacing, your SR wallet periodically just so that all links with & ties to other wallets - which could potentially be compromised or traced via blockchain - are gone. Mostly it's just peace of mind mixed with a bit of paranoia.

Its worth regularly replacing any wallets, SR, online or wallet.dat that you use to channel money to SR just to break the chain leading from the BTC purchase to your SR purchases.

Might not be necessary but its worth doing anyway just incase.

Don't know if funds would find you or not, I just take care not to change wallets when I'm waiting for money to arrive.
