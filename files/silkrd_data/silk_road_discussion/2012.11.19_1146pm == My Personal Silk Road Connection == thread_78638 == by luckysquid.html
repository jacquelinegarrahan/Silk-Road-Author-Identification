Title: My Personal Silk Road Connection
Post by: luckysquid on November 19, 2012, 11:46 pm
A moment ago I took a long hard look at myself and truly realised something.

I've been a member of Silk Road for a while now and have enjoyed steadily been completing successful orders. To this day I appear to have been quite lucky and through proper use of the forums and appropriate research have always had fantastic service from the road.
I currently find myself checking both the forums and the main site almost every night, Silk Road has very quickly become a part of my Daily Routine. Often I'll daydream about the pleasurable experimental possibilities and the exciting transactions i'm looking to make next.
I'll catch myself researching for hours about new security measures and updated methods to staying safe on the Road. My understanding of the BitCoin world has grown tremendously thanks to the resources and incredible guides available in this forum.
When the road went down recently I genuinely felt quite a strong emotional heartbreak for my beloved community.
The 'world under the world' had gone offline and I found myself religiously following the thread from DPR anxiously hoping this world would return, the whole time doing everything in my power to block out the droning sound of the trolls.

DPR and his team have created something truly powerful here, not only do we have easy access to some of the world's most sought after substances but we also are part of one of the most engaging, helpful and trustworthy communities available. For this I cannot be more thankful to both the Silk Road team and it's sellers but also the members that contribute every day. I look forward to each and every day here and I see no reason for that to stop anytime soon.

Just wanted to share, if anyone is game I'd also love to hear their connections with Silk Road.
