Post by: MasterS on October 16, 2012, 01:26 pm
I can make edibles without any smell. The smell comes from terpenes in the plant which do not have to be included in the plant. If I want to make stealth edibles I do a wash similar to water curing. I use this process with good bubbles hash/dry ice hash. You soak it in cold water and mix well. Let sit for 12 hours while terpenes and other residue plant matter get absorbed by the water. Strain and let hash dry. Use coconut oil to infuse the cannabinoids into the saturated fat. Because coconut oil is so high in saturated fat you can have a much higher concentration than other oils especially worthless butter. When you get the correct concentration into your coconut oil that requires a small amount of oil you make something as normal using a small amount of coconut oil and the rest of another oil such as olive oil. A dog is very unlikely to smell it and if detected the trainer and other humans wouldn't be able to smell it, pass it off as a hungry dog.
