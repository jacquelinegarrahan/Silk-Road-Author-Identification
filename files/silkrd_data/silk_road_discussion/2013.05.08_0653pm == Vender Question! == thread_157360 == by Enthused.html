Post by: jagfug on May 09, 2013, 05:48 am
I'd say that screams - "Don't do it!" .  Send a fake one first, and ask the recipient to "login to your account and PM me" at least.

I've seen so many vendor rules about no PO Box (while I know some will ship to those), no "Post Restante" and other "no-nos", though you'll always find an exception to the rule.

I try to stay out of the rumour mill of SR, but I can't help remembering a story that sucked me in, about an American college campus where a bunch of "kids" (let's face it) - ordered a shitload of "molly" (X), to a frathouse, and being a frathouse with every move being Facebooked/Twittered and God knows what, - THEY GOT BUSTED!

Haven't heard of any other SR busts in the US before or since that one.

If I were a vendor? - I'd cancel the order. - If you think there's a  good enough chance it will go off without a hitch, then at least send a notification letter first, asking them to PM you. This may save both of you a lot of headaches and regret.

But, hey, that's just one persons opinion.
