Title: Re: New seller: how to build reputation? Send free samples?
Post by: nospainnogain on March 04, 2012, 01:52 am
Free samples or reduced price samples are a fantastic way to establish a good reputation for new vendors. Galindoi, one of my absolute favorite vendors, began his "SR career" by sending out 100 free samples of 10g encapsulated sclerotia on a first come first serve basis. The only cost to buyers was a three dollar shipping fee. What established him as a reputable and reliable vendor was not only giving out free samples but also shipping out quality product that arrived promptly and discreetly. Within a week of creating his account, a massive amount of positive feedback and successful transactions began pouring in.

In my opinion, if you wanted to build up a good reputation, calculate how much of your product you can afford to offer as samples. Then fulfill all sample orders and regular orders in a discreet and timely fashion. I feel like this is a very effective way of building up a good reputation in a short period of time!!

I would recommend around five samples, depending on how much each will cost you. If you cant do free, just offer at cost for you or a free gift (like a little extra), anything to spark some interest given your lack of feedback.

Do more than 100 mg, since with most mdma this is not a full dose and you dont want to leave people thinking your product is not potent from the start just cause they didnt do a full dose, but 500 mg is way more than necessary. I would say around 125 or 150 mg which is usually enough pure mdma for a great roll.

The most important thing if you charge anything to start off with, is do it within escrow! Requiring finalizing early is a sure way to alienate people initially
