Title: Re: questions about weed appearance/quality (photos inside)
Post by: DrGoNZOhear on January 07, 2013, 10:12 pm
Its a fine line sometimes between high grade and AAA stuff but ill do my best to explain the things i look for.  Usually medical grade has more crystals and if its green will be lighter in shade.  The buds will be manicured which means one stem per bud and most the leaves will be trimmed off, no seeds.  Also, medical grade bud usually has a stronger smell and a unique smell depending on the strand.  You can tell also from the taste but that is harder to explain u can just tell in my opinion whether it is or is not based on the taste.  But generally you can tell by looking at how nice the buds are manicured and how much trichome coverage there is. Hope this helps if not the more AAA stuff you get and smoke the easier it will be for you to tell

Post by: xtc on January 08, 2013, 10:46 pm
Sativa buds are smaller nuggets. They are mostly lighter in green colour and really don't have as much trichomes build up. The high is heady and cerebral. Good for socializing or for doing stuff while stoned.

Indica buds are fat and dense. Some have high concentrate of trichomes other don't. Indica is usually dark green and taste stronger. All medicinal pot is Indica and very couch lock.

You do have a hybrid of both but from experience the hybrid tends to be more indica dominant.

As for outdoor/indoor..depends on the method of grow and how much its fed. Theres this theory around that hydro and outdoor are two completely different strains, which is all crap. It's just the area or medium where it's grown.
