Suspended or banned? Typically the reasons for a vendor to be banned will be either scamming or threatening buyers. The other reason is that a vendor who has gone rogue has already pulled a scam, and they are morphing to new buyer identity, along with a new vending acc./ and deleting the old one in the process.

In either of these cases, when you click on a name you will be directed to compose a message instead of loading the vendor's page.
