Post by: JackBlue on March 13, 2013, 09:29 am
I'm pretty sure that the SR rate is the 24 hour weighted average Mt. Gox rate.  That makes it less volatile than the spot rate (most recent transaction) on Mt. Gox.  Unless you're trading in and out of bitcoins frequently, it shouldn't make any material difference whether you're coins are in Mt. Gox or SR.  Having them in SR wallet means they are available when you want to buy something.  Isn't that the reason why most people on SR own bitcoins?  It can take a couple of hours to do a transfer from Mt. Gox to Silk Road at times, which can be a serious pain in the ass when you want to make a purchase and you end up waiting around for the BTC to arrive from Mt. Gox.
