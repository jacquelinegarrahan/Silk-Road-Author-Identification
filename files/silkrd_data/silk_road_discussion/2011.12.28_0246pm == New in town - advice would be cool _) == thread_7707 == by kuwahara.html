Welcome!

You've already made a great decision by registering on the forums!

One piece of advice I can give that is surely covered in those FAQs - NEVER go out of escrow.

As a new buyer you will be asked probably numerous times by sellers that because you are new you need to go out of escrow.

There are plenty of established vendors on SR that will let you work inside escrow on your first purchases - I have never gone out of escrow and am at 19 successful purchases.

Your selection for your first few purchases may be a little more limited than you like but it is worth it.

Enjoy!
