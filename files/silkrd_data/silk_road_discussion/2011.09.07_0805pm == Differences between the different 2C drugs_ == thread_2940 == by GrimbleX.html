Title: Re: Differences between the different 2C drugs?
Post by: doc on September 07, 2011, 08:29 pm
There is no general favorite, different people like different ones. I am not going to go into a lot of detail here but based on my tests:
- E and T-7 produce VERY powerful psychedelic experiences, P can do the same but it lasts too long for most people
- B and I usually lead to milder and more euphoric experiences
- C and D are quite mild compared to any of the above
My experience with thiolated 2C's other than T-7 is limited at best so I have not mentioned them.

I suggest you read through relevant PIHKAL entries and Erowid experience reports to get a more detailed picture. This topic has also been discussed a thousand times on many forums out there.

Cheers.
