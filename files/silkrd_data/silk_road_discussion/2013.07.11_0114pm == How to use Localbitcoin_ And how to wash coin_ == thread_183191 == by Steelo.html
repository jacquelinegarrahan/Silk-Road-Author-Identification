Title: Re: How to use Localbitcoin? And how to wash coin?
Post by: saulgood on July 11, 2013, 06:04 pm
You can get your own address that you're in full control of by running a bitcoin client like the ones found on bitcoin.org.

You can also use a trusted wallet service to handle the coins for you. blockchain.info and coinbase.com are trusted, but don't use any other ones without asking around! There are a million wallet service scams out there.

SR supposedly "washes" coins, and you can also use blockchain.info's "Shared Send" feature to do this if you're extra paranoid for a small percentage fee.
