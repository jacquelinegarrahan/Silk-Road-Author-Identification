Post by: grahamgreene on August 22, 2012, 03:35 pm
I always mail my order information to myself on SR in case there's a problem with the order, as transaction # etc. is removed after 12 days [I think, may be a couple of days off either way here] and it's possible for there to be an issue with an order that is yet to arrive after that time period.

A lot of vendors, having large amounts of orders, will need the transaction # in order to be able to help you with any problems after that, so it helps to have the order details in your SR inbox just in case.

Once you have received the order and all is well, you can go ahead and delete the order information.

(As for your feedback, as wasta said, it's viewable in your Account > View Feedback pane.)
