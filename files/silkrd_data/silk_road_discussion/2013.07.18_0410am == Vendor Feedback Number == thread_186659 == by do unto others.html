   Checked out your vendor page, 100% stats, feedback going back a month; Might have something to do with many of your feedbacks being for items no longer listed. However, there is feedback on some of your items? It could be me, but the feedback looks a little odd.

   I'm definitely not accusing you of anything, I'm just curious by nature, no offence intended! I've seen similar before with another vendor who made a whole bunch of listings, put them in stealth mode at a really low price, bought them himself with dummy buyer accounts, jacked up his feedback, and then raised the price and made the listings public.

   I'd message SR Vendor Support, because you really should have a score if you've been selling pills at that price, I would think.

   Vendor forum access reqs are min. 6 weeks vending, 30 successful transactions totaling at least $1500. It's the same for your bond, you get rewarded with both. Check your sellers guide for details.

   Best of luck, AB.
