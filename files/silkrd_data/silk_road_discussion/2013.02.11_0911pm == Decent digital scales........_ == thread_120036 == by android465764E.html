Post by: Stray Cat on February 12, 2013, 12:41 am
If you live near a big flea market there might be a head shop or two at the flea - no shit - I've seen it with my own eyes!  I suppose alot depends on which state but I counted 1/2 dozen head shops at a flea market in a state where pot & paraphernalia are illegal.  Go figure.  They had some nice Blade brand scales for less than $20 as I recall... good luck with your search!

Weight of nickels from above:  I actually looked this up a few years ago ... weight of all U.S. coins varies due to dirt, debris, damage and plain ol' wear & tear.  Old nickels with lots of silver obviously weigh more than today's nickels, silver nickels stopped sometime in the 1940's, I'm too lazy to look it up. 

Today, a clean nickel in good shape should weigh between 4.9 grams and 5.1 grams so there may not be anything wrong with the scale  ::)
