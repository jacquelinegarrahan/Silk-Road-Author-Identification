This method allowed me to carry just over an ounce of mushroom on board to Amsterdam for some friends and I.

I powdered them and put them into the do-it-yourself gel pill capsules from a vitamin store (I'd recommend powdering your pills and capsulizing them as well if you've got colourful tabs with pressed logos).  I then bought two bottles of vitamins (two different types).  I made sure to remove the paper covering the cap very carefully as to not rip/tear it.
