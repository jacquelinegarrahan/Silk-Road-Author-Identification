Post by: NW Nugz on July 20, 2013, 11:49 am
I raised my prices recently because Btc are expensive to turn into cash safely and after buyers make a purchase, they often drop in value before I can ship and then some more before it arrives and gets finalized and some more before I can convert them to dollars.
Also, this time of year is festival/camping season. Days are longer and people smoke a lot more weed in Summer. At the same time, the supply of fresh outdoor and greenhouse weed in the Northern Hemisphere has been nearly cut-off due to the seasonality of weed growing. It takes a lot of work or expense/tech to get plants to produce buds this time of year. Most outdoor/greenhouse growers still have their crops in vegetative mode this time of year. Indoor growers often have to use expensive air conditioning to do well indoors when it is easy to grow outside instead.
I was not here to see SR prices last summer. Even in the land of plenty, supplies usually dry up during August. I am already seeing my suppliers raising prices and I would not be surprised to see SR weed prices double before the outdoor crops start coming in. I am curious what it was like last August here?
