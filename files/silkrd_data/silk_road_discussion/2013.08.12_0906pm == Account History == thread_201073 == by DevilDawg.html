Post by: Pharmington Rex on August 14, 2013, 02:52 am
Only the last 30 days of account activity is displayable. If you're really that paranoid, make it a rule that you will only accept orders that use PGP in the address field. State that you will cancel any and all orders that do not use PGP.

You can even enforce PGP use for all communications. This way, if SR was ever infiltrated, while the infiltrator would see what was ordered, they couldn't see to whom the orders were about to be sent to since that information would be PGP encrypted.

Another thing is that SR doesn't display what BTC address coin was sent to but they do have that information internally. So use an external tumbler or an intermediary of some sort to add plausible deniability to the coins you withdraw.

You really don't have that much to be paranoid over. LEO needs to know who you really are. You take diligent measures to conceal your true identity. Think deep about what might reveal who and where you are. Then take steps to misdirect and conceal that.
