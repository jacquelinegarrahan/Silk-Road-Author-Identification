You are not giving enough details to get a helpful response.  What quality of weed do you plan to sale (indoor, greenhouse, outdoor,  mids,  mexican brick,  ect..),  what weight increments do you intend to sale,  and what kind of cost do you intend to attach to said increments?  Where will you be shipping to and what country are you shipping from?  This is all information that you will need to provide before anyone would be able to give you an accurate answer.
