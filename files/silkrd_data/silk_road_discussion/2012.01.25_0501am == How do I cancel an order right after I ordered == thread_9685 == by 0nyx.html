Unfortunately you cannot cancel that fast.
There have been a number of scam buyers as well, so SR put certain restrictions on the accounts.
Even if the vendor hasn't click Finalize, they could be at the post office mailing that package when the buyer hits cancel.  This just gives all parties time to think about what they've done.  LOL
There are only two ways to get out of the four day wait now.  PM SR to help you, OR PM the seller and just tell him you wish to cancel your order.  Even if he is a scammer he can't screw with you too much at this point, as you haven't finalized... though I suppose he could try to strongarm you with your address.
