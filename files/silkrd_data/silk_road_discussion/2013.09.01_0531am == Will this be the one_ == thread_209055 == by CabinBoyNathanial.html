Title: Will this be the one?
Post by: CabinBoyNathanial on September 01, 2013, 05:31 am
I have lurked the forums here for quite a while now, and in the past month I decided to take the next step and get active.

So far, I have had mostly great experiences on the Road. I have purchased from several vendors, have been happy with all of my product, and nearly always happy with the process (stealth, listing style, etc).

--Yes, this topic AGAIN coming next--

I purchase small amounts of various things for my own personal use, and like things that can be easily hidden away and used covertly. I am not a teenager living at home with my parents, but I still need to be stealthy.
Blah blah blah etc...
After a bunch of reading and deliberation, I decided to try an O Pen vape since it is stupid simple, easy to stash and so forth. I did some searching, and only found a couple vendors on the Road selling the thing and the carts for it. One vendor had it a lot cheaper than the other, so after a couple questions were quickly answered, I placed my order.
Here is the THAT part part of the ramble :
One vendor may have required FE depending on stats and such (I may have qualified, maybe not), the other vendor definitely required FE for that item in particular. So there I was (rock, hard place, don't buy). What do I do? Which choice is best? I made the choice to go for the cheaper one that did require FE from the outset. I figured that I really wanted to try this, and if I am to get scammed, it would be less painful if it were less money. I mean, I fully hope that I didn't make a mistake since for me, a hundred-ish bucks isn't something to just toss away.
The forums here are great and terrible at the same time. Reading feedback threads are very interesting as things can seem great, but then turn to shit near the end. When I decided to go on the purchase, the vendor I chose had great feedback in their forum thread. The last time I checked the thread, my vendor was being called a scammer, etc. No fun reading that when you just sent out some money that you would never have a chance to get back.
Well, never FE is something that is drilled into you in the first few minutes of browsing the forums. You can't not see someone saying to never FE. You can also rarely go a day without a thread or two of such and such scammer this, such and such person got scammed when they FE'd etc. Each of those last threads will have your cast of characters calling the OP an idiot (deservedly) FE on their purchase. I hope to not be the next one to be called an idiot for being scammed, but if I am, I am coming back to post about it and will never (probably) FE again.

If you want to get a head start, feel free to insult me now before anyone knows the outcome of my order. :D
