Title: Vendors: how much time off SR do you schedule?
Post by: Ktown99 on February 09, 2013, 09:46 pm
Some vendors have it in their profile their SR 'shop hours' to give the buyer an idea of when they'll reply to questions and when they process orders.

I've seen vendors who work Tues, Wed, Thurs and that's it.

From a buyers perspective I can see how this might increase the potential for mistakes.

There becomes a backlog of messages to answer and orders to process. Orders and messages will be missed and even quantities may not be accurate.

This is only a buyer making assumptions and they could be wrong but its something I've encountered lately.
