Step #1 - READ THE FORUMS/WIKI. I hate to be that troll but honestly its for your own safety.

1.)Getting coins:

   C.)Use a trusted exchange site to purchase coins through a series of hoops ala dwolla
      Btc-e/campbx/tradehill/mtgox/
2.)Where to start your career as a drug user
   A.)Pot; where else.
   B.)Mom and Dads medicine cabinet
   C.)LSD/Mushrooms - much like using heroin, there really is no reason to worry about learning to swim before jumping in the deep end.
      C-1.)Look into Option B.) for something like xanax or similar pill to bring you back when you inevitably lose it.
         Alternatively, buy some here.
   D.)Cocaine-Research Rick James   
3.)Mining for coins
   At this point its a waste, however since your not paying for electricity maybe its worth joining a mining pool
4.)How do you make a purchase - bitcoins
5.)Shipping risks - there is always the risk that someone will find out, in which case one of two situations usually occurs
      A.)Letter - when small amounts are seized, often you, the recipient will get a letter stating mailing contraband is illegal and its left at that
      B.)You don't want me to describe the alternative
         B-1.)If your a minor, maybe they wont try you as an adult.
6.)Shipping practices - Its in everyones best interest to be discrete with packages and shipping. Most vendors make every attempt to make their packages blend in.
   A.)Standard practices - most use Printed labels, correct postage, VALID addresses, flat rate mail via USPS
   B.)Drug specific practices - If it has a detectable odor like weed, it should be vacuum sealed at least 2x etc

hope that clears some of it up for you,
