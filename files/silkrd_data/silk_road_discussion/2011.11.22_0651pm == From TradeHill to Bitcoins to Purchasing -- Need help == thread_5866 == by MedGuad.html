I'm pretty new here too, but here's what I did before making my first purchase. Did some research on reputable vendors here on the forum and read through seller profiles and feedback on the main site. Checked if they sold to new buyers or had any special instructions like only allowing small orders or finalizing early (which I'd avoid, but there's a lot of debate on the practice here). Once I found one that I liked, I just placed an order and included my PGP encrypted shipping address. A lot of sellers encourage contacting them if you have any questions, but it might take some time to get a reply depending on how much business they do. I placed my first order a week ago and my seller was helpful with my questions and I should be getting it any day now. I plan on making smaller buys to build up some SR rep.

You should move your BTC a few hours before you intend on making a purchase since it takes some time to transfer.

As far as getting your PGP setup, I used this guide which is simple and took me no longer than 5 minutes.
http://p3lr4cdm3pv4plyj.onion/guides/shepj.html
