Title: Re: How can I cancel my order sooner?
Post by: 46&2 on December 03, 2011, 11:24 pm
you will have to wait for the cancel option to be turned on, it is in the the order section with your order status.
it takes four days after you place your order for it to be an option.
you can ask your vendor to cancel the order, but, that means the vendor will have to read the pm, look through his orders and then click on the cancel button. sounds easy, but, it seems that for some vendors it is easier to ignore their customers and let the days go by so that the customer can cancel himself.
good luck
