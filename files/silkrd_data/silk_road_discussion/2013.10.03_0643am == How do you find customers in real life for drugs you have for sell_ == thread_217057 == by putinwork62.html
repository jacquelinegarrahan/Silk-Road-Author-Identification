DO NOT SELL if you need to ask this question.
You need to build a network of people to sell to.

It starts by selling to a few trusted friends, then expanding to friends of friends, etc.
But you need to stop somewhere. My advice would be to never get too big.
Personally, I buy bulk and sell to 4-5 other people who buy semi-bulk regularly and also sell smaller amounts to up to 70 people IRL.
I know them all and I don't just sell to randoms.

It took me a long time to establish myself. It doesn't just happen overnight.
Slow growth is safe. Or if you want, sell on BMR / Sheep.
But be very careful and  think everything through many times before you begin.
