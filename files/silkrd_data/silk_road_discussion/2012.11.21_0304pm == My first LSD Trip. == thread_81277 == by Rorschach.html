Title: Re: My first LSD Trip.
Post by: Wazup7 on November 21, 2012, 04:32 pm
I have found that even with no tolerance, weed has absolutely no effect on relaxing an LSD trip.  I have tripped a few times where after 10 hours, we would start blazing up like an ounce between 4 of us, and 12 hours later (i.e., after being awake all night and as the sun is now coming up), we had almost no weed left and were still pretty wired.

Another thing is it is acceptable to trip if you have things to do after the trip, but I wouldn't recommend it if you don't know how the tabs you are taking will affect you.  It's not fun in any event.  AND, smoking mad weed while you're tripping will only serve to give you a massive weed hangover the next day when you have shit to do.  Regardless of how easy your job may be, and how isolated you will be while working, you will probably want nothing more than to do whatever else you feel like doing, and that will not be sitting at work.

(As a side note, I am a computer programmer, and I find that LSD "magically" helps me do the design work portion of my job like a champ.  Most other jobs probably don't work this way with LSD use, but I have taken small doses of LSD a couple of times for that express purpose).

Bottom line, if it's your first time, save it for a time when you have no responsibilities and a good setting to trip in.

Title: Re: My first LSD Trip.
Post by: Wazup7 on November 22, 2012, 01:15 am
Quote from: Rorschach on November 22, 2012, 12:56 am
i ended up doing some meth and now i really want to take the lsd. I'm thinking that if i keep some meth for work ill be alright how's meth doing with lsd?

I'm not sure I like where this is going.  Meth is a serious enough substance when taken by itself (my least favorite comedown of pretty much anything, for a variety of reasons).  So is LSD.  Your problem wont' be that you are too tired to work.  It will more likely be that you are too distracted or mentally drained to do any useful work.  I'm sure meth could help you get through your job the next day, but it's much better to simply take the LSD when you can be free from responsibility for a couple days. 

I think taking meth on the LSD comedown will distract you from digesting the LSD experience. 

And I can't stress this enough--you have never taken LSD before, so don't waste it by taking it at a time when you will know you have something to do soon.  Just knowing that you have work in the morning will detract from the trip.  LSD does lose it's magic, and I highly recommend that you make your first LSD experience as perfect as it can possibly be.  You will still have your tabs this weekend, or next week, or next July, if you wanted to.  Just save them.
