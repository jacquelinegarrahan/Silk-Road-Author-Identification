Title: Re: Faking reviews?
Post by: CaptainSnowFlakes on August 07, 2013, 02:46 pm
Yes, a vendor can fake reviews. But, he would have to set up orders with himself or people he would know. Those repeats you see are probably multiple orders from the same person, writing the same thing for each. If a vendor has a lot of feedback, then you are probably safe to order with that person. It would be really hard to do that much fake feedback. Plus it only lasts for a certain period of time.
