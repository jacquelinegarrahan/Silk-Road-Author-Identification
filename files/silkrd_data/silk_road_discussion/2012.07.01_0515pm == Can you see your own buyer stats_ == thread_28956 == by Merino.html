Title: Re: Can you see your own buyer stats?
Post by: ZenAndTheArt on July 01, 2012, 06:09 pm
I can suck my own... oh, hold on, different subject.

Click on 'account' at the top.
Then click on 'Purchasing stats' on the right, on your account page.

Remember, purchasing low value items (such as e-books), may give you a greater number of purchases. But your 'Total spent' will still be low. This may (or may not) influence a vendor, who's making his decision based on your Purchasing stats.

Good luck fellow Roadie!
