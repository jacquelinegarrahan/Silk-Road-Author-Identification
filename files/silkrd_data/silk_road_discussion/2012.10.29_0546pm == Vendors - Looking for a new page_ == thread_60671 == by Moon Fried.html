Title: Re: Vendors - Looking for a new page?
Post by: newjac on October 29, 2012, 11:59 pm
MF (sorry, nothing meant by that, lol), I kinda like that idea, and I, like you, prefer to see a well-written vendor page; I agree it gives a certain amount of confidence that you're dealing with someone who's got their shit together, and isn't out to scam you -- but isn't that the risk in doing what you're proposing, that you might inadvertently bring in more victims to a vendor whose ultimate goal is to scam, or for whatever reason ends up taking people's money and skipping out?

I know there's no way to know who's going to eventually go rogue/scam -- I think some just get overwhelmed, or get hooked on their own supply, or have things happen IRL that lead them to do something they initially had no intention whatsoever to do.  And others just start out from the get-go with that last payday in mind, it seems there's more of both happening lately, end result either way is lots of people losing money.

If you provide these very well-written profile pages for vendors, you take away the ability of customers to glean what they can by seeing what the actual vendor would write.  I've seen lots of posts about how someone came to the conclusion not to buy from someone based on a gut feel, and the only way to get that "feel" is to see what the anonymous person on the other computer wrote;  that, along with actual purchase experience, feedback and forum posts as well all play into it, but you'd be taking away one of the buyers' possible protections.

Just my .0002 BTC, and I'm certainly open to hearing other opinions.  I agree with you that it would help a lot of vendors, and it's definitely a service some of them need, just hope you wouldn't end up helping the wrong ones.
