SR doesn't keep their commission on canceled orders. What happens is that if the vendor is hedging his escrow account (converting his BTC to USD while they are in escrow, then back to BTC when the orders are finalized) then you will have lost the 4% that this service costs, +/- any change in the value of bitcoin. If the vendor is not hedging his escrow then you will get back the exact amount of bitcoins you paid. They may be worth a different USD amount now though, +/-.
