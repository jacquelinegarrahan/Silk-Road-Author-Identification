Title: Re: What's your Theory???
Post by: Magic Ceelo on October 09, 2012, 07:44 pm
I hate when vendors do this, they keep you waiting for so long that sometimes your order ends up auto-finalizing. The best thing to do is, never finalize early, always check feedback, and wait for up to 12 days, if nothing arrives just go into resolution center and ask for refund. Vendors most of the time scam the new buyers, so if your new, buy a couple of products that are relatively cheap from vendors with (100) rating to have better stats and more transactions complete. Personally i only buy from vendors with 97-100 rating and thats it. A lot of vendors dont mess around with established buyers. If it seems like you dont know what you are doing, vendors will take advantage. Dont FE when your new!
