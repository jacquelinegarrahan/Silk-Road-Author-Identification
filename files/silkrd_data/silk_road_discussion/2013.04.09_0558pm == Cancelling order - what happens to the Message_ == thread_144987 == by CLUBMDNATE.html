Title: Re: Cancelling order - what happens to the Message?
Post by: balltripper on April 09, 2013, 06:45 pm
So long as they didn't take a carbon copy of your address it'll be deleted.
You will get your coins back, but it depends if it was hedged against the dollar. If so you will receive the dollar amount you spent in bitcoins back, if not you will receive the bitcoins you spent.
It won't affect your buyer stats in any way.

Hope this clears it up
