With an order that is FE? Probably zero refund. He can say anything knowing SR can't do anything about it.

Consider your order a loss. That doesn't mean you won't get it. But if you do, you'll be pleasantly surprised. :)

Simple rules to live by on here:

1. Never finalize early. If there's only one vendor who has what you want but requires FE for it, then there is no vendor who has what you want.

2. Never finalize early. There's a number of variables which are out of your control. FE adds yet another variable that is outside your control.

3.Never finalize early. The only people who lose money are those who FE. Those who never FE, only lose time.
