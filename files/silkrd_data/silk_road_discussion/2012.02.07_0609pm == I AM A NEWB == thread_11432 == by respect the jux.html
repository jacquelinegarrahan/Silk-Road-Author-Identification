Title: Re: I AM A NEWB
Post by: White Light on February 07, 2012, 06:33 pm
Hello ! And welcome on SR !

Finalize early is not a rule, some vendors accept to work 100 % in escrow even for new buyers. When a seller wants you to finalize early he writes it on his profile, or sends a message to ask for it. Are your orders marked "processing" or "in transit" ? You can't finalize before it's "in transit".

In any case it's never a good idea to finalize early, after a few months on SR and about 20 transactions I finalized before receiving the product only one time, it was for small amount and with one of the top seller.
