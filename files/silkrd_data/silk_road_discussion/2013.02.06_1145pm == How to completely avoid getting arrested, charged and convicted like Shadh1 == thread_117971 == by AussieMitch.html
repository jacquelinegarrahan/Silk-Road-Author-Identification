Title: How to completely avoid getting arrested, charged and convicted like Shadh1
Post by: AussieMitch on February 06, 2013, 11:45 pm
Poor guy, no-one deserves to get locked up for supporting our civil libertarian right to put what we want into our own bodies.

Lets examine a few steps he could of taken to prevent this from happening, and that we can take to prevent this ever happening to us:

1. If you are dealing, and not just buying off Silk Road for personal use, don't have drugs sent to your address! Pay other people to receive the packages or pay them to setup mailboxes if you have to, just don't have drugs sent to your place as that is what got the cops onto him in the first place.

2. If you are dealing, DELETE YOUR TEXT MESSAGES AND SECURE YOUR PHONES. Have a text-only burner phone that you regularly change so your voice is never on it and get people to save it as a fake name, so you can always deny that it's yours. Because you change it regularly (Ideally every-time your credit runs out) this makes it much harder again for police to gather evidence on you. Ideally you should also never discuss specific drugs, prices, names or locations on this phone either. Also NEVER DISCUSS SILK ROAD ON THE PHONE EVER.

3. Secure your computers with full-disk encryption and only access Silk Road through an amnesic means like Tails, Liberte or a VM running from a USB. Have a clearnet computer for general use obviously but never use it for suss Google searches and don't put anything suspicious on it.

4. If you must possess suspicious items like a money counter and 35 stun guns, get a friend to hold onto them or store them away from your house. As for things like zip-lock bags and scales, create plausible deniability like having a couple of zip-lock bags full of lollies next to where you keep them and having the scales on-top of some Creatine or a gym supplement that needs to be weighed. As far as I know Shadh1 wasn't even caught with drugs at his house, it was just a bunch of corroborating evidence the police found in the raid that allowed them to charge him.

If this guy had not followed step 1 but had followed all the other steps he would have been safe when he got raided and if he had just followed step 1 he never would have been raided in the first place! With some simple precautions what happened to him should NEVER happen to you.

Stay safe guys, I really hope that no-one else gets caught like he did. Feel free to chime in with any other advice you have or mistakes that Shadh1 made, so we can learn from this experience!

2. Make sure they don't tell you over your normal phone that the package is in. Just have them send you a tormail or a pre-determined coded text message (like 'Do you want to go see a movie this weekend?') to your burner phone.
3. Once the package is in have them write 'Return to sender' and WAIT for 24 hours.
4. Have a runner pick up the packages for you after 24 hours, or have them dead-drop the package to you in the mailbox of a trusted accomplice or another pre-determined dead-drop location. 4. Check it at night only and sporadically, or have a runner check it for you.
