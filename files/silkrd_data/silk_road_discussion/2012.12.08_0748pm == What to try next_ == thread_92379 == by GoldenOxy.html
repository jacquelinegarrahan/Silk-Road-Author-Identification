Title: Re: What to try next?
Post by: firehawk on December 10, 2012, 01:50 am
If pain control is your actual goal, ask about attending a pain management clinic. Also ask about fentanyl patches and lollipops.

If that gets you no results, change doctors. Find a doctor who is a pain specialist.

If you'd just prefer to get high and have your pain managed as a bonus, THC (edibles are a good idea considering your circumstances) is probably the way to go.

Ketamine is a fantastic pain-killer in low doses, problem is it doesn't last long, you won't be entirely coherent (even on small doses you will slur your words noticeably), and the usual route of administration is 'snorting'. You could always use IM, but that involves needles (obviously) and a reliable, clean supply of ketamine. The chances of your family cottoning on to this are pretty high.

Stay away from anything like meth, heroin, cocaine or speed. You sound like you have the addiction threshold of Charlie Sheen.
