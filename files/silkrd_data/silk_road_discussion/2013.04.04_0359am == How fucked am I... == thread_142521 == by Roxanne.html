Title: Re: How fucked am I...
Post by: DrugsAreFun on April 04, 2013, 04:13 am
You're fine most likely.  In order to be used against you, the clearweb site would have to be subpeonad or working for LE and then they would have to immediately subpoena the ISP of the exit node and do a real-time wiretap (because TOR exit nodes don't keep logs) to trace what else you could be looking at.  Onion sites are encrypted end to end so there's no way they could see what's going on there (same for clearweb with HTTPS, though they may be able to which websites you're accessing but not the content) and even in the unlikely scenario where all these things happen, it still wouldn't prove anything because multiple users can be using the same exit node at a time and you can simply say it was another user.

Unless of course when you say "went onto a clearweb site in the tor browser and entered in some personal details" you meant you logged onto your local police station's website and sent an email like:
