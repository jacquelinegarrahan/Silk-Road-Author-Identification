I'm sure you're fine, but I'd recommend in the future avoiding such vendors. No vendor should make you FE on small purchases (i.e. <200-250 dollars) unless they have a really good reason to do so, and even then only if you trust then. Otherwise, there's little you can do if they decide not to honor your order after you FE and jump ship.

That being said, the vast majority of vendors wouldn't do that, but I would still find one that will allow you to keep things in escrow until you get your package (THEN you release the funds) He should answer messages within a day or two (unless they've indicated they're swamped with messages), beyond that I think you're getting to the "bad service" point.
