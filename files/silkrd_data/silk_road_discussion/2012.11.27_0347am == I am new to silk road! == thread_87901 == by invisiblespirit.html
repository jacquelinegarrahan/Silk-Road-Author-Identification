Yeah, can't stress it enough--research, research, research!

There is a huge amount of information about doing business on Silk Road, regarding security/anonymity, bitcoins, vendor reviews, common buyer/vendor etiquette, etc.  Acquiring bitcoins is only a small part of doing a transaction on SR.  Make sure that you are patient, and that you have read up on the correct way to do every step of a SR transaction.  Make sure that others have vouched for whatever vendor/item you are purchasing. 

Just take it slow, and be safe, and your time on SR will be wonderful.  Good luck, and enjoy!
