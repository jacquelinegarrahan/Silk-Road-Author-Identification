Title: Re: how to make good XTC pills
Post by: BuddhaNature on September 18, 2012, 02:39 am
If you want to make pills you need a pill press.

There are two types:

Manual - ~$200. Upsides: cheap. Downsides: SLOW. ~150 pills/hr. Not all pills will be uniform in height.

Automatic - ~$5000 Upsides: customizable stamp, uniform pills, higher pressure exerted to make harder pills, FAST. ~6000/hr. Downsides: price

If you can afford the automatic then there's really no reason not to go with that. Now, actually getting one without attracting attention is the hard part...
