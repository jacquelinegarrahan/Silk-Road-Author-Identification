Title: Re: Is this site just a huge scam site...
Post by: cacoethes on March 07, 2012, 01:41 am
It's legit.

Tips for not getting scammed, and ensuring your orders make it to your mailbox:

1) Don't rely on vendor feedback alone.  Use the forums to research the vendors you plan to use.

2) Keep your orders domestic if possible,  Having to go through customs greatly increases the odds of your package getting intercepted.

3) Use a real address, and a real name associated with that address.

4) Be patient.  It takes time to acquire bitcoins, and shipping isn't always as fast as we'd like it, either.  Some vendors will require you, as a new buyer to finalize early.  Some won't.  Choose your vendors wisely, based on the buzz in the forums.
