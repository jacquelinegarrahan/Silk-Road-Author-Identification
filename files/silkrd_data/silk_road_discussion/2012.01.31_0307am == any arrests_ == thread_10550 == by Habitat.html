Title: Re: any arrests?
Post by: xx138xx on January 31, 2012, 05:43 am
it's called civil forfeiture here in the USA. If they find you in possession of cash when you go down for drugs/guns/hoes etc, without immediate proof it was made legitly, they can immediately seize the cash and any property they deem was gained from the illegal business being conducted. The real bitch about it is, since it's a "civil" forfeiture, it's up to the defendant to challenge it in court and carry the burden of proof (opposite of criminal proceedings). When they catch you with $100k+ it's difficult to dispute the seizure to put it mildly, especially when police here routinely seize stuff they have no chance in hell of proving was tied to illegal activity. Keep in mind also, he still ends up having to pay for the public defender eventually in the form of probation/parole fees or court ordered restitution to the state. If he knew he was busted, he may have feigned brokeness so they wouldn't keep snooping around and potentially find other funds he had stashed or hidden.
