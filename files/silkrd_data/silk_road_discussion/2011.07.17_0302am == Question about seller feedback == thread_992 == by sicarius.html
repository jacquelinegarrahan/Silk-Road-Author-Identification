Title: Re: Question about seller feedback
Post by: Kind Bud on July 17, 2011, 03:24 am
You get a rating after 10 completed sales.

total feedback %
The formula is total points earned (counted in x/5) over total available.
4/5 + 5/5 + 5/5 + 4/5 would be 18/20 or 96%
while
1/5 + 3/5 + 1/5 + 3/5 + 2/5 would be 10/25 or 40%

It's great to have rating though this system has its quirks.
The lowest rating is a 1/5 (at least as far a I know) so the lowest is 20% not 0

Also a 4/5 might be a good review but is a 20% markdown.

Statistically scores are weighted more toward the middle than eBay. 4/5 is a big hit for a "good buy" while 1/5 is lots of points for a ripoff.

The listing sorting system is a little odd (Upgrade is on Silk Road's TODO list). For example GreenCo has over 180 transactions and a 100% rating. But they are displayed BELOW mrnewseller with only 10 transactions because somewhere along the way GreenCo got a 4/5 rating. They system counts them as 99.998% vs the 10/10 mrnewseller has

In general the rating is OK but it best to look at what the buyers wrote and how many transactions and the forum.
