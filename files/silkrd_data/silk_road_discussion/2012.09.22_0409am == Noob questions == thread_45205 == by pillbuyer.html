Title: Re: Noob questions
Post by: CiscoYankerStuck on September 22, 2012, 05:00 am
- FE = Finalize Early
- LE = Law Enforcement
- Yes. You may want to test out your encryption first by sending a vendor an encrypted message and asking them to respond (don't forget to include your public key in the message). This is also a good way to see how responsive a vendor is.

Also, when you make your key, use your SR username as your "name" and a fake email address or a tormail address as your "email address."
