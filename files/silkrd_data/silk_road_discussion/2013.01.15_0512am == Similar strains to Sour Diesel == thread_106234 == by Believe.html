Title: Re: Similar strains to Sour Diesel
Post by: sourman on January 16, 2013, 01:00 pm
OG Kush (the original) plus many of its crosses are similar to Sour D, as well as popular on SR. Skywalker OG, Fire OG, and Wi-Fi OG are closer to the legit East Coast Sour Diesel which is strongly sativa dominant.

Most people are actually getting various backcrosses and offshoots of Sour D because it's a rare clone only strain. Every time I've seen it sold on the street outside of the East Coast, it's been either NYC Diesel, some OG, or the rezdawg IBL seed. There are at least a couple sellers on here that have the real deal Sour Diesel, but it's hard to catch unless you like keeping your account loaded.

You can also find some chemdawg on here from time to time. It's a rumored parent of the sours, and you'll see why from the smell. To me, OG kush is more sour-like while Chemdawg is sedating but still close enough.
