Title: A few little things that I've noticed that lack on SR...
Post by: SugarRush on August 06, 2012, 05:16 am
My apologies if these things have already been discussed, but as I'm new I'm still getting to grips with the system, so please forgive anything I may have repeated!

Hopefully I've come up with at least one new idea!  ;)

SR is a wonderfully smooth system, but I have noticed certain little things are lacking, and as we know, sometimes, little things can make ALL the difference!

Price Low/High and High/Low

PRICE: Highest First
PRICE: Lowest First

PRICE + P&P: Highest First
PRICE + P&P: Lowest First

It's annoying seeing an item you wish to purchase, and you try to buy it, but it's out of your reach because of postage! So, this feature would be so helpful there!

If you like this item, you might also like feature, would be cool, as so much is out there, something good might be missed! :)

Vendors being able to leave their customers feedback, and see their prospective clients % score, like we can see theirs!

It happens on every other major online market site, and if this is supposed to be the Ebay of freedom, why not here? :)

It would also assist the vendor to know whether the client is any good or not, so that would better for their security!

A blacklist feature where consumers can block vendors, and vendors can block consumers, if you don't get on, or if the vendor is a scamming arsehole!  >:(

BTC Conversions in GBP, CDN, AUS, YEN, etc - Would make life much easier!

A Today's Deals/Deal of the Day, section would be good, where vendors can do special offers!

Just a few little things I've noticed, that could be improved on, if it's necessary! ;)
