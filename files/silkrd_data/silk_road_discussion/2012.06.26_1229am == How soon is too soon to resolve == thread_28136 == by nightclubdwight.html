Title: Re: How soon is too soon to "resolve"
Post by: anonaddict on June 26, 2012, 12:33 am
If you haven't received the product in the time that the vendor outlined in the listing, then try to contact the vendor. If they don't reply in a couple off days. Click the resolve.

If the auto finalize is about to count down to 0 and you still haven't received the product. Click the resolve.

Just try your best to work things out with the vendor first

Title: Re: How soon is too soon to "resolve"
Post by: Wazup7 on June 27, 2012, 11:47 pm
Just to be safe, I wait 16.5 days, then resolve, and contact the vendor politely.  Mail is slow sometimes, and vendors are people, so I usually give them the benefit of the doubt, and try to avoid bothering them if I can avoid it.  Once you're in resolution, you can wait I guess indefinitely, to square things with your vendor. 

Also, I'm sure you know but, avoid refunds if you can avoid it, and try to work things out outside of the SR transaction.  That way you won't spike your refund rate.  Many vendors will send coins or a gift code as a refund.

But always be polite, understanding, and patient.
