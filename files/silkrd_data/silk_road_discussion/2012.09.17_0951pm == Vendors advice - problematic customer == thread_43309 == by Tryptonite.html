Title: Re: Vendors advice - problematic customer
Post by: jameslink2 on September 17, 2012, 10:52 pm
To be honest, I have had 6 out of 112 sales total. They were all mushroom related.

I personally think that some try it to get free product.

You have two choices,

A) Ship them a replacement product and hope they leave a decent rating.
B) Tell them to fuck off and take the hit to your score.

Ok, enough of the hardline stuff  ;)

To me a lot of it has to do with the customers attitude and how they approach you.

From the message do you feel that he is just trying to get free product?
Did you point out that you have sold lots of the product and it is highly rated?
Have you tried the product yourself and if so have you let him or her know that it is tested by you?

Have you had them explain how they took the product and was there an issue with the method they used?
The above applies to mushrooms from my point of view. Several of my ratings were from people who took 1g or less and did not trip hard so they felt that it was no good. One even admitted to eating a full meal before taking 2g and "did not feel much of anything" I don't know if MDMA is as touchy as eating mushrooms or if the dosage could be in question but if it is I would ask.

Once you have gotten a feel for the person and the issue I would simply ask them what they would expect you to do to rectify the situation. Sometimes it is simple, sometimes they demand something that is unreasonable. At this point it is a negotiation you simply need to find out if s/he is being honest and how you can make it better. Remember there are a lot of things you can offer including discounts on there next purchase or even free shipping. Heck you can even give them a % refund with a coupon code that they can only spend with you or even give them a coupon code that is good for the cost of shipping when offering them free shipping on there next purchase.

You will note that I only stated two choices and nether was a refund of their bitcoin. If they are demanding their money back and will accept nothing less, they are a scammer in my book.
