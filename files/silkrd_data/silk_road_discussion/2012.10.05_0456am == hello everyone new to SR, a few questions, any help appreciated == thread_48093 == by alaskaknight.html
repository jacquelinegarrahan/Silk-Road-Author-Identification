Post by: Slugger on October 05, 2012, 08:45 am
I use bitinstant. Just click the first link in the drop down box "major bank branch" and then "bitcoin address". I did an instawallet address that I bookmarked and saved to a file just in case. That should bring you to trustcash website, put your local zip, pick a location, put fake name and a Tormail email account if you want. Once you do the deal at the bank, your coins should be in the wallet in 1-3 hours. It may take longer though, but thats how long mine took. Then I transfered over to SR from the instawallet. Took a few hours for it to show up on SR also.

Some people also go the route before sending to SR, where you "tumble" it. Basically use a website like bitcoin fog and then you sporadically get your coins over 6 hours (minimum). The tumbler is 100% safe I believe. I didn't use it, but I feel it is definitely worth it to if you are exceptionally paranoid or making huge purchases. Remember everybody takes fees from the BTC. Bitinstant, bitcoin fog, SR takes some fees from vendors which raises prices a bit.
