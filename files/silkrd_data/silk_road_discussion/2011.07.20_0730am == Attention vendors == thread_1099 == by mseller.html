Title: Attention vendors
Post by: mseller on July 20, 2011, 07:30 am
To all vendors, be careful in reveling any info about you, location, age, nickname in real life and similar data, no matter how such data can really be harmful.
Phishing individuals (for product or money), buyers exist, first fendly and even they send some amounts of money, they will blackmail you and threat of exposing you online. They are not connected with LE.
So be extra careful what you say and reveal to anybody. Be smart and dont rush into things. Think all trough and dont try to be friend in this business, especially not after few days of start knowing opposite person.
In this are vendors targets not buyers. Vendor have goodies and can not do much about in such case. Buyers are different and can do "something" and ask for help.
