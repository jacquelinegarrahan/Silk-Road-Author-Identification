I've personally found when traveling the same ratio of dilution is 70g to 1 liter. Any more and you risk chronic re-crystalisation in the container when exposed to extreme fluctuations in atmospheric pressure. However f you're traveling by land, train etc then dilute as far as the solution still look clear. Trick to complete dilution is to dissolve the raw K crystals in boiling water , leave to cool completely then bottle in appropriate containers. Be sure to use a container not marked for human consumption.  Facial Toner containers make for a good cover !
