Post by: BlackIris on July 17, 2013, 09:50 am
Dirty,

That's sadly a risk that all of us that write reviews for vendors have. We risk every time that a scam vendor will use the popularity s/he gets from the review and our name for backup to then scam people.

There's little, very sadly, to do about this, because you can never know in anticipation. If the product is bunk it's easy, but if the product is perfect, the price is good, stealth is fine etc. etc. you have no motivation to not endorse that particular vendor, but if that vendor wants to use this to scam s/he can do it without problems and you can do nothing about it. You will be caught in the middle and that's all.

For this it's anyway very important for users to beware and take responsibility in any case. It doesn't matter if the vendor is endorsed by reviewers, threads (as LSD Avengers or Deemsters etc.; how many times it has happened btw that a good endorsed vendor on those lists has gone rogue? It has happened a lot, especially in the Avengers - what they can do about it if not to immediately let others know? Nothing) and whatever else, the user must always be attentive and not trust blindly a priori.

Just because a buyer is endorse by a review or thread DON'T FE anyway or don't trust him/her blindly in any case. It is not worth to do it, no matter what. If a vendor is legit the reasons to ask for FE are very little (only in certain particular cases they can be understood, in the others it's just obviously a way to go out of the system; even if not with the intention of scam, however the vendor will have the upper hand no matter what in any circumstance) so don't do it if not in those very particular cases, elsewhere if you do then you must understand that it's your own responsibility (and nobody's else but yours) and you can either lose that amount.

I understand that you are sad about the happening and I would be too in your shoes. It feels extremely bad to be used, but there's nothing you can really do about it and it will surely happen again, no matter what. Just know, however, that the advantages of reviewing products greatly overcomes these bad things. You are still helping hundreds of people making a quality purchase and in 99% of the cases the buyers have bought a good product thanks to your help.

Best regards,
BlackIris.
