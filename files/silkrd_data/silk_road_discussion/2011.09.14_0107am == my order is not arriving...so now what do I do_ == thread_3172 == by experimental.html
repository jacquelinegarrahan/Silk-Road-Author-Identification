I had 2 different orders never show up. One the vendor had many of his packages intercepted (this was the claim anyhow) and the other guy disappeared for a month after I placed the order. I got 100% refunds on both. Some vendors will only offer 50% but from what I have seen the good vendors that are here for the long haul and understand the value of their reputation will offer 100% or a reshipment. Honestly if you use a real name at a legit address and the vendor packages everything properly the package loss rate is very low. I have not been around as long as some sellers but I have not lost  package yet.
