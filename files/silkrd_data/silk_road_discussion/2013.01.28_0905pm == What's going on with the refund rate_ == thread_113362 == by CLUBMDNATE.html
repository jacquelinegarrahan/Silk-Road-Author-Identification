Title: Re: What's going on with the refund rate?
Post by: acider on January 28, 2013, 09:19 pm
The most recent transactions have a stronger effect on your stats.
From buyer's guide:
"The statistics are weighted toward the present, so you must keep up your good behavior, but if you have a bad streak, you can recover by doing well going forward"
