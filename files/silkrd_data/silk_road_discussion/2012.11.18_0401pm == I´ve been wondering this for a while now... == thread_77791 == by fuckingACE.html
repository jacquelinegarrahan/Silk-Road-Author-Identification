Check out the heroin thread in the Rumor Mill. There are a few users there who are certainly opiate addicts. They write about going through withdrawals when a vendor is late shipping (common), having to pawn stuff (less common), things like that.

Others write about life before SR: having to drive two hours to a big city to score on the street, driving around the corner to do a tester to see if it's any good before going back to buy more, even having to stick the drugs up their asses ("because you KNOW you're gonna get pulled over.") Definitely some of the most interesting reading in the forums.

Don't get me wrong though- the majority of the regulars seem to have it together, and a few of them seem to be middle-aged or older with money to burn.
