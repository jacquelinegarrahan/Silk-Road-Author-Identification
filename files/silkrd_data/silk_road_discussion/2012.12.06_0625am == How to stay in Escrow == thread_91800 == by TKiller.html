Title: Re: How to stay in Escrow
Post by: saitekman12345 on December 06, 2012, 07:22 pm
When you add an item to your cart, it is in your "Shopping Cart"

When you checkout, the BTC are now in the hands on the SR staff.

Your vendor does not get the bitcoins until you finalize the order (You received the package).

If you did not receive it, don't finalize. Take the process into the SR Resolution center.

From there, you and the vendor will dispute the issue with SR staff. Depending on the evidence and exchange of words, the SR staff will decide

who is right or wrong.

P.S. Don't try and scam vendors, they most likely have DCN on ALL of their packages. This will be CLEAR evidence they sent the package and it was delivered like it was
supposed to.
