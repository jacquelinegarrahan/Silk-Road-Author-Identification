I think that the advice you offer is good but there are plenty of simpler ways to attain your goals and further protect your identity without going through the process you mentioned.

The changing network cards is not necessary, the only identifiable part of it that is encoded in the TCP header is the hardware address, there are plenty of programs to change your MAC address. without the need of changing the actual adapter.. In fact you can go 1 better still. If you use Liberte or Tails linux distros, it will automatically assign a random MAC address every time you start up. This means that you will never browse TOR with the same MAC on 2 different occasions. If you would like to test my theory download wireshark, in the TCP header you will see your mac address, if you use linux run WLAN CONF command  to change the MAC and observe all your traffic originating from a different address! The part that should cause most concern is a non proxied IP address, with one call to the ISP you can find out its exact physical location. This is why it is infact better to visit clearnet or onion sites through a TOR exit node, they will either load or display a message saying that its not supported through proxy, the important thing is to always remain anonymous! always browse through a torified connection unless the website you are trying to reach specifically will not allow it (MTGOX, certain banks etc..)

As for the dedicated machine, your right, you should keep normal browsing and TOR browsing separate. But you don´t need separate hardware to do this. Buy yourself a 8 or 16Gb thumb drive, the tiny ones the same size as a fingernail. wear it on a chain around your neck. Copy the contents of the Liberté ISO to it and every time you want to connect to TOR out comes the thumb drive into the PC and reboot. This is good for 2 reasons 1. Liberté runs on an encrypted volume, so even if it does fall into the wrong hands you will stand a better chance than if they get your unencrypted hard drive (make sure you use a long passphrase to encrypt) 2. it being always on your person and small at the slightest hint of trouble swallow it. If you really are that paranoid, and then absolutely all trace of your online activities has dissapeared.

And of course scrub the shit out of your coins as the poster above me mentioned..

SO all in all, it is not necessary to browse from locations that are not your home if you dont want to because all your traffic is torified. It is not necessary to fuck around with another machine, just put in your thumb drive and reboot. Don't change network cards and always use PGP for messages and files.

Be sensible, scrub your coins, don't click on links that redirect to clearnet. Darknet was made possible because of TOR, not the other way arouns.
