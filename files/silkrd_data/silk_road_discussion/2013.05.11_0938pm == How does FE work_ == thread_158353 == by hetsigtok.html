Let's not generalize, please. It is not said that just because you did FE you _surely_ will get scammed. I did FE on my first purchase (I wasn't even asked to but I did it willingly since I had a problem and I didn't want to cause problem to the vendor in case I couldn't resolve it in time for auto-finalization to come) and the order did come without problems.

Naturally it's much better to never ever FE, however it is also true that in some (few, but it can happen) cases you have no other option, and in this case it is not said that you will automatically lose money, at all. I think that if you have to FE then you should use intelligence and: A) don't do it for bulk orders but for sum of money you can accept losing in case something goes wrong, B) try to establish a contact before with the vendor and talk with him/her (in many cases if you do this also if the vendor states he requires FE, if s/he feel that you can be trusted he will not require FE anyway; the forum can also help in this), C) consider well the feedback and how many people who did FE (in case) have updated the feedback with successful shipments.

So, OP, even if you did FE (but remember it's better if you don't do it in the future, less risk) it is not said that you got scammed and you will not receive your order, at all.
