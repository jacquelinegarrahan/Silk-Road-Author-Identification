Title: Re: Trying to finalize
Post by: phubaiblues on November 07, 2011, 01:26 am
Some reputable vendors *do* ask newbies to finalize early.  Make damned sure this is a reputable vendor tho, by checking these forums first, as this is also the way scammers operate.  You have no protection once you finalize: you are basically telling SR that your packages has arrived.

If, having said this, you decide to 'finalize early' anyway...you still can't do it until vendor posts 'shipped' ... so you are still waiting until he does this...he just hasn't got around to it yet...
