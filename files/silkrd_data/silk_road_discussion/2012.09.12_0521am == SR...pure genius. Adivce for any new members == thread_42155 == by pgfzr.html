Title: Re: SR...pure genius. Adivce for any new members
Post by: j3an on September 12, 2012, 10:45 am
1, Learn at least a bit about Tor, and its benefits.

2. Use the security subforum, read the stickied threads and take on as many of the suggestions as practically possible.

3. Stay domestic unless you live in China/Russia/Aus etc. Yes, weed might be cheaper and better but is the payoff really worth all the extra LE checks?

4. Use PGP. Vendors have disclosed that, very generally speaking, only 10% at best use PGP to obfuscate their addresses. Chances are if your vendor is busted the police will have full access to your address, regardless of it being encrypted; however if TSR server is busted there will be no way for LE to find out what name and address are attached to your account name.
 
5. Trust your intuition. If an offer's too good to be true, or a vendor's profile looks like its been written by a 14yo do you really want them to hold your personal details?

A CLASSIC example of this was a weed vendor called greyfoxx who sold really nice weed at decent prices.

In his profile he would visciously attack vendors selling average weed for high prices. He sounded like like a psycho, so avoided him despite good reviews posted by customers.

He began posting transcripts with customers, and the final straw was posting a customer's personal name and address on his vendor profile.

_______________________________________________

I would say if you follow all these you will be absolutely fine. There is a lot of low-hanging fruit as I mentioned above, who do not use PGP, do not read the forums, FE on ounces of weed, accept "free samples", use skype to talk to random investigative journalists as evidenced in another thread etc etc etc. Don't be one of them.

Good luck :)
