There are referral codes and I think it saves you a tiny bit, plus referrer gets maybe 10 percent of your orders or something... but it would be a huge risk to link two accounts from anyone here at SR. You are about to link your bank acct (bank transfer?) to the tradehill acct through bitinstant, so unless you are doing a cash deposit and always access TH through tor it just seems really dangerous to me. I have never heard of anyone exchanging the referral codes unfortunately.

I would actually recommend the cash deposit through bitinstant to TH, even though you will pay a couple extra percent points for the fee. You have the money in the TH acct by the time I am back from the bank and its so easy. Plus I feel it adds a tiny bit more security than linking a bank acct.

A couple things to note about tradehill... Withdrawing money is not the easiest unless it is in the form of bitcoins I have heard but I dont know if this has been straightened out. Buying and pulling coins is never a problem. But do NOT use the instant buy feature... it never works and was frustrating when I first came. Just do a buy order for a little higher than the current avg and you can get coins right away and it is still cheaper than the instant buy or using get-btc.

Good luck,
