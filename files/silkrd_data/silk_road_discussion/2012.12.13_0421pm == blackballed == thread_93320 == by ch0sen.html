Post by: gracevan3569 on December 13, 2012, 05:00 pm
Generally, a rule of thumb is to give at least 24 hours for a response and perhaps more.  A couple of things could be happening:

1) Holidays and vendors are taking a break (my vendor is)
2) Holidays and vendors are swamped or selling out
3) You are at the bottom of the totem pole being new

It's pretty much unlikely that you are blackballed if you have good stats (even if it's only a couple purchases)
