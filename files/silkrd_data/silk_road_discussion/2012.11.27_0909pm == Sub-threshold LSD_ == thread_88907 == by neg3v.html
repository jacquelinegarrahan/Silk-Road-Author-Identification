Title: Re: Sub-threshold LSD?
Post by: saulgood on November 27, 2012, 09:39 pm
Yeah, it's usually referred to as "microdosing", and I think it's great!  Nice replacement for a cup of coffee in the morning : )

The good news is - you don't need to find any low-dose blotters.  The trick is that LSD is completely water-soluble, so just buy a small glass vial, stick some tape on the side, and mark it with 10 equally spaced vertical ticks, one per dose.  A vial with a 4 dram volume is a pretty perfect size.  Buy distilled water (important, as non-distilled water will destroy the acid), pour it to the top of the 10th marking, throw one ~100ug hit of acid in there, and shake it up.  When you're ready to dose, shake it up again, pour 1/10 of the vial into a spoon and let it absorb in your mouth for a bit before swallowing the water.  I'd recommend trying to get ~10ug per microdose - 20ug makes you feel a bit trippy and brings the effects more into consciousness, whereas the microdose tends to be more subconscious and subtly energetic.

A great article called "The Heretic" came out in July that everyone should check out: http://www.themorningnews.org/article/the-heretic (edit: search in the article for "How many of you have heard about micro-dosing?" toward the bottom to read about the microdosing study)
