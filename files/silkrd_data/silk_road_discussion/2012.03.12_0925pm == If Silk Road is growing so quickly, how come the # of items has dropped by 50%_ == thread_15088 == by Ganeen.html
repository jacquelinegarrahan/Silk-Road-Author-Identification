Title: Re: If Silk Road is growing so quickly, how come the # of items has dropped by 50%?
Post by: sonic on March 12, 2012, 09:38 pm
Stealth mode.  Vendors can make their listings invisible to everyone but those that have became a "fan."  It is a way for vendors to quell large influxes of new buyer orders and still service orders from their regular consumer base.

The listings all still exist, just not to you or anyone else unless they are a fan of the vendor.
