Title: Re: Is there a quicker way to get BC?
Post by: theradblur on January 08, 2012, 12:19 am
VirWoX. It's not really economical but it's fast. Once your account is verified through Second Life, you can depost USD. Then exchange USD to SLL, then SLL to BTC, then withdraw to your SR address. You can place market orders if you need it fast, but limit orders make sure that you get the best conversion rate at the time. Sometimes it can take a couple of hours to fill a limit order, sometimes it takes less than an hour.

Only think that sucks is that when you have a new account you can only deposit ~90 USD, and it can take up to 48 hours for the Bitcoins to be withdrawn. But once you have invested $400+ you can deposit up to $700 USD and they seem to process your withdrawals a lot faster! I withdrew 40BTC the other day and it was pretty much instantaneous. SR takes a little while to show the funds in your account though.
