Title: Re: change main screen in sr to show all sellers not just domestic?
Post by: WinterMoon on January 04, 2013, 10:44 pm
Select a category from the pane on the left
When the page loads. look for the long white SEARCH box (top center of the screen)
Just below the Search box you will see SORT BY and DOMESTIC ONLY.  Un-tick Domestic Only if necessary
Press UPDATE
