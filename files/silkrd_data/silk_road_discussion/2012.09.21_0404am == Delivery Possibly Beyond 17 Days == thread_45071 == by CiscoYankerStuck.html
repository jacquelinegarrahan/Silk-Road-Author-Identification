Title: Re: Delivery Possibly Beyond 17 Days
Post by: quinone on September 21, 2012, 04:14 am
Click on resolve before it autofinnalizes.  It will allow you to offer the vendor the choice of refund, reship, or extension on delivery.  Just offer them another 7 day's or whatever, if it hasn't arrived after that then you'll have to take it up with the resolution center and the vendor.
Title: Re: Delivery Possibly Beyond 17 Days
Post by: uniwiz on September 21, 2012, 12:48 pm
The simple answer is yes.
The above person is correct, but I always PM a vendor (once, not 3 times a day) and try to work with them BEFORE resolution.
You will find;
You make a new friend.
They will hopefully guide you to the best solution, if they give a crap.
You go to resolution together it makes it easier on SR Support, they will extend almost instantly as they can, cause you two are in agreement, which is encouraged around here.

Most vendors want a perfect score so they are more than willing to help.
It's during  these times you learn who to trust.

At this time;
Do not trash the vendor
Do not threaten the vendor
Be nice
Do not PM the vendor daily, just enough to get info.
If the vendor doesn't respond, you can go to war, but give them a chance to make good.

This all takes extra time.
