I don't mind either way, and prefer to err on the side of caution (and therefore use PGP). Then again I'm not a high volume vendor so I have time that others don't.

Title: Re: Hello SR and an etiquette question
Post by: vanilla on November 18, 2011, 12:19 am
I do not require it. I offer my public key for those that want to use it. It really is a good idea to use encryption with anything that could be incriminating but it is up to the buyer's discretion. I always respond to inquiries in the same fashion that they are delivered. If someone uses pgp to message me, I use it to reply if I have a key for that user. If someone messages in plain text, I respond in kind. Most vendors that do require it will say so in their profile page. Generally it is a good idea to check there before messaging a vendor.
