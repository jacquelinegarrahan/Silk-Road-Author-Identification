Hello gals and guys,
This time I would like to share you a few tips about how to use the fake IDs.
Most of my clientele are youngsters who want to get into clubs and bars.

I am making this public post because I am being bombarded with questions regarding to the quality of my IDs and they ask if the IDs will be working in-state in nightclubs.
The short answer is: it depends on you.

If that doesn't satisfy you, let me expand on that.
I have a vast clientele and I have adults with very serious shit using them every day bypassing TSA checkpoints at the airport, opening bank accounts and PO Boxes. None of them have a problem, and they are recurrent customers as well. This is the testimony of the quality of my IDs.
All the security features are there, and visually it is indistinguishable from a real one if you put them side by side.
I have raving reviews from military veterans and from other people with experience making fake IDs.

Ironically youngsters don't seem to have a consistent result. You know why? Because you don't really know how to handle yourself, your body language betrays you.
I used to work as a bartender, and I barely looked at the IDs, I directly spurted: "this is a really good fake".
I had cases of idiots replying "thanks", but normally, even if they are not that dumb, guilty people act guilty.
What I am really looking is not at the IDs, these days the replicas are extremely good and you truly can't tell a real one from a really good fake, it is much easier to read your reaction to the accusation.

Everyone is different, but the signs are consistent. A long silence, lowering your face, scratching yourself, scratching your head, stuttering, hesitating, overreacting and/or just being FROZEN (you will not notice it because you are all nervous, but a second for you, is an eternity for me)
If an honest person gets accused for something that is false, they react as honest people. They either laugh it off or they react offended if I insist (getting into the "wait... are you being serious?" mood state), but the honest offended reaction is gradual, not explosive like the guys who are nervous for being busted.

The accusation itself is the test, and entering immediately into the "oh fuck, fuck, fuck, I got caught" mental state just exposes yourself.
That mental state is translated directly into your body language, and bouncers who are well experienced on bullshit they are pretty good at reading them as well.

Another thing is that the State guidelines encourages bouncers to ban the access to anyone they *suspect* to be underage, even if they have no proof of that. A case in point is my cousin who used to have a hard time getting into nightclubs because she has a cute baby face, even when she was almost thirty. Bouncers were telling her that she was underage and that her ID was fake, and she would get really pissed. Sometime she was allowed in, and sometimes she wasn't. But one thing was sure, she always got her ID back. And that is another sign, if they give back the ID is because they really can't tell if your ID is real or not. If they were certain that it was a fake, it would have been retained or cut in half.

Learning to act is harder than you think, you must train yourself to be able to handle yourself. Most kids are too overconfident about themselves, and their attempts of -untrained- bullshitting become so stereotypical that it is actually kinda fun to watch.

TIPS FOR TRAINING YOURSELF

First of all, you must put your shit together and know the basics.
Remember your name (if you used a fake one), your address (if you used a fake one), your age, your birthday.
Another typical question is "what is your major" or "where is your college". These are less personal, and legitimate driver license holders will definitely reply to these without any hesitation. College kids will reply immediately, kids in highschool will choke as it catches them by surprise.
Now, always remember my favorite question, remember the zodiac sign of your fake persona. Even people who don't care and don't believe in horoscopes, knows their own zodiac sign. Fakers never think about preparing themselves to that extent, especially men are vulnerable because they really don't give a shit about the zodiac.

Nobody should hesitate or even think when they are asked about their biographical information. Your response *must* be immediate.
If it takes two seconds to respond, you failed the test. If you even try to go around the questions, going off on a tangent, you failed the test MISERABLY.

You must train yourself until you can reply as naturally as possible, in the same way that you would reply about you real identity.
Also have a friend to ask your ID, give him your real one and make him ditch it saying that that it is fake. React to that naturally.
Now try again but this time give him your fake ID and try to react in the same way as before.
Get a friend to ask you in random times of the day your name, your birthday, etc... you can even film yourself and see how unnatural you look when you respond.

That's what I do with my friends, I ask them randomly "when is your birthday" in any time of the day and they are like "eer... December..." and that's it, they failed.
There is no "eer or uhms" when someone asks your real birthday or any of the questions above, unless you are mentally challenged.

Quality-wise, my products are visually indistinguishable from a real one. You'll really need lab equipment to tell a real one apart from another.
Will it work for clubs and bars?, most probably yes, but that depends more on your psychological preparation and your body language. You must believe and feel to be entitled to be there, and react accordingly.

ASKING OPINIONS ABOUT THE FAKE YOU RECEIVED

If you want an honest opinion about the IDs from an cop/ex-cop friend, check their bias by first showing them a real authentic driver license first and ask them their opinion on "this fake ID". I guarantee that most will say that it is good but not perfect.
Then you'll know it is their ego speaking, and they are not being actually objective.
