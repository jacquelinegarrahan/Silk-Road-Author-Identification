Title: Improvement for SR
Post by: phooook on January 16, 2012, 04:17 pm
Hey guys,

I just thought of a nice improvement concerning the order status of a delivery.

Many sellers change the status to "in transit" very fast (within hours) because that way the finalize options pops up.
But I'd really like to know when they shipped it cause that way I can get a much better estimate on how long I have to wait/know when to worry.

My first transaction last year was shipped within Germany. About 90% off all letters send inside of Germany reach their destination within 2 days. Sp if you don't get your mail after 2 days something is strange and after 3 days you can start worrying.

It took 4 days back then and arrived fine, but it made me worry and that could have been avoided.
I can understand every seller that changes the status to "in transit" ASAP cause then we can pay the faster.

So, what I propose is: add a "order is processing" status that enables the "finalize" button too.
That way I know that the seller has the goods and is going to ship them, so I can pay now if trusted. And when he's heading for the post office the next day and tossing my letter, he can change it to "in transit" so I know it's on its way now :)

What do you think?
