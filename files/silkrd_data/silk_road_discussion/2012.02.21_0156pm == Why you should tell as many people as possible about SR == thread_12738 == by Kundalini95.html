They don't need money and power to take SR down.

If enough attention is brought to the silk road it could very possibly get to the point where we have LE dispensing pounds of legitimate drugs, only to build up a huge reputation. Believe me, there are people using the SR for doing massive deals ($20,000-$40,000). It could quite literally lead to a complete shattering of a good majority of SR's MAJOR vendors.

If major vendors were to start disappearing, and were prevented from accessing a computer, we would have no way of knowing who exactly is the undercover LE with a massive reputation that he built up because of some stupid act that got passed that allowed him to dispense real drugs because silk road became way to involved in the public eye.

SR reflects the real world drug trade. While the majority of the SR is bottom level drug users, buying grams... then there's another level of low level disturbers... then medium level distributes who give to the low level, then high level distributes who give to the medium level... then there's the Major Players, while there's not many of them, there are a few, these are the people moving 10-20, even up to 50-100 pounds/kgs of substance at a time.

Most people are very lucky to run into a Major player in the real world, just like you would be very lucky to come to contact with one of them and have them reveal their identity on here.

It's an entire hierarchy, a pyramid. Don't think that just because the majority of people on here are low level users, that political attention will just completely ignore the major players that are on here. If these major players get cut off, there's going to be a lot of circulation cut off down the chain.

Yes telling people about SR would increase bitcoin stability, decrease prices, increase vendors, and increase options, but with all these benefits it will also bring a lot more negatives in with potential for politicians to add new acts that will change the rules of the playing field, which could ultimately kill the SR. SR isn't invincible, as much as we all would like it to be. When we live in worlds were governments can introduce acts that take our even our basic humans rights freedoms away(NDAA), they can pretty much do whatever the fuck they want. The best move is to minimize the amount of attention.

Don't tell people. Let them find it on their own. If you're going out telling people you may end up telling people who would have never searched for drugs in the first place online, and that information may go from them to their cop buddy. Don't fool yourself, not every single cop or sherrifs office knows about the SR. Cops aren't likely to go put in a lot of effort to "search for drugs online", especially to the lengths it takes getting on TOR and SR.

The best move is to let people find it on their own when they are searching. I don't even tell my friends about it. Never tell someone something that you wouldn't mind having repeated to at least 5 different people whom you have no control over, you never know, someone on the other team may be pointing a gun at their head one day.
