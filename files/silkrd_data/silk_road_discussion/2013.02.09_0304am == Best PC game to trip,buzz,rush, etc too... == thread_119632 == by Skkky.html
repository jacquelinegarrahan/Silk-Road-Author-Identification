S.T.A.L.K.E.R. is not a run-n-gun kind of game, it requires thought, strategy, patience and lots of reading. I have only just started replaying it this month as a few new mods have been released/updated.

If you play Vanilla Stalker (running the game without any mods), you will be very disappointed, it's full of bugs and glitches, and runs like shit.

I am going to recommend you to download either of these two game mods, **use only one though**, they are both great. Each will drastically transform the game, upgrading everything, textures, sounds, gameplay, EVERYTHING.

First is STALKERSOUP by tecnobacon - originally made by russians over the last 5-7 years (since the game came out), it has finally been translated into english. get it here - http://tecnobacon.com   (it is over 5gb download from file host sites - taken me 4 days to download)  - warning! this mod is NOTORIOUSLY DIFFICULT (unless you cheat of course), be prepared to DIE!!! Quicksave is your BFF.

Second is L.E.G.E.N.D.A.R.Y. mod by Majorsoft - available here -    http://www.filehosting.org/file/details/403451/L.E.G.E.N.D.A.R.Y.7z    - it's about 1.5gb, it doesn't have the scope of stalkersoup though, here is the discussion board for it - http://www.gsc-game.com/main.php?t=community&s=forums&s_game_type=xr&thm_page=1&thm_id=21854&sec_id=17


But the best part is, Legendary mod has over TWO HUNDRED real world firearms added into it. STALKERSOUP has about 250. Vanilla stalker has about 50 firearms.


Also, with 'any' version of this old game, don't forget to go into the options menu and change the renderer to "Full Dynamic Lighting", maximize the resolution (i use 1920x1080) and set all the graphics options to their maximum values. The original in-game Anti-aliasing and Anisotropic filtering settings are broken, set them to max anyway, exit the game and use your video cards control panel to force AAx8 and AFx16 and VSync on. With these settings, the game will be much more playable, especially on todays computers.
