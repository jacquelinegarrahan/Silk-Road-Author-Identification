Title: Re: Buyer vs Seller Cancel Order
Post by: tan on August 02, 2012, 09:24 pm
The 4% is the hedging fee.

If the vendor had hedging activated for your item, the 4% belong to sr the moment you buy an item. When you buy a hedged article you'll in any case loose the 4% but you are not exposed to bitcoin value fluctuations. The  bitcoins you get will be worth the the same in  $. What you get back is (paid amount - 4%) +-  BTC fluctuation.

If your item was not hedged, you'll get the same amount of bitcoins back, but they may be worth more or less than when you bought the item, meaning you are exposed to bitcoin value fluctuations.
