Title: Re: Get quick Bitcoins in Germany
Post by: MaxMad on December 02, 2012, 06:24 pm
Hi mupfel! Just registered to answer you. I´m also from Germany. To buy bitcoins I use bitcoin-24.com. There you can deposit your money via sofortüberweisung, which means, your money will be immediately on your account to buy bitcoins. I have made some transactions until now and there was only 1 time a little problem, but that was caused by sofortüberweisung and the support there was helpfull. If you make your first transaction on bitcoin-24, you are limited to 50 Euros for the first day, I guess. But you can make a few transactions one after another. But you have to pay a fee, so it´s more expensive. In my opinion, the fee isn´t very high and I personally have no problem with it. You have to decide on your own, but I would recommend this side (and I have nothing to do with it, I´m only a customer there)
