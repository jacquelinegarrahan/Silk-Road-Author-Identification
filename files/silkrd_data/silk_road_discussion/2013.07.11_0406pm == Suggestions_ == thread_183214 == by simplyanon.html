Title: Re: Suggestions?
Post by: Pharmington Rex on July 11, 2013, 05:54 pm
Once an order is in resolutions, it won't auto-finalize.

If your last entry before the review period begins is for a 100% refund and the seller never countered, SR resolutions will grant you your refund.

You may want to put a note in your final resolution entry for SR resolution staff to consider NOT allowing this refund to affect your purchasing stats on account of vendor non-responsiveness. It's a similar situation to a vendor going AWOL and leaving many patrons with unfilled orders. In those cases, the refunds aren't reflected on those patrons purchasing stats.

Title: Re: Suggestions?
Post by: Pharmington Rex on July 12, 2013, 05:14 am
Quote from: zandermanreturns on July 12, 2013, 02:35 am
Is there anyway to get a refund without blowing your stats to bits? 2 orders from same trusted vendor are no-show with no love letter and i dont want my stats to take a dive.

Yes, you can ask the vendor to execute your refund via the "Send bitcoins to another member:" feature found on the account page. This will not reflect on your purchasing stats as a refund. It may turn into a game of trust if the vendor requires you to finalize first and then the vendor will refund you using the funds you just finalized. You can give him/her a certain time frame in which to accomplish this before leaving a 1/5 for non-compliance. The effect of a 1/5 is immediate. So that is generally incentive enough for the vendor to execute this transfer in a timely manner. Figure on anywhere from instantly up to 24 hours.
