Title: One feature that SR needs...
Post by: stinkybuns on April 21, 2013, 04:48 am
This will be a bit of a contentious issue, fraught with obvious problems, but...   What SR needs is a way for trusted buyers to privately sell things to other users.  As an example, I have all sorts of little baggies of reagents that I no longer want to dabble in.  Things that I don't have the means to sell to anyone I know.  What I would like to do is put it all together in one lot, and then sell it for bitcoins right here on the forum.  I am not looking to be a vendor, just looking to get rid of some stuff.

I know there are issues.  There is no real connection between SR and these forums, there is no safety or escrow or anything else in place for the kind of thing I am talking about.  But I do have my standing as a buyer on SR as leverage, my honor to put on the line. 

Has there been anything like this out there?  obviously selling to a vendor would be ideal but of course they would be foolish to expose their mailing address like that.  The site itself needs a modest little 'mini vendor'  section where something like this would be possible.
