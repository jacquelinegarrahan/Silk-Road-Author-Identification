Just remember water boils at 212F at sea level and at temperatures above 180F the active ingredients will start to degrade.
If you are evaporating off the water to make goo it's best to use a double boiler setup or a very wide pan with a small fan blowing over it.
I found the lemon juice to be unnecessary but in regards to pod tea there are many many different styles, just avoid boiling if possible.
