Title: Re: Soon to be new seller on silk road - fees for doing so?
Post by: madamebradley on December 21, 2011, 04:35 pm
The commission is 6.25% on each item. If you use hedged escrow you usually lose 4% of your listed price.

for example. If you list an item for $100 it will show to buyers as costing $106.25. Once the buyer finalizes you will have ~$96 transferred to your account.

I hope this helps answer your questions as this information is to the best of my ability. (If anybody reading this finds an error please let me know.)
