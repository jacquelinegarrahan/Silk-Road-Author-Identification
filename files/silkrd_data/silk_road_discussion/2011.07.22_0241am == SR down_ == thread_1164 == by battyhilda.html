Post by: btcfreedom on July 22, 2011, 11:25 pm
@Xmachina, love that fuckin Signature. lol

If this persists for you guys, just start from the ground up with powercycling/rebooting network equipment. If you have a static IP this matters too...it resets any logging generated and refreshes your relay circuit connection to/from your ISP.

Sometimes our Tor bundles crap the bed, and don't do the identity change fully. If you refresh the page that checks your Tor connection, it'll tell you if you've changed IP's or not.

Change Identity, Go To https://check.torproject.org/?lang=en-US&small=1, Refresh page, IP should change.
Keep changing it till you get a different subnet or range that you think is different from the subsequent connections, and give that a try too.

Don't fuck with your torrc file either. Also, if you're relaying Tor, try connecting to SRM as well. I have luck doing that sometimes, but that could be just my connection.
