Title: Re: Curious what are people calling/considring good buying stats?
Post by: NorCalKing on November 10, 2012, 05:22 am
We'll look at all the numbers, but the only one that will get a message from us before we fill an order is the refund rate.  While a number of vendors are concerned about getting their money soon enough so they can order more product, they should be more concerned about a skittish buyer with a higher refund rate.  There can be a lot more issues with someone who has had issues in the past with refunds,  their not above causing issues, can tend to be more picky, & it will more than likely end up taking longer that a auto-finalized transaction with less $ & more time.

NCK
