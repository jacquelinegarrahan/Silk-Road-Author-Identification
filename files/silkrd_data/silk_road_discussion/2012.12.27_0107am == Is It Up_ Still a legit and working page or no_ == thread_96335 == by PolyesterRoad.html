I believe it was WarWeed who made that script to indicate whether SR is up or down. It hasn't worked in a long while. Last time it worked was before SR was updated with the new format and design. So that would be months ago was the last time that link worked.

As for not being able to get on, have you tried clicking "new identity" on the Vidalia control panel? That often solves many issues. 

You can also use onion.to which is a Tor Proxy to see if SR is up/dn. Just type onion.to in your tor browser then enter Silk Road's URL. Once it's up, don't login. Instead you can open another tab on your tor browser and load silk road's address from there. That little trick has worked for a number of people.
