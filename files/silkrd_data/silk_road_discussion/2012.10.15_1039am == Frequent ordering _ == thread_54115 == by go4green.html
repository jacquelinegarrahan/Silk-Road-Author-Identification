My impression from the lurking I did around the boards before registering is this:
The main factor is the country you're in. If you're in the US, you're far more likely to receive a controlled delivery than, say, if you live in Angola.
Second would be where the packages arrive, third would be the nature of the packages. Frequent small packages from a PO box are much less suspect than large packages delivered to your house addressed to your name.

Just use common sense, read the security forum and minimize your exposure wherever you can.
