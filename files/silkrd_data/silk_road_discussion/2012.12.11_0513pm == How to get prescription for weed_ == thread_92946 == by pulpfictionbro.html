Post by: midwestmedical on December 11, 2012, 11:53 pm
Since marijuana is still a DEA schedue 1 drug, no one can have a real perscription for it.  The closest you could come would be a marinol perscription, and i would love to hear if anyone knows how to fake their way into a script for that.

The way medical pot works, at least in my state, is the doctor simply writes a recomendation that marijuana may help you with your condition.  The state law should have the acceptable conditons in it, just find some that are easy to fake and go with it.  Obviously you cant fake the serious condions like cancer, glaucoma, and aids but there are usually some vague conditons like chronic pain, chronic nausea, or muscle spasams.

Like others have said, find a pot doc.  I have never heard of anyone who filled out the paperwork and paid the fees for the program being denied.
