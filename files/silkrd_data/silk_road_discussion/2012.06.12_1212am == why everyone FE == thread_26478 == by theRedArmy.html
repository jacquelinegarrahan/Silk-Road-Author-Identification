If you have good stats then you wont be asked to finalize early on regular orders(bulk is another story). If you have low stats then the seller is attempting to protect themselves. Buyers scam much more then sellers. For this reason FE is the choice of many vendors. I suggest either deal with it or build up your stats and deal with select vendors. I start off slow and small with a new vendor. I like to sample the product and evaluate service and product quality first hand. I try to limit test order amounts to $50 or less. If that goes well I make a few more larger orders, lets say $100, $175 and $200. By the time those orders are taken care of me and the vendor have dealt with each other a few times and we have a level of trust. Its at this point that I would ask the vendor for a larger order.
