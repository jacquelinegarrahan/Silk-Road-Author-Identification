Litecoin is nearly identical to Bitcoin. The main difference is that it is harder to build computers for the purpose of mining them (ASICs).

It has all of the shortcomings of Bitcoin, except that it's worse because there's virtually no economy supporting it.

Atlantis, which was known for only accepting Litecoin, just started taking Bitcoin.

As far as I can tell, all the alternative *coins are useless.
