I turn up when I say I'm going to turn up. I bring exactly the amount they ordered. I even carry around a little bit of change in case they don't have the correct money. The way I see it, I'm a businessman, not a dealer. I produce a product off my own back, using my own skills. I market it, I run my own quality control, I price it, I bag it, I deliver it. It's not that hard to do... if you're professional.

The way I see it, BTC's and SR have created a hole in space time. It feels like we're in the Wild West, a new frontier. I have a product that I produced with my own hands using my own skills, and you want some. No Government regulation, no red tape, no tax, and using BTC's and Tor, it's pretty much anonymous. New vendors should be appreciative of the opportunity before them, as I do.
