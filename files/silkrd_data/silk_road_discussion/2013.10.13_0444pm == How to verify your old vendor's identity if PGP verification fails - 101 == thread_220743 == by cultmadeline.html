Title: How to verify your old vendor's identity if PGP verification fails - 101
Post by: cultmadeline on October 13, 2013, 04:44 pm
If your vendor updated his PGP key immediately after the SR takedown, you likely will not be able to verify who he says he is.  In this case, I suggest you provide him/her 4-10- random usernames, one of which should be your old username.

If you are a frequent customer, your vendor should have no problem identifying you and your purchase history.  If you are a new customer, wait until the feedbacks of frequent customers kick in.

I hope this helps ease your mind.
