Title: Re: My pin number
Post by: UKGrower on August 12, 2012, 11:51 pm
When you originally made your account, the system should have asked you to create a pin at that point, in addition to your primary password.  You won't need to supply this pin to anyone else, but you will need it to transfer funds from your account to vendors, or to withdraw bitcoins.

I'm not sure on the specific details, but I believe that you generally have to wait 7 days to have your pin reset.  Check the "technical support" forum for more details (http://dkn255hz262ypmii.onion/index.php?board=8.0), and maybe repost your question there if you can't find a solution in one of the existing threads.
