possibly to prove that you're telling the truth? i have no experience with hash and i dunno what it even looks like but im guessing if 50% of it is missing it would be half as "big".

one vendor once sent me the totally wrong product (tempazepam instead of diazepam) and asked for a photo of what i received, i guess this just proves to the vendor that i have in fact received the vendor's tempaz instead of the diazepam and that confirms he has made a mistake and then sorts it out.

if you're worried, there are Onion service web hosts and you can strip all the data from JPGs using online services too so it willl be untraceable, then use PGP to encrypt the file address.
