Title: Funny SR stories - Buyers Or Vendors, C'mon in!!!
Post by: MrAnonymous on June 16, 2013, 09:02 am
Basically, when I began vending I sold a bulk amount (100g of my super pure MDMA) to a buyer here who had great stats, no refunds, never auto-finalized and had spent 3k+.

I did not tell the buyer but I sent it tracked (he didn't have to sign or anything, just the delivery guy ticks a thing on the little computer thing once it has been delivered to notify me)

The package arrived fine 12 days after posting to the other side of the globe - but the buyer never finalized. I left it till day 20 and asked him to finalize. Then he decided to claim that it had never arrived and that I needed to honour my 100% refund to him if it never arrived in the next 10 days or I was getting feedback from hell.

So I sent him a link to check tracking and I sent him the tracking number and told him that this would be enough proof to SR staff to side in my favour.

His reply was 'It never arrived but what the hell I'll finalize anyway' - obviously this idiot was trying to scam me.But when he realised I was a step ahead decided to give up.


The exact same thing happened with another customer of mine who was a repeat bulk buyer - he made a purchase every 2 weeks, like two weeks on the dot from the day he received the product. But on his 4th order it was only 5 days after he received the product. That looked strange to me, so I tracked the packet.


20 days later - he claims it's not shown and says 'Look at all the orders I've placed with you, you know I wouldn't rip you off'. Tracking says it arrived 10 days prior. I call him out and show him the tracking. He never replies and his funds auto-finalize.


All of you buyers always talk about being careful (and you all should be - this place is ripe with scammers) but vendors need to be just as careful because you never know who will fuck you over. Like I state above one of my best customers tried to pull one over on me.








Anyway today is a pleasant Sunday and we are experiencing same main site problem so I thought it would be a good idea to share some stories!  :)
