Title: Re: Silk Road Sale
Post by: mdpro on May 01, 2013, 12:40 pm
Whether you saw a difference in prices on vendor sites depends on their account settings.  Those vendors who list their items with "post commission" pricing would have no change in the price of their listings.  Those who list with "pre commission" pricing would have shown a decrease in the price of their listings that was their asking price only.  I don't know how most vendors choose to set up their listings, so I can't provide as much insight as I would like.  I do know that my listing prices were lower during the sale because the SR fees were not added for that time period.  Between the volatility of the bitcoin and the fairly nominal fees (some complex structure I would have to look up to tell you), the prices of listings didn't seem that different.  But DPR was true to his word and suspended fees for those few days.
