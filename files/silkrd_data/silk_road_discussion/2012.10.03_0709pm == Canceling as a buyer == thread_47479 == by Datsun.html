Title: Re: Canceling as a buyer
Post by: frank-butcher24 on October 03, 2012, 07:21 pm
It won't affect your buyer stats. I've only cancelled once. I had some coins which weren't doing anything much, so I placed an order. The vendor was away for the weekend, so after I had thought about whether I really wanted the product, I cancelled. I'm sure the vendor wouldn't give a shit, and I may be wrong but the they may not even know you've cancelled, if they haven't got around to doing anything with your order yet.

I believe you may get back fewer coins than you paid. Something to do with the fee for a hedged amount, or something.

I would say, if there's anything at all that's making you feel uncomfortable, cancel.
