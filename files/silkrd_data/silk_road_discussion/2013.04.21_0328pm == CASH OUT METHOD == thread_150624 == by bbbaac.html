Title: Re: CASH OUT METHOD
Post by: SycoPunk on April 21, 2013, 03:50 pm
Money is stored in your SR account in Bitcoins so if the value of Bitcoin goes up then yes, you would have more than $50.

The easiest way to get out your money is to go to bitinstant.com and have it get sent to your paypal but thats also fairly expensive.

The cheapest way is to get an account at mtgox.com and dwolla.com and verify your account at mtgox. Then send btc to mtgox and trade it for USD then send your usd from there to dwolla then from dwolla to your linked baqck account. This method costs you 0.6% (only 60 cents per $100) and a $0.25 fee as well.

Some vendors decide to hedge their orders though.
Which means that as long as the order isn't finalized, SR will make sure they are paid the same amount in $.
So if a $100 order is finalized but the coins have since dropped in value, they will still get $100 in bitcoins at the time of finalization.
However all the coins already in their wallet are subject to the rise and fall of the Bitcoin price.
