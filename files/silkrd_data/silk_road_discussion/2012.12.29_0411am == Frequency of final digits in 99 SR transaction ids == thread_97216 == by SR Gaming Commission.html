The Lotto game (wins when final digit is 7) is paying out somewhat more than expected, but not too much.

What really killed us was the triple sevens game. Of those 99 tx ids, we had 3 occurrences of triple 7s. Two is predicted (2.2% chance of triple sevens). Unfortunately two of those occurrences were in the super slots game (where triple sevens pays 10B) and only 20 tickets had sold. That's a loss five times what "should" be the case. Like you mention astor, randomness is... random.  We have enough bankroll to get through the occasional high net loss. Too many night like this or worse though and were going out of business.

Just goes to show you variances can do real damage with high payoffs. We're probably going to hold off on 100BTC and higher payoffs until we get a good handle on this.
