Title: Re: Mt. Gox to instawallet?
Post by: grandaddyturtle on February 24, 2012, 02:37 am
I used bitinstant and that gave me a mtgox code, which i redeemed and then I had to buy the bitcoins.  Be VERY careful using instawallet.  Bookmark the wallet with your BTC in it, and make sure you dont click on ANYTHING before doing so.  I clicked the FAQ and before I knew it I had a newly generated blank wallet.  I had to dig through my history to find the account with the BTC in it, so thankfully I wasn't using tor at the time. 

So pretty much the flow is like cash -> mtgox credit -> BTC -> instawallet -> SR
Not sure how buybitcoins operates, but if they themselves are a wallet and give you credit in BTC instead of dollars/mt gox coupon codes you shouldn't HAVE to use mtgox.
