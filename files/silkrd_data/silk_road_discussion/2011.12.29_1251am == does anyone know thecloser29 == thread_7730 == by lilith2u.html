Title: Re: does anyone know thecloser29
Post by: madamebradley on December 29, 2011, 05:02 am
I do not know thecloser29. If you made a transaction outside of escrow then it's likely that you've made your first noob mistake. Trading on the forum is very rarely a good idea, although there have been successful transactions. However they're very unique as someone will front their product to a well respected member of the community who will pay upon receiving the goods. Even that poses risks as there have been a few very high profile vendors who have simply picked up and left. What I'm trying to say is *always* deal in escrow unless you've developed trust with a vendor, and even then be very careful.
