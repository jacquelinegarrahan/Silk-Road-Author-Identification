I wouldn't read too much into these things... having shipped over 800 packages so far I have seen the weirdest things...

I shipped a priority package 4 states below mine which has a two day guarantee... it sat at a sort facility between me and the customer for seven days before finally moving on and finishing it journey... I just knew it had got hit but everything went fine... sometimes i think packages fall off conveyor belts or just get sent to the wrong place.

unless you get a letter or something else happens that makes you wary of something possibly happening I would just remain cool and for gods sake - DONT SIGN FOR ANYTHING!!!
