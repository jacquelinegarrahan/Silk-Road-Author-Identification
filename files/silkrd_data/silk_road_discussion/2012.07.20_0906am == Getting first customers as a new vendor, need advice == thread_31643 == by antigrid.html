Title: Re: Getting first customers as a new vendor, need advice
Post by: RxKing on July 21, 2012, 05:32 am
The worst thing you can do is give free samples. They are not buyers. And there reviews won't do shit.

Think in terms of running this like a legit business. You need to advertise. You need people to know you are selling and that you have your shit straight and will get them what they want as fast as possible.

I think what works best is offering  customers free pills. You get legit buyers and they get hooked up. So much so that they will take a chance on you.

Also you said you lowered your prices...Why didn't you just start low in the first place? You are trying to get customers to use you instead of the other vendors. They need a reason. Price is #1.(as of right now for you, it won't be in the future). They need a reason to switch to you. You need to make sure your vendor page is in order.

From what you said you sell Adderall, so I will use that as my example.

Lets say you have 20mg IR, barr brand, orange pills. Say the average price on SR is $14.00 a pill. You should sell for $10. Then offer something like if you buy 10 you get 5 free. Charge for shipping. Explain on your vendor page that you will ship same day as purchased if before a certain time.... say 4pm EST. Make sure your shipping prices are fair. By fair I mean the exact price that it cost you. Make sure  to state your packages will be stealth and they will get their pills not broken. You need to convince the buyers you know your shit and you will take care of them. You also need to know your shit and actually take care of them.

You need to put up "adds" in the forums section for product offers. Don't spam but at the same time, you need to make sure people know you exist. Also make sure your vendor page is a full description of everything you offer and all your policy's, how you ship, where you ship to, I mean everything.

Make a post introducing yourself. Make sure all your post are in the right forums.

I do not understand why you wouldn't use the same name on both the forums and on SR. I don't know what the benefit of keeping them separate. I think that is a dumb idea. You should tell people what your "other" name is. Or you need to just have 1 name in both spots.

Bottom line is you need to get the word out there and you need to be on top of your shit. It is not easy. In fact there are tons of things I am not even bringing up. Being a vendor is a lot of work. Especially in the beginning.

SR needs more great vendors. By great I mean vendors that ship their products as fast as they can, that understand how to package and to make sure your package gets there without anything being broken. That you respond to messages fast. And that you have exactly what you state you have. The bottom line is all people care about is getting what they want as fast as they can.

 And I think you being new you should be available 24/7 basically. You should return all messages within 15 minutes. If you can't be online a lot, then put on your vendor page the hours you are online. So they know when you are available.

And most important is staying good. You should only get better as a vendor. Not worse. There are plenty of posts from vendors telling about our mistakes. Read them and try to learn from them so you don't make the same ones.

 Just be honest to buyers, do what you say you are going to. Don't let an order sit there. Don't mark in transit when it is still at your house.

And don't take any shit. There are a lot of scumbags. But there are a lot of great buyers. More then enough for you to sell all of your product.

Just do your homework and be smart.

Good Luck, I hope you make it!
