Title: Re: What's up with SR BTC value
Post by: MushroomMagician on June 06, 2013, 09:52 pm
The biggest cause of the discrepancy is probably the fact that most vendors set their pricing pre-commission meaning that they tell SR how much they want to charge, then SR automatically adds their commission on top of that. So if a seller sets a price that is equivalent to 100 USD, it will display a few dollars higher than that.
