Hello HighGirl,

As far as I'm aware 'Seller Statistics' have never been available for safety and security reasons, the only statistics available are the 'Purchasing Statistics'. If you wish to calculate if you've achieved the required $1500 for access to 'The Roundtable' you can do so from your account history (you'll be able to view six weeks of records, older records are automatically deleted), which you can access by clicking on account ฿-.-- (-.-- your Bitcoin amount here) > Account history. Hope this helps you :)
