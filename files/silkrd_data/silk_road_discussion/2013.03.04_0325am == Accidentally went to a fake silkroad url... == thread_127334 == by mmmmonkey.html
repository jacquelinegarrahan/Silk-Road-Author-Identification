Title: Accidentally went to a fake silkroad url...
Post by: mmmmonkey on March 04, 2013, 03:25 am
I reinstalled my Tor browser and couldn't find the silk toad url, so I went to a hidden wiki mirror to find it.

I got to the page and saw the same normal login, except with a "PIN" field , apparently for extra security.
After typing in my credentials 3 times and not being able to login, I looked at the URL and it wasn't the normal URL.
I looked for the silkroad url I was familiar with and logged in with no problem (and without a "PIN") field.
I went into my account settings and changed my password and PIN right away.

Hopefully this will keep me safe :\

Just a friendly reminder to check your URL, and change your password and PIN on a regular basis!

Post by: Fallkniven on March 04, 2013, 08:30 pm
you can backup your bookmarks before you uninstall old TBB and import them after you install the new TBB


While the Tor browser is open:

press Ctrl-Shift-B (or click the Bookmarks menu, then click Show All Bookmarks)

click 'Import and Backup', click 'Backup' to save your bookmarks to a familiar location.

click 'Restore > Choose File' to reload the bookmarks back into Tor browser.
