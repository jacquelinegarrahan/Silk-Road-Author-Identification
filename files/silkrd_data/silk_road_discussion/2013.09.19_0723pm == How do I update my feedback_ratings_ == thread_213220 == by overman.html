Title: Re: How do I update my feedback/ratings?
Post by: plainsman1963 on September 19, 2013, 08:08 pm
There is no way to edit your rankings/reviews. If you elected to leave no rating, then you have permanently left no rating. In the future, after finalizing, don't leave a rating or review until you are confident what that rating/review should be.
Title: Re: How do I update my feedback/ratings?
Post by: NW Nugz on September 19, 2013, 09:23 pm
A vendor who encourages ratings and feedback from new customers before you receive the product would make me nervous.

If you click to accept the default choice of 'no rating', you just rated them a no rating and cant change that. If you don't click on it, you can choose a rating later. I'm not sure if you can do feedback and then a rating later or a rating and do feedback later. I think you can. But, if you click to choose the 6th rating choice, you just rated them permanently. SR ought to make this much easier to understand. Maybe add the word "permanently" to the no-rating choice and the accept-rating button.

Title: Re: How do I update my feedback/ratings?
Post by: doodoo79 on September 20, 2013, 09:31 am
You don't have to leave feedback immediately. Any time after you've completed your transaction but haven't left a review/feedback yet, visit that vendor's page, right under his/her name there will be 4 options instead of 3 options written in green. You'll see the normal "message", "discuss", and "report" buttons but until you've left your written review there will also be a fourth button written in green that says, you guessed it, "review". And it can never be changed. This has been the rule for about a month now.
