Title: Re: What is the most euphoric drug you have ever done?
Post by: PiggyPie on June 29, 2012, 08:00 pm
AMT followed by MDMA

Ok so MDMA is pretty great euphoria wise, lots of lovey feeling to go along with it. Great party drug but often a bit of trouble sleeping, and come down isn't very nice (joys of a serotonin deficit). Also it can be a bit out of control in my opinion like a bit like loosing yourself which is fine if that's what you are after. I also find it quite moreish, ive taken over 1g in a night (not clever, i know) and that really was quite the experience.

BUT my favourite drug for euphoria is AMT. Do a bit of research on erowid blah blah if you haven't heard of it (seems alot of people haven't) but its a psychedelic that lasts up to 12 hours (does include a slow comeup of up to 3 hours though) that provides pretty mild visuals initially but what really hit me about it was just how happy it makes me. Less stimulating than MDMA but imo far more euphoric and so so so much cleaner feeling. Not really any urge to redose, i personally have never experienced any negative side effects (vomiting prior to coming up is common). Also it has no comedown, at all. In fact i normally feel better the day after i took it than the day before. Final point, in the UK at least, it is legal ! Its a research chemical (illegal in the US) and at least in the UK its pretty cheap, huge amount of bang for buck. For me (using street prices, not SR which is really cheap for MDMA) a night on MDMA would cost £20 for .5g and i would be needing to constantly rehydrate throughout a night (makes for quite a sweaty drug), AMT would cost me £1.50 with no need for re-hydration. The choice is obvious (at least for me) =)

I found LSD pretty euphoric but i think that was more just because i had been a little nervous before trying it, and i was just so happy and amazed by the visuals, really recommend it ! =)
