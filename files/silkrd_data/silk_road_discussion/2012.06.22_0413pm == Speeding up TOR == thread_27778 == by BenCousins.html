Title: Re: Speeding up TOR
Post by: anonaddict on June 23, 2012, 02:47 am
Add this to the end of your torrc file
Code: [Select]
# Try for at most NUM seconds when building circuits. If the circuit isn't
# open in that time, give up on it. (Default: 1 minute.)
CircuitBuildTimeout 5
# Send a padding cell every N seconds to keep firewalls from closing our
# connections while Tor is not in use.
KeepalivePeriod 60
# Force Tor to consider whether to build a new circuit every NUM seconds.
NewCircuitPeriod 15
# How many entry guards should we keep at a time?
NumEntryGuards 8
I don't think it works too well with the Bundles.

I use tor stand alone and installed torbutton in firefox. I usually get DSL speed while going through SilkRoad.
