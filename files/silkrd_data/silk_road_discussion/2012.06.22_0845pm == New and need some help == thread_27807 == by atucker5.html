Post by: Kappacino on June 22, 2012, 09:07 pm
on FEing:

This is where they ask you to release funds early from escrow. The normal protocol is that you release the funds when the product arrives

However as you are a new buyer, almost all vendors will ask you to finalise early as there is no guarantee that you won't just lie and say you didn't receive the package.

It is ultimately your choice whether you do or not. Many people are very skeptical of FEing now because of a recent scam by one of the sites most trusted vendors that cost the sr community about $250,000

Yet at the same time, it is seen as necessary for new buyers to prove themselves, and after you have a few purhcases vendors will trust you more and allow you to stay within escrow

I would look at the guys feedback and make sure that he is 100% legit and has a lot of transactions before you order. Even so, there is no guarantee that you won't be scammed, it is unlikely, but it does happen. And as you are a new buyer, if you claim you didn't receive anything, SR admins will obviously side with the vendor as he has an established reputation

So your choice is to either take the relatively small risk, or find a vendor that will allow new buyers to FE (you might be hard pressed to do such a thing)

However when you have a few transactions under your belt and you are still asked to FE, be very wary.
