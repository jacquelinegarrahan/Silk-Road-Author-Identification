Title: Re: Acceptable refund rate vendors please weigh in.
Post by: TrustusJones on March 02, 2012, 01:57 pm
I hate to say it but I try and just go with my gut and a little common sense.
If someone wanted a pound of weed and they didn't have stellar, off the charts purchasing stats I would  break it up into quarters and ship it a quarter at a time... get one, finalize... send another.
In the end I am the one who is taking the majority loss if things go bad so I don't think asking someone, with not so solid stats, to work with me is asking to much.
 'ALL' sellers need some kind of 'Refund Policy' on their main seller page and on each item listing... yeah I know it is a hassle especially if you have a ton of listings but if it came down to SR making a decision on a dispute between a buyer and a seller I would think the Refund Policy would hold considerable weight.
Lastly I am not Pablo Escobar... I cannot afford to lose shipments to the post office and cheating buyers. My 50/50 policy is designed to lessen the impact of an interdicted package on myself and the buyer and in the case of a cheating buyer he only gets half his money back so hopefully he will pick a richer seller instead of someone selling cheap weed.

Unfortunately there are far to many loopholes in this system to ever make me feel safe to sell to anyone... in the end all we have is our gut and a little common sense.

Thanks!
TJ
