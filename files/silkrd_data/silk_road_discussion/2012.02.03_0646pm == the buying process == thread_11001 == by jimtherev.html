Title: Re: the buying process
Post by: Horizons on February 03, 2012, 07:12 pm
In the top navigation bar of Silk Road, there's a link that says "account($0.00)". Clicking on that link will take you to a page which, among other things, displays the address of a bitcoin wallet associated with your account. You fund your SR account by transferring bitcoins to that wallet. They take a few hours to show up, because SR tumbles them a few times on the way.

Once your account is funded, bitcoins will be automatically deducted from your account and put into escrow when you confirm an order. If the order is cancelled, you get the coins back; and if it finalizes, the coins get sent to the seller.
