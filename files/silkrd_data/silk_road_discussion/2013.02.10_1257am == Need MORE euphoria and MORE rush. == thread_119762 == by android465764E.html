Title: Re: Need MORE euphoria & MORE rush.
Post by: Cimicon-Rep on February 10, 2013, 02:01 am
Obtain 50-75mg of Ice Shard Methamphetamine.
Obtain 20-30mg of Oxycodone (either as Percocet which contains tylenol, or pure like a Roxi) - use only 1/2 to 1/3 if you have a low tolerance to oxycodone.

Crush all items into a fine powder. Add mixed powder into a shot glass and add orange juice or Gatorade to mask the taste and to dissolve the power. I would recommend stirring with a glass stirring rod or a toothpick.. You want to dissolve as much of the powder into the liquid as possible.

Drink but hold the drink in your mouth. Do not swallow yet. After about a minute, you can swallow. You might want to chase it down with some other flavorful beverage (preferably non-alcoholic). Eat some chocolate also.

Enjoy the sweet result. But know your limits. Don't re-dose until after 8 hours has past. If it wasn't as strong as you hoped, then next time up your quantities by 25%. The recommended span of time for re-dosing is for your protection. The goal was to enjoy yourself, not harm yourself.
