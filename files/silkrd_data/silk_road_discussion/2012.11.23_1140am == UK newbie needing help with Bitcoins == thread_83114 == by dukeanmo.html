Title: Re: UK newbie needing help with Bitcoins
Post by: Hellenge on November 23, 2012, 11:51 am
I'm pretty new myself, but you could try localbitcoins.com.  Find a bitcoin dealer local to you, check their rating looks ok and place an order. You can then send a private message to the vendor to ask about method of payment.

You could buy openly by bank transfer then 'launder' the bitcoins through various wallets or fogging services (depending on level of paranoia). Or you could meet the dealer in person and hand over cash (the BTC are kept in escrow, but the dealer can release them using their mobile, which triggers a message (and secret passphrase) being sent to you to confirm the BTC are yours.)  The latter method means there won't be an electronic link between you and the coins.
