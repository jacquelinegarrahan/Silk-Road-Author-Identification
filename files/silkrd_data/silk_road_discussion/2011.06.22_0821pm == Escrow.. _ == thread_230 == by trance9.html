Title: Re: Escrow.. ?
Post by: Innocuous on June 22, 2011, 08:49 pm
If you hover your mouse over the words "Resolve" or "Finalize" it should tell you.

From memory, the order will automatically be "finalized" in 28 days - you might want to check though, I'm not 100% sure on the exact amount of days. As soon as you click this it should send the btc once the tumbling and the confirmations for each transfer (in the tumble) has happened.

Resolve will become enabled after 18-20 days I believe. So you have a window of opportunity to bring up any problems/scamming. This is where the btc gets "tied up" though if you ask me its a small price to pay for getting your money back! It will not automatically return you the btc as far as I know, I believe Silk Road him/herself has to do that.

If the seller does not pick up the order, then in 4 days a new option "cancel" will appear.

Hope this is of some help!
