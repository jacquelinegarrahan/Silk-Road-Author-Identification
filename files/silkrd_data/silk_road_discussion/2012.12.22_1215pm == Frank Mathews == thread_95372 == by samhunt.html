Post by: HOUSE on December 22, 2012, 07:50 pm
It's my personal policy not to do business with vendors who require FE.

It's the easiest way to get scammed. Vendors don't risk anything by using the escrow system (yeah, they don't get immediate payment and their BTC are tied up for a while, so what?) since they will always get paid eventually if they deliver what they promise. However, the buyer risks EVERYTHING by FE.

And it has happened time and time again - a vendor starts off not requiring FE, builds up a reputation, starts requiring FE, continues selling for a while without problems, and then, all of a sudden, they disappear with a lot of people's money.

So I've decided that the risk of getting scammed and having my money stolen far outweighs the benefit from buying the products these vendors offer.

If everyone reasoned the way I do, you can bet that most vendors would stop requiring FE. But people don't and so they encourage vendors to do so and give them an opportunity on a golden plate to steal their money.
