You can calculate the current Bitcoin price used by SR by going to the "Settings" link at the top of the page & changing from "display prices in Dollars" to "display prices in Bitcoins."  Divide dollars by Bitcoins & you have the Bitcoin value that SR is using. 

SR has to get the current Bitcoin pice from somewhere in order to have the correct value of a single Bitcoin.  Why this value can't be displayed is a mystery, but it doesn't have anything to do with security.
