Title: Re: Please update feedback with drug quality
Post by: BenJesuit on April 21, 2012, 07:50 pm
Well for pharmaceuticals, there really is no need. In the off chance that they are fake, it will be represented in the feedback. Like "weak benzo, freakin' sugar pill."

For weed and psychedelics, it's too subjective an experience to leave information about quality in feedback. Those require reviews which detail environment, amount taken, method of administration, etc.

For things like Cocaine, Meth, and Heroine... past quality is not necessarily indicative of future quality. They also deserve review threads. Though a note in feedback like "wasn't as potent as advertized" or "was exactly as described" would give an indication of a trend of quality. But really, even here, the method of administration can have vastly different results due to the variation in bio-availability per method of administration. So a review thread would better serve in these instances also.

Your idea sounds good on paper but if implemented may do more harm than good.
