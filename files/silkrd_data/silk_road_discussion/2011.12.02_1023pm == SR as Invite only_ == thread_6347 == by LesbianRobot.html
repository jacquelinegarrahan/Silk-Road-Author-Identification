The expression, "A fool and his money are soon parted" is quite accurate on SR.   

I am convinced that there exists no fool proof AND easy way to prevent scammers in an anonymous environment.  There will always be scammers.  We just have to make it more difficult for scammers and that is what these forums are for.  If a person refuses to read these forums in spite of repeated encouragement to do so and then they get scammed than I frankly dont have much pity for them.  As a new buyer you might be understandably asked to finalize early - if you are buying from one of the long standing, reputable vendors then you have nothing to worry about.  If you are a new seller you must go through escrow and if you dont want to sell to a buyer with no history, then I dont blame you.  Eventually you will build the rep to demand out of escrow purchases from unestablished buyers. 

If both buyers and sellers are established there is no reason to go out of escrow.
