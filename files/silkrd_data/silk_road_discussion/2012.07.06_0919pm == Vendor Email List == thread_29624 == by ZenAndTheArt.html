When I first started traveling along the Silk Road, I noticed that some vendors have their contact address in their profile page. Most of them stated that if Silk Road should ever go offline, they could be contacted on this email address.

This got me thinking. If Silk Road was ever taken down, it would be a real shame to lose contact with some of the great people I've meet here! So, I started to create my own contact list of favourite vendors. This then became a list of  'vendors I may wish to use one day'. Now I'm thinking of compiling an extensive list of ALL vendors! Which, if the worst did happen, I could some how share with other Silk Roaders.

I'd like to know, firstly, if this list already exists? Do you think it's worthwhile compiling such a list? If Silk Road went down, how could this list be made available to the Silk Road users safely, without being made to public?

Maybe it could be started as a post on the forum, with vendors adding their email addresses to it?

If this hasn't already been done and you guy's think it's worthwhile, then I'd be happy to start and if any vendors wish to be included please either message me or post your email address here.

Anyway, much love to my fellow Roaders  :)
