Title: Re: Best Strains of Cannabis on the Road?
Post by: sourdieselman on March 18, 2013, 05:26 am
The best is subjective. Asking what is the best strain of weed is like asking what is the best flavor of ice cream. As a fairly popular weed vendor on SR and someone who s whole life revolves around it I can honestly say there is not one type of weed that is clearly better than another. It is sad to see weed being so trendy these days. People don't stop and ask themselves what they believe to be the best for them, they let someone tell them what is the best. Usually this influince comes from rap songs  and geographic prefrences. In the north east they will say the best is sour diesel. In the south they will say that it is kush. In Florida and other inner city areas they will say that it is purples. Just because a strain is high yielding and popular among growers like blue dream does not mean it is any less potent. In fact if you look at the numbers of crops grown and the quality that comes out of them, strains like Blue Dream and M39 are very rarely screwed up and ussually grown very nicely. On the other hand strains like Sour Diesel Chemdawg  or any of the kush strains, very commonly become hermaphrididic and stressed in one way or another.  It s sad to see people who think that  Sour Diesel that is completely stressed and hermaphrididic is better that well finished Blue Dream because little wayne says he s smokin on sour and all of his friends tell him that Sour Diesel is the best weed.

Think for yourself
