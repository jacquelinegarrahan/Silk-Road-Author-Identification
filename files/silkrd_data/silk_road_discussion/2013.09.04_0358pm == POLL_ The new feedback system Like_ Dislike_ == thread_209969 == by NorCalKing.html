Just as buyers are having to adjust to the new feedback system and determing through reviews and discussions who they wish to continue ordering from, vendors need to adjust as well and realize that a 5/5 just shouldn't be the expected norm under this new system.  If you're a top 1%-5% vendor, always provide outstanding customer service with quality product and fast shipping, then I can see expecting a 5/5 on all orders.  If you just get it done, take days to respond to messages, are slow on shipping but still get the described product to a buyer with decent stealth, then I see no reason why a 4/5, or if you're just an average vendor, a 3/5 should be insulting.  Not all the vendors I've dealth with on SR have been what I would call outstanding, but this historical trend of always expecting 5/5 ratings and blacklisting buyers who don't leave stellar feedback makes it really hard to differentiate between the truly outstanding vendors and the ones who just get it done.  In a few months, with this new system in place, you'll see a wider range of ratings and it will be easier to judge who is truly a top vendor.  Personally, I'm not a huge fan of the new feedback system for other reasons, but if it starts getting more accurate and honest feedback to judge vendors by and gets the community away from this pointless, everyone should have 5/5 mentallity, then at least some good will come of it.
