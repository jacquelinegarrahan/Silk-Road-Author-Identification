Title: Re: Why does SR charge a fee when an order is cancelled ?
Post by: MaidMandy on April 24, 2013, 09:58 am
If a vendor hedges their transactions, the 4% hedging fee is an irreversible charge taken by SR as soon as the order is placed. It sucks when an order is cancelled by no mistake of your own, but that is how it's done, unfortunately. :(
