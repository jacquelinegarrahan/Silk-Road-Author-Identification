Title: Re: Seller Shipping Mistake
Post by: Reseller on March 05, 2012, 06:15 pm
Hi FunkyNuts,
 Speaking from a vendors point of view, I can say that it is very possible that he simply mixed up your address with another customer.

I imagine as you state 'he was an established vendor', he must have been receiving at least 5-10+ orders per day as is the norm for most serious vendors.

I would wait until he returns from his vacation and send him a PM. Do not bombard his inbox with PM's.

The reason I say this is I once embarked on a 7 day snowboarding trip in Patagonia. I forgot to check the box for 'vacation mode' before I left. When I returned I had 187 orders and 244 PM's. Don't add to the vendors probably overflowing PM box.

One PM should suffice.
