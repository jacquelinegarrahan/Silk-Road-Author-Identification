Post by: mrroboto187 on July 19, 2011, 07:28 pm
When you place an order it goes like this:
1. add item to cart
2. checkout
3. your funds get transferred to escrow (deducting that amount from your account instantly)
4. funds stay in escrow until you hit finalize
5. hit finalize, funds are send to seller from escrow
