Post by: sharkonwhisy on August 16, 2011, 04:47 pm
Hawaiian Pan cyans in particular are definitely my fave species and strain. Consistently provide an amazing experience. You can grow them easily if you are capable of cubensis, they are just fussier, and also lower yielding. However they are MUCH more potent and fruit sooner than cubensis, and the dried mushrooms retain potency much better than dried cubensis as well. Also Pan cyan spore prints are easy to take and have a much longer shelf life than cubensis spore prints. Will be growing these for sale on Silk Road when I'm done with uni.
