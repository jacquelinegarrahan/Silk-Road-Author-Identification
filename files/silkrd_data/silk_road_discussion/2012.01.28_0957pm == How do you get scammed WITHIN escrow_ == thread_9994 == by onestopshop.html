Title: Re: How do you get scammed WITHIN escrow?
Post by: Silpheed on January 29, 2012, 05:29 pm
There are many ways buyers can scam vendors, and many ways vendors can scam buyers.

However, talking about those scam methods can lead to more people using those scams. I suppose people might be able to avoid some scams that are discussed (don't finalize early, don't go out of escrow, etc). But other scams might just spread more, especially if they're not easily avoidable.

I don't really know how the "resolution center" handles everything, but even IF a buyer gets 25% or 50% of their money back, they've still thrown away 75% or 50% of their money for nothing. Losing half your money for nothing is still a scam.

Maybe the vendor is honest and the package was seized, but a buyer may still feel scammed. Or maybe the package arrived, but didn't contain what the buyer thought it would. Or maybe the quality was not what they were expecting.
