Title: Re: Selling my shroomz
Post by: DigitalAlch on February 23, 2012, 09:06 pm
At 100 an O I guarantee people would buy. It cost around ~$180 worth of BTC to become a vendor, plus you will need to get a vac seal, and some colored tissue paper(the kind they put in gift bags).  Put the shrooms in a ziplock with a small air bubble (so they don't get crushed when you vac seal), vacseal, wrap in a layer or two of tissue (in case the package gets accidentally knocked open when traveling in the mail), and ALWAYS print the labels (you can get vac seal, and printer shipping labels at major department store). Once you have your account, find the smallest dose that will make a slightly heavy / large person trip decently. Make this your sample amount, and charge a small amount; sell 10 -20 samples. Then start listing ounces.
Good luck, and other than that just treat your customers great and you will go really well.
