Title: Re: PSA: You don't have to leave feedback when you finalize (early)
Post by: tan on April 11, 2012, 08:57 am
The FE helps you understand that the seller has asked for FE. The seller's / item page's often state "no FE" but the seller still asks for FE. In this (frequent) cases it is good to know that the seller likely will be asking me to fe if i order from him/her.

One other benefit is that if a seller suddenly starts having loads and loads of FE's, you can see this as a possible fraud warning. In my opinion it is quite ok to let others know about the FE but the feedback should be revised after receiving the order so it actually reflects the whole transaction.
