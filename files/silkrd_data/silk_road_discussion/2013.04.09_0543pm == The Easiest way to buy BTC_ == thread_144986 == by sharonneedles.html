Title: Re: The Easiest way to buy BTC?
Post by: abby on April 09, 2013, 06:58 pm
If you're completely new to sl, this is what you do.. (the detail of some steps I've omitted as they have changed since I last did it)

Go to virwox and create your account
go to secondlife.com and create your account. 
go back to virwox and put in your account name
download the sl viewer (there'll be a link on the sl account set up page) and once it's installed open it
put in your account name and password and click login
when you arrive, switch voice off (unless you like the sound of racist, pig ignorant knuckle draggers baying at you) and don't respond to anyone (scammers galore).  If you fancy doing the newbie tutorial on how to do the basics, go ahead but if you don't..

go back to virwox and go here https://www.virwox.com/terminals.php
pick any active link (no warning signs beside it) and right click > copy link

go back to the sl viewer and ctrl-h, you'll see a chat history window open that has all the drivel that those around you have been spewing in text
paste the copied link into the chat bar at the bottom of the page, check your chat history window and it should show the link then click on it.  if it asks you are  you sure you want to tp, say yes
when you arrive, you should be in front of the terminal (looks a bit like a silver pulpit and has the virwox logo on it), if you don't see it, you should see a red arrow on the screen which indicates you need to walk towards the arrow.  use your keyboard arrow keys for the walking thing

left click the machine and it should give you some options and it should say it's owned by euroSLEX Euler .  Check the name before you do  anything as scams do happen.

it should give you an option to verify, so do that... 

then you're done (unless you need to transfer money that way).

if you want to log out you can do so and make sure you restart the viewer and change the destination to last known (or something like that) that way you'll always log back in to that terminal if you have to.
