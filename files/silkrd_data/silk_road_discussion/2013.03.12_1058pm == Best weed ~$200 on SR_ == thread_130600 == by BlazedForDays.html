Can report that I have tried product (grams or 1/8s) from the vendors who sell $200 ounces and was majorly (underline that please - majorly) disappointed with the quality.  Then again, I'm a chronic smoker w/ high tolerance.  If your tolerance is low, you might be happy with the buzz of $200 bud.

Have also noticed a decrease in price for primo nugs since the 1st of the year.  My favorite vendors were selling ounces for around $320 in December & January. Prices are now below $300 - at around $270-$280 for some of them now.

I would be more inclined to spend the $280 for an ounce of premium or to spend $150 +/- for a 1/2 ounce of primo.  I'm done sampling garbage weed to save a couple of bucks but weed is so personal - tastes vary so widely. Good luck with your search!
