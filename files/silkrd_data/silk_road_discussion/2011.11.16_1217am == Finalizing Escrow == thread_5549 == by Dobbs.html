Title: Re: Finalizing Escrow
Post by: phubaiblues on November 17, 2011, 11:52 am
Quote from: Pandoh on November 17, 2011, 02:43 am
Hey I have a question. How do you 'finalize early'? I see it everywhere but with no explanation. thanks

After something is shipped, you are supposed to finalize as soon as you get product.  'Finalizing early' means you do this before you actually get product, so vendor can recover his money.  It helps the vendor, but if you have problems, then you have no recourse, no appeal.
