Title: Re: can you change feedback?
Post by: Variety Jones on December 18, 2011, 02:11 am
Yes you can - go to your accounts page, clicky on the view feedback link, and you can see and edit all your feedback entries.

That said, it's waaaaaaaaaaay too soon to be doing that in the middle of the Christmas mail rush. It can take 10 days or more for a first class letter to travel 100 miles this time of year. Be a wee bit more patient. You'll look quite the fool if you fuck with his/her feedback and receive your mailing the next day.

As to not responding, if it's sent, all he can respond with "It's been sent!" A top vendor may get well over 100 PMs in a day, and between answering PMs that have legitimate inquiries and sending out orders, I'd not be surprised if they didn't answer ones that they really can't give you any more information than you already have.

I know it's annoying as shit waiting for an order to arrive, and I feel your pain, but honestly it's premature to take any action besides camping by your mail box and perhaps muttering some ancient mail-speeding enchantments under your breath. Lots of time later to shit all over the vendor if he really did fuck up. Give it a few more days.  ;)
