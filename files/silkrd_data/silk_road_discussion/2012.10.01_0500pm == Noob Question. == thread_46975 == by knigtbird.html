Title: Re: Noob Question.
Post by: Goldust on October 02, 2012, 07:45 am
Whatever you decide to do you need to buy the Bitcoins from Mt.Gox, Bitinstant or another exchange service. These then need to deposited into a wallet like blockchain.info. Remember to stay in your Tor browser. From there I would send them straight to SR. If you bought the coins on Mt.Gox I would strongly suggest sending them through a washer like bitcoinfog.com before sending them to SR. This hopefully means your transaction log will be untraceable.

The reason NOTspacecase recommended Bitinstant was because you can make it anonymous by making a MoneyGram transfer. Unless you use a washer then the Bitcoin chain can be tracked back to you if you were ever investigated. By using Bitinstant you eliminate that risk and get the Bitcoins into your account faster.

Goldust
