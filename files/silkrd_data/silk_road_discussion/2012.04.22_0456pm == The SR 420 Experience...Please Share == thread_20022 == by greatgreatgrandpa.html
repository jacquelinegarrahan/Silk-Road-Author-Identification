I think the sale brought in many new customers. It blew the doors off my little vendor business and I received more orders in two days than I had in the previous two weeks! I can't wait for the next sale!!


Some people are talking about how prices, even with the sale, still seemed high. Before I was a vendor I couldn't understand this either... it seemed that stuff should be downright inexpensive here, right?


There are a number of hidden costs that vendors have to pay that buyers might not notice... those envelopes and packaging the item comes in, the postage, the daily trips to the post office and making the rounds with local blue mailboxes, and lost mail that a good seller will just swallow and re-ship, and of course the SR commission (applicable on days other than 4/20!). Then there is a big challenge of getting Bitcoins converted back into currency to buy more inventory, that alone can eat up almost 10% when it's done very safety conscious. In all, it's expensive to be a seller!


But this is the cost of being anonymous and so much safer than buying or selling on the street somewhere!
