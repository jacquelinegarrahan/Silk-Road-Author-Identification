Title: Re: Looking to become a vendor
Post by: TheCostelloGroup on July 30, 2013, 11:22 pm
Do at least a months hard research before jumping in to anything. Better to be safe than sorry.


We shipped drugs before we came to vend here, so we knew about stealth and how to deliver anonymously but we had to learn how BTC worked and how to cash out and launder safely but we have a working system now. Took about 6 weeks to get down to a T but we are happy we could profit 30K-50K in the next year and make it look legit, while storing about 500 pound away per week in cash in case we ever need a quick get a way. (something I suggest all dealers do.)

You could have the best security in the world but one slip up is all it takes to get caught.


ALWAYS WEAR GLOVES WHEN PACKAGING. If you become a big time vendor and LE order from you and your prints are on file, do you have a reasonable explanation to why your prints are there? If not, then you're fucked.


Other than that, enjoy vending it's good fun, also try to be careful  :)

   Tip on wearing, you want a set of gloves which are soft and smooth as doctors glove but thicker, when your hands start to sweat you prints will show and probably leave them on the stamps, envelopes, everywhere basically, in terms of law fingerprints don't mean shit, they just help build a case, its like another piece to the puzzle. They can't put a time when you touched, or a place, or a reason. See my point? Anyway, still best don't leave them :)

   Someone in an earlier post probably the most important, you should not worry too much about what happens AFTER you get raided, cos more then likely, they will have a good reason to convict with what they already have, let alone find. You should concentrate your energy more on HOW NOT TO GET RAIDED. Then when you think you've covered that, do some more research, and when that is done, focus on fallback strategies.
