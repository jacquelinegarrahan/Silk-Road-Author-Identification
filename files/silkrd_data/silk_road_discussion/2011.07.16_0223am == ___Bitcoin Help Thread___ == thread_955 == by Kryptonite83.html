On Virwox you can trade Second Life dollars for bitcoin instantly. You can either send money into Virwox from a second life account or buy the second life dollars directly on the virwox site, then use the second life money to exchange for bitcoins.

They do have some nasty fees, not sure if they are higher than other exchangers though.

Post by: braves821 on July 18, 2011, 03:34 am
if you're using bitcoin exchange i would have them send to a permanent wallet  that you OWN (on your client on your computer that is encrypted) from there, you can do a transfer to mybitcoin.com if you want to add an extra transaction block (  mybitcoin.com's online wallet has been known to "magically" have coins emptied from wallets be warned) from mybitcoin.com you transfer to SR, if you use virWorx you can transfer straight in to silk road.  The extra adding of the address to mybitcoin.com is not really necessary in my opinion, but i would not try sending from any exchange directly into Silk Road because of possible delays.   Silk road has increased the limit from 6 to 12 hours which is good but i would not risk trying to dump coins from an exchange directly to my account.   

The fastest way i have found to obtain BTC's is to purchase moneypaks using cash and then going on bitcoin forums and selling them there or going on to the bitcoin IRC chat and finding some one to sell there.  There are online escrow systems you can use, and reputable buyers will have feedback they can provide you.  If they cant provide feedback i wouldnt go near them.
