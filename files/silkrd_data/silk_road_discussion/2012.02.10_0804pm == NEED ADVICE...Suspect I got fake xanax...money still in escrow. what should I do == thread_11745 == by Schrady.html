Here's what I would do: if they were sold as genuine Xanax and you've got generic, you've simply received the wrong product. I'd take it up with the seller and see what happened. Maybe he made a mistake (he's selling genuine and generic and mixed up your order), maybe he's willing to take some of the price or maybe you can get a full return. In the end the seller made the mistake and I think you should figure out with him how to fix it, before using the escrow-service or the rating system.

Now, I assume they're just generic. From my experience with prescription meds the generic is just as good as the real one. Xanax is just alprazolam + some binding agents to make it into a pill. However, if it is fake (not the real product) I'd immediately use the escrow system to get a return (I'm not sure if you can rate afterwards, but if you can, also leave a minimal rating). Sending fake pills is simply scamming.
