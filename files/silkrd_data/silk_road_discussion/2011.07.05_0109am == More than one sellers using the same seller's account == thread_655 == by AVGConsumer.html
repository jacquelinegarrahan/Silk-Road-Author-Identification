Title: More than one sellers using the same seller's account
Post by: AVGConsumer on July 05, 2011, 01:09 am
I was wondering about the pros and cons.

pros:
- wider product range ( A sells weed while B sells blotters)
- less initial cost (buying a sellers acc)
- more continuous supply - less "out of stock" (if A and B also has the same product )
- quicker rep buildup
- higher reputation - possibly more sells because of these
- shipping from different locations
- if cannabis supplies are low, growers can form a group, have a bigger stock while still remaining legit. (for ex: if growing 10 cannabis plants are allowed by law, 3 individuals can grow 30 plants thereby having 3 times more in stock and still remain legit by the law)

cons:
- if sellers have the same product, they have to :
        - share the profit.
        - keep track of sells on both sides, and have to decide who sends the package, this needs additional channels for coordination especially if more than 2 sellers involved.

Thoughts:
- more frequent cashout needed (less money kept on the shared account is the better if one decides to run)
- they don't need to know each other as far as I can tell.

Any thoughts?

GreenCo is a cooperative with several members. The continuous supply and the continuous demand that goes with it is our main reason for teaming up. The smaller quantities that are popular on Silk Road is not their main focus but I am trying to get other members more interested and grow that part. We have endured though marriages, divorcees, births, and deaths but partnering up, especially involving drugs and money is very hard on most people.
