Title: Re: New to SR - Made an order - Question
Post by: vanilla on October 27, 2011, 05:02 pm
No it will say processing until the seller ships it. Then you will see a finalize button. Don't finalize early unless the seller requests it. This is a bad habit to get into. If you finalize early, you have no escrow protection and could possibly be ripped off. Just be patient and it will work out.
