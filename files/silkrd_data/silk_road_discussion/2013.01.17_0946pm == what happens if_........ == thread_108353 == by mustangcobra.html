Title: Re: what happens if?........
Post by: BenJesuit on January 20, 2013, 01:18 am
And sometimes they ship before marking it shipped. That can happen with the busy vendors who ship things out then settle on later date to go through all the orders and mark them as in transit. When you're a big vendor, Tor's slowness can be a pain in the ass and slow productivity. So you pick a down time to do that kind of work.

The only thing that truly sucks is when vendors mark it shipped but haven't actually shipped yet(as in dropped it off into a box). But that mostly only occurs with the crappy vendors with low ratings. The good vendors, which most are, don't do that.

So hang in there.
