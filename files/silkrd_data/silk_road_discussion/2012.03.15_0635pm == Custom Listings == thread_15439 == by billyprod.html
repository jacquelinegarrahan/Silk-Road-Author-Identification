Title: Re: Custom Listings
Post by: Appa on March 15, 2012, 07:08 pm
You don't know they're at standard prices, since you don't know what comes in any given custom order.  It's only listed for everyone to see because there's not a private option.  Only the person who the order is for will be allowed by the vendor to buy it.
Example: silkroadvb5piz3r.onion/silkroad/item/0d084470e3
That's a custom listing for someone with the initials PB.  What is being sold?  We don't know.  Maybe it's an amount of product in increments 3Jane doesn't normally sell in.  Maybe it's a product she only told PB about and doesn't want everyone to know yet.  Maybe she and PB are friends, and the custom order is discounted.

Perhaps I'm misunderstanding your question?
