Title: What happens when SR steps in on a resolution?
Post by: morphinate on January 23, 2013, 11:19 pm
I ordered a product from overseas that hasn't arrived yet, but still could be on its way despite the fact that I've almost hit the auto-finalize mark. I didn't want to be forced to finalize early, so I went to the resolution center and requested an extension to ensure the product arrived.  Now the due date for the resolution is almost here and the vendor hasn't responded yet.

If it goes to resolution and I get the extension will I be able to request another resolution if it never arrives or can it only go the admins once?

I'd rather not request a refund now as it's still in a possible (albeit on the long side) time frame for the overseas order, but if I won't be allowed to take it to resolution a second time once the admins step in I will.
Title: Re: What happens when SR steps in on a resolution?
Post by: psychedelicmind on January 23, 2013, 11:23 pm
You can propose another time extension if you want and that will give the package a few extra days to arrive. You can keep proposing resolutions as far as I know. If the vendor isn't responding to any of them, you could ask for a 100% refund on the grounds that you made an effort to resolve the issue and the vendor ignored your requests.
