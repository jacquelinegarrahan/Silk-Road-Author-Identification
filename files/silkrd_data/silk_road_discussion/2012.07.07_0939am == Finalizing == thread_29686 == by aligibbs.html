Title: Re: Finalizing
Post by: HeyOItsMe2 on July 07, 2012, 09:55 am
This feature was implemented to prevent new vendors from having people finalize early. The only way around it is to wait til the vendor has more than 35 orders. Too many new vendor were popping up and asking their "customers" to Finalize early  and then running off with the money. I think it was a good idea, even if it has that protect people from themselves feel.
