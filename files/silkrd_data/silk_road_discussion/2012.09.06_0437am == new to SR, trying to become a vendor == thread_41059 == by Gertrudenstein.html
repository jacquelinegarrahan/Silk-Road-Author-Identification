I would recommend that you spend a few months as a buyer before attempting to vend.  This will give you time to see how stuff works around here: shipping techniques, proper etiquette, etc.  It's easy to end up getting yourself in over your head as many vendors have.  You may think you have it all figured out but the best way to learn is from the masters and the best way to do that is to spend some time as a buyer yourself.  You will realize what you want out of a vendor and can do your best to be that super professional guy.
