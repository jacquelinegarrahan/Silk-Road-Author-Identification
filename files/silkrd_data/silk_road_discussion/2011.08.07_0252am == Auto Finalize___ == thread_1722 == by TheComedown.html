Starting a resolution is not awful so if you get worried, than go ahead and do it.  You are best off communicating with the vendor. Give them 24 hours MINIMUM to respond, possibly more over the weekend. Most established vendors are much more worried about their reputation/rating than a single sale and because you can rate them at any time, this is in your favor.

One problem with resolution is that it involves a Silk Road Admin. They are great but it does mean they have less time to do other stuff (web site improvements). They offer only 2 options: deadline extension and refund %.
I recommend:
1:Try to contact vendor and work something out
2: Bring it to the forum (being very polite and discrete) and look for advice.
3: The Resolution Process (Bringing in a Silk Road admin to mediate of you)
