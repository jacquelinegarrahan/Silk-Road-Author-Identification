Title: Re: Mistake with Purchasing Stats?
Post by: MagicMan on February 12, 2013, 10:43 pm
The refund rate takes into account two main aspects. The first is how much was refunded, the second was how long ago was it refunded (more like how much money was spent since the refund). Your refund rate is really high right now because you were just refunded. Make a few more purchases, spend a few more hundred or thousand or tens of thousands more dollars and your rate will drop down to the low single digits eventually. Don't stress too much over it and just work on getting it down.
