Title: Re: bought something on SR, what now?
Post by: sun on October 05, 2011, 05:48 pm
Do not finalize the order before the package arrives to your door unless the seller has a very good reputation.  Finalizing will release your money out of limbo and into the sellers hands.  If you're confused as to what is going on then communicate with the seller to clear things up.  If the order has only displayed processing for a few hours then don't worry about it, the seller probably just hasn't logged on yet.  Just give it some time and if nothing happens then shoot the seller a message and ask whats going on.

Sun.
