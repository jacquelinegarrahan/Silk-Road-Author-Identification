It is so easy to lose respect and or appreciation for something when it is always within arms length like this is for us  so how about we all before we log on to this utopia each day or as we are logging on  lets take a second to say thanks for the silk road and those whop allow  it to be here for us and last but not least the good  venders who make so many of our lives happier each day by being here with the products we need/want.

Lets try and keep it all in retrospect. I have only been here a few weeks and already fear losing it  to some  evil Government so as is my usual practice I am staying humble and always thankful   for the things  and the people in my life that make it just a little bit more tolerable and happier. Thank you silk road and  THANK YOU to all you honest venders out there. You really do make a difference in many of our lives.  Many of us suffer depression or ADHD or ANXIETY or panic attacks and your products allow us to have a better life so again THANK YOU. This is one family member who will never take this place or you guys/gals for granted.

As far as the scams go. Escrow is there for a reason and no matter a venders rating Escrow is there for protection. Just cause a person has never wrecked a car would you let them take your new mustang out for a drive all weekend without  him being insured? Same concept for your orders with escrow.  No good vender will hold it against you for practicing a policy that siilk road  has in place for a reason. I hate to  see anyone get ripped off and lied to or taken advantage of but many of us at times bring it on ourselves cause we are too trusting in others. I have done that many times in my life too in the real world and it sucks we can't have absolute trust in someone  but the truth is  tat vender may not  be ripping you off. He may be in the hospital or locked up or heaven forbid died and you are out your money.

So lets just be thankful for what we have here and for each other and use some common sense when ordering.  Thanks for any and all who read through this garbled mess. I am DrDoom74 on the road by the way.  Take care everyone. Hope my package gets here this morning. ;o)
