Title: Re: Lots of vendors with no listing or in stealth mode
Post by: sdesu on June 22, 2012, 05:34 pm
They usually do this when they have a large amount of orders. By removing their listings, it allows them to catch up with current orders without simultaneously getting flooded with additional orders.

I've seen this a bit with cannabis vendors mostly, since a good majority of buyers are looking for cannabis compared to heroin.
