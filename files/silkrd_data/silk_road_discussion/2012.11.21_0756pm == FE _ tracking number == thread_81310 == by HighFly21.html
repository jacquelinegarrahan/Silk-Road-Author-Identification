Title: Re: FE / tracking number
Post by: Wazup7 on November 22, 2012, 01:25 am
Any vendor who asks you to FE, will not typically be the type to send you something before being paid, be it the actual product (which wouldn't be FE), or a tracking number.  If they do send you the tracking, it will probably be either minutes before the order goes to In Transit, or minutes after. 

The fact is--don't Finalize Early.  Money is escrow is money belonging to the vendor, as long as the vendor satisfies SR Support, if it comes to that.  Reputable vendors realize this, and build their business around utilizing escrow and being reliable.  If you are buying from a reputable vendor, then as soon as your funds are in escrow, they are no longer yours (with rare exceptions).  If you try to scam the vendor, the reputable vendor will probably still end up getting your funds.  Although reputable vendors tend to be more helpful to those rare problems.

Just don't FE.
