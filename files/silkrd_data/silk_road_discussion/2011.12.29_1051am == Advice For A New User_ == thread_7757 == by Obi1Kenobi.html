Title: Re: Advice For A New User?
Post by: spd on December 29, 2011, 01:26 pm
-The wiki has a lot of good info for people just starting out - http://dkn255hz262ypmii.onion/wiki/index.php/Main_Page

-Research who you're buying from - if you go to the "Rumor Mill" section of the forum - there's a bunch of stickies in that thread for most popular items here - that'll get you a list of sellers that have been vouched for over time.  Once you find something on the main site, look up the sellers name on the board here - you'll usually find some reviews of their service and products.  Also might find that they post in the forum - good way to see how they deal w/ customers.  If you don't see anything about the seller on the forum...i'd stay away, especially if you're brand new.

-Set yourself up a bitwallet - lot safer than transferring funds directly from a site like mtgox to silk road.

-Read up on PGP and get that set up - kind of a pain to learn, but there's a lot of threads here explaining it, and it's an extra layer of security.
