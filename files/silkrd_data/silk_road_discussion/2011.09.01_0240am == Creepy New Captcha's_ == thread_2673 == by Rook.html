Title: Re: Creepy New Captcha's?
Post by: Gall Anonim on September 02, 2011, 12:22 am
this is perfectly normal optimization being introduced here.
the purpose of the captcha is to make OCR (Optical Character Recognition) difficult for a machine, so that no mass spamming / account creation can take place. to a computer program doing OCR it is irrelevant if the characters have a meaning or not (though one could argue that brute force dictionary attack could be easier, but since those are random, then it's a moot point) BUT it makes it much easier for a person to get it right. I can't tell you how many times I fucked up the random ones. The new ones are much easier to punch in!

Good job devs!
