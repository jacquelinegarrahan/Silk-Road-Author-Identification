Title: Re: Have anybody here ever bought low online and resold for higher online also?
Post by: UltimateSolution on October 10, 2013, 10:08 am
Yes you can, but you probably won't make a lot of money unless you think it through.

Buying something online domestic and selling it domestic probably won't make a lot. Buying bulk from another country where its cheaper and selling it in smaller quantities domestic could make you quite a bit, but it does involve risk. Risk that the market you're operating on gets shutdown, risk that customs take your order and so on. You need to look at all the things you are able to buy cheaply then look at what there is a demand for where you are then go for the best options. Selling offline could be better depending on where you live and some other variables. This is really just common sense.

Stay Safe.
