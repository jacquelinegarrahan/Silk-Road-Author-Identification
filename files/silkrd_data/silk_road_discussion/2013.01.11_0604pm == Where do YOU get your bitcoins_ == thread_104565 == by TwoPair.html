Not trying to put BTC vendors out of business here, but this is what I do. 

BitInstant is damn fast.  It uses MoneyGram bill payments to process cash deposits from different locations--I use Walmarts because they don't ask for ID, so I can use a fake name, but it doesn't matter because I mix my coins anyway, so a real name wouldn't incriminate me.  The coins are sent to my wallet literally before the receipt even prints at Walmart.

Downsides are that they have a $500 a day limit (I think per person, or per destination, not sure on that detail), and the exchange rate is a bit higher than you might get if you went through the exchange yourself.
