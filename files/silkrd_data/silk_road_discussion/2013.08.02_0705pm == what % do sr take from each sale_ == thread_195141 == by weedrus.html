You... yeah.... well... Mmm....

See, it's like this. It's an algorithm. One that uses the price in BTC. Here goes...  6.7 x e^(-p/6) + 4  where -P = price in BTC.

But forget about all that. Set your account to pre-commission pricing. This way you don't have to factor in the SR Fee. It will be added on automatically to the listing. Not on your listing page but when you click a listing on your listing page. That is the price buyers will see. And doing this, you can figure out what the fee is. But it's not something you'll have to factor into your bottom line, per se.

Anyway, as a ballpark, the fee ranges from almost 10% for orders under $50 to just below 4% for orders over $1000+
