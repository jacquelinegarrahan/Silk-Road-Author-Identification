Title: Re: How many transactions do I have to do before the % shows up by my seller name?
Post by: BlarghRawr on November 25, 2012, 02:04 am
Items over $10 count, items under $10 do not count. (Always true)

And then there's also something about needing a minimum of 4 transactions. It seems a bit more finicky than that, in truth, but the price-limit is something that was enacted as a response to a large scam after one guy got a fuckton of feedback on a lottery all saying 5/5 and built up 300 transactions... then he switched over to moneypak-scamming and a lot of people got ripped off.
