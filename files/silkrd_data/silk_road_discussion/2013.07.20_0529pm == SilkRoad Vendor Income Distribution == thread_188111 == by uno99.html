SR Unofficial Census thread? :-)

Anyway, I'm interested in alot of the census figures for stuff on SR. Obviously there are things that can and can't be obtained without relying on estimation. But I wanted to see what your thoughts on some figures were.

There are alot of figures that I'd be interested in knowing, but I think the most interesting and fun out of the figures is the age old "So how much you think he's making // Celebrity Networth.com question"

SO that being said...How do you think income is distributed among vendors for these sales figures. We already have information on so and so vendor being in the top X% of Silk Road vendors, but how do you think that correlates with income?

**Note we are using BEFORE TAX INCOME not SALES** While I think both can be reasonable and fun to estimate, I picked it because ultimately its the more descriptive statistic (esp at the lower levels) and decided to stick with it...despite the SilkRoad top % Statistic being based on Sales and therefore correlating better to this as well as being easier to estimate**

$15,000-$25,000
$10,000-$15,000
Less than $10,000

ex. respond by matching the quartile to the percent of vendors you believe fit the criteria.
1%, 25%, 50%, etc. If you think only 0.01% (1/10000) vendors is taking home $1,000,000, then put 0.01% next to the $1,000,000+ option.

Curious to hear responses.
