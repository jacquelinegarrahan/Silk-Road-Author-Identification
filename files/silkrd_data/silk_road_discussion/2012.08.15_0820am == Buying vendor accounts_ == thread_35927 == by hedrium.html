Hi hedrium,

Buying a Vendor account outside of the proper channels is against Silk Road rules, and should the site Admin(s) find out that this has taken place, the account will be suspended. This rule is in place in order to protect the community from scammers who otherwise would be able to buy an account with excellent feedback, put a policy of early finalisation in place on their listings (which new vendors are prohibited from doing), and scam those willing to finalise because the account has excellent feedback. Buyers are recommended NEVER to finalise early as they immediately abandon the protection of the Silk Road escrow system, and thus Silk Road's resolution system should a problem arise.

Even if you did manage to buy an account, if it WERE allowed, you shouldn't; you have A LOT more reading to do before you even consider doing business here and risking future customer's safety and anonymity.
Did you come here from The Farmer's Market by any chance? Those guys sure did love their PayPal, and now they're the most successful providers of goodies on the deepweb! Oh.. wait.. no.. They got busted. Because they were using Western Union and PayPal. Silly fellows.
Bitcoin, and Bitcoin alone, is used here for a reason; if it were safe and anonymous to use PayPal, we likely would do so. It's not, so we don't.

- grahamgreene
