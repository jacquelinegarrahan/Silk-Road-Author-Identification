Title: Re: message to the sellers...are u suspicious of first time buyers
Post by: choquex on September 20, 2011, 12:41 pm
Honestly, I think you might be over-analyzing the situation a tad bit. I could, of course, be completely wrong about that. However, as a seller, I know that I'm not suspicious at all of first time buyers. I mean, I suppose I do feel just a tiny bit more at ease when somebody places an order with me who's had 30 successful transactions in the past than I do with somebody who's had 0 transactions... However, I'd honestly never even think of treating somebody differently just because they're a first time buyer. It's certainly no excuse for the seller in question to be failing to follow the standards for quality shipping that they chose to enforce upon themselves.

I'd recommend simply confronting the seller directly about everything you mentioned in your post... I feel as though it's the quickest, most direct, & most promising way to go about seeking a resolution to your aforementioned concerns that you feel is satisfactory. &, if a satisfactory resolution unfortunately never is reached, you should certainly keep in mind that this seller WILL have to face some consequences for their less-than-stellar behavior & flakey policies when it comes time for you to leave them feedback.
