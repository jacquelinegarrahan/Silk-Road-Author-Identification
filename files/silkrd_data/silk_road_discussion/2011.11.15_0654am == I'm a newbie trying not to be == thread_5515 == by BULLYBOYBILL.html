Welcome to the Road, BULLYBOYBILL

Be sure to read SR wiki. Volunteers are working hard to collect information to make life easier for those who are making first steps here.
http://dkn255hz262ypmii.onion/wiki/index.php/Main_Page

I am sure you'll find answers to most of the questions you might have there.

I can give you a few tips:
◄ Pick a vendor carefully. For my first purchases I choose vendors who are in Top20
◄ Choose vendors what are in the same continent/country. If you're from USA and you're ordering XTC from NL you are facing real risk of your order being intercepted by customs. You want your refund rate as low as possible.
◄ Try and build relationships with vendors. If you're buying from the same vendor all the time he will likely remember you.
◄ You might be asked to release funds early. That is one good reason to keep first few orders small. Read vendors profile and his policies concerning new buyers. Pick a vendors that is considered trustworthy by community.

Best of luck. Share your buying experiences here on forums and help others on picking a vendor:)

Bob.
