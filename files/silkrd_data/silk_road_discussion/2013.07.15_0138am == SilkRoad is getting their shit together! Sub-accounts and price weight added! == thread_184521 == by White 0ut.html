Title: SilkRoad is getting their shit together! Sub-accounts and price weight added!
Post by: White 0ut on July 15, 2013, 01:38 am
First lowest to highest & highest to lowest search options added, now this!

ON A ROLL DPR GO GO GO!!!!



This is an automated message to all sellers at Silk Road :

We've added a new feature for vendors that I hope will be useful for some of you. You now have the opion to create sub-accounts under your main vendor account that have limited access to your account functions. For those of you that employ others to assist you, I hope that you can reduce your risk and have better control over your operation by limiting some people's access. If you want a sub-account with full access, just set all premissions to full. Keep in mind that your PIN will still be needed for some functions.

In a few days, we will limit account logins to one at a time, so if there are multiple people working your vendor account at the same time, sub-accounts will be a requirement. A section has been added to the seller's guide on the wiki with all of the details.

http://dkn255hz262ypmii.onion/wiki/index.php?title=Seller's_Guide#Sub-accounts

We are also making an adjustment to the function that governs the price portion of the weight for reviews and sales when calculating vendor feedback and "best selling" items.

old function:
0.5 * log(0.07p + 1) + 0.001

new function
1 * log(0.01p + 1) + 0.001

where p is the price of the item in USD. The effect will be to give more weight to higher priced items, hopefully making it harder to manipulate a vendor's feedback score.

Best regards,
Silk Road Vendor Support
