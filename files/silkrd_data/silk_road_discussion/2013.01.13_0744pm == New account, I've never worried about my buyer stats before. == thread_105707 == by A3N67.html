bear in mind any vendor worth dealing with will check your stats in detail (i do), and i tend to consider a large amount of transactions of low value more suspicious than being a new buyer with no stats. its a technique used by scam buyers to bump stats, so obviously you want to avoid being painted with the same brush.

until you've built up your stats a little bit just stick to low stat friendly vendors and remember the golden rule, don't finalize early!
