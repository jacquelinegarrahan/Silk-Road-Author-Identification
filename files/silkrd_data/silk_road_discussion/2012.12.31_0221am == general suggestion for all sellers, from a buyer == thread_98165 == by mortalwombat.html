Title: general suggestion for all sellers, from a buyer
Post by: mortalwombat on December 31, 2012, 02:21 am
If you were to raise your prices slightly to include shipping in the cost of the item itself instead of having it be separate, I think more people would feel like they are getting a good deal. maybe this is like "psy ops" or something? I don't know. but many sellers do free shipping on here already. for example instead of an item priced at $150 plus $12 for shipping, a flat $165 with free shipping would actually seem like a better deal, even though the price is higher than before, so it would be a win-win for both seller & buyer. I think people love Silk Road because it feels like there's no middleman taking a cut; whether or not that's the case is beyond the scope of this topic. But, to blend the shipping cost into the item price would provide the illusion of having even fewer middlemen. Just my opinion. Thanks for what you do.
