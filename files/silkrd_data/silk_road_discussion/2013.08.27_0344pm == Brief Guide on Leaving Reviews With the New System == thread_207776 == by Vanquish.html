Title: Brief Guide on Leaving Reviews With the New System
Post by: Vanquish on August 27, 2013, 03:44 pm
1) Buyers finalize, leaving their rating for you.

2) Then in order to actually leave a review, head to your account settings.

3) Click on Recent Orders, and then the Post Review button.

4) From there you can leave your feedback and then you should be good to go.

**For the time being, neither the rating or review/feedback can be edited after clicking confirm.**

Hope this helps anyone that is confused or simply hasn't grasped the changes yet.
