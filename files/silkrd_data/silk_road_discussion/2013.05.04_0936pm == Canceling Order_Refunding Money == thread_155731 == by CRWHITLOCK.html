I had someone place an order right before the site went down and it is now showing as late. I contacted the buyer and explained the situation. I informed him I would cancel his order and refund his money for now and if he'd like to place a new order that would be fine. Title: Re: Canceling Order/Refunding Money
Post by: psychedelicmind on May 04, 2013, 09:44 pm
On the orders page, where you click 'Confirm' and 'Go' to mark an order sent, click where it says 'Confirm' and you will see it is actually a drop down menu. The other option is 'Cancel'. Just select 'Cancel' and hit 'go' and you're sorted.

Hope this helps :)

Title: Re: Canceling Order/Refunding Money
Post by: Shroomeister on May 05, 2013, 02:36 am
No; it doesnt.

It shows "late" (use to be 4 days and now DPR has moved it to 7 days)

7days from when a buyer places and order until a vendor "MUST" have it out the door. This is also the time frame in which the BUYER can not cancel the order.....


If you as a vendor have an order "late", but your buyer is cool and you mark it "shipped"...and SHIP IT...this DOES NOT DOES NOT DOES NOT count "against" you as a vendor in anyway.

Providing your customer still gives you 5/5 feedback.

The feedback score of 1/5 - 5/5 is the only thing that affects your vendor ranking.

Best of luck!
