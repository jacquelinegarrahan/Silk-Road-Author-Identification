Title: Re: Just a quick newbie question!
Post by: Autumn E on November 25, 2012, 11:46 pm
Yes, if you gave them the bitcoin address that is on your SR account it will be deposited directly into the SR account. But it will take about an hour or so as the bitcoins are being tumbled before being accepted. Also, if you want to be cautious it would be best to get a wallet or something. That way you can shuffle the bitcoins some more before actually sending it into the SR account.
