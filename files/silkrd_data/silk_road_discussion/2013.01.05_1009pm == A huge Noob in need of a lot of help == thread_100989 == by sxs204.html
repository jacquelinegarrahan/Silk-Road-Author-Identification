Post by: SNSD on January 06, 2013, 01:27 am
Beyond the guides linked:

Quote
How do I convert US currency into Bitcoins?
Not sure on US, but there should be plenty of info on guides

Quote
How do I set up a wallet?
Chose one of the various services available

Quote
How to transfer bitcoins to my silk road account?
From the wallet you can just transfer them out to the wallet address that is listed on your SR account page (this address will change every now and then so check before)

Quote
What is a PGP code? (This by far confuses me the most because I'm not sure how to encrypt stuff as I have never done it before)
Check out GPG4WIN, probably the simplest around to use

Quote
What is escrow?
Essentially a middle-man, you pay for a product and the money leaves your account, but is held by SR. Then once you receive the product you release the funds
