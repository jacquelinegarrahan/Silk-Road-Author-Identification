Wow.  Working with my vendors was a pleasure and a breeze!  The communication by ROOK was great and his patience is greatly appreciated. 420Med4U and TweetB were also in a race to my door, but ROOK's package arrived moments earlier.  All three vendors had very professional packaging, and odor free. Until you start to open the merchandise. The Blueberry Kush from 420Med4U was so pungent, I had to move it out of my library because it odorized the entire room. Funk Dankity!  The Blue Cheese from ROOK is wonderfully spongey and aromatic.  Fine weed to burn in a big greasy spleef. Nice daytime buzz!  The Purple Sour Diesel from TweetB was the deepest, most blue of the smokes.  The flavor was muskier and the stone was thumpier.  Definitely an evening smoke.  Nice and soft however aggressive it comes on to ya.  3 Amazing experiences to have all rolled into the same day!  Will rinse and repeat soon 8)
