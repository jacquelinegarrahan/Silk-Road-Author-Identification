*NEVER SIGN FOR ANYTHING*

Definitely use your real address.  The name kinda depends on your living situation...if you have an rental that still gets mail from old tenants you can probably use a fake name without problems. If your family has lived on the same farm for 30 years and never gets mail for other people it might be better to use your real name..

Not sure about German drug laws but 10 x pills isn't much in the grand scheme of things.  If for some reason you get caught, the police/prosecutor would have to be really bored to bring charges against you (although of course they can for even one pill). Buy from a reputable dealer with lots of good feedback about their product and shipping and there should be no reason for anyone to open your package.  Ive only bought weed from SR but I'm amazed at some of the concealment methods. Pills should be even easier.  Even got weed from SA through customs.

 SR automatically  deletes names and addresses as soon as the vendor marks tour item "in transit". Its against SR rules to save personal info, but you can never really be sure who you're dealing with on here (the beauty and the bitch of it)

Stay safe, use discretion, look up vendors in the forums to get feedback (don't do solely on their number as this can be manipulated) and you'll be fine.
