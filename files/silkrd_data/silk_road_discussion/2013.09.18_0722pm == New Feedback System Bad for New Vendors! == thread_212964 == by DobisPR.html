Title: New Feedback System Bad for New Vendors!
Post by: DobisPR on September 18, 2013, 07:22 pm
Hi Everyone,
I'm not here to bash the feedback overhaul. I think it is a major improvement from the last system. However, I think a few tweaks are necessary.

Firstly, having buyers have the "option" to leave written feedback, or give it later, is very misleading. As a new vendor myself I gave out 8 samples and only received 6 written reviews. I thought at first that the two people that didn't leave reviews did it intentionally, but then I realized on my buyer account that I had left a 5/5 feedback with no written review for an order I had placed about a month ago when the system was first overhauled! The only reason I realized this is because the vendor sent me a message asking me to place a review and I told him I already did! I had no idea there was a "Pending reviews" link on my account page.

I believe written reviews are very important, especially for new vendors. Even though the buyers leave a 5/5 rating, the written feedback shows how many people actually bought the product. The customer that was my first "actual" customer finalized and gave a 5/5 but left no feedback and I believe that has negatively impacted me as a new vendor.
