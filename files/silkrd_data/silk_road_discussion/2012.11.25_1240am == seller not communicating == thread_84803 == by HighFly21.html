Title: Re: seller not communicating
Post by: AAfive on November 25, 2012, 07:03 pm
Hey buddy,

     I hate to be the bearer of bad news, but if you FE'd (which you said you did), the SR team will not be able to do anything.  They specifically say in that post everyone can get to from their account page, that IF YOU FE THEY CANNOT DO ANYTHING TO HELP you. 

I know it sucks, and I am very sorry to hear you might have gotten scammed, but unless you completely trust the vendor, NEVER FE... EVER.

I personally refuse to buy anything that "requires" FE (I know some very legitimate vendors require FE sometimes, so this is just a personal rule I go by)  I know sometimes vendors are running short on cash on hand because of all of their money being tied up in escrow, but still you have to realize the nature of the business and the website.  The reason SR is so successful is because it offers you a safety net (escrow) to place a "buffer" between you and the vendor ( and also works as a safety net for the vendor, since he/she doesn't know you from adam)

I hope you get what you ordered, but if you don't... well it will just have to be taken as a fairly pricey lesson learner.
-AAfive

PS:  I have FE'd before, but I wasn't asked to, and it was a vendor I had done business with on numerous occasions.
