Post by: sdesu on June 25, 2012, 06:11 am
There are some users who will not deal with new people at all, just for their own safety. However, there are a few vendors that will either:
A) Sell to new users without any problem.
B) Sell to new users, but require them to FE (finalize early).

Finalizing early, depending on the reputation of the vendor is usually trouble-free. You're basically paying them for the item before you actually receive it. However, if your item is lost/seized/stolen, you're out of luck as far as refunds go. (Some vendors will work with you, but not many that I know of, at least.)

Some people will recommend buying little random items to build up a transaction history, but I never did this, and everyone sells to me without a problem.

I hope this helps,

sdesu
