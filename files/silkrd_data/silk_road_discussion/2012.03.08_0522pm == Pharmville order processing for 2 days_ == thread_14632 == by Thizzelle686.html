Post by: BenJesuit on March 08, 2012, 07:28 pm
Exactly. Both Tony76 and Pharmville get the lion share of orders on Silk Road. And they both always ship out with 24 to 48 hours. Rarely if ever make a mistake.

PMing on TOR is a slow process. Imagine answering hundreds of PM which because of Tor's low speed, takes 5+ minutes to respond to each?

Seeing processing is much better than having to finalize before a vendor will ship.

You order will likely say in transit on the day you receive your order. And your buyer stats will look good because you'll be finalizing upon receiving shipment.

If by Saturday your order doesn't arrive, title your PM, "Ordered Monday, It's Saturday and nothing arrived." This way, by title alone, they will see that it is a real issue and not just another impatient noob thinking this is Amazon dot com.
