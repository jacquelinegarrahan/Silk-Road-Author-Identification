Title: Re: I feel like splurging...
Post by: fullmetal on May 06, 2013, 12:53 am
Your list looks to be good, covering all of the fundamentals. I'd grab a gram or even more of Ketamine and share it with my buddies if I hadn't tried it before; 200 mg of K is just not going to work out for anyone (too small of a dose!) There's numerous vendors on here who are fairly priced at around 30-40 USD for a gram. Take the plunge and report back to us :)

MXE on the other hand is quite potent and even that small dose will suffice quite nicely to sample.

If you want something that has a fairly brief duration (3~ hrs) that is experientially somewhere in the gradient between mushrooms and DMT, try 4-AcO-DMT. I find a lot of people who respond really positively to this one.

As for 2c-e, it's definitely one of the most unique and uniquely weird substances I've ever done (if dosed high enough). 18 mg is good for one trip (are people really selling in such small increments?) but I'd go for at least 100mg and share the experience with friends.

Hope this has helped, good luck.

Edit: I wouldn't really recommend any of the DOx's, the trips ring hollow for me and are usually too long and arduous to be worth the time or money. Go for a few more tabs of LSD, you aren't missing anything.
