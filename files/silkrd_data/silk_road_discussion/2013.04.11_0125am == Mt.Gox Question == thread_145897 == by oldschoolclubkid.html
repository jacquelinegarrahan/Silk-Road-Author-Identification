Title: Re: Mt.Gox Question
Post by: trolley on April 11, 2013, 01:46 am
It's nothing to do with MtGox charging a fee the fee is for the miners on the bitcoin network that process the transactions, they can choose not to accept blocks to process that don't have fees.
https://en.bitcoin.it/wiki/Transaction_fees (clearnet)

Adding the voluntary fee means your transaction will likely be processed quicker then one that doesn't have one hence an hour processing time vs three days with no fee.
