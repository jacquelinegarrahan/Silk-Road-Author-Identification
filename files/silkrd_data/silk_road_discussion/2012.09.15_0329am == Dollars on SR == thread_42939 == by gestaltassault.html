Jalapeno?

Butterfly Labs Jalapeno... it's an ASIC card tuned specifically for bitcoin mining.

On my pool right now (bitclockers) the fastest miner hashes at about 40 GH/second - it'd probably cost you about $30K to put together a few rigs to hash that fast.

The Jalapeno plugs into a USB port, hashes at 7.5 GH/second and costs $159.00.  Their single SC hashes at 40 GH/second and costs $1299.00. 

At current prices, if you bought a single SC and ran it for about 2 weeks, you'd make your $1299.00 investment back.

Smart money is on once these trickle into the markets in October/November, bitcoin miners are going to be hashing blocks faster, increasing the amount of coins flooding into the market. That's why GestaltAssault says the value of BTC will drop.

Keep in mind this is temporary... at the end of the year, miners will only get 25 bitcoins for solving a block, unlike the 50 BTC they currently get.  So even though miners will be solving blocks faster, there will be less payoff from it and prices will res-tabilize.
