Quote from: flashlight5 on July 01, 2013, 07:44 pm
does any1 know, if and how one gets his bond back if doesn't want to be a vendor anymore

You can't.  That's the point.  You're making a commitment to be an asset to the community as well as not to scam.  The bond is the collateral for that.  You get it back once you've proved yourself.  Getting wet feet or getting caught in an untoward situation which requires you to terminate that contract is not the fault of the institution with which you've enrolled.

Sorry Bro.  Best advice I can give you is just sell what you have and meet the requirements to get the bond back.  Then you'll have profits, plus returned bond.  If at that point you wish to quit the community you can do so and even sell your vendor account if you wish.  I DO NOT CONDONE THE SALE OF VENDORS ACCOUNTS.  I am actually quite heartily against it, but you asked, I'm just giving information.
