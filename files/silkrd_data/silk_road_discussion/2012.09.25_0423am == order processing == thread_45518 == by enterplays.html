It just depends on when the vendor can get the product packaged and mailed to you.  Typically the vendor will mail the package and mark the order "in transit" next day or withing 48 hours.  If it takes longer than 48 hours I usually message the vendor and if I get no response after another 24 hoursI will  cancel the order (an option to do so will pop up on your order screen).  You could try messaging the vendor if it does not go into transit by tomorrow afternoon.
