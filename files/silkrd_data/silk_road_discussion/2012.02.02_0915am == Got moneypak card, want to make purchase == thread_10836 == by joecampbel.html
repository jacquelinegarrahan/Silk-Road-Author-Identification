Post by: shoosha2 on February 02, 2012, 07:36 pm
Get-Bitcoin.com, which by the way is a pretty great site with a variety of options to purchase BitCoins, claims that it will start accepting MoneyPaks in the future (January 2012, which is now of course since past), but currently do not. Very possible other sites do, although from what I understand the government is cracking down on these cards and making them harder to use.

My one piece of advice is do not use the card to buy something here. Some vendors will let you pay them by putting the proper amount on the card and then giving them the number. While this is theoretically a quick method it is easier to trace the cash, and you are also left with no protection. There is nothing to prevent the vendor from walking off with your cash, and no way to get a refund through SR. I have been ripped off myself from a vendor who at the time was very trusted, so you can't ever be sure. That being said, I don't think it's a common option any more, and certainly there are vendors who have used it honestly, but why take the risk? Escrow is your friend. :)

Any way, good luck with getting your BitCoins--it takes a while to figure out but gets easier and is definitely the best route to go.
