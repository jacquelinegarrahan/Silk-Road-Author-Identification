Title: Re: Refunds on custom issues
Post by: Variety Jones on January 11, 2012, 05:53 am
My policy is to replace seized orders, since 99% of my orders go through customs somewhere, and I need my clients to feel confident in ordering. I figure if I do my job properly, it shouldn't come up often enough to worry about.

My seizure policy:
Quote
Any orders stolen by customs will be replaced and re-shipped at absolutely no charge to you. It's not your fault if the thieving bastards intercepted your order, and I believe it is my responsibility to package your order stealthily enough to make it to your door. If they steal it again, I will replace it again, once again at absolutely no charge to you. I will not stop until you get your order. I may request that you use a different mailing address for replacement orders. I fucking hate those goddam customs wankers, and want you to be confident you will receive what you pay for.

Now, I've been doing this for years, and I'm pretty confident in my shipping methods, so replacing an occasional theft by customs is worth it for the goodwill factor.

Generally speaking, I think a 50/50 split - a half price replacement - would be a reasonable policy.

Whatever policy you do decide upon, you should make it clear in your profile.
