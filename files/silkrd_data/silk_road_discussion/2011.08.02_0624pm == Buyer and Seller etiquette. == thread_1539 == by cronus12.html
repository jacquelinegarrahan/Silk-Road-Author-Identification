Title: Buyer and Seller etiquette.
Post by: cronus12 on August 02, 2011, 06:24 pm
Here are a couple suggestions to buyers and sellers to keep the community a happy place:

Buyers:
-Once you receive a product, finalize as soon as possible.
-Provide feedback on all orders.
-If there is an issue, talk to the seller before leaving negative feedback
-Always use PGP for any communications.

Sellers:
-Only confirm orders that are actually shipped.
-If there is a delay, advise the buyer.
-When sending a quantity of item, ensure that you at least send the quantity that is ordered.  If it's 1g, make sure it's 1g and not 900mg.
-Respond as quickly as possible to a buyer questions.
-Always use PGP for a response, even if it's only a few words.
