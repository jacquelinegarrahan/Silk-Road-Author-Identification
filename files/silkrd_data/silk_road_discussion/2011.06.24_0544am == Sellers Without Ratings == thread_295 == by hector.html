Title: Re: Sellers Without Ratings
Post by: cronus12 on June 24, 2011, 03:25 pm
There is nothing wrong with new vendors and many may be great vendors in the end.  The one rule with a new vendor is to start small, order a sample or two of their various products and rate them based on your experience and quality of product.  Then slowly move up to larger quantities once you feel you have enough trust.

It's kind of a catch 22.  We are afraid to buy from new sellers, but if no one buys from them they will never get ratings and become established.  I'd suggest sending them a message and discussing your purchase as well as purchasing a small quantity to start.  Get a feel for the seller to determine if you are comfortable buying from them.  I've had positive experiences with established sellers as well as new sellers by simply getting a feel for them before purchasing.
