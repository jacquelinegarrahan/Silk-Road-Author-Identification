I'm sure there is a thread on this, and has been said many, many times. But let me clear up something again. PRIORITY MAIL (envelopes) does NOT GET THERE ANY FASTER THAN FIRST CLASS MAIL. Yes, large boxes that are shipped priority will possibly show up a day or two faster, but if you're talking envelopes or those smaller packges, they do NOT get there any faster. This is the big "scam" of the postal service, they get to charge people extra, without it really getting there any faster. Priority Mail arrives 2-7 days from Coast to Coast (anywhere in the US). This is also true of priority. Check the USPS website and learn more. You can also google people's experiences with what I've stated.
