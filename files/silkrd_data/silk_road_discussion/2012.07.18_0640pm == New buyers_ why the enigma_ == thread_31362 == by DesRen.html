Title: Re: New buyers: why the enigma?
Post by: 751a696c24d97009 on July 21, 2012, 03:59 am
Vendors have buyers who have been around longer than you and me, buyers with more money, buyers with excellent ordering history, buyers with established relationships, and buyers who are professional and respect the requests of vendors.

Yes, some vendors are stricter than others. But there are plenty who are willing to help out a first time buyer. I was interested in purchasing an eighth of cubies for my first buy; a small purchase of a product I enjoy to get myself started, but the vendor said I'd need at least five orders, no returns, and I'd have to FE. I looked around for about 30 seconds and found a domestic vendor with better prices, sent him a message asking if I'd be able to buy from him, and he said yes, no FE, no previous purchases necessary, no limited or required purchase amount, etc.

There are plenty of vendors on SR for most products, if you disagree with a certain vendor's requirements to make a purchase, keep looking. They are busy people who are risking their safety every day to provide us with products that normally are impossible to obtain, so I think them showing a little discretion and caution as to who they sell to is more than acceptable, if not expected.
