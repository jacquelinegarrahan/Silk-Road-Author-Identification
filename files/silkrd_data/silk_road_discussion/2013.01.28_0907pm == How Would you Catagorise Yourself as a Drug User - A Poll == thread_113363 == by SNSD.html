Title: How Would you Catagorise Yourself as a Drug User - A Poll
Post by: SNSD on January 28, 2013, 09:07 pm
Hello SR,

I thought it would be an interesting idea to see how we all rank ourselves as users and the results between us. I wanted to keep drug specifics out as this isn't about what you take - but how SR has impacted your use.

I know for me personally, I have been thinking about this quite a bit recently, and have found myself moving up a category or two since finding the road. Definitely broadened my availability which is something i have taken advantage of, experimenting with new drugs that were unavailable to me before. If the Silk Road had not existed, I probably would have tried these new things IF they became available - the main difference is the regular available supply, which in turn has moved me up a spot.

I realize the categories are a little vague, as say a 'Daily User' could be quite different from someone using weed to someone using a hard substance daily, so I tried to separate them a little while keeping it simple.

This is not a thread to brag about your personal use or disrespect others for how they use. Keep it clean and encourage discussion based on if and how your drug use has changed since finding the Silk Road.
