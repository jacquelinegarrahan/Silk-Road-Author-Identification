Post by: lelmeriodici on April 01, 2013, 04:45 am
I apologize, I'm not really interested in reading your thread but I love the sound of my own keys clicking away, so I'll type you up a little bit of info that's hopefully helpful. ;)

Recourse for orders that do not meet your expectations, in the order you should use them:

1.  Contact the vendor through SR and work something out.
2.  Click "Resolve." If you're reputation is great, you may want to consider letting an order go, however.
3.  Go to the forums and post about your experience in the rumor mill.

Throughout all of this it is important to be completely honest and take the high road.  If your story has the slightest hole you're fucked.  Plead you case and be patient. 

Option 3 is a last resort, vendors do not like being called scammers on the forums and usually take it seriously.  Do not post an angry rant, there is no use in being angry or making threats while on Tor, it just makes you look pathetic. :P

Good luck,
