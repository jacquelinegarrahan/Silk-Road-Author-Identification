Title: Re: Looking for people who tried white MDMA from more sources
Post by: SmCoop on December 12, 2012, 10:14 pm
There are a lot of opinions and preferences out there.

The color of the final product has virtually no effect on the quality of the MDMA in my experience.  The color is a result of how the product was synthesized (not a chemist).  My understanding is that the white is achieved if the cook does a wash on the crystals to remove some impurity.

Without calling out any vendors...both the best and worst MDMA I've purchased on SR have been white/clear.  The big disadvantage I see to getting the clear is that once it is crushed to powder form, it's probably the easiest to cut with just about anything.  The brown is a bit more of a challenge I would think (unless there's a goto I'm unaware of).

Bottom line:  Don't worry so much about color on SR.  Buy small amounts from different vendors and find out who your favorite is.  Everyone is different.  I've bought from at least a dozen vendors and not one of my friends agree on which batch they preferred.  Sometimes, the extra money is worth it and sometimes not.  ;)

Title: Re: Looking for people who tried white MDMA from more sources
Post by: mnak on December 13, 2012, 10:42 pm
In my opinion there is a lot of reasonable quality MDMA on SR.  The difference between vendor A and vendor B is usually going to be something like 70% MDMA HCl vs 80% MDMA HCl, so you'll obviously need a little bit more of the 70% one than your would need of the 80% one.

There are a lot of reports on SR about the subjective experiences of MDMA from Vendor A and Vendor B.  However, MDMA HCl is MDMA HCl.  Take the same MDMA from vendor A 5 different times and you're going to get 5 different experiences because your environment and existing mood and things like that play such a huge role in MDMA experiences.  To say that MDMA from one vendor is more happy feeling and MDMA from another vendor is more euphoric is bogus UNLESS the MDMA is cut with another active drug.  While cutting is very common on the streets and with ecstasy pills, MDMA on SR is generally not cut with other drugs.

When I came to SR I only wanted white or nearly white MDMA.  Since then I've gotten MDMA from rinran, F16, CloudSurfer and dutchaanbod.  The stuff from dutchaanbod was the least white but it was at least as good as the others in my subjective opinion.  The tan smell and color are indicative of safrole, a key ingredient in the typical synthesis of MDMA.  A lot of people in the street are buying stuff that is clear/white but it isn't MDMA at all: it's methylone.

All of this said, I don't want MDMA that is dark brown.  If it is that dark then the chemist should be ashamed of themselves for not giving it a decent wash.
