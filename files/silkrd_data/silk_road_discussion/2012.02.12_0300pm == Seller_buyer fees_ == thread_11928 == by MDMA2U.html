Title: Re: Seller/buyer fees?
Post by: bleep on February 13, 2012, 12:47 am
the 4% would come off of what you receive at the end of the transaction and i think no fee's are aplied to the amount you set for postage.
a seller can set it up so either SR fee is taken off of their price or so that it is applied to what the buyer pays.
You want $55 after commission does this include your postage cost?,so you would add 4% for escrow hedging and then set it so the commission is applied to the buyer and not you .
Because your item is listed for $55+4% then your item would fall under the 8.5% commission so your buyers would be paying  $55+4% + 8.5% + postage if you set it.
hope this helps
