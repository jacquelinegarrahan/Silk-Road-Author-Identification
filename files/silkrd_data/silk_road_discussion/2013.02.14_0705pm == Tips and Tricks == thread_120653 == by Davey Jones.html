The posts on mail security are right on.  Once you have the gear, then what?  I have some friends who live in an apartment building that has a basement.  They can use this basement for extra storage.  So, if they don't wanna keep everything in their place they put it down there.  All kinds of room.  Then, when they need something, they just go down and get exactly what they need.  So, unless they sell to a cop, they're good.  There's also a neat little device that manually seals metal cans.  You can put whatever you want in the can and seal it, label off a can from the store.  Its grocery delivery time.  Then, enough cans can make a case, a case can be put on a pallet of identical cans and specially marked, the pallet gets shrink wrapped and put on the back of a truck.  Off it goes, where it ends up only certain people know.  Make sure bills of lading are right.  Have fun
