Title: Re: question about tumblr vs bitfog
Post by: shadrock on November 28, 2012, 04:47 pm
I've used BitcoinFog and would recommend it to others. They were down for 48 hours or more at one point, and they aren't available every so often, but the site is usually working. That is the biggest thing to be aware of.

I have no experience with the tumbler.

Whether you need one of these services to get coins into Silk Road is up to you. If you bought the coins with cash and a fake name then you might not deem it necessary to use a service like this to get the coins into your SR account. If you got your coins on an exchange where your account is linked to your personal information then you should use a mixing service before moving coins into SR.

Every cash out service I know of (other than taking cash for coins in a private transaction of course) requires your real name. Taking coins from SR and getting funds into your bank account would be another instance where you should use a mixing service like the Fog.
