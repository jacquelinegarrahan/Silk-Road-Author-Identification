Title: Re: I need bitcoins via paypal
Post by: ignus on October 26, 2011, 02:15 am
Try virwox.com.  You can deposit with PayPal, buy Linden$ (SecondLife currency), then use the Linden$ to buy BTC.

There is a daily limit for PayPal transactions, $60/day initially then $85 a few days later.  It'll take you a few days to get 30BTC but it's the only 100% safe way I know of given your situation.

You _may_ have to download the SecondLife client, log in and find a VirWox station to verify your virwox account. Not sure if this is required to get BTC.

Also note that  you will lose a little bit of money at every step.  PayPal will charge you a fee and VirWox will charge you two: once when you go $->Linden$ and again when you go Linden$->BTC.

Hope that helps!
