Title: Re: What sort of fees do vendors face?
Post by: Pharmington Rex on June 19, 2013, 01:40 am
Quote from: MissNatural on June 18, 2013, 07:10 pm
So everything under $50 has a 10% tax, everything between 150-300 has a 6% tax, etc? Just like that?

Not exactly. It's cumulative.

SR fees:
10% of first $50 (max $5)

8.5% of $50 - 150 ($5 + max addl $8.5)

6% of $150 - 300 ($13.5 + max addl $9)

3% of $300 - 500 ($22.5 + max addl $6)

2% of $500 - 1000 ($28.5+ max addl $10)

1.5% for everything over $1000 ($38.5 + 1.5% amt >$1000)


Hedging is on top of all that since it is based on the order total. If your profit margin is wide enough, you needn't use hedging.
