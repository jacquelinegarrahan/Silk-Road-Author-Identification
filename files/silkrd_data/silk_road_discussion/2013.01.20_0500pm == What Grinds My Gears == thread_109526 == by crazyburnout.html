We ask people to not FE specifically for several reasons.
First, it looks like shit when people are trying to get an idea of a vendor and they check out the feedback & half the feedback says "FE for trusted vendor", you have to go through 5 more pages of feedback to get what a non FE vendor would give you,
Secondly,  even though customers think that their doing a favor by getting a vendor their money sooner & showing that they are giving them their trust, the real situation is, it makes it harder for a vendor to follow a transaction if there are complications when they FE, which is the second reason why we ask that no one FE's.
