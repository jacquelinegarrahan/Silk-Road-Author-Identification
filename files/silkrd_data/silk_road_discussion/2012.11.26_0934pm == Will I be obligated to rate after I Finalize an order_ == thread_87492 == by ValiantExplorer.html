If you wish to FE - and I strongly recommend you do not, but if the vendor has 35+ transactions and has been a member for more than 1 month then the choice is yours - but do not wish to leave feedback immediately simply click the 'Finalize' link, and when the next page (the feedback page) loads, just click the "Silk Road" home page button, or your Account button or similar.

You can then enter the feedback on that item and submit it at any time afterwards, and it won't show up on the vendor's page until you hit "submit".
