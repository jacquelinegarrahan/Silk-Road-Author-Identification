Title: How to link Vendor Account to Forums Tutorial
Post by: hmdd on December 20, 2012, 03:26 am
You must:

1) Be a vendor for more then a month
2) have your vendor profile linked in your forum signature.
3) have at least 55 (last time I checked) illicit transactions

.....that means E-books do not count.
Things that are not generally "illegal" do not count.
Mushroom Spores are another example of things that mostly do not count.


With those things in place...contact the forum admin to apply for "SR Vendor" status on the forum. This puts the SR Vendor  under your name here and also grants you access to the largely under-impressive "vendor forum" section of this forum.


I say largely under-impressive, because before I had access. I swore once I did it would be the key to all the mysteries of SR. Its not.

Generally speaking you will already have most things figured out before you are ever given access.


PS - Those items listed above are the things you MUST have before you would even be considered.
These items do NOT promise you access.
Access is left entirely up to the admins/mods, some people do get access and then get thrown out, based on acting a fool.

Hope that helps.

- Shroomeister
