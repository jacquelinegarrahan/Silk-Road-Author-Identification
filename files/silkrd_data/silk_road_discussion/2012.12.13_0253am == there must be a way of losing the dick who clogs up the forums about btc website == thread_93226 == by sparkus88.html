Post by: xx138xx on December 14, 2012, 12:25 am
I can think of a couple of ways to mitigate the spam problem. They're not 100% solutions since the spammer could attempt to adapt his methods (though I'm guessing the whole spam thing is a bot anyway). A regular expression could be added server side to automatically prevent any post with the site name from being accepted. You could schedule this to run via Cron so automatically clean up spam post meeting the pattern. Another method, depending on if the forums are on a server running mod_security, would be to add a mod_security rule to do the same thing. If the patern matches post doesn't get through. You could also try the hidden form field trick for forum registrations to try and trip the spammer up if it is a bot. Humans wouldn't see the hidden form field but bots process the entire thing and would stupidly try to fill the hidden field in. On a clearnet site you'd have more options, but these are the few I can think of off the top of my head that would help without completely damaging our privacy.
