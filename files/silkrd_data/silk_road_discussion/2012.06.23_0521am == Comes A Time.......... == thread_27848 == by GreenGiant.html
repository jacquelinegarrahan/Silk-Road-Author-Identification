Title: Comes A Time..........
Post by: GreenGiant on June 23, 2012, 05:21 am
I would like to start by thanking all my customers, it has been fantastic serving you all! I had been working online privately for around 3 years before Silk Road was created. The idea of working so openly worried me and the thought of dealing within escrow just seemed crazy, i signed up anyway thinking i would never use the account!

Needless to say after a couple of months of reading on the forums, browsing feedback and generally watching Silk Road evolve i ended up taking the plunge and Well here I am pretty much a year later. I have had a couple of lows but most of the journey on the Road has been great, I have been amazed at how well the system works and how positive the large majority of my transactions were.

Dread Pirate Roberts I commend you, what you have created it is not only great but it is special, It really has shown me that 99% of the people out there are good honest caring people who aren't just out to abuse the system. Buyers, Other Vendors, Silk Road Staff And Dread Pirate Roberts, I really do thank you all we have all played a part in something special and i hope it continues on until the world powers end the wasteful war on drugs.

I am sorry to announce this, but due to life commitments and personal needs as of the END OF JULY I am taking a break from vending and I will no longer be a permanent fixture on Silk Road. I may pop up randomly once or twice every couple of months with small quantities, if i have anything available, and if i did it would be very limited amounts.

I do know I will be back toward the end of the year, maybe the start of next year, so hopefully I'll pick up where i left off but better ;)

It really has been a pleasure serving you all,
If your reading this, take it easy and smoke a fat one!

GreenGiant
