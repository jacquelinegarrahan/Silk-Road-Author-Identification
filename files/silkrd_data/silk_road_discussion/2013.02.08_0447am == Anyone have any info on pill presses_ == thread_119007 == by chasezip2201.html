Title: Re: Anyone have any info on pill presses?
Post by: AussieMitch on February 15, 2013, 12:31 am
IMPORTANT: IF YOU ARE IN AUSTRALIA DO NOT ORDER THE PILL PRESSES OFF EBAY THAT SAY ARE SHIPPED FROM WITHIN AUSTRALIA.

Me and some of the boys I work with contacted 2 of the sellers listing these who confirmed the presses were already in Australia and would be shipped domestically (The smaller ones through Auspost and the larger ones through courier). An anonymous payment method was setup and 2 different presses from 2 different sellers were ordered to separate addresses controlled by us but not associated with our identity's in anyway.

No controlled delivery was attempted however both addresses were raided by the police about 2 weeks later (both raids a couple of days apart), and the occupants of the houses were questioned about purchasing pill presses. Needless to say neither press arrived.

Just a word of warning.
