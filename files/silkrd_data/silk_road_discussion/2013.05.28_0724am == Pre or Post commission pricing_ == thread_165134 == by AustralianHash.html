To the OP....I think you are looking into this way too much. I do not know what you vend...but I have found that pricing is about the 5th thing buyers care about in my year of vending here. Though every new vendor believes pricing is number 1 ...and they all come in with there prices a lot lower then the competitors as they believe that is how they will get business. It simply is not true. Though I know in these forums you will here about wanting lower prices...but that is to be expected as every buyer would want lower prices...but I can tell you that the fact is pricing is not a major concern. Being consistent, being reliable and shipping FAST/process even faster will get you all the business you could want.
