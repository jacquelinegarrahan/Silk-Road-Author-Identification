Title: Re: vendor messaging courtesy
Post by: ArmTrax on April 12, 2012, 10:45 pm
First, it is totally cool to message the guy and ask him whats up.

Second, it doesnt mean that you will not get your shipment, the fact that the listing is down is only indicates the vendor has no more of that product for sale, it does not mean that all of the product that sold is gone.

Third, two days is a little long, it could already be in the mail and he simply has not hit the button yet.

Bottom line, message the guy and see whats up.
