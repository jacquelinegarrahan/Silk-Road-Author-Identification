Title: Re: MDMA PURITY
Post by: cryLOTT on June 19, 2012, 01:54 am
I believe there is much misunderstanding surrounding the question of purity of MDMA, and other drugs.

The MDMA, or "molly", you buy is crystalline MDMA.HCL, which means it consists of molecules of MDMA (CO2C9NH2) that are weakly bonded with HCl molecules to form the hydrochloride salt (CO2C9NH3+:Cl-).

If you wanted "pure MDMA" you could have the "freebase" i.e. not bonded with anything to make a salt, but this is not preferred, as salts are far stabler than freebases.

Of course, the addition of the HCl makes each molecule heavier, and so you have to have proportionally more to have the same effect.

The "84% is pure" notion arises from the fact that in a MDMA:HCl molecule, 84% of the weight is the MDMA, and 16% is the HCl. This is a bandied around number because in a mass spectrometer, where at high energies all the weak bonds get broken, 84% of the mass flying through the machine is MDMA, hence the readout.

If you've genuinely got 84% MDMA:HCl, throw it away! 16% of other shit is too much! I've done some basic chemistry on the mandy I take, and it seems to be upwards of 98% pure MDMA.HCl.

tl;dr 84% of a pure MDMA.HCl sample is MDMA
