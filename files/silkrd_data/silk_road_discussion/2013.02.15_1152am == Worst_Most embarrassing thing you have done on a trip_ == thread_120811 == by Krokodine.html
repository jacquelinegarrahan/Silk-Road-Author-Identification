Title: Re: Worst/Most embarrassing thing you have done on a trip?
Post by: lamonteyoung on February 15, 2013, 12:27 pm
The joy of anonymity empowers me with the courage to say mine, so while the road is down at least there can be a chuckle at my expense, rather than indulging the varying levels of collusion, foreign attack and possible robbery associated with the maintenance...

It was a few years back when we had the joy that is legal mushrooms purchased from Camden high street, and was going to one of my friends mid afternoon to sample that pleasant taste, mmmmm, and have a little psychonautic adventure with a few pals, celebrating the flats recent acquisition and renovation, it was pristine as a home showroom.

So we proceeded to eat a comprehensive volume of Mexican mushrooms, and as the effects too hold of the others, it didn't seem to effect me?? at the time I thought it was because I was at a party the night before and participated in little light MDMA therapy, so I took more..

40 mins later, everyone else was wankered and I was just feeling a little stoned, so discouraged, i went to the bathroom to take a piss and as I shut the door, turned around and looked at the toilet, it was covered in 'shit...runny, gloopy sandy brown shit, and to make things worse the mushrooms seemed to be kicking in, as the 'Shit', was waving and slightly breathing as things do on psychedelics...

Now knowing my friend well, who was an absolute clean freak to an OCD level, I started to question the 'Shit being there', as I didn't notice anyone using the toilet since I had arrived, and he definitely wouldn't have left it there/freaked out, so I came to the conclusion that this was some pretty weird hallucination.....but it looked so real I was questioning it...I had an idea,  rugs always get me when I am tripping, so looked down at the rug under the sink, and it was crawling and changing colour...Yes...I am tripping!!

As I went to tell my friend about my funny experience, I turned around for one final look at this funny vision, and for some reason felt compelled to touch the moving faeces to make 100% sure.......

....and it was shit, smelly soft mushroom shit...someone had just shit all over the toilet, and now I had it on my hand..

...then I had to explain this to my friend, who still to this day thinks I shit all over his toilet.....
