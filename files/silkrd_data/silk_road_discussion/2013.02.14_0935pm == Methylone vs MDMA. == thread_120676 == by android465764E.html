Title: Re: Methylone vs MDMA.
Post by: zipp on February 15, 2013, 09:48 am
Methylone is very much underrated imo.  You have to dose higher than MDMA, yes, but the effects are quite similar.  It has a strong come up, lots of euphoria, lots of chatting, lots of hugging, closest substance to MDMA you will find.  There are rumors about Methylone being sold as MDMA, this is, however, extremely uncommon.  If someone wanted to rip people off, they would not put a substance in which is worth nearly as much as MDMA in value.  If you got sold a Methylone pill, it would not be easy to distinguish from E.  Are you sure you were sold Methylone?  Did you test it with a Marquis reagent?  One night I did Methylone with 20 other people, and we all had a fantastic time.  All of us loved it.  Quite a few people even said they preferred it to MDMA, because they said the euphoria was the same, but the crash was lighter.  About an equal number of people said they preferred MDMA, and the rest said they couldn't tell the difference.

I have done them both together before, and they mix quite well.  One of the best mixes is 2:1 Methylone to Mephedrone, but recently Meph has become overly popular and has been given a pretty bad rep.
