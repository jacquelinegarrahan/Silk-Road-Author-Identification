That's right. Once a vendor confirms the order, the address disappears

As to confirming the order ( marking it in transit ), it is up to the vendor to decide when to confirm the order. Personally I don't confirm the order until it has been mailed.

I don't know what country you are in, but you also have to realize that domestic shipping could still take a while. For example, in Canada or the USA, domestic shipping could still mean sending it 5000 miles depending on the vendor's and buyer's locations. I have had Expresspost shipments ( supposedly 2 day delivery ) take over a week to arrive.
