Post by: saintgabriels on December 29, 2012, 12:08 am
Thankfully you didn't FE so you have a few options. The resolve button won't be available for 15 (i think?) days after you place the order and it's been marked in transit. Both seller and buyer enter into resolution and explain their respective sides, come to a solution if anyone gets money back/paid, or a time extension to let the package get there.
If you ordered sunday, the vendor said it'd ship monday, and they didn't, I'd be rather upset. If they have not sent it, ask them to cancel so that you can order something that will get there by the time you need it. Hate it for that vendor to lose out on a sale, but if they can't ship when they say they will (provided there wasn't an emergency situation that came up-like a death or hospital visit), then you should order from someone who can ship on time.
What kind of product did you order? Are you willing to say from whom?

Don't finalize. Have the vendor cancel if it's not been shipped. If it has, wait for it, and if the resolve button comes up in the mean time, hit that shit and ask for a) a time extension, and/or b) a refund of sorts.
