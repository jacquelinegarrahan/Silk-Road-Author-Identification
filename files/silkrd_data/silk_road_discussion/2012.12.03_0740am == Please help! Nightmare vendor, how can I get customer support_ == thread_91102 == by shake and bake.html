For future reference, feedback is usually the last resort. Before you finalize, you have an option to "resolve". You make your case, vendor makes his, and SR steps in and makes a final decision. Great way to shine the light on a shady vendor's practices. Also, leaving a feedback before giving the vendor a chance to respond to your claims is bad form, vendors should be given the opportunity to make things right before being put on blast.

Because you finalized already and the vendor sounds like an ass who doesn't care about customer service, you may not have many options. Definitely spread the word about what shitty treatment you received on the forums. Start a feedback thread if he does not have one yet. Continue to express your lack of satisfaction to the vendor in calm terms, point out if they are not abiding by SR rules. Don't give ultimatums though, that comes off almost like blackmail.

So he is claiming the pills are USA made, but they are not any kind that come from US? Did he say what kind of pill you were getting when you ordered (were there pictures or descriptions?) If the vendor promised one thing, but sent another, you would have had a strong case in resolution. It's also something to mention on the forums.
