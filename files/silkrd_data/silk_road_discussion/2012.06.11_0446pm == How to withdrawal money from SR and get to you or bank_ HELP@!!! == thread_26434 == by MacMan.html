Title: Re: How to withdrawal money from SR and get to you or bank? HELP@!!!
Post by: captainjojo on June 11, 2012, 05:10 pm
You really should never be going from SR to MTGox or MTGox to SR.  You should always have an intermediary. 

I always transfer the BTC to a personal wallet, then to another wallet then to SR.  If I was going the other way I would transfer from SR to my own wallet first.  You might also want to transfer them in batches instead of all at once.  If you transfer a fixed amount to your own wallet, then transfer exactly the same amount to somewhere else it is I suppose feasible to track the same amounts using a block explorer (although I may be being a bit paranoid).

As far as getting them to your back account once they are in your own wallet you may have to go with a BTC trader if you do not wish to give your ID to MT Gox.  Most legitimate BTC buyers and sellers are asking for ID now, to conform to the banking acts in Canada and the US.
