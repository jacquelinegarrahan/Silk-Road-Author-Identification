This is untrue - you do upgrade a buyer account into a seller account. You can do this by clicking the 'become a seller' link in your Account control panel, and paying $150 in Bitcoin.
However it is highly recommended that you open a new account and purchase a vendor account from that as vendors that you have ordered from previously may still have your address (not all vendors erase information as they are supposed to), leaving you open to blackmail from that vendor and / or arrest if it transpires that the vendor your previously ordered from was a member of law enforcement.
