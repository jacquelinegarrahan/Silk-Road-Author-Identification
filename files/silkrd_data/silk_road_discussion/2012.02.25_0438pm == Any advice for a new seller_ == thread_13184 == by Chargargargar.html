Title: Re: Any advice for a new seller?
Post by: microRNA on February 25, 2012, 06:27 pm
If you can offer a product for cheaper, more power to you! You should do it to at least establish yourself with the community and hopefully the competition will drive the other prices down. Some categories prices just keep going up cause one person takes advantage of people and then everyone's prices are basically extortion just because they can be and will post it for as much as people will pay. I just refuse to buy from these vendors... so I am grateful to see you want to offer your product for cheaper and I completely support you.

Secondly, if you are within the US, use delivery confirmation on your packages. You can pick em up at the post office and attach extra postage to pay for em. These will prove that a package was delivered to the address, and prevent scammer buyers from saying that a parcel never arrived. There are many good threads about package profiling and the correct way to ship your product to ensure you do not attract unnecessary attention and it is important to adhere to these policies for the safety of your buyer and yourself.

I also support the sample idea, for the price it costs you... to reputable members, usually >100 posts or whatever the vendor decides. This should be done on the main site through escrow, and you would just request each person leaves a review for you in exchange for the discount.

If you pharm just happens to be oxycodone (or related opioid) I would be interested in trying a sample and leaving a review of your service most likely.

Feel free to ask any questions, just search first cause theyve probably been answered nearly a hundred times.
Good luck,
