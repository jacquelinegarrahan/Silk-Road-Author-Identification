Title: Re: IS this a legit SR practice....???
Post by: Veetano on August 17, 2012, 05:46 pm
I'm almost 90% sure that shipping fee is not excluded from the SR Commision rate

Vendors put all of the listing cost in the shipping costs for a few reasons:
Be able to have 1 particular listing, but multiple items listed under it.
This allows their products to be easier to browse
It prevents over spamming on SR. Instead of having 100 listings of the same item but different weights, or maybe different strains(weed)... they can have a single listing, where all their reviews will go under, with all their weights, etc etc.

In my opinion it's a very smart way of doing things. It prevents SR from being clustered and helps buyers have a better time finding items they want
