Title: Re: Advice for making The DMT Machine
Post by: eclipsin on December 15, 2012, 01:55 am
You need the Hennesey bottle.  The way the bottom is shaped makes it perfect.   Don't use a drill.  Just get an awl ( a screwdriver like thing but with a sharp point.  Just hold the bottle and the awl in the center of the bottom and tap it with a hammer.  You may want to get two bottles in case one breaks too much.  You can clean the hole up a bit after you make it with a file or something.

Don't forget to burn your steel wool with a torch to clean any coating off of it before you put it in the bottle.
