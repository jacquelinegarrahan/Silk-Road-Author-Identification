I got nervous when I made my first transaction, because I just typed my address, encrypted it, cut and pasted it.  It's more than just type it, check it, and submit, so I thought if I misspelled the original cleartext, I wouldn't know once it's encrypted.  anyway, I was careful regardless, and the order was a total success.

Since then, I've "polished" my address by making it seem more professional (or "less shady" if you will).  I put Dr. in front of my name ;-), and used a middle initial.  I also ran my address through the USPS validator thing.  it helps with routing by adding the 4-digit sub-zip-code, but it also "cleans" the other parts.  Like, apparantly "Circle" should be written as CIR, with no period, and a few other things.
