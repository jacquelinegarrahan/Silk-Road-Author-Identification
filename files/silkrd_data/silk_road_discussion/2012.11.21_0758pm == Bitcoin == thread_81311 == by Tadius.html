Yep, part of paying your dues on here is learning how to use bitcoins.  I'd just wade thru the forums a bit, you'll figure it out...it would take pages to post all the things we've been thru, and what we've learned, but basically, first decision is whether to go outside of SR, and use MtGox or one of the many outside vendors, or to use one of the inhouse vendors.
