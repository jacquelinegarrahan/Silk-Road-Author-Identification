Title: Re: What kind of buyer protection do you have?
Post by: thesearstower on November 04, 2012, 04:06 pm
If you do your research on the forums, you'll very quickly get an idea of who has quality product. By dealing with an established vendor, you can eliminate 90% of buyer headaches.

The resolution center (accessed by clicking 'resolve' next to your order) is your friend. If your order didn't show, wasn't as expected, etc, etc, the SR staff will work with you and the vendor to resolve the issue. It is generally a good idea to PM the vendor first, as many times things can be resolved just between the two of you. If you finalize early (FE--meaning you release your funds before you recieve your product), then there is nothing SR can do to help you. FE is the number one way that buyers get scammed, and by avoiding it, you'll lessen your risk of getting ripped off.

Lastly, when your order is a few days late, don't immediately come to the forums shouting 'WTF!! VENDOR XYZ IS FUCKING ASSHOLE SCAMMER STAY AWAY!!!!!!!!!!" Impatient newbies do this, and it is fucking lame. If you're having issues, calmly and respectfully send the Vendor a PM on SR and then take it to the resolution center if need be. If you're cool about it, I promise things will turn out better.

Lastly, remember that we're buying illegal drugs on the internet and through the mail. Things are going to go wrong. Also remember that this is probably the ONLY place on the entire planet where you can ask your drug dealer for a refund without being laughed out of the room.
