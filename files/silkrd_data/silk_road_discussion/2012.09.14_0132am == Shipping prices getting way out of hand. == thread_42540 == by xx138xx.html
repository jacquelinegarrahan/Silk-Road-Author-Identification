Title: Shipping prices getting way out of hand.
Post by: xx138xx on September 14, 2012, 01:32 am
Let me start off by stating I personally have to ship computer parts, electronics, and other legit goods multiple times a week. Sometimes first class, sometimes priority. Packaging material can add up price wise when I have to use peanuts, foam, bubble wrap, anti static bags, eggcrate cartons, and even vaccum sealed mylar for some delicate items. So I have a pretty clear idea how shipping and packaging costs go.

Recently I've seen a lot of feedback stating people are being charged for priority and receiving via first class. In addition, while browsing for items I wanted, I've put the breaks on several purchases after seeing what some of these people are trying to pull with their shipping charges. It's one thing to try and recoup some of your costs as a vendor for packaging and such, but baiting customers with low low prices then gouging them on shipping is just piss poor business. One vendor was even trying to charge $10 for shipping 5 god damn pills.

I can completely understand a vendor wanting to ship weed only via priority so the smell has less chance to permeate the packaging in transit, but forcing people to have to pay for priority on things that doesn't carry that same concern is kind of a dick move. First class and a dcn would suffice in those cases and not attract as much heat as someone showing up at the post office every day with nothing but priority mail. Legit looking fronts tend to have a variety of mail. Not just  steady stream of premium rate postage constantly.

So to sum up my rant, quit fronting about your shipping costs. Most of us with a brain can look up the cost of a priority flat rate box, figure in the cost of some packing stuffs, and tell when you're trying to get one over on us. Several vendors, mainly the pill guys, have lost a few hundred dollars in potential sales from just myself due to your weak ass game. By tacking on those ridiculous shipping charges you make it way more viable to source my shit locally just to not reward you for being trifling.

And no, I'm not saying everyone is doing it, I just chose not to call people out individually as all of you can browse through and see the ones I'm reffering to.

I really have not looked at others shipping costs but I do know about shipping on SR.

Mine is based on the cost to ship an item ($3) The cost of the box, tape, bubble wrap, etc ($2) so $5 to ship domestic for a single item.

However, SR has a little bug.. If I list shipping at $5 and the person puts in a quantity of 5 then it is still $5 to ship even though my $3 postage cost just jumped to $4.95

However, if the person shipping is using a flat rate box as many vendors do it is around $6 to ship before they include the cost of bags, vacuum sealing, tape, stealth packaging, and the time to drive it some place to drop it off. It is not like shipping computer parts, we can not leave it in our mail box or ask the postman "Hay, can you stop by I have several drug shipments I need you to pick up" Many of us drive upwards of 30 miles to drop packages off at different locations so as not to give away where we are.

So, depending on the pills and if they can be detected by sniffer dogs, I can see $10 in shipping. Is your safety and the safe delivery of your item not worth $10?
