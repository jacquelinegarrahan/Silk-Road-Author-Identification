Hi Mak54291,

It is not possible to buy Bitcoin via credit card due to the risk of chargeback - this is where you pay the seller, they receive the money and send the bitcoins, then you contact your bank to say there was an unauthorised transaction on your card; they take the money back from the seller, credit your account and the seller ends up with no money and no Bitcoin.

It is possible to buy Bitcoin using PayPal from some members on bitcoin-otc.com but you'll need to have a well established Web of Trust rating in order to use PayPal for any purchases there, again due to the risk of chargeback. I presume you don't have a WoT rating, therefore making this option impossible.

I would suggest that you search the forums for one of the many "How to get Bitcoin" threads - you're guaranteed to find a method which is available in your country.

VirWox lets you purchase Lindendollars for traditional currency using Paypal.  You can then trade your lindendollers, also on virwox, for BTC.  You cannot buy BTC directly with Paypal.  They also make you wait some number of days to withdraw the coins, to prevent chargebacks.

So you use Paypal to put dollars into your VirWox account.  Then trade the dollars for LindenDollars, then trade the L$ for BTC.  Then wait # days, then withdraw them.

I would not recommend buying coins using a credit card, but if you are careful to mix them well after withdrawing, and patient enough to wait, then it shouldn't be a problem.
