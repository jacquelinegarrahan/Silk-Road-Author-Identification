Not really.

As a new buyer attempting to purchase off established vendors; you're stats as a buyer do pretty much all the talking. I'm sure you know that sellers are given limited information about your transaction history here on SR to determine if they'd like to do business with you or not. They can see how many purchases you've made, the dollar amount you've spent, number of refunds you've received etc. Check your Purchasing Stats under the account tab; you want to keep these stats as PERFECT as possible. This means 0% refunds, 0% auto-finalize, and a high number of transactions. If you don't have many transactions, but have spent a few thousand dollars between those transactions, that can also help assure a seller you're legit.

That's the biggest part.  There's more to it than that though.


Don't give a vendor any reason to think you're sketchy. No unnecessary questions. Do not ask for details of any sort. Encrypt messages and sensitive info. Be patient and polite, but stick to the point and be as brief as possible with everything. Use your head here and act in a manner that keeps all involved parties anonymous and safe.
