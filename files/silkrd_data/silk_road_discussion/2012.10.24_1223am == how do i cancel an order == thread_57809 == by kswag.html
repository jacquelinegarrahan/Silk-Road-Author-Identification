Since the vendor stated that he has enough stock to cover any orders that have already been placed, you need to remain patient while he/she/they catch up.  I've had orders sit in processing for over 5 days before due to a huge backlog of orders vendors sometimes face.  If you do cancel your order, and the price is "hedged" you will receive 4% less btc back due to the fee that SR charges for this service.  This may hold you back from purchasing a similar product from another vendor.  In regards to your vendor not responding to your message(s), this is also most likely due to the fact that your vendor is backlogged and simply hasn't gotten around to replying to it yet.

If you view your sent message logs on SR, you can see if the recipient has viewed your message by it being in regular text rather than bold. 

Unfortunately, its a well known fact that if you conduct purchases here you have to be patient and just let your vendor work, as well as the postal system.  As a common courtesy here, it is frowned upon to ask questions about when they are going to do what, you'd be absolutely astounded at the volume of messages vendors receive on a daily basis and usually a good majority of them are questions that could be answered by simply reading the listing/vendor profile or viewing their thread. 

Good luck with your first purchase, and welcome to the revolution my friend  ;D
