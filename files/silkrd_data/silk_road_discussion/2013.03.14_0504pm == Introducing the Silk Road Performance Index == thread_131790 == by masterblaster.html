Title: Introducing the Silk Road Performance Index
Post by: masterblaster on March 14, 2013, 05:04 pm
This simple formula will give you a clear forecast of Silk Roads Performance on any given day:

(S * U) + (C - K)
____________
            U

 S = Does site load? (1 = yes/0 = no)
K = How long it takes in minutes to load
C = Comparative loading times of CP sites
U = Number of status reports from staff
