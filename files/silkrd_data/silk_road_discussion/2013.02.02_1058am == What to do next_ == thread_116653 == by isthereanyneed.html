Title: Re: What to do next?
Post by: inyala on February 02, 2013, 11:23 am
Hi isthereanyneed whatever you do don't let it autofinalise or that will affect your stats even more and you will have no chance of any refund. You need to take this to resolution - this way you can come to an agreement with the seller:

1. I suggest your first step be to extend the time for item to arrive e.g. by 5 days etc which is probably the best thing to do just now as items coming from overseas can take a while depending on where   
    it's come from and where it's going.
2. If item still doesn't arrive go for a refund or resend. Personally I prefer resend as getting a refund takes its toll on your stats :(  This will depend on the vendors terms and conditions - as you say he offers 50% which is not a total loss!

If the vendor you bought from has good feedback you probably don't have anything to worry about and your package should arrive. At least if you go to the resolution centre they will facilitate an agreement being reached by both of you and I'm sure the vendor will respond to that!
