Post by: The Laughing Man on December 22, 2012, 06:53 am
I had a breakthrough trip on salvia this past summer. Don't remember it that well, sort of like a half-remembered dream. I do remember being in a completely different reality. I tried as hard as I could to see real reality but it wasn't possible. My mouth kept slowly opening and something was trying to climb out. I kept closing it though so it couldn't get out. I wonder to this day if I was sitting there in my chair slowly opening and closing my mouth (I was alone so no idea). When I finally came back to reality I was very euphoric and happy. It was not a frightening or bad experience in any way (never had a bad salvia experience personally). I would say this was a level 5 trip on the scale.

I also had a salvia trip that wasn't quite breakthrough, this one was probably a level 4. With my eyes open and focusing my mind on reality I could live in reality but when I closed my eyes and let my mind wander I would be transported to an open deck on the back of a house an a tropical beach. Again euphoric and happy.

My other experiences with salvia I didn't experience hallucinations, just a strong high and the usual strange salvia body high (which has never bothered me).
