Also, make sure you get PGP set up and confirm it's working.  Go to the security sub-forum, read the stickied PGP thread.  There's also a thread for posting and testing PGP.  You can PM me to test it once you get it set up. 

Don't buy several oz's of anything for your first purchases. Vendors tend to be very cautious of first time buyers that want weight (with good reason.)  You're probably going to have to finalize early for your first purchase, especially if it's international.  Find a seller with good feedback and a thread on the forums, buy a small amount, and be patient. Get a couple of small successful transactions and move up from there.

SR is the greatest site I've found in all my years on the web, and it works well, but nobody dives right in their first couple of days.  Patience is key.

Post by: phubaiblues on December 03, 2011, 07:09 pm
First: As we tell everybody, you have to study up a lot more, on your own.  We can't take the time, and pages to pass on the knowledge, but your concerns are understandable, it's just the nature of SR, that we expect newbies to do exactly what we had to do, which is study study study. 

I'd start with the 'Security' forum.  Take a couple hours and go through that forum backwords, starting from the first posts in this forum.  It take s a couple hours, and you can breeze thru a lot of it, but it will really help you.

You wouldn't be here if you didn't understand TOR.  Many of us also use a live linux distro, for purchases, which, if you are interested, means reading up on TAILS or Liberte Linux, and see what we've had to say on that...

PGP, the cursed thing, takes study on your own.  You seem to have gotten where we all got to, which is to ask for a partner to send encrypted msgs to, and that is sort of a constant learning process, it took me a couple days of messing round with it to get comfortable.

I use pgp when it is provided by the vendor, don't worry about it when it isn't.  I use Tails or Liberte for purchases, plain old TOR on the forums.

BTC, read up, and you'll see that it is an ongoing dialog, that we don't all agree on.  There are some honest vendors on here, like SugarMama, but you don't have any protection if you buy from SilkRoaders.  But this is just a new, volatile currency, and you don't really have any protection no matter *where* you buy them, as mtgox itself has gone under temporarily.  I use get-gitcoin.com, and we've posted plenty on how to use them, and the website itself is explanatory.

On escrow: this is a huge and well fought area here.  YOu really are going to have to make your own decisions.  The site is based on escrow, but some vendors insist that you go outside of escrow, or 'finalize early' so they are protected, but then you have no protection at all.  I deal with vendors I trust, *or* make only small purchases from unkown vendors, if they have posted on here, and are just getting started.  But I"ve been ripped off--IP-- and I thought I knew better, so it can happen. 

Shit, I wrote a whole page, and didn't say much at all...yep, got to do what we all did: get some bitcoins, keep it small, pm your vendor, and don't trust *anyone* until your've read up on this forum...which is to your credit that you came here first!

Good luck.
