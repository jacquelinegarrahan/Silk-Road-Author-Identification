Title: Re: Is there a Nordic market on SR?
Post by: FlinnSteak on March 30, 2012, 09:24 am
Looking at vendor pages there are a lot of people who explicitly mention the Nordic countries, so you have to assume they get inquiries.

Personally, weed from a Nordic source would be a big win.  Germany/Spain are close, but not as close... Long mail times and smelly cannabis across borders seem to be a bad combination for personal security.

The big challenge there IMO, which another poster mentioned, would be price and profits on your side.  Even with the SR fees, shipping, etc you can find primo bud on SR shipped from the EU that's about half the price decently connected people are paying on the street here.  While I personally would pay a little premium for the short shipping distance some people are more price conscious, and you'd be competing with the other EU vendors.

There are a lot of Scandinavians on here, and not too many Nordic sellers, so there's opportunity.   Anecdotally I saw a Norwegian vendor pop up some a cannabis listing that was sold out in a few days...

I'll agree with the other posters: test listings and the product forum are probably a good place to start.
