I don' think it's too common that they forget to ship or mark in
transit. Vendors want their money so unless it was a plan to selectively scam it makes no sense. After four days the buyer can cancel too, so taking too long puts them at risk of scammers. If the buyer cancels and the item is shipped but not marked in transit theu could get their drugs and not have to pay. Vendors can't get the btc unless the item is in transit too.

If a vendor ships first, then marks in transit they may forget to change it. Otherwise look on the vendor' profile. Many will mention shipping delays or processing times. If you are concerned about a certain vendor and can't find anything on their profile send them a pm on   SR. The vendor is the only one who knows for sure.
