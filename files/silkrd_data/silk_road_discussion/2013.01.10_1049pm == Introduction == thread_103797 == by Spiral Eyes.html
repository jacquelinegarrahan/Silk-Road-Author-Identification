Post by: Batin on January 11, 2013, 09:04 pm
Yea, that first order is a leap of faith, always a little nerve wracking. I don't know what you have heard or what you plan to do, but even though it sounds risky your best bet is to ship to YOUR address with YOUR name, because if the name is fake it could attract suspicion, and if it is not being shipped to your address, well then you are just bringing more people or things into the mix that are unnecessary. Plus unless you have a friend to ship it to, whatever name you use on a package not being shipped to your own place of residence won't match to the occupants registered there. Remember, as long as you are not shipping to a PO box, you always have plausible deniability that you know what is in that box or that you ordered it or anything. In most cases the worst that will happen is your package will be seized, and a lot of vendors will work it out with you if that happens to get you some money back, or to reship your goods. Crazy cool, huh?

Just make sure you order from an established vendor with a lot of positive reviews. And you can vet your prospective vendor further by checking his reviews out on the forums. All the best vendors will usually have a review thread in the "Rumors" section of the forums, so search there. If you are smart and do your homework, your first order will be smooth and easy, and you will be able to enjoy your new freedom to get your hands on whatever it is that makes you happy. We are not all worthless drug addicts here, we are intelligent, hardworking people who are not satisfied with government approved alcohol as our only form of intoxication. Make sure to use responsibly!
