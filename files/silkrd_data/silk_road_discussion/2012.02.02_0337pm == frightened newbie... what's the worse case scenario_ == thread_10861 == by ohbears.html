Title: Re: frightened newbie... what's the worse case scenario?
Post by: dogeater on February 02, 2012, 04:31 pm
I'm pretty new here with 10 transactions but my paranoia has pretty much subsided. Some things you can do to minimize the risk:

Read the forums. There is a lot of useful information here regarding security.
Make sure to research your vendor on the forums before placing an order. Feedback will only tell you so much.
Don't finalize early for any reason, even from a trusted vendor. This is the only thing that protects a buyer from being scammed.
Ordering domestic means no customs risk and faster shipping times.
Don't use a fake name.
Always use GPG when sending your address.
Do not sign for any packages.
