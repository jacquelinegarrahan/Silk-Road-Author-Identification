Title: Waiting at the post office ...
Post by: a10101 on March 22, 2013, 03:41 pm
I'm waiting in line at the post office because I was supposed to receive a package yesterday, and it didn't come. It was guaranteed delivery by 3PM. The USPS website says that the package was undeliverable, but it listed the wrong zip. It looks like they're gonna try again, hopefully with the right zip this time. I called several times, but I can't get a clear story about where the package is, although they seem to know which PO it's supposed to be at. The local post office doesn't answer the phone, so I have to go in.

I figure there are 3 possibilities. 1. The vendor put the wrong info. I know my info was right. I have the same text file that I use for each order, so I can't have messed that up. 2. The PO screwed up and put it on the wrong truck. This seems the most likely. 3. It's a sting. They want me to come in and claim it and slap the cuffs on me.

If it's 3, I'm screwed. But they have my name and address, so how much worse can I make it by showing up? If the address is wrong, it will get returned to a bogus address, no doubt, and I can't have that happen. I have to go get the package. My mailman doesn't come until 4PM every day, so I can't wait until then to know. It would kill me to wait that long.

The people at the PO are really helpful. I give them the DCN and they send me to a manager. The manager tells me to wait for a few minutes and goes to the back. This is where I start to think about the possibilities. Did the vendor pack it up in a rush and leave some trace? Did the package tear and the drugs can be seen now? I look around for the cops, expecting them to come in at any moment to drag me away. Well, it's too late now. I just have to wait to see what happens. A full 5 minutes pass. A very long 5 minutes.

And then I see it. Thank god. It's a beautiful, fully sealed package. I know I'm in the clear now. I have to sign for it, but I've come this far and I'm not backing down now.

In the end, it's all good. Thank the SR gods.
