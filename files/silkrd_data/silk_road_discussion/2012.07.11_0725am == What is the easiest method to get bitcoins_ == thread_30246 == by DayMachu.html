                      BEST METHOD
1.Go over to bitinstant.com on the clearnet.
2.on the options select "deposit at major bank branch" and than it asks where to send the bitcoins and select "bitcoin address"
3. it will ask for the bitcoin address you want them sent to. i always just send them directly to my silk road address which can be seen when you click on the "account tab" on silk road. its the 32 digit number/letter combo. make sure you copy and paste it!!!
4. continue all of the steps as outlined on the website. it will make a deposit slip for you
5. print the deposit slip, or just open it from your email on your phone. and bring it to the bank, show it to the banker, make the deposit. your bitcoins will be sent directly to your silk road account

                    SHITTIER METHOD
1. go grab a "greendot moneypack card" at wallmart, fred meyer, wallgreens, etc.
2. put desired amount of money on this card at the register.
3. go to the "money" section on silkroad.
4. add to cart a listing that says something like "bitcoins for your moneypack"
5. put your moneypack number in the area where it says to put your address. and the seller will send them directly into your account
