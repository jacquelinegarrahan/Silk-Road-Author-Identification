Title: Who has such a friend?
Post by: joe on October 26, 2011, 04:55 pm
From the buyers guide:

  "Use a different, unrelated address than the one where your item will be kept such as a friends house"

I thought about this one, and here's the criteria such a friend would have to match:

 * Must be fully trustworthy, now and in the future
 * Must be clean and never take any drugs, let alone have any at his house
 * Must accept my drug habits
 * Must be OK with police searching his home
 * Must be resistant in case of questioning by authorities
 * Must be able to forgive me in case he gets into trouble

Now who the hell has such a friend, I mean, does such a person exist? It seems to me these properties are conflicting. Who is ok with his friends doing drugs, police searching his home and asking questions and at the same time, does no drugs himself?

I'd like to ask, are people really having the stuff delivered to a friend's house?

I'd be devastated to see a highly trusted friend get into trouble because of me, so I just have the stuff sent to my own address and live with the consequences should shit hit the fan.
