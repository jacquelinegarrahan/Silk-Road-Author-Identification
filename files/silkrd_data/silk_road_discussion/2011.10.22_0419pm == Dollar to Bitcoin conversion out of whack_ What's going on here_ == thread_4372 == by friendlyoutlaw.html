Title: Re: Dollar to Bitcoin conversion out of whack? What's going on here?
Post by: Dread Pirate Roberts on October 22, 2011, 09:50 pm
This is not a bug and it has always worked this way.  We use a 24 hour moving average of the exchange rate to adjust vendor's prices, not the up-to-the-minute exchange rate you see in the bottom left.  So, when the price moves quickly, there can be a discrepancy between these two rates.  That's the point though, we don't want sharp intraday fluctuations in the exchange rate to push the prices on the site all around.
