Title: Re: Just an idea ' Should we all stop telegraphing destination country'.
Post by: Galtourpa on May 05, 2013, 09:49 am
Not wanting to appear petty but 'Telegraphing' in this context is a word that means 'to unintentionally alert an opponent to one's situation ' being 'packages are getting through'. As opposed to 'feinting' which is' to disguise ones intentions'. It is commonly used in unarmed combat and other sports.

I also appreciate that people don't give specifics as I have read many many reviews but in the case of naming a country it then gives customs a heads up that packages are getting through and they need to pick up their game . If no countries where mentioned it would be giving nothing away. In regards to my country I would much rather keep them guessing  than confirming their suspicions. I know they have many ways to gather intelligence but every piece missing is a picture less clear.
