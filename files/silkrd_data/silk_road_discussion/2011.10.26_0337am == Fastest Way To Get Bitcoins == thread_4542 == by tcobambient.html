Yes: best way now, as others have stated, is with get-bitcoin.com  Go to their site, and click on the 'buy' button...you'll need an email addy and a 'name;... they will then email you the transaction# and account# for get-bitcoin.

Trot down to closest Wells Fargo branch with the amount of cash stipulated, fill out deposit slip with info they gave you, give to teller, get receipt.  No i.d. required.

Get home, take picture of receipt, and send email with this receipt pic to get-bitcoin. 

btc will be in your account within the hour.

btw, they didn't ask for pic, I just sent it, so there wouldn't be delays, and they seemed to like that...

Any problems, they do respond to email.
