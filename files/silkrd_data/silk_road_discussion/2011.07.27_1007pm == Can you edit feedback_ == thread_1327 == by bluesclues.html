Title: Re: Can you edit feedback?
Post by: CaptainSensible on July 28, 2011, 03:09 pm
Yes you can edit feedback!  Go to your Account link at the top right of the page, click on it.  Then you'll see View Feedback at the right of the page.  From there you can see the feedback you've given.  At the right of the page is an Edit option for each feedback you've given.  You can edit what you wrote as well as the score you gave the seller.
