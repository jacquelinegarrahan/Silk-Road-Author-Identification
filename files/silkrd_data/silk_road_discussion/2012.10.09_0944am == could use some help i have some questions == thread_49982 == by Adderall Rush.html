1) cash bank deposits through bitinstant is one way - no name or anything needed

2) recommended to use your real name or else the package may not be delivered
 - you should never sign if you werent expecting to have to. some orders, such as express from CA to US require signature and some will sign since they are expecting, others just wont order express, but tracking enables refunds in many situations
 - if you get a controlled deliver your entire household is fucked. they will search everything, everyone and all computers will be taken if they suspect any illegal electronic activity

3) FE = finalize early, this means releasing the funds from escrow before you receive the order. this is dangerous and not recommended. i wont support vendors who require FE although especially new buyers are sometimes asked to in order to protect the vendor from scams as well
