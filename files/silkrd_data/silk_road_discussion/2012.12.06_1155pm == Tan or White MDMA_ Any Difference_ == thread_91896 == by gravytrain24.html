Title: Re: Tan or White MDMA? Any Difference?
Post by: mnak on December 07, 2012, 02:27 am
All of the real MDMA you get is going to give you the same effects.  The difference is that one MDMA might be 70% and another might be 80%.  So you'll need a little more of the 70% one but it isn't like MDMA #1 causes a bodyload while MDMA #2 is more euphoric.

Pure MDMA hcl (when we say MDMA we're talking about MDMA hcl) is clear/white.  That means that tan MDMA isn't 100% sure.  But it might be 99.9% pure.  Or maybe it is 80% pure.  Just a little bit of an impurity can significantly change the color.

The tan color usually comes from the safrole.  You can smell it.  The safrole isn't going to give you any MDMA effects but it is an indication that you're getting real MDMA.  You can also smell the safrole.

So is white MDMA 100% pure?  Probably not.  There are a lot of impurities that are also white/clear.
