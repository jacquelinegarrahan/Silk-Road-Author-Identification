Title: Re: Setting up Bitcoin?
Post by: NevilleNobody on August 10, 2011, 07:38 am
Couple of ways to go about it. If you have and established online rep, such as high ebay fb, you may be able to get an account at Bitmarket. Some traders allow you to pay with paypal. Most others allow Moneybookers, which is similar and you can set up in a few mins. Bitmarket coins are always above market rate but when you need coins quick it's the go. Dwolla ain't gonna happen for someone in Oz, which is where you are as your previous posts suggest.
If in Oz, you can d/d at the NAB to MT Gox a/c and you will have the BTC in about 3-4 days. This works OK so long as you don't want to sell the BTC and take cash from Mt Gox. You are also exposed to BTC fluctuation should you do this.
Have a look at the bitcoin.org forums for some more info.
