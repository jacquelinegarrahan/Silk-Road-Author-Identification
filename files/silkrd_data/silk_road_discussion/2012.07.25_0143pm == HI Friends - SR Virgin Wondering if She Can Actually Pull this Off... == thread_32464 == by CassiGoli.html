Welcome to Silk Road CassiGoli.

It's always good to keep learning, but they're right, this is not quite as complicated as it may sound at first.

Here are a few simple steps when you're ready to get started:

1. Install Tor by going to https://www.torproject.org/  Just click "Download" and follow the instructions. (Oh, well, you've already done this because you're using the forum :)

2. Go to Silk Road http://silkroadvb5piz3r.onion/ and login. (Or create an account if you don't already have one.)
3. Click on "account" and select and copy the bitcoin address where it says "to deposit bitcoins, send them to this address." (Below you will buy and send bitcoins to this address in order to deposit them into your Silk Road account.)

4. Go to BitInstant.com, and follow these steps:
a. Under "Pay from" select your preferred location under "Cash Deposit."
b. Under "Pay to" select "Bitcoin address" and in the section below where it says "Bitcoin address" paste the bitcoin address from step 3 above.
c. Under "Amount to send (USD)" enter the amount of dollars you are going to deposit.
d. Under "Notification email" enter an anonymous email address, such as a tormail address. Don't use your regular email address. To create a tormail account, go here http://jhiwjjlqpyawmpjx.onion/signup.php
e. Click "Send funds" and follow the instructions.

5. Go to the location you selected from step 4a. and hand over the cash. (Fyi, no one knows that you are sending bitcoins to a Silk Road account, and this is not connected to your name or identity.)

6. Wait about an hour for the bitcoins to be credited to your Silk Road account.
7. Now you're ready to buy :)

Everyone, please feel free to make any additions or modifications to my tutorial above, or provide any additional details re buyer security.
