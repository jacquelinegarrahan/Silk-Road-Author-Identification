What's up with there being no forum moderators? What the fuck?

I made a thread in the Forum Requests with respect to the "Official BTC shop" threads: http://dkn255hz262ypmii.onion/index.php?topic=96763.0

I doubt anyone will give a shit.

DPR booted the last of the moderators about 3 weeks ago, without any explanation. I remember one of the mods (I'm not sure which one now) stated that they spent upwards of 10 hours a day on the site, deleting spam and looking after things. Guru described his abrupt dismissal as a slap in the face, and were I in their shoes, I'd probably take it the same way. Some of the mods put in very long hours here, and they were treated very shabbily.

In the thread titled "What happened to the Mods?"  there was considerable speculation as to what happened to the mods.  One of the Admins, Inigo, chastised those participating in that thread, stating that the Admins were "very busy" and didn't have the time to inform people as to what happened to the mods. They exhorted everyone to "be patient" -- doubtless in the fullness of time we would be informed as to what transpired.

I made the point that, there seemed to be no shortage of time for both DPR and the Admins to post in DPR's Book Club threads; indeed, there appeared to be endless time to debate the Libertarian equivalent of  the Medieval theological argument: "How many Angels can dance on the head of a pin?" as well as other minutiae of Libertarian thought.

It is now almost 3 full weeks since the last of the mods were booted, and we're still waiting for an explanation -- I suspect we'll all be dead of old age before we get one.

NC
