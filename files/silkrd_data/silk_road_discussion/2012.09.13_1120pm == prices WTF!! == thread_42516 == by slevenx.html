What I've found generally:

Prescription prices on SR are higher. This is because prescription drugs sold on the streets are generally coming from someone who has insurance and pays next to nothing, so any money is profit. This isn't the case for people making a business out of it.

Prices for illicit drugs are equal or cheaper here than on the street. If they're not cheaper, then the product is better.
