Hi kobebryant0824,

If all you're seeing when you try to view thevendor's main page is the message form then their vending privileges have been removed; this happens when a Silk Road administrator opens an investigation into the vendor due to reports of buyers being scammed.
When a vendor is in stealth mode and you have their URL, their page will come up with "This vendor is in stealth mode" written on it.

Unfortunately it looks like you've been scammed. I would suggest finding a different way to buy Bitcoin in the future, or else use a reputable Bitcoin seller on Silk Road such as SugarMama, RxKing, Chronic Crew, or Blue Sky Traders (as mentioned by CaptainMal).

- grahamgreene
