Quote from: mito on October 25, 2011, 10:52 pm
Good sellers interact a lot here in the forums, they're active.     They're very well known too.

Exactly.  Check here, and look for consistency.  Some sellers are good, some are sometimes good, and some are bad.  After a while you can tell, know who to trust, and when some buyer is just having a bad day, and blaming their sellers.  It happens, I've done it, but the other buyers will step in, and even out the scales. 

I would never go just on feedback I'd read on the main site.  Unfortunately, it's new people that usually get ripped, since they don't know to come here first, and find out how people feel about somebody.

The good vendors get a good rep and keep it...nobody can take it away from them, only they can.  Everybody has bad luck once in a while, shit doesn't arrive, some nutcase buyer will leave bad rep...but the overall picture is easy to discern.
