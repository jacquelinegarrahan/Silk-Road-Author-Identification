Title: Upgrading Tails and keeping Tor browser bookmarks and persistent files
Post by: NW Nugz on March 05, 2013, 05:35 am
I followed the Tails website instructions to destroy the data on a thumb drive to update from a new version dvd I had burnt. Then I searched harder and found you can do an upgrade that retains your Tor browser bookmarks and persistant files. When you boot up from the new disk there are some options to update in Applications>> System Tools>> Tails USB Installer.

I don't want to say exactly how to do it as I don't remember the details, but I did it both ways and the upgrade way seemed to work and retain my persistent stuff :-)   Just wanted to mention it as Tails website does not make this option obvious.
