Post by: ZenAndTheArt on June 17, 2012, 02:44 am
A couple of things may be happening,
[1] The vendor has removed that listing. For whatever reason.
[2] The vendor has gone into stealth mode, this is where only his fans will see his listings. Again there are several reason a vendor does this.
[3] The vendor has changed their shipping settings on this item, and no longer ships to your country. You can check this by changing you SETTINGS to location: Undeclared.
[4] The vendor hasn't logged in for over three days (I think it's three) and Silk Road automatically puts the vendor on 'holiday' and takes down their listings, until they log back in again.

I may have missed some, but these are the first to come to mind, there may be some more...
