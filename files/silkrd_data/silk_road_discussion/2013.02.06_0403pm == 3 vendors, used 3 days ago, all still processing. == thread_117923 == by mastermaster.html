I've dealt with vendors who don't have perfect feedback (90%+) and had great experiences with them, some vendors are large outfits and are dispatching 100's of transactions per week,  no matter how great their product is or how fast they ship, somebody may not be satisfied for whatever reason, or it could be a rival vendor trying to bring the vendor's feedback rating down (unfortunately this happens sometimes). Also, if I see a new vendor who's quickly amounted 'too many' 5/5 transactions quickly (unless they're for samples), I would be extremely cautious, it's very easy to fake feedback on a vendors page as there's no way of checking who's left the feedback (all it costs the faker to create their fake rep is SilkRoad's commission). I agree with you too, the vendors who update their vendors page regularly take their business seriously and want your customer satisfaction.
