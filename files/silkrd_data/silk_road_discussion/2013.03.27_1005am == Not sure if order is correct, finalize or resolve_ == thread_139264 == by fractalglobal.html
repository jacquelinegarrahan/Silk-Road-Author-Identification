Hi, I'm not sure if I can help too much but I can tell you from my point of view as a stoner/grower is acceptable.

Firstly if you want a big bud, it has a big stem in the middle, end of conversation. Now I tend to take a very close angled cut to minimise the amount of stem but keep nice big buds together, I do my best to have no visible stem, if it isn't surrounded by bud it shouldn't be there.

That said, you should still really finalize, just reflect what you felt in the feedback, if there was stem sticking out  you could point out that it was not particularly well manicured but personally I don't feel it is a reason for a majour complaint unless more than 25% of the weight was stem.

Stems are much lighter than buds provided they are properly dried, most of the wieght of bud(30%) is the resin, dry plant matter is very light so it probably didn't affect your weight too much.
