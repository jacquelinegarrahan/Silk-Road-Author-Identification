Title: Re: How to look at a sellers feedback. pls help.
Post by: patswin on March 05, 2013, 01:28 am
His positive rate is good and he has a decent number of transactions. I usually page back trying to find the ding on the stats and see what the deal was. I also try to watch out for irrelevant stat padding by things like lottos or unrelated cheap items. If they have lots of good feedback on the same type or exact product you're buying you should be good to go.

FE is another whole matter. I'm not a fan but won't start a rant here. Vendors are generally protected from bad buyers by escrow system as long as they can show they shipped and you got by using delivery confirmation. It's a trend now though especially when they are selling to buyers without much history. I generally won't FE until I've known a vendor and long time and even then only rarely. Still, if you want the product and he's the best deal or only vendor or best vendor you make the call. In general if the market forces worked to not patronize vendors who required FE then it would go away (did I say I wasn't going to rant...?)

Good Luck - A vendor with those numbers looks legit so you'll most likely be just fine.
