Title: Re: SR Market acct\ount question
Post by: sl0wturtle on July 21, 2013, 04:25 pm
If the listing is hedged, it will show you that you have USD in escrow. If it is not hedged, there will be only bitcoin in the escrow wallet.

Hedging pretty much converts the bitcoin to USD, so that it is not affected by the price of bitcoin while it's in escrow.
