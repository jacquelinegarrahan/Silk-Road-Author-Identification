Title: Re: Packages arriving before 'finalize' is a buyer option
Post by: Wadozo on August 23, 2012, 09:10 am
As lefthandspinner has said, new vendors need to successfully complete 35 transactions before they can ask a buyer to FE. This is the reason the finalize option is "greyed out" until they hit 35 transactions. After 3 days, a buyer is then able to finalize the order for the vendor who has less than 35 transactions. This helps stop a vendor who may set up shop with the idea of asking buyers to FE and then not fulfilling their end of the deal by not posting  the goods. :)
