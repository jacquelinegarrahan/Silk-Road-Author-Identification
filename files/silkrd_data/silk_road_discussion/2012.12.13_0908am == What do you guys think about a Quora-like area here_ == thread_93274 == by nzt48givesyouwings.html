Title: What do you guys think about a Quora-like area here?
Post by: nzt48givesyouwings on December 13, 2012, 09:08 am
If you don't already know, Quora = the awesome answers site with knowledge on basically everything. The thought is it would be a great way to keep track of all the info that's here getting buried

All content relevant to certain subjects (the best explanations, howto's, what to do, not to do, drug use practices, etc) could be aggregated in one place, voted up or down accordingly to make sure everyone can get the information in the most effective way possible. You could even follow topics. The easier access to knowledge would probably help drive the interaction of the community, and the awareness of products/best practices would drive sales for the vendors too. Win-win. The best use would be for things with static knowledge and not as a replacement for discussions/the forum at all but in addition to them.

Would you use it?
