So you have bitcoins in your Mt.Gox account? It is generally a good idea to transfer those coins to your Bitcoin Wallet (bitcoin.org) and/or eWallet instead of directly transferring it to SR (don't want to leave any traces). Only thing you need in order to initiate the transfer is the unique Bitcoin address for your wallet(s).

You can get an eWallet @ instawallet.org and that will create a unique link which you will need to bookmark in order to access it for future purposes.

Then you transfer the coins from your wallet to SR, using your SR Wallet Bitcoin address. Hope that helps.
