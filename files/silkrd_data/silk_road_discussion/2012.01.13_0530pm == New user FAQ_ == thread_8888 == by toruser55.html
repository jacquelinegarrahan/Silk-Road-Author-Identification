Quote
- What security precautions should be taken for shipping? (Is it ok to use my real name/address?).
Yes. What you should do is as soon as you get it, write "return to sender" across it and not open it that day. That way if police are waiting, it looks less suspicious. Also, NEVER sign for anything. Basically you use your real name because it looks less suspicious going to your house, and they have no proof you ordered it. Anybody could ship you illegal stuff without your consent.
