Hi shoehorn,

You can message SR Support from your SR account with the value, date and approximate time of the transaction and they should be able to track the info down for you. However, this would be a very, very low priority for them, and there's a possibility that you will get no help at all. This isn't Amazon or eBay, and the admins have a lot more important things to attend to. The fact that we have any type of customer service, for the kind of business this is, is absolutely amazing.
It is your own responsibility to keep secure records of your purchases.

One way that I use to keep track of packages that get automatically deleted from my account history after 10 (+/-) days is to mail the transaction details (order number etc.) to myself. It will then stay there for a month before being automatically deleted, or deleted by myself when the package arrives and I'm satisfied with the contents.
