Title: Re: (New to this) PO Box?
Post by: kitkat82 on September 08, 2012, 10:55 pm
No reason to worry IMO about having it sent to your home vs a PO Box.  If you are getting busted you are getting busted.  The PO Box is actually more suspicious to LE than just sending it to your home.  They have your address and know who you are with the PO Box, so it is really a false feeling of security.

Now if you are afraid to have it sent to your home because you are in High School or college and live at home with mom and dad then I would say you should probably refrain from buying drugs on SR altogether.
