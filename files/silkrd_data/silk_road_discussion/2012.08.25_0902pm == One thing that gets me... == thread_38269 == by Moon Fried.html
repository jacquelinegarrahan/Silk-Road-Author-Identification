Title: One thing that gets me...
Post by: Moon Fried on August 25, 2012, 09:02 pm
When people leave reviews and can't stop themselves from saying things like "buy with 100% confidence" or "the best/most trustworthy vendor on SR". Especially when it's orders of a small amount. It can be misleading at times. A vendor could just be fulfilling small orders, waiting to scam and act like it was not their fault on a bulk order.

Hate to sound this paranoid, but it's just not a good idea to advertise that a vendor is the greatest just because you got what you ordered. Don't want newbs to trust the vendor from the feedback alone and go ahead and FE and never get a reply or product in the mail.
