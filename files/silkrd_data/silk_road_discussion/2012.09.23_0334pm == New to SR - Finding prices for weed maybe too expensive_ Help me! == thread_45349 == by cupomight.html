Post by: MistressPink on September 23, 2012, 06:21 pm
This is an online market place not the street.

Street dealers don't have to deal with currency fluctuations, sales commissions, postage & packaging, seized shipments, complicated and expensive money laundering setups, but vendors here do. Street dealers get paid immediately, Vendors do not. That is a massively important point.

There are many reasons why people buy from this website. The beauty of a free market is that you're not obligated to buy anything. If you can get a better price elsewhere and are comfortable buying your goods that way this website has little in the way to offer you. For others less fortunate it is an incredibly valuable market place.

I think prices here are very cheap considering the amount of work involved in Vending.
