Title: Re: Establishing credibility, how?
Post by: BenJesuit on December 06, 2012, 12:15 am
Sending out samples is a sure way to start off.

But here's the bets way to do it:

Start a stealth link with the sample priced a minimum of .01BTC or whatever price you want. You can ship free or charge for shipping.
You limit the quantity to the number of samples you wish to send out. .01BTC is best of course but many are willing to pay shipping. Your call.

Then you start a thread in the product offer section titled appropriately asking who would like to sample.
Pick established posters as their opinion carries a little more clout than a noob and they won't appear as shills.

By making the stealth link for samples, when the recipient receives the sample , they will finalize and give feedback. When 5 of them do that, provided that they each give you 5/5, you'll have a 100% rating plus a review of your product on the forum.

Post by: mnak on December 07, 2012, 02:35 am
If you aren't in a hurry, then just offer a quality product at a good price and eventually a few people will buy and provide feedback which will help convince others to buy.

I do recommend only shipping things tracked or with delivery confirmation.  As a new vendor you aren't allowed to require people to FE until you hit something like 30 transactions.  Without proof of delivery it is the word of others vs your word.

If you're unable to ship with proof of delivery then absolutely don't sell to anyone that isn't established with good stats.
