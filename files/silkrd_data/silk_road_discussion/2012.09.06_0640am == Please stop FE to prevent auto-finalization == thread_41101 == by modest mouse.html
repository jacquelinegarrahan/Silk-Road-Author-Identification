Title: Please stop FE to prevent auto-finalization
Post by: modest mouse on September 06, 2012, 06:40 am
i see a large number of people leaving in their feedback that they finalized early because auto-finalization was about to occur.

just so everyone is aware, you should not generally finalize if your order has not arrived. you are paying for the service of escrow and should take full advantage of the protection.

just click "resolve" and you can then set how many day extra you want to wait, still in escrow... believe it or not.  not an automatic refund or anything like that, always more waiting

no reason to risk losing your money, if my package hasnt arrived im not gonna finalize... and neither should you. starve the scammers with smarts
be safe - love you all
