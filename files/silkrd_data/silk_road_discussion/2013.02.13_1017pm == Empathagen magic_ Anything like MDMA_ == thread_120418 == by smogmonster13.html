Well,
on both MDMA and LSD you can feel very euphoric.
But MDMA is in "in your body" experience, while LSD is an "in your mind experience" (and sometimes even out of your body).
LSD is also a stimulant and can keep you awake for some time (which is nice to enjoy the feeling), but MDMA can put you asleep quite quickly if you take it at a moment when you're tired.

I've had bad experiences on both products also ("bad trips") but when that happens on LSD you learn a lot from it, when it happens on MDMA you just have a chitty time.

MDMA is probably "easier" to use than LSD (set and settings are not an issue when taking MDMA, at least not in my opinion - while with LSD set and setting and guide make the whole difference).

You can feel "mushy" or "fluffy" on both products, but with LSD that feeling will be more intense.

Open those "doors of perception", that's something that can change your life (tmo for a better) and that will not happen on MDMA...
but, to be honest, both are lots of fun if you use them wisely!  ;D
