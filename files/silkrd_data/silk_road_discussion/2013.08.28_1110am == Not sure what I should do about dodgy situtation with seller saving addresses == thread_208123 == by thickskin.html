According to the SR vending rules, vendors are not allowed to save addresses. Tracking numbers is what needs to be saved. Then if a buyer extorts you, you make them verify their zip, locate the tracking # associated with the zip and presto. Even then, you cannot tell what the address of the destination is as long as you didn't save the address originally. If a vendor is known to save addresses they get banned. So, if you are certain that is the case, report the vendor that you are suspecting.
