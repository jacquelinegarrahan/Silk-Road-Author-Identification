Here's my review of the img hosting hidden services you linked:

IMGuru - http://p7d2k2xiioailnuu.onion/ - Says it works with jpeg and gif, I tried to upload my jpg anyways, didn't work, so I went to another site.

http://wuakd3inzzy5iz4w.onion/ - all it has is an FTP-looking site with a ./ and it just says Index Of.  No real image hosting that I could find.

Hidden Image Site - http://wyxwerboi3awzy23.onion/ - uses 4chans layout, I'm not totally familiar with 4chans layout, on 4chan posts timeout.  decided to look at others, and found qicpic.

QicPic - xqz3u5drneuzhaeo.onion/users/qicpic/ - ended up being my choice.  Provided multiple file selection boxes, so it was much easier to upload multiple images at once.  Supported the .jpg format my files were in.  Uploading was quick, and provided direct links that were easy to copy.
