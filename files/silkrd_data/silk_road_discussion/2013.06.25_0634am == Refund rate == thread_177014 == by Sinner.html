Title: Re: Refund rate
Post by: Pharmington Rex on June 25, 2013, 04:58 pm
If the vendor is truly a scammer, then a refund you get on account of this vendor would not affect your refund rate.

If however, the refund is due to a disagreement of some kind, the refund will be reflected in your stats.

If you refund rate is <10%, most vendors won't care. Plus you can always explain why you have a refund rate to and vendor you are interested in.
