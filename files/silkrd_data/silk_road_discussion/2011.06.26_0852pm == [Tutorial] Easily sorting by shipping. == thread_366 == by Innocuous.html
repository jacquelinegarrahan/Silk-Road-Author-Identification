So I wanted to easily sort the silk road menu to group items by country for ease of use, and found this javascript 'bookmarklet'. It works great for the shipping column but it is kind of broken for price though.

Credits go to squarefree.com.

Works in all browsers and is real easy to set up. Just right click your bookmarks bar and add a new book mark then copypasta the chunk of javascript as the location/address and hit save. Now when you are browsing silk click your new bookmark and a new "a/d" should be at the top of each row in the main table. Enjoy!
