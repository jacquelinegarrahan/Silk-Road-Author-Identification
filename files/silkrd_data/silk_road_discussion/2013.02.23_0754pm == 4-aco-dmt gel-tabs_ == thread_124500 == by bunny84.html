Post by: jaria8 on March 16, 2013, 03:03 pm
Trip report with puffdaddy's 4-aco-dmt feel tabs.  This was my first experience with 4-aco-dmt. I took a full tab (10 mg) on a full very full stomach. About an hour and a half later I was bummed because I was only experiencing barely noticeable effects and thought I got ripped off. Somewhere between the second and third hour I started realizing I was smiling so hard it hurt. From there on out it was general euphoria. Jokes were so funny at times that I cried, slight shades of color were very noticeable, couldn't tell if I was warm or cold, etc. This peaked for probably just under 2 hours. Total duration (once I felt it) was a little over 5 hours. No open eyed or closed eye visuals. Clear headed enough that I could have normal conversations with sober people fine. No nausea at all. A no point would I was actually tripping, but I felt right on the verge of it. As many people have said in the past, I found it very similar to a shroom trip.

Also, a friend also took a full gel tab, and experienced about the same effects, however he felt it with a quicker onset (could feel the effects by the end of the first hour). So I attribute my slow onset to taking it on a full stomach.

I just wanted to update the community for anyone on the fence about this. In the future if I want to really trip, I will probably take one and a half to two tabs (15-20 mg). But one tab is great for a first time using it and would be ideal for social situations where you have to interact with a lot of other people. I would recommend these and though i have eight left, im already thinking about getting more.

Also, thanks for the heads up oldcactushand. I would definitely recommend puffdaddy's tabs, however I would be interested in JoR's product as well.
