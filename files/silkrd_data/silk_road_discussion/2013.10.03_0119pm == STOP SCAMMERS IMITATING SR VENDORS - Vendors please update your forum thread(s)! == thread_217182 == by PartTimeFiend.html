Title: STOP SCAMMERS IMITATING SR VENDORS - Vendors please update your forum thread(s)!
Post by: PartTimeFiend on October 03, 2013, 01:19 pm
I've noticed more than a few top SR vendors are appearing on the other marketplaces.  Although some of these may be genuine, we know some of them will be scammers looking to cash in on the good reputation of the REAL vendor.  This has happened previously when the SR has been down for a few days and especially during that 2 week period earlier this year!

I've also noticed some well-known vendors coming on with the "I've forgotten my SR forum password", so my new name on the SR forums is INSERT SIMILAR VENDOR NAME and my new profile page on Sheep or BMR is INSERT LINK, etc....  hmmmmm!

While I accept some of these vendors may have actually forgotten their passwords and are genuinely migrating to other marketplaces, we also know this is a classic scammer tactic.  So all you REAL vendors out there, if you can update your forum thread(s) with a message to let us know your plans that would be great.  That way we can name and shame the "imitation scammers",  OR if you are migrating, we can go to the alternative marketplace knowing that this is REALLY you, and continue to do business with you.

Cheers,
