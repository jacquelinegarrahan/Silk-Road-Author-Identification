Title: Re: why no BC rate on SR anymore?
Post by: CaptainSensible on January 07, 2012, 09:31 pm
Quote from: czxtvr on January 07, 2012, 05:03 am
So where do we get the rate from again please?

The current rate at MtGox isn't always in sync with the rate used at SR.  SR tends to be a a few hours behind the rate at MtGox.  If you really want to see what your Bitcoins are worth on SR go to the "settings" link at the top right of the SR page and divide your currency amount by your Bitcoin amount.  So if you change the setting to display in dollars, for example, and then change to "Display in Bitcoins" you'll divide dollars by Bitcoins.  This will give you the current Bitcoin value on SR.
