Whilst I would always encourage buyers never to FE, this is normal in this scenario. As far as the Silk Road system is concerned you have received your order and are satisfied with it. It is now completely out of the Silk Road system.

If you choose to FE in future, you should private message yourself the transaction details (by clicking on your name beside the shopping cart icon) so you will at least have a record of the transaction number, date / time of ordering, and amount spent.

Again though, if there is an issue with the order, it is absolutely none of Silk Road's business as the transaction has been completed satisfactorily as attested to by you when you clicked the 'finalize' button.
