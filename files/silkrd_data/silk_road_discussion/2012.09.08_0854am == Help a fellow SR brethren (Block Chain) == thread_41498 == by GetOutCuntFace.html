Title: Re: Help a fellow SR brethren (Block Chain)
Post by: xdjx on September 10, 2012, 09:59 pm
This is the way I track my bitcoins going to the road. There may be better out there but this is what I do.
Go to blockchain.info and enter your SR BTC address into the search bar.
This should bring you to a overview of that BTC address showing the latest transactions going in and out of that address and hopefully you should see your recent sends in the list which should match up to what you have sent to your SR wallet. Once it shows 5-6 confirmations your coins should be on the road soon? (Not 100% on how many confirms SR needs)
If you can't find them using this try putting your sending wallet address into the blockchain.info search, this will then show you the addresses you have sent coins to recently, always double check the BTC addresses here because if your drunk and not copy pasting its really easy to mess up the send address. (Done that once before myself)
If you accidently sent the coins to a wrong address im pretty sure they are lost in the nether and can't be retrieved.
