Post by: dipset on July 30, 2013, 07:19 pm
If out of nowhere, you start having multiple packages a week to a drop, or repeats from the same vendor that uses the same packaging and return city/area, that may start to seem odd. However, if you also order things online that ship USPS and have those things delivered in between (and with) your SR packages, they should start to seem more legit. That is my opinion, anyway. Also, try to limit the number of packages if possible. Every one is another opportunity for something to go wrong. I get nervous about the volume of packages, and too many repeat orders from the same vendors that don't switch up mailers. But all in all, the odds are heavily in your favor, if you stick domestic anyway. Most mail is being processed and carried by zip code until it hits your specific post office/mail carrier.  Your mail carrier is the one that will first likely notice a pattern being created, but they are unlikely to care or draw conclusions. Most just deliver your shit, and take off.
