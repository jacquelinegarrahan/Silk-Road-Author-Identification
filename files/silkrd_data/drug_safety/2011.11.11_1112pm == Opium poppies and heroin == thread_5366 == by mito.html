To answer your question technically, all opiates come from the poppy plant, but when i say plant, i really mean the poppy pod. However, the pharma companies that have poppy farms process the pods, stalks and even the roots (the roots were discovered to include a ton of alkaloids as well).
Out friends in the golden triangle probably just take from the pods. Raw crude opium that is excreted after making a slit in the opium pod during the peak of its growth contains a number of alkaloids that we all love and know such as morphine, codiene, thebaine.... These alkaloids are processed to make synthetic opiods such as oxycontin, fentynyl, etc... (sorry for the spelling, im stoned, but the information is correct). Yes, it is true that we can get both pharmacutical morphine and oxycontin even tho one is natural and one is synthetic, but one can be found directly out of the opium pod, the other must be manufactured.
The raw crude opium crap that comes out of the pod is processed into morphine base, which is a high crude brown paste, the shit we know as opium. Heroin is one step further... heroin is produced by acetylation of morphine which creates one of the worlds most ridiculous drugs ever... diacetylmorphine, or heroin, comes to us in different purities, resulting from multiple chemical purification processes that costs tons of money to produce due to the low yield.
