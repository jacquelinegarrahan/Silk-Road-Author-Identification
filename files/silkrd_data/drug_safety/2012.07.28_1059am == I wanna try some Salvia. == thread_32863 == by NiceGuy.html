1. Please do lots of research on it to know what to expect.
2. Have a sober person there to keep you seated and safe.
3. Do NOT smoke all of it at once. Try one or two long tokes at most, then try again another day.

You may feel that time slows down to a stop.
You may feel dizzy. Lie down, it's okay.
Your post smoke might have cold sweat and intense thoughts.

Prepare some cool water for after, the smoke may burn.
It can be a 'fun' trip, but remember Salvia can freak you out fairly easily if you haven't experienced it before.

Title: Re: I wanna try some Salvia.
Post by: Sname on July 30, 2012, 08:06 am
Salvia is a highly trippy drug with virtually no "fun" value to it. Some may find value in the other worldly experience, but I just find it terrifying. Here are my thoughts however:

1: The drug seems to have a reverse tolerance. Every new time I tried it I would get way more high. This may or may not be due to mastering the technique, which brings me to my next point.

2: The ingestion technique requires some skill to maximize the effect. If you want full effect, use a big bong or the tube from a box vaporizer. Take slow, long and deep draws and only do one. If you perform this part right then you will be out of this world.

3: If you are to get really high, you won't be able to function in normal life. Where I live this is legal so I would do it in public places. Nothing like a crowd of people in a park gathering around you come back to reality after ripping a load of salvia from a vaporizer tube.

4: If you do salvia while already high on other psychedelics, get ready to have the experience prolonged more than anybody would want. I took a very small, threshold amount of mescaline one time and then hit the salvia bong a few years ago back in college in an outdoor setting. This caused an intense salvia high which lasted for an hour. I had to have friends help me around as I could not see the world around me and was stuck in another dimension. Very terrifying.

5: if you understand the power of the drug, go for it.

Hope that helps, bud!

Title: Re: I wanna try some Salvia.
Post by: simulacra on August 26, 2012, 05:22 am
I smoked a good pinch of 20x salvia extract last friday night with a mate. First experience.  He was eager to try it first but after a big bong hit, he went through a terrifying experience - seeing the dense sea of atoms that he said he was drowning in and was afraid he would be squashed in the tiny 'wormhole' he had to fit through - understandably he was terrified. All the time I was comforting him and trying to get through to him by stroking his arm and back and  reassuring him that he wasn't drowning or hadn't died - like he had imagined (it seemed he may have gone through a type of spiritual death and rebirth experience?) I kept reassuring him that it would be over in minutes and to try to just observe what is happening and detach from it.

Anyway within 5 mins he was back and even began giggling a little. He had remembered almost every part of his experience and we talked about it, although he was clearly frightened by it. Then it was my turn. I put approximately the same amount in the bong with the damiana mixture and inhaled until my throat couldn't take the heat any more- and literally in a flash I found myself - firstly in some kind of outdoor (it was about 11.30pm real time) shelter with benches and people on a normal sunny day. One guy who sort of resembled my mate, but wearing sunglasses was lying sideways on a far bench (later my mate told me he was sitting next to me on the low sofa the whole time) staring at me! It irritated me not knowing if it was him or not. Then I was in a room of pale, yellow, glossy wood with a long rectangular window on one side from which I could see bright green foilage. First I wondered if I was in some southern US state? But I live in Australia. There were nondescript people around just like at the outdoor shelter (bus terminal?). There was also a grey mist/cloud filled with people at the edge of the obscured misty side of the room. They were communicating with me - very friendly - and I remember I was very curious about being shown through the grey mist. I think I said to them - telepathically - ' i'm so grateful but you'll make me forget all of this, won't you.? but they never answered clearly. Next thing I know I was back in my mate's living room! He told me I said "Am I back'?.

I usually do thorough research into what I'm taking and I had admitted to him that I hadn't had time to do enough research on salvia before we had smoked it. But because he was keen to try it, I decided to go along - although I had wanted to increase the amount by small amounts. Oh yeh, we used two bic lighters to make the mixture burn hot - because I had heard a butane lighter is better for salvia and we were out of butane.

Summary:  Although I have never tried or been interested in dissociatives - salvia felt like one to me. I was totally disconnected from any living. organic reality and switched to what I would describe as a lucid dream state. It had absolutely no spiritual/psychedelic feeling about it. And even though my friend  had experienced frightening effects of fabrics of atoms and molecules about to crush him to fit through an infinitely small hole, he also agreed it felt, very much like a lucid dream, so realistic that one can be fooled it is reality. I also found it devoid of the spiritual or religious qualities - experienced with LSD, mescaline, psylocybin or psyilosin.  Today I looked it up on wikipedia and found out that salvia is classed as a  dissociative and deliriant. Anyway, that explains the bizzare, unusual and for a lot of people - terrifying effects of salvia. I have yet to try the 'fun' doses of 5x ... but it's still an interesting drug -  if you're into dissociatives.
