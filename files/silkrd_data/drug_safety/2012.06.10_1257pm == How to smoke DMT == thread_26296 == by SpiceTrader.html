Personally, I use "the machine" to vapourise it. Really simple to make. Amazing results.

Post by: msft1 on June 13, 2012, 12:33 am
Personally I don't have access to glass drill or steel wool easily, so I have made a simple but effective "DMT pipe".

I take a cheap wooden pipe that has about 1/2-inch opening on top and put a few of those round pipe filters in it (they're just a bit larger than 1/2-inch, but flexible, so they stay in the pipe secure). The steel filters provide even heat-up and vaporization of DMT, and wooden pipe provides for easy delivery (DMT doesn't stick to wood like it does to glass). I then put the right amount of freebase DMT on top of the mesh filters, and gently place flame from the lighter on top, and inhale (carefully, so as not to get any liquid DMT in).

It works like a charm - I got huge hits of DMT from that. Zero assembly required and only two pieces - wooden pipe and some mesh filters (any smoke shop sells it, together around $10).
