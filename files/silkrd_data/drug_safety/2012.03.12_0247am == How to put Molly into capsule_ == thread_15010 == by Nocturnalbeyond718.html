Title: Re: How to put Molly into capsule?
Post by: 328502E on March 12, 2012, 08:16 pm
Here's how I do it.

Get out my mg scale (yes, mg.  if you don't have one, GET ONE). 
Scoop out ~125mg mdma onto the scale.  Note how much it weighs.
Dump powder onto a piece a paper folded down the middle.  I use lab weighing paper, because it has some very nice non-stick properties.
Prop open capsule up on something
Take creased paper and set it on the capsule (with the crease in the capsule).  Tilt down into the capsule and tap the paper.
If done correctly, the MDMA will slowly move down the paper into the cap.
Cap the pill.
Get out a pen, write the dosage on one side and the chemical on the other.  In this example, you could use "M - 130" to signify that it is MDMA, and the quantity added was 130mg.
Swallow.

Yes, I would highly recommend getting a milligram scale off of Amazon. I got one for $14USD off of Amazon here:

http://amzn.com/B0012LOQUQ

I can't emphasize how important it is! Eyeballing amounts can be pretty dangerous, and dosages for stuff like MDMA is important. I also ordered tiny metal spoons as well. In my experience, a level scoop with one of these spoons (I forget which size spoon) gets you just about 100mg of crushed molly:

http://amzn.com/B000MOYI5W

Here's how I measure out and fill my capsules.

1) I use an index card with the sides folded up, and one end is creased and cut so that it forms sort of a pointy funnel. I make sure to tare (re-zero) the scale after I place the index card on top of it.
2) Scoop or pour the molly onto the index card and weigh out your desired dosage.
3) Grab an open capsule and use the index card to funnel the molly into it.

I'm sure there are better ways if you get creative, but that method's worked for me.

Title: Re: How to put Molly into capsule?
Post by: Gibbroni on March 14, 2012, 04:33 pm
Forums are indeed the perfect place for this sort of question.  I am quite proud of my recent cap-packing achievements actually, and it's quite fun to sit at home with some music making some caps, thinking about all the good times you will have on them!

I am proud because I managed to borrow a work mate's decent milligram scale for a night, managed to pick up some empty caps from local headshop and pack 2 grams worth!

Initially what I did was make a makeshift cone-piece looking thing from a small circle of cardboard, rolled into a tiny cylinder after cutting out a pizza slice size (relative to the size of the circle, which was about 2cms in diameter), dab of sticky tape to hold the cone-like funnel together.

Keep in mind, some caps can weigh around .1, I discovered, so I reset the tare every few caps with an empty cap, checking it was consistent, say it was say -.09 (almost a point) with nothing on it... (minus zero)

Crushed my moonrock mdma crystal (hehe!) in a tupperware container with the end of a heavy solid dinner knife, took the other end of the knife and put 3 or 4 small scoops into the cardboard cone (with a hole in the bottom) going into one of the cap halves.  Put it on the scale and adjusted by tapping some back out into the container if it was over, or putting more in.  Put other cap half back on.

I made 130mg caps

Held them all up to the light, perfect!

One thing to look out for though, is what the cap is made out of.  I made the mistake of buying gelatin capsules, which I later found aren't broken down in the stomach all that fast....  Unfortunately A friend of mine who had never had mdma before bought one from me, and I was all excited for him and his come-up, peak etc, and he never had it "hit" him. It crept up over an hour to an hour and a half (far far too long for it to come on imo), and when he was feeling it was the one hour we were separated at the festival. Oh well, you live, you learn!

I've been to another festival since, and my preparation/smuggling methods were my best to date!  Many would probably consider it overkill etc, but I don't think anyone ordering from this site should be complacent with things they can definitely avoid. 

To take your molly caps into a festival (festival stealth!) or gig etc, take the pre-measured cap contents and roll into about a 1/2 of a cigarette rolling paper, twist, trim etc, as long as it's a medium density little ball.

I took 2 x 130mg caps and put about 190ish split between two paper balls, and the remaining 70mgs in another, making a total of 3.  The first two balls I put back into one cap, and I put the one ball in the other.  I then hollowed out a few cigarettes completely, slid the caps in, and densely packed tobacco over the top.

Total and complete stealth!  So I knew the cap with the two paper balls was for the initial dose, and the one with only one was for the second!  You really get a feel for these methods after a while!  And my oh my, they hit within 20 minutes!

Good luck to you soon-to-be cap-packer friend!

P.S - I put miniature joints inside cigarettes the same way! Peace of mind is a great thing at any event that might be targeted by LE.  ;) :D
