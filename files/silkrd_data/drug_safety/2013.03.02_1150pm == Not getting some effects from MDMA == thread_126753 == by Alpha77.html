Title: Re: Not getting some effects from MDMA
Post by: DiamondSky on March 04, 2013, 06:01 am
As an MDMA lover I can safely say the eye wiggles (nystagmous) are more common on the first few trips and normally just an effect of taking a little too much. Your body acclimates to MDMA in my experience and more often than not most folks don't get insane eye wiggles unless they take it rarely or take bigger doses.

Spacing out the rolls as others have mentioned will help some. A month is a pretty reasonable amount of time to wait but the longer the better. You have a finite amount of MDMA joy available in life. You can burst through it quickly or let it recharge longer between rolls to stretch the fun out longer.

A few tips.

Try some MDA sometime, it's more like the first time with MDMA than MDMA is the third or fourth time out. Heck, the first batch might have had a little MDA in it which increases the visual stuff as well as the physical euphoria in my experience.

If you are adventurous, consider plugging the dose (yeah, up the butt, I know, but hear me out). By plugging MDMA you bypass a lot of variables like last meal, stomach acid composition, bio-availability issues and the like. It hits harder, faster, and lasts about as long as taking it orally. Obviously, there's a stigma associated with the whole idea but most people who actually give it a try end up agreeing it's simply better than eating when conditions allow for privacy of administration.

Setting makes a HUGE difference in the roll you have. I take mine with my wife or girlfriend and we just sit around being in love. It's consistent for us because we always do it the same way. If you take it on a night out on the town or with new friends over it's going to react to the environment. The same drug will feel different based on something as small as the person you are taking it with smoked some pot earlier that night or got a little drunk. The key is to just go with it and see what you get out of that night. More often than not you'll get to bond with someone or find out something about yourself or just enjoy a little well earned bliss even if the rolls feel a little different.

Have fun though!
