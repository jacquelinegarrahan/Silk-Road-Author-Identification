Post by: wolfstein on August 17, 2012, 07:59 pm
Valium (diazepam) is the go-to choice for aborting an LSD trip. That's not to say you won't feel under the influence of the stuff, but it mostly eliminates any accompanying anxiety/ worry and can perhaps curtail the duration a bit.

In fact, IV diazepam is what is used in hospital settings to treat patients having a bad trip. DO NOT ever IV diazepam tablets, though -- there's no way to leech out the binders and, to my knowledge, valium isn't water soluble. Ss unless you have an IV ready solution, just pop a couple valium and wait.
