Title: Re: Amphetamine Dosage
Post by: le botbahn on June 29, 2012, 04:07 am
I'm expecting mine tomorrow (from UGS). I'll have a review up by tomorrow evening.

I agree that 20 mg should be plenty for a pure amphetamine (like adderall) for a new user. Even as an experienced user of amphetamines, 20 mg jazzes me up nicely if I've been sleeping & eating.

FWIW, I believe the 70%+ may be akin to the MDMA 83%+ thing. That0 is, a molecule of amphetamine sulfate salt has a molecular mass of ~185 [~135 amp + ~50 sulphate]. The amphetamine part accounts for >70% of the salt molecule.
That's why all speed and MDMA vendor write 70%+ and 80%+ (respectively), because those numbers (if accurate) leave little room for anything else (impurities).

Obviously, this doesn't mean shit, as evidenced by all the garbage sold by european vendors with the same claims.

I've never bought from him before but I've been aware of him for a while now and he's a solid vendor (super nice too). I doubt he'd sell garbage with a rep like his. And it's listed at a damn good price (but so are some of his other items).

Neither substances should be that high imo. Personally I prefer speed but it quickly loses it's appeal when it approaches the price of pure d-meth.
