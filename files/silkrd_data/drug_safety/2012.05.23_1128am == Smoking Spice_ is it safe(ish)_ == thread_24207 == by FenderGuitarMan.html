Title: Re: Smoking Spice: is it safe(ish)?
Post by: tedka on May 23, 2012, 12:28 pm
hello!
i have a lot of experience with indole class synthetic cannabinoids (used more than 120g, mostly jwh-122 and jwh-018) and can give you a  share of my educated opinion.

synthetic noids of the JWH family and its close relatives dont compare too good to weed; they are much more potent, develop tolerance way faster, have a kind of different spectrum of desired effects (more munchies/painkilling/aphrodisiac/sedating effects and less euphoria/possible sociability compared to cannabis) and some of them can produce SEVERE side effects if you arent a veteran stoner. while weed at worst may produce mild to average paranoia, some JWHs average to strong anxiety (and some GI discomfort), AM-2201 (very popular in commercial blends, short duration, instant effects, very potent) may actually (proxy)kill you if your endocannabiod system hasnt been around the blocks a couple of times;
unless you are a good eyeballer or have an expensive scale with constant power supply (not these less-than-150usd-battery pieces of rubbish) it is VERY easy to overdose on it; if you havent been smoking pot for the last 5-10 years, an AM-2201-overdose goes something like this:

after 2min: complete shutdown of muscle control. person just drops on the spot, no matter what they are currently doing. person remains on the floor, starts making strange noises

after 2-5min: twitching all over the body while making VERY loud noises like someone deaf who is getting shitkicked; absolutely no visible reaction to whats going on around them. seriously, i pointed what appeared to be an unlocked and loaded gun at people who have had too much am2201, because i thought they where trying to play a practical joke on me; no reaction whatsoever. just stayed on the floor, muscles twitching all over the body, moaning/shouting like they finally lost it permanently. its very frightening to watch and just painful to listen to.
 
after 5-20min: state described above persists and gets slowly weaker. when you can finally get a verbal reaction out of them again, they usually just remember having a great buzz till they lost motor control and wonder what they are doing on the floor and why half the stuff from the kitchen counter is also on the floor (since they swept across it while making a sandwich before hitting the ground)

ending up in such a state can be a difference of .5mg from a nice buzz for some people.

i have witnessed it three times so far. one time i had the opportunity to apply 2 milligrams of clonazepam to the victim. this stopped the twitching muscles very fast and gave back general consciousness after less than 5 minutes.
none of the victims reported to have experienced a particularly unpleasant experience, one actually was like "AGAIN AGAIN AGAIN".
still, if you have a benzodiazepine at hand; just put a solid dose under their tounge, put their head on your lap, keep them from moving around/hurting themselves and be there for them till they "come back". that should prevent the worse, like stupid neighbors calling 911 from all the shouting and serious damage from the muscle spasms or wriggling around on the floor, knocking over and bumping into stuff.  if you dont have benzos ready, its you decide if you let them ride it out or get them medical attention; unless they have less than 10min reaction time, it will most likely be over till they arrive though (unless they hurt themselves bad in their initial fall)

in a nutshell; steer clear from commercial blends unless you can tell for sure whats in them, if you still want to try synthetic noids better get some pure JWH-122 or JWH-250, they are pretty safe and have some of the nicest effects in that area; 250 is the only one that really comes close to weed in all aspects and has mild potency, making it easy to handle, 122 is very long lasting, has reasonable potency and nice effects.

stay safe and have fun

i beg to differ.

the whole deal with weed losing effect after you smoked your share of JWH is a complete myth. or most likely was concluded because a bunch of stoners kept smoking pot after their 5g bag of JWH-xxx was empty. and for some reason people keep repeating this information, even though it isnt really the case.
