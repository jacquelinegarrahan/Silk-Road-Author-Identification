I'll chime in on this one...

I personally have never gotten any good euphoria from oral morphine. IV is where it becomes euphoric, but I've only had that in a hospital setting.

20mg's of hydro might not be enough for you. If you took it on a completely empty stomach, absorption is different than if you took it on a full stomach.

Some people just don't get much out of hydrocodone. I know I had a hard time "recognizing" the euphoria from opiates until I was somewhat experienced with them. Now hydro is fairly euphoric, but I'd give it a 6/10 compared to other opi's.

If you wanna get some surefire euphoria, go for a decent dose of Oxycodone. It's more (unmistakably) euphoric, and its oral activity is great.

Truth be told, you probably just botched your first shot at hydrocodone. Give it another shot, maybe next time at 30mg's or so.

If you don't feel much, try Oxycodone.
