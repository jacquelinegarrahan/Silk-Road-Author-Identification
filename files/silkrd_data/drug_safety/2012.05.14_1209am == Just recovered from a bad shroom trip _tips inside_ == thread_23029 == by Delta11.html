Title: Just recovered from a bad shroom trip *tips inside*
Post by: Delta11 on May 14, 2012, 12:09 am
Well last night some friends came over with some shrooms and I was pretty excited because I haven't had shrooms in over a month. I am sensitive when it comes to psychedelics so a little bit is a lot for me so I found my sweet spot with shrooms at 1.5g of regular dried cubensis. I weighed out everyone's dose (most people doing 3.5g) and then turned into a tea. I normally feel shroom tea within 10 minutes of ingesting it but I only had a mild body high and some enhanced vision, only one person was fully tripping and my two other friends were on the same level as me. Anyway, we waited an hour and we were still on the same level so we ate some more (I ate another 1.5g so 3g in total) and right after I ate them I began to trip on the shroom tea full on. I started enjoying the visuals until about 30 minutes later the other 1.5g hit me hard and it was too much for me to handle and started spiraling down into a bad trip. I can't really explain it much without sounding crazy but I could see myself as a human being for the first time and was freaking out about it, I couldn't look at my hands without freaking out, it was as if I was observing myself third person like in a video game. I began to have an anxiety attack for about an hour straight but I didn't want to worry my friends so I just kept rolling with it until it got to the point where I needed medical attention.

Normally I would have xanax on hand but I was completely out and the only thing I had were amphetamines so I was all alone in dealing with it. Finally my good friend came to the rescue with a big bottle of vodka and made me a really strong drink and I started downing it and oh sweet jesus I came down and was pretty drunk but feeling good. The shrooms/alcohol wore off at about 7am and we went to get breakfast and I have never felt better in my life. I felt a lot stronger mentally and really feel like my psyche is grounded now, I probably won't be taking shrooms for a while and will stick to my good friends 2c-i/LSD. Anyway, here are some tips for anyone that is going to be taking a psychedelic.

-Always have a benzo on hand or alcohol, I normally do but assumed I would be okay, bad mistake.
-Don't over think things, I spiraled out of control by over thinking how many shrooms I consumed instead of just enjoying the trip.
-Always be around good friends, I couldn't have imagined going through this experience with strangers or at a party.
-Dose correctly, I have been doing psychs for years now and have never really needed to go higher up in my doses.
-Always remember that it's all in your mind, relax, focus on your breathing and tell yourself that you took a psychedelic and it will wear off and you will be okay.
-Don't let it traumatize you, everyone will have a difficult trip at some point but you can't let one stop you from continuing exploring/enjoying psychedelics.
-Know your boundaries, I can't smoke weed while doing a psychedelic, it's very common for people to enjoy cannabis while tripping or coming down from a psychedelic but personally it gives me bad anxiety, just because everyone does something doesn't mean you have to. Everyone has different boundaries so find what yours are and respect them.
