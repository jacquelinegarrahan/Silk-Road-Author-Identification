Title: Re: Anyone tried those newfangled APB compounds?
Post by: Ben on June 16, 2013, 01:40 am
You should always be darned careful to try any RC substance.

That said, these synthetic agonist can be extremely potent. It is not uncommon for a synthetic to have 100 fold the activation of the natural substance (think thc vs synthetic analogues). The most important issue is if the synthetic is better, e.g. more specific, compared to the natural substance.

Once the potency has been established it boils down to that question. At the moment i don't know if any of the APB series offers any benefit over mdma, but they are called research chemicals for a good reason...
