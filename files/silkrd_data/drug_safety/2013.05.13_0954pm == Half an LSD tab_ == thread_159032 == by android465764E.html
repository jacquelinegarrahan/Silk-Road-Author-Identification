It's rarely evenly spread. Some SR vendors like Tessellated (whose product is fantastic, btw!) have been testing various blotter paper with droplets of food coloring to see how evenly the liquid holds. Such sellers have found better paper for more even distribution, but it's still not a perfect science as the acid is often not dissolved equally into the liquid that holds it, and water mechanics are very sporadic.
