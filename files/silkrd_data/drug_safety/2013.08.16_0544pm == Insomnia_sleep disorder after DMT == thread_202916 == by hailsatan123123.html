Sometimes after intensive use it *can* happen in certain circumstances. The DMT "trip" is very similar to what happens in the heavier phase of REM sleep and so DMT can actually influence that phase during sleep cycles. If it depends on the pharmacology or on what happens during the experience (or a mix of the two) it is unknown to me at this moment, what I know is that it can happen, especially (as I said) during intensive use. The same thing happens, btw, when you practice meditative states and you reach certain levels.

If you are bugged by it I suggest you to simply stop the use of DMT for some time, after about 10 days everything should return to perfectly normal. This or you hold on and it will pass by itself but it is a hard step to pass and I don't suggest it if you don't know what you are doing.
