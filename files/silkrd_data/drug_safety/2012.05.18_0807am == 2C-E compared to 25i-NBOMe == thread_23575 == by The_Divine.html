Title: Re: 2C-E compared to 25i-NBOMe
Post by: l1llykins on May 18, 2012, 05:55 pm
If it's a choice of one of the above, definitely 25i 500ug unless you're a big person, then go for 1000ug. There's a little "happy" built into that one so you're more likely to have a smooth trip.

2c-e is not something you want to start with if you never tripped before. That's one of the more intense and unpredictable 2c's.

For a first trip, I recommend 2c-c ... 2c-i and 2c-b are a little less noobish and less expensive but 2c-c is about as safe as you can get.
