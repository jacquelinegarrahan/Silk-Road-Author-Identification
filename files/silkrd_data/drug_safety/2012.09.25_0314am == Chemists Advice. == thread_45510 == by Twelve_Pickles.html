I don't think Ehrlich will do much of anything with DOC, its a spot test for alkaloids, primarily indoles.  Theres a list of color reactions here https://www.dmt-nexus.me/forum/default.aspx?g=posts&t=25771 , there's also a huge thread somewhere on bluelight with Marquis+Mecke color reactions but I can't seem to search it at the moment.

You need to use multiple tests to get the most conclusive results (Ehrlich+Marquis). But that can be pretty tricky because Marquis will react paper, so you have to dissolve the substance on the paper, with something like pure Ethanol (95%) and then use something non-reactive with Marquis like Glass.

Even with all of the above, being 100% sure requires a GC/MS test.

P.S. DOB is scheduled and I'm sure DOC could be considered an Analog.
