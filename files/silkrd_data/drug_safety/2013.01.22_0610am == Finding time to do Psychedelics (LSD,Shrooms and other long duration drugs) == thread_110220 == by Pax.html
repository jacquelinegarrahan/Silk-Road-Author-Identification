Title: Re: Finding time to do Psychedelics (LSD,Shrooms and other long duration drugs)
Post by: Wazup7 on January 22, 2013, 06:15 pm
I used to trip at home occasionally, but after some thought, I realized that tripping at home is kind of a waste, because I spent a good portion of my time being distracted by (or having to deal with) house-related things.  Like my family coming home unexpectedly, or an emergency call from work.  Even more than that, simply being at home would prompt me to be thinking about common, typical things that are in my thoughts daily. 

I much prefer to take a trip to take my trips.  Being somewhere novel has always given me more bang for my buck with LSD (my psych of choice).  New environment, new thoughts, and new ideas are what I enjoy most about LSD, and being somewhere that's not ordinary is my best way of achieving that.  So I usually allocate my trips to times when I am free to take a vacation for a couple days.  Like an overnight camping trip in the woods, or a night at a beach resort or casino, or my favorite, a weekend music festival where drugs can be used without worry.

So needless to say, when I trip on LSD, I usually spend about $100 on the gas, food, accommodations, etc. that go with a one-night vacation.  Sometimes, I take a proper vacation (flight and hotel, more than 1 night) and take my trip there, in which case I spend a lot more on the vacation, but get a lot more out of it.

My main point is that for me, tripping in "the usual places" has gotten old, and I never find myself in a situation where I am at home and thinking that dropping LSD is a good idea.  I much prefer tripping on vacation, in which case I will have already made the time to take the vacation.  Those days of tripping at home are probably over, unless it's a good mix of people and the right opportunity comes around..
