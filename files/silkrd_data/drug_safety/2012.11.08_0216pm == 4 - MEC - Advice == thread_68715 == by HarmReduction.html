There isn't as much information out there about this particular drug, however people consistently seem to report that it produces a strong urge to re-dose so keep that in mind when deciding how much you plan to use. It may be wise to only give yourself access to a small, set amount from the beginning to avoid ending up taking more than you would like to.

Also, since the typical doses are in the range of 50-60mg, its important to be able to accurately measure out this amount. Many scales don't go down to the milligram level and some that do are highly inaccurate. There are some fairly reliable (and inexpensive) scales available, such as this one http://alturl.com/4hrxq which is approximately $23 at this time.

Good luck, and enjoy!! :)
