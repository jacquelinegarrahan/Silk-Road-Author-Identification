SSRIs work on the SERT (Serotonine transporter) receptor. Most psychedelics (LSD, NBOMe, shrooms, 2C, ...) are 5-HT2 agonists. This means they don't interact because they have different pathways and you can do them while on SSRIs without problems. Serotonin releasers (MDxx) show reduced/no effect though.
