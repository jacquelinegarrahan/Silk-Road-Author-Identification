Title: Re: How to use freebase pipe for dmt
Post by: spacewasp on June 06, 2012, 07:40 pm
using one of these pipes is far from ideal, as you're almost guaranteed to burn the drug.  like someone else said, the machine is best. 

that said, i've had breakthroughs with the meth pipe.

hold the lighter a couple inches beneath the bulb while holding the stem with your finger tips.  this way you can rotate the pipe back and forth to keep the flame from staying in one spot under the pipe.  alternatively, you could just rotate the lighter in a small circle while you're heating the pipe.  take your time, if you do it in a way that will minimize burning, it could take 30 seconds or so before it starts to vape.  once it does take the flame away and inhale, when it stops smoking, apply a little more heat to get it going again.  smoke as much as you possibly can.

oh, and definitely DONT put your finger over that hole, lol.
