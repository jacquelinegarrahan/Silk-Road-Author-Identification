Heyenezz, i would say ingesting a substance of unknown purity and strength brings with it an inherent risk of an overdose or bad reaction.

I would say yes, you can OD from smoking H.  Your respiratory system can become so depressed if you are nodding really hard that your body can just forget to breathe.  Or if you nod off and happen to lie down on your back it is possible you can vomit and then aspirate it.

I would say if you really want to mitigate the possibility of something going awry you should get a sitter.  Or some naloxone if you feel like you've gone too far but are still conscious enough to know it.

One more thing. If you are getting a .5: take your time, do a hit then wait a bit, if it feels ok do another then wait some more.  I personally wouldn't just go rushing in balls out your first time.

In terms of safety of the various ROAs for heroin, I think smoking is the safest. All that said, you should always be careful (especially if you have low or no opioid tolerance.)
