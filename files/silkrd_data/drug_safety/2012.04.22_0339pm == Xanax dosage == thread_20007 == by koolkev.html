Post by: frogger on April 23, 2012, 11:47 pm
Naturally you're bound to get a mixed bag of recommendations on this question.

The time of day really affected the results I got from Xanax.  The first time I tried it, I took half a bar (1Mg) at lunch time, and had a good head-high,  A friend picked me up at the airport and dropped the half totem-pole in my hands and told me to eat it; I did.

We then went for lunch and eventually back to her house.  After getting to her pad, I unpacked my stuff, and met her back in the living room.  I asked her 'When are we going to eat lunch, I'm starving!?"

She laughed and told me 'you're prob. feeling pretty good, huh?"  I replied 'yeah'. and she told me that we had lunch after picking me up from the airport!

I could take 2Mg in the morning and early afternoon without a problem but if I took 2Mg after normal work hours I would pass out in an hour or two at most.

What worked for me was a lunch time pill on an empty stomach.  I'd feel the effects within 20 minutes or so, and full strength at an hour into it.

As a note, if I had any beer/alcohol I'd have absolutely NO memory from the time I ingested.  If I had more than 2 beers, I'd pass out after 2-1/2 hours or so, so be careful what you do after you start.
