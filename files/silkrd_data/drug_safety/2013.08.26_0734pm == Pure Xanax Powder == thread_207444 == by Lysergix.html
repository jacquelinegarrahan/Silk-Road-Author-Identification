Title: Re: Pure Xanax Powder
Post by: junjo on August 27, 2013, 09:16 pm
DO NOT eyeball or "toothpick" the dose with such a potent chemical unless you enjoy blacking out.
Safely dosing alprazolam is actually quite easy without an ultra-sensitive scale.
Simply make a liquid solution of it.
Alprazolam is not water soluble, but is freely soluble in propylene glycol, which you can find for quite cheap anywhere on the internet.
Measure out 100mL of propylene glycol, add that and the 100mg of xanax to a bottle, and shake vigorously.
Voila, you have a 1 mg/mL Xanax solution!
There's really no way you can screw this up, and you always know the exact dose you get every time.
You can also make a lot more concentrated solution and put droplets of it on blotters, candy, or anything the xanax will stick to.
That's pretty much all there is too it.

Title: Re: Pure Xanax Powder
Post by: Ben on September 07, 2013, 01:31 am
I would not recommend dabbing, toothpicking or by any other means trying to eyeball something like 1 mg. If you are familiar with the substance you could do this with reasonable accuracy (as in off by a factor of no more than 2). If you are not, you can make grave mistakes.

You can try this by putting some piles of kitchen salt onto slides and ask friends to eyeball which is closest to being a milligram. You'd be surprised how wrong people get that simple test - often under-estimating how much is really there.

With salt this is a perfectly innocent game, but if you mis-dose 10 mg xanax for 1 mg you would probably end up in serious trouble.
