Title: Re: MDMA Dosing
Post by: SequelTo33rpm on April 25, 2012, 03:36 am
Whenever you re-dose on MDMA, there is a certain point in time where any additional dosing probably won't do anything but make the comedown a bit more unpleasant. Your body develops a very acute tolerance to MDMA while it is still at peak levels in your body and as such, taking more really won't add much. The reason why it didn't affect you Saturday is simple, the acute tolerance has made your brain its bitch and your brain's serotonin reserves are a bit depleted. If you want an effect for multiple days in a row, you need to dose much higher on each subsequent day, but that's generally a poor idea. Wait at least 1.5 to 2 weeks minimum before rolling again, a month to be sure, and 2 months to primed and ready for another magical day. Hope this helped ya out and stay safe.
