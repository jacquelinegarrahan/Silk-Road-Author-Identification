Post by: sdesu on November 08, 2012, 10:19 pm
Definitely limit the number of people present, and make sure that there is at least one sitter at all times.
In regards to music, I haven't noticed a difference in my trips based on what music is playing. I tend to have almost the same exact visuals/experience whether I'm listening to Sigur Ros, Ephixa, or Mogwai.
Bright lighting can make for an interesting come-up and comedown visual experience especially right before and right after the plateau. Try looking at a wooden door, carpet, or your face in the mirror (or anything with an abstract pattern).

If you do the sandwich method, be sure to just barely hold the flame on top of the cannabis. The heat will cause the DMT underneath to vaporize for you. Take slow and steady hits - it will taste weird, so don't try to take a huge hit very quickly because you will cough.
