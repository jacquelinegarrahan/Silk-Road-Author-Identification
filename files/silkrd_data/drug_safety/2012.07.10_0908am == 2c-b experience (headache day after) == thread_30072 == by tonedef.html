I presume you ingested these caps orally both times? If you snorted them, I might chalk your experience up to lingering sinus pain.

Did you make sure you were well-hydrated and adequately fed on both occasions? I often find psychedelic experiences can get me so distracted I forget to drink or eat and wind up feeling shitty until I put something on my stomach. 

Barring either of those, it is difficult to say.  I know that Mahakala has clean 2c-b available and there are a few other well-regarded 2c-b vendors on here as well.  If you dosed a third time with a known amount of clean 2c-b and woke up again with a headache I'd say it could be an idiosyncratic reaction your body has to 2c-b and you probably should avoid it going forward.

Have you tried other 2c-x's? If so, what kind of reaction did you have?
