Quote from: dirtybiscuitzz718 on May 27, 2013, 12:58 am
Just dont ever take more than one at a time, even if you think its not working..

This. +1

NBOMEs takes sometimes a little time to take hold. Many people re-dose thinking they are not feeling powerful enough effects only to find out the full blow hitting just a little after they have re-dosed (and many times, depending on the invididual, this is enough for them to start panicking, especially with RC in where there has been reports of OD).

One thing you don't want with psychedelics in general but even more with NBOMEs is to go where you didn't plan to in the beginning just for your impatience. This can turn a wonderful experience in a nightmare soon enough, especially if you are not accustomed to the effects of the substance from previous trips.

A thing I always say concerning psychedelics is that there's no hurry and nothing to prove to yourself or others. These substances are to be taken with calm, courage, responsibility and sacredness. Hurrying up or wanting to play bravado serves nothing but to harm yourself in the process.

Approach the experience (in its totality) in a sacred way and you will have a sacred outcome; approach the experience with the intention of killing time and it will be much more probable that time will kill yourself.
