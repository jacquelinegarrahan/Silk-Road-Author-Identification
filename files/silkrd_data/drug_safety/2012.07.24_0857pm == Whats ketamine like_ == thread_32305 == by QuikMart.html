Title: Re: Whats ketamine like?
Post by: FrenchOnionSoup on July 26, 2012, 07:57 am
Start small. A high enough dose will definitely put you in another universe. Pretend that you have never smoked weed or drank alcohol before. Now, with this in mind, imagine taking ten large bong rips of top shelf weed and following that up with ten shots of whiskey. This is what a heavy K trip, or K hole, feels like.

My first experience with K involved a small ~10 mg bump and a quite interesting, yet slightly dysphoric experience which resembled being slightly buzzed and slightly stoned at the same time. It was quite enjoyable. A few days later I became cocky and took a fat ~100 mg line. This was a very bad idea to say the least. Within fifteen minutes of taking that line, I had the worst nausea of my life, my whole room was spinning like a gyroscope, and every time I closed my eyes I had a new dream. It was an interesting experience that I never wish to repeat.

Here are some tips for you:

- Crush the crystals up into a fine powder using a blunt metal object such as a scale weight or the rounded handle of a butter knife. Your schnoz will thank you.
- Start with a small bump. Do not dive into lines unless you love gyroscopes.
- Be aware that nausea is a very common side effect. For this reason, I would avoid eating at least two hours prior to ingestion.
- If you get sucked into a K hole like I did, keep telling yourself that it will be over soon. This should not happen if you take my second tip and start small.

Have fun!
