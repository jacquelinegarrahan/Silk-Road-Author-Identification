Post by: jhancock1984 on April 16, 2013, 07:29 pm
A week should be a good amount of time for physical tolerance to get back to baseline (or close enough it won't be noticed).  Psychological tolerance is another factor though and depends completely on the person.

I've never taken 2c-b before MDMA but I've heard that 2c-b AFTER MDMA is supposed to be really nice.  Generally speaking I like to take my psychs on the comedown of MDMA rather than vice versa.  The Ecstasy afterglow really cuts the anxiety out of the trip for me.  But I think doing it in reverse could be okay too.   As to the dose of MDMA 200mg is pretty high unless your friend has a tolerance.  Another option would be to go 150 with a 50mg booster an hour and half after you drop the first one.  But I think between 2c-b and MDMA a booster dose might be a bit much.

Good luck to you guys, hope you have a blast :)
