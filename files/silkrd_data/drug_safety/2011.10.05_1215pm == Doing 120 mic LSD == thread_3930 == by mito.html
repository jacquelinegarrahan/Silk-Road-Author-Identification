Title: Re: Doing 120 mic LSD
Post by: Spunkaroo on October 06, 2011, 12:00 pm
My first experience was on 180 mics (as best I can tell) and I found it pretty mild. Not that that was a bad thing, I was in control and went for a nice long walk, got caught in the rain and didn't care, a really great day actually. I tried twice that next and spent the entire time in bed unable to speak, just watching the colours and thinking life through. Again, not a bad thing, but there is no way I would be able to socialise in that state.

I've also tried close to, or maybe more than, 1000 mics (7 tabs) and that was one hell of an experience. Lasted about 14 hours before I got to sleep, vomited a couple of times and thought I was going to lose my shit a couple of times. But again it had it's positive elements. I learnt a lot about myself that night, and once I got a few hours in I could settle and enjoy it more. And I will always remember the sunrise that morning :)

So to sum up, the above posts are right on the money, everyone is different, and it's probably best to start small and build up as once you have an idea of how strong your tabs are, and how they'll affect you, you can dial in the experience you want.

One more thing I would say is have music ready, if you start heading to a place you don't want to be I find music can be really calming. Sugary foods are also good for this, or really anything that would normally make you happy will help keep you happy if you have negative thoughts. Other than that, remember that you'll come down, and to enjoy yourself and you should be sweet.

This is all just me, so take it with a grain of salt, but hopefully it helps. Have fun mate :)
