There are several Opiate Conversion Calculators. What I can personally offer is based on Morphine. Heroin is Diacetylmorphine. It is 2 times stronger than Morphine if you happen to come across some good Fire Heroin of at least 70% purity. All that being said, using 10 Milligrams of Morphine as an example, would typically equal 5 Milligrams of Diacetylmorphine. If you can figure out X Milligrams of Oxycodone Equal Y Milligrams of Morphine, then should give you a fairly good Guesstamation.
