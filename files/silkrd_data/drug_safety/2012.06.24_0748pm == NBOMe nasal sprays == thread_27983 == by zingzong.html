Buy Afrin in the metered dose pump spray.  You need the metered dose pump, not the regular nasal spray.  Unscrew the top and pour out the liquid into the sink.  Carefully weigh 20 mg of 25I-NBOMe HCl powder and place it in a 50 mL beaker.  You need a good scale for this, and you need to be careful when working with this substance.  (If you don't know why, you shouldn't be messing with it.  Seriously.)  Mix about 10 mL water with about 10 mL of your favorite vodka.  Now, using a volumetric pipette or syringe, carefully measure 10 mL of this liquid and put it into the beaker.  Using a stainless steel spatula or glass stirring rod, stir the mixture until the 20 mg of powder goes completely into solution.  It should take a few minutes.  Carefully pour the solution into the Afrin bottle and replace the cap.  You now have a nasal spray pump that is approximately 200 micrograms per pump.  A single dose should be 1 to 3 pumps, depending on the individual and where you want to go with it.

The key to this process is that each pump of the Afrin metered dose pump spray delivers 1/10 mL of liquid.  To be safe, I highly recommend you verify this yourself with your Afrin bottle.  (Remember, you need the metered dose pump spray, not the regular squeeze kind.)  A good method to verify the volume of liquid dispensed in each spray is to use the fact that 1 mL of water weighs 1 gram.  Here are the steps:

1) Fill the bottle with about 15 mL of water and prime the pump by pumping it a few time till it sprays.
2) Weigh the bottle and record the weight. (initial weight)
3) Pump the spray 50 times.  Press the pump firmly and completely each time.
4) Weigh the bottle and record the weight (final weight)
5) Calculate as follows:
single pump volume = (final weight - initial weight) / 50

The calculated single pump volume should be very very close to 0.10.  If not, you have to adjust accordingly or go get the kind of Afrin bottle that dispenses 1/10 mL.

Disclaimer: If you decide to try this, you are responsible to verify these calculations.  If you aren't capable of doing this correctly, don't try it.  You do this at your own risk.  If others trust you to do this for them, they trust you at their own risk.
