Title: Wierd 25i-NBOME expierience
Post by: snipeemfl0 on April 28, 2012, 07:13 pm
This isn't a full trip report, just some weird things I noticed on my trip last night.

Dosage: ~1mg of 25i-NBOME from kwiktrip, help under the upper lip for ~20-25 minutes, not swallowing saliva, then swallowing the tab itself (I always do this, I don't know why ...)

I've done around this dosage once before, maybe a little less (~800mcg), but this time weird things happened.

NO, this wasn't a bad trip, just peculiar.

T+30m - I feel really anxious and jittery, almost like a light roll, and it feels like my stomach is churning, but it isn't. I feel weird feelings in my lower abdominal, hard to explain, it wasn't pain, just a weird feeling in select spots that would go away.

T+1h:30m - I felt the trip really starting to hit, but it wasn't a morphing hallucinations like before, it was more so a lack of focus and things I would focus on would move, but not morph, or be "hazy" like before.

T+2h - I start to have the weirdest part of the trip, I have auditory hallucinations. The anxiety has gone down considerably, but still, think I'm hearing footsteps outside my room or people talking, listening to music with headphones helped that, but still slightly heard it, so I knew it wasn't happening. (Unless my house is HAUNTED ?/?, just kidding...)

For the rest of the trip it was quite enjoyable, the whole thing was, just these weird/different things happening was different. The rest of the trip was the same as the last.

I think part of the problem was doing mushrooms last weekend, so visual hallucinations/serotonin/receptors were not at 100%, creating a different trip.


I'm taking a break from phycs for a month or so. Hopefully to make it better next time.


I <3 the SilkRoad :))

The closest I have placed doses of Solaris (25i-NBOMe) together is 8 days between each ride, and there was no discernable difference to me. I have only done it 4 times though: 2mg, 1.5mg ( threshold ), 2mg ( same as 1st ride ), and then 1.75 mg ( mild ), so I think 2mg is the jump off point for my biology ( 43 year old male at 6-1/250 lbs ). Some folks also have the ability to hyper-metabolise PEAs, so be careful and find your 'sweet spot'. Start with the recommended EROWID dosage of 1mg and work up steadily if it doesn't get you there...
