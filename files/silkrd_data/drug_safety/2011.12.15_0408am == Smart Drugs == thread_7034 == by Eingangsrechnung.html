I've taken modafinil when I've needed a bit of a boost for about 8 years now. The longest stretch was 10 weeks in 2008 when I was finishing my MSc.

100mg, twice a day is a good dosage. At doses of 300mg +, rather than boosting my concentration I just felt jumpy and had palpitations, a little like drinking too much coffee, concentration was massively diminished.
My routine was to get up at 5AM, have strong coffee and 100mg modafinil. I could then work without a break from 6AM to 2PM. Then lunch, more coffee and another modafinil, then work until 9PM. I found it good for revision, my brain was like a sponge when I was on it. It's also good for bashing out essays.

You must get adequate sleep though, if you're sleep deprived modafinil will give no enhancement and at best will make you feel normal.
