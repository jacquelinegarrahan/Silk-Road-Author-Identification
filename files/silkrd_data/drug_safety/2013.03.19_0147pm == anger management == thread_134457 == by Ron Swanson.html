I was having a problem with depression and "rage", and yes they were there before my introduction to drugs.

Then I started exercise, and meditation.  It's only an hour ~3 days a week, but makes a night and day difference on how I feel the rest of the week.  I am far less likely to have a spike in attitude.

Dedicate some time to "rage out" at a gym or something.  If you burn it out of you there is less fuel for that later.

If meditation isn't your thing, find something to just be quiet with for like 10-15 min.  I feel kinda silly about this but I have been enjoying a lot of ASMR videos on youtube.  If I find the right one it really puts me out of my head, and give my brain a good reset.

Edit: I forgot gyms cost money.  I've been there.  I made "kettle bells" out of gravel and grocery bags.  There are a crazy amount of kettle bell work outs on youtube as well.

I am pretty bad with having weed around as well.  It got easier to make smarter choices if I told myself I must do "x" and then I can smoke.  (like go work out and then blaze)
