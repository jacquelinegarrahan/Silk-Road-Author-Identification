Title: Fun practices while Tripping/Rolling
Post by: ianfleming on October 02, 2012, 03:37 am
Ok, so I wanted to start a thread about fun suggestions on things to do while tripping or rolling.
Please suggest ideas, activities, or practices and if possible provide the drug that you do this with, or think it would go well with.

Here are some of mine so far.

-Wait till night or dusk and turn off ALL lights in the house. Throw some cool colored glowsticks in the shower (I suggest 2 of the large ones as putting 8 or so small ones might make it easier to slip and bust your ass). Take a cool (or warm shower). If your with someone try caressing them. Watch out for your tempreture if you are using hotwater. This is especially fun to do after taking a long walk or hike and working up a sweat. I find that the ideal drug for this is 2c-i thought Im sure it would be fun with MDMA or probably anything trippy.

-(Another involving water) Be CAREFULL with this one. Fill a bathtub with hot water and salt the ever-loving shit out of it. Try to only put enough water to barley allow you to float (which is much easier with all the salt). Wait for the water to get lukewarm. Then turn out all lights (or blindfold yourself). Eliminate anything that might make noise (expect maybe a trip sitter (which is suggested if your playing with water)). Now sit and float in your own personal sensory deprivation chamber. It's QUITE interesting to see where your mind will go when you remove most external stimuli. BTW having someone else to talk to you and watch over you dosent diminish the sensory deprivation effect much and I think it's worth it to not drown to death. This works GREAT with 2c-E

-Lastly, when rolling try getting a vicks inhaler (the ones with L-meth in them). These are fun alone but are increased 10 fold when you have someone take a hit, hold it in their mouth and slowly blow it out across your face (the eyes feel great). Also fun to kiss someone after they've taken a hit.
