Title: Re: MDMA crystal/powder expiration?
Post by: Limetless on June 28, 2012, 01:30 am
Just to expand on this the Phenethylamine chemical class and subsequently the Amphetamine chemical class in the vast majority are very stable compounds. They pretty much last forever so long as they are kept away from light and in a sealed bag. Even if you leave them out in the air and they oxidize they last ages. There are the rare exceptions but on the whole they are very stable. The ones you have to worry about are Tryptamines (the LAD compounds being the obvious example) and Tropes (Cocaine takes in moisture and it knackers it).
