Title: Re: Ideas for Safe 3 day Festival Drug Lineup
Post by: AnimusVox on April 04, 2013, 08:49 pm
The rise in 2C-B dosage is due to the cross tolerance experienced from taking the 4-AcO-DMT. :) While 4-AcO-DMT is a tryptamine and 2C-B a phenethylamine, you will still notice a slight cross tolerance and it's always good to plan your dosage accordingly. Also, you'll be taking LSD the day before which also exerts a slight cross tolerance on both 2C-B and 4-AcO-DMT, so that's another reason I advise such dosages. Much love! :)
