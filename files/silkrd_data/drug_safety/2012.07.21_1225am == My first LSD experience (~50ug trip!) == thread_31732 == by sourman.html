Title: My first LSD experience (~50ug trip!)
Post by: sourman on July 21, 2012, 12:25 am
For my first time, we went to a place out in the woods that we've only been to a few times. We had a great day hiking, fishing, exploring, etc. and at the end of the night, my friend and I split a single tab down the middle and popped the cid as we sat around a bonfire with a bunch of other people. Keep in mind that this was supposedly a 100ug tab, from momiji right here on SR. We both tripped balls for around 10-12 hours from those halves.

The tabs were miniscule, had no taste, and did not take effect instantly, so I doubt it was a RC. I remember seeing the embers at the bottom of the fire begin to form shapes and figures. At this point, it felt like I "smoked 10,000 joints", to quote another trip report, but it did not feel like I took anything new. Before I knew it, I was staring into the base of that fire, checking out the skulls and endless caves to hell as the stars above me would shoot apart and then contract across the night sky. My briefcase would "feel" small, and then "grow" to normal size despite it looking the same to me visually. Everything was profound and felt new, like I was a little kid again. Everything was bright, kind of like my internal "digital vibrance" and highlights settings were set higher. Music was intensified in a way similar to cannabis, but the experience was way stronger and more profound. We began making fun of new rap lyrics and then sitting around solemnly listening to old Biggie tracks while passing around a spliff. Did I mention we smoked like 3 blunts of AAA medical bud plus honey oil? I also drank the equivalent of a two cups of vodka. Perhaps that had something to do with the intensity of the this trip?

After we got back to our room, shit really started getting freaky. Paintings would melt and change into other paintings, reflections in the mirror looked like a window to another time and place. Then we got to talking about the government and how it brainwashed the older generations into thinking LSD and weed were these terrible substances. After getting sad and freaking out for a bit, we regained our composure and continued to avoid the introspective parts of the trip. At this point, I noticed a purple tint to some things in my visual field that were out of focus, and all those songs with lyrics referencing a "purple haze" started to make sense. I then became conscious of the roller coaster type feeling in my gut that was ongoing for the past 6-8 hours. It was like smoking 7g of bud to the face as your first time, then sitting backwards in a reclining chair with your eyes closed. Remember that face tingling and falling backwards in a roller coaster feeling you felt the first time you got high? That's what it felt like, except it didn't stop and I didn't have to lay back or close me eyes. My friend, who is more experienced with psychedelics but never took LSD, said the roller coaster thing reminded him of 2 C-I. He was actually freaking out more than I was at this point.

Although the effects wore off about 12 hours after administration, we both got flashbacks for the next day or two. I took a tramadol the following evening and it almost brought the trip back, which makes sense given the seratogenic effects of both drugs. Just don't ever take tramadol and LSD in the same 24 hour period. After 3 days, all effects were completely gone. I am still the same person as before, with no changes to my personality or sanity. No HPPD either. I learned a few things and got to experience some shit I never felt before, but it did not change my life at all. Then again, despite the fact that I personally feel as if I tripped balls, this was still a tiny dose.

But yeah, that's about all I remember of that night. As awesome and weird as the trip was, it still felt oddly familiar. Perhaps that's why I don't feel any different. Remember when you were young and everything was still new and impressive; back when you'd notice scary things in your room at night while looking at shadows and objects in the dark? It was like returning to an even more exciting rendition of those days with the knowledge and maturity of my adult self. I am definitely going to do more LSD. It's like weed when you first start smoking, except far more intense and profound. And that was just half of a 100ug tab.
