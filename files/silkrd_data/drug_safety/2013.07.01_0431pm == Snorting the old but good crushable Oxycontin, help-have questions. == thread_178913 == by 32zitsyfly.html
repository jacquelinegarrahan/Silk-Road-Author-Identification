Post by: ralph123 on July 01, 2013, 05:08 pm
well an OC 20 has a coating on it so 1st thing I would do is put it in my mouth for a few seconds then take it out and use a peice of cloth, sometimes I use my shirt that I'm wearing and wipe the coating off the pill. After I get it clean, it should look white or an off white color. Crush it the best way you can. I like to use  a make shift grinder made from a radiator hose clamp bought new at an auto parts place or walmart. I just unscrew the screw and take some tin snips and cut the screw part off then use a hammer and beat the rest of it straight against a hard surface maybe a cinder block or some concrete. An anvil is idea. Wash it off and make sure its clean and dry. Cut it to the desired length. Now you got a new pill grinder

Get you a flat plate and grab the cleaned OC 20 with your thumb and your index finger and rub it against the proper side of your grinder. You will know which side to use because one side does not do to good but the other side will grind the pill into a fine powder depending on how much pressure you apply. Hold the make shift grinder firm with your other hand

Ultimately you can just use an old debit card and crush your OC but always remove the coating. I think the 10mg OC's have no coating but the rest do. OC's don't really burn that bad but if you snort the coating it may cause a headache
