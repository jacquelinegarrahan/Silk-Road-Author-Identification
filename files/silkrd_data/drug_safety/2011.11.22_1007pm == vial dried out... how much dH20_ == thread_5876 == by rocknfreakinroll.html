Title: Re: vial dried out... how much dH20?
Post by: tuck on November 25, 2011, 02:15 am
a drop of water does not have a standard volume. The size of a drop depends on both the material and geometry of the opening that the water is coming out of. That said, drops formed from the same dropper will have a fairly consistent volume. For example, the volume of a drop of water from a 30 ga stainless steel needle will have a different volume than ones from a glass eye dropper, but drops made from either will be pretty consistent.

My suggestion is that you use the same type of container to figure out your dilution as you're going to use to actually dose with.
