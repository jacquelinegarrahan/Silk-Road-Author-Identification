Title: Why I will never do nBOME's again....
Post by: jokerman2000 on June 13, 2013, 10:14 am
I am an avid user of psychedelics. It is hard for me to think of a drug that I have yet to try.

I started using NBOME's about a two years because of the lack of LSD where I am situated. I was lucky to find some vendors selling NBOME's on a regular website (biggest mistake of my life, thankfully I'm still here today and without any issues).

So I had used nbomes on plenty of occasion, I was mainly using 25i but I had tried all of them. I had taken up to 5mg of 25i in one night without having any negative side effects.

One day I was attending a rave and I decided I would take a 1000ug blotter of 25i... Everything was great, I was having a great time. The lights and everything was beautiful. This was some real potent 25i as all I needed was one blotter for the entire night. I can remember everything perfectly, I was really enjoying myself. It was near the end of the night and I had taken nothing else except 200mg of MDMA. Me and some friends went over to a stage to see Nero.

This is when the issues started. I started getting this feeling in my head and eyes where both my eyes felt like they were getting pulled to the left and straight back. I had never felt this before, it wasn't the normal eye wiggles you get on mdma, it was something different. I almost had the feeling of just throwing myself to the ground cause it was a terrifying experience. The only way I could control it was cover my eyes and look straight down to the ground. I was like that for about 20-30 minutes and finally decided it was time to leave. It was the last set of the night so I head to my car. Every several minutes I would get the same feeling, it almost felt like I wanted to seize up. I had never had any issues with seizures in 26 years of my life.

I continued to get this feeling for several hours till I was able to fall asleep.

To this day I have yet to use nbomes, I will strictly use LSD but I really love 2c-i and thats what surprises me because I can take large doses of 2c-i and have never felt that way.

Be careful with your drugs especially research chems.
Title: Re: Why I will never do nBOME's again....
Post by: mrmdma on June 13, 2013, 10:24 am
NBOMes cause severe vasoconstriction in some users, including me. Mixing the drug with an phenethylamine-type drug is just asking for problems. Some might be just ok doing so, but keep in mind that you'll have two drugs restricting fluid and blood flow then. Eyes have small veins and are very, very fucking sensitive to changes in the volume of veins. On my first NBOMe trip (The last one I'll possible ever do), my right eye was left with this odd ball feeling for a couple of days after. It felt like someone had showed a tooth pick into the eye. I had also experienced severe vasoconstriction on the trip (was cold and my feet were a bit swollen up) so I can't really think of a better explanation than that NBOMes can fuck up your eyes by restricting fluid exchange or something.
