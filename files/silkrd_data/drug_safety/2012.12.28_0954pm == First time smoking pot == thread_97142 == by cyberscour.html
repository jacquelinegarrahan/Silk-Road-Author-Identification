Title: Re: First time smoking pot
Post by: Jopular on December 29, 2012, 12:49 am
Be careful not to whitey. Avoid alcohol. Suck mints. Drink a cup of tea.

Use a mild quality cigarette instead of rolling baccy.

Use mostly weed cut up fine with scissors, and just a little baccy evenly spread to help it burn. (important for health if you are to hold the smoke in for a long time).

Use a good roach. Tight spiral of thin non glossy card is good. People often get neater results rolling with the roach in but takes practice to mach the size of the roach with the thickness of the joint at that end. Place the roach at the left side if you are right handed (or try the other way if you find it easier).

after rolling, tap a little but not too tight, poke the end down and smoke.

If  not rolled tight enough you will get the skin burning unevenly and waste weed.
If too tight and / or the weed is damp it will not burn (especially if pure).
