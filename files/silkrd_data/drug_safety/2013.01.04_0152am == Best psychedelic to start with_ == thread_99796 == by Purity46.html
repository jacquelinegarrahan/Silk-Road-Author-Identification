Ecstasy is also a good intro into psychedelics... but if you want to know the truth, the best psychedelics out there are LSD and 2ci.  LSD is the best drug out there though, and I've sampled a lot of them.  When you do decide to do a psychedelic, here's a list of things you'll want to consider doing.   :)

If you did even 1/4 of the things on here, you'd have the best trip ever.   ;D ;D

8.  Watching the Winamp Visualizer MilkDrop  This is the best best best hands down visualizer ever!  You havent seen a visualizer until you've seen this on a psychedelic.  Trust me on this one!!!
9. Eating fruit and Vegetables...actually taste pretty good.  There's something very primal and rewarding about biting into a strawberry while tripping.  Try Blackberries, Green Beans…Fresh juicy things taste good.   Meats and Cheeses usually don’t taste so good.
10.  Go Paintballing on Acid...I have yet to try this but I hear it's crazy.  Might be a little too crazy.
11.  Stare in the Mirror, Stare into your eyes in one of those super magnified mirrors...watching them dilate is nuts.
12.  Listening to music on expensive studio headphones.  Sennheiser or Bose or something that wraps around your entire ear.  Trust me, this is how music was meant to be listened to.
13.  Have sexual intercourse (Punch anyone in the face who says otherwise...they're lying to you.  Sex on Ex or Psychedelics is the best and is so worth it.)
14.  Writing on each other or painting on each other.

20. 21.  Throwing Glow Sticks, buy the biggest ones you can get.

26.  Go to a water park.  (I don't recommend this to anyone except super experienced trippers)
26. Get into a hot tub or bath tub with Food Coloring and drop drops into the water and watch the dye move through the water.  (This may stain your skin for a couple days if you put a lot of color in.)
27. Hook up a fog machine in your room and let that thing go off for a while.  Trippy lights or a strobe will help this.  *Fog is just cool as fuck while tripping, but be sure to unplug any fire alarms, as they will be set off.
28.  Ask a sober sitter to drive you around town for a little bit.  Or get a Taxi (Do not drive while tripping, it never ends well)
29. Vicks Inhalers are fun on ecstasy...not too sure about other psychedelics.  I remember blowing some in my eyes sober once and it hurt so badly.  It was not at all pleasant like when you're rolling.
30.  Buy some Poi Balls off of a website.  They are like 10$ each or so...totally worth it.  They are likely LED but swinging them around is fun and makes for amazing light shows.  Also hula hooping is fun.
31.  Go to a planetarium or laser show (Pink Floyd Laser Light show is ideal)  Check your local IMAX or Mosi Science Theater

Post by: Foxy on January 05, 2013, 01:35 am
I would go for the lowest dose of it and you can always take more if you are liking it.

Salvia was my first psychedelic and I don't really recommend it... It scared me, as I took a huge hit of the strongest extract there is and seconds later I completely forgot what was going on as the scenery melted into a cartoon world. Fortunately Salvia is very short lasting and a couple minutes later I was back to normal, yet not the same as I was before, as I had just witnessed the most extreme psychedelic visuals I ever had. I have yet to try DMT to compare, but after doing Salvia a second time with similar results, I would believe DMT to be a nicer drug.

I recommend cubensis shrooms. Not Aminita (very scary trips unless experienced.. and even then...), or any really potent mushroom which will give you a lot of hallucinations. Cubensis keeps you totally sane and you wont ever loose control. You know exactly what is going on while experiencing the effects of your dilated pupils (light blurs etc), closed eye visuals, and clearer thoughts and understanding. People can and do see hallucinations on cubensis but not everyone does, it depends on the setting (dim lights/dusk helps) and the dosage/your tolerance to it.

Trip safe, hope you have fun!
