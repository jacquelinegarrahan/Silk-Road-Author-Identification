Title: Re: Redosing LSD?
Post by: jhancock1984 on September 24, 2013, 06:20 pm
Tolerance sets in pretty quickly, builds until you come down, and dissipates over two weeks.  If you're going to redose you'll want to do it within the first hour or so.  After that point you will run into diminishing returns.  Dosing the day after you tripped is generally a waste of acid.  Spacing it out every other day will be fine for a couple of days but you won't trip as hard as the first day without increasing your dose.  All tolerance is cumulative.
Title: Re: Redosing LSD?

In my experience redosing may reset the duration of a trip(have had 20+ hour trips through redosing) but it will not increase the effect.

You want to wait 2 weeks since your last dose and then take the full amount you want to do all at once.
