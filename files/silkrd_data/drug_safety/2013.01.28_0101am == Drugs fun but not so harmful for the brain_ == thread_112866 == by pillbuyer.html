You shouldn't feel this way with LSD. The reason you feel drained the following day is probably because you don't eat enough when tripping (LSD changes your appetite, plus eating can upset the stomach), you aren't staying properly hydrated, you are staying up to late, and/or you expend more energy when tripping than you normally do. LSD is not harmful to the brain.

My suggestion is to get in shape so that LSD doesn't make you feel drained. When I was out of shape, LSD made me feel drained too....

You're going to be hard pressed to find a drug that makes you feel good without having a comedown of some sort (just kind of the name of the game when rebalancing whatever part of your body/brain you fucked with). That said, proper supplementing and nutrition intake, both before, during, and after can greatly reduce, and sometimes eliminate, detrimental effects. 

Others have mentioned 5-HTP for MDMA but for pretty much any stimulant I have a regimen of supplements I take beforehand--Vitamin C, generic multivitamin, chelated magnesium, sometimes another antioxidant supplement and sometimes racetam(s) [although I'm not sure what protective qualities they have, if any, or if they're commonly accepted for this purpose yet].

 I also drink a lot of juices rich in nutrients throughout since I know I will only be able to force sparce amounts of food into me. Also, try eating fruit if you have an opportunity during your use. I find fruits to be fairly easy to eat and provide additional hydration and nutrients and will help settle your stomach if it's been empty for a long time.

Hope some of this helps.

Multiple usage of MDMA can cause damage to the dopamine reuptake centers.  Your brain floods with serotonin, so much so, that the serotonin finds its way into the dopamine reuptake centers and when it goes through, the chemical breakdown is similar to bleach in your brain - fries it out.  Best thing to do (most doctors agree that permanent / long-term damage can be nullified) if you take some sort of anti-depressant 6-8 hours after your last dosage of MDMA.  It may not help for the post-roll physical feelings, but it will definitely help your roll in the future sessions and reduce the chances of permanent brain damage.
