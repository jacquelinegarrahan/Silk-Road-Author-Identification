Post by: 00OOIlI00lO1O0 on July 11, 2012, 02:23 am
Well, I generally don't recommend going down the road of opiates, especially if unintended. Personally, I'd just dispose of an opiate if I received it by mistake, but I'll get off my high horse and offer some practical advice:

Oxycodone has a half-life of 3-5 hours. Methadone has a half-life of 15-60 hours. In other words, you're at great risk of the methadone accumulating if you take doses several times in one day. Be sure to consider this if you have work/school/driving or other physically/mentally-demanding things to do even the next day. The same goes for the side effects.
