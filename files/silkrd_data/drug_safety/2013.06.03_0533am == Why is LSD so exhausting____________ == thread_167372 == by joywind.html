Title: Re: Why is LSD so exhausting????????????
Post by: strangeman on June 12, 2013, 12:50 pm
I used to be utterly exhausted the day after an acid trip. The morning after my first real trip, I ate breakfast straight out of the fridge, laying on the floor, and then stayed there for about an hour before I dragged my ass to the couch for a nap. The physical exhaustion wasn't as bad as the mental fatigue. I could barely think and had absolutely no patience for other people. This effect was constant regardless of when I dosed, how I dosed, whether I smoked weed or not, what I did on the trip or even the purity of the acid.

At some point, though, the fatigue effect stopped. I'm not exactly "refreshed" the day after, but I feel fairly normal. A little spacey, but functional. I don't really know what caused the change. Possibly just experience. The same batch of L that had me floored the next day now leaves me feeling just fine. A few variables have changed, though. I was already eating  normally during my trips, but now I make sure to have a half-gallon of orange juice and some gatorade on hand, and I pop a couple of 5-htp before I go to bed. I also fast for about 8 hours before I take the acid now, which seems to cut down on a lot of the come-up side effects for me (that and a couple of beers).
