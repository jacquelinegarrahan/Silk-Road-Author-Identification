Title: Re: Laying blotters myself with 2c-b - anyone experienced?
Post by: wowzers on April 16, 2012, 10:13 pm
I posted a tutorial on this but I can't find it.

I used standard pre-perforated acid size blotter. 2C-B is soluble in alcohol but to lay blotter you need to use the minimum volume of liquid, a little water improves the solubility significantly.
1200mg 2C-B.HBr dissolved in warm 70%ipa/ water up to 12mL volume (100mg/ mL), then using 10uL micropipette add 1 drop to each hit, dry under a 60W lamp, then repeat on the reverse of the sheet, then once more, giving a total loading of 3mg/ hit or 12mg per square. This seems to be the maximum loading for blotter, and higher and powder forms on the surface of the blotter.

Gammagoblin did some 2C-B blotter at 18mg/ square but the hits were larger than standard blotter.

I've tried that method but there's no way of telling what the dose of each hit is. There always seems to be residue left behind, the blotter tends to sag in the middle of the solution and the hits on the outer few cm of the paper always seem to be lower dosed.
