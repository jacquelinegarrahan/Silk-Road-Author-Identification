Title: Re: what to take.. what to take..
Post by: bwompah on April 17, 2012, 05:31 pm
I'd go with MDMA as well. I just think there's a better chance of everyone having a good time if you're only taking small doses of MDMA versus any kind of hallucinogen. Don't get me wrong--shrooms are great--but since most of your friends on this trip are complete newbies to all types of drugs, I wouldn't necessarily trust everything to go entirely smoothly. I definitely wouldn't want to be out in the woods somewhere and have one of my friends freak out or need a babysitter all night or not have any benzos on hand to calm someone down or not have a familiar, comfortable place to take someone if they need it, etc...there are just too many variables. Since it sounds like everyone is going to be trying something new that night, be it weed or the shrooms/MDMA, I'd want to minimize risks as much as possible while still having a good time.

The other thing is that MDMA will almost certainly make you more social, whereas shrooms may make you want to just go off by yourself for a while. Truthfully, I've had times on high doses of pure MDMA that have made me completely blissed out to the point where I couldn't really talk or move. I could only lay there and let the waves of euphoria wash over me, but I definitely wasn't complaining. Anything ~100mg (or even less) of good MDMA should still make you feel really, really fucking good, but you'll still want to interact with your friends.

Also, if you have easy access to some benzos, I'd bring a few along for the trip. I try to keep some on hand no matter what drugs people are doing, especially if it's their first time, and even more so if I'm the one exposing people to a new substance. You never know what kind of reaction somebody is going to have and it's nice to be able to immediately calm someone down if necessary. This is more important if you're tripping or something, but just good practice in general, I think. If nothing else, it always makes the entire experience a little more relaxing to know there's an easy "out" if needed.

Good luck and I hope you have a great time!

P.S. You should still take shrooms in the woods with a few friends a different time. It will be awesome--just probably better suited for a smaller crowd/maybe during the day.  :)
