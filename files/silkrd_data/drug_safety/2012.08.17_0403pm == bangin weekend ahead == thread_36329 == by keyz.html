Title: Re: bangin weekend ahead
Post by: wolfstein on August 17, 2012, 08:09 pm
It's downright stupid to take MDMA on three consecutive days. You could do that with just about any other drug, but not MDMA. The stuff releases massive amounts of serotonin in the brain, and the brain needs time to restock and refuel your depleted serotonin levels.

Think of it this way: you're running your car's motor in a high speed race on less and less oil and you're going to ruin the engine.

Don't get me wrong. MDMA is wonderful and I love the stuff. It's just important that you know how it effects your body. Ideally you should give yourself at least a week between each roll.
