Title: Re: LSD 300 mcg
Post by: busta999 on November 01, 2012, 11:41 am
One will give you a nice trip - two or more will probably give you a profound experience.

If you take more than one make sure your settings are pleasant and safe. LSD is not a "party drug". Bad trips can get pretty bad and chances of having one are way higher if you are with strangers or in unfamiliar surroundings.

In a safe place with experiemced trippers around I trust I would not worry about taking a high dose. If you're alone or the settings are not fully under your control take a low dose or postpone the trip until you can arrange it better.
