MDMA - Body roll much more present, euphoric, tactile, should be quite calm and will make you melt, less neurotoxic
MDA - More speedy, body roll less present, less intense euphoria/tactility, more psychedelic than MDMA, more neurotoxic, great to have sex on
Methylone - Like a toned down version of MDMA, light body roll, short duration but can be topped up over and over
