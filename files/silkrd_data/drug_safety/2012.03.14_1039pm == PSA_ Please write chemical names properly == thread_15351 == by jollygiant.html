Title: PSA: Please write chemical names properly
Post by: jollygiant on March 14, 2012, 10:39 pm
A growing trend, people abbreviating or misconstruing chemical names.  This is a dangerous habit and would be good if ya'll can make an effort to write them properly.  It's scary that vendors will mess that up sometimes, even the short ones.  I understand we're all human and make mistakes but please try and make a conscious effort.

2c-i

not

2ci
or
2-ci

The farther away from the original chemical name we get, the more dangerous the information researched on the chemical can get.

Please help make the scene safer through awareness and respect.
