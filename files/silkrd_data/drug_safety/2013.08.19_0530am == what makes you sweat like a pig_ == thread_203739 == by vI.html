Title: Re: what makes you sweat like a pig?
Post by: Ben on August 20, 2013, 01:42 am
Once suspect substance would be atropine. In the nehterlands there have been several incidents of cocaine cut  with atropine, which would result in the effects you describe to some degree.

Atropine will actually inhibit sweating to some point, but you may feel the sensation of overheating and break into sweat at some point beyond an initial small dose. If you experience this you need to be very weary as in many cases relatively small doses of atropine (about half a gram) can be fatal.
