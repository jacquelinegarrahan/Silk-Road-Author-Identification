Sounds like you have the flame to close. Now, i normally develop a sore throat after smoking H in general, but i have notice i will develop a cough if my flame is too close. Sometimes leading to sharp chest pain, extreme tightness, and this can be quite alarming if you haven't experienced it before.


I would suggest practicing your technique, using a smaller flame, and taking smaller hits, and taking breaks between hits. Remember, take your time. It only takes one bad hit to ruin the night.

Oh, one last thing. Make sure you're buying brand name "Heavy duty" foil. Reynolds wrap is the go to brand. Use new pieces as often as possible also since using the same piece, you'll eventually get holes which will burn your dope leading to a bad hit.
