Title: Re: Upgrading from DXM
Post by: psychedelia on June 01, 2012, 05:25 pm
I'd go with LSD or mushrooms.  They are physically the safest of the drugs you listed, and also provide the best psychedelic experience, in my opinion. DMT is great but it may be too intense if you've never experienced psychedelics before. MDMA and ketamine are worth trying but there is some evidence, although it has not been conclusively proven, that they damage the brain.

As for preparation, just make sure you start with a small dosage, and make sure you are in a good mood, and in a comfortable and safe environment.
