Post by: Love on June 17, 2013, 03:58 pm
Hey guys we're a new vendor of 2C-E in the UK. Don't mean to shill for myself but I've done a few of the 2C's although mostly 2C-B and 2C-E.

As far as I know the main risk that 2C-E previously carried was that there were a few places selling Bromo dragonfly as 2C-E, BromoDF is a lot strong if I remember rightly.

Dose wise the erowid.org charts are pretty much bang on. Snorted doses of 2C-E are MUCH smaller than oral doses and hurt for about 10-15 minutes. The advantage of snorting is that the trip is shorter at about 6hrs and come up is much quicker. This is pretty much universal for most drugs but you really notice the difference with 2C-E (most of this applies for other 2Cs as well)

For dosing I recommend volumetric dosing as the powder is quite fluffy and has a habit of sticking to items you use so for example 100mg into 100ml so you can dose by the ml (orally or snorted).

I personally think whoever originally said 2C-E is the LSD of the 2C-xs is correct.
