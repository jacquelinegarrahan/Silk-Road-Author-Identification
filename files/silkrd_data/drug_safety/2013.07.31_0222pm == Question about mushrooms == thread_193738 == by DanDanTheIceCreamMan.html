Title: Re: Question about mushrooms
Post by: ukshroomsmail on July 31, 2013, 08:47 pm
hi,

firstly are they dried or fresh?

as HCeline said the aborts are fine, and super strong! thats why its better to dry and grind the shrooms up so the dosage is consistant in my opinion.

unless there is a problem with the grow the white fuzz generally comes from the last flush when the mycelium is getting old and tends to grow up the stems, its not very nice, i dont sell it, but it wont do you any harm. the weight of the fuzz will be very small though so you wont have lost much product because of it.

the stringy stuff, or roots, is probably where the shrooms have been picked not quite so carefully as they could have been, its likely just the surface of the mycelium around where the shroom was growing. again it wont do you any harm, but i wouldn't leave it on mine. apart from making your shrooms look unappealing it damages the surface of the mycelium unnecessarily and it will likely have a detrimental effect on recovery time and future flushes.

the grey fuzz up the middle of the stem is not uncommon and isnt really anything to worry about. are they slightly wrinkly looking?

so yeah, overall they're fine to eat, just not so pretty maybe.

thanks

UKshrooms
