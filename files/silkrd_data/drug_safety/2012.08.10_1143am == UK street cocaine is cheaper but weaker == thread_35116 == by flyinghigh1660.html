I snort all my "coke" for the night, go home and struggle to sleep.

Now on to real coke, like the 80%+ stuff on SR:

I take a tiny line, maybe 60mg-120mg, it doesn't burn at all, almost like sniffing air. After about 2-4 minutes, I start to feel warm... and happy... real fucking happy.

I feel fucking great, at this moment I realize exactly why cocaine got so popular in the world.

I feel like Scarface! (LOL) Huge motivation, chatty, not a problem in the world. A real nice dopamigenic euphoria.

The high lasts about an hour and a half and if I decide that's all I want to snort I can usually say no to anymore - it isn't half as fiendish as the street crap.

Occasionally I'll feel a little dodgy but nothing major. 

At least that's my take on the drug that can be so beautiful when not abused or fucked with.
