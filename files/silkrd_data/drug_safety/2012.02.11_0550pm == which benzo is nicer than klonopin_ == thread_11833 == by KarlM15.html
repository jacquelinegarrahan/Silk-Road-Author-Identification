Title: Re: which benzo is nicer than klonopin?
Post by: sourman on February 12, 2012, 04:07 pm
Here's my 2 cents:

Xanax (alprazolam) - Hits the hardest and the quickest, short half-life, feels like being drunk but without the effects of alcohol ingestion. Good for insomnia and panic attacks, especially if you need quick relief that doesn't last into the next day. I've taken tons of this stuff and never really felt any euphoria, although it does enhance weed highs and the like. Grapefruit juice enhances the effects of Xanax for me, as well as making it last longer.

K-pins (clonazepam) - Takes a while to kick in, even if you try to take it sublingually. Not as good as Xanax for recreational use, but feels great when you need it for actual anxiety. You can still function more or less normally on this stuff, and it's great for helping rough sleepers actually sleep still throughout the night. The half-life sucks though, especially if you take it "once in a while" only to realize it's been in your system for weeks. I actually felt some mild withdrawals that way! Grapefruit juice didn't really have much effect when I tried it with k-pins, not that it's needed anyway lol.

Ativan (lorazepam) - Feels a lot like Xanax to me, except with more amnesia and even more sedation. Great for insomnia!

I never tried Valium, although it's rumored around here to be the most euphoric benzo. My friends hate it, but that's because they rip you off for it in this area.
