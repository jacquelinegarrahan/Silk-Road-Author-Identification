Title: Re: Combining fentanyl with other drugs same for ghb
Post by: microRNA on November 03, 2012, 12:03 am
GHB most certainly is not a barbituate, its most similar to the neurotransmitter GABA

barbs have a specific chemical backbone and gamma hydroxybutyrate has a different structure

GHB effects GABA-B receptor, while alcohol and benzos effect GabaA receptor

also, GHB specifically targets its own receptor

therefore the effects are potentiated when you mix two drugs that alter the activity of the same transmitter

opiates are dangerous to mix because they too impact GABAergic transmission, just at the mu receptor increasing gaba which then effects the gabaA receptor...

 so basically it causes much greater respiratory depression and can result in death if mixed with alcohol or opiates - which is why its not recommended...
