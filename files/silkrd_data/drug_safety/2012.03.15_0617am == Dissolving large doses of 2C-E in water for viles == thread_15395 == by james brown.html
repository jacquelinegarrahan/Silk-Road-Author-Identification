Title: Re: Dissolving large doses of 2C-E in water for viles
Post by: Tagore on March 15, 2012, 12:34 pm
Would definitely recommend a small amount of Everclear instead of water. If you use water, use distilled.

2c-e is highly soluble in any case. If you just want to make 1-hit vials, just go for 1ml/4mg or so... That would give you a 800mL of solution from 3 grams though. It's a good balance between dosing accuracy (+/- 4mg with a mL syringe), and keeping the amount of solvent to a minimum.

If you want more exact doses, you can go for 1ml/mg but that would be a hell of a lot of solution; if you want smaller vials, you can go to 1ml/10mg or more... Just personal preference depending on how concentrated and how closely you want to be able to measure the doses; just make sure you remember exactly which ratio you used! Write it down if necessary!

I used to mix large batches like this, that way fairly exact doses could be measured out with a mL syringe... Then into some OJ for breakfast.

P.S. 12mg vials seems a bit low to me.- my sweet spot was between 15-20mg, but sometimes I liked up to 25-30mg... Just something to consider when you decide how saturated to mix your solution... In general, go for the most saturated solution you can ACCURATELY measure. ;)
