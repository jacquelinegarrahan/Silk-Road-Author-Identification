Title: Re: 2cb can be administered all ways
Post by: wowzers on April 19, 2012, 09:18 am
Hey tittytwister, I've PM'd you regarding the other part of your question but regarding the effects:

Was this your first 2C-B experience? Why did you decide to take it rectally? Next time I'd recommend 18mg orally.
Underdosage does feel exactly as you described, I know how disappointing it is. Mis-fires on psychedelics tend to make me feel slightly goosebumpy & I get chills & feel like I've had a strong cup of coffee. Then they disappear and that's the end of the 'trip'.  :(

I have found 2C-B to be a finicky drug. Sometimes 16mg will barely shift me off baseline, whereas other times I'll get a full high powered trip. Sometimes I start tripping fully within 35 minutes but once it took 2 hours to come up. My trips & setting are pretty controlled (late evening trip, light meal 5 hours before, usually with the same people in similar setting, maximum of 3-4 beers over the course of the evening) but I still find 2C-B to be very variable.

Good luck with your next experiment, please let us know if it was more successful  :D

***edit- The vendor who you bought your 2C-B from supplies the hydrobromide salt which is about 14% less potent than the hydrochloride. I certainly wouldn't recommend jumping from 14mg to 20/25. The dose-response curve for 2C-B is very non-linear. Try 18mg orally, then increase it incrementally by 2mg each time.
