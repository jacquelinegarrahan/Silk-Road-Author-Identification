Title: Re: MDMA Dosage and the Loss of Magic
Post by: entreterra on May 18, 2013, 01:34 pm
First, what is your source for mdma? If you are getting it locally there is a good chance that the quality is not consistent. It could be cut, or it may be another chemical altogether much of the time.

Also, how long are you waiting between rolls? You need to wait at minimum 4 weeks, but the best would be to wait 3 months. 6 months would be even better!

If by your third roll you are already experiencing greatly diminshed effects with 200+, I am almost certain the quality of what you're taking is very low and/or you are waiting only a few days between rolls. It's likely you are waiting several days to several weeks between roll, so my guess is the quality of your mdma is sub-par. Do you have a test kit? You've got to have one if you're buying locally and it would be a very good idea to have one if you're getting mdma off the road (though it's more likely that reviews and forum posts will inform you of mdma quality from the road; still a good idea to have one though).

Keep in mind that most users do not experience the same intensity and awe of their first actual roll in any subsequent experiences. Still though, if you are taking high quality mdma like what you can find on the road in amounts at 200+mg, you should still have an incredibly strong and satisfying roll. I sense there are other variables involved. I don't think you would have lost the magic by your third roll. It's possible, but I've never heard that before.

-Make sure you are definitely taking quality mdma.
-Don't expect your rolls from now on to be as good as your first roll. (you will be surprised however, as some later experiences may surprise you and produce effects that comparable to your first time. Many of my friends who've taken several month breaks and taken very pure mdma have stated that it felt like their first time.)
-Change up your setting. The difference of a roll in the dead of winter when you're huddled in your dwelling trying to stay warm whilst rolling vs. a warm summer night listening to music at a festival can be a night and day difference. mdma can produce a beautifully variable experience in different settings. On that note too, cold + mdma do not mix imo. cool-warm is best. Hot is not good as studies have shown an elevated body temperature while rolling greatly increases the risk of neurotoxicity.
-Take proper supplements: alpha-lipoic acid, vitamin c., vitamin e., various other antioxidants (green tea, grape seed extract etc.), magnesium for jaw-clenching, and eat well several days prior and after the experience).
-Finally, which is the point I'm at now. If you really can't seem to get back the effects you are seeking from mdma, try to change your perspective a bit. Don't allow yourself to be underwhelmed. Try to see that the difference of effects you may be experiencing are just the changing relationship you have with the molecule. It is the familiarity you are gaining. Nothing can stay new forever. Take long breaks and instead of yearning for the original incredible newness, just try to appreciate the experience for what it brings to you. Even if it may not be the same as it was, it is still providing you with something totally unique and beautiful.

best of luck to you!
