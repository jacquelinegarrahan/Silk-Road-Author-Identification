Title: Re: calling benzo experts.
Post by: Ben on January 26, 2013, 01:56 am
4 mg is quite a bit. If you have no tolerance that should knock you out within the hour for most people. Bit even with a pretty large tolerance, you'd definitely be able to tell if you took 4 mg alprazolam or not.

As for the taste of it: xanax is definitely bitter, very noticable if you chew down a tablet. Inert compounds may give a chalky/dry feeling even with legitimate tablets, but the bitter flavor is hard to miss. If a tablet is supposed to be 1 mg xanax and its not bitter when you chew it down, there is something going on there. Perhaps it contains some substance to mask the bitter flavor, but unless that was stated explicitly (comparable to 'banana clonopin') i'd assume there is something wrong.
