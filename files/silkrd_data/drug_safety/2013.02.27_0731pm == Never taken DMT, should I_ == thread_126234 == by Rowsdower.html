Post by: sl1pknot on February 27, 2013, 07:42 pm
Yes, by all means, you should! More chilled out than salvia? HA! No, it's just as intense, if not more. However, the trip has a much more warm feel to it, after the first hit from the pipe you get a warm rush over your body, colors become more vibrant, everything starts to shimmer and music sounds AMAZING. DMT is different from shrooms and LSD in the aspect that with the aforementioned psychedelics, you have to kind of guide your trip. With DMT, you are guided. Basically you get catapulted into a peak experience, which you should be ready for before you take it. I compare it to a roller coaster. You're in line, all anxious, and then you strap in and you're even more anxious and nervous/excited. Then it blasts off and you have an amazing intense experience and come back from it literally ~5-10 minutes later and you are back to baseline and you will be babbling about how that was the craziest thing you've ever- and then you'll realize you can barely remember most of it. That would be a large dose though, once big enough to "break through." I would recommend purchasing some DMT, and smoking a few smaller hits to get the feel of DMT land before diving headfirst. However you can't really get a feel for the real DMT land when you blast off into another dimension from the smaller doses, but you can get an idea. It's an incredible experience and I would highly recommend it to anyone in search of some deeper understanding (or utter astonishment) of the lives we live. A peak experience will change the way you look at life moreso than a heavy LSD/mushroom trip. You truly meet the divine, DMT is a connection to the spiritual realm that I never would have thought existed before using psychedelics.

edit: Forgot to mention that I personally HATE salvia, however LOVE DMT. The salvia trip as I'd explain is is just downright fucking weird. Not in a good way. In a weird, strange, fucked up way. I have had out of body experiences with both substances and can assure you the DMT trip is much much more pleasant.

Post by: DMTisinME on February 27, 2013, 09:26 pm
Yes, DMT is an amazing little substance. Every time I smoke it I am amazed at how fast your body is able to process it. I would describe it as you literally being able to feel it pass through your body and the high leave you. The post-peak is what I'd imagine your "soul" leaving your body would feel like. IMO however, it is entirely unnecessary to educate yourself in terms of what you are too expect because words are always a disgraceful  description of the experience. However, you should certainly know how to consume it.

In terms of ingestion, a glass vaporgenie is the tool of choice for most experienced DMT smokers or a glass vaporizer that is similar. You use it in conjunction with a torch lighter (must be a torch lighter). If you so choose you can use the alternative of a bubbler or similar pipe. You should pack any smokable herb -- cannabis or parsley are usually recommended. Simply because many people like weed and people usually have parsley around already. You should pack a small base, then sprinkle 25-50mg of fluff evenly around, then sprinkle a thin layer of herb on top. Be sure to be inhaling when the flame is brought near the herb. The DMT should NOT be touched by the flame, the herb should spark and that heat vaporizes it. Take as many hits are  you can before the rush onsets. Three is the magic number for a novice.

After you have consumed it for the first time going back and reading others experiences with it makes a whole lot more sense.
