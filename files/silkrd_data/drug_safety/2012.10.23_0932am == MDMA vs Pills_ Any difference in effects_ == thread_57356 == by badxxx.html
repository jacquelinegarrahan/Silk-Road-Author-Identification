Title: Re: MDMA vs Pills? Any difference in effects?
Post by: knexx on December 04, 2012, 02:52 am
I think Bluelight is a really good place to look if you're looking for basic MDMA discussion and information. I've learned a lot in my years there.

But to answer your original question,  MDMA is MDMA is MDMA no matter if it's in pill, capsule, powder, or dissolved in liquid. If you have 100mg of just MDMA in a capsule versus 100mg pressed into a tab with fillers and binders, it's going to be exactly the same thing. There are no differences in effects, despite what many people have formulated their opinions on. The difference in effects between presses and powders only comes from the quality of the source.

If your friend feels like he rolls better on pills than on powder/crystal, his dealer might just have a better connection to a good pill supply than the other stuff.
