I've been a long time fan of aromatherapy and an avid purchaser of scented candles, being the westernized individual that I am.

However, I was recently shopping in a hipster-y mall complex a town over (which exists thanks to the local college) and stumbled upon incense sticks. I decided why not try a few- the cheap holder was only two bucks and the various sticks were $0.25 each, cheaper if you bought a bundle.

So I did, got home, lit one up- they're amazing! Smells absolutely fantastic, I bought the brand wild-berry [ clearnet: http://www.wild-berry.com/ ]. Think the one I love the most is Shangri-la, it's very soothing and sweet, mixed in with the scent of the incense ashes.

So, psychonauts- have you ever burned incenses while tripping? I presume it'd be a phenomenal experience- the smoke is so beautiful to look at, it'll do twirls and spirals and holding out from itself all on its own; words and pictures won't do it justice.

Any particular brands you enjoy? Speaking of course of aromatic incense- not the 'incense' which is a clever way of avoiding selling a drug product illegally (like K2).
