Post by: curiositymatrix on March 06, 2013, 11:15 pm
Rolling a piece of tinfoil like a hollow J, and pinching the end into form a bowl, makes a decent hash pipe. Light it from the top, drawing the flame over the hash for a second or 2.

You shouldn't get it so hot that the tinfoil wrinkles, or that it feels hot against your lips.

Basically, you're taking the tube, cross section like this:

____________________________
____________________________

And reshaping the end to look like this: Bring the upper portion back, exposing the inside of the tube, then push it down, creating a small hole for airflow. You'll have a little scoop on the end, which you can shape up into a bowl.
       _________________
\__/__________________

My first SR order ever was hash, and I was unprepared to smoke it, so that's what I devised. Using a pencil to form the tinfoil could help.
