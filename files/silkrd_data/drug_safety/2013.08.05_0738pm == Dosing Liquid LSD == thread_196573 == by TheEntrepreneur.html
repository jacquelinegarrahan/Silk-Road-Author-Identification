Title: Re: Dosing Liquid LSD
Post by: NewStem on August 05, 2013, 08:00 pm
I transferred the contents of my vial from JoR into a 1 dram dropper vial I got locally, but can easily be found online for very cheap. I tried to dilute the contents to half the concentration using distilled water, but due to the different densities, I did not get a consistent number of drops, size of drops or dose per drop. This was the best acid I've ever had; however, when I dropped it on a sugar cube, it had barely any effect. I don't know why that is- perhaps because I ate breakfast shortly after, the LSD reacted with something in the sugar cube, or the drops were that uneven in dose (the most doubtful reason, I think)- but I wouldn't repeat that method. I like it in the eyeball.
