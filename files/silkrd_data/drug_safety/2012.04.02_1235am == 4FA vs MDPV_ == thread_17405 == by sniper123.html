Title: Re: 4FA vs MDPV?
Post by: poolsclosed on May 04, 2012, 12:09 am
Fortune has it that I have tried both drugs recreationally and for studying.

4-FA has a great mood-boosting aspect to it that can really get you pumped and excited to do stuff. Unfortunately, I find that it gets me over-excited and I'd rather do housework and sing and dance. I have a hard time just sitting still, although I do get more into what I'm focusing on. It really is better suited to be a party drug, honestly.

MDPV is more focused but it has a terribly moreish quality to it. I also experience jitters with it. Oh, and it builds up tolerance quickly (although tolerance goes away pretty quickly too). I'm currently using drpvmd's B grade MDPV to combat my narcolepsy. It lacks most of the euphoria and it's less potent by weight, so it's good for a wake-me-up. His A grade is great stuff but it's too moreish for me to use for any practical day-to-day application.

Personally I don't like either for studying. So far, besides the classic prescription study meds, I find ethylphenidate to be decent for studying.
