Title: Re: Research Chems
Post by: Electrogasm666 on October 26, 2011, 09:33 am
I cannot recommend 4-aco-DMT enough if you're looking to wet your feet in RC's -- it's all the good parts of mushrooms without the side effects (it metabolizes in your body as psilocin, a highly unstable chemical found only in freshly picked shrooms). If you're looking to try out the 2c-x family I would recommend starting out with 2c-b -- it's shorter lasting and less physically intense than it's relatives. 2c-e is wonderful, and can be a powerfully insightful trip, but be careful how much you take (it has a really steep dose-response curve - a couple mg too many can drastically increase the effects) and take a benadryl with it to relieve the over-stimulation (a common side effect).

A couple favorite RC's of mine:
4-aco-DMT
2c-b
2c-e
2c-c
MXE (a dissociative, not a psychedelic)
6-APB
DIPT (most unique drug I've ever taken - 24 hours of almost exclusively auditory hallucination/distortion)
2c-t-7
methylone/mephedrone (empathogens/stimulants; similar to MDMA)

A few I've taken but disliked (wouldn't personally recommend):
MDPV
4-MEC
5-meo-DALT
2c-i (most people do like this, I've just never been a fan)
2c-t-2
DOB

Everyone has different experiences, though, so don't rule any of these out. Just do your research before taking any new RC, and be absolutely sure you trust your source.

Post by: PartOfTheDance on October 26, 2011, 11:34 am
2c-i is a good one to start with.  Personally I kind of find it to be similar to 2c-b but not quite as good, but I do have a few friends who swear by 2c-i.

For a more light mellow trip 2c-c and 2c-d are great.  Not really much to worry about in terms of anxiety.


As already mentioned 4-aco-dmt is a really nice one.  I enjoy mushrooms more (chemically they are very similar - psilocin is 4-ho-dmt) and get a smoother trip from them, but it seems to be the opposite for most people.


If anxiety is an issue at all I would definitely not recommend 2c-e.  It can be a pretty overbearing trip that is hard to control.


OH, and 2c-t-2 is a great one.  Very similar to mescaline.  There's a good chance you will get some nausea on the comeup, but it passes pretty quick and the rest of the trip is fantastic.
