Title: Re: smoking meth - sweet puff
Post by: Beastie on July 29, 2012, 10:11 am
If your meth is clear crystals it is basically the pure methamphetamine chemical. When you "Smoke" it from a pipe you are not actually smoking it, your Vaporizing it. That is, you are raising it's temperature to the melting point and then to the boiling point. You don't want to raise it's temperature to the thermal breakdown point.

Rolling it dose two things. First, it distributes the heat to help prevent the meth from reaching the thermal breakdown point. Second it increases the surface area and creates turbulence to help the molecules evaporate into a gas. I find it is best to spin the pipe and hold the flame under the thin trailing end of the moving drop instead of under the droplet itself.

Because you are not smoking i.e. your not burning it, and assuming the crystal you have is nearly pure,  all of the vapour you are inhaling is nothing but Methamphetamine molecules. Logically there is no reason to not want to hold in the vapour until all of it has cooled back down and dissolved into the fluids in your lungs and intern the blood stream.
