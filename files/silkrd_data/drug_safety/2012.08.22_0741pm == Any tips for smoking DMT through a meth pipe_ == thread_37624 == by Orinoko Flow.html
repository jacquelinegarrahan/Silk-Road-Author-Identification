Post by: john_snow_da_bastard on August 23, 2012, 04:47 am
I believe the flower pipes are for crack.

Anyways, you want to take steel wool or a brillow pad. Flame a small piece until it no longer produces fumes. Stuff it into the end. Leave room to add dmt in front of it.   Then hold upright with dmt on top of brillow. Gently geat to melt dmt into brillow matrix. Remove heat, let dmt solidify in matrix. When ready to smoke, apply heat/flame to heat up brillow matrix. The heated matrix vaporizes the dmt. Inhale as vapor appears.

Title: Re: Any tips for smoking DMT through a meth pipe?
Post by: Joey Terrifying on August 23, 2012, 08:33 pm
is it the kind with a bubble and stem, or is it just a straight tube?  the straight tubes are good for crack, but not so good for DMT.

definitely do NOT hold your finger over the hole....just hold the flame an inch or so under the spice and roll the pipe around a little so the heat spreads out a little.  once it starts forming a heavy vape start sucking on that pipe for as long as you can.  if it stops vaporizing, add a little bit more heat but don't burn it.

my first breakthroughs were from one lonnnggg giant hit off a meth pipe, but i do have a very large lung capacity.  so its possible.

good luck!
