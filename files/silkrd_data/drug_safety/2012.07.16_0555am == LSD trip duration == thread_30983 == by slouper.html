Taking large dosages of LSD certainly does increase the duration. You won't be "peaking" longer than usual, per se, but with high dosages, it will be kind of like being on speed while "coming down", until you're able to get to sleep, which will be more difficult to do at a "reasonable" time the higher the dosage. A high enough dosage will have lingering effects past the 24 hour mark if one is not able to get to sleep. Consider this before taking a large dose in your parents house.

Title: Re: LSD trip duration
Post by: slybootz on July 17, 2012, 08:04 pm
You should have no trouble fitting a lucy trip into a 12 hour time span.  Higher doses don't really add any length to the trip, just intensity.  As others have said, I'd recommend tripping out in nature instead of in the home.  Even on lower doses of LSD, tripping at home can get boring/awkward in a heartbeat, and higher doses will just intensity the uncomfortable feeling if one arises. 

I, too, would warn you to avoid taking 600-700ug of LSD, you won't find it much more profound than a 400-500ug dose.  I've taken several >1mg doses before, and while it is definitely a bit more intense than normal sized doses, it's really not going to take you anywhere new.  I find that above 500ug, it becomes much harder to function and explore both physically and mentally, and it also becomes harder to remember those ego-shattering, life-changing self-realizations that LSD can initiate.  Having explored this chemical quite a bit, definitely the most out of any psychedelic, my sweet spot is around 300ug.

I still learn new things from 150ug trips that all the time; LSD has more to teach you than you even know, even at standard or sub-standard doses. 

TL;DR:  Yes, you can fit a 6-700ug LSD trip in the span of 12 hours from come-up to nearly baseline.
