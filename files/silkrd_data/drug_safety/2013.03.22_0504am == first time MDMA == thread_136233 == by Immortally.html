Title: Re: first time MDMA
Post by: DiamondSky on March 22, 2013, 05:36 am
Yeah no need to mix these two drugs especially if you haven't done them before.

What you described is pretty typical come up for MDMA though. Most of the time the first time people take it they can get a little anxious and almost always if they do the thought that pops in their head is "Did I take too much?"

In the future you will want to do MDMA alone and measure out your dose. I personally don't recommend snorting MDMA based on years of playing with the stuff it just seems to waste more of it than all the other ROAs's. Nothing wrong with snorting, lots of people do, just personal preference.

Most people start with an oral dose of between 100-120mg and that seems to get them where they are going. You'll still have the come up thing next time but since you know what it feels like it won't be nearly as bad. For most people, if it gets a little rough, they will puke at that point and don't get all scared if you do. About a second after puking the world turns amazing.

Depending on how much mephedrone you did, you may or may not be able to fully enjoy all the goodness of MDMA so you might want to wait a few days and give just MDMA a go on it's own.

Good Luck!
