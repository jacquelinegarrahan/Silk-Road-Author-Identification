Lain, there is a reason why Erowid edits experience reports that use "you" instead of "I." I realize that you describe your trips, which is correct to do, yes, but you are making it sound like he will experience the same with LSD.

The truth is that 100ug isn't by any means a heroic dose... at all. Hell, if OP wanted, he could cut the tab in half and take just 50ug. Or into 3/4. It's his life. I have a wise friend who says that you are ready to trip when you seek a trip. Sure, he's probably going to be floored by it, and your concern for him is appreciated, but your admonishing is not.

So, that is to say, everyone experiences chemicals differently and just because you find LSD to be weirder than 5-MeO-DMT, or so your post seems to imply (and I apologize if I misunderstood), that doesn't mean he will. 2C-P is a great example of how some people can take 20mg and have a mild and pleasant trip, and others can take 14mg and spend 24 hours fighting toxicity in a living psychedelic hell. I read a thread a while back where a SR user said 5mg of 2C-B was too intense and weird for him - meanwhile 2C-B didn't get "weird" until I took upwards of 30mg, and Shulgin recommended a 24mg max. I'm sure you already understood what I'm saying and I sound completely patronizing, but I don't think I should be any less explicit here because I want OP to have a good time and to enjoy his first trip!

Even if he is a minor, let's just give him constructive advice on how to trip safely and happily, and hopefully he will make a responsible decision and not take acid on a Wednesday night in mom's basement :)
