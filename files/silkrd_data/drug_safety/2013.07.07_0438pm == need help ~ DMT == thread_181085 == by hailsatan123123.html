Post by: flyinghigh1660 on July 08, 2013, 09:27 pm
Smoking DMT is a skill.

You've basically two options.

Ease yourself in - work up to your doseage

Go balls out - drop enough for  breakthrough the first time you try it.

Easing in

If you want to ease in. Start with small doses, 10 mg say. A small bong is ideal, lay dried herbs such as mint or mullein ,or ever weed ash. Place the measured DMT on top. Stay with the flame well away from the bowl. And then bring in closer as you draw in breath. You should aim to be reaching 2/3 maximum draw just as the DMT starts to melt. The white vapor appears, and at this point don't take the flame closer.

Just vary the power of your breath to take the flame into the bowl. If you want to melt more draw harder, less melt, let off a little.

Once you have filled your lungs stop and hold your breath. Most people make the mistake of holding their breath right from the start. This is a mistake. your lungs are not yet full. You have just reached then of your diaphragm contraction.  Relax your diaphragm, and then take another breath. You can do this without letting much air out of your lungs.

This allows you to take in more oxygenated air, and hold your breath for longer.

Hold for a good 15 secs release slowly and take another hit, using the same technique.

Half way through the second git, the world will get fucked up. Finish the second hit, go for the third,but close your eyes. Make sure you have planned somewhere safe to drop your bong/smoking device. Doing this way and working on your smoking technique. Means you fine the exact dose you breakthrough at with good smoking technique. Probably around the 30 mg mark with a good set up.

Going balls out

You will need to go a few mg over a breakthrough dose, say 50mg. This is because your smoking technique will be rubbish. No matter how good you'll think you will be. You'll most likely burn the DMT first time around. Then it will taste like shit. And you'll need to get and keep a good lung full to ensure breakthrough. Try to use the above technique, but getting a breakthrough first time around is more about shoving 50 mg into a bowl and drawing as much in one go and holding it for as longer as you can.

What ever way you do breakthrough. The next trip after that, man that's the fucking scary one, because you know what the shit can do.

I tried the machine to no avail (first time using it), but it was just so much more convenient for a regular bong rip.

This is very similar to the above mentioned methods.

Here's how I did it with a standard bong rip (NEVER TOUCH THE FLAME TO THE WEED):

1. Take bowl, put a pinch of weed on bottom. This is just so the DMT doesn't completely get sucked into the water

2. Put DMT on top

3. Put another layer of weed on top. This is the protective layer

4. When hitting, you want the EDGE of the flame to just FLOAT over the top layer, trust me, even if you don't see the weed cherry, it's vaporizing the DMT.

Chances are though, you'll be like me and some of the flame with ignite the weed on top, but that's ok, it's there to protect the DMT for as long as possible. Immediately, and I mean IMMEDIATELY, put the edge of the flame over the weed again if you accidentally cherry the top layer. Just make sure YOU NEVER LET THE FLAME GO THROUGH, and KEEP IT FLOATING for as long as you can. The DMT is very sensitive and will vaporize easily with just heat floating over it

You'll notice a very plastic-like taste coming up. Do this for 5 seconds, and if you really want to be ballsy for your first trip, hit the weed completely.
