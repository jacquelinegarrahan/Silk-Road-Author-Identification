There's no reason for pure amphetamine to be a paste.  It's a myth.  100%.  What's making it paste are solvents, fillers, or some other shit you don't want in your body that some asshole added because somehow somewhere people got the idea that paste is the pure stuff and the stronger the smell the better the product.

It's not true. Amphetamine is essentially odorless and a pure white or off-white powder, depending on how the base molecule is bound (as in amphetamine sulfate/hydrochloride/saccharate/etc.).  So you probably want it to dry out.  Whatever's in there, it ain't what you want.
