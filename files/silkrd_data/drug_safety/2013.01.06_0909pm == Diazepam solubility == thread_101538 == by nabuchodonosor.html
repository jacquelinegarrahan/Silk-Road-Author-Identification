Title: Re: Diazepam solubility
Post by: Ben on January 07, 2013, 02:07 am
It should dissolve quite readily in pure alcohol really, up to concentrations of about 50 grams per litre at room temperatures.

If you are attempting to dissolve tablets, however, there may be many other components in the tablets that do not dissolve well - filtering off whatever dissolved out of tablets ground to a powder should contain all the diazepam.

Note that readily doesn't mean rapidly though. Especially when dealing with tablets it may require mortaring those down to a fine powder and even some patience after that while stirring things to get the diazepam to actually dissolve.
