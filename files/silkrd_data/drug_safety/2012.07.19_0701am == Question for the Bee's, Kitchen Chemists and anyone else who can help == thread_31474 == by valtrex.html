Post by: modest mouse on July 19, 2012, 10:33 am
purifying mdma is a good idea all around, but especially if your mdma smells like licorice which is indicative of saffrole... a common contaminant after the synthesis that is highly carcinogenic (banned by FDA, like from root beer, for the danger) you should use a non polar wash

you can use acetone as you mention, just test the purity using an evaporation test on  a mirrored surface to look for a lack of residue. next it is very important because acetone is hygroscopic to remove the water or else a significant portion of the mdma will be lost given its aqueous solubility. 

to wash the mdma:  (use caution as acetone will dissolve some plastics)
you need to buy magnesium sulfate (epsom salt) and bake in an oven at about 420 for around an hour till the appearance changes to a more powdered sugar/ baking powder look to dry the hydrate to an anhydrous form which will absorb water... 

take a portion of the acetone and dump a good bit of the anhydrous salt and shake well, then let sit overnight to allow the MgSO4 to settle to the bottom. Then you can just add you mdma to say a shot glass, carefully pour or use a pipet to carefully transfer the some dry acetone (no salt...) into the shot glass.

Break up and mix/stir around the mdma and then let it settle for a few hours. dump off the acetone leaving the settled material and save the acetone in another container, then repeat by adding more dry acetone to the mdma until it become as white as possible and the acetone removed remains clear.

take the mdma after washing and just dump onto a mirror and allow to dry, then scrape your crystals.  (take all the saved removed acetone and let that sit for a good while, at least overnight to make sure no mdma remained in solution as well)
