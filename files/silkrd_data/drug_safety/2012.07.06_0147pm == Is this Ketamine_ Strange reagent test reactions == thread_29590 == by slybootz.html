Title: Is this Ketamine? Strange reagent test reactions
Post by: slybootz on July 06, 2012, 01:47 pm
So last week at Electric Forest Festival, my buddy ground scored a little plastic case with two gram baggies full of shards.  The shards looked very similar to grains of rice, white/clear and fairly uniform.  All ketamine I've gotten in the past has been either sugar-like crystals, or needle-like un-uniform shards.  However, I had read online of people receiving legit ketamine that looked like this.

I had my Mecke, Marquis, and Mandelin test kits on me, so we decided to test this ground score.  Even though only Mandelin tests for Ketamine, I ran a test with all three kits.  When I test my amazing ketamine with Mandelin, it usually goes orange almost immediately with some bubbling.

Here were the results: 
Mandelin:  Color change to orange, but took about 30 seconds for a full color change. No fizz.

Mecke:  A fairly quick color change to a strange brownish-rust color with some bubbling.

Marquis:  Yellow, no real color change, but did bubble as a reaction.

I will have pictures posted tonight, once I get them off my iPhone and scrub data.

Any initial thoughts on what this could be?
