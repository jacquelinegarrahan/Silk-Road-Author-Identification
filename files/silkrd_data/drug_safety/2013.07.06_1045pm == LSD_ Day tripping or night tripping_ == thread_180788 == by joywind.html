Title: Re: LSD: Day tripping or night tripping?
Post by: Internal Freedom on July 07, 2013, 05:00 am
There are a lot of things I prefer to do during the day, LSD is not one of them. I like taking it around 8-9pm so I'm still tripping at 4am in the morning, if I'm with friends we go walk around outside while everything is completely silent and there are no cars on the road, its utter bliss. I also don't like being around, or seeing people when on acid (or shrooms) unless they are close friends. It freaks me out and I get the paranoid feeling "Shit am I acting normal? I wonder if that person thinks I'm on something."

I only took acid during the day once though... compared to dozens of times at night. I took 400ug of it a while back and went out to run some errands and do my shopping, I also got on a bus absolutely full of people during rush-hour when I was trying to get home from shopping. I walked into a coffee shop near where I live after and stood right behind two cops in line smiling to myself at how funny the whole trip was going.

The ONLY thing I regret about that trip was the strange look the lady at the head-shop gave me. I went there to buy a pipe, and I had spoken with her before when I had been in there. She knew me fairly well and I must have looked off to her (could have been my pupils or the fact I was having a hard time formulating sentences haha) because she gave me this alarmed look, then awkwardly smiled and didn't say much to me afterwards.

The whole thing was fun as fuck though, watching the floor of the bus bubble and turn into goo on the bus ride home. Should have brought sunglasses with me...

After so many people writing positive things about night tripping, I tried it out yesterday with a friend. We both took 3/4 from Light of Pi's 130µg tabs (had them before, they're very good) and laid down on a meadow. Man that was some beautiful shit! We were listening to trance/techno music while watching the stars and airplanes above us. As we started tripping we saw more and more stars, even the very tiny ones and they seemed to dance to the music and forming shapes.
I saw a low flying airplane and it was like a ship sailing through a sea of dancing stars, leaving a trail of light behind it. We even saw some shooting stars, my buddy thought one of them fell down near us and he got up to search it ^^
Later we went to my place cause it was getting cold. I turned up a black light lamp in my room and we listened to music all night long. My friend was scared cause he saw some monsters on my walls but I calmed him down. At around 5am we went out to see the sunrise. We saw birds flying around leaving colourful trails and trees with perfect oval shapes. You could feel the perfectness of nature and it was like you were at home and a part of it. Very beautiful.
