Post by: gtgeorgz on August 18, 2013, 09:58 pm
I usually take the MDMA, have fun with that then wait for the peak to be over. So about 2 to 4 hours after I last dosed, then I snort around 5-9mg of 2cb.
I've never taken it orally so I can't comment on that side of dosing.
But careful with quantity though! I would not go over 10mg if you're snorting (unless you're experienced of course) because as I learned the hard way, 2cb can be extremely unforgiving.
Comes on in about 5/10mins, brings back the MDMA euphoria but with a new edge to it, extremely chatty, slightly rushy/stimulating, sensual, usually comical/nature orientated visuals along with the trippyness, a head space that's not really 'earth like' but extremely enjoyable.

As far as comedowns, it certainly stops me from sudden 'crashing' although if you have decent MDMA this doesn't happen anyway. It's certainly a less harsh comedown when the 2cb starts to wear off yes, but the next couple of days I still feel pretty glum. Next day you do get a nice afterglow though.
