i dont think meth is the best decision for adhd - especially not for daily use due the stress on the nervous system. perhaps regular amphetamine would be a little better

definitely not recommended to make up aqueous solutions and store for long periods because they become cultures for bacteria and fungi which will ruin the entire batch of drug. it will also promote degradation of the chem in aqueous solution particularly if exposed to increased temp or light. for this reason if you are going to mix it up do it in smaller quantities with bacteriostatic water you will use within a week and keep in the fridge. it would be best to use an alcohol solution to prevent contamination growing though

probably be better just to buy some capsules and make the doses up that way even though you will need a decent scale - well worth the expense if you plan on following through with this plan to use meth in that way
