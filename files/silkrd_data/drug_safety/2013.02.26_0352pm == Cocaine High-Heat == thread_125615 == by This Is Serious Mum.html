Hi-heat refers to a better time, a time before hundreds of different cuts were being used.

When heated, the cutting agents melted before the actual cocaine, I.E If you shit melts really fast (low-heat) then it's mostly cut, and hi-heat is mostly cocaine.

But now with levimasole and that kind of crap it's irrelevant, because it melts at the exact same temp and cannot be removed with acetone.

It's just like calling it fishscale, shale, w.e just means good shit.
