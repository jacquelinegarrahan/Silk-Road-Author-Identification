Title: Re: First time Cocaine Advice, Setting? Dosage? What to expect?
Post by: Theaides on October 30, 2012, 12:12 am
Quote
1 What are the main effects of coke, we have heard Confidence and euphoria?

It makes you very extroverted, and confident.  There's some similarities to MDMA in my opinion but not as entactogenic or "lovey" and more of an energetic feel-good high like everything is right with yourself.

Quote
2. Is a nightclub a good setting to take coke?

I don't have much experience using coke in clubs but I'd imagine it is if you can do it without gettin caught.  Most of the clubs here have a bouncer chilling in the restrooms though so it could be pretty obvious depending on where you go.  Some people use a nasal spray technique though to get their bumps.

Quote
3. What sort of dosages would you recommend?

If you don't know how strong the batch is start low, 25mg, then titrate upwards and see how comfortable you feel with each amount.  Some people have more intense reactions to it at lower doses, find your comfort zone but always start low and figure out what you can handle and what feels "right".

Quote
4. How long does Coke last?

The full high from dose to peak, to the crash is about 40m-1h or so total, but around 20min you're probably gonna start itching for another bump.  30min is manageable but you'll be up and down a lot if you wait too long, and it can be kind of uncomfortable going from high to low all night like that.  I tend to start with a 100mg line split into two and then do 50mg bumps 20-30min apart for a couple hours and then I'm good for the night usually (helps me get motivated to clean house, get things done).

Quote
5. Can you sleep after, or would you recommend Benzos?

I'd say stop coking up about 3-4 hours before you want to sleep.  Some people can get to sleep without much of a hassle in an hour, but I have a hard time.  Some weed helps me a bit about 2hrs after my final dose, I don't have/use benzos so I couldn't tell you but I hear they're a commonly used way to kill coke insomnia.  Some people say drink a beer. 

Other tips I've figured out (I first tried coke about a month ago, also):

Vitamin C after your last dose helps with the crash.

Always eat before you start doing lines.  Once you're up you won't feel hungry, and once you start coming down, if you're riding on an empty stomach you're going to feel like absolute death and then it becomes a game of fiending just to avoid feeling sick.  Eventually you'll have to stop, and my worst night with it I was going to only do 3-4 lines but the nauseating feeling I was getting as it was coming down made me go through the rest of my 3/4g, ultimately leading to me being up till 6am and feeling overly toxic, sick, gross, it made me swear off coke for good for a good week...and then of course I came crawling back, great!  But after that experience I've been able to avoid getting addicted and now only do it once a week or at the occasional house party.  It's really easy to convince yourself you can keep it under control but the first day or two after your first time you'll probably get the itch to try more, and then I could easily see how it consumes someone's life

The good news is its not hard to quit cold turkey, easier if you just don't buy more, but when it's in your closet it's hard to resist pulling it out.  Or you could just do what I did, go on an empty stomach binge and train your brain that it's an awful thing to make a more-than-once-a-week habit at the very most.  You'll feel like absolute shit but it will most definitely kick in some respect for the drug sooner than later.

Honestly it's fun to have around, but I cannot stress enough how much you need to be in control of the drug instead of the other way around.  I seen too many smart people fall victim to coke addiction and it becomes #1 priority in their life before they even realize it.  You also should let your nose heal so you don't do too much damage too quickly, coke nose feels dry and annoying (I couldn't stop rubbing my nose the day after that horrible binge night I had..)  But with responsible use it's just another drug that's fun.  I caught up with lots of old friends and got to talk with some of my closer ones on a deeper level, so there are some positives to it.

Oh, and snorting is the least addictive ROA though, I'd suggest sticking to that.  Smoking it is the most fiendish and IV is probably the riskiest in terms of physical health.
