Post by: microRNA on October 13, 2012, 10:16 pm
it should dissolve without acid

try snorting a bit first to get an idea of the potency and make sure what you received was quality H before shooting

in the interest of harm reduction please consider a different ROA

its very difficult to shoot up yourself the first time. expect to stick yourself with a needle multiple times, and each time you do you need to change the needle (backload into fresh syringe) for maximum safety and to prevent infection and vein damage. definitely practice pulling up to register then pushing down with one hand without moving the needle before actually trying to stab yourself


also for harm reduction:

disinfect your spoon with IPA pad

add COLD tap water (less bacteria than warm) - few units should do it for a small dose and will make plunger easier to push
- i would recommend to boil in the microwave for 10-15 min to disinfect

add dope

either let cool if nuked or heat just barely (dont boil -it wont disinfect, you just want to warm the water to body temp and to facilitate dissolving the gear

ball up tiny amount of cotton from qtip for example

use syringe to pull solution through filter

backload into fresh syringe (because the needle was dulled by the filter, some do this - many dont)

check to make sure solution in syringe is clear and free of fibers/particulates

tilt needle upward, tap and then push plunger up to make sure majority of air is out (tiny bubbles wont hurt if you cant get em all out)

use IPA swab to disinfect injection site

tourniquet up if needed

inset needle bevel up

REGISTER!!!

(remove tourniquet)

push slowly and enjoy

there are much better guides on the internet but thats the basics of it, been a minute though so someone correct me if i forgot something
