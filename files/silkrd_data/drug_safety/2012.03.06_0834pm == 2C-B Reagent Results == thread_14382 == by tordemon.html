The marquis results sound a little odd. My 4 different samples of 2C-B all turn dark olive green then fade out to yellow.

Simon's reagent is to test for 2° amines, so you'd expect no result from 2C-B. The smokiness was probably a bit of excess HBr reacting with the sodium carbonate in the Simon's reagent.
