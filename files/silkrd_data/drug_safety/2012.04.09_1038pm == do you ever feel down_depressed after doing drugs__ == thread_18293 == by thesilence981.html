Title: Re: do you ever feel down/depressed after doing drugs??
Post by: tordemon on April 09, 2012, 11:12 pm
Yea, dude, that's how drugs work. You get high, you come down, and then your brain chemistry takes a while after that to get back to normal.

I get the feeling that the more often you take drugs, the more likely you are to think that perhaps you're taking them too often. I personally always found my favorite part of drug experiences to be the novelty. If you think that's what it was for you, you can keep exploring drugs as experiences, or you can broaden your horizons and explore other experiences.

I personally would suggest the second one. I kinda cycle in and out of doing drugs regularly, to try to keep the novelty there. For a while I try Ketamine, Heroin, Methamphetamine, 2C-G-5, Butylone, and Mescaline (among others), and then I take a while without them to try and find new ways to experience.

I'd say you should avoid the assumption that the weekend means drug use. The weekend is certainly an opportunity to take drugs, but there are so many other things you could do with your weekend as well. Think of it as an exploration; I think you'll enjoy it.
