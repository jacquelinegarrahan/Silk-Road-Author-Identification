are you doing 60 mg of oxymorphone at once?!

i would guess you must not be extracting it completely

if you are getting a full 60 mg dose of oxymorphone you might be disappointed by H honestly, OM is one of the most potent opioids there is and thats a pretty massive dose - enough to kill many people, so i am assuming you must have a pretty decent tolerance

however - if you get comparable effects from 4 mg hydromorphone, then i would bet the extraction is not working all that well or else you should be tolerant to that as well...

so i would base the dose conversion on the 4 mg hydromorphone and not the 60 mg of oxymorphone or else you might not get the correct amount you need. 

too hard to give recommendations safely - start much smaller than you think you need and work your way up. some might need 20 mg, some 50 mg, and others maybe even 100 mg H
