Nice post, the translator did a fairly decent job. This is a fairly good idea, especially for those who end up having to score on the streets or purchase uk gear. What has been suggested is a simple recrystallization with methanol to remove the cutting agent by utilizing the property H is soluble in methanol while most adulterants are not. Filtering the solution will remove the impurities not soluble in methanol and the diacetylmorphine is then obtained by evaporating off the solvent.

However, after this step he was saying you may be left with impurities still soluble in methanol, so you could do another recrystallization except this time with water to further remove the impurities. However it is very important to note that if you used #4 then the salt will just dissolve in the water as well, and even if you use #3 i would still suggest that one adds a very slight amount of base, say NaOH to raise the pH enough to ensure the H will precipitate out of the aqueous solution. Then filter and collect the solids which is the purified H, this time discarding the solution with the impurities.

Theoretically, if one was to purchase pure #4 and wanted to smoke their gear, they could also just dissolve it in water and then use NaOH to basify the H and convert to the more readily vaporizable form.
