Title: Re: MDMA: orally ingesting powder vs capsules?
Post by: Theaides on October 30, 2012, 01:48 am
You'll need a scale that can measure at least in 10ths of a gram.

Capping it will make it take longer to come up since the capsule has to dissolve first, but it eliminates the gross taste, and it's easy to carry around, but the roll duration wont be any different.
Title: Re: MDMA: orally ingesting powder vs capsules?
Post by: dead salmon on October 30, 2012, 02:30 am
$20 will get you an ok mg scale accurate to within plus/minus 5 mg or so - that's the only quick & easy way to measure doses like that.  Capped dose just means slightly slower come-up, and maybe a slightly lower peak, probably not enough of a difference for you to notice much if at all.
