Title: Re: When to know that you've over-dosed?
Post by: Ben on October 22, 2012, 01:01 am
Quote from: go4green on October 08, 2012, 02:25 pm
Long story short,
What makes you believe that a moment is THE moment you should make the call.

It's a hard call to make yourself. A proper panic attack can certainly make you feel you are about to die right then and there, while there is no medical reason to assume that's remotely likely. Ideally you should have a friend to judge the whole situation and consider if emergency services would be required, or if its something you'll sleep off.

In absence of any friends, i suppose its best to err or the side of caution. People call for ambulances quite frequently during panic attacks, drug induced or otherwise. I'd say its a legitimate reason to call if you have never experienced it before too. If you are familiar with the sensation you know it will be okay in half an hour or so, but if you are not, it is very difficult to sense the difference between a panic attack and a heart attack except for chest pain - something you may not really notice in severe distress.
