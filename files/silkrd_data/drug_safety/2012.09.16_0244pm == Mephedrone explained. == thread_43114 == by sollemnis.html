Title: Mephedrone explained.
Post by: sollemnis on September 16, 2012, 02:44 pm
Ok, so recently there has been a lot of people asking questions about Drone. Or people asking about the best 'RC's'. I'm constantly giving people info about Mephedrone. So now I've decided to write a thread about it.

I'll try to explain how it feels the best I can - What doses to take - How long it lasts - It's relation to MDMA and other drugs.

Originally, Mephedrone was an Ecstasy replacement. People took it because it was legal, and therefore easier to get hold of than MDMA. Now, even though there are some similarities between the two drugs, there's also a lot of differences. (At least in my experience). Everybody reacts differently to every drug.

I'm going to start with what Mephedrone and MDMA have in common in terms of effects -

Music Appreciation
Empathy
Pupil dilation
Increased temperature

Now with MDMA, you feel love for the world and others around you. On Mephedrone this feeling is slightly different, you don't feel 'love' for others - It's more of a feeling that people are AMAZING... The world is amazing, everybody around you is amazing.

Mephedrone has similarities to Amphetamine and Coke, these being -

Stimulation
Euphoria
Talkativeness
Feeling like superman
Increased self confidence

Now even though MDMA gives people a feeling of euphoria, Mephedrone euphoria is more like Coke's euphoria... It's a LOT more stimulating than MDMA, as MDMA isn't too stimulating - Hence why it was known as the 'relaxing Amphetamine'.

I'd describe Drone as the perfect mix of Coke, MDMA and Amphetamine. It has all the best properties of the those three drugs - With its own unique feeling too. Personally Drone makes me more talkative than Coke and Speed.

Once you've left the peak of an MDMA roll, you can't bring back the peak effects. (Until your next roll 2+ weeks later) With Mephedrone re-dosing brings back the peak effects. Which is why it's really fiendish.

A typical dose of Mephedrone is 100-150mgs when taken nasally. The peak effects only last 30-40 mins. Every 30 mins taking a 50mg top up brings back the peak effects.

When taken orally 250-300mgs is a good dose, for a nice intense (But not too intense) experience. Peak effects last just over an hour, with residual effects lasting a good 3 hours.

What I like to do is parachute 200mgs, then snort 100mgs. I find this gives the intensity of taking it nasally, but the duration of taking it orally.

Overall I rate this drug a good 10/10... IMO its more euphoric than MDMA - However MDMA is an overall more pleasurable experience.
