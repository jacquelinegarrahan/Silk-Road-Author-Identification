Post by: flyinghigh1660 on December 17, 2012, 10:47 pm
I take Piracetam every day, you don't have to take it everyday, but worth thinking about the day after a roll. Feed the brain with some blood.

Think about your diet as well, if you are taking drugs regularly. Maximize your health in other areas, exercise and get plenty of good quality sleep.

You don't have to go overboard, but you would be amazed at the different good structured diet and exercise can do for your drug taking.

If my great grand parents didn't think of it as food, then I probably shouldn't eat it.

Rest up, and leave reasonable gaps between heavy rolls.  You can dose MDMA lower to remove social anxiety, in these cases shorter spaces between doses is acceptable.
