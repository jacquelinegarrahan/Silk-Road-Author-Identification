Title: Re: What is the best way to avoid sickness before taking 2ce?
Post by: poolsclosed on March 09, 2012, 10:56 pm
You should let your stomach be empty when you take it, just make sure you eat something low GI and high protein so it'll stick with you.

I am pretty sure that the reason why you throw up is the serotonin HT3 receptor agonizing, so I don't think there's a lot you can do along the lines of the conventional. There is an anti-emetic called Ondansetron (try online pharms) which antagonizes these receptors and works particularly well for 2C-E trips according to SWIM.

Also, once your body absorbs the drug, throwing up won't eject any of the substance. Don't worry about the oral ROA, in fact the only drug I can think of that exits your body when you throw up is alcohol. (Correct me if I'm wrong.)
