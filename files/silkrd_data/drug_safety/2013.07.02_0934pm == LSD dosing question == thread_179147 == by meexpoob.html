Title: Re: LSD dosing question
Post by: busta999 on July 03, 2013, 07:10 am
In my experience I can handle 200ug in a public setting and behave not too strange. Also I can manage my thought processes.
On 400ug I will be seriously trippng - at least during the first 3-4 hours and what goes on in my mind is not really under control and I would be worried that I would not handle things well if events turns uncomfortable. Most bad trips I have seen and had reported are people taking a large dose (which 400ug is) in a bad setting.
Typically in company of people who start acting agressive or strongly negative. Its not uncommon to get a certain amount of paranoia during a trip (like feeling people stare at you or want to hurt you or steal your stuff) and in a huge strange crowd this can quickly turn ugly.

I guess it mainly depends what your tolerance is, what kind of festival it is, who you will be with (will there be someone who will take care to save your ass if things go bad) and what kind of audience it attracts (if you can safely act weirdly and agresive assholes are few).

For most people I would say that the right place to first take a large dose of LSD is not in a crowd of strangers
