I know people hate hearing a response like this, especially at a place like this.

To be frankly honest, until you max out your natural potential or at least plateau, you really aren't ready for Anabolics.

I've done multiple cycles and had to work 2-3x harder while on cycle. Your best bet at the moment is diet first, train hard, and cardio.

However, I assume you'll heed my advice as much as I heeded the same advice when I received it.

If you want to be safe and gain some good mass. A good beginner cycle is Testosterone Enanthate 400mg/week, 2 injections a week (Mon/Thurs) 200/mg per shot. (15 weeks max)

Always have PCT and ancillaries on hand. PCT usually begins 2 weeks after your last injection. Clomid (50mg a day) and Nolvadex (20mg a day) for 4 weeks.

As for ancillaries it is wise to keep a decent anti-estrongen on hand like Aromasin or Arimidex if you have any gyno flare up.
