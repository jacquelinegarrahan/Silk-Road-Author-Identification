This is basic maths. Assuming you filled them all around the same level. If you fill one side to the top each time, this is good, because they will all be basically the same dose. If you had bigger caps and were eyeballing a 1/4 full cap, then you'd have big problems with over and under dosed caps.

If you weighed your MDMA at 7g. That's 7000mg. You make 56 caps. 7000/5=125mg each. Perfect. Using a scale that shows 0.1g or 0.2g is basically worthless. Neither does the size of your caps matter.

If you have poor scales, you could weigh 60 caps, and divide the result by 60. This will help. Although 0.01g scales are needed, although cheap 0.01 scales still fluctuate +/- 0.05g.

If you can't work out 7000/56=125mg, I have to worry if you're ready to start distributing or dosing (or even taking!) drugs on your own. :-) You didn't mention your intended dose per cap, or how much your 7g actually weighed. 7000/60=116.667mg per cap, so I don't know why you wanted 60 caps, since this isn't a dose most people would choose.

That sounds about right if you're dosing ~125mg per cap.

My friend actually devised a system that's really clever.  He makes differently dosed caps since our group has differing sensitivity to MDMA.  He uses colored sugar (like sugar cookies) to "label" a cap's dosage.  Fills up the cap with the intended dose of molly then fills the rest up with the colored sugar.

E.G.:  Green = ~60mg
Blue = 100mg
Yellow = 150mg
Red = 200mg

That way you can still have low doses if you dont feel like having a mega roll one night.  They're not 100% accurate and I've definitely rolled harder on the same colored cap some nights because I think there might be an extra 10mg or less 10mg some times, but it's close enough that it'd be hard to overdose, plus it makes it really easy to organize your pills when distributing to friends.  It also makes it real easy to figure out what caps are good for a redose.  He doesn't really make a lot of the Yellows/Reds because only two of us actually can handle them, but even if those run out they can take 2 Blues, or a Blue/Green for the 150'ish dose.

Anyway just a suggestion for other people who cap their own supply, it's really effective and efficient :)

Take your Molly(ground to a powder) and dump it out on a surface where it's well contained(so you don't lose any).  The lid of a small box works well especially if you line it with gently curved foil(so as not to kink it and get MDMA stuck).

Separate your caps and hold the larger end between thumb and forefinger.
