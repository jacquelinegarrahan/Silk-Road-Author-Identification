Hey Lim - I know I've told you how to make a solution at a certain concentration before.  So I hope you remember how to do that.

Otherwise, what you need to do is figure out how much a blotter holds (in liquid).  Get a few sheets, put one into a dish that fits it, and drop water into it to see how much is required until the sheet is fully saturated.  For me I think it was something like 3ml.

Now, prepare a solution with your desired concentration.  If you want 100mg on the sheet...you want 33.3mg / ml (at least, in my example).  Drop the solution on, let it dry.  Done!

I personally went a step further and added food coloring to my solution to make sure that it dried evenly.  Don't want the edges being super intense or anything...
