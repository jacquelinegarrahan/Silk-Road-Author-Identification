Hey Quantaflux, that's how I take MDMA too.

Like yourself, relatively inexperienced, only used it a couple of times before, but I like to do it properly ;)


If you've evenly divvied up the gram between 14 capsules, you'll have ~70mg in each one.
From my experience, and Erowid - https://www.erowid.org/chemicals/mdma/mdma_dose.shtml
~100mg is a good hit when taken in 2 or 3 parts during a long evening.

So, my advice would be, take some of those 70mg caps and half them again.
Take 1 70mg capsule at the start of your night, and top up again later (at least an hour) with the ~35mg capsule.

Does that sound reasonable?
