Title: Re: yet another LSD storage thread
Post by: sourpatchkid on July 17, 2012, 03:19 pm
I keep mine in an altoid tin :)

This is my set up: tinfoil around the blotters, which are inserted into a tiny plastic bag (squeeze all possible air out), which is placed in an altoid tin can with a silica gel pack. This tin can is placed in a dark drawer closest to the floor. Though, I'll be hoarding as well soon, so I know I'll have to find a different way to store them because my tin isn't that large either.

As for the freezer method, I've read that works great on a lot of forums, though I have also read that it is overly-cautious and unnecessary. If you do plan to keep it in the freezer, I would still recommend to wrap in tinfoil, plastic, etc. Do whatever you have to do to keep light/air/moisture out! :)

Oh, and FRIDGE? Please tell me you meant freezer at least! There is soo much moisture in a refrigerator. You are facing the risk of condensation degrading your tabs. (EDIT: This is what I have heard on many forums, at least, and it seems plausible to me. Though, Erowid Answers doesn't mention anything about refrigerator condensation, so who knows!)

P.S. I'm an LSD newbie, but I've researched a lot a lot before trying the drug, so hopefully my advice will be of some help (though it's probably just the same information you've already read countless times, regurgitated) ;)

As for the fridge that's not a good idea.

Freezer - You could storage them in there, but allow to bottle it's stored in to return to room temperature before opening the bottle. If this route was taken I'd recommend the hits in a small jewelry like baggie all air pressed out, that wrapped in foil, then placed in a small glass amber vial. 

Alternatively I'd recommend the hits placed in a small baggie with air pressed out as previously mentioned, and that just placed in an amber glass vial.
