Title: Re: Drug storage?
Post by: Appa on May 24, 2012, 05:02 am
Everything you mentioned can be successfully stored at room temperature (20-25*C), so you can keep it all together if you wish.

The pills can be stored almost however you desire: most of the chemical is locked up inside the pill away from light and air, and those drugs are relatively stable anyway.  Putting them in an air/light-tight container would be a step up.

It is true that mescaline is quite stable.  You could just leave it as a powder in a zip baggie if you wish.  As before, air/light-tight can't hurt.  I like dosing it out into gel caps and putting them in a medicine bottle, zip baggie, or mason jar.

LSD is the most sensitive chemical listed by far.  As I'm sure you know, it's degraded easily by light, heat, and air.  The best way to store it is air/light-tight somewhere cool.  The "best" way to do that, according to most LSD enthusiasts, is to wrap it in aluminum foil, put it in something small and air-tight, and put it in the freezer (or refrigerator).  This is definitely effective. 
However, I disagree that blotter stored in a drawer for a year is "most likely inactive;" it might be, but depending on exactly how it was stored, it could still be about as potent as when it was purchased.  I have successfully kept blotter at room-temperature (simply wrapped in foil and put between the pages of a heavy book) for over three years, and have heard many reports of this method standing up to a decade of storage with no noticeable loss in potency.  20-25*C is not very hot, when it comes down to it.
So OP, depending on which side of OCD you're on (keep all the drugs stored together, or store things as "optimally" as possible?), you have a choice to make! :D
