I have partaked in Hatter's opana elixer several times.  It's a treat - a very expensive treat.  Quick background on me:  by the time August 2009 came I was on 180-240 mg oxycontin.  Detoxed and had been pretty much opiate free until 2011.  Due to ongoing pain issues, bought poppy pods to make tea.  I make it every few days, one cup at a time.

First tried Hatter's 5ml-5mg opana.  Did really nothing so I went to his 5ml-25mg.  Read up on his safety & warnings (much appreciated, Hatter!) and took the plunge (bad pun).  What I have discovered...
Due to my insane opiate thresh hold, it requires 2ml for a generous rush.  I worked up from .5ml.  The high does not last very long.  I can easily go through 10ml (2 bottles) in about 5 hours with very little dopiness after affect.

But this is the price we pay for IV usage.  A bigger rush in the beginning but half the time it stays in the system.  Which is why while in the hospital they will feed you narcotics on the hour.  That is all about the time it will stay in your system before it drops off.
