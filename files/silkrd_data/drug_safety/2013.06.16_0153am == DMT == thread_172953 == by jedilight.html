Title: Re: DMT
Post by: fiveotwo on June 16, 2013, 06:18 am
DMT is the OG strong 20 min psychedelic trip, it's usually extracted from bark, doses are like 30mg.  4-AcO-DMT is a synthetic psychedelic, very similar to shrooms (it's thought to metabolize to psilocin, one active component of shrooms).  Doses are around 10mg, and it is very much a whole different drug to DMT, the name can be misleading.  The effects are around 4 hours.  5-MeO-DMT effects are close to that of DMT, although I've heard less visual, with a slower comeup.  The doses are VERY small, as in around 5mg.
But seriously check out erowid before going into this blindly, and you should definitely have a milligram scale before buying. 

edit:  5-MeO-DMT is naturally occurring (but requires a complex extraction) like DMT, my mistake
