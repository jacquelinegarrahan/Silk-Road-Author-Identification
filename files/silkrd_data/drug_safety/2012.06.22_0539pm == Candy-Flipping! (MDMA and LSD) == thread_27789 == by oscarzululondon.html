Title: Candy-Flipping! (MDMA & LSD)
Post by: oscarzululondon on June 22, 2012, 05:39 pm
I've been a long time user of MDMA, but recently I've been candy flipping (taking MDMA & LSD at the same time) and some really strange things have started to happen, but not what I would have expected.

Before taking LSD but even with MDMA I used to have what would be considered very Right Wing (Conservative) political values, such as self sufficiency and running your own business, not using any kind of state benefits including healthcare, education etc, however after my first LSD session, and note this happened a few days after, not whilst I was in it, I started to have more what I would consider Socialist or left wing thoughts, just in every day, from walking past hobo's on the street and having a chat with them and giving them money even though I know they won't do good things with it to feeling sorry for people when their businesses go bust and having a desire to help for nothing in return. These are things I would never ever ever have done before.

After my third or so LSD trip other weird things started happening, like now I'm convinced God exists in some form and that everything is somehow connected, although I can't explain how. I understand how, but I don't know how to explain it to someone else. This is weird because I was the most atheist person I know and always used to defend atheism when having family debates etc and constantly criticized religion.

I've also noticed a few other weird things, like I was having an evening forest barbecue & bonfire party with my girlfriend and several friends last weekend, and everyone at the party was taking stuff, but only a few of us were taking LSD. However the people who took LSD, including me and my girlfriend could talk to each other without talking. It wasn't immediate, or even quantifiable, I think it happened after about 4 hours and lasted for about 2 hours, but I could know what they were thinking. Me and my friend even had a whole conversation in our heads from opposite sides of the fire (a good 20 meters), other people at the party confirm we never ever went near each other that night or talked together, but a few days later me and him had a chat and the fire conversation had actually happened. I knew things about him that he had never told me. How can this be? Am I just going crazy?

Other than that things are better than ever, my business is doing really well and physically I feel better than I have in years or decades even, I even look better, I have a glow I haven't had since childhood. I've been trying to research this stuff online but there's a severe lack of proper empirical scientific studies....
