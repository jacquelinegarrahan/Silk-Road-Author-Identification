Title: Re: How can I store hash oil?
Post by: dex on February 27, 2013, 02:20 am

Title: Re: How can I store hash oil?
Post by: Ben on February 27, 2013, 02:31 am
Thats pretty standard lab operation indeed, usually you would put a nitrogen atmosphere over it, seal the vial and put it in cold storage. In a lab environment that would probably mean putting it in a sealed vial in a -80C freezer, but i suppose you do not have that at home.

Next best thing is to use a container as small as possible to there is as little air in there as can be, and storing in in a -20C consumer freezer.

Title: Re: How can I store hash oil?
Post by: curiositymatrix on March 02, 2013, 08:05 pm
Not rez in the sense of combustion byproducts, it's actually largely THC - if you consider the composition of the vapor you'd get from kief, that's likely 80-90% THC. Slowly pipe it through a chilled glass tube, to capture it all, then run a plunger down it. You'll get oil with nearly zero contaminants that solvent extraction might include.
