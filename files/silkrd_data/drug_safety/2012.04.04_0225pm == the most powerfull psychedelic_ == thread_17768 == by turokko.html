If you are afraid of it, don't do it. It must be something you are mentally prepared for and ready for otherwise it can turn quite scary.

If you are interested but scared, start with a low dose of about 10-20mg to see how you feel. Post by: qualiyplant on April 12, 2012, 12:14 pm
IME

Psychadelics can be divided between man-made and natural

so man made would include: LSD, all the '2C' family, ketamine

natural: shrooms, LSA, DMT, Mescal etc.

IMO you are less likely to freak out on natural substances because they contain certain alkaloids that facilitate a good trip. However, if you DO have a bad one it is worse on the naturals then the man made.

Naturals mix badly with man made drugs - you see, LSD and MDMA is a classic combo but MDMA and shrooms is not great.

As to what is stronger its the wrong question. It's like you taking 5 of your best friends and asking the question: who has the 'most' personality. It doesn't make sense. Same with psychadelics they are each unique and incomparable in their own way. If you want to trip balls just take more - combine them if you want.

To be safe, its best to try them all in small amount and see which one works best for you.
