Post by: edgeoftruth on November 24, 2012, 10:27 pm
I've been taking Ability (2mg/day) for several weeks and I haven't noticed any changes to the effects of amphetamine, other than that it tends to even out my energy levels in general. Lately I've been taking the latter fairly regularly.

Wikipedia says Ability somewhat intensifies the euphoric effects of amphetamine and decreases those of cocaine. I haven't experienced this, but my dose of Abilify is rather low.

Abilify is a dopamine partial agonist, but I'm not experienced enough to know what kind of interactions this would cause.

My psychiatrist says there shouldn't be any dangerous interaction.
