Title: Re: Xanax powder into Blotters or Candy's WTF!
Post by: blowdrobro on September 26, 2013, 01:56 am
I use everclear, a insulin syringe, a mg scale, altoid mints, and a tiny jar. I weight out 10 mgs and draw out 10 ml of everclear with the syringe, I mix them together in the jar till it appears to be fully dissolved then I drip 1ml per mg on the mints for desire dosage. it evaporates within a day sitting on a table at room temp.
