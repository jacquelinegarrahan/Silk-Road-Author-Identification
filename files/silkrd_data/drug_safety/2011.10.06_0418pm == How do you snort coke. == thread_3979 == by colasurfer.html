Post by: Torn on October 13, 2011, 10:13 pm
Intecta, I'm with you brother. I enjoy both knowing and the process. I'm about to start measuring the same things.

So far, this thread has been more about "how much" and not generally "how". I'm really curious to compare notes on more of the general "how".

I use a hand grinder (the plastic one with a spring loaded plate that pushes it through metal mesh) and will typically mill it about three times. Doing this I can hardly hear any crunch if I try to chop it any further with a blade, so I think its getting to be about as fine as I can reasonably get it.

When I snort, I have a smallish diameter straw that I use. I used a lighter to dull the edges of it where I cut it so it doesn't cut me. I put it well into my nostril so that everything is delivered higher up and I don't waste any product immediately inside my nose where it won't really get absorbed. Another benefit of this is there's no white powder visible inside my nose.

I love the bullet, but I find that I have a white halo inside my nostrils when using it. I've made a very short straw just for bullet use (edges dulled again for safety). One end slips down into the bullet just over the rotating cup and the other end is just long enough to again go well up into my nostril. Doing this leaves no white halos in my nose.

Now for my questions: I tend to inhale at a medium-slow rate. I've read lots of different advice about this.  What's your preference?

What about line width? Skinny and long or fat and short? Unless you're paying TV or CFMF to send me an 8ball, fat and long isn't an option. I find that I have zero preference for the form of the line other than the enjoyment of just making lines, which gets us back to just enjoying the process.
