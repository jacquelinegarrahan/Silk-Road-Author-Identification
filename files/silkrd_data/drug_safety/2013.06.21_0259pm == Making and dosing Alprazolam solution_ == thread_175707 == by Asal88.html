Title: Re: Making and dosing Alprazolam solution?
Post by: tree on June 21, 2013, 09:47 pm
You can use vodka or grain alcohol to dissolve the alprazolam and use a syringe to dose.. It seems like the easiest way to me. You can also make blotters by dropping the measured dose on some blotters, that way you don't have to bother storing a solution and measuring some each time you want to dose. I'd recommend making a concentrated solution to make blotter, use like 0.1mL per blotter (easy to measure with a 1mL syringe).
