I wouldn't really suggest snorting it.  I imagine it would burn quite a bit.  And there isn't really any reason to do that.  Depending on your tolerance, put 20-30mg in some juice and drink it all.  It will take some time to take effect, probably a few hours, although you should feel something in the first hour.  That's why you have to be careful with methadone.  Some people take a dose, don't feel anything, then take some more, 3-4 hours later they are overdosing.
