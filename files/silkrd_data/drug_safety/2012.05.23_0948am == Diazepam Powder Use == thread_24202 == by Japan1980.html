Title: Re: Diazepam Powder Use
Post by: tedka on May 23, 2012, 12:54 pm
hi!
i had a gram of diazepam powder once and ended up putting most of it in 50mg capsules because its pretty hard to make a solution with household solvents.
if you dont want to cap it, putting your dose under your tounge, maybe with a little sugar and letting it melt in your mouth is an allright option. taking benzos sublingual doesnt really change the bioavailability, but can provide a great placebo effect and slightly improve the time till it takes effect, especially if your stomach is full. 

making a solution was really tricky. in 95% ethanol it would not properly dissolve at 250mg/25ml. there always was some at the bottom. what kind worked was isopropanol 99% (also 250mg/25ml), but only if you stored it somewhere moderately warm and shook it well before you got your dose out of the vial. in such small amounts, consuming IPA is no big deal. if it still bothers you, it evaporates very, very fast. if you want to drink it, you can first put the diazepam solution in a whisky glass (or something with a similar large bottom surface), stir it for a minute and add some juice to chug it down. the stirring should get rid of most of the IPA.

in general: unless you get a really sweet deal on the diazepam powder, have a good scale or access to better solvents; keep using pills, its all not worth the hassle :)

no problem, glad to help, better than having people running around doing stupid stuff :P

before i ended up capping the diazepam powder i just licked my finger, put it on the pile on the scale, licked my finger again, took a sip of juice (any juice will do) and started squishing it around in my mouth till it felt all dry and "pelted". if you put like 50mg of something with such bad solubility as diazepam in your mouth and distribute it evenly, there will be a certain sensation of dryness in your mouth. when this was archived, i swallowed the juice.

by the way, this is a great method to dose anything that doesnt qualify as an emetic in terms of taste or has horrible potency (not advised for properly dosed mdma or mephedrone). with most 2cs you can even save up to 50% of material for same(ish) effects, DOx compounds will start having effects withing a couple of minutes.
10mg of 2ce dosed in that manner will have similar effects as 20mg in a stomach working on a small meal. effects will also be "undeniable" in less than 15 minutes, rather than more than 45.
