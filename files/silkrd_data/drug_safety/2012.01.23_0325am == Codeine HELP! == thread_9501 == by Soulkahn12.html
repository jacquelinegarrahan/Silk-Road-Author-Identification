Title: Re: Codeine HELP!
Post by: wowzers on January 23, 2012, 05:55 pm
Codeine has the odd property of being more soluble in cold water than hot.
So what you can do, is mix the syrup up in boiling water, stick it through a coffee filter and let the filtrate cool.
Put the filtrate in the freezer for an hour but dont actually let it freeze. The apap will crystallise out, you can filter it again through a new coffee filter.
The remaining solution will just contain codeine (and sugar, colouring etc) which you can drink without fear of liver damage.

150mg is a good start with codeine if you have zero opiate tolerance.
