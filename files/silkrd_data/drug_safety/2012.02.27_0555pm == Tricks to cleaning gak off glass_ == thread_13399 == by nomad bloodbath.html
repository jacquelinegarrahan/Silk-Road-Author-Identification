Title: Re: Tricks to cleaning gak off glass?
Post by: preezak on February 27, 2012, 06:39 pm
Assuming its one of the good ones that has a thick bulb and not just a thick stem, just hold the bulb of the pipe over a gas stove turned up high for a few minutes, sometimes longer depending how dirty, but at such high temps everything will eventually burn off leaving the glass clear, you can even buy the portable camping stoves really cheap that just run off tins of butane.

Otherwise 'melamine foam' which is in commonly found in cleaning products like mr.clean 'magic eraser' works just as well.
