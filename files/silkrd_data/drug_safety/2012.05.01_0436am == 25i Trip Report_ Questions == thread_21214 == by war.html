Title: Re: 25i Trip Report/ Questions
Post by: telperion on May 01, 2012, 03:46 pm
I have been dosing, tripping, smoking, shrooming, rolling for 24 years, and I had some tabs from DR which I thought were 500ug so I took 2. It turns out they were 1mg ( 1000 ug ) tabs so I tabbed 2mg buccally. I held the tabs between cheek and gums for 40 minutes and then swallowed my saliva and continued to gnaw on the blotters until they disentegrated. It was a long come up ( around 55 minutes ). I felt the first alerts around 40 minutes, but at 75 minutes I was on my way to peak. It took roughly 45 minutes to get to peak with some significant body load ( read vassoconstriction/hypertension ), but after reaching peak, the body load chilled out and the effects of the drug came steady in waves for roughly four hours of plateau. I was able to eat during peak/plateau comfortably without affecting the ride ( sub sandwich and tater chips ). Now that was surprising.  I have never been able to eat on LSD, MDMA or shrooms, and eating on Solaris was EASY. I'm diabetic so that is a MAJOR plus for me and others in that boat. The trails from this substance are amazingly long and cause other visual distortions in their wake.  Played fetch with my dog for an hour and she was 9 feet long all over the back yard with that NEON green tennis ball bouncing along in multioles of 3 and 5 everywhere. These visuals were intense but not sustained. They came in waves about every 15 minutes or so. I never experienced any 'brain rape' like might happen on LSD. It seems to me it had a similar body rush and amphetemine type high as MDMA and the visuals aspect was more shroomy and warpy than electric like LSD.  The space was very pliable and plastic, and 'easy to drive' if that makes any sense. Not particulary spiritual or enlightening, but certainly an interresting molecule. Bit of a body load with the vassoconstricition/hypertension, so have plenty of weed to smoke so you can vasso-dilate to offset the circulatory tension. I've heard benadryl eases some of the tension on come up as well. Be sure to have sunglasses if you are a daytripper. My pupils have never been sooooo dilated ( well maybe at the eye doctor )!!!  So I think for most people 1000 ug might be a good starting point. I did 1.5 tabs ( 1500 ug )  8 days later and it didn't quite get me 'over the hump', so consider the source. I take 120mg of Cymbalta, an SSRI, and I never stopped taking my head meds and I had a great ride. Maybe I'm just used to the downstepping that my SSRI causes though. Like I said - consider the source. Be carefule. Learn about the molecule before you dive in. It is a potent HT2A antagonist, and that may be of some help to read about. The ability to hyper-metabolize this molecule is also potential ( I have read of people dosing at 5-8mg on this crazy stuff!!) That kind of dose I wouldn't be able to handle considering the 10 hour experience I had on 2mg.
