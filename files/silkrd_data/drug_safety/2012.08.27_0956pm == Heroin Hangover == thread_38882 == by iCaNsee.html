Title: Re: Heroin Hangover
Post by: ZenAndTheArt on August 28, 2012, 12:20 am
Because the strength of H can vary a lot and your tolerance (or lack of one) also plays a part, there are no real guidelines. Just start off small (like you did) wait 15+ mins before doing any more. Try to get an idea how strong it is. Unlike coke or some other drugs you don't have to get the amount right first time to get the best rush. You can build up by re-dosing until you feel as high as your comfortable with. Just don't rush it and overdose, the full effects can take up to half an hour to come on and then several hours to wear off. Like I said before, the strength can vary a lot. Some good H and 0.05g could get you really high (for someone who's new to it), but if it's crap H then you my need ten times that amount! Please take it slow though and you shouldn't do it on your own in case you do get into trouble and need help.

I really don't think you should ever get a headache from snorting H, unless it's cut with something bad. It's a painkiller.

Have fun, be safe. :)
