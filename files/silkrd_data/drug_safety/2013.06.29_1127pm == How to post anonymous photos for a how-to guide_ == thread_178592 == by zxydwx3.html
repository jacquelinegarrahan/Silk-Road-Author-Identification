Post by: b0lixtrader on June 30, 2013, 12:01 am
Wait I don't understand, you want others to help give you info so you can make a guide and get tips?

Hosting images anonymously is easy as hell.

For clearnet you can use this site:
http://www.anonmgur.com/

For darknet you can use this:
http://xqz3u5drneuzhaeo.onion/users/qicpic/

But before you upload your images you have to erase the exif data from the photos.

1. Open up image
2. Edit if needed
3. Take a screen shot
4. Paste into paint and crop if needed
5. Save as jpeg.

Finished.
