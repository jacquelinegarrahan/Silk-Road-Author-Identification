Title: Re: Can you snort the Dicod301 hydrocodone caps?
Post by: sourman on February 29, 2012, 12:57 am
Sorry for the thread necro; I thought I'd add my experience with these guys in case anyone is interested.

So a friend ordered some Dicod 301s from some South African pharmacy. We each took one about an hour and a half before this post was written, and it certainly feels like I'm on an opiate right now. It doesn't feel as strong as regular M305 Vicodin but then again I've been crazy buzy and haven't slept much in days. It relieves my muscle pain and soreness, which is all I really needed them for anyway.

For some reason, it doesn't feel like I can nod off these, at least not from just one. It seems like 10mg of confirmed US hydrocodone produces more of a 'high' than the supposed 15mg in these Dicods. The pills also varied in weight from 420mg to 550mg (includes capsule) and I took one of the lighter ones. My friend took one of the "stronger" ones and reported an overall better high than I did. He just took a 2nd one a few minutes ago and is starting to feel stronger opiate effects like itching and light nausea, despite whatever is in there that's supposed to lessen that.

No idea if you can snort it or not. Should have mentioned that earlier lol.

I will edit this post if necessary, including if and when I take one after actually getting some sleep.

EDIT:

Someone sent these in for testing at ecstasydata: http://www.ecstasydata.org/view.php?id=2440

Turns out they contain Codeine, Cyclozine, and Diphenhydramine. Although they don't truly contain hydrocodone, it seems that there is enough codeine in there to give a pleasant, drowsy buzz. The combination of all those damn anticholinergics plus my sleep deprivation certainly made it feel like I was nodding, although I doubt there was enough codeine in there to actually cause me to nod in a strictly opiate fashion. It definitely felt stronger than a Tylenol 3 and was better for my pain too, FWIW.

At this point, what's in those Dicod301s and the CMDs (100mg codeine + diazapam) is anyone's guess. The online pharmacy selling them swears that they come as advertised, claiming that ecstasydata's tests are not thorough enough to differentiate between Codeine and its derivatives.

FWIW, the Dicod301s do not feel like codeine to me personally. It feels more like hydrocodone than it does Tylenol 3s or Codeine/Promethazine syrup, but not like an actual 10mg Vicodin/APAP pill. Either it's a very high dose of Codeine along with all those antihistamines to make it feel different, or it's a low dose of hydrocodone, as in way less than 15mg. They absolutely do work for pain though; not as well as real Vicodin did for me, but take 2-3 Dicods and it's close enough. Watch out though, as the doses in the pills are NOT uniform at all. Some had 50-100mg more powder inside than other did, and those were much more potent.

Those red CMD pills were getting excellent reviews just a few weeks ago, but now my friend over at the OP scene tells me that recent shipments of the pills have been weak. I will ask him again today and see what he says.
