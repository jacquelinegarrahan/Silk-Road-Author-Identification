There's a lot of bad MDAI out there. The best MDAI I had was sparkly white. Pretty sure most tan MDAI is very iffy. 200mg bombed gave me a pretty strong high for 1.5-2 hours (total duration ~3-4.5hr). It's a more sedating roll than MDMA or mephedrone. It's very nice when combined with a high-dopamine drug like 4-MEC, 4-MMC.
