Title: Re: Heroin then Mephedrone
Post by: tree on June 01, 2013, 01:25 pm
I've never done H and drone specifically but I've done strong pharmaceutical opiates and stimulants (mostly speed, sometimes ritalin) the day after.. The euphoria from the stimulant isn't lessened in my experience if you did an opiate the day before. What can happen is that the stimulant is smoother if you have some after-effects from the opiate but that's it. Stimulants and opiates work on completely different mechanisms so there shouldn't be any effect like that.
From my experience and in theory, the mephedrone should only be affected by the afterglow/hangover. From what I've heard it should mostly be a hangover than afterglow with H so it may not be as enjoyable. If you have no hangover then it won't be affected at all, there shouldn't be any cross-tolerance but I may be missing something. Maybe the H depletes your dopamine a little so you'll need more mephedrone but in my experience that sort of thing doesn't happen. Taking both at the same time or taking H during the mephedrone comedown should be highly enjoyable though.
