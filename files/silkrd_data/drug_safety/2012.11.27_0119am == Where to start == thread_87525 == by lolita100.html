Title: Re: Where to start
Post by: lesseroftwoweevils on November 27, 2012, 01:48 am
That really depends on your age and the type of person you are. If you're willing to research the shit out of everything you do and take responsible doses, there's little I'd recommend that you avoid. If you're a mentally unstable and inexperienced 16 year-old who just wants to get "high", then I'd ask that you take it easy until you mature, both mentally and physically.

That being said, starting off with weed isn't a bad idea. Although I'm not a huge fan of it, marijuana is a fairly benign and safe drug. After that you can delve into psychedelics like LSD and mushrooms, although only if you're mentally healthy and in a good setting. MDMA is also a must, both for use in public settings (like a music festival) and alone for therapeutic purposes.

If you like feeling in control when you're under the influence, you could also check out stimulants like speed/adderall. If you don't mind having the trip take over, exploring other psychedelics is also good option.
