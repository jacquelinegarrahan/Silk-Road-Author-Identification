For someone who does not use regularly like yourself, 0.1 g is enough for several nights out, if your keeping it to yourself.
10-15 mg should be plenty (snorted). If you don't have a mg scale, this would be be a pile of powder smaller than a match head.
Start small. You may or may not feel the need to redose. If you redose, again 10-15 mg (match head size pile of crushed powder or smaller).

Do not consume 0.1 g all at once. I use frequently, often daily, and would never even think of taking that much at once. I've rarely needed to even approach that amount in a single day. More like 50-60 mg or less on average during periods of heavier use.

You'll see people reporting high doses all over here, but these these are frequent/heavy users.

I've never failed to get off for the night from a matchhead size bump or two. Any more and you're probably gonna have a bad time honestly.
