This is rubbish, there's only two types of DMT,  NN-DMT and 5-MEO-DMT.   The different colors of NN-DMT you see being sold depend on the solvents they used, whether or not they defatted during the initial step (if its synth'd dmt it will always be pure white), their solvent filtration process, if they recrystalized enough and how quickly they did, and lastly if they crystalized properly to begin with. This is a major factor in how it ends up as when DMT becomes too dense in a solution it crashes out into a red oil which you can dry into waxy crystals, which people call "jungle dmt", which guess what, is exactly like white/yellow dmt except because of its density it tends not to smoke as easily. This is easily remedied by taking a small sample of the red crystal and mixing it into a warm solution of naphtha and letting it sit at room temp for 24-48 hours, yellow crystals will naturally begin to form on the sides of the container. If you're really impatient you can stick it in the freezer which will give you a "white crystal powder" which is exactly the same as yellow and red dmt except its less dense! Yes, the coloration you see is largely to do with its density. The major reason you see red pigment to begin with at higher densities is that DMT is extracted from mimosa hostilis rootbark which produces a very deep red dye, so some of that helps color the crystal.

I dont know much about 5-meo except you cant extract it from anything safely, leaving you with colorado river toads (endangered) or synthesis.

Title: Re: DMT colour?
Post by: stoneyjonez on August 10, 2012, 11:37 pm
What up everyone. Kinda new here but I thought I could shed some light on this particular subject. DMT generally is made by extracting from a variety of different plants. I have personally extracted DMT myself several times, successfully. Mine was always a very light yellow for the first couple pulls, then the next few get a little bit darker because more plant oils are extracted along with the DMT. This doesn't necessarily weaken it or make it any worse in at all. On the other hand, if it is more orange than yellow, then it's more likely to be DMT-N-oxide, which still gives a very similar effect. Most of the time if you get some that is clear or white, it either means a more advanced extraction was used OR they did a very simple extraction like myself, and then recrystallized to purify it. I know the first time I made it, it was basically like a powder consistency, but sparkly. Then the second time I recrystallized and it was much clearer and looked more like meth. Bottom line: white, clear, or light yellow is what you want.
