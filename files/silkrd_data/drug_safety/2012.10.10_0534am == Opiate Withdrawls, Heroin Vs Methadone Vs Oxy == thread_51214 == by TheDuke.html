Post by: TheDuke on October 10, 2012, 05:34 am
For those with experience with a wide range of opiate withdrawals how would you compare them? Heroin has this horrible rep but I will take H withdrawals any day over methadone or oxycodone. I am a long time opiate user and have been through withdrawal many times. Some worse than others. First H withdrawal to me is the hardest hitting but shortest lasting. Oxycodone doesn't hit as hard but lasts me for weeks. Then there is methadone withdrawal which kicked the shit out of me for 6 weeks. Anyways if you have been through any sort of opiate withdrawal you know they are hell. If you have not been through opiate withdrawal just imagine the worse case of the flu possible combined with no sleep etc. Anyways im curious how others with experience would compare different forms of opiate withdrawal. I can't imagine anything being worse than methadone but maybe that's just me.
