Title: Re: Driving on acid
Post by: echo_ on January 30, 2012, 12:58 am
Guys— it's not about "what's stronger" it's about how acid affects YOU. Know yourself. You know whether you can drive or bike on acid or not. Me personally, I get much *better* at driving, because I feel like I have greater sensitivity to all of the parts of the car, more alertness, and a faster and more artistic mind. I understand competitive driving really well, from the side of being in the driver's seat, and I don't hallucinate at all.

HOWEVER, if you are the type of person, who takes acid and can barely function either from it just being overwhelmingly beautiful, or it taking apart your mind, then maybe you shouldn't drive, eh?

The question is not one of a generality, but one of a specificity. People experience different effects taking the same drug.
