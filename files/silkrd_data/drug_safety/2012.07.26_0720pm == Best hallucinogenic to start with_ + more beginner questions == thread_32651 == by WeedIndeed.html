I guess it would depend on what you are initially trying to get out of the experience. If you're looking just for a good visual stimulation I'd recommend the 25i compound -- It will be mild at 1mg and intense at 2mg but with a hint of euphoria effect to keep a solid mindset if shit gets weird :) 2C-B is also good for that but I don't have as much experience with it as the 25i.

Shrooms, done at night, have always been a more "gothic" style trip for me if anyone can relate to that, but have a lot more pronounced effect for creative thinking and personal insight. I haven't done LSD for years but I sure do remember the last time I did...  :-X

Edit: Pro tips
Start small. Its much easier to add more than taketh away.
Erowid Erowid Erowid. Knowledge is power. Know what to expect, how to prepare your setting and how the substance is affecting your body and mind.
If it becomes intense, concentrate on your breathing and observe your surroundings slowly with curious and scientific speculation; it has a very grounding effect.
Talk to yourself  :D
