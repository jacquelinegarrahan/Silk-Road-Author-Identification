Title: Most Hardcore Drugs
Post by: bynter on August 02, 2013, 09:29 pm
Well the other day when I had to go through my first mild benzo withdrawal(which sucked SOOOOOO much), I decided to look up a few Clearnet lists of "Worst/most hardcore/most dangerous drugs" to see how hardcore the public eye thinks benzos are. I knew most would probably be stupid to me, as I'm become really detached from the public drug ignorance since I found SR, but boy was I surprised. So few people were even aware of the concept of classifying drugs. There was one ten item list, with three desperate spots taken up by methadone, heroin, and oxycodone. For the ordered lists, meth was almost always within the top 3, with one quote I remember in my head. "Meth is perhaps the most physically devastating. Most meth addicts don't survive long.". LSD was within the top 6 on most, while Xanax was rarely ever past 8, if even on the list at all.

As childish as ordered lists are, I thought maybe the good community of SR might want to make an ACTUAL list.(though really this is just an excuse for me to post that little story I just wrote). I'll start. The criteria is most likely to fuck up your life/you should be careful with it.(and my ordering of the items is loose)

I: Freebase Cocaine - it's crack.

II: Benzos - Just read the Wikipedia page on benzo withdrawal. It's called, "Benzodiazapine Withdrawal Syndrome". Yeah, it's so fucking bad it's considered a syndrome.

III: Opiates - A few that come to mind as the most hardcore of their class are fentanyl, because of it's strength, heroin, because it's heroin, oxycodone, because it's basically heroin, and methadone, because it's basically heroin with a euphoria that you almost instantly become tolerant to.

IV: Ethanol - It has a very similar pharmacology to benzos

V: PCP - Because it's like a hyper-stimulant with severe dissociation

VI: MDPV - It's like meth, but can induce psychosis

VII: Meth - Really not that bad if you don't neglect your body or take neurotoxic doses.
