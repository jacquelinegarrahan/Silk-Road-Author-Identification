Title: Re: 25I + 25C Combination?
Post by: l1llykins on September 04, 2012, 02:37 am
No one can guarantee that you won't experience negative affects or even severe negative affects with this or any psychedelic regardless of the dose. It's the risk we take when we take new things or combinations. Some people feel horrible on some of these NBOMe's or combos, I'm unfortunate to feel horrible on all NBOMe's if I don't preload. And even then I have some discomfort during the come-up.

The dose you're planning is not unreasonable.

As I said in my pm, the good thing about insufflation is: come-up is a lot faster which is the time period where it might be uncomfortable. Make sure you have some music or TV going when you take it. If you don't need it, you can turn it off and do whatever you have planned for your trip. If you end up feeling really horrible, tell yourself that it will be over in about 15 minutes ... lie down, and make yourself comfortable until you start to feel good. Try to focus on the music/TV, think good thoughts, and once it passes --have a great trip!
