c) How functional would we be? Can we walk around campus, or should we just stay in our room? How obvious would we be to non-trippers?

You CAN walk around, (I receive the worst side affects from 25i of our whole group and its just muscle cramps in my legs) but being around people that aren't tripping feels really weird.  I would recommend you stay in your room and just enjoy the trip because you will end up talking about nature and you will really want to go run around in the trees but promise yourselves beforehand that you are just gonna remain low key in your rooms. 

d) Would you recommend some weed on the come down, or will that prolong/intensify it?

I think weed and 25i is REALLY interesting and something that hasn't been discovered yet.  It seems that smoking weed on the comedown WILL prolong it and if you are going for lots of visuals smoking during the trip will help too.  Personally I have never smoked during the trip but noticed that smoking a few hours after comedown brought back some mild affects.
