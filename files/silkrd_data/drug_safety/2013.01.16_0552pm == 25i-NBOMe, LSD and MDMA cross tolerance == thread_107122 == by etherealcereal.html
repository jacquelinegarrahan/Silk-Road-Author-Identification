Title: Re: 25i-NBOMe, LSD and MDMA cross tolerance
Post by: Ballzinator on January 16, 2013, 09:41 pm
MDMA is not a 5-HT2A agonist but a serotonin releasing agent. It might give you a slight tolerance on all serotonin receptors but not enough to ruin the effects of other psychedelics so you should take MDMA first.
LSD and 25i, on the other hand, are two of the most powerful known 5-HT2A agonists so you might want to decide between one or the other. If you want to do both though, don't do the 25i first. You don't want to waste your LSD.
So I'd do it in that order:
MDMA
LSD
25i
