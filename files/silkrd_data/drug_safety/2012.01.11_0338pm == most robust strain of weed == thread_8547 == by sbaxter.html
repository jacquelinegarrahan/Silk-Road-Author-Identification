Title: Re: most robust strain of weed
Post by: xx138xx on January 11, 2012, 07:18 pm
I might catch flak for this but in light of you stating your light schedule might be chaotic, I'm going to suggest either Iranian autoflower by Dr. Greenthumb or DJ Short's Blueberry. These two flower regardless of light cycle, have a short grow time, and stay nice and short. Trying to grow normal plants without a set light schedule will get you a bunch of hermed out seedy bud because they need a set amount of darkness during flowering. Autoflowering plants can literally be grown with the lights on 24 hours a day after they root and veg. Because of this you can have a rotating crop growing in a small space and not have to worry about having separate veg and flowering chambers.
