Title: LSD+weed bad combo?
Post by: pulpfictionbro on November 01, 2011, 07:48 pm
So i had taken some lsd and weed about a month ago and had the most terrifying, profound and deep trip ever. Although it wasnt my first time on either drug it was my first time mixing the two.

This past weekend i repeated this but was keeping tabs on how i was feeling. So here's what happened (written in 1st person),

1 pm - first lsd tab
2 pm - minor lsd effects, minor visualizations, high heart beat rate, overall calm and feeling good.
2:30 pm - second lsd tab since the first one didnt do much.
3:30 pm - feeling awesome and high.
4:15 pm - Smoked the first bowl.
5 pm - Enhanced lsd effects, probably the peak of the trip?
5:30 pm - Not feeling great anymore, dark shadows in corners, paranoid, delusional, not able to concentrate on a single thing.  I think the marijuana makes it harder to keep control very confused.
6 pm - all lights are on, beginnings of a bad trip (same as the one a month ago), trying to be rational and sane but its hard. Watching tv but the character on the tv looks scary. What are they even talking about? Cant concentrate, multiple thoughts at the same time, lost in my own mind. Who am i? Getting a sinking feeling in my stomach, naseous maybe because i am hungry? Havent had anything since 1.
7 pm - get a grip, dont lose yourself, this has happened before you can get through.
8 pm - looks like the thc is wearing off, feeling better now
9 pm - In control, lsd is still active but not scared anymore

After that i just rode it out easily so i think maybe the weed is what effects the trip or did i take too much? It was only after smoking the weed that i started having bad ideas and sorta lost control and thats when the lsd takes over and really messes me up.

Am i the only one? I know bad trips occur for a number of reasons but i have taken lsd a number of times in the past and not had any bad experiences its only when i combine the two when shit happens.
