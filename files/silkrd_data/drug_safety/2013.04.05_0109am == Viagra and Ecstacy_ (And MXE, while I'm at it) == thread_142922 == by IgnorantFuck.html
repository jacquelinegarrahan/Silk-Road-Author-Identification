The short answer is it's not a recommended combo, especially if you might have any cardiovascular issues, but plenty of guys have done it (in moderation at least) and lived to tell the tale. I did it once, and my gf said never again...it was simply too much and left her very sore.  :o

I'd say most guys struggle to perform adequately on E, but there are always lucky exceptions, and they tend to give a misleading impression... Perhaps you just need to find a better combo rather than force anything? I'm looking forward to trying some "Moxy" very soon (5-Meo-Mipt) as it's basically described as "MDMA for lovers" ;-))

You don't say how old you are, but you should be aware that anecdotally at least, younger healthy men who use viagra on a regular basis can begin to experience ED when not taking it. The body has weird ways of letting you know it's annoyed with your behaviour...

Also worth thinking about is where you go after you've had the wildest most loved-up sex imaginable, fuelled by whatever crazy combo. Plain old vanilla sex can start to seem unappetizing, and this can lead to issues of another kind. Personally, mephedrone was the wildest sex drug I ever tried. Used to call it "dirty coke", which it kind of is, but the sex was wild, uninhibited, and completely without problems! Unlike e.g. real coke... However, meph consumes you eventually, so again, best as an occasional treat.

MXE on the other hand, lovely stuff! Have used it lots (insufflated) with all sorts of things, and never any issues. Swallowed some once (by accident) and that wasn't too good, left me seriously monged out for a few hours. Just remember that it has a long half-life (about 4 hours back to baseline) so don't stack it too quickly or you can find yourself in a strange place. Seems to be about neutral for sex for me, not especially horned up, but not incapable either.

Have fun, be safe, and make videos!  8)
