Post by: NewStem on September 11, 2013, 09:21 pm
It's interesting, that is how I've nearly always experienced and enjoyed MDMA. Psychedelics make me dance, MDMA chills me way out (at least back when I did it). When I did want to dance in those times, I would find some hits of "ecstasy", because it had what I like to call the "party mix". With the party mix out of the picture, I think you'd be looking at proper pre-loading, to which there are many guides on the web. I would think that, if dopamine is the main neurotransmitter involved in the speediness of the experience, you could consider pre-loading with L-tyrosine. Tyrosine is an amino acid and dopamine precursor, and is available in health stores. I would check that for consensus.

A light psychedelic would maybe find you the grooviness in the music. I'm not a big fan of mixing and would just side with acid any day of the week, but that is a different experience all together.
