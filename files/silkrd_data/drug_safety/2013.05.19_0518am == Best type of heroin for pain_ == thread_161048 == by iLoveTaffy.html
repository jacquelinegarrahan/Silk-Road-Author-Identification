In theory heroin is heroin is heroin.  The cuts might have some of an effect, but the base molecule is the same regardless of tar, powder, 4,3, etc.  Tar is thought to have more 3 and 6 monoacetylmorphine in it, so it might have a different feeling to it then the others.

Heroin 4, china white, ecp whatever you want to call it, is generally thought to be more pure then the other forms of heroin.  You can cut anything though, so the answer isn't as straightforward as you probably want.
