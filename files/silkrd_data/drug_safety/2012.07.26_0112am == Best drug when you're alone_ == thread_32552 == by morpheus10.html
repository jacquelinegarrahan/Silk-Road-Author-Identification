Post by: arutha on July 27, 2012, 04:12 am
DMT is a very safe option because it occurs in your body anyway, if you are a bit worried/anxious (understandably!) take a low dose to start with. In fact to be honest, it is very harsh so your first few times with DMT you might not be able to break through to the full on experience even if you try. Get 20-30mg, smoke/vape (don't burn it directly, research techniques first) and you will find colours go crazy, patterns start moving and appearing everywhere, but you probably won't get any full on hallucinations. Best of all, if you don't like it, you're done in 10-20 minutes. If you get close to breakthrough, you may start feeling a bit uncomfortable almost like you are going to feint, don't try to walk during or directly after!

Low amount of LSD would be very close second option for me. ~100ug and you probably won't even hallucinate. It is quite mild and gentle at low doses, and if the need arises to appear normal around people, its not too hard as long as you don't have to do much. It will obviously last a lot longer though.

Most importantly, as mentioned, start small. Also very important, do loads and loads of research first. The better informed you are the less nervous you will be, and the more relaxed the better the experience.

Post by: flyinghigh1660 on August 15, 2012, 10:52 am
I mostly trip alone since starting out with DMT as my first psychoactive drug. Go in at the deep end and swim towards the shallows were my initial thoughts. Who can find 12 hours to trip on LSD with a family life? Because of the family life and disapproving partner, I trip/use drugs alone or with a close trip buddy if I can. Using drugs alone alone can be great fun, rewarding and also a little scary.

So your selections depend hugely on your personal life circumstances.

In my case I use canabis, DMT, 2C-B, LSD, and mild ayahuasca. XTC  alone can be ok, but it is a very social drug, and while 2C-B is great socially it can also be good alone.

If tripping or using any drug alone there are some key points that I consider.

I am always over cautions with the dose amount, for anything other than cannabis, for the obvious reasons. Large does of any of the other drugs can lead one into issues when alone.

I am careful with the desire to re-dose, you should set your limits before you trip decide what you are comfortable with and stick to it. Re-dosing alone can lead to some nasty outcomes. I have done the usual locking myself out of my apartment with drug paraphernalia laying around after caving into a desire to re-dose. Much better to dose, re dose to a set plan and increase the next time rather than chase a high this time.

Life is long, you will get another chance blah blah blah

I chose my time. place and setting carefully. In the locking out of the apartment, it was sunny, my partner was in another country so I knew that I wasn't going to be faced with an argument, time at the relationship shrink. As my recreation drug use has caused before   ;)

I know that a good friend who if I warn that I may be tripping using drugs alone will never refuse a call from me. This has come in handy on the re-dosing issue. Choose this person carefully, one who has experience of the drugs you are taking who won't be judgmental, and will be sober enough to be able to help you out.  In general we are bad intuitive decision makers when outside of our comfortable normal mental state.

Using any drug alone is about reducing the potential harm not just to you but to others. Sorry to sound like a middle aged bore here, but you will make mistakes taking drugs alone if you do not consider the risks.

You'll make mistakes taking drugs socially as well, but there are few old bold drug takers, as there are few young bold ones for long either... ;)

Edit: Yes and I give the above talk to my teenage daughter as well  ::)
