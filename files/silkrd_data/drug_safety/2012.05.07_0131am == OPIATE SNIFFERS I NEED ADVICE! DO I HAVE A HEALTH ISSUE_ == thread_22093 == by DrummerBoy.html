Title: Re: OPIATE SNIFFERS I NEED ADVICE! DO I HAVE A HEALTH ISSUE?
Post by: Banjo on May 09, 2012, 07:54 pm
Oxycodone lowers blood pressure, so if you already have low blood pressure, or poor circulation, then taking oxycodone will exaggerate this effect, which is why your extremities feel cold. Low blood pressure can be pretty serious, as it increases your risk of overdosing. I'm not saying that sniffing a pill here and there is going to make you OD, just that you're at higher risk for it.

I'd go get a blood pressure test at a pharmacy, and if it's seriously low, make a doctor's appointment, or at least be careful when doing opiates or sedatives.
