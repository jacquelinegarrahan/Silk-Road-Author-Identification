Shulgin spoke very highly of 2c-b on an MDMA comedown, claiming it was a brilliant and therapeutic combo.  It sounds like you had a good experience on the two. 

35mg oral is a pretty hefty dosage and 30mg intransal is no joke at all.  Assuming you had clean 2c-b this also shows just how benevolent 2c-b really is.  A 35mg oral dose of 2c-e would likely have slapped you silly, and 30mg of 2c-t-7 going up your nose can kill you. 

What exactly were you expecting from 2c-b?  It's not the most visual of the 2c's: in my experience both -e and -t-7 give you a better visual experience.  OTOH, I find it the most euphoric and easygoing of the 2c-s, with a pleasant glow instead of an uncomfortable body load and a generally nice headspace. It's also one of the most forgiving phenethylamines and, for me, one of the least likely to give you a bad trip.
