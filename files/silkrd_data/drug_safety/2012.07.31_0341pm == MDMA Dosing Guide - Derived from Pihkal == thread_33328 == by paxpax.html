Hey Everyone,
  I thought I would post a chart for anyone curious on how to properly dose on MDMA. I did not create the chart but it is based on Shuligan Pihkal - 109. The recommendations are sound and seem to produce the best effects with the least amount of hangover. It is worth noting that two doses should be used. An initial does and a booster after 1.5 hours of taking initial dose. It is worth noting the booster is taken 1.5 hours after dose and not 1.5 hours after effects are felt. For the math guys out there dosing is 1.5mg/kg initial and .5mg/kg booster

House:
  Purity and exp certainly play a roll in dosage. If one is at the point of having to take massive doses to achieve a decent roll it is probably time switch out MDMA for another favored psychedelic for some time. IIRC overdose / toxic levels for humans is close to 3 grams (citation needed) so it is possible to take a very large dose without setting a meeting with St Peter, although doses over the levels recommended above have diminishing returns and greatly exasperate side effect symptoms.
  Personally I weigh 200 pds, my preferred dose 160/50, total dose 210mg, about 15% over recommended. I also allow almost 2 months between sessions.
  A simple anaology. View your brain as a sponge soaked in water. A proper squeeze will wring the sponge of a majority of the absorbed water with very little effort. The Sponge will spring back and be none the worse for wear. Sure you could go ape shit on the sponge and get out every last drop, but what would that do to the sponge?

Post by: paxpax on July 31, 2012, 09:11 pm
Hi Tom:
  It depends on the depression and more so on any medication you may be taking to combat depression.

First, if you are currently taking an SSRI then you will be unable to have any effect from MDMA and you also risk seratonin syndrome, which is possibly life threatening. It has been postulated that users with a lengthy past history of using an SSRI or MAOI may have their receptors  permanently modified to the point that MDMA will no longer bind. 

If you are currently on anti-depressants you would need to stop medicating. Prozac, by far the most commonly prescribed unfortunately has the longest half life. A min of 30 days would be needed in an otherwise healthy individual,

If you are diagnosed as depressed and not on medication it would depend on how your brain chemistry differs from that of a non depressed individual. To borrow from my sponge analogy earlier: Your sponge may hold less water. But it still holds water so you should have some type of effect.

My guess is either you are currently on anti-depressants, are only recently off of them or had porr quality MDMA.
