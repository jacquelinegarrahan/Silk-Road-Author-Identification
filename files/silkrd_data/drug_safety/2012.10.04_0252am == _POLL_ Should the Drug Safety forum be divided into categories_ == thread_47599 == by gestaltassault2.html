Title: Re: *POLL* Should the Drug Safety forum be divided into categories?
Post by: Ben on October 06, 2012, 12:43 am
Quote from: BobSacamano1 on October 05, 2012, 01:52 am
That's way more complicated Ben.  Speaking from experience on other drug forums, basic categorization of substances, with similar enough substances grouped together, is the best way to go just as the thread starter suggested.

The downside of that is that it will be hard to categorize posts that concern combinations of drugs. If someone wanted to know about, for example, using a benzo to end a bad trip caused by lsd, should that be in the hallucinogen section, or in the benzo section?

Many posts here deal with combinations of substances that would fall in different categories in the suggested list. This is why i would prefer a split in 'simple drug use' and 'complex/experimental use', the latter dealing with combinations of substances and perhaps idiotically large doses of a single substance as well.

My reasoning is that people are likely to want to read about what will happen to them when they take a tablet of valium, xtc (or two), separated from the question of what will happen to you if you took all kinds of drugs in one session and downed them with a glass of vodka. In either classification system there will be overlap, but i think that splitting it by substance would somehow require topics to be visible in two or more boards - i'm not sure if that is technically feasible using the forum software - i'm presuming it is not.
