Title: Re: Reagent Test Questions
Post by: phunky on September 09, 2013, 01:38 am
Get them from Dancesafe.org -- they are legal and test for a variety of substances.  You can read all about the instructions on them there.  It is really important to get and do all of the tests, IMO.  They are $20 each or $65 for all 4.   But you should also read up on the reagents because they don't provide all of the information there.  For example, Simon A/B reacts to secondary -amines (like methamphetamine).  Their chart shows a few of them but not all of them.  It's worth doing a little homework to understand what each reagent actually reacts to in more technical terms.  You don't need a degree in chemistry to learn what they are capable of and what they're not capable of.

Each test can identify usually a set of substances, but with the additional data of subsequent tests, it becomes possible to rule out certain ones and confirm others.
