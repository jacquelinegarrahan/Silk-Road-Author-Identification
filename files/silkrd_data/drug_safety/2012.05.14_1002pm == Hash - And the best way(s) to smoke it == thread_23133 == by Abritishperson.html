Title: Re: Hash - And the best way(s) to smoke it
Post by: ProudCannabian on May 14, 2012, 11:32 pm
Best way to smoke hash without wasting any on drifting smoke and charred lumps of shit in your pipe, is blades.
Grab one of those propane plumbing torches from a hardware store.
Cut a large soda bottle so you can put the small end in your mouth, and the cut part of the body is your cone.
Pinch of small 1-hit beads of hash (I like to do 15-20 of these at a sitting) that will be enough to fill your lungs.
Heat up two butter knives in the torch until red-hot.
Tap one of the beads with a hot knife and squash it between the two blades, just under your cone, as you inhale deeply.
Scrape knives together to remove ash/char and repeat.
When you are high as fuck, do one more and you should be good.

The best way to do it is bottle tokes!

Make little pieces of hash and set them on a tray like a cd jewel case or book or something.

Take an empty plastic bottle with a cap and burn one hole in the side big enough to place a cigarette through.

Light a cigarette.

Touch the tip of the cigarette to a piece of hash so it sticks, blow it once or twice to get it streaming smoke, and insert it into the bottle.

Watch it burn.

Take the cigarette out and put it down.

Unscrew the cap and inhale.

Repeat.

Post by: Spedly on May 19, 2012, 06:20 am
I just use a plain 'ol water pipe. Break the hash into several small balls, put them in the pipe, and add a little heat. It's not as efficient as hot knives but I feel it's a more casual, calming way to imbibe.

Hot knives are great but you can go overboard pretty quickly if you have very good hash. That's not a lot of fun.

Another really good way to smoke it (if you have lots of product and a few people) is to make a volcano. Roll the hash out as flat as possible, then roll it into a hollow cone shape. Stick a safety pin through the top and light it from the bottom. Smoke it using a hooter of some sort (empty pen, straw, etc.) When done right this is a fun, sociable way of smoking it. But again, it's very easy to overdo it. I've seen people literally turn green from smoking hash using this method.

I don't quite understand why some people like to roll it with tobacco. The thing I love the most about smoking hash is the taste. I wouldn't want to wreck that spicy flavor by adding tobacco.
