Post by: Addy on September 27, 2012, 12:41 pm
Just a friendly reminder to be careful where you buy your tools. Whether it's precursor, lab equipment, pill presses, etc. you must be cautious, especially on sites like eBay. Your name is on your PayPal account, and making any purchase leaves a paper trail. Also, who's to say that the vendor isn't being paid (or required) to report all sales to the govt.? You don't want your name on some "Pill Press" list in some database, especially if you get caught one day.
