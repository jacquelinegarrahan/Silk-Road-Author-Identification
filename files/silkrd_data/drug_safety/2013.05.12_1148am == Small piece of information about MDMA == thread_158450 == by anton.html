Title: Small piece of information about MDMA
Post by: anton on May 12, 2013, 11:48 am
An extract from "ECSTASY: THE COMPLETE GUIDE"

MDMA also known as Ecstasy (X, XTC, E, Rolls), is a semi-synthetic drug,
since it is related to many chemicals found in nature. The tree that gives us
nutmeg and mace (IVlyristicafragrans), as well as its essential oil safrole, is the
most commonly known relative of MDMA, but the sassafras root is, in fact,
a much more potent source of safrole. Safrole is the major natural precursor
in the synthesis of MDMA. Other natural elements that have chemical simi-
larities to MDMA are parsley, dill, and the calamus root.

I am interested to find out more about the parsley and dill, and if it is in-fact possible to get high on these herbs!

Did a little digging on parsley...
!!!!!!!!CLEARNET WARNING!!!!!!!!!!!
http://www.erowid.org/archive/rhodium/chemistry/dmmda.shulgin.html   
http://wiki.tripsit.me/wiki/Parsley   

Drugs CAN be made from parsley oil (The entire plant contains an essential oil consisting of myristicin, p-apiol)
DMMDA was synthesized directly from apiole (obtained from oil of parsley) using the same sequence of steps (isomerization, beta-nitration, and hydrogenation) that was successful in the conversion of myristicin to MMDA

So yes... drugs from parsley are possible but it appears yields are very low
