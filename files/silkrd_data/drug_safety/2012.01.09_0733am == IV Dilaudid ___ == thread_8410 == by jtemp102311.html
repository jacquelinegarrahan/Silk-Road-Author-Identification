Post by: A. Dubois on January 25, 2012, 06:29 pm
For years it was popular to "dry shake" dilaudid. People would just add water and the crushed pill into the body of the syringe itself, then just shake it up and inject all of it. People thought they were getting it all because there are sometimes as many as 5 "washes" left in the powder when you use a spoon and filter to draw it up. The problem is that all this buffer was ending up in people's lungs and causing massive scarring and eventually death. That is in heavy users of course. Plus I myself like the washes. It gives me something to put back and have when times get tight.
