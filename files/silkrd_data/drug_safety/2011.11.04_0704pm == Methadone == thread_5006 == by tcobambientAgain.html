Title: Re: Methadone
Post by: sandycheeks on November 09, 2011, 01:32 am
I use methadone recreationally regularly. I have a solid job that requires me to be 'with it' and so far it has provided the perfect mix of being high and being able to function. my 'better half' also uses it, at the same rate i do and loves it just the same. we buy it every other week and use for about 9 days and then take a break until it's time to re-up.

it took a month or 2 to figure out the right dose for us, but we stick with 20mg per day in the morning and that's it. sometimes to make it last, we just do 10mg, and because of the long half-life and the lasting effects from the day before, it's enough to keep us going.

MY WARNING: i got my stuff from a friend that had been on a maintenance program for a long time. he takes a ton, everyday. my first couple weeks i was taking 30 mg. i couldn't keep my eyes open while driving at night, i would fall asleep smoking cigarettes outside.... then someone recorded me while i slept, and we realized it was giving me apnea (i would stop breathing in my sleep for 30-60 secs, then gasp for air, then do it again, ect... ). It was quite the reality check, and caused me to lower my dose and be happy with it. afaik these results aren't typical, but methadone isn't a weak drug. it gets people off heroin, it's used for pain control because of it's longevity, and recreationally it provides a great bang for the buck. just be careful, because it's easy to underestimate its potency, and (to put it in layman's) it blocks your ability to get high on other opioids for up to 72 hours, which causes a lot of addicts to OD (they try to 'break thru' the 'block' by taking lots of dope)...

just my thoughts and opinions, good luck!
