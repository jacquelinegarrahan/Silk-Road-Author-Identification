Post by: JackBlue on April 21, 2013, 09:18 pm
GBL actually is more potent per ml so it should have had more effect that the equivalent GHB.  But you also really don't want to be doing GBL.  It's really hard on your stomach (it also melts some kinds of plastics -- it's a solvent).  It's easy to convert it to GHB which is a lot less harmful.  In doing the conversion you end up with more grams of GHB that the GBL you started with, so it's
 really what you should do with GBL.
 
 If you didn't get an effect from the GBL you did, I'd say it was probably fake.  It would have tasted pretty damn nasty if you were drinking straight gbl.  And the mls you were taking, if not diluted or totally fake, would definitely have worked.  DO NOT EVER COMBINE WITH ALCOHOL!

Quote from: pillbuyer on April 21, 2013, 09:14 pm

duffman: afaik, gbl is ghb converted to liquid
GBL is not GHB converted to liquid.  GBL is an industrial solvent used in a lot of manufacturing proceses that also happens to have a drug-like effect when ingested.  It's really not good for you but is relatively easy to convert to GHB.   Recipe can be found on Erowid.  GBL 99.9% is produced in China and in Germany by BASF, and is a liquid.

GHB is commonly seen in solid or liquid form.  In solid form, it's either the potassium or sodium salt form (depending on whether you use KOH or NaOH to produce it from GBL.  It's commonly diluted with H20 for convenient dosing.  GHB is much less harmful than GBL on your system.  So, GBL is a liquid.  GHB is a white powder by can be sold in either solid or liquid form.  If it's sold as a liquid, the key thing you need to know is dilution.  How many grams of GHB are in what volume of liquid.  This stuff kills people all the time if they don't know what they are doing.  Happened a lot in the US before they effectively got rid of it here.   Almost impossible to get here these days.  NEVER combine it with alcohol.  The short answer is you can pass out, vomit and choke on your own vomit.   Not a nice way to go.  At big parties in the US for a while virtually every ambulance was due to combination of GHB and alcohol.  And the dosage range where it has an effect and the dosage that's too much is pretty narrow.  Be very careful with this drug.
