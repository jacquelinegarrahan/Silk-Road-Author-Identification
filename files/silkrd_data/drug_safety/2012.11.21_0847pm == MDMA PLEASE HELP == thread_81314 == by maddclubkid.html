Quote from: AnimusVox on November 04, 2012, 08:47 pm
Please, for the sake of your health and cognitive functioning, spread your rolls out at least 2-3 months. The reasoning is simple. MDMA releases then depletes serotonin in vast amounts and inhibits the enzyme responsible for producing more serotonin, tryptophan hydroxylase (TPH). The inhibition is permanent, so the body must compensate by creating more and this restoration typically takes 1-3 months. Taking MDMA prior to this restoration process is altogether stupid. Your body's reproduction of serotonin is already inhibited and by taking the substance during this healing period will start the entire process over.

MDMA is a psychedelic drug... It's effects totally depend on your state of mind.

I've taken MDMA 6 times.

I want to talk to you about some of my trips.

My first 3 trips were fucking amazing.

On my 4'th trip, my girlfriend broke up with me in the middle of my roll, cause she's against drugs and shit, whatever. While I didn't get depressed, that shit totally killed my roll, and I had a solid 2-3 hours left.
While before, I was dancing like crazy and feeling like the king of the world, after the breakup-talk... I completely sobered up in about 10 seconds and the rest of my roll I was left thinking: "Wtf just happened? Is this MDMA crap? But I've taken it before and it worked, why am I not feeling ANYTHING now? It must be my girlfriend! Fuck, I need to find another girl around here. - Then my trip was to find another girl to hookup with - to no avail. The rest of my trip I just felt almost normal, except for the teeth grinding and huge pupils... no euphoria no nothing"

After that, I took MDMA again. I was in a normal state of mind, wasn't upset or anything. And it worked like magic, the feelings came back and I, once again, felt like nothing could stop me.

Then, the last time I took MDMA was... guess when... After I broke up with my girlfriend AGAIN. I had 4 hours of wondering why the fuck isn't the pill working (it was a ferrari/shield, on which I have rolled before and had amazing times).... In the 5th hour I finally realized that I was trying to justify giving her up but I just couldn't... I loved her & shit. So after I made the decision of getting back together with her, I had 1 hour of euphoria, just like that - SNAP.

So a roll, really depends on your state of mind and expectations.
You really have to not be sad, and most important, not have worries on your mind. Worries will kill your roll.

You have to let go of all your fears, worries and problems and just embrace the beauty of life. That is, if you wanna roll balls.

MDMA is not like cocaine, although similar in effects. Cocaine will make you feel like the shit even if you're super depressed & crap. But MDMA won't do that, if anything it will amplify whatever feeling you have.... it's more like LSD in this matter.

Hope this helps.
