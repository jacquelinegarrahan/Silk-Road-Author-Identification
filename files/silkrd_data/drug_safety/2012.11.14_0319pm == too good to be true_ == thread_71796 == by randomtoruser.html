Post by: Ben on November 16, 2012, 01:46 am
Typical reagent kits do not test concentration though, just presence. A powder mixture containing 1% cocaine and 99% inactive crap would still result in a positive reagent test result, but worthless.

As for the bulk price of cocaine: It entirely depends on where it is located. A kilogram some the exact same stuff can have a value of $1000 in the country of production, or of $80.000 once it reaches a major us city and is sold by the gram.

Without analysis its hard to tell what you have there, but there is no reason why a kilogram of cocaine could not be available for $6000. It would just be silly to sell it at such a low price within the US.
