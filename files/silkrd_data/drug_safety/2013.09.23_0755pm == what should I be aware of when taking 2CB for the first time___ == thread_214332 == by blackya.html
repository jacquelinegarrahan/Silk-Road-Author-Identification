2C-B is like a trippy disco-themed roll in my opinion. You'll feel great, be pretty euphoric, and be in love with colors and life. Generally positive moods and thoughts but if it gets a bit dark, just move to a new setting or scenery. It's really easy to pull out of a negative headspace with this one.

Quote
it is not as deep as LSD, right?
Nope.
Quote
is it safe to take it alone at home?
Sure, but you *might* want some company to share the amazing feelings you're having. Maybe some sexytime or just some bonding conversation or dancing or taking a nice walk :)
Quote
is it really something between LSD and MDMA but on its own way and also not so intense considering the provided dosage of 19mg???
Yes, this is a pretty good common dose for 2c-b.
Quote
Any other helpful ideas, guys?

Be ready for a possibly rocky comeup. Be somewhere comfortable with pillows and blankets like if you were taking MDMA. Have a selection of music ready, because music definitely colors this experience greatly. Think about possibly taking a walk somewhere. This drug enhances just about anything you see and changing scenery is a very welcome feeling. Once you start walking you won't want to stop :)

If your 2C-B is clean, then you should be able to sleep pretty easily after about 4-6 hours.

Have a great time!!
