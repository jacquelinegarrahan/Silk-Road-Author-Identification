My personal advice, however, would be to combine the MDMA and 2C-B on a single day when your favorite artists will be performing. I have found taking 160mg of MDMA followed by 20mg of 2C-B T+4:00 to offer the most amazing and all around rewarding experience. I would also limit your alcohol consumption leading up to doing this in order to avoid a hangover affecting your trip. Be sure you eat plenty of healthy food (fruits, vegetables, soup) both before and after tripping and pump yourself with liquids (within moderation). May I ask what you will be pre and postloading with? Also, do you have access to any LSD? LSD is one of the greatest substances for festivals in my experience.
