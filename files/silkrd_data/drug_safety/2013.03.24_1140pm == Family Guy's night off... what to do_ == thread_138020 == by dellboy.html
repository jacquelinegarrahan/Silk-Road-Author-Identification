2CB actually isn't a bad idea. It's not too hard to dose, I was extremely worried about it when I first started but bought a bunch of it and sort of figured the stuff out. I put together a little faq over at http://dkn255hz262ypmii.onion/index.php?topic=106130.0 if you want some ideas.

I think I skip 2CB sometimes just because it's not very trippy but in your case that would probably work out for the best. It's a fully functioning psychedelic at sub 20mg doses but I'd start off in the 5-15mg range and work from there. You'd want to snort it for a few hours of adventure. It sets it MUCH faster that way - minutes vs. hours - and only lasts a few hours. It's not like Acid where it messes with your head much - just gives a nice body high and some visuals if you take enough - but doesn't create a sense of wonder and beauty that will leave you communing with god.

The key to MDA is to just wait until ILF gets back from vacation (http://silkroadvb5piz3r.onion/silkroad/user/5be5f2e73a) and order from them. I've never heard anything bad about there stuff and have gone through multiple batches of it with each being great.
