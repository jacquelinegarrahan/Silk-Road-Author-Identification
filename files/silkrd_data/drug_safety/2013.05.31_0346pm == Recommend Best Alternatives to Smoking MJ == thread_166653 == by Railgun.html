You have to buy a dabbing rig. It has a vapor dome that comes off the where the bowl area would be on a tiny water pipe. There is either a quartz or titanium nail that sits inside the dome when you place the dome on top. Then you take a decent size jet torch and heat the nail till it turns red hot. Then place the vapor dome on top. once that is done take a drop of BHO and drip it onto the nail plate. Inhale. It gets you super stoned.
