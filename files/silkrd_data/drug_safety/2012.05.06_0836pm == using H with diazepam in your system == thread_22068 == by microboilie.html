Title: Re: using H with diazepam in your system
Post by: cacoethes on May 06, 2012, 10:26 pm
I don't think the half life of a drug is nearly as important as the peak serum level when considering the contraindications of combining two drugs.  And since your'e smoking, it's even less critical than if you were going to IV it.  The exception could be combining oral methadone and benzos, simply because it takes a while for peak serum levels to be reached with methadone, and both have very long half-lives.  People "abusing" oral methadone often end up taking more and more until they feel the effects, which will continue to build even after the last does is taken.  Combine with a quantity of benzos, and you could just stop breathing.

Take a toke and see how you feel.  Wait a few minutes.  Want to get higher?  Hit it again, and wait a few minutes.  Rinse and repeat.  Smoking will hit you pretty fast, you'll know when to stop.

Any residual diazepam from 24 hours ago isn't anything to worry about.  Always take it slow, and you'll have little to fear.
