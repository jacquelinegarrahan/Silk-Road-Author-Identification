Title: Re: Is it possible to smoke meth efficiently out of a lightbulb?
Post by: eggyman on August 07, 2012, 02:06 am
All I used to do was:

Hold the light bulb between my legs so it doesn't move around too much
Tap the bottom part with a hammer until it cracks
Pull out the filament
Pull out the glass bits (enough so you don't cut yourself and so glass bits don't fall in there) with tweezers or needle nose pliers
Pour salt in the bulb and shake it around to clean off all the paint
Then just stick a straw in your mouth and roll it like normal (Jack in the Box straws are extra big)

If you did something like that, and you can keep your puddle liquid without burning it (I always sucked at multitasking and had someone hold my lighter while I rolled the glass) you'll be fine.

They're right above about it being different from other sorts of glass (thinner and larger surface area).

About snorting:  I tended to prefer it.  As I recall, it was a more mellow, longer high.

People told me IV was crazy good (1/4 hitting your heart all at the same time), but "it steals your soul."  I don't know either of those from personal experience, though, so take it how you want.
