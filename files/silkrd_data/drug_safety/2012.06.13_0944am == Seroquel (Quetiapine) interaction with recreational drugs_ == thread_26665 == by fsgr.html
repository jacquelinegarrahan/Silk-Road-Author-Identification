Title: Re: Seroquel (Quetiapine) interaction with recreational drugs?
Post by: sdesu on June 14, 2012, 03:19 am
This may be advice you do not want to hear, but I'm going to say it anyway, for the sake of your health.

If you are on Seroquel, you should not be doing any drugs, especially since you're taking antipsychotics.

Cannabis (THC specifically), inhibits the formation of hepatic/intestinal enzyme CYP3A4, which breaks down metabolites produced by THC.

Seroquel and Ketamine both increase sedation, so the combination of those two could lead to respiratory depression.

As far as MDMA goes, you MIGHT be ok, but the seroquel is going to reduce your roll, if not totally prevent it, which is going to leave you with a super shitty hangover with nothing to show for it. Also, MDMA is metabolized by the same enzyme as cannabis (CYP3A4), which is inhibited by the Seroquel.

I don't know your situation or what you are willing to risk for a simple high, but I strongly suggest not stopping the Seroquel, regardless of why you are taking it.

Please PM me or talk to your doctor before doing anything that may put yourself or others at harm.

sdesu
