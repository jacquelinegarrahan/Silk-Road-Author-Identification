Title: Re: Long-term effects of Mephedrone: what are they?
Post by: AussieMitch on June 24, 2013, 01:03 am
I did about 3.5+ grams of Mephedrone everyday for just over 6 months, often around the 7gram mark and occasionally I did even more. Once or twice I even snorted 1gram lines.

Psychologically I was a mess. I suffered from extreme paranoia and constantly talked to my girlfriend about how I thought my business partners were secretly planning to fuck me over or snitch on me. I stopped trusting anyone and became really closed in and 'tweaky'.

My elbows and knees were almost permanently blue, and getting my penis hard took some serious effort. I was constantly sniffing and blowing my nose, and had no sense of smell whatsoever from all the snorting. My eyes always had bags under them and my skin went a very pale pallor of white.

Every time I started going to sleep, I would hear faint electric sounds and often would get a really intense 'zap' sound that would shoot through my head and jolt me awake. This was generally followed by sleep paralysis, where I would spend 20-30 seconds desperately trying to move my limbs before all of a sudden I'd sit up with my heart beating a million miles an hour panicking. This happened literally every-time I went to sleep... Zapping sounds followed by being paralyzed.

After about 6 months of this some serious drama happened and several of my business partners I was getting the Mephedrone with went to prison. I had a bit of a psychotic break and spent a couple of days in a mental health ward, where a really nice doctor sat me down and told me if I didn't want to kill myself or go permanently crazy I needed to take some serious steps to get off whatever I was on.

I took a completely break from all drugs after this for 18 months, didn't even have a drink or smoke a joint, and all the sleep related problems and zapping sounds dissipated within about 6 months. My limbs stopped being blue after a few weeks and the bags under my eyes disappeared and my skin looked healthy again.

Mentally 3.5 years on I still suffer from anxiety (which I never did before taking Mephedrone) however I don't suffer from paranoia and the anxiety is nothing like when I was caning Meph. I'm generally much happier now too and my mood swings are nowhere near as severe. When I got angry on Mephedrone I'd throw phones, punch holes in walls, scream, threaten and occasionally fight, now I'm very chilled and even when I'm extremely pissed off about something i'll just take a breather and go for a drive to think things over and calm down.

My nose is still very fucked and my sense of smell is probably about 50% that of a normal person. Every morning I wake up and have to blow my nose alot and it just generally doesn't function very well like it used to. I also suffer from recurring vertigo which I believe was caused by doing the Mephedrone.

Cardiac function though I'm in very good condition at the moment, I work out 5 days a week, do a lot of running and my heart has been tested and functions much better than the average, with a low resting heart rate and high VO2Max.

I just hope there's no other long term effects that pop up!
