Title: Re: Shroom tolerance?
Post by: qrbr6 on August 11, 2013, 09:23 am
Hey mate, I think I can be of some assistance here, I'm in the same boat with being experienced with acid and have just got into shrooms recently. The general rule of thumb is to wait 7 days for tolerance to go down. If you do that you should be fine. But take note that other psychedelics have a cross tolerance and repeated weekly use of even just one may mean you need a break to lower tolerance again. 3 weeks ago I took 300ug of LSD, 2 weeks ago I took 3.5g of mushrooms, 1 week ago I took 1200mg of 25i-NBOMe and last night I did 7g of mushrooms. The first 3 trips were very intense, as was the one last night but the one last night was a little weaker than it should have been given it was 7g (that being said it was still more intense than 3.5, just not as much as it should have been). So after prolonged use I would recommend a little break. Hope this helps.
