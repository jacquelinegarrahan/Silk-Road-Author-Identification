Ok so I'll shut up and tell you what I'd recommend.  Be safe about your set and setting (as I'm sure you probably already understand), wait for a reputable LSD vendor to come around who's dosages are confirmed by others on the forums, buy their tabs and try to only eat 100ug.  Cut the tab up if you get a stronger one somewhere.

Good luck.  LSD can share an absolutely stunning, beautiful, and important way of thinking.  Be safe and understand that you shouldnt expect yourself to go do normal people stuff while you are on it.  I think the biggest stress is being somewhere/with someone that does not want you to be that way.  I hope this helps

Title: Re: Alllove's LSD blotter + 1st time
Post by: Blackflysoup on October 19, 2012, 02:51 pm
My advise -
1.  knowing what to expect.  Check out erowid.org all your information you could possibly want
2.  start small 1/2 the dose or some thing so you know the feel of the uptake and the plateau, then after 3 hours you can take the other 1/2 or so.  Feeling like your being shot out of cannon and wondering how high you will go is not fun and you don't want to have your whole trip being consumed by that one thought.
3.  Very important - pick the right time, the right place with the right people.  comfortable surrounding, no distractions, no work the next day and choose someone that has a good sense of humor and is a little bit of a deep thinker.  you will be laughing so hard and discussing such new insights.  This is the trip you want, especially the first time.  You won't regret it.
4.  As with many things you want to push the envelope but still remain in control.  Loss of control is not what you want for the first time.  Save the psychonaught trip for another day..
