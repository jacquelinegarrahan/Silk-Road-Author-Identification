Title: Re: Moist cocaine. What to do?
Post by: Mushman1973 on June 03, 2013, 07:06 pm
Take a thick glass oven safe plate and put it in the oven for 5 minutes or until its hot. Take it out of the oven and dump your coke on the hot plate, spread it around a bit. Shouldn`t take very long to dry the moisture out of your coke. Don`t put the blow in the oven it`ll melt. Microwaving a regular dinner plate for a few minutes till it gets hot works the same too. Just don`t put it on till you heat the plate up. Hope that helps you.

Title: Re: Moist cocaine. What to do?
Post by: offbeatadam on June 04, 2013, 11:45 pm
Quote from: Hendrix99 on June 04, 2013, 10:03 pm
Im surprised the rice didnt work??? It always works great for us . in the hot humid summer we just store it in there . You gotta make sure the rice is able to extract the moisture but sealed from pulling moisture from anywhere else.

Also, remember that rice isn't exactly quick at its absorption rate when it is removing moisture from another molecule. It will also absorb it anywhere it can get it, which means if the atmosphere is wet, the rice will sap it from the atmosphere as well as from the coke. It will do it eventually, but it could take time. If it is particularly good coke, the time is extended, because it wants to hold on to the water as well.

That being said, also consider the reality of that fact: if it is good coke, it'll keep pulling moisture from the air, if it is available. You could leave rice there for a week (i don't suggest it, you'll risk some nasty toxins from the rice going bad...), and the coke would still be moist. If it is going on for a couple of weeks, or if it is persisting, chances are you are fighting your environments atmosphere as well. Humidity is the enemy of many things, especially hydroscopic drugs. The hot plate is probably the best idea in terms of speed, though you want to be sure you don't make the plate too hot and scold it. This is of course harder to do while the coke is wet, but if it dries and the plat stays too warm you could have the opposite effect to it.

Is it in mylar or other types of moisture barrier containers, or is it in a typical plastic baggy? Remember that plastic baggies, especially ones that are essentially thinner (or thicker) cellophane, are permeable. Cellophane especially. It is EXTREMELY easy for moisture and humidity to affect drugs in these poorer containers. I learned that the hard way early on in my enjoyment of festivities when I had some pills in my pocket in a ziplock and they sucked in all the sweat.
Title: Re: Moist cocaine. What to do?
Post by: vaper on June 05, 2013, 12:16 am
Whenever I have moist coke, I put a plate in the microwave on full power for 3 minutes. Then put another identical shaped plate on top of it (snug fit) then the coke on top of that plate.  This stops the coke from melting or burning but is hot enough to dry it quickly. 

It stops you accidentally burning your hand as you spread out the coke to dry and the plates will stay warmer for longer than with a single hot plate.
