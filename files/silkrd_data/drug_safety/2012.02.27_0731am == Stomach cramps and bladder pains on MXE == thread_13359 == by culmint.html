Title: Re: Stomach cramps and bladder pains on MXE
Post by: wowzers on February 27, 2012, 03:18 pm
Be very careful, K cramps are horrific but do subside after you stop taking it. The bladder problems however can be permanent, you get scarring and interstitial cystitis. With continued use of K, these can result in the bladder having to be removed or particularly nasty surgery in which the bladder is temporarily removed and stretched to restore some of the lost elasticity from the scarring.

The guy who invented MXE claimed that it was more 'bladder friendly'. This is bollocks. There is still little consensus on the cause of ketamine induced bladder damage- it could just be a general side effect of arylcyclohexylamine use and MXE could well be as dangerous. As it's a fairly new drug, there's still plenty of time to see what damage it could cause.

You can prevent nausea on K and MXE by not swallowing the drip, it is possible that the cramps are caused by ingesting the drug in this manner.

If I were you, I'd seriously consider having at least a 2 week break and cut out daily use. The antidepressant effects of ketamine have been demonstrated after a single large dose, there's no evidence to suggest that taking it every day is more effective and could result in dependance and physical damage...
