=======================
TRIP REPORT : 25I-NBOMe
=======================

SUBSTANCE : 25I-NBOMe (non-HPBCD complexed)
DOSAGE : 1.5mg (2x 750ug blotter)
SOURCE : ENBOOM on SR


LOG
----------------
T-0:10 = 2:00 PM
Brushed teeth and gums in preparation for buccal application

T+0:00 = 2:10 PM
Applied blotter between upper gums and top lip. Plan to hold in place for 30+ minutes, without swallowing.

T+0:23 = 2:33 PM
Slight changes in perception. Really getting into the music. Minor visual perception changes are hard to explain - large chance that this is placebo at this point. Mouth full of saliva.

T+0:30 = 2:40 PM
So much saliva. The effect to my sight can be compared to a low-dose tryptamine - my eyes are recognizing patterns more readily than usual. Slight glimmering on some surfaces/textures.  Effects still quite mild.

T+0:40 = 2:50 PM
Swallowed the saliva. Still holding blotter in place. Taste is mildly bitter. Slight jaw-clenching is becoming apparent. Moved the blotter to another spot on my gums - initial spot feels slightly numb.  Slight warping is beginning to occur - most prevalent when looking at text.

T+0:52 = 3:02 PM
Coming up now. Some jitters in my hands, and a bit of anxiety, but still what I'd consider a "low-anxiety" comeup. Mild feelings of nausea.

T+1:10 = 3:20 PM
Removing the blotter now. Pretty anxious at this point. Nothing new to report visually.

T+1:20 = 3:30 PM
Drank some water, anxiety is subsiding. Slight halos and trails in vision. Very minimal. Some effect on speech and communication, but my mind is fully lucid and without confusion.

T+1:32 = 3:42 PM
Wow - the marked difference in anxiety is astounding. I am feeling great now, very loose. The halos mentioned above are still present. Thinking about administering one more dose of 750ug.

T+1:40 = 3:50 PM
Contemplating the second dose. On the computer, I'm experiencing warping! Slow-period warbling of my surroundings, but only upon intimate inspection.

T+1:50 = 4:00 PM
Second 750ug blotter applied. Still feeling great, and I hope to move the visual effects a little farther from baseline :D

T+2:05 = 4:15 PM
Experiencing muscle jitters again, similar to those experienced earlier.  Music appreciation is mildly elevated at this point. So much saliva again!

T+3:34 = 5:44 PM
Just spent the last hour in Costco, shopping and observing. Held it together no problem. Shadows/Lights.. were like someone playing with a dimmer switch - colors were falling onto each other

T+4:23 = 6:33 PM
Just got back from the beach, and it was one of the most beautiful experiences I've ever had. Details are so sharp! Colors are so _____(colorful?)! There appears to be a geometric mesh just beyond the surface of everything, binding, defining, and comprising it.

T+5:01 = 7:11 PM
Tears are flowing a lot - happens all the time with mushrooms for me. Feels great. Clean out the eye-plumbing. Watching a show about dolphins.

T+5:35 = 7:45 PM
Coming down for sure now, pretty relaxed.

T+6:31 = 8:41 PM
Had a bit of weed, comedown nothing out of the ordinary

T+7:30 = 9:40 PM
Checking in - feel nearly back to baseline. Pupils still dilated.

T+9:30 = 11:40 PM
Back at baseline.

T+10:40 = 12:50 AM
Pupils no longer dilated. Ending the log here.


REPORT
----------------
Wow, this stuff is incredible! I tend to recommend 2C-I to inexperienced users of psychedelics due to it's easiness on the mind (little confusion/mindfuck), but 25I-NBOMe now takes the cake for my favorite "light-hearted" psychedelic. During the trip, I was having a bit of a hard time speaking (fumbling over my words), but my head was quite clear and lucid.  I managed to spend part of the peak shopping in Costco with my trip-sitting buddy, and damn was it fun.  However, where this substance really impressed me was outdoors at the beach. Colors and patterns are perceived in such a beautiful way - admittedly less "deep" than some psychedelics, but I was in awe of nature - able to inspect things down to incredible levels of detail. Colors were "falling into each other" - ever look at 3D footage without glasses? Where the red/blue images are slightly misaligned? It was similar to that, but much more complex and less predictable. The effects were quite nice!

I was very pleased that there was very little body load at all - one of my main dislikes of the 2C-x family.  I did experience slight muscle twitching, but again, much less than an "equivalent" (effects-wise) dose of 2C-I. There is certainly more to explore at higher doses, and I plan to employ the incremental-dose strategy again.


SUBJECT NOTES
----------------
WEIGHT : 180lbs
EXPERIENCE : 2C-I/2C-P/DMT/Mushrooms/4-Aco-DMT
TOLERANCE : Moderate strength DMT trip ~2 weeks ago - expect no effect to tolerance.

Quote from: MrRibena on November 01, 2011, 01:05 am
Have you ever taken 25-c nbome? how does it compare?

25c is more visual, less mind-fucky. Tends to have more of a launch to it. New visual dimension of breathing walls, morphing textures etc.. while 25i for me was mostly color bleeds and enhanced edges etc.

Another way to describe it... 25i you get a 'feel' for a new plane of existence, 25c you 'see' it.

Yet another way to say it... 25c is the entertainer (visual and fun), while 25i is the deep and meaningful (revelations, realizations etc).

If there was a trip that was all encompassing which hit you hard with visuals and mindfuck (shrooms, lsd maybe??), then I would say that 25c and 25i would each represent flip-sides of the coin.

Oh yeah, all this is how it effects *me* personally, it may be different for you, although from what I see at bluelight, other people are reporting similar (and exact same) results.
