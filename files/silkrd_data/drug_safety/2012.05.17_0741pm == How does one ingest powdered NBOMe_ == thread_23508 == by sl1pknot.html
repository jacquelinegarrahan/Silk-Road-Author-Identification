it is impossible to accurately weigh a dose of nbome, around a mg with a mg scale, because the range of accuracy is at least +/- 5 mg in either direction... you could end up taking nearly 7 mg thinking it was 2.  With the non-linear dose-response curve and highly vasoconstrictive properties one would certainly at least be in a potentially dangerous situation.

this is why people just buy the tabs, its easier and safer if you dont know what you are doing...
