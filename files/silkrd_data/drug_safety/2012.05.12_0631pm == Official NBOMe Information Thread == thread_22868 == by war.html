What is a NBOMe?

The NBOMe series of chemicals are primarily N-o-methoxybenzyl analogs of the 2C-X family of phenethylamines, and are 5-HT2A (serotonin 2A) receptor agonists. A few are amphetamine analogs. With few exceptions (e.g. mescaline-NBOMe), compounds in this group are active at very low sub-milligram doses. These chemicals have nearly no history of human use prior to 2010 when they first became available online.

Who makes up the NBOMe family?

1. 25c

2C-C-NBOMe = (25C-NBOMe; NBOMe-2C-C)

   phenethylamine

2. 25i

2C-I-NBOMEe = (25I-NBOMe; NBOMe-2C-I)
2C-I-NBOMe →
   4-iodo-2,5-dimethoxy-N-(2-methoxybenzyl)
   phenethylamine

3. 345

mescaline-NBOMe = (345-NBOMe; NBOMEe-mescaline)
Mescaline-NBOMEe →
   3,4,5-trimethoxy-N-(2-methoxybenzyl)phenethylamine

What other drugs give similar affects to the NBOMe family?

According to most users, the NBOMe family (specifically 25i) produces similar affects to LSD.

Is NBOMe safe?

All the NBOMe's are still RC's (Research Chemicals) which basically means this substance hasn't had enough tests in controlled environments to determine the safety.  Popular Clearnet drug information sites such as Erowid and Bluelight strongly suggest against taking more than 4mgs of the substance?

What is the best way for me to take NBOMe?

This issue is still being debated however unlike LSD, NBOMe must be absorbed bucally, meaning if you swallow a blotter paper containing NBOMe your stomach acid will just destroy the substance.
