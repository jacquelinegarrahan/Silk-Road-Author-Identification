2c-e
Oral Dosage
Light: 5 - 10 mg
Common: 10 - 15 mg
Strong: 15 - 30 mg
Heavy: 25 - 40 mg

Insufflated Dosage
Light- 1 - 3 mg
Common- 3 - 7 mg
Strong- 6 - 10 mg
Heavy- 10+ mg
*Tips/warnings for insufflation: When 2ce is insufflated, it usually bypasses most of the nausea caused by taking it orally. but let me warn you; it burns really badly. Make sure you have a milligram scale and don't ever try to eye out your dose if you don't know what average dose looks like. Do not exceed 10mg at once if it is your first time.

Duration
Total Duration: 4 - 9 hrs
Onset: 20 - 90 mins
Come Up: 15 - 30 mins
Plateau: 3 - 7 hrs
Coming Down: 1 - 2 hrs
After Effects: 2 - 4 hrs

Notes: Takes the longest to kick in, lasts longer, has cooler and more interesting visuals, and has stronger mental effects (relative to 2c-i & 2c-c). 2c-e has the potential to cause truly profound thoughts and insight on yourself. I find the mental effects to be stronger and than those of 2c-i and 2c-c, not to mention much more meaningful. I have also found the visuals of 2ce to be much cooler and more intriguing.
