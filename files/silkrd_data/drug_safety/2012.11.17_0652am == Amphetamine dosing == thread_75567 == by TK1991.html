There's seriously about 50 pages of discussion on speed as a replacement for pharmaceutical ADHD meds (adderall/dexedrine), their effectiveness/ineffectiveness (usually ineffectiveness, but not always), and alternatives.

In a nutshell:

* 2-FMA may work for you (2-fluoromethamphetamine, NOT 3-FMA or 4-FMA, you want 2-FMA).  Some people seem to prefer it to adderall.  I most certainly do not, but that's just me.  It isn't much fun at all to abuse, so don't expect it to be.
* Clean meth will work almost as well.  Actually getting a dosage in the 10-30mg range when you're staring at a shard the size of your thumbnail though, now that requires a milligram scale. Have one?  Great, give it a try, but it's kinda costly (less so than buying the pills here though).  Don't?  You're gonna fuck up your dosage and take more than is clinically sanctioned and be able to think less well than you could in the first place.
* Speed is a poor replacement for pharmies.  Very poor.  In some cases, totally useless replacement.  In other cases, kinda does the job, but almost never "as well" or even "almost as well."  Read the speed review topic for dozens of pages of information about this.
* Don't listen to anyone who says you should do anything with whatever you decide to try, except swallow it.  If you're looking for medication replacement(s), ALWAYS swallow it.  It always works better for intellectual enhancement if you swallow it (read: always).  Don't even try anything else.  You might like it, then you're stuck being reliant on a drug that you know in the back of your mind you *could* snort or *could* smoke on occasion... the problem is that you probably won't ever forget if you really like it, and it's your medicine that's going to be right there in front of you every day forever.  Just not a good idea.

Now don't get me wrong -- abuse your meds if you want, I mean I take enough speed to kill a small mammal most every day... I'm not saying it isn't an option or your right to choose if you decide that's how you want to live.  I'm just saying, "why risk a life of having to resist the desire to abuse it if you don't have to."  That's all.
