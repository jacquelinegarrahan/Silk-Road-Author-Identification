Post by: l1ardream3r on July 28, 2011, 12:22 am
My rules for doing it (these are for beginners/moderates)
1) Doing another line with a heart rate above 120bpm isn't safe. Not that it's going to hurt you, just IMO lines under 120 (small, <100mg lines) are not going to hurt you. Over 120, there's a small chance. Over 150 you shouldn't do more. Over 180, you're going to seriously harm yourself.

2) you should ALWAYS acetone wash your shit. Klean-strip acetone, glass funnel, glass beaker, medium flow filter papers and you're set. This is $20, what was that ball? If you read up on it you should use a dropper for the acetone, but on the first go you can just pour (esp cooler/drier). This will take you a day (acetone needs to evaporate) but that's about the only downside; the upside is your shit will be cleaner, any hint of oiliness will be gone, and you know that what you're doing is mainly psychoactives (not necessarily coke, but better than blowing lines of god knows what).

3) Never buy when you're high. Set this rule early and if it gets drilled in you will help yourself potentially down the line.

4) 90% of people i know who do coke a few times feel the desire to do more. Don't start doing it more than once every month or longer unless you aren't at risk here. This isn't addiction, but it can affect people. For rich fat white girls who want to be pretty and skinny though, this can turn into addiction (or others with money/desire to use beyond high).

5) Have some something nearby to deal with OD (valium or xanax works well). Snort a full dose of the downer, these are similar to what an EMT would give you and will help for an ODer. Also note, coke OD begins with shaking beyond trembling and extreme paranoia. If this happens to you, take deep breaths and seek medical attention (or get to some public location so if you keel someone can call 911).

6) Check your laws. a lot of places have laws saying trace amounts (i.e. on your iphone) or paraphernalia (a cut straw or flat mirror is enough) is a felony charge.

7) If you are getting caught DO NOT EAT MORE THAN .5g. Friend of mine thought it was much less psychoactive orally (it's significant) and ate a ball when he got pulled over and his heart got fried. (you can possibly eat up to a g, but I wouldn't risk eating as it hits you somewhat slower and can't effectively be puked up).

I could go on but that's enough

Post by: RaFaeL5 on January 10, 2013, 11:09 am
My rules are:

always use clean gear. If you snort use a clean miror (not a dirty CD from somewhere in your truck) and clean that miror after every party.
never use a rolled money to snort, use a straw and NEVER share that straw - if you use with friends just have 1 straw each (very important for hepatitis)
never snort cleaned coke - it's to pure and will burn your nose (even if you will not feel it burning because of the effect of coke)
if you smoke it: stop smoking it (the adiction is worse than shooting H and there is no product such as methadone that can help you quit)
at the end of your night have some nasal water (H20 + NaCl) and clean your nose - don't do it between your lines because that will moisten your nose and because of that you will harm it more than if you'd lett it dry (cocaine dries your nose so that on the next line there will be less C that sticks in your nose - if you moisten your nose more cocaine will stick to it and thus it will harm your nose more/longer)

with a new product always start with half a line - you never know...if the products doesn't look or smell as it should, don't try it (better to lose money than health!)

I prefer to snort half the line with my left nostril and half with my right nostril, it gives a better feeling and it's half the load on each side

if you want another snort it's a good idea to check your hartrate, but that's so different from person to person AND from product to product (some high quality coke will barely affect your hartrate) that it's not a full proof method. Best is to "know your chit" and then you can quite well say when you're ready for your next line. don't overdo, never forget that coke makes you (self/over)confident and certain of yourself, thus you will probably overestimate your capacities - beware of this effect of coke when you use it!

if you have to drive, wait for the "rush" to be over (about 30 minutes after your line) because it can affect your balance and that's extremely dangerous when driving

if you drink beware for your liver. drinking & cocaine are nice together (think of the song "breakfast in vegas on cocain and gin"), but it's almost impossible to get drunk when you snort cocaine - so watch out that you don't completely fuck your liver. I've had nights when I drunk more than 2l of vodka and still didn't feel a thing...except the next day: because of the alcohol the "down" is very hard and painful (has nothing to do with the C)

also: never put C in your mouth! it's poison for your gums and lots of diseases are transmitted because of people going over a miror with a wet finger (saliva) and someone else doing the same 5 minutes later over that (still wet) miror - C is not made for oral consumption!

C is not very addictave in a physical way (compared to H or meth) but the mental addiction can go pretty quickly. It's always best to buy the quantity you need for one night and not more than that. If you're "weak" and have left-overs give them to a friend or even throw it away. Avoid having anything left in the house the next morning (especially when you smoked it!!!!).
Once you know the product and you know how it affects you, you might be able to keep a little "reserve" in the house, but you'll know very soon if you're a possible addict or not.

I almost always have some reserve in the house and I can use for days in a row without problems, but when I stop I stop for a few months.
Beware of the mental "come down", coke has 3 of them. The first one is 3 days after using, the second is 10 days after using and the third is around 3 weeks. Beware of that, it'll make it easier (and less dangerous for your family/friends/...). That's also a good reason why you should wait at least 3 weeks before doing it again (so that your body and your mind have had time to "reset").

For questions don't hesitate to PM me.

Again: use clean gear & don't share the straws!
