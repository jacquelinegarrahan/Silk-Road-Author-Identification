In general Mescaline (especially when part of the total cactus alkaloid complex) is more gentle on the psyche than either LSD or Psilocybin, and phenethylamines are in general more forgiving than the indoles/tryptamines. I concur with DMtryptamine285 in this regard.

That said, if the dose is high enough you will know all about it - and I concur precisely with valakki in that regard! ;)

Whole cactus or full-spectrum Mescaline alkaloid complex, when used in a conscious shamanic way with proper context, support and intention to heal can definitely serve - even in relation to what you describe as your "mental health problems", but ultimately you would need to decide if it is the right path for you. And if you do explore start slow and start humbly...
