Title: Re: cocaine negative side effects.
Post by: ChaxChax on April 26, 2012, 07:16 am
I'm an old guy. I don't say that to come across as worldly or super-experienced, just that I am old enough to have been buying coke in the mid 80's and early, early 90's when the most common cut was still only B12 and maltose.

Good, pure coke, taken in moderation will not make you jittery.  Make no mistake about it it, it's going to make your pulse race and I would never call it healthy for your heart, but those physical effects are eclipsed by euphoria and what we used the call the "fuck yeah!" feeling whereby you felt in total control of your situation.

You become chatty, you feel friendlier (up to a certain point), you just kind of bliss on the buzz.  Once you red-line (over-do it) those euphoric feelings no longer become predominant.  You feel twitchy and nervous, some people get extremely paranoid.  You start doing fatter and fatter lines, trying to get back to where you were 20 minutes ago. It usually ends up being a shit night if you let it get that far.

The one difference I can say definitely about the majority of coke I've bought these days is it takes far fewer lines/bumps to get to red-line.

Make of that what you will.
