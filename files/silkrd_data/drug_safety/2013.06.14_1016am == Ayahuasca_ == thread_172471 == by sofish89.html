What do you want to know? You didn't ask anything specific. It's difficult to give you information if you don't ask what specifically you want to know.

The term ayahuasca encompass many different brews, all of them having subtle differences (for example the effects depends a lot on the harmala vs. DMT content and in part even on the plants used to obtain those substances), so the first thing you should research upon is on the particular brew the vendor sells (and that s/he has sent you for an error) so as to know better what to expect and how to approach it the best you can.

In any case ayahuasca is a very wonderful medicine, but since it is a very powerful one if you have not a good prior experience in psychedelics or on shamanism/meditation etc. doing it alone can also not be the best choice (but it naturally depends on your knowledge of certain things and also on your nature).

However rest assured of this: A) this "mistake" can actually be for the best; if you approach ayahuasca in a sacred/respectful way you can really have a life changing experience, B) coincidences and mistakes not always happens just for a casualty (in reality almost never, but I know that if I say so many will think I'm a lunatic, so let's pretend that "not always" they happen for a casualty).

Quote from: tedrux on June 18, 2013, 03:53 am
what is the difference between smoking DMT and drinking Ayahuasca
?`

A lot, it's a whole different beast. Smoked DMT lasts a short amount of time, maybe 15 minutes. Ayahuasca lasts for a couple of hours. Their subjective effects are vastly different.

OP and anybody else interested in ayahuasca. I recommend checking out the documentary Ayahuasca: Vine of the Soul. It's well worth the watch. Also note that one component of ayahuasca is the MAOI (monoamine-oxidase inhibitor). DMT when taken orally is inactivated by MAO. This is why you can't swallow 100mg of freebase DMT and trip. The admixture plant (often B. caapi) contains plant alkaloids that are MAOI (harmine, harmaline, and others). The MAOI prevents the DMT from being broken down and then you trip.

There are risks with MAOIs! You don't want to mix certain foods and drugs with them, please do some reading and research on the topic. I also think it would be worthwhile to research the traditional entheogenic usage of ayahuasca in the Amazon, it's very interesting.
