Title: Re: DMT in a Volcano Digit?
Post by: ZA6wUw8a on June 20, 2012, 07:35 pm
Yes, I've tried vaping NN-DMT in my volcano before I designed my own custom apparatus for DMT vaporization.

The results:
1) (overall) I never perfected the technique with the volcano. As opposed to the 50mg typically required, 200mg was required to produce a sub-par experience. I went through 500mg in a sitting trying to get this to work and didn't have any breakthroughs (experimental or mental). 
2) Temperature was varied between roughly 100 to 250F. The best results were achieved from 160-200F (with the 200mg).
# DMT vaporizes around 140-170 F, but these numbers DO NOT correspond to the temperature of the vape.
# DMT tends to start burning at a temperature that's not too far from when it's vaporizing
3) It badly stunk up my vape, despite my cleaning efforts, leaving a hard residue. This was NN-DMT from my own extraction after Re-X in hexane.

I did a bit of reading on other forums and it seems no one really had any great (and verifiable) experiences to report.
