Title: Re: Old Pot cookies, safe to eat?
Post by: Ben on May 31, 2013, 01:30 am
As far as the cannabis in them goes they will be okay - no worries about that degrading into something dangerous.

When in comes to they actually being edible as cookies, i suppose it is up to you. They may have become too brittle to eat by now, but they should still be effective if you manage to get them down the hatch in some way or another.

Mold is generally not a problem for baked-try cookies like these, they simply do not have enough water to sustain mold growth unless you keep them in a really damp place. If you do see any sign of mold growth you should obviously discard them, but in my experience these things have an almost eternal shelf life unless you store them in a condensing environment.
