Title: Re: Alcohol and drugs?
Post by: blockrockinbeats on February 24, 2012, 03:03 am
I recently drank quite a lot of beer during an acid trip. I did this for three reasons:

1) To calm my anxiety during the come-up and just relax.

2) To do something that felt normal to me so I could feel more grounded whilst tripping balls and going through all the feelings of tension, anxiety, confusion, and fear that I tend to experience at times on this drug.

3) So I could say to the people I live with "Oh I was just drunk," if they happened to ask why I was up all night making noise and moving around, etc. lol!

My body felt like crap during the comedown though - bloated and dehydrated (ie: hungover). I probably wouldn't do it again or recommend it.

I can't even tell you how much I ended up drinking that night - I lost count because I kept getting confused and distracted, but I can tell you that it was a lot. It's easy to just drink like a fish on acid and not realise how much you're consuming... so be careful with that!
