Thanks for taking responsibility and admitting your mistake. Glad some people here still take accountability for their actions, even in the veil of anonymity.

And going back to the scaleless measurement, I would more than likely suggest dissolving soluble substances in a high proof ethanol/water tincture (e.g. everclear), and just taking small amounts of that, since the amount of ethanol in said tincture would almost never be enough to actually alter the drug's effects of you make it concentrated enough.

However, my number one recommendation, as before, is just BUY A GODDAMN SCALE lol.
I got a 0.001 g (milligram) scale off amazon for $20 2 years ago, and it still works to this day.
I would NEVER trust it with ultra-sensitive substances such as the NBOMEs, but it works fantastically for anything with an active dose in the 10+ mg range (e.g. MDXX drugs and tryptamines).
The small investment is truly worth it in the long run.
