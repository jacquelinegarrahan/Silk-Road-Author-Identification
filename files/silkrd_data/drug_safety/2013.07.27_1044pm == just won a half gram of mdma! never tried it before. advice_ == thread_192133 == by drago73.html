Title: Re: just won a half gram of mdma! never tried it before. advice?
Post by: ianfleming on July 28, 2013, 07:40 pm
Go hard your first time, 1mg per every pound you weigh, trust me.
Your first roll is always your strongest and generally your best, don't waste it. Your first roll will probably last longer than average as well.

Make sure you fast and are in a comfortable environment. Rolling is a lot more fun if your with at least one other person. MDMA is a social drug, meant to be enjoyed with others. I suggest a lover or close friend/family member if possible. Make sure they are someone you are very close with and can be VERY honest with, as there is some possibility that you may delve into your psyche and wind up saying things that your always held close or deep inside yourself.

Don't snort it, it's a waste of the drug, it wares off to fast, is too stimmy, less empathic and somewhat painful, not a smart idea.

You should fast the day of and not consume any breads or heavy foods as this will lessen the effects. Also, you will want to buy some 5-HTP for the day after as you will feel quite emotionally and physically tired, 5-htp helps your body replenish serotonin faster and will make this downtime less intense and shorter.

I promise you, follow this advice and it will be one of the best experiences of your life
