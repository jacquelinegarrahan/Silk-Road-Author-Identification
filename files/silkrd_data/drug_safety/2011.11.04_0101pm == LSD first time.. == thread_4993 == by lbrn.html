Title: Re: LSD first time..
Post by: ®eptile on November 11, 2011, 08:09 pm
you have probably already made your decision, but my advice is this.

You only get ONE first time, so swing for the fences!

that being said, set and setting are most important. as long as you feel safe and secure in the environment you choose to trip in, don't fear Lucy. She will be whatever you want her to be. LSD is nothing to be afraid of, but my experience is that under dosing causes anxiety, and the fear can creap in. you need to be able to let go and allow the magic to wash over you.

Remeber that LSD can't kill you, but the fear can(your buzz anyway), so keep friends around, and always remember that it is a drug you have chosen to take, and the effects are temporary.

Also, as to re-dosing, LSD tolerance builds VERY quickly, so re-dosing should be done within the first two hours of your trip, and most people I know are content with their first peak, and dodn't re-dose in time, and end up underwhelmed with their experience as a whole, so again my advice is to swing for the fences.

My first trip was 2 tabs of ~100µg about 15 years ago, and since I have gone on many journeys with higher doses, but none shine in my memory like the first time. when I look back on that first night, however, I only wish I had taken a bit more.

you will not be dissapointed.

Title: Re: LSD first time..
Post by: pannerjack on November 18, 2011, 08:27 am
Pretty much all good advice here.  In my experience, the most important factors in determining if you will have a good or bad trip is your frame of mind prior to going into your trip and who you are tripping with.  Depressed?  Don't drop.  With people you're not quite sure about?  Don't drop with them. 

The main thing to remember is LSD will amplify whatever vibe you're on prior to dropping.  Do it in an setting you feel safe about, and with friends you trust.  The most common trigger for a bad trip is the irrational but unshakable feeling that you are going insane and the trip will last forever.  This can get triggered if you're tripping alone, or if you're tripping with sober people.  But remember this--I've probably dropped over a couple hundred times at some pretty heroic doses and never had a trip I didn't come out of within 24 hrs, so remind yourself of that if you start to freak out.  It's usually a good idea on a first time to trip with a trusted friend who's tripped before, to act as a kind of guide.  But as long as you're with good people you should have a blast.  Go with it and above all have fun!
