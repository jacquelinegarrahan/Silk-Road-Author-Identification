Title: Re: How to I.V. Dilaudid
Post by: tree on July 05, 2013, 09:06 pm
Well crush the pill, put sterile in a disinfected spoon or other container, stir with the plunger of the syringe (don't touch the plunger before doing that), and add no heat!! Don't cook it. You should then use a wheel filter because injecting without one isn't very good. Filter though cotton twice if you really can't get a wheel filter, preferably sterile cotton but no cigarette filters or anything like that. I'd say order some wheel filters, they're not very expensive.

To find a vein, make a fist while making a tourniquet. You should also disinfect the area around the vein you're gonna use. The key is using sterile things and filtering insoluble shit out. Also, 4mg is a nice dose, if you're not tolerant use between 2 and 4mg.
