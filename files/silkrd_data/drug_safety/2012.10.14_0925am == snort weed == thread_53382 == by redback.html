The below information is my current understanding as far as all of the research I've done extends. I do not guarantee the accuracy of it.

Any comments about curing are 100% conjecture. There is far too little knowledge about what curing really does, aside from turning junk to dank.
++++++++++++++++++++++++
THCa, which is what the cannabis plant manufacturers via enzyme-reactions(among other cannabinoids) is not psychoactive in the human body. THCa slowly converts to THC when cannabis is curing in a jar. Or laying out in the open. Or existing. THCa very rapidly converts to THC when heated, however. Like when making cannabutter.

THCa is also, I've read, water soluble. As is CBDa. The little "a" at the end is for the carboxle group, or however that is spelled. Decarbing is the process of rapidly converting THCa to THC and can be done via making cannabutter, or even just heating the cannabis in an oven at a very low heat. Or curing it in a jar{BLARGH NOTE: I'm not actually certain about this}. Curing has the benefit of preserving the terpenes which are lost from heat. Too much heat will start to boil off the lower cannabinoids.

As far as what I've read goes, and as far as my understanding extends, you want to keep the heat at 200f or less. The "problem" with oven-decarbing is that the terpenes will boil off from the plant material and the chlorophyll will break down(turn brown). Good for stealth and stealth-edibles(as in, make cannabutter after oven-decarbing), bad for bag appeal and artisan-smells. Also, this doesn't mean that brown weed is good weed.

Oven decarbing will also dry the bud more, making it more potent BY WEIGHT. This doesn't mean it magically becomes stronger, though. Just that as more weight is shed, the ratio of cannabinoids-to-plant material goes up, in that there is less plant material.

There is rumors on the clearnet about how heating your bud in the oven will make it "stronger" because it decarbs all the THCa into THC. There is no science to support this or contradict it but based on my, admittedly limited, understanding of the myriad processes, I believe those people are just stupid and don't realize that the lost weight means they end up packing more cannabinoids into a bowl.

More specifically, the reason I think it is bullshit is because the process of smoking, vaporizing, or anything else involving heat and inhalation will convert THCa into THC.
++++++++++++++++++++++++
I highly suggest not trying to make "snuff" though. Just... don't snort it, really. Nicotine is FAR more psychologically per mg than THC is, that's why snuff works for tobacco. Nicotine also absorbs through your skin with relative ease... as in, if you drop liquid nicotine onto your skin, you need to go to the hospital.

However, an oven-decarb will(should) allow you to eat the weed straight and get high from it. Plus, you could just stick it in your mouth like chewing tobacco or something.
