25C and 25I both last too long. If you take enough of a dose to get intense visuals, you'll trip for 8-12 hours. 25B also lasts longer than 4 hours for most people, and is thought to be less visual than 25I. Unless you somehow got one of the few batches of 25E-NBOMe, 25H-NBOMe, or 25N-NBOMe the only commonly found NBOMe with intense visuals and a 4 hour duration is 25D.
