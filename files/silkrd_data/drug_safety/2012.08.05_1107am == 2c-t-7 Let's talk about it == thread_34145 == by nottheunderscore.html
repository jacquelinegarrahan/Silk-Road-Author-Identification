Title: Re: 2c-t-7 Let's talk about it
Post by: TheGrandWizard on August 05, 2012, 07:41 pm
I started a thread about this earlier: I had some 2c-t-7 for sale and took it a couple times in the name of science. Anyway, here are some of my observations.

2c-t-7 is a very idiosyncratic compound. Two people can have wildly different reactions to an equal size dose - and the same dose can have wildly different results on the same person in a different set and setting.  I also find it to be one of the more introspective 2c-*s.  Although it got its start as a party drug, I find 2c-t-7 makes me very introverted: it has none of the social and erotic quality of 2c-b.  The trip is great but it sends you looking inward rather than outward. 

People have called 2c-b a cross between MDMA and LSD.  2c-t-7 is much more LSD-like than b.  It has fantastic open-eye and closed-eye visuals -- some of the best I've ever experienced on any psychedelic.  It also improves the sound of music very nicely: I can see where some people might enjoy it at a rave or a festival.  I did find it had a noticeably higher body load than 2c-b: where b gives me warm fuzzies t-7 gives me lingering cold chills and faint cramps. These aren't necessarily a deal-breaker but they are something to keep in mind when dosing.  t-7 has a pretty steep dosage/response curve, and taking more might increase the icky feeling without improving the good parts. (It may also extend the length: I've found that 2c-t-7 lasts considerably longer than b, sending me on a 6-8 hour trip).

2c-t-7 is notorious for causing some deaths. Like syrian rue and b. caapi, 2c-t-7 inhibits the activity of monoamine oxidase.  While this is a good thing when you're doing ayahuasca, it's a Very Bad Thing when mixed with certain classes of drugs. Combined with SSRIs, stimulants and other drugs that act on serotonin -- including MDMA -- MAO inhibitors can cause all kinds of nasty complications.  Unfortunately, when t-7 was sold as "Blue Mystic" many people took it with Ecstasy, thinking the combination would be as lovely as an MDMA/2c-b honeyflip.  Others tried snorting it with tragic results.  So long as you avoid insufflation and combining it with MDMA, M1, amphetamine or cathiones like mephedrone, flephedrone, etceteradrone, &c. you should be fine. 

While I had some good experiences with vaporizing, I suspect that vaping can cause the same kind of rapid MAO inhibition as snorting and would advise sticking to oral or rectal usage for this one.  Start with 10mg or so and see what happens.  If you don't get satisfactory results at 25-30mg I'd say you might want to think before going higher: you may get more unpleasantness rather than a better trip.

Overall I'd say this one is best for intermediate to advanced psychonauts, but it is definitely a worthwhile substance and certainly deserving of its place in Shulgin's "magical half-dozen."
