How long have you been on Lexapro? If it's been a while you'll likely have to ween off it for a month in order for MDMA to do much of anything for you- and psychiatrists don't take kindly to self de-medication.

That being said, I got off 225mg of Effexor for my first Ecstasy experience- stopped at the beginning of October and took my molly in November. And Effexor is one of the hardest SNRI/SSRI antidepressants to get off- tons of dizziness, mental haziness and my mind getting stuck in places away from my body before snapping back. Yet it was for the best as I had a fantastic MDMA experience and discontinued Effexor permanently; it was horrid for my anxiety.
