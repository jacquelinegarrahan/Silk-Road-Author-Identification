Title: drug recommendations needed
Post by: daphnee on June 06, 2012, 07:00 pm
I figure where better to ask for drug recommendations than here, right?

Here's the situation: I'm a late 30-something executive in the corporate world. Normal life, house, dog, husband, kids. All the usual bullshit. My life is good. I am not escaping reality, but I have slight anxiety problems and trouble relaxing. Like any good ol' American I'm a fat lazy slob that works too hard and wants to dumb down her brain on the weekends.

Why don't I just snag a 6-pack like everyone else, right? I've done my share of drinking alcohol, but I just can't do it anymore. Makes me feel like shit, and aggravates my allergies and constant sinus problems. Not to mention the calories and metabolic effects. I'm looking for something else.

Requirements:
* Won't put me out of commission too badly for more than, say .. 8 hours. No weekend benders for me.
* Not interested in hallucinogens.
* Bonus points for pills. I'm not mixing up any mayan herbal shaman concoctions in my kitchen and shit.
* I can't be noticeably fucked up. I have to be able to function in the real world, or at least be fucked up in ways that resemble the effects of alcohol. I gotta conform to social norms, yo.

What I've tried, what I liked, and what I didn't like:

* Alcohol: the buzz I get after 2-3 beers after not drinking for a while is about perfect for me. I relax, my brain engages. Once upon a time I'd sit in the bathtub plowing through a bottle of armagnac and reading fuckin karl marx and shit. I can drink like a champ -- a 12pack was nothing. Not anymore, though -- the health ramifications are too much. My liver says no more, and it's just not fun.
* Marijuana: enjoyed this a fair amount in my requisite stoner years like any teenager. Stopped enjoying it in my 20s. I suspect it interacts badly with the antihistamines I take. These days it just gives me a cloudy headache and makes me intolerably drowsy. and not in a good way.
* Kava kava: I actually quite enjoy the resulting intoxication from this stuff, but it's like drinking dirt, and it makes me nauseated as hell. Pass.
* Xanax: hardly does anything for me. The anxiolytic effects are obviously there as I do relax a little bit, but it's barely imperceptible. If I take any more than 1mg I just get exhausted and pass out. Pass.
* Zolpidem/Ambien: this is an interesting one: the buzz that I get from ambien around 15-30 mins after I take it is awesome, but of course after an hour or two I pass the hell out (as intended). The reports of the effects of ambien (night terrors, sleep-walking, etc.) scare the shit out of me, but I've never experienced anything even close, myself. If I could find something like ambien that didn't have the massively sedating effects, I'd be quite pleased. I was hoping xanax would be this missing puzzle piece, but alas, not even close.
* Oxycodone: makes my head swim, and then I projectile vomit. Soooo.. yeah, that's not my thing.
* Hydrocodone: The only time I've had this stuff was after some serious surgery, and it made me feel so good that I threw the bottle away. I need a stress-reliever, not a life-ruiner. I don't have an addiction-prone personality but this stuff scared me.

So, any suggestions? Should I give any of the other benzos a try? I've thought about giving clonazepam, or even basic valium a try. But based on my experiences with xanax, I'm not impressed. It's obviously a useful anxiolytic and handy for a long planeride, but otherwise not fun.
