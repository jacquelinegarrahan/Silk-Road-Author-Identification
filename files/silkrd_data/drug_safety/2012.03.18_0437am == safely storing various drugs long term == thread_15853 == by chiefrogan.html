Title: Re: safely storing various drugs long term
Post by: 12guhdfg3 on March 18, 2012, 07:10 am
I'm assuming that by blotters you mean LSD, so keeping it in a cool place where no light, moisture, heat or oxygen to allow it to decay and you should be able to keep it on hand for a long period of time. Basically wrap it in foil, vacuum pack it and chuck in that freezer your keeping your weed with and it should be fine. I would suggest treating your DMT in the same manner (minus the foil if you want). You would then probably want to put these vacuum bags in the jars you mentioned to prevent moisture seeping through the plastics over time.

Cocaine hydrochloride should stay pretty much as it was for a very long period of time as the compound is inert, your problem there is it may be cut, and it's the substances that it's cut with there that you should worry about, so I can't really help you there, the same principals with meth.

With heroin, just keep it away from moisture and it should stay fine, MDMA is the same. Keep both in a dark, dry place and they will remain potent. I have no idea how ketamine is stored so you'd have to look for that one yourself.

Just get yourself a freezer without an internal light (this is optional, not necessary, as sunlight is the real killer of your more sensitive chems), and keep it in your basement, chuck something on top to disguise it and you will be set for a few years, enjoy (:

EDIT: just to specify, you would be better off also putting your coke, herion and MDMA in some sort of container within the freezer to keep them from moisture.
