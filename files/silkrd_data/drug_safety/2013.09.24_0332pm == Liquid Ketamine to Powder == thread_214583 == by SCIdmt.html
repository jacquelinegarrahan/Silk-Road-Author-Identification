this was the only way to get powder back in the day.

Title: Re: Liquid Ketamine to Powder
Post by: dirtybiscuitzz718 on September 24, 2013, 04:57 pm
That is one way and prob the most common way i hear of people cooking their K.

There is many many ways to do this. Loads of people will swear by particular methods be able to leave you with a higher yield of dried ketamine than other ways. The more rapid the rate at which you evaporate the liquid solution, the lighter your end result of dried k is said to be. I personally can not say for sure, only because ive never got weighed it up after a cook.

The idea of cooking K, is just that. Evaporate the solution to be left with ketamine flakes, which you chop to powder. Some other ways to do this are: using the microwave (not recommended, unless for large large amounts), simply putting on a pair of ovegloves or oven mitts and doing it directly over a flame ( this requires skill and experience with cooking k, cause it is very easy to burn the k) in a pyrex casserole dish, or least common just dumping the liquid onto a plate or something then letting it set out either atop a radiator or under a heat lamp to slowly evaporate over a few days time (said to give the best yield).

How ever you do it, the idea is.. apply heat, evaporate solution, DO NOT BURN. Good luck.
