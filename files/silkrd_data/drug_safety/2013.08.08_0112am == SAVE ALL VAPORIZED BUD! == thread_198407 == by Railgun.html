Title: SAVE ALL VAPORIZED BUD!
Post by: Railgun on August 08, 2013, 01:12 am
I see a lot of people disregard their vaped bud because it's no longer seen as viable.  It is viable.

Unless you are vaporizing at near combustion temperatures (421 F or so), only over 60% of the THC and other goodies are being vaped.:

*Clearnet
https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vaporizer_%28cannabis%29
*Clearnet

This makes sense too. When you smoke a bowl, you pretty much scorch it until it's ashed or kicked-so it's only logical that there's a lot more left when the bud hasn't burned.

From what I've read and experienced, I'd say vaped bud is about 30% of the original potency, largely because the THC (which vapes at ~340F) is the first to vape off.  It results in a pretty indica/hash-like buzz, irrespective of the original strain because the CBD is in higher proportions.


A good method is to just take a bunch of vaped:
-Use 99% isopropyl alcohol and douse it just enough to get it wet.
-Squeeze the droplets from this bud/alcohol mixture onto some fresh pot.
-Let the fresh pot dry.
-Vape the fresh pot.

Thus, you have made your new bud about 30% more potent. The bowls last longer each time. The more vaped bud you use, the more it's going to result in couchlock.  Strangely, when I add it to Sativas, it gets more trippy but in a calm way.

Anyway, enjoy you fucking potheads.
