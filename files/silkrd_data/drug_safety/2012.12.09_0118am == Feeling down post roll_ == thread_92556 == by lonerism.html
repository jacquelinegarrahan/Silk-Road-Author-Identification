It's very common especially with overuse as you did.

2 rolls in one week is not so much of an abuse but most sensible users space out their rolls at least one month from each one.

And since you already rolled twice in one week, your serotonin levels in your brain are pretty much down. You should wait for it to replenish itself in which good diet and some supplements help.

I'd suggest you look  more on the serotonin's work on the brain, how it's affected by mdma and check some of the pre/post loading guides they exists even some threads down here.
