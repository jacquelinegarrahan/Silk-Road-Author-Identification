Post by: poolsclosed on May 16, 2012, 11:02 pm
Antipsychotics will kill a trip. In a bad way. They're only for last resorts, like when you are trying to stop someone who has entered full psychedelic psychosis and is out of control violent.

Benzos and alcohol will blunt a trip. They reduce the intensity of the trip to a small degree and also have anxiolytic properties that help calm you.

Failing that, there is melatonin which will sedate you.
