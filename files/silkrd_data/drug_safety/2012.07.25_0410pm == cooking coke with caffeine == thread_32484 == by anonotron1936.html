Title: Re: cooking coke with caffeine
Post by: gambino on July 27, 2012, 04:39 am
Yes, I think you should be able to separate the caffeine quite easily.  Free base cocaine is not soluble in water but free base caffeine is.  So rock it up with baking soda or preferably ammonia and then wash it with water and filter.  Repeat wash several times.  One gram of caffeine dissolves in 46 mL of water.  Cocaine base is only slightly soluble, with one gram dissolving in 600 mL water.
