Title: Re: Crystal MDMA. to sniff or not to sniff..
Post by: DiamondSky on February 06, 2013, 08:55 pm
Plugging is my favorite ROA. Snorting is fine, it hits faster but has a much shorter duration.... plus it tastes like sniffing salt so there's that.

Plugging has a higher bio-availability, gets into the system nice and fast, by-passes stomach contents so it works even if you just had a big meal, lasts about as long as oral, you use less to get the same high and it helps mitigate issues with people that sometimes get nauseous off eating MDMA.

Downside of course is you now have to put the drugs in your ass...

You get used to it pretty quick though and the high comes on so fast you'll probably be happy before you even take the plunger out a few minutes later.

If you always take it on an empty stomach and have a endless supply, the high isn't really any different than eating the stuff. If you're on a budget or want to take it after a big meal though hard to beat a little booty bump to get the drugs in you fast.
