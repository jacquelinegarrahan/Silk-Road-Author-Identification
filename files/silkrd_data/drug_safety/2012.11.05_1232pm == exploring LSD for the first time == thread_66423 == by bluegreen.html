Post by: shoophaha on November 05, 2012, 10:16 pm
Hey! My first acid trip was with 120 mics. I think if you have good, supportive company, and a good setting, you should be just fine. If you haven't thought about your past issues recently, they're unlikely to surface. It's not guaranteed obviously, but I personally found my trip to be focused on my current situation and I didn't really think about the past much. It could have been my company/setting.

Do you like to smoke weed while doing psychedelics? Smoking during the middle part can accelerate the trip into crazy territory (where reality truly started to deconstruct for me and some bursts of paranoia which I found easy to fend off started creeping in), probably more like what you're familiar with on such a high dose of 2C-B (especially with regard to visuals), but otherwise it should be pretty chill, which sounds like exactly how you want it to be.

The Erowid Guide to the Psychedelic Experience is fucking fantastic and does a great job of explaining how to prepare and what to expect: *CLEARNET* http://www.erowid.org/psychoactives/faqs/psychedelic_experience_faq.shtml *CLEARNET*

Hope you have a great trip! :)
