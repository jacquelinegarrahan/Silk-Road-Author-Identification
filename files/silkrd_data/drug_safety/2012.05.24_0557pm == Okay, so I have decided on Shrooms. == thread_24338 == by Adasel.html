Post by: blackend646 on May 27, 2012, 08:06 pm
The best advice about dosage is to go with what you feel at the time. If you know you are going to be anxious about diving in with a huge amount, take a smaller amount. If you know you are going to be wondering "What if I took more", then take a large amount. A "bad trip" can happen at any dose, it's all about what is going through your head at the time. Take however much or however little you need to ensure that you won't be thinking about how much you took.
