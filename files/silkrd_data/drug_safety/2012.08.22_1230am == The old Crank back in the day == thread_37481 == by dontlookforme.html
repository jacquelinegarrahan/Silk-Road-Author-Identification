Title: Re: The old "Crank" back in the day
Post by: le botbahn on August 22, 2012, 12:48 am
Crank was racemic methamphetamine, meaning that it consisted of 50% l-methamphetamine and 50% d-methamphetamine, which are mirror images of eachother. It was usually synthesized from the precursor P2P.

In contrast, the meth of the 90's onward was mostly synthesized from ephedrine, pseudoephedrine, or PPA (due to wide availability of those precursors on OTC diet pills). It was composed of d-methamphetamine exclusively. It's supposed to be more euphoric and as a result has higher abuse/addiction potential. This crystalline form can be smoked (vaporized), which makes it particularly addictive because it gets delivered to the blood/brain much faster than snorting or swallowing. For whatever reason, this method caught on in the 90's.

Crank was mostly pushed to the wayside in favor of this because it was easier to obtain precursors and it was more potent.

The different ratios of d and l- methamphetamine produce slightly different experiences. The preference for one or the other is subjective.

What it ratios it consists of these days seems to be variable according to subjective and anecdotal reports, at least according to the SR review forum. There's been quite a bit of discussion and speculation but members consistently report 2 distinct experiences among batches received from SR-- dubbed either "speedy" or "elixor of sex" by forum members. 

Most members attribute the former to higher l-meth content  (similar to crank)  and the latter to d-meth, though impurities, cutting agents, and individual biochemistry could also be the culprit in subjective differences.

The relative lack of all precursors in the US and purported bulk of production by superlabs in mexico make either type a possibility. Any that is produced in the US is most likely to be d-meth because ephedrine/pseudoephedrine are still available but to a lesser degree than in the past (while P2P would be extremely difficult to procure in any quantity).
