Title: 25i-NBOME - first time experience.
Post by: android465764E on May 02, 2013, 11:20 pm
I tried 25i-NBOME for the first time today, thought I'd share my experience. Call it trip report if you like, it's just what happened.

Body weight - 220lbs.
Not eaten all day.
No other drugs in system.


16:00 - Popped a 1000ug blotter on my tongue. Held it against the roof of my mouth for about 35 minutes, resisted the temptation to swallow any saliva during this time. Eventually washed it all down with water.
Almost immediately after putting it on my tongue I noticed a numbing sensation. This gradually spread to my lips, and remained there throughout the whole experience. It wasn't unpleasant, but it did make smoking cigarettes a bit of a drama.

16:35 - The onset of this drug took me by surprise and was a bit of a shock.
I suddenly became incredibly anxious. Clammy hands, and some really painful cramps in my hands and wrists. My fingers felt clenched and twisted.
I realised I was starting to lose the plot a bit so I put on some music. It took me a good 10 minutes to sort my head out.

16:40 - Now starting to relax and enjoy it. I could definitely feel a stimulant effect and some euphoria, as I stood up to get a cigarette I noticed that I was pretty unsteady on my feet. Kinda like being drunk. Wasn't a fan of this.

17:00 - Standing up, my legs seemed very far away but too short at the same time. A very odd sensation, similar to what you might see in a Fun House mirror. Walking wasn't really an issue, that is to say I wasn't at risk of falling, but if someone saw me they would definitely think I had issues. lol
Other changes in my visual scope manefested themselves in the form of a near-crippling, inability to judge perspective. Kept putting my cigarette in my eye, pressing the wrong buttons on a remote, etc.. 

18:00 - Around about this time I noticed that surfaces around me had started to breathe. I also started to notice some "fluttering" around my peripheral vision. Imagine someone a long way off has a strobe light, and you can just make it out in the corner of your eye. This is what it felt like.

19:00 - At this point I was bored. The stimulant effect was still very much present, and now I started to feel some cramps in my neck. Again, quite painful. I think I may have been clenching my muscles without realizing it. The visuals were still present, but now I could see the same strobe effect with closed eyes.

19:30 - Over about half an hour the visuals seemed to rise to their peak. Slight swirly patterns with closed eyes, colorful and quite enjoyable. Surfaces are still breathing, but now they seemed to be sort of twitching. Like the way a person's chest moves when they cough.

20:00 - At about this time I could feel the drug winding down. The stimulant effect had diminished considerably. Walking was no longer a drunkard's outing, and the closed eye visuals had stopped.

20:45 - Seemed like it was all over. I had a really dry mouth, and my neck & wrists ached like a bastard. Hunger descended and a made some toast.

00:14 - Logged into SR Forums and wrote this.


_______


I'm not entirely sure what to make of this tbh. I didn't do myself any favours by almost freaking out at the start, but I'll chalk that up to experience.

I *was* hoping for some more interesting visuals.
But as it stands, I've experienced the same hallucinations from simple sleep deprivation.

I think next time I will take 2000ug and see what occurs.

Summary?  Disappointed, but still got x5 1000ug blotters left. Will try again.
