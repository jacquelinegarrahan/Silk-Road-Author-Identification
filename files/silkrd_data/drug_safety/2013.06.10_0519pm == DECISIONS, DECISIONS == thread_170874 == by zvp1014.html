It's one that can become an everyday part of your life, because once your tolerance is up, it's surprisingly easy to take it in large quantities and still function - to a degree. It was an everyday part of my life for about 6 months when I was in my early 20's, then one day, whilst high on it, I had a particularly introspective experience during which I decided to give it up. So I did, as easy as that.

Ever since then my ketamine use has been rare, and each time I have used it it's been a cathartic, and ultimately life-affirming experience.

Except this one time in a squat party in a disused warehouse, when my wife and I took it together and both agreed we felt like giants amongst all the other people there who were like little munchkin people, about 4 feet tall. That was an MDMA+ketamine one though. I think actually they were all quite small though.

Give it a go. It's a hell of a ride.
