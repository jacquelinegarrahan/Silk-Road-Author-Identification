Title: Re: Laying Alprazolam blotters?
Post by: Jack N Hoff on July 02, 2013, 06:24 pm
Ethanol.  Don't just lay all of the liquid on then let it sit there.  The dosage range from tab to tab will be completely uneven.  Figure out the absorbancy of the blotter paper that you're using.  Use a variable volume micropipette to lay each tab.  Or use a multichannel micropipette to lay several tabs at a time and get the work done faster.  Or buy a auto pipette liquid handler to lay sheets at a time.

Here is a quote of mine from another thread that should be able to prepare you for the task.

Quote from: Jack N Hoff on May 02, 2013, 01:28 pm
Weigh the dry sheet.  Figure out the retention volume of the blotter.  Saturate the sheet for a few minutes.  Hold it for a couple minutes to allow the extra to drip off.  Weigh it again.  The difference is the solvent retained in the sheet.  One gram of water is about 1ml at 72 degrees.

Say it's a 100 tab sheet and it retained 15 grams.  Divide the retained solvent by the number of hits to get the maximum amount of solvent that can be absorbed per tab, aka 0.15ml or 150ul.  The dispensed volume should be about 30% of the max saturation limit, aka 45ul per tab.

Title: Re: Laying Alprazolam blotters?
Post by: Tessellated on July 03, 2013, 02:01 am
I don't know about alprazolam but I can describe what I do with LSD.

First I decide how much drug I want on how much paper. So lets say I want 10,000 hits of 200ug blotter.

I cut up a hundred 2.5x2.5 inch square pages, plus 20 extra for calibration. Each 2.5 inch square paper can be divided 100 times making it 10,000 hits worth of paper.

Then I do some math, 10,000 hits at 200ug: 10,000 * .0002 = 2 grams of LSD

Then I take clean solvent and dip 20 pages into it. I measure the amount that the 20 pages absorb. I then multiply that by 5 to figure out how much 100 will absorb.

So lets say that 20 pages absorbs 30ml of liquid. That means I need 150ml of solvent for 2 grams.

Now, you don't want to be running out on the last page so you make more solution than you need. Multiply both by 120%, so I get 2.4 grams in 180ml of alcohol.

Once I have a solution of LSD and solvent I completely submerge and saturate each sheet of paper in the liquid. I do it fast and close the container between sheets to prevent evaporation. The remaining liquid can be used in the next batch.

Take non-absorbant thread and make a mesh from it to dry your pages on. Do not stack the pages on top of each other. Take care not to let your solvent evaporate too much, with LSD an extra kick is nice but with xanny this may be an issue.

With xanax you are going to want at least 1mg on each tab so I don't know how big you will have to make the tabs. If you put too much on a small tab it will flake off.
