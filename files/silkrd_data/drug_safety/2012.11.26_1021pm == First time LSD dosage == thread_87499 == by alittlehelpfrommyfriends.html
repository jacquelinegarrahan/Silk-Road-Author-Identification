Post by: psychedelia on November 26, 2012, 11:22 pm
I've never redose with LSD, but it is my understanding that redosing an hour later only prolongs the trip, but does not intensify it. I could be wrong.

I recommended two earlier, but if you just want to have some fun, then 1 is probably ideal.  Two is usually the minimum if you want a spiritual experience. It depends on what you want, I guess.

"At somewhere between 200 mcg and 400 mcg of LSD, there appears to be a critical level beyond which psychedelic mystical experience becomes possible, which can be compared to the minimal amount of thrust needed for a rocket to launch itself into orbit."
