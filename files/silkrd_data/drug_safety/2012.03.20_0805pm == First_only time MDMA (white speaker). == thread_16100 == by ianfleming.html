Title: Re: First/only time MDMA (white speaker).
Post by: White Light on March 20, 2012, 09:12 pm
Hi !

For me taking some MDMA with my girlfriend always sounds like a good plan ! 

I would just add some gums and take some magnesium before dosing to avoid jaw clenching. Don't forget to plan what you need for the "after roll", personally I hate to have something to do after rolling !

Take the whole pill or an half is a huge debate ! You will trip harder if you take the whole pill and might be overwhelmed when it peaks but it won't last long and shouldn't be a problem if you are home. You may vomit too, but not a big deal.
If you take an half, your roll will be less intense but last longer, it's a smoother sensation that may disappoint people who look for an heavier trip. As it's your first time with MDMA I would recommend to begin with an half, don't forget  some people are more sensitive than others, we should always start softly when we try a new substance.

It's definitely not a good idea to plan driving 6 hours after dosing, in fact it's not a good idea to plan anything after that, except cool things !

Be safe and enjoy, first time with MDMA is always an amazing experience.

WL
