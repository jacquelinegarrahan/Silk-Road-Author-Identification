Talking from a UK perspective:

Yes and No.

Yes it's legal to have NBOMes, but...

NO it's not legal to have 2c-x in ANY quantity. Since these are precursors they will always be present in the finished product. Under UK law the penalty for "cut" drugs is the same as for pure drugs and the 2c-x 'contaminates' the whole blotter and you'll be done for possession of 2c-x even though it's a tiny amount.
