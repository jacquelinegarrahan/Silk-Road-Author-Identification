Title: Re: Opium tea some help please
Post by: DMtryptamine285 on June 25, 2013, 07:15 pm
Morphine is destroyed by vaporization at 82.6 degrees Celsius so you definitely don't want the water to be too hot (ie: definitely not boiling).

Opinions are mixed on whether hot or cold water extraction works best, although I've always got great results by doing a CWE.
You shouldn't just stir, I always recommend shaking vigorously. Get a two liter soda bottle and put the finely crushed pods in there, add a little lemon juice and either hot (but not too hot) or cold water. Put the cap back on, Shake Shake and Shake some more until your hand starts to hurt. Poke holes in the lid with a tack or a safety pin and squeeze the liquid into a cup (this way no coffee filter is required.)

You can still get alkaloids by doing a second and sometimes third wash on the material. This works with poppy pods as well as 1lb+ of poppy seeds.

Post by: SourGrapes on June 25, 2013, 09:46 pm
i've got to say, 5g of opium tea is NOT very much, especially if you're an experienced opiate user. i would guess i used closer to an ounce of pod powder per cup of tea i used to drink, but then again i had a hefty tolerance and a large supply of poppy pods.

and, i used to simmer, almost-boil my tea. every time. after i dumped just-boiled water onto my ground pods in a big pitcher with a few tablespoons of lemon juice, (about 1 cup pods, 1.5-2 cups JUST BOILED water) i'd let it sit, stirring every few minutes, for at least 30-45min. then, after straining, i'd simmer it down to as little liquid as possible over an hour or two. i never experienced very diminished effects from accidentally boiling for a minute or two; a few times i'd nod out and accidentally boil for a lot longer than that (granted excessive boiling did seem to affect the potency somewhat.) using 10-15 medium-sized pods got me to essentially the same place every time, whether i accidentally let the water boil or not. the one time i used lukewarm water, i didnt get anywhere NEAR as strong effects as i did with the hot water -- when i re-washed that gunk with hotter water, i got more out of it than i did the first wash. so honestly i dont think you need to worry so much about the water being too hot. eating opium and drinking opium tea was my almost-daily DOC for several years, and i never 'destroyed the alkaloids,' at least not to the point of not being able to get high as a kite off my tea. the effects would often last up to 24hours if i made a particularly strong batch.

re-use the pod slush you have, once. 2hours after drinking it, if you're not feeling where you want to be, i suggest taking more. 5grams of powder, i'm not sure exactly how much that equates to in volume, but i generally used about 1 US cup of powder to 2 cups of water per glass of tea. that is a LOT; i would suggest maybe 1/4-1/3 cup of powder if you're experienced with opiates. if not, well, try doubling your first dose next time.

also i suggest using a clean sock or foot of pantyhose to strain the tea. put the sock in a tall cup, wrap the leg-part of the sock around the rim of the cup. dump pod goo into sock. pick up sock, strain out. WAY easier than trying to use a coffee filter; the only danger in this is that i've burned my hands fairly badly while doing a second wash on my pod gunk, because of the heat of the water and the effects of the first wash.

Title: Re: Opium tea some help please
Post by: anton on June 30, 2013, 08:29 am
One step may have been missed. Try to grind the poppy heads/straw into a powder. mortar and pestle for small dry lots, or a blender for some moisture in the pods. Larger surface are = larger alkaloid extraction.

Shut the kettle off before boiling, add till the poppy powder is just covered, add a good squirt of lemon juice.
Then sit for at least 15 minutes, overnight sometimes
Squeeze the liquid out using a cloth or tea-towel.
Mix 50%-50% with an orange juice and drink.

Be aware there are several types of opium buzz and it can vary on your physiology and age as well.

In my experience a smaller dose of opiates will make me buzzy, all up and active (although a bit stoned too). As I increase the doseage I will get higher pain relief and more sedation. I personally like the sedation part so I always go for a big dose.

Working your way up will get you used to the various stages of poppy use and allow you to tailor your dose. It takes practice and experience, you're dealing with plants not pharmaceutical tablets.

Be aware that it is easy to get a habit from poppy tea, it may well take a few months and you will be okay, okay, okay and then one day you will wake up with a monkey on your back. Please be careful and enjoy this amazing plant with respect.
