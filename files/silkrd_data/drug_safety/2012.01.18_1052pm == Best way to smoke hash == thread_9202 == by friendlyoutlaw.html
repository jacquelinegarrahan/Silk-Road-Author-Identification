Post by: 77Tjm on January 20, 2012, 12:40 am
The Volcano might work- the key is breaking it up really, really small- like grains of sand. FWIW, I've had good luck vaping hash in a SSV, not so good luck w/ a PD or a MFLB (powdery keef hash works ok in the latter two, black rock hash doesn't, IME).

My first & favorite method is the Torpedo:

Empty out a cigarette by rolling it between your fingers (leave the filter in place).

Collect the tobacco on a folded sheet of paper, throw out a pinch or so (to make space for the hash)

Crumble the hash into sand-sized pieces (use a lighter to heat for ~2 seconds if the hash is too hard)

Mix in with the tobacco on the sheet

Scoop the tobacco back into the cigarette tube (this takes practice- massage the tube periodically to get the mixture to settle)

Once it's all packed up, twist the top like a spliff

Make a paper filter that's about 5mm longer than the cotton  filter (magazine insert, cigarette pack, business card- cut to size, curve it by rubbing it on the edge of a counter top, then roll into a tight cylinder- it needs to be springy enough to grab the edge of the cigarette tube and not fall out)

Roll the filter end of the torpedo in your fingers to loosen the filter

Squeeze the filter and use your teeth or tweezers to pull the filter

Pop in your tightly coiled filter- the extra 5mm should give it a good final packing from the filter end.

Other, easier methods:

Ubie Vaporizer- break it up to about the size of sea salt, place the chunks towards the middle, hold a lighter about 2-3 inches below the tip, inhale slowly.

ABV and hash spliff- sub vaped greens for tobacco- harsh, but nicotine free

Title: Re: Best way to smoke hash
Post by: billyman on February 25, 2012, 12:54 pm
In Scandinavia they have a very unique approach to smoking hash. This method resides mainly in Denmark, but has also influenced their way of smoking in Sweden and Germany and so on.

There is a very strong subculture concerning hash in Copenhagen and Denmark, partly due to the free town of Christiania in Copenhagen. (Really worth checking out if you should for any reason visit Denmark.)

Anyhow, the most comfortable way to smoke hash in my honest opinion, is by smoking it in joints with toasted tobacco. The tobacco is toasted by waving a cigarette slowly back and forth over a lighter flame, until smoke vapor starts going out, and then you put the cigarette in your mouth and blow air through it until it's free of vapor. Then you continue this process until the cigarette is nice and crisp all over (not too dry) and has a kinda sweet bread smell. This can be hard to do in the beginning and you will probably burn a lot of cigarettes, but once the techniques is down you'll see the advantages of toasting the tobacco in this way.
Easier methods to gain the same effect, but where you can do it easier and in larger quantities, is for example by toasting pure tobacco in a pot or pan in the kitchen, or putting tobacco in the microwave oven or cooking cigarettes in water and then letting them dry in the sun or on the radiator.

By toasting the tobacco in this way it burns the hash better (too good if you toasted it too much and it's too dry, so watch out, you don't want your hash disappearing in an inferno), the tobacco tastes more gentle and gives a better taste to the hash, and you don't get sick from nicotine, tar and other additives, because you removed them in the toasting process!

Then you mix it with the hash, which you crumble into very small lumps. Depending on how much hash you smoke, you might wanna put in more tobacco (I use about one cigarette pr.  0.5 gr of hash).

Then you wanna roll the cone, which should in my opinion be as cone shaped as possible for the burning to do best. You can use all the same methods as when you roll the Dutch kind of joints, so I recommend smoking them with one layer of paper for best burning or no glue for best taste. In Denmark they have the tradition of rolling the joint cone BEFORE filling it like it was a container, because it is a lot easier than rolling it with the hash tobacco mix inside. I don't do this because of my super jedi rolling skills, but I can recommend this for beginning joint rollers as it will greatly help you in making pretty, good burning joints.

I hope you understand my walkthrough of my vision of an ideal joint and will try it out, I could maybe submit some pictures for elaborating on the process if people are interested.
