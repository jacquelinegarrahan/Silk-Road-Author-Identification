Brainwash yourself, make yourself thoroughly believe that there is no such thing as a bad trip (IMO there isn't, even the negative ones teach you something)
It's all mind over matter baby.
Also make sure you don't have any mental conditions like depression, anxiety or schizo type stuff otherwise it may tip you over the edge.

Title: Re: how can i have a good trip (shrooms)
Post by: moonflower on August 16, 2012, 05:29 pm
first of all, get this "good" and "bad" trip nonsense out of your head. it's just an attempt to paint the experience in black and white. every trip is a learning experience. as far as keeping a positive mindset when things get rough, it really comes down to surrendering to the mushroom. do not resist or try to control the trip, and you'll be fine. accept whatever you are shown--you're shown what you need to see. if you enjoy weed, that can help keep you calm as well.

Bearing in mind what everyone above has said I would also recommend starting out with a rather small dose. You are uneasy because you do not know what to expect. That is understandable.

You'll have a great time.
