Title: Let my mistake be a lesson to you-Ketamine P.S.A.
Post by: DeadRa7 on March 05, 2013, 09:43 am
Hello, my best of friends :) 

I thought I'd take a moment to write a little story about a rather interesting experience I went though last Saturday.  Soooo, here goes.

Me and the girlyfriend got a pretty decent amount of mdma this past week and right on Saturday, after 14 some odd days waiting, my little package of MIMM's 1G ketamine showed up at the house.  Yay, time to party-we went to a decent sized rave party where I had about a quarter oz of said mdma all bagged up.  Me and the g.f. decided to sit on the K till we got home, or the next day.  She left before me with some friends, and while I was bagging up all this molly I couldn't help but keep staring at it.  It was just sitting there on a plate, looking all pretty like (this was the first time I've ever gotten K off the SR, and it was absolutely gorgeous-better than anything I've ever seen).  SOOOOO, instead of being patient-I snorted a tenth of the fucking shit and went back to bagging out this molly.  FUCK, that got difficult.  Anyways, about half a hour later I made the drive on down there and took some shots in the car when I arrived. (fuck paying $6.00 a shot!)  I had the K on me, in a separate pocket, in two bags so I didn't get the MDMA and the K confused.

So, here I am-sitting in this show and have all this work on me.  See some people I know-sell a few grams and was actually pretty intoxicated within 20 minutes of arriving.  It was at that point where I decided to do some more K, against my better judgement.  Well, my dumbass decided to stick a bill into the whole fucking bag.  BAD IDEA!!!!!!!  I don't think a K-hole would be appropriate to describe what occurred-lets try K-ROCK.  I completely blacked out, had my molly sacks all over the fucking place-and had to be taken care of by my friends and my g.f.  Here I am, drooling all over myself-TOUCHING ON ANOTHER GIRL THINKING SHE WAS MY GIRLFRIEND-and dropping my drugs and money. 

It was a complete shitshow, and I don't remember a fucking thing.  I wake up to driving to a friends, with a huge headache, and missing most of my shit (R.I.P. my little laser pointer and my favorite hat).  When I came back to, I was just sitting on the couch at a friends-driven home by another friend-and being told about what had just conspired.  It was literally, to this point, the most embarrassing event that has ever happened in my life.  (Even when I was on heroin back in the day and went to the ER after falling out in a public bathroom-least I didn't know anybody who found me and never went back there)

Anyways, I learned a lot last Saturday and I thought I'd share this with you in hopes that other people are very mindful about their dosages-I could have easily wound up robbed, arrested, broke bones, raped-who the fuck knows-and that's a very, very scary state of mind to be in! 

tl;dr-
1. Never underestimate the STRENGTH of your drugs, especially one's you get off SR as they tend to be more pure than most can get in their local markets
2. Be mindful of DOSAGES, always test out your product with a small amount before going all in.
3. Be aware of your ENVIRONMENT-some drugs are just not appropriate for certain places
4. RESPECT Your body, mind, and friends/romantic partner.  If you fall out, they gotta take care of you and it fucks with their night and makes you look like an idiot that can't handle their shit.


I am nor an addict, an immature kid, or inexperienced with most any substance.  However, that night I let bad judgement cloud my mind and made a really stupid decision which I regret like a motherfucker.  And since I can't really post this on any social media or tell most of my friends about it, I thought that I'd just leave this here in hopes that my story may prevent someone else from making the same mistake as me.  I got very lucky, but if this were to happen again I bet it would turn out bad.
