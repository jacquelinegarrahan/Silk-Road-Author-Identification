NIK KITS:

http://tritechforensics.com/store/product/nikr-test-poucheskit-refill-pouches/]

They have some for LSD/Pot/PCP/Meth/E/Benzos/Ketamine, pretty much all you can think of.

These kits are great and possible the strongest way to tell what you are getting with your organic compounds.  I would never trust anyone to give me pure anything on a black market, purely because it's too easy to have substitutes that are cheaper or more readily available.  Now there are great people selling and manufacturing these compounds, but to ensure your safety, employing one of these $16 dollar kits can save you a lot of heartache, even literally.


Note: I don't recommend buying these from the site above as they are probably tied in with LE; it is purely for listing and reading at your convenience.


Some tips:
-- Use kits to test a random pill or so, depending on your level of suspicion.
-- Use in conjunction with other tests to test for other known adulterants.  I am not too drug savvy as I pretty much stick to gang, but ensuring that your meth has no PCP additive in it for example, may be worthwhile
--Try to keep these kits away from the place of purchase.  Having these basically says, "I was WAITING for this shipment".
--These are basically aqueous acids.  It is possible to store them in an innocuous looking container, but it must withstand the pH (do research)
--These will most likely NOT tell you anything aside from drug category, so if you are getting a benzo, you will not know which one it is
--Don't order these if you are a big time vendor/seller. They are too much of a red flag. Have a friend or even a trusted buyer get them for some reduced price goods and/or peace of mind.
--These aren't 100%.  Even in LE, they must send the goods off for GCMS (big time/almost no error) testing.  Since you can't get a hold of those, this is prob the best alternative.

Stay safe
