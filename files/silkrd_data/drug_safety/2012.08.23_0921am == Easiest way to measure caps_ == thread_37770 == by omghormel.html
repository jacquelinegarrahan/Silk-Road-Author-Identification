Title: Re: Easiest way to measure caps?
Post by: Turkey for Breakfast on August 23, 2012, 12:05 pm

Construct a make-shift funnel out of a piece of business card, or heavy stock paper. Anything will do, could be a piece of a cereal box. Roll it into a funnel shape and tape it down with scotch tape. Pour the amount of powder you want to cap up onto a scale. Then use a small spoon, preferably the kind that comes with the scale, call it a 'dosing spoon' but one small enough to pick up small amounts of powder and pour  them into your funnel or right into the caps. As you fill your caps, watch your scale. When 100mg has come off your weight you know your cap is accurately filled to 100mg.
Title: Re: Easiest way to measure caps?
Post by: Ben on August 23, 2012, 11:53 pm
When moving around powders to get 100 mg samples, it can be helpful to use the handle of a plastic coffee stirrer or something similar. You'll quickly develop an eye for what 5, 10 or 20 mg of powder looks like in a small pile of that, making it easier to prepare reasonably accurate dosages using precision scales. These things are so cheap and disposable it makes sense to use a brand new one every time you work with it, avoiding contamination with other substances you may get on scoops included with scales and such work - clean work is important!

This is what I do.

Take a foot long or so piece of cardboard and use a pen to poke a hole the size of your cap bottom. ( a bit smaller.) every inch or so until you have 10 holes. Shove your cap pieces into the holes until they are in all the way and even with the top of the cardboard.

Then, take a gram of product, and pour it into a relatively even line. (Make sure the edges arent thinner than the middle, even height, etc). Measure it. Divide that measurement by ten. Whatever size you get (say, 1 inch). Mark off 1 inch on the cardboard, then, using a razor or credit card or something, guide each section into their caps. Once the caps are full, push em up and toss the tops on.

(I use big capsules)

It's a brutish method, but it works and it's quick. And if you're selling, just sell at (AROUND 100mg)
