Title: Re: Noriegas #4 Asian white heroin
Post by: tordemon on May 07, 2012, 06:57 pm
I hate to speculate about product that I haven't personally seen, but there are multiple things to be taken into consideration.

First, pure heroin should be white. The complex array of bonds within the heroin molecule (and most drug molecules because they are complex) allow a wide range of frequencies of light to be produced, creating a white color. At the same time, heroin is usually brown because the starting point for the synthesis of heroin was with poppies, and so the brown sludge created from the poppies (desired for their morphine which must be acetylated twice, in effect, to create diacetylmorphine) is left over from the original production.

Most heroin I've seen has been brown. The white stuff usually seems to be fentanyl, cut with dextrose or something else to that effect. It's particularly less euphoric, lasts longer, and is much more dangerous. If people are thinking it's poisonous, it's possible they have taken a higher dose of fentanyl than they should have. It's even possible that Noriega doesn't realize that his source is fentanyl and not heroin. But that's all just speculation.

I'd recommend you check it with a testing kit if at all possible. It won't prove anything, but it'll add some certainty at least.
