Hey,

The look of K can vary from batch to batch but in general, especially if it is coming from India, the K should be in small shards or granules. Without a test kit you arent going to be 100%  its K until you try it

If your k is in a shard/grain form I recommend dissolving your 1/2g in a couple of table spoons of boiling water. Then place the K solution onto a plate. Place this plate on top of a pan of boiling water and let the heat of the stream evaporate the liquid off from the K solution. After 10/15 mins you should be left with the recrystallized Ketamine on the plate. Make sure there is no moisture left in these crystals then scrape them up with a razor or card. Work these crystals with your card until you are left with a nice fine powder.

For a starting dose I would recommend 30mg-50mg line. That should give you a slight "wonky" feeling. You  can work your way up from there. I would say 90mg-150mg for a first timer should be enough to hole. I guess a hole is different for each person but for me I really love it. You cant really describe the sensation to anyone. If i were you I would put some headphones, close your eyes reach a hole :) great feeling.

Now just dont go abusing it too much, tolerance builds up very fast with K, which I know all to well :(

Hope that helps

I'd go slow on the dosaging. I'm pretty good about keeping my anxiety under control when doing drugs but just start slow and play things safe because it is pretty intense.

If you do decide to snort it (or however) I suggest taking it someplace where you can relax and be comfortable. Don't go places or try it in public. As to the antidepressant or anxiolytic effects, I think that's the kind of thing that will very from individual to individual. If that's what you really want then I'd start with a 15mg line snorted and when it kicks in see if your mental state is any different(it'll be subtle) and see if it changes over the next two hours(from administration). A lot of drug use seems to be a crutch (speaking as someone who uses multiple crutches) so it won't be a miracle cure but likely a temporary one. So just go in with realistic expectations.

My best Ket experience was on about 100mg of Ket snorted over the course of about 5-10 minutes and then smoking DMT. (Don't do this!) If the Ket turns out to be good I'd try measuring your based on your initial, low-dose, experience and your body weight. I find it can be very therapeutic to lay back and relax--it's like an easy and intense way to meditate so it could prove beneficial to any negative thoughts/emotions you ahve.

If you don't have or aren't willing to get a test for the ket I'd start very small, 10mg line snorted, and work your way up to no more than 50mg over the course of an hour and see if you feel the effects that are reported by Ket users.
