Title: Re: Purple MDMA?
Post by: wolf2wolf on September 14, 2013, 07:52 pm
Pure MDMA/HCL is colorless and has no smell. Any other color (tan, purple, brown) or odor (sassafras,licorice, etc.) is just the result of impurities left over from precursors or by products in the synthesis.  It doesn't take a lot of other chemicals to discolor or create a scent, so you can still have very potent brown or purple "molly" that smells like a root beer float, but it isn't pure MDMA/HCL.

Ironically, a lot of people now think brown is better, so manufacturers have even less incentive to properly wash and purify product.
