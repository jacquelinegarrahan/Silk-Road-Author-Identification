Title: Re: First time LSD use
Post by: zvp1014 on April 04, 2013, 07:52 pm
Google is your friend. But literally- put the paper on your tongue and hold it there. It might dissolve, it might not. Five minutes is generally considered good. I just keep it on there for as long as I can before I inevitably accidentally swallow it, or begin to feel symptoms.

Some people recommend placing it under the tongue instead of over, but I find this annoying.

In terms of mental state– My honest advice is to not worry about it. Don't clumsily take LSD on a day when you've been down or arguing a lot- but don't worry about having a bad trip, or else it'll become a self-fulfilling prophecy.

First time I took LSD, I'd been incredibly depressed for years, albeit off any SSRI/SNRI. On the day of, I just tried to be happy and think about positive things; despite my depression I had a phenomenal trip. Don't worry!
