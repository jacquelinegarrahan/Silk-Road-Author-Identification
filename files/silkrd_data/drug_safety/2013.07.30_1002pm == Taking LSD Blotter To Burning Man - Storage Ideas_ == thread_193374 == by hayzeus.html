Moisture and light are the big ones with L.  I would use a jar as suggested and put a good sized sachet of silica gel in the jar to keep it really dry and wrap the outside in black tape.  The silica gel can be dried out in an oven to "regenerate" it.

I keep all my "supplies" in jars with silica gel to keep stuff as dry as possible. and it realy helps keep things perfect.
