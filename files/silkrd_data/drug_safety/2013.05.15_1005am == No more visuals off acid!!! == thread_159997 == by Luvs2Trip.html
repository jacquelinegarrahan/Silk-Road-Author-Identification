One of the "bad" approaches you see around concerning hallucinogenics is that people expect them to do all the work, so to speak. This in turns means that you need a lot more of the drug and that the trip will be casual in its outcome.

Learn to use LSD (or whatever else) in conjunction with a trance practice/ritual practice; in this way you direct the trip where you want to and the visuals will be even much greater than even a 100x normal dose would give. When I talk with people that use hallucinogenics (even DMT) they never experienced something like transmutation (for example turning into a wolf, crow or whatever) or similar things just because they expect the drug to do it all by itself.

These substances are means, not the end. Learn from how shamans use them if you want to have the real experiences you can have from them. It is not difficult to learn a simple trance method or to use the drug ritualistically and you will really experience things you never imagined possible if you put a little of effort in it.

Title: Re: No more visuals off acid!!!
Post by: BlackIris on May 16, 2013, 03:35 pm
Quote from: Luvs2Trip on May 16, 2013, 01:44 pm
Thanks for the advise! When i first started taking acid i didn't have to do anything for it to give me the magical experience i fell in love with, but obviously this approach is not working for me anymore so I guess i will have to take another approach!

This can happen, especially if with your conscious mind you try to rework the experience; what it will happen is that your conscious mind will literally block the unconscious from giving you that experience.

As a first thing don't expect to have visuals, take the acid just for the sake of the experience in its totality, then *work* to have the visuals (instead of *thinking* about having them); for example you can focus on shades till they morph or you can try to "see" things in shapes etc. until you see them. This will bypass the conscious filter and let you experience again what you experienced before.

One of the greatest obstacles to the unconscious is your conscious mind. It is like in optical illusions: your eyes see something but your conscious mind cannot accept the reality of what the eyes see and you end up seeing all another thing altogether.

There's a fine line between directing a trip and blocking it altogether with your conscious mind. Many people that approach hallucinogenics ritualistically will pass by the stage you are in (also if for different motives) until they learn how to balance in that line without passing through one or the other part. In your case you just need a little "boost" from yourself to let the visuals happens again along stopping bothering yourself consciously about this not happening.

P.S.: Also be aware of what Pakchoi23 has told you. It is more difficult to have visuals in a background that you know too well (for example your room with full light) and certain substances can block the effect altogether. So be sure you are not taking any medications that can influence the action of LSD and choose a place that you don't know too well or it is not in full light (so that you can see/not see; this in turns block again your conscious mind to try to rationalize everything and stopping the effect).
