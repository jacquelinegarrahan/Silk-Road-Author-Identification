Title: Re: How does LSD affect sociability
Post by: junjo on August 29, 2013, 02:17 am
I would suggest doing LSD for the first time in a peaceful, positive environment with one or a group of your closest friends to trip sit for you. LSD is nothing like MDMA. When I did it for the first time, all I did was curl up comfortably in my bed, put on some headphones, and went on some wonderful adventures in my own mind. Socializing just feels odd on LSD, and it's a lot better to use it for self-exploration IMO. I highly suggest not doing LSD for a club situation. If you want to do a psychedelic at a club, 2C-B will give you a far better experience. Or you can just stick to good ol MDA and MDMA.

TLDR; LSD is not a great club drug, and it's a lot better suited for intimate environments
