Title: shrooms + LSD?
Post by: oldtoby on March 28, 2013, 12:11 am
I wouldn't normally think to combine substances that exhibit cross-tolerance - what would be the point? But recently on the forums I was reading someone's claim that shrooms + LSD makes for a better trip than a larger dose of LSD on its own.

Anyone have any experience with this? What about timing? I'm imagining the roller coaster climb coming up on both simultaneously might be a bit intense...

Further, if both are good together, what about as a base for a candyflip? I'm due, and could easily put the three together (or four, with mj).
Title: Re: shrooms + LSD?
Post by: DMTisinME on March 28, 2013, 12:31 am
They combine very well. The come up is identical to taking one or the other IMO. The cross tolerance only matters if you trip on LSD one night and shrooms the next. It's identical in terms of tolerance to taking LSD and more LSD or shrooms and more shrooms. I feel that taking both gives you a fuller trip, in regards to the effects given. The visuals combine very nicely. With shrooms, things are always moving a lot more than on LSD, and on LSD you get the colors and fractals more often, so the combination makes the visual aspect much more intense. As is really all the other effects. I recommend taking them both at the same time. If you take them at different times it would be easier for one to overpower the other. Balance is key.

On MDMA, I shouldn't really comment since I gave it up long ago, but when I did, I took the three together a few times and the result is bliss.  :)
