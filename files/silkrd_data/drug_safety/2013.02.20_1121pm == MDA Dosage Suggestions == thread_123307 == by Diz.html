Title: Re: MDA Dosage Suggestions
Post by: DiamondSky on February 21, 2013, 08:49 pm
Just to put it in perspective I normally roll on MDMA with about 200mg and MDA at about 120mg. For me, those two doses get me to about the same level of spun.

If you are looking to get floored I would probably start at 120mg and bump from there. The trick is, when you take too much, you're not really there to enjoy it. My girl and I did a double bump of MDA a while back and we were just fucking floored. For like an hour. Couldn't talk. Don't really remember it. Just checked out for a while.

For me part of the fun is being aware enough of the experience to actually enjoy it. 180mg of MDA would be pushing it for me in a single dose. Plus, since MDA lasts longer, it's easier to start small and bump up to a bigger dose to climb to a higher and longer peak.

Have fun though, I love the MDA!

Title: Re: MDA Dosage Suggestions
Post by: DiamondSky on February 22, 2013, 07:03 pm
Yeah I was trying to catch you before you jumped onto that ship... it's a fuck of a ride.

It's not that it's not fun, it's just that when you go over the top like that you end up not remembering the freaking trip!

The really cool thing with MDA, unlike MDMA, is you can get to the good spot, 120mg - 150mg depending on where you want to be, and bump to keep yourself there a really long time, unlike MDMA where it's more of a roller coaster ride with lots of ups and downs of diminishing returns.

Most people really just aren't prepared for how visual MDA is compared to MDMA. It's a whole different world.

So next time, give the lower dose a go and see how it goes. I will tell you that the physical / mental side of the high has a sweet spot at the dose I suggested while the visuals just continue to escalate as you go up from there. It can get pretty damn odd as you keep putting more of that stuff in you.
