You read about psychedelics exacerbating underlying schizoaffective disorders. If you were worried about psychosis, you wouldn't be a "heavy ritalin user."

Title: Re: Want to venture in psychedelics, without the psychosis...
Post by: TheDude413 on November 13, 2012, 10:01 pm
I will second the post cautioning against salvia - tried it a couple of times when it was still legal and had terrible experiences...

I think you're on the right track by focusing on whether your mind is ready for the trip; if shrooming or dropping was only about the physical effects I don't think they would have near the cachet among those who swear by them (although the visuals and physical giddiness are my favorite parts).  Keeping yourself in a good state of mind throughout the trip is the best advice anyone can give you and having a sitter will help immensely.

I would also start with a smaller dosage - DO NOT LISTEN to those who tell you to start strong with multiple tabs or 3+ grams.  Especially if you have a couple of days, start with a smaller dosage (maybe half a tab) and then increase as necessary.  You can always take more but can never do less.  At the same time, remember that psychedelic tolerance comes on faster than for Ritalin or opioids, and if you try to trip 2-3 days in a row you will most likely feel nothing by the end.     

There are a lot of guides online accessible through both TOR and clearnet - don't hesitate to read and read and then read some more as well as crowd-source feedback from the forum and IRL friends who might be experienced.

Again, sounds like you're on the right track to be ready for the experience when the time comes, just keep doing your research.
