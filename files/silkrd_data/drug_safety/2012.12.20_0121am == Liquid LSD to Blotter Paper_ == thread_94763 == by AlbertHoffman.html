Title: Re: Liquid LSD to Blotter Paper?
Post by: Drippy on December 21, 2012, 11:09 pm
For a quantity such as that, I would definitely choose a less conspicuous medium.  Altoids, sugar cubes, sweet tarts, smarties, and gummy bears are all pretty popular choices.  When I want to get extra stealthy (and if I'm not planning on taking or giving out any heroic doses), I really like taking one side off of mini oreo cookies, making a little divot in the frosting to drop inside of, then replacing the top.  Unlike all the mediums listed above, oreo cookies will better protect your crystal from 2 of the main 3 elements that degrade L - light and air.  Also, if you happen too get caught w/ an open bag of oreo minis it looks MUCH less suspicious than mediums above which LE are already well aware of.
