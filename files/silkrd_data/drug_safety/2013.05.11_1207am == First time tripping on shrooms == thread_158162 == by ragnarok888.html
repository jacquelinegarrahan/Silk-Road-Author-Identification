Title: Re: First time tripping on shrooms
Post by: happertand on May 11, 2013, 12:25 am
Don't create unrealistic expectations for the trip night, just take it slow and ease into it, I'm sure you're well prepared.

What will the condition of the shrooms be, dried out or fresh? Dried out is the best and most potent, I've never had a "psychedelic" trip on fresh magic mushrooms. How will you be taking your shrooms? Sugar/honey/syrup invigorates the effects of shrooms slightly. What I did was to grate (with a cheese grater) the shrooms onto four sandwiches which contained syrup and peanut butter for me and three friends. This helped with the ingestion of the nasty tasting shrooms and strengthens the effect somewhat.

I can't comment on the weight as I had shrooms quite some time ago. One grow kit (sold legally in the UK at one point) between four people was more than enough for a nice strong trip, provided it's cured or dried out. But I can't tell you how much such a grow kit yielded in terms of weight.

Setting sounds absolutely perfect!

Post by: jameslink2 on May 14, 2013, 01:14 am
Ahh, I remember my first trip, 2g of mushrooms I grew my self. Ground them up and put them into a shot glass with cranberry juice.

How about a little advice.

Inside or outside is all dependent on where you feel the most comfortable. Mine was inside but that is just me. It needs to be a place where you are comfortable and feel safe.

As to planning, I can not count the number of times I planned a trip. Not a single one went to plan! Things you thought you would like while tripping turn out to be boring and the smallest things will catch your eye. It is hard to explain.
I would suggest that you plan your music! Take a lot of time, listening to songs and asking your self how that song makes you feel. It is important to get the right music for the trip and only you can decide on what is the right music. Make sure to have enough music as well, 6 to 10 hours should be enough to get you through the tip.

Never fight a trip! No matter how it goes, just let go and let it take you where you need to go. If you fight it, you'll never win!

I think you've got the right plans.  Whenever I take psychedelics I always try to get as far away form civilization as possible.  A remote place in the beauty of nature is idea.  Also, mushrooms are most visual at night, so that is also a nice plan.  Have you ever taken a psychedelic before?  If you've ever taken acid you'll find mushrooms less intense and not as profound, but still very nice.  I also like your idea of taking a moderate dose to begin with and then boosting the dose once you've got a handle on it and feel you would like more.  A mushroom trip is considerably shorter than acid, so I think you'll be able to know whether you want more an hour after taking the initial dose.  Also, re/ food, I would suggest not tripping on a full stomach.  Eat at least 2 hours before you trip.
