Title: Re: Handling blotters
Post by: weirdal on February 21, 2013, 05:43 pm
I've stored blotter for over a year in a small plastic baggie(powdered drug size) without loss of potency. I made sure it wasn't exposed to light except when being handled(tearing off a few for a fun weekend).  I try to minimize contact with my hands or anything else really while handling.  It can absorb through the skin and I don't want to weaken the blotters by having some of the 'cid wipe off on my fingers.  I DO lick my fingers afterward anyways in case. :P
Title: Re: Handling blotters
Post by: catfishinmysocks on February 21, 2013, 06:27 pm
Light and moisture are the main problems, followed by heat. A lot of people worry about it too much though. I've had blotters that were good after a year sat in a drawer in northern queensland which is super humid...

Do you have some desiccant in the jar and let it come to room temp before opening? You should.
