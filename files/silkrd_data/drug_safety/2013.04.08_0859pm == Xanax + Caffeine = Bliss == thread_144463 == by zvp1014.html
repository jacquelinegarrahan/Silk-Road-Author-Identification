Title: Re: Xanax + Caffeine = Bliss
Post by: Ben on April 09, 2013, 01:02 am
I would not recommend this to everyone, but there certainly is merit to the combination.

As I understand you have been a heavy caffeine user for a long time. Many people are used to a decent dose of caffeine though, even if its just 3 mugs of regular coffee in the morning, it is definitely a stimulant you depend on. Personally i drink about 3 mugs in the morning normally, and i get nasty headaches and such when i don't do so (traveling, no time for coffee, run for the airplane - you get the picture).

Studies have shown that caffeine can actually increase feelings of anxiety, though it would be hard to tell if that is a factor if you are already used to taking caffeine in large amounts as a part of daily life.

From personal experience i'd certainly say that a small (0.5-1mg) dose of xanax can still be effective if you also drink a few mugs of coffee. The idea of downing a xanax with coffee seems very paradoxical to me too, but i've found little downside in doing so. When you flip things around you could say that the coffee mitigates the hypnotic effect of the xanax, while leaving most of its anxiolytic function intact.

I have never tried large dosages though, and despite i have pure caffeine and alprazolam powder available, i don't think it would be a very safe idea to take very large dosages of them combined.

If you are into caffeine that much i would suggest getting it in powder form - its not very expensive at all.

I found the nicest way of taking it is actually adding it to filter coffee. Just drop the desired amount in with the coffee as brew it as you  normally would. Caffeine dissolves quite well in hot water, so it will end up in the brew. Obviously you could also just weigh it out and stir it into a hot cup of java.

You will taste little to nothing off the added caffeine in coffee since its already bitter and has some caffeine to begin with. I find this preferable over taking things like energy drinks that usually contain a shitload of sugar and odd additives.

Considering that i could be an interesting idea to put xanax into your coffee brew, though i find the taste of xanax to be more foul than that of caffeine. Both are bitter to the taste, but xanax bitter taste tends to linger longer somehow
