Holy cow, I hope Koala came back with his mind in one piece!

DON'T DO A FULL EIGHTH of Rocker's shrooms unless you absolutely know what you're doing. The only shroom experiences I've had is with Rocker's stuff, and 1.5g for me triggers plenty of open-eye visuals and slight aural/visual synesthesia. I know that I would not be ready to deal with social situations, but ultimately it's up to you and how experienced you are. 3.5g of Rocker's stuff would probably make me puke and drown in a puddle, hahahaha.

If you haven't done shrooms  before, and you're looking into Rocker's goods, I'd shoot for 1.5g if it's your first time. Grind it up in a coffee grinder, throw the ground shrooms into a mug, and pour near-boiling water into it, like a tea. Makes it hit faster and shortens the trip from what I've heard, but it'll also reduce indigestion/nausea. Good luck and have fun!
Title: Re: Rocker's Mushrooms
Post by: gharyeee on January 18, 2012, 06:51 pm
Wonderful shrooms, I hope you enjoy.

As many are saying, it comes down to experience and what you can handle personally. If you are worried about nausea I would also recommend making a tea (Rocker's review thread has a recipe) or just be sure to crush up the shrooms finely.

My experience with Rocker's shrooms:
2g of the shrooms you can have a decent trip and still be functional. I felt the trip lacked a certain fullness though. Good for a free afternoon imo, maybe a walk through nature.

3g definitely increases time dilation and for me set in for a full trip and I was on the ride. Some confusion (trying to select music, use remotes, etc), but conversation didn't seem to take a hit. When I'm alone however, the 3g dose becomes very internal and I get lost in thoughts quickly.

4g seemed to have much clearer visuals and thrashed my depth perception for most of the trip. Slight nausea, subsided after ~45 minutes. Mostly the same effects as 3g except the euphoria was greater and my thoughts and CEV's were much deeper, with CEV's bleeding into my open eye vision at times.

5g Had nausea on the come up, but it subsided once the trip hit hard. Became non-functional pretty quick and I did get sick at the end of the trip but it was very relieving. I felt my mind was mushrooming up and puking, leaving me incredibly clear headed with AMAZING visuals after I thought they were over with. The puke was probably quarter sized, which is what caused me to think it was mental. Overall they were great shrooms even up to 5g which at times can get very heavy for me. However, if you haven't tried 5g before I definitely wouldn't recommend it with Rocker's yet. They are quite potent.
