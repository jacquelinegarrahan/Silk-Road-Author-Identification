Post by: offbeatadam on June 04, 2013, 11:52 pm
MDMA, like many drugs, act upon the system by causing the brain (or, rather, hormonal/chemical pumps powered by and empowering the brain) to release chemicals into the bloodstream in an unctrolled, far greater manner than would normally be released (if released at all). Any amount of a drug, if a sufficient reservoir exists, will still act upon its duties. Depending on the time since doing MDMA prior to this minor dosage, even a tiny dosage such as 20mg will still trigger a release of seratonin, no matter what, and it'll be a release that is far from normal, which will give you the feeling. This of course is dependent on the amount of MDMA that is actually found in a particular 20mg sample.
