Title: Re: Spacing out MDMA trips?
Post by: Addy on March 05, 2012, 01:08 am
If you wait 3 weeks between those rolls, you'll be fine. But don't make it a habit and wait 3 weeks every time you want to roll. I would recommend a month between rolls, as I noticed that while you'll roll fine if you wait 3 weeks, the longer you space them out the harder it'll hit you. There's "this feels great, I feel chatty, things tingle, I'm happy" and "this feels amazing, I have to rub up on everyone I know, my skin is electric, I'm ecstatic." You'll certainly achieve the first one if you roll every few weeks, but wouldn't you rather the second?

I dunno, just my $.02.
