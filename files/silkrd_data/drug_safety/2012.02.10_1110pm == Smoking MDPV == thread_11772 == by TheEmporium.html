Post by: anytimesoup on July 28, 2012, 02:14 am
I am clearly leaving my run out the gate here a bit late (about half a year or so) but I thought I'd mention this just in case it helps one person.

mdpv only really took off in my city a few months ago and here it is generally smoked, though many have tried eating and sniffing it.  Why I'm not sure, the taste is horrible and my friends and I all found smoking delivered the best results.

Smoked through a water pipe same as weed except we stack the cone with fresh cigarette ash and place no more than 1/3 of a point on top of the ash.  Burning it slow and pulling gently with no intention of ripping the ash cone through delivers (we found) an almost instant high that would last for about three hours depending on body weight, personal disposition etc.  Never dosing sooner than an hour between cones and never more than 1/3 of a point per dose resulted in nothing but positive experiences for the majority (about 25 people).  Avoiding weed and meth during sessions also proved very positive for most. 

Some people can get real fiendish on it though and without some self control the results vary from no sleep for 4-5 days to intense paranoia, shaky nerves and all round "i wanna die" feelings, resulting from taking too much too quickly for far too long. 

Personally I love it and use it often (as per above) with no adverse effects as yet.  Caution should be advised for first time use and as long as users are honest about their behavior and dose/method with themselves and others it's really no worse than the first time someone took a pill, mdma or meth etc in terms of come down/potential for shit times.

Like I said real late here but I hope it helps someone, cheers.
