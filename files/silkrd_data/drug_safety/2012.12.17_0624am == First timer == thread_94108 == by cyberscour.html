I am glad you are asking for good advice before starting out.

General advice on drug taking

It is good practice to reduce your risks as much as possible. Reduce the risk of being around or near law enforcement while on drugs or attracting the attention of law enforcement.

Always have on hand  a close friend who would be sober and able to act if anything went wrong, or you need support. Having this person there physically is the best situation. On the end of speed dial is ok as well. Make sure they know that frantic calling means they should pick up.

Think about the risks of your chosen ROA. If by mouth, will eating before, during or after effect me in a negative way? Think about what you will eat, want to eat while on your drug. MDMA makes you avoid food, while fresh fruit and vegetables taste great on LSD.

Set aside a time and place for your drug taking, make sure that you will be comfortable taking that drug in that place. No shooting up needles in dance clubs, no LSD in crack dens, different environments, different vibes.

Make you understand the length of the drug effect, don't take LSD at 10 in the evening if you have to be at work/school in the morning.

Look after your diet and fitness. Eat fresh non processed foods, and lean meats. If veterinarian make sure you are taking in and adding the correct supplements.  Exercise regularly 2-3 times a week.

Get good sleep, sleep restores the brain chemistry balance to normal. Work with your brain chemistry and not against it.

Be careful with the desire to re dose. Re dosing can cause you problems, set a re dose limit and stick to it.

MDMA or LSD

Depending on what you are after they are different drugs, different effects. Your set, setting and environment should choose which one you do first. Go to a rave, or good dance club with close friends and all take molly. You will have a great time, read about the dangers of too much water consumption, and overheating drink normally not excessively.

If you have a free day, good weather, safe environment, 12 hours free of life interruptions, go for the LSD. Have a sitter or one on hand on the phone if needed.
