Title: Re: Meth and Clonazepam. To maintain, not come down.
Post by: le botbahn on November 05, 2012, 10:12 am
This is my preferred combo and at times life saver in terms of dealing with comedowns, especially during times when life demanded full functionality. It'll take me from paranoid/anxious hermit to my normal self in minutes.

Considering that you rarely take benzos, I recommend staring with 0.25-0.5 mg. Allow it time to kick in, up to an hour. For me the effects begin very soon, especially on an empty stomach (<20 min). If that doesn't cut it, increase by the same amount (0.25-0.5 mg).
A full milligram may put you out depending on your sensitivity. 2 mg probably will probably put you out if your well into your crash and haven't touched meth for several hours our more.

Personally, I find benzos are a must when I indulge. Both to take the edge off comedowns (often virtually eliminating them), and to balance out some the body load that comes with some of the less desirable batches.

Too much benzo, however, will hinder the desirable effects (though clonazapam is much more forgiving in this regard and one of the many reasons I prefer it over alprazolam).

Clonazepam, when used in therapeutic dose/moderation is safe to use for this purpose.

It's a wonderful drug and it's a shame that it's so scarce/costly on SR relative to alprazolam (which also does the job, but I prefer the later).

Title: Re: Meth and Clonazepam. To maintain, not come down.
Post by: Ben on November 06, 2012, 02:22 am
I doubt its a good combination really. A stimulant and a hypnotic at the same time seems like asking for trouble since they will counteract eachother on the main mechanism of action.

You may experience the side effects of one of them, or both, and that can be a nice experience, or a very bad one.

A psychedelic experience should not be triggered by either substance on its own, but it can happen since the main effects cancel each other out, leaving you whatever remains. This can be a nice trip, but also a very bad one, with no sure way to tell how it turns out.

Personally i'd rather take something that is supposed to cause a trip like LSD, with the option if ending a bad one using benzo's or such. Benzo's are far less likely to stop a problem induced by stimulants since they are counteractive.
