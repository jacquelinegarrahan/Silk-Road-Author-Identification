Title: Re: LSD first time
Post by: ahead on June 17, 2012, 02:10 am
if it is truly 150ug then you will definitely be acting odd. i would suggest taking it alone or with one or two really close friends that are either tripping with you or that have tripped before and are baby sitting you. at that dosage you can expect mild visuals, a pretty intense head trip (especially if this is your first psychedelic), time dilation, a moderate intensifying of everything you experience (e.g. music is more intense, watching a movie, even thinking). the most prominent effects will be the mental aspect. in regards to comparing to hash, if i had to give a quantitative estimate off the top of my head, you'll probably be like 3-5 times "more high", but the two can't exactly be compared like this. the first time i tried LSD i had supposed 150ug blotters (were probably in the range of 110-150ug) and had a blast, due to the novelty of the experience. it'll be like nothing you've experienced before. i notice with other drugs like opiates, stimulants, benzos, etc., a good writer can describe the effects pretty reliably. however, the mental "trip" of the psychedelic experience is near impossible to convey without actually experiencing it (the visuals are pretty much just icing on the cake).
