Title: Re: ODing on 2C family?
Post by: Bob Arctor on February 03, 2012, 08:08 am
Investigate the chemical you're going to take. Do 'allergy test' before dosing. Always use well calibrated precise scales. Start with small/average doses and work your way up. Have a sitter for the first time with the chemical.
