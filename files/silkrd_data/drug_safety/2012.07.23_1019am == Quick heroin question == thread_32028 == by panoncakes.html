Title: Re: Quick heroin question
Post by: modest mouse on July 24, 2012, 09:00 am
you cant just take number 4 and cut it to produce #3 because its been converted to the salt form. #3 is freebase, which is easier to convert to salt for dissolving in water than it is to do the reverse. 

smoking #4 is extremely inefficient because the vaporization and combustion temperature are too close, most of the H is wasted. the freebase #3 form vaporizes before it burns and degrades...  cutting will help the vaporization but still most of the #4 will be wasted.
Title: Re: Quick heroin question
Post by: asdfsquared on July 25, 2012, 01:23 am
Why exactly do you want to do this?

it is entirely possible to buy some HCl solution, dilute it, dissolve #3 in it, evaporate the water completely, dry the compound, and you will be left with #4. It won't be any purer, whiter, more soluble in water, or better for administration in any way though. when dope is mostly filler (which it is), its the filler that determines its solubility, color, strength, and purity, and this removes none of that.

purification of said #3/#4 is a different process entirely.

Why does heroin #4 seem "better" than #3? because the process of conversion most typically used includes a purification step.
