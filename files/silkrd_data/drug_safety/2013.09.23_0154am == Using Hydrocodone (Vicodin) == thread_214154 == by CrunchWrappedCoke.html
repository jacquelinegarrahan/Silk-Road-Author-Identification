Title: Re: Using Hydrocodone (Vicodin)
Post by: junjo on September 23, 2013, 03:12 am
I assume by Vicodin you have 5/500 hydrocodone/acetaminophen pills?
If you're just taking two, you should be perfectly fine, since 1000mg is the maximum recommended single dose of acetaminophen.
Just make sure you don't have any alcohol in your system before and during use (very dangerous), and take them on an empty stomach for the best effects.
I'd suggest having benadryl on you in case you get itchy.
In fact, I always take a 25mg benadryl before taking hydrocodone because it potentiates the sedative effects a bit, and removes the itch.
I doubt you have much to worry about, just don't mix them with anything other than a light dose of benadryl and you should be good.
Just don't be disappointed, as 10mg hydrocodone will likely be a very light experience.
You should definitely be safe though, enjoy!
