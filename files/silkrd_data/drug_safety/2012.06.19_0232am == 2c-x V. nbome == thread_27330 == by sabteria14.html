Whats the difference between the 2cs and their nbome equivalents?

The NBOMe compounds are more selective and potent agonists for serotonin 2A receptor than the 2Cs without the NBOMe moiety.  For that reason, they are active in sub-milligram doses, which makes them trickier to administer than the non-NBOMe 2Cs.
