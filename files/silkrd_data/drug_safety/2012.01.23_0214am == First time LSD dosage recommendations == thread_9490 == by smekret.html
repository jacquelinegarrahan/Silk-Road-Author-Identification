Title: Re: First time LSD dosage recommendations
Post by: unborn on January 24, 2012, 04:46 pm
I just want to weigh in a little here.  I have had alot of experience in this, and I want to remind you you will only have 1 first trip.  Not that its a huge thing, but it kinda is.  IT's wierd, its more than anyone could ever explain, but at the same time it's almost not as big of a deal as you are led to believe.  I would not take any less than 2, you can't go wrong.  And if you are worried about some "crises" just have about 4mg of xanax of something similiar on hand and you can just pop that and loosen up.  There isn't this huge freak out risk that everybody seems to try to act like.  I have been to countless festivals and raves with up to 25,000 people and very few people are in the safe tripper tent losing it and when they are its generally from polydrug use, consuming an unknown bs pill(like pcp/ephedra being pushed as rolls), and of course the few with underlying disorders that the tripping brings to the surface(which is actually a good thing because you can identify it and deal with it).  Even if you have a largely negative trip(I highly doubt it) you will more than likely be a better person afterwards, espcially if you use it correctly.
On that note LSD is not a cure all fix all style mind drug that people act like it is.  It very well can be, but with the effort you make when you are sober with the information from yourself from the trip.  I equate it like a microscope.  Scientists don't just look through a microscope day in and day out, they look, gather info then assess it. 

Now mushrooms... thats a different story altogether.  Seriously though, do yourself a favor and go for the 2, you will be happy you did later.

edit:  I also believe that the more you take the less likely you are to freak out.  If you dose 10 hits you won't know what an experience is enough to have a bad one.  Now this might not be always the rule, but I have found it to be true.  Terrence McKenna also shared this belief.  Here is a relevant quote by him, if you are not familiar I would listen to some of his lectures.

 "psychedelic drugs can often bring about psychosis in people who have not taken them"
Terence Mckenna
