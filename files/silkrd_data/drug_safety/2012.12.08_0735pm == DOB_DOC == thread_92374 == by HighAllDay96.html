Title: Re: DOB/DOC
Post by: Joey Terrifying on December 10, 2012, 10:51 pm
never done DOB, but i took DOC once...it was fuckin awesome, and one of the best days of my life.

i took it at midnight and was a little drunk when it was offered to me.  soon though, it cut right through the effects of alcohol and i was very awake, and very much tripping.  as soon as it started kicking in i had one of the best fucks of my life that lasted at least a couple hours....it had the energy of fucking on amphetamine and the spiritual revelations of sex on acid.

after that the girl (not tripping, lol) went to sleep but i was still pumped full of energy.  we listened to records and played with the turntables and i could FEEL the sound so precisely...really got into it.  i felt like i was in total control of the trip...i'd look at a printout of some fractal art, hold it up to my face and jump right in, making the patterns swirl as i desired or act out little dances and stories.  at one point i was looking at the ceiling and thought "i want it to be orange" and it changed just as i wanted.

it was a long trip, like 12 hrs.  around noon the next day my best friend's girlfriend woke up and i was like "YOU HAVE TO TRY THIS DRUG!" so we dosed again and had an equally amazing experience.  i woke up the nymph who was in my bed and went at it again, just as intensely as the previous night, then proceeded to have a pumped up, fully controllable, wacky daytrip.  towards the end i was really hungry and had a wonderful time eating a bunch of weird combinations of food that was in the kitchen.  discovered that spanish olives and pepperjack cheese together are great, amongst other things.

i don't know why i never did it again.  my friends and i were occasionally throwing raves and i thought it would be the perfect new party drug for the kinda scene we were trying to build.

Post by: poolsclosed on December 13, 2012, 02:30 am
I took DOB from a vendor on here (think it was mahakala) and it was great. I think I had 2.6mg, all I know was I took two of his tabs and although I was up for a very long time (and at the end I was damn exhausted), it was full of awesome energy and very alive visuals... it was also a bit of a rollercoaster ride emotionally, going from giggly euphoria to somber contemplation and everywhere in between. My favorite moment was when I saw my friend's face float off his head and project onto the wall. Great fun.

I had a friend who had sex on DOC as well. He said he experienced heavy synesthesia during it, very much to his liking. Joey Terrifying gives a decent account of what I assume DOC is like.

The DOx are apparently very trippy and very fun. DOM is one of Shulgin's magical half dozen.
