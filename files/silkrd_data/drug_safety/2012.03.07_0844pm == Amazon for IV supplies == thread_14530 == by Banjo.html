Title: Amazon for IV supplies
Post by: Banjo on March 07, 2012, 08:44 pm
It was mentioned in a post about IV use, but I thought I'd post it here for more visibility. Amazon is a great place to get IV supplies. Syringes, needles, sterile injection water, and even wheel filters. Although it's legal to get syringes in most US states, I've found you can avoid the awkwardness/condescension you get when asking for syringes at a pharmacy, and you can get filters, which I wouldn't inject without. They also have handy alcohol swabs, tourniquets, and milligram scales. Pretty much all you could want for safe injection.
