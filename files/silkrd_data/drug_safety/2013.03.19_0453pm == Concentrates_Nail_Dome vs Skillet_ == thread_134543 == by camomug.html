Title: Re: Concentrates:Nail/Dome vs Skillet?
Post by: Drippy on March 21, 2013, 10:37 am
I prefer ti nail/dome setup, but it's really a personal preference thing.  If you take earth-shattering sized dabs and aren't accident prone I'd vote skillet, otherwise nails are just more my style.  I've seen some pretty gnarly burn marks from people getting too high and grazing the back of their hand on ti-pads though, so keep that in mind.

If you go w/ a nail setup, I'd highly suggest spending the extra bucks on one of the adjustable ti nails.  That way if you ever get a new dome/female you won't have to worry about the height of the nail being correct.  Also, some of them come w/ different nail heads, so you can keep a small one on most of the time to minimize heating time and then screw on the big boy when you're trying to get weird with it.

My buddy was trying to explain to me these new domeless nails the other day, but I couldn't really grasp the concept/don't smoke enough to care lol.  I'd say do some research into those though - he's a huge oil-head, and was pretty fucking excited about them after hitting one.
