Title: Re: K-Hole and Throwing Up !
Post by: nuyt on December 24, 2012, 10:27 am
First question. Do you vomit a lot or something? What's with the near certainty that'll you'll be vomiting the moment you fully let go of your senses? Do you suffer from severe vertigo or some other condition which can cause extreme nausea? I'm just curious.

Unless you have a strongly affirmative answer to any of those questions, I think you're needlessly worrying. I'm still pretty new to ket, so take this with a grain of salt. But I would recommend starting out with s isomer. Consensus seems to be that it provides a gentler k-hole experience. So far I've only been able to compare one batch of s isomer vs another of racemic, so piddling sample size, but I came to the same conclusion. But even with this racemic, to me the "k-hole" experience is almost totally free of any anxiety or fear. It's not that I'm immobile, or unaware of my surroundings. If the oven caught on fire while I was k-holing, I'm sure I could stumble over and put it out. It's just that k-holing is that amazing lucid dream-like experience, so as long as you're peaking you don't really want to move or engage with your environment.
