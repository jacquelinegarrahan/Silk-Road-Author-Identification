Title: Re: LSD Storage
Post by: jamihebel on July 29, 2012, 11:49 pm
This is a question that has been addressed before on the forums.  Please utilize the search feature next time.  Regardless, it won't hurt to give you some advice.

I would go to a pharmacy (CVS or Walgreens) and purchase 35mm film.  A four pack should run you about ten to fifteen dollars.  Throw out the film and keep the canister.  Inside the canister, store your LSD wrapped carefully but securely in aluminum foil.  The more foil, the better.  Then, inside the canister, put a package of silica gel, which should control moisture and humidity.  If you want, wrap the film canister in another sheet of aluminum foil for additional protection.  Then, store the LSD in a dark, dry, cold place.  A dry freezer or in a container of dry ice is good.  If you don't have access to either, a closet or a desk drawer will do fine.

And yes, heat will degrade the LSD.
