Post by: DiamondSky on March 23, 2013, 02:00 am
I've used it a few times. It's not bad but it's not particularly obvious to me either. It provides some mood elevation, a bit of visual enhancement, a slight body buzz but nothing like "Fuck I'm high!".

This is based off a single sample of the stuff taken in doses ranging from 5-35mg. My results, coupled with my wife as well, seem somewhat unusual so it might be the supply I got was weak or not all Moxy.

As an example, my wife and I took about 20mg each and she got some insane laughing fits out of it to the point she was actually in tears. I wasn't quite there but it was definitely fun. They say it's supposed to make sex amazing but when we gave it a go after the laughing subsided there really wasn't anything there... she was physically aroused but not emotionally if that makes sense.

I'm going to grab some more from a different vendor and do a little more research on it. I had fun taking it but I felt like I was just missing something from it. I got elevated but not high. Things felt good but not great. I had hoped upping the dose would sort it out but I never found an amount that did it for me off that supply anyway.

I know this may not help too much. I just figure lots of people are like "It's the shit!" or "It's the bomb!" so I figured I'd give you a bit more detail to decide for yourself.
