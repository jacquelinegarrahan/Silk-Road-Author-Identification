Post by: Triazolam on May 16, 2012, 06:15 pm
Take your comfortable dosage of ritalin an hour before you begin to study.  About 15 minutes after ingestion, immediately start on whatever you want to focus on.  When it kicks in, you will literally be stuck/addicted and forced into doing whatever you were doing when the pill starts to work.  So if you take a pill, go play video games and wait for it to kick in, you will probably be stuck playing video games for the duration of the pill.  Be in a comfortable environment with no distractions, and start you're work/study as soon as you can after taking it.  From my experience, that's the best way to use stimulants as study-aids.
