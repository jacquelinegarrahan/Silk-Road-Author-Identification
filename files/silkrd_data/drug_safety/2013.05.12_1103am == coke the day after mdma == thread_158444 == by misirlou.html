MDMA is a serotonin releaser.
Cocaine is a dopamine reuptake inhibitor.
Sure MDMA has light effects on the dopamine system, but with the dosages you provided, there should be little to no interaction between the two drugs.

I wouldn't recommend it though, as your already low on serotonin, why fuck with your dopamine system? You're just going to feel much worse than if you did either one.
