bruzzcuzz, as I've said before, it's all about self control and moderation. Meth does make you want to fuck anything and everything (figuratively speaking of course) but at the same time, you have to realize that there is a time and a place for everything. If your having trouble deciding how to stop thinking about porn so much, you answered your own question by stating you should pack it away! Out of sight, out of mind!
