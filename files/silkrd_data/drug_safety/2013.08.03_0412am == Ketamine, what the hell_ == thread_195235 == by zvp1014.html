Post by: BlackIris on August 03, 2013, 12:36 pm
IMO with K you have to decide: or you use it as a recreational drug or you use it as you would a strong psychedelic.

If you go with the recreational route then stick to low doses (naturally taking care of tolerance that increase quite fast). If you go for the psychedelic one instead go for a k-hole. The space in-between the two is usually uncomfortable in both cases because it's not one nor the other and it gives no benefits for what you want to do. If you want a recreational feeling then a too high dose will just make you feel sort of drunk and not able to do what you want to do and if you want a mystical or psychedelic experience everything lower than a k-hole is a waste and  will not give what you search.
