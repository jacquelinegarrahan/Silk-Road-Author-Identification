Title: Re: Wanting to try magic mushrooms
Post by: snark on July 18, 2013, 10:59 am
Start slow with mushrooms, even though you are experienced with LSD they are different drugs and give different experiences. Some people who love LSD become uncomfortable on shrooms (and vice versa!). Shrooms are known for having a "weirder" feeling to them than LSD. I would start with around 2 grams for your first trip and work up from there.

Title: Re: Wanting to try magic mushrooms
Post by: bluedev1 on July 18, 2013, 11:56 am
check out the Shroomery :)

shrooms will latch onto your mental processes slightly differently than LSD so be prepared for that, you should bring your curiosity.  it's a very heady trip.

i had done shrooms over 100 times before trying LSD and got my shit scrambled a few times because of it.  i was just trying to fit it into a mold that it wasn't.  so my suggestion would be to go into it like it's a brand new experience to you, regardless of any LSD experience.

start with no more than 2 grams, no less than 1.5.   or you might like to check out 4-AcO-DMT (Psilacetin) which is a compound analagous to one of the psychoactives in mushrooms (Psilocin - 4-HO-DMT).  some say it's essentially the same experience without much nausea and other weird uncomfortable bodily sensations.
