Title: Re: LSD Ringing in ears?
Post by: Ben on September 13, 2012, 01:35 am
It may be both, one of them, or neither.

Tinnitus is a condition that can be set on by numerous causes, from loud music to substances to no apparent reason at all.

If the symptoms persist i would seriously consider a consultation with a specialist about it. It may seem very innocent at first glance, bit it can have profound effects on quality of life, and in some cases be a synptom of the onset of deafness. This doesn't have to be the case at all though, but only analysis by an expert will be conclusive.
