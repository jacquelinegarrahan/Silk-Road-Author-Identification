Title: Re: Methylone Information/Your Trip Reports
Post by: strangeman on November 18, 2012, 05:35 pm
I had a lot of fun with methylone. I gave it up because I was getting depression symptoms for almost a week after every roll, but that was after a period of weekly use. My wife and I had a lot of long, really powerful talks on M1. I think it really strengthened our marriage and helped both of us talk about things we'd never really been able to before. It also makes just about everything fun. One of the best times I ever had on it was just getting caught up on housework and listening to music.

You want to keep it away from light and most especially moisture. It can be stored in solution for a little while, but it will breakdown and oxidize (ends up looking like piss). I kept 10gs for about six months without noticeable potency loss. I kept it in in a baggie inside of blacked-out mason jar. If you're thinking longer term you might want to invest in some amber vials.

Re: dosage, waiting until the comedown to redose is a waste in my experience. I usually took a bump (about 50% of original dose) right after the plateau. It's tricky, since M1 comes and goes in waves. M1 is pretty forgiving compared to harder drugs, but it can get unpleasant if you take too much (brux
