Title: Re: Most balanced/convenient drug between good/bad
Post by: Gary Oak on April 26, 2012, 02:17 am
Well, for my body, I can tolerate repeated use of Marijuana and MXE. I've been using both daily, with no noticeable effects the next day. Not to mention both last a short duration, which means neither has changed my daily routine at all.

As for Mushies or LSD, the occasional weekend use is something I look forward too for the entire week. As opposed to MDMA, I actually feel amazing after consuming these drugs rather than drained or depressed. They leave a remarkably positive afterglow which can last a few days or even weeks after consumption.

MDMA is worthwhile despite the rather nasty hangovers I receive from it's use. I'm normally quiet and reserved, but MDMA really brings out my personality and gives me the confidence to say what I want. It's made me more comfortable with people and can make some really memorable nights. MDMA also has a shorter duration than mushies and LSD does, which is another factor to consider when comparing these drugs.

DMT is just a god tier drug. It can create some of the most life-changing experiences in only a matter of minutes. If I ever felt the need to, I could drive home, smoke some DMT, and then return to work all within my 30 minute break and just continue working as if nothing had happened. But that would be irresponsible and would be disrespectful to the DMT.

25i-NBOMe is sort of a mix between LSD and MDMA, it's like it's got several wonderful features of both drugs. It's duration is somewhere in between that of MDMA and LSD, and it gives me a slight afterglow but a bit of a hangover feeling to it. It's a bit confusing to explain for some reason, but I would definitely describe it as a good weekend drug.
