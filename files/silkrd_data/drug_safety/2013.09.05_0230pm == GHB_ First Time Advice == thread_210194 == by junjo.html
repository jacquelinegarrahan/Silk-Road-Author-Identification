Title: Re: GHB: First Time Advice
Post by: LeadSingersDisease on September 05, 2013, 08:13 pm
1 - Check with the vendor if your unsure or the listing is unclear. If your still unsure you may want to find out how much GHB can dissolve in 1ml so you can at least minimize your risk by diluting and taking a tester dose.

2 - Do a light erowid dose. GHB is naturally occurring so your body knows how to break it down safely and quickly. Then when you dose again gauge it from there. I don't think you can get the full spectrum of effects in one dose.

3 - Not a bad idea. I have a tendency to black out with a lot of downers but I didn't have a problem at all with GHB but everyone is different so handing it to a sitter is a good idea.

4 - H and etizolam both have a fairly short half life I believe. I would leave it until next day just in-case due to your inexperience with GHB and H + benzo (or thienobenzodiazepines) not really good due to risk of respiratory failure adding GHB in the mix isn't a good idea especially if your still feeling any effect.

5 - With my limited experience with it it's kinda like alcohol but without the hangover/asshole drunk effect. Nice and chilled but has quite a steep dose responsive curve.

I'm fairly inexperienced with GHB but follow some basic harm reduction and you should be fine. You may find a little tolerance especially if you have any built with your etiz use. I found diazepam seemed to produce tolerance for GHB so im assuming anything that works on GABA receptors is probably going to produce tolerance
