Post by: Banjo on June 01, 2012, 09:26 pm
Since no one's actually answered your questions:

Do's:
-Use a wheel filter. Even if it looks really clear, there are likely things in there that you don't want in your bloodstream.
-Get a good milligram scale so that you can accurately measure how much you're taking
-Start off very small. Start with 5mg, and work up from there. Heroin is (give or take) twice as potent as morphine. A standard medical dose of IV morphine for pain management is 10mg. If you've never done opiates (vicodins don't really count, in my book) then maybe even start off with 2mg, although that's probably pretty conservative. But better conservative than dead.
-Use a new, clean needle each time
-Properly prep your injection site. Wash your hands with soap and water, and clean the injection site with alcohol
-Follow the 8/72 rule: use for no more than 8 hours at a time, and stay off completely for a minimum of 72 hours afterward

Dont's:
-Order a whole gram for your first time. Since your tolerance is likely low, a gram will last a long time, and unless you have amazing self-control, you'll be more likely to use longer/more than you should.
-Assume all H you order is the same strength. Test each and every batch you get with a small amount so that you can gauge how strong it is before you take the amount you usually do to get where you want.
-Combine with other depressants (alcohol, benzos, etc...) until you're used to how it effects your body alone. Better not to combine at all, but definitely not till you're comfortable with what it does to your body.

Be prepared for:
-Never wanting to do any opiate any route but IV. It feels so much better, you'll never want to go back
-Severe nausea the first few times. If this happens, lie on your back and take slow, deep breaths. This should help. Also try eating some ginger, or making some ginger tea (from fresh root). It helps me out quite a bit.
-An urge to do IV opiates that never really goes away. Even if you're not addicted, or even close, there's always that small, nagging feeling that tells you it would be so nice if you could IV some opiates.
