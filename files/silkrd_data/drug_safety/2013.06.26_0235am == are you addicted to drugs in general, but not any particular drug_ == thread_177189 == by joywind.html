Title: Re: are you addicted to drugs in general, but not any particular drug?
Post by: SourGrapes on June 26, 2013, 10:58 pm
Yes, i am definitely a poly-substance abuser. i prefer opiates, benzos, alcohol, marijuana, barbiturates, and 'downers' in general. off and on, i am physically addicted to various substances -- i can't count the number of times i've been through withdrawal from alcohol, benzos or opiates... right now i'm dependant on opiates, and possibly benzos as well (i'm not sure on the benzos atm.) i just need to slow my mind down, drive out the panicky anxiety and depression, and calm myself down/provide some comfort... any way i can. since drugs are the most pleasurable coping mechanism for me, that's the route i choose. plus, with the opiates and MJ, i live with chronic pain that can be severe, so that both necessitates opiate/medical MJ use and exacerbates the depression i self-medicate for.

as for whether it's 'bad,' well, i think that is subjective. if you're unable to cope with daily life without some sort of crutch, i wouldn't call that healthy. more than likely there are some psychological stressors you're avoiding with your drug use. but some people use drugs as a crutch. some people use religion. some use alcohol. some use computer games like World of Warcraft as an escapist technique. any of those methods can be used moderately, or it can be overdone and ruin lives.

some people can handle everyday drug use and function, some cannot. if you're going to use something every day, just try to understand why, and try to keep grounded. take a day off now and then to reconnect with your non-drug self, and maybe examine the root of the need to be high all the time. try to take breaks to avoid becoming physically dependent on anything, and to avoid avoiding your own consciousness completely. don't lose sight of who you really are or why you're doing whatever it is you're doing.
