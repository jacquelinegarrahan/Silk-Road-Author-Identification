Title: Re: Sup with Shroom Grow Kits?
Post by: GrimWaldo on August 20, 2013, 12:03 am
1) I live in US, so no idea.
2) I've never purchased one, but considering the simplistic nature of the requisite supplies I would assume that they're adequate.
3) Sure do. As a novice you should read FungiFun's 'PF-Tek for Simple Minds', here: http://www.fungifun.org/English/Pftek
All you really need is a spore syringe. Everything else (like brown rice, bird seed, canning jars, foil, pressure-cooker) should be available at your local grocery or hardware store... except perhaps vermiculite. I understand that vermiculite can be difficult to obtain in other regions. You can buy 2 cubic feet for $20 here in the US (that's more than plenty).

Read and follow the Tek exactly and you WILL have success. This shit is easy if you can follow directions.
Once you have a grow or two under you belt, hook up to the Shroomery.org. It's probably the biggest and best collection of amature mycology techniques available on the Net.
Once you have a forum login, be sure to check out 'How Frank Gets Shit Done' to see the whole process from a professional grower's point-of-view.
http://www.shroomery.org/forums/showflat.php/Number/17897163#17897163
Title: Re: Sup with Shroom Grow Kits?
Post by: HenryC0833 on August 20, 2013, 12:22 am
The key to the legal question is whether or not the spores are legal in your country. 

Psilocybin is illegal pretty much everywhere but psilocybin mushrooms aren't specifically illegal in many venues, you get busted because they have psilocybin in them.  (By that logic, you could get busted for carrying your DMT-containing brain around with you, but that's another story).  Since the spores don't have psilocybin in them, you're good to go, unless in your area the government specifically made them illegal.
