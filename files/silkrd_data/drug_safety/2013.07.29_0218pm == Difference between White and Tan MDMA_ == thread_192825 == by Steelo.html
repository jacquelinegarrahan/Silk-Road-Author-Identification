Post by: freecondomsale on August 04, 2013, 07:53 pm
Technically white could be clear that has been crushed  :P.  But yeah, what eskimo said.  Discoloration would stem from impurities.  Whether they are from unreacted material or a product of side reactions depends on the reaction.  MDA will typically never be a precursor.  Sure you could use it to make MDMA but why bother?

Crystals not powder is always a good sign.  Even if the crystals are discolored it's probably just superficial.  Clear and odorless would be the best.  I've noticed molly has a slight sassafrasy smell if it hasn't been recrystallized (even when it's high purity).  Most discoloration can be removed with an acid/base style work-up (that often gets neglected in many reactions even though it should be standard... I've skipped it before  :-[).
