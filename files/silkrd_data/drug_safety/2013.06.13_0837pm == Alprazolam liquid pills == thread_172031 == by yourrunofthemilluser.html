Title: Re: Alprazolam liquid pills
Post by: Ben on June 14, 2013, 01:38 am
As long as you are getting a real solution with the everclear, just regular stirring/mixing will suffice to get an equal distribution of xanax through the batch.

The question probably is if you are getting a true solution, or something that resembles one but actually is not. If you manage to dissolve the tablets completely you should have a clear liquid, nothing precipitating to the bottom or floating to the top. This is not always that easy to see however. One thing you could do is leave your solution over night in a sealed container and see if it still looks clear and even the day after.

The solubility of alprazolam in water/alcohol mixtures increases with temperature, so do not put the solution into the fridge or anything like that. Just leave it on a tabletop at room temperature but covered with a stopper/lid/shrinkwrap for a night.
