Title: Re: Drug testing and NBOMe
Post by: le botbahn on August 20, 2012, 10:14 pm
You probably won't find any solid information on the topic at this point and time.

However, the dosage for NBOMEs are extremely low. I'm not a toxicologist, but it seems likely that one of it' s metabolites would be a 2C/phenethylamine compound. Typical 2C doses are at least 10-20x + higher than that of NBOME. For example, 2C-B is usually taken in doses of 15-20 mg, while NBOMEs are typically dosed at 1.0 mg.

Given that most common phenethylamines are undetectable after 24 hrs, and at most 1-3 days...I'd imagine that NBOMEs would be undectable by the 24 hr mark (if at all). I wouldn't be surprised if they didn't even make it past the cutoff concentration.

I'm only speculating so don't hold me to this. And I'm speculating on urine and saliva tests only so don't bet your freedom on it.
The body doesn't always operate logically so best to err on the side of caution.

That said, I was in his shoes and was confident that I'd have 24-48 hrs to clear a piss/swab test, I'd probably feel ok risking a single typical dose of 1.0 mg.

But again, take this with a grain of salt.
