Title: DMT - best drug ever?
Post by: Joey Terrifying on December 08, 2012, 10:29 pm
i originally wrote a long and rambling post about my whole history with DMT, but decided to ax it and mostly talk about my experiences as of late:

i tried DMT years ago and scared myself shitless.  i was headstrong and cocky about psychedelics and was so eager to dive right into the deep end of DMT, hard and fast.  it was way more than i had bargained for.

so i thought about my experiences.  for a few years actually.  i had tried smoking it again a couple times but would get the shakes so bad i couldn't hold the lighter.

for some reason though, i knew i wanted to use it again.  so i got some MHRB and did an extraction, and then just held on to it for about a year or two.  my wife was excited and bought a glass vapor genie.  but i remained apprehensive.

so i don't know what happened, but lately i just started going for it and smoking the stuff...and it has been FANTASTIC.  i've been keeping my doses low, so far, slowly working my way up...i started with 10, then 15, then 20, etc.  the last couple times i smoked, i was kinda bummed that i hadn't put more in the pipe.

i think my problem before was that my goal was to get as big of a hit as possible and then go fucking mental for 5 minutes.  but now, i'll take a normal hit and let it mellow...see where it takes me.  i'll take another hit or two (oooo, the GVG hits so smooth) until the walls melt around me, which is endlessly entertaining.  it feels like a full body orgasm, every time;  it is just that wonderful.  sometimes the feeling is so overwhelmingly good that i just close my eyes and have a major moment of "space out."  its like taking a hit of nitrous after a bump of K while tripping on acid.  but then it all fades and within 15-20 minutes of my last toke i'm back to back to baseline with absolutely no hangover.  i just go about my day.  its actually, the opposite of a hangover....i feel better, sharper...

the best day goes like this:  i wake up, go to the gym for a 45 min workout.  come back home, take my dog out for a walk, then crawl back into bed with a bowl of DMT, trip balls for 15 min, then go make breakfast/coffee, shower, and get to work.  that thing about it being "the businessman's trip" has had me laughing a few times when i'm doing work an hour after tripping balls.

to be able to enjoy DMT to the extent that i currently do, i had to unlearn everything i had read or heard about it.  all the shit about gnomes and alternate realities and peeking at the afterlife....forget all that shit.  learn to dance with confusion and not care about quantifying or explaining things.  just tell yourself "there's only now."  and go slow, because DMT is the real deal magic spice...not for kiddies.  learned that the hard way.

its been over 6 years since my first toke and i feel like my relationship with this drug is just beginning.  so i'm certainly no expert, but i'm starting to get the feeling that DMT is a very malleable drug experience.  in other words:  DMT is whatever you want it to be.  if you want mystery you'll get it, if you are afraid you'll get fear, if you want psychedelic bliss...well, thats what i've been getting lately :)

hope some other seekers out there find their way to the loving embrace of this non-drug.

I classify drugs according to their effects on consciousness.

Emotional-social. consciousness: Alcohol, amphetamines, LSD.

Sensory-somatic consciousness: Marijuana, MDA, LSD.

Aesthetic consciousness: DMT, mushrooms, marijuana, LSD.

Astral-imaginal consciousness: DMT, mushrooms, LSD.

Cosmic consciousness: LSD, mushrooms, mescaline.

It will be noted that LSD is perhaps unique in that it can induce all levels of consciousness, although with respect to astral-imaginal consciousness, DMT is undoubtedly superior to LSD.

Nevertheless, you can't get any higher than cosmic consciousness, whether through psychedelics or other methods. Everything else is form, creation, Maya, potentiality. And that includes astral-imaginal entities just as much as the physical world.

Can you experience cosmic consciousness on DMT? Perhaps, but astral-imaginal seems to be the norm.

Quote
i'm also confused as to why you classify LSD and Psilocybin as "spiritual" and DMT as "not spiritual."

Well, the word spiritual is used in different senses by different people. A lot of people would use it in a sense that encompasses both astral-imaginal consciousness and cosmic consciousness. If that's how you are using the word spiritual, then I agree with you; DMT is very spiritual, in the sense. I'd just place cosmic consciousness above astral-imaginal consciousness, and therefore I would consider mushrooms and LSD to be 'better' than DMT, at least for spiritual purposes.
