Title: Re: Drugs to avoid while on antidepressants?
Post by: Railgun on September 17, 2013, 09:09 pm
Pretty much most of the serotonin receptor agonists are going to be either moot or so weak that you will notice almost nil effect. The reason being is that SSRIs block the reuptake of serotonin-and, unfortunately, chemicals with similar structures. So shrooms, lsd, mescaline and anthying that you research that works on 5HT receptors are probably not going to work.

 MDMA; I am unsure of this one. I heard it works more dopaminergically; however, it does have some mechanistic 5HT action, which I have found so profound that it similarly kills the MDMA.

MAOIs such as syrian rue that are often used in conjunction with serotonergic drugs, such as DMT, may be fatal in conjunction with SSRIs. The reason is that these keep monoamines from breaking down; since the SSRI blocks reuptake, you can wind up with serotonin syndrome and die due to surplus serotonin.

Hope this helps.
