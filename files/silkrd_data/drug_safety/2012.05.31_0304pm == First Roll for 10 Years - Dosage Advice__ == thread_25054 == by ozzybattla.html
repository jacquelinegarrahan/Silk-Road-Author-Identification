Title: Re: First Roll for 10 Years - Dosage Advice??
Post by: sollemnis on May 31, 2012, 08:42 pm
I think it really depends on how intense you want your roll to be. Sure, you can roll from 100mg if you have no tolerance, but it won't be an 'intense' roll.

Please remember that street pills are cut with all sorts of shit... I remember pills that were very stimulating because they were cut with speed/meth. MDMA itself is relaxing, but not in the same way as a depressant. MDMA is a class of its own - it has magic that no other drug has. The sense of peace and forgiveness you feel towards the world is insane. You'll find yourself thinking about all the people in your life and truly understanding them. It's a life changing experience if used for the RIGHT reason. Don't use MDMA to get a quick 'buzz' - fuck it does uplift your mood but so do a lot of other drugs. It's MDMA's own unique properties that make it 'magical'.

It's hard to have a bad experience with MDMA - anxiety? Hell no! It takes all your feelings of anxiety and pain and lets you focus on what truly matters rather than what we ourselves think matter.

I'd highly suggest a starting dose of 140mgs if you're feeling nervous - The 'rush' so to speak can be intense... after a while though you begin to enjoy a good rush.

Also, re-dose when you're at the peak! It extends it by about an hour, 40mgs is enough as a 'booster' dose.


Sollem
