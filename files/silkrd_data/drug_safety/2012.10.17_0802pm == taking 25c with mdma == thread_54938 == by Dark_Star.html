Title: Re: taking 25c with mdma
Post by: Ensine99 on October 19, 2012, 10:39 am
I've done it. took two 600 Âµg tabs (HH) and 150mg of dutch's MDMA
took the first tab at 10:40 (0:00)
2nd tab was placed in at 1:40 (+3:00)
MDMA was taken in a cap at 2:10 (+3:30)
I was starting my 25c peak around +2 at a party, after which i felt like going all the way. After getting home from the party I put in the 2nd tab and took the mdma.
It should be noted I'm around 100kg and ~180cm/5'11
My advice would be to take both tabs around 3 hours maybe longer before the mdma, wait till you feel the peak starting, because I ended up finishing the mdma peak at around 5:30-6am (+6:30-+7)
but still had solid visuals, OEVs and CEVs till around 9am or (+10)
Also I was unable to sleep till 11AM maybe a little later.
It was an extremely powerful experience and gave me a really focused head-space where I worked through a lot of emotional stuff that I didn't even really realize was weighing on me (I kinda did, but not as much as it actually was). I do have to say though, keep yourself away from Facebook/Skype/your phone as it can lead to some overzealous messaging, and if possible do it with someone else so you can share things on it (I was with drunk people so it wasn't as bad as it would have been with sobers, but I still felt that I was a little over the top for them at times)
My CEVs were quite focused, kinda like a mid dose dmt trip- people walking around talking (there was a literal party in my head) there are also flashing lights, color spectrum etc. no real geographical stuff in the CEVs but closer to a very vivid dream-scape.
OEVs (in darkness) consisted of tracers, visual noise, and hallucinating cartoon caricature faces - much like old school loony toons. and some fairly light color activity.
OEVs (in light) consisted of "3d vision" melting/breathing walls, swirling vision, geometric patterns, and enhanced colors.
Honestly I don't think I would have enjoyed it that much at a rave, I was very "clean oriented" sweaty people grossed me out a lot, heat was a disphoriant (that said I dislike heat to begin with)
And at one point I found myself washing my hands for about 10 minutes (just on 25c btw) because I didn't like how they felt, they felt a little sticky (maybe from beer)

Its an amazing trip and I'll be repeating it again, although I think I'll dose both tabs at the same time and tone back the mdma a little (135-140 probs, maybe mix with mda? 70/50)
That said I've had friends go to raves on both 25c and mdma independently and they enjoyed both, so the discomforts I listed might just be specific to me.
