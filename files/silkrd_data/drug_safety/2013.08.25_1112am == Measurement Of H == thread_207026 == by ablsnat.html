Post by: DigitalDream on August 25, 2013, 12:18 pm
Measure it volumetrically, and then just shoot the liquid if you use needles, and if you don't use needles then evaporate all the water you used to measure volumetrically and you'll be left with powder whose mass is defined by how concentrted you made it when you made a solution from it (eg. 10mg/ml  if you take 1ml and evaporate off the water you will have 10mg of H).

Idea is you buy a mass you can weigh on your scale, put that whole mass in a known volume of water, eg. you have 500mg, add that to 50ml and you'll have 500mg/50ml .... or 10mg/ml)
