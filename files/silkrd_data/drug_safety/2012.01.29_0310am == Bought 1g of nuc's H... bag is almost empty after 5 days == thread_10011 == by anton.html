Title: Re: Bought 1g of nuc's H... bag is almost empty after 5 days
Post by: cacoethes on January 30, 2012, 05:39 am
Quote from: lowberry on January 29, 2012, 06:40 am
and I don't think its possible to develop a physical dependance/addiction after 5 days unless you were taking other opiates before that

Yeah, I agree.  It's unlikely that you'll have any physical wd symptoms after just five days, unless you've been a weekend warrior for while.  When I first realized I was developing a pill habit, it was like having the flu, or a cold...  That's what I actually thought was wrong with me at the time.  This was after months of nearly continuous use of OC.  Should have done something about it then, but I just didn't give a shit at the time...

My advice: take a break for a while.  Being dope sick is hell on Earth- like the worst flu you've ever had in your life, combined with an anxiety so deep you'll want to jump out of your fucking skin.  You twitch.  You whine and moan.  Your legs kick aimlessly around while you lie there writhing in misery.  You will likely vomit and shit at the same time, and you'll stink badly- and your sense of smell will be hyper-sensititive.  You'll sweat like a pig, and then you will be freezing cold.  You'll feel miserably depressed, and you will hate yourself, your dealer, your job, your life.  You WILL NOT be able to function normally, and you will not be able to hide it.  It's fucking awful, and if you ever progress to full blown dependency, you might be surprised at the things you'll do to avoid feeling like this.

When you get to this point, the greatest joy you will ever experience is how it feels going from horribly dopesick to gloriously well in a matter of seconds...
