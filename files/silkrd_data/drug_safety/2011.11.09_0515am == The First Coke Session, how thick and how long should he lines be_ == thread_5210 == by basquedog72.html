Not necessarily.  Everyone's different as far as their tolerance level.  How did you feel after the 6 hour session?  Were you sweating, heart racing, generally not feeling well?  1g in 6 hours is not excessive in my opinion but it is a little on the high side for me personally.   I generally keep my consumption around .5g over an evening.  I measure it out before I start, then put the stash away.

As far as your lines, draw 'em any way you like 'em.  Thicker, longer lines puts more powder up your nose all at once possibly meaning more waste and sinus irritation.  If you get like me, and enjoy the process of measuring, chopping, then drawing the lines (all the "prep" work), you'll probably do more, smaller or thinner lines.  Mine tend to be on the order of 20-25mg so over the course of 6 hours, I would do 20-25 lines vs. 3-4 @ 1.5g or whatever.    Also, the finer it's chopped the better for obvious reasons.

Enjoy!
