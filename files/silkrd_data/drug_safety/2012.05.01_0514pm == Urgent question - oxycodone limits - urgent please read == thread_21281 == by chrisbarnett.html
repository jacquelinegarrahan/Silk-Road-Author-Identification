Title: Re: Urgent question - oxycodone limits - urgent please read
Post by: captainjojo on May 01, 2012, 05:25 pm
Just to be clear, you are talking about percs with 10mg of Oxy in them, correct?

Percs last about 4-6 hours and you will usually peak around the 30-60 minute mark.

I would say if you are used to taking 2, then one more would be fine. 

I don't think taking one more perc is going to hurt you if you can take 2 without any problem.

My tolerance is fairly high, and right now I could probably take at least 10 without any problem.  When my tolerance was low, I would take one or two and get a nice high.

My best advice to you would be this.  If taking two gives you a nice high, wait a couple hours before taking another 1 or 2, that will give you a slightly longer buzz.

Also, I find Oxy's can make it hard to get a good nights sleep if you take them too close to bed time.  You feel great, and you figure you can sleep no problem, only you end up lying there either not able to fall asleep, or you wake up in an hour.

Yes, everybody is a little different.  Which is why I usually say start with half your normal dose, whatever that might be, and work your way up.  Remembering that these things can take some time to work, so wait at least an hour or so with oxy's.

The one to really watch out for is Methadone.  It takes 3 hours to peak.  So some kid takes 20-40mg and doesn't feel anything for 90 minutes, starts taking more, then passes out 3-4 hours later when it all starts to hit.
