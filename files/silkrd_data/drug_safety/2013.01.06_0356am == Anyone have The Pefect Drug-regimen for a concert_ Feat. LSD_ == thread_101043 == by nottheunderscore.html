Post by: c0smicsh4rts on January 06, 2013, 10:51 pm
My favourite party/rave/festival combination:

140mg MDMA + 60mg MDA

... enjoy the roll, when it starts to fade at t +3:00-4:00 drop 20-50mg 2c-b + ~300ug LSD (I tend to go for 40-50mg 2c-b as I like the visuals to be quite intense)

This takes you into a wonderfully playful partying headspace, yet still leaves you clear-headed enough to have some good conversations or deal with boring shit if need be :)

2c-i / 2c-e are quite fun in this combo too, but i prefer 2c-b

Personally... I would not mix in DMT or 2c-t-7 for going out... DMT sends me away and would not be fun at a party, much better at home. 2c-t-7 also better at home in my opinion... when mixing with LSD at least.

I'm also not too much of a fan of the usual method of candyflipping... stacking the peaks. I find the MDMA frequently overpowers the LSD experience, speeding things up and even reducing the visuals a bit for me.

The order I mentioned above... it feels like you're rolling for 8-10 hours and the visuals stick around for a good time. The LSD lets you spiral off into little experiences, while the 2c-b helps you stay present, so you can spiral off into a mini universe half way through a sentence & come back to the sentence, carrying on as if you'd never gone anywhere.

It's a fantastic time :)
