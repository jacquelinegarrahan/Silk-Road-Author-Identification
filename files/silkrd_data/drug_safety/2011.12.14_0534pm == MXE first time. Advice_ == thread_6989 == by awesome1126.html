Title: Re: MXE first time. Advice?
Post by: senile on December 30, 2011, 04:21 am
I had read a lot of debate about routes of administration for MXE on the internet - most people said oral was ineffective, others seemed to confused sublingual with oral, and all in all it is confusing.
Usually, I take 30mg sublingually and that puts me in a pretty weird, enjoyable state, but I must say, not swallowing for 10-15mins is a pain.
So, last night I did an experiment. I made a gelatin capsule with 30mg mxe and swallowed it down, then went out for supper. About 15-20mins later, I felt the first signs of it kicking in, and from there it escalated quickly into the same level of dissociation as I feel from sublingual administration.

So, if anyone has been put off by the debates around mxe routes of administration, I can say that I have found straight up eating mxe to yield identical effects as subbing.
