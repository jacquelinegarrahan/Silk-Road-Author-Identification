Title: Re: Extract HTC from weed using Alcohol?
Post by: UKGrower on July 21, 2012, 01:37 pm
For best results with Alcohol extractions, go the QWISO route.  The "QW" part means "quick wash", which means that you don't leave your material soaking for any real length of time.  THC dissolves very quickly in isopropyl, and the longer you leave the material in, the more other stuff dissolves in with it (chlorophyll, plant waxes etc.). 

You can always do a second, longer run with the material afterwards, but the first extraction will give the best quality and the majority of the yield.

Plenty of stuff about this on youtube etc.
