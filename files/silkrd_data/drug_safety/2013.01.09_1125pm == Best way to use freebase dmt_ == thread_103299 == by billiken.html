Personally, Ive tried several methods and will list them all:

Freebase, in a joint sprinkled on top of a layer of tobacco, approximately 50mg (eyeballed). To share with my significant other, has allowed for quite a relaxing effect for about 5 minutes. Some nice geometric-pattern based visuals and very mild closed eye visuals (CEVs). Bit of a waste but its nice for relieving the days tension without blasting off.

In Changa. Using approx 200mg of freebase dissolved into 5ml of pure isopropyl alcohol, then placing your herb of choice into the liquid (in my case I used Passionflower herb which has mild MAOI effects to prolong the experience). I used about half a gram of passionflower in the alcohol-dmt solution and allowed to evaporate overnight; infusing the herb with DMT. By then smoking normally through a bong/water pipe I found breakthroughs were quite achievable with generous cone-sizes. The prolonging effect of the passionflower stretched out each cone to about 20 mins long, with the first 5 minutes being in hyperspace..

Freebase in a glass vaporiser. Not my preferred method as the technique must be exceptionally good and is often more trouble than its worth. If youre an experienced vaporiser (ie. ice or meth or crack through a glass pipe) then its worth a shot. Risk of burning the liquid and producing an ugly tasting vapour is the trouble here. Breaking through with 1 hit is very rare unless your technique is good OR you have a chore boy (piece of wire scrubber) in there. I have a glass pipe called The Ubie and have another meth pipe (bulb type) on the way and will give that a go. I also read good things about the GVG but I cant afford to shell out for one!

Freebase sandwiched between the herb of your choice. In my case, I have given up smoking hooch and am more interested in lengthening the DMT experience with other herbs which are more pleasant to smoke and more readily available and cheaper than hooch. There are a bunch of great herbs out there, each with their own note, flavour and some say plant-teacher within the herb.  Personally I like using herbs which have an MAOI effect to lengthen and potentiate the trip.

Herbs like passionflower, caapi, pau dárco, damiana, mullein etc.

I LOVE CHANGA but I also really like the sandwich method. Itś foolproof and almost always guarantees a breakthrough hit in one lungful, so long as you BURN IT SLOWLY and allow the DMT to vapourise slowly rather than burn and taste disgusting. Ive never coughed or gagged on a bong-hit of DMT yet with this method and by fuck I have hit the edges of hyperspace.
