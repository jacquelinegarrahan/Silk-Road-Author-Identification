At 265 lbs you should definitely drop the entire pill from the get-go. You may want to also pick up another one of those "?"s, trust us. The first time is always magical.

If you could provide is with more details on set and setting, we will be able to tailor our responses for maximum enjoyment :) However, here are a few universal tips:

Before you roll:
1. You don't need to prep anything beforehand since this is your first time. Just make sure you aren't already high on anything, and have had some time since you last tripped for maximum enjoyment (1-2 days of sobriety)

Dropping:
1. When you drop, please do it at least 2 hours after eating. Since you have experience with shrooms, you know how it is tripping when you just ate.
2. Prepare music. Electronic Dance Music (EDM) works exceptionally well when you are rolling. You will seriously 'feel' the music down to your soul. Here are some solid song selections that never fail to impress and invigorate:

Play at high volumes!
Sebastian Ingrosso ft. Ryan Tedder - Calling (Lose My Mind) < get the extended version for even more fun
Eva Simons - I Don't Like You
Zedd ft. Matthew Koma - Spectrum (the vocals will shoot you to the stars)
Hardwell - Spaceman
Calvin Harris ft. Example - We'll Be Coming Back
Knife Party - Antidote (watch the explicit music video, it will rock your socks)

3. Find someone else to do it with you (with another ?). MDMA is a seriously social experience, especially the first time.
4. Make SURE to drink at LEAST 1 pint of water per hour. MORE if you are a sweaty sweaty mess on the dancefloor. If you can, you should also prepare at least 2 bottles of sports drinks (Gatorade) to replace essential salts in your body. Doubly important if you are a sweaty mess.
5. Prepare MENTHOL cigarettes. You may not like them usually, but when you roll they are freaking amazing.
6. Get a VICKS INHALER. Uniquely exciting for the nose.

Post roll:
1. Eat a well balanced meal the next day.
2. Take 5-HTP supplements. Since it's your first time you probably wont' have a hangover, so this is optional.
3. Reflect on your night. Seriously, you have to think about how awesome it is because it's so awesome. Play another EDM song to see if you still feel it down to your bones.
4. Smoke a joint, it helps alot with relaxation.
5. Drink more water!! The dehydration will last for at least 2 days if you're not careful.

Fun things to do while rolling:
1. Dance. Loud music feels so awesome
2. Talk. Connect with your best friends, MDMA brings people very very close
3. Laugh. The world will look like a brilliant brilliant perfect place.
4. Scream. Show everyone just how much you are up there
5. Watch a sunrise. It is beautiful as FUCK.
6. Roll around in the grass. It feels amazing.
7. Of course, sex. If you can somehow get it up, it feels gooood.
8. Get someone to drive you on a highway with music.
9. Hugs
10. Massages feel great. Really really great.
