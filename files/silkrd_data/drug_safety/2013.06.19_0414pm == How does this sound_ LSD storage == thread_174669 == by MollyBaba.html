Title: Re: How does this sound? LSD storage
Post by: ☼LightOfPi☼ on June 19, 2013, 09:07 pm
Wrapping in aluminum foil + vac sealing + putting somewhere dark sounds about right.
This will keep your tabs potent for months up to a year at least.

If you're looking for storing them multiple years I would definitely put them somewhere cold.
You can for example put the plastic bag in some sort of container (e.g. a flat tupperware box) and put them in your freezer or fridge. Just let the container come to room temperature before opening it to avoid condensation. Adding some desiccant or rice also helps for this.
