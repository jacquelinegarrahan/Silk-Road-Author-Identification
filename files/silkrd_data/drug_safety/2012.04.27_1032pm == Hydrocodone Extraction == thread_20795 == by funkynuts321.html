Title: Re: Hydrocodone Extraction
Post by: cacoethes on April 27, 2012, 10:38 pm
Easy, peasy.

Hydro is soluble in cold water.  APAP is almost insoluble.

Crush the pills.  Add enough cold water (think ice water, just above freezing) to wet the pill mass, make a sort of runny slurry.  Filter off the pill mass, keep the filtered water.  The amount of water doesn't  matter, but it should be enough to make a runny slurry.  You're going to drink the water, so you might not want to use pints, get it?

Drink and enjoy.

EDIT:  This is an extremely simple process.  Don't muck it up by making it more complicated than it needs to be...  You need not have good "lab technique" to make it work, so don't even worry about following some of the more elaborate write-ups out there.  The idea is to isolate MOST of the hyrdo from MOST of the APAP, not 100%.  The difference in solubility in cold water between the two compounds is enough to achieve your goal.
