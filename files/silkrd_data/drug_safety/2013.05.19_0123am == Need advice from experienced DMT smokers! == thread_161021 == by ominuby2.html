Title: Need advice from experienced DMT smokers!
Post by: ominuby2 on May 19, 2013, 01:23 am
I've been vaping DMT for a while.  I'm starting to understand the different aspects of it, but I need advice from people who have more experience with it than I do.

On one hand, I seem to be able to 'tune in' to something a kind of 'place', the way I would access a chat room on the internet, or a livestream room.  There's like a grid that comes at me, expands into these pathways, and I'm able to reach a destination.  It's an extraordinary structure.  It's beautiful.  This happens when I close my eyes and concentrate on it.  I can sort of focus on this journey, and I 'get' somewhere.  It is like I'm developing this ability.

My question is-- if this stuff is my own brain, can I use this to create dreams?  Can I develop control over the mechanism and create dream-like experiences?  Can I create environments that I can then experience at night when I dream?
Title: Re: Need advice from experienced DMT smokers!
Post by: 013517 on May 19, 2013, 02:54 pm
From my experience I can say you can't control a DMT trip.  The molecule has a tendancy to take you where you need to go regardless of what you want.

I will say that I have extremely vivid dreams after using DMT.  Sometimes it borders on lucid dreaming, and there is a strange connected feeling that goes along with it.

Don't try to control DMT.  Relax into it, and trust that you will go where you need to go.  It sounds like you've broken through, but you could try carefully upping the dose a bit to see if that gets you to the place you want to be.  Do it carefully, and respectfully.  You shouldn't just mess around with DMT in my opinion.  It is just a bit too profound, and primal.  You don't want to approach it like you would an acid trip as far as I'm concerned.

Obviously what you experience will be different.  I love to think back on trips right after while the aftertrails are still running through my vision.  Have fun with it.  I wouldn't consider myself experienced, but I have certainly explored DMT to an extent.

Quote from: ominuby2 on May 19, 2013, 01:23 am
Can I develop control over the mechanism and create dream-like experiences?

Developing control into DMT is one of the most difficult things to do because like in OBE experiences your unconscious take the lead. If you try to grasp for control you will much probably fail because the unconscious bends only to love. Instead of trying for control while you are in the experience it is for this much better to create a direction BEFORE it and then create control in an indirect way. The indirect way to create control is directing your awake consciousness in a certain way so that when you are in the dream state you will react in that same way.

Magic/occultism/shamanism/sorcery are not only tied to the moment of the experience, they are paths that go on also on the "normal" state of awareness just for this.

Quote from: ominuby2 on May 19, 2013, 01:23 am
Can I create environments that I can then experience at night when I dream?

You can, by setting the "intent" before (the longer the time and the stronger the intent, the stronger the experience will be tied to that intent; depending on what you have to do there are people that go on for days thinking only on what they want to achieve before starting - btw who you are or what do you think you are already has a factor just for this and why all the talk about set and setting in psychedelic circles, albeit in that ambit they just scrape the surface of the thing).

For example you can execute a ritual before the experience so that your intent will be set and you will go where you want to. One example of this is using an Enochian call before the experience (search on google about the term and you will see what it means). Or you can use a tarot card or similar to implant the unconscious BEFORE you go. In shamanism you do this with special chants etc.
