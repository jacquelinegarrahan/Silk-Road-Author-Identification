Title: 2c-t-7 trip report
Post by: TheGrandWizard on July 24, 2012, 06:20 pm
Since I haven't seen a lot of reports on 2c-t-7 (aka "Blue Mystic"), I thought I'd write up my own experiences along with what I learned from my research.  I'd be happy to hear from others: I'd also be happy if we had a number of threads concerning various psychedelics and other drugs sold on SR. 

2c-t-7 has a reputation for being idiosyncratic in its reaction. Two people can split a 50mg bag: the guy who swallows 40mg feels mildly altered while his friend is barely able to put out a coherent sentence after 10. I've found this to be true even between doses.  I vaped 20mg and had one of the best visual trips I've ever had, albeit with a mildly uncomfortable body load. A couple weeks later 10mg made me twitchy, tense and dysphoric - like AM-2201 anxiety combined with fuzzy thought processes and an anti-aesthetic visual effect that made everything around me look remarkably dull and squalid.  While the trip improved after 2mg of clonazepam, it remained mildly unpleasant at best throughout the experience. (By contrast, I've always had at least an enjoyable time on 2c-b after the rough comeup subsides). 

I found 2c-t-7 to be much more visual than 2c-b or even 2c-e. But where 2c-b gives me a very extroverted and outgoing experience, t-7 is much more introverted and introspective. It's also far less erotic than 2c-b: 2c-t-7 doesn't really make me feel sexual at all. In some ways I think it's a more useful psychedelic for consciousness exploration and therapeutic work.  There's a greater depth to the mental and emotional headspace and a more profoundly spiritual feeling, at least for me.   

Now that I've sold the last of the t-7, I'm not entirely sure I'm going to re-up.  I may keep some around for personal use, as I think it's a very profound and useful compound in the right set and setting.  But the safety profile worries me. People have died from snorting 30mg of this stuff or from combining it with MDMA.  (It appears to have an MAOI effect in some people that can do some really nasty shit when combined with other stimulants).  My MO on Silk Road is to be a small and unobtrusive vendor, one who is so small-time that it wouldn't be worth the LEO efforts required to locate him.  If some college kid decides to hoover 100mg of my t-7 and dies therefrom, the Grand Wizard is suddenly a much more valuable target.  And I don't want to wind up being called a "scammer" because I sold to someone who was t-7 resistant and who assumes I sent baking powder. 

I'd welcome any comments on experiences from other people.  This is definitely a compound worthy of further study, and I'd like to see some serious psychonauts making the exploration.

Details were: very, very intense visuals.  At one point during experience #1 I looked out my window and realized I was standing in the middle of a Renoir painting. I also got a lot of Baroque and Rococo CEVs and some gorgeous multicolored patterning in my OEVs that looked like Persian/Arabic geometrical patterns.

During my second (negative) experience this worked in reverse.  Everything around me looked incredibly dingy, dull and drab, like it was covered with a layer of dust and a thick psychic grime.  It wasn't frightening so much as unpleasant. The best way I can describe it is the world looked like cooking cabbage and stale tobacco smoke smell.   

The bodyload for 2c-t-7 is heavier than the 2c-b load: I definitely had to deal with nausea and discomfort on both come-ups.  And where the bodyload from 2c-b often manifests itself (to me) as warm fuzzies and MDMA-like sensitivity to touch, this one is just uncomfortable.  It's not as bad as the load I get from 2c-e or 2c-i, and it fades as the experience settles in and you reach plateau. But it's definitely there. 

One of the reasons I didn't talk much about my experiences with t-7 is because it's such an introspective drug.  A lot of the time I spent on t-7 I spent up in my own head and includes a lot of stuff I wouldn't feel comfortable sharing.  Not bad things, just personal stuff that wouldn't mean much to anyone else but which was extremely useful for me.   For me t-7 is not at all a social drug: in that respect it's much more like LSD than MDMA.

The unpleasant experience I had was not by any means what I would call a "bad trip."  There was no overwhelming sense of panic, no feeling that I had lost control or that demons were taking over my mind.  The main problem was a persistent irritation and (for lack of a better phrase) cosmic boredom. I took a klonopin an hour in because I knew the experience can last for 6+ hours: I probably would have ridden it out on 2c-b because b is pretty short-acting.

As I said, I definitely like 2c-t-7 and want to keep some around for a personal stash. But I feel it's an ally one needs to approach with caution and respect. Let me know if you have any other questions and I'll gladly answer to the best of my abilities.

Based on an Erowid report, I vaped app. 3mg for my first try.  I noted some light effects at that dose so re-upped a couple times within the next 15 minutes until I was at a bit over 10mg.  As I finished that last hit (primitively packed between two screens and sprinkled on some cooking weed) I got an intense rush that left me gasping for breath.  It felt like David Byrne was standing behind me screaming "AND YOU MAY SAY TO YOURSELF 'MY GOD, WHAT HAVE I DONE?!?!?!!?!"). As I got over that I found that the world had turned into various lovely shades of pastel, complete with rare tropical birds singing in the distance.  It only lasted for a few seconds, but it's the closest thing to an opium vision I've ever had from a 2c-*.  I smoked a bit more over the next hour (about 20mg total over the night) but never recaptured that trip to Xanadu, although music sounded delightful and the headspace was intellectually stimulating and very pleasant. 

I strongly suspect that vaping it causes the same kind of MAO inhibition that snorting does, although it's almost certainly a less efficient delivery system.  So I would argue for erring on the side of caution when vaping 2c-t-7. 

The effects lasted for about 6 hours total, with about 2-3 hours of plateau and 3-4 hours of fadeout.  The comedown was actually not bad, although it was difficult to sleep.  But it appears 2c-t-7 lasts considerably longer than 2c-b no matter how you consume it.
