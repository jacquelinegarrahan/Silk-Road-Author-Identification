MDMA == MDMA == MDMA. The difference is that sometimes ecstasy contains other adulterants, but if you buy a good press from a good dealer, and test your pills, you can get pills that are MDMA only.

Probably the two most common types of pills sold as "ecstasy" that aren't pure MDMA are:
- various piperazines, which often feel more trippy but not necessarily in a good way like your standard psychedelics, and also often come with heavy nausea and unpleasant feelings that can persist for a very long time.
- MDMA + Some stimulant, often Amphetamine or Caffeine, this is because while MDMA is a stimulant in itself, it doesn't have as much drive and push as most stimulants and so some people like a bit of a kick to give you energy for those all night raves etc.

Pure MDMA is your classic loved up euphoria, while combining it with a stimulant turns it more into the dance drug it's touted as - it's often much nicer just on its own because the energy is still there, but not overpowering, and the love is unparalleled :)

As for the safety of pure MDMA. Abusing it and similar drugs leads to some neurotoxicity and serotonin downregulation - what this means in simple terms is that overuse is quite damaging, and this is also the reason for people "losing the magic", as when your serotonin axon count falls and your receptors downregulate there's a lot less serotonin flowing around in future rolls and you don't get the full effects of the love or empathy any more.

Alexander Shulgin, who popularized MDMA found that 4 times a year was his maximum with MDMA before it started causing problems. This varies from person to person, with a minimum break of around a month between rolls being good for most people and with it going up to 3-4 months for some. Avoid repeated redoses too as these cumulative effects are more damaging (Stick to one redose a roll max imo), and of course make sure you test what's in your pills :)
