Post by: captainjojo on November 12, 2011, 10:31 am
First, don't go thinking snorting 1 bag is the same as doing 1 bag IV.

The bioavailibility of H is 40-60% when you snort it, it is 100% when you do it via IV.  Then you have to take in to account the possibility of different strengths between one batch and the next.

For the first time I wouldn't do more than 50-100mg via IV (basically 1 bag).  I would suggest splitting one bag between two 1cc needles.  Do the first one and wait at least 10 minutes.  If everything seems good, do the second one.

You should be able to judge from that what your tolerance will be.  I figure if you can snort 1 bag without getting high you can probably do 1-2 bags via IV.  But never go as high as you think you can the first time with any batch.  Make it a rule of thumb to try 25-50% of your usually dose when you start a new batch, or you go from snorting to IV'ing  the first time.

When trying a new batch I'll push the plunger down halfway the first time and wait about a minute, just in case, and I always start off at least 50% of what I would normally do until I know what I have isn't 4 times as strong as the last batch I tried.

Better safe than unconscious on the floor.  Tends to put a crimp in your day.
