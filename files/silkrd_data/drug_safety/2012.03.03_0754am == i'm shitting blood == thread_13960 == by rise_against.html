Are you *shitting blood* or are you *wiping blood* ? Big difference. What another poster said about bright vs. dark red is accurate - Brighter red = closer to exit.

If it's pain in your "guts" and you are shitting blood and it's dark red, that's a sign of internal injury and can be a medical emergency.
