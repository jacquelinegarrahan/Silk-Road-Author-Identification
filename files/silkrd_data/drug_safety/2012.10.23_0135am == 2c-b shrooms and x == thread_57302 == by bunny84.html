Title: Re: 2c-b shrooms and x
Post by: Aoxomoxoa on October 23, 2012, 02:40 pm
Apparently mixing MDMA with 2c-b is called Honey Dipping. 2c-b being called "bees" you know.

There's several different ways of going about it, but the most common and generally preferred way seems to be taking 2c-b when you start coming down from the MDMA peak. Say, about 3 hours after ingesting the MDMA. The best way of administrating 2c-b seems to be either snorting or rectally, but snorting can be pretty harsh and oral administration might be preferred. I haven't had the chance to try it myself yet. I've placed an order recently and will try within a month or two. Remember, since the drugs have a synergistic effect in combining them, take a lower dose of each compared to if you would take them alone.
