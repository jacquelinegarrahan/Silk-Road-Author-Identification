Post by: solidsnake66 on September 13, 2012, 05:32 am
Only thing I can suggest is the same thing I suggest to any of my friends/family when they want to trip for the first time.

First off, I suggest having someone with you familiar with the drug so if you do start freaking out they have at least somewhat of an idea of what you're going through and can better help you through it. If I do plan on having someone with me I usually make sure I have enough for both of us to take so we can trip together if they want. The extra money out of my pocket is worth the good time with my friends. I would never suggest tripping with someone unfamiliar or uncomfortable with the substance you're taking.

Outside of the first time I suggest going with whatever you're comfortable with. I have tripped alone before and will again (probably tomorrow), but whenever I plan on taking a higher dose than I have before I get nervous and anxious and having a friend or my girlfriend with me makes me more comfortable. The more comfortable you are with what you're going into the better off you are.

Happy tripping and enjoy your first time.

No matter what type of psychedelic you're doing, if you're not experienced with them you need to be in a safe, secure and comfortable environment the first time around.  Environment is EVERYTHING (including the people in it) until you're a bit more experienced with the other side and have a better handle on what to expect on how to somewhat guide yourself through the trip without getting confused or lost.

After you've experienced the incredible things that psychedelics open your eyes to or study the things you already know it's a wonderful time alone or in groups, in private or public in some cases once you know how to handle yourself.

You don't necessarily need a 'babysitter' but I would highly recommend your first time be with an experienced person or at least someone you absolutely trust.  Things can get strange out there and you don't want to question your company when it does.

Have fun and be safe!
