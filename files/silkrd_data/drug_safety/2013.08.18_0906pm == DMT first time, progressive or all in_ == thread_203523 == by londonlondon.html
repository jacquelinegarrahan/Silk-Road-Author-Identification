It depends what you want really.

If you just want to trip and see some unbelievable visuals, take your time, and go the gradual route. You won't be disappointed.

If you want to experience some 'take the blue pill' (or is it the red pill???) type of stuff and hang out with intelligent beings from another dimension, then go for the breakthrough. I recommend going into this experience with a VERY open mind. May want to do some reading up on the subject too, so good on you for already doing that.

However, once you breakthrough, you probably won't care about sub-breakthrough doses anymore, so if you want to appreciate sub-breakthroughs, maybe go for the gradual route. Up to you ultimately. You sound like you could probably handle a breakthrough, despite having just 'mild' prior psychedelic experiences.

IMO smoking DMT alone is fine as long as you arrange beforehand so that you can easily/safely/hygeinically put down your smoking device as you slip away into hyperspace.

As far as moving around, you pretty much have full/normal awareness and control of your mind and body, it's your perceived environment that changes. The following would be very unlikely to happen, but, if for some reason, you percieve some threatening environment and think you're going to jump out a window or do something crazy, just sit tight, stay where you are, and ride it out for a few minuites. But again that's highly unlikeky.

And anyway, I find a DMT trip is best experienced lying down perfectly still with your eyes closed. Also, those neck pillows that you take on an airplane are fantastic for quickly, safely, and comfortably laying down and enjoying the ride.

Title: Re: DMT first time, progressive or all in?
Post by: Internal Freedom on August 28, 2013, 03:31 am
Whenever I am introducing DMT to a friend I always give them a small dose of 15-20mg and smoke them on that so they can get a feeling of what the body load is like along with mild visuals/fractals. If they enjoy it or want to go further, I tell them exactly what they are going to experience and how it will go down. Then if they're ready I go right to a 50-60mg dose.

Definitely do a small dose first though, there is a huge body component to DMT and you might find it weird if you go right into a breakthrough dose and are seeing multi-colored pyramids and shapes flying at you over a grassy plain while also experiencing a huge body high.

Its pleasant, but you might feel a little overwhelmed.
