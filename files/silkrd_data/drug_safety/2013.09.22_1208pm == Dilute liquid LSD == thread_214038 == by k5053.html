Title: Re: Dilute liquid LSD
Post by: junjo on September 23, 2013, 07:47 am
Dilution is simple math.
You need a measuring device so a syringe would work.
First of all, I need (and you need) to know your volume of liquid LSD
I'm going to assume that you have 5mL
So if you want a 150 ug/drop solution, you multiply 5mL by the ratio of your original solution to your desired solution.

e.g. 5mL*200/150 = 6.6667== 6 2/3 mL
The amount of water you must add to achieve such a solution will be 6.6667 mL - 5 mL = 1.6667 = 1 2/3 mL water

Therefore, measure out 1 and 2/3 of a mL of distilled water in your syringe, and expell it into your 5mL of 200 ug/drop LSD solution, and voila! You have 6.6667 mL of 150 ug/drop LSD solution.

That's all the math there is behind it.
If you can't figure out the calculations on your own, give me your exact volume so I can calculate it for you.
