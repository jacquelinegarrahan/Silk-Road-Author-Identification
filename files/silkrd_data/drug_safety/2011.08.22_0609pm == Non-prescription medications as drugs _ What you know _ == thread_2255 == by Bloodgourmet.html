Post by: Fred Flintstone on August 30, 2011, 02:03 am
Morning glory seeds have LSA in them which is chemically similar to LSD but the trip is very different. It isn't a medicine but you can get them at flower stores and such.

As far as non-prescription drugs go here are your options:
1) DXM
2) Diphenhydramine (benadryl)
3) Dimenhydrinate (dramamine)
4) Propylhexedrine(Benzedrex inhalers)

All of which are crap, nothing like LSD and can be really dangerous/unhealthy if used incorrectly. Of those listed, DXM is the only one that I enjoyed, but that was many years ago before I knew the difference between good and bad drugs.

There are plenty of RCs (research chemicals) such as 2c-i, amt and a million others that are classified as hallucinogens. If your goal is to stay away from "illegal" drugs but want to trip then RCs are probably your best bet. Just be careful.
