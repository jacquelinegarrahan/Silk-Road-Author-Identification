Title: Re: Thinking about trying MDMA.
Post by: mrmdma on July 20, 2013, 04:36 pm
1. Be aware of any medical conditions and medication your friends might be taking. MAOIs should never be mixed with any mono amine-drugs. Tramadol is a "dirty bomb" drug and taking MDMA while on it can lead into severe medical problems and even death. SSRI medication + MDMA is something that could be debated on more. Some say it's safe, some say it's not. Again, probably depends on what SSRI you are specifically taking.
This really should not be a problem though, since you said that your friends are experienced with MDMA already.

2. Weigh out the desired amount of material

3. Make a rizla bomb or what ever floats your boat (buy gelatin capsules?) and drink down with a glass of water

4. Have fun

5. If in a hot environment, drink 0.5 liters of liquids per hour if sweating - DO NOT OVERDRINK! People actually often times drink too much and end up in a serious condition as MDMA is antidiuretic and will cause a piss-lock-up. This can really strain your kidneys.

6. If unsure of anything, consult bluelight.ru. You can find everything there.



Some urban legends which might have some sort of factual background to them, but I can't say for sure.
1. MDMA should not be consumed with a highly acidic drink such as a juice from a citrus genus plant ie. orange juice, lemon juice. Same goes for soft drinks. - I have never experienced lowered effects from swallowing MDMA down with a glass of coca cola though.

2. MDMA is an amphetamine and amphetamines absorb better in alkaline environments in the gastric system (this is TRUE). Alkalizing your urine before hand may therefore produce greater effects. (There were/are some talks about this in bluelight and some people said it actually works) - Urine can be alkalized by taking a glass of water and simply putting a small spoonful of baking soda into it and drinking it. Won't kill you, but keep in mind that taking too much can really fuck up your blood's plasma levels.

MDMA can be taken in almost any given way. Orally, intranasally, shooting up your vein (shit that sounds so nasty hhhrrrr) and even plugging it up your bum hole.

I have only once in my life snorted a small line of MDMA, which I really thought was a stupid waste afterwards. I highly recommend to take MDMA orally. The bum hole administration method is nice also I've from what I've heard (really not that messy either).
