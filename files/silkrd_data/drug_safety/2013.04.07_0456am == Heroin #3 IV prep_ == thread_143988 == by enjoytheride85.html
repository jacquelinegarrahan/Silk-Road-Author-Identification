Post by: motek on April 07, 2013, 05:54 am
OK I did this in the heroin thread but for you I'll say it again

#3 is more alkaline than #4 and has trouble dissolving in water, so for a 100mg shot a small (0.01g) of citric acid (from the supermarket) OR 4-5 drops of lemon juice will allow the stuff to
dissolve with a bit of heat!

  Citric acid is very mild and the small amount used (roughly 1/10th the amount of gear) should see you fine ;)

With lemon juice, MAKE SURE you use a freashly cut slice of lemon (cut with a CLEAN KNIFE..unless you want to risk a dirty shot  :o ) and just squeeze a few drops in ...some dope might need a little nore, some a little less

After a mild heating, NO BOILING is necessary, the solution should become "clear" (grey brown pink!  YES I've seen "pink rocks") and then you filter it and you are good to go ;D


BTW  the tiny amount of fluid, acidic or otherwise (as long as it's 'clean') will not cause you ant heath problems

WARNING:  I do NOT suggest using anything but #4 for IM  aka  "intra muscular" injections .... your blood has the most "immune cells" in it and will beat any shit faster than if it goes into a muscle  or under the skin (skin popping) 

Do your very best to keep EVERYTHING clean/new and you should enjoy a lovely warm rush from good gear  8)

I hope this helps ..... pm me for info IF  you need it!
