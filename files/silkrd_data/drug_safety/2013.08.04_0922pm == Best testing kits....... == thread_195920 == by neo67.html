Title: Re: Best testing kits.......
Post by: Ben on August 06, 2013, 01:32 am
Most of these tests come in the form of single-use packages. You basically add the substance you want to check to the sachet, and wait a bit for a color change.

You should know that positives can be false on most of these though, especially when the producers of a drug want it to test positive that is easily done even if their product contains little or no active substance at all.

These tests are a pre-screening measure only, and are not reliable enough to serve as evidence in court. Proper analysis is required to determine what substances really are in a sample. and at which concentration. While these quick tests are reasonably good at detecting active substances when trafficking it is very easy to produce and inert substance that will trigger the test but without any activity whatsoever.
