I suppose it depends on what RC you are interested in.  Honestly, at this point, why would you care if they're legal?  I mean it would be nice to be able to not go to jail if you're caught; but honestly, if you're caught with a powder in a baggie, you're going to jail.  AFTER they run a GC/MS test you'll probably be let out providing you can prove that it's not intended for consumption, but even 'LEGAL' RC's are still illegal when intended for consumption. 

You can bet your ass they're gonna try and prove they're intended for consumption and you better have a pretty good amount of evidence to prove otherwise.  The burden of proof is on them, but they can state that it's for consumption and if you call BS [say that's not true] the burden of proof just switched to you and in front of a jury of your 'peers'(that wouldn't know the difference between an amine and methyl group) you won't have the chance of an ice cube in hell.

Besides, why would you want to consume even LESS researched drugs where the long term(let alone short term) side effects are unknown?
