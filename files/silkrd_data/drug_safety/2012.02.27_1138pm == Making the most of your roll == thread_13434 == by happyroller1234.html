Honestly, a tried and true method for all amphetamines is an antacid, such as tums. Not the drug you were expecting ey?

The oral bioavailability of amphetamine molecules is increased in alkaline conditions. Therefore, taking an antacid will raise the pH of the stomach causing more of the drug to be absorb into the bloodstream.

In this way you will actually potentiate the effects of the same dose of mdma requiring you to use less

Many of the other potentiators of mdma such as maoi are extremely dangerous and often lethal so can not be used. Post by: tordemon on March 09, 2012, 07:23 pm
The two things that would really do it AFAIK are:
1. pre-loading
2. MAOI

Somebody earlier said that chocolate as a natural MAOI is safe. That is 100% not the case. It just has a much smaller amount of MAOI in comparison to other substances. While pre-loading with vitamins and such can be done rather easily and safely, you have to be careful with the MAOI. You'd need to study the effects of serotonin syndrome very carefully, and start at a very very small dose of MAOI and work your way up to a level that works well for you. Finding the right amount of MAOI could potentially take a year, presuming you don't roll too often. But, serotonin syndrome has the potential both for death and permanent brain damage, so if you start getting the signs you should go to the hospital IMMEDIATELY.

Actually, with the chocolate, I wasn't aware that it had such good MAOI properties. I thought most of the effects were just the caffeine, theobromine, and phenethylamine in the product. But MAOIs are usually just bad ideas. =/
