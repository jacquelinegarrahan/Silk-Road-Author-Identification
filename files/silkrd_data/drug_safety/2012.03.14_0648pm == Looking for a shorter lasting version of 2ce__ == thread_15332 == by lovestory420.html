Title: Re: Looking for a shorter lasting version of 2ce??
Post by: Appa on March 14, 2012, 08:39 pm
2c-e tends to last around 6-8 hours for me, which is a good portion of a day, but pretty short as far as tripping standards go.  Mushrooms are similar in duration to 2c-e, LSD lasts 8-12 hours, and DOx can last significantly longer.  I can't think of anything similar to 2c-e in the 1-4 hour range.  Using the lemon tek on a dose of mushrooms seems to make it a more intense, shorter experience, but I wouldn't consider mushrooms that similar to 2c-e.

The other two options I can think of for short trips:
1. DMT or salvia--these can both be smoked for a very short trip (~5-20 minutes), but they are very different from 2c-e.  They both tend to be pretty intense, if you smoke a large enough dose, you'll "break through," where you'll have ego loss, full-on hallucinations, etc.
2. Insufflation (snorting/sniffing) 2c-e itself--I don't really recommend this.  It will burn like a motherfucker, the trip will come on fast, and accurate dosing is very important.  Some people have died by snorting too much 2c-b, and 2c-e has similar toxicity.  Please refer to erowid.org for dosing information before attempting this.

Maybe someone else will think of a chemical I'm forgetting that will suit your needs more directly.  Good luck!
