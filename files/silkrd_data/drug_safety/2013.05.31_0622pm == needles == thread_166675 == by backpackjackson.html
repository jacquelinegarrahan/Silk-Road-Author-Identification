Post by: tree on June 01, 2013, 12:09 pm
You can buy amazon gift cards or paysafecards with cash and then order then from clearnet anonymously, they'll only have your address but even if it's your own it's anonymous enough I would think, it's nothing illegal.
Needles on clearnet are extremely cheap, amazon.de has like 12 different gauges of needles and also wheelfilters, sterile empty injection bottles, etc. (a lot of other medical supplies too). 100 needles costs a couple euros there, syringes cost around the same (they're sold separately). Amazon.com only sells syringes and blunt needles last time I checked though so you'll have to order needles from germany... You'll pay more in shipping than in product but it'll still be cheap. Maybe $10 for 100 needles. You can buy insulin needles at drugstores in the US too in some areas, or you have to go to the pharmacy.

In a pharmacy, buy some vitamin B12 (injectable), so you can just be like "Oh yeah, I'll need needles too please" and avoid suspicion. I prefer amazon but it's up to you... There's also needle exchange.
