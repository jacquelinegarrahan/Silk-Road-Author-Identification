Title: Re: First time psychedelic
Post by: microRNA on November 05, 2011, 07:25 am
I agree with LexusMiles that the dose is actually more important than the substance you start with. Do not underestimate the power of psychedelics. Start with low doses and then work your way up because you never know what your personal sensitivity to a chemical will be. Clean LSD could provide an amazing but not overwhelming experience at a low dose. I appreciate LSD because of how non-toxic the dosages are.

I started with 1.75 g of shrooms, which is a fairly decent place to tread into the water... the benefit of psilocin is that it does not last as long as some of the other experiences. However, it is also slightly more difficult to dose exactly, as the potency and quality of effects vary between strains. After experiencing other psychedelics however, I no longer enjoy shrooms as much.

I would actually recommend that you start with mescaline if you are prepared for a long experience. At a low dose (approx 175 mg) the experience is mildly psychedelic (so easy to handle) and provides energy and euphoria somewhat comparable to MDMA without the neurotoxicity. Mescaline is the classical, natural psychedelic that all the phenethylamine RCs are based on, so I would recommend it before trying them. It can be more difficult to find however if you are not willing to do an extraction from the legal cacti. Extractions really are not that difficult if you research the process and it can be extremely rewarding to trip on something youve pulled if you have the capability. You would need access to a good scale to measure the dose.

Start with a classic!!! The longer the history of safe human use and the more research done is for the better! LSD and Psilocin have been used in human patients in very recent medical settings, so you can be sure that their general safety is not in question. This can help relieve some of the anxiety that can be caused by psychedelics. Some of the research chems can be deadly if misused or dosed improperly so they should not be used by inexperienced individuals!!!

Branflakes, at 18 mg I did not find 2C-I to be very intense. It was fairly enjoyable and mildly psychedelic, so 15 mg may be a decent place to start. You could take 25 mg of 2c-c but it would be a relatively disappointing experience most likely.
