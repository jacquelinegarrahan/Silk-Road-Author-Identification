If orally ingested, that is the main make up of huasca.  If you're talking about rue (oral)+dmt(oral), you've got what is called "Pharmahuasca".

I've brewed yage before and rue is my usual MAOI.  The effects will potentiate no matter what you do with them (you can smoke the rue and then smoke the DMT, or ingest the Rue and then smoke DMT after you start feeling the rue (1/2 hour to 45 minutes)  If you breakthrough for around 5 minutes with a total of fifteen for effects like most reported cases, this will cause you to trip for much longer.  Up to an hour is what I've experienced.

It's definitely worth a go, but make sure your Syrian rue is crushed up if you're eating it.  Rue smokes cleanly, not harsh at all if they're not loaded with pesticides, so if you go that route you don't need to crush it up.

Cheers and good luck!  If you need help making Yage sometime, PM me.  I haven't done it much but I've got a bit of experience worth noting ;)
