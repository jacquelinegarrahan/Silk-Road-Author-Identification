Title: Re: Oxycodone Comedown...?
Post by: captainjojo on December 15, 2012, 04:18 am
Not from just one 30mg Oxy.  You might feel a little bit down, but odds are that you will feel fine.

Now, if you start doing 30mg Oxy's every day for a week, yes, you are going to probably feel like shit about 1-2 days after you stop taking them. 

The trick is to not do them more than a day or so in a row, and wait at least 1-2 weeks before doing more.  The more you do, the more your tolerance will build and the worse you will feel when you stop taking them.

Keep in mind that Oxy's are just Heroin by another name (some forms of Oxy such as Hydromorphone are even stronger than H).
