Title: Re: DMT----The Machine----
Post by: BlackIris on June 29, 2013, 09:48 am
You don't need to remove the Brillo to charge "The Machine". Put the DMT on top of it and gently heat the neck of the bottle until the DMT melts *into* the steel wool (but you can also heat the DMT from top till it melts, it will work the same; in this last way it is faster but it's somewhat easier to burn the DMT if you are a little careless. Heating the neck is slower but it usually ensure that the DMT will correctly melt inside since more surface of the Brillo will heat - this is btw less necessary if you have a copper mesh).

I know that there are some people around that say that you have to charge "The Machine" from the bottom (as from the hole you drilled), and you can do it if you want, but there's really no difference. When you melt the DMT on the Brillo it will become an oil that will go inside, so it will make no difference whatsoever from charging it on top or bottom. Moreover "The Machine" works by toking while you apply heat, so the vapor is automatically sucked in the bottle.

The people that insist that the "correct and original" way is to put the DMT on the bottom clearly didn't read well the original article where "the machine" appeared for the first time, because there it is clearly specified that DMT must be put on TOP of the Brillo (with this last already positioned) and the neck of the bottle gentle heated until the DMT melts inside. This is the quote taken from there:

Quote from: Welcome to The Machine
To charge The Machine, enough [DMT] for one or more good doses is directly applied to the surface of the steel wool at the mouth of the bottle. The mouth of the bottle is then gently heated just enough to cause the alkaloid to melt into the steel wool plug.

As you see, the article clearly states that the DMT is to be put on top of an already present plug, so the people in some forums saying that putting the DMT from the bottom is the correct way as specified from the creators of the method are, in fact, wrong (willingly or not). It works also that way, but it is more hassle for no difference whatsoever, because the DMT will melt inside in both cases and in both cases vapor will be sucked inside by the toke.

As for your question about taking more than one hit etc.: the beauty of "The Machine" is that one toke is usually all that's necessary. That's because you need to charge it with much less DMT (15-30 mg should be enough for a normal breakthrough) and so you can easily vaporize and suck all the vapor into one single hit. The trick is to do a slow and steady toke (and if you want to be extremely sure on the first times when you are almost to go out of breath suck in as strong as you can) while you apply heat to the brillo. Anyway you will be able to do a 2nd toke without problems in case you cannot get all the vapor in one single hit.

That's difficult for me to say because where I live the brand Chore Boy doesn't exist so I cannot personally testify one way or the other. What I know is that there are some brands of rubber pads that say "pure copper" when in fact it is isn't, it is just steel covered with a thin layer of copper that when you apply heat on it it goes away (it is in fact what it gets burned out when torched along oils and other impurities; in short those rubber pads are frauds - copper costs a lot more than steel and they cheat). However also what your roommate says it is true; with an high temperature the copper oxidizes and so it turns in a greyish color (however it's a different color than oxidized steel, you should be able to recognize one from the other). This happens normally with pure copper pads when torched (it happens with all copper, as copper wire).

I had an hard time to find where I live a PURE copper steel wool of the right coarse mesh. Since I cannot know if Chore Boy is, indeed, pure copper, in your case it can be both things. One thing is sure: if it is not pure copper then it's much better simple stainless steel.

As for the hole, it is fine. If you can enlarge it a little it's better but if you have fear of breaking the bottle leave it at that, it will work without problems.

Post by: BlackIris on June 30, 2013, 09:45 am
Quote from: CryptoTree on June 30, 2013, 12:28 am
Why do people use The Machine? Can't you just use any old vape, or a crack pipe? I hear a crack pipe works fine, right?

There are many motives, the principal being that the DMT oil tends to "run away" from the heat and this becomes a problem after the first hit with a normal oil burner or crack pipe. You will simply not be able to vaporize the DMT correctly since it runs everywhere and you cannot keep up while DMT begins to make effect (and it's impossible to take all in one hit with a crack pipe).

Instead with The Machine this "issue" is used as an advantage. Since the oil will tend to "run away" this will make the oil turn into vapor - without burning - while it tries to escape from the source of heat, because of the heat transfer capability of the metal.
