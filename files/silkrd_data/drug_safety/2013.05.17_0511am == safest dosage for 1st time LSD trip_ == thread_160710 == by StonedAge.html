Title: Re: safest dosage for 1st time LSD trip?
Post by: mrmdma on May 17, 2013, 10:51 am
100 micrograms should do the trick for the first timer.
Some say that if you take less than that, you might be having trouble of "letting go". Don't try to hold back, just let the LSD take you where ever it wants to take you. Also, Set-and-setting is the key to a good experience. Get a close, good, sober friend to watch over you and possibly comfort you. Drop the LSD at day time and eat well before.
Title: Re: safest dosage for 1st time LSD trip?

Dont try to re-dose, after the initial dose. Its some what pointless.
