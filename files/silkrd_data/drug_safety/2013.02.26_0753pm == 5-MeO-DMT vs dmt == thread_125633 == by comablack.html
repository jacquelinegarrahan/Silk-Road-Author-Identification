Title: Re: 5-MeO-DMT vs dmt
Post by: fractalglobal on February 26, 2013, 10:35 pm
Most of the info you are looking for can be found on erowid.

5-meo-DMT requires a much smaller dose(10-15mg) and the trip is often described as more intense.  Visuals are much less prominent, it's more of a inter-body trip.  NN-DMT is much more psychadelic, lots of visuals etc.  Standard dosage is roughly 50mg.

I definitely suggest DMT first, but I also suggest you do a bit more research into it before trying it.  DMT isn't something to be taken lightly.  It's easily the most intense hallucinogen I've done(bar 5-meo, however the tree's that I extract from contain both so I get both sides of the trip) and it can freak people out pretty badly if they aren't ready for it.
