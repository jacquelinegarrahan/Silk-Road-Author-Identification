It wasn't coke, and even if it was and had levamisole in it, the amount of levamisole cut wouldn't be nearly enough to cause such ill effects. Levamisole generally intensifies the coke euphoria, not make you vomit and tired.

You could try the following test if you like -

put a small amount onto a piece of foil and heat it up from underneath with a flame.
if it melts into a dark, waxy kind of blob and white smoke comes off it, it's probably heroin. It might also smell a bit like candy.

A less definitive test would be just to smell the product, if it smells a bit like kerosene or solvents and if you put a tiny bit on your tongue and it causes numbing, it's probably cocaine. If it smells a bit like vinegar or more "clinical" like clean band-aids or vitamins and doesn't numb your tongue, it could be heroin.
