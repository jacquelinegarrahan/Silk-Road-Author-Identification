Title: Re: MDAI - Experiences?
Post by: android465764E on March 26, 2013, 11:01 pm
Well, 3 hours later and it's pretty much all over. Not impressed tbh.

My normal mdma dose is 300mg. I'm not a regular user, but when I do use I like to go big. Maybe 4 - 5 times a month with a single 300mg dose.

400mg MDAI has not been a very memorable experience.
Not eaten a great deal today, so I was expecting something a little more than I got.

About 40 mins after swallowing the cap I started to feel something. Not sure what exactly, but definitely a similar feeling to an MDMA come-up. Only, watered down.

About an hour in, things looked promising. Felt some slight euphoric-ish sensations. And definitely felt the same squirmy happy feeling that I get from MDMA, but again, very diluted.

Two hours in, moderately buzzing. But no body high at all. No dilated pupils, no teeth clenching, no long sighing, or sexual stimulation. (I always get super horny on MDMA). 

Now, just over three hours later, it's all over. :/
Feel tired and ready for bed.

No nausea, no headache, no bad side effects etc..  but a *really* tame high.

No wonder this stuff is so cheap, lol.
Definitely won't be ordering any more.

I hear it's better when combined with other substances, but meh. Too weak for my tastes.
