Title: Re: how do you make XTC pills....
Post by: b0lixtrader on July 15, 2013, 10:18 am
Microcrystalline cellulose as filler and binder.

Magnesium stearate for the pill to have that shine/gloss.

Powder food coloring

I don't think ratios are that big of a deal.  For example an ecstasy pill that has 200mg mdma  content aren't always going to be exactly 200mg unless obviously it was pressed into the pill with just mdma which would probably be crumble away from being too brittle because of the no binder.

So you could probably just do 200mg MDMA, 150-200mg microcrystalline cellulose, a extremely small pinch of magnesium stearate, and a small pinch of powder food coloring.
