Title: Re: Daytime MDMA rolling
Post by: LexusMiles on September 30, 2011, 01:53 am
Its going to depend how you define 'hangover'. The up-all-nite + mdma hangover is going to be worse than the daytime + mdma hangover by way of reason (think about it.. you feel worse after being up all nite, even without drugs). Either way though, a brain depleted of its serotonin supply is going to feel affected no matter how pure the MDMA, and if day or nite wont matter what. There are ways to minimize effects such as pre-roll and post-roll vitamins and 5ht etc (as others already mentioned)... But these are slow processes and even with all minimization techniques accounted for, there will still be a lull period where, if you are in tune with your senses and emotions, you will feel the negative effects.

The simple fact is, a good roll will deplete your brain of a good amount of serotonin and in most cases the person's going to feel somewhat eff'd up afterwards. Of course there are always exceptions to the rule, and there are some friends who report no negative after effects at all. These people are probably simply not as consciously aware of changes to their serotonin levels and unfortunately probably also get less enjoyment from their high (less awareness of the low in congruence with less awareness of the high).
