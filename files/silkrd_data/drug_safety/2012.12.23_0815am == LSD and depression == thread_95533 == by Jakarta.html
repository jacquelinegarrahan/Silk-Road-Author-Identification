Post by: nopuke on December 26, 2012, 12:17 am
I found ketamine made me very depressed the day after using it (in large amounts)....

Anyway shortly after I started taking LSD years ago I decided to stop taking Prozac for a number of reasons. I would just like to say that quitting it cold turkey can be an absolute catastrophe (especially if you've gone on and off of it before). It can take up to three months to start losing your mind and it really sucks ass. If you really want to quit you should wean off of it over a period of time that depends on how long you've been taking it. For example it took me two years of gradual dose reductions after taking it on and off for four years. I used a powder scale and empty capsules...

As far as LSD and depression, if you feel okay the day you take it you should be fine, and it can in fact be very beneficial. Just don't take it if you're depressed at the time. Also something interesting is that Prozac is supposed to render LSD almost completely impotent, but when I first starting using it, it was perfectly capable of doing its thing.
