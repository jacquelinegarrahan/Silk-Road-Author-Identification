Title: Re: Ritalin
Post by: Red Rover on July 03, 2013, 04:00 am
As others here have said, Ritalin is not much fun as a recreational drug.  The "high" you get isn't fun; it's just productive.  Like, you're not gonna take it and then wanna go out dancing.  You're gonna end up spending the night frantically cleaning and organizing your home. 

Like many people, I used to use Ritalin in college, whenever I had an entire paper to write in one night, or anytime else I was behind on my work and had a deadline.  It was incredible.  I could work for over 6 hours straight, no breaks, and at the end, the paper wouldn't just be done, it'd be GOOD.

But it also made me very nervous and jittery and irritable.  There's no euphoria, and no desire to socialize, like there is with coke or meth.  It didn't make me feel GOOD.  It just helped me do what I needed to do.

It's really not a party drug at all.  It's just a useful tool to have around in case you have a lot of stuff to do in a short amount of time.
