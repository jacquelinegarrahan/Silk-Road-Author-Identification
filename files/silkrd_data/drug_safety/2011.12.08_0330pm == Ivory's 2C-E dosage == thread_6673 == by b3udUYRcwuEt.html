So here is my short review of Ivory's 2C-E:

My roommate and I each took 18mg last Saturday. After an hour, I felt huge energy flows through my body, and we both were extremely euphoric and restless. My roommate had open eye visuals similar to LSD, I personally only had very very light open eye visuals, although the closed eye visuals were not bad. After two hours, we both took another 6mg, which did intensify the experience. Music (especially Psy-Trance) was very intensive to hear, but I sadly didn't get more visuals. Compared to LSD there was absolutely no mind-fucking, only very good feelings. Next time I'll try this at a party.
