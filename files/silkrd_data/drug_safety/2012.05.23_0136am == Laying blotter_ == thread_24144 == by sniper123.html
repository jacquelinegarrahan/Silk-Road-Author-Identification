Title: Re: Laying blotter?
Post by: echo_ on May 24, 2012, 11:47 pm
If your chemical is not water-soluable (like 25X-NBOMes) then you need to dissolve HPBCD with a ratio of 9:1. Dissolve in a solvent like ethanol (grain alcohol -- 191 proof everclear is 95% ethanol) or isopropyl (though this may make the blotters ultimately taste worse).

A better way, if you are laying more than a couple sheets worth is to get a baking dish or casserole dish close to the size of your sheets. Measure your sheet and then bring the info and a ruler to your local store. Then measure about how much of your solvent it takes to fully cover the sheet, then get a small bottle and shake/stir all the materials together until you get a well-mixed solution. ;) Any more tips and I got you
