Post by: gtgeorgz on December 07, 2012, 03:21 pm
A good starting dose would be 1.5 mg/kg.
In other words that's 1.5mg's of MDMA per kilogram of body weight.
For example, if he weighs in at 80kg's, and good dose for him would be 120mg's. (1.5x80=120)

If it's not as intense as he's like, you can always drop another 50/60mg's or so.

Title: Re: MDMA for my 65 year old father?
Post by: gtgeorgz on December 07, 2012, 04:56 pm
You can take the MDMA by simply measuring out the dose and putting it straight into your mouth and swallowing, but I really wouldn't recommend this as it tastes like hairspray. I usually crush the measured dose of crystals up into a fine powder and then wrap in a cigarette paper and swallow like a tablet. Or you can crush up the measured dose and pour into some juice and drink. You can snort the powder but I wouldn't recommend this ether as it its hurts and doesn't last as long.

Make sure you:-
1) Measure out the MDMA correctly (using a 3 decimal scale if poss.)
2) Take on an empty stomach, wait +3hrs after you last ate something.
3) Follow some sort of pre/post load regime, as MDMA can very taxing for your brain and serotonin receptors.
4) Keep hydrated, but not to much. Sip through 1 pint of cold water every hour for 4 hours after it kicks in.

Here is my pre/post load regime every time I take MDMA:
3DAYS PROIR
-MultiVitamin

-2DAYS PROIR
-MultiVitamin

1DAY PROIR
-MultiVitamin

ON DAY OF ROLL
Wake up
-Vitamin E (500-1000iu)
-ALA (600mg)

1 hours before drop
-Vitamin E (500-1000ui)
-ALA (1200mg)
-Vitamin C (1000mg)
-Piracetam (2400mg)
-Magnesium (500mg)
-Glass of grapefruit juice

AS ROLL FINISHES
-150mg 5HTP
-1mg Xanax (to help with sleep)
-Vitamin E (1000ui)
-ALA (1200mg)
-Vitamin C (1000mg)
-Multivitamin
-Piracetam (1200mg)
-Magnesium (500mg)
-Tall glass of fatty milk

FOR 2/3 DAYS AFTER ROLL
-150mg 5HTP
-Vitamin E (500-1000ui)
-ALA (1200mg)
-Multivitamin
-Piracetam (1200mg)

There is a possibility he may suffer with a comedown. If you keep the dosage sensible and acquire some good MDMA, he shouldn't really experience one. If its his first time, he may even experience an afterglow.
I only ever get a comedown after doses exceeding ~160mg's, which makes me a bit more emotional than normal for about 2days afterwards. But this is easily fixed with about 150mg's of 5htp supplement and a diet that consists of a lot of red meats and full fat milk.

What to expect:
T:30mins after drop of MDMA - Starting to come up, could start to feel overwhelmed, if so just tell him to think about happy things and in no time he will be rolling.
T1hr - Peaking, strong feelings of euphoria, empathy and openness.
T3hrs - starting to comedown, usually a gradual comedown which ends in sleep. But sometimes you can experience a 'crash' where you all of a sudden feel very anxious. Follow the pre/post load regime to possibly avoid this.

He may find it difficult to eat for up to 24hrs after taking it, but its best just to force as much food and fruit down as possible. Drink as much full fat milk as you can and eat as many red meats as you can. They contain high amounts of Tryptophan (a serotonin precursor).
