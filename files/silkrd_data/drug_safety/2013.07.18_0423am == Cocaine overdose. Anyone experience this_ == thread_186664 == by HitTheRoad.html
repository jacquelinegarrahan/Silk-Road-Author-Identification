You can OD with cocaine quite easily, in fact, if you inject.

It never happened to me personally but I had a friend who had a cocaine OD by IV.  Luckily he was not alone and the ambulance came very quickly or he would have had much probably a stroke or heart-failure. The idiot wanted to do the hero dosage to pass as the great guy in front of some girls and he injected almost 1 gram of quite pure cocaine in the veins (while he was used to pretty shit street cocaine and not either 1/2 of that dose).

You can die from respiratory failure, stroke, cerebral hemorrhage, or heart-failure.

With snorting is much more difficult but it can happen also in that case; but usually all OD deaths by cocaine are mostly accidental than not pure "too much use at a time" kinds.
