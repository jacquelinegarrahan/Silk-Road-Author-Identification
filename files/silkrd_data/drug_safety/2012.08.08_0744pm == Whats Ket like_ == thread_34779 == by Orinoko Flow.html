Ket is like being psychedelically drunk, without the mind-slowing. You know how you lose your peripheral vision when you're drunk? like that, the visual effects are very similar, however ketamine's tend's to be much clearer as your intact non-sedated mind allows you to appreciate the visual effects. You wont be able to walk normally at a lower dose and wont be able to walk at all at a higher dose.

50mg up the nose, crush the powder up. Dont bother cooking it - this is just to make it fine powder, you may as well just crush it. I suggest a mirror and a something round flat and metal - i use my grinder and crush it real good against the mirror, you get the finest powder ever.

It's safe as chips, lasts an hour, dont take it on a night out until you're experienced. I dont think it goes well with E unless you're chilling in a house, it's not very anxious at all - until you get toward a k-hole then it is quite scary for a rookie.

Have fun, also id personally recommend MXE over ketamine - it's cheaper, feels better, and lasts longer. Ket = 1 hour, mxe = 6 hours +. Ket is a better before bed though as MXE keeps you awake with it's dopamine effects.

Post by: slybootz on August 14, 2012, 02:05 am
Quote from: turquoisecrayon on August 14, 2012, 01:06 am
Is ketamine more pleasurable than nitrous?
That's subjective.  When I describe ketamine to people who have never used K, but HAVE used nitrous, I tell them "imagine the effects of a balloon of nitrous that are spread out over the course of an hour".  That's not really the best definition for ketamine, since I believe there is a lot more depth and exploration to be done with Ketamine than nitrous.  Both are fantastic substances; nitrous can give you that quick and intense boost while on other substances that is really enjoyable.  Ketamine can take other substances to a new level, but in a more spread out and relaxed fashion. 

In my experience, there is a much more narrow band of effects from nitrous than ketamine.  When I say narrow-banded, I don't think of it as a negative thing, necessarily.  With nitrous, I feel like there's not a HUGE increase of intensity between taking half a balloon and a full balloon(or one whippet vs. two whippets).  For me, inhaling nitrous sort of reminds me of smoking DMT:  there's a "breakthrough" point after you inhale a certain amount over a set amount of time, and after you reach the breakthrough point, you get thrown into the dissociative vortex of nitrous-y bliss.  I sometimes call this the N-hole, the nitrous version of the K-hole.  The N-hole while on LSD is probably my favorite combination in the entire world of recreational drugs. 

With ketamine, the effects can vary a whole lot more between small doses and large doses and everywhere in between(my experience has always been through insufflation, not IM/IV) .  This is why I feel like there is a lot more depth to ketamine as opposed to nitrous.  Small key bumps of K (10-20mg) can add a nice, relaxing body effect without completely dissociating you.  I love taking small bumps of ketamine while on MDMA and dancing.  Large lines of Ketamine can throw you into the K-hole, which is (for me, at least) an amazing, blissful, unbelievable fantasy-land of dissociation.  When taking large K-hole doses of K, I prefer to be alone in a comfortable place with music and lighting to my liking.  I also like slowly building up to the K-hole by taking ~25mg lines every few minutes.  There's a lot more to be said about ketamine, but I'll let you discover that on your own.
