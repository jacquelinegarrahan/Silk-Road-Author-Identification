It depends a bit on what you are using the benzo's for. Taking uppers and downers seems totally wasteful, but it doesn't always have to be.

There are plenty of people that do it, a mug of coffee and a tablet of xanax in the morning to get them going and suppress anxiety problems during the day. There really isn't that much wrong with doing that, but if you take benzo's for sedative effects in combination with a stimulant it will be a rather odd experience. This could lead to using high doses of both without getting much benefit from either.
