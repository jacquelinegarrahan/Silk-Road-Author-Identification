Title: Re: Reducing pupil dialation on MDMA?
Post by: djan8015 on July 09, 2012, 02:20 pm
Unfortunately, there really isn't much you can do to shrink your pupils while you are ON MDMA, unless you want to wear color contacts. MDMA acts on your serotonin level and stimulates the 5-HTA serotonergic receptors which induces pupil dilation or mydriasis. Since the increase of serotonin is what is repsonsible for your MDMA high, you really can't reduce the pupil dilation without blocking serotonin production, thus destroying your high.  Which is not recommendable  :) The next day, you may have some less pronounced pupil dilation as well. If you have work or a family function, IMO I would say either I didn't get a good sleep (which can induce pupil dilation) or you had to go to the optometrist yesterday and he dilated your pupils. Hope this helps a little.

djan
