Title: Re: Daily Prescription Stimulant Use Effect on Psychedelics
Post by: bionic1 on April 11, 2012, 02:08 am
I prefer a week or two of amphetamine abstinence before using psychedelics or mdxx, in order to give my body time to recover. The reason is that in the days after amphetamine I feel like "psychedelically exhausted", and I need some time to regain that very deep access to myself that psychedelics address to. There is to say that I use amphetamine for fun, and not medically. So I cant really say if it's the same as if you take it daily as a medicine.

A few thoughts on serotonin, which plays the essential role in the mechanism of psychedelics:
Although amphetamine does not significantly release serotonin, it induces stress by its effects on the autonomic nervous system, and it's proven that stress decreases the serotonin production. Again, I can't say for sure if this applies to a medical use of amphetamine.

I had two important experiences with amphetamine and psychedelics:

1. mixed acid and amphetamine. very nice trip, but after the come-down, this typical harmony with myself and the world (that I usually got after LSD) was missing. This showed me that amphetamine somehow blocks the access to a deeper area in the mind.

2. took acid the day after a three day amphetamine binge. worst experience ever (but also due to some other reasons...)!!!

Just keep in mind that these are my opinions/experiences, and it may be different for you. I don't want to scare you off... =)
