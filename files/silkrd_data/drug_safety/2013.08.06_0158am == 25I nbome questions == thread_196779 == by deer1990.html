Post by: postrex on August 06, 2013, 09:25 pm
To achieve the full effect of 25I, you need to keep the tab and ALL of you saliva in your mouth for over 30minutes.  The effects will increase up to about an hour of sublingual administration.

Beware tho, 1g of 25I taken properly can be stronger then most reports would indicate.  Many people swallow their saliva, or keep the tab in their mouth for only 20mins or so, and so see significantly diminished effects.

That said, I think 25I at 1000mg will be a fairly tolerable experience for most people.  It's a bit of a long trip tho, so better to start with mushrooms.
