Title: Re: 2-CB info and questions
Post by: Spunkaroo on October 22, 2012, 08:56 am
I'll just add that I'm pretty sure 2C-B (like most psyches) DOES interact with serotonin receptors. The difference is it interacts directly instead of stimulating the release of serotonin (as in it reacts with the receptors in place of serotonin.) This means you should be able to safely use more often from a physical perspective. Even though it's considered milder than Lucy or shrooms it is still a psychedelic, so you don't really want to abuse it and risk your mental health. I'd limit it to once every two weeks at most, which helps avoid a build up of tolerance as well.
