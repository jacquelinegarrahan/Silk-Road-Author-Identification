Title: Re: Dosing for a KittyFlip
Post by: hoobydoobydoo on May 31, 2013, 08:43 pm
As far as the dosing goes.... I would recommend to simply roll as you were planning to, and dont do any kitty until the end of the roll when you are coming down 3 to 4 hours into it.

Then I would just try a couple bumps to see how you like it.

Then when you are ready for more of an intense experience go somewhere in the 50-100mg range for a nice first experience.

Put on some nice relaxing music that you dig, and make a nice comfy place to lie down before doing do.  Blow the line, lay back, and enjoy.

K is a great complement to ecstacy - but only for the comedown in my opinion.  Pretty much will overwhelm all of the other ecstacy effects that you normally like if you do it too early on.
