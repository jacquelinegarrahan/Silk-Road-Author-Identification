Title: Re: MDMA + Alcohol
Post by: mrmdma on July 25, 2012, 05:25 pm
Don't drink and roll god damnit. First of all alcohol destroys/minimizes your peak like a motherfucker. Your roll lasts for a much shorter time if you drink. Alcohol causes hangovers and for some users hangover+serotonin depletion=hell on earth (I have witnessed it myself).

Alcohol also impacts your body's ability to absorb fluids from your small intestine, which when combined with MDMA (which causes some users to sweat like being in a Finnish sauna) is definitely not good for your body and the next day can be quite painful. For me alcohol and MDMA together have caused serious dehydration which for example caused my lips to swell and hurt as if I would have received a botox treatment. Since then I have never taken MDMA while drunk or drinking. 2-3 beers is the absolute max I can take with MDMA.
