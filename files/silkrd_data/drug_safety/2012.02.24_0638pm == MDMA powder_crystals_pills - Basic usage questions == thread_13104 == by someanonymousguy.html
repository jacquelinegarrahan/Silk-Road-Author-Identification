Post by: jochem on February 24, 2012, 10:04 pm
I used to not weigh my doses. What I did was dip my finger in the baggy, put it in my mouth and wash it away with some drink. I'd do this throughout the party, which would get the desired results. I've learned since than: I understand this method is not so good for your teeth and also I've got scales by now ;). Now I'd recommend parachuting or getting capsules to take it. Also easier at parties.

As for dosing without scales: In that case just guess based on the total amount by splitting it in a couple of equal piles (6 for ~165mg, if you have a gram). Maybe even split those piles again to make redose-capsules. If your gonna use drugs more than twice or so, I'd really recommend getting good .01g scales. I've got one pretty good and cheap from dealextreme.com.

For how satisfying MDMA crystals are: I find them a little more mellow than pills, but generally I don't really notice a big difference. It really depends on what kind of pills you've used before (what they had in them, next to MDMA) and the quality of the crystals you're going to use (they are also probably cut with other stuff).

Title: Re: MDMA powder/crystals/pills - Basic usage questions
Post by: jochem on February 24, 2012, 10:27 pm
I had my concerns as well, but they're definitely accurate. Tested it by weighing money (which has known weights, check out wikipedia) and by comparing it to the scales of a friend. Also get a weight to calibrate the thing (which will also tell you if it's accurate or not). I got this one, btw: http://www.dealextreme.com/p/precision-digital-pocket-scale-100g-max-0-01g-resolution-15002

Something else to keep in mind: you don't have to be extremely accurate with MDMA. Difference between 180mg and 200mg is not very noticeable. Some other drugs (like 2C-*) require very small dosages and fuck you up if you overdo them, so they really require that you weigh them properly. MDMA just makes you happier if you take too much (OD'ing impossible).
