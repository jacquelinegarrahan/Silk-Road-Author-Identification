A couple years back I happened upon 2c-t-2 and remember it being quite amazing and quite special.  Somehow more special than 2ci, 2ce, 2cb, and 2ct7.  I recently ordered from Morpheu5 and had quite the pleasure of receiving and trying his product. 

So I dosed my friends and myself based off the dosages I had of 2ct2 a couple years ago, and wow this stuff was way stronger. 

Our dosages were as follows:  *Names have been changed

40mg - Myself aka Mitch (Male 180lbs)

24mg - Chassidy   120 lbs

26mg - Christina - 135 lbs

35mg - Frank - 170 lbs


We took this stuff at a club…with the knowledge that if it were to be too intense we could always just cab-home.  Wow…it was way intense. 

The 2ct2 really kicked back in our roll…we had all taken some Molly about 2.5 hours before and the 2c-t-2 made it feel like it was back full force.  About a hour after we all took the 2ct2 we realized we needed to go home. 

The trip was super intense for me as I had taken 40mg, and it was definitely a little uncomfortable at the beginning.  No one ever experienced any intense nasea or body-load but Cassidy threw-up once bc she was extremely new into the tripping scene. 

2.5 hours after we had dropped it, everyone was having a good time, but for most of our crew…the first little bit was a really wild ride.  Being in a club was not a good idea.

Our bodis all felt amazing and we all definitely took advantage of the heightened sexual energy we all had.  As for the visuals…It seemed like someone was holding sparklers in our eyes.  Everything we looked at kind of was just exploding with little sparks and of course the colors were very pronounced.  After the first 2 hours, the sparkling wore off, and the traditional "breathing" rooms and swirling tiles of the 2c Family visuals were happening. 

I remember looking through my drawer of clothes thinking "Who's Clothes are these?"  The all looked so foreign to me and the colors of all my clothes had completely changed.  I kept thinking "wow, this is so much more intense than last time."  (Last time I dosed 2ct2 years ago it was around 32-35mg) 

We took at 1am and by 7am we were all still feeling pretty good.  3 of us all opted to take a little more around sunrise and we all took 6, 12, and 13mg boosters.  I didn't really feel too much more by taking the 13mg booster but i'm sure it prolonged the trip for me. 

In Summary:  I would likely take the 40mg again, but I wouldn't go any higher than that.  My vision was seriously altered at this level and it stayed very altered for at least a couple hours.  Once the trip settled in everyone was having a lot of fun and dancing and joking and laughing…it was a very amazing experience and hopefully someone can now base their dosage and their experience based off the notes I have given. 

My personal recommendation:  Watch the sunrise, Paint, Swim, play winamp's Milkdrop visualizer and shower in absolute darkness, ABSOLUTE DARKNESS (No door-cracks with light coming in or else you'll rob yourself of the experience of completely losing "where" you are.
