Title: Re: The Marijuana Fuzz/Squidge-head side effect
Post by: junjo on September 21, 2013, 09:21 am
The simple solution is avoiding marijuana entirely. Marijuana tends to induce and bring out weird neurological conditions, that can simply be avoided by abstaining from the substance entirely. I began to get similar "head pain" to a significant degree, in addition to extreme anxiety every time i smoked, and I decided that it just wasn't worth it any more.

Sorry bud, I know it sucks to give up a drug that you once loved, but there's no magical solution to  some of the neurological abnormalities marijuana causes.

Just like HPPD, science has not been able to explicitly identify any root cause of these symptoms, and there is no "cure" other than abstinence.
