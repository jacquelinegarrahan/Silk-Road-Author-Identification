Title: Re: Trying 2C-B for the first time
Post by: wowzers on February 24, 2012, 09:33 am
It's so fucking cool :)
I'm still fairly new to it, probably taken it about 10 times. But yeah start low, it's true what they say about the dose-response curve being steep.
I'd say go with 14-16mg if you take it orally, that way you get fully into it and don't get that tweaked out, not-quite-there psychedelic experience.

The first time I did 2C-B was on the tail end of MDMA and it remains my favourite combo. It's really smooth that way and there's very little body load- it seems after MD that your body is already 'primed' and can deal with additional drugs much more easily. Also the positive push of the MD is there which goes a long way to ensuring you have a good trip. Take it about 2 hours into the MD, the comeup can take quite a while but be patient and don't redose. Smoking weed seems to help bring on the come up for me.

 There seems to be a sweet spot around 22mg where you lose the perception of your skin as a boundary, similar to those isolation tanks. This makes for seriously interesting sex. The CEV and OEVs are very cool. For me, the CEVs tend to be lots of very artificial pastel colours and soft, rounded shapes. The OEVs are very digital.

Oh and food is 100x tastier- stuff like mangos and ice cream are very palatable on 2C-B.
