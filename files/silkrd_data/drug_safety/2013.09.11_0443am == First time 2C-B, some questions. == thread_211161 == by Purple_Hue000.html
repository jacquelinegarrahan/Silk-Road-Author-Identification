As I said in the previous post I have tested 2C-B.
Had 100mg in 5 capsules straight from Bulma and her Capsule Corp. ;)

First I want to say: It is very light on the nose! Unlike all of the reports I have read. After a few seconds you don't notice it anymore and the initial feeling is not painful at all to me. The same night I snorted MDMA and that was very heavy in the nose even though were perfect clean white freshly crushed rocks and then for about 20 minutes I had to drink like half a liter water because of the taste in my throat, very intense!
With MDMA I would always prefer making myself 120mg capsules, so I can drop 2, and then later 1 or 2. With 2C-B this seems to be the best method also, 20mg capsules are good. 10-12mg would not be enough for me for anything.

I made combi-capsules with 10-15mg 2C-B and 120mg MDMA. Also I made myself a few lines with it. Because of it being so soft on the nose and because you need only like 10-50mg it is really tempting to consume it nasally. The onset is much faster of course, and it seems to stimulate the CNS more this way. But as I said oral is the best for MDMA and for 2C-B. The effect of both substances is in my opinion best experienced with oral consumption.

I will not write about the experience because it is VERY personal, even more than LSD.
So much Sensuality and body feeling, so much insight and also light visual effects. Enormous. I don't know Mescalin but a common name for 2C-B is Bromo-Mescaline, early in the trip I liked the name Bee's more, I thought it was more fitting, later as the visuals came into place I thought that bromo-mescalin is a better name for it. Nexus and Venus are of course excellent names too.

So I took about 200mg MDMA and 10mg 2C-B as the initial dose. I know I know one should take the 2C-B at t+04:00 or even later when taking MDMA. I didn't do it like this. ;)
I was very wild and UBER sensual. I redosed MDMA a lot in the following hours, and started to snort the 2C-B, about 5mg per hit, sometimes more. I would say after a few hours I had about 400-500mg MDMA and 40-50mg 2C-B in my system. Also I ate 3 blotters but that was much much later t+12:00 or something like that, they didn't have a strong effect, but they made the visuals of the 2C-B a little bit more prominent. Also I smoked good amounts of Super Silver Haze throughout all of this.

The comedown was manageable, especially because I was swimming in awesome body feelings when I touched something or myself, very relaxed at the end, and I STILL had sex until I had a major orgasm that made the lights out you could say, near-death-mode and unnaturally long and intense, 5 minutes after that I felt asleep, which does not happen to me anytime, but I was pure exhausted, REALLY. I would not recommend anyone to take that much top quality MDMA without
a) being 110kg+
b) have a tolerance, at least a bit.
c) know your body well and being able to control it.
   (d) have a titan stomach (check)
   (e) have a strong heart (doppelherz - check)
