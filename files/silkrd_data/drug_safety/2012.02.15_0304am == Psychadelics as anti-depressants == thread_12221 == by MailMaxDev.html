Title: Re: Psychadelics as anti-depressants
Post by: One Violet on February 17, 2012, 11:50 am
I suffer from clinical depression and I've been told it's best to stay away from psychedelics such as LSD, shrooms and anything trippy or causing hallucinations. The reason is because anxiety occurs frequently as a comorbid disorder with depression, so taking psychedelics could induce anxiety/panic attacks in susceptible individuals.

I have not tried them for fear of getting panic attacks/bad trips, but then I've been diagnosed with anxiety and am on medication for it so you probably won't experience the side effects that I'm likely to experience if you don't have anxiety, or if your depression is not severe. But I still think you should approach them carefully just to be safe, such as starting with much lower doses than normal and being in a relaxed state before trying them.
