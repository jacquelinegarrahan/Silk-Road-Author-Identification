Title: Re: 2cb newbie needs some advice
Post by: DiamondSky on January 10, 2013, 07:42 am
Yeah this is one of those odd drugs I actually feel better the next day after taking it. Cool thing about it is that it is active at very small doses. While most people take 20-25mg for a nice body high and visuals, even just 5-10mg will get you to a nice place so it's easy to start small and get to know it. Gotta be super careful with the doses though. The difference between 25 and 30mg for some people can be a lovely fun high and something that is far too intense.

Snorting gets you high really fast.

Oral get you high really slow.

Plugging seems to be a happy middle ground if you are comfortable with that ROA.

Haven't heard of too many people IV's this one.

Anyway, start small and you'll be fine. If you do it with other people don't assume your dose is there does. Some people trip ass of 15mg and others don't. With something like this though once you get to know your dose you are pretty well set.
