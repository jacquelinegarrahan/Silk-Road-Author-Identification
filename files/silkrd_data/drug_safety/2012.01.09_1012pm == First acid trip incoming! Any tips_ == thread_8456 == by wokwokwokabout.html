There have been a few threads lately about the strength of bloomingcolors tabs, not sure if they are up to the advertised 180ug yet or not. If they are, you will have a very very strong trip if you take 2. Take 1 to start, and see how you are feeling after an hour. If its fairly mild, drop another.

Separate your home into two halves: High stimulation and low stimulation. The living room can be the high stimulation environment, it will be well lit and used for average volume music or movies.
