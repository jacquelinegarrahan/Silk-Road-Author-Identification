I also agree that it sounds more like hash than opium, and somewhat poor quality hash at that.  Opium really shouldn't be green, and hash "mostly" shouldn't.  Often, though, hash is indeed green because plenty of leaf particles get in during the process, which lowers the potency considerably.  Hash made from Mexican brick schwag tends to be quite green, perhaps almost olive-colored, forming in chunks that crumble to powder with little effort.  It's not very strong, which may explain why you had no effects when smoking it.  If it were good hash, you should have at least gotten a bit high...
