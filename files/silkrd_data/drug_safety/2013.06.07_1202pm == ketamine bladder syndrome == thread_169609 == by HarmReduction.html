Title: Re: ketamine bladder syndrome
Post by: LeadSingersDisease on June 29, 2013, 04:09 pm
It seems to be caused by the gall bladder spasms. It is one of the most painful things i have ever experienced. I'm surprised I never called an ambulance, it was only the fact that I knew it was the K.

It came after about 2 years of non stop K use between 2-5 gs a day usually. After a while you just get caught in the world of K and you start having audible hallucinations during the day and schizophrenic like breaks. Not very fun at all especially when you gotta try and work around this.

A lot of people will disagree and say that it doesn't happen or wont happen if you don't swallow the drip or if you cook the K. All of it is bullshit. Seem to be the metabolite nor-ketamine that does the damage. At one point I though my bladder was done for and I thought K was going to kill me. 2-3 years later I'm pretty much normal but I still have a huge tolerance to K and most other drugs for some reason, and my bladder (thank fuck) feel almost normal.

Heavy K users beware. It hasn't got the nickname psychedelic heroin for no reason
