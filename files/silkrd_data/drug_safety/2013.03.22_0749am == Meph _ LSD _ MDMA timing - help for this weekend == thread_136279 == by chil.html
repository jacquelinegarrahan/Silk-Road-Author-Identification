I would do the LSD and MDMA the first night and the Mephedrone the next night with expected diminishing returns.

We all know a good candy flip works out well but if you dun sapped your serotonin the night before with Mephedrone's not terribly effective use of it coupled with the high desire to redose, I think you'd end up on Mephedrone the first night and then have a fairly lack luster candy flip which would probably take a pretty good dose of both CID and MDMA to even get off the ground. Title: Re: Meph / LSD / MDMA timing - help for this weekend
Post by: DiamondSky on March 22, 2013, 09:40 am
So two hours in is pretty nice but I normally end up bumping if I take that ride. I think for most people the MDMA at two hours in sets a nice tone that the LSD just rides on. If you tend to come down hard on MDMA the landings off a candy flip are normally a little more graceful. The goal with the two hours in is that you want to be peaking on the LSD and MDMA at about the same time since they are delicious together. Some people take the MDMA earlier but often that seems to make the LSD less felt in my experience. Others take it at the four hour mark and just come down off both at the same time.

I find I get the most fun out of my night by doing the first does of MDMA at the two hour mark and then a good bump at about four hours if I am feeling it and then I just coast in the rest of the night on LSD at which point my brain has had enough and I'm ready for bed after a nice 8-10 hour ride.

The trick with candy flipping is that you are still going to be really high on LSD and so you don't feel that sharp MDMA drop off as much as you do on MDMA alone.

Just for the record, it's strongly advised to have done both of these on there own before you flip them. MDMA on the come up for a newbie can be a little harsh and that could change the tone of the night if you don't know what fun awaits you after the initial rush stops messing with you.

Otherwise, you'll love it and have a great time.
