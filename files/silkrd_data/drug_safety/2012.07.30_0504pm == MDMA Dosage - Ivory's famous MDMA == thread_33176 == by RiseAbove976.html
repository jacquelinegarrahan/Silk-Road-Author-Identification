So I had an interesting experience using Ivory's MDMA and wanted to share and get some thoughts since I'm a bit confused.

I had been hardly waiting to test some of his MDMA and got a batch. Regent, Mecke and Simon tests were perfect! This stuff is textbook.

Two of my friends and I (one girl, one guy) went to a show and decided to take it. Background: I have done MDMA 5-6 times in my life, most recent 3 months ago. The girl has done it only once also 3 months ago. The other guy, was his first time. All 3 of us are skinny and in shape (me - 165 pounds, girl-125 pounds, guy probably 140pounds). To start, I took 125mg, they each took 100mg.

One hour later nothing for any of us. I took a 40mg kicker. At the 1 1/2 hour mark the guy said he was feeling slightly different. Myself and my girl didn't feel anything so took another 40mg kicker each. At almost 2 hours I was feeling some of the effects and so was the girl. I took another 40mg (so by now 245mg total). Shortly after I was rolling ballz!! The girl stopped at 140mg total and said she had a mild roll. The guy eventually took another 40mg kicker for 140mg total and had a decent first trip.

Summary:
Me - 245mg (125mg with 3 40mg kickers) - no effects until about 1 1/2 hour later - rolled ballz for about 4 hours afterwards.
Girl- 140mg - (100mg with 1 40mg kicker) - good mild, clean roll
Guy - 140mg (100mg with 1 40mg kicker) - good roll, but not mindblowing like your first roll ever should be

Question - What happened????? Clearly we all underdosed for the initial dose and the effects took forever to come up. I don't particularly have high tolerance to MDMA (usually roll balls off one US pill which are usually very weak) and my gf was out of her mind off one pill the first time she rolled 3 months ago.

Did I get a weak batch? The stuff was fizzling and smoking when I tested it so it couldn't be. I'm very confused since I have read dozens of posts about how 120mg of Ivory's stuff is plenty.

Any thoughts? I'm a bit confused right now...

140MG should be enough for someone who has done MDMA just a couple of times with no tolerance.
