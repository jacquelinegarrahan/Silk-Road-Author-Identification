My current inventory (shared among a couple of friends) is as follows:

5g MDMA (dosed in 100mg caps),
30 hits of LSD,
50 hits of 25i-NBOMe (1mg tabs),
a little over an 8ball of cocaine,
2g M1 (bk-mdma)
A handful of mushrooms left over from years past (I'm not a huge fan of shrooms)
and more than enough weed, hash oil, and nicotine to last me.
Beer and other Alcohols which are mostly just to gift away

I'll be in the desert for about 8 nights in total,  and I don't want to over do things too early. Previous years I've just alternated between LSD one day and molly the next (increasing the dose each time), but this year I have a lot more than I've ever had for a single event in the past. I know LSD and 25i are cross tolerant, I'd rather save the LSD for the night of the man burning but I'm not sure how much i should take then (if i where to have had a dose of 25i the night or 2 before for example). Molly seems to work well enough (at least for energy and general socialization) in regular doses, last year I even took it twice on some days and still felt a good deal of bliss near the end of the even.

Any advice is greatly appreciated.
