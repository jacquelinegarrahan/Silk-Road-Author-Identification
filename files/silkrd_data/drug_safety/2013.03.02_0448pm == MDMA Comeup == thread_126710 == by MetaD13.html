Title: Re: MDMA Comeup
Post by: mrmdma on March 03, 2013, 01:34 pm
The scared or feelings of paranoia can be the cause of highly elevated cortisol levels during the kicking in of the MDMA. Your cortisol levels peak like crazy and can produce paranoid sensations. It really helps when you acknowledge what's happening to you. When ever I get the "scares" I just keep telling to myself in head that "it's soon gonna be over and I'm going to start rolling, chill the fuck out", does the trick every time.

Some scientific info about MDMA & cortisol from Pubmed:
"Cortisol release is increased by stimulatory factors, including physical activity, thermal stress and stimulant drugs. In laboratory studies MDMA leads to an acute cortisol increase of around 150% in sedentary humans. In MDMA-using dance clubbers, the cortisol levels are increased by around 800%, possibly due to the combined factors of stimulant drug, physical exertion and psychosocial stimulation. "

Source: ***CLEARNET*** http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC2826870/ ***CLEARNET***


Google "cortisol and paranoia" = "People may suffer from emotional disorders like depression, paranoia and anxiety due to elevated levels of this hormone."

Yeah the comeup is sometimes a pain. In taking far too much of it I come to enjoy it because I know that after I walk through that wall there's an hour or two of bliss waiting for me. If it helps, for me, the hardest part is actually overheating and if I counter that at just the right time with some cold fresh air I can normally pass through it relatively unscathed.

The thing I always tell myself and others is that if you do end up puking it's just fine. Everyone always feels better after the puke, sometimes they fight for a while and suffer when a quick purge would leave them rolling hard and happy.

If it helps, I find by plugging I normally avoid the nauseous stage but that may not be true for everyone. Staging the dose as mentioned earlier can help too. 80mg than 50mg a half hour later. If you're like most of us though you'll learn to love the comeup. You'll know you're going to be fine, have a bucket handy in case you puke and be ready to have a great time once it's over. If I'm eating my MDMA I normally do it on an empty stomach and drink lots of water so if I puke it's an easy puke instead of a "I shouldn't have eaten all that pizza" sort of deal.
