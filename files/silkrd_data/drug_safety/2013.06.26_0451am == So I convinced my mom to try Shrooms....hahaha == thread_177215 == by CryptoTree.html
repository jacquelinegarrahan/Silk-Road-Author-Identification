Title: So I convinced my mom to try Shrooms....hahaha

The first question my mom asked wasn't "Oooh deeary me, why have you fucked up your poor widdle brain?"

I probably wasn't the ergot, probably just all the pain killers, since they don't give you all that much ergot, but since then she'd been a little curious. Also I guess you could say I was born out of a psychonautic trip.

I laid all the risks to her, the reason why I trip, and stuff like that.  I finally asked her a couple of weeks ago if she'd be interested in trying it out, she gave me a cautious yes.

So I'm curious on people's feedback how I should handle it. I think I should start her out really low. Like made 1/32nd. Barely active dose, just to demonstrate to her that it's not the boogie woogie that people make it out to be. Also she's kind of older (like mid 60s) and has had some health problems in the past, so I wouldn't want to overload her. She doesn't even drink by the way. She used to some in the past, but she's like some kind of elder Straight Edger.

Thoughts on other things to look out for etc? When I trip, I usually paint, or listen to music. What should I have her do? Go out into nature seems like a good idea, but being in public would probably add too much anxiety to it for her. The only thing she is really worried about is getting arrested, which isn't really an issue, but she thinks it is. Any suggestions?

Anyone who is of an older age, please chime in. Some people of her generation might be able to give greater insight on this perhaps.
