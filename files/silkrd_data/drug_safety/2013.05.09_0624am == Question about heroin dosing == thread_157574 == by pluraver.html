Depends on the vendor, I have no idea what you get on the street in the USA, but generally higher, sometimes A LOT higher (street heroin here in Austria is around 5-15% pure on average, while from some vendors here you get almost PURE stuff)

Quote
2. I have heard heroin tolerance never really reaches zero again even with extended breaks.  In your experience is this true?  Having never quit and come back to it like many people I never had the luxury of a tolerance break.
It doesn't really reach zero, like if you were an heroin/opiate virgin, but it goes down by quite a lot

Quote
3. Assuming high purity, what would you suggest as a starting IV dose for someone with about a year break if I want a good rush?
Better be safe than sorry, start with max 50 mg, it will probably be too little, but you can always take more, especially since your veins should be fine after such a long break, approach your "limits" with care!

Quote
4. Also assuming high purity, how far do you think a half gram will go for two people?
It will maybe last you 2-3 days
