It really depends on your current tolerance and how long you are going to be away.  I would be really skittish about going away for 1-2 weeks without anything at all on hand.

Without knowing how much you are taking daily, it is hard to judge.  But even if all you are taking daily is some 5-10mg Oxycodone/Aceto pills you could have enough of a tolerance that you could be into heavy WD's within 4 days.

What I normally do is go to the pharmacy and find a vitamin that has a pill that looks close to what I want to take with me, then put the pills in with the regular vitamins and pack them in my luggage.  Never had a problem.

You do not want to be going through the WD's on vacation, believe me.  I did it once, never again.  I barely made it home, I was throwing up on the plane back.
