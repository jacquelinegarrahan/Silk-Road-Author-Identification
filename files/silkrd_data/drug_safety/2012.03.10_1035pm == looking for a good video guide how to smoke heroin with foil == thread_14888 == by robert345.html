robert345, I've never smoked heroin out of foil, so I can't give specific advice.  However, the general goal is to heat the foil without actually directly applying flame.  You just want the hot foil to heat up the heroin until it vaporizes, so you can suck the vapor into your lungs.  If you apply flame directly, the foil or heroin could burn, ruining the product.

Get the cheap, thin foil. Thin is the key. Don't worry about urban myths regarding smoking off foil (go ahead and research it for yourselves). It also helps to have a lighter with an adjustable flame. You will want to set it on low.
Rip off a large piece of foil, 6X6 is a good size. Don't let the foil get too crinkled. Place a small piece of Heroin near the outside of the foil so that it has room to run the length of the foil. I think the tube from a Bic pen is best for chasing the dragon. Cut it in half so its not too long, you will have better control over the smoke that way (a long tube swings around more). Aim your tube behind the heroin because thats where the vapor will be, and gently bring the lighter flame under the foil where the Heroin is. It will melt fast and run down the foil leaving a trail. Be gentle with the lighter, it doesn't take much heat to vaporize heroin. Just gentle taps of heat with the lighter.
Once you get the hang of it you'll be a pro and hardly waist a bit.
