Title: Re: The fear
Post by: MerryGoByeBye on September 01, 2011, 06:33 pm
That's pretty much where I am as well, OP.  Used to thoroughly enjoy it, but paranoia crept into my brain over the years to the point where the thought of smoking weed without a little chemical assistance creeps me the hell out.  Really sucks, because I remember so well how much I loved it in my younger years.

A couple of years ago my doctor prescribed me klonazepam for my anxiety/blood pressure, and it worked wonders.  After a few days, I thought I'd try to pick up the old bowl and see how it worked out under a benzo.  And there it was - exactly how I remembered it - nice, relaxed weed high. 

One other thing that seems to help a little is to have a few beers in me before toking up.  The anxiety is still there, but nowhere near as powerful.  I do like the old "one-two punch" with alcohol and weed, but it's only for special occasions these days when amongst friends.
