yeah 2c-b is very much like acid but more visual and not as giggley. it was for me anyway - a kaleidoscope of colours and patterns dancing in front of your eyes. acid is a bit like shrooms but far more intense, and if you are having a heavy one with shrooms you can lie down in a dark room and it calms you down - with acid that doesn't happen, if you have a bad one it's your tough luck and you have to ride it out.

good times ;)
