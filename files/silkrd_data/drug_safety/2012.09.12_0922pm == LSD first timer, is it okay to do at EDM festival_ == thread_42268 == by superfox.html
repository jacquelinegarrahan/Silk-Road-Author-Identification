Title: Re: LSD first timer, is it okay to do at EDM festival?
Post by: solidsnake66 on September 13, 2012, 05:44 am
Everyone is different so if you've never taken it before it's impossible to know how it will be for you.

A low dose would most likely be ok, but a dose of 150mic for my brother was fine (trippy but without visuals) and the same dose for his friend freaked him out (visuals and complete mind fuck). My girlfriend took half the dose I took when we both tried it our first time and she had a really intense trip compared to mine.

If you have the time and the acid I would suggest dosing ahead of time (at least a week in advance due to tolerance) to see how you do on it and then go into the trip at the festival more comfortable with what's to come.

Hope this helps and happy tripping.
