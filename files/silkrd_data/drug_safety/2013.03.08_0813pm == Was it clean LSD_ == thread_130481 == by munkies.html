Title: Re: Was it clean LSD?
Post by: Skeezer on March 08, 2013, 10:41 pm
I'm not familiar with that vendor. Regarding it possibly being a RC, it is in fact possible for LSD to taste slightly bitter. All the research chemicals I've ever tried through a blotter burned and made my gums numb. If bitterness was the only problem, I wouldn't worry too much.

As for the weak effects, two things I can think of. First they could be weak tabs (not saying the vendor you purchased from isn't legit, but you should check out the LSD Avengers thread for vendors who advertise accurate doses). Second, not every trip is going to be visual. I've had trips where I saw next to no visuals, just slightly enhanced colors and stuff. However it put me in that certain state of mind if you know what I mean so it was in no way a waste.

Hope I helped, peace!
