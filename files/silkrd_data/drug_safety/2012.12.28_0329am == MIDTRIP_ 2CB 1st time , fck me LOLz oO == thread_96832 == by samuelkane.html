I am still pretty new to 2c-b, first time was only about 2 months ago with 1.5 of the beez envious was selling, and I have only had it 4 times since from other vendors ~30mg each time (the beez were strongest) and so far I am not sold on it as a stand alone drug. It is hard to describe what I want out of it, but I guess I could call it an emotional component. For me, most psychedelics are extremely emotional, but lack in the visuals. 2c-b was quite the opposite, with 95% of the effects being visual and 5% being body high with no discernible emotional response. I really do like the short duration of the trip, and that is why I will continue to use it (I am always looking for short duration psychedelics to ward off boring ass Wednesday evenings while allowing me to still get some sleep for early Thursday meetings). I will probably continue to mix it with other short acting chems until I get bored with it (could take months to bore of the visuals). Overall, I suggest adding 4-aco or shrooms or something else to the 2c-b experience to round out the trip, but that is just my opinion.
