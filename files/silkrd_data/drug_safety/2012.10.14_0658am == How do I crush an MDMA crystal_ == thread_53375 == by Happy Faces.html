Title: Re: How do I crush an MDMA crystal?
Post by: Japan1980 on October 14, 2012, 07:12 am
depends on the size of the crystal. For smallish crystals simply put the crystal on a flat service like a table, get a credit card and while covering the front and back of the card, just simply push the card down till it crushes. Get another credit card to get every bit off the one you just used. Repeat this until the crystal is crushed enough for your liking.
