Title: Re: 2CI tips for a virgin
Post by: ianfleming on March 13, 2012, 01:30 am
Quote
Are there any safety tips you can impart? What kind of dose would you recommend for a beginner/lightweight; I'm interested in a threshold/light dose.  Is there any kind of loading protocol that you found beneficial?
2c-i is nice in that it requires very little preparation. It's my drug of choice, I don't drink, smoke or anything. 2c-i is all I want or need.

The only preparation would be to eat light or not at all. Go with fruit and shit, no dairy or greasy food.
Take it on an empty stomach (no food for at least 4 hours prior).
As far as dosage goes I'd say go with 10mg and then boost with another 5mg at the 2 hour mark.
The trip will last about 6-8 hours like that.
It has a 1 1/2 to 2 hour come up, so be mindful of that.
You wont really need supervision or anything as it's fairly clearheaded.

Personally I've found that while your tripping you can kick of the trip for a moment of near perfect clear-headedness, however trying to do this for too long seems to leave me confused once it catches back up. Basically, the trip will continue while you avoid it and once it pulls you back in you'll be all weird because you didn't flow with it. Just watch out trying to really push your brain too hard.

Stay home, dont overload yourself, no uptown/downtown adventuring or anything.

NO GREASY FOOD while tripping, it WILL make you ill.
NO WELLBUTRIN, if you take 2c-i and your on wellbutrin you WILL have a seizure.

Other than that I've never had any real issues, no nausea, no motion sickness, nothing but fun.
