Title: Re: Whats your favorite 4-substituted tryptamine?
Post by: AnimusVox on November 07, 2012, 07:15 am
4-AcO-DMT is my favorite. In comparison to 4-HO-MET (my second most used 4-tryptamine), the head space is a lot clearer and brings about a wonderful body high I have yet to experience from 4-HO-MET. 4-AcO-DMT's visuals are a lot more sharper in my experience as well with distinct patterning, whereas 4-HO-MET seems to be more bold and more prominent tracing. The one thing 4-HO-MET has over AcO is the euphoria which is indescribable, almost MDMA-like. A friend of mine has found it to be very dysphoric, however, which always seemed odd to me.

I have always found 4-tryptamines to be some of the most diverse of all psychedelics in regards to how they affect each individual. Reports are so varied that it's hard to pinpoint exactly what their effects are truly like. Perhaps this is due to the niche appeal and lack of quantitative data, but LSD, mushrooms, 2C-X, and so forth seem to be much more consistent in effects. This adds to the appeal to me, personally.
