The biggest problem people have with taking DMT and a MAOI. Is that they don't ingest enough MAOI for the body to stop metabolising the DMT. And the sensitivity between people varies greatly.

The clue is in the name Monoamine oxidase inhibitors. You have to completely inhibit the activity of  monoamine oxidase thus preventing the breakdown of monoamine neurotransmitters (DMT is transported by monoamine). Sorry to say it has nothing to do with stomach acid etc. either you didn't prepare the MAOI correctly and it wasn't strong enough or you took too little to inhibit fully.

The best way is to dose the MAOI on its own without any DMT. And find the dose that induces a psychedelic state. Take enough until you feel the effects and see light traces.

Start at 50 grams of Caapi brew and work your way up. Once you have found the dose that gives you noticeable effects, introduce the DMT the next time around. Take a look at https://www.dmt-nexus.me/forum/default.aspx?g=topics&f=51 [clearnet] for really good information.

It also depends on how good your extraction and brewing method is. I have had really good results using the herbal percolator also clearnet - http://wiki.dmt-nexus.me/The_Herbal_Percolator

With only 30 grams and acidified water is enough to inhibit monoamine oxidase for me, like I said you could be completely different.
