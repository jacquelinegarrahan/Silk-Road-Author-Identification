I picked up a 200mg sample from DigitalInk a while back which was uber high quality methylone btw, and thought that was a perfect dose for me.
Reports vary  from people saying 180-200mg is the sweet spot and anything above that does not increase euphoria/empathy feelings, only speediness and sweat.  There are also reports of people going as high as 350 and having a grand ol' time.
If I were you I would drink half since it has been 9 years since your last roll.
You definitely want to stay hydrated but I wouldn't worry too much about pre/post loading as you don't imbibe often.
The comedown from Digi's methylone was nice and easy with unassisted sleep about 5 hours after dosing.
Have a great time! :D
