Title: Re: Snorting suboxone
Post by: HOUSE on June 30, 2012, 04:08 pm
Yes, as he said.

Depends on whether you're addicted to full agonists or not.

Snorting vs normal sublingual administration does not have any impact on whether you'll get precipitated withdrawals or not. The naloxone is not effective unless injected. And even then, buprenorphine's own affinity is so high that the naloxone just can't compete. That's why people can shoot suboxone without going into precipitated WD's

So - if you're already on bupe, go ahead and snort away.

(as an aside: the generic bupe pills are harder to crush than the brand name ones. Get the brand name ones if you want to snort)
