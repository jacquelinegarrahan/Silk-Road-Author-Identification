Title: Re: whats in the shitt hash they sell on the street?
Post by: PrincessHIGH on October 14, 2012, 07:37 pm
I'm not from Scandinavia, so cannot say for sure what adulterants (pollutants) are in your hash, here's a list of the ones commonly found:

Animal turds, aspirin, barbiturates, beeswax, boot polish, glues and dyes, ground coffee, henna, ketamine, milk powder, pine resin, turpentine, and carcinogenic solvents such as toluene and benzene.

Soapbar is the worst offender of polluted hash, if it's not too polluted, it's possible to clean it.

In a saucepan, boil up a small amount of water (2cm or so). Add your polluted sample of soap. Slow down to a simmer and gently crush the hash block with a spoon. It's likely the water will turn yellow, this is due to the henna, or worse, dissolving cut. When the hash is completely powdered up, drain off the water, and add the remaining powder onto kitchen towels to dry out. As cannabis oils are insoluble in water the potency of what remains will not be too badly damaged. The downside is that you'll have a considerably smaller stash, the upside is that you've purified your stash, making for a safer and healthier smoke. This method is not recommend to clean quality hash (there's no need to clean just enjoy it!), this method is only recommend for polluted hash.

Hope this helps you :)
