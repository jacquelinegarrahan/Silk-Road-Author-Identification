Title: Re: Mixing cocaine and MDMA
Post by: nugz on March 24, 2012, 09:00 pm
This is not a combo you should be doing, here why....

When you do MDMA, it makes your brain release lots of Serotonin (5-HT), which is what makes the magic of MDMA.  MDMA also releases Dopamine.  After rolling for 5+ hours your brains runs out of 5-HT, which is why taking more MDMA at that point gives you more of a speedy effect and less empathy and euphoria  (the MDMA is cauing your brain to release mostly dopamine at that point, your out of 5-HT). 

Problem is, brain damage occurs when Dopamine is taken into 5-HT (serotonin) reuptake transporter.  This makes h2o2 (or peroxide) in your brain. Needless to say not a good thing, as this instantly kills brain cells.  Now, you may be wondering, what does this have to do with coke?  Well, coke releases mad amounts of dopamine, which is going to expodentially increase the odds that dopamine is going to get taken into a 5-HT reuptake transporter and make peroxide, causing brain damage.

Moral of the story, if you MUST do coke with MDMA, do your coke in the first few hours, and then DON'T TOUCH IT.  Way Easier said then done, and I wouldn't put myself in that situation, because I can turn into a feind when coming off MDMA.
