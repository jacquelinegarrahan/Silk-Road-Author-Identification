- It's made in a shitty backyard lab
- It's not pure
- It's still got precursor chemicals in it

Finding "pure" white MDMA made in a real lab is like finding a unicorn though. Brown still gets you were you want to go, and if you take illegal drugs you've probably already ingested lots of cuts and precursors over time anyway.
