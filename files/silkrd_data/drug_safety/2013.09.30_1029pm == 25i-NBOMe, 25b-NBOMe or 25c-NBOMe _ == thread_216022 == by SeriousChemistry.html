0.5 mg (1/2 tab) is a sound decision for your first trip. That's what I went with on my first trip and I don't regret that. The comeup caught me off guard my first time even at 0.5 mg. It was pretty fantastic. In fact I think it's time I step it back down to 500 ug and try a low dose again one of these days.

As far as storing it is concerned, NBOMEs are very hardy and nothing likeLSD in that regard. It's as hardy as MDMA imho. They should last as long as you want to keep then. Like all drugs, it's best to keep it in a dry place and away from constant/direct exposure to light. Put it somewhere dark in a dry, sealed baggie and I'd imagine it would last many years (I'd wager a lifetime or more).
