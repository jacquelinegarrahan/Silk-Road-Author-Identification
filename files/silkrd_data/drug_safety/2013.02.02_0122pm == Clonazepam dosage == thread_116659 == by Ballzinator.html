Title: Re: Clonazepam dosage
Post by: Snoopish on February 02, 2013, 03:57 pm
Benzos are kind of hard to make a call on, for me anyways. What do you define as getting a feel for a drug and what do you want to use it for? If you want to use it therapeutically, I'd start with just .5mg and let yourself go about as normal and see if you can notice the effects.

If you want to use it in a more abusive fashion, and if you are pretty confident that you have no tolerance, then I would start with 1mg dissolved in your mouth before swallowing. Give that about 45 minutes and walk around your place a bit (if you are experimenting with benzos I strongly recommend not going anywhere because they can easily ruin your ability to drive/get around) and see if you are feeling physically disoriented (this is indicative of a HIGH dosage of benzos in your system and you should not take anymore unless you are willing to face the consequences of increased tolerance and being extremely fucked up). 

If you feel you are not where you want to be at 1mg by t: 1:00 I'd pop another .5mg. You could take 2mg at once and definitely feel the effects but you probably wouldn't be able to do much besides lay around.

When I first started 1mg was a solid dose to get into a recreational level of relaxation. It's tempting but I wouldn't go further than that (and avoid alcohol).

Be careful as I'm sure you've read how dangerous benzos can be and it's all true. Also, you said you have a bunch but unless you want them all to disappear try to taper your use to at least once every three days. Xanax has a relatively short half-life but it will still hang around your system and fuck with your tolerance.
