Title: Re: LSD after having taken 25i?
Post by: 328502E on July 04, 2012, 03:01 am
LSD is the grandaddy of psychedelics, but that doesn't mean it's the best.  Each psychedelic is it's own, and some offer advantages (and disadvantages) in comparison to LSD.  Treat each as a drug in their own right, don't force a comparison to LSD, and you will get the most out of each.

There is a fairly large cross tolerance between LSD and 25i-NBOMe.  Waiting a couple days will significantly reduce that, and waiting two weeks will hugely help.  However, if you have it now, here is what you can expect:
25i targets the 5ht receptor responsible for giving visuals the most (which is why 25i has such amazing visuals).  It does not target the receptors responsible for chaotic thoughts or spirituality as largely or at all, respectively.  This means that were you to take LSD right now, you would get less visuals than a normal LSD trip for that dose, but you would still get most of the mindset and the spiritual thinking.  Taking a larger amount of LSD would increase all of the above.
