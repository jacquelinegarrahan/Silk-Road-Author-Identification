Haha, I love this song because the lime in the coconut thing is a pun.
1.) The mixed juices of lime (the fruit) and coconut are a traditional remedy for belly aches used by some tropical tribes IIRC.
2.) To increase bioavailability, coca leaves are chewed with lime, not the fruit, but calcium oxide. And that's the pun.

Pretty fucking ingenious if you ask me :D
