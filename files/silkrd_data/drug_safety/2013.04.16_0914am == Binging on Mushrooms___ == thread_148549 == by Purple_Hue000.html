Title: Re: Binging on Mushrooms???

In my experience, it is actually pretty difficult, due to the diminishing returns. If you trip two days in a row on mushies, expect to double up on yesterdays dose. Waiting a week is better.

You basically end up shoveling more and more mushrooms into yourself in order to satisfy a need that the mushrooms themselves will never succeed in doing. It is the psychedelic equivalent of cutting your nose off to spite your face.

YMMV though. Namaste.
