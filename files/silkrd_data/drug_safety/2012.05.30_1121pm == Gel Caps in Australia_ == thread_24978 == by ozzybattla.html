Plus you can spark up an interesting conversation with them about your imaginary film, which distracts them somewhat.

The other story is simply that "I'm looking for some empty capsules that I can fill with a mix of body building supplements that I ordered online, tryin' to bulk up a little bit *smile*".

The main thing I think, is just to be completely relaxed and friendly and maybe act like you're not sure if they stock anything like that (gel caps).

The only "issue" I had once was the person I spoke to didn't know anything about empty caps and called the pharmacist over, who then offered to order them in. I politely declined and said I'd go to somewhere else as I needed them for the coming weekend.

Stick to the large Chemist Supermarket/Warehouse type stores. They're usually busier, sell a shitload of supplement stuff and have a more "in/out" kind of vibe.
