Title: Re: DMT Tolerance
Post by: BlackIris on May 28, 2013, 06:25 pm
Different people react in different ways on DMT tolerance, but usually it works this way: less than 20 minutes you waste it since there is a very high tolerance, from 20 minutes till an hour many people (included myself) report instead the opposite (i.e. you can literally build your trip up by consecutive doses, as for example coming down and smoking again or doing 2-3 threshold doses then a full one where the last one - the breakthrough dose - becomes much longer and intense, I've used this many times in the last months), after about an hour tolerance resets to normal.

As I said, however, mileage may wary. DMT tolerance builds in many individual factors that are tied on how your body "attacks" the DMT.
