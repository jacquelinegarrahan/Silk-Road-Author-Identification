Title: Re: LSD, mushrooms, and breathing
Post by: psychedelia on September 02, 2012, 07:04 am
I know what you are talking about. The first time I ingested cannabis, I got exactly the same feeling that you described RE breathing, almost leading to a panic attack, and I've had the same thing happen with LSD. It stopped happening to me when I realised it was all in my head.

It's normal for your body to feel different when tripping. With the heightened awareness of psychedelics, if you fixate your attention on your breathing,  it's easy to conclude that you aren't breathing properly. Focus your attention on something else.
Title: Re: LSD, mushrooms, and breathing
Post by: psychedelia on September 02, 2012, 07:55 am
Assuming that the experience you are describing is psychosomatic, and not the result of some kind of physical condition that is brought to the surface only under the influence of LSD, which I think is unlikely, you may want to consider practising yoga and meditation while tripping.

I used to get bad trips all the time. They were usually triggered by a feeling that my body wasn't functioning properly. My attention seemed to be overly fixated on the internal sensations of the body, which you would expect to feel distorted during LSD inebbriation. I discovered that by doing some stretches, I could reliably prevent a bad trip from occurring the moment I saw it coming. I have since learned that stretching is a common and effective method of alleviating panic attacks. Ever since I started practicing yoga and meditation in conjunction with LSD and mushrooms, I've never had a bad experience on psychedelics.
