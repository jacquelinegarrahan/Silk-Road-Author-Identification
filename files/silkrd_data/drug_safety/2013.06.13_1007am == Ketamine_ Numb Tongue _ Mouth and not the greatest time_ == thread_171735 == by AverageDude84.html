I've had numerous occasions on K where I've wondered whether I was dead, at the moment of death, in a coma, missing limbs, et cetera. But no tongue biting. It can be pretty intense watching movies on K as well. Sometimes I get so immersed in the action that I feel like I'm interacting with the scene/characters and totally forget that it's simply a film.

K can be great with nitrous as well. Nitrous on it's own can be kind of meh, but a lung full of nitrous on K will totally blast you off. MDMA/molly is absolutely serene with K as well, and makes for a much more sociable, long-lasting experience. K/MDMA can make for the craziest sex ever, though you'll almost certainly need Viagra/Cialis and a partner who is equally as fucked up. It's totally and indescribably intense.

I've done all sorts of K over the years but I don't think I've ever experienced a (noteworthy) numb mouth or tongue. Certainly the drip will burn and numb whatever it touches. Generally I just end up with numb/burning nostrils and sometimes a dull aching headache or sinuses. If you do K frequently enough however, it will damage your bladder and you will have have pains in your torso.

The mind-loop (common to other psychedelics) and numb mouth/tongue doesn't really sound like K, but everything else you've described is very common. I've found the S isomer to be great for introspection, but everyone has different preferences. The S isomer also tends to clear out of your system very quickly without residual effects. I won't even do racemic K anymore because of the lingering effects, hangover, lasting uncoordination, etc.
