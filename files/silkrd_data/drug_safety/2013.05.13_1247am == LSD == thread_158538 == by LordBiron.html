don't take it in the museum of natural history.   you don't want to be out in public or around strangers while tripping. too many things going on. on your way home you'd also have to go through traffic,  etc. That can be scary when tripping.    Ideally, you want to be out in nature alone with your friends, but if that's not possible: your backyard,  your living room, bedroom, etc. Have some good music playing.

once you've tripped a dozen times, you may find that you can handle taking it in public places, but definitely not for a first trip
