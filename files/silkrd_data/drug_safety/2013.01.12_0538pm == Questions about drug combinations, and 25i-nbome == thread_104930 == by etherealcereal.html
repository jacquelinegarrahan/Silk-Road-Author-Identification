Title: Re: Questions about drug combinations, and 25i-nbome
Post by: strangeman on January 12, 2013, 09:56 pm
Seems like a bad idea to me. DXM is a serotonin reuptake inhibitor and MDMA also affects the serotonin system, sounds like a recipe for serotonin syndrome, or at least increased risk of neurotoxicity and long term damage. (Anybody with more knowledge about pharmacology feel free to correct me.) Also, 2cb, MDMA, DXM and 25i are all known to have cardiac side effects like increased blood pressure and tachycardia. You'll be putting a lot of stress on your heart, which combined with festival conditions also seems like a bad idea. 25i isn't well understood yet, either, but people have died from it and while I love the stuff, I'd be real careful doing it in combination with anything.

Re: 25i dosage, 1mg of complexed blotter was manageable for me as a first time trip, 1.5 was almost too much. I might start a little slower, especially given the setting.
