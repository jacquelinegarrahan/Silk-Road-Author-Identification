The onset of effect should be fast - there is no metabolic delay involved, though it may take some time to absorb the drug into your system when taken orally as with most substances.

Ritalin is a drug that does not have the same effect on everyone however. It's clinical use is often intended to achieve the opposite of the recreational effects, and only works in individuals sensitive to it. That is. it acts like a stimulant to most people, but more as a sedative for those suffering from ADHD. Since its a neuromodulator such contra-intuitive reactions are to be expected.

If you are not sensitive to its effects, Ritalin may do nothing at all for you, or perhaps have an effect comparable to a few cups of coffee or a low dose tablet of ephedrine. If you are certain the tablets were legit, I'd recommend not trying it again since the effects will most likely be equally disappointing.
