50mg is an ape-shit mental dose. I wouldn't even recommend doing 30mg at first. Seriously, depending on how much you have got at your disposal, get acquainted with the substance first on smaller 10-20mg doses. Even at about 20mg, if you vape it efficiently enough, that can send you right into orbit.

One of the lessons of DMT, is that your own thinking dictates your trip. Think good, and your trip will be ecstatic, think bad and be prepared for a fucking rough ride. As simple a philosophy as this may sound, being able to remain stable when psychedelic chaos is ripping up all around you is quite another matter and is something that requires practice and experience.

On the otherhand, if you do just want to dive right in to a hefty dose then doing so whilst on MDMA, is highly recommended.
