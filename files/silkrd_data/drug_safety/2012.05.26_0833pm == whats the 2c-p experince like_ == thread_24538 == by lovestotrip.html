Title: Re: whats the 2c-p experince like?
Post by: 4l| on May 28, 2012, 02:25 am
2c-p is a favorite of mine.  It's a long trip obviously, so there is less pressure to perfectly sync up your peak with whatever music/activities/etc. you have planned, there is time to do it all!  Also, I've always experienced 2c-p in waves, I'll be almost perfectly lucid for a few minutes and then the visuals come back in full force and the experience becomes very intense.

I commonly get pretty bad nausea while on it, and I've seen others mention it too, so be wary of that.  Most of my doses have been 12 mg, but 10 mg sounds like a good amount to start out on.

Try spending some time near water while your tripping.

Title: Re: whats the 2c-p experince like?
Post by: poolsclosed on May 28, 2012, 12:39 pm
Tried 12mg 2C-P recently, I have a high PEA tolerance.

Unlike other 2C-x there was little revelation. I felt some psychedelia but unfortunately I did not experience any epiphanies which is uncharacteristic of a psychedelic trip of mine. I did however experience very pleasant visuals and a great body high. However it kept going far too long and I found myself trying to sleep but experiencing disturbing auditory hallucinations when falling asleep. The entire experience took over 12 hours, and the day after I slept 16 hours out of pure exhaustion.

2C-P is interesting and I should probably explore it more, but it seems that it's mostly just a very long and tiring trip. And unlike the DOx it is quite lucid, at least for me, so it doesn't make for a crazy time. I don't know if I like it or not.
