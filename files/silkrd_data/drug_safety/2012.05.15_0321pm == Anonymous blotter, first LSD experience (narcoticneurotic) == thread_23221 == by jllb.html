Title: Anonymous blotter, first LSD experience (narcoticneurotic)
Post by: jllb on May 15, 2012, 03:21 pm
I am just landing from my first LSD trip. I've taken an Anonymous blotter at 11:00am. I can't find anymore the vendor, which was narcoticneurotic (if I remember well).

Just to say that it has been a nice experience, quite spiritual, very sensorial. I think the blotter was not very strong, since I only had very mild visuals, sort of breathing ceilings. I've been alone, at home, laughing a lot. Listening music was incredible. Also eating a banana, or feeling any skin contact was amazing. Insights like  how absurd is to take oneself seriously were very vivid.
I had also a very mild headache, a kind of feeling of stress or overexcitement. The whole trip lasted about five hours, which also makes me think the dose was not very high.

Well, this small report just in case may be of help to someone who is as scared as I was a few hours ago.
I've been all my life interested in trying LSD, but is is so absurdly difficult, being illegal,  that I had to wait until the discovery silkroad.

Thanks to narcoticneurotic and to silkroad.
