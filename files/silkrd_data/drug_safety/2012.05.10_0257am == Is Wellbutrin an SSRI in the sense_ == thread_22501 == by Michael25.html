Title: Re: Is Wellbutrin an SSRI in the sense?
Post by: raven92 on May 10, 2012, 09:49 am
Wellbutrin isn't a SNRI or SSRI, its a Norepinephrine-dopamine reuptake inhibitor. With very little effect of 5HT receptors.

Being so it's likely to have more of an effect with cocaine and amphetamines, and not much with LSD/DMT. It will likely make amphetamines more potent, so be vary careful as this may cause increased cardiac stress. If your blood pressure is already high, Wellbutrin will likely increase it, and DMT with high bloodpressure is very dangerous.

With all this said, everyone reacts very differntly to almost everything. Be careful when combining anything, start slow to gauge how you react. Not tripping as hard as you want far better then ending up in the hospital or dead.

This is not medical advice, just my two cents.
