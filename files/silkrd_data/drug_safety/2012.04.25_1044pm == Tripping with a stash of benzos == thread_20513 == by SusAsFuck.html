Title: Re: Tripping with a stash of benzos
Post by: RootZero on May 08, 2012, 01:59 pm
My preference is diazepam just cause its easily available to me, has helped me out in some difficult times. Any of the short lasting anxiolytic benzos would work well. I occasionally get anxiety or feel overwhelmed by its intensity, in many ways that's part of the rollercoaster of tripping for me. I would also never describe any of my many trips as being 'bad'. Some have been very scary and intense but I've got more out of those trips than others. Its a bit like sky diving, it can be scary but that can be fun. ;D

Taking them does have its down sides for me, trips definitely feel less intense and significant. It reduces my memory of the experience and has also caused me to make some poor decisions while tripping, similar to alcohol does. Also, it isn't an instantaneous fix for anxiety, it will take minimum 20-30 mins before it kicks in and that can feel like an age while tripping. One main risk I can see is it could be easy to OD forgetting you had taken it or thinking they weren't working so take more and end up passed out for the rest of the trip, so be careful if you do keep them around while tripping.

I agree its good to find ways of dealing with anxiety or 'bad trips' without resorting to benzos. Sometimes just changing the setting can help, move to another room, go outside, change the lighting / music, put on a movie, find something to occupy yourself with. Any anxiety usually passes quite quickly so try not to reach for the benzos unless you really feel you have to, you will get more out of it.

Happy tripping. :)
