Id say that good H is always better than Fent for a few reasons. Usually you get fent in a paperish patch or if you're lucky you can still find the gel patches. The gel ones you can smoke which is pretty good but if you got a regular patch, you have to suck on it to get faster onset than the usual stick on and wait routine.

With H on the other hand, you can IV, smoke, snort and the come up is QUICK. I love that rush. Fent is stronger but it seems to work less on the "feel good" opiate receptors and more on the "kill pain" receptors. H is more euphoria imho.

As for cross tolerance, fent is notorious for raising peoples tolarances super fast. You can look up what the conversions are for fent to H and then pretty much assume ~70% cross tolerance. The gram would make you feel better once you're in withdrawal but as for how long it ill last, that all depends on quality and Route of Administration. For example, smoking lasts quicker than snorting if I recall correctly.
