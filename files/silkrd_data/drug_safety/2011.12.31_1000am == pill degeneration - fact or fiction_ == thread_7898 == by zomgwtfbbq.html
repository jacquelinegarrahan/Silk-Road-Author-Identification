Title: pill degeneration - fact or fiction?
Post by: zomgwtfbbq on December 31, 2011, 10:00 am
Has anyone ever noticed that roxys (maybe it happens with other opiates or other pills in general, but this is where I've noticed it) start to disintegrate after a few days?  I can have a few pills in a bottle that's otherwise empty, put it in my desk drawer and leave it there for a few days - and then when I come back to it, some of the pills will look like they've eroded away - they're thinner, or chunks appear missing, or whatever.  It's like they're just disintegrating - but yet there's no discernible extra residue in the bottom of the container. 

We're talking a container that's completely undisturbed - and I live in a dry climate, so I'd like to think it's not ambient humidity.  Are roxys inherently unstable?  Anyone else seen this and figured out how to keep it from happening (other than just consuming all your pills quickly enough that you don't have to worry about it) ?

For comparison, a bottle of 1mg xanax shows no such effects, even if it's jostled around, carried with me from place to place, etc.
