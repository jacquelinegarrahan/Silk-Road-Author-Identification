I'm glad you had a good time, MDMA is one helluva drug. I want you to enjoy your MDMA experiences so I'm going to share some advice.

Please don't snort pills. They are in pill form in order for you to swallow them.
The binders used to keep pills together are not good for your nostrils - I'm not saying that MDMA is good for your nostrils, but please do not put your smelling holes through even more stress than needs be if you're not going to benefit from the effect.

Insufflating MDMA can be a different experience than oral use, it hits a lot harder and the effects last considerably less. Not to mention it REALLY hurts.
