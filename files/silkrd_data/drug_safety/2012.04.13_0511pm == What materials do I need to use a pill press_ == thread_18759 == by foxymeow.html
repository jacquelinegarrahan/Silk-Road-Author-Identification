Post by: mju7 on April 13, 2012, 05:53 pm
To work out how to dose each pill:
- Press blank pills with desired filler only.
- Weigh them.
- Calculate product to filler ratio depending on what dose you want to have in the pills.
- Mix very well
- Press and see how many pills you got out of the mix to work out real filler:product ratio

Some people just use lactose as a filler. Some people use Microcrystaline cellulose and Magnesium stearate. The later generally makes for more professional looking tablets, but is generally more expensive too. None will be hard to find.
en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Excipient

Most places which sell dyes will sell pharmaceutical/food grade dyes.
