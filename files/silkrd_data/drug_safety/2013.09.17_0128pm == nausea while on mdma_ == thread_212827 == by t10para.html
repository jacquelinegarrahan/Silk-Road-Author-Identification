Remember, serotonin is largely used to regulate the 'nausea' feeling. It may be very well that this is a sign of a slight overdose in terms of your body chemistry. You may try weed or some type of anti-mimetic (e.g. motion sickness drugs) in conjunction to mitigate such symptoms. However, if you notice a wide range of other symptoms related to serotonin syndrome, please stop.
