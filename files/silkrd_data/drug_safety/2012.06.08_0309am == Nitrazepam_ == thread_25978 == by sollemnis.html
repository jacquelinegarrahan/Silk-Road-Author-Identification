Title: Re: Nitrazepam?
Post by: Ben on June 14, 2012, 01:27 am
Nitrazepam (mogadon) is THE benzo that is used for inducing sleep under normal conditions. It was commonly used as a before-bed drug in hospital patients until not that long ago, and is effective as such.

I wouldn't suggest any type of recreational use - just keep them around for those times you can't sleep but really want to. Also recommended for intercontinental flights ;)
Nowadays oxazepam is more often used for this purpose due to its lower side effects, but nitrazepam is a proven substance with decades of experience by now.
