Title: Re: CrackerJacks from ChronicConfections - 180 pulse rate
Post by: Ben on July 15, 2013, 01:02 am
So how much cannabis did you actually ingest? It's not very clear to me what concentration was in those cookies, so its hard to find out what was going on there.

Elevated heart rate is not uncommon when hallucinating on whatever substance you are using to do so. The experience can be frightening and heart rate shooting up over 160 is nothing odd when you get a good scare - from drugs or otherwise. Usually this rapid heartbeat comes down close to normal levels quite quickly and poses no threat if you are in good health.

A numb feeling in your extremities could be something to be concerned about though, but it doesn't have to be in all cases.

Perhaps the whole ordeal simply caused you to also hyperventilate which usually produces a tingling sensation in the extremities, but in some cases also causes a numb sensation.  In itself this is no reason for immediate alarm though, once the fear subsides your breathing and heart rate will return to normal values and the feeling in your arms and legs also returns to what you are used to.

Calling emergency services may be prudent if you are unsure of what you have ingested or how much of it. Otherwise, just lying still on your bed may prove significant relieve within minutes - focus on your breathing and keep to taking a normal breath every 4 seconds or so.
