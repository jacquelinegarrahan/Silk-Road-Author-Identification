Title: Re: DMT & "The Machine" - advice needed please
Post by: BlackIris on May 13, 2013, 07:14 pm
The good of "the machine" is that it is difficult to burn the DMT for how it is done: when you melt the DMT the powder "disappear", but because of the heat transfer properties of the metal, the DMT evenly condenses on the cold, coiled metal. Then, after the DMT is evenly distributed, the same conductive properties ensure it vaporizes at near the same time, allowing the full dose to be ingested at once, in a single lungful, rather than being subject to the conductive and heat transfer properties of the glass, or much thicker metal.

To charge DMT just simply apply the spice (start low to try) on the neck (above the brillo) and then gently heat it until it melts inside (don't touch the DMT with a flame, stay above); usually just one single "flash" of the lighter is enough since DMT melts very easily (at 60-80 C).

To smoke simply reverse the bottle and heat the brillo while you take a full toke (suck as you light the DMT in the brillo, this is the point those accustomed with a crack pipe usually go wrong because they are accustomed to wait; with "the machine" the vaporization is immediate, so begin toking immediately) then hold the breath for at last 10 seconds. Done.

Very straightforward. Practice makes perfect but with "the machine", as I said, it is very difficult to waste the DMT because it is more difficult you burn it (the only way to burn it is at beginning when you try to melt it inside). Also if you cannot take a full toke at beginning (for inexperience) just take another, nothing is wasted.
