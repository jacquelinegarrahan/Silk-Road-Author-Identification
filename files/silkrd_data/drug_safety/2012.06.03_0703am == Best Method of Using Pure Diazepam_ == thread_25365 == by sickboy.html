Title: Re: Best Method of Using Pure Diazepam?
Post by: Ben on June 06, 2012, 12:56 am
I'm not too sure about blotters, but you can dissolve diazepam or alprazolam reasonably well in ethanol. You could use the resulting solution to make blotters, but also to pipet out smaller doses for future use.

For diazempam you can probably weigh 10 mg doses with a good balance, but if you want to divide up 100 mg of alprazolam into 1 mg doses, dissolving it may be a better solution. You could, for example, dissolve 100 mg alprazolam in 1 ml of ethanol, and then pipet 10 uL aliquots onto a sugar cube each.

Obviously sugar cubes aren't any good for further distribution, but for personal use they can make a very convenient medium as they absorb liquid well and can be kept in a dry place for a very long time once prepared.

The United States Pharmacopoeia lists diazepam as soluble 1 in 16 ethyl alcohol, 1 in 2 of chloroform, 1 in 39 ether, and practically insoluble in water. So your best bet for IV usage is everclear at a ratio of 15mg per ml.
