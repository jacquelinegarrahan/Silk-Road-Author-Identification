Title: Re: Is weed the most psychedelic plant to smoke?
Post by: Horizons on May 17, 2012, 12:25 am
Psychedelic = serotonin 2A receptor agonist.

Weed is not a psychedelic, even if it might put you in a similar headspace at extremely high doses.

To answer OP's question: Salvia, datura, peyote. Also, plants with high enough DMT concentrations on their leaves can be "smoked" - actually vaporized - but it's usually terribly inefficient. Extracting the alkaloid and then vaping that is a lot better and worth the extra trouble.

But since THC isn't a psychedelic at all, the correct answer is that every single plant in the world is, at least, as psychedelic as weed, and some of them are more so.

First a question for the OP.
Have you ever tried LSD, mushrooms or DMT?

Now, I felt a little trippy the second time I smoked real sinse back in the 90's, but I wouldn't call it psychedelic.
The term psychedelic has changed over the years, as "drug education" has vilified cannabis to a large extent to include it on the list of substances that change your consciousness.  I've smoked stuff that made me lie there like a little baby, when I was new to it.  I'd say now there is no cannabis left in the world that can make me feel that way, not even after taking a month long hiatus.  I don't get giggles when high either.  The only stereotypical stoner symptoms I have now are slight munchies and red eyes, otherwise I just feel relaxed.

Most genuine psychedelics you cannot smoke as the molecules are so fragile that they break down very easily, and those that don't you would have to smoke an awful lot of to have any effects.  They would be very harsh on the lungs.  Could you imagine trying to smoke 600mg of mescaline freebase or five or six big peyote buttons to break through?  Cough, cough.

https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_psychedelic_plants is a list of many "psychedelic" plants.  Yes it includes MJ/THC as a psych. I just don't feel as though it has ever really changed my thoughts, or made as lasting an impact as LSD or mushrooms have in my life.

Smoking DMT containing plants, and smoking PCP are said to fuck folks up the most.  I have yet to try though.

One interesting plant I saw on that list is a poppy that contains beta-carbolines.  I've actually smoked some self-harvested opium from this poppy, and I have to say it was the craziest opiate high I've ever had.  Me and a buddy smoked it together and spent the next three hours tiptoing through downtown in slow motion.
We were even doing those cartoony huge steps as we were tiptoing.  Lots of odd stares from folks, but hey, it was a blast.
