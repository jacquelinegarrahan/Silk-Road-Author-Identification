Title: Re: Good setting to take MDMA on my own?
Post by: Spunkaroo on September 06, 2012, 10:58 am
Firstly I want to say that 2C-I and psychedelics in general have never been really Euphoric or Empathetic for me at all. The only time I can think that was would be my first LSD trip. When I started getting visuals I was all, 'Holy fuck, this is actually working. Everyone needs to try this as it so fucking amazing. AND I BOUGHT THIS ONLINE! HOW IS THIS EVEN POSSIBLE?!?!' It was my first SR purchase as well :) 2C-B is meant to be a bit closer to rolling but I'm yet to try it.

As for actually rolling, I've only done this once and it was alone. I wasn't sure what to expect or what I was going to feel like doing so I did a quick google search for ideas, headed down to the shops and picked up some Vicks, menthol cough lollies, glow sticks and a new towel (that was an impulse buy), got home and had a light meal, waited an hour then downed some vitamins (mainly anti-oxidants) and one of the caps I had. It was all waiting for the next hour, music and random net surfing till I felt something.

Once it hit me it came on fast. I ended up just swapping to some heavy dubstep and pumping it as loud as I could and just going with it. I don't dance normally, even at clubs, and would normally feel stupid by myself, but I did that night anyway just because the simple act of moving felt amazing.

After a couple of hours of that I just chilled, very euphoric still but spent some time pumping drum and base and thinking about things. Mainly friends and family and that. I did want to 'connect' with them, but it was never overwhelming or enough to make me feel lonely. It was honestly really great and therapeutic without meaning to be.

Moral of the story is there is nothing wrong with rolling alone. I personally think it's one of the best experiences I've had in a long long time. Enjoy it mate :)
