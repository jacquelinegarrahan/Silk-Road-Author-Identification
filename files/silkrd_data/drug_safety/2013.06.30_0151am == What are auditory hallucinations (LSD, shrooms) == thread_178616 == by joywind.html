Title: Re: What are auditory hallucinations (LSD, shrooms)
Post by: ianfleming on July 14, 2013, 07:03 pm
Quote
You need to turn upside down to empty your lungs completely.
Thats a myth, sulfur hexaflouride can be expelled from the lungs very easily with just a few deep breaths while standing up straight.
Be careful if using this substance as there is some risk of asphyxiation. That feeling like you are out of air and need to breath is not caused by being out of air but by having your lungs full of CO2. If you have a different gas in your lungs you will not feel like you are in need of air or like you need to breath, therefore you can pass out and/or asphyxiate yourself. This is not something I would try if I was tripping.

Also, Nitrous makes LSD insanely powerful for about 3 minutes and when you exhale it (don't hold it very long (more than 10 seconds) for the same reasons as listed above) you get a nice deep voice, which makes it lots of fun to say demonic-esque things right before taking off.
