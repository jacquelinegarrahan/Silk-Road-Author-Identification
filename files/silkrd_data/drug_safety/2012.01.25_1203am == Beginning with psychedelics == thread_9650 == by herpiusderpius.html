Title: Beginning with psychedelics
Post by: herpiusderpius on January 25, 2012, 12:03 am
Before I tried any "real" psychedelics I'd smoked tobacco on and off, weed once every few days for a few months, and done about 200mg of molly every 2-3 weeks for a couple months.
Then I ordered DOC blotter from ENBOOM.

I did 2 tabs, 1 mg each, held buccally for 45 minutes. The trip lasted about 14 hours and I was telling myself I couldn't let myself fall asleep because I might go insane. Things seemed to come in waves, undulating between positive, warm, good visuals and thoughts all the way to jagged, pointed, discontent in all of my senses. I felt watched, whether by something good or bad I don't know. At times I was fearful of losing my sanity.
I'll admit I may have fucked it from the beginning. I was watching Alex Jones' "Inside Bohemian Grove" until I got too scared. Then watching a roommate play Arkham City on 360 for a while during which I really "came up" I guess. Started laughing maniacally at things that weren't that funny. And the game's green/magenta color palette persisted throughout all of my visuals for the remaining 12 hours or so. And now that I read about HPPD, it fits exactly what I wasn't quite sure I was experiencing for some days afterwards. The effects like enlarged eyes/undulating faces of people and distorted voices would randomly be triggered, albeit in a milder form.

Shitty first experience on psychedelics right? Now I have some 25i-nbome and 5-meo-dmt on the way and I'm curious where I should go from here. Tips for dosages, set/setting, activities to do while under the influence, etc. would be greatly appreciated.
