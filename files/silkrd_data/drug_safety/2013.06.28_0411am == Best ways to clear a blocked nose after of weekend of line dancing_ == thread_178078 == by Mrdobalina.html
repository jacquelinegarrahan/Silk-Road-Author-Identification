Post by: newbottles on June 30, 2013, 08:37 pm
IMO, based upon long periods of no drugs in nose, and also regular but not constant insufflation of H#4, cocaine, meth:

Stay away from any OTC nasal sprays with any active ingredients (anything other than just saline solution).  These are extremely habit forming and if you enjoy putting fun drugs up your nose, you don't want to add that habit.

I have never tried the pure nasal saline products, only a full neti pot routine.

Neti pot is fantastic.  Google it up on clearnet.  I would universally recommend this to everyone, whether or not they ever insufflate drugs.  There are a few crucial points regarding neti pot use:

Always use distilled bottled water.  NEVER use tap water.  Heat the water up on the stove.  NEVER EVER EVER USE HOT TAP WATER.  Normal bottled water isn't the end of the world, but distilled is far less risky.

You want the final temperature and salinity of the water to be similar to tears.  Boo hoo.

If you are too congested to make the neti pot process work, don't try.  It will only make things worse.  There is some catch 22 here for nasal drug users.  But if it isn't happening, just abort mission.  You need some flow or you aren't helping things.

Fully exhaling all the water from your nose when you are done is a very important step.  Any neti pot instruction will include these details.  Don't skip this step!  Even better, use the neti pot right before a proper aerobic/yoga/running workout.  That will lead to amazing results.  Still - don't skip the quick heavy exhale step before the workout.

As far as unplugging the nose, I don't have much to offer.  Sometimes snorting more drugs helps  :o
