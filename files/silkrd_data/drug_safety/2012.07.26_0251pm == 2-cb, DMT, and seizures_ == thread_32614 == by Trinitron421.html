Title: 2-cb, DMT, and seizures?
Post by: Trinitron421 on July 26, 2012, 02:51 pm
I will start off by saying, I am an expereinced psychonaut. I have dabbled in many psychedelics including all the classics (besides mescaline) and plenty of the new emerging RC's. Last night my brother and I decided to go deep with our new batch of 2c b. Because of our experience, we have a rather odd dosing strategy. We almost always seem to start uncomfortably high with doses, and work our way down to a comfortable level. Maybe not such a smart idea, but that is what seems to happen. Before this experience we had only had 2c-b two days prior for the first time. Started with 25mg bombs and slowly redosed about ~5mg at a time intranasally for a few hours. The end result was an amazing experience, but I didn't feel I had REALLY experienced the drug. Visuals were there, but not as intense as say NBOME. So last night we decided to try again, this time with 50mg bombs. Woah. Couple hours in, I had the ecstatsy from 25I and visuals to compliment. Visual stimulation was almost overwhelming. We decided THAT wasn't enough and we wanted to kick it up another notch. 10mg up the nose. This ramped up the visuals and body buzz x100. I was never confused, or had any sort of headfuck, like nbome it was just clean psychedelia. We then decide that smoking DMT would be a great idea as we started coming down. I loaded a bowl of enhanced herb I had made and we passed the bong back and forth. Before we started, we were both having some uncomfortable neck tension and a slight headache. After inhaling a few times, everything melted away to bliss. One more hit later, and the world LITERALLY turned into legos. My brother looked EXACTLY like a lego man, including the whole room around us. This was not just what I obeserved, but him also. I began to have some of the most intense revalations in my life. There were no more fractals, or many visual distrotions, but the world was distinctly made of legos. Some of the most impressive visuals I have seen in my carreer including MANY DMT journeys. But this was different, because it was so grounded in reality. We then finish the bowl, and from one second to the next lego-land dissolved. We sit and think (which it is becoming increasingly difficult to, talk, think, or process anything) about how to maximize that combination again. We only have a bit of enhanced herb left, and because of coughing and chest congestion decide not to redose on 2cb. Load the last bowl, and get about half way through it. No more lego, visuals ramp up a bit. Then what happens is what I like to call "poo brain" if anyone has ever mixed a MAOI and MDMA, you might know what I am talking about. I couldn't think, I couldn't talk, I would look at my brother and his face would disappear to what looked like a seamless bandana tied around his head. No eyes, nose, mouth. Just freaked me the fuck out in my state of mind, faces are an integral part of human recognition and it had just flew out the window. I start breathing heavily and making weird noises and we both decide we should stop. Suddenly I feel an EXTREME pressure in my bladder. I piss like a racehorse, and my brother ploughs in the bathroom after me. I wanted to take a shower, but he had to go pretty bad too. He seemed WAY too pumped to be taking a piss, like scary so. I walked in after he was done and told him to get out so I could shower, he looks at me with a stone cold face and in a cracked out voice tells me "I have to wash my hands, I have to wash my hands, I have to wash my hands" almost like someone with OCD twitches. He would not leave until he washed his hands in a rhythmic fashion. I was getting worried about underlying neruological conditions this may have unraveled. My bladder hurts, and so does my whole body. I push it aside, and hop in the shower when he is out. I turn it on and begin to wash. Visuals begin to ramp up, and it begins to get hard to breathe because of the steam combinded with the out of breath sensation 2c-b gives me. I cough a few times, and I begin to violently shake inthe shower. I'm slipping about to fall, I can't think, I don't know anything, I'm just uncontrolablly shaking. My brother yells, but I can hardly hear because I feel like I am going deaf. I try my hardest to hold on and get out of the shower, which I finally manage to do. I get out, and my hearing is gone completely. I'm still confused and wet, and my brother sounds angry at me for making so much noice. I didn't know what had happened, so I try my hardest to put my clothes on my soaking wet body and get out. I didn't tell him what happened, because at that time I just wasn't sure. I was scared shitless, I didn't know what had just happened. I sit down on the couch, and tell him to let me sit on it a while before we smoke some more because i wasn't feeling so hot. He stands up and begins pacing, starts to walk to the bathroom and stands still. He takes off his shirt, and begins to take off his pants and I ask "are you okay dude? why are you stripping?" no answer, he stares down at the floor with a blank face and begins to wobble. I get up and grab him while he starts shaking violently, I push his tongue back inside his mouth. I knew now we had both just had seizures. I pull him to the couch, because he refused to sit down but couldn't stand or make any sense. After about 5min we are both just sitting on the couch left in wonder. I have never read of any seizure reactions from this combo, and it doesn't make sense that it would. I'm not sure what kind of answer I am looking to get out of this that I haven't already thought of, but it makes me feel better to share this story with others.

What a fucking night. Today, I am at complete baseline. I feel a little tired, as expected, but fair nonetheless.
