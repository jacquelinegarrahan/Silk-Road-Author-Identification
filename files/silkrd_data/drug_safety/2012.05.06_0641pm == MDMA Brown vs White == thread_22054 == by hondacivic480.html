I see lots of MDMA for sale most of it brown and some white. I also been reading a lot that White is what you really want. Something about the chemist cleaning the MDMA in final process with acetone? Are you  really going to notice any difference in brown vs white MDMA?

Color is not indicative of the percentage of MDMA content in the product.  However, the pure .HCl salt of any molecule should be clear, colorless, or white.  Brown crystals may still be just as good as white crystals, but indicate that the chemist didn't take the time to properly recrystallize and purify the final product.  Purity does not necessarily equal potency, although there is almost always a correlation.
