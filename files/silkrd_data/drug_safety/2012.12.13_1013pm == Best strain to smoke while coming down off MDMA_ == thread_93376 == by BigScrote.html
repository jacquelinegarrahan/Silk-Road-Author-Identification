Title: Re: Best strain to smoke while coming down off MDMA?
Post by: harrypot on December 14, 2012, 01:45 am
Definitely recommend indica over sativa. You'll want that sedative high over a strong mental rush that is provided by a sativa. Indicas in general have really high levels of CBD, which is the pain-relieving and anxiety-fighting cannabinoid you want. Look up a cannabinoid chart to see.

Definitely look for strains with high CBD; a solid Kush should do the trick. My choice would be Blueberry--nice indica that relieves anxiety and boosts nice euphoria :D Good luck!
