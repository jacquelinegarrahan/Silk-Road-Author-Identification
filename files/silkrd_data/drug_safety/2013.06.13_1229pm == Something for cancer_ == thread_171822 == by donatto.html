Title: Re: Something for cancer?
Post by: TreadLight on July 05, 2013, 10:28 am
You will not/not find a cure for cancer on SR. You may hear all kinds of anecdotes about this or that curing cancer but there is no hard evidence to back it up. The thing about cancer, though, is sometimes people DO go into remission. And it is human nature to attribute whatever it was they were doing or taking to that remission. But all evidence points to it just being coincidence. If you went into remission while taking Ayahuasca or some other substance you would have gone into remission without it.

Think about it, if such a substance were proven to cure cancer someone would have been awarded the Nobel prize for it already.

Now, there are plenty of things available at SR that can help you with the discomfort of cancer and with the discomfort of going through treatments like chemo.
