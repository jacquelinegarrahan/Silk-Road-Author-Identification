Post by: remoulade on July 13, 2013, 09:21 am
For the DOx series I can highly recommend keeping some amphetamine and / or benzodiazepines on hand to smooth out the experience. In my heyday I had an experience with 120ug LSD, 3mg DOB, 30mg 2C-B, speed and some bk-MDMA - I honestly couldn't have gotten through that if I hadn't also been on speed. It kept me grounded, despite tripping harder than I thought possible [with the exception of DMT of course]. It was a full 24 hour experience, probably closer to 30 hours to baseline. Approach it like a meditation, allow yourself the time to trip and come down entirely. Most of all, enjoy it!
