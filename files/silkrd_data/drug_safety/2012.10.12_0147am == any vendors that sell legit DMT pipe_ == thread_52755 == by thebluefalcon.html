Currently I'm using a home made crack pipe I fashioned out of a metal tire gauge and a chore boy.  Basically I pulled the tire gauge apart, soaked the metal tube and a chore boy in rubbing alcohol, and made two copper mesh screens for the pipe, each about half an inch thick

I insert one of the screens into the wider end of the gauge and push it down far enough that the second screen will fit.  I load the DMT, heat the outside of the pipe with my lighter until I see all of the DMT melt into the screen, and then put the second screen in as a heat sink (so no direct flame contacts the spice).  When I smoke I hold the tip of the lighter to the heat sink and inhale slowly.

It works pretty well now that I've gotten used to it.  I'd still prefer something glass though so I can see when the spice starts to vaporize.
