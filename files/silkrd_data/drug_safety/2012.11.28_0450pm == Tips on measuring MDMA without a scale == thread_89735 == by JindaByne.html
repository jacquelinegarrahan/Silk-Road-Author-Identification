Title: Re: Tips on measuring MDMA without a scale
Post by: The Mustard Walrus on November 28, 2012, 05:59 pm
Well, first of all, seriously consider going for less than 140mg for her first time. That is a heavy dose for a first timer, especially a female (unless she's fairly overweight.) You really should err on the side of caution for a first-timer with MDMA as individuals' sensitivities can vary greatly, and some can be VERY sensitive. For my first time I was only using 50-60mg and rolling pretty hard on that, so be careful. If she's small, I'd go with about 80mg, if she's a bit bigger, maybe 100. Even if you have to re-dose and she doesn't really get much out of the first time, you're better safe than sorry until you figure out her sensitivity.

So about the measurements. Here's what you can do:

Crush the MDMA into a powder or very small grains. Put it on a piece of paper and flatten it out using a knife or other flat, firm object. Don't crush it down too hard, just use moderate pressure so that you have it in a nice single flat layer. Next, shape it into a circle. Have the grains tight enough together so that there's not really any space between them, but not so much that you're creating a pile with grains on top of each other. When you have a flat circle of the powder/grains that is the size of a dime (American, Canadian-- not sure about equivalent sized coins in other countries) then what you have is approximately 120mg. From there, you can add or remove fractional amounts to get various measurements. Remove one third of it and you have 80mg for example.

Another thing you can do is if you have a normal scale that weighs in grams. Weigh out 1g of it, crush it down to powder or small grains, then divide into 4 equal piles. Then you'll have approx. 250mg per pile. You can further divide each of the four piles in half and you'll have about 125mg per pile. You get the idea from there. These are the methods I used before I got a mg scale. Hope these methods work for you.

Again, if what you have is good clean MDMA, I really would not give your first-timer friend as much as you're thinking. It could give her a very uncomfortable rush, she could throw up and/or have other negative side effects. Always be careful with a first-timer! 80mg should be plenty, and if she's not feeling anything after an hour or so, try another 40-50mg.

Good luck!

Title: Re: Tips on measuring MDMA without a scale
Post by: astor on November 28, 2012, 11:17 pm
MDMA is stable in water. Take a water bottle and empty it. Add back 10 cap-fuls (as in the bottle cap, not a gel cap) of water. You could also use shot glasses, but the bottle cap works just fine. Dump 1 gram into it and make sure it completely dissolves. Now you know each capful of water that you pour out (filling the bottle cap) is 100 mg. If you want more granularity, you could dump the gram into 100 capfuls of water and dose yourself with 14 of them to get 140 mg. It's a bit of work and not good for filling gel caps, but it is a cheap and relatively accurate solution for not having a scale.
