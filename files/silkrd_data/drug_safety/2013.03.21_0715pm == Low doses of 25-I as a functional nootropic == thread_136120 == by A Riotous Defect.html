Title: Low doses of 25-I as a functional nootropic
Post by: A Riotous Defect on March 21, 2013, 07:15 pm
I've been using 25i for the past week or so every day in low doses ( 250 mics a day ) as research into its effects, to see if it shows nootropic effects in low doses as many other psychedelics do. So far I've found that using 25i has led to me being much more productive with these sub-psychedelic doses, as well as much more apt to not give up on long and tedious tasks like cleaning or playing music or drawing; I also appear to have more an understanding of mathematics which has always been a subject I have struggled in. The only detriment I find to this regiment is the negative bodyload associated with the drug, however after some experimentation I found that administering an opiate along with the 25i leads to an extremely functional state with almost none of the bodyload.

What usually works best for me is 50 - 100 mg of orally administerred morphine along with a half-tab of 25i, as my tolerance to the 25i has increased as time has gone on.

Has anyone else attempted to find a nootropic dose of 25i? What have your experiences been like if you have?

So far I haven't noticed any side-effects, but if I start feeling strange I'll probably cut this little 'experiment' out.
