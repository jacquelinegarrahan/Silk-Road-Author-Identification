Title: Re: Best way to smoke/vaporise Jwh-xx
Post by: Gurumes on January 18, 2013, 11:16 am
The alcohol method described above has various flaws.

First off, JWH compounds are much more soluble in acetone than in alcohol, so you should use acetone. This way, you have less liquid which is easier to spray on your herbs. Also, acetone evaporates much quicker, so you won't have to wait as long for it to dry.

But i would recommend not smoking this stuff at all, instead vaporize it. Smoking, in my experience, requires higher doses to have the same effect, the effects last shorter and it is also unhealthy.

You can use a lightbulb vaporizer (google it) to vaporize the powder. Alternatively, you can also vaporize it with a meth/crack pipe or eagle bill shake n' vape or similar vaporizing apparatus. Not only is this healthier and more economical (smaller doses required, no need to buy solvents and herbs) but less time consuming and more practical as it does not produce any smell.
