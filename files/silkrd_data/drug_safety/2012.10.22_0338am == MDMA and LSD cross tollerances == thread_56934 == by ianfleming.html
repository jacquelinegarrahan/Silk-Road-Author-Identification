i dont know for 100 percent sure, for every individual things are different

but no, i dont believe using mdma once a week and a half before the lsd should produce a significant reduction in the experience.

i know during festivals people take mdma one night, then the very next day they are still able to trip with normal doses

technically the flood of serotonin from mdma could down regulate the receptor lsd acts mainly on - but only using once shouldnt produce much down-regulation, and even so after a week the tolerance would be back pretty close to normal. since lsd is in a way over stimulating the 5ht2a receptor, one should still be susceptible to the neural changes that occur even if the receptor levels are slightly lower

generally psychedelic cross-tolerance is assumed to be around 3 days to one week, so waiting 10 days should be perfectly fine
