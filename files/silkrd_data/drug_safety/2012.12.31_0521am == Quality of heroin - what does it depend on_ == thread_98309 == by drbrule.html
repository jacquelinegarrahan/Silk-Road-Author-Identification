Title: Re: Quality of heroin - what does it depend on?
Post by: StickMeQuickly on December 31, 2012, 06:53 am
The difference between the 100% pure and 10% pure is you're sticking 90% shit in your body and that could be anything from baby powder to cement dust. It's always better to have the 'pure' simply because it's better for you even though theoretically it would be that same as long as the other shit didn't badly react in your body. As for the whole #4/BTH thing it's all down to personal preference. Most people I know would take BTH over #4 all day long because of the stonier more narcotic effect but I personally would take #4 over all else simply for convenience.

The numbers system is basically just steps in the manufacturing process. If I recall correctly, #1 is plain old opium, #2 is morphine base, #3 is brown heroin which needs a little acidity to be ready for injection and #4 is the hydrochloride salt which only needs water to be ready for a shot. This is all repeated knowledge apart from the fact I know for a fact #4 is the salt and #3 is the brown which needs the acid.
