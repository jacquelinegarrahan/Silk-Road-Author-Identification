Title: Re: Is MDMA a mild psychdelic?
Post by: SequelTo33rpm on April 25, 2012, 02:50 am
Neuropsychology major here. MDMA is considered a psychedelic but it first and foremost falls into its own unique class called Empathogen-Enactogen. MDMA can result in psychedelic effects along the lines of very mild visual and auditory distortions, like a flanging effect on music and peoples' voices or an enhanced texture on objects. It also can result in elaborate and distorted mental imagery, but besides that there's really nothing else. Hope this helped and stay safe.
