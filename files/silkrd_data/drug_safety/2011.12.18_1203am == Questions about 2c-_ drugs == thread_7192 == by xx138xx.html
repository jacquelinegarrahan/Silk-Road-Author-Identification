Title: Re: Questions about 2c-? drugs
Post by: senile on December 30, 2011, 04:12 am
25mg of 2c-i is now my "default" trip. I have tried many 2c-xs and 2c-i is a good combination of plenty of visual activity with completely manageable body load. As far as nausea goes, if you eat some ginger (I have ginger candies) or a little bit of benadryl before ingesting the 2c-x, your stomach will thank you. Also, try to have a few hours between a big meal and ingesting 2c-x or you may get quite gassy and uncomfortable.
