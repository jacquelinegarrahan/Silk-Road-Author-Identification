From what I've heard, stamp bags around here are consistently underweight, to the point where you're lucky to get even 60mg. You're supposed to get 100mg in a stamp bag.

Like the other guy said, start slow. However, I imagine you'll end up having to do it all fairly quickly to get the results you want.

If you wanna try some decent heroin but you're low on funds, I'd recommend Purest. He's got a listing for a .1 sample of black tar heroin.  The quality was okay for me (low-medium tolerance, IV'ed). The price isn't that great for the quality, but it's still the cheapest listing that isn't a stamp bag.

Right now, I'm waiting on a half G of powder black tar from Einstein777. The price per .1 is much better than Purest. If the quality is at least the same, I'll be happy. Einstein said the shit was pretty strong, but we'll see in a day or two.

Keep in mind that I've never tried DD's stamps, so I don't know for certain. I'm only repeating what I've heard about stamp bags in general. By no means am I talking shit about anybody.

But yeah, start small and start slow, just in case.
