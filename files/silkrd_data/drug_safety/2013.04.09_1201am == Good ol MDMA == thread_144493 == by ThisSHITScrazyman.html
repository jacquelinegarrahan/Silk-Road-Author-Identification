Post by: gtgeorgz on April 09, 2013, 12:18 am
The LD50 for MDMA (the lethal dose for roughly 50% of the population) is over 5grams at once. So you're not near a lethal dose but doses that high aren't to good for your brain. If I were you I would not take it for another few months to allow your brain to heal. Make sure to drink a lot of water and to take lots of vitamins and anti oxidants before, during and after.
