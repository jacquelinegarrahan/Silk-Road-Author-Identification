I ordered various smaller amounts of some very similar looking chemicals.  Most of them white powders, some of them RC's.

The sellers, all being very responsible and professional, shipped them well packaged and unmarked as expected.  Unfortunately this leaves me with several small bags of...of...Something.

I know as a go forward, the best plan is to differentiate the orders somehow, either in weight, or shipping address, etc, but what do I do with what I have now?  Short of running them through a mass spectrometer, is there any way to go about identifying them?

Title: Re: Feeling dumb
Post by: Appa on March 26, 2012, 08:57 pm
This seems to be a somewhat common problem, actually, so don't feel too dumb.  Assuming all of the products look alike and weigh the same, you could try contacting the vendors privately and asking them to confirm something about their packaging (the way it's packed, the type of stamps, something like that).  If they weigh differently, you should be able to figure it out once they all come in the mail.  If you're still unsure, proceed with caution and try a small amount of whatever to gauge its effects.  Good luck!
