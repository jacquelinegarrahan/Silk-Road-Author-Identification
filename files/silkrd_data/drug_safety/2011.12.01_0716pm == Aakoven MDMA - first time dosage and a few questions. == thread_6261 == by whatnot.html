Post by: Gigles on December 08, 2011, 12:59 am
For a first timer I would recommend 125mg (assuming near purity), with a possible 50mg redose 1 hour in. Make sure you convince yourself beforehand that you won't do more than whatever amount you predetermine, because first timers are often so blown away they want to keep taking more and more (personally I took 4-5 pills the first time I rolled, which is excessive and I regret).

Also if you are worried about not having enough energy (if you're going to be partying/dancing), it would be perfectly safe and appropriate to drink a 8.5oz Redbull (80mg caffeine) with the MDMA. Personally I can dance for hours without stopping on pure MDMA, but I can do the same on cannabis or when sober. Many of my friends are much more "floored" by pure MDMA and just want to cuddle or whatnot.
