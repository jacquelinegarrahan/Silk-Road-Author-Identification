Title: Re: Adderall 30 XR
Post by: dothisthing on November 07, 2011, 04:57 am
Crushing stops some of the time release of the XRs but not all. XR has more than one property that makes it extended release. One, is that some of the beads dissolve at different rates than others so the products are released at different times. Crushing the beads gets rid of that time release. However, XR is also made up of chemically different salts that will peak at different times, crushing does nothing to change that.
