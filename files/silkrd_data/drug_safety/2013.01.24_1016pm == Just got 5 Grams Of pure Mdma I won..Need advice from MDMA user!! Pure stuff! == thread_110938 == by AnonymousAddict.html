Quote

Assuming both are unadulterated MDMA, then no, neither one is better. Powder is easier to snort, though I wouldn't recommend that anyway, and is also more likely to be unadulterated.

Quote
How do i gauge how much to take?

Use a test kit to verify that it is MDMA, then measure out 80-100mg and eat it. If you don't have a test kit then start out with a really low dose that should be inactive, say 10mg or so to make sure it isn't something nasty.

If you don't have a scale, then get one.
