Title: My DMT experience so far (with intresting side effects?)
Post by: mwarf on September 21, 2012, 11:59 am
Last weekend I started messing around with DMT for the first time. After reading some posts by other members on here I decided to start out slow. I started by loading 30mg on a bed of tobacco (yuck) into my bong and gave it a shot. It wasnt a good experience at all as the smoke was so harsh I couldnt hold the smoke in for all but a second or two, but I think this was compounded by me just getting over some nasty summer flu. The end result was me coughing and spewing phlegm all over the place for atleast a minute. The only effects I felt was a very mild body high that lasted around 10 minutes or so. After that experience I decided that I would give it a couple more days and beat this flu before giving another attempt.

A few days passed and I felt the urge to give it another shot. This time I loaded the same dose, around 30mg, into my volcano vaporizer and filled up a bag. The smoke was so clean compared to my previous experience and got a good four hits in before I started to feel the effects. It started with a creeping body high that felt like it just continued to climb until I was totally couch locked and fairly incapacitated. It was not a breakthrough or ego death experience, but a good 20 minute trip with a great body high and minor visuals. I had a blast and Im looking forward to experimenting with higher doses, but since that trip I have noticed something very different with my sleep cycles, I am having more vivid dreams and actually remembering them to the detail when I wake up. I usually never remember my dreams, if Im lucky Im able to remember maybe one per month, but the last few days I have been finding myself waking up from them semi-astonished to find that Im just laying in my bed and I am able to recall each dream to the detail. I am kind of blown away by this change since I dont think I have dreamed this vividly since childhood, and I was wondering if anyone else has had similar effects after using DMT?
