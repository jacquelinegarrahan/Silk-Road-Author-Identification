Post by: gharyeee on March 11, 2012, 07:52 pm
I haven't experimented with RC's, but I'd recommend LSD, mushrooms, or starting with a low dose of DMT.

Mushrooms are great, but I find LSD better for a "fun" time. Mushrooms can be pretty raw and get very introspective at effective doses. 2.5g of mushrooms alone will be a decent trip I'm sure (you're eating mushrooms after all) but the real magic for me comes at 3g+ so I usually just eat a full 8th. Do not eat more than 4g until you are experienced however.

I'd recommend LSD (if you can find one in your price range) if you want to be more active and functional during the trip. If you are a little weary I'd say drop 2 tabs (200ug+) and you won't be overwhelmed at all. A single tab, much like <3g mushrooms, won't provide me much for visuals other than some slight weirdness to everything. Mental effects are there, though a bit weak as well on one tab.

And my personal favorite... DMT. Since you mentioned you don't want to be overwhelmed I'd recommend some of DiMiTriSpice's Changa. It is a mix of DMT with an MAOI containing plant. This basically allows for the DMT trip to come on slower and last longer, making it much easier to manage in the low-dose setting. Pack 50-100mg in a bowl, light it up, hold it in, and you'll essentially be on a 20-30 minute low-dose mushroom or LSD trip. When coming down from the short trip you can simply re-dose to extend the trip. If you want to trip a little harder just smoke a little more before coming down. If you decide tripping is fantastic and want a breakthrough you will not be disappointed. Take a good rip 3-4 times, hold in each one fully for 15-20 seconds and enter the world of love and spirits.

If you prefer to take it slow you can always work your way up the ladder with any of these substances. Low doses are nice for putting your mind at ease with a substance. Once you know in your mind that you will be OK and whatever is happening is all in your head and just a part of the trip then higher doses will only amplify the amount of fun and enlightenment.

Best of luck and happy tripping friend.
