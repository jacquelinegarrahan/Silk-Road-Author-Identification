Title: intense unexplainable lsd trip.
Post by: mrxempire on September 23, 2012, 04:04 am
hello everyone. I had a strange, unbelievable lsd trip the other day, and i was hoping someone else has had the same experience.

Anyway, I had recently ordered a 5 strip of juergen2001's lsd, and received them a few days ago. They are advertised as having

300mgs per tab (possibly only 250, but thats still alot). Anyway, i have taken decent acid before, 3 or 4 tabs at a time, so i know

what its like to trip. Anyway, the other night, i decided to take one and a half hits at around 10 00 pm. about 30 mins into the trip i

started to get pretty intense visuals. my entire bedroom was melting. I sort of expected this however. I knew that this was probably

the most lsd i had ever taken, so i was ready for something like this to happen. I was not ready, however, for what happened about

2 hrs into the trip. I was watching a movie on my laptop in my bed. I was alone (prob a bad idea). It was strange, because while i

was watching the movie, i felt aalmost as if i could see a 3d panoramic view of what was going on. This should be impossible, as

this is a 2d screen obviously. Anyway. I glanced up from the screen for just a moment. The room was completely black, except for

the soft glow of the screen. All of a sudden, as i was staring ito the blackness, the room erupted with these bright neon rainbow

shapes that filled the entire room. I looked down at my hands and they looked like they were made of pure energy. I was

completely dumbfounded, and just repeated to myself out loud, "what the fuck is going on". These visions became more and more

intense, so much so that it made me forget i had taken lsd, and that this whole event wasnt real. I though the world was ending. I

had to keep checking to make sure i was still alive. I started to get a little frightened. I saw things i cannot possibly even describe.

It felt as if i was on like a 2 hour dmt trip. I couldnt see my room, or the computer infront of me, only a see of random, impossible

shapes made out of bright beautiful colors. When i was younger, i had always heard rumors of people taking acid, and literally

hallucinating things that werent there, like cartoon characters or other rediculous objects. After the first couple times i had taken

acid, i had assumed this rumors to be false. But what i saw the other night was about 300 times more intens than simple seeing a

cartoon character in my room. Anyway, this whole ordeal lasted for a few hours. I actually needed to call my friend to come over

and trip sit me, cause i was so surprised of what was going on in my head. Idk if it affected anything, but about 20-30 minutes

before i had started to have these messed up visions, i believe i snorted about 100-120 mg of mdma, i am unsure, because i was

still hallucinating pretty heavily. After this crazy, 2 hour interdimensional journey, my friend left and i attempteds to lay down and

rest at about 3 or 4 in the morning. I knew i would not be getting any sleep for at least a couple hrs, but i could at least give it a

shot. Crazy thing was, i ended up having pretty decent visuals for almost a total of 24 hours. From 10pm thursday night, to 10 pm

friday night, i lay in my bed, tripping, mentally and physically exhausted, just trying to sleep. Eventually i managed to sleep, and

today i am fine. I almost want to consume that same amount of acid again once i get my tolerance down. Now that i know whats in

store, i can lay back in the complete darkness in my room, and perhaps listen to some music, while i further explore this new world

i have discovered. I was just curious if anyone else had a similar experience on lsd, and if they could name this phenomenon, and

explain more about it, and share their own experiences as well. anyone wishing to try this be warned: you will need a prolonged

recovery period (over 24 hrs), so dont be expecting to be going to school or work the next day
