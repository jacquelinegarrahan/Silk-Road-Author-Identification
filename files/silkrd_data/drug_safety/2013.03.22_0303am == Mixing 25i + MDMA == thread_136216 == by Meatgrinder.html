Title: Re: Mixing 25i + MDMA
Post by: 46andtwo on March 22, 2013, 04:42 am
One must stress SET and SETTING for this mix. I had a terrible experience with this over the weekend at a concert. I heard good reports on this combo (similar to mdma+lsd, aka candy flipping), but I was still very cautious as it was my first time taking 25i.So I only took 50mg of MDMA, and 1 tab of 25i. I took the 25i in two halves, not feeling the first half after 45min I foolishly took the second one.
   
I was getting impatient with the 25i, and wondering why it hadn't kicked in yet; I had three other tabs on me and luckily I did not take them. When it kicked in I felt very disoriented and scared, not having tried 25i on its own before.  I would HIGHLY recommend doing it on its own first, at home or in a comfortable setting. I get crowd anxiety even when I'm sober, and there I was in the middle of a huge crowd, pressed against thousands of people, and no quick and easy way out. After getting help from my girlfriend to exit the crowd (my motor ability was gone, and I was feeling and experiencing events 5 seconds after they occurred), I walked around in the deepest fear I've ever experienced for 2 hours. It felt like days. I was afraid of going anywhere for fear that I would get lost and disappear forever. I couldn't even use the portapotty to pee on my own! The looping was very hard to go through, and I could not find any clear head space. It felt as if I went insane and would never find reality again. Luckily, it did subside, as I went away from the music and flashing lights. Looking into my girlfriend's eyes also brought me back at times, for some reason. Deep breaths and a bottle of water helped too. I was not prepared for its effects and was expecting something totally different.

I say if you are experienced and know the effects and your target dosage it would be fine. I wouldn't do more than 1/2 a tab. And remember, never do more than 3!

Title: Re: Mixing 25i + MDMA
Post by: Yoshitoshi on March 23, 2013, 02:53 am
Don't panic! If really bad things were likely, you would have heard already...

Fully agree that each individual should try each substance in isolation before starting to combine them. Time dilation (i.e. seconds feel like hours, can occasionally be a problem with 25i. Not that it's really a problem, as the longer you spend in the space the better imho. But not so good if you're trying to cross the street, or run from the feds etc...)

I had a bit of an episode with 25i, 25c & ket (full report on "Tyler's Strawberry" thread)

But happily went back to all of them ;-)

Main thing to remember is combined effects are often multiplied rather than added, if that makes sense. Start with a sub-optimal dose of molly (e.g. 80mg) just to ease you in a bit, far from a full on roll, wait an hour, and then take the 25i. Haven't tried the reverse order (sherbet-flipping?!)

There are lots of reports about MXE being the ideal runway to 25i and that is definitely a lovely combo.

Equally, 25i is so nice, you can skip any pre-dosing altogether and still have a great time. But I understand, if you have the toys, you want to play with them...

Our favorite is: soaking 2x 1200 µg tabs of 25i in a capful of vodka for 2-3 hours, then dissolving 100mg MXE in the same solution. Dry out, on a heated plate, then snort 50mg of product. Fucking beautiful smooth vivid clean trip. Have used this with complete virgins (to both MXE & 25i) and they LOVE it. The MXE works some magic and calms your body before the ride.

A good heavy dose of K towards the end just wraps things up perfectly. The session will feel as good as a two week vacation...!

Obviously, none of the above should be attempted away from a safe friendly environment...!

Think you've a bit of a maths typo there mate.

100mg MXE with 2 x 1.2mg tabs of 25i will give two 50mg lines with 1.2mg 25i in each.

I find  this amount snorted (1 tab) is roughly equivalent to 1.5 - 2.0 tabs buccal, so this 100mg of product should be sufficient for at least 2 people, in most cases.

The plate should be only just warm. It's a tiny quantity of liquid, so a couple hours on a radiator is plenty. A few more if it's just in a warm room. Avoid getting it properly hot, I've noticed some MXE can change from crystal to waxy if it's heated too much, and that's a fucker to chop and snort.

Title: Re: Mixing 25i + MDMA
Post by: Yoshitoshi on March 25, 2013, 01:07 pm
Er, I wouldn't really like to try, besides the taste isn't going to be carried over in any useful way.

I use vodka as it's a pure combo solvent, it's "food safe" and will definitely evaporate more quickly than water alone, leaving absolutely nothing behind. Some things maybe dissolve easier in ethanol too.

All liquor other than plain unflavored vodka, contains other stuff besides water and ethanol. This may screw your results up. The one time I deviated (using lime-flavored vodka, as it was all I had to hand) I got sticky brown sludge instead of dry white crystals. Maybe the limonene (or whatever they add to it) fucked it up.

So no, would not recommend it!
