Title: Re: 25I capsules Is this possible?
Post by: 328502E on May 28, 2012, 06:52 pm
First...yes, you can take 25i-NBOMe orally.  It will be 16 times less potent and be metabolized into 2c-i before it actually gets to your brain.  So you might as well just buy normal 2c-i at that point.

BUY A SCALE.  This is absolutely essential.  If you don't have the money for a scale, you don't have the money to be messing with these drugs.  End of Story.  I don't care what excuse you have, get a good, accurate, milligram scale.  You CAN NOT get by without one. It is absolutely irresponsible to attempt anything without one. 

Do not eyeball a dose.  You will have a bad time, and put yourself or someone else's life at risk.  Buy.  A.  Scale.

Also...no, you cannot cut it with an inert binder and then measure that out.  The problem with solids is that they do not disperse evenly.  Ever played with knex or lego's as a kid?  Notice how the small pieces are always at the bottom?  The exact same thing will happen here.  The powder will not be evenly mixed no matter how long you mix it for, and some doses will end up inert while others can be 10x as potent.  Don't do this.

I have actually been curious about how one would go about getting a small amount of powder into a gel cap.  For larger amounts of powder, one could let the liquid evaporate, and then know exactly how much is in the pile.  But you can't do this here, as that would result in a large loss. 

If you are serious about this, I would get a micropipette.  The kind that accurately measures micro-liter doses.  The smaller the better.  Then squirt that much liquid into the gel cap.  It may help if you have it heated so that the liquid evaporates quickly.  If done correctly, my guess is that the liquid will evaporate before dissolving the gel cap, leaving the powder behind.  I know there is a capsule that takes longer to dissolve (vegetarian?), so these may be the way to go.  Of course, once you have your milligram of powder in there, you have to snort it...and good luck with that =)
