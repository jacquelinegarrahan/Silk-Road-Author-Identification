a few of my friends and i actually dissolved some xanax in everclear (95 percent EtOH) and then just put them in regular clear gelatin capsules

they held up surprisingly well, some leaked a bit but were able to be stored for at least a few weeks.. then id just put my bud in the bags to soak up whatever leaked. i wouldnt recommend it though really

i dont really remember that summer. lol except that it was awesome

if you are measuring doses to take orally, as mentioned just measure weight of capsule then directly fill from initial container with a small scoop to correct weight.  use small slick surfaced folded paper weighing dishes and i like to use a cut straw actually to scoop. if you want to snort you can just pop open the cap and pour out the desired amount or just snort the whole capsule directly

for storage, the most important thing is airtight and in the dark to prevent degradation. some things are sensitive to moisture and should be kept dry (especially lsd). the cooler the better generally, but room temp is usually sufficient cause fridges and freezes cause moisture build-up. many dont like plastic baggies cause they can split and arent as airtight or things stick, and recommend small glass vials. i like using opaque capsules then just fill a normal vitamin bottle (of same appearance or no description) with em so you cant even till its not the vitamin (as long as no one else has access thinking they are actually whats on the label)
