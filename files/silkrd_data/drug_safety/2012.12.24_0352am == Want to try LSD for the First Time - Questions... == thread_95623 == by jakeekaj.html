1. Is some acid mixed with other things? For instance do some people sell "purer" acid?
LSD isn't cut with other chemicals, but there are varying degrees of LSD purity. Various ergot alkaloids and cycloalkamides of lysergic acid can contaminate the final product. In addition to manufactured impurities, impurities can also arise from decomposition of LSD.

None of the commercially sold drug-testing kits is capable of determining purity.

Impure LSD is physically harmless, but it gives you more of a body high, as opposed to the pure mind-expansion of pharmaceutical LSD.  You're less likely to have a spiritual experience with impure LSD. Most people these days have only taken impure or weak LSD.

There is also the possibility that it may not be real LSD, but some analogue or research chemical.  For this reason, I would suggest ordering from one of the 'star' vendors in the Avenders LSD thread, which can be viewed here: http://dkn255hz262ypmii.onion/index.php?topic=45270.0
