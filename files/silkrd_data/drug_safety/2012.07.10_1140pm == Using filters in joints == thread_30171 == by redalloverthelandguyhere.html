I've rarely seen people using filters for joints here in the netherlands. Surely the piece of crumpled paper is very popular to prevent inhaling any lose pieces from the weed and tobacco, but filters like those on cigarettes are rarely used. Perhaps they absorb a significant amount of thc - i'm not really sure.

Rolling tobacco is pretty popular here though, and you can buy cigarette blanks that come with the filter and paper and just need to be stuffed with tobacco (or cannabis, etc) at very low prices. There is a little device that shapes a pile of tobacco to fit these and insert it as well, also inexpensive.

Personally i rarely smoke pot and really do not worry about the tobacco, but for daily users it could certainly be a concern.
