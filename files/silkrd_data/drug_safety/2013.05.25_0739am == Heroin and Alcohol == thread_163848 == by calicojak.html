Can cause respiratory arrest. Both are depressants by different mechanisms. Heroin inhibits GABA, and alcohol inhibits glumate excitation. Or it induces vomiting, which, in addition to you passed out, causes you to basically drown in your own vomit. Watch your dose, don't overdo it and you should be fine.

Edit. Even if you don't die you risk aspiration pneumonia. Then being in hospital further exposes you to hospital acquired infections. Stay safe.

Title: Re: Heroin and Alcohol
Post by: newbottles on May 28, 2013, 07:59 pm
Good harm reduction advice here.  One thing I would add is make sure you stop the H for good after that early dose.  Don't go back to it later when you are feeling good.  I have noticed alcohol and H have a significant multiplication effect and you can quickly become way more intoxicated than you anticipated if you are combining them.

The safest approach here is start your day with the small H dose, and put it away for good.  Also, when you start drinking, go slower than normal at first and make sure you are grounded before you really pour it on.

I have felt some significant respiratory depression and reduced cardiac rate after combining only one beer with some H #4 and it is alarming.  Pay attention to your body as always.
