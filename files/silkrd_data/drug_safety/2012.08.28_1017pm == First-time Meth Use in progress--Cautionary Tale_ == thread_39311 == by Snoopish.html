When I first started using it I was snorting and had no trouble whatsoever having a bag in my drawer for weeks at a time without touching it. When you start smoking is when you realize how addictive it can really be. Smoking is a different beast altogether - no side-effects whatsoever, just pure clean euphoria and stimulation. This fact means to me that I want to be on it every second of the day. Then by day 4 I'm feeling kinda funny....

However all this experimentation was done while unemployed and EXTREMELY bored, lonely and devastatingly homesick. Now I'm back in action and have had a month with no real drugs to speak of - cravings for stims have gone. So I have ordered a small bag and will see if I can keep it under control when I have much more responsibility. If not, I'm leaving the stuff for special occasions only. I will attempt 100mg once a week or something. Not sure what to expect.

Another thing to note is that there is a 'honeymoon' phase with smoked crystal where you can't believe how utterly fantastic you feel. Once this period is over, as with any drug, you are far more in control.
