Title: Re: Lasting a whole day on MDMA
Post by: lemonz0r on March 04, 2013, 03:06 pm
Yeah, you simply can't roll on MDMA for that long - you run out of serotonin. In addition, any redosing you do just causes further harm (neurotically, worse come-downs). MDA lasts longer than MDMA and has similar effects (some people even prefer it), so you could switch to that, or combine the two. If you leave longer between rolls (up to two or three months), I expect they'll last longer and be stronger as well.

Other than that, perhaps you could try mixing/switching to other drugs? Candyflipping is supposed to be very good (LSD + MDMA) and LSD lasts about 8-12 hours, and you wouldn't necessarily need/want any MDMA for the first 6-8 of those. In addition, taking 2c-b as the MDMA wears off is supposed to be an amazing combination, reducing the comedown and having better effects that 2c-b alone. Hopefully I'm trying LSD->MDMA->2c-b this weekend, so I can let you know how it goes.

Other than that, you may just have to find an alternative or live with it. Apparently AMT has some similar effects (but a few different effects as well) and lasts longer. 5- or 6-APB would be worth looking into, too.

EDIT: forgot to add: dosing MDMA orally lasts longer than snorting it. The ideal dosing schedule seems to be your initial dose, then another 1/2(ish) that dose again about an hour or so later. Any more and you're causing more harm for less benefit, and really only getting the stimulant effects rather than the lovey-dovey-ness we all love about it.

Honestly though there's only so much fun a brain can handle for a day before it has to stop seeing it as being fun. Getting past 12 hours on a non-Meth high is going to be hard and a meth high isn't exactly the most fun at a festival with everyone on MDMA. Personally I would just Shroom or Cid in the start and end the night with a good roll if you didn't want to hurt the brain too much.
