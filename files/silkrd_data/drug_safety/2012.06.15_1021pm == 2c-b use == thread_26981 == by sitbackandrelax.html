Post by: oscarzululondon on June 15, 2012, 10:46 pm
I just took MDMA & 2C-B together for the first time last night. A batch came through from Ivory and hell it was amazing. The MDMA blew my mind off, I took it about 10:30pm and came down around 2:30am then I took 50mg of 2C-B (yes I know that's a huge dose but I seem to enjoy to taking a lot).

It takes an hour to kick in but wahey I was buzzing off my face, the visuals and insights were amazing and I was more chatty than on the MDMA, but still completely mentally in control unlike LSD or mushrooms. I then took another 50mg for some stupid reason about 4:30 and had a whole another trip which was really cool. It seems to level out like you can't overdose and is a very balanced trip (no up down up down like MDMA, just up all the time).

My partner who wasn't taking anything and said she had some great conversations with me, and I seem to remember everything. It also had the effect of me being able to go to work at 7:30am the next day after only 3 hours sleep and feeling fine, no MDMA comedown, which a few people have reported 2C-B seems to do.

One thing I will advise is that with the trippy visuals whilst I could walk around, go to the toilet and make drinks etc etc I couldn't even find the button to turn off my TV even though I was looking straight at it (small objects are hard to focus on), whilst objects far away such as tree's seem really sharp and clear. So I wouldn't advise driving etc after it. Even with the huge dose I had no full blown hallucinations like some people report. I loved it though, had no negative thoughts at all and felt great. It would be a great anti-depressant in small doses twice a week.

One tip I found is that my partner cut me up half a fresh orange in the early hours of the morning which I ate, and I came back down to earth in 10 mins, high to sober, enough to drive etc so if you need to come down quick and nicely, that's the way to do it. No idea why it works, as I was drinking orange juice all night which did nothing, but the fresh orange was like BOOM back to earth.

There are definite physical and mental effects to a decent (say 18-32mg) dose of 2c-b. Honestly, 2c-b is one of the least visually awesome compounds, in my opinion.
