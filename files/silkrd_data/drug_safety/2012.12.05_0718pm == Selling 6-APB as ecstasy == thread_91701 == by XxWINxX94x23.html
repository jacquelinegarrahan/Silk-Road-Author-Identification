Title: Re: Selling 6-APB as ecstasy
Post by: Ben on December 07, 2012, 02:15 am
And not only on SR, but in general.

The chances of getting caught selling something counterfeit are larger on SR, but you should principally never do that.

It may seem trivial to sell something with a similar effect for something more valuable, but this can backfire. Consider the situation of a user taking an overdose and seeking medical treatment after. The best course of treatment can differ between substances that have similar effects, and if someone is treated for an overdose of mdma while the actual culprit is 6-apb, that can result in complications.

Perhaps this may seem far fetched, but would you even consider risking your clients life by telling such a lie? You can sell something as 'equally effective' or 'similar', which is all fine if that is the case, but never make someone think they are using a different substance than they actually are.
