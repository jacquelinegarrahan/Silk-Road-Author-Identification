I took 1mg of complexed 25i buccally. And that was pretty good. Comparable to a hit or 2 of cid. But I wouldn't want to do much more as the pins and needles/tight muscles were a tad annoying.

I would say make sure you have a good amount of magnesium (and potassium) in you no matter what. Some people claim a cross-tolerance effect with MDMA. Whereby nbomes dull the MD effect. I noticed this somewhat but I'm not sure if this was just a little psychological tolerance to MDMA itself. But after 10 days clean I did not think there was any tolerance to the nbome or MDMA.
