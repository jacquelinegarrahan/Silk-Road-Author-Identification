Post by: hyruleantoker on May 27, 2012, 03:45 am
Most people here are saying to p-chute the molly. I would disagree there.

My first time rolling, the friend who hooked me up made me eat a point and he looked at me and told me to REMEMBER THE TASTE. It'll taste bitter, and pretty bad, and it won't leave your mouth for awhile. Make sure to have a drink on hand.
But the taste is a pretty good indicator of quality. I wouldn't want to p-chute molly.
Plus, after coming down from that very first roll, I looked at my friend and told him "It's really not that bad" referring to the taste. It kind of grows on you.

As for everything else, yeah, ~150mgs should do the trick. If you don't have a scale, some people just lick their finger, dip it in the baggie, and eat it. You can't really go wrong with your dose on molly. If you aren't feeling it within 5-10 minutes, try another small bit to get you there.

What I love about MDMA, among other things, is how easily you can just eyeball it. You don't easily OD on it, and you can redose if you aren't rolling as hard as you would like.
JUST REMEMBER: DRINK WATER! Pretty much the only reason people get so fucked up while using MDMA is dehydration. Just drink lots of water during your roll and you'll be good.

As tolerance builds for MDMA so quickly, always space your rolls at the very least three weeks apart. Most people suggest 1-2 months+.

One last word of advice: For your first roll, I would HIGHLY recommend going to a show for it. I think most other people would as well. Find a big name you like in music, and find when they'll come perform near you. Rolling at a show is so much fun.

I don't know anything about acid yet.

OH! And a perfect website you can and should always check about any drug: http://www.erowid.org
There you will find information on dosing, time duration, effects, and experience reports for almost all drugs.

Good luck and have fun,
~Toker
