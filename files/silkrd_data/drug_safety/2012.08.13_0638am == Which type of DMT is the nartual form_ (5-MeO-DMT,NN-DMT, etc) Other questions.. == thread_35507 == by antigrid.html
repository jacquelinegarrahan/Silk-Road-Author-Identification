Both 5-MeO-DMT and N,N-DMT occur in nature actually. 5-MeO-DMT is most popularly extracted from certain species of toads. N-N,DMT is usually found in plant matter, and Mimosa Hostillis is probably the most common source used for extraction.

They have different effects, 5-MeO-DMT is usually regarded as less visual and more near death type of experiences, while N,N-DMT is usually far more visual and is known as the "Spirit Molecule".
