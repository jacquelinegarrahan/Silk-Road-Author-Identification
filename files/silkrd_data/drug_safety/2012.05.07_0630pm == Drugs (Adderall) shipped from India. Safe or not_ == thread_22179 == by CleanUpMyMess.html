Post by: tordemon on May 07, 2012, 06:46 pm
Googleyed had a pretty good reputation last time I checked, but I wouldn't know personally. India is a big source for lots of drugs, both legitimate and counterfeit, but it's hard to determine which would be which.

While it's highly unlikely they would purposely sell you something that would damage your health, Indian pharmacies might do it to save money, or even more likely, just send you inactive ingredients. But Googleyed trusts those people; if they sent out bunk products, he would find a different source.

I'd recommend you make your decision based on how much it costs, though. If it's not too expensive, it might be worth the risk to investigate them. But, be careful when you get them.
