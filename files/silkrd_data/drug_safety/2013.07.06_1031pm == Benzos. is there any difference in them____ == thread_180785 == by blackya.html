All benzodiazapenes are pretty similar.
Lorazepam is the generic name for Ativan
Diazepam is Valium
Alprazolam is Xanax
and I've never messed with Etizolam but I heard its very similar to Clonazepam (Klonopin) as it's a little longer acting and has some beneficial muscle relaxant properties as well.

If you're just coming down from MDMA or speed bender, I'd just get some Xanax. They've always got the job done for me.

If you're having chronic sleep issues though you could try Klonopin given it has a longer half life but because of that they always made me feel 'groggy' or 'brain fog' in the morning. At the end of the day they're mechanisms all effect you the same.

They are extremely difficult to shake if you become dependent on them though. Just a warning.
