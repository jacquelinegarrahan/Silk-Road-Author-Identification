Post by: Internal Freedom on July 04, 2013, 09:08 pm
I'm assuming by glass pipe you meant a crack/meth pipe. Personally, I don't find them to work very well. I read on the DMT Nexus a while ago that DMT likes to slide/move away from heat, so its very hard to use a crack pipe for DMT as it tends to go all over the pipe and you will not be able to inhale all 100% of it.

If you do not want to go to the effort to hunting down an electric vaporizer or build a DMT-Machine, then just use a normal glass pipe that you would smoke weed out of. Put the DMT powder/crystal on top of some ash and use a NORMAL butane lighter. Move the flame close to the DMT but do not touch it, once you are around a centimeter away it will turn into a gel and slowly liquify into the ash. Once its been totally melted, put the flame directly into the ash and smoke it like you normally would :-)

The first 10-20 times I smoked DMT it was that way, its extremely simple once you get the hang of it and its an easy breakthrough every time. I didn't make my own DMT-Machine until around my 30th time doing DMT, and even know I like to go back to the traditional pipe method from time to time.

Or do the same thing with a bong, hits you even harder! I like to use fresh weed instead of ash to melt the DMT into when hitting out of a bong though.

Good luck tripping!

Post by: BlackIris on July 05, 2013, 06:14 pm
1. Be sure to torch the brillo before using it, elsewhere you toke toxic smoke.

2. Which type of brillo are you using? Copper or steel? Steel is much easier to use for a beginner, copper transfer heat much more strongly and as such it is easier to burn the DMT with (but when you know how to use it you can vaporize the DMT even better, it is just more difficult to use at beginning).

3. The toke should be slow and steady; DON'T go in forceful lapses, the vapor will turn into smoke and it will be harsh. Slow and steady is the primary concept here.

4. YOU DON'T NEED 50 mg with The Machine. It is counterproductive. Start with 15 mg the first try (some suggest to even start with 5-10 to learn how to do it properly). This because at beginning you will anyway waste DMT no matter what, secondly because if you burn a lot of DMT it will be a lot harsh - the less you burn the better you will be able to tolerate until you learn, thirdly because you don't need more than 20 mg to breakthrough if you vaporize correctly and you want to learn how to do it, fourthly because if you don't vaporize correctly ALL the DMT a resin will remain in the Brillo that changes the trip by and by the more it stands there. Always use the less DMT you can, NOT the contrary.

5. Don't pack too much the plug, let it be a little loose. If you press it too much it is easier to burn the DMT.

6. Hold the vapor (or even the smoke if you have to because you burned the DMT) at last for 10 seconds, elsewhere you will feel much less of an effect. If you cough immediately it will not make any difference if you vaporized 50 mg or 5 mg, you will feel almost nothing in both cases.

Lastly (but not in order of importance) DON'T SNIFF FREEBASE. You will only waste DMT and make you sick in the process. Learn to smoke it, it's not difficult with The Machine. IMO the only great error you have really done is put that much of an amount to try. At beginning you cannot cope with it without coughing because you are going to burn much of the DMT and if you aren't used smoking (as with cigarettes) it will be a lot harsh.
