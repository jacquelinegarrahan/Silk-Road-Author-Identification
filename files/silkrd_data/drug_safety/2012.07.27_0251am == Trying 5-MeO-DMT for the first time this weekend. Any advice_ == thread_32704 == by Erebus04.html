Title: Re: Trying 5-MeO-DMT for the first time this weekend. Any advice?
Post by: digitbh on July 27, 2012, 02:56 pm
If you're taking any MAOI's do not take this. If you have a history of mental illness in your family, do not take it either.

Because of the potency of this drug, make sure you have a very nice scale that measures milligrams out with little error. Because it's your first time I would start off with 10mg for smoking it.

The smoke is harsh, like that of DMT. For this particular version I prefer to use a standard meth pipe. I usually choose to smoke 5-MeO as it provides me with the quickest duration, and with minimal after effects (generally lasting about 20 minutes).

If you choose to snort it, be prepared for a nasty nasal drip, as well as after effects lasting for easily over an hour, and a total duration lasting a little over an hour as well. Smoking gives you a duration of 20 minutes or so.

You can also take 5-MeO orally, but I've never done that and have no knowledge on duration or hangover.

For any method though, start out with 10mg as this is a light dose. If you want to just try a threshold dose, start off with 5mg.

Make sure you have a friend nearby to take the pipe from you if you're smoking it, and preferably they will leave the room after you start your trip. 5-MeO is a solitary drug, and external stimuli (talking, movement) will take you out of the trip. Also, water is a good thing to have no matter which way you take it. I always find myself thirsty after a trip on 5-MeO.
