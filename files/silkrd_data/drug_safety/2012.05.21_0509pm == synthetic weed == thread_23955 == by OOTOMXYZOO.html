You're under thc drug tasting? That's my dream job!

While I don't smoke synthetics, my cousin who is on probation and is tested frequently has been trying several kinds of the the past year and a half. He has me try them but I haven't cared for them, and the ones I've tried (K2, Blue Label, Serenity Now [cool name for Seinfeld fans], and High Fly) were nothing like a weed high. Most seem more of an energy type feeling, which I guess some people get from weed but I don't, even from pure Sativas.

They make me light headed but it's hard to tell if it's the spice or because I cough up a long every time I take a hit. Remember when you were a kid and you chain smoked a few cigs or inhaled on a cigar and got that light headed feeling but your stomach hurt and it tastes like shit? That's about the extent of it.

My cousin smokes it for the same sort of reason people trying to quit smoking cigs feel consuming bags of lollipops help them quit. The 'high', if you want to use that word, only lasts like 10 minutes. Plus the shit is way more expensive than weed.

I wouldn't recommend it to anyone for any reason.
