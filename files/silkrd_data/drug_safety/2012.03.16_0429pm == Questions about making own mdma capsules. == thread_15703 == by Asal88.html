Post by: wowzers on March 16, 2012, 04:37 pm
100mg fills about 75% of a '0' size gelatine capsule.

I use different coloured caps for each drug/ dose for easy ID in a dark nightclub.

Mannitol is a good inert cutting agent, I like to pack my gel caps tightly so they don't crush so easily in my pocket.

Title: Re: Questions about making own mdma capsules.
Post by: redalloverthelandguyhere on March 28, 2012, 04:12 pm
If you cannot find different coloured capsules - then mark them with some permanent pen. You could have the untouched capsules as 100mg - and if you do any more powerful doses - colour them black. Top up doses of around 50mg can be coloured in half.

Its wise to weigh up mdma crystal before going out! Judging mg when already rocking a bit is not easy.

Good safety advice here.
