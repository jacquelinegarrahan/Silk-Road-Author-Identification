Title: First REAL DMT experience. Advice/Opinions?
Post by: ominuby2 on March 13, 2013, 10:54 pm
A couple of years ago I did several DMT extractions from MHRB.  I recrystallized, I was careful, did all the right stuff.    I've done mushrooms and acid, I did MXE, meth, heroin, even high dose salvia.  But I was too scared to take "the big hit" of my own DMT.  I took tiny puffs and it made me a little nauseas but I thought the visuals were beautiful. 

Well, I've been taking alprazolam for social anxiety, and I thought I'd give DMT another go.  So I started taking larger doses of it.  No sick feelings, no fear anymore, just the most mind-blowing visual experiences-- beyond anything I could have imagined.  It's like every breath and every beat of my heart expanded my perception into new dimensionality that I can't describe in words.

About a half hour ago, I took the biggest hit I've ever done. I laid down on my bed and ... then the __VOID__.  I forgot who I was, I forgot I'd taken a drug, there was nothing but this otherness, and this intelligence, way bigger than me.  I got the feeling of incredible MALEVOLENCE, and as I came back from it, it was like waking from a deep, deep, nightmare, like I was being observed by some cruel, malicious intelligence far greater than my own.  I began to remember being alive, being human, what thinking as an individual was like, and then finally me, myself.     

Biological life is part of something much greater-- I'm NOT talking about religion or mysticism; I'm somewhere between agnostic and atheist; I don't want to use the 'G' word or the 'D' word-- but I think I saw hyperspace!  It's coherent, and it's conscious, it felt like I was inside of it.   And it scared the fuck out of me. 

Is this what happens when we die??? 
Has anyone had similar experiences?

To anyone who takes this stuff lightly -- BE FUXING CAREFUL.
