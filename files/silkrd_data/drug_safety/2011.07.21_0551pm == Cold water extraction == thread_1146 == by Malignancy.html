Title: Re: Cold water extraction
Post by: cronus12 on July 22, 2011, 07:24 pm
1. Crush up all pills for a dose

2. Put 60ml of water at 104 degrees Fahrenheit(40 degrees Celsius) into a small glass.

3. Throw the crushed pills into it and stir for a few minutes to ensure all large clumps and pieces appears as a fine powder in the water.

4. Cool the glass + contents to 50 degrees Farenheit(10 degrees Celsius).  Putting ice cubes into a shallow bowl with water will cool it much faster than putting in a freezer.

5. Quickly take the glass and contents out of cooling and pour all the contents through a coffee filter. (General lab grade filter paper is better) You will probably want to cut the coffee filter smaller so that all your liquid does not absorb in it.

6. As soon as the liquid has filtered carefully squeeze the clump of powder in the filter to get as much more of the liquid as you can.

Testing your product.
***This is an optional step to see if it worked ok or not.  Once you do this a few times you will get the hang of it and be able to whip up a batch in ten minutes***
Cool the liquid to 50 degrees Farenheit(10 degrees Celsius) and see if it appears cloudy or not.  If cloudy your filter was not good enough so you will need to double them up or buy a better filter paper.


You can now slam the liquid back and take a swig of juice and a few soda crackers to chase it down.  This has been used time and time again with no ill effects and really reduces the chances of getting too much APAP in our solution.  1g of APAP is the max single does and is impossible to get even close to that amount using this method.  Be careful with your techniques as you can lose your liver with APAP, a very painful way to die.
