Title: Re: How much Ecstasy can someone handle?
Post by: TheMask on May 02, 2012, 11:29 am
Ecstast is a great drug, I understand your craving for more. It is the most blissful effect in the world. But.

You're not respecting the drug you're taking. Ecstasy is not a drug you take every day, every week or every month. When you use it you expend a backup resource of hormones (serotonin), generally it takes months to fill itself up again. When you take a lot in a short time the drug will hardly effect you because you're out of serotonin. When you persist to drain your stockpiles your brain chemistry will change, eventually leading to serotinin syndrome. You really don't want your brain to stop producing the hormones that make you happy. Respect your drug and it will treat you right. Go all out but do it far apart (I recommend 4 to 6 months), dose up as high as is comfortable and save it for special occasions. MDMA can be a really special experience, don't waste it all on the dancefloor.
