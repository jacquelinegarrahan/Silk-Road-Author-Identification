Post by: DiamondSky on March 28, 2013, 07:02 pm
So 100ug is a pretty light dose - don't expect full blow visuals or too much mental madness off that dose - which is probably a good thing if you are doing it at a festival for the first time. Normally I'd suggest about 200ug for a good first trip but you would want to do that somewhere safe, comfortable and predictable. Most acid heads shy against an abort button but if you have any hesitation going into it, you might want to track down a Xanax just in case things get too weird for you - but at 100ug I think you'll still have pretty good control over the world.

Acid can take a good long time to hit so if you want to do 1 hit I would only bring one hit or through complex drug induced math you might find yourself eating the rest an hour or two in.

Also, acid is very dependent on state of mind. You might not feel like you are feeling much of anything until you sit down and stop to appreciate things. Try and find a few minutes of quiet time to just gaze and something and you might be surprised and what you see gazing back at you.

My personal advice would be to forgo the weed the first time or two out. Weed can really amp up the visuals of the trip but it has a pesky little habit of sometimes introducing a little paranoia too. A clean LSD trip can be a beautiful thing on it's own so no reason not to get to know it a little before you go mixing in other substances. When you do decide to mix, even though I know you've sworn it off for a while, a little MDMA really is magical on the stuff.

Anyway, enjoy the trip!

Post by: DMTisinME on March 28, 2013, 07:18 pm
When you smoke weed on psychedelics, you really can't even feel it. Most of the time I do it while tripping it, to do something while conversing with others. If you do do it, I'd suggest smoking near the end. But like GSE said, since it's your first time you may want to understand the single substance the best you can. Although I doubt it'd take much of anything away -- a lot of people love smoking while tripping.

I'd suggest taking it 8 hours before you usually go to sleep. That way you can trip, unwind, then nod off. I usually eat a protein hearty meal before and then don't eat until after -- usually. But yes, keep water on hand. I am more thirsty than normal while tripping, mostly because I end up talking a lot to other people.

Listen to music you really enjoy yourself, and take notice to the enhancement. I like GSE's suggestion for instrumental music, but I would do this if you were looking for a "soundtrack" to whatever you're doing. Otherwise, listen to what you really like.

Make a list of idea's that you can do from your location so that you have some go to activities. Suggestions if possible are outdoors ones, such as hiking or just sitting on the porch in the sun, lying on a bench and looking  at the stars. If you like doing creative things, obviously do those: drawing, playing/making music, and writing are all great. Only use a movie for a backup. Something I'd suggest is to keep a notepad near and write down any revelations that come to you so you don't forget them. Sometimes my short-term memory is absolutely fucked when I'm tripping, but I think on 100ug this isn't likely.

Also, I think 100ug is a good starting point, maybe 150ug. So if you want to do  1 and 1/2 that'd be good.

Oh, also, it depends on the person, but for some people I suggest putting your phone in the dresser drawer and forgetting about it. You don't want to be getting calls from people you shouldn't be talking to in that mindset and it's really just a distraction.

So yeah, make a list of things that you think would be fun and on hand, and then once the stuff kicks in, pick whichever you are in the mood for. :)
