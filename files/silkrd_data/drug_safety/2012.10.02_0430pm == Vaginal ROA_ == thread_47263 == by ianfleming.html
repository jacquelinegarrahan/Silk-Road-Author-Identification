Post by: gestaltassault2 on October 03, 2012, 09:55 pm
the vagina is not connected to the digestive system so any drug that's orally active will not work...

however drugs that are sublingually active like LSD, NBOMe's, and DOx's should theoretically work...

another caveat is that the vagina maintains a specific pH which is linked to a woman's reproductive health...putting chemicals that aren't intended to be down there could throw this pH off and cause health issues down there...
