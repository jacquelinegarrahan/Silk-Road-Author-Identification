Title: Re: Beginner questions on cubensis strains
Post by: skeletid on September 26, 2013, 08:45 pm
All Cubensis are Cubensis and you won't find much different between their relative alkaloid ratios. Some cubensis may be more potent per gram, but the relative alkaloid content will be the same.

Now, between different species of the genus Psilocybes IS where you will find differences in relative alkaloid content the way that you would among cannabis species. P. Cubensis (which includes most "strains" like PE, B+, amazonian, hawaiian, etc.) is only one of these species.
Other species lile P. cyanceans, P. semilanceata (liberty caps), P. mexicana, P. azureans, etc., will have different ratios of active alkaloids the way that cannabis does and vary in potency per gram.

The active alkaloids responsible for the differences are of course psilocybin, psilocyn, and baeocystin.

You can find some of these other species available on SR from time to time.
