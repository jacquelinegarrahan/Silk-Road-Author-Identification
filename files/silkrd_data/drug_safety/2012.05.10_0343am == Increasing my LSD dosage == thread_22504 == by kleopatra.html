Title: Re: Increasing my LSD dosage
Post by: davebowman on May 10, 2012, 06:56 pm
I find that after doing psychedelics enough times you get psychologically conditioned. Last year I remember this crazy mushroom night with 12 people and I probably only had a half quarter but I was rolling around in this sandbox laughing my ass off like a fool. More recently I find myself taking 3 hits of lsd and wandering the streets and forest by myself at night, high as fuck but completely in control. In fact I find the outside world gets scarier when I am tripping with others than when I am by myself. I find I can think more clearly when I take lsd, everything looks and feels whack but mentally I have the same rational internal ego as when I'm sober. Basically I wouldn't worry about taking too much, but then again it affects everyone differently. But then yet again you don't know what it will be like until you just eat however much it is and see for yourself. Bottom line is that if you're otherwise healthy and its legit lsd it wont kill you or be a lasting detriment in any way.
