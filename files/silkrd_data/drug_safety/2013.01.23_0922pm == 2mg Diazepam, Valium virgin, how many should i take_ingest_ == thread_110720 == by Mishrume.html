Title: Re: 2mg Diazepam, Valium virgin, how many should i take/ingest?
Post by: Ben on January 24, 2013, 02:24 am
If they are 2 mg benzodiazepine tablets you may be taking quite bunch of them before noticing much of an effect.

With no tolerance at all you may notice effects from doses of 5 mg up or so, making you feel drowsy, sleepy etc.

One thing that is important to note with valium is that it has an extremely long half life if you include effects from metabolites. It depends a bit on the person, but its fairly common for the dose to have half its initial effect after 2 full days.

Don't expect to take enough valium to make you sleep at night and be okay in the morning. It's not that you will not live to see morning, just that you will be affected  80% as much as you were the evening before. This could obviously be a serious problem driving to work, and even more so if your work requires you to be concentrated to avoid serious mishaps.
