Yeah, I love this combo. I am very careful when mixing the two though. Say I do a fat line of coke...I'll only take half or less of heroin or whatever opiate I'm using than normal. So say I take 30mg of heroin or 20mg of oxycodone normally, I'll only take 15mg heroin/10mg oxy. I typically redose on the cocaine a lot more than the opiate too for whatever reason.
I'd rather not accidentally overdose. If I'm going to overdose and die, I want it to be intentional...and awesome. Just sayin'
I'm also a snorter so I feel like it's a bit easier to control the dose than IV. I feel like I've heard way more stories about overdose with this combination in IV users than snorting...but it's still dangerous. Too much of the opiate is what causes death typically. The stimulant causes your heart rate to rise and then when it wears off your heart rate falls dramatically because of the opiate and when it goes too low (or you nod out) your heart will stop/you'll stop breathing and never wake up :/
Stay safe friends (and high!)
Title: Re: Heroin and Cocaine - Experienced Users, Please Give Input
Post by: b0lixtrader on September 19, 2013, 02:52 am
The speedball effect is so great because you feel the best of both different types of drugs in harmony.

The euphoric stimulant of coke that may have a slight tweakiness/speedy feeling is neutralized by the heroin a euphoric downer.  They balance each other out in the best way so you feel the best of the both.

But since heroin lasts much longer, you need to be careful to not dose the dope too high over the coke.  Remember a heroin high will last more than few hours while a line of coke or two will only last 40min-1 hours.
