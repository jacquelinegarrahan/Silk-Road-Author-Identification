Title: Re: MDMA + OJ
Post by: smogmonster13 on March 20, 2013, 11:53 am
And I believe we might be talking about two different things. OJ when you swallow your MDMA, vs. OJ after you're rolling (therefore have already absorbed it). I was speaking to OJ when you take it, which will inhibit the absorption due to its acidity. MDMA is better absorbed in a higher pH environment (plugging). This is not a perceived effect, but a property of the pharmacodynamics of the substance.

Grapefruit juice is a different matter. It contains narinijin which inhibits your liver's ability to break the drugs down. Don't ever do this. It may harm your liver and you.

I agree with Sirius. There doesn't seem to be anything proven to make your roll more magic (there's anecdotal evidence about piracetam). There are things to inhibit it. There are things to make it safer.
