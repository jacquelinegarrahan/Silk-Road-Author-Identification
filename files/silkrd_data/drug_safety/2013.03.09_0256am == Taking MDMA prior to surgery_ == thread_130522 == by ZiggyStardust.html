Title: Re: Taking MDMA prior to surgery?
Post by: Ben on March 25, 2013, 02:25 am
Good to hear that all went well.

I suppose a bit of cannabis use will do no harm, but around surgery be very careful with drugs in general.

For people that stumble upon this thread in the future: be frank with your anesthetist and other treating physicians about your drug use. Anything you discuss with them will be safe under doctor-patient confidentiality, and could be of importance. If you have, for example, a high tolerance for opiates, barbiturates or benzodiazepines, it is best you let them know about that, since it can influence decisions that must be made while you are knocked out.

Once you are under it is very difficult to guess why medications do not work, and they have to work on assumptions that could be incorrect.
