Title: Re: Getting a dexedrine prescription
Post by: skeletid on September 29, 2013, 03:37 am
Were you just recently prescribed ritalin? At any rate, all you have to do is this- when you return for your next followup and they inquire as to how it's going just tell them the truth. It gives you headaches. Maybe... anxiety and irritability too? Right? Right. Maybe it helps to some degree, but the those side effects kinda negate it.

Ritalin is often the drug they'll try first. It's often the case that it doesn't go over well due to side effects. From there they may try Concerta (extended release). More likely, they'll move on to try an amphetamine. Depending on where you are located, they'll try either adderall (or xr), vyvanse (essentially dexedrine xr), or dexedrine. Or a combination. They may allow you to participate in the choice.

If you don't like their decission just be cool and address it in the next followup and they'll tweak your Rx further. And so on. Eventuallyyou'll be prescribed what works best for you (or what you desire, hopefully it's both).

Lucky for you, you don't even need to be dishonest. Just be honest about the side effects, maybe slighty exagerate the severity.

Stratera is probably off the table at this point considering you've already been prescribed a true stimulant. Unless you come off as a drug seeker, it's unlikely that Stratera would come up again unless it was some last resort attempt if all other meds failed.

Patience and will reward you.
