Hi all, have yet another question for the brilliant minds of the SR forums. This is in regards to the 2ci and MDA combination. I was shocked to come across a news article on a death associated with 2c-i, particularly one in combination with MDA.
 
       
Quote
"The paper by Drees et al describes the case of a 39-year-old woman who presented with a massive hemorrhagic stroke after using home-made 2C-I synthesized from a recipe found on the internet. Cerebral angiogram revealed Moyamoya Disease. MDA — another hallucinogenic stimulant — was also detected. It is not clear to what extent the individual drugs — alone or together — played in the unfortunate outcome. Treatment for exposure to any of the drugs in this class remains the same: providing good supportive care, implementing aggressive cooling if indicated, and anticipating complications such as rhabdomyolysis and acute renal failure.

I would personally be worried about a couple of other things besides medical issues. First is the bodyload of 2c-i. I am not a fan of it, and in medium doses even it can be too much. Maybe you don't mind the bodyload of 2c-i, but maybe you will mind it with MDA involved. It may become confusing and unnerving and too much to handle. The second thing I would be worried about is your mind-state. Now of course this may not be your experience, but I feel like should mention the possibility.

Honestly, I think 2c-i would not be a good match-up with MDA in high does. In smaller doses, 2c-i may be enlightening and energizing and a good addition to the MDA.
