Title: HydroxyPropyl Beta CycloDextrin (HPBCD)
Post by: Tryptamine on January 22, 2012, 02:02 am
HPBCD is a ring of sugar molecules that can be complexed with hydrophobic drugs to improve their solubility. In practical terms this renders them more capable of passing through mucous membranes, including those in the nose and mouth. To date I've heard of HPBCD used on 2c-i-NBOMe blotters, but never with any other psychedelic drugs. Has anyone tried doing so? I'm probably going to try it within the next few days anyway.
