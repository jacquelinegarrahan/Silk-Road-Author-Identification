Title: Re: Newb DMT question
Post by: Appa on May 24, 2012, 03:21 am
N,N-DMT refers to the freebase form of DMT, which is less stable than the fumarate form, but has a lower boiling point (easier to smoke).  Both of these crystals can be ingested via vaporization, but the freebase doesn't require as hot of a flame.
Changa is basically some herbs (any kind: mullein, mint, caapi) soaked in a DMT solution, then allowed to dry so the solvent evaporates out and leaves the DMT behind inside the leaves of the herb.  Think of it like smokable DMT blotter paper (but not paper :P).  It's simply a much easier way to smoke DMT, though some people also like to use an herb with its own effects.  Some of these herbs are claimed to prolong or intensify the experience.

Quote from: Trainwrecked on May 24, 2012, 02:20 am
Okay, so Changa...  How much would be considered a fun time for me and my g/f and maybe a friend or two to enjoy together for a day?  Been interested in DMT, and extremely interested in Changa.  I've got a few friends who would enjoy it, and I know me and my g/f want to try it.  So how much should I get so we can sit around for a day/night and enjoy?

Your question is difficult to answer for a couple reasons.  First, changa has variable amounts of DMT, depending on how it's made.  DiMiTriSpice, for example, sells changa that is 1/3 DMT by weight, so smoking 0.1g of it would be like smoking 33mg of DMT (a medium dose).

Second, no offense intended, but perhaps you should do a bit more reading on the subject of DMT before diving in head first.  It's not really a social chemical, one that will provide a "fun time" with friends.  If you correctly smoke DMT and break through, you will not be capable of communicating with other people.  You will probably not know you are a person or that you have a body, and you won't be able to see your environment or interact with it.  A breakthrough DMT experience may as well transport you to another world in another dimension, with its own laws of physics and ways of doing things.  I don't want to frighten anyone, but it's not an experience to be taken lightly, and it's nothing like doing LSD, mushrooms, 2c's, MDMA, etc. and having a fun trip/roll with your mates.  Even if you all wanted to trip at the same time, I would recommend against it.  Someone should be sober to ensure everything goes well (you don't drop the bong when you blast off, for example) and the tripper(s) stay safe.  Also, I personally would be uncomfortable going into a DMT experience with an audience.  Having one safe sitter with whom you're comfortable and familiar is one thing, but having a few people sitting around watching you trip out?  Not my cup of tea.

The experience will probably last 10-30 minutes, though it will probably feel much MUCH longer to you.  I doubt you'll want to do it over and over again to make a day of it.

I hope all of this didn't come off as negative or scary, I know reading about DMT can be overwhelming.  DMT can provide the most beautiful, enlightening experience, but I would be remiss if I didn't discuss the other side of things.
