Quote from: blackfedora on December 16, 2012, 03:16 am
-is there a dosage amount per hit? is some more potent .ect?
100 micrograms is a good dosage for recreational use, and there are several vendors whose acid is dosed at around 100 micograms per hit. But if you want to experience the full spectrum of LSD effects, you usually have to take 200 micorgrams or more (i.e. 2 hits, assuming that it is 100 micograms per hit).

Quote from: blackfedora on December 16, 2012, 03:16 am
-how much f that dosage would be borderline OD?
You can't OD on LSD, if by OD you mean taking a life-threatening amount of acid. At least, you would have to consume an amount that is not accessible to most people -- like the equivalent of 100,000 hits of acid, and even then you probably wouldn't die. Nonetheless, for a first time user, I would recommend 100-200 micograms of LSD. 300 if you have a good sitter.
