Hey Nuyt,

DMT is a much more stable compound that LSD.  I haven't done a full on stability test (oxygen, light, heat, and time for each), but I have retested product after a few months of sitting around not in the fridge and not worrying about O2 or light, and there was negligible degradation.  To be on the safe side though, just throw it in the fridge or keep it somewhere dark and cool...seal it if in the fridge because you don't want all your food to taste like it haha!!! 
Hope that helps a bit.  I'll try to get way on stability testing so I can give you a more formal answer!
