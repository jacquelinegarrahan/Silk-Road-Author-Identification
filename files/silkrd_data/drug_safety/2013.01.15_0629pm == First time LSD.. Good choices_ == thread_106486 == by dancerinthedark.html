Title: Re: First time LSD.. Good choices?
Post by: Wazup7 on January 22, 2013, 07:47 pm
I took shrooms a bunch before I took my first LSD trip, and I would say the shroom trips only partly prepared me for LSD.  For me, I describe shroom trips as fun and crazy, but LSD trips are deep and intense. 

I am torn as to the advice to give you.  My rational part is saying only take 1 hit (I consider 1 hit a standard baseline dose, no matter the actual ug dosage--some prints require 3 hits, other require half a hit).  This will potentially disappoint you, but will most likely not result in an experience that is too intense to be enjoyed.  My gut, though, is saying you should take 3 hits.  This will definitely show you what LSD is all about, and there is a very real chance that it will be so intense that it's overwhelming.  Assuming you are a mentally-healthy individual, there is no documented medical risk to massively overdosing on LSD.  The only risk is of having a negative trip, which can be mitigated by preparing set and setting.  The choice is really up to you--do you feel that you are well enough prepared to enjoy an overwhelmingly intense experience that you have never gone through before? 

I personally took (what I now know was) a fairly large dose, 250ug.  (We at least confirmed that each hit contained 250ug of the source crystals, but not the quality of the crystal--I've never seen crystals since then and I really don't know how one would verify the quality of the crystals...anyway...)  I was blown away but in a good way.  Set and setting were optimal and I had a great, albeit overwhelming, time and fell in love with LSD.  I've had many trips since...I've lost count ;-)

As for sourcing, blerbadoo said it--check out the LSD Avengers thread. 

That's the name of the group that took it upon themselves to centrally verify LSD sources and batches on SR.  In the early days here, LSD was in relatively short supply, a ridiculous amount of vendors were cropping up and advertising "STRONG 300+ug LSD" which was in fact blotters laid with other RCs.  Other vendors would selectively scam or switch from selling legit LSD to RCs on the same prints.  So the LSD market on here was extremely ugly and difficult to work with, and too many new members were falling victim. 

Now though, thanks to the LSD Avengers and other helpful forum members, there is enough collective feedback that it's not hard at all for a new member on the forums to find a reliable LSD source.  There are a good number of established reliable vendors now, so scammers are much less common, and much easier to differentiate from the reliable vendors.  Also, new vendors are usually blacklisted immediately and made to (well asked to, unofficially) supply samples to the LSD avengers so that they can non-blacklist the new vendor, and give the vendor an opportunity to gain a customer base.

so in general, when you are looking for a vendor, look for one that has been around for a good amount of time (>1 year preferably), has good feedback, and preferably a vendor that has a following on the forum.  I personally buy from 3Jane, as she is well established, friendly and respectful, and generally honest about her product.  But check out the avengers thread to stay current

Enjoy!!  I think you'll have a great time if you're in the right place!
