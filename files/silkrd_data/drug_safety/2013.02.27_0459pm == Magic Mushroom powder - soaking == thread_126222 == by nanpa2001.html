Title: Re: Magic Mushroom powder - soaking
Post by: wtfisgoingon on March 17, 2013, 03:12 am
Assuming your shrooms were dried correctly and are potent still (some ppl STILL use too much heat when drying and murder the potency) then just use warm water, lemon juice and powdered shrooms. Let sit for 30 mins. Strain and enjoy. Don't boil or microwave or use boiling water. Bathtub temp is more than enough. I use a cup of water per 7g and about 1/4 of those little lemon squeeze bottles (or a whole lemons juice).

It definitely makes it more visual and slightly more potent, not twice or anything but the still the only way to go. It kicks in quickly and makes coming up fast and not nearly as unpleasant as eating them will.
