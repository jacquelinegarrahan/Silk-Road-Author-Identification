Title: Re: MDMA Hallucinations
Post by: Djubrovnik on September 22, 2011, 10:37 am
It's not unheard of MDMA giving people visual and auditory hallucinations, especially on higher amounts.
I once ate about 0.5 gr of highest quality Dutch mdma (that i got on SR :) ) and the effects were overwhelming, and sure enough I had unmistakable hallucinations. They weren't like traditional acid or shroom hallucinations in nature, they were more blocky (pixelated) and it was also like I was watching a movie with missing frames, pictures were jumpy.
Imo with mdma there is a sweet spot dose after which you get negative effects like nystagmus (eye wiggles) and jaw clenching much more pronounced. Sometimes less is more, and that's definitely the case with mdma. :D

On the other hand MDA is much more likely to produce hallucinations, but i believe it's pretty rare and uncommon substance to run into.
