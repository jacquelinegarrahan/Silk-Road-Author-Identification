10.  Go Paintballing on Acid...I have yet to try this but I hear it's crazy.  Might be a little too crazy.
11.  Stare in the Mirror, Stare into your eyes in one of those super magnified mirrors...watching them dilate is nuts.

15.  Floating down a river in an inter-tube.

18.  Smelling extreme smells, preferably good smells...candles, incense, coffee grinds, Salt.

30.  Buy some Poi Balls off of a website.  They are like 10$ each or so...totally worth it.  They are likely LED but swinging them around is fun and makes for amazing light shows.  Also hula hooping is fun.
31.  Go to a planetarium or laser show (Pink Floyd Laser Light show is ideal)  Check your local IMAX or Mosi Science Theater
32.  Play an instrument...or play with laser pointers if you have no musical talents.
33.  Get some dry ice and have some fun (try not to burn yourself) 
34.  Get a sober sitter to take you on a drive...go through a car-wash, while in the car wash...hot box with a blunt while listening to awesome music.  This is my favorite way to smoke blunts.
35.  Go to a museaum or look at paintings.   Paintings become their own little movie.  They will move and are quite entertaining. 
36.  Drink Wine or Beer?  I’m told this is cool on Acid but I have yet to try it, lots of people recommend it. 
37.  Play some visually cool Video Games
38.  Watch some classic Cartoons (Bugs Bunny, Inspector Gadget…)
39.  Gambling is supposedly fun and some say you are better at it whilst tripping.  I imagine being in Vegas would be the ideal situation on Acid. 
40.  Go skiing or snowboarding.
41.  Watch a 3-d Movie.
42.  Film yourself.  Set up a camera on a tripod in the room and forget about it.  Or actively film some or all of your trip.  Watching the footage when your sober is very entertaining and often will help you keep some of those amazing memories that are almost always forgotten when you trip balls.
43.  Skydiving is said to be intense, I can’t even imagine this though.
