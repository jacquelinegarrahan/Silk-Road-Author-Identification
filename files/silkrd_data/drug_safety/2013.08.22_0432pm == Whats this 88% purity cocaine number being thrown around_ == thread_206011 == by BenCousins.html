Title: Re: Whats this 88% purity cocaine number being thrown around?
Post by: klemmo on August 22, 2013, 08:07 pm
The hydrogen chloride group is present in cocaine, as it is in MDMA. If the HCl group was not present, you'd have crack cocaine (or freebase MDMA, an oily liquid).

The percentage of freebase depends on ratios of the masses of the pure (or "freebase") drug, to the common compound salt containing that drug:

molar mass of cocaine: 303.353
molar mass of HCl: 36.46
total molar mass cocaine.HCl: 339.813
Amount of cocaine in cocaine.HCl: 303.353/339.813 x 100% = 89.27%

You could make MDMA or cocaine phosphate, acetate or any number of salts, and the percentage of "freebase" in the salt would vary according to their molar mass.
