Title: Trip Report: 25i NBOME (Bad Trip)
Post by: Frostbite on May 05, 2012, 02:16 pm
This happened exactly a week ago and I came close to almost getting hit by a train.... anyway even after reading up about the importance of set and setting i honestly didn't think the situation could have ended up as bad as it did but oh well.. here it goes.

I met up with some of my friends at night on an oval for some drinks, i took the 1000ug blotter as soon as i met up with them and within 15 minutes i had a huge smile on my face and i couldn't get rid of it, it was the best feeling in the world and slowly i started to lose the sense of been normal, within an hour everyone was their, i went to the bathroom and the pattern on the wall was moving around like a bunch of bugs, some of my friends were drunk and yelling and screaming a lot so the police were called, when i was running it felt like i was flying, like the world around me was moving extremely fast; we had to jump through a barb wired fence and when the trip was over i had a few cuts, but i felt almost no pain at the time, anyway we went up to the street to a house were one of my friends lived, i couldn't recognize him so i said something really bad, evidently he and some other people were gonna bash the sh**it out of me, so i was told to go the nearest train station, by myself and to avoid the main road.

This is when the bad trip was setting in.. i was walking down a street where a loud police siren kept repeating itself, i looked at a street sign but it was just a bunch of random letters put together, my thoughts kept repeating themselves; i saw a huge highway appear out of nowhere in the middle of the sky.. a plane flew over my head, it looked huge and sounded like it was right next to me. So this is where the bad trip began, i kept stumbling and all of a sudden a giant rat was running right at me... then it disappeared, an old person walking past looked like a witch and i had no clue where i was or what i was doing.. my thought patterns were in 'the loop' and I walked near some shops and i could hear cars and trains but i couldn't see anything except the sidewalk.

I recognized one of my friends which probably stopped me from dying, evidently i was walking on the train tracks and almost got hit by a train( which I have NO memory of what so ever)...its funny what your body subconsciously does when you have a bad trip, i got called a taxi home and when i got home i read up on bad trips, on Wikipedia it said stuff like, people must have a happy life and get laid ( which made me laugh), people should NOT mix psychedelic drugs with alcohol,if they do they are fucking  idiots  . I felt cold and weak so i turned on the tv to see some effects, people were talking but i couldn't hear anything.. a couple of seconds later i could hear a subliminal message telling me to buy something or that the world was gonna end, i found it funny but i was tired so i went to sleep with heaps of patterns distracting me. the trip told me a lot about myself, not in what i saw but what i felt after the bad trip. I felt a lot better about life in general

TLDR: Almost got bashed, almost died, trip was bad but it pretty much told me a lot about myself, I know i was really stupid to do it in a setting like this one and next time ill make sure I'm in a safe place with a sitter :)
