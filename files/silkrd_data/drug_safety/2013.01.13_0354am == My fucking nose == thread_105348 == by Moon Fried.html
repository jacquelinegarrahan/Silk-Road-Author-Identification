If it hurts ... stop doing it.

You've burned your nose and possibly the mucosa internally and above your nostrils.  So you should firstly stop snorting anything until the flesh in your nose has had time to heal, at least a week or two.

Second you should research whether or not you even need to snort the drug your snorting, in this case MXE.  Many drugs have higher bioavailability when just taken orally and many just snort them because they're convinced it's the norm,  or the 'real man's' way of doing a drug.  Reality is that half the drugs people snort (and MXE is one of them) are more active and definitely last longer if taken orally.

You'll also find 10 years down the road permanent and ugly disfiguring nostril damage if you snort drugs that have more absorbed when taken orally anyways.  It may be true that snorting with many (BUT NOT ALL) will give you a higher rush for a very brief period, but then only keep you high for a fraction of the time then if you had put it into a small piece of paper, folded it over and swallowed it.

If you INSIST that you need to snort your drug for it to be absorbed by your mucous membranes then I suggest again you give your nose a break until its fully healed and dissolve the drug in water/solvent and squirt into your rectum with a syringe.  The lining of your anus has more mucous membranes then your nose does anyways so .... plugging which I think ppl call it is how you should do it until you let your nose heal.

Treat your body like a temple and let your nose heal before going back and damaging it again.  This may mean abstaining altogether for a bit (why are you taking MXE every day if you don't mind my asking, that's excessive), eat it (in a small piece of paper or just eat it like a man, just chemical taste), or 'plug it'.  But don't keep causing further damage to your nose if it's hurting like you describe, this will lead to permanent damage .... just respect your body please :(

Post by: Wazup7 on January 23, 2013, 08:02 pm
I have a notoriously bad nose.  It takes like pretty much nothing for it to get irritated.  If I smoke, like 1 or 2 hits of weed, my nose will be stuffy and itchy for like a week.  (This hasn't stopped me from smoking daily though ;-))  So when I snort a drug, I know that I'm asking for a world of trouble with regards to my nose.

I've found that using saline mist spray regularly helps a ton to alleviate nasal irritation due to insufflation.  What I usually do is use the saline spray to irrigate my nose (like a neti pot) by squirting in one nostril and letting it run out the other.  I'll do that, wait 5 minutes, blow my nose, then give each nostril a spray to moisturize them.  Then I'll sniff the drugs into my nose and let them chill for a minute.  Then I'll do another spray in each nostril to re-moisturize them.  Wait about 10 minutes, spray again.  Spray like every 5 minutes after that, and then after about 45 minutes, I will flush my nose out again and blow my nose and then keep moisturizing a few times an hour.

If I don't use any saline, and just sniff and move on with my day, my nose gets completely congested to where I can't breathe through it at all, and will stay that way sometimes for days afterwards.  To the point where it's so uncomfortable that the experience with the drug is killed.  When I do use the saline like I described, I get very little discomfort, and while it's a lot of work (I need to be at home pretty much, with a sink available and some privacy), it's well worth it to avoid  the problems.

I hope you take the saline advice and it helps to prevent the discomfort.
