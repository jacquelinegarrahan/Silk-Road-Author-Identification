Title: Re: LSD+MDMA+Shrooms(+2C-B?) combo
Post by: oldtoby on April 03, 2013, 02:20 am
Funny you should say - I contemplated exactly that combination this last weekend, and ended up doing without the 2C-B, and with a mid-trip dose of mj.

Depends on what you're used to, dosage-wise. I'm new to mixing LSD and shrooms. On their own, I'm likely to drop 360-450ug LSD or 4-5g of shrooms, so for this combo I did 270ug LSD and 2.5g shrooms (whole, dried) and it was a pretty fun ride. Then an unknown dose (pill) of MDMA at T+2 hours, 4 or 5 volcano bags of good quality mj at the T+4 hour mark, and finally another 90ug LSD and 2.3g shrooms at T+9. I was trying to be a little conservative since I was new to the mix.

Your dosages seem a little light but again, depends on what you're used to. I figured I'd want to do a little over half of my regular solo dose of LSD and shrooms when putting them together and was pretty happy with the result, so if you're tripping on 150-200ug LSD or 2g of shrooms, you might be working with a similar formula. It was an unexpectedly level-headed, lucid trip, maybe because I held off on the mj and didn't mix in any alcohol. I hear good things about tapering with the 2C-B and will certainly try that in future.
