Title: Re: Gunpowder heroin?
Post by: junjo on September 23, 2013, 07:34 am
"Gunpowder heroin" IS black tar heroin, in every single way except it's final appearance, which still isn't very different.
Basically, black tar from the Mexican cartels is mashed up into chunks and sold as "gunpowder", in an attempt to make it seem like a different product than BTH.
In reality, they are the exact same thing.
As with BTH, quality varies dramatically, anywhere from 90%+ active alkaloids to completely bunk.

So now addressing your questions directly:

1)"What's the best way to get the most out of what I have? "

I would highly suggest plugging. If you're not aware of this method, read my tutorial in http://dkn255hz262ypmii.onion/index.php?topic=207873.15
No other method other than IV has a BA that comes even close

2)"How much of it is suitable for 1 dose? I am an adult human female, I weigh 112lbs., and as I said I have a very low opiate tolerance."

As I mentioned earlier, purity is EXTREMELY variable. Sometimes, BTH can be laced with fentanyl as well; an obscenely strong synthetic opioid that can make you OD quite easily.
Your best bet is to start off very small, with a dose <5mg. If you feel absolutely nothing, try between 5-10 mg. If nothing again, push it up another 5mg and so on until you''re finally satisfied with the effects you are getting.

Make sure to space these doses 3 days apart at least if you want to avoid tolerance and addiction.

Feel free to ask me any more questions if you'd like, have fun and stay safe!
