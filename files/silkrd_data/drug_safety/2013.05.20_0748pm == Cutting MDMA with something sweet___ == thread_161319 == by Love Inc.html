Title: Re: Cutting MDMA with something sweet???
Post by: zvp1014 on May 20, 2013, 09:01 pm
Marquis should go a black-purple. However the presence of a chemical that'll test MDMA positive in a Marquis will generally override the colors of any adulterants; it's not a lab-grade result.

If it was sweet, it could literally be sugar or a sweetener. But there's really no way of knowing what you got without lab testing, especially since you said it felt like MDMA. Was their decreased euphoria, or extra speediness?
