Title: Re: First time LSD, going to candyflip. Any advice?
Post by: nikkiluit on September 12, 2012, 02:55 am
Generally speaking, a nominal 300ug dose should give you sustained and pronounced visuals. Nominal 300ug doses on all the LSD I've bought from SR in the last year have given me visuals for 8 hours, on average, with intensity and character varying from batch to batch but always present. Different batches of acid have distinctly different "signatures" with respect to the distribution of effects across the visual, mental, and somatic spectrum as well as developing along different trajectories. Sometimes it's a steep climb during the comeup with an intense peak then a short plateau followed by a correspondingly steep drop to baseline. Other times the transition is slow and leisurely. With the candyflip you want the MDMA to hit once the trip has leveled off mentally but the visuals are still going strong and avoid being still spun by the LSD during the MDMA comedown, which is why people typically advise the MDMA at around the 4 hour mark so everything is back to baseline by hour 8 or 9. Adjust accordingly.
