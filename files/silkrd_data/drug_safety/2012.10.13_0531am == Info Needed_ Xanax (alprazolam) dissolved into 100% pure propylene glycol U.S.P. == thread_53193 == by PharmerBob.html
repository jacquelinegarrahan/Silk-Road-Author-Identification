Title: Re: Info Needed: Xanax (alprazolam) dissolved into 95% ethanol 5% water
Post by: Ben on October 15, 2012, 01:39 am
Obviously methanol should never be ingested. It can be a useful solvent to make aliquots or blotters though, and poses no danger if it s evaporated off before the final product is completed.

Also, methanol is not insanely toxic or anything like that. You could relatively safely ingest up to the order of 1 gram a day without ill effects. The stories of methanol causing blindness, insanity and even death are mostly from liquor that contains methanol instead of ethanol, where people ingest dozens of grams of it on a single evening, and sometimes for several evenings in a row.

The risks of getting small amounts of methanol in your system are minimal though - its frequently used to wipe things clean in a laboratory setting, and wearing gloves doing so is done to prevent fingers getting into the sample, not for preventing methanol getting on your fingers.
