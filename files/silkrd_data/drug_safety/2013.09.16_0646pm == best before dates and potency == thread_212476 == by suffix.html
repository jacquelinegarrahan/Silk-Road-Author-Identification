Title: Re: best before dates and potency
Post by: alwaystired on September 17, 2013, 12:37 am
Heat, light and oxygen are not your friend when it comes to storing chems, especially delicate ones like tryptamines. In general, dark amber vials stored in a cool, dry environment is adequate. If you want to go wild, get you a vac sealer and argon, which is commonly used in welding and also available as a wine preserver available on amazon. I'd store shrooms vac sealed in a freezer.
