First a few notes:
-Listen to your body, test early. Know what your limits are.
-School is good, but staying alive is better. Keep stuff in focus.

So quite frankly Adderall and such costs a ridiculous amount on here. I have no idea why and I dont really care. So here is my alternative. This is to keep focus, and avoid sleep. This is only for people who dont have learning difficulties that require scripts, but rather for those of us who simply need a few more hours of focus.

1. I bought 2G of speed paste from MR.Importer here on the forums. A 5/5 transaction. Registered 1.98G on my scale. Close enough. Costs about $40.
2. Buy some Gel Caps. 0 size is overkill but whatever is handy. Cost me $9 for 150 units.
3. Take speed paste and put it on some tin foil. Put tin foil on very low heat on electric stove.
--Good way to gauge temp, is you should be able to touch the stove without burning yourself. Not keep your fingers there but a brief touch.
4. Every 5 minutes or so take it off heat (you should be able to hold tin with your bare hands) and use a small piece of plastic to chop it up. Maximize surface area.
5. After about 30 minutes most of the humidity is gone. (should be kind of like scaly flour sort of = hard to describe)
6. Get your scale. Each of my gel caps weighted .1G exactly. Measure an empty one of yours.
7. I know my tolerances so I put 40mg per cap. So the scale should read .14 full.
8. I got about 600MG of speed after baking. Was pretty disappointed by this, but I only need like 5 caps for finals week so its not an issue for me.

Here are a few notes on usage:
-Take it in the morning. Pill is good for about 8 solid hours for me.
-Take it with food
-I got no shaking, no weird side effects, although I was kinda cold for a while.
-No side effects to notes. Took it at noon. Went to bed at 3AM, woke up at 9AM still feeling very fresh.
-Drink lots of water throughout the day. This will help flush you by the end of the day + water intake has a positive correlation with brain activity.

So basically for $50 or so you can have about 10-15 40mg speed pills. Hope this helps.
