Post by: GoodGollyMolly on February 25, 2013, 11:26 am
XxWINxX94x23

Glad to hear you're making it to EDC. This will be my third year going and for my first EDC in Cali I was freaking out about the same thing. A few dozen festivals later and we seem to have figured out what works for US, but its taking a lot of experimenting with the process.

About 14 days leading up to it, I'll take an htp booster, but not after that, don't want to have those levels all over the place before rolling. I don't know if its more psychological at this point, but I always seem to feel better with the booster taken in advance like that.

The week of I make sure to stick to my regular workout schedule and eat really lean. Lost of veggies, lots of fruits, absolutely no fast food or garbage (I’m a health nut and exercise freak anyway). Always make sure you get good sleep the night before because the first night will set your pace and you don’t want to be limping into day 3.

Day 1 I usually will roll with 125mg of moonrocks with a 50mg booster about +2/2.5 hours into it. That will usually last me all the way back to the hotel and I tend to sleep like a baby on the come down.

When you get up make sure you stick to that healthy food choice and rest.

Day 2 I try to mix it up a bit. I’ll usually take about 75mg of mdma and break up an 1/8 of shrooms throughout the night to keep the trippiness going. I actually did that while riding the ferris wheel last year. Fucking amazing ride that was.

At the end of day 2 I usually cheat a bit. If you are going to Vegas EDC then there is a raver hotel that is NOT widely known by tourists, but if you ask around some nice locals or even EDCers will be able to point you to the right direction. The beautiful thing about this hotel is there usually is a part time RN that works there and her mission in life seems to be to take care of ravers (she actually had a brother who died from being given heroin instead of mdma, so she is on a mission to work with the local community to educate on proper drug use). If you’re nice to her and listen to her give a small lecture on proper usage, you usually can get a nice IV from her to replenish your hydration levels. Hell, if you’re lucky you’ll probably run into me there too!

Day 3 is our be all end all day. We go hard and out body knows it, so make sure you rest all day. The heavy headliners will be closing out the main stage and you will see some insane shit that night. I usually won’t take more than 200mg and that will take me to wonderland until I get home the next day.

Meet people, enjoy the atmosphere, and make sure you stay in touch with the friends and family you make. I have a pretty good festival family and we have made it to the last few EDS, Coachella, Ultra, electric zoo, nocturnals, and this year we are adding burning man to that list. The family I’ve met at these places are some of the coolest, most diverse, and realest people I know.

Enjoy yourself and be safe.

Another suggestion would be to go out one weekend and take a bit of a higher dosage than you usually take just to see how your body will react. To prep for the first festival I did 300mg in one night just to see how my body would react to that much stimulant in me. It was a wild fucking night. That is a small gauge as to what to expect.

I’ve been doing MDMA on and off for about 10 years now and it never gets old. Just be safe and happy rolling.
