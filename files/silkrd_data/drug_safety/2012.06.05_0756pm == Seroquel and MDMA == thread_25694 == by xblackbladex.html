Title: Re: Seroquel and MDMA
Post by: sdesu on June 05, 2012, 11:38 pm
It shouldn't be a problem since Seroquel isn't an SSRI.
But if you're taking seroquel for schizophrenia or psychosis, MDMA may exacerbate these problems.

If it's just for anxiety, you should be fine.

Also, you should know that seroquel may make it harder for your body to cool down when it gets very hot. While you are taking seroquel, you should avoid excessive exercise (dancing, ect.)

If you're going to stop the seroquel, be sure to gradually go off, never cold turkey :)
