I just did this combo a few days ago. Since I worked hard the last few years, I quit my job June 1st and have been on a great drug binge ever since (although I gotta start looking for new job soon or I won't have money for more drugs!). My conclusions on hippyflipping are as follows:

It doesn't matter what you take first, it just matters how much of each you take. There is a sweet spot balance wherein both drugs form a synergistic high. If you don't find this sweet spot, one or the other usually takes over. I like to drink a few grams of so of some mush tea first, and then dose 100 mg MDMA while the shrooms peak starts about an hour or two after dosing. Then, a couple hours later I take another 50-80 mg of MDMA depending on how i'm feeling. My usual concern is that I love the first half of a shroom trip and the unbridled thoughts I gain from it. However, the downward comedown sometimes creeps me out a bit, and that is when I want the MDMA to take the reigns of the trip. Of course you can always just drink a six pack on the comedown instead.

Anyway, have a great trip man!
