Title: Re: First time Ketamine
Post by: Snoopish on September 11, 2012, 04:46 am
I hear good things about Fartbomber. My first K experience involved about 100mg. It can burn something fierce but the best way to get a feel is to snort it in a short amount of time. I staggered it out rather like you have and it diminishes some of the effect. If you bought a gram, without a scale I'd suggest emptying out the bag and dividing it into two equal-ish piles. Then do that a second time(for four piles). Then do it one more time(for eight piles, or just keep halving one).

You should end up with a general idea of what 100-150mg K looks like. Put away the rest of your K and crush up what you got in front of you nice and fine. Cut up a straw (or roll up a small piece of paper or something) to snort the line(s) with. I find it easier to break the ~150mg pile you have into 3-5 lines and snort a line in each nostril (closing whatever nostril isn't doing the snorting with your free hand) and then lightly pinch your nose shut not all the way but enough that you can snort the powder back further if you feel it's going to fall out your nose.

Wait a couple minutes and finish up what you have in front of you. Then lay back and get cozy and enjoy.

Cheers!
