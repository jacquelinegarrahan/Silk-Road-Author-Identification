Title: Re: A question about DMT and mental health...
Post by: Snoopish on September 11, 2012, 10:43 pm
As the others have said, you are the best authority in knowing if your mind can handle these manner of drugs. I and my family have some various mental issues but I have been able to enjoy several psychedelics without repercussions (long-term, who knows?). If it's something you really want to experience, I'd suggest start with DMT (it's what I started with). The chemical is naturally occurring in the human body and your body knows how to handle it and it processes quickly (details I'm sure you've already read about).

Drugs as a whole, but psychedelics I believe most of all, will yield a certain amount of their power if you have enough force of will. I've experimented with lots of drugs with minimal withdrawal/unwanted side effects by respecting the drugs and starting them in moderation and keeping a positive expectancy/mindset for the event.

I first took DMT probably about 30mg or so (in a non-optimal smoking fashion). Last time, I vaped ~92mg and had a blast. Start slow and gradually work your way to something you are comfortable with. And be self-aware enough to know when you have to draw the line.

And if psychedelics don't work out for you, there's plenty of drugs out there.


Cheers and be safe!

Title: Re: A question about DMT and mental health...
Post by: echo_ on September 12, 2012, 12:03 am
walter is out of Denmark, if you can get a vendor in your country that is typically better. He is of course perhaps the original DMT vendor on this site, and is still going.

The quality of DMT on Silk Road is in general a quality that you may not be likely to match yourself in an extraction, so there's nothing to worry about as far as that goes. If you are a science type, however, I might recommend doing it yourself if you think you can do it. Flammable fumes will be created, but it is a fairly safe procedure as long as you do your research right and don't get anything in your eyes or in your throat that shouldn't be there (very bad).

DMT is a controlled experience, in the sense that you can control how much you smoke, and the trip is over within a certain amount of time. Do not however, think that the act of taking psychedelic drugs is in any way,  a "controlled experience." You're signing up for uncontrolled, and you're going to get what you want.

That said, for those who are truly independent in their interest and have a strong interest in preparation and safety, valium and xanax are excellent trip aids that temporarily create a calm, cool, sober state, in the middle of what can be a hard trip.
