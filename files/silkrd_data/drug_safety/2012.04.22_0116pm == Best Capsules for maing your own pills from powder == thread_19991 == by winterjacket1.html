Title: Re: Best Capsules for maing your own pills from powder
Post by: andyki on April 22, 2012, 01:38 pm
I use #3 coloured capsules and put about 120mg MDMA in each and give to friends.

Size 00 are too big in my opinion... Size 2 might be a better choice if you're going to put more than one type of active ingredient in, maybe even size 1 if you want to make supercaps.

People don't like the idea of giant caps with a little bit of powder... a small cap that is filled looks nicer. At least that is what people tell me.

This is what i do to make them:

1. Take your active ingredient and crush it into a uniform size with a mortar and pestle (stainless steel one works well)
2. Measure out the quantity of active ingredient you want using some high quality scales
3. Make a little shovel using a piece of straw that has been cut at an angle
4. Scoop up your active ingredient so that it fills the little shovel you just made - measure where the product gets to in the straw and mark it with a sharpie. Tip out the product.
5. Hot crimp it just where you marked - so that each scoop will be about the right dose- using the tip of pliers heated very quickly on a gas stove
6. Fill one capsule and seal it. Weigh the resulting capsule on your scales and write that down.
7. Continue filling all your caps, weigh them and check that they're not too much over/under and remake your little shovel if necessary.
8. Eat a freshly made cap for quality assurance ;)
