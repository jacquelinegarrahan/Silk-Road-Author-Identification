I took it with morphine, and hydrocodone. I think it was about 60mg of oral morphine and 20mg of oral hydrocodone, keep in mind that was a low dose for me. I think if your trying to do a combo to get a high with parts of both do oxy first while your still buzzing on the methylone than hydro later on just because its a little more sedating that oxy. Make sure you keep your doing in a safe range because you will be able to take more of both without feeling side effects so its easier to overdose. Personally if i were to do it i would dose 170-200mg methylone with about 15-20mg of oxy together, than 20mg of hydro right after the peak but if you dont have an opiate tolerance cut the dose down by 25-50%.
