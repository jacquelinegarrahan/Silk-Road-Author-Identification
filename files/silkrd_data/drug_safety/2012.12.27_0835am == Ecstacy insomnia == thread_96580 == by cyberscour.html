Hehe... I think I mentioned in another thread that based on the comeup you had I really wasn't sure that what you took was just MDMA. Meth is a pretty common addition to MDMA if you didn't test the drug before you took it and it might have been something all together different too like methalone as well which some people find a bit more stimulating.

Most of the time I can sleep pretty good after a nice clean MDMA roll. Not always, but most of the time. I normally take a couple of Tylenol PM and a few melatonin about an hour before I want to crash. With that said, once you eventually do get to sleep you are likely to have some fun there too. Often after MDMA I get the most insanely lucid dreams ever. I'll be in another full and complete reality for an hour or two, wake up, remember all of it, roll over, and quickly move back into a similar head space. For me, the post MDMA dreams are always interesting.

The good news is, if you just lie down in bed, stop thinking for a while, you'll get some rest eventually most of the time. On roll nights I normally take 4 hours or so of sleep after, get up, do my day and then plan a good long nap for the next afternoon. If you have a boy / girl friend you roll with I find it easier to go to sleep for my 4 hours if they are snuggled up next to me but that's likely just me.

Good luck!
