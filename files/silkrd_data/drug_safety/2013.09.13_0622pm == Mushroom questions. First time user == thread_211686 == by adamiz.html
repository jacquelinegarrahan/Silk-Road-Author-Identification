2.5g is a good place to start off.  Since you already have experience with some psychedelics.  If you weren't even 1.5-2g is enough for the average beginner.

I don't have experience with DMT so can't compare or help you with that. 

Honestly I don't know how to explain the differences very well, maybe someone else can chime in.  Mushrooms definitely cause more body load and you feel way more like jello.  A lot of the time you feel like you are melting in to your seat or bed.

Eat it all, stems and caps.  No point in only eating caps.  Yes, caps are more concentrated but there's still quite a bit in the stems.

Chew it as best you can and swallow it down with some orange juice.  Really chew it well.

You can definitely eat a light meal and wait 2 hours before consuming your mushrooms.
