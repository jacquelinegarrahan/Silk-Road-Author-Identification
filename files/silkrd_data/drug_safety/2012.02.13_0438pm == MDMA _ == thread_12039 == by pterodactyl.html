Title: Re: MDMA ?
Post by: wowzers on February 13, 2012, 07:49 pm
Just dirty polymeric crap clinging to the crystals from where the chemist didn't clean his product. MDMA comes in all colours but if it's truly pure it will be blindingly white or absolutely clear. The colour has absolutely nothing to do with the effects, how clean it feels etc though. Because the impurities are often very highly coloured, it only takes a fraction of a percent to discolour the product. On SR there's some filthy looking MDMA being sold which is actually pretty pure.

Last year I had some MDMA with little dark purple chunks in it, the dealer told me some bullshit about how it had been smuggled over in wine bottles. Dealers always have to spin a tale to justify selling impure product.
