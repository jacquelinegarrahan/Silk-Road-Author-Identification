Quote
just wondering what you guys normally do in this situation and if you have any ritual or thoughts you like to do before a session.
You're in luck because people have been asking the same question for hundreds of years and many cultures have developed their own rituals. I'm not a superstitious or religious person, so I don't have any traditions in terms of burning special incense to ward off spirits, or smudging the area with certain materials to welcome positive energy. It's hard for me to believe that these things have any effect beyond placebo, so when I smoked, I usually:

1) had about an hour of exercise, followed by a shower and a meal
2) an hour later, I dimmed the lights (a completely different experience from them on)
3) prepared my custom smoking device and all materials
4) practiced breathing techniques and meditation for about half an hour (or until I felt I was ready)
5) if I was feeling anxious still, I'd load and smoke 10mg. Then I'd load 20mg. Then 30... etc until I hit around 50. Since I always had plenty of DMT, conserving material was not as big a deal as a comfortable experience. if I wasn't anxious, I'd go for 50 from the start.
