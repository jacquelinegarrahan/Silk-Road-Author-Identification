Title: Re: Could I have damaged my LSD??
Post by: Vanquish on July 31, 2013, 05:26 pm
I keep mine foil sealed wrapped inside a plastic bag inside a mason jar in the refrigerator.
Light, air, and heat are the main degradation factors.
Depending on how well you have kept those away from the blotter would give you your answer.
I'd say you are more than likely fine - June wasn't that long ago.

Enjoy your trip.
