Title: Re: MDMA / Molly Capping
Post by: Crooked on April 23, 2012, 03:16 am
it's just a bitch dude. best way ive found is making a 2 line assembly line.

You need an area where the two man team is working. We used this big glass mirror that I have and set it up on my kitchen table. One person is working with pouring molly into capsules while the other is weighing out the powder. We used two notecard pieces of paper creased in half. we made sure the two notecards weighed exactly the same, that way you don't have to keep hitting tare between each weigh. So u scoop into the pile with the first notecard, and get the 100mg on there. Then, you hand that carefully to your partner, who begins putting it into the capsule. As soon as you hand it off, you grab the other notecard and begin to weigh out the next 100mg.

The glass mirror was great cause if there were any spills in handing off or scooping with the notecards, excess powder just falls on the glass and can be pushed back into the main pile. Whoever is doing the capping should always knock loose molly back onto the glass since their job is going to be slightly faster.

There's another thread on this that I've seen. It's pretty long and idk if anyone has listed a better method there but I'd search for it.

Post by: mrmdma on April 23, 2012, 10:40 am
My personal way of doing this is really simple but relatively effective.
Take some printer paper and cut a piece of it and fold it into a funnel and tape is so it won't open up. Make the funnel so that it fits the gel cap half tightly.

Then get your scale ready and weigh your "goodies" on top of a piece of paper.

Pour desired amount through the funnel into the cap.

iherb.com is really good for getting cheap empty gel caps. Try to avoid vegetarian ones, as they dissolve veeeeery slow in your stomach resulting in slower MDMA release. I always go with animal gelatin caps.
But yea, just type gelcaps in the search in iherb and select "sort by" "cheapest first" or something
500x 0-size caps are like 9 dollars... I recommend ordering some heavy duty b-vitamin complex tabs also along with 5-htp. Now Foods is a good brand and also cheap.

Title: Re: MDMA / Molly Capping
Post by: snipeemfl0 on April 26, 2012, 02:40 am
I would use a device to hold one half of the cap upside down so the opening is on the top. Then, weigh your mg amount on the scale. From the scale, pour the powder onto a piece of crisp printer paper folded in half (baking the paper at low temps, 200*F, can dry the paper making it less susceptible to the power sticking). Then use both hands, lightly tap the paper as the powder flows into the cap, then remove the cap and add the other half.


Pretty tedious, but can work when doing relatively small amounts <50 or so.
