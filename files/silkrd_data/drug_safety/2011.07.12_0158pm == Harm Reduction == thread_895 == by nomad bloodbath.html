Harm reduction...." Never take anymore than you can handle and always know your dealer"- Mr.Show w/ Bob and Dave. Done deal. If you are experimenting, go small. Learn what you are doing and how it effects you. Go small a few times to make sure you understand the effects on you over time. Then after a few times, gradually increase dosage until you reach the desired effect, if you haven't reached it already. There is no need to go "all in" on time one, two, or three. Go big or go home doesn't apply here! Everyones level of tolerance differs. Remember that. And when someone dies (people die everyday), it's not SR's fault. They would have found "it" somewhere or done something worse. They were already on a self destructive coarse. Even the casual user of "it "has to be careful. Think about what you are doing. Just b/c it was great last time doesn't mean much this time. It's been a while. Hence the term casual user. It may effect you differently now. Good or bad. Be careful and think!!
