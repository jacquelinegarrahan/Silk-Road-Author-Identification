Post by: TK1991 on November 18, 2012, 05:37 pm
Water over 80 degrees will kill psilocin so I whip out my handy dandy candy thermometer and set it in the cup I'm about to use. I too use whole homegrown mushies but as someone already explained take a coffee filter and put the mushies in the middle. I wouldn't sift it through, rather just fold up the coffee filter like a christmas present and staple in the center so as not to lose any powder. I boil my water, place in about a teaspoon of a herbal tisane (any herbal tea containing no caffeine is prime, my stomach gets upset from mushies so I stay away from the green teas and what have you) and stir. From 100 degrees down to 80 will take about 5 min, just long enough to steep your "non-psychedelic" tea bag. Looking at your handy dandy thermometer you can toss in your mush. Like I said I always use whole mushrooms so I ATTACK them with a spoon (brutally) till I am sure I have pressed and poked as much as possible. Stirring regularly (because it's in a "teabag") the brew will be ready in about 10 minutes. You will metabolize the psilocin much faster in this liquid form and in turn will have a shorter come up and a shorter high. With a group of people we usually use more mushrooms and this same method to make a pot of tea, then just dole out shots in little tea cups , and enjoy!
Note: I have done salvia, dmt, over 500 ug of lsd at once, but mushrooms have still fucked me up MORE than anything else I have ever done so be cautious when dosing, specially because it's much easier to over do it with shots of tea.
