Title: Re: viagra/coke ?
Post by: junjo on September 25, 2013, 08:23 pm
Viagra is a vasodilator, coke is a vasoconstrictor. Although it's not exactly healthy, the combo isn't gonna kill you. Dangers tend to arise when you have a combo of vasoconstrictors or a combo of vasodilators that cause a sharp spike or drop in blood pressure, e.g. combing viagra and isobutyl nitrite (which has claimed many lives).

As long as you don't make the viagra and coke combo a habit, your health shouldn't be that worse off.
