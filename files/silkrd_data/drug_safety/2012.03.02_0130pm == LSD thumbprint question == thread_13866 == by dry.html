a thumbprint is nothing compared to eating tabs.

1gram of crystal = 10,000 tabs (very good tabs)
Lets say you stuck your finger into .1 grams, 1,000 tabs worth

eating 1,000 tabs would be LESS potent and LESS intense than eating the .1 grams of crystal as is. Not to say 1,000 tabs would be weak by any means. Just explaining to you how crystal is more potent than the tabs will ever be.


So you can eat as many tabs as you want but your never gonna thumbprint unless you actually have crystal in your presence.

Also, Liquid LSD is really no diffrent than eating tabs. I'm not sure why you put crysal lsd and liquid lsd in the same category. You drop the liquid  onto the tabs.
