Post by: oldcactushand on May 31, 2013, 02:34 pm
I have only seen Enter the Void the one time, but I think it would make me feel a bit uncomfortable while tripping! Fantastic movie though, and I'd choose it over Irreversible if I had to watch one of them on drugs, that's for sure!

I recently enjoyed Waking Life, The Fountain and Up all under the influence. Adventure Time is definitely one of my favourite shows to watch, as well as Off The Air which you can find on youtube.

If you're doing it with a few friends, and are stuck indoors... I like to make the room completely different to what everyone is used to, to turn it into another place. One way of doing this is to blow up 150-200 balloons - as many as possible really. What you do with them depends on the room. One idea is to create a sort of balloon pen by pushing sofas together. You can then wade through/lie in hundreds of balloons. Kick one balloon, watch them all shoot up in the air and float. Depending on your access to cheap and plentiful balloons, you can do this for under $10. Turn your living room into a nonsensical parade.

Ideally though, get yourself tripping in a beautiful and isolated place. I really like tripping out on large hills that can see endlessly over the land.
