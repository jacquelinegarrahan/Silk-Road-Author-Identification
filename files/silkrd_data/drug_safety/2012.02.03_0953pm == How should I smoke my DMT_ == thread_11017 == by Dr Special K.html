Title: Re: How should I smoke my DMT?
Post by: Tagore on February 03, 2012, 10:22 pm
I would suggest for a first time smoker:

Use a normal pipe (which you might use for MJ), fill about 1/2 bowl with some inert herb: Parsley, or shake or something similar, or better yet some kind of nice herbal blend which tickles you right...

Sprinkle the required amount of Deem powder lightly over this nice bed you just made.

Use a match (!) not a lighter, and hold it as far above the bowl as possible... Lower it slowly while inhaling and stop when it gets close enough to melt the DMT without burning it!!! This is important, as you want to *vaporize*, NOT burn the DMT. This is the number one reason IMO that people fail to 'break though' even after many tries. If your herbs are burning, it's too hot!

Inhale slowly and deeply, for as long as possible. Hold it. Hold it...

Keep holding it.

Now, do it again.

Three times... Each time, inhaling as long and slowly and evenly as possible!!!

That's it. You're gone... or, HERE!


** Bonus Tips **
-Don't use your 'everyday' pipe... People have been known to load a bowl the next day or weeks later and get some very unexpected bonus.
-Enlist a close friend to help. When you succeed you will be very happy someone was there to grab the pipe from your hand...
-Light a candle to light your matches from, and put a glass of water next to where you are sitting to drop the match into after. Less to worry about.

Title: Re: How should I smoke my DMT?
Post by: gharyeee on February 04, 2012, 12:08 am
Crack pipe type pieces will work, I've used one called the Ubie. But with larger doses I find that the melted DMT can be somewhat messy. I'd recommend a bong/bubbler, but a regular pipe will work too. It'll help to put multiple screens in the bowl to help stop the melted DMT from falling through into the piece. I'll usually leave a bit of ash on top of the screens and then lay the DMT directly on top. Laying it on weed always seems to alter the trip for me a bit, but it's a bit easier to smoke that way and the trips tend to be more strange.

Tagore left some great tips though and highlighted the most important. Keep the lighter a couple inches away and instead of letting the flame burn the DMT, let the heat vaporize the DMT.

I also find it helpful to slow my breathing before hitting it, but make sure that once you inhale you hold it for 15+ seconds. I count to 15, then keep holding it in longer w/out counting, then exhale. I usually feel an electric tingling sensation rising up from the bottom of my body to the tip of my head as I hold it in longer and longer.

Crack Pipes easily burn the DMT lowering the amount you actually get into your body

Glass pipes or Glass pieces in general are not advised - DMT sticks to glass once liquified and it is difficult to actually get the full dosage you are expecting due to this




You want a wooden pipe.  Any type will do, i suggest just a functional cheap one I paid about 7 bucks for my wooden D pipe and its very trippy/colorful and does the job fine.  Now you can either do it one or two ways:

1.  Put a metal screen down.  Take a cigarette or similar tobacco product and smoke/burn it, put the ash in the bowl on top of the screen(about half a cigarette's worth of ash).  Now take your finger and pat the ash down into a little "pillar" shape; this is where the DMT will go, on top of this pillar.  DO NOT PACK THE ASH DOWN ALL THE WAY, IT WILL CLOG THE PIPE.  Just enough to make a pillar down inside the bowl.  Take the D and put it on the pillar, take your lighter and when you hit D you never want the flame to touch it; you want to vaporize the DMT.  Take your time with it and dont rush things and you will get the correct dosage you planned out.

2.  Instead of using ash, you can use a bunch of metal screens all on top of each other, and then use the same smoking method as above.  Just load the D in the middle of the screens and blast off.  I do not advise marijuana to be used at any point near or during the trip - it tends to cloud the mind slightly.

Another great way, my personal favorite actually is by Volcano Vaporizer.  You just need to do a quick google search and find the right temperature setting so it doesnt destroy the deemster.

Have fun.
