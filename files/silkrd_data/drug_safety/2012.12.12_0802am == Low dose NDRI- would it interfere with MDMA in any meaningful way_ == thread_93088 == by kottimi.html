Title: Re: Low dose NDRI- would it interfere with MDMA in any meaningful way?
Post by: poolsclosed on December 13, 2012, 01:14 am
Buproprion + MDMA = seizure. I dunno about pyrovalerone, but I would assume any NDRI would have the same risks as buproprion.

MDMA does not mix with most pharmaceutical antidepressants because it is a triple releasing agent. Any releasing agent, when mixed with a reuptake inhibitor, is likely to cause flooding of neurotransmitters... which is risky. High doses of MDMA alone can cause serotonin syndrome because MDMA is not only a SRA but also a SRI. Wouldn't you know!

edit - I may actually be wrong about MDMA acting as a serotonin reuptake inhibitor, so go ahead and correct me if I am. I know it's a releasing agent and an agonist at the very least...
