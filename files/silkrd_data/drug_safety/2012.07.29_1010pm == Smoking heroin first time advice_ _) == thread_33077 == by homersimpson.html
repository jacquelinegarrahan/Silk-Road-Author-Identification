Title: Re: Smoking heroin first time advice? :)
Post by: perdu on July 29, 2012, 10:33 pm
Hey Homer, don't reckon a crack pipe is your best option. Foil is the best way to chase. The liquid h could run all over the place, even into your mouth if it's really cut and the pipe would be black with residue really quick. Just get some decent tin foil, give the dull side a good cook with the lighter to burn off the crap and put the point of a knife's worth on. You'll have to make a sort of a trumpet shape of foil to inhale the smoke through or you can use the cardboard tube of a toilet roll. Don't use a straw, pen case or anything narrow like that, you'll lose all your smoke. Get one of your pals to either hold the foil (with the gear on) while you chase the h with the lighter (on the underside of the foil) or he can do the lighter and you hold the foil. The flame on the underside of the foil will liquidise the H and it will run along the foil. You then inhale the vapour as it rises. Anyway, you won't od but don't be doing it if you've been heavily drinking or taking benzos or stuff like that. You may well puke, this is fairly standard with opiates so have a bucket handy. Check out Heroin Helper's site http://www.heroinhelper.com/         He's got loads of info. It's kind of trial and error unfortunately to start off with. The main trouble starting is trying to actually catch the fucking smoke  :) Come downs off smack aren't like other drugs. You'll just go for a night's sleep and maybe have a slight headache the next day. Or a puke. Smack and puke are old pals  :) Don't bother with any of that stuff except maybe a joint or 2 but personally I wouldn't even want that. The rest, forget it. Just relax and enjoy it. To be honest, the first toot can be underwhelming because it's best to have someone with you to show you the ropes. Heroin helper has quite a good solo method on his site but your pals and you will work it out.
have a nice one, Homer!!!!!
