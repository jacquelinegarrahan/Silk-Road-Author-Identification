Okay, so quite a bit ago (I'd say 3 weeks to a month ago) I dropped acid for the first time. The trip itself went fine, I was very euphoric and occupied for the vast majority of the trip but got very anxious near the end because I did it alone and my mother had awoken. The effects also lasted a lot longer than I thought they would (dropped at 11 pm, came up at midnight, was awake until 2 PM next day), so that added to my anxiety and my overall level of discomfort. Fast forward a bit and there are no side effects that I could notice. Prior to dropping, I was having a bout of brain fog on and off in which my thoughts were hard to discern, which seemed to get worse afterwards (but could have been mere coincidence). Then, a bit later, I had a bout of what seemed like depersonalization/dissociation. I felt like myself, but I didn't really have an active thought process - everything seemed subconscious, so to speak - and would often speak without really thinking of what I would say. From what I'd read, it seemed like a minor episode of DPD, but it seems highly unlikely that DPD would be caused by one pleasant acid trip (or any, for the matter). I decided it'd be best to ignore it unless it got worse/persisted, and time seemed to make it better. Fast forward to now - nearly a month after the trip - and while the effects have gotten better, I wouldn't say they've totally subsided. Additionally, before I go to sleep I seem to get a bit anxious for no reason, and as of last night, I'm seeing something very weird visually.

Last night, I went to the bathroom before bed in the dark and looked at the wall. I saw a kaleidoscope like thing (similar to something I'd see while tripping, but not something I actually saw) which has persisted since. When I look at anything, when I go to sleep, close my eyes even, I constantly see this. It's a red circle in the center with a bunch of colors around it, it's in the center of my vision and the only way to describe it is kaleidoscope-esque, but even that isn't totally accurate. If I focus on something - i.e, writing, playing a game, etc. - it's not really noticeable and I only notice it if I stare at something intently. It doesn't seem to manifest as well, or at all, if it's a very very bright surface (i.e, this text box I don't really see it in) and if I close one eye it seems to be worse in my left eye.

When I woke up today, it seemed gone, but I went to the bathroom in the dark again (different bathroom, but same style of wall/same house) and it reappeared. Could acid have anything at all to do with this? I'm inclined to say no but have no idea what could be causing the problem. I'm otherwise healthy, no mental illness, nothing I know of that runs in the family and nothing that has happened to cause any of the things I've described. Nothing I've read about describes really my symptoms, so I'm getting a bit worried.

Does anyone have any idea what could be causing it, if acid (or any RC) could cause these effects near a month later, and any idea where I could go for help? It seems really benign, but it's hard not to get worked up about and dealing with it while trying to sleep is not a pleasant experience.

Any thoughts? In reality it's fairly benign, as I said, but it's really disconcerting. I didn't expect anything this long-lasting with LSD or psychedelics, I have no history of genetic mental illnesses like schizophrenia in my family that I know of, and I'm generally a very well grounded person. Anything to help/lower my anxiety would really be appreciated.
