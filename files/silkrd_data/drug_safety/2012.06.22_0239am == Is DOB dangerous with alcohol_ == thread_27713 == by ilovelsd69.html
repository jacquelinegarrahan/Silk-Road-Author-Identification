Title: Re: Is DOB dangerous with alcohol?
Post by: tordemon on June 22, 2012, 04:26 pm
With most phenethylamines in my experience alcohol extremely extends the duration. A 2ci trip that would normally last, say, 6 hours or so, went on for days (an effect somewhat similar to tomso-ing). Since DOB is already rather likely to last 18+ hours, I don't think that you're going to need something extending the trip at all. If you took it with anything, I would suggest a benzodiazepine (depending on the dosage) because DOB is rather speedy and will definitely interfere with sleep.

Title: Re: Is DOB dangerous with alcohol?
Post by: tordemon on June 22, 2012, 07:34 pm
You're welcome!

Just a little more caution with the DOB: I took the 2.6mg blotters (presumably the same ones you're planning on taking) with two friends. Two of us handled it very well and enjoyed the shit out of it, but the third saw true psychotomimetic effects. He continuously talked to people who weren't present and complained about things that "aren't okay," though we couldn't get him to explain any more. We eventually gave him Lorazepam when it was clear that he couldn't break out of it. The friend who didn't freak out also drank with it, and he was still getting CEVs two days later.

I'd just recommend that you make sure you're prepared for it (as it is kind of a high dose according to erowid, though not in a dangerous zone), and if you've had troubles with other trips in the past, I might suggest you cut the tab in half or something.
