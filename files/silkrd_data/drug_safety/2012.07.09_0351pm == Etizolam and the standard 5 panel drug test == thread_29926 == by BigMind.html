Title: Re: Etizolam and the standard 5 panel drug test
Post by: om on July 12, 2012, 05:29 am
That's a really good question.

Logically I don't think you'll have a problem. If you're talking about the piss test you shouldn't have an issue.

The benzo panel will be testing either for unmetabolised benzos (ie that pass straight through) or for a specific metabolite, a chemical that the body produces that has a chemical reaction in the test to show up "positive" if you've used them.

But etizolam is not a benzo, it is a benzo analog (check the wikipedia page).

According to wiki etizolam has a very fast metabolism, 6 hour half life - I reckon as long as it's been 3 days you'll have no worries at all.

If the test is for pre-employment screening and if you're really concerned about it just tell them your mum gave you a valium because you were so nervous about getting an awesome job :)
