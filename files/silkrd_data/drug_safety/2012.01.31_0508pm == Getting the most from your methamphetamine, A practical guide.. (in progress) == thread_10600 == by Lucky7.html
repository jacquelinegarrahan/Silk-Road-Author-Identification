*In my step by step guides I am not going to make additional steps for stupid stuff like "buy meth", so this guide will assume you already have high grade meth, and maybe some common household items and most important, common sense.

So lets begin.

Someone was asking in another thread about how to properly do a hot rail, so I am going to start with that... Hot railing meth combines both smoking and snorting into one very enjoyable practice. *Note- you will need a small mirror{best} or metal surface)

1- Go to your local gas station and buy a glass stem (used to make a crack pipe, will be sold wherever they have meth pipes, ask for a glass rose) the thicker the glass the better your experience will be.

2- Take your meth and crush/chop as finely as possible, you want straight powder for this, no pieces left or your gonna be in pain. After you have your powder, place a normal size line on your mirror, or other heat resistant surface, you don't want it all bunched together but you don't want it exceptionally long either.

3- Using a quality butane torch lighter or better, a propane torch, heat one end of your glass stem, the bottom 1/4th of it will do. You need to really heat it up good, at the least 45 seconds, but I always go for a minute. Now if your using a propane torch with a large flame then you will need to heat for less time. Make sure to get the entire end by moving the flame around evenly, you'll know its done when it starts to glow red.

4- Immediately after removing the flame (if using a propane torch, take the extra second to turn off your torch before proceeding!) and quickly, bring your nose to the top of the stem, or as close as you can get ( a thin crappy one will transfer heat and may be too hot to put directly in your nose, but a thick glass one will be fine, and works the best). Using the stem as a straw, or 'tooter' as some say, bring the super hot end to the beginning of the line, holding it at an angle,  take a deep sniff, finishing your line in one motion.

And that is a hot rail! Exhale, if that line comes out as a cloud of vapor then congrats, you did it properly and are now high as fuck! :-)

If you sniff dry meth regularly, you will most likely like the feeling in your nose, I do, but if not it will burn. Hey, no pain no gain!

That is why its important to make sure its hot enough, the meth will vaporize instantly when passing through the heated part of the stem, if its hot but just barely not enough, you could possibly end up sniffing up molten meth that hasn't completely turned to vapor yet, which will suck. Rule of thumb, its better to heat too long than not long enough.

The best upgrade for this is a decent size (4in or so) pyrex tube, the pyrex will hold the heat MUCH better, ensuring that it is hot enough to vaporize lines on the sniff, plus it doesn't transfer heat up the tube so theres no worry about burning your nose or finger if your in a hurry or are real fucked up.

The hot rail works the best for people with a tolerance that require larger amounts, amounts you couldn't smoke out of a ball pipe or off foil in a single hit.
