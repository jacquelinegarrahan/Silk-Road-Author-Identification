I've messed with insufflated 25i a lot. I once bought over 40 of Tyl3r's 25i strawberry snuff caps about a year ago. Each contains 600µg of 25i mixed evenly into a "snuff", a kinda carrier powder. Snorting these is extreme stuff and you have to have lots of experience with psychedelics! One 600µg cap produces effects about 2-3 times as strong as my only experience with LSD at 150µg. So one cap is about equivalent with three tabs of LSD.

It was epic and I've never hallucinated that hard :D
Don't do that though. Buy a handful of caps from Tyl3r and start with half a cap.
