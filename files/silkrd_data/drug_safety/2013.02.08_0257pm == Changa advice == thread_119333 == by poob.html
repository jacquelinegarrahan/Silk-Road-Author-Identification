Finally got around to this over the weekend!

Really was a pleasant trip. Over the course of about 4 trips we did about half the bag; which weighed in at about 0.7g full

We managed to pick up a Vapor-Genie to hit it with. Quite an acquired taste, the changa! I didn't mind it, however the other half had to mask it with some weed accompanying it in the bowl.

I know they weren't exactly heroic doses by any means, but we thought it safe to take it easy as it was our first time.

But sweet-mother-almighty regardless of the above we did fairly blastoff!

It was as if someone just came along, gave you this big, warm, loving hug and lifted you into the naphta. I was always conscious of the fact I was tripping balls, which I'll assume was down to dosage, and as I said earlier was fine. I was looking at the coffee table during one trip and the wood stains on it coalesced to form this belly-dancing girl, moving in time to the music, It's funny, I can now, looking at the table, see the outline that triggered it. The OEVs overall are really quite animated, sparkly, but not intense. Music wasn't affected, it was just a welcome accompaniment as we drove down Moonview Highway :)

The CEVs were incredibly detailed, mostly geometric patterns and fractals. At one stage I was watching these patterns move into a black hole, I was underneath it with a view of the funnel below. Time was never really knocked off any sort of linear path throughout. The trips lasted about 5-7 minutes each. It was funny as the both of us would come to at about the same time. We weren't talking to each other while tripping, just enjoying the ride.

My other half said at one stage the place we live in transformed back to how they imagined it was in the 60's, complete with furniture, decor etc!

I think next time we'll definitely hit a little more, was nice to test the water. I think we nailed the dosage for our first time. It seems like a love you could lose if you don't give it its due respect. Right now I'm debating rolling a joint with just a wee sprinkle of it therein. Could be good for a giggle!

Overall, really delighted with how it went.  Thanks for all the advice. You guys really are the balls.  ;D
