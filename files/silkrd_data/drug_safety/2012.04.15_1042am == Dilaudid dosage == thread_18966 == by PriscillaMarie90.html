Even when I had a habit, a 4mg dillie shot made me feel pretty good for a short while, and I could tolerate 8mgs without incident. My tolerance at the time was moderate- less then some, more than others, even though I used daily for years I always tried to keep it low.

Since rushes are associated with IV use (and smoking, I suppose), there is no need for you to worry about doing ENOUGH at one time.  Snorting or eating dillies won't give you a rush, just an opiate high, and they have pretty low oral bio-availability. I don't think it's much higher insufflated.  4mg oral is definitely not even close to 4mgs IVd.

Just remember that you can always do more if you want, but you can't put the toothpaste back in the tube once you've done too much.  Rail 2mgs, wait 20 minutes, and see how you feel.  Wanna get a little higher?  Do a little more.

I won't preach about not starting on the needle- it's just another route of administration as long as you're safe about it.  Just know that for most people who start down that path, it soon becomes the preferred method of getting chemicals into one's body.  To be honest, dillies kind of suck unless they're IVd, and even then there are other things I'd much rather shoot.  I much prefer oxycodone or hydrocodone over hydromorphone every time.

I think that for someone of your size, with little tolerance to opies, 2-4 mgs oral, or insufflated nasally, would be a good starting point.
