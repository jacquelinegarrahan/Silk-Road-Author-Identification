Title: Re: Oxycodone help
Post by: chakrakhan87 on March 25, 2012, 05:34 am
I'm a lightweight who really enjoys opioids.  I weigh 125 pounds, so it doesn't take much for me to feel stuff.

Back in the day, a single 10MG Vicodin tablet (that's hydrocodone) would knock me on my ass, but I was only taking one tablet every 5 months or so.  I started taking them more frequently, and now I find that a 10MG tablet  doesn't pack the wallop that it used to.. but I don't really like to feel walloped, so I'm usually  pretty happy with a 7.5.  I feel happy, fuzzy, and good... but I can still function.  My beau and I went to see some live acoustic bands a few weeks ago and took 7.5 each, and had a really lovely time.

10MG of Percoset (that's *oxycodone*) will still knock me on my ass.  I've taken 15MG of oxycodone once and it was very lovely as long as I was laying down.  My beau and I just laid around touching each others' faces because it just felt so nice.  Having my head and back scratched was also really nice.

These days I probably take 7.5 MG of either hydrocodone or oxycodone (whatever's around) 2-3 times per week... nothing huge... and I don't do it every week; maybe every other week.  I just take it orally, just like it's prescribed. 

I definitely notice I've built a tolerance.  My high, happy, fuzzy funtimes only last about 90 minutes now that I'm using more frequently... back when I was only using every 4-5 months it would last hours.

My advice is: Start slow!  Be someplace where you don't have to move too much.  Comfy couch and a blanket is simple, and can be pretty wonderful.
