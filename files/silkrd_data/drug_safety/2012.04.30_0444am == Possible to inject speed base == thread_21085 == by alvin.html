It's not so much a 'wash', but you'll need to convert it to a 'salt' in order for your body to be able to absorb it properly.

Look at any medications and the active ingredient will be xxx (HCL) (this stands for hydrochloride). The base of the active ingredient was mixed with hydrochloric acid to make a water soluble salt. Any drugs that are xyz 'citrate' were mixed with citric acid.

I would recommend for an easy solution, you just mix your gear with some lemon juice to make amphetamine citrate, which will be soluble in water and absorbable by your body. I also recommend (if you can) getting your hands on some wheel filters to filter your gear before you shoot it (you don't want a stroke from any lumpy bits).
