Title: Re: Hallucinogen Persisting Perception Disorder
Post by: fordingtheharrison on June 08, 2013, 01:39 am
A lot of HPPD is self-diagnosed, which is about as solid as self-diagnosed autism. Most people just read about it online and then attribute common and normal visual idiosyncrasies to the drugs they've taken. Yours actually sounds pretty legitimate from your description, however. HPPD should be pretty noticeable and probably not something you have to convince yourself of.
