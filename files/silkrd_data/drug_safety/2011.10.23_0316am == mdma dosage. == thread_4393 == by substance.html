Title: Re: mdma dosage.
Post by: triangle_list on October 25, 2011, 07:03 am
80 is what I would consider the minimal active dose to do anything at all, but it won't be much. 100 will be definitely active and 120 is generally considered the typical dose for full efficacy. But that's 99% pure MDMA, I would add 10% right on the top. If you are not very experienced I wouldn't take more than 130 at first, but since I am I would start at 150 on a new batch and might take 180 or 200 if I know it's weak. I have heard these dosages are usually pretty consistent across body weight but nothing scientific to back it up. :P

You can always take more. I always do. But not less. There is one caveat to keep in mind. Sometimes in my, and others, experience, and it doesn't even necessarily happen for any particular reason, you may feel absolutely nothing for several hours despite taking the proper dose of good stuff... then suddenly it just all comes on at once.  The body is weird. So you don't want to go taking half a gram just because nothing happened for 2-3 hours. This is probably more likely to happen if you have eaten any food whatsoever in the previous 4 hours so I try not to.
