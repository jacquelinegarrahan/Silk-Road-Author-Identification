Toss and Wash - Directly putting the fine kratom powder into your mouth, and then chugging it down with juice or your beverage of choice. (Avoid Carbonated Beverages)
                            This method takes practice, and an iron stomach.  It can also be extremely messy and lead to sneezing, vomiting, and choking if you're not careful.
                            To be honest, for extracts this is probably the preferred method.  Or for someone that isn't taking large dosages.


Shake and Chug - Essentially a reverse toss and wash.  Take the powder and throw it into your beverage of choice. (Avoid Carbonated Beverages)
                             Then you shake or stir it, and quickly gulp it down.  Orange Juice, Grapefruit Juice, or any other type of fruit juice is always a solid choice for this method.


Kratom Tea - This method involves quite a bit of effort, and it's a tad bit messy - but you can make some very potent tea.  Kratom itself is incredibly bitter, especially batches high in mitragyna.
                     Usually this method involves bringing water to a very light boil and then dropping either crushed leaves or powder and letting it simmer for 15 minutes.
                     Doing this you can usually do the process twice, still extracting the alkaloids.  The third is much less and often completely unnecessary.
                     Here is a tried and true method, that I have used for many many years.  *clearnet warning* http://www.drugs-forum.com/forum/showthread.php?t=16165


Capsules - By and large the easiest, fastest, and most effective way of taking kratom.  I personally use size 00 capsules.  Place the powder on a flat surface, or a bowl with slight edges.
                  Split the capsule, and proceed to fill.  You can usually cap each one in about 5-15 seconds depending on fullness and hand strength.
                 

Closing Thoughts - I have done every method over the years, many times and by and large capsules are the only way to go.  Quick, convenient, and no choking or puking risk.
                              Toss and wash is great for extracts, which in my honest opinion are not worth the money over plain leaf.  But some people especially those with high opiate tolerance and fat wallets, partake in.
                              Kratom Tea is definitely worth trying, but mainly only if you have extremely high potency crushed leaf. 
                              99% of the time a really microfine powdered grind is by and large the best option for purchasing kratom.  Leading to quicker absorption, and being easier to quickly capsule.
                              Depending on which vendor you use, they all use slightly different grinds.  Some are gritty, some are fine talc, and last but not least course.


                              Batches of kratom vary greatly in potency.  Headshop kratom is nearly inactive, while fresh leaf from Indonesia/Borneo will knock you on your ass.
                              The best batches I've ever had were definitely strongly narcotic in nature, and almost to the potency of Oxycodone.  These are incredibly rare, and high in alkaloids.
                              Kratom like that doesn't come around that often.  But usually if you have solid vendors the quality is always consistent and in many ways I prefer kratom to hydrocodone in almost every way possible.
                             

                                                                                                                                                                       Vanquish
