Title: COCAINE: An interestingly strange effect.
Post by: This Is Serious Mum on January 17, 2013, 12:29 am

Many years ago I had developed 'shingles' on my face. Despite the excruciating agony this caused, not to mention the insipid bubbles of flesh and putrid wounds, I was left with a pronounced decreased sense of smell.

After a number of weeks, the wounds healed, the scabs fell off and I could, once again, show my face and get back into life. But one thing plagued me and that was my sense of smell...it hadn't come back.

Since those years a number of ...cough....products had been up my nose. By this time I had a small sense of smell, but nothing to write home about.

A couple of weeks ago some of Frank Williams Coke came my way. So I lined up a line, bent down....and proceeded to hoover a smallish line up my nose. I stand up, sniff up the remnants once of twice then...Bam....my sense of smell returned. Wow, how utterly amazing! So, cutting a long story short, I've been like the lead character out of the film Perfume.....discovering a World of aromas and new found sense of smell.

All of which would not have been possible if not for the Road and the lovely cola from Frank Williams!

End.
