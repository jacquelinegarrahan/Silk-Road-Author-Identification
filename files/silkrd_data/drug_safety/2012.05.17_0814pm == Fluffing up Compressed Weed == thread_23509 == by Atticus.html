Title: Re: Fluffing up Compressed Weed
Post by: Meister on May 17, 2012, 08:20 pm
I wouldn't use an orange peel, I've seen people do that before but it was usually trying to soften up bricks of regs with it. I've had compressed nugs before from being vac sealed for long periods of time and what I did was put them in tupperware with wet (obviously not dripping wet) q-tips taped to the top. The container shouldn't matter, only reason I used tupperware instead of a jar is because it's laid out more evenly and I had room to tape several q-tips.

You don't want to overdo it though or it can get moldy.

Title: Re: Fluffing up Compressed Weed
Post by: Delta11 on May 18, 2012, 02:13 am
I use orange peels all the time, it really works! The trick is you have to use a sealed container, I use a tupperware that seals in pretty good (you can use mason jars if you're not using a large amount). The trick is not letting the weed touch the orange peels or else they'll get too moist and just turn into shake. What I do is put a layer of orange peels in my tupperware and then lay a sheet of wax paper on top and then put the bud on top of the wax paper so it won't be touching the bud. Leave it there for a couple of hours and you should be good, it'll get moist, fluffy, and get that awesome tangy smell.
