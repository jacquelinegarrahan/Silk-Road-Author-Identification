Post by: l0574l0ne on August 12, 2012, 10:11 am
H will give you the most bang for your buck, but you seriously want to treat it with respect, and not just from a DARE perspective. I mean if you take too much in one go you're going to collapse, puke, and maybe choke on your own vomit. If you are opiate naive (which it sounds like) the first time using H is almost always going to make you spew. Do not take it alone to begin with. This ain't like smoking a joint and getting fuzzy for a few hours. Use minute amounts. You can always take more until you get where you want to be, but too much will mess you up and waste your drugs. So tiny little piles many times is the way to do it.

Also, the reason why H is cheap is because what your buying isn't produced medicinally. Vicodin and Oxy and all those things are hellaexpensive because they had to be stolen or bought off people with scripts, or counterfitted, whereas H just has to be made by some random dude in central asia with a big tub, a spoon and a bottle of acetic anhydride.

Personally I'd say that if your looking for fun, then H is not the place to start. Its where you end up when the other things aren't doing what you want anymore. Morphine will get you really very high, as will Hydrocodone, and with a lot less risk. Remember, every opiate just turns into morphine in your body, you are always getting high off morphine, it just depends how much of it is turned into morphine. Using H has new and alarming dangers compared to just about anything else that there is to use.

Treat it with respect. It's inhumanly addictive, and tolerance builds like lightning. Be very careful.
