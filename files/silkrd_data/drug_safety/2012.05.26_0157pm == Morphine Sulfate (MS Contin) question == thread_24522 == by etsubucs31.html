Assuming you mean 40mg time release oc swallowed? If so then you might wanna wait a bit til your tolerance grows. Or just break it in like 4 parts to sniff.

Only you know your usual usage so just use a conversion chart found on any search engine. But for HR purposes Ill explain bioavailability or b/a. Bioavail. is referred to in percentage, and will usually list different  percentages for different ROAs or routes of administration. These percentages mean xx%(the amount that will effect you) out of the entire dose (100%). The only way to get 100% is paternal (IV/"shooting up")

If you come across text that is not intended for recreational users, but for medical professionals then usually only the oral b/a will be listed.

Just remember to always always always do your research before attempting anything that your the slightest bight unsure of. The internet is your best friend.
