Title: Re: Opiates and urinating. Anyone else?
Post by: captainjojo on January 31, 2012, 08:38 am
It is actually a symptom of the opiates relaxing the muscles required to evacuate your bladder and bowels.  The more opiates in your system the harder it becomes to "push" with those muscles.  As you taper off, the effects become less, and the muscles return to normal.

Water pills can help.  If you are going to use them, be careful.  They can increase the amount of potassium your body excretes, which can be dangerous if they get too low.  Taking a supplement is advised if you are going to start taking water pills (diuretics).
