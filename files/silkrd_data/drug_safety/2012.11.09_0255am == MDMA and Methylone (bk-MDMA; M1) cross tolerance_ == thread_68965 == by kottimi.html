depending on which precursor was used to synthesize it, most MDx substances have a sweet maple syrup/black licorice smell...

this comes from using safrole which comes from sassafras which is used as a flavoring for traditional root beer hence giving it a sweet smell...

i have read a report where a person took MDA from a trusted source and he said that it smelled like rotting carrots which oddly enough is also sweet smelling like maple syrup...

my experience with methylone is limited but from what i gather, it's like MDMA without the empathic "push" or urgency...

the psychedelic effects of MDA are actually quite subtle...it's more like tricks of shadow and lighting rather than full blown OEVs...like you think you see something there but in reality it's just patterns of light and dark...

as for cross-tolerance...i always space my rolls far enough apart that it's never an issue so i wouldn't know...
