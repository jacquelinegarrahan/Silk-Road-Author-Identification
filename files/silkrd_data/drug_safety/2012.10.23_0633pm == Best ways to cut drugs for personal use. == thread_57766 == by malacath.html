Title: Best ways to cut drugs for personal use.
Post by: malacath on October 23, 2012, 06:33 pm
This is a thread where everyone can post about how they cut their drugs for personal use (if you cut them at all), I emphasize personal use because this is about cutting high purity drugs so that they are easier to measure out or use, this is not about sneaky cuts so you can sell drugs as pure when they are actually cut.

For example, I buy 90%+ pure meth, mix it with sugar, and pulverize it with a mortar and pestle so that every teaspoon of the sugar-meth mixture contains about 10mg meth, which I add to my coffee on days that I need a boost.

One of my favorite cuts is water, I usually dissolve enough meth to make a 10mg/ml solution which I store in an old (cleaned) essential oil bottle and measure out with a graduated oral syringe.

Another advantage of these methods is if I ever get searched, they wont find any drugs unless they decide to test the sugar jar next to my coffee maker or the essential oil bottle in my bathroom!

What do you do?
