Post by: 328502E on March 21, 2012, 07:32 am
Getting it in - Crotch is always popular, in your socks can be done as well.  Sometimes in your wallet.  If female, you can open a tampon, replace it with drugs (obv in a way that could pass as a tampon) and reseal it with a lighter on the opened end. 

Order of drugs - I would save stimulant use for day 3 to avoid harsh comedowns during the event.  I'd take a break day 2, but I realize most people don't like going sober (why!?).   Which leaves MDMA and a psychedelic.  If you keep MDMA dose low, you won't really have a comedown / deplete your serotonin, so I'd do that day 1, followed by a psychedelic day 2.  If you do the psychedelic first, you may notice a large cross-tolerance on the MDMA. 

If it were up to me though, I'd take a break day two.   Take some selegiline or MJ.  And yes, selegiline is surprisingly good at raves, just don't expect it to be a candyflip =).

Firstly, an easy way to bring drugs is to pitch your tent before hand and put your stash in the tent pockets and pack it back up, they'll never assemble your tent to find your drugs (we got a lot of mdma/scales/caps/opiates) in that way. Or just put them on your person in "discreet locations".

I did some very potent LSD at a festival in Ontario and I have to say it was a cross between Louis-Ferdinand Celine's Journey to the End of the Night and Soluble Fish by Breton. What happened you might ask? Well it was something like hell on earth, in a good way; there was an awareness of the absurdity of the human condition, as I stood amidst a sea of tents soaked in urine, vomit, rain, and mud the irrational extremes of hedonism I was witnessing assumed Apollonian features. People fornicated in the mud, danced in the rain, but their willingness to forgo rationalism and temperance showed no becoming will. Where there could have been a will to life there was only the will to anesthetization and thus a subversion of anything esoteric, individualist, or self-affirming. For the week after that every bird song, circular saw, and dump truck sounded like drum n bass.
