Title: Re: Morphine Sulphate ER
Post by: coldwash97 on July 28, 2011, 08:49 pm
you can get the morphine out of ms contin or the oxy out of the new formula OP by chopping it up, soaking it in methanol, filter, then evaporate the methanol. make sure the methanol is completely evaporated because it is toxic. what is left over can then be brought up in water with no gelling and filtered for IV. this method will recover nearly 100% of the opiate. use 1mL of methanol per 2mg of opiate. it works well and doesn't take to long to evaporate the methanol if you heat it gently. i can give you more details if you want.
