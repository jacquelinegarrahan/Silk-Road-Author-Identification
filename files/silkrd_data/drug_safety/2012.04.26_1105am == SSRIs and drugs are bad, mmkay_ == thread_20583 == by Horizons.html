Title: SSRIs and drugs are bad, mmkay?
Post by: Horizons on April 26, 2012, 11:05 am
Hello, everybody! I'm here to deliver a quick PSA in response to some threads I've seen lately.

DO NOT DO CONSCIOUSNESS-ALTERING DRUGS WHILE ON SSRI MEDICATION!

This means no psychedelics, no MDMA, and no heroic doses of dissociatives. Why? Because YOU WILL FUCKING DIE.

SSRIs are trip-blockers. They greatly lower the potency of any mind-altering substance you consume while on them. Some people notice this, and decide that the solution is to take more drugs. But, kids, more drugs are never the solution (mmkay?). The goal of an SSRI is to make the availability of serotonin in your body higher, by inhibiting its reuptake (hence the name Selective Serotonin Reuptake Inhibitor). Psychedelics and MDMA work by making your serotonin receptors extra-sensitive, and dissociatives put your whole fucking brain in overdrive. Both result in your head being flooded with more serotonin than it could handle.

What's wrong with too much serotonin? Well, it turns out you can OD on serotonin. And it's extremely easy to do so. If you're on an SSRI, and then take twice your normal dose of whatever drug tickles your fancy, you'll be putting your serotonin levels at close-to-toxic levels. This can lead to some pretty bad shit.
Quote from: Wikipedia

If you are on SSRI medication and wish to do a psychedelic or MDMA, you will require some foreplanning. Take a week or two to slowly phase out your SSRIs (stopping instantly will usually give you brain zaps, which I can tell you are not very pleasant) until you completely stop the day before tripping (meaning that your last actual medication was two days before). You may resume your SSRI treatment two days after tripping. Work up to your usual dose over a few days, as well.

Trip safe, live long!
