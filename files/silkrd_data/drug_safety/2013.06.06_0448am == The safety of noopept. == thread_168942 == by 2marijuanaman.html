Title: Re: The safety of noopept.
Post by: dimmu on June 09, 2013, 04:13 am
I have been dosing on noopept for around 3 months.

3 x 10mg capsules daily supplemented with
2 x 150mg of choline bitartrate.

After 1 week I noticed my mind was noticeably quicker and my ability to recall memories had improved. Colours seemed to "pop" more then usual. My creativity may have also improved however that is difficult to substantiate.

3 months on, I'd say the benefits have waned. Perhaps I should consider UPPING the dose. I have also read that you should cycle 3 months on, 1 month off. Maybe that's what I need to do.

RE safety, I have experience zero concerns. I have consumed alcohol and acid however, I tend to only dose once on the day of acid consumption. I have also read that taking any amphetamine type substance and noopept is a NO-GO as the combined effect of both substances has a toxic effect at a neurotransmitter level on the brain.

Noopept seemed to create a mild brain fog when I 1st consumed....adding choline bitartrate may have eliminated the fog or maybe the fog diminished over time...difficult to say. If you are studying long term I would recommend noopept. Do not expect "Limitless" style experiences as you will be bitterly disappointed. To conclude my own experiences with Noopept...

Colour perception seems to have been enhanced. I have noticed as a musician, that I have written more elaborate music though it is difficult to say whether this has been achieved through noopept consumption, acid consumption or a combination of both. I cannot gauge its effects on studying apart from noticing a general improvement in my memory. I will say that my memory improvement appears to have diminished though, as stated earlier, perhaps a dosing issue.

Give it a go for 3 months after researching dosing and drug mix discussions. I look forward to reading about your experiences.
