Title: Clean up your MDMA part 2!!
Post by: wowzers on February 01, 2012, 09:34 pm
Back when I first joined this forum I did a little tutorial on how easily clean MDMA.
I was bored this afternoon and had a few grams of Aakoven's new batch of MDMA lying around so I thought I'd do a little photo tutorial.

Hope you enjoy :)
Pictures in link below.
http://4eiruntyxxbgfv7o.onion/imgzapr/upload/md.jpg

You'll need 3 beakers/ flasks, 2 pipettes, a spatula, some IPA, ether, acetone, MDMA, hot water.

Place 1.0g MDMA in a beaker (fig 1).
Heat 10mL IPA in a conical flask immersed in freshly boiled water (cover flask with parafilm to stop water getting in). (fig 3).
Add 2mL of the hot IPA to the MDMA (fig 2).
It probably won't dissolve immediately so cover the beaker with parafilm and immerse in freshly boiled water (fig 4).
Don't swirl too much or MD will stick to the sides of the beaker and crystallise out.
In solution! (fig 5)
Allow to cool to room temp then put in fridge for 1/2 hour, then freezer for 1 hour (fig 6).
Cool about 10ml of both acetone and ether in the freezer for an hour.
Crystals covered in IPA solution of yellow impurities (fig 7).
Remove the IPA with a pipette and wash the remaining crystals sequentially 5mL of ether then acetone then ether again. (Fig 8)
Crystals look cleaner with each wash (fig 9).
Scrape crystals out onto clean surface and dry under a lamp, I took this photo immediately after washing (fig 11).
After 1 hour of drying they were super sparkly!
Marquis results (fig 10, left to right- crude, white gunk which coprecipitated out, clean sparkles).

I got 930mg pure back from the 1g I started with. When I crystallised it out, there were the usual needles plus some weird white gunky material which I fished out with a spatula, I reheated the beaker to get the rest of it to go back into solution then cooled again and there was no more gunk.
The gunky crap weighed just over 50mg, dried to a fine white powder and tested black with marquis (middle vial on fig 10). Obviously it's very empirical but it didn't taste of MDMA! :P Not a clue what it is, maybe just some polymorph of MDMA?

Post by: wowzers on February 01, 2012, 10:07 pm
Pro chemist tip number 1: never chuck anything away until you've got the product weighed out, then in the worst case scenario you can boil off the solvent and start again. There's really nothing that can go wrong if you follow the steps exactly. I did 400 quids worth this afternoon, I wouldn't risk losing that unless I was confident it would all come out the other end...

Why do you want to turn your chunks of MDMA into powder? The little sparkly needles you get out the end of this tutorial are very fine and brittle and can be chopped up into micro-fine dust with a card or razor, you can even snort them whole- they melt up your nose and the drip is quite 'refined' compared to the impure stuff.
