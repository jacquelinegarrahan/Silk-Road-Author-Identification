Title: Re: 2C-B - Advice
Post by: awesome1126 on June 05, 2012, 03:36 pm
Hey there, 2C-B is a great chemical! To answer your questions:

I'd take it by putting the powder into a gelcap and swallowing it on an empty stomach, or mixing it into a shot glass with distilled water in it and drinking it down. Plugging works well too (up the butt), but is not desirable by many. Plugging essentially reduces the dose by half.

As for weighing, get yourself a nice milligram scale, and don't even attempt to eyeball a dose. This chemical has a high dose-response curve. Being just a few milligrams off can drastically change the experience.

I'd shoot for 28-30mg for a strong but great experience! Any time I take less than 25mg I'm a little disappointed.

Title: Re: 2C-B - Advice
Post by: frogtrap on June 05, 2012, 07:56 pm
Good advice from the other posters. Just thought I'd add a few things (and perhaps repeat what they've said for emphasis):

1. I've taken 2C-B the last two weekends, and both times I've measured my powder on a milligram scale (definitely a must), mixed it with a little orange juice, and drank it down. I rinse the container/glass with another couple of drinks of OJ just to make sure I'm not missing any residue on the sides. You can put them in gel-caps, but it seems to take longer for the effects to be felt. I definitely agree about taking it on an empty stomach, but eat breakfast and give it a couple of hours to digest so you're not famished. It's a short trip, after all, so you don't need 12 solid hours for the experience. The plateau really only lasts for around 3-5 hours, at least at the levels I've taken and with my body chemistry.

2. As far as dose goes, people respond differently, so I'd start out with a lower-dose. As the saying goes, you can always take more, but you can never take less once it's ingested. I started with 20mg, and it was a perfect introduction to a new compound. Granted, it was a light trip, but visuals were definitely there. It was like a taste of the 2C-B experience. Yesterday, I took 25mg, and it was a MUCH stronger trip. On my path, my next dose will be 25mg again so I can become more comfortable with the material before taking it to a higher level. I got mine from gammagoblin, so the doses may not be equivalent.

Just for reference, my 20mg dose was purely recreational-feeling. There was no introspection that I could tell, just a novel visual field with patterns running on surfaces, but a mostly clear head. It was a nice, easy plus-two.
My 25mg dose was, like I said, much stronger. This time, everything in my visual field was warped/morphing, clouds would take on any form I projected, and the forms would rotate as fully solid three-dimensional cloud-structures. Light poles swayed as if in a breeze, birds left a colored energy trail as they flew through the sky, and I felt infused with positive energy. Music was incredibly intense, and I found that if I let it, my emotions (and the visuals) would be carried away by the music. There was definitely introspection at this level, not forced-seeming, like with LSD, but if you want to you can open yourself up to the experience and examine some pretty deep stuff. Definitely a plus-three.

3. As far as snorting it goes, that's up to you, but this material feels so kind when taken orally that I wouldn't want to start the trip off with terrible burning in my nasal passages...

4. I do feel mild nausea during the transition, but once I start feeling that, I know it's time to pack a bowl and go outside. :) Pot makes my stomach feel just fine, and in fact, you'll likely get hungry while you're tripping, so make sure to have some easy-to-eat but nutritious food already prepared and lots of beverages. Fresh fruit tastes GREAT with 2C-B.
