Title: Re: how do i smoke opium?
Post by: b0lixtrader on May 30, 2013, 05:51 pm
Grab a steel butter knife, place a small bit on the end.

Turn on the stove, grab another steel butter knife and place it over the fire. 

Leave it there till the end gets very hot.

Once ready get ready to inhale and place the very hot end gently on the opium on the other knife and inhale the smoke.

Repeat.

However, I recommend you dedicate a pair of knives to this if you plan on more than trying it once, as the knife can become blackened by repeat use. Make sure you start inhaling before you touch it with the hot knife and to touch it as lightly as possible.

Title: Re: how do i smoke opium?
Post by: Ben on May 31, 2013, 12:45 am
Something like a crack pipe is actually the original implement to smoke opium - it has been for centuries. In opium dens around the world a pipe is used that is heated over, traditionally, an oil lamp. This pipe holds the opium and has a small hole in it on the 'hot' side so you can breathe in air and vapours through it, just like a modern meth pipe typically used with a lighter as the heat source.

This is not the only way though, i've seen people roll in opium wiht tobacco into 'joints' and use it that way, but that seems to be something popular in the far east where raw opium is abundant and cheap.
