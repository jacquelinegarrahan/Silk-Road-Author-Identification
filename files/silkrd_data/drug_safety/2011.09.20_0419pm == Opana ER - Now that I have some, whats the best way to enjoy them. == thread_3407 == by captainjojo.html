I'm not an IV person myself, so naturally insufflation is my preferred ROA.  It takes about 10-15 minutes for it to kick in decently, and then fully probably around the 30 minute mark.  Eat a nice, high fat meal prior to doing any.  Although it normally only helps when taking the pill orally I believe, there's inevitably going to be some drip, so might as well enhance what does drip.

And although I'm not an IV person, if I were going to IV something it would for sure be an Opana or a Dilaudid.  The nature of the drug is such that the bioavailability is VASTLY higher(like 10% oral as compared to 100% IV, it's also around 40% intranasally, for reference) when going the IV ROA.  So, assuming you want to get the most bang for your buck, as well as not have to wait, I would avoid the 90% iso method since from what I've read it's not that effective.  I would drop a few pills in some water to allow the extraction process to start, and while you're waiting, insufflate whatever you need to keep you nodding until your extraction is done.

As for tips with the "teks."  Insufflation is pretty straight forward if you've ever ground up a pill and snorted it.  As for the IV route, give this thread a read: http://dkn255hz262ypmii.onion/index.php?topic=1983.0.  It seems to have the most information.
Title: Re: Opana ER - Now that I have some, whats the best way to enjoy them.
Post by: captainjojo on September 21, 2011, 04:13 am
Yes, as with most of us, I wanted to get the most bang for my buck, but I wanted it now, not later.

So the first thing I did was put one Opana into a 4cc locking syringe (the kind used to inject into an IV bag, they don't use a needle, they screw onto a port in an IV line), filled it with 4cc's of distilled water, put the locking cap on and put it aside until tomorrow.

Then I took another Opana and cut it into quarters, cutting up one quarter really fine, then snorted it as two lines.  I waited about an hour.  Not really as strong as I was expecting.  But my tolerance may be up quite a bit, I just went through quite a large number of 20mg Oxy IR's, IV'd.

I then did the 90% ISO on another quarter.  It seemed to work OK.  I added hot water to the film left over after the ISO evaporated, put in a filter and sucked it up in a 1cc syringe.  It seemed to work OK.  Again, a small buzz, nothing major.

I ended up snorting and ISO'ing the rest of one 40mg, plus one more, over the next 8 hours, waiting 4 hours between the first try and the last.  Just trying to be careful.

Given my reaction so far to what I have taken, I think I would need to do 15-20mg via the ISO method or 10-15mg via insufflation to get a good rush from the Oxymorphone.

In the morning I will try 1ml/1cc of the one that has been sitting in water for 24 hours.  That should work out to a 10mg dose, and supposedly the 24 hour method has a better conversion factor, meaning I should actually get 10mg from 1/4 of the 4cc.  I am pretty sure I am losing 10-30% when doing it via ISO and snorting has only a 43% bioavailability anyway.

Hopefully the morning dose will give me that rush everyone talks about.
