Post by: Ben on September 19, 2012, 01:12 am
The recreational value of benzo's escaped me as well - for me they are purely therapeutic medications that help dealing with anxiety or insomnia, and hard to obtain legally in most of the western world.

The recreational value of a drug like xanax is very limited - i may give you a bit of a drunk buzz if you are not used to it, but don't expect this effect to be larger than drinking a bottle of wine.

I suppose the main reason of things like xanax being popular is their ability to end band trips fairly quickly. This could be of great value to people experimenting with hallucinogens.

So, some people will order these to cope with everyday anxiety issues whereas others may order them as a precaution to have available for a trip gone bad. These audiences are completely different, but tend to order the same substance either way.

Don't be overly concerned by that though, plenty of people require a nightcap of some alcohol with a benadryl to get a good nights sleep.

As for the combination of benzo's with alcohol: anterograde amnesia ia -very- common.
