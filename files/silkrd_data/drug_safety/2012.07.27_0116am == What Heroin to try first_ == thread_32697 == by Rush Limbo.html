I would recommend going with one of the vendors from our lovely neighbor to the north. In terms of quality, OPI's #4 pebbles are the best of the best, but also the most expensive. A cheaper, but still high quality option, is #4 from a vendor like No GenRx or Tomorrowman. If you're just looking to sample a little bit, then maybe try Deluxe Delivery's NYC stamp bags - they have great reviews and I've actually just placed an order to try them myself.

Some good vendors from the EU include Psilocin's or drNyx's #3, but I believe they're both out of stock at the moment unfortunately.

If you're a snorter, stick with #3 or #4 to start, as black tar cannot be snorted unless it's dissolved in saline (or a similar liquid) and then dripped into the nostril.

As a first time user, please be careful - snort a very small bump to start, you can always take more - but not less. Have fun and enjoy the awesomeness of H responsibly! :)
