Title: Re: Grinding weed with a grinder.
Post by: ecstasydude on April 22, 2013, 06:24 am
Wooden grinders have round edges on the teeth.
Causing the weed to be pulled apart, rather then shredding to bits.

Metal Grinders have diamond cut steel teeth.
Causing the weed the be shredded, and trichomes falling in to the keif tray.

Wood is the best choice.
