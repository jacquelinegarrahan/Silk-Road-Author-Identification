Title: Re: Missed a shot of tar heroin
Post by: AnimusVox on November 14, 2012, 09:41 pm
You most likely have a calcification (mineral build up in the soft tissue) or an abscess in your arm. The former typically go away relatively quickly on their own, but considering it's been three weeks, I would highly recommend you go to the doctor because it's probably an abscess which are NOT to be taken lightly and can lead to serious bacterial infections. Consult medical attention as soon as you can, my friend. Be honest about the cause and they will help you.
