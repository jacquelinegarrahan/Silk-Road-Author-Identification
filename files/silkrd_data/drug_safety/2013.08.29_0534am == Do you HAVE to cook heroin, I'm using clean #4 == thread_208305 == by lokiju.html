Title: Re: Do you HAVE to cook heroin, I'm using clean #4
Post by: captainjojo on August 29, 2013, 08:08 am
#4 heroin will dissolve in water with just a little heat.  It does not require large amounts of heat.  To actually kill any bacteria you would have to heat it for 10-15 minutes.

From past experience I would suggest using distilled or sterile water, a good micron filter (cotton if you cannot get that) a new syringe and just enough heat to get the #4 to dissolve, also make sure to use a clean or new cooker each time.  Use alcohol to clean the spoon or whatever you are using to heat the solution, use an alcohol swab where you plan to do the injection.  As long as you follow those few simple steps the chances of infection are next to nil.

Don't use the same injection site each time, move it around. 

The one thing to look out for when IV'ing is abscesses.  Pay attention to your skin, check it every now and then for redness and swelling.  Abscesses start out small, usually a small red swollen section of skin.  If left untreated in several days it could swell up and spread over your whole forearm.  If you do see an abscess start, don't wait too long, go to the doctor and get some antibiotics. If you treat it early it will disappear within a 5-7 days but if you leave it you could end up at the hospital with them cutting it open to drain it and having to give you intravenous antibiotics.

If they ask how you got it, say you think that's where your cat scratched you some time ago.
