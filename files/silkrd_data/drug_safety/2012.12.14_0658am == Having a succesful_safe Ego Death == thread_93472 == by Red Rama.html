Title: Re: Having a succesful/safe Ego Death
Post by: RedCloud on December 14, 2012, 07:30 am
Well, my first major ego death experience was on 2 hits of Space-monkey blotter acid. I don't necessarily think you can force an ego death upon yourself. Ego death is something that comes to you whenever your mind/spirit thinks it's ready.

Halfway through my acid trip I had no sense of time and thought the trip had ended. Little did I know I was only halfway into my trip when I decided to leave my friends and go back to my apartment. I was in my room by myself and all of a sudden I started to peak. The lights were turned off and I just sat there for 6 hours going through the most wild memories of my childhood. Good and bad things. I eventually got a feeling that I was starting to die. And the next day I woke up and was completely reborn.

^That was absolutely life changing.

All in all, if you want to attempt to force it upon yourself just do what I did and trip by yourself. It's LSD or whatever psychedelic you use vs. YOUR mind. Having no distractions helps such as friends. The trip will suck you in to an endless mindfuck until the drug wears off.

It was very difficult to integrate the experience but 6 months later I was back to normal, but a new person.

Try it out.  :)
