Title: Re: DMT smells very chemmy- is that bad?
Post by: Tagore on March 27, 2012, 09:35 am
Hard to say which smell you are referring to without a better description... DMT has a very distinct smell, but hard to describe.

As long as you don't detect any SOLVENT smells, you should be OK.

If you want to try washing (though any responsible chemist should have already done this!) you can:

Put your DMT on filter paper in a büchner funnel, or just in a coffee filter if you don't have one. Slowly pour COLD ammonium (about 14mL per gram) over the crystals on the filter paper. Spread the filter paper out flat and allow to dry completely. You now have beautiful white crystal DMT. You will lose a small amount of product when washing, but it should be negligible (< 5-10%) and in my opinion itʼs quality over quantity in this case!

This will also remove any possible traces of lye left in the product.

Title: Re: DMT smells very chemmy- is that bad?
Post by: Tagore on March 27, 2012, 06:53 pm
Sure, you can recrystallize, but it's not necessary. I'd suggest the wash first, give it a try, and then if you want to recrystallize, do a small amount and compare. Some people actually prefer some of the more yellow crystal to completely pure white/clear. The only difference is the amount of natural plant oils which may be left in slightly yellower product.

RECRYSTALLIZATION- Something just for the geeks. After you have collected your crystals from the last step above, you will have nice pile of whitish powdery crystals. If you want the absolute purest possible product, (and something beautifully shiny to look at) you can do a solvent wash and then a recrystallization. Simply dissolve your product in the minimal amount of HOT solvent (usually Naphtha or Hexane) and swirl to dissolve. Slowly add solvent until the product is completely dissolved and no more. Allow this flask to cool very slowly to room temperature for an hour or so. Then move the flask to the fridge for about two hours. Then to the freezer. This way you will grow larger, more defined crystals which are incredibly pure. Pour off the excess solvent and allow crystals to dry. The crystals should now be almost clear as glass and quite well defined.
