Post by: Spunkaroo on August 07, 2012, 10:13 am
The NBOME series isn't orally active, you have to take it sublibgually. If you're game to be guinea pig, don't take any psychadelics for a couple of weeks then try these tabs again and swallow them straight away. If you don't get anything I'd be betting on NBOME. From your experience I might also lean towards this as it could be a diminished experience from the small amount of time you kept the tabs in your mouth.

The other common chemicals on tabs are the DOx series. They are orally active, but you can tell these by the trip length. It can be 18+ hours. If you were down in the time you mentioned I'd say it's probably not DOx.

All of this is from second hand research only though, I haven't tried either of the above myself, so take this with a grain of salt.

One last option is a really low dose of LSD, i.e. it was lower than advertised or has degraded in the heat/air/moisture. If you really want to find out you'll need a test kit.
