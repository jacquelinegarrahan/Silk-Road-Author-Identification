Post by: A Riotous Defect on December 19, 2012, 12:11 am
I don't have many connections IRL, as most of my business goes to the SR, so most of this will be anecdotal stuff I heard from friends who can't be arsed to get their shit online.

Location: middle of east-coast, USA

Coke: I don't mess with it, but it  is usually 60 bucks a gram. The only people I know who really do it are members of an all-boys college who are notorious for being rich coke-fiends, and from what I hear the coke they get is alright but not too good.

MDMA: Most of the MDMA around here is actually Methylone, which I enjoy but is significantly weak in comparison to legitimate MDMA. However, if I want real MDMA I can drive an hour and get it for 20 a point.

Pressies (Rolls): 15 a pop, usually something like mephedrone. For some reason I've seen a lot of rolls with mephedrone in them. Kids love them, but they don't know shit about their drugs. I can absolutely attest to Chubs' claim that kids around prefer amp combos or other RC's to actual MDMA. No one wants that MDMA magic anymore. In fact, the people I've sold methylone too have commended it saying they liked it because it just made them chatty and not all touchy-feely like MDMA usually does.

Meth: I live in a smaller area, so meth is definitely prominent. Never really done it or been around it that much, though. I did used to know one tweaker who wanted to buy amphetamine off me, but I don't think he mentioned a price.

Opiates: Standard prices. Have never seen heroin around here, although I know it is. Since I'm only in college, though, that could be a reason. 5 mg hydros go for 3 bucks a pop and the norco-10s go for 5 or 6 depending on where you're getting them. Oxy goes for a dollar a miligram, as usual. Haven't seen tramadol or opana or dilaudid around, but I'm sure you can find it if you look hard enough.

Dexedrine/Amp's: College town, so amps are everywhere. Never seen legitimate dexedrine, but 20/30 mg amphetamine salts go for 5 bucks as well as 70 mg vyvanses. The vyvanse is hard to find, though. Concerta is pretty prevalent, but the dosages are small.

Benzos: Xanny's are hard to find but klonopin is usually in good supply. 3 bucks a pop, don't know mg's because klonopin has always just made me nauseous and I don't fuck with it.

DMT: Extremely rare. Contemplated making it myself. I have looked at this as a business opportunity in my area before because I've talked to people about it and they are more than willing to pay 20 - 25 dollars for a 'breakthrough' dose of 50 - 60 mgs.

LSD: 10 bucks a hit of around 90 - 100 ugs, not very prevalent. 70 bucks for a ten strip.

Shrooms: Hard to find, as with most psychedelics that aren't RC's, 45 a quarter.

Weed: 10 a gram for shit weed and 20 a gram for alright weed. Everyone says they have 'the best chron' but unless I drive to the nearest large city I don't get good weed. Not that I smoke much, so it doesn't hurt me.

Heroin: No clue. I've never known anybody to do heroin, since the only opis around here are scripts.

RCs: RC's have a pretty good market here, and I sell them around when I have them in stock. Never bullshit anybody with them, though. Since psychedelics and empathogens that aren't shit are lacking here, I mostly find people looking for M1, or 2cs. I've sold methylone, 2c-e, 2c-i, and then 25i when the NBOMe's got popular. I sold the NBOMe at 10 a hit and got pretty good business with them, just because of the lack of supply of anything else. I didn't like to deal with the 2c's because I don't have a miligram scale, so when I sold them I took them to my buddy who does and paid him some money to cap them up for me. Sold 2-ce at 20 for a good oral dose. 2c-i for the same. Again, moderate business. Methylone was extremely popular, sold for 15 a point, and my business booms whenever I get it. People love it. I enjoy it myself, too, so it's always nice to have it in stock. I like it I have a tendency to binge when I do drugs and methylone feels a bit less harsh to binge for a day or so on than legitimate MDMA.
