Post by: IcedupLlama on January 14, 2012, 04:45 pm
depends on what kind of pills.
the few times i snorted OCs a few years ago, before the OPs, i'd scrape the external coating off with a knife or razorblade, then crush using the side of a knife and chop into powder, with roxis i just use the side of a knife or razorblade, push down, it'll turn to powder pretty much instantly, chop out and snort.
other than OC and roxi, only pills i've snorted are pharm amphetamines (adderlols, dexedrines and mostly XRs), llamas method for crushing pharm speed, for when you need to crush down 6-10 dexedrine quickly at once (so you can snort them, cause you're impatient and wanna get high real bad but wont settle for half assedly crushed XR beads like a nub/dumbass) that for me can be done far quicker than the way with a lighter+piece of paper, lighter+dollar bill, shotglass+screwdriver and all the other ones i've heard of people doing, and works just as good
take a small ziploc bag, SMALL, the smaller the better, but not like dime bags, as in small ziploc sandwich bag kinda things, open your dex/adderlol capsules, dump all the beads from the capsules (however many, i can crush down 10 dexedrine 10mg XR spansules to powder in just about 2-3 minutes....) in the bag, zip it shut, then take a spoon (metal obviously, the larger the better....) and roll the spoon over the beads until all the beads are crushed and you get a somewhat fine to very fine powder depending on how much effort/care you put in, oh yeah, last thing, once you have all the beads crushed down in the bag, you can snort that powder as is, but its best to basically get all the powder in the bag to one side/area when you crush it initially, and move the crushed shit to the other side of the bag, and then rolling the spoon over it a little bit more, it'll break it down even finer,
protip: if you use my method, if you can hear like crunching noises like from the bag, you havent crushed it down all the way...
i've found no better way of crushing XR pharm amphs, and i've got quite a lot of experience using RX speed under my belt...
