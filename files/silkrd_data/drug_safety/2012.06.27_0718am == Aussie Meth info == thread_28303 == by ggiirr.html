I've actually found a bong shop that sells pyrex tubes that are about the same diameter and thickness as a sweet puff but one of the ends has like a funnel opening so it's diameter is about twice as big and makes it easier to get thicker lines in.

There's a guide in the Drug Safety forum, but in summary you're best to have the pipe no longer than 4", then:
crush the ice as fine as you can and make a line that's more wide than tall (which is why the funnel ending on my pipe is useful)
heat one end (funnel end for me) up to about 1/3 of the pipe length with either a butane or propane torch until you see it glowing red
rack that line up in one smooth move like you'd normally snort powder without the heat

If you only feel a light burn in your nostril from the heat and not the ice snort burn, you've heated up the pipe enough... now exhale that massive cloud of smoke and be ready for an uppercut from mike tyson!

Main point of a hot rail besides the actual technique of the activity, is that you can get a much larger amount of ice in one go without putting holes in your nostrils!

I've got a medium tolerence, and even hotrailing 30-40mgs of good stuff can make me jump!

Try to limit hotrails though, you can go through half a gram pretty quickly with a mate in a night!

Also once the pipe cooled down, after a couple of rails don't be afraid to scrape the inside down, you should get enough unburnt ice for another decent rail!
