Title: Re: MDMA & Epilepsy
Post by: weedsaves on June 18, 2012, 08:32 am
Everyone has a "seizure threshold". A person with epilepsy has a seizure threshold that is lower than that of a normal person's. Drugs like MDMA lower this threshold even more and can greatly increase the risk of seizure. I would not want to be the one to supply your friend with MDMA simply due to the risk of physical injury from having a seizure.

If you decide to supply him I would only give him a half dose but I still think it is a bad idea.
