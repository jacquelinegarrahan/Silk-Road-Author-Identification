Title: Have you seen DMT elves and other creatures?
Post by: msft1 on August 08, 2012, 10:00 pm
I've been smoking DMT and gradually getting to the breakthrough stage. So far I've seen a few weird creatures (which I'll describe now). What I want to hear from you is - which creatures have you seen? Can you briefly describe them? Has anyone seen "DMT elves"? I've read http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Machine_elf but I've not encountered anything like that yet.

What I've seen so far:

* I'm stuck between moving giant worms (kind of like being stuck inside giant intestines) and I see that some people (humans) are stuck inside those worms. One human has tried reaching me with her hand (as if asking for help) when she was being swallowed.
* Some giant alien-looking creature trying to stuff me into its belly. Like, a giant kangaroo. I resist, the whole vision goes away.
* A giant man right above me trying to "lift" reality. As he's doing that, the reality (room) around me has this wireframe mesh that appears to be lifting up. I get scared and vision goes away.
* At the beginning of almost every trip I see these fractal shapeless colorful creatures that are getting closer and closer to my face, it's an uncomfortable (weird) feeling. They don't seem friendly and I feel like they're trying to "snatch" me. I feel like their "hand" are stretching towards me and trying to grab my face.

Share what you've seen, especially about the elves! What are they like?
