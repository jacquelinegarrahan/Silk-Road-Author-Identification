Title: Re: What is up with SR drugs?
Post by: UKMJ on June 11, 2012, 02:52 pm
Couple of things in play here, firstly SR vendors are higher up the supply chain, so they have cleaner products and benefit from bulk price buying. They have little need to adulterate the product because they can charge higher prices on SR for a cleaner product. Also they can hold less weight, say a vendor has 1kg coke at 90% they could sell 1000 x 1g deals, if they cut it to 60% by adding a cutting agent like levamisole they have 1.5kg of coke now (that is what LE will say) plus they now have to ship 1500 x 1g deals, so the product sells for less becUse the quality is less and their postage costs and personal risk level increases.

Also because of competition, there are plenty of vendors for most products, the differentiator for buyers is price and quality, most buyers who made the effort to get on to SR will pay higher prices for better quality as the main alternative is street quality (shitty or overpriced).
