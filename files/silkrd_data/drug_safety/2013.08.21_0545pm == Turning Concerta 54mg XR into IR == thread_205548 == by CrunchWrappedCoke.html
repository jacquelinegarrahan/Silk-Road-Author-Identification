Title: Re: Turning Concerta 54mg XR into IR
Post by: b0lixtrader on August 22, 2013, 08:22 am
Get a warm damp paper towel, rub all the colored coating off.

Take a knife and slice it down the middle. Work at it and try to crack pieces of the coating off.

Once you have that off, take the knife and cut a little down the middle and snap off the brown piece.

Grind the white piece in to powder.  There's no real other way unfortunately.
