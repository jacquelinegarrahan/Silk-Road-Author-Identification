Post by: SpoonyBard on June 20, 2012, 04:19 am
Quote from: CoughDrop on June 20, 2012, 01:27 am

First, good for you.  If you're feeling conservative and your instincts advise you to take baby steps at first, there's absolutely nothing wrong with that.  Don't let anyone tell you otherwise.

Erowid.org says the "threshold" dose for LSD is 20 micrograms, and 50 is the middle range for a "light" dose.  It's going to hit everyone a little differently, but I think it's pretty reasonable to expect to feel mildly but pleasantly trippy.  You and your friend may feel silly or giggly as it's setting in, and your minds will probably wander.  There's often a bit of dizziness or detachment as well.  To me a shallow acid trip can feel a little like a strong hit of good weed.  It lasts a lot longer, of course, and it's easier to be contemplative with it. 

It's very unlikely that you'll get any open-eye visual effects at such a low dose (it never happens to me at less than 200 to 300), but you may get some decent closed-eye visuals, i.e. mental imagery.  If you relax and settle into yourself and find the pictures in your head are more colorful, more geometric, or more 3D than usual, that's the acid.  You may imagine other interesting things with other senses as well.  It won't seem "real" at that dosage; probably more like vivid or unusual daydreams.

The duration will probably seem a bit shorter than the normal 6-8 hour because the tapering off will make you feel closer to "normal" sooner.  You might feel a bit tired or drained the next day, but not too badly.  If you don't feel much an hour or two in, smoke some pot.  It often complements psychedelics.  (My first mushroom trip felt very subdued until a good hit of weed "unlocked" it.)  I wouldn't advise MDMA if your goal is to avoid intensity. 

Most of all, relax and enjoy it.  At a light dosage like that, an acid trip may feel a little weird but should be fundamentally non-threatening.  A bad trip is very unlikely, and I think it diminishes to _extremely_ unlikely if you go into it with a positive mindset and without heavy expectations.  If you have a good experience the first time (and I suspect you will), you can always try more the second and subsequent times.

Title: Re: What would a 50 microgram acid dose feel like?
Post by: SpoonyBard on June 20, 2012, 04:45 am
Oh.  And while we're on the subject, that half gram of DMT...  You can take baby steps with that too, if you want.  Everyone talks about the crazy intense timeless machine-elf visions you can get from DMT, but that's when you're hitting a strong enough dose to break through. 50 to 60 milligrams does it for me.

If you're only doing a _little_ of it, say 10 to 20 milligrams, you don't get that.  What I tend to get is a moderately strong but brief wave of dizziness, a somewhat sharper sense of 'dimensionality' in whatever space I'm in, and a bit of color distortion...  And then it's gone.  It's kind of like walking into a patch of land where the gravity is slightly different and then walking back out again.  Weird but not unpleasant.  I might also feel just a little bit lightheaded for 10 to 15 minutes, like a pot high but without the euphoria.

The catch of course is that DMT has a very sharp slope.  There isn't a lot of mass difference between 20 milligrams and 50, but the difference in effect can be dramatic.  Messing around in the midrange could take you in either direction.  I also think that if you don't _eventually_ want the intense full breakthrough experience, there isn't that much point in bothering with DMT.  If you just want to feel slightly weird, weed tastes a lot better and lasts longer. 

But you can still start with small doses and work your way up to it.  And just like I said with acid, there's nothing wrong with starting conservative.
