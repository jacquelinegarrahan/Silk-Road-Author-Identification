Title: Re: LSD + 2C-B - Any experiences/recommendations?
Post by: Internal Freedom on August 16, 2013, 11:23 pm
I did this combo at a music festival + MDMA. It was actually very nice, the hallucinations lasted a VERY long time though. Long after I went home that night. Started at around 11am and was still tripping out at 2am the next day.

I did 19mg of 2C-B, then took 100mg of MDMA an hour later. After the third hour I took the 110mic blotter of LSD and was tripping for a solid 10+ more hours. I smoked a small hit of DMT while me and a buddy were close to the stage, as soon as I inhaled the stage split in half and I instantly started zoning out HARD.

Maybe try taking the LSD, wait an hour or two for it to kick in, and once you're feeling the effects take around 15-20mg of 2C-B, I wouldn't go over that as it has a very steep dosage curve and the acid will already be in full effect. It really depends how hard you want to trip.
