Title: Re: Smoking opiate tabs?
Post by: ChronicallyMedicated on September 20, 2011, 06:30 am
There seems to be a small but vocal minority of opiophiles who claim to enjoy 'smoking oxycontin' (the original OCs, not the new OPs), but then there are just as many people calling them out saying they've tried it and it doesn't work.

You may be able to 'smoke' a pill, but it's going to be horribly inefficient and probably painful if not harmful to your lungs.

Your best bet, if you are fixated on smoking opiates from a pill, is to perform an extraction (e.g. the 24-hour water extraction for Opanas), filter the resulting liquid, evaporate it under low or no heat, and smoking the residue which should be reasonably pure.
