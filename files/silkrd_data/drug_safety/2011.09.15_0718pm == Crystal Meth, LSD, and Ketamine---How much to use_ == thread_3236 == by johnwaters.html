Title: Re: Crystal Meth, LSD, and Ketamine---How much to use?
Post by: ruddyRudeman on September 15, 2011, 11:26 pm
Well, ketamine won't last that long no matter how much you will dose.

Read this guide for dosage : http://www.erowid.org/chemicals/ketamine/ketamine_dose.shtml

It will keep you around an hour. If you will try higher dose, you will just pass out and won't remember a thing, but it won't prolong the trip significantly. And in case of overdosing you will just sleep forever =]

So if you really want to take a 36 hours marathon, you will have to redose several times, which can be unpredictable. Don't recomend it. What I can recomend is either take several short lived things (ket, dmt, sally) and explore them one by one or just abandon the idea of 36 hour lasting trip. Alternatively just have some psychedelic like lucy or shrooms. The 6 - 8 hours would be long enough to change your mind, and you can always redose with no real danger.

If the 1.5 days from one dose is a real aim for you, then read about 2c-dragonfly and datura. These things are pure hardcore. Dragonfly can last about 30 hours, while datura can hold you up to 3 days depending of dosage. However they are really dangerous in their own ways, so don't give them a try if you are not experienced with mind altering substances and your reactions.
