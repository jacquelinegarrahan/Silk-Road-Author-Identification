Post by: magenta296wok on August 26, 2013, 06:58 pm
I bought 3 different kits from them (the Ecstacy, Bathsalts, and Cocaine Purity). All 3 came through no problems at all. They're great for testing products on the go (like at festivals), but the single use factor makes them every expensive if you're doing a lot of testing. I found the Cocaine Purity test a little difficult to read, but it's definitely a good test. They also have a Cocaine Impurities test that will react in the presence of common cuts. I almost always give street bought coke a wash with ACS reagent-grade acetone to get rid of most of the cuts. Granted, with most coke I've found outside SR, I lost 50-80% in mass, but the product left from the wash was 10x's better than with the bunk material.
