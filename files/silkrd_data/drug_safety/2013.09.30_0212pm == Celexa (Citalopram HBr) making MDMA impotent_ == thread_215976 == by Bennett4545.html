Title: Re: Celexa (Citalopram HBr) making MDMA impotent?
Post by: Ben on October 01, 2013, 12:43 am
SSRI's bascially ruin your mdma experience by definition. It is their very mechanism of action that prevents you to roll on mdma, and it does not matter that much which exact ssri you are on. These effects are widely known for medications like fluoxetine (prozac) and citalopram.

If you are interested in the biochemistry behind you can probably find a bit about that on this forum, wikipedia and other sources, or i can explain it a bit further if you want to know.

An important notice is that this is valid for all ssri's, regardless if prescribed for depression, anxiety or otherwise. Other anxiolytics like benzodiazepines do not have this effect and allow mdma to be effective, although they may reduce the intensity of the trip and in high dosages prevent/cancel it mostly.
