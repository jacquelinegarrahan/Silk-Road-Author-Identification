The most important thing to note is that edibles can take a WHILE to kick in. I have had them take over an hour/hour and a half to fully hit me. Don't make the mistake of not waiting long enough and then eating more if you don't want to risk getting super fucked up. As for the high, I have found it's more of a body buzz, with psychedelic features like colour enhancement and so on. They also last much longer than smoking a joint, sometimes I'll be quite high off a cookie for 2-3 hours.
