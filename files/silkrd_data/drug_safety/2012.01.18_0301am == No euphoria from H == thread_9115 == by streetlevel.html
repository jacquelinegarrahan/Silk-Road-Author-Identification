Title: Re: No euphoria from H
Post by: zephnial on January 19, 2012, 04:47 am
Lower dose, maybe? Sometimes with opiates, less is more, especially when you're just starting out. There's a sweet spot you should be looking for -- too little and the effects are weak, too much and you feel nauseous and fucked up (not in a good way). Start out small and slowly work your way up until you find the dose that works. Dosing too high will only make you sick and drive up your tolerance way too fast. Remember: you can always redose if the the effects are too weak.

Opiates can be an acquired taste. They can have a lot of unpleasant side effects for the naive user, but with time your body will build tolerance to the bad effects (hopefully a lot sooner than for the good effects).
