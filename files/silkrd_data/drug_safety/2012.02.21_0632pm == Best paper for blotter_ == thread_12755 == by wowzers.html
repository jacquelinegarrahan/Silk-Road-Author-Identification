He just said to use 100% unbleached cotton paper and not to use commercial home-printer ink. Looking into it further, unbuffered neutral cotton paper is available, designed for long-life applications such as archiving important documents.
Obviously heavier paper is better for higher compound loading and small grain size allows accurate dosing on smaller hits as there is less spread resulting from capillary action.
