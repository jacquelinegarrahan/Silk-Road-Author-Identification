You will very quickly develop tolerance to Oxycontin/Oxycodone ... or any other opiate for that matter.

If you take them too often and your system gets used to it .. then withdrawal will become a definite possibility. Withdrawal isn't fun. At all.

The plasma elimination half life is around 3-4 hours .. if you are using any SR/XR varieties  (eg MS Contin SR 10/30/60 etc) keep in mind you're in for a longer ride ......

If you want to get the "best buzz" on these substances, try to give your body a break of at least 3 odd months- even longer works better.
