Title: Re: How do I measure 50mgs?
Post by: PerPETualMOtion on July 01, 2013, 03:25 am
Hmm... Scale is best, but if you want serious alternatives...

1. Do you know the qty you started with/have left?
2. Find something it is soluble in. It doesn't seem to be a water soluble compound, judging by its structure, but I could be wrong. It has a low solubility in isopropanol, but that doesn't rule out ethanol.
3. Take your stash, dissolve in a measured amount of liquid. Now you can estimate you mg/mL solution to take the desired dose.

For example: 1 g of MXE in 1000 mL everclear = 1mg/mL. 50 mg = 50 mL.

If it isn't soluble in ethanol, water, or oils, then you might be forced to get a high quality scale.

Post by: popjoe on July 01, 2013, 12:45 pm
HAH! I can understand, being high in front of your family is veeery awkward.  That's why you should avoid it.

Get a decent scale that measures mgs.  There's pocket scales that are used to measure precious stones, they also measure mgs very precisely.

DO NOT skimp on the price.  If you buy it from the right place it'll be worth every penny.  A scale worth about 80£ is the minimal investment.
You can find one for about 20£ that will accurately measure mgs for a few months but that's as long as they will last for.  They will start making mistakes and you won't always realize it without comparing.  Kitchen scales can be purchased for 20£ they're not made to measure milligrams.  For the same price you get the same quality as a kitchen scale or worse, that's why they'll just cock up and screw you over.

Get one with a weight so that you can re-calibrate it.  Don't worry about the price, share it amongst a friend or two that would have a need for one.
If you think you don't need it after a few months you'll be able to sell it off and get some of your money back.

The brand I have is Kern, I've had it for over a year I re-calibrate it every month or so.  It's even got a guarantee.

Do this and you'll never pass out in front of your family again  ;D
