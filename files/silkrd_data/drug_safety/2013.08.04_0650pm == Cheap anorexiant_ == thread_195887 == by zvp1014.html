Post by: maf on August 10, 2013, 10:56 am
I've never seen any modafinil that could be described as cheap. I have a prescription and I get the stuff from Cephalon; I haven't tried it from other companies, nor have I tried adrafinil. If you're after appetite suppresion, I don't think it's worth spending money on modafinil. Most people seem to lose a few pounds at first, but then they adapt to it all too quickly. Also, while this never happened to me, I've seen some people for whom modafinil had the effect of increasing the appetite. Weird!
