Post by: CX on June 11, 2012, 06:07 am
I use a rolling paper on my scale. Tare the scale. Add 100mg or the dosage you want onto the paper. I use my swiss army knife to get crystals from baggie to scale.

Cut out a circle from some reasonably strong paper, then cut out a quarter of the circle, tape it together and cut off the tip. You have yourself a nice little funnel. Experiment with circle sizes till you find the perfect fit.

I then poor the the crystals from the rolling paper into the funnel, using a blow gun dart to stuff the powder through the funnel if necessary. I use size 3 caps, they hold 300mg.

Can usually fill around 30 caps per hour. Takes a steady hand though, not good if your a twitchy mofo.

Title: Re: Whats the easiest way to accurately dose and fill gelatin capsules with mdma?
Post by: ZA6wUw8a on June 13, 2012, 03:10 am
Preparing capsules can be a bitch, as can chopping down a tree with a hammer.
The most efficient method depends on how many capsules you need filled and the tools you have at hand.

Assuming you have no tools,
* If you have a small amount of capsules, it's no big deal.
* If you have something like 100+, there are many ways to go about this.
Here's a low-maintenance idea:

1) Weigh out your desired dose per capsule of MDMA (say it's 100mg)
2) Put the MDMA in the open capsule, and put that on the scale, tar it
3) Fill the rest of the capsule with something like sugar, and note the weight (say it takes 30mg) it takes for the powder to reach the brim
4) Say you have 10,000mg (10g) of MDMA you want to put into capsules. Mix evenly with a proportional amount of sugar (3g) or food-safe item of your choice
5) Simply fill each capsule up to the brim with your mixture. This way each capsule will contain around 100mg MDMA and 30mg sugar. This will be much faster than weighing out 100mg 100 times. You can expect a little variation in the dose, but that depends on how evenly you mix it.
