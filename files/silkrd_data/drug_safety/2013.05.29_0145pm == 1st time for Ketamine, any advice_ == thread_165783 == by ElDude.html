You have pretty much got it covered.
First time can be a little overwhelming if you haven't got someone about to reassure...
Just don't drink alcohol with it, and take it slowly, bump at a time... don't swallow the back drip - this is what fucks up the organs and bladder more. I followed that advice and it's proved to be good advice.

Your right about not wanting to K hole too quickly as it can scare people off, and can put peeps off.
It also seems to change depending on what emotional state you are in. The more positive the better.

I'd also advise on cooking up the shards into powder if you can as it absorbs better into the nostril and doesnt screw up the nasal passage as much, or if your feeling gutsy, IM it....... amazing experience if your safe and clean. 1ml insulin needle, alcohol wipe and a filter and boooom. But best get used to K first.

Plenty of info on cooking up K. Put it in a pan, with a little water, if its 1 gram of K then a couple of T spoons. Low to medium heat. slower cooking the better, it will start to bubble up and slowly turn to powder, as soon as it turns to white powder, take it off the heat and check its not damp, if it is, just hover it over the heat for a bit and then scrape it out with a card when the pan has cooled.

Hope this has helped. Any other qu's feel free to ask, have done K  for 4 yrs now

Post by: tree on May 29, 2013, 02:52 pm
Quote from: ElDude on May 29, 2013, 01:45 pm
What kinds of things do other people enjoy doing while on it? How long to wait between doses?
If it's a good dose, then lying in your bed listening to electronic music/techno is what you're gonna want to be doing :) Even for doses that don't get you to the K-hole it's better to lie down.
If it's a small dose, you can use it to party, it makes you want to dance, a little like a low dose of DXM (but way better). It's a nice alternative to alcohol at this range, but with a nice mood lift and less side-effects.

It's better to cook it, but if you can't you definitely have to thoroughly crush it, it hurts really bad if you don't and it doesn't get absorbed well, but cooking it is still better. IM or subcutaneous are the way to go though, you have no nasty drip which can be a distraction.

For dosing, it's advisable to do one small bump in each nostril every 5 to 10 minutes until you feel you've reached a comfortable level. When you know your dose you can take higher doses at once.
