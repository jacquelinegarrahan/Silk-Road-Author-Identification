Title: Re: Snorting H
Post by: Ungoliant on February 05, 2012, 10:09 pm
Used to have an addiction problem with H for about 6 months. Stopped using ~11 months ago.

Originally started insufflating it, which is good but not as potent as smoking. Snorting effects tend to last longer, but require slightly more powder than smoking to reach same effects.

I am not sure how people over the world prefer to smoke H, but in our community freebasing it off of tin foil with a straw was popular and my method of choice. It can be tricky not to waste, especially first few times trying. Start with a little until you master the technique. Eventually it is easy and highly effective. Effects do not last as long but are more potent.

For new users to substance starting out with insufflation, a matchead size bump is probably a good starting place. You have to really assess the quality to determine if you want/need to take more.
