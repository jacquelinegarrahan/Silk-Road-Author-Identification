Title: 25i NBOMe Suprisingly Strong Trip? First Experience (Trip Report)
Post by: Ourist on May 11, 2012, 09:58 pm
(Turned out to be quite long I'm afraid! Sorry in advance)

Hey guys, a lurker here!
First of all I have little psychedelic experience having only a few threshold doses of acid. I also apologize for my poor story telling, I find it very hard to explain what I was feeling and seeing. 

To set the scene I bought around 15 tabs 1mg from DudeRugs about a month ago but never got around to having any myself and instead a few of my friends dosed. My friends had a great time, but from their experiences, they didn't seem as intense as mine... even while having 2-3 tabs at once.

Now recently (I like in the UK) I purchased just a few 550ug tabs from 'pureogkush' to save me getting some from America and so I could test it asap.
I had just one yesterday and after reading up on doses and wasn't really expecting much at all from what most have said on here and from research online.

How I was wrong..?
Dosed at 3:00pm

00:30 mins +
Around 30 mins after taking the tab I felt the effects coming on while sitting in my friends car. A slight body rush and a nice tingly feeling were the initial feelings. I took out my phone to double check the time and already the apps on my iPhone were waving. I found it quite hard to text at this point. There I was thinking "Damn, this is hitting in quickly".

We were heading up to an airfield to thrash my friends car around for his final goodbye before it gets scrapped so I was in for a hell of a time..

00:45 mins +
After about 45 minutes I had intense body rushes and felt amazing for a good twenty minutes while we were driving. I couldn't describe it but it was draining the energy out of me. I then looked around the car and everything was distorting especially the stereo. The LCD screen that displays the song names was morphing with the text enlarging then decreasing in size over and over.

1:00hr +
By the time we had got to the airfield I was tripping pretty hard and this was only an hour in. Colour's were extremely bright and were slightly saturated and the tarmac (was wet) had very light shades of green, purple and blue (like when there is oil on a wet road).

My friend got straight on it and took me for a spin around the airfield and started handbrake turning. This was incredibly fun but very intense for me with the tarmac pulsing very lightly with the colours I mentioned above. I was still able to function pretty well at this point however I was having quite a bit of trouble forming proper sentences and only just making sense to my friends.
After a quick spin around we switched over and I jumped in the drivers seat as we were in a safe environment and I was still thinking logically. As soon as I got out of the car I felt very heavy and as I stood up everything felt slightly lop-sided and tilted to the right. This quickly adjusted and I got into the drivers seat. I've driven my friends car before but this time the steering wheel seemed much larger than normal and it was very alienating. Nevertheless, I thought I'd give it a go. I had no trouble driving naturally around (slowly of course, I was tripping quite hard by this point with my depth perception pretty fucked).

This is when I started getting auditory hallucinations. The window was open and as we went around the airfield and as we went through small puddles I had an incredible delay. I would here the initial splash, and then about 3 or 4 more slowly fading afterwards. It was very atmospheric and I could barely here the car and only these sounds.

I can't remember much else of driving, but I only drove about 10 more minutes around then let my friends have a go.

After I had driven I took in the scenery around me. It was very beautiful to me even though it was a murky day. I then sat down and looked at the clouds. The clouds morphed into words, but I couldn't quite make them out so I continued to look around. I could barely string words together at this point and found it very hard to communicate.
I kept wondering to myself "Fuck, I've only taken what I thought was a small dose.. how intense is this going to get?" I managed to keep the bad thoughts at bay though and rode through the experience. The grass surrounding the airfield and the tree's then seemed to have eyes and almost seemed to be alive as they waved together in the wind.

3:00hrs +
After about two hours at the airfield and after relaxing and letting my trip morph many objects around me I felt like I was coming down slightly. The visuals got slightly less intense and I thought I was coming down.. Within ten minutes of thinking this I came right back up and started tripping even harder. My friends faces were changing colour, their bodies had a saturated outline, I had intense trails and I found it completely impossible to even get my phone out of my pocket. For a moment it felt like my hand was stuck and when I finally got it out I was incapable of typing my passcode correctly due to the numbers moving all over the screen.

This coming up and down continued for a good 3 more hours with similar visuals and quite a few peaks. I won't go into details though as there is quite a bit and I've probably written too much already. I couldn't even get to sleep until 2am that night, that's 11 hours since I dosed. This morning I had a slight headache, which I find understandable as my brain had an incredible amount to process yesterday.

Now has anyone had an experience as intense as this from such a small dose? Loads of people don't seem to get much off just even 1mg. Is it me being very prone to this substance or could the dose have been larger than stated?
