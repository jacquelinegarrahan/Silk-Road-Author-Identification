Title: Re: What are some fun things to do on shrooms?
Post by: KatEverdeen209 on May 14, 2012, 03:57 pm
Stay away from clouds and public places. Nothing stirs up fear and paranoia like feeling everyone around you knows you're tripping and judging you.

Trip with people you know and have someone there tripping and someone there not. Its great to have someone who can relate to the you who is tripping and better to have someone who can relate to you wanting to be back in the real world.

From the beginning make sure you're going through no hard times emotionally. Ie death of a family member, loss of job, seperation of a loved one. Any and all thoughts may be put in front of you to deal with the second you're not so distracted by the trippynesss



Bring shit to avoid distraction. Toys the tripping shroomer loves toys. Try kaleidoscopes, foil gift wrapping paper, playdough, anything weirdly textured or wildly colorful will do the trick.

Hide anything sharp or any weapons.  You don't need to accidentally play with anything.

Always remember its just a drug and drugs subside, enjoy it while you can because no matter how wild it gets you'll miss it when it goes.

Please be safe and have a happy trip.
Good vibes.
