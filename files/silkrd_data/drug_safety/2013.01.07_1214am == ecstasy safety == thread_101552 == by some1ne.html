Just to expand on what gestalt said: Anything has a chance to kill someone who has been dealt the wrong cards. X could kill someone with an underlying heart condition/defect but the same could be said of taking too much caffeine--if it's a heart issue (I don't know why these 2 anomalous deaths occur on average). Some of those deaths might be misinterpreted dehydration deaths (sure the X caused you to party and sweat hard but the person could have prevented problems by staying hydrated)

If you are considering ecstasy, it's important to trust where you're getting it from and know what you're getting (MDMA[purity? 80% etc.], MDA, some RC being called ecstasy). When I first tried X, it was just loose MDMA crystals and I had an idea of the reputation of who I got it from, I had equipment to measure out an appropriate does(always better to start low end), and then I tried it in a controlled environment before ever going into the world.

If you are thinking of a more allergic-reaction style impact then just scrape a piece of the pill or shard(very small amount will do) on the tip of your tongue and see if any irritation shows up, which won't take long ~minute after contact.  (although pure MDMA is very bitter and might turn you off the taste of it for a while--better to bomb it anyways I think). You may also be able to do a scratch test with it on your inner arm but now we're wandering into the dangerously paranoid territory.  :P


Hope some of this helps! Good luck, have fun, be smart
