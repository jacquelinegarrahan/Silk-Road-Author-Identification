Post by: The ILF on October 24, 2012, 07:43 pm
Jack!  So glad you took the plunge ~~ you can probably already feel this, but your life may be changed in ways that you cannot even anticipate!

As far as the original question, the issue of alcohol with E (and probably just alcohol in general) is a dicey one.  It's anxiolytic, and the early stages of the buzz can be nice, but trying to maintain that slightly-less-than-two-drinks feeling is really difficult and often results in excess and mess.

One of the ILF was a pretty heavy drinker, discovered E, and wouldn't touch alcohol while on one for a good while.  But when the "loss of magic" started to rear its ugly head, the temptation to compensate with the ethanol-euphoria was hard to avoid.  But the next day hangovers were brutal.  Shakes and shame a plenty, sometimes for imagined stupidity, sometimes for real idiocy.  Realizing this, he's decided that if he's on one, he'd rather deal with, explore, and work out some of the anxiety or negative emotions that can still arise, particularly on the more psychedelic MDA, even in the middle of the roll -- especially since the mind is in a better state to do that work -- rather than drown them in alcohol and make things worse.

One of the ILF was a pretty heavy drinker, discovered E, and wouldn't touch alcohol while on one for a good while.  But when the "loss of magic" started to rear its ugly head, the temptation to compensate with the ethanol-euphoria was hard to avoid.  But the next day hangovers were brutal.  Shakes and shame a plenty, sometimes for imagined stupidity, sometimes for real idiocy.  Realizing this, he's decided that if he's on one, he'd rather deal with, explore, and work out some of the anxiety or negative emotions that can still arise, particularly on the more psychedelic MDA, even in the middle of the roll -- especially since the mind is in a better state to do that work -- rather than drown them in alcohol and make things worse.
