Title: Re: Question Re Cocaine and dopamine agonists
Post by: Diz on May 17, 2013, 01:42 pm
It's never a good idea to combine agonists, whether partial or full, with substances in the same category. For example SSRI's and MDMA, bad idea. You can definitely do coke while on cabergoline although it isn't the safet thing in the world. Chances are you will miss a lot of that euphoric dopamine high. If you are using a dopamine agonist to lower prolactin and you were previously stacking or using some sort of anabol, steer clear of cocaine. There is nothing like coke induced rage during or after a stack.
