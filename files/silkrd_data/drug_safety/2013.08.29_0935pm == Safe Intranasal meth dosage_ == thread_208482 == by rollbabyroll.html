Title: Re: Safe Intranasal meth dosage?
Post by: junjo on August 29, 2013, 11:10 pm
I would highly recommend against pushing meth beyond 50mg, but most people tend to do so anyways. Nasty side effects can start beyond that range for some people. Of course, people will dose far, far beyond that and be "fine", but it's quite risky. Just dose enough until you find your good "place", and try not to push too far beyond it. The more you redose, the more nasty effects you can have as well.

Basically, you know your own body, so keep it reasonable and don't push it farther than you need to. Erowid is always a great resource for these things as well.
