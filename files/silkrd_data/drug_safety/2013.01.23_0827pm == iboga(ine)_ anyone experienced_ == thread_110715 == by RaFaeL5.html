Title: Re: iboga(ine): anyone experienced?
Post by: WolfmanJack87 on March 23, 2013, 06:31 am
I actually traveled to Mexico to get a legal dose of therapeutic Ibogaine HCL and also the alkaloid extract. This is NOT a drug to do recreationaly. The high lasts about 2 days and you wont be able to get good sleep for at least 1-3 days, but on the other side it does clean out your body of opiate addiction and almost rids you of withdrawl symptoms.

The way I was administered it was by taking about a 200mg hcl dose to make sure I didn't have an adverse reaction to it. Felt almost nothing from that. They then gave me a flood dose of the HCL of about 750mg. After about 2 hours I had very noticeable 'tracers' from lights in the room, and I started to feel very introspective. With my eyes closed when the high hit, my past literally flashed before my eyes, yet it is a very controlled experience. It was weird how I wasn't thinking about going into my past before I took the drug, yet as the visuals started coming it was almost impossible not to catch a glimpse of a past memory, especially very emotionally charged memories and drug usage memories.

It really gave me an outside perspective on how I handled myself as a person and how my actions and behaviors influenced my decisions, especially about using hard drugs. It's weird because everyone who takes it experiences nearly the same sort of thing where they go through a 2 hr 'viewing' of things, then about a 10-15 hr 'intropsection' stage. During this stage, you do have a small amount of visuals that are left over, and you're brain feels like its running at the speed of light through thoughts, memories, everything. It is by far a very interesting thing that this drug does.

However, beware that for at least 12 hours of taking a flood dose, you WILL NOT be able to stand up on your own. It literally makes the room spin like crazy if you try and get up. If someone isn't there to coach you, 95% of you will throw up instantly. They actually had us not eat or drink 12 hrs before hand to minimize the amount of times we'd get up. After the introspection stage it just basically felt like my ears were ringing and my brain was fried from so much thinking and not being able to sleep for at least a day. Being bedridden for that long and not sleeping is exhausting, as Ibogaine makes you think so damn much.

There is another form of the drug called the alkali extract that contains other similar drug components directly extracted from the Iboga root itself. This seems to produce or more visual experience at a lower dose (500mg) without the extenuous mind exhaustion and sleep deprivation. To benefit fully from this drug, I would recommend taking a flood dose, and using it to aid yourself in beating a current addiction to opiates.

If you are going to get it anyways for recreational use and choose to ignore my personal advice, get the alkaloid extract and not the HCl. I was told by the guy who ran the clinic I went to that it creates a more visually pleasing experience with less of the harsh side effects of taking an HCl dose. As far as dosage, I am not 100% certain of the exact mg I was given except for the flood dose (which was around 1g of the HCl), but they did give me a booster dose a week later of the total alkaloid which was 2 or 3 capsules that were about 250mg caps. That created a manageable buzz of visuals and head games that lasted about 6 hours. The tracers actually lasted about 2 days after that which was a neat effect, especially at night.

Hope that gives a bit more insight to this little known but much sought after drug that is primarily used for an alternative opiate/heroin addiction treatment. And it actually does work!! You just have to want to stop living that life if you expect it to give you any sort of clean time. You have to do the work after the Iboga plant does its job and rids your body of that horrible withdrawl.
