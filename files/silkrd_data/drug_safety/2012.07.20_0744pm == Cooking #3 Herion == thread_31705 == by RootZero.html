Title: Re: Cooking #3 Herion
Post by: oppyate on July 21, 2012, 03:19 pm
There are two main types of diamorphine consumed by heroin users: diamorphine base (brown heroin) and diamorphine hydrochloride (white heroin). White heroin, produced mainly in the Golden Triangle (Burma, Laos and Thailand), is the traditional heroin consumed in North America. It is the most refined heroin you can get and is often referred to as No. 4 heroin. Brown heroin, produced mainly in the Golden Crescent (Afghanistan, Pakistan and Iran), is most common in the UK and Europe. It is less refined and is often referred to as No. 3 heroin.
Confusingly, No. 1 and No. 2 heroin are not really drugs at all; rather they refer to the materials and stages used to make street heroin.

How much citric or ascorbic acid is required to dissolve pure base heroin?  (for every 369.4g of diamorphine base, you need 64.03g of citric acid or 176.1g of ascorbic acid  or  a 5 to 1 RATIO ). Example: As you mentioned, 150mgs of  #3 require anywhere from 15 to 30 mgs of Citric Acid.  150/30= 5.

Boiling?
Heroin, be it #3 or # 4 Should NOT be Boiled. It should be Heated till you see some steam. Tar is usually heated Twice, once to melt it, twice to separate it.

oppyate
