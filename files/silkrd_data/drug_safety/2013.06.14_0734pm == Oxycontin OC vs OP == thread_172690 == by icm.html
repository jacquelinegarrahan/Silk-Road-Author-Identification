Post by: EternalSunshineMan on August 10, 2013, 05:47 am
Quote from: tommygun on August 10, 2013, 12:54 am
Whats the difference between roxy and the old OC lick and crush?

Roxy's are IR, so no need to remove coating.  I usually see them in the 30mg formulation, however they also come in lower doses. 

To be totally honest, I've taken a lot of OC and in my experience, there's no need to snort them.  I used to get the OC 40's all the time and tried everything with them; smoking, snorting, eating...well not everything, I never got a chance to bang em as I wasn't into banging at that point.  But I really found no difference in snorting or eating them, other than faster onset.  And if you chewed them up on an empty stomach, the onset really wasn't drastically different, and the wave of euphoria seemed to come on harder when I ate them (IME anyhow, you may be different).  OC has a high oral bioavailability, and snorting honestly doesn't really increase it at all.  I mean, by all means if you want to insufflate it, go right ahead, it certainly won't hurt...I just found no need to do it. 

OP oxy's kinda suck, and seem weaker than the old OC's.  Even when I eat 3 or 4 80's at once, I wouldn't feel anywhere near as high as chewing a single 80. Obviously because it's much more difficult to break the time release with the new formulation, and slow release sucks compared to IR.  Still, you'd think 320mg of oxy over 12 hours (they're 12 hour time released) would get me blasted...not so much.  I could still drink a 5th of whiskey no problem. 

You are probably aware that you can break the time release on the new OP's, but it leaves a big gelatinous glob...which you then proceed to swallow.  I've been told to use lemon juice, or coke (the drink), and soak the pill in the liquid in a shot glass.  From what I understand, that's not really necessary, as water will do the trick just fine.  When I have got OP's in recent months, I just popped a few at once, and it lasts a good while anyhow, so its not all bad.
