i dont think there is any evidence that it will affect your genes or sperm

chronic, excessive consumption of cannabis may impair your short-term memory.

i say 'may', because the evidence for all this isn't conclusive.

for me the only negative effects of marijuana - laziness, depression - occur with excessive daily consumption.  they go away after i take a break.
