Title: Re: Popping MDMA cherry
Post by: NeveWitch on December 23, 2011, 03:47 am
I'm not really a SR vet, but I have done MDMA and E a number of times and I've followed the major MDMA-related topics on the forums.

People seem to recommend DRamsterdam, mitanox, Ivory, and juergen2001, on both service and the quality of their MDMA, so I don't think you can go wrong with any of those guys. I personally haven't tried any MDMA from SR yet, but I do have 1g of Ivory's on me and 2g of juergen2001's coming in (hopefully soon!).

Dosages vary depending on MDMA quality, and I've seen recommendations usually run between 100mg and 150mg. Since you and your friend haven't done rolled before, 100mg-120mg should be more than enough, especially with the higher quality stuff offered on SR. You can take your starting dose of ~100mg-120mg, then follow it up with a booster that's 1/2 the amount of your starting dose (~50mg-60mg) between 1-1.5 hours later, right as you peak. If you do opt for the starter+booster, just take one booster dose and avoid the temptation to keep taking more and more. A lot of people who have tried redosing past the first booster dose is a waste, and only makes them feel speedy without the euphoria and other good stuff. Don't eat right before! An empty stomach is ideal, so you should fast 4-6 hours before you drop. Also avoid alcohol (mixing uppers and downers is never a good idea). You can pop it in a gelcap, mix it in a sports drink, or snort it, but I prefer gelcaps just for simplicity and it's easier to take with you. You should also probably look into 5-HTP supplements for afterwards, as that helps in restoring your serotonin depletion and helps you bounce back faster.

Be with some good friends, and MDMA will take care of the rest. Clubbing and dancing is so, so fun, but I've chilled out in the company of a few close friends in more private settings, also with good results. Other than that, enjoy yourself! Stay safe!
