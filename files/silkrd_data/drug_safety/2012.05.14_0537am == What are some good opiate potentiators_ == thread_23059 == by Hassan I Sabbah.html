Title: Re: What are some good opiate potentiators?
Post by: modest mouse on May 14, 2012, 09:22 am
There are a ton, but the term "opiate potentiators" is too broad, not specific enough because certain potentiators only work for a specific opiate, opioid, or even ROA...

for example, when ingesting orally, the pH of the stomach can affect bioavailability... but if you are shooting straight into the bloodstream then the absorption in your stomach doesnt matter.

There are too many to list, chemicals include first generation anti-histamines like promethazine and hydroxyzine among many others, H-2 antagonist cimetidine, alpha-2 adrenergic autoreceptor agonist clonidine, anti-cholinergics like hyoscamine and dicyclomine, drug efflux pump inhibitors such as omeprazole, other otc opioids such as loperamide (occupy peripheral receptors), and catabolic enzyme inhibitors such as tagamet and compounds in grapefruit juice. Plenty of others I am sure I am forgetting... like DXM, naproxen and quinine. A high fat meal has also been reported to increase bioavailability, and many report noticeable effects just from even a glass of milk due to the fat especially with oral oxymorphone which generally only has TEN (!!! - shock) percent  biovailability.

check out site below for more in depth info and a great list
http://opioids.com/opiates/index.html

The reason many are probably saying the P450 enzyme inhibitors such as grapefruit juice and tagamet do not work is because they were probably using the wrong opioid, in the case of oxycodone and hydrocodone the enzymes that breakdown oxy are inhibited, therefore producing decreased 1st pass metabolism which results in more reaching the brain when taken orally, and then also increasing the half-life, extending the legs. They are not myths, but very factual reasoning based on pharmacology and experience. However, if you take codeine and say it doesnt work, well, its because you blocked the conversion to morphine... reinforcing the idea not all potentiators potentiate everything!

PLEASE BE SAFE. Diamorphine is more potent than you probably think even though you are on morph dont load up with potentiators for your first dose. Start small with the H alone, then work your way up to your sweet spot. Then decrease that amount significantly and take a dose of ONE potentiator as well... the way people OD is by mixing a bunch of CNS depressants, or one depressant and trying to potentiate their high with too many things. Just love you and want you to be safe
