Title: Re: Best cannabis strain for energy?
Post by: Makai on August 31, 2012, 06:40 pm
Sativas have a high THC content and a low CBD content. CBD (Cannabidiol) counteracts some of the effects of THC and makes you stoned. So Sativas produce a head high, while most Indicas produce a body high.

You could also use a vaporizer with adjustable temperature to get the head high. CBD vaporizes at a higher temperature than THC, so if you use low temperatures you will (mostly) only vaporize the THC. Then you could collect the partly vaporized weed and vaporize it again at a higher temperature later when you want to go to relax/sleep. Or you use it for cooking or creating oil with mostly CBD content.
