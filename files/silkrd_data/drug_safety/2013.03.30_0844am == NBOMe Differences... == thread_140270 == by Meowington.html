Title: Re: NBOMe Differences...
Post by: l1llykins on April 26, 2013, 04:47 am
25b is one of my favorites. I agree about the clear headspace. I also find that it heightens my emotions and senses, not to mention euphoric in a 2c-b kind of way.

The first time I did it I watched Secret World of Arrietty and wow, that movie came to life. Sounds felt like they were coming from all around me. Colors swirled throughout each scene and burst out of the screen at certain parts. Lots of adrenaline started pumping during various action sequences also, so much fun! Candy was also interesting, tastes would stretch forever ... for instance, I'd pop a skittle and let it dissolve but instead of biting it after a time because the taste faded and I needed a burst, I could let it dissolve completely (though I didn't have much of an appetite for food in general). Another time I saw a comedy and I laughed myself to tears. Sex can be very tantric as well ... the euphoria combined with the heightened sense of touch ... it's very niiiiice.

So yeah, it's one of my favorites. 1mg is my usual dose and lasts a good 6 hours.
