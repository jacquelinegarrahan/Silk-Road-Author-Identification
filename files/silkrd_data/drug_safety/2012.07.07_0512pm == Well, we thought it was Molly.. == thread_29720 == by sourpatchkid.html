A slight correction: It will bind to physiologically active proteins and kick out the 5-HT that was previously bound; 5-HT is correlated with feelings of tropor, lethargy, and helplessness, which is why anti-serotonin drugs like LSD and MDMA are so wonderful, and why drugs that increase 5-HT activity, such as SSRIs, may substantially increase the risk of suicide. To say it 'floods' your brain with 5-HT implies that the 5-HT is producing the effects; it's actually the other way around.
