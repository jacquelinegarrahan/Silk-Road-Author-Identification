Title: Re: MDMA + Alcohol + Weed + LSD + 2C-B?
Post by: tordemon on April 01, 2012, 05:45 am
There'll be a lot of stimulation, but I would say that that combination is relatively safe. A few things that I would recommend:

1. Test your LSD. If the acid has been substituted, it might be another phenethylamine in which case you may be even more stimulated, and then you might run into overdose territory there.

2. Don't mix alcohol with 2C-B. This is just from personal experience, but 2C-B was one of the materials that "TOMSOed," and so my experience was EXTREMELY intensified with the addition of some alcohol, far beyond what I would have expected or hoped for, and quite some time after I'd thought I was almost done with the trip.

3. Separating things further apart is usually safer. I'd also try to make sure none of you are at particular risk for epilepsy, but that might be bordering on paranoid preparations.

Have fun and let us know how it goes. =]
