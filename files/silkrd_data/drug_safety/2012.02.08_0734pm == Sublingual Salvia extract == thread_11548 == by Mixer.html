Title: Re: Sublingual Salvia extract
Post by: Mixer on February 14, 2012, 02:18 pm
Ooowww i had only half of what i thought was left :( i think i weighted it wrong... anyway i was nicely surprised because i thought it wouldn't do anything. Here's my recipe:
Quote
0.18g of Salvia Divinorum standardized 5x extract and 20ml of acetone were cooled to -10Â°C for 4 hours. 10ml of the acetone was added to the salvia and the mixture was stirred and left in the dark for 2 minutes. The mixture was filtered, the liquid was kept and the salvia was returned to the other 10ml of acetone. The mixture was stirred, left in the dark for 2 minutes and filtered. The salvia was discarded and the liquid was added to the first one. The solution was reduced to a couple of drops of volume evaporating the acetone on an hot water bath. The solution was put on two blotters using a pipette and the blotters were dried.
Each blotter has about 1mg of salvinorin a. I took one keeping it in my mouth without swallowing for 15 minutes. At T+0:20 i felt slight effects, i guess i hit the threshold dose. I was slightly lightheaded and i felt off of baseline though i can't describe it. My friend felt the same, so it's definitely not placebo. He also noticed a bit of euphoria. It wasn't a great experience but it confirms the blotter works. I'll get more salvia asap and try higher doses :)
