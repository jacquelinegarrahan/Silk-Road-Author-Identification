Meth is a drug demonized by the media. As with every drug that gets demonized to this extent the results of recreational use and extreme addiction get confused as we get bombarded with pictures of worst case scenarios.
Have you seen an alcohol worst case scenario? It looks fucking horrific. I still drink wine from time to time...

Guess what: if you stop sleeping, stop brushing teeth, sell your ass on a street corner, get STDs and smoke/inject cutting agents your beauty might fade a bit. Occasional use will not harm you though. No matter if you snort it or smoke it...
The health risk of smoking is that it is more fun to most people and you might like it a bit too much. That smoking makes your teeth rot is nonsense (it's because stimulants suppress saliva flow and hardcore users don't brush). That smoked meth recrystallizes in your lungs and therefore damages them is bullshit, too (of course smoking anything is hardly healthy).
The danger of addiction is highly exaggerated by the "not even once" ads and its kin. Just be aware how often you take it in which doses.

http://dkn255hz262ypmii.onion/index.php?topic=22378.msg230562#msg230562

You put it in a methpipe melt the crystals and heat it careful until it vaporizes. Take away the flame if it vapor appears. You can  roll the pipe a bit while you do this (especially if you put a lot of crystal inside).
Its thick white vapor. Some resin might stay in the pipe and turn dark.
