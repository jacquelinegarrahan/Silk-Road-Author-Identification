Title: My 2nd go with 2-CB: 30mg dose Warning inside
Post by: samuelkane on February 06, 2013, 06:02 pm
My first try was a 20mg dose, it gave me some visuals, some gigles, great euphoria especially after i smoked some weed.
That launched me up high for an hour but i easely regained full control when the weed starting wearing off.

This time i have ordered 5 pills and partly because im currently out of weed and partly because i wanted to try a higher dose i went with 30mg.
I tried to cut one pill in two with a knife but was left with alot of little chunks and halve a pill.
I put these on my tongue and swallowed with some soda.
The trip itself started out like i remembered my first trip felt like, i had alot of gigles, objects started breathing and glowing.
I put some infected mushroom on and downloaded winamp to check out its visualiser which turned out to be VERY good way better then wm and it kept me in awe for an unknown time.
The visuals distortions kept getting more intense and i also started feeling a bodyload, partly a stoney dizzy feeling partly altertered sensations of touch and taste.
I experienced the most sensations on my tongue where i had put the small chunks, it felt tingly and elektrical and i had a strong metalic taste making everything taste awfull.
I reached a point where the music started feeling to loud and started feeling anxious, i shut down winamp and was left with loud buzzing noise my computer makes.
Its a very hot cpu (pentiumD) with a standard intel high pitched sounding cooler which runs full speed almost 100% of the time.
I started feeling poisoned by its sound and my mood turned from very uplifted and gigly to very down, feeling sick and depressed.
I tried escaping this feeling by turning off the computer and all the lights and laying on my bed but the noise from outside also felt deafening and poisoned.
I learned escaping a hard trip is not an option and the term eyes wide shut now has a new meaning for me.
The trip turned introspective and i experienced a whirlwind of emotions mostly of things wrong with my life, all the while accompanied by intense CEV's.
I was quickly heading to a panic attack but managed to accept the bad thoughts and the fact the trip itself was not something i was gonna escape.
I made peace with the fact i had to ride it out and let everything come to me without fighting it. I learned alot and also started thinking about how to better some aspects of life, i slowly started feeling at ease again.
I decided to try to keep busy to distract my brain and fired up my PS3, i played the demo of heavy rain which turned out to be an awesome game. At this time i was allready coming down and playing the game reduced the intesity of the visuals alot.
The game also had some funny, be it dark, characters and i started gigling again and my trip turned to smooth and uplifting again.
I still had the altered sensation on my mouth though and i felt pretty exhausted. I also started having a pretty bad headache the more i came down, this prevented me from falling asleep.
I took a painkiller and a high dose of GHB to knock me out. This morning a woke up feeling like a wreck and i called in sick to work.

After my first time i thought i had found a new very enjoyable toy for me to play with, this second experience kicked my ass.
2CB is VERY powerfull when you take more then 20mg and it will turn into a full trip not just some cute visuals and giggles.
So be warned, if you want to try higher doses go in small steps and expect something alot more intense and deeper.
That being said, despite waking up still exhausted and having experienced a bad and dark episode this trip, i mentally felt ok. I'm at peace with the bad stuff and plan to use some of the advice to better my life.
After calling in sick and went back to bed and woke up fully rested and amazed with everything i went through. I still cant wait to try it again although i wont be taking 30mg's again for now hehehe
Next time will be 25mg hopefully with some weed.


grts,

sam
