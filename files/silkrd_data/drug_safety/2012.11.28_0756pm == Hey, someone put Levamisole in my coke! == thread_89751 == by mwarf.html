Post by: shadrock on November 28, 2012, 08:33 pm
It has been a while since I looked into the details, but I believe acetone does not remove levamisole, you have to use chloroform.

If I remember correctly cocaine is not soluble in acetone and neither is levamisole. There are other common cuts and fillers that are soluble in acetone though, so you can wash your coke with acetone and then let the coke dry.

To get rid of levamisole you'll need to use chloroform. Dissolve the coke in the chloroform, levamisole is not soluble in chloroform and will not dissolve. Then you filter out the things that haven't dissolved and then evaporate off the chloroform and then you've got clean coke.

For the price of coke it had damn well better not have any nasty shit in it to start with. To pay today's prices and then have to spend hours doing home chemistry is ridiculous. Just use a contaminant detection test and if the coke is bad never buy from that scumbag again.
