Title: Re: Best way to avoid spins at sleep time?
Post by: Ben on January 15, 2013, 02:54 am
As far as the choking hazard goes: Try not to fall asleep laying on your back. If you happen to pass out and throw up, that is the worst position to be in since there is no way for the vomit to go except for back down into your lungs. If you pass out laying on your side, chances are most of the vomit would leak from your mouth allowing some passage to breathe.

It's not that common for people to choke on vomit when using only alcohol though. The breathing reflex is quite strong, and will often force someone to shift position to facilitate breathing even if otherwise unable to stand upright and such. In combination with other substances this is another story entirely though, and accidents can and happen
