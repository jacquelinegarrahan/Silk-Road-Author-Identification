For one, I noticed I have a lingering "smell" in my nose that ONLY occurs on 25i. It is completely unique to 25i and I couldn't compare it to anything from real life either. Neither lsd nor shrooms leaves this smell. It comes out about 30 minutes after I dose and it pretty much indicates that my trip has started. The first few times I tried nbome, it was a fantastic aroma but it has become a little tiresome. At 500ug, it's not a big deal but when I dose at 1mg it's more intrusive to be honest. What could be causing this smell?

Also, has anyone checked their itouch/iphones while on it? For me, each icon on the homescreen appears to be wriggling like it's ready to be moved and the second I blink, the icons appear to be waving as if they were flags. I was jogging the other day and I pulled out my itouch to change the songs... but the entire screen looked 3D. Almost as if I were looking through glass. I stared at it for probably a good 10 minutes looking like an idiot on the trail haha. Meanwhile, the leaves on the trees around me were morphing colors from green to grey to blue and back while the branches seemed to be wriggling like arms. Shadows also seem to be appearing and disappearing as if the sun were gone from one second to another. These visuals have grown on me and I love seeing them whenever I dose.

Lastly, my gums seem to feel a little sensitive. If I press my tongue at the roof, it gives me a weird sensation.

Let me know if these effects ring a bell to anyone else (you know, since everybody is making 25i threads recently)
