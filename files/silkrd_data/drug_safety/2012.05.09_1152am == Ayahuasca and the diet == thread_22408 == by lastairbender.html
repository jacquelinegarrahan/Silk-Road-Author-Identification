The thing I'm wondering is, how necessary/desirable is the cleansing diet in the ~4 days leading up to the dose?

I'm not a very spiritual person (although that may very well change after the weekend haha!). I mainly rely on proven science and personal experience for determining my behaviour.

So I'm curious as to how much of this is to do with the - I don't want to say it but... - "hippy" aspect of this ritual and how much of it is SCIENTIFICALLY going to contribute to a more positive, enriching and intense Aya experience.
