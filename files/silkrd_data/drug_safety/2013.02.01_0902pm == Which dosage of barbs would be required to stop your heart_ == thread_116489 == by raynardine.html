lethal injection uses 2-5 grams of Sodium Pentothal but only to render the person unconscious.

then 100mg of Pancuronium bromide (Pavulon) is administered to paralyze the respiratory muscles and cause the person to asphyxiate. Pancuronium, is a non-depolarizing muscle relaxant (a paralytic agent) that blocks the action of acetylcholine at the motor end-plate of the neuromuscular junction. Binding of acetylcholine to receptors on the end-plate causes depolarization and contraction of the muscle fiber; non-depolarizing neuromuscular blocking agents like pancuronium stop this binding from taking place.

then 100 mEq (milliequivalents) of potassium chloride is administered to stop the heart.
