Title: Re: What other substances are in pills (ecstacy)

There are usually binders and fillers in tablets. If not, a 200 mg MDMA tablet should weigh no more than 200 mg - it may be possible to press mdma into tablets without any additives, though such pills would probably end up being hard, brittle and off-white.

Common additives in pill pressing are inactive substances like lactose and cellulose. There are used in real, big phamra produced, medications on a very wide scale as well. If the pills have any color then a colorant is used as well, obviously.

Obviously there are blatant fakes on the market as well, containing no ore little mdma, but other psychoactive subsances instead. Often these are substances that mimick the effects of mdma, but in some rare cases you can be in for an unpleasant surprise. I know of one case where 50 mg oxazepam tablets were coloured and then sold as xtc tablets on a festival. Tbe dealer in that case figured he could get away with that scam, since all hit victims would be sedated long enough for him to escape. This tactic worked, albeit only once.

Title: Re: What other substances are in pills (ecstacy)
Post by: AussieMitch on March 18, 2013, 02:34 pm
An ecstasy pill, along with the active ingredient, generally contains a few of the following:

Binder:
Microcrystalline Cellulose, Xantham Gum, Hydro Ethyl Cellulose and Lactose are the most common.

Lubricating agent:
Generally Magnesium Stearate is used to improve the flow of the mixture through the machine, although other lubricants are occasionally used instead

Coloring agent:
Generally Iron Oxide, Natrasole or a food-grade pigment.

Hardening agent:
Not generally needed but Copovidone is sometimes used to strengthen the tablets if the active ingredient is very crumbly.

Of course all these are what an experienced presser would use, the majority of pills around Australia are pressed by idiots who have no idea what they are doing and use shit like chalk in their pills. I can jump on a TDP-0 hand operated press with Xantham Gum, Stearate, Iron Oxide and an active ingredient and churn out better looking tablets than 90% of what's on the market at the moment.
