Post by: thyme on June 02, 2012, 09:26 am
The prescriptions and OTC you may be likely to take will make a difference.
Prescriptions can cross react - such as, say, Effexor/PCP - which can result in send-out and more sensitive screening.

A 12-panel catches more than 12 substances. Cross reaction of all of these would be many pages. I have the article, should you desire it. ?
Here's what's actually *on* the panel.
- methamphetamine
- amphetamine
- MDMA
- MDA

- Cannabis

- Cocaine (cocaine metabolites)
 
- Barbiturates

- Benzodiazepines

- Opiates (morphine/codeine/hydrocodone/hydromorphone/opium)
- Methadone
- Oxycodone (and oxymorphone)

- PCP

Let's start there...
