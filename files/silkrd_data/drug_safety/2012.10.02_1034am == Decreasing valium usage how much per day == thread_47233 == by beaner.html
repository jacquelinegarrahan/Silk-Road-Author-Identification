Hello!

Firstly serious kudos for taking this step, respect!

I did a bit of medical journal searching and got this http://www.benzo.org.uk/amisc/ashdiag.pdf
which recommends tapering your dose by 1/10 or 1/8 every one to two weeks -i think if you're younger and healthy you could try 1/8th every one week and see how you get on, but you'll have to adjust this accordingly to how you feel.

PLEASE keep in mind benzodiazepines have the most dangerous withdrawal sequale, including convulsions, psychosis, and a perpetual epileptic state, so if you notice shaking of your hands or just generally feeling not right slow down your tapering, as youre getting close to running the risk of those nasty neurological symptoms. In this instance having a short acting benzo like Xanax or Zolpidem will help, i appreciate this may seem a step in the wrong direction but you must be careful.

also avoid caffeine and codeine, as theres evidence to suggest it lowers your seizure threshold.

Good luck!
