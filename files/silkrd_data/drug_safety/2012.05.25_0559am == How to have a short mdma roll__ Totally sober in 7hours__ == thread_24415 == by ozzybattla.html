Title: Re: How to have a short mdma roll?? Totally sober in 7hours??
Post by: Appa on May 25, 2012, 06:17 am
The best ways I can think of to speed up the experience would be to get it working as soon as possible (so don't cap it, just eat/drink the crystal straight, parachute it, or snort it if that's your thing), and don't take as much as you would expect to take for a solid roll.  If you snort it, you'll need even less, and it would probably be one of the fastest methods with a quick, intense come-up.  Not for everyone.  If you eat Ivory's (I'm not familiar with Pf's), I agree with Knomo that you should take about 70% of your normal dose (which I would define more broadly, maybe in the range of 1.5-2mg/kg) for a faster, though lighter, experience.
