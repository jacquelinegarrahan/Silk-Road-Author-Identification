How does the testing?
Pills
Pills For we have a recognition system . On the basis of the appearance of a pill (including diameter, thickness, color and logo) and an acid test to see whether the pill prevents the recognition list. This list includes pills in the past period several times in the laboratory were analyzed. The list is updated weekly. If your pill on this list, you get to hear the results. That is to say, which substance (s) is in it, and how much. If the pill is not recognized, you can use the pill to be sent to the laboratory.

Powders, capsules and liquids
In order to determine exactly which substances are present in it and in what quantities, these drugs always forwarded to the laboratory. For powders and capsules there is an immediate acid test is done to get an indication to what means it goes. Of powders, we have a little less than 1/10 gram (80 mg) are required for analysis in the laboratory.

The results
after a week you can call for the results. You get the full laboratory results. For some drugs (GHB and LSD) takes longer (2 to 4 weeks).

Conditions for testing
at the test and recreational drugs information center will also give details about the various (entertainment) drugs.
You can get tested resources such as ecstasy, cocaine, speed, GHB, LSD. The following resources can not be tested: mushrooms, drugs, hashish and marijuana, steroids and raw materials for drugs.

We test only for consumers. We did not test for dealers, parents or police.
You remain anonymous, we note only information about means, not about individuals.
You may up to three times per drug samples tested.
For powders, a maximum of 1 g and 10 ml for liquids. If you exceed the above quantities with you, we test not for you.
We test any ingredients that can be used for drug production.
The test result can not legally binding.
The tester, the participating organization and the laboratory can not be held liable for the consequences of the use of any drug.

Self-testing
in smart shops and via the Internet, self-testing (eg EZ Test and Dope Test on sale). In these tests, it is a color test, based on the Marquis-test. It is not possible on the basis of this test a reliable judgment on the contents of a pill. The color reaction is merely an indication of the content and gives no information about the amount of a substance.
The great danger of a self-test is that the test has a false appearance of certainty can give.

Pills that higher amounts of substances to give a self often confusing or misleading results. This danger exists especially with pills next to a XTC-like substance (MDMA, MDEA, MDA) another undesirable substance. Examples of such substances from the past, strychnine (rat poison) and PMA.

The only way to the actual content of a pill to find out, it is tested at an official testing services on this site. These testing services also use the Marquis test, but only as an indication. In a testing service allows you to get complete results on the contents of a pill (or another drug).
