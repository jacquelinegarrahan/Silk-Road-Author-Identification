I find that if I'm using MDMA fairly regularly I started to get big blank spots in my memory. I think the stuff really plays with your memory and has to be treated with a lot of respect. It's treated like aspirin by a lot of people (in the UK especially!) and has an undeserved reputation for being harmless.
