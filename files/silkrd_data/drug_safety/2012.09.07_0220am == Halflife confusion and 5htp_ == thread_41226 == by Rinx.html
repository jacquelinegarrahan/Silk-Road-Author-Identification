Half life is the time it takes for your body to break down 50% of the original dose. For MDMA the half life is about 6-10 hours, which means that after you dropped the first xtc pill (say a 100mg one) 6-10 hours later there is 50mg left over in your body..
So every time you re-dose the 6-10 hours start all over again for that dose.

400 mg is quite a high dose if you ask me, if I'm not mistaken the recommended dose is around 1.5mg/kg bodyweight.. So 14 hours later there is still a rather high amount in your blood.
