Post by: madamebradley on December 21, 2011, 07:45 pm
Start using an antibiotic ointment like Polysporin onto the wound. It looks like it's just on the surface so if there is an infection you don't seem to be in that bad of shape.

The redness could be caused by using rubbing alcohol and scrubbing the area, both of which I recommend you stop doing. Using isopropyl alcohol to clean a wound initially is okay, but not repeatedly. I'd also stop brushing it and let it develop scar tissue.

The signs you should be looking out for are localized redness, heat, swelling and pain. A couple of those symptoms might be caused by your treatment regime so if these symptoms continue to exist or get worse then you should see a doctor.

Edit: It should go without saying that you should stop using that area to IV until it heals. Make sure to use a fresh needle each time, sterilize everything, and rotate veins.
