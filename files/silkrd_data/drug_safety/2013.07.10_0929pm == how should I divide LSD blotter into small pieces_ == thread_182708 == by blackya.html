Title: Re: how should I divide LSD blotter into small pieces?
Post by: Rastaman Vibration on July 11, 2013, 03:58 am
Hey @blackya, I've been microdosing with LSD bloter and cutting it the following way:

I used an X-Acto knife, medium thick blade. First I scored the blotter into however many pieces I'm trying to cut. I cut mine into 16ths but this is totally up to you. Then I will cut a piece off as needed. That way I dont have to mess with 16 little tiny squares; it all stays together and I just cut one off and eat it.

When cutting a piece off I just cut along the scored lines. You only have to make 2 cuts at any time, so its not too difficult. I just repeat the stroke slowly along the cut lines until the knife cuts all the way through. If you do it too fast or use too much force, you run the risk of sending a little tiny LSD square flying through your house. Trust me, they're a bitch to find ;) so I just prefer to go slow and deliberate.

I hope this helps!
