Title: Re: Advice on drying wet coke!
Post by: Raoul Duke on June 21, 2013, 12:36 pm
get it out of the bag. scrape the contents onto a plate/mirror/anything that isn't a sponge

if you want to cane it any time soon use a hot plate - stick a plate in the microwave for 30-40 seconds depending on the power of the microwave, then dump the coke on it (AFTER the plates hot - don't nuke your gear!). dries out into choppable/snortable powder quick enough. careful when chopping it though as it can get a bit crunchy and could ping everywhere

if you aren't planning on using it for a while as jack says use a lamp
