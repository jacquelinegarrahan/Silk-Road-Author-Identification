Moksha,

To answer your question, No not really, pre and post-loading does not mean you can take MDMA with less breaks. The goal of pre and post loading is not to shorten intervals between usage, but rather to mitigate or prevent any potential damage that can result from MDMA use. 5-HTP is a source of debate right now in the psychopharmacology community ( which I am a part of, so hopefully that maybe helps my credentials) as there has been confounding and conflicting evidence about its effectiveness in "restoring" serotonin reserves. Serotonin is the key neurotransmitter involved with MDMA and it will always "regenerate" pretty much at a fixed rate, regardless of what you take. Unless you've got some sort of neuromuscular illness like Parkinson's or have destroyed your brain with synthetic opioids, your personal serotonin stashes will come back at the same rate.  As for your diet vs occasional supplement intake inquiry, it depends. A multivitamin and foods & drinks high in anti-oxidative properties should be part of your diet anyways as it's just a matter of maintaining decent health, something those of us who engage in illicit drug use should ensure anyways. As for magnesium and 5-HTP, it seems you will be alright with taking them for a week before your planned roll and a week after your roll. For the few times I have rolled I always make it a priority to have a mug of green tea, a multivitamin, a bottle of Gatorade in my system beforehand or during if I am out for the night. LSD is a funny chemical too, depending on individual body chemistry, tolerance can diminish as quickly as yours. What I report is just averages from what little clinical research has been done and an amalgamation of all the trip reports I have read and taken note of. Hope this helped and if you have any other questions, please ask.
