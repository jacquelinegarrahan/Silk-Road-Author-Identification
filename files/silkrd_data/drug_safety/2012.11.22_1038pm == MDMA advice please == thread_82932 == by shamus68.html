Dose like what Ballzinator said...1.5mg of MDMA per Kg in body weight.  Then redose at one hour and a half into the experience with half that amount.  DO Not REDOSE if you are taking MDA.  MDA redosing really really hurts your brain.  Don't redose more than once or you will be hurting your brain more.  And do not mix with adderall or cocaine.  My experience with both combinations has lead me to believe that they lessen the experience.  Cocaine sobers you out of rolling, so don't do it.   8)   

LSD mixes well with MDMA...2cb, 2ci, 2ce etc. those mix well however you should roll first then take one of the RC's of the 2c family, not the other way around.  Shrooms also pairs nicely with MDMA, and the rule follows with that too...roll first then take shrooms.  The halucinogenic you take will happily bring you from rolling to tripping.  But Shrooming then rolling will just kill your shroom trip dead, and hinder you from really feeling the MDMA 

Snorting MDMA is fine, but it shortens the duration of the trip.  Pull apart some Valerian Root capsules or something and load them up with some Molly...have fun!
