I've done so many searches and I thought maybe I got a dirty hit, but I can't find any real information on that and someone hacked saferinjecting.net so that's no help, and I'm guessing it wasn't cotton fever because it didn't last very long. I think the whole thing lasted 5 minutes. I was thinking maybe I'm a little allergic to morphine, maybe in "high" doses at once? But I take all other opioids IV fine (H, oxyC, OxyM, hydroM).
