Title: Re: 25C NBOMe: What the hell is this?
Post by: l1llykins on August 09, 2012, 01:33 am
As long as you're not new to tripping (doesn't sound like you are) and you're  in a good mindspace when you start, I'd say you're fine to do this one alone. Just start with a small dose and work your way up. 300ug is pretty safe, 600ug is a solid trip.

If you're prone to nausea or stomach upset, preload with some drammamine and magnesium an hour or two before dosing.
