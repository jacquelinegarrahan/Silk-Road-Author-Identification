Title: Re: High doses LSD/Psilocybin
Post by: AnOn.edu on September 22, 2012, 06:03 am
I also prefer lone wolfing it.  Not even for the paranoia but for the journey.  I personally don't see an issue with those amounts, though it could get a little interesting.

Make sure to have your benzos handy and if you're doing it at home make sure to remove your stash and equipment in case you go a little crazy and 5-0 gets involved.  I'm all for pushing back the bounds of insanity but make sure you have some rules in place, for example hide your keys in a way you can't find them (driving on a 5+ grams of shrooms is a very bad idea, and I've done it) and know what your physical boundaries are.  If you put some fear & emotion behind your rules as prep you will be less likely to break 'em.
