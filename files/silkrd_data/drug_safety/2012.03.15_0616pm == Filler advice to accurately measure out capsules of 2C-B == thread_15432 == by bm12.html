Title: Re: Filler advice to accurately measure out capsules of 2C-B
Post by: Appa on March 15, 2012, 08:47 pm
Yeah, I like to measure volumetrically as well.  But if you want to use capsules, I would just put the chemical in there directly, no filler.  If they ask, just say you used no filler and point out how amazing it is that such a small amount of powder will facilitate such an experience.

As for measuring, you really need a mg scale, 1/100 isn't enough for 2c-b.  The error is too big.  Even with a mg scale, for precise dosing like this, adding a bit of weight to the scale should help keep things more accurate.  Weigh the capsule, then fill it up until it's X mg heavier.

Really, you can't go wrong with volumetric measuring, using distilled water since 2c-b is soluble in water.

Title: Re: Filler advice to accurately measure out capsules of 2C-B
Post by: wowzers on March 15, 2012, 09:39 pm
Haha, I literally have just finished making some 8mg 2C-B capsules from 500mg of EuroPEA's powder, took such a long time!

Last night, I dissolved 500mg 2C-B in the minimum amount of hot Ph Eur 70% IPA and made the volume up to 6mL with more of the solvent.

Then I weighed out 60x 200mg portions of mannitol. Then to each pile I added 0.1 mL of the solution using a microsyringe and allowed it to dry under a lamp.

Today I capped up each pile into 00 gelatine capsules (cheap as anything from eBay) which was pretty arduous. Testing tomorrow night.
8mg caps are nice as you can fine tune the dosage for friends.


**edit- just read the bit about you not being able to use glucose. You can lay 2C-B on blotter at a loading of around 10mg on a 12x12mm square, using 70% IPA. You have to apply the solution to the blotter paper, let it dry under a lamp for an hour then reapply on the other side. A few free samples usually helps sway sceptics :D
