MDMA is not soluble in acetone, which is why it is good for washings. However, personally I think an extraction/recrystallization would be your best bet, since I'm not sure how well acetone would work on the mixture you have there. So do the following:

1. Dump your solid mixture into a container that you can pour liquid into
2. Heat up some IPA (grocery store, 91%, make it anhydrous by baking some MgSO4 (epsom salts) and using it as a drying agent)
3. Pour a little acetone into the IPA until a cloudy mixture is formed
4. Pour The IPA/acetone mixture into the container with the solid mixture in it (make sure it the container can handle the high temp)
5. Gravity filter whatever did not dissolve (the crap you don't want)
6. Let sit 30min at room temp for recrystallization to occur.
7. Filter (keep the remaining liquid)
8. Wash crystals with acetone
9. Throw remaining liquid in the freezer for a couple of hours to recover a second crop of crystals
10. You've now got your crystals, free of dirt, AND they're more pure than when you originally got them!
11. By the way, that second crop should be washed. It is supposedly less pure.
