Why are people suggesting powerful psychedelics?

The whole point of safely tripping is being in a secure mental state whilst doing it. Otherwise negative feelings will manifest. This can be healing to some extent but at the same time it's not for everyone.

If you do decide to go down the psychedelic route, I would suggest going to Peru and do the proper ayahuasca in which you are guided through your past and emotions etc.

The only drug which will make you not care is H or other strong opiates. I'd seriously advise against them though, espescially if your depressed and having identity issues etc. you're at great risk of becoming heavily addicted. Also no drug will be a substitute for therapy. They only deal with the problem in the short term, they are not a cure.

I hope it all works out.
