I know little of its effects, but it was one of the first drugs Shulgin ever invented. At the time it was the second most potent psychedelic known to science (after LSD), and for a period it was commonly used in biochemical and neurological research. It was a (if not the) source of 'bad acid' in the late 60s, but it seems worthwhile if you have the time for it.

a newbie user "norvillerogers" sent me a message describing his DOM experiences so i thought id post it here,

"I've tried DOM on a few occasions. First time I took DOM i took one hit that had a 3micro gram drop on it (very bitter, not like acid). I proceeded to see My Bloody Valentine in 3D (terrible remake, terrible movie, just terrible). I felt barely anything. Then I forgot about the rest of the sheet i bought, and two years later i found it. I decided to save it for just a little longer as i was goin back to school and had my own place. First week back i think "hey, I'll pop some before bed and wake up trippin". I took 6. never was able to get to bed either out of excitement or just not tired. an hour later I think I'll take another two cause its old and i figured the dose dissipated some. It didnt. in about three hours i start my trip, and being alone in my dark room i start to drift. Soon enough I am engulfed by very opaque colors (like the hippie color burst design, but fainter in coloring). I am completely melted into my bed, and i just roll around in it for seemingly hours. Then of course i forget that it is synthesized with amphetamine properties so not only am i trippin but i am high on energy. unfortunately that does work well with the mind. i've candyflipped and hippie flipped and even a concoction me and my roommate later came up with: SLEDding (Shroom, LSD, ecstasy, and DOM), and these were no where near as crazy on the brain as this was. The only thing I could compare it to would be when i dropped 30mg of 2C-I. Taking this high of a dose (8 hits) of DOM was so much that I never even thought to listen to music. I was so far gone in my own head i couldnt work my phone. SO, the moral of this whole story is, don't do too much and just cause you can handle LSD or other combinations it does not mean you can handle this. The amphetamine rush you get is enough to set you off on a bad trip. DOM is also known as STP...Serenity, Tranquility, and Peace. So it is easy to get lost in what you do, where you are...more so than LSD. Have a plan of attack before you take it. No two psychs are the same. :)"
