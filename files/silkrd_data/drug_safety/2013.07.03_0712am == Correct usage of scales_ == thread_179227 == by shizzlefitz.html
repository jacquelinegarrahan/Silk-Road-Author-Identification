Title: Correct usage of scales?
Post by: shizzlefitz on July 03, 2013, 07:12 am
I have a digital scale that measures to 0.01g which should be alright for weighing out DMT hits. How do you guys weigh out powder without wasting it on the scoop (I'm using a nail file) and then wasting it again on the scale bed? The powder sticks to each and gets messy.
Title: Re: Correct usage of scales?
Post by: psychedelicmind on July 03, 2013, 07:57 am
What I do is fold a tiny piece of paper in half, put that on the scale and press 'TARE'. Pressing 'Tare' brings the scale back to 0.00g. Then I weigh out 0.05g (+/- 50mg), scooping it onto the paper. Transferring it from the paper to the pipe/vaporizer is easy as the fold in the paper serves as a miniature spoon.

Also, I wouldn't use a nail file, as they are coarse, so would leave some DMT stuck to them. I use the small side of a medicine spoon that comes with children's cough bottle. Always works a treat for me ;)

Hope this helps ;)

Title: Re: Correct usage of scales?
Post by: Luvs2Trip on July 03, 2013, 08:29 am
To save me from losing anything I get a king size rizla and twist both ends so it makes a kind of boat shape. I then rip of one of the twists leaving a boat shape with an opening at one end. I then place that on the scales and press 'Tare' (Or you can place on the scales before you turn them on which will provide the same result as tare). Finally I pour my powder into the rizla at the weight i want. I can then pick up the rizla by the twist i left on and pour the powder out of where i ripped the other twist off (into a bag or on a tray or where ever you want to put the powder)! I don't lose anything doing it this way!

Hope this makes sense!
