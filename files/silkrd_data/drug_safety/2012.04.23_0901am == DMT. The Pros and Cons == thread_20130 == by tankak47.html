Quote from: tankak47 on April 23, 2012, 09:01 am
1. What are the different types of DMT and why? is one better than the other?
DMT is a chemical (N,N-dimethyltryptamine, specifically).  There aren't really "different types" as much as different purities and different ways of consuming.

Quote from: tankak47 on April 23, 2012, 09:01 am
2. What are the methods of consumption? does it make a difference?
Smoking (vaporizing, really) is probably the most common method, and is the method I recommend to beginners.  It has a somewhat short duration when smoked (<20 minutes, usually), and is generally very intense and quick to come on.  DMT can be vaporized by laying the crystal between a bed of herbs (or ash), with a globe (crack) pipe, or with other devices (research "the machine" if interested).  There's also the option of smoking DMT that has been laid onto some sort of herb; this is generally called "changa."
DMT can also be taken orally, but it is normally destroyed by monoamine oxidase, so it must be eaten with some sort of MAOI.  This is often what ayahuasca is, though there are many varieties of ayahuasca containing many different plant alkaloids.  Oral dosing will be significantly longer acting than vaporizing (a few hours), but less intense and a slower come up.  You could also administer it via injection (IV or IM) if you so desired, but that's more for a clinical setting with an experienced injector/sitter (you'd be gone before you could get the needle out).

Quote from: tankak47 on April 23, 2012, 09:01 am
3. How much is enough for a single persons first time?
~20mg might be a reasonable first time dose (smoked).  See erowids DMT dosage page (erowid.org/chemicals/dmt/dmt_dose.shtml) for more information.

Quote from: tankak47 on April 23, 2012, 09:01 am
4. Where should it be done?(I.E. - Alone, with friends, etc.)
It should be done in a comfortable, familiar, safe setting that you can lie down in, with a friend who is experienced in psychedelics.  That friend should be someone who won't freak out when you lay there acting all weird, won't try to bug you while you're deep in the trip, and will make sure you're safe or talk you down if necessary.

Quote from: tankak47 on April 23, 2012, 09:01 am
5. What should I expect to feel?
It's not easily describable, so I'll leave that to others.  Erowid's vault of experience reports is a gold mine, though (erowid.org/experiences/subs/exp_DMT.shtml).

Quote from: tankak47 on April 23, 2012, 09:01 am
6. anything else I should know?
It's not what you expect it to be.  It will be intense.  It will be moving.  Don't go into it light-heartedly.  Respect the strength of this chemical, and it will afford you great experiences.
Also, invest in a good, sensitive mg scale.  There's not much difference between 10 and 60mg...
