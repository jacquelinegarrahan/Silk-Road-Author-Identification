I AM NOT PREACHING.   8)

However, for those asking questions about taking a new drug for the first time---don't go BALLS OUT---there's no need to. Everyone's body reacts different--and just ramp up as you learn to enjoy the experience. My 6'2--300lb buddy (because he does EVERYTHING in excess--his first time he dropped 2-hits..not wise for a first timer...but he thought he was not tripping (when mine kicked in)--because he needed "more" because of his "Size' (doesn't work that way).  And he was BALLS OUT tripping---THANK GOD---stuff did not go side ways with him----none of us could have controlled him. 

I'd hate to hear so and so overdosed...and think about it---if it's your first time, do you really want a Cop and a Priest showing up at your Parents house to inform them you're at the Morgue from an overdose....at lest me...I don't want my Pop having that as his last memory of me.

I'm 100% pro Legalization. I think it's CRIMINAL Marijuana is more "outlawed" than Cocaine (I was given Cocaine for a Nose Job--as an anesthetic)---just STUPID Marijuana is. It's just my "nature" to be a kind,caring, "look out for someone"--kind of guy. AGAIN, *NOT* preaching---just my advice..take it for what it is
(and DEFINITELY know what kind of "supplies" you need whlle Partying--lots of Water with MDMA---something "special" on a Trip (you know, Music you love that can chill you out if you start having a bad trip)--doing other "hard core" stuff...probably best to stay away from the public so you don't get yourself int a fight

Heck first 5-times I tried to smoke pot (in High School)--Private school Dorm--my roommate had to "teach me"--he said the only thing he could think is I was not inhaling enough...So, I'd go outside and smoke it in our Garage--And I'd puff out my cheeks---as long as I could hold it (like that Trumpet Player dude)---and finally got stoned

So, just know a lot of times you might get ripped off, or wont work as expected. Don't use that as an excuse to double/triple up the next time you take something.

Ironically, the only good Ecstasy I've ever had was in Dallas, TX (it was one of the states it was legal the longest---so can get some good stuff from there)---best trip ever.     8)
