Title: Re: Modalert by Sun Pharma 200mg Modafinil
Post by: fiveotwo on May 09, 2013, 01:17 am
The effectiveness varies from person to person, people with a val/val genotype as opposed to a met/met genotype will experience the effects more strongly.  100mg is a good starter, 200mg is fine if you can't be bothered splitting, you may feel a little uncomfortable at this level though.  I don't think any route other than oral has worthwhile benefits.  Have not experienced jaw clenching personally.  Even if you wanted you wouldn't use them to "get high".  But I have heard of people obsessively re-dosing occasionally.  The effects are much more subtle than you will likely anticipate, but they are great for allowing your body to simply "forget" it's tired for 8 hours or so.
