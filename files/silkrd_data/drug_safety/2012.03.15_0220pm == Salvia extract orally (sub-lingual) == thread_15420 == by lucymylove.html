I have a great amount of respect for Salvia.

First, to address your question, most likely it would not work with such a small amount. The quid method (chewing and holding the salvia leaves works; however, that is due to the number of leaves and the length of time).  The small amount of extract would start to deteriorate quickly - too quick to get anything off of it.

The sub-lingual method works with salvia tincture (fantastic, btw).  I've heard that it is much, much better than smoking as the entire trip can last up to three to five hours vs. the short period when smoked.

Also, if your friend does smoke it make sure they use a torch lighter.  In order to activate Saliva needs an extremely high temp.

Good luck and let us know how it goes.
