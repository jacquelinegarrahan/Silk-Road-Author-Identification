Title: Re: smoking opium?
Post by: Ben on September 25, 2013, 12:45 am
You could do all that, but the old fashioned way to smoke opium does use a pipe, heating the opium over the flame of an oil lamp. While we have no opium dens as such anymore, i suppose using something like a lighter and a crack pipe would actually be an easy replacement.

Using opium this way goes back a long time, and predates all problems with people injecting the stuff and suffering the severe consequences of that. As I recall from the notes of an explorer about Laos: "it is easier to smoke an opium pipe here than it is to obtain a glass of beer".

By now this is no longer the case however, as Laos has banned opiates but also brews the excellent beer Lao, famous throughout indochina.
