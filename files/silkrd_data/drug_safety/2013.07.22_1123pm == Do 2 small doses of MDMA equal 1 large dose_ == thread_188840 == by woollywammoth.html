If you don't or haven't used MDMA much, then taking half of a 145mg pill would still produce a pleasurable effect but it probably wouldn't be quite the full therapeutic effect of taking at least 100mg. I have a friend who took 140mg once at home and had a good experience but nothing that blew the hinges off. The same friend took 140mg of the same batch at a festival and had to sit down because of how intense it became (he said it was almost a bit too much). Keep in mind that setting matters, the energy around you like at a fesitval can greatly enhance the feeling.

Also keep in mind that MDMA dosing is not linear. 140mg may have been too intense because it was just a few mg more than your sweet spot. Just 10mg less at 130mg could be you perfect dose, you really just have to try a few different dosages to find out, but starting lower is typically a better way of doing this. Finding the sweet spot is difficult, but I consider it to be the dosage where the euphoria is maximized while the impairment is minmized. For most people who are new to mdma this is between 100-120mg.

My suggestion would be to take 3/4th of the 145mg pill, then about 1.5 hours later take the other 1/4. That means you'd have approx. 110mg to start, then 35mg as a booster at the 1.5 hour mark.
