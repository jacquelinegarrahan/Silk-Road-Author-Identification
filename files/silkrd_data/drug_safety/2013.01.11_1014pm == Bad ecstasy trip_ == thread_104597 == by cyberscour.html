Title: Re: Bad ecstasy trip?
Post by: DiamondSky on January 11, 2013, 10:48 pm
Bad ecstasy trips are very similar to bad orgasms... haven't had one yet but I guess it's possible.

It's important to understand that MDMA is more often than not wildly euphoric but it's still your brain so you do get to steer the trip a bit. I used to take MDMA with a girlfriend and we would love it up and talk about how great everything was. Eventually though towards the end of our relationship she would steer the conversations towards what was wrong with us and that just sucked the joy right out of everything. I think getting to a negative place while being on MDMA just requires a bit more effort than a lot of other drugs out there.

If you haven't done a lot of mood altering substances in the past one of the golden rules is that the difference between a bad time and a good time is the conviction of thought that declares "I am going to enjoy this thing that is happening to me right now". As long as you beleive it it's going to be true.
