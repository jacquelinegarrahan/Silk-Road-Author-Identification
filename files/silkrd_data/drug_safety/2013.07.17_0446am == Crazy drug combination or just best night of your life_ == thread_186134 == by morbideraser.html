Title: Re: Crazy drug combination or just best night of your life?
Post by: National Direct on July 17, 2013, 04:59 am
That's a lot of substances that can have unforeseen interactions. Benzos are HIGHLY recommmended if you intend to mix the substances mentioned.

If you are new to any of these substances, DO NOT mix them, as you may experience an allergic reaction, learn that you are deficient in an enzyme required to metabolise one of the said substances. Be careful with cocktails, and never attempt shaking or stirring one up without extensive knowledge and experience. The fact that you're posting this tells me that you should second think your plans.

I have a  lot of experience with cocktails, but more on the narcotics side of things, not psychedelics/dissociatives (although MDMA and K created pseudo hallucinations that made the night sky look 10x nicer than the northern lights could ever appear).

IMO LSD is liable to be destroyed if you mix it with mushrooms, so appreciate the LSD on its own (I love lsd, but don't like shrooms unless I'm drunk; very unstable for me, whereas acid is very stable after you passs the come-up).

I would take the mdma and lsd together, as they make a great combo, but leave the K out... that can really fuck your mind up with the synergy of M and LSD. The DMT should be fine as well. It's short lived and there are plenty of reports of the combo.
