Title: Re: How To perform cocaine purification?
Post by: tinytim on January 29, 2012, 01:56 am
I have been studying this a lot recently.  I am doing a review on all the major coke vendors and need to get an objective measurement of  purity.

There are lots of options online, but this one is by far the most precise:

(dont copy this exactly - look it up elsewhere, my instructions are just pseudocode):

1) Cook the coke in to freebase (which basically means put it in a simmering spoon with baking soda).  this turns  cocaine HCL to cocaine (base)

2) chop it up super fine and dissolve in water.  The (free)base cocaine will not dissolve but amphetamines and ephedrine will

3) filter that out, keeping the solid (which is freebase cocaine)

4) after drying, stick it in some 99.9% acetone (do not use 98.5% acetone, you will lose a lot of coke if you do).

5) add some muratic acid (aka hydrochloric acid) and wait for the coke to recrystalize

6) At this point you should have cocaine crystals


This pretty much filters out everything except for levamisole.  I'm still working on figuring that one out (see my other post asking for a chemist).
