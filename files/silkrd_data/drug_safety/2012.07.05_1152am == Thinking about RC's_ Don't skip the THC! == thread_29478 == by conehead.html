Title: Thinking about RC's? Don't skip the THC!
Post by: conehead on July 05, 2012, 11:52 am
I've taken 2ci-nbome several times in the past month or two. Each time I had a very strong experience, but I was taking anywhere from 2 to 6mg. 1mg didn't even seem to have any effect outside of some slight color exaggeration.

Yesterday I took 1mg of 2ci-nbome combined with a high dose of THC from some bubble hash. I took 3 large hits of the bubble hash and the experience is incredible. I certainly do not recommend people do RC's. They are probably bad for you. But if you ARE going to do them, I highly recommend waiting until you have some pot available before you dose. It's 100x better on THC. The open eye visuals are much more profound and the entire experience is more imursive. Before I felt that 2ci-nbome was purely visual apart from the light speed/cardio effects (which results in hypertension for me.) The combination is incredible. I had heard this before, but I felt it the experience warranted repeating.
