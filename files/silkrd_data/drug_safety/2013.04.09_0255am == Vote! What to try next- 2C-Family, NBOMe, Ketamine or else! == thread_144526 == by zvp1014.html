Post by: curiositymatrix on April 10, 2013, 02:22 am
Well, I'll answer according to which ones you've listed that I have experience with, and suggest one you'll like

25i has been compared to candy-flipping, in that it has a very speedy, energetic component. It meshes AMAZINGLY with weed, changing the high you get into something far trippier. When tripping on 25i, the visuals are moderate, but if I then get high, they get really intense, full-on "acid trip" visuals.
 In fact, I'd say having weed with 25i makes it 5-6 times better, because if you feel yourself getting stuck in recursive or stressful thoughts, a toke will lift you right out.

25b has been described as less stressful, more pleasant (much like 2C-B compared to 2C-I, which in fact, they are the direct NBOME's of), and though I havn't had it myself, I'm inclined to suggest it first, from what I've read- especially since you say you aren't too keen on feeling "amped"

DXM: Interesting, sort of a drunk / lurch-y high, felt like it turned my brains ability to process on its side; overall pretty fun. Definitely "disjointed"
However, it did leave me feeling somewhat drained the next day, to the point that I don't really take it anymore.

I would suggest aMT! Given your description of what you're looking for, its perfect. It's around $5-10 a hit, and has been very favorably compared with LSD - often cited as being like it, but more relaxed / empathetic.

Salvia will make you leave your body, but everyone has mixed results to it. I seem to find a correlation between people who enjoy salvia, and those who really enjoy hallucinogens, so you might want to give it a try.
