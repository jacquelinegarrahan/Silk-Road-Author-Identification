Post by: Oompaloompa on February 22, 2013, 02:34 pm
Think you mean LSD, not LCD - seriously don't eat a Liquid Crystal Display ;)

2C-B is good & well worth trying, much smoother & more forgiving than other 2Cs. While the 2Cs are still probably widely considered RCs or Research Chemicals they've now been around for about 30 years & there's a lot known about them. Check out Erowid & you'll see how much info there is. If you're interested in trying a psychedelic other than shrooms or LSD I'd definitely go for 2C-B or one of the other widely used common RCs (2C-x, DMT, 5-Meo-DiPT, etc) rather than some of the newer ones.

For your first time I'd definitely go with one of the classic psychedelics though, shrooms may be best as for me my 1st LSD trip was a bit of a let down - expectations too high & little effect. If you try shrooms don't expect to get far on 1g though 3.5g dried (P. Cubensis) is the standard for most peple I think.
