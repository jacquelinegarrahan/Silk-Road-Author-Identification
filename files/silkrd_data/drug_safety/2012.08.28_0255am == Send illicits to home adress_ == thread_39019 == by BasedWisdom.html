Title: Re: Send illicits to home adress?
Post by: LaSirene on August 28, 2012, 09:15 pm
I am much happier sending things to my home address than risking a work address or having to sign up for a PO box and provide ID.
The sensible thing, as others have said, is to keep the orders small and to read feedback from other buyers regarding packaging. I personally use different names for each vendor with my home address, because we get a lot of junk mail here from previous tenants anyway I just feel more comfortable with that than using my own name.

Another thing is to read up on which countries are an auto-flag for your customs - I know that where I am from anything being sent from India usually gets seized as a lot of online pharmacies operate from there and send to here.
Good luck :)
