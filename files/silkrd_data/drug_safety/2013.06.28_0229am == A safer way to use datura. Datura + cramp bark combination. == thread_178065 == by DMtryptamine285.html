Title: A safer way to use datura. Datura + cramp bark combination.
Post by: DMtryptamine285 on June 28, 2013, 02:29 am
We all should know datura and other tropane containing plants are very dangerous poisons. Datura contains the alkaloids atropine, scopolamine, and hyoscyamine, all of which are anticholinergics (meaning the block the action of one of the bodies regulatory neurotransmittors called acetylcholine.) These compounds essentially systemically shut down high brain functions, completely blocking memory formation, and causing very realistic hallucinations that are indistinguishable from reality, not to mention causing quite a body load and in some cases death.

Cramp bark contains scopoletin which potentiates acetylcholine release from synaptosomes, amplifies hippocampal long-term potentiation and ameliorates anticholinergic impaired memory. In addition scopoletin alters and potentiates the visual effects of datura alkaloids. This combination allows one to experience the positive dreamy effects of datura at extremely low 10-15 seed doses while eliminating all of the possible side effects.

Many users, and myself included, have noticed quite an amazing effect from this combination. Its not like psychedelic visuals or even delerient visuals like one would normally experience with datura. Its like fading into a lucid dream where the world feels entirely surreal but yet one is able to maintain sober cognition if you have to. Its like I could navigate in and out of the state at will, very relaxing too.
