Title: 25i nbome - mind fuck for you?
Post by: Meister on May 29, 2012, 02:50 pm
I had never taken, or heard of, 25i nbome before SR. Plenty of experience with other psychedelics but I got some of kwiktrips 25i nbome a couple weeks ago and my first trip (4x 550ug tabs) was great, I actually preferred it to LSD and until LSD prices come down I think I'll stick with 25i. However, everything I read about others experiences with 25i nbome was that they didn't have a mind fuck, I went into the trip thinking I wouldn't, and was wrong and had a very intense mind fuck early on in the trip.

I thought perhaps it was because it was my first time with the drug, and my first times with LSD and shrooms were intense as well. This weekend I did another trip (4x 550ug tabs, another 2 tabs a couple hours into the trip) and it was a great experience, both visually and mentally, but I just don't see how anyone can say the mind fuck component isn't there as it's quite prevalent to me.

Visually it was top notch, surpassed any LSD trip I ever had which is impressive, but mentally it was a roller coaster, each 'trip wave' being either extremely calming and relaxed or overwhelming and anxiety causing. At one point I broke down into tears crying, probably the first time I've cried in I don't know how many years.

So I'm curious if I'm in the minority and 25i nbome is purely visual like many claim it to be, or if others have had mind fucks identical to LSD as well.
