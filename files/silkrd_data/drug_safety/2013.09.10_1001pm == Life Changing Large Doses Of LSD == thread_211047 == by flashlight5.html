Title: Re: Life Changing Large Doses Of LSD
Post by: busta999 on September 11, 2013, 08:26 am
Acid is a tool not a miracle cure. It won't do all the work for you of changing your outlook or ways. It works way better when you are already putting serious effort into unsticking yourself.
Set and setting has long been known plays a huge part in the experience - set being the mindset with which you approach the trip.

Additionally there is no guarantee that such an experience will be at all pleasurable. You never know what comes up and if the life situation you are trying to unstick is a severe situation or condtion chances are that much higher to run into seriously disturbing stuff or triggering an even more severe condition.

That said the really transformative experiences do usually occur on stronger dosages of 300ug - 500ug and frequently not until one has several trips under the belt. It is not uncommon that it takes some trips before one is able to allow oneself to surrender to the experience.

Only few users have the gods talking to them, dissolve their personality or merge with the universe until after 5 - 10 trips. Patience, determination and large doses will get most there.
