Title: Re: LSD based advice
Post by: microRNA on March 02, 2012, 06:48 am
If you generally enjoy drawing or painting etc, I would have some paper and supplies out and easily accessible. Once tripping even digging through a closet for some markers can seem like an utterly daunting task at some more intense points.

For example, the first few times tripping on acid, packing bowls was particularly difficult, the little pieces of weed are crawling all around the table and you cant distinguish if you got all of it in the bowl, or if theres specks all over the table, and then why its all over your lap, and well... you get the idea.

Some of my favorite things to do are look through books like really colorful and detailed book about the milky way galaxy... or one of Dali's paintings. Lots of amazing images to get lost in and they are perfect catalysts for getting your brain off wandering through previously inaccessible passages of the universe.

I always enjoy fruit juices, some of the flavors are so incredibly overwhelming its surprising and unbelievably satisfying. Personal preference really.

If you ever get the chance, swimming is an incredible experience just fyi, being under water and the tactile sensations are incredible (or even a shower)

You really dont need anything to make your environment more trippy, the thing about acid is it just promotes this child like sense of wonder and even the most mundane and uninteresting objects become more fascinating that one could ever imagine. Simply examining all the various textures of a room could drive one mad. The main thing is just to make your environment comfortable for you.

If you do start to become uncomfortable while tripping or become focused on something and need to distract yourself, simply get up and turn the lights on, change the song, or move to a new room and the entire feeling and attitude of the trip will change.

You will have a great time!

I had really strong visuals too, but one of the best parts of the trip was the laughter. I don't think I had laughed that hard before at any time in my life. It was different laughter too. It made me feel so genuinely happy,  because it wasn't just that I thought something was funny so I laughed, it was that I was so happy with myself and life in general, that all I could do was laugh as an expression of pure happiness. I wasn't even laughing at anything most of the time, and whenever I would realize that, that I'm not even laughing AT anything, I would laugh even harder. Ah man, so beautiful. Remember that. Good LSD makes you laugh like you've never laughed before. You also have to dose high enough though for that to happen. I'm not so sure that 150ug is enough for that. I never remember laughing that much on 1 tab trips.

That's one of the criteria I base my judgement of the quality of LSD I get on. If it doesn't make me laugh a deep, genuine laughter, that refreshes my being, then I usually reach the conclusion that it was low quality LSD. Of course there's many other factors as well, though.

So basically what I'm saying is, that's kind of how an LSD experience is supposed to be. If you feel underwhelmed, or like your expectations weren't met, then you didn't dose high enough. Because when you dose right, you KNOW you dosed right. ;) There's no lingering questions,  unmet expectations, or uncertainty as to whether or not you had a proper trip, just pure bliss and happiness, and a feeling of contentment, knowing that you definitely just had an LSD experience.
