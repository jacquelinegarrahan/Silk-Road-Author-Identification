Title: Re: DMT orally or smoked?
Post by: flyinghigh1660 on October 17, 2012, 06:02 pm
Depends on what you are after,

A quick hit?Then smoke your DMT, most people start with smoking first anyhow.

But as microRNA says you have to stop the DMT being broken down before it reaches your brain. To do this you need to ingest a MAOI (Monoamine oxidase inhibitors), you can ingest a MAOI by smoking or eating it.

Infusing DMT onto herbs and adding a MAOI makes changa, which can be smoked. This lengthens the trip and brings in more of a spiritual quality.

You can ingest a MOAI as well, and then ingest or smoke pure DMT. This is really another level your trip will be intense and sometimes very overwhelming. Not something to rush into in my opinion.

There are dangers, and potentially fatal ones, mixing MAOI's  with other substances, so you must know what you are doing. Like others have said look on the DMT nexus for high quality information before trying anything with a MAOI.

Otherwise just smoke pure DMT first and then move up, most people will end up ingesting it with a MAOI eventually if you really want an experience to write home about.

Post by: The ILF on October 17, 2012, 07:54 pm
Yeah, MAOIs are not necessary to smoke freebase.

Also, there is a third way, a middle road, if you like, that has come up recently in the Nexus: insufflation.  Might be a bit caustic on the nasal tissue, but it does offer some nice features.  It also requires no MAOI.  In addition, there is a less "shocking" come-up -- when smoking, it's really more of a oh-my-God-what-the-fuck-is-happening blast off -- and sniffing lasts a bit longer, with a peak of maybe half an hour.

Experiences from smoking it can be difficult to integrate, I think, because the transformation is so radical and it's over before one knows it.  A member of the ILF readily admits that intense fear and even shame, though by no means the totality of the experience, were certainly a part of one of his rocket rides. Still, it was an incredible, even life-changing experience.
