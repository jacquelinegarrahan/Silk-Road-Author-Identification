Title: Re: A good Pain pill
Post by: sourman on August 02, 2012, 10:25 pm
Most people without a high tolerance can certainly feel tramadol, especially when taken with fatty foods. You either love the feeling or hate it though. The opioid effects are preceded by a serotonin/norepinephrine spike, which is great for people feeling down, but can make those who feel more 'up' nauseous and dizzy like you said. The later category usually enjoys tramadol with benzos, which lower the seizure risk associated with taking too much (or mixing tramadol with seratogenic drugs), and also keep serotonin levels from rising to uncomfortably high levels in general.
