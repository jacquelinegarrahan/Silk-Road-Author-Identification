Post by: photonsounds on February 16, 2013, 06:27 pm
Rafael5 hit the nail on the head.

But I will add another dynamic to it: Genealogy.

You need to have a positive, relaxed setting, but also you need to be sure that you have no family history of Schizophrenia or bipolar disorder. From my experience, I have seen people with personality disorders have an absolutely horrific experience which even affected those around them. Even if you are just an angry person in general, psychedelics (this is particularly aimed at LSD) will magnify those negative traits and can lock you in a bad trip for hours and leave you with some horrible memories.

Even if this were the case, I could say try psilocybin first. As opposed to LSD, mushrooms typically come with a dose of euphoria to the eye candy they induce. They give you a bit of euphoria and also the OEV's.

And as commonly known, stay away from anti-depressants and even some painkillers. First off, mixing chems is a serious thing to consider and you want to prepare accordingly or avoid altogether with some mixtures. But with psychedelics in particular this won't be so much a physical danger to dying, but more of a danger to the mind. I had a friend who mixed mushrooms with I believe it was trazodone and he blacked out and ended up destroying his house and even harming his wife.

Good luck, op. And good on you for asking questions first. I know when I was a kid I just took it and went with it. Fortunately never found myself too bad of a situation like some of my friends around me.

I can see how people perceive that they get euphoria from LSD, but really that's only internal to your mind. In my opinion, LSD itself isn't euphoric and they only positive excitement you may feel is that of knowing you're about to trip. In my experience, LSD actually emphasizes the emotions you already feel. Therefore if you're in a positive mood and are excited about having a fun trip, then you may have a magnified affect of this. On the other hand, if you are stressed out in life, or feeling depressed, or anything negative then the drug may exaggerate that as well.
