Title: Re: Storing Hash
Post by: UKGrower on July 12, 2012, 02:26 am
Heat, light and air exposure will affect potency to some degree.  Moisture is bad too, but more for mold reasons.

I wouldn't say hash doesn't store well, but the above factors are the main things that will oxidize thc into cbn.   Well pressed hash will store much better than fragile crumbly hash, due to the fact that the outer shell will protect the majority of the material inside from oxidization.
