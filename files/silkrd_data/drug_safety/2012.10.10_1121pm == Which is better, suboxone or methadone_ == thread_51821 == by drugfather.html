Post by: 1100101 on October 11, 2012, 07:34 pm
Methadone has the potential to be more pleasurable than suboxone.   

Like with most things, if you take it every day, you aren't going to feel anything from it.  But, it will help you maintain.

Methadone is also more effective for treating pain (for me at least). 

If you are on either of these every day, neither will cause a buzz. 

I am one of the few 'success' stories that I know of using Suboxone.  But, after 4 years I still take it every day (1/8th of a 2mg tablet).  No relapse yet though. I also have to give a HUGE thanks to Cannabis for helping me stay off of opiates.

A lot of the people that I know have NOT had good luck with Suboxone and are on Methadone.  Where we live, you have to go to a clinic every day for the methadone and that just doesn't work for me.  I've become a bit of a hermit and when I was initially fighting my addiction I began living a cloistered life.  I had to cut myself off from all of the people that were a part of that old lifestyle.  I even started working from home so that I only have to leave the house very rarely.  I'd like to think if you were to put a needle and some IV'able opiate in front of me that I could refrain from doing it, but I don't know that I could. I can refrain from ordering on SR because it isn't instant ... and because I have so many nice buds to spend my btc on :]
