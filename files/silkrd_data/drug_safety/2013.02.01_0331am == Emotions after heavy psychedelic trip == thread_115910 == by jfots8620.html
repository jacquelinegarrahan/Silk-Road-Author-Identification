I think it effects different people differently. Honestly I rarely ever get an E hangover unless I go overboard on redosing like the OP did. I'm pretty sure that the effects you were describing were more personal than medical. Most people, after a single dose of 150mg MDMA will actually end up with a bit of a glow for a few days followed by a bit of a down for a few days but nothing like a month long hang over unless something else fundamentally changed in your life besides a single pill. Not saying it didn't happen, since I obviously wasn't there, but it would be unusual for a single dose to have any more than a passing effect on most people.

If you ever do do it again, stick with low doses (obviously) and just make sure you get lots of rest the next day. If it still sits wrong after the normal turn around than I guess you are one of the lucky few that it's just no fun for. If the next trip recovery goes better you might have just gotten the flu from a pretty girl you were kissing that first time, stranger things have happened.

I look at E as a cash advance on happiness. It'll feel great but it will have to be paid back with interest.
