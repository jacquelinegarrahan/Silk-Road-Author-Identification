Title: Re: MDMA + Alcohol = ?
Post by: DrugBuddy on February 08, 2013, 04:20 pm
If you start drinking first, the MDMA roll will be less intense and taper off quicker.

If you take the MDMA first you will roll harder and the alcohol will seem to be ineffective, and you can accidentally drink more than you should. Either way, drinking alcohol with MDMA is wasteful and pointless.

Drink water regularly, and don't overheat. Get some air and also remember not to confess your soul to some stranger just because you've got MDMA trust and confidence booster :)

Stay safe,

Your DrugBuddy.
