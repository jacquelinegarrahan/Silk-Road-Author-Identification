Hello people's just wanted to let everyone know about my first trip on LSD.

I know a few people on the forums me included have been very interested in acid(LSD). But have been very scared of trying it.

So I decided to buy 10 tabs of 150ug LSD. Got it in the mail 3 days later then did some serious research on it. I have read about LSD before but if I was going to try it then I was determined to do some heavy research into it so I spent a few days doing that.

Ended up booking 5 days off work and picked a day where I would be alone for 12+ hours.

I know what everyone says if you trip do it with people you trust...But all my friends would tell me drugs are evil and there not as open minded. I love my friends but they just wouldn't understand.

But with that said I took a lot of precautions before starting my trip.

My safety list (Before I started my trip I did everything on this list)

#1 Keep sharp objects out of reach in rooms where you will be tripping
#2 Unplug your phone + mobile
#3 Don't go out side in public on your first trip (I wanted to do this but knew it would be a bad idea if I did)
#4 Make sure all your doors are locked windows closed
#5 Keep music volume at a reasonable level don't want people coming over complaining
#6 Keep benzos handy. It wont abort the trip but may calm you down if you get to over stimulated or paranoid.

Great time.  Oh: and the girl and I are talking about seriously dating now, and probably having a master/slave dynamic continue.  So I can't say acid made us do anything we wouldn't have done otherwise.  If anything it just helped us get there a bit faster.   ::)
