Title: Re: is SR cocaine really worth it?
Post by: DrColdPillow on June 22, 2013, 12:59 am
As a very occasional user, the short answer is: Yes.

I've done cocaine only a handful of times, and I felt just like you; it was less than stellar. Sometimes you'd have to snort like 5-10 lines to get a really good "feeling", but it'd hurt to snort, and would be accompanied by a slew of other hyped up feelings that are nonexistent in pure cocaine. Then afterwards you'd feel like shit and wonder why you even paid to feel that way.

I saw the prices on SR and laughed. They were/are ridiculous in comparison to the stuff I could find on the streets. A few months went by, and I continued to read experiences from the cocaine thread. Vendors had gotten European lab testing done on their cocaine, and this gave you the power to know exactly what is in your cocaine. Vendors now have cocaine that is 100% pure, and others that are in the high 90%'s. I recently went with Bungee54 who has 98.7% pure cocaine w/ no cuts and it really is completely different than street coke.

A gram on the street will last a night if you're lucky, but I can stretch grams from SR for 2-3 weeks. The potency of pure cocaine lets a gram go a long way, and you don't have to snort as much for the effect. The come down is mild and just brings you back to baseline. The high peaks for about 45 minutes and lasts for like an hour and a half or longer.

So yes, if you're curious and don't mind paying to know what's in your coke, then it's completely worth it.
