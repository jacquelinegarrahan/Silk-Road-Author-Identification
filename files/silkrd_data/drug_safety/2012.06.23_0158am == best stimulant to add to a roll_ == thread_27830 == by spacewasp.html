Post by: thisworld on June 25, 2012, 09:54 am
I would recommend Amp/speed.  I don't think there's usually meth in bombs. Meth is pretty spendy, where as amp is rather cheap. 

on the note of 4-fa. I really like it's wake-ful qualities at ~20mg.  I have only used it once recreationally, however i like to dose 20mg or so at the beginning of a day for 8-10 hours of wakeful energy.  not bursting with energy and need to dance or anything, just very awake and aware.  I DO notice i sweat alot more than usual and i'm usually in a great mood.  When i dosed ~60-80mg i wasn't as awake or alert, more high like molly where i was lethargic, though my CNS was stimulated. 

On the other hand, my fiance won't feel a thing on 10-25mg but on 30-40mg she's lethargic, has difficulty concentrating(i didn't experience that even on ~80) and while she had a good time, thought it was rather a waste.

Just keep in mind it depends on your body chemistry.  4-FA might be a good choice to try, but i definitely think amphetamine would better suit your needs.  I've never tried any amp from SR, but from everything i've read the stuff on here is rather hit and miss, even with reputable vendors.
