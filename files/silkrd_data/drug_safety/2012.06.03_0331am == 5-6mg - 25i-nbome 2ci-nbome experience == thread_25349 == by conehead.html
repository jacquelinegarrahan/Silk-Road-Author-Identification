Title: 5-6mg - 25i-nbome 2ci-nbome experience
Post by: conehead on June 03, 2012, 03:31 am
So last night I got drunk. I had some friends over and I drank about 2 bottles of wine before eating, then took some 2ci-nbome. I started with 1 hit, then another about 30 minutes later. I find this helps the come up to be more smooth.

I was still drinking while the tabs were in the mouth. I'm unsure exactly how much was wasted. I ended up eating 5-6MG of the chemical total in a 3-4 hour period. With the last tab still in my gum we went to the movies to see the dictator.

One side effect I have been noticing is the strange body clamminess and sweating. During the movie I peaked and had to take off my glasses because the sweating was fogging them up so bad I couldn't see well. I sweat particularly hard on my forehead and around my eye sockets. Usually a bath solves this. I don't know why. I am very passive when tripping so things like that do not bug me.

The way home was intense. Leaving the theater and walking the hallway that was alive with plasiticity and color, and then seeing the movie posters that seemed to smile at me. Not the people in the posters, but the posters themselves seemed happy. I touched Will Smith's face and said, "I have a black friend."

After some terrible confusion as to what vehicle was ours, we went home and I fell into the swimming pool, rounding out an extremely good evening with friends. Good wine goes very well with this drug, but I recommend keeping a closer account of how much you take.

I do not recommend high doses of this drug because of the vascular side effects. I'm almost scared to check my blood pressure today because I usually have lingering high blood pressure for days from this substance. I have gone as high as 185/120 during the come up on this before. I find sex reduces the blood pressure effect greatly. I have no idea why.

I find the body effects of the drug unsettling. I would much rather do LSD, if not for that, then for the euphoria that you do not get on this drug. This drug for me is like the visual aspects of LSD, with some body load, and no head trip. It's purely visual. It's very psychedelic. Closed eye visuals were insane last night. I could close my eyes and still see everything that was there with my eyes open, and things would continue to happen. People would move, walk around, twist and turn into weird patterns and colors. It was very intense. In terms of "realness" or "vividness" of the hallucinations I would say it is around 4/10 closed eye. They are very much present, but in terms of looking as real as with my eyes open, it is only about half way there. It is fun to get lost in there watching everything happen, but I find that I do this drug around people and they want me to stay present. HAHA :)

Today I had 2 hours of sleep and had some things to do. I had lingering effects such as breathing walls/floors, etc. I had closed eye visuals, although very weak, as late as 30 hours after the dosing. I also feel very spent, which is certainly mostly the alcohol's fault. I have 4 hits left here and I believe I'm going to wait for a day off and dose 2 each with a friend. This will be my last 2ci-nbome experience. I quite enjoyed it and would caution others from doing it. I think it is a good idea to use chemicals that are known to be more safe, such as LSD, mushrooms, etc. I do not like the idea of making myself a test subject. I also do not recommend anyone with high blood pressure takes this substance. My blood pressure is controlled via lisinopril ~40mg. It skyrockets on this substance. Last night I did not record it because I knew it was sky high and didn't want to worry. It was not as if I was going to go to the hospital without having a stroke first anyway, so why bother myself.

Thanks, and good journeys! A big thanks to duderugs for making this possible for me!
