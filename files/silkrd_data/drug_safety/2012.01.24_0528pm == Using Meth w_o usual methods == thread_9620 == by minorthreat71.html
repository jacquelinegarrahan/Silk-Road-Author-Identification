Title: Re: Using Meth w/o usual methods
Post by: 328502E on January 24, 2012, 09:50 pm
If you don't have a good mg scale, there is another option - volumetric measurement.  Basically, dissolve 100mg in 2.5 cups of water.  Then, when you want to dose, measure out 1/4 cup of water (after mixing, just to be sure) and drink it.  Since there is 100mg in 2.5 cups, each 1/4 cup contains 10mg.  You can play around with the measurements as you wish, just double check your math if you are at all unsure.

I'm not sure if there is a danger of meth degrading if stored in water over time.
