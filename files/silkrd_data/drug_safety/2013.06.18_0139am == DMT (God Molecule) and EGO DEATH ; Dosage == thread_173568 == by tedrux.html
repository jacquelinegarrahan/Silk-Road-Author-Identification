Post by: BlackIris on June 18, 2013, 01:57 pm
It should be directly under, with the bottle held vertically, but without touching the brillo for best results.

But it mostly depends on the type of lighter you have. If it is a normal lighter with a low flame you can either touch the brillo (actually in this case you would have to if you don't have the lung capacity of an elephant), if you have a good torch lighter the flame should be some inches below because it produces a lot of concentrated heat. I think having a torch lighter is almost a must, it is much easier and faster to vaporize the DMT correctly than with a normal lighter (btw the torch lighter should be set as high as to produce the purplish halo).

In both case one of the thing that get not much mentioned is the type of toke you must do, but it is very important. It should be slow (VERY slow), steady and long. The more slow and steady the better. If the breath is not slow you bring too much hot air inside, hence turning the vapor into smoke, and if the toke is not steady air circulation and combustion become erratic.

To say these things it may seem that the procedure is complicated but in reality with a good torch lighter it is extremely easy to vaporize the DMT correctly with a machine. It is just a matter of understanding the right distance of the lighter from the brillo and how to toke; both things just 1-2 tries will make you good at it soon enough (btw also in the very first times it's easy to tell if you are too near or far; if the smoke is harsh or hot then you are too near, bring the lighter a little back, instead if there is no vapor rising bring the lighter closer. You have time to adjust correctly with a slow toke).
