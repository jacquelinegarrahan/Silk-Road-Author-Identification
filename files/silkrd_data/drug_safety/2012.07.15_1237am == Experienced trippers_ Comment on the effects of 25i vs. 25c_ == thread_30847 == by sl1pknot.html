Title: Re: Experienced trippers: Comment on the effects of 25i vs. 25c?
Post by: l1llykins on July 21, 2012, 06:42 pm
25i is eye candy with a mood boost. There is euphoria but it's mild compared to 25c and 25d. It makes everything seem pretty and flowing with life ... the mood boost gives you a sense of awe and excitement when you're looking at things, like the way you imagine children discovering things for the first time. I normally need to concentrate to actually see visual distortion, slight morphings going back and forth of various edges. When looking at photos, parts of the people in them shift a little bit, kind of a toned-down version of those Harry Potter pics.

25c is a euphoric chem, a nice and steady stream of bodily goodness fills you and stays for the duration of the trip. Visuals are strongest in the beginning but as the euphoria kicks in the visuals start to die down.

25d  is also euphoric but instead of a steady always-there kind of euphoria, it comes in rolls. Think molly/mda but the rolls happen less frequently and also peak for much longer --about 30 seconds to a full minute before tapering back down. The euphoria surges into you and fills you up, then resonates within you for a long while before settling down to a steady buzz of bliss. There's also some time dilation so the rolls can sometimes feel like they're never-ending. The visuals are similar to 25c in that they start to die as the euphoria kicks in.

P.S. 25b is one of the more forgotten nbomes because it's difficult to obtain but it's eye candy on steroids. The visuals make everything look like they're filled with vivid technicolor life, a sense of magic is infused into everything. It also allows you to hear things with crystal clarity; I remember zoning into certain sounds only to realize later on that they were just footsteps of a neighbor walking up their stairs or the water trickling into the ice-maker (made me feel like superman at times). Music sounds awesome, you can pick up any instrument in the background. It also felt like my blood was flowing with adrenaline the entire time and my emotions were all heightened. It's lots of fun so pick some up if you ever see these gems up for sale.
