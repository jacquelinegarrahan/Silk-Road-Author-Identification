Title: I'm going to say 3dames saved a life yesterday.
Post by: ChaxChax on April 23, 2012, 02:07 pm
Some of us were camping up north (like fly-in north, not drive-up north).  We had rented a cottage and brought along some recreational party favors for the few nights we would be there. 
Most of us were pretty accomplished phychonauts, there was an assortment of shrooms, and coke and booze mostly.
As the shrooms wore off, and the night was winding down, we started doing a few pin rails just to keep the energy up enough to catch the sunrise.  We bought the coke here, I won't name the vendor, but it was primo, and stronger than most of the street-stomped stuff we are used to.  After 4 or 5 lines, we headed outside to catch the sun.  The most inexperienced of us who was still shrooming a little decided "he didn't feel anything off the coke".  So he stuck his straw in what was probably .75g left in the baggie and did a bag rail, inhaling almost a full .25 (at best guess) on top of the lines he did with us. 
He threw up immediately and came out to tell us what he had done and that his heart was beating too fast and he was having a hard time catching his breath. It likely wasn't a lethal dose, but he was obviously shocky.
We got on the sat-phone right away to call the plane back to come get us (4 hours), but in the mean time, we made him throw up again, and ran a nettie pot through his sinus canals to clear out any un-absorbed chunks.
He was pretty pale and hyperventilating, probably from shock as much as anything, so I took 2.5mg of 3dames k-pins I had for helping me sleep the rest of the day away, and crushed them and had him parachute it with a light beer to potentiate the sedative affect.  Within 20 minutes he was calm and relaxed, his pulse was no longer thready and he was asking if what happened was normal.
We were all pretty freaked out, but I guarantee you having those benzos around helped level this guy out of his shock at least (the shock can kills as often as the overdose).
Pro-tip.  If you are going to do coke, and there is a chance you'll do too much.. have a healthy supply of genuine benzos along in case things go sideways.
Thanks 3dames!
