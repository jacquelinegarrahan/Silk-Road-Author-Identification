Title: Re: Best for stress?
Post by: mcguire39 on August 09, 2013, 06:12 pm
Cannabis can work for stress but I think it's really dependent on what is causing the stress. If it's just a heavy workload, too many things going on, it can probably help you unwind and lift your mood. But if it's something like a death in the family, something like that, I think it can almost make that kind of thing worse because the emotions associated with it become even stronger.

For cannabis types there's sativa and indicia primarily, then many hybrids. Sativas tend to elevate mood, make you giggly and feel good, more of a 'heady trippy feeling' some say. Indicia dominants are good for relaxing, they have more of a 'body load' and make you want to get comfortable somewhere and just veg out.

I'd say it would require regular use during the stressful time. There's not many things you can just take/do one time and go 'wow poof my stress/problem is gone'.
