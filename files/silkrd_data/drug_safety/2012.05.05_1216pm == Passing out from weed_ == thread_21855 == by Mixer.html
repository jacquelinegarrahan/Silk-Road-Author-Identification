Title: Passing out from weed?
Post by: Mixer on May 05, 2012, 12:16 pm
Yesterday I got some Kali Mist and of course I wanted to finally give a try to a Sativa. I don't smoke frequently (last time had been two weeks ago) so I don't have tolerance and I usually smoke shitty weed...
I loaded 0.1g in my mini pipe and took 5 small hits, holding them in, because I wasn't feeling much. I smoked about half of what I had put in the pipe. After 5 minutes I realize it's coming up slooooowly. Another 5 minutes, and I start tripping (I had never tripped on weed). OMG weed CEVs are fantastic :D And I got good OEVs as well.
I was sitting and I start to feel lightheaded and I realize I was fainting... I laid on the floor and put my legs on a chair to get more blood to the brain, and I fought for some minutes to not pass out. I ended up staying on the floor for 3 HOURS before I could get up without fainting. Two or three times I almost passed out while I was lying! I thought it could be low blood sugar or low blood pressure, so I told my friend to measure them (around T+2:00). My blood sugar was 120 mg/dL (it's fine, I'm diabetic), my pressure was 150/87 mmHg and my beat rate was 120bpm! Still I felt like I was fainting from a low blood pressure. When I got up I was at 120/66 87bpm. I don't think it was a panic attack or something like that because I was happy and laughing at things (except when I was fighting against passing out). Today I still feel a bit lightheaded and I'm really tired. I just measured my BP and it is 109/61 75bpm (both BP and BR are a bit low for me).
Before smoking the weed I had exercised for an hour (breakdance) and I was on an empty stomach.

Have I just smoked too much? Has it ever happened to you? I was thinking of giving it another try this night, taking only a hit every 10-15 minutes until I am satisfied. Would you advise against it?

Thanks :)
