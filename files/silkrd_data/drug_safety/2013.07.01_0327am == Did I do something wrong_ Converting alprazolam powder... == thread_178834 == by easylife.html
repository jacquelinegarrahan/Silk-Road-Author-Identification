Title: Re: Did I do something wrong? Converting alprazolam powder...
Post by: Ben on July 02, 2013, 01:08 am
One measurement that certainly is wrong: 1 cc should be once cubic centimetre, hence 1 millilitre.

This is also a fairly large volume of liquid compared to candies and such. You may be just able to absorb it into a sugar cube, but something like a small mint is far smaller than one ml.

If you are in any doubt about this: a centimeter is just under half an inch. So you you wanted to add a cubit cemtimeter of liquid to a piece of candy that candy needs to be about a  0.4 inch cube to be equal to the volume of 1 ml.
