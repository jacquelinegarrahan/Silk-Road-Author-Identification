Title: Re: iv Combo of Butylone and Etylphenidate/Heroin
Post by: Mckay69 on April 20, 2012, 09:37 am
Obviously risky to IV anything, let alone RCs - however - I have happily IV'd Ethylphenidate multiple times and can say that it gives a fantastic rush/euphoria followed by a strong stimulation for a few hours. The urge to keep on repeating the injections is very strong so you need a lot of self-control to not end up looking like a pin-cushion. It's a vasoconstrictor too, like many stims, so trying to get into a vein after the first shot is both tricky and not recommended.  Repeated doses of EP just overstimulate the user and the euphoria gets smaller each time.

I have mixed what, for me, is a moderate dose of EP in a moderate shot of Heroin to make a pseudo-speeball. I was sitting in a large park on a bench away from prying eyes but with a view of a beautiful lake. It felt fucking awesome - as good as a coke/h genuine SB but with longer-lasting qualities and a fantastic feeling of place and time. Thing is, I have the constitution of an elephant and have got away with some very dangerous things in the past. There's no guarantee that I might not drop dead at any time because of the things I've tried so think carefully before you experiment with this, know your individual drugs and your tolerance to them.  BUT - For FUCK'S sake keep away from Butylone. It may not be the same for everyone but it did not feel good to me by any route of administration.
