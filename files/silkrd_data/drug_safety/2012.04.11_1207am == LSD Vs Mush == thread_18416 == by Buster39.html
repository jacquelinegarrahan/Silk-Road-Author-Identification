As for Mushies versus LSD, the differences are these little nuances. LSD puts you into more of a stony-headspace where you can more easily zone out and watch the visuals and your thoughts flow like water and evolve. Mushrooms keep ya a little more clear headed and allow you to better focus on what's going on. I duuno if this makes much sense, but mushrooms feel more " earthy" while LSD carries a more "mechanical" feel to it.
