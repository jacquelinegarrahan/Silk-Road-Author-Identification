Title: Re: Please give some Advice on Selling Marijuana.
Post by: ukmonkey on December 05, 2012, 05:41 pm
I sold a reasonable amount of weed in my youth. I don't know how much of this would apply to Malaysia, but if I were to start dealing again (and learn from my mistakes) I'd say:

1. Never lie about your weight or the quality of your weed.
2. Never invite buyers to your home - always meet them somewhere.
3. Never carry more than you need to.
4. Encourage your customers to text you rather than call. Get a prepaid SIM for this.
5. Never give people weed 'on credit' unless you're happy with not getting paid for it.

..If I were dealing somewhere with the death penalty, though, I'd take much more precautions. For example I would have a trusted friend collect the money, and leave the drugs at a 'dead drop' to be collected.

Title: Re: Please give some Advice on Selling Marijuana.
Post by: ukmonkey on December 06, 2012, 08:40 am
The 'texting via prepaid SIM' card idea is for a few reasons:

1. It stops your customers knowing anything about your real identity, so they can't get you in trouble, and:
2. If you decide to stop selling, you won't have loads of people still calling you (which was a problem for me)
3. It means you don't get calls when it would be awkward to answer
4. You can keep a record of where you've gotta be and when (which you don't have with calls)
5. It means (here in the UK at least) you can't be traced just from owning the number

Since you have to show a Citizen's card, that rules out the benefit of 5, but you'll still get 1 - 4. I'd probably get a drug-free friend to register it for me, though - as long as they're okay with lying and saying they lost the SIM if they ever get busted. And even if they tell all and lead the police to you, it would buy you some time to get rid of your stash.
