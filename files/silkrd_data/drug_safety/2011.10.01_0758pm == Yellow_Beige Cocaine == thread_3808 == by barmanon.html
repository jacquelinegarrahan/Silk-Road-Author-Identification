Title: Re: Yellow/Beige Cocaine
Post by: Greynick on October 02, 2011, 11:34 pm
Pure cocaine hydrochloride is totally white. It's a crystal and it doesn't smell of anything harsh - in fact, it smells quite nice. Cocaine hydrochloride hydrolyzes in moist air to give methyl benzoate - which is what drug dogs are trained to smell. This is the odour most cocaine veterans know well.

Want to find out for yourself? Do an acid/base extraction ... even an acetone / water wash will give you a very good idea, provided you let the acetone evaporate :-)

Anything other smell or color will be as a result of adulterants (active/inactive cutters)
