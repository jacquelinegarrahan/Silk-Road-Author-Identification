Just throwing this out there, you cannot "cut" marijuana.  Unless of course, you are buying preground buds (which is mindless anyways).  You buy the buds, and nugs off of the larger buds.   What you had was most likely an outdoor grow that was sprayed with anti-pest chemicals or poisons by law enforcement (if you live in an area that receives southwestern US grown, naturally occurring, marijuana).  I would have just thrown the bud out as it is no good and won't do much for your health.  If neither of these is true, you simply got a synthetic, which is as good as garbage.  The whole JWH-0XX family is just a trash heap of a drug, I would recommend finding a new dealer or seller.
