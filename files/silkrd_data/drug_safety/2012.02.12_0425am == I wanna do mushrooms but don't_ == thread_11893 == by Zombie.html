Title: Re: I wanna do mushrooms but don't?
Post by: wowzers on February 12, 2012, 11:00 pm
Jump right in, they're awesome. Eat 2g, you're pretty unlikely to have a bad trip on that kind of dose.
Sometimes they can get a bit scary as you head up towards 3g and beyond, but the dark bits are unlikely to last longer than an hour (though it might feel like longer). Do it with properly experienced trippers, people who have had balls to the wall bad trips are likely to know all the tricks to help you have a smooth and happy trip. As robotzombies said tryptamines are spiritual, they call up very deep, atavistic parts of your mind.

Top tip- this may sound a bit stupid but try not to 'think' too much until you're past the midway point of the trip. Dwelling on what the fuck's happening to your mind can make you internalise the trip and start to panic as you stumble through normally mundane phenomena like time, reality and perception  :D
If things start to get weird, engage with the external. Dance, eat some cakes, watch something light, play with some engaging toy (wire mandalas are cool, as is that ferromagnetic fluid). Roll some spliffs before you come up, it get's exponentially harder with time.
