Title: Re: Some MDMA questions?
Post by: DiamondSky on March 30, 2013, 09:27 pm
So vomiting on the come up is pretty common as others have pointed out. As you take the stuff more and more you'll start to be able to mitigate the nauseous feeling in my experience. For instance, when it just starts to hit me a little hard I start to heat up a bit and if I simply turn on a fan and blow some cool air on me at that point the feeling passes in just a few minutes.

The good news with puking on MDMA is that the moment after you purge the world is just a whole lot better. It's like some invisible barrier that your body passes through on it's way to getting really high.

Since you asked if there was anyway to avoid it, plugging (i.e. liquid dilution of MDMA applied in the butt) seems to cut down the nauseous feeling for a lot of people. Wouldn't recommend it with a bunch of casual friends but me and my wife do it all the time and enjoy it a little more that way.

Otherwise keep a fan handy and a little puke bucket nearby while appreciating that it's just a quick passing phase.
