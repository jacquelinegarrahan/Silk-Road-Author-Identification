MDA and MDMA are quite different drugs. I'm not an neuroscience expert (so correct me if i'm wrong) but the difference are as follows :

The thing is MDA hits the 5ht2b receptors harder then MDMA... i.e. it stimulates the serotonin more - so its a harder rush (not that it noticeable to me) thus depletes serotonin more then MDMA. Notice as well MDA has stimulation on 5ht1a and 5ht2c which is what LSD works on, but not 5ht2a which is a key ingredient LSD's visual trips.

MDA does not touch the Muscarinic receptors. The M receptors I dont know a lot about - but wikki seems to indicate this has to do with muscle and gland function, which im guessing is the touch sensation that MDMA has - also impairs motor function. This is why ppl seem to prefer MDA for dancing and stuff less motor skill impairment, but you lose out on hugs.

Really they are quite different.... i think the stimulation of the 5ht2c receptor in MDA is what causes some hangover issues. Higher effect on 5ht2b I think means you what to dose it lower then MDMA.
