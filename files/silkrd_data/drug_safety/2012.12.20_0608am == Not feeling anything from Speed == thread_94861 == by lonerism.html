Title: Re: Not feeling anything from Speed
Post by: hl887 on December 20, 2012, 06:53 am
The amphetamines tend to amplify moods and mindsets, so when a negative one comes up it is important to check whether or not it is real before blurting out to the non-tweaker public in a panic (or even tweaker friends for that matter), "Did you see those shadow people?!" The cut conversation is a similar thread of negativity that ought to have some objectivity brought to it.

First, it is entirely possible to dose with real 100% pure gear and feel relatively nothing after some period of regular usage. There are sensitivity effects which researchers still do not fully understand about amphetamines. This includes the sensitization regiments for increasing the feeling as well as spontaneous desensitization or sensitization.

Second, measure the melting point. Taste it. Smell it (solid and vapor). Write it down. Based on these observations, is it what you think it is? If not, what could it be? What are ways to separate or identify or distinguish those possibilities? Not being high usually helps prevent accidents and breakage along these lines.

Third, crush the contents of the stash and mix them together. If there is cut or impurity the average actual dose will be consistent (no ghost hits, phantom rails, etc) and one won't get shafted on the last dose.
