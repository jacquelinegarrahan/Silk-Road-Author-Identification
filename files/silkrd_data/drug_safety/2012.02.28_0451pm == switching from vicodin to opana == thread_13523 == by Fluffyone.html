Opana is a very VERY strong opiate!  I believe the conversion you used is probably pretty close in dosage conversion.  However, Opana has a super low oral bioavalibility meaning taking oral is pretty much a waste.  I suggest you snort the opana if you do decide to go this route.  Just remember if you are snorting the opana you will want to cut that 2mg dose in half.

Something else to do with opana is that it is crazy addictive!  Be careful with it as your tolerance will skyrocket on it in no time at all.  Also opana withdraws are horrendous so be very careful, I would say that opana withdraw is IMO the worst of all opiates I have used on a reg basis, and I have used almost all of them.

I am not trying to scare you away from opana, I am simply trying to warn you of the consequences so you can make a more informed decision.  I really think if this is going to save you money in the long run that you should go for it!  Just be very careful my friend, I hope you the best!
