Title: Modafinil/dexamphetamine (Adderall) cross-tolerance?
Post by: metacontxt on September 26, 2013, 03:06 pm
Does anyone know about this?

After using Modafinil on and off over a period of about 4 months, I now need to take about twice as much as I did when I first took it.

It seems that my tolerance to dexamphetamine or dextroamphetamine (which I use much much more infrequently) has also increased by a similar amount even though the small amounts I've been taking and the months-long breaks between using don't really explain to me why my dex tolerance would have gone up so much.

I know they're different drugs, but it seems to me that is some relation between their mechanism of action or whatever it is in the brain that they tweak. If I use dexies two days in a row, on the second day I don't get much of a buzz and they're less effective for concentration. If I take modafinil on one day and dexies the next, I get the same reduced effectiveness of dexies the day after taking modafinil.

Anyone else have any experience with these two drugs?
