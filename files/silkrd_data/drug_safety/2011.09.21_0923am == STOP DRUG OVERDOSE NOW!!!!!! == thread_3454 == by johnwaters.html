Title: STOP DRUG OVERDOSE NOW!!!!!!
Post by: johnwaters on September 21, 2011, 09:23 am
Hello SR Family!!!

If you are looking to find a great way to end a potential overdose then read this!!!!

I am a frequent speed-baller (Cocaine & Oxycodone). I have taken up to an 8-ball of cocaine and up to 180 mg of Oxycodone in 1 day. When doing this there have been times when I feel as if respiratory depression is setting in. I get to the point were i can barely breathe. What I take as a precautionary measure is activated charcoal (You can Google it and buy online, or visit your local health food store). I keep a bottle of activated charcoal on hand and mix about 6 heaping tablespoons with a glass of water and guzzle it down; and repeat if needed. Continue to drink as much water as possible after this to get the charcoal flowing through your system. This will absorb the drugs in your body and prevent overdose, and it just might save your life. Always keep this on hand especially when combining drugs that should not be mixed.

Just keep in mind though that taking activated charcoal will absorb all toxins and drugs in your body, and it will be about 12 to 24 hours before you can get high again! But I guess that's better then never being able to get high again because you did not make it though!!! {{^_^}}

Post by: MissyCat on October 26, 2011, 01:05 am
Everybody please be cautious with this one and do your own research...  I was always under the impression that, as mentioned in this thread, ingesting activated charcoal only stopped poisons (drugs, whatever) from being absorbed through the GI tract, and then only PARTIALLY so.  Anything in your bloodstream already, your stuck with!  And administering drugs through a different route (IV, IM, insuffilated, etc.. anything other than swallowing lol) would bypass the charcoal altogether.

BE CAREFUL

In fact after some consideration I decided to come back and edit.  Please don't rely on this, especially considering the context that the OP provided.  I assume OP is not "eating" his speedballs!  The ONLY ROI this is indicated for in case of overdose is oral ingestion, and the activated carbon will only slow the release of whatever active chemicals in the gut that  haven't been absorbed already.  It's not totally effective even at that.

Please be careful with dosage and source.  Activated carbon likely will not be of much help.
If you are messing with opiates having Naloxone on hand could be a life saver however, and I believe one vendor on SR was holding some.  DO your research!
