Depends on half-life of antidepressant you are using. Paxil have very low half-life - i'm not a doctor, but i believe 2-3 days would be enough, and i waited this time myself before using psychedelics when i was using paxil, and i noted no weaken of their effect. I also used tramadol and got no seizures, and when i was on Prozac and took tramadol in 1.5 week after stopping prozac i got serious seizure (and almost fallen on the path of coming train), because this SSRI antidepressant was still active. Prozac have long half-life, and you should wait 2-3 weeks, maybe more If prozac is used for many months, before consuming substances incompatible with SSRI.
