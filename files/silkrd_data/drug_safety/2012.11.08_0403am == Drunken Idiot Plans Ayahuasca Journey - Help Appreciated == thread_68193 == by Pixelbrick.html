Post by: sdesu on November 08, 2012, 04:26 am
You will need to stay off your antidepressant medication for more than just a few days. Also, never stop cold turkey. You are putting yourself at an increased risk of relapse. The longer you have been on these meds, the more harsh the withdrawal can be. Effexor is associated with some of the worst withdrawal symptoms because they clear out of your system quickly, leaving little time for your body to adjust to the sudden drop in the neurotransmitter serotonin.

If you still wish to try and make a DMT/ayahuasca brew, BuddhaNature posted a tutorial back in August.
