Title: Re: So what's the deal with morphine
Post by: daRwin on July 31, 2012, 08:19 pm
Morphine is not a good drug to take orally. If you swallow morphine sulphate, almost 70% of it is eliminated by the liver during first pass metabolism after being absorbed from your intestines, so it is only about 30% bioavailable. Others like oxynorm have much higher bioavaiability at 90%. Obviously, if you are talking about IV administration, thats a different story.
