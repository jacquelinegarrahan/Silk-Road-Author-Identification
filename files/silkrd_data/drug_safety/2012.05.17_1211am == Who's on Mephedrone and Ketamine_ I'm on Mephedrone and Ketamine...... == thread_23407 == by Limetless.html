Yesss it is but the varieties of cathinone analogues is huge. For example Meph is like a cross between Bing and MDMA, Methylone is like a weak version of MDMA, MDPV is more like Bing with speediness but turns you sex mad, Buphedrone is a cold stim, MDPPP is an offshoot and weaker version of MDPV and the list continues. They may be in the same family but the effects are highly varied. It's essentially like comparing say DMT, 5-MEO-DMT and 4-ACO-DMT because they are derivatives of Tryptamine but have hugely varying effects.
