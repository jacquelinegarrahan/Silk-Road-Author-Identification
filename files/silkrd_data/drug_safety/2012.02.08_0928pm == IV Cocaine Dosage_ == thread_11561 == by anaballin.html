Title: Re: IV Cocaine Dosage?

ALWAYS start low and titrate up on a new batch. Especially when you're dealing with potentially strong stuff, 20-30mg is a good place to start. In my experience, the rush isn't diminished on subsequent doses like it is with opiates, so there's really no downside to starting with an amount that would be safe even if the stuff was 99% pure.
