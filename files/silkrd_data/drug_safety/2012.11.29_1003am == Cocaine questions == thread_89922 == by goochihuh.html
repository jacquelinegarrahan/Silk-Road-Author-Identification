Title: Re: Cocaine questions
Post by: PrincessHIGH on November 29, 2012, 11:35 am
By the sounds of your coke, you need to dry it out, a hot plate, lamp within an inch of your coke on a plate, or leaving it near a radiator over night are all good choices, I tend to go for a lamp or radiator over a hot plate, but that's just my personal preference, a warm plate works perfectly well. You need to ensure your coke is dry before you chop it up into a very fine power, take your time over this, your nostrils will thank-you for doing so, and it helps you to get more out of your coke! Before your snort, adopt a good nasal hygiene practice, cleanse your nose with a saline nasal spray, moist mucus membranes make for for better absorption. Cut a straw about 4 inches long, this is far more hygienic than a bank note in my opinion (where has the bank note been, how many hands has it traveled through, what might it be contaminated with, straws do not go through any of this, that's why I think they're better for snorting). Do not cut the straw too long because you want your cocaine to go all the way up in one good snort.  Rack your lines on a smooth surface. something like mirror or marble surface is a good choice, not wood as coke can get stuck into the grooves, either using a card or razor blade, I think a razor blade makes for finer chopping. I'd recommend two 50-75mg lines, it's a nice way to start without blowing your head off! Your now ready to snort your first line (save your second for 15 minutes later), put one end of your straw to your nostril and use a fingertip to hold the other, snort your line fairly hard (like when you have a cold). If you have good quality coke your nose will start to feel numb followed by the high shortly after. At this point snort your coke back repeatedly, the drip of the powder will proceed to the back of your throat, by doing this your helping the coke to pass quicker as well, keep snorting back, until you feel the drip at the back of your throat kicking in, repeat as necessary, and enjoy! :)
