Title: Re: what is in a good Roll?
Post by: n1ll0 on January 13, 2013, 03:47 am
It depends on the person and the experience they are looking for.. a lot of street pill presses have additives because the added chemicals have similar qualitative effects and are cheaper.. They do alter the experience though. Sometimes people take these because they are all that is to be had. On the other hand a lot of people enjoy the empathic qualities of MDMA but are looking for a more specific type of high. Adding meth is fairly common to produce a more stimulant type high, which some find good in high energy settings.  Addition of MDA will cause a more hallucinogenic roll (ie open and closed eye visuals, etc). Also people will often combine MDMA with other distinct dosages of drugs like LSD for candy-flipping or , as you mentioned, ketamine for kitty-flipping, or mushrooms for hippy flipping.. etc. It all depends on the desired experience
