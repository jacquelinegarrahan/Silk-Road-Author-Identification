Post by: flyinghigh1660 on August 15, 2012, 12:44 pm
The first question I would ask is why do I need to wash in the first place?

Unless you have introduced lots of contamination from pulling the DMT from your base you are good to go. Washing will only reduce yield, so unless you have a thing about some really nice pure crystals. Which I get by the way, because it is nice to try and make a clean pure final product, or contamination there is no need to wash.

You can remove base contamination by;

Dissolving your DMT into the minimum amount of hot naphtha, using a safe method to heat the naphtha up. Try to use a small glass bowl for this so you can see the liquid.  Let the solvent cool a little and then you will see any base contamination fall to the bottom of the bowl. Either pour out naphtha into another bowl leaving the residual behind. Or use a syringe with cotton wool in to draw the naphtha out.

Leave this to evaporate over a day.

Nice large clean crystals will form.

The less solvent you use the better, remember that it is the volume and temperature of the solvent that dictates how much DMT you can dissolve in it. But be safe when you heat naphtha up. No open flames please! look at how to melt chocolate in water for ideas.
