Title: For those experienced with LSD
Post by: arutha on November 07, 2011, 06:20 am
I've tripped 3 times now. First time was 100ug, not really a whole lot happened though I probably did way too much weed. I was basically very high all night, but different from a weed high. It somewhat cured my alcoholism; drink seemed boring in comparison to that night.

Next I dropped 180ug. Very in control all night, I got some patterning on objects happening, got fascinated with a brick wall, gravity changed direction, some colours changed. It was cool, but nothing too crazy.

Third time I dropped 300ug. That was absolutely fucking insane. I was seeing alien plants growing everywhere, everything went amazing colours, objects appeared to melt, bugs crawled out of everything, it was nuts. But then at the peak of trips during it, I completely lost control of my body, was unable to move, and at times had very vivid and 'real' feeling hallucinations. One example, I honestly felt like I had been abducted by aliens and was strapped to a table. I was still able to realise that it was the drugs, and this wasn't actually happening, but parts of it certainly weren't pleasant. A lot of it definitely was though in hindsight, and I had many personal revelations that have changed my views on many things.

Is this kind of thing normal for a strong dose of LSD? With a different setting and mindset, would it be brighter and happier? What strength would be good for hallucinations and colours everywhere but not quite going into these deep trips?
