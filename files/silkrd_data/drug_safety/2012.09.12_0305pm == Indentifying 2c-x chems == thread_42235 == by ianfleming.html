According to that table, 2c-i will turn dark green to black with a Marquis test, while 2c-e will have no reaction: a Mecke for 2c-e goes light yellow-green while 2c-i will turn reddish-brown/drying blood colored.   

If you're getting random powders in the mail from vendors (including yrs. truly) I definitely recommend picking up a Dancesafe kit.  They aren't expensive and they are the best way to make sure you're getting what you ordered.
