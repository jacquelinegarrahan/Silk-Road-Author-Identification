Title: Re: Blunt Wraps
Post by: jimmyg on March 20, 2012, 09:56 pm
To be honest, it'll probably never roll like new again. But there are definitely things you can do to make it useable, at least.

You need to rehydrate it slightly. I've done this by wrapping them in lettuce, or by putting them in a jar with a piece of fruit for a night or so. You get the idea, just be careful, as dry blunt wraps are brittle blunt wraps.

Hope that helps, good luck
