Title: Re: 500 Morning Glory seeds
Post by: germx99 on January 10, 2013, 04:21 am
My first psychedelic experience involved me ingesting a similar quantity. I ground them up and then made a little cranberry lime cocktail which I downed, seeds and all. I popped a Zofran to repel emesis and smoked a few bowls. Funny thing is, even after my experiences with NBOMe, psilocybin, and LSD, I have yet to reach the same calibre of tripping that I acheived through my first experience with Morning Glory. In attempts to repeat the experience, I've tried consuming only the liquid extraction and leaving the seeds in a tea bag. Even with doses of up to 1000 seeds, I've yet to reach the same level as my first trip. I'd advise against taking it on a FULL stomach. Have a slice or two of toast and drink plenty of water beforehand. Be ready for anything. Since seeds aren't quite standardized for their ergoline contents, dosing will be hit or miss.
