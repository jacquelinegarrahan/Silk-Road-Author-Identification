Title: Re: How much to overdose and die?
Post by: Ben on June 02, 2012, 12:29 am
It greatly depends on the drug indeed, and whats probably more interesting is the ratio between a dose that is effective and a dose that is lethal.

For opiates like heroin or morphine the ratio between a fatal and a recreational dose isn't all that high. A tenfold mistake on the high side is likely to kill you, or at least get you hospitalized.

If you consider cannabis on the other hand, its virtually impossible to self-administer a fatal dose. You'd probably be too stoned to hold a joint very long before the dose becomes life threatening, though you might regret that experiment for quite some time after ;)

Alcohol is an interesting drug in that regard: While a fatal dose is not that much higher than a dose that gets you totally drunk, it is difficult to drink yourself to death in a single run - as long as you take it one unit at a time. What actually happens is you find yourself on the floor with a glass the next day, very much alive, and probably complaining of the worst headache you ever had.
