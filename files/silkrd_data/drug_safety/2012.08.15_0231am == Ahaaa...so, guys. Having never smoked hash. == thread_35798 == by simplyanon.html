This sounds more like hash OIL rather than hashish.  I am an expirienced hash maker, my favorite way to smoke it is through a BUCKET BONG with screen.  It often helps to hold flame slightly away from the hash depending on the type of hash, this may not even burn some types.

Other than that, any pipe, bong etc, with a screen or packed on top on cannabis buds.  Buds are good but they take away the true hash flavor, tobacco even more so.  Same problem with joints/spliffs.  Nice hashes have a wide variety of fine tastes, blond melt hash often being the best. 

Spotting, or hot kniving through a glass SPOTTLE or cardboard HOOTER can be an economic way to smoke more solid, pressed hash.

Keeping price in mind, I'd recommend something like http://silkroadvb5piz3r.onion/index.php/silkroad/item/d446511ed2

Hashish to buds is like wine to beer.  You can get really fine, really nice variety of tastes, or you can get cheap shit.  If you get nice shit.  DONT KILL THE FLAVOR :)
