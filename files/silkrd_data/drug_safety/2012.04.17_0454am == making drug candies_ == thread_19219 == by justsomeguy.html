Title: making drug candies?
Post by: justsomeguy on April 17, 2012, 04:54 am
I'd like to make 2c-b candies (probably using smarties or something similarly absorbent, like I've read about around here) for long-term storage and security of my personal stash.  I'm planning to just make a vodka solution of 100mg/mL, then use a metric dropper (20 drops/mL) to put one drop on each candy.  My main concern is that the liquid either won't absorb properly, leaving a layer of 2c-b on the outside that is easily rubbed off, or absorbs really well but turns the candy to mush.

I'd also be like to do the same with higher-dosage chemicals like MDMA, but I can't imagine how to get enough on each candy to be worth it.  Maybe if I could get the solution up to around 10mg/drop, I could do multiple drops (allowing it to dry in between each), to get up to around 30mg/candy.  Then I could just eat a few to adjust my dosage.

Can someone with experience provide some enlightenment?  Thanks!
Title: Re: making drug candies?
Post by: wowzers on April 17, 2012, 05:30 pm
2C-B will dissolve at 100mg/mL with 70% ipa/water, so you could make 10mg candies using love hearts (US smarties or similar) without worrying about the candy dissolving.

Thanks, wowzers, that's some good info.  I guess IPA would be a better choice, since it's so cheap.  I've looked at those pill presses, and I would LOVE one.  I wish they were affordable, or there was some sort of low-tech thing I could build, since I'm only doing this for personal use.

I PM'd foxymeow, who I know sometimes sells 2c-b smarties and asked him his thoughts.  He responded with the following:
Quote from: foxymeow on April 17, 2012, 03:12 pm
It doesn't work with higher dose stuff. Also most other RCs and especially real drugs are not soluble enough to make smarties with.

I suggest grain alcohol. It wouldn't turn the candy mush and there will probably be a thin layer of 2c-b on top of the smarty but it will be absorbed into the smarty. You really don't need to use that much especially if you are using grain alchohol. Just microwave it for 7 second to help it dissolve quickly.

I'm not sure why it wouldn't work for larger dose stuff, as long as you wait for each drop to dry before applying the next one.  Maybe there's simply not enough room in the candy for much more than 10mg of anything?  Ideas are welcome!

As far as grain alcohol goes, I could have sworn I read around here that using a high-proof alcohol was unwise, since some can evaporate before you finish dosing out the candies, which would clearly fuck up the doses.  I'll probably just use IPA as wowzers suggested, unless someone can think of a reason not to do that.
