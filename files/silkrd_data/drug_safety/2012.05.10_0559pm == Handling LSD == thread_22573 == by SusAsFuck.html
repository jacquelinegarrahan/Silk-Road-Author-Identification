Title: Re: Handling LSD
Post by: radi8power on May 11, 2012, 02:03 am
You want to keep it airtight, dark, dry, and cool. Tinfoil wrap it and put it in a Ziploc in your freezer or something. When you handle the tabs, molecules will absorb through your skin. Try to handle edges only, or put on a pair of rubber gloves. It shouldn't ever be a big enough factor to make you trip or ruin the tab, unless you put a couple of them under your headband before your morning jog.
