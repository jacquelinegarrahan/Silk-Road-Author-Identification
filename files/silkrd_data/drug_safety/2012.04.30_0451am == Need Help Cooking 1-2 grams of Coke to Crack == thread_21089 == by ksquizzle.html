Just a spoon with ammonia.
put your coke in the ammonia.
Heat the spoon and melt he coke.
Dip a tip of a knife (or so) in the molten coke.
When dip in and out again and again, slowly!, you will see the ball form on the tip of the knife because of the crystallization of the coke.
Ammonia is the opposite of acid.
So now you have the free base of coke , the crack.
You can chose to coock the coke again in water for it is now the free base the coke will not dissolve in the water anymore.
The raw coke does disappear/dissolves in water.
When you have a few grams, you can take a pan and a little water and dissolve the coke by heating up the water.
 The coke will disappear !!!
Don't worry, when a lot of ammonia is added, the coke will not be able to be dissolved in the water anymore and will appear again.
Like a drop of oil you will see the coke come floating to the surface again.
The same way as i described before.
Dip in and out and let the crack crystallize on the tip of the knife.
You can also stir the coke a little bit around in the spoon itself.
The coke will crystallize that way too.
Separate the coke (the crack) from the ammonia.

Take a mirror or a piece of glass or something, anything with a very smooth surface.
Squeeze the coke from the tip of the knife on the mirror and start to press it on the surface and start wiping like you are putting butter on your bread.
Keep wiping until it's dry.
The better the coke is, the sooner it will dry.

take a razor-blade for the fine final cut,
now you have the free base ready to smoke.

It's not suitable for snoring anymore.
It (the crack) will not melt in your lungs and nose.

Try a little bit at first.
To make the first hit from your pipe, right away a so called ""good one"" is dangerous.
See how the quality is and wait a few minutes to be sure.
A overdose is often the result of misjudging the quality.
