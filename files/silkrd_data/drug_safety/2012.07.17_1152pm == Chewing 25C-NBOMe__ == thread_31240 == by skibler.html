Title: Re: Chewing 25C-NBOMe??
Post by: OrganicConnection on November 02, 2012, 02:49 am
As I understand it (as someone who's enjoyed the NBOMes but has no deep knowledge of their chemistry) the active chemicals in the NBOMes are destroyed in the stomach, as many things are. So, just as you're recommended to put LSD under your tongue to absorb through the thinner membrane there before swallowing, NBOMes you must keep under your tongue (or in your cheek, or you can try plugging them, but I digress) since, unlike LSD, none or essentially none will be absorbed from your stomach.

I've generally heard it advised that you keep the tab under your tongue for 45 minutes, and try not to swallow your saliva. Basically you just want the membranes in your mouth awash in the good stuff for as long as possible.

As far as chewing - I usually keep blotters (of anything) under my tongue for however long, then chew them quickly, then back under the tongue for a bit more before swallowing, hoping that by chewing I'll wring some more of the chemical out. Does that actually happen? Maybe not, maybe it all comes out of the blotter naturally, but it costs nothing so why not.

So, the gist is put it under your tongue for 30-45 minutes, try to not swallow your saliva for as long as possible, and feel free to chew at some point if you so desire.
