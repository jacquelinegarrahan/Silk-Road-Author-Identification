Title: Re: 2C-b - experiences?
Post by: PriscillaMarie90 on May 20, 2013, 06:12 pm
2C-B was the first psychedelic I ever tried, and my first time was very mild. I don't remember from whom I purchased it from, but it honestly wasn't very good compared the my other, more recent experiences with the drug. The first time I stayed up all night talking to a friend of mine, I had very subtle hallucinations and an all-around good feeling. But a few months ago I received some 2C-B from a good friend of mine. The first time I tried some, I swallowed about 10mg and snorted 10mg (VERY PAINFUL FYI), the second time I swallowed about 30mg and both times I tripped. HARD.

Each trip started with some intense hallucinations of colors and patterns accompanied by occasional laughter. The rest was basically sensory overload, as well as racing thoughts that seemed to constantly loop. I felt an uncomfortable nervous feeling in my gut for most of the time, but I also felt really good. It was different from mushrooms, I like to do mushrooms and then gather my thoughts and have a fun time, but with 2C-B I have found that gathering my thoughts is impossible. I felt overwhelmed and underwhelmed at the same time, like I was expecting more to be happening but I didn't want it to. I don't know, it's hard to explain, but I remember thinking the same thoughts over and over and over and over, and I KNEW I was stuck in a loop, but I couldn't think anything else.

If you're going to try this drug I recommend doing it with a friend who also does drugs, even better if they've done 2C-B before.
