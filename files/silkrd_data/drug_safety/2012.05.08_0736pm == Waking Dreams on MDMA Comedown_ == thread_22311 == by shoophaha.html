Title: Waking Dreams on MDMA Comedown?
Post by: shoophaha on May 08, 2012, 07:36 pm
This happened to my friend and I when we did MDMA and I have never heard this effect of MDMA described before so I am intensely curious as to why it happened. Basically, we had waking dreams. I had smoked some weed to help me sleep (he didn't) and when we tried to go to sleep we were instead distracted by these dreams. We saw our friends in dream-like situations and wanted to talk to them and the weirdest thing is I nearly did even though I was wide awake; I remember mouthing some words while thinking that it was weird that I was talking to them at all because I knew they weren't real. I pinched myself and it hurt so I knew it wasn't a lucid dream. I was aware that my phone was under my pillow (where I always keep it) but I also had a phone in the "dream" and when I tried to read my texts they were all garbled letters and numbers like onion URLs and the letters kept dissolving and changing. Eventually I ended up just passing out and going to sleep. Does anyone have any similar experiences or insight?
