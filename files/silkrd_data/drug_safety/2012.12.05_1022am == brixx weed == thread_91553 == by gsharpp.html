Title: Re: brixx weed
Post by: Jopular on December 26, 2012, 12:10 am
If you mean sprayed with brixx then if it's too nasty to smoke maybe try washing it.

Let it sit in clean water for a few hours. Then when water gets dirty, replace with fresh water.
Maybe try letting the buds sit in a large bucket of fresh tap water for a day. (maybe a second day with some fresh water)

This might also remove some chlorophyl (good) and some terpenes (good or bad depending person) and some of the brixx stuff (good).

Just let the weed dry out somewhere like you would with freshly cut buds so it doesn't get mouldy.

This is known as a water cure.
