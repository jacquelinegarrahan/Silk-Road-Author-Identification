Sure, if you'd like a nasty headache, dizziness, and anxiety from quitting cold turkey. I doubt they'll be out of your system entirely by then, but they'll be diminished somewhat.

Olanzapine has a half-life of 21-54 hours (one-two days); half of the dose you take will be out of you within that time frame.
Abilify's half-life is closer to three or four days.

Brother the best way to have a great trip is the set setting and mindset.  With these types of medications I would always recommend waiting at least one week, and ideally 2 weeks.  Now for some people a week or even 2 are too long to go without meds.  If you are worried about anxiety about having a bad trip I would recommend a xanax .5mg an hour before tripping.  It is fast acting and after an hour you will feel mellow.  Yes I know the benzo will interfere with the intensity of the trip but this is a last resort for people that want to trip.  A low dose valium is a better choice as it is oil based and it has longer duration of calming effect.  I am curious you said previous you tripped however it was weak? What dose did you take? If you have any questions feel free to PM me. Good vibes and love to you brother.
