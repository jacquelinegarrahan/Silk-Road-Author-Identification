Title: Re: Tolerance of various drugs
Post by: Snoopish on January 11, 2013, 10:13 am
I like the idea of this getting fleshed out but I'm too lazy (at the moment) to do too much work on this (I already spend too much time on drug projects on SR  :P)

Here's a couple first thoughts:

Amphetamine: tolerance. Not sure on how long to wait but probably a 4-7 days will get your tolerances down (although, as with anything that you can abuse heavily, I think there's a possibility of permanently fucking up your baseline).
Cannabis: Definite tolerance. Tolerance levels may not ever go back to baseline but I'd say at least a week-a month of abstaining will show marked decrease in the amount needed to get high.
DMT: Immediate tolerance. No redosing for, ideally, 48 hours. If you hit some and don't get where you need to be a reload will likely be a waste. You just got to wait it out. I flip on this idea a lot but better to conserve your drugs.
Ketamine: My own view on this is a couple weeks. Also, I believe it has CT with certain opiates and shouldn't be combined with benzos/opiates
Methamphetamine: same as amphetamine only I think the buildup of tolerance is faster because, depending on the meth, it can be so easy to keep hitting it up.


Just some of my own experiences. Definitely no websites to back this up off the top of my head but a rough outline of some of my experiences/attempted delays.

Good luck!
