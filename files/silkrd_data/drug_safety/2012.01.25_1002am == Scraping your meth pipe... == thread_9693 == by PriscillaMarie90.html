Title: Scraping your meth pipe...
Post by: PriscillaMarie90 on January 25, 2012, 10:02 am
So as most of you may or may not know, when you smoke methamphetamine with a glass pipe, the vapor cools as you inhale it leaving an even coating of white residue. The residue is meth of course, and the more you use the pipe, the thicker the residue gets. After a while you can scrape the white coating off and have an entire bowl of meth you might not have even known was there!

Anyways, I usually use a straw or the plastic ink thing out of a pen to scrape it. What do you guys use? Straws or pens are okay, but sometimes the residue is really stubborn and won't come off. I'm just curious as to whether or not there is something better out there to scrape off more/all the meth back into the bowl. What would be perfect would be to find a really tiny metal tube or something lol.. But I've found that if the end you are using to scrape is rounded at all, it doesn't work. I don't really know how to explain it, like if you use a straw it works, but if you then melted the end of the straw with a lighter just a tiny bit and made the end slightly rounded, it won't work anymore.


I dunno, anyways your advice is appreciated! ;)

Title: Re: Scraping your meth pipe...
Post by: subtlety on January 25, 2012, 12:28 pm
Not sure how efficient this is, but after I've scraped and smoked as much as I can, I drop a tiny bit of water down the stem.  Then I carefully boil the water in the bubble and create a little steam. After the steam rolls down the stem, it's usually looking pretty clean.  Then I slowly boil of the water, and can usually get a few more hits off the residue left over.

This might be wasteful, but like I said, I do it after scraping anyway.  It does leave your pipe looking nice and clean for your next order 8)

Do this carefully if you try it.  The steam makes the entire pipe hot, and I usually hold the stem with a kitchen towel and boil slow.  Depending on how much water you've added, it can take a few cool-downs before you get it all.
