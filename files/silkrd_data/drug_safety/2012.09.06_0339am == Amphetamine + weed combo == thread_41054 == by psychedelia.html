Title: Re: Amphetamine + weed combo
Post by: sollemnis on September 06, 2012, 01:57 pm
I'd advise against this combo if you struggle with anxiety at all. Both drugs have a tendency to cause anxiety/the jitters. Combining the two could lead to a panic attack (If you can't handle the increase in heart rate). If you've never had a panic attack, let me tell you they're not pretty.

You notice an increase in heart rate
You try to stay calm, tell yourself its ok
Your heart rate is still high
You keep breathing but you're convinced you're going to have a heart attack
You get a bit light headed, right you're convinced your having a heart attack

It's just a horrible cycle... most panic attacks don't last that long, but they're not nice!

If you don't struggle with anxiety and you have experience with both drugs, you most likely will be fine and have a good time. But start off taking low amounts of both, and gradually build up until you're at a comfortable level. Don't go all out and take a lot of both, as this may be too intense and you might panic.

Have fun, take it easy and stay high ;)

Another good combo is MD + Weed. MD takes away anxiety and paranoia, so combining Weed with MD is fucking brilliant! Weed really does increase MD's euphoric properties. I'm not a smoker myself, but I love that combo!
