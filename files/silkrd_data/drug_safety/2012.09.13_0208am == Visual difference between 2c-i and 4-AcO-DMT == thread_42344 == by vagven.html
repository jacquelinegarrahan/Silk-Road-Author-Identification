Title: Re: Visual difference between 2c-i and 4-AcO-DMT
Post by: gambino on September 13, 2012, 04:11 am
You can't tell the difference by appearance.  They are both white powders, and both could have colored impurities.  Your best bet is to use reagent tests, such as those from Dancesafe (Mecke, Marquis, Simons).

The 4-aco, being an acetylated compound, was likely synthesized using acetic anhydride and *might* have a slight vinegar smell if it wasn't purified well. 

But if you are going to use these compounds, you really need to get reagents and learn to use them.  See (CLEARNET) http://www.bluelight.ru/vb/threads/512598-Marquis-Mecke-Mandellin-results-for-stim-empathogen-2C-X-RC-s-amp-more
