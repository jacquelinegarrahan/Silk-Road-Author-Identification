Have you used cocaine before? Are you planning on eating the cocaine or snorting it? I've heard eating it works. Never tried it, but I can't imagine it would be anywhere near as good as snorting it.
Cocaine + Music festivals aren't the best idea. Cocaine has a short high and you'll have to be topping up all the time. Because music festivals usually don't have many toilets, and you can't snort cocaine in public, it would be very difficult to do. However if you do use cocaine at the festival, I'd recommend using it in the last few hours because yes, every line of coke you have after the first few will be weaker than the last. Also you will feel very tired and fatigued after it wears off and you don't really want that for the ending of a music festival.

I'd recommend amphetamine &/or MDMA for a music festival. Their highs last a lot longer and are more suitable for music festivals because you don't need to keep having more.
For music festivals in the past, I've found my favourite thing is to have amphetamine in the morning before the music festival, and as that is wearing away as it heads into the evening I have MDMA. I wish Icould take MDMA for the entire thing, but MDMA isn't really made for using over an 11-12 hour period. 6 hours is probably the most you'd get from MDMA. After that when you top up, it will be a lot weaker and the comedown will be harsh.

Be careful though because mixing 2 stimulants can be dangerous and some people cope better than others with speedy drugs.
