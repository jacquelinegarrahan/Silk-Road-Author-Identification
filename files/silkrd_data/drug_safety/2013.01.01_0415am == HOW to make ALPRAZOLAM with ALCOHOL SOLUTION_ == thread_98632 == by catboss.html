furthermore, i have found my solution.

mix 100mg of pure alprazolam powder in 100ml of 91% isopropyl alcohol and add miralax (a OTC laxative) for stability. dose = 1mg per 1ml.

alcohol first, add miralax 2nd, lastly add powder. mix in that order. may warm the alcohol in warm water bath to mix faster. store in airtight containers so the alcohol does not evaporate. dose on candy, paper, or bread. wait few hours for alcohol to evaporate, consume.
