Title: Re: Want to get high - but can't
Post by: Railgun on September 18, 2013, 01:23 pm
SSRIs are going to kill MDMA, LSD, Shrooms, etc. Commonly, when people want to enhance drug affects, they use MAOIs, but this can kill you on SSRIs.  Taking a huge dose of MDMA may also cause serotonin syndrome, as it works by increasing serotonin, yet your body cannot take it in (it is being blocked by your SSRI).

Opiates, cocaine, and marijuana will not suffer this attenuation. They will be all that will work for you while on SSRIs, so hand up the MDMA.
