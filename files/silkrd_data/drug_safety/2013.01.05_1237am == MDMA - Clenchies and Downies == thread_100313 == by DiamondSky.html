Title: Re: MDMA - Clenchies and Downies
Post by: trancesend on January 05, 2013, 12:46 am
What kind of magnesium are you taking?  It should be magnesium chelate, any other kind is not as bio-available, and you should be taking 500-1000mg.  This has reduced jaw clenching in half for me.

Do not take 5-HTP with MDMA, it can lead to serotonin syndrome.  Taking it after can help with the depression, but eating well, exercise, and getting lots of sleep are just as important to let your body recover.  Repeated doses in one night are also much harder on your system and lead to exponentially greater neurotoxicity.  If fiending is an issue, take only as much as you plan to do with you, and leave the rest of the drugs in a hard-to-get-to location.  Even doing it once a month is pushing it, I would suggest cutting back to once ever 3-4 months.
