Title: Re: Candyflip with VitaCat acid
Post by: AnimusVox on January 07, 2013, 10:33 pm
"Too intense" is usually subjective. Some people associate it with a LOT of visuals, loss of ego, total interconnectedness with the world, etc.. while others associate it with a chaotic and confusing trip. One VitaCat Maya shouldn't be enough to offer for a chaotic trip, especially if you follow proper set and setting guidelines. They've been established to be around ~250ug, which is a strong yet beautiful dosage. As for candyflipping, it's my favorite, and any chaos or negative feelings that is brought on by the higher dose of LSD will be counteracted by MDMA's empathogenic qualities and will offer for a very fun, positive, and beautiful trip. Make sure you follow a timeline so that the LSD and MDMA peak and comedown at around the same time. My recommendation:

T+0:00: VitaCat's Maya
T+2:00: Schizofreen's Partyflock

Cheers!
