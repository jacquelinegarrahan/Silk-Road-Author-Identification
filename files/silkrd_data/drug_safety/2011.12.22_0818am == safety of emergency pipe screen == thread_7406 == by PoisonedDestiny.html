Post by: arutha on December 22, 2011, 01:29 pm
As a general rule, torch the hell out of any material before putting it in your bong to get rid of any coating. Most metals will have some kind of coating. Hold onto it with a pair of pliers and burn it for a couple minutes. Watch for smoke coming off of it as a sign of the coating burning away.

I find it is easier to jam a chunk of weed into the hole to act as a screen and stop the finer stuff falling through, even a piece of stem is enough usually. If you only have hash, you could jam a piece of parsley in.
