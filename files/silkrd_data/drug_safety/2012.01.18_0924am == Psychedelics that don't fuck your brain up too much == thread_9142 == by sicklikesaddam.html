Title: Re: Psychedelics that don't fuck your brain up too much
Post by: novocaine on January 18, 2012, 10:50 am
I would say that it was accurate for that person however it may definitely be inaccurate for you.
Psychedelics are like that.

I could say that psychedelics should only be used by those who have mentally prepared for the trip but this is inaccurate as well, because another can take psychedelics without any preparation, anywhere anytime.

If you are after full on visuals you get the whole package. Sometimes it would be great just to get the visuals but unfortunately for some, they get the head fuck as well

But overall the general consensus is... Prepare your mind and prepare your surroundings. Know you are about to trip and think and see things you may or may not like. Set and setting etc etc.

Personally I only enjoy a trip if I am solo. If I really lose touch on things I usually have somebody on speed dial back in reality to touch base with.

Have you tried any of the 2c-x??

The most favorable experience I have had has been a acid mescaline combo. Beautiful ;) But once again it was the right time and place.

edit- Also try lighter doses. It sounds like you are after awesome visuals but imo start off light and work your way up to higher doses. You may not get the visuals you are after but you will become familiar with how you react and think while tripping lightly.
