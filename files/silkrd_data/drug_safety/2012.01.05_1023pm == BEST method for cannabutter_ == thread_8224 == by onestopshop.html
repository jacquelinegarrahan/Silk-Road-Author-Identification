Recipe for potent CannaButta by redforeva(sr)
10lb Real Butter
12 oz Olive/Coconut/Palm/Any Oil
5lb untrimmed Cannabis Flowers (Frosty Sativa-Dom Hybrid)
25lb frozen carbon dioxide
1 120 micron screen
1 70 micron screen
1 large mirror(1meterX1meter recommended)
Scraper blade (metal paddle)
1 Large Pot for absorbing cannabis oils
1 30 gallon plastic drum
2 xxxl rubberbands
1 pair of good cutting tools
1 bottle rubbing alcohol
1 box of Gloves

Chop buds into smaller than dime pieces. Then add the dry ice (pellets are better)
Put the 120 micron screen across the top of the drum and secure with rubber bands.
Turn upside down over the mirror and shake the drum to release the resin glands from the flower.
Scrape resin and store for later in a ziplock bag.
Melt 10lbs real butter in a large pot, once melted spoon off all foam.
Add 12 oz oil* and stir well.
Add stored resin stir well and let it set to absorb on low heat.
Take your 70 micron screen and put it across  the top of a funnels opening to filter the butter later into the final storage vessel.
After 30 minutes at lower than 150F but higher than 100F Filter the liquid into bottles.
Let cool and store in fridge. Last 2-3 months depending on temp.
If butter isnt wanting to come out of a bottle try heating with warm tap water in the sink.

Cheers, Redforeva

I've been making cannabutter for years.  It's pretty simple.

1) Thoroughly dry the weed.  We're talking completely, totally, bone dry.  This converts the raw THC in the plant to it's psychoactive form, a process that usually occurs when the cannabis is burned.  For more on the chemistry involved, google "marijuana Decarboxylation".   Preheat your oven to 250F, TURN THE OVEN OFF, and put the weed in on a cookie tray.  Wait a half hour or so, remove the weed, and repeat the process until the weed crumbles into dust when you crush it.

2) I like to crumble the weed into powder and sift it through a kitchen strainer.  This doesn't affect potency (so you may choose to skip this step), but it's nice not to encounter stems and twigs when eating the butter.

3) melt the butter in a double boiler (with low grade weed, I'll use 4grams of better for every gram of powdered weed), then add the weed.  Cover, reduce heat so the water is just simmering, and leave it for a few hours, stirring occasionally.

4) You're done!  Some people like to filter the weed out of the butter, but the greasy plant matter you filter out is still chock full of weedy goodness!  Why throw it away?

Post by: Silpheed on January 25, 2012, 11:10 am
Are you talking about cooking cannabis and dairy butter, or making a concentrate?

Look into making bubble hash. Although there are lots of ways to make hash: I've read about using a screen, a blender, a drum machine (I think Jorge Cervantes has a video about washing machine hash), and a bubble bag with micron pores. Or even nylons. Supposedly bubble hash is the best (it's called bubble hash because it bubbles when heated). The goal of making bubble hash is to save only the trichomes I think.

Also, you should be eating that finger hash, or scraping it off and smoking it. For centuries, people have made hash by simply rubbing cannabis in between the palms of their hands (make sure your hands are clean). Or there's a story about "goddess hash" where naked women would walk through cannabis fields and the resin would stick to their body and pubic hair.

THCA is in cannabis trichomes, and when it's burned it's decarboxylated and converted into the more psychoative form THC.

Regarding heat and cannabis, another thread has this information:

70 F, the most volatile terpenoids start to evaporate leaving a pungent odor
87 F, less volatile terpenoids start to evaporate leaving a more pungent odor
102 F, pretty much all terpenoids start to evaporate rapidly
122 F, the water molecule in THCA evaporates and THCA decarboxylates and is converted into THC
150 F, CBD melts and begins to evaporate
365 F, CBN boils
392 F, THC boils

from here:
http://dkn255hz262ypmii.onion/index.php?topic=7190.msg64535#msg64535

So it would make sense that bubble hash bubbles because when a flame is applied, THC is boiling.

I've read elsewhere that it's been hypothesized that THC can act like sunscreen for cannabis. And supposedly THC can absorb UV light. If THCA turns into THC at 122 F, then the THC would absorb UV light and the plant would be protected from radiation. (Although maybe the UV light especially UVB is just degrading the THC). Also, I've read that precursors of THC in the plant have antimicrobial properties.

Post by: GaltRR on January 25, 2012, 06:23 pm
This is way I have usually done it. Bring a pot of water to a boil, throw in 2 sticks of butter and 1/4 of ground bud. Let it simmer for a few hours. Let it cool for a little bit and pour the container into a tupperware and put it into the fridge. The butter/THC/actives will form a solid layer on top of the water, pour the water out. You have cannabutter. Some people will pour the water/butter through a coffee filter to get rid of the plant matter this is just a personal preference.
