Title: Re: MDMA + 2C-B
Post by: BlueGiraffe on December 30, 2012, 08:58 pm
Best time for 2C-B is just before baseline of MDMA - i.e. later than you think.

If you want to tweak that very nicely then put a dash of acid at the beginning (low 25ug - 50ug) and start on GHB once over the peak of the 2C-B (about T + 3 hours).
