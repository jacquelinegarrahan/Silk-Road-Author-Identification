Title: Re: Ketamine and Alcohol
Post by: Yoshitoshi on March 02, 2012, 10:29 am
Agree with the other respondents, there is no enhancement, and a risk of (at best) sleeping through the K experience, or worse, depending on quantities consumed of each.

Equally, you don't have to be completely alcohol-free, but you should be nowhere near drunk. Social is fine, slurring is not.

As the others have said, because K has strong sedative effects, if you're combining it with anything, the other stuff should be stimulant in nature. Best times for me are coming down from mdma, buzzed and relaxed enough to enjoy a really rich experience, probably having had a mixer drink though when getting in after the club.

Which is another point, if you've not tried K before, you're best doing it behind closed doors with a small group of trusted companions. K-holing in public is not a good look...!
