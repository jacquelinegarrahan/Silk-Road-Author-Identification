Post by: DiamondSky on January 09, 2013, 09:33 pm
As far as testing the blotter is concerned it's a little tricky. Assuming you are using a liquid color test kit it is the liquid, not the drug that should be changing color. The issue with the blotter is that it is not necessarily consistently covered with the drug. Most people will dose the middle and the liquid will spread out towards the sides but not equally. If I was going to test paper... sadly... I would likely just drop a whole tab in there. I guess if you really wanted to try and save most of it you could fold the blotter to a triangle with the center at the top and a tiny part of the top off to drop that in and test. If it reacts you are set and if not probably drop the rest of the tab in.

Also, if you are going for visuals and you haven't tried it before you might want to hook upo with some MDA. Much more visual than MDMA and still a similar body high. I find chasing visuals on MDMA is expensive and hard to do but on MDA I can take a more reasonable dose and still get some trippy visuals.

If you want to try something a little different, 2CB seems to pretty cool tool. Nice body high / euphoria, little clearer head space than MDMA / MDA, and much more visual than MDMA - at 20 - 25 mg for me it's about as visual as a LARGE dose of MDA. Totally different trip than acid. LSD is often philosophical while 2CB satirical.

Lots of people love the MDMA / MDA combo. Normally they dose 75% MDMA to 25% MDA - So 100mg MDMA and 50mg MDA. I'll be honest though, I chase the visuals and that just doesn't get me to where I want to be. If anything I would probably reverse that formula or just take the MDA alone.

MDA is a little different than MDMA though in that it really holds the high quite a bit longer the more you dose. With MDMA if I take too much I get too high but return to baseline on a pretty consistent schedule. With MDA the more I take the higher I get AND the longer it keeps going.

I'd say if you are pretty comfortable with bigger doses of MDMA and the visuals / come up aren't going to put you in a bad mental place (hard on either of those drugs really). I'd start with 150mg and bump from there an hour or two in. In my experience, I think thanks to the fact that it just lasts longer than MDMA, it's much easier to stay high on MDA at a pretty constant level with well timed bumps than it is with MDMA where I go up and down more between the bumps. I normally plug stuff instead of taking it orally just because it takes a little less and the bio-availability is more consistent (i.e. I don't have to worry about the burger I ate a few hours ago getting between me and my roll). As a big guy, I was well off base at 120mg plugged.

At higher doses, I do find I get pretty shaky / sweaty and the visuals definitely get more intense. It goes from bold colors - fuzzy shapes - locked moments in time - fractal insanity as you get more and more in you.

I normally order my MDA from ILF (http://silkroadvb5piz3r.onion/silkroad/user/5be5f2e73a). Gets here nice and quick in the US, doesn't cost too much, and... well, he's the only one selling it. It' looks like a nice light brown very fine sugar powder. Taken orally me and my GF just drop it in some water, swirl it around and slam it with a nice fruit flavored chaser of some kind.

Hope that helps, always fun to play with new drugs and I think MDA is probably closer to the high you are looking for off X so make sure to let us know how it goes if you try it.
