Title: Re: Does pure cocaine make the nose bleed?
Post by: phunky on September 02, 2013, 10:05 am
there are harsher cutting agents that will definitely make the nose bleed more.  if your nose starts bleeding, it is time to stop and let your nose heal for a few days at least.   continuing to do blow will eventually begin to eat away at the very sensitive lining in your nose and you could end up doing permanent damage that might not be so fun to deal with.

one thing you can do is make sure you get your powder as FINE as possible.  heat it up, push it through a gold tone coffee filter or grinder screen -- something really fine. 

i find it's also helpful to clean your nose out before but definitely important after use. 

get a neti pot and learn how to use it properly (using distilled or boiled water is important, tap water can apparently contain brain-eating amoebas that could kill you -- not even joking, this is real). 

also when blowing your nose it's actually bad to hold one nostril closed and blow out the other -- something i did for years, haha!  you can damage your ear canals this way and it also strains your nasal cavity.  it also tends to just clog one side of your nose for some reason so after you run saline through it try to blow through both nostrils evenly.

also do not take decongestants or any kind of steroid based nasal sprays for the nose, they can actually cause more damage if the nose has already shown blood.
