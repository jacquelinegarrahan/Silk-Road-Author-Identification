Title: let's have a serious discussion about DXM
Post by: apples on November 02, 2012, 03:02 am
i wanted to get your guy's opinions on dxm/ your experiences with it/ lasting effects (if present from the drug).


my reasoning of at least wanting to get some kind of input on this is as follows.

there's a lot of internet literature (well not really literature but a lot of really bad drug rehab websites or psuedo forums full of people who can't spell) describing instances where people who were occasional users developing lasting negative side effects (severe depression, HPPD, almost psychosis symptoms, etc) after short periods of time. there have also been stories (erowid, various reports) of people with dxm habituation or previous habituation (myself included) but a pretty nice chunk of these reports from people i've previously talked to/befriended didn't report lasting negative effects following cessation.

i took dxm pretty much daily for about 8 months following heroin cessation. kind of as an attempt to ween myself away without having to use methadone (which i've heard horrible things about) and buprenorphine (which has always made me fucking sick no matter how small the dose and how long it had been since i'd taken opiates). doses were generally around 500-700 mgs in the beginning then a switch to the polistyrex probally about 3 months in due to longer suppression of opiate withdrawls. average doses were between 1200-1800 mgs (usually 1800).

my reason for highlighting my own feats in being a freak who pounded cough syrup is that the doses were pretty significant and i personally have never had any negative long lasting effects from my previous use.

what are your guy's stories? have you had complications (liver/kidney/heart damage) or any other crazy fucked up lingering side effects and is there really any truth to the plethora of just mong english written reports of people becoming vegetables or crazy about their 'cousin' or whatever. also do you guys believe it really is as dangerous as a lot of people saying it is (discounting serotonin syndrome as a cause of interactions with different drugs) (and also obviously the risk of death increases with dose. i am asking in general with dxm intoxication).

i'm also going to say i'm not at all a cheerleader for the drug anymore if that's how i come across.

DXM capsules indeed.

Cough syrup containing dxm often contains so much sugar that it will make you sick drinking an effective amount of it. Capsules are another story though, since you can take 10 or 20 of those to get a beneficialHe effect without ingesting a couple of pounds of sugar in the process.
