Title: Re: First time LSD
Post by: Spunkaroo on September 24, 2012, 01:48 pm
My advice means squat really, but I would start with one blotter of whatever you end up getting. If it's rated strongly (i.e. over 200 ug) I'd start with less. You can always take more, but never less, and re-dosing in the first 90 minutes should still be effective if you feel the need.

At less than 200 ug the effects are usually subtle for me, no closed eye visuals and subtle open eye distortions, but the mental state is great and definitely well worth it. If you're hell bent on a strong trip aim for 200 - 400 ug. It can be intense for a newbie so you may want to be inside or even have a sitter.

Personally I would go low first as you will be able to appreciate it more without shattering your world in one go, and can explore deeper on subsequent trips.
