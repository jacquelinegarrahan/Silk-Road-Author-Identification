Title: Re: Storing Shrooms?
Post by: hallucinating horse on August 01, 2013, 10:59 pm
Hi, neo67.

Most important thing is moisture and heat. Don't store them someplace damp. The ideal situation is vacuum sealed in a mason jar with a silica packet for moisture.

Moisture will grow mold
Heat will cause the mushrooms to lose potency.

Shrooms stored this way will stay for a year or two without any kind of potency loss.
Source: I have grown pounds of mushrooms.
