Post by: Evanescence on January 31, 2012, 06:15 pm
Hey man, first know this - you are not alone.  Other people fighting the same battle you are and we are wishing positive things for your life.  Trite maybe but I like people reminding me of that. :)

1)  .5mg a day is bullshit, I would find another doctor or buy from here if you have to.

2)  Are you in therapy?  Remember the science shows CBT combined with drugs makes the most difference.  Drugs alone cannot match drugs + CBT, there is a lot of data proving this.  The only thing Freud got right IMO.

3)  Have you tried to find activities that help like meditation, exercise, etc (singing obnoxiously to music I love can dial me down a notch)?

My point is the anxiety game is not about cure it's about lowering the level.

For example if your anxiety is a 10, maybe drugs can get you down to an 8, add in CBT to get down to a 6, then meditation to get to a 5. 

At a level of 5 then maybe you can get some things done and enjoy life a little.
