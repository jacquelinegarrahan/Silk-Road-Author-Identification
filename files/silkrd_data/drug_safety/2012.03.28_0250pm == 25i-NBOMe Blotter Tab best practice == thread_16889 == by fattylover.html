Wanted to include this 411 for archival purposes regarding those who have circulatory issues from previous trauma, or for whatever reason, and the consumption of 25i-NBOMe. I am a 6 foot tall, 250 pound, 42 year old male who, in 2004, was the pedestrian in a pedestrian versus automobile accident. Coma for 54 days, 120 days of rehab hospitalization, two steel rods - one in each leg, 11 hour facial/skull reconstruct - 8 pieces of titanium in my spine, yadda yadda. Anyway, all of my large deep veins in both legs were crushed in the accident and I am left with a network of much smaller veins to return my blood to the heart, so I am very sensitive to hypertension/vasoconstriction.  If anyone else has the tendency to have  DVT ( deep vein thrombosis - a blod clot ), this report may be of interest to you.

I have been using psychedelics since 1985 and I have experienced most of the champions: LSD, peyote, psilocybes, tampenesis, MDMA, salvia, DMT, no ayahausca yet but you get the idea of my realm of experience.  None of these aforementioned substances have ever caused me vasoconstriction or vasoconstriction with the exception of LSD, which has only given me hypertension a couple of times. After having three experiences with 25i-NBOMe (2x 2mg 1x 1.5mg). The RC was complexed and the 2mg trips were very nice. The 1.5mg dose didn't quite make it over the hump, but I did notice some pretty serious CONS for those of us with lower extremity circulation issues. What few veins I have left in my lower legs hurt all the way through the come up and didn't quit until after T 6:00. For me 2mg is the starting point for this molecule, but I won't do more than that because of the circulation thang....

Thought I'd let you guys know who may be on the seniors tour or have circulation issues.
