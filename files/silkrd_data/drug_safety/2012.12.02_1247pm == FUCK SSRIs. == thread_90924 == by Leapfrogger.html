Title: FUCK SSRIs.
Post by: Leapfrogger on December 02, 2012, 12:47 pm
So recently, after maybe three months of preparation, I finally placed my first order on the Road. I decided to buy some MDMA, because 1) I haven't rolled in five years, 2) it's a buyer's market for MDMA right now and, 3) my friend and I didn't have any plans for New Year's yet.

I came home to find the box waiting for me. I stared at it in astonishment... okay, no, let's be honest, I pretty much ripped it apart in giddy excitement as soon as I was away from prying eyes. Then I came upon The Product. Holy shit, it was so beautiful. Wow, it really does smell like licorice. And these crystals...this looks like a fucking prop, I mean it's too pretty to be real. (Keep in mind I'd never seen pure MDMA before, nor have I ever seen meth.)
 
So, yeah, I bought it for NYE, but my friend (who I won't be seeing until then) knew there was no way I was gonna have molly just sitting in a drawer for a month- and I had a whole gram! I had to try it out, even if I was gonna be alone. I held out until the weekend at least.

I put a total of 140 mgs into two capsules (it wouldn't fit into one) and, for the first time ever, plugged them.

I sat, put on In Rainbows, and waited.

An hour went by. An hour and a half.

Are these some exceptionally thick capsules I used? Let's check to make sure everything went as planned up there... Hm. Not feeling any capsules. What the fuck...

Maybe they just went up really far and still haven't dissolved yet.

Two hours. I feel a bit more excited, but that might just be from the anticipation.

Fuck this, I'll take what was supposed to be the 80mg booster now. Orally this time.

Oh my god, my jaw is clenching! This is good, this is good.

Fuck it, lemme rail 50 grams.

YOW! That burned.

I wonder how my eyes look. WHOA, my pupils are fucking huge.

Two and a half hours. Man, that's a nice nasty drip I got going. I feel warmer than I did before, I guess.

Three hours. It becomes obvious that it's not gonna hit me.

This doesn't make sense. This exact product has been described as at least "great", if not the best MDMA they've ever had, in every single review I read.

And then I remembered. Last weekend, before making any plans to buy MDMA, I had been drinking all night and wanted to pop some pills before going to bed. I didn't have anything on hand, but I wanted to swallow SOMETHING, so I washed down one or two or three 20mg-Citaloprams with my last beer. Citalopram is some anti-anxiety bullshit of which I was once prescribed a non-pharmaceutical (he instructed me to cut the pills into 1/4ths) dosage, by a psychiatrist whom I never visited again.

Citalopram is an SSRI, and SSRIs tend to, ahem, "weaken the effects" of MDMA. Weaken the effects to almost nothing. And one study gives you the impression that Citalopram is the absolute worst of them.

The infuriating thing is that I fucking KNEW this- about MDMA and SSRIs in general, anyway. I even remember when I stopped taking Citalopram a while ago, thinking, "Well, it doesn't seem to be making much of a difference, and any MDMA experience I might have is going to be far more therapeutic than this shit, so I'd better be ready!" I wasn't in denial about there being a potential problem, I'd just honestly forgotten I'd taken it.

The lesson here? Don't swallow random pills just to do it. Pay better attention to what you're putting in your body.

I know I'm to blame for this. And it's testament to the purity of the product that I felt almost nothing- if it was cut with speed or even caffeine, those would have come through. But I didn't even have a caffeine buzz.

So it looks like we're waiting till New Year's Eve after all. Until then I'll be jogging ten miles every day and taking oatmeal enemas every night in hopes of clearing this zombifying, ecstasy-killing SSRI out of my fucking system.

Good thing I've got some shrooms coming in next week.

God bless Silk Road
