Post by: gestaltassault2 on November 22, 2012, 08:59 pm
The majority of vendors on SR are reliable, reputable, and trustworthy. If you look next to the vendors name you will see a number. This number is the vendor's rating on a scale of 1 - 100 with 100 being the best. Any vendor with a rating over 90 will be selling you exactly what you were expecting to get. Also you can read other user's feedback on the vendor's service and product quality.

Even though the majority of vendors on SR are reputable, you should always test your drugs before taking them. You can get various tests like the ones on http://eztest.com/ to see if your drugs have been cut or adulterated.
