Title: Re: Plugging MDMA, Sildenafil Citrate and Bremelanotide, safe dosages?
Post by: sdesu on June 11, 2012, 06:16 am
As far as I can tell, there should be no reaction between the three, unless you're taking the sildenafil for hypertension (which is unlikely, but a possibility nonetheless).

Why are you taking these drugs rectally? I understand the intention of quicker absorption, but this totally changes the dosing of the medicine.

For instance, the normal dose for Sildenafil via oral administration is 50mg by mouth, 1 hour before sexual activity. However, the IV dose is 10mg three times a day (When used for anti-hypertensive purposes).

Since rectal administration is somewhat similar to IV administration, in regards to metabolism, the dosages may likely be similar as well.
