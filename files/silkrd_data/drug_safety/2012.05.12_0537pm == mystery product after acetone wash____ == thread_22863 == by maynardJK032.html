Title: Re: mystery product after acetone wash????
Post by: eternalcausation on May 12, 2012, 07:55 pm
Man there is really no way to tell. Most of the time the acetone will remove water in-solubles and such.  Most synthetic cocaine or partly synthetic cocaine contain random water soluble cocaine-like highs..Usually stuff like mdppp, mdpv, naphyrone and more cocaine substitutions. So you probably removed most of the high from the "cocaine" with the acetone wash as these compounds are probably acetone soluble as well as water soluble. Did you save your acetone after your wash to evaporate? This will tell you a lot. Did you dry out your acetone first? Meaning did you remove any water content from your acetone or did you use lab grade acetone that contains no water? Hope you did because what little cocaine there might have been in your cocaine could have easily been stripped with water containing acetone.

After your wash, your product probably looked shinny and  more crystalline in nature than when you started. If the prodoct is super clean then your remaining crystals might be ALL cocaine (RARE) If your product is half clean, you might have a decent percentage of cocaine, with some other "caines" , and if your product is bunk, more than likly all remaining crystals are a mixture of anesthetic like Cocaine derivatives commonly used in VET practices or Dental practices..... lidocaine ,benzocaine, Procaine, Prilocaine, Novocaine...or just choose one of the many "Caine" research compounds out and about on the web and its probably in your  "cocaine". And they arnt gonna get you high at all...

So all in all, if your cocaine is synthetic cocaine, you might be removing the synthetic high with the acetone. And what you got left over is the numbing aspect to the fake cocaine, or the eye candy aspect to the fake cocaine to look like fishscale when its not.

I take my crystals which usually contain 25-50 % cocaine hcl, and 50-75 % "caine" cuts and I use baking soda to basify the crystals. This slow reactions compared to ammonia allows me to use the fact that these "caine" cuts act different as oils, then they do as crystals. I can use there weight property, color and miscibility to separate the Cocaine Oil from the "Caine" oils. I then take my Cocaine freebase and either smoke it or return the Cocaine back to Cocaine HCL with deluted hydrochloric acid and evaporate...And wash with acetone again if I used to much acid.
