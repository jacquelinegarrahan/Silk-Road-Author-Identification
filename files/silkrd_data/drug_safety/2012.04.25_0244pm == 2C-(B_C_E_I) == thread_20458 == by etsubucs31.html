Title: Re: 2C-(B/C/E/I)
Post by: Horizons on April 25, 2012, 03:07 pm
2c-b and 2c-e seem to be the most indicated 2cs for inexperienced trippers. You should start with a light dose, and DO NOT redose the first few times. If you aren't used to how a particular drug interacts with your body, you can't be sure if you're still coming up and redosing might lead to a higher dose than you're ready for.

I recommend you visit www.erowid.org to read up on the drugs you want to compare. The site has info on doses, effects and precautions to take, as well as a huge database of trip reports. It's a wealth of information that will surely help you to make your decision.

Always remember that each body interacts differently with drugs. No two people have the same trip. Be cautious, and you'll be fine.
