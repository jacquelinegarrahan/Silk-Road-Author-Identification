Title: Re: MDA question
Post by: Nocturnalbeyond718 on August 21, 2012, 06:05 pm
MDA is more hallucinogenic than MDMA alone. Its effects come on strong in slightly smaller doses than MDMA. For example, if you usually take 150mg of MDMA, it is generally held that about 100mg of MDA would produce similar effects. MDA also lasts a bit longer than MDMA (6+ hours compared to about 3-5 for MDMA).

I also agree that re-dosing on MDA is not a great idea. MDA can be more neurotoxic and if you take too much or re-dose a lot, you may start to have a weird trip and start to see things.

I really enjoy mixing MDA with MDMA with about a 60/40 ratio of MDMA to MDA. You get the best of both worlds! :)

If I were taking MDA alone, I would probably take about 120-150mg (I have a relatively high tolerance).
