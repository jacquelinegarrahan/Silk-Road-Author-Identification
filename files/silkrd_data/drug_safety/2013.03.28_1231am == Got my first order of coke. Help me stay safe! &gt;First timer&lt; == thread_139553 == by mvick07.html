Post by: JoeHartley on March 31, 2013, 03:42 pm
A few things which I wish I had been told before I started snorting coke:

1) Sterilize the surface on which you chop up your C! Alcohol wipes are an easy way to do this. I normally use a sturdy laminated book cover as my surface because I found that crushing rocks on a mirror or piece of glass left me with little coke crumbs flying everywhere, whereas a book cover has a little more give / spring in it and is thus much less likely to cause this to happen.

2) Sterilize a credit-card to use to chop up your C! Again alcohol wipes are an easy way to do this. I prefer a card over a razorblade because although a razorblade is sterile initially I've found they rust quickly and rust is definitely not healthy for you! Also, slightly angling the card on its side provides that nice thin edge which is the only benefit of razorblades.

3) Chop up your coke as much as physically possible! Chop it into grains of sand, and then make it finer. This increases the surface area of the coke and makes it much more easily absorbed into your sinuses. Also, a line of big grainy coke when properly chopped up can turn into two fine powder lines of the same size. This makes dosing much safer as you can snort a 50mg fine powder line that measures 3cm for example, instead of a 3cm grainy line that weighs 100mg. I've found that the finer the powder, the smoother up the nose it goes ;)

4) Don't use a rolled-up bank note as your snorting straw! This can transmit diseases very easily, and I'm assuming you don't want that. Instead use a drinking straw fresh from its packet, as these are pre-sterilized. If you prefer something with a wider diameter then trace a bank note onto a clean fresh piece of white bleached paper that has no ink on it. Cut it out, and then cut it in half as regular paper is thicker than money paper. Roll this up like a regular bank note and tape it in place.

5) Take roughly 50mg as your first dose, and then leave it at least 30 minutes before re-dosing. It's also a good idea to measure your heart rate after a little while, and if it's higher than 160bpm as you are at a complete rest then it might be a good idea to visit the hospital. Stabbing chest pains are also a good indication that you should seek medical help. If you have any previous heart problems then it's best not to touch the stuff in the first place.

6) For your first time, don't mix coke with other drugs. Just see how it affects you on its own.

7) If it's good coke then you'll want to do more and more. I don't recommend doing more than 0.3g in your first sitting, as you could end up as a shaking sweaty mess with a thumping pressure in your head.

8) Don't buy more coke until you've finished the gram you already have. A coke habit is ridiculously easy to develop.

9) If you're a guy, then having a lady at your disposal during a coke session is amazing. Railing a line off a nice round ass before fucking it is one of the best feelings ever.

That's about all I have to say, I hope you have fun, and I'm sure you will. Just remember, coke is like an attractive wife; at the start shes great all day long, but before you know it she's gotten old, ugly and starts sucking the life out of your soul instead of the cum out of your dick ;)
