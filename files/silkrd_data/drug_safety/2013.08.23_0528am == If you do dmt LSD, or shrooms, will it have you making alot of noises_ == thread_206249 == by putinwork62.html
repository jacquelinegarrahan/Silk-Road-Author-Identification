Title: Re: If you do dmt LSD, or shrooms, will it have you making alot of noises?
Post by: Alecks on August 25, 2013, 12:14 am
Have you tried any of those yet?

Honestly if you don't have much experience with psychedelics I wouldn't recommend taking it in a setting like that. Doing it in a place where you don't want someone to find out, or you're trying to hide that you're high can really make things go bad. ESPECIALLY if they were to find out and made a big deal about it, that would definitely push me into a bad trip.

Personally one of the best experiences I had on shrooms was me and a buddy of mine took 2 grams each and just chilled at the park for like 6 hours, although this park wasn't a very popular or large one, kind of secluded which helped. If you have somewhere you can go like this, outside without running into a bunch of strangers. This will allow you to talk and move around without fearing that someone is going to hear/find out that you're tripping. Also like everyone else mentioned already, you do not want to seclude yourself to just 1 room. You may not feel like you'll get a "caged-in" feeling but trust me, it will be there and you will want to move around/change scenery.

Or if you really want to do it by yourself, do what I did a few times when I was living with the parents and rent out a cheap motel room for a night (40 bucks for a night totally worth the experience). This will make sure you are by yourself with no one else influencing your trip, that and you can easily leave and go on a walk if you wanted to.

Just my 2 cents hope it helped.
