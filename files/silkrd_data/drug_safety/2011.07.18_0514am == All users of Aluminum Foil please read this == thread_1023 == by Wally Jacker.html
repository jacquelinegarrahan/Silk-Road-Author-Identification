Hi folks!

I wanted to pass on some important information about using tin foil to smoke your favorite "products".

First off it should be noted that whenever you put something on foil and chase it around with a straw as heat is applied underneath you really are not smoking.  You are actually vaporizing.  There's no combustion therefore there's no smoke, just vapor.

But that's not the important part.  Sorry if this is information you are already privy to.  But in any case it bears repeating.

You can poison yourself if you don't vaporize the right way when using aluminum foil! 

A friend of mine nearly died.  His tongue had turned black, his liver distended and his pores were oozing toxins.  He didn't want to go to the hospital but we forced him too.  Good thing because he was nearly dead and we got there just in time.

His blood had been heavlily contaminated from breathing aluminum vapor over a long period of time.  Primarily from using cheap foil with a torch lighter.


Please observe the following suggestions:

1) ALWAYS use heavy duty, good quality, brand name aluminum foil.
2) NEVER use a torch lighter or any other lighter that produces an unusually hot flame for vaporizing.
3) Keep the shiny side of the foil down and the dull side up.  (Put your "product" on the dull side and heat the shiny side.)
4) ALWAYS "stripe" the foil prior to adding any "product" to it.  You do this by slowly running a flame under the foil allowing it to burn off any contaminants.  Stripe the entire piece of foil.
5) Use the minimum amount of flame that you can to get the job done.  (This also saves "product".)

Happy chasing people.  Stay safe.
