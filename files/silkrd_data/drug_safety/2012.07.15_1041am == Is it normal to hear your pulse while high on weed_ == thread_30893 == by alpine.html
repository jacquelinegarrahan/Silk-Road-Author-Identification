Title: Re: Is it normal to hear your pulse while high on weed?
Post by: sdesu on July 23, 2012, 12:00 pm
There are two possible answers.

1. You're just being paranoid and are noticing things you usually wouldn't, such as your pulse. This usually will happen when it's quiet and you're stoned.

2. You could be having heart palpitations, which are unusually strong heart beats.

More than likely, since you're only hearing your pulse the possibility of a palpitation is low. However, if you FEEL your heart is pounding, racing, ect, and you aren't doing much physical activity, then that becomes a problem. Next time you smoke, check your heart rate. If it's unusually high and you're not doing anything besides sitting on the couch, then do your best to take it easy until you are sober. It would REALLY be recommended to see your doctor just to make sure everything is okay. You don't have to worry about them telling the cops you smoke cannabis, so you don't have to lie to him/her.

sdesu
