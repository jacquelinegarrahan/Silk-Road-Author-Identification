The very first time is better to use no more than 10-15 mg (even 5 can be enough). From this dose you are not going to get the full effect but there are two reasons why you should begin low: the first is that batches can change and the second that's the more important is that the first times you are going to smoke/vap (especially if you try to vap) DMT you are most likely to waste a lot in the process.

Now, if you start with 50 mg for example (an high dose) you are sure that even if you waste a lot some effect will happen; instead if with 10 mg and you feel nothing at all then you are sure that you are doing something incorrect. Learning to vap/smoke correctly is very important because it naturally waste less product (so more trips in return) and more importantly there's less casualty tied to the process: if you don't learn to smoke/vap it correctly and you use always large quantities to receive an effect you can receive with much less, it can in fact happen that for a case or for "luck" you will once in a while be able to smoke it fine and then you will have an effect that you didn't want to obtain and this can give you trouble. With DMT you want to know what you want from it.

When you have learned how to smoke/vap it correctly the usual dose (for a so-called "breakthrough" or NDE) is 20-30 mg; lower doses (10-15 mg) are so-called "plateau" ones and larger doses are for experienced users only if you want to do something specific (if you know how to vap/smoke DMT correctly there's usually no reason to do that much until you have not a normal resistance to the drug).
