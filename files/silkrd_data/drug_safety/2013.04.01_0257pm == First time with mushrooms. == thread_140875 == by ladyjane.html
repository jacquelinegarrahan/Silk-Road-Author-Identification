Title: Re: First time with mushrooms.
Post by: oldtoby on April 03, 2013, 02:58 am
mj is a nice catalyst for shrooms if you have some handy. Nature is good. Shrooms can play interpersonal havoc so make sure you like your sitter a lot; if there's any tension there, maybe try to have them nearby but not actually present. If you do go exploring, take the sitter, and avoid sober people. Ensure that you're not going to have to worry about your safety whatsoever.

For the first-time shroomer, be aware that thought loops are very common. Try to not get anxious about them. You may find yourself puzzling over something you've already thought about several times, and subsequently lost track. Take it easy. Nothing needs to be solved. Just go with the flow.
