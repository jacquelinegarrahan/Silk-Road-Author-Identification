Title: Heroin for the first time
Post by: InfiniteRegress on March 30, 2012, 07:48 pm
I just did heroin for the first time last night. Technically, it wasn't the first time, because about 12 years ago I did a small line, but it has been so long that I don't even remember how it felt. So, as far as my body is concerned, yesterday was the first time.

First, I should mention that I took a 50 mg tramadol about an hour or so before my package arrived. I got a .2 sample of tar from PJ, and was already prepared for it. I initially thought that about a quarter of it would be a good dose, but I decided to just do a little match head size to be safe, and I am definitely glad I did. I mixed it with a little water, put it in one of those syringes you use to give a baby medicine, layed on my bed with my head hanging off and squirted it up my nose. And holy fuck! In about 2 minutes, I felt a nice surge of happiness. Pretty much just like oxy, but different in a way that is difficult to explain. Soon after this, I started to feel a little nauseous, and thought I might throw up. I layed on my bed and that feeling went away. For the next hour or so, i drifted in and out of sleep and just felt pretty damn great in general.

The nausea came back, so I decided to make some dinner because I thought that would help. It didn't. I had two beers with my dinner, which was also probably a big mistake. It had been four hours since the initial dose, and I could still feel it, but I thought that it was beginning to fade, so I decided to dose again. I did about half as much as I did the first time, since it hit me so hard before. I got a nice little rush again, but the nausea was much worse this time. I went outside to let my dog out and smoke, and I started getting chills. I came back inside and went to the bathroom thinking that was going to throw up, but it went away. Any activity I did that required motion made me feel sick. Once I layed down to go to sleep, I felt fine.

The weird thing is that I still felt high for hours. I just kept drifting in and out of sleep, but whenever I woke up, I still felt high. This probably lasted for about 7 hours. That seems like way longer that it should, though. Is this normal if you don't have much of a tolerance (I only do opiates occasionally)?

Today I did an even smaller amount, and felt it, but had no nausea at all. It only lasted about an hour and a half, though. It seems really difficult to get just the right amount to feel good, without the nausea.

Anyway, that was my experience. I just felt like typing it out. Hopefully this is the right section of the forums to put this in.
