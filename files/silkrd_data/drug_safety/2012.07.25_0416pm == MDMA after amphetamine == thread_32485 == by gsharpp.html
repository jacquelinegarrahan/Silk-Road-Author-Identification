Title: Re: MDMA after amphetamine
Post by: digitbh on July 25, 2012, 07:04 pm
Don't drink alcohol when you do drugs. Generally it's just a bad idea. Most drugs cause you to become dehydrated, and alcohol just makes it worse. Alcohol will actually inhibit the MDMA and you will waste a perfectly good drug.

Don't mix MDMA with amphetamines. Both raise your heart rate and you get warm. Not a good combination. And the fucking comedown is a bitch. Enjoy them separately, not together.

Erowid actually has a lot of stories and experience writeups on combinations. I bet you can find this combination on there. A simple google search on my part turned up a lot of info.
