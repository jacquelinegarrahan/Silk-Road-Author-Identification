Amphetamine base is an oily liquid at room temperature so it has to be made into a salt (hydrochloride, hydrobromide, sulfate etc.) to become a crystalline substance you can actually use. Similarly, cocaine for snorting and injecting is actually cocaine hydrochloride, although cocaine base (aka crack cocaine) is used for smoking. Same thing with heroin.
If your amphetamine is a solid, even if it's wet, it's an amphetamine salt and good to use :)
25% purity would be rather high-end gear where I'm from.
