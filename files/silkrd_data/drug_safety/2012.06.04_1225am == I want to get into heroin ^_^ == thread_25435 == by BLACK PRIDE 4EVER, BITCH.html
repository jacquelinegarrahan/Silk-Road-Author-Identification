"Oxy" is short for "Oxycodone"

Oxycodone is sometimes referred to as "Hillbilly Heroine" as pain meds are super popular in the south and Oxycodone is an extremely potent opiate (Heroine is a similar opiate) that is available via prescription in large doses without anti-abuse measures (like acetaminophen**, which can cause serious damage to your liver)

Oxycodone comes in many forms. Most commonly:

Percocet** -
5mg Oxycodone/325mg OR 500mg Acetaminophen
7.5mg Oxycodone/325mg OR 500mg Acetaminophen
10mg Oxycodone/325mg OR 500mg Acetaminophen

Oxycontin -
10mg Oxycodone
20mg Oxycodone
40mg Oxycodone
80mg Oxycodone
160mg Oxycodone (Discontinued)

Roxys - 30mg Oxycodone (a.k.a. Roxycodone)

if you take any pills with Acetaminophen, you shouldn't snort them. Additionally any more than 1000mg at a time (i.e. one dosing) of Acetaminophen will put you at risk of liver damage, and doing over 4000mg of Acetaminophen over the course of an entire day also puts your liver at risk.

Some oxycodone pills you can snort, others you can't. Make sure you look up what you have and learn how to prepare it for snorting if don't want to take it orally.
(Oxycontin you could snort very easily, which is why it was criticized as a recreational drug)

My friend was an ex-junkie (Heroin addict) and said that Oxy's were honestly better than Heroin, but about 4x as expensive for the same effect. He said they were pretty much the same and I've heard that a lot. They're personally my pain med of choice, but I don't do Heroin or high doses.

There are lots of other forms of pain medication which a lot of people enjoy as well, others include fentanyl, dilaudid, oxymorphone, hydromorphone, etc.
