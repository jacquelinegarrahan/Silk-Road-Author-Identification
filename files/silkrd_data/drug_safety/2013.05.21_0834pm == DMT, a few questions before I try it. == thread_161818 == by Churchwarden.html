Tried twice today and no luck breaking through. My Machine was packed with wool too fine so once I started sucking it just caught fire which was decided unpleasant.

That was your error. Don't use steel wool, use Brillo. The steel wool is too thin and it will catch fire. You must use a steel wool that is very coarse, as the typical rubber pad, i.e. Brillo or Chore Boy (remember to burn the coating just in case; some brands of the rubber pads have a coating + various oils to sharp it that produces toxic fumes, you must burn them off.
