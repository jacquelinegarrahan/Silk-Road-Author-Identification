So you wanna smoke meth huh? Great, I'm your guy.

First, buy a milligram scale. Seriously, stop reading and buy a milligram scale. Don't come back until you have a milligram scale and STOP SMOKING until you have a milligram scale. Understand?

Next, how much can you smoke in one sitting and still be fine? That depends on what you mean by "fine". By now, you probably have developed quite the tolerance so I can't tell you to take the normal medical dosages which is 1.0mg/kg. You can probably take more than that. I HIGHLY suggest that you stop using and taper your tolerance until 1.0mg/kg is enough for you to get a great experience. However, you probably won't listen to that piece of advice huh? Okay then, your best guideline on a safe dosage is low enough that you don't experience any disabling comedown effects. You know that hangover the day after drinking too much and you can't get out of bed? That means you have incurred massive neurotoxicity. Alcohol causes minor permanent damages and nowhere near as bad as amphetamines can. If you have a significant comedown, YOU ARE TAKING TOO MUCH. Please heed this piece of advice. Use the comedown as a lesson for your future dosages - it should only be very minor symptoms if any. Don't worry about smoking though. You are lucky in that meth is not anymore dangerous with smoking as long as you are not binging. If you want to binge, (DON'T BINGE) use insufflation, oral, or anal. If you like taking high dosages, take 3-4 days break in between each dosage. For chronic daily use, the max you should use is 0.5mg/kg (although you could probably take higher on account of your tolerance).

If you like meth AND minimize the chances of fucking up your life, don't fucking binge. Set aside a maximum of one gram per month.
