Title: Re: Heroin: Tar compared to powders?
Post by: phubaiblues on October 24, 2011, 12:40 am
Welcome to SR!  First off, a recommendation of a great 'normal' website for any matters opiate/H related is opiophile.org...it will bounce you to forums.opiophile.org, if I remember right.  Great site, good, understanding people, and they know everything about all this.

My own take is always going to be pro-powder.  It's just real H to me, no comparison.  *Especially* if you are injecting.  I've seen twice now, the ravages of this sort of skin eating crap that can hit you if you *miss* tar...these guys had their arms bandaged all the time, and it was gross/gruesome to see what it had done...

The other, I've only heard about, but way too many credible stories of amputatons due to tar...and most of us have found out the hard way that it all too quckly destroys veins; they're gone, never come back.

That's the negative, and it's just one opinion.  I've had powder heroin in vietnam and in thailand, and it was  pure, no cooking, perfect high. 

Trouble is, in US, in past 20 years or so, the mexican cartels have just driven out the old powder sellers, and you are stuck with tar.  Many, who've been around a while, just will not 'fix' it...plugging is what works best, but it's just too nasty to describe...again, go to opiophile for details ;)

Quality is where there is plenty of room for argument: I've had really good, strong tar, and really cut-to-shit powder.  But any port in a storm.  And I remember when tar first hit up in chicago area, and some of my buds preferred it to powder, said it was stronger.

I'm a powder guy, in most areas, it wins, hands down.  Colombians do make good powder, and I can't for the life of me understand why the cartels don't just get a colombian chemist to show them how to make it.
But, just opinion...
