I've done it both ways ....

The glass tube (sold with a plastic rose in it) and metal mesh stuff is the easiest way to make a crack pipe, and it is completely effective. You can get huge hits off it.

I've also done the foil and ash method. I used a small plastic water bottle, put an empty pen tube in the side of the bottle to draw off of, used foil pressed and shaped in the bottle top to make a bowl, poked some holes in the foil, filled the foil bowl with cigarette ash, sprinkled crack on top.

You really can't go wrong with either of these methods. They both work extremely well. Myself, I prefer the foil and ash method, but for simplicity you can't beat the glass tube.
