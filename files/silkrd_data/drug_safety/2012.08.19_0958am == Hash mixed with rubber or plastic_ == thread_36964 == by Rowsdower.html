Use a magnifying glass to watch how it reacts when heated with a lighter. If it bubbles at all when it meets a naked flame don't touch it. A plasticy taste is common and can indicate that it has been mixed with henna.

You can also look at the colour of the smoke. It shouldn't be dark / black. Also, good hash / weed doesn't result in red-eye.
