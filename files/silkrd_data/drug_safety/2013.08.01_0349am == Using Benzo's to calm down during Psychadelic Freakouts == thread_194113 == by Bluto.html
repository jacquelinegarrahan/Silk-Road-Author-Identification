Title: Re: Using Benzo's to calm down during Psychadelic Freakouts
Post by: preacherman444 on August 02, 2013, 08:03 pm
Benzos more or less kill trips. You won't be sober. But anxiety, headspace, duration, and all other positive and negative effects will be greatly reduced. I recommend only taking them during a trip if you really need them, and even then it is better to try and work through the emotions instead of killing them with a pill. You have to be ready to deal with things that you may be unconsciously suppressing on psychedelics, and they can be painful on the way back out. That is one of the beautiful, healing aspects of psychedelics though, bringing those issues to the forefront and being able to deal with them.

That being said, I almost always have a benzo on hand while tripping, although I haven't needed it in a while. I find a good friend/experienced guide is better than any benzo.

Although I have not had a lot of 2C-B experience, I prefer it on the light side as far as doses. Sasha Sulgin called the minimum dose that could be felt but not noticed by others (that you're tripping) a "museum dose" as he apparently went to museums while on psychadelics. 16mg was the museum dose for 2C-B, and my first tries were with 18mg. I had later took some 28mg caps and had the same amount & duration of psychadelic effects, but a bit of a stomach ache for the first 1-1,5 hrs. But I have never been much on amphetamines or similar compounds because of the body loads.

2C-B isn't likely to cause a bad trip (IMHO) as the effects are almost entirely visual - everything had a rotoscoped look, like the movie "A Scanner Darkly".
