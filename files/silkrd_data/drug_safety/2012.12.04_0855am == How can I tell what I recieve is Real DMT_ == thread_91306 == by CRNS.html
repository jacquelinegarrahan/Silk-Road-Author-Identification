Post by: nothingmattress on December 04, 2012, 11:18 pm
DMT has a very specific smell, but the color and consistency of the powder can vary greatly depending on the method of extraction. Synthesized DMT is pure white, fine powder. Extracted DMT can be a shade of yellow, red, or brown and is usually chunky lightweight powder. Darker DMT means less purity, but that's not a bad thing. Darker DMT means more plant material / chemicals (harmala alkaloids they are called, I believe) are left in it, which increases its strength and lengthens its effects. For first-timer, it is suggested to go with pure DMT (lighter colors), it's crazy enough of experience as it is.

To get familiar with the smell, I think the best you can do is order small amounts from 2-3 vendors and compare smells. If they all smell the same, then you know what DMT smells like.

Here's how DMT behaves when heated. Even with small amount of heat (from a lighter placed 0.5 cm away) DMT will first become liquid, and then begin evaporating into white smoke. After it cools down, it tuns into black resin-ish stuff that sticks to glass like crazy. It can be washed away by acetone, alcohol, or bong cleaning liquid, I think.
