Got done farming, took a shower, and then went to a party.

Felt much the same as before I smoked earlier in the day. Took two monster hits of hash (second time ever BTW), and after that was blown the fuck away. Real hard to describe what was going on after that.

Next day I was tired as fuck, but felt great.

Never have I seen anything that synergizes so well with cannabis. NEVER.
