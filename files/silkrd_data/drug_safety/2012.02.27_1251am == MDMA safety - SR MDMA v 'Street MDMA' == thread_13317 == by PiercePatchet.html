Title: Re: MDMA safety - SR MDMA v 'Street MDMA'
Post by: catstate on February 27, 2012, 02:49 pm
Quote from: PiercePatchet on February 27, 2012, 12:51 am
I'm assuming it gets cut to shit like cocaine/heroin?

No one cuts MDMA with cocaine or heroin. Both of those are more valuable than MDMA; you'd have to be a complete moron to mix them into a cheaper drug and then sell them as the CHEAPER one. When people cut MDMA they usually use either an inactive cut, a random cathinone, or caffeine*. Check out ecstasydata.org for more details.

As for SR versus street, unless the E scene where you are is way better than it is where I am, you're much better off getting from a reliable seller here. I wouldn't put too much stock in the exact purity % people list here - any of the top rated sellers will hook you up with the best MDMA money can buy.

Title: Re: MDMA safety - SR MDMA v 'Street MDMA'
Post by: wowzers on February 27, 2012, 03:04 pm
Street MDMA in the UK is actually pretty pure, most of it comes from NL through the same sorts of channels that SR vendors acquire it.
The only thing I've seen MDMA cut with in the UK is sucrose and it's not just mixed in, it's been co-crystallised. In my experience though it tends to be near enough as pure as the stuff on Silk Road.
Occasionally cheap shit like 4-MEC is passed off as MD but it's not that common. You'd never see it cut with coke, the effects are totally different and coke is way more expensive.
