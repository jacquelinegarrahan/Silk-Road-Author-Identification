@shadowrundeusex

It all depends on dose if it's your first time taking LSD or any other psychedelics then you should start off with a 150-200 µg dose.

Anymore than 200µg for a first time user new to psychedelics then you may end up freaking out as I find that LSD tends to show you same rather unique and fucked shit from time to time...(lol that's a bit of an understatement).

All I can recommend is that you do your research and then decide for yourself if LSD is the right medicine for you.

Also it's nice to have some diazepam handy incase your trip turns bad if that happens take about 20mg diazepam and you will be fine you will still see colors and visuals but your anxiety will be gone. Also note if you have to take diazepam during your trip it will make the rest of the trip very boring.

Out of 8 LSD trips that I have done so far I have only had 2 bad trips and to be honest the bad trips ain't necessarily a bad thing yeah they can scare the shit out of you but the 2 bad trips that I have had I have learned a lot from them.

Hmm it seams I tend to ramble on when I post but the bottom line of what I am trying to say is if you do LSD start with a 150-200  µg dose and don't be afraid of potential bad trips embrace them and remember it's only a drug everything that is happening to you is only in your head and will go away in about 12 hours,
