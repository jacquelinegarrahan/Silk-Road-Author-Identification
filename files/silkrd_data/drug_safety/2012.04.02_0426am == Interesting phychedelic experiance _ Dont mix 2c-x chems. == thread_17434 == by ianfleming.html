Title: Interesting phychedelic experiance / Dont mix 2c-x chems.
Post by: ianfleming on April 02, 2012, 04:26 am
I had a hell of a trip with 10mg 2c-e and 15mg 2c-i, mixed together.
The 2 people I was with had a much better trip but only one of them had what might be called a good trip.

I had taken MDMA only 2 weeks prior and I blame that, and differences in bodychemistry to my experience.
In general I was not in control, I still do not remember bits of my trip. And the bits I do remember were somewhat mindfuckish.
I had auditory hallucinations (helicopter passing over (a few times) chattering teeth (other peoples), etc). I expected the 2c-i to make sure that the 2c-e trip stayed happy and good. Well that failed, miserably.

When I more or less regained control of myself (as I was coming down), about 6 hours after dosing I was still very tripped out. I realized I had chipped one of my front teeth quite nicely. One of my freinds had lost a lens of his glasses and a screw had popped out. For the next hour I worried that we had been tripped out of our minds for days and that we were losing our teeth like methheads. I checked my freinds' friends teeth (which was weird). I did not know that the female with us had a previous chip in her teeth, so as soon as I saw that I flipped. I got really worried that I had caused permanent damage to myself and these other people. They eventually got me talked down and I finished the trip nicely. (though the other male attempted suicide early in the trip, it was a pretty half-assed thing though (one of the crappiest attempts I've ever seen)).

It was VERY visual, many paintings (of airplanes (I have a few)) looked like those handheld "hologram" things that you tilt back and forth and they move a bit. Things moved and there were new colors and shit was off the wall. To quote a 'Talking Heads' song, "and things were looking like a movie". That was pretty cool.

The weirdest thing though, was that sometimes time would get really choppy and glitchy. It was like a video that was buffering every so slightly. It wasent like time just slowed, but rather progressed in little stunted increments. Has anyone else experienced this?
Is there a name for this phenomenon?
Usually this only lasted for a few moments.

Either way, it was PRETTY fucked up, the bits I do remember were wild and I went all kinds of inside and outside my own mind. I didn't really have fun throughout most of it. Not an experience I want to repeat.
