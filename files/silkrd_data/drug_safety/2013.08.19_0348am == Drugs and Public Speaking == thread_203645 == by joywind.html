I know that people have used cocaine for public speaking. Cocaine drops used to sold regularly for those who had to do public speaking. If you are worried about blushing and other effects of elevated heartrate during public speech, beta blockers might be a good option. In normal doses they would have very little side effects when giving a lecture, though you may want to stand behind some kind of stand to hold on to if the do cause dizziness and such.

You could combine things like alprazolam with amphetamines to basciallly speedball your way through the ordeal, but that would take very good timing and could end very badly if mistimed or misdosed.
