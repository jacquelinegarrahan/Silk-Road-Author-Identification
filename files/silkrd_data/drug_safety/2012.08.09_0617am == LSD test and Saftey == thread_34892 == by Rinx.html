Post by: Spunkaroo on August 09, 2012, 01:02 pm
I can't say anything about testing kits as I really don't know. But about the swallowing the tab remark, what you're referring to is the fact that NBOME chemicals aren't orally active, so swallowing the tab should have no effects if that's what's on it. That's an OK strategy, but be aware that DOx chemicals can fit on blotter and are orally active. Not only that but I consider them to be (from second hand research) the more dangerous of the two categories of RCs. So don't rely on that to prevent an unwanted trip with an unknown chemical.

Also, it should be noted that legit LSD tabs can have a taste if certain substances are used to lay the blotter (high proof alcohol I believe will do this) or if they are heavily printed (ink can be bitter.) I've had tabs with a taste that were legit as far as I could tell.

Above all, definitely start small dose wise until you're confident with what a substance is. Minimise any risks that way.
