First off, if you're ordering in the next two days, you want JesusofRave. No question at all there. However, he's closing down after the 24th, but taking & delivering orders until then. If you're not ordering this quickly, just check out the Avengers thread. They have plenty of good recommendations.

As far as dosage, what do you want to get out of the trip?

My first dose was reportedly ~200 ug. It was very fun; walls moved to music, grass flowed like water, etc. I did this with my roommate & it was a blast. But I still felt relatively normal & had an almost everyday mental state. We were able to function fairly normally. Went for a few walks around other people, went to a convenience store & bought Orange Juice (which I highly recommend!), & talked to people without them having a clue we were on acid.

However, my next dose was 440 ug & I felt like this was my first actual "trip". I could see music. At one point, one of the guys there had multiple, multicolored faces. When I closed my eyes, I was instantly in another world. This was an entirely different experience all together. Personally, I'd recommend a similar dose for your first time, unless you'll be in a public setting.

The most I've done up to this point is ~550 ug. This was also my first solo trip. I can't put into words what this experience was like honestly. On 440, I felt like I could see music, but on 550 I actually saw it. Music is more than just sound at this dose. It's a physical being & a story. The closest I can get to describing it-440 was my interpretation of the music. 550 was what the music actually was at its core. The mental space was great-I was the most at ease with the world & myself that I've ever been. Unless you've done similar substances, I would not suggest starting this high.
