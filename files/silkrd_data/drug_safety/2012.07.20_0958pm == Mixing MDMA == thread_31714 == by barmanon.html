Post by: 328502E on July 21, 2012, 12:27 am
Ok, here's the thing: if it's pure MDMA, there will be absolutely no difference if you add them together.  There aren't "types" of MDMA, so you are basically adding two of the same thing together and then taking it.

If they aren't both MDMA, you are mixing drugs.  Depending on what the drug is, it may or may not be safe.  But generally, if your dosage is low and your dealer isn't a total asshat, it is safe to combine the two powders, as most drugs that are similar to MDMA do not react badly with each other.

Don't mix drugs with alcohol. It's generally a bad idea. Especially with MDMA. Consider what MDMA does to your body. It fucks with your body temperature and raises your heart rate and dehydrates you. Do you really want to throw alcohol into that mix, and become more dehydrated? You won't have a good feel for if you're thirsty or not. You pretty much just have to force yourself to drink a glass of water every hour or so.

Also, alcohol inhibits MDMA and you won't get a proper roll off of it.

If you've never taken MDMA before start off with 100mg. There's a ratio to how much you should take based on how many pounds/kilograms you weigh. Try to find that. Overtime you may develop a tolerance, and that's normal. To avoid this tolerance try to only do MDMA once every 4-6 weeks. There's a good chance you won't lose the magic.

Taking it: if you have crystal, measure out 100mg. Crush the crystals up fine, put it in a bit of toilet paper, and swallow it whole. Easiest way if it's your first time. You can plug it anally, but why don't you just stick to oral use for the first couple rolls? Don't insuffulate MDMA unless you enjoy a hardcore nasal drip and burn for your entire ride.

Here's a good place for you to start reading if you're very interested in MDMA: http://wiki.bluelight.ru/index.php/MDMA:_Essential_Guide

Make sure you take the preloading/postloading process very seriously. It will help you in the long run.
