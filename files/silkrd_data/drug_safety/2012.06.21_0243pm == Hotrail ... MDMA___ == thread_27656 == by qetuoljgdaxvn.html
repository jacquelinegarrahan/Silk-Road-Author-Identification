Title: Hotrail ... MDMA???
Post by: qetuoljgdaxvn on June 21, 2012, 02:43 pm
was chilling with my partner at home last weekend, did a hotrail or 2 of ice and got to thinking how what mdma would be like hotrailed..

For curiosity's sake I've chucked small (20mg or so) md rocks into a clean pipe a couple of times before but while I could feel a tiny bit of a buzz all over the body, it not only made me waste 5 mins cleaning the super sticky brown/orange goo that md melts into, but stunk up my place and my lungs no doubt. A hotrail is a bit different though since the MD doesn't get the chance to sit in a hot spot as the powder literally gets superheated in milliseconds as it rushes into the nostrils, and I Just received 5g of PF's md and thought why not, let's give it a shot.

made a line of 20mg first to test it out.. Doubted I'd feel anything from it but I wanted a half decent amount of powder without it being too much in case the vapor is really bad or it ends up being shit, etc...

Heated up the pyrex straight pipe I use for hotrailing to about the same level as I do for meth, maybe a tiny bit less and railed the powder, and then................
Surprise!
Barely got the terrible smell/taste when you vape in a bowl, blew out a great big cloud of vape and i around 30 seconds started to feel a very mild come up/rush...
The "hot" tip of the pipe did get some goo on it as the powder unavoidably got stuck to part of the wall near the entrance, so I'd guesstimate me on only consuming 15g -ish

Partner wanted to try next and wanted a decent dose to begin with so I laid out 60mg which was hotrailed in 1 impressive motion.. This time there was considerably more goo on the tip of the rail (used a different) and I realised this happened because I heated the pipe too much. It seemed that when you hotrail mdma, you want the glass to be colder somewhat...

My turn agani! Laid out 60mg for myself and this time heated the pipe a bit less while still being very hot of course..
Railed the entire line, very minimal goo on the pipe's tip and not much on the mirror..  MASSIVE cloud of vapor and another 20 seconds later mdma signs everywhere! As soon as I uttered my first word I knew MD was hitting me because my voice sounded different, then so did the music, etc..

Didn't last a whole lot of time however and so in my opinion it's a petty wasteful ROA, however brings the fun of hotrailing - yes it's fun when you are good at it! Plus if you've eaten heaps and haven't had vitamins etc, your absorbtion would be pretty poor if you eat a cap but smoking bypasses all that.

Next time I do md which will of course be a standard 125 - 150mg cap, I mght do a hotrail once to top up, see how it goes!

Any thoughts on this? Or have others tried this already?
