Pre-notes 23/6/2012 -- Private notes

At some point today I intend to ingest by my calculations 4x 70µg (purple lotus flower blotter) of lysergic acid diethylamide (LSD) = 280µg

I've been asking myself lately why...What has driven me to experiment on myself with psychedelic drugs.

In the beginning of my journey I just wanted to see cool looking visuals and trip and escape reality.....aka party like a stupid dumb ass....but along the way in the middle of all the LSD and 2C-B experiments I have found something more.......something that transcends definition....challenges the very thought of what we think of as a reality. (mystical experience?)

But yet I don't truly understand what it is.....Are psychedelics a gateway to staring at the face of God and understanding the creation of man and understanding oneself. Can you break down the gates of heaven? I don't know.

LSD for me has personality challenged the very concept of a reality and even the existence of a God. (I just want to add a personal note that I consider myself religious I believe that there is something out there that in essence could be considered a God but yet I don't go shouting the lord's word in the middle of town like a fanatic) But since using LSD I no longer have faith/believe in organized religion it's just a lie I see it as people who pose as priests and saints with the only goal of money and power over people.

Sorry I keep rambling on about my personal beliefs/opinions...To be honest I don't even know if am going to post this on the SilkRoad forums as this is all very personal to me. but I need to type out my feelings I need to know where am going and how/what psychedelic can show me.

I want to understand myself and God if there is such a thing....There is something out there but weather or not it could be considered a God is debatable....

As you may have noticed am I'm experiencing a bit of a loss/crisis in terms of my spirituality. (Sometimes I envy atheists) But yet I cant understand how someone can't believe in a God.

But anyway it's my hope that psychedelics may provide an answer to what am seeking. I have tried LSD and 2C-B and out of the 2 compounds I have tried I have only found LSD to be useful in terms of mystical experience and mind expansion. While with 2C-B I have found is more of a party drug with very little spiritual depth.(Might be good for sex though)

As well as using LSD I intend to use at some point Psilocybin (magic mushrooms) But I have yet to find anyone who sells them in the UK and there is no way in hell I'm going to order mushrooms international.

I don't know but I will say that this will be my last trip for a while at least 2 months. I been using LSD a lot lately and at times I fell like am becoming a schizophrenic madman.

I just don't know I guess I will see what happens after my trip. But with that said if I do go super crazy I got diazepam and Xanax about 130+ pills on hand in total lmao.........But no worries I wont OD I have to much to live for and I love life even if am an LSD induced schizophrenic madman trying to understand the face of God and the creation of mankind



Depending on the outcome of my trip I may or may not post this log on the forums:

But if I do I would like to here your opinions on life and the nature of humanity and the possibility of a God. It might make for a good discussion. Also I would like to know how psychedelics have helped other people and how it has changed you for better or worse.

But anyway I'm going to listen to some music lift my mood watch some Futurama and then take some LSD and venture into the unknown recesses of my mind like a true Psychonaut.

Psychonautics what a truly fascinating word this will be LSD trip #11 + about 8 or so 2C-B trips but yet I fell as though I have barely scratched the surface of my mind.....life what an interesting cycle life/death/rebirth
