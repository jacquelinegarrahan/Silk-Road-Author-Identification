Post by: Yoshitoshi on March 27, 2013, 07:57 am
Don't cap it! It will be a waste, it can make you nauseous, it can cause loss of muscle control in e.g. legs, and will not result in any pleasurable psychedelic effects. I know this cuz years ago I was sold some pills as E that turned out to be K.

Personally, I don't like needles for RCs.

So you can snort it, or if you want to be more exotic, you can plug it i.e. stick it up your ass! As a first-timer, your nose is the better bet.

Dosage well covered here:

http://dkn255hz262ypmii.onion/index.php?topic=135877.msg926095#msg926095

I like to take it at home, after some stimulants, with deep sounds to propel the journey.

It does not combine well with other downers, even alcohol.

Title: Re: First Time Ketamine Suggestions (Dose/Method)
Post by: Yoshitoshi on March 27, 2013, 08:12 am
And another thing, for your nose, you want it be as fine a powder as possible.

Often, it arrives as crystals, and these can hurt, and won't be absorbed as efficiently.

Your best bet is to use a little pestle & mortar thingy, to grind it into "chalk dust"

Use a smooth marble or ceramic one, not a rough wooden or pottery one.

You might have one already, or be able to find one in a homeware store for a few bucks. It will pay for itself in no time!

Post by: jorg796 on March 27, 2013, 09:44 am
Snort the K. Snorting isn't as bad as you think. It just tickles your nose and a slight burn, but nothing unpleasant.
I've heard IM (injecting into muscle) is also good. I don't inject, so I can't say. But snorting it works well. If you eat it, it don't feel the same and most people feel sick if they eat K.
You will feel a bit drunk to start with and as it hits you more, you will want to sit down or lie down. You will feel very heavy and walking / moving will be a lot of effort.
You will also feel dreamy and in a bit of a good mood. Shut your eyes and enjoy it.

Also, since it's your first time it won't be as good. Most people get a bit anxious / worried / nervous about what the drug will do to them when they drug a new drug.
So the first time probably won't be as good as your future experiences. Also, if you mix with weed it is very nice. But be careful mixing it with alcohol or weed. Both make you drowsy / sleepy and combinations can be unpredictable. I had some alcohol, weed + some K once. The K smashed me after the alcohol and weed. I was so out of it. Luckily K doesn't last long though.

Enjoy!

I'm fairly new to Ketamine, but I would recommend getting yourself a magic bullet. Any decent head shop should carry them. It's just a trap door that fits onto the top of a vial for taking bumps. Start by insulfating small bumps (you can dump them out to see the pile size) every 5-10 minutes until you reach the desired effect. If you get a little too high, don't worry.
