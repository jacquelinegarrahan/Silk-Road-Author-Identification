Title: Re: Best cannabis for sufferers of panic attacks?
Post by: sourman on August 02, 2012, 10:43 pm
Dunno about the OP (who mentioned hallucinations), but the 2nd poster seems to have classic panic attack symptoms.

Most people who freak out after smoking weed are having panic attacks, not psychotic episodes. Unless you weren't you and/or didn't know what was real anymore, odds are you were suffering from the former. I would talk to someone about the high anxiety that likely caused your panic. That shit is not good for you, especially later on in life when you have to worry about your heart, blood pressure, and strokes. Get it taken care of while you still can, but try not to get hooked on benzos or anything like that. Psychedelics will be hit or miss, as in you may have a life changing revelation or a crushing bad trip that quadruples your anxiety.

If anyone has similar problems, try smoking smaller amounts and in good settings. If you can find indica strains like Master Kush, blazing will be far easier on you than if you were smoking a sativa. There's a good chance your panic attack will return because you are now conditioned to associate weed with the feeling of panic and bad thoughts. Expect this, but also know that it is just anxiety, and you don't have to think bad thoughts. It's all in your head.
