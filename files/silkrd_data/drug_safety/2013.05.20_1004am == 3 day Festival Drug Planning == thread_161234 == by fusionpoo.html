Post by: leaf on May 20, 2013, 04:21 pm
Mmmm!  that sounds like quite a exciting line up.   I would give a thumbs up to mid doses of 2c-b and MDMA as both being safe substances for wild festivals(Cannabis is a good neuro-protectant, so smoke lots while your on these stimulators).  Both tend to leave your Ego in tact, worst case scenario you end up trusting someone more then you should(smiles, hugs, etc).    Be careful with the LSD if your not a very experienced tripper or don't have a really tight crew that will be keeping a eye on you.  Especially don't go mixing it with Cannabis unless you are feeling especially safe, that can boost the effects to mind melting levels.  Xannax and a safe place to retreat to is a good idea in any case(dissolve under the tounge you will feel the loops melt away).   Also, with the DMT if you can find a place in nature that you can sit peacefully for 10 minutes you will probably appreciate it more than you would in a hotel room,  but if you have to, maybe burn a candle and turn the lights out with some good music.  I have also found light doses of DMT mixed in with my joints on the dance floor have always been a welcomed boost, but you have to know what a light dose is.
