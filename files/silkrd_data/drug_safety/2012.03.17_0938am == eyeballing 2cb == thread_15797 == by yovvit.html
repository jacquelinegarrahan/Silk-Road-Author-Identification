Title: Re: eyeballing 2cb
Post by: 12guhdfg3 on March 17, 2012, 01:31 pm
Because of the tiny differences in-between a small and a large dose of 2c-b I really would recommend investing in a scale that is accurate to the milligram, as the mistakes you could make by just guesstimating the amount of dose you're giving to yourself/someone could result in either a hugely overwhelming or underwhelming trip. Besides, if you're an SR member, who knows when else that could come in handy in future ;)

EDIT: Come to think of it, 2c-b is also soluble, so if you had a means of accurately measuring small amounts of water you could dissolve what you have into the water, just make sure you memorize/write down the ratio. Something like 125mg in 300mL of water would give you 2.4mg per 1mL of water, so you simple apply 12.5mL of your solution to whatever you're consuming for a 30mL dose. Remember, only do this if you have an accurate way of measuring out your solution.
