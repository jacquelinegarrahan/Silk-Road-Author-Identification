Let's start off with the Concept of a Digital Identity. A Digital Identity is compromised of two Elements, the first Element is something that verifies a Person with some Personal Information along with a Photo. The second Element is something that Verifies the Person is living at a certain Address. For a more in depth guide on what constitutes acceptable Aspects of the Elements see the thread Mt.Gox AML/KYC Process Explained: https://bitcointalk.org/index.php?topic=86224

So the Key Aspect here that makes a Digital Identity Service different from say a Physical Fake ID Card used to get into a bar is it's entirely Digital as all the Content resides in Computer Images and nothing else. I made a post some time ago detailing all Forgery Services provided at Silk Road at that time and the majority are for Physical Fake ID Cards as can be seen at this post: http://dkn255hz262ypmii.onion/index.php?topic=184022.msg1335061#msg1335061

Known Services that Accept Digital Identification Online: Banks, Bitcoin Exchanges, Mail Forwarders and Virtual Office Providers.

What needs to be kept in mind is while not all Services requiring Identification keep track of IP Addresses there's a very high possibility certain Banks and Bitcoin Exchanges do and if they aren't will eventually be forced to do so. A solution for this dilemma can be found in the following post: http://dkn255hz262ypmii.onion/index.php?topic=177551.msg1535342#msg1535342 - I will most likely offer a Service where I've already registered some Dedicated IP Addresses through this method at Silk Road in the somewhat near future.
