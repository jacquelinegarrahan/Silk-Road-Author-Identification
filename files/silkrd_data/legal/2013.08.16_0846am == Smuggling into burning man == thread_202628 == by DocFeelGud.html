Title: Re: Smuggling into burning man
Post by: datalore on August 16, 2013, 10:51 am
We've gone for several years and never had any troubles with LEOs. Just make sure you obey all traffic laws, don't give any reasons to be pulled over and you will be fine. Always keep any substances in your trunk and NEVER consent to searches (except to the BM staff who are only searching to make sure you aren't sneaking in other humans).  As long as I have been going (this will be my 5th year) there has been no police or dogs at the gate. They do patrol around the streets and especially around sound camps, but as long as you are not using anything in the open you should be fine.  If you can help it, try to not have any drugs on you on the way out, the police are usually out in full force trying to write every last ticket they can before the city clears out. If you do get caught with weed you'll probably just get a ticket, the Nevada ACLU has a camp at BM and can help out if you end up needing to fight a minor ticket for herb or other bullshit charges like 'disorderly conduct'.

Also, just a general word of caution, remember that you're in a seriously harsh environment on the playa. Drink lots of water and avoid alcohol like the plague while on other substances. If you OD or hurt yourself doing something stupid, the nearest hospital is a very expensive helicopter ride away.

Post by: HenryC0833 on August 19, 2013, 09:19 pm
Altoid tins make great ash trays.  But you don't have to hide your drugs in one.  Your vehicle will be searched at the gate, but read the FAQ's: "We aren't looking for your stash."  If you gave the dude a roll he would probably kiss you on the lips. 

There are  a lot of LE at BM, they are welcomed and BM staff are in radio communication with them.  This is good, it's a big town and emergencies do happen from time to time.  They will bust people for obviously using drugs.  The most common drug bust is for smoking weed.  They just follow the smell.  They do not hassle you for running around out of your mind on hallucinogens. 

My advice is to just be discreet.  Don't have any drugs out in plain sight.  Drop in privacy and you should be fine. 

Speaking of which, nobody walks from camp to camp begging for drugs.  Ignore this behavior and for pete's sake don't give anybody drugs just cuz they asked for them.  Unless it's some ultra-hot naked chick, of course.  Then it's OK.
