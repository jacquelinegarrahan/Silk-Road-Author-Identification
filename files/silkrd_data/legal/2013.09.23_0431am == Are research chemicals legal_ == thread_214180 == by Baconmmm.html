If you're in the US, tell your friend to do their research.

First, define "research chemical." Name something Dr. Shulgin worked on, it's been scheduled in some form or another. Look up documentaries on "bath salts" that discuss the whole concept of designer drugs. If one thing's chemical composition is named illegal, just adjust the chemical structure to make a new chemical that is now quasilegal, but not the same as the illegal one previously.

Overall, research chemicals are going to be illegal in all cases, because their chemical composition, synthesis process, effects and risks are known. Maybe not known very well or in-depth, as reflected in Erowid's warnings regarding RC's, but they are undoubtedly recognized by LE, and that's the answer to your question right there.
